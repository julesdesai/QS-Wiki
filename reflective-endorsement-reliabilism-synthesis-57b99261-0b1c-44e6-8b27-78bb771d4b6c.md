# Reflective Endorsement Reliabilism

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Is reliability sufficient for knowledge if one is unaware of the process's reliability?](is-reliability-sufficient-for-knowledge-if-one-is-unaware-of-the-processs-reliability-antithesis-c223cf53-5f6f-4ac5-a3ca-33b6a6e3d9dc.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed through a reliable process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person endorses the reliability of the belief-forming process upon reflection**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is formed through a reliable process, and they endorse the process's reliability upon reflection**
