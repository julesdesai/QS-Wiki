# Can true belief based solely on context be considered knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9e08113a-2c38-4abb-b271-b86568816840
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism on Epistemic Justification](contextualism-on-epistemic-justification-synthesis-c8f3ec75-75e9-400a-9969-d95699e610cc.md)

## Content

**In a situation where a person’s belief is true and justified only by a specific context but false or unjustified in others, it challenges the idea that knowledge is dependent purely on context.**, **In such cases, it is counterintuitive to assert knowledge when the justification is fragile and context-dependent.**
