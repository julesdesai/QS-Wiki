# Does belief always imply knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ba4793d0-f366-4acf-87d1-af28c4fc97d1
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism with Pragmatic Constraint](infinitism-with-pragmatic-constraint-synthesis-44356aca-8ef0-4341-a1b2-865cb5949b96.md)

## Content

**A person can hold a belief while also acknowledging doubt, suggesting they may not truly know the proposition they believe.**, **Suppose a person believes a false proposition due to misinformation. Intuitively, believing does not necessarily equate to knowing if the proposition is false.**
