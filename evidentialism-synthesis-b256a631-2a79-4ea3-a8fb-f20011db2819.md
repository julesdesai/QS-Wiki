## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have adequate evidence to support the proposition**, **The adequacy of evidence for knowledge does not change with conversational context or stakes**
