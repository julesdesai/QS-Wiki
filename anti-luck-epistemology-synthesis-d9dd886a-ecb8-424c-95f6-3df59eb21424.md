## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that justification eliminates epistemic luck**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that belief, truth, and robust justification eliminate luck**
