# How can direct perception be verified if devices are involved?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** db14b8bd-5562-4323-b47e-0d0bed0c1340
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Critical Direct Realism](critical-direct-realism-synthesis-6462aad1-66cc-4ce3-ad99-1e3d9c26fcac.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a person uses a virtual reality headset that perfectly mimics reality. Intuitively, we either have to say that they are perceiving directly or that they are experiencing a simulated reality. If we say they are perceiving directly, then it challenges the notion of reality. If we say they are experiencing a simulated reality, then it is not direct perception.**
