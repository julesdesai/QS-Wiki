## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres with a system of beliefs in a holistic manner**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief coheres holistically with the system**, **Emotional biases must be integrated into the system through holistic coherence**
