**Parent:** [What role does the clarity of intellectual virtues play in constituting knowledge?](what-role-does-the-clarity-of-intellectual-virtues-play-in-constituting-knowledge-antithesis-e63bb5eb-f53d-4954-850b-439ee4a8f68c.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief tracks the truth by forming true beliefs and avoiding false beliefs**, **Truth-tracking involves forming beliefs through processes sensitive to the truth even if not through intellectual virtues**
