## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief tracks the truth consistently across close possible scenarios**, **Truth tracking does not depend on fitting a specific design plan**
