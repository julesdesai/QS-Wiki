## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief could not easily be false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief could not easily be false, they believe the proposition, and the proposition is true**
