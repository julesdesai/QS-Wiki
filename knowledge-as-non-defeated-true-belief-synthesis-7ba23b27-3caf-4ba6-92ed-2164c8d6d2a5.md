# Knowledge as Non-Defeated True Belief

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 7ba23b27-3caf-4ba6-92ed-2164c8d6d2a5
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge be useless?](can-knowledge-be-useless-antithesis-8342035c-9026-400f-a9c5-75934a284046.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is not defeated by new evidence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is not the result of manipulation**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, their belief is not defeated by new evidence, and the belief is not the result of manipulation**
