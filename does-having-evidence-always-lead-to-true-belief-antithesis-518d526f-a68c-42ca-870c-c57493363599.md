# Does having evidence always lead to true belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 518d526f-a68c-42ca-870c-c57493363599
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Evidentialism](evidentialism-synthesis-bfc3ee3a-740c-4ee5-bb7b-12c5efc1c03c.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case where a person has overwhelming but misleading evidence that a chemical is safe, leading them to believe it is safe. Intuitively, we either have to say that having evidence that is misleading does not ensure the truth of the belief, or that the belief is not justified. If we say the belief is justified but false, then we must acknowledge that evidence can lead to justified false beliefs.**
