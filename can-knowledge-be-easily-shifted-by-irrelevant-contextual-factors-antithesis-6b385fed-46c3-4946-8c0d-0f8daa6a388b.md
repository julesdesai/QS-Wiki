# Can knowledge be easily shifted by irrelevant contextual factors?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 6b385fed-46c3-4946-8c0d-0f8daa6a388b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Moderate Contextualism](moderate-contextualism-synthesis-a2419722-fa3b-4f65-af9b-d94e8c32837e.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person knows a proposition while in a quiet room, but when moved to a noisy room, others claim they no longer know due to distractions. Intuitively, it seems incorrect that such irrelevant factors should alter knowledge status.**
