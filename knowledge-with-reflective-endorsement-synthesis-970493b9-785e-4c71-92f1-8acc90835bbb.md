# Knowledge with Reflective Endorsement

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 970493b9-785e-4c71-92f1-8acc90835bbb
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is a properly functioning cognitive system enough without understanding?](is-a-properly-functioning-cognitive-system-enough-without-understanding-antithesis-c99ef5cb-7302-4e66-9461-620b9b46ff9e.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief to be produced by a properly functioning cognitive system**, **Reflective endorsement of the belief by the person is necessary for knowledge**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is produced by a properly functioning cognitive system, and the person reflectively endorses the belief**
