## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person has a belief in the proposition formed by a reliable cognitive process**, **Intuition is considered a reliable cognitive process in cases where intuitive knowledge is present**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the person's belief in the proposition is both formed by a reliable process and the proposition is true**
