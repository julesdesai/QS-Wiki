# Is belief necessary for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 332170bf-9bc4-4c38-b7b8-81d38d231651
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Epistemic Justification Theory](epistemic-justification-theory-synthesis-d0523946-79ba-4279-979e-9ba317ba7953.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which someone has all the evidence and correct deductions regarding a particular truth but does not actively believe it. Intuitively, we might still consider that they have knowledge.**, **It is implausible that belief is strictly required for knowledge, as one might base actions successfully on true propositions without actively believing them.**
