# Is coherence alone enough to justify belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d53c859a-522f-400c-ad67-4bb0bc071543
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism with Empirical Integration](coherentism-with-empirical-integration-synthesis-ad29c58b-c93d-496f-a5cd-3f8247d72658.md)

## Content

**A belief system can be internally coherent while being entirely detached from reality.**, **A person could hold a coherent set of beliefs based on fictional content without any contact with empirical evidence.**, **If coherence alone were sufficient for justification, then fictional universes could be considered knowledge, which is implausible.**
