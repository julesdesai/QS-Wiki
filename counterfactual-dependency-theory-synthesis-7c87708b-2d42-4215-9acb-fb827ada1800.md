## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief would not be held if the proposition were false**, **This dependency ensures that beliefs align with how things actually are**
