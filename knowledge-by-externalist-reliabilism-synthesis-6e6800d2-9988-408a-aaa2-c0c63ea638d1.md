# Knowledge by Externalist Reliabilism

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 6e6800d2-9988-408a-aaa2-c0c63ea638d1
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is justification always necessary for knowledge?](is-justification-always-necessary-for-knowledge-antithesis-fa9799f3-da32-4fda-8914-b15b05614735.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed by an epistemically reliable process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is formed by an epistemically reliable process**
