## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arise from intellectual virtues or reliable cognitive faculties**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their true belief is produced by intellectual virtue**
