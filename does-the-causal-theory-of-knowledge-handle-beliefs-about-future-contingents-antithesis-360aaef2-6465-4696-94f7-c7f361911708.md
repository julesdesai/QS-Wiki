# Does the causal theory of knowledge handle beliefs about future contingents?

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that beliefs about future events, like "It will rain tomorrow," often lack a present causal connection to future outcomes, yet can be known if based on reliable prediction methods.**, **The causal theory struggles to accommodate knowledge claims about future events where the causal connection is not yet established.**
