## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no defeaters for their true belief**, **A defeater is a fact that, if known, would undermine one's justification for a belief**
