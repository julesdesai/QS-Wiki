# Can practical success be consistent with changing truths?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 442e455b-4a14-4382-a747-d8dbd2c57138
**Depth:** 2
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatism](pragmatism-thesis-503162b2-d578-477c-93d4-606bce767109.md)

## Content
