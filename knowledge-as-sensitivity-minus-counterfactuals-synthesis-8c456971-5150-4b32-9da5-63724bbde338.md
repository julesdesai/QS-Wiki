# Knowledge as Sensitivity minus Counterfactuals

**Parent:** [Does sensitivity handle cases where belief is resilient against necessary truths?](does-sensitivity-handle-cases-where-belief-is-resilient-against-necessary-truths-antithesis-a9158467-1604-4b2d-90ee-3dbbd1152e0b.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for belief to be generally sensitive to the truth of similar propositions being tested**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is generally sensitive to similar contexts**
