# Is knowledge possible without belief rooted in an understanding of reliability?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9873e1dc-ea48-4293-a7f8-ea73909039dc
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilist Virtue Epistemology](reliabilist-virtue-epistemology-synthesis-a48fe91b-846b-4740-a801-397d1be1c53f.md)

## Content

**A person can acquire true beliefs without understanding or considering the reliability of the process by which they were obtained.**, **If knowledge requires some form of understanding of the process, lack of awareness of the process challenges the knowledge claim.**
