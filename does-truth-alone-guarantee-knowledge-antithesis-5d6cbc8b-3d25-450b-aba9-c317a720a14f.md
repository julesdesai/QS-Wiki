# Does truth alone guarantee knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5d6cbc8b-3d25-450b-aba9-c317a720a14f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Truth-Conditional Contextualism](truth-conditional-contextualism-synthesis-531a396e-31ae-4fe8-8636-7f56a6debb5b.md)

## Content

**A scenario in which a proposition is true solely by accident does not constitute a case of knowledge for someone who merely believes it to be true.**, **An event in which a proposition randomly becomes true after being false still fails to confer knowledge on someone who believed it when it was false.**
