# Does Nozick’s theory accommodate knowledge from testimonial sources?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f2ec08a1-efa7-4e03-9c3b-8214b685c835
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Knowledge via Nozick's Tracking Theory](knowledge-via-nozicks-tracking-theory-synthesis-d0a4215f-4dc1-4dd8-847c-fc9822412f21.md)

## Content

**Consider a case in which Alice believes a historical fact because a reliable historian told her, and it is true.**, **In nearby possible worlds, the historian could be mistaken or deceptive, yet Alice might still believe the fact due to reliance on trust.**, **It seems incorrect to say Alice doesn’t know the historical fact simply because of these testimonial vulnerabilities in some nearby possible worlds.**
