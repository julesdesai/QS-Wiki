# Can past experiences influence the justification given by 'seeming'?

**Node ID:** 163d8d29-aa3b-4e50-9e70-be4a8e903d1d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that past experiences can shape the way things seem to us, introducing bias. This principle together with the idea that all 'seemings' provide prima facie justification entails that biases could lead to unjustified beliefs being treated as justified.**
