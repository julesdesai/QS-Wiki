# Knowledge as Process-Externalist Justification with Safety

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can external justifications adequately address higher-order doubts?](can-external-justifications-adequately-address-higher-order-doubts-antithesis-bee89207-70bf-49cc-ada9-44fe65afedb5.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed by a process that would not easily deliver a false belief (safety)**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is formed by a process with safety**
