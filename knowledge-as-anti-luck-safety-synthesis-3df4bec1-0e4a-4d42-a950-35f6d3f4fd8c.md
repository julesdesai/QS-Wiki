# Knowledge as Anti-Luck Safety

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 3df4bec1-0e4a-4d42-a950-35f6d3f4fd8c
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge be too easily undermined?](can-knowledge-be-too-easily-undermined-antithesis-c6dc11ad-a3b9-4bd7-a666-011a03a26e23.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is not true merely as a matter of luck**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is not true merely as a matter of luck**
