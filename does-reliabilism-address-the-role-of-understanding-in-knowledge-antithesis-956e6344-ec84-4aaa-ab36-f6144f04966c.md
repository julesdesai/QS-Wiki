# Does Reliabilism Address the Role of Understanding in Knowledge?

## Content

**Consider a person who has a belief produced by a reliable process but lacks understanding of the mechanism behind it. Intuitively, understanding seems necessary for knowledge, suggesting a gap in the reliabilist account.**, **There is a plausible principle that understanding is crucial to knowledge, which might challenge purely process-based accounts.**
