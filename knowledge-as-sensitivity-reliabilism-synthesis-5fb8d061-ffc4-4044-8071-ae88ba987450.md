## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **A belief is sensitive if it would not be held in the nearest possible world where the proposition is false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is sensitive**
