## Content

**Knowledge requires that one's belief tracks the truth**, **A person's belief tracks the truth if, in relevant scenarios, they would not believe it if it were false**, **A person's belief tracks the truth if, in relevant scenarios, they do believe it when it is true**, **The relevance of scenarios must be recalibrated over time as new evidence emerges**, **The tracking condition is satisfied only if the belief continues to match the current evidence**
