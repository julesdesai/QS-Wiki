## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that belief verification methods confirm the belief's safety**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe it, the proposition is true, and the verification of belief safety is fulfilled**
