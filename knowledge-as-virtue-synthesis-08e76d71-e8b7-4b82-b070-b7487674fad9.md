## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief stems from an intellectual virtue**, **An intellectual virtue includes the reliable formation of beliefs in accordance with the truth**
