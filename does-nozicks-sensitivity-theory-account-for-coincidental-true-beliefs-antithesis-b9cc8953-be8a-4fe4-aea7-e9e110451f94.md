# Does Nozick's Sensitivity Theory account for coincidental true beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b9cc8953-be8a-4fe4-aea7-e9e110451f94
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Nozick's Sensitivity Theory](nozicks-sensitivity-theory-synthesis-6e1ddae7-9a08-404e-ac91-e01a19448f49.md)

## Content

**A person might believe that a flipped coin landed heads based on their intuition, and it happens to be true**, **Nozick's theory states that a person knows a proposition if their belief is sensitive**, **Trusting intuition alone does not ensure sensitivity even if a belief is true by coincidence**
