# Can knowing a false proposition be virtuous?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9c20027f-50b1-4d64-861f-7547715d268e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Integrated Virtue-Responsibilism](integrated-virtue-responsibilism-synthesis-06dcde70-c679-48b5-94ca-324e3064105a.md)

## Content

**There can be beliefs formed through an intellectual virtue like open-mindedness that lead to belief in a proposition that is false.**, **Intuitively, the display of virtue in belief formation does not require the resulting belief to be true.**
