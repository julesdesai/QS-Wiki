## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is at least one strong justification for the belief**, **Pragmatic consequences can be a form of justification but are not required**
