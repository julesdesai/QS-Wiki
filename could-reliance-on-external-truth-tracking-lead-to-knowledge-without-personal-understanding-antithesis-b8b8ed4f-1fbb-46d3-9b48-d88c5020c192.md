# Could reliance on external truth-tracking lead to knowledge without personal understanding?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b8b8ed4f-1fbb-46d3-9b48-d88c5020c192
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Nozick's Truth-Tracking Theory](nozicks-truth-tracking-theory-synthesis-1a215329-c53d-4dbe-9509-6b517d1bb8ad.md)

## Content

**Imagine a case where someone holds a belief in a technologically advanced society where all false propositions are instantly corrected by an external system. Intuitively, we might not want to attribute knowledge to beliefs held due to passive reaction to a tracking system.**, **It is implausible that dependency on external systems alone without individual insight accomplishes true knowledge.**
