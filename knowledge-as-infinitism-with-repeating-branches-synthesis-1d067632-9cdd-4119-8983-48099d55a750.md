# Knowledge as Infinitism with Repeating Branches

**Parent:** [Does the requirement of non-repeating reasons create a paradox?](does-the-requirement-of-non-repeating-reasons-create-a-paradox-antithesis-19b8cab8-c754-4da3-ab5b-bec0c3db8a18.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification for the belief consists of an infinite series allowing for repeating branches of reasons**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification consists of an infinite series allowing for repeating branches of reasons**
