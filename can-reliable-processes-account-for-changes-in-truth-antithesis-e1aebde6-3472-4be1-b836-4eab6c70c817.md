# Can reliable processes account for changes in truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e1aebde6-3472-4be1-b836-4eab6c70c817
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism](reliabilism-synthesis-27039b77-724d-4113-8b7b-b992ae688652.md)

## Content

**A reliable process may produce a true belief at one point in time but the belief later becomes false due to changing circumstances.**, **In this changing scenario, we may intuitively feel the belief should no longer be labeled as knowledge.**, **Reliability over time requires the cognitive process to adapt, which may not always occur.**
