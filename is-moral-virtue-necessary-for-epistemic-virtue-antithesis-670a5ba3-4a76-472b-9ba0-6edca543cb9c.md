# Is moral virtue necessary for epistemic virtue?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 670a5ba3-4a76-472b-9ba0-6edca543cb9c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology Summary](virtue-epistemology-summary-synthesis-10eec969-c917-4d6d-bc9d-ac9f02dc3f98.md)

## Content

**Consider a person who holds true beliefs due to their honesty and diligence, which are often thought of as virtues.**, **There are cases where a morally questionable person, through manipulation or deceit, holds true beliefs.**, **While intellectual virtues may promote knowledge, moral virtues may not be necessary for true beliefs.**
