# Knowledge with Verified Reasoning

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [What if manipulation leads to justified beliefs?](what-if-manipulation-leads-to-justified-beliefs-antithesis-fcda31cf-0de1-4468-9bbb-e1d9d579e203.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief has practical consequences or usefulness**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief can be verified by independent reasoning**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief has practical consequences or usefulness, and the belief can be verified by independent reasoning**
