## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person can rule out relevant alternatives**, **Knowledge involves an ability to distinguish the true proposition from relevant false propositions**
