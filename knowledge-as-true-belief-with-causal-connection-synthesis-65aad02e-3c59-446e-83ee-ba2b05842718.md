# Knowledge as True Belief with Causal Connection

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is a causal connection between the fact and their belief**, **A person knows a proposition if it is true, they believe it, and there is a causal connection between the fact and their belief**
