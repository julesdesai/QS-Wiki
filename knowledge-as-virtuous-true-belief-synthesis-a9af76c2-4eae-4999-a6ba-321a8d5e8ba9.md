## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed through an intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they hold a true belief formed through an intellectual virtue**
