# Is it possible to conceive of knowledge as inferentially constructed rather than perceptual?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0e22c5b0-1279-43a2-8fb5-93115dc334b4
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Phenomenalism Summary](phenomenalism-summary-synthesis-ab29b70f-4a42-463b-b303-b38517d8aa22.md)

## Content

**Consider scientific theories that are constructed from data, models, and calculations, rather than solely from raw perceptual input. Intuitively, such constructions still count as knowledge, which implies that knowledge may not be wholly dependent on perceptual experience.**
