# Nozick's Truth-Tracking Theory

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 3
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge be attributed without direct causation?](can-knowledge-be-attributed-without-direct-causation-antithesis-e742d100-cafb-4286-880f-928761b33e2b.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief tracks the truth**, **A belief tracks the truth if it would not be held if the proposition were false**
