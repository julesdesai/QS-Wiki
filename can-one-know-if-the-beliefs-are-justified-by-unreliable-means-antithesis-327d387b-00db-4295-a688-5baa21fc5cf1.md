# Can one know if the beliefs are justified by unreliable means?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 327d387b-00db-4295-a688-5baa21fc5cf1
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Safety Theory](safety-theory-synthesis-a867b6c0-f011-4cc2-a9c9-8c3a93b64de5.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where someone uses a broken clock that coincidentally shows the correct time; their belief that it is a certain time is true and safe from error but without reliability**, **Intuitively, we would judge that this reliance on an unreliable process prevents knowledge from being established despite the belief being true and safe**, **Safety from error does not imply knowledge if the methodological basis for the belief is fundamentally flawed**
