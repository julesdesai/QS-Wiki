# Are all reliable processes conducive to truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3034b421-991a-4051-9b06-4cab723efe15
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Epistemic Externalism](epistemic-externalism-synthesis-539989cd-f86f-4268-aeb1-80f5fff257d5.md)

## Content

**Consider a situation where a person's belief system relies on a generally reliable process but produces a belief based on false premises due to misinformation. Intuitively, we would not judge this belief as knowledge, despite the process's general reliability.**, **It is implausible that all instances of reliable processes necessarily lead to truth, as reliability can be context-dependent.**
