# Is there knowledge without truth in evidence-based belief systems?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** aa5f7f6b-304e-4a44-9aba-728fdaf22bb5
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Evidence-Based Theory](evidence-based-theory-synthesis-3a180c4f-30b0-4e6f-87b5-c20a77cbb8ff.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which everyone believes a well-corroborated scientific theory which is later disproven. Despite robust evidence, the initial belief was never knowledge since it wasn’t true.**, **In legal proceedings, a jury might convict based on overwhelming evidence, yet if the verdict is eventually overturned due to new evidence, it challenges whether the initial belief constituted real knowledge.**
