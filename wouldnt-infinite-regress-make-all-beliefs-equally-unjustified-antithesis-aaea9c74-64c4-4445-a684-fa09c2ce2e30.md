# Wouldn't infinite regress make all beliefs equally unjustified?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** aaea9c74-64c4-4445-a684-fa09c2ce2e30
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism Summary](infinitism-summary-synthesis-a48a630f-d4e8-4dc2-b25a-518a64dbd1fb.md)

## Content

**If every belief requires an infinite chain of reasons, then no belief can surpass another in justification, rendering them all equally unjustified.**, **Infinite regress as a demand for justification removes hierarchical distinctions in belief strength, potentially negating differences in rational acceptability.**
