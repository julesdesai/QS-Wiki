# Are various corroborations essential to establish knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 56fb97a0-9844-4a6f-908a-82b6a9fa9060
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Phenomenal Realism with Consistency Conditions](phenomenal-realism-with-consistency-conditions-synthesis-05f9226f-1221-4391-b2ae-790270c7a451.md)

## Content

**A person may have knowledge of a proposition due to direct immediate insight without needing corroboration through independent experiences. Intuitively, such immediate knowledge seems plausible without failing in its nature as knowledge.**, **Consider a scenario where immediate experience provides certainty that multiple independent corroborations might not. This questions the necessity of diverse corroboration to justify knowledge.**
