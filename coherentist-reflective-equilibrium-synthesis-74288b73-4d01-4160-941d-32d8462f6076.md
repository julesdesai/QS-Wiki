# Coherentist Reflective Equilibrium

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does justification require infallibility?](does-justification-require-infallibility-antithesis-0eaece6c-5713-47c2-8d94-88db2471775f.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief coheres with an interconnected web of beliefs and is subject to reflective equilibrium**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief coheres in such a manner**
