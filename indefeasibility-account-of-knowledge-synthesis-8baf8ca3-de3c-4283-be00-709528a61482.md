## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **The justification for the belief must not be defeasible by any potential defeating evidence**, **Knowledge requires that no relevant information could undermine the justification in the future**
