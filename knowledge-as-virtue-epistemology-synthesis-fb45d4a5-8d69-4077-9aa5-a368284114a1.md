# Knowledge as Virtue Epistemology

**Parent:** [Might perception lead to knowledge without distinct causal connections?](might-perception-lead-to-knowledge-without-distinct-causal-connections-antithesis-9a3203e2-2761-401f-9dd0-a1ea3a9054c4.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is a result of exercising epistemic virtues**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient when the epistemic virtues lead to a true belief**
