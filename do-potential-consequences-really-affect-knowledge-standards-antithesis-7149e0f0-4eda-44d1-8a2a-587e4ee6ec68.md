# Do potential consequences really affect knowledge standards?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7149e0f0-4eda-44d1-8a2a-587e4ee6ec68
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Subject-Sensitive Invariantism](subject-sensitive-invariantism-synthesis-a38d2af6-3ec4-421c-a558-c594d0cbc8b7.md)

## Content

**Suppose an individual is confident about the answer to a question, and the answer is true, with negligible consequences. Even when potential consequences are highlighted, if their confidence and evidence remain the same, intuitively, the knowledge itself seems unaffected.**, **A principle is that if knowledge depends solely on evidence and truth, introducing high potential stakes without any changes in evidence does not alter what is known.**
