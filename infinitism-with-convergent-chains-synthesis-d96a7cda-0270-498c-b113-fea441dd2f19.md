# Infinitism with Convergent Chains

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [How can an infinite chain be unique?](how-can-an-infinite-chain-be-unique-antithesis-2106cefc-0f00-43df-9010-0e7f569535db.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification involves infinite, potentially convergent chains of reasons**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there exist infinite chains of reasons justifying the belief, which may converge**
