# Knowledge as Environmentally Supported True Belief

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they are in an environment that supports their belief formation**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient if the environment reliably supports the formation of true beliefs**
