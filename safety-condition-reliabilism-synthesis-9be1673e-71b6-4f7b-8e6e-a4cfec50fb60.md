## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have a true belief**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief is safe**, **A belief is safe if, in nearly all nearby possible worlds where the person forms the belief in the same way, the belief is true**, **Safety prevents lucky true beliefs from counting as knowledge**
