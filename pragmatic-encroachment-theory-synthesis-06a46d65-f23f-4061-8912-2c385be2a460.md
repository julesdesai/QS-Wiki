## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the practical interests of the person are not undermined by having the belief**, **Practical interests can affect the standards for what counts as knowing**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief aligns with the individual's practical interests**
