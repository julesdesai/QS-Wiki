# Can a defective design plan result in knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c3b77522-55dc-401c-9f53-3ebcc33c8d78
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Proper Functionalism](proper-functionalism-synthesis-252aa5eb-1857-4367-af60-24f7afe1c9be.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person believes a true proposition but their cognitive faculties are malfunctioning, yet they still arrive at the truth by chance. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that the person does not possess knowledge.**, **Suppose a person has cognitive faculties that are designed improperly or are damaged, yet they arrive at true beliefs. This leads us to question if proper functioning as per a design plan is necessary for knowledge.**
