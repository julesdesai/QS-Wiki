# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with a Reliable Process Condition

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can pragmatic encroachment challenge knowledge?](can-pragmatic-encroachment-challenge-knowledge-antithesis-c9cc5819-c4be-447e-820c-7d12293c8a4a.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person has justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process, and they have justification for believing the proposition**
