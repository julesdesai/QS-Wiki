# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with Defeater Condition

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Does knowledge require defeating all alternatives?](does-knowledge-require-defeating-all-alternatives-antithesis-a7f06b81-83d4-450c-87ec-da8504ec4fdf.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person has justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no defeaters present that would undermine their justification**
