# Knowledge as Proper Function and Truth

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 6bd947f5-182b-44f0-8371-8bbf476076b1
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is accidental truth still considered knowledge?](is-accidental-truth-still-considered-knowledge-antithesis-9cd95bf4-3cd2-465c-bcb1-4fb3eaa6dd23.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the cognitive faculties are functioning properly**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the cognitive faculties function properly, and the proposition is true**
