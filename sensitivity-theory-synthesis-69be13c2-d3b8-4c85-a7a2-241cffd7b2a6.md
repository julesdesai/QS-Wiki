# Sensitivity Theory

**Parent:** [Can a justified true belief still fail to be knowledge?](can-a-justified-true-belief-still-fail-to-be-knowledge-antithesis-3abd70c9-bddf-42b8-a53d-0713131446e9.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary and sufficient that the belief is sensitive, meaning it would not be held if the proposition were false**
