# Knowledge as Relevant Alternatives

**Parent:** [Does sensitivity exclude too many cases of genuine knowledge?](does-sensitivity-exclude-too-many-cases-of-genuine-knowledge-antithesis-d4a4a2b8-e66d-48a5-8323-1451cbf62e85.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person can rule out relevant alternative possibilities where the proposition is false**, **A relevant alternative is a scenario sufficiently similar to the actual situation that it can cast doubt on the belief if not ruled out**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and they can rule out all relevant alternatives**
