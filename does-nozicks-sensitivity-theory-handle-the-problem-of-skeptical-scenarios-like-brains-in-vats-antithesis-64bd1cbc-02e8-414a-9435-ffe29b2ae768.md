# Does Nozick's Sensitivity Theory handle the problem of skeptical scenarios like "Brains in Vats"?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 64bd1cbc-02e8-414a-9435-ffe29b2ae768
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Nozick's Sensitivity Theory](nozicks-sensitivity-theory-synthesis-6e1ddae7-9a08-404e-ac91-e01a19448f49.md)

## Content

**In a skeptical scenario where someone is a brain in a vat, they might still believe they have hands based on experiences**, **In such a scenario, the belief that they have hands is not false under their perceived reality**, **Nozick's Sensitivity Theory suggests the belief is not sensitive since it remains even in the false scenario of being a brain in a vat**
