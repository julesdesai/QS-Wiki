# Is the reliability of cognitive faculties sufficient for knowledge in every situation?

## Content

**Imagine a person in a perfectly simulated environment where every cognitive faculty functions as expected but is fed false inputs from the simulation. Intuitively, despite proper functioning, the beliefs do not constitute knowledge.**, **Proper functioning of cognitive faculties does not ensure knowledge if the input data or environment is deceptive.**
