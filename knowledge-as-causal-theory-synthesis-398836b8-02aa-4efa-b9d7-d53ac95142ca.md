# Knowledge as Causal Theory

**Parent:** [Can knowledge be acquired through unreliable cognitive processes?](can-knowledge-be-acquired-through-unreliable-cognitive-processes-antithesis-a7ae6df8-b647-4243-93ff-20a26a609f7b.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is causally connected to the fact of the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is causally connected to the fact of the proposition**
