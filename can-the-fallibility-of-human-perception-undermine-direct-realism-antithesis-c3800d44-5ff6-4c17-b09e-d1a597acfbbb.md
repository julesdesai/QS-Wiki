## Content

**Suppose a person fails to notice a change in a familiar environment, such as the disappearance of a key piece of furniture. Intuitively, they do not perceive the true state of that environment.**, **If perception is subject to error or oversight, it might not reliably grant immediate access to the true nature of objects.**
