# Is it possible to have knowledge without a belief being formed through virtues?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d1ae9803-fc42-400e-87f2-7201123f0d3f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue epistemology focusing on intellectual virtues](virtue-epistemology-focusing-on-intellectual-virtues-synthesis-f3a48039-3cf5-452a-9c79-9ac1b05ad9d2.md)

## Content

**Consider a person who is told a fact by an unreliable source but believes it due to a lack of other information and it turns out to be true by coincidence.**, **Intuitively, despite having true belief, the person’s belief was not formed through an exercise of intellectual virtue, questioning the view that intellectual virtues are necessary for knowledge.**
