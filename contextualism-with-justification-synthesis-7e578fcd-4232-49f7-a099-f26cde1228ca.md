## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **The standards for knowing vary with different contexts**, **For a person to know a proposition, they must meet the justification standards of the relevant context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and they meet the contextually relevant standards for justification**
