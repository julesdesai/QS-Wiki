# Can contextualism accommodate universal truths?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 92976cef-e1eb-4bab-82bb-1f598fb1a848
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism](contextualism-synthesis-c0d03a39-c775-403c-87e2-d078dab04731.md)

## Content

**Consider a universal truth that holds regardless of context, such as basic mathematical truths.**, **If contextualism suggests that the standards for justification vary, it raises the question of whether universal truths are also subject to this variability.**, **If universal truths are treated with context-dependent justification, it could undermine their universality.**
