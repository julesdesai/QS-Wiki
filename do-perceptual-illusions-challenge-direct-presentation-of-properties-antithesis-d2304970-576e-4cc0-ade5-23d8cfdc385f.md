# Do perceptual illusions challenge direct presentation of properties?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d2304970-576e-4cc0-ade5-23d8cfdc385f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Intentionalism about Perception](intentionalism-about-perception-synthesis-61a99e6e-ccdd-4f9c-a128-c382d15947b5.md)

## Content

**In cases of optical illusions, what is perceived can differ systematically from the actual properties of objects.**, **Illusions suggest a mismatch between perceptual content and the properties of objects.**, **If perception directly presents properties, illusions should not occur, or should be rare anomalies.**
