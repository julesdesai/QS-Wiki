# No Defeaters Reliabilism

**Parent:** [Does the possibility of epistemic luck challenge reliabilism?](does-the-possibility-of-epistemic-luck-challenge-reliabilism-antithesis-1f460173-fd67-4e7f-a903-f09de3c982f3.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief in the proposition results from a reliable cognitive process**, **A reliable cognitive process is one that consistently produces true beliefs**, **There should be no defeaters present, where a defeater is evidence or information that would undermine the belief**
