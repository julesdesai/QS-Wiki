## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is the result of a reliable cognitive process**, **A reliable cognitive process incorporates social and communal factors**, **The reliability of the process includes integration with other knowers' contributions**
