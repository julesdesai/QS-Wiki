# Knowledge as Proper Function

**Parent:** [Does sensitivity apply to historical knowledge?](does-sensitivity-apply-to-historical-knowledge-antithesis-c9db9915-fb78-495a-ba15-be6c1c43c7b5.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their cognitive faculties function properly according to a design plan aimed at truth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, the proposition is true, and their cognitive faculties function properly according to a design plan aimed at truth**
