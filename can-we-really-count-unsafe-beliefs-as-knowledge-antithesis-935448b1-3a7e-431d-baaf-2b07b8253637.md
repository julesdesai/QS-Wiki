# Can we really count unsafe beliefs as knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 935448b1-3a7e-431d-baaf-2b07b8253637
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [The Safety Condition](the-safety-condition-synthesis-1c250b1c-da7f-4cab-a10a-178f18915234.md)

## Content

**A case in which a person believes a proposition because they are in a deceptive environment where that proposition is often false is not a case in which that proposition is known.**, **A belief formed in a deceptive environment could easily have been false, even if true in this instance**, **If beliefs formed in deceptive environments are not known, then not all safely believed propositions are known**
