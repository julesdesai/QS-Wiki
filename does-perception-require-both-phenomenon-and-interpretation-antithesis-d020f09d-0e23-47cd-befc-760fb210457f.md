## Content

**Consider a scenario where two individuals witness the same event but interpret it differently due to biases. Intuitively, while their experiences are subjective, interpretation plays a role in their knowledge claims.**, **Knowledge drawn from perceptual experience may rely as much on interpretative frameworks as on the perceptual event itself.**
