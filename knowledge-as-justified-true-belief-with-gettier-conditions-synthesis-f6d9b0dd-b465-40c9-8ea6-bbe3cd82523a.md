# Knowledge as Justified, True Belief with Gettier Conditions

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can intellectual virtues account for knowledge in all domains?](can-intellectual-virtues-account-for-knowledge-in-all-domains-antithesis-d93a0559-ae01-4706-8f58-87951b929bfc.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no Gettier-style counterexamples affecting the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, the proposition is true, and there are no Gettier-style counterexamples affecting the belief**
