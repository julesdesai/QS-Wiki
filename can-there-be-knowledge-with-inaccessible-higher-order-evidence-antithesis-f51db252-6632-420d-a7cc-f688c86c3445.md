# Can there be knowledge with inaccessible higher-order evidence?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f51db252-6632-420d-a7cc-f688c86c3445
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Hybrid Internalism](hybrid-internalism-synthesis-3d9ce0d9-6ba2-4665-a054-ed06708d6ee0.md)

## Content

**A person might have a belief that is true and justified, yet there is higher-order evidence that they are unaware of which undermines their justification. Intuitively, we would judge that ignorance of higher-order evidence diminishes their claim to knowledge.**
