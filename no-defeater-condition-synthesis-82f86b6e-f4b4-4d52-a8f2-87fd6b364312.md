# No Defeater Condition

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Must internal defeats be evident for belief to lack knowledge?](must-internal-defeats-be-evident-for-belief-to-lack-knowledge-antithesis-1acf8e1f-b4a6-41b3-994d-42c39fb0778d.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that no defeater exists which the person is aware of and that would undermine their justification for believing the proposition**
