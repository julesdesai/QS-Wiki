# Knowledge as Sensitivity-Oriented Externalism

**Parent:** [Can accidental truths undermine the safety condition?](can-accidental-truths-undermine-the-safety-condition-antithesis-5e3c2b2a-4f41-456f-8515-0a1a97e35875.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and that had the proposition been false, they would not have believed it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is sensitive to changes in the truth of the proposition across relevantly similar situations**
