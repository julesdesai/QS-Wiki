## Content

**Future-oriented beliefs might have peculiar implications under the sensitivity requirement.**, **Consider someone believing it will rain tomorrow based on weather patterns and, in nearby worlds where it doesn’t rain, might still have held the belief due to consistent patterns; intuitions might falter if calling this scenario knowledge.**
