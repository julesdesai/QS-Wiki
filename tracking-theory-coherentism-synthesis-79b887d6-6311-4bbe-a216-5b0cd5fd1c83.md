## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief tracks the truth across possible scenarios**, **For beliefs to constitute knowledge, they must track the truth while embedded in a coherent belief system**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe it, it is true, and it tracks the truth within a coherent system**
