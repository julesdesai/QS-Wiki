# Knowledge with External Justification and Awareness Requirement

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 40e81af5-e918-41f8-8fd1-e3a64d5eed10
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge exist without consciousness if conditions other than consciousness provide justification?](can-knowledge-exist-without-consciousness-if-conditions-other-than-consciousness-provide-justification-antithesis-09a1950d-9802-41d0-b35a-298baf014cf4.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is safe from error in nearby possible worlds**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person is consciously aware of the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, some conditions other than consciousness provide the justification, the belief is safe from error, and the person is consciously aware of the belief**
