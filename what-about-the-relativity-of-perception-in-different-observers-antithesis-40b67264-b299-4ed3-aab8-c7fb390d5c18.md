# What about the relativity of perception in different observers?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 40b67264-b299-4ed3-aab8-c7fb390d5c18
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Naïve Realism](naïve-realism-synthesis-960c5650-a545-46a7-9f9f-61ad44274751.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where one observer perceives a dress as blue and black, while another perceives it as white and gold. Both observers cannot be directly perceiving the dress as it is in the world if they report different colors.**, **There is an empirical fact that individual differences in perception can lead to different reports about the same external scene.**
