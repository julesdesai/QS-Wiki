# Knowledge as Sensitivity to Defeaters

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 193e2cd5-f604-4b72-bf7e-704dd1212992
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does adding a defeater remove knowledge?](does-adding-a-defeater-remove-knowledge-antithesis-4bf3ffe7-5579-499f-ac84-9588834167a6.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no defeaters that should block the belief if considered**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, their belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process, and there are no defeaters that would obstruct the belief**
