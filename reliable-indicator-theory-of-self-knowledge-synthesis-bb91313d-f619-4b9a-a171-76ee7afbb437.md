## Content

**A person knows a proposition if their belief is a reliable indicator of the truth of the proposition**, **The proposition being true is a necessary condition for it to be known**, **Self-knowledge is an exception to the need for an external causal relationship**, **A belief’s usefulness or practicality is not required for it to qualify as knowledge**
