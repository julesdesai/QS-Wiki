# Do shifting but benign environments question the safety of knowledge?

## Content

**Imagine a botanist knowing a plant species based on distinctive flower color observed repeatedly. Due to a benign but widespread mutation in nearby areas, the flowers change color. Intuitively, the botanist's confidence in their knowledge seems baseless.**, **When external environmental shifts alter factual understanding, a belief can become false in nearby possible worlds, raising doubts about the belief's safety while still meeting a truth criterion.**
