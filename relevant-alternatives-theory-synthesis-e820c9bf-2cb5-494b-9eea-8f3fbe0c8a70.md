## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they can rule out relevant alternatives to the truth of the proposition**, **Relevant alternatives are those which might plausibly be true instead**
