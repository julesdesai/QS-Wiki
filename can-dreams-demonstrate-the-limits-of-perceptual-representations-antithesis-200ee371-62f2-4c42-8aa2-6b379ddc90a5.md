# Can dreams demonstrate the limits of perceptual representations?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 200ee371-62f2-4c42-8aa2-6b379ddc90a5
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Representationalism](representationalism-synthesis-e49b01dd-7eac-432b-990b-a606f4616c7d.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a dream presents a coherent narrative perceived as reality during the experience.**, **Intuitively, the disconnect between dream perceptions and waking reality highlights the limitations of representations as reliable indicators of external truths.**, **This raises doubts about whether perceptions, including dreams, can consistently provide accurate representations of the external world.**
