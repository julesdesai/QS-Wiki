# Can different perceptions undermine the reliability of basic beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e312ed37-860d-4938-aed6-4e5c130890e3
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Moderate Foundationalism](moderate-foundationalism-synthesis-83772ba8-e93f-47f7-a15a-9e5a39b9dc9d.md)

## Content

**Suppose two individuals perceive the same event differently due to differing sensory experiences. Intuitively, both claim to have justified basic beliefs about the event.**, **If subjective experiences can lead to different basic beliefs, it challenges how such beliefs can unconditionally justify further beliefs.**
