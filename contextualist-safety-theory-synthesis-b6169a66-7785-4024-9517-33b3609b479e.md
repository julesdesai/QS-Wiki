## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief could not easily have been false in contextually relevant possible worlds**, **Contextually relevant possible worlds are those considered salient in the given conversational context**
