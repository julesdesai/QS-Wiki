# Knowledge as Tracking the Truth

**Parent:** [Does hindsight reliability erode the basis for knowledge?](does-hindsight-reliability-erode-the-basis-for-knowledge-antithesis-447717b4-3d73-46be-bc19-d7b1d54ebb5a.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, they would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were true, they would believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and they both would believe it if it were true and would not believe it if it were false**
