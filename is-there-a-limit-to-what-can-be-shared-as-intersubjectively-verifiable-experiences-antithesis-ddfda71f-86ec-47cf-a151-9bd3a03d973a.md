# Is there a limit to what can be shared as intersubjectively verifiable experiences?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ddfda71f-86ec-47cf-a151-9bd3a03d973a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Objective Phenomenalism](objective-phenomenalism-synthesis-91c0e065-6d6c-4b9a-9519-a161a622cd2d.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where an individual's perceptual experience is deeply unique, such as a synesthetic event where numbers evoke colors. Intuitively, we would judge that these experiences hold knowledge for the individual despite limited intersubjective verification.**, **There is a plausible principle that some aspects of perceptual experiences may remain inherently private or subjective in nature.**
