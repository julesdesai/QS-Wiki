## Content

**Knowledge depends partly on facts about the subject's situation**, **The standards for knowing a proposition are invariant across different contexts**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **A person's justification is evaluated based on both objective standards and subject-specific factors**
