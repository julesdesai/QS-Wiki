# Contextualist Account of Knowledge

**Parent:** [Can knowledge be justified circularly without tracking all conditions?](can-knowledge-be-justified-circularly-without-tracking-all-conditions-antithesis-cbe83e9b-5ddf-4636-a978-645f139c8812.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person believes the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified based on the context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is true and justified, given the context**
