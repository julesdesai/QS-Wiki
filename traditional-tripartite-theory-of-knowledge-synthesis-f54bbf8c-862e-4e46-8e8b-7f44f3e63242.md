# Traditional Tripartite Theory of Knowledge

**Parent:** [Is belief a necessary motivator for pursuing knowledge?](is-belief-a-necessary-motivator-for-pursuing-knowledge-antithesis-3af02d59-bfb8-4a9a-93e0-6a269f8c7ee2.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **A person knows a proposition if and only if the proposition is true, they have justification for it, and they believe it**
