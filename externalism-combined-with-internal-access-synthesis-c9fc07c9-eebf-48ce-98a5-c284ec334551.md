## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is some reliable process producing the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, a reliable process produces the belief, and they have internal access to justification**
