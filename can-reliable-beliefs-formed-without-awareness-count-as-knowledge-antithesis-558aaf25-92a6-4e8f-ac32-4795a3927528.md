# Can reliable beliefs formed without awareness count as knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 558aaf25-92a6-4e8f-ac32-4795a3927528

**Parent:** [Reliabilist Knowledge Summary](reliabilist-knowledge-summary-synthesis-4cf24049-6407-4422-bb1c-63a1d2cbefdb.md)

## Content

**Consider that a person under hypnosis has memories implanted accurately by a reliable hypnotist but is unaware of the process. Intuitively, we question if these can be regarded as justified beliefs.**, **The reliability of the source does not seem sufficient to justify the belief without the subject's awareness.**
