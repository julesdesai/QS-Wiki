## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for their belief**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no defeaters for their belief**, **A defeater is a true proposition that, if known, would undermine the person's justification for believing the original proposition**
