# Knowledge as Externalist Justification

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is justified by factors external to the person's subjective perspective**, **Externalist justification can include processes like perception, memory, or testimony that reliably lead to true beliefs**
