# Does cognitive penetration indicate perception isn't a direct route to reality?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2edab560-f562-4daf-b6d1-859a2ac56aa2
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Selective Direct Realism](selective-direct-realism-synthesis-744f6327-3f79-490f-be88-a0aa209da42b.md)

## Content

**Cognitive penetration refers to the influence of cognitive states, like beliefs and desires, on perceptual experience, altering what is perceived.**, **If cognition alters perception, the perception doesn't relate directly to unaltered foundational parts of reality, as it is influenced by subjective states.**, **Influence of cognitive factors on perception means variability and instability are introduced, challenging perceptions' role in maintaining stable knowledge.**
