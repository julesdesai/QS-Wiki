# Is contextual understanding necessary for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ca0441cf-93c3-453e-a1bf-cc8b71de16dd
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [No False Lemmas View](no-false-lemmas-view-synthesis-031e6f6f-2b49-47e4-821c-d2421562acef.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a tourist accurately describes a historic event without knowing the culture or the context. Intuitively, we either have to say that the tourist knows the event accurately or that they do not. If we say they know the event accurately, then they don't require understanding of context for knowledge. If we say they do not know accurately, then lacking context affects the reliability of their belief.**
