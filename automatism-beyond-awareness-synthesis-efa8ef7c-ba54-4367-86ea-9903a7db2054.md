## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition through expertise, automatic responses based on past justifications can suffice**, **Automatic responses do not require conscious awareness at the moment of forming belief**
