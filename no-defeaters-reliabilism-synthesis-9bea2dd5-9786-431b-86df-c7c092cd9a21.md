**Parent:** [Does reliabilism account for the Gettier problem?](does-reliabilism-account-for-the-gettier-problem-antithesis-2ecb7989-079f-4ca6-8b2f-a1a26a650d15.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **The person must not have any defeaters for their belief, where a defeater is a true proposition that, if known, would undermine the belief**
