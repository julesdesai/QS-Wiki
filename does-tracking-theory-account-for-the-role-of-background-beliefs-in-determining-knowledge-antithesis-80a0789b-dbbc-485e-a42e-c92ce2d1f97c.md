# Does tracking theory account for the role of background beliefs in determining knowledge?

## Content

**A belief formed independently of background beliefs might align with truth without dependency on such context, complicating the idea of necessary tracking across worlds**, **In scenarios where external circumstances prompt true belief regardless of shaky theoretical grounding, the tracking theory may falter in addressing how contextual beliefs interplay with truth**, **Not all beliefs rely on a background that ensures truth tracking; thus, they may resist the role of belief context in knowledge claims**
