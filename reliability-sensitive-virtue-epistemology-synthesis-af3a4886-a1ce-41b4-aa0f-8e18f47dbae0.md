# Reliability-Sensitive Virtue Epistemology

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is a belief reliably formed but unverifiable still knowledge?](is-a-belief-reliably-formed-but-unverifiable-still-knowledge-antithesis-d7e10d00-a126-4300-b28f-a2fb1fd822c4.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from intellectual virtue or competence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is reliably formed and modally robust**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief arises from intellectual virtue or competence, and the belief is both reliably and modally robust**
