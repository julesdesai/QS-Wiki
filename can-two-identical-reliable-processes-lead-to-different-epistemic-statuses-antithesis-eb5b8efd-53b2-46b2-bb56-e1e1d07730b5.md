# Can two identical reliable processes lead to different epistemic statuses?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** eb5b8efd-53b2-46b2-bb56-e1e1d07730b5
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism](reliabilism-synthesis-41e7a61f-ad45-42cd-8dd7-ba40ca519ba9.md)

## Content

**Suppose two identical cognitive processes produce the same true belief in similar subjects.**, **In one case, the belief accidentally matches reality, in the other, it does not.**, **If identical reliable processes can lead to different epistemic statuses, then reliability alone is not sufficient for determining knowledge.**
