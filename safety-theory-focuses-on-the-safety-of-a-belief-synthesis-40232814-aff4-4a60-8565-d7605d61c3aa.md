# Safety Theory focuses on the safety of a belief

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 40232814-aff4-4a60-8565-d7605d61c3aa
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does having a true proposition guarantee knowledge?](does-having-a-true-proposition-guarantee-knowledge-antithesis-d5c13129-b0c9-4491-8e2b-1a919a121fa1.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that, in nearby possible worlds, the person would not easily believe the proposition without it being true**, **A nearby possible world is a scenario that is similar to the actual world**
