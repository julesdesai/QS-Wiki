# How does coherentism deal with the alternative systems objection?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c8c60e44-7387-4f27-baac-a815889a6761
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism in Epistemology](coherentism-in-epistemology-synthesis-03124e37-be18-4868-b2cf-e370b5186105.md)

## Content

**Multiple, equally coherent systems of beliefs can exist, which are all internally consistent and supportive.**, **Two individuals with equally coherent systems might disagree on fundamental beliefs, such as ethical principles or metaphysical claims.**, **Intuitively, it seems implausible that both individuals possess justified knowledge if their beliefs directly contradict each other.**
