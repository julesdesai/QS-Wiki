# Can foundationalism include non-terminating justificatory elements?

## Content

**Foundationalism suggests certain beliefs are self-supporting or axiomatic, which contradicts the notion of endless justificatory reasons.**, **The concept of foundational knowledge implies resting on irreducibly basic beliefs, but infinite chains never allow for a resting point.**, **There seems an inherent tension between beliefs needing infinite support and also being foundational and self-justifying.**
