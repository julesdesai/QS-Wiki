# Is justification sufficient for knowledge if reliability is unknown?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e637c856-1038-404a-a0c1-3fdfc5969bd6
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Process Reliabilism](process-reliabilism-synthesis-456c7378-2f2e-48dd-af81-ab7fd499203a.md)

## Content

**Knowledge does not require the knower to have reflective access to the reliability of the process.**, **A person may not know if the process they rely on is reliable without reflective access.**, **If a person cannot determine the reliability of their belief-producing process, calling their true belief 'knowledge' raises doubts.**
