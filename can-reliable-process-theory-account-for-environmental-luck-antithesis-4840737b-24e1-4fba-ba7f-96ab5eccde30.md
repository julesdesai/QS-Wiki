# Can Reliable Process Theory account for environmental luck?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 4840737b-24e1-4fba-ba7f-96ab5eccde30
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliable Process Theory](reliable-process-theory-synthesis-2b23f056-a980-462c-bb6c-659be3049cf1.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person correctly identifies a species of bird in their backyard due to their reliable identification skills. Unbeknownst to them, most birds in their environment are cleverly painted lookalikes. This is not a case where their belief is reliably linked to the truth due to environmental factors.**, **Intuitively, their belief seems lucky rather than knowledgeable, due to the context not being conducive to truth.**
