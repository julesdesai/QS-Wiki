## Content

**Knowledge is true belief that arises from intellectual virtue and is safely formed**, **A belief must be adroit, displaying intellectual skill**, **A belief must be apt, deriving its accuracy due to primarily intellectual virtue**, **A belief is safely formed if, in most nearby possible worlds, the method that produced the belief results in a true belief**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief is adroit, apt, and safely formed**
