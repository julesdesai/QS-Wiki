# Safety Theory of Knowledge

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in all nearby possible worlds where they believe the proposition, the proposition is true**, **A belief is safe if it is true in all nearby possible worlds where it is believed**
