# How can implicit cognitive functions guarantee propositional knowledge?

## Content

**Implicit cognitive functions like intuition may lead to accurate outcomes without understanding the propositional content.**, **Imagine a person who chooses the correct answer on a multiple-choice test by gut feeling.**, **The person may have no justification for their choice in terms of propositional content, challenging the role of implicit cognition in knowledge.**
