# Knowledge with Contextual Justification

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 3dfdba37-2a11-4986-a96e-f9ecb8570b46
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can a belief from a reliable process lack justification?](can-a-belief-from-a-reliable-process-lack-justification-antithesis-36048d97-a59f-43bd-97c2-be0587e394b2.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a process that reliably produces true beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification for believing is contextually appropriate**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the person believes the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is produced by a process that reliably produces true beliefs and the justification for believing is contextually appropriate**
