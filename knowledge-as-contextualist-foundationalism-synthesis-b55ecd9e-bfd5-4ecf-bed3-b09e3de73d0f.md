**Parent:** [Can a true belief that coheres without satisfying context still be knowledge?](can-a-true-belief-that-coheres-without-satisfying-context-still-be-knowledge-antithesis-c3e176a6-a6bd-480b-80d9-342d4b8214ef.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is supported by foundational beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the standards for knowledge depend on the context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is supported by foundational beliefs, and it meets the contextual standards**
