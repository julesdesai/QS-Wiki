## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition through a reliable cognitive ability**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is the result of exercising intellectual virtue**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe it, the proposition is true, and the belief is appropriately caused by their intellectual virtue**
