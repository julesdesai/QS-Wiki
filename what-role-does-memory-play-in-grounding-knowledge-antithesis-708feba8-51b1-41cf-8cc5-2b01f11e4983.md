## Content

**There is a plausible principle that memory can sustain knowledge when original evidence is no longer present.**, **Consider a scenario where a person cannot access their original evidence for a belief, yet confidently remembers its truth. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that memory plays a critical role in sustaining their knowledge.**
