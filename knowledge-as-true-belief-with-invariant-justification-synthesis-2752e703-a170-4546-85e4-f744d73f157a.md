# Knowledge as True Belief with Invariant Justification

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does justification being contextually sufficient threaten the objectivity of knowledge?](does-justification-being-contextually-sufficient-threaten-the-objectivity-of-knowledge-antithesis-7b551b73-9f0c-464e-990c-13a9b5bbaabc.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification for their belief is invariant across contexts**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification for their belief is invariant across contexts**
