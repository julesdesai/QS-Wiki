## Content

**Justification involves having a context-dependent series of possibly infinite reasons**, **Knowledge requires beliefs to be justified by a chain of reasons whose length depends on the context**, **Each reason in the chain provides support for the subsequent reason given the context**, **The series of reasons may extend indefinitely but is often finite in practical contexts**
