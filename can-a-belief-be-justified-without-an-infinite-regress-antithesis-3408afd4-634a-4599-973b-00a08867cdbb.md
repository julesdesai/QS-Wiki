# Can a belief be justified without an infinite regress?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3408afd4-634a-4599-973b-00a08867cdbb
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Infinitism](pragmatic-infinitism-synthesis-954ef6f8-808a-47cf-95ad-717cbaeffb04.md)

## Content

**There appears to be no practical way to verify an infinite chain of reasons in human life.**, **If justification requires verification, then infinite regress would never allow verification, making any belief unjustified.**
