# Knowledge as Sensitivity-Based True Belief

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can a belief be aware of all potential defeaters?](can-a-belief-be-aware-of-all-potential-defeaters-antithesis-47854db8-2666-4456-8aaa-92c84017683a.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person wouldn't believe it if it weren't true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and they wouldn't believe it if it weren't true**
