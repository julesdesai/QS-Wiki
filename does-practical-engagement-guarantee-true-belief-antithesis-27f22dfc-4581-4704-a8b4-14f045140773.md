# Does practical engagement guarantee true belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 27f22dfc-4581-4704-a8b4-14f045140773
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Disjunctivism with Practical Knowledge](disjunctivism-with-practical-knowledge-synthesis-59d0a627-a21e-4454-81e9-2bdfcb3b8f52.md)

## Content

**Practical engagement can be well-executed even if the underlying belief about the situation is false.**, **Consider a case where a person, through skillful engagement, appears to solve a problem correctly while holding an incorrect belief about the problem's requirements. Intuitively, we would not say that their belief is true simply because they acted effectively.**
