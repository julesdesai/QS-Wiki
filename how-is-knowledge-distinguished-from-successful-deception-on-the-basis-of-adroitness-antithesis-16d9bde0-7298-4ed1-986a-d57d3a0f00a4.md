# How is knowledge distinguished from successful deception on the basis of adroitness?

**Node ID:** 16d9bde0-7298-4ed1-986a-d57d3a0f00a4
**Depth:** 4

## Content

**Imagine an individual who deceives successfully, leading someone to hold a true belief due to the deceiver's skill. The holder's belief is accurate but unknowingly manipulated into truth.**, **The true belief's formation involves adroitness from the deceiver, not the believer, raising an issue about internal versus external skill realization in defining knowledge.**
