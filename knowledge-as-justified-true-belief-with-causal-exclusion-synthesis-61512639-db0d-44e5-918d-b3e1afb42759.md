# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with Causal Exclusion

**Parent:** [Does misleading evidence undermine the sufficiency of causal connections for knowledge?](does-misleading-evidence-undermine-the-sufficiency-of-causal-connections-for-knowledge-antithesis-6dbfed05-1966-4602-8c8f-6c7a70060306.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is justified, true, and causally excluded from misleading influences**
