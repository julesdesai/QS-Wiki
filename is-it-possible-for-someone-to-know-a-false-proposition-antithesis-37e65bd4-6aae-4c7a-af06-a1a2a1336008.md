# Is it possible for someone to know a false proposition?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 37e65bd4-6aae-4c7a-af06-a1a2a1336008
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Sensitivity Theory of Knowledge](sensitivity-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-ba84f32c-948c-4607-9039-09527bd88c50.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person believes a proposition that is in fact false. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that the person cannot know the proposition.**, **If the proposition were true, then the person would believe it. However, since they believe it when it is false, the sensitivity condition does not apply.**
