## Content

**A person knows a proposition if they believe it and the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, if the proposition were false, they would not believe it**, **Strengthened sensitivity requires that, if the actual world were slightly different but the proposition still true, belief persists without luck**, **Knowledge requires belief that tracks the truth robustly across close possible worlds**
