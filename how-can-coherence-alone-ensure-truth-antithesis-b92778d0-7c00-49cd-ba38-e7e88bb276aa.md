# How can coherence alone ensure truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b92778d0-7c00-49cd-ba38-e7e88bb276aa
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitist Coherentism](infinitist-coherentism-synthesis-a30e3b29-79f2-4d20-a375-1e170fe170c4.md)

## Content

**A coherent system of beliefs can be internally consistent but entirely disconnected from reality.**, **Truth requires correspondence with the way things are, not just internal consistency within a system.**, **There is no necessary link between coherence and truth, as coherence does not imply factual accuracy.**
