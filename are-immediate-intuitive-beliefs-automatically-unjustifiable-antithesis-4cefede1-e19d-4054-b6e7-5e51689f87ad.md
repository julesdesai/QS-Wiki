# Are immediate, intuitive beliefs automatically unjustifiable?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 4cefede1-e19d-4054-b6e7-5e51689f87ad
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilist Infinitism](reliabilist-infinitism-synthesis-31746681-ada4-44ae-aa39-47d03ad8abbb.md)

## Content

**Immediate, intuitive beliefs, such as perceptual beliefs, often lack an overt justificatory process and seem justified.**, **This contradicts the requirement for an explicit infinite chain of reliable belief-forming processes.**
