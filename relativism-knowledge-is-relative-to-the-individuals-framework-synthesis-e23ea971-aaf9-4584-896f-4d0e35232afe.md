# Relativism: Knowledge is relative to the individual's framework

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can context-dependent knowledge claims be reconciled with universal truths?](can-context-dependent-knowledge-claims-be-reconciled-with-universal-truths-antithesis-f4dbfab1-54e6-4247-bc0b-1762f73b0458.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that justification depends on the individual's framework**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification meets the particular standards of the individual's framework**
