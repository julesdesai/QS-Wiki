# What about the epistemic regress problem?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0916182b-f5cf-4c87-8af2-3020caece471
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism with Justified Belief](coherentism-with-justified-belief-synthesis-01e10c3b-52a8-4097-b02c-3f010fc56355.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which each belief is justified by its coherence with another belief, prompting an infinite regress. Intuitively, an infinite regress does not itself account adequately for justification.**, **A case in which there are no foundational beliefs to stop the infinite regress is a case in which no belief is truly justified.**
