# Does idealism account for shared experiences?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2f013bdc-6098-4cb0-87da-303495fad90a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Idealism](idealism-synthesis-53ec409c-4a9b-4261-be64-9dda9f92eb94.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where multiple scientists independently verify a phenomenon, such as a chemical reaction. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that the phenomenon exists independently of individual mental constructs.**, **There is a plausible principle that scientific consensus indicates objective reality. This principle, together with idealism's claim that reality is a construct of the mind, entails a contradiction if consensus indicates something external to mental constructs.**
