## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that infinite reasons create a coherent belief system modified by practical concerns**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and pragmatic coherence over an infinite chain occurs**
