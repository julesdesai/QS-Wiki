# Reliabilist Theory of Knowledge

**Parent:** [Is belief tracking truth against counterexamples?](is-belief-tracking-truth-against-counterexamples-antithesis-c48c943b-e721-4965-9d3d-4fb2a7c447ad.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief be produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**
