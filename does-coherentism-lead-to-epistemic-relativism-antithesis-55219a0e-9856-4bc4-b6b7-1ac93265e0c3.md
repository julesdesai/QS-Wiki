# Does coherentism lead to epistemic relativism?

**Node ID:** 55219a0e-9856-4bc4-b6b7-1ac93265e0c3
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism without Infinite Regress](coherentism-without-infinite-regress-synthesis-1802290e-23e3-475c-be0d-a870496d2311.md)

## Content

**Consider the possibility that different individuals or cultures have divergent yet equally coherent belief systems. Intuitively, such diversity suggests that coherence might only preserve relative truth rather than objective truth.**, **There is a plausible principle that if coherence is the only measure of truth, then multiple, conflicting truths might simultaneously exist.**, **The idea of coherence without external validation risks making truth or knowledge a matter of perspective or social consensus rather than objective fact.**
