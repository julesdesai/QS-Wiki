# Can perceptual errors undermine direct knowledge claims?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 47d1c525-6a84-4c75-ac06-daadb7265563
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Disjunctivism](disjunctivism-synthesis-15b36dc9-362d-489d-8156-bddc0dd6b522.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person looks at a white wall under a red light and perceives it as red. Intuitively, this shows that perceptual errors can lead to false beliefs about reality.**, **If perceptual errors can cause mistaken beliefs about reality, the claim that knowledge through perception is direct and reliable is problematic.**
