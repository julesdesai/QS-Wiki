# Is intellectual virtue applicable to all domains of knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 8b88d27f-c201-4731-bd7e-9ee807dfb5dc
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-5dae303e-e392-4dd3-a9fb-c518398dc86e.md)

## Content

**Some areas of knowledge, such as mathematics or logic, may not require intellectual virtue in their belief formation but still yield true propositions.**, **This challenges the claim that intellectual virtue is necessary across all domains of knowledge.**
