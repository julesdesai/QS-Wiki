# Knowledge as True Belief with Independent Justification

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Do communal norms hinder individual intellectual progress?](do-communal-norms-hinder-individual-intellectual-progress-antithesis-8176592e-e81f-49b4-b1d0-c9be54042183.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification is based on individual critical reasoning and analysis**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification stems from rigorous personal reflection**
