## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is produced by a reliable process external to the person’s belief system which includes self-correction mechanisms for errors**, **Belief formation processes are relevant but do not require direct awareness by the individual**, **Reliability requires that any self-deceptive elements are automatically corrected**
