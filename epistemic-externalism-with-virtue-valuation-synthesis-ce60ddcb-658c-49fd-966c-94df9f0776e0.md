## Content

**Knowledge is true belief arising from external reliable indicators**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that there are reliable external indicators supporting the belief**, **Intellectual virtue is valuable but not necessary for relying on external indicators in knowledge formation**
