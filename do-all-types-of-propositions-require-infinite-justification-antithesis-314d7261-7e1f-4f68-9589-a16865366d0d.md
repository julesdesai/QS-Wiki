# Do all types of propositions require infinite justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 314d7261-7e1f-4f68-9589-a16865366d0d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherent Infinitism](coherent-infinitism-synthesis-f2c99927-f862-4ee8-8b5b-2fefd2717ee2.md)

## Content

**Consider mathematical axioms, which are accepted as knowledge without needing infinite justification. Intuitively, we might judge that not every proposition gains its knowledge status through infinitism.**, **It is implausible that infinite justification is required for propositions already deemed self-evident or axiomatic.**
