# Is the tracking theory sufficient for explaining knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 730d1e52-cf70-4008-9b6b-a85edc559974
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Tracking Theory of Knowledge](tracking-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-6d302593-f2de-4e10-8195-85a314e81400.md)

## Content

**Suppose someone believes a true proposition only because they were told by a notoriously unreliable source.**, **In this scenario, intuitively, the person does not have knowledge even though the belief is true and could track the truth because of happenstance.**, **A plausible principle is that knowledge should require evidence or justification beyond mere truth-tracking.**
