## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **A proposition is known if there are no defeaters for the belief**, **The closure principle is maintained since knowledge claims are only made when all defeaters are accounted for**
