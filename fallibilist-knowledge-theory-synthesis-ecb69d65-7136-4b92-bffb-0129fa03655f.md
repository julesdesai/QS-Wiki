# Fallibilist Knowledge Theory

**Depth:** 3
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can one ever truly attain absolute certainty?](can-one-ever-truly-attain-absolute-certainty-antithesis-9f27664d-6c7f-4a2f-aa9a-1cbc2907d227.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition despite the possibility of being wrong**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is compatible with the belief being open to doubt**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition acknowledging potential error, the proposition is true, and the belief is justifiable under possible doubt**
