# Is belief justification context-independent?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3d15502d-e7df-432b-8658-41c742380397
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Moderate Contextualism Summary](moderate-contextualism-summary-synthesis-54895291-30c8-42cf-8322-baea34575bd1.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where two individuals in different countries believe the same fact following distinct justification methods due to cultural norms. Intuitively, justification should apply equally in terms of securing knowledge, challenging the variability across contexts.**, **It seems implausible to suggest that a universally true fact could vary in its requirement for justification solely based on geographical context and cultural practices.**
