# Can true beliefs be considered knowledge without evidence?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 160dd33c-fa9f-414c-b8c6-746e62ec53ab
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Justification Theory](pragmatic-justification-theory-synthesis-838d6a9b-2af0-4e99-882f-62ebc82bed50.md)

## Content

**A person believes a true proposition solely based on a hunch without any supporting evidence. In such a case, it is implausible to consider this belief as knowledge.**, **Knowledge generally requires both truth and justification, not merely an arbitrary true belief.**
