# Can belief alone suffice for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a9327263-a31f-49a2-ac4c-69a0bada6f39
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Moderate Contextualism Summary](moderate-contextualism-summary-synthesis-de0e1052-baa8-4c82-8e86-ff2d76e7b586.md)

## Content

**A case in which a person believes a proposition because they are randomly guessing is not a case in which the person knows the proposition.**, **For knowledge, a proposition must be more than just a belief; it must have a basis that provides justification beyond guesswork.**
