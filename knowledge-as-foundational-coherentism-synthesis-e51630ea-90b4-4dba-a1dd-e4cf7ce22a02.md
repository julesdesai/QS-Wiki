## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that some beliefs are foundational and cohere with other beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that foundational beliefs are true and coherent with the system of beliefs**
