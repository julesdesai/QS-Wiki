# Do variations in truth standards challenge the universality of knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** be0e608b-724f-40e9-8ba2-f86797e42782
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Multi-Standard Contextualism](multi-standard-contextualism-synthesis-9865cf66-a535-4334-b5b4-390c78d28080.md)

## Content

**Suppose the standards for truth vary significantly, such that a proposition believed to be true in one context may be false in another. Intuitively, if truth is context-dependent, it challenges the universality that is often associated with knowledge and can lead to contradictions in knowledge claims across different contexts.**
