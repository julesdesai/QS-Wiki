## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief results from the exercise of cognitive virtue or skill, which does not require conscious awareness of the use of the skill**
