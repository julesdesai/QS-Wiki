# Is external justification necessary for practical belief to be knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 443fa88f-9d30-4a1a-ad3a-5be72f85667a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Instrumentalism with Externalist Justification](instrumentalism-with-externalist-justification-synthesis-69903d30-4d37-4781-884e-3e9ef74484e3.md)

## Content

**External justification requires some evidence or reasoning outside of the individual's belief that supports its truth**, **A belief may seem useful or practical but without external justification, it may not be reliable or truth-conducive, like believing a lucky charm brings success without evidence**, **A belief should be supported by external justification to qualify as knowledge**
