# Externalist Justificatory Reflective Equilibrium

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** db1ae5ef-3149-41cc-8b9e-7a25e60c3f91
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does justification require infallibility?](does-justification-require-infallibility-antithesis-0eaece6c-5713-47c2-8d94-88db2471775f.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their justification is based on external factors and forms part of a reflective equilibrium**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their justification is based on external factors in such equilibrium**
