# Knowledge as No Defeaters

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** d85d69ab-af8b-4f7e-8366-51ed57015da1
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can a true belief stemming from external influences be knowledge?](can-a-true-belief-stemming-from-external-influences-be-knowledge-antithesis-5b33dd17-ebb7-4b9e-a1d0-debf380c91c8.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, there must be no defeaters for the belief in the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there are no defeaters for the belief**
