# Knowledge as Non-Accidental Truth

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can virtuous belief still be insufficient due to external factors?](can-virtuous-belief-still-be-insufficient-due-to-external-factors-antithesis-48e3e3a6-8c12-4675-a44d-e1911ef8aa52.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, the proposition is true, and the truth occurs in a way that is not accidental**
