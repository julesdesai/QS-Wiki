# Knowledge as Safety

**Parent:** [Is evidential support really a necessary condition for knowledge?](is-evidential-support-really-a-necessary-condition-for-knowledge-antithesis-02d72faa-2911-4c8e-bdf1-95661598a8db.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person would not easily have believed the proposition if it were false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and they would not easily have believed the proposition if it were false**
