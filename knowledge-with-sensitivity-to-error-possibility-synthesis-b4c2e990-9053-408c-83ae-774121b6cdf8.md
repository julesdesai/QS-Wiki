# Knowledge with Sensitivity to Error Possibility

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** b4c2e990-9053-408c-83ae-774121b6cdf8
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Are all alternative possibilities relevant for knowledge?](are-all-alternative-possibilities-relevant-for-knowledge-antithesis-568455ce-e939-4eff-9ae2-8b50c1917f5b.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they are sensitive to the possibility of being wrong about relevant alternatives**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, the proposition is true, and they are sensitive to the possibility of being wrong about relevant alternatives**
