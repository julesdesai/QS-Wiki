## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that consistent standards for knowledge are met across all contexts**, **The standards for knowledge apply uniformly and are not influenced by shifts in conversational contexts**
