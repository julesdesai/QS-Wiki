## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person has strong justification for the proposition**, **The absence of defeaters, strong justification, belief, and truth together are sufficient for knowledge**
