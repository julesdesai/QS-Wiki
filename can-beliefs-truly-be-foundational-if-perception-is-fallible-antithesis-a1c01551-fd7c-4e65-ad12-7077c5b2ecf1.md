# Can beliefs truly be foundational if perception is fallible?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a1c01551-fd7c-4e65-ad12-7077c5b2ecf1
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundationalism in Epistemology](foundationalism-in-epistemology-synthesis-4c27bb26-6321-4022-8e66-a85ea5d939d9.md)

## Content

**Perceptual experiences are sometimes unreliable or deceptive.**, **If perceptual experiences are unreliable, then basic beliefs derived from them may not be self-justified or evident.**, **If basic beliefs are not self-justified, then the foundational structure of knowledge is undermined.**
