# Can unknown unreliability undermine purported knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9e8bd3d2-f1f7-470a-aa97-2457eacb585e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Process Reliabilism](process-reliabilism-synthesis-73062a2b-1357-42ba-b901-d397b26dce96.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person relies on a process that turns out to be unreliable unbeknownst to them due to changes in environment or knowledge base. Intuitively, this invalidates their supposed knowledge.**, **Suppose a routine cognitive process like remembering names becomes unreliable due to neurological fatigue without the person's awareness. We would intuitively judge that there's no knowledge of names had.**
