# Are there cases where improper functioning still leads to warranted knowledge?

## Content

**Consider a malfunctioning lie detector that only malfunctions in the presence of lies, thus producing true beliefs about honesty unintentionally. Intuitively, these beliefs might still be considered knowledge even though the mechanism is not functioning properly.**, **Improper functioning can accidentally yield reliable, truth-directed outcomes considered to be knowledge.**
