# Invariantism with strict epistemic standards

**Parent:** [Can contextualism account for the closure principle in knowledge?](can-contextualism-account-for-the-closure-principle-in-knowledge-antithesis-b5a2e972-ad30-40d1-baa5-7f55a894376e.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification for the belief meets fixed epistemic standards**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification for the belief meets fixed epistemic standards**
