## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their justification for believing is the conjunction of independent supporting conditions**, **A justified belief must rest on non-overlapping conditions that each add independent support**
