# Do subjective experiences never influence perception?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 45ba97da-eefd-4d1e-8d72-6c60a2ae3bf6
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Direct Realism](direct-realism-synthesis-dd7d3552-f5e8-48f4-9a89-f17b9eced87a.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that subjective experiences are independent elements that do not alter direct perception of reality because emotions and memories have been shown to influence perception.**, **Consider a scenario in which an individual in a fearful state perceives a neutral expression as threatening. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that subjective experiences do alter perception.**
