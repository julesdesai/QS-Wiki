**Parent:** [Does memory as a reliable process always justify knowledge?](does-memory-as-a-reliable-process-always-justify-knowledge-antithesis-039f9bb3-d40f-4a39-b245-dd3d3f8c42b8.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition is true, the person would believe it**, **Belief sensitivity to truth is more crucial than process reliability**
