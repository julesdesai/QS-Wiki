# Does sensitivity truly differentiate knowledge from mere belief in typical cases?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 4c2f6c28-e0a0-4e91-bf86-22d2dd869246
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Process Reliabilism with Sensitivity](process-reliabilism-with-sensitivity-synthesis-3b7ba929-b462-4672-9048-c64e1338414e.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person knows they are seeing a red apple on their table. Intuitively, the belief about the apple's color is knowledge.**, **In a similar scenario, replace the apple with a fake red apple under extremely rare lighting conditions causing the observer to still believe it is a real apple, indicating they would hold the belief even if false, challenging the necessity of sensitivity.**
