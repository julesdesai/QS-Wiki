# Knowledge without Epistemic Dependence on Random Events

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Is knowledge truly excluded by epistemic luck?](is-knowledge-truly-excluded-by-epistemic-luck-antithesis-3ad0c478-a675-461b-985f-db8fbf8434b9.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the truth of the belief not depend on random or coincidental factors**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the truth of their belief does not depend on random or coincidental factors**
