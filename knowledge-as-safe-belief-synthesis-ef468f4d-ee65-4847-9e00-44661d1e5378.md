## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition could not easily have been false given the context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition is true, the person believes it due to a reliable process**
