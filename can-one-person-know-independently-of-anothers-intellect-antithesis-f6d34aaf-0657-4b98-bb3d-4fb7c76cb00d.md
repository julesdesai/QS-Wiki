# Can one person know independently of another's intellect?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f6d34aaf-0657-4b98-bb3d-4fb7c76cb00d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-b6d42ffa-59fb-457e-877f-3bcb8ac1af86.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person copies a true belief from someone who used intellectual virtue to reach the conclusion, but the copier does not exercise any virtue themselves.**, **Copying a belief does not involve the copier's own intellectual virtues.**, **Knowledge ideally should arise from one's own cognitive faculties and virtues, not merely from duplicate beliefs.**
