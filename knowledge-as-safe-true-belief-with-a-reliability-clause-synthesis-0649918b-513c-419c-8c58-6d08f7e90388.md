# Knowledge as Safe True Belief with a Reliability Clause

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 0649918b-513c-419c-8c58-6d08f7e90388
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does knowing require more than merely having a true belief across nearby possible worlds?](does-knowing-require-more-than-merely-having-a-true-belief-across-nearby-possible-worlds-antithesis-c847be1b-cf03-46ec-beb4-2390ca8987ea.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in nearby possible worlds where the person believes the proposition, the manner in which they arrived at the belief would not lead to falsehood**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and in nearby possible worlds where they believe the proposition, the manner in which they arrived at the belief would not lead to falsehood**
