# Do all true beliefs indicate knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 01cf9c55-28ea-45bd-9d67-f0b28cfa3c3b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism](reliabilism-synthesis-d45aac74-7303-443b-9ed5-2296b7025d59.md)

## Content

**A person may hold an irrational belief that happens to be true, such as believing in a superstition that coincidentally aligns with a factual event**, **While the belief is true, intuitively, it seems incorrect to label this as knowledge**
