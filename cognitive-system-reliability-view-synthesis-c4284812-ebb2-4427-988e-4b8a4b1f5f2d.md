## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their cognitive system produces beliefs in a reliable manner**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is not necessary for the reliability to be explicitly understood or acknowledged**
