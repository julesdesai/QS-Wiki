# Are dispositional abilities applicable to all types of knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 07a16ed7-7431-47d9-84df-92714a205ec1
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Dispositional Account of Knowledge](dispositional-account-of-knowledge-synthesis-697a3559-6974-4820-bdde-37c51f6f9430.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which an individual knows how to ride a bike, a type of procedural knowledge, without having the dispositional abilities related to propositional knowledge like belief in facts. In such a scenario, intuitively, we judge that the knowledge does not depend on propositional abilities**, **It is implausible to assume that dispositional abilities related to propositional knowledge fully capture all forms of knowledge, such as skills or know-how**
