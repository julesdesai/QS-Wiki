# Can intuitive insight be a form of adequate evidence for beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 83301618-dc56-4a6a-8999-949de797f92d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Evidentialist Theory of Knowledge](evidentialist-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-d871be87-85c4-4634-87bb-b00a8ec22277.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person develops a belief based on a strong intuitive insight, which consistently leads to true beliefs. In such a case, intuitively, we might view this insight as providing adequate evidence.**, **This scenario challenges the idea that only conventional evidence can justify beliefs and highlights intuition as a potential alternative form of evidence.**
