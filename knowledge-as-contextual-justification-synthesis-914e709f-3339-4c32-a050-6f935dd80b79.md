# Knowledge as Contextual Justification

**Parent:** [Can intellectual virtues be reliably identified and distinguished?](can-intellectual-virtues-be-reliably-identified-and-distinguished-antithesis-9fe5a52b-a8e1-4ba3-8039-32e64982f0cf.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification is considered acceptable in the prevailing context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification is considered acceptable in the prevailing context**
