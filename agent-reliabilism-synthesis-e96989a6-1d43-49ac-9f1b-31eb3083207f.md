## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief results from the exercise of reliable cognitive abilities**, **Reliable cognitive abilities are those which are generally truth-conducive across a range of situations**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief results from the exercise of reliable cognitive abilities**
