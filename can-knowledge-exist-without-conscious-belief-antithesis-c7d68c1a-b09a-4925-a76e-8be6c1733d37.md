# Can knowledge exist without conscious belief?

**Parent:** [Embodied Cognition with Reliance on Bodily Skills](embodied-cognition-with-reliance-on-bodily-skills-synthesis-e9d28d24-22c1-4ccc-a9a1-a716398534d0.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that knowledge requires conscious belief. This principle together with the claim that knowledge does not require conscious belief entails a contradiction.**, **Consider a scenario where a person, while unconscious, gains a belief that matches reality but they are unaware of this belief. Intuitively, we would not consider this person to have knowledge.**
