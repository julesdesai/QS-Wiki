# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with No Undercutting Defeaters

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no undercutting defeaters for the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is justified, and there are no undercutting defeaters for the belief**
