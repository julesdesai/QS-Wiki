## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they arrive at the truth through an intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification through intellectual virtue, the proposition is true, and their intellectual virtue reliably leads to truth**
