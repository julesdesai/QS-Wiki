## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A person knows a proposition if they have justification for believing the proposition**, **A person knows a proposition when belief, truth, and justification are present without additional conditions**
