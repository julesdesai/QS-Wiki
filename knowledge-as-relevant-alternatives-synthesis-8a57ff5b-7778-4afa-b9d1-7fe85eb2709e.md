## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that among all the relevant alternative propositions, they distinguish the true proposition**, **The range of relevant alternatives is context-dependent**
