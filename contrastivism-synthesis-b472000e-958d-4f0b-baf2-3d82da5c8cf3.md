## Content

**Knowledge is a relation between the knower, a proposition, and a set of alternatives**, **A person knows a proposition if they believe it in contrast to relevant alternatives**, **A person knows a proposition if the proposition is true in contrast to relevant alternatives**, **The context influences which alternatives are relevant, but not the standards for knowing**
