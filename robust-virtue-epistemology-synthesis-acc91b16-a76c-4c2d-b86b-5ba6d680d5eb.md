## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from an intellectual virtue that is not environment-dependent**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the true belief is not due to intellectual luck**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief arises from a non-environment-dependent virtue, and the belief is not a matter of luck**
