# Does the ability to counterfactually rely on truth-tracking equate to knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2c45c2e9-d467-4556-be10-300049ae8d2a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Process Reliabilism with Nozick's Condition](process-reliabilism-with-nozicks-condition-synthesis-5d350bc0-f5e6-4b33-b9c5-feeee7d84d21.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person has the capability to truth-track but fails to counterfactually exhibit this ability due to unrecognized interference. Intuitively, failure to demonstrate consistent truth-tracking creates doubt about the status of their belief as knowledge.**, **It is implausible for knowledge to depend solely on potential, unverified truth-tracking without practical counterfactual validation.**
