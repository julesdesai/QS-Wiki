# Can Reliabilism solve the problem of epiphenomenal beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 96239361-2799-47e2-8f49-d05b21f029d6
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism](reliabilism-synthesis-e69ee86a-1c0a-4d0e-840e-f25c67fd8e9a.md)

## Content

**Suppose a belief formed as a side effect of a cognitive process, like a computer accidentally outputting a correct result without being programmed for it. Even if the belief is true, it seems counterintuitive to consider this knowledge.**, **A process leading to belief formation that is reliable by chance without being designed or intended to produce true beliefs may not be considered knowledge-generating.**
