# Does high stakes lead to genuine belief revision?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 26478387-3512-468b-9988-86b664df7941
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Subject-Sensitive Invariantism](subject-sensitive-invariantism-synthesis-a38d2af6-3ec4-421c-a558-c594d0cbc8b7.md)

## Content

**In a situation where the stakes are high, causing doubt in a previously held true belief, intuitively, the original knowledge of the true proposition is simply obscured, not truly lost.**, **It is plausible to assert that genuine revision of belief requires a change in evidence, not merely changes in stakes or consequences. This challenges the notion that stakes inherently affect the status of knowledge.**
