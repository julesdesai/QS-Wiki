# Justified True Belief without Necessary Belief

**Parent:** [Is it coherent for someone to be skeptical and yet know?](is-it-coherent-for-someone-to-be-skeptical-and-yet-know-antithesis-ed514629-0ee6-4dd7-afbc-655fc9cf3227.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is true and they have justification for it**, **Belief is not a required component for knowledge**
