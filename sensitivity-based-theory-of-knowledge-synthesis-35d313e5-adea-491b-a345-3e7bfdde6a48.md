## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, the belief must be sensitive to the truth of the proposition**, **A belief is sensitive if, were the proposition false, the person would not hold that belief**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief tracks the truth across relevant possible worlds**, **Knowledge requires that beliefs respond appropriately to changes in the truth of the claim**
