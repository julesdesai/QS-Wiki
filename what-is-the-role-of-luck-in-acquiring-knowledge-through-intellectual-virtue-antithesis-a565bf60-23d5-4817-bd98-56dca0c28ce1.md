# What is the role of luck in acquiring knowledge through intellectual virtue?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a565bf60-23d5-4817-bd98-56dca0c28ce1
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-b6aa4282-ad09-4174-b191-6cfd3fcbdf83.md)

## Content

**Suppose a case in which a person forms a true belief through intellectual virtue but only due to luck, such as being in the right place at the right time**, **Intuitively, knowledge should be stable and not merely a product of luck. If true belief is achieved by virtue, but due to luck, then it questions whether it constitutes knowledge**
