# Can group knowledge emerge without each individual possessing intellectual virtues?

**Node ID:** 1869976d-f5b2-4f9d-a29e-8bfcd9a9434d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a group collectively generates knowledge through the contributions of individuals with varying levels of intellectual virtues. Intuitively, we must decide whether the group's knowledge depends on all individuals possessing intellectual virtues.**, **A group could possess knowledge even if not every individual member possesses the necessary intellectual virtues, suggesting an independent collective process.**
