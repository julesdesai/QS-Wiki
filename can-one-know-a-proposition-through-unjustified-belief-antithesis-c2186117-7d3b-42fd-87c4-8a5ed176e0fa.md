# Can one know a proposition through unjustified belief?

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that for knowledge, a belief must be justified.**, **Consider an individual who holds a belief without justification due to biases or misinformation but meets the contextual criteria for truth. Intuitively, this leads to the notion they cannot truly know the proposition without some form of justification.**
