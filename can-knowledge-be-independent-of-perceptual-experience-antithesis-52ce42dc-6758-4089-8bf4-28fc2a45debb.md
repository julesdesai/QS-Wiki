# Can knowledge be independent of perceptual experience?

**Node ID:** 52ce42dc-6758-4089-8bf4-28fc2a45debb

## Content

**A plausible principle is that knowledge can be obtained through testimony or other indirect means.**, **Testimony does not always directly rely on perceptual experiences.**, **If knowledge is obtained through testimony, it can be independent of individual perceptual experiences and operations.**
