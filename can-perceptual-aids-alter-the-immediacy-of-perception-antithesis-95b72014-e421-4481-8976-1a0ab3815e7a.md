# Can perceptual aids alter the immediacy of perception?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 95b72014-e421-4481-8976-1a0ab3815e7a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Direct Realism Summary](direct-realism-summary-synthesis-7349b7af-1197-471c-ac46-8eb6cd6bb07e.md)

## Content

**When using night vision goggles, objects are perceived differently than with unaided vision.**, **This suggests that perception can be mediated and not immediate, opposing the idea of direct access through perception.**
