# Does knowledge require more than intellectual virtue if two people have equally virtuous processes but one lacks knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 6427e5ce-2142-43de-bb31-1dad6f9707de
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-e399f75f-498c-40d7-978e-5c4c6418f273.md)

## Content

**Two people might form beliefs through equally virtuous processes, yet only one results in knowledge if environmental factors differ.**, **The dependency on external factors suggests that intellectual virtue alone cannot account for knowledge.**
