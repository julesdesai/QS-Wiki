## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they can successfully communicate the truth of the proposition to others**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they can successfully communicate the truth of the proposition and the proposition is true**
