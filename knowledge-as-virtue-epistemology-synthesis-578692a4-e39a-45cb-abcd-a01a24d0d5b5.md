# Knowledge as Virtue Epistemology

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 578692a4-e39a-45cb-abcd-a01a24d0d5b5
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can coherence ever override truth?](can-coherence-ever-override-truth-antithesis-0ff1b178-16c3-444c-807e-72a9d217ea5f.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is due to the intellectual virtue of the person**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is due to their intellectual virtue**
