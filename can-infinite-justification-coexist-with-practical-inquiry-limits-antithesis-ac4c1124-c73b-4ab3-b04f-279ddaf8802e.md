# Can infinite justification coexist with practical inquiry limits?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ac4c1124-c73b-4ab3-b04f-279ddaf8802e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Finite Infinitism](finite-infinitism-synthesis-ec56b28f-9687-4f6e-b141-48dc2e075636.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person stops inquiring because their practical purposes are met, yet lacks infinite justification. Intuitively, they do not possess knowledge.**, **The requirement for potentially infinite reasons conflicts with the notion that inquiry stops when practical purposes are met.**, **If inquiry ends for practical reasons, the justification requirement conflicts with the finite nature of human reasoning processes.**
