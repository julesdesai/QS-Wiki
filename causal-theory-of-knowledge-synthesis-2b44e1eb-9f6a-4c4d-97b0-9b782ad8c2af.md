# Causal Theory of Knowledge

**Depth:** 3
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is a true belief that serves no practical purpose still considered knowledge?](is-a-true-belief-that-serves-no-practical-purpose-still-considered-knowledge-antithesis-d94f1031-3fba-4f8c-87da-660de351bc68.md)

## Content

**A person knows a proposition if their belief is caused in the right way by the fact it is true**, **The causal relationship must reliably connect the belief with the truth of the proposition**, **The proposition being true is a necessary condition for it to be known**, **A belief’s usefulness or practicality is not required for it to qualify as knowledge**
