## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, their belief must be the result of an exercise of their intellectual virtues**, **An intellectual virtue is a trait that reliably yields true beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is safely true, meaning it could not easily have been false**
