# Knowledge as Safety Theory

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can a reliable process result in a true belief without knowledge?](can-a-reliable-process-result-in-a-true-belief-without-knowledge-antithesis-3928ce1b-b1dc-4a6f-821c-3a252f3c5065.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in nearby possible worlds where the person believes the proposition, it is not false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and in nearby possible worlds where the person believes it, it is not false**
