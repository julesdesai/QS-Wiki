# Can sense data lead to perceptual errors that challenge knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 380d5c2b-6505-4111-ad39-1273969e5cc1
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Indirect Realism](indirect-realism-synthesis-6f280b3f-d71e-41be-89e6-75cad68b6820.md)

## Content

**Sense data may cause perceptual errors when external conditions affect sensory experience, leading to misrepresentation of reality.**, **There can be cases where sense data reflect illusions, such as a stick appearing bent in water, which distorts reality.**, **Illusions challenge the reliability of sense data to mediate accurate perception and thus impugn knowledge.**
