## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A belief qualifies as knowledge if it is produced by a systematic and reliable cognitive process**, **A reliable process consistently produces true beliefs over false ones**, **A systematic process implies the absence of luck**
