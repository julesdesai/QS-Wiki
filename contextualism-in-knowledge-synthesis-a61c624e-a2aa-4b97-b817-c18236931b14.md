## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the standards for knowledge vary according to context and these contextual standards are met**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief meets the context-dependent standards for knowledge**
