## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed by a reliable process**, **The reliability required for knowledge depends on contextual factors such as stakes and what is at issue**, **A person knows a proposition if they believe it, the proposition is true, and the contextual standards deem the process reliable**, **A reliable process is one that produces true beliefs more often than false ones**
