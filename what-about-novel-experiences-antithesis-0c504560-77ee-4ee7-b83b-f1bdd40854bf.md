## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person has a novel perceptual experience that does not fit into their existing coherent web of experiences. Intuitively, they lack immediate knowledge of how this new experience relates to the world due to its novelty.**, **There is a plausible principle that encountering new information necessitates reevaluation and cannot be immediately integrated into knowledge without a period of adjustment.**
