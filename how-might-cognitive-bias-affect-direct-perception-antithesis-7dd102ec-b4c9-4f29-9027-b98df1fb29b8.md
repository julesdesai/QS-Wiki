# How might cognitive bias affect direct perception?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7dd102ec-b4c9-4f29-9027-b98df1fb29b8
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Direct Realism](direct-realism-synthesis-63f38132-cda9-42dc-9c71-38670cc01e77.md)

## Content

**Cognitive bias refers to systematic patterns of deviation from norm or rationality in judgment, influencing perceptions, such as seeing what one expects to see.**, **In cases where biases influence perception, the perception may be distorted from reality.**, **If cognitive biases influence what is directly perceived, then perception may not necessarily provide immediate, undistorted knowledge of external objects.**
