# Can forming beliefs by a method that is generally reliable still lead to error in specific cases?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 91167961-f28c-4c59-9eed-2fc727c2e0f9
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Safety-based Account of Knowledge](safety-based-account-of-knowledge-synthesis-4053710d-87a3-4da5-a24a-d38f32fb1dbb.md)

## Content
