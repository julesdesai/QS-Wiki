# Is mere reliability sufficient for true belief without awareness?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 168d5370-15f9-48c5-9439-3fb48602b4eb
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Epistemic Externalism](epistemic-externalism-synthesis-3f203114-9a4a-4d29-9451-dfb5538c3c76.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case where someone's belief is accurate due to environmental factors they are unaware of. Intuitively, we either have to say they possess knowledge or they do not. If we say they possess knowledge, then mere reliability suffices. If we say they do not, then awareness is necessary for knowledge.**, **It is implausible that accuracy alone confers truth without the believer's awareness.**
