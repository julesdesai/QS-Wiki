# Is empirical evidence always necessary for belief justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 64e4a28e-8f4e-477c-8122-bf0f8ee4a443
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Evidentialist Theory of Knowledge](evidentialist-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-d871be87-85c4-4634-87bb-b00a8ec22277.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where cultural or personal experiences, not empirical evidence, provide the foundation for a strong belief. Intuitively, these experiences might be deemed sufficient for justification.**, **This challenges the idea that empirical evidence is the only form of adequate evidence for justified belief.**
