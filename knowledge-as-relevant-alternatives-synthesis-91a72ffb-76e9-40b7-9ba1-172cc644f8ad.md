## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that, among the relevant alternatives, the proposition is the true one**, **The set of relevant alternatives varies depending on non-epistemic factors, such as practical concerns**
