# Can infinite justification lead to practical epistemic paralysis?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 91e712e3-abbc-456a-822d-e3fa11f57acd
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism with Finite Justifications](infinitism-with-finite-justifications-synthesis-69ad7e03-7f20-4a4f-85ef-ebc999d85fe8.md)

## Content

**A scenario in which a person needs to account for an infinite series of justifications might lead them to run into practical difficulties that obstruct their ability to claim knowledge pragmatically.**, **If justification involves potentially infinite reasons, individuals may find themselves in a state of paralysis as they attempt to ascertain each reason, suggesting an impracticality in actual knowledge acquisition.**
