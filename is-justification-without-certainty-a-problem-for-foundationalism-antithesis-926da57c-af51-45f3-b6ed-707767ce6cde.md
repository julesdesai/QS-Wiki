# Is justification without certainty a problem for foundationalism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 926da57c-af51-45f3-b6ed-707767ce6cde
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundationalism with Infinite Background](foundationalism-with-infinite-background-synthesis-b6351c4d-7dab-447a-b3c7-52e8418919cd.md)

## Content

**Foundational beliefs are considered to provide the certainty for non-foundational beliefs.**, **If the background structure is infinite and potentially uncertain, the justification of foundational beliefs might be tentative.**, **Tentative justification of foundational beliefs does not secure the certainty needed for knowledge.**
