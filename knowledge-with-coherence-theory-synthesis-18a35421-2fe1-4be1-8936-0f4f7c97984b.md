## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres with a system of mutually supportive beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is not the result of manipulation**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief coheres with a system of mutually supportive beliefs, and the belief is not the result of manipulation**
