# Does evidential support always guarantee knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f2a05150-8f67-4872-af7a-a91eac10af6f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Evidentialist Foundationalism](evidentialist-foundationalism-synthesis-8074c848-6a3d-411d-b5c9-e6a07bd8fe19.md)

## Content

**Even if a belief is supported by foundational evidence, there is a possibility of error.**, **External factors, like deception or illusion, can undermine the link between evidence and the truth.**, **Having foundational evidential support does not always secure knowledge if the context is misleading.**
