# Sensitivity-based Knowledge

**Parent:** [Does contextualism adequately address the possibility of error?](does-contextualism-adequately-address-the-possibility-of-error-antithesis-49a66f86-649c-4f97-860c-ec3f4703190e.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief would not be held in the nearest possible world where the proposition is false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief would not be held in the nearest possible world where the proposition is false**
