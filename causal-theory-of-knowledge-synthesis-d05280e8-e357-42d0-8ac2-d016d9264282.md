## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is an appropriate causal connection between the truth of the proposition and their belief**, **A person knows a proposition if it is true, they believe it, and there is a causal link between the truth and the belief**
