# Does sheer coincidence override reliability?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9f542ca6-b446-4e28-a55a-7ea537c1211a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilist Theory of Knowledge](reliabilist-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-fec1f444-460d-4835-9da9-5fd5405f3ea1.md)

## Content

**Consider a person who believes a true proposition because of a completely unrelated coincidence that aligns with their belief. Intuitively, we would likely deny that the person has knowledge of the proposition.**, **Coincidental truths that align with a belief formed reliably challenge the validity of knowledge based solely on reliability.**
