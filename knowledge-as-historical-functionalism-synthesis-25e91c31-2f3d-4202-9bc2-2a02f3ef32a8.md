**Parent:** [Does the historical reliability of cognitive systems matter for current knowledge?](does-the-historical-reliability-of-cognitive-systems-matter-for-current-knowledge-antithesis-32e9099a-aaa1-49d5-bd29-daf34a0a57bb.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief to be produced by a properly functioning cognitive system**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the cognitive system to have a history of reliable functioning**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is produced by a properly functioning cognitive system, and the cognitive system has a history of reliable functioning**
