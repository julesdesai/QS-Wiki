## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed under conditions that reliably produce true beliefs**, **A person can know a proposition even if the justification is not consciously accessible**
