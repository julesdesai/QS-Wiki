## Content

**Consider a scenario in which cognitive biases influence a person's perception and lead them to perceive things inaccurately. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that the person does not have true knowledge.**, **A person knows a proposition if perception and cognitive aids together correspond to reality, but cognitive distortions could potentially disrupt this correspondence.**
