## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they acquire the belief through cognitive skill or virtue**, **A person knows a proposition if it is true, they believe it, and they came to believe it through intellectual virtue**
