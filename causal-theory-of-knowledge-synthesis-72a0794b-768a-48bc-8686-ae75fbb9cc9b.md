## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person believes the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the person believes the proposition, the proposition is true, and there is an appropriate causal connection between the belief and the fact**
