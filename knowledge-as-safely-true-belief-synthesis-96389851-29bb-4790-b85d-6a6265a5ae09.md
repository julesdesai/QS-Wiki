## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief could not easily have been false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **Knowledge is a true belief that is safe from being false in nearby possible worlds**
