# Does Infinitism conflict with the simplicity of everyday knowledge claims?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e21757c1-4c46-4676-86a5-8472325c821a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism](infinitism-synthesis-dd7098a7-016a-4f9d-9b36-944f2be4f1f4.md)

## Content

**Everyday knowledge often relies on simple and finite chains of justification.**, **The requirement for infinite justification seems at odds with the ordinary practice of claiming knowledge based on limited evidence.**, **There are many cases where we consider ourselves to have knowledge that do not involve infinite chains of reasoning.**
