**Parent:** [Can sensitivity and reliability coexist as conclusive conditions for knowledge?](can-sensitivity-and-reliability-coexist-as-conclusive-conditions-for-knowledge-antithesis-7deca36a-2174-4e05-89e1-a031dbe002db.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief arises from intellectual virtue**, **An intellectual virtue is a reliable cognitive faculty or trait that generally leads to true beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief arises from intellectual virtue**
