## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the person tracks the truth across close possible worlds**, **Truth-tracking involves the belief being true and would not be held if the proposition were false**
