# Is the theory able to account for testimonial knowledge obtained through others?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d60072ed-02f1-4d54-a659-5805df9f5e95

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Nozick's Conditional Theory of Knowledge](nozicks-conditional-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-ff394d52-e522-4d27-8272-d4808dc162bd.md)

## Content

**Suppose an individual bases a belief solely on someone else's testimony without direct evidence but ends up true and reliable. If testimonial knowledge aligns with Nozick's conditions devoid of personal verification, intuitively it seems insufficient.**, **Trusting testimonial-based belief blindly questions robustness of knowledge sourcing without first-hand verification.**
