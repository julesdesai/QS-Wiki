# Knowledge as Non-Contextual Internalism

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 48c119c9-43b3-4cfc-b22e-9731786226b9
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is context-sensitive justification sufficient for knowledge?](is-context-sensitive-justification-sufficient-for-knowledge-antithesis-a4864c17-7c36-450b-9a05-a8a283337568.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification is internally accessible to the person**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification is internally accessible to the person**
