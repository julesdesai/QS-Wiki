# Knowledge as Infinitism

**Parent:** [Does coherence ensure the truth of interconnected beliefs?](does-coherence-ensure-the-truth-of-interconnected-beliefs-antithesis-78eebc44-4032-44b2-b221-6b8857c8a273.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is part of an infinite chain of reasons**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is justified by an infinite chain of reasons**
