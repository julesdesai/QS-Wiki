# Does knowledge really originate solely from intellectual virtue?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a7cecaad-3b7a-4b03-ab25-63d3d560e303
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology with Defeasibility](virtue-epistemology-with-defeasibility-synthesis-802a6edd-3a94-41b9-8765-bb5c975aae31.md)

## Content

**A person can come to a true belief via accidental factors unrelated to intellectual virtue, yet this would not count as knowledge.**, **Intellectual virtue must play a causal and essential role for a belief to be considered knowledge, excluding accidental or irrelevant factors.**
