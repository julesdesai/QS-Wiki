# Non-defective Virtuous Knowledge

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** f52e1373-2e52-4c98-8e5a-96086247da8e
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can fundamentally flawed intelligence yield knowledge?](can-fundamentally-flawed-intelligence-yield-knowledge-antithesis-87990e63-8926-48e6-afb3-f294bb391772.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed through a virtuous process free from fundamental defects**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is formed through a virtuous process free from fundamental defects**
