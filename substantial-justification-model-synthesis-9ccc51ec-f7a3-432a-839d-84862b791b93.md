## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief has substantial justification**, **For a belief to have substantial justification, it is not necessary for the person to be aware of the justification**
