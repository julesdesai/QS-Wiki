# Does pragmatic justification adequately capture our concept of knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 93a27b67-ebcb-4dd4-b9bf-3f0cfe8de6de
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatism: Practical Consequences](pragmatism-practical-consequences-synthesis-0f8107eb-2a4a-42d8-a5a9-2983c41a2844.md)

## Content

**The practical consequences of holding a belief may vary between different situations.**, **Knowledge is traditionally understood as objective and not situation-dependent.**, **It is implausible that justification should vary with practical consequences if knowledge is objective.**
