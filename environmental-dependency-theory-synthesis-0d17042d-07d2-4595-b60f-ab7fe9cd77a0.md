## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed by a cognitive process**, **The reliability of the cognitive process is determined by its effectiveness in the particular environment where it is used**
