# Knowledge Given Reliability Over Autonomy

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** e458ec5f-4a86-4a21-917e-13122f6d722a
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can beliefs formed under duress be knowledge?](can-beliefs-formed-under-duress-be-knowledge-antithesis-fe972929-e5e4-4829-b660-58e13f0c6ac0.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief to result from a process ensuring reliability**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is not necessary for the belief formation process to prioritize autonomy**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief results from a process ensuring reliability**
