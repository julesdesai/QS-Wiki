## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for the proposition**, **An individual may dispositionally believe a proposition, contributing to their knowledge**, **A person knows a proposition if it is true, they have justification for it, and possess a dispositional belief regarding it**
