# Knowledge with Understanding

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is epistemic luck exclusion sufficient without understanding?](is-epistemic-luck-exclusion-sufficient-without-understanding-antithesis-d2b6cc51-85c3-42bf-86f1-1b67f16420a7.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is not true merely due to epistemic luck**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they understand the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, their belief is not true merely due to epistemic luck, and they understand the proposition**
