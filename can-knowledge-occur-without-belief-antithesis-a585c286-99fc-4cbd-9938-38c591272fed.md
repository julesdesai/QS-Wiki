# Can knowledge occur without belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a585c286-99fc-4cbd-9938-38c591272fed
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Evidentialism Summary](evidentialism-summary-synthesis-dea02623-290c-44c3-896b-2f967081eb1b.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person has a true belief formed by accident and without any awareness of the evidence. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that the person does not have knowledge.**, **A case in which a person actually possesses all relevant evidence but does not consider it consciously may still result in true belief without genuine knowledge.**
