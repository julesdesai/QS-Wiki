## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A belief is non-lucky if its truth does not depend on luck**, **A belief is justified if it is non-lucky**, **To know, a person's justified true belief must be non-lucky and not contingent on chance**
