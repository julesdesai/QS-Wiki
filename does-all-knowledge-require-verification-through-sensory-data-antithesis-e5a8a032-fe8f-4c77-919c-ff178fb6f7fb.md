# Does all knowledge require verification through sensory data?

**Parent:** [Empiricism Summary](empiricism-summary-synthesis-4e15833e-fa6c-416c-a78e-6563a82d7628.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which someone acquires knowledge that a friend is kind through consistent observation and interaction rather than direct sensory verification. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that verification through sensory data is not necessary for this knowledge.**, **Some forms of knowledge involve complex social or psychological understanding that isn't always verified through straightforward sensory data.**
