## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, their belief must be apt**, **A belief is apt if it is true due to the intellectual competence of the group involved**, **The truth of the belief can stem from the collective skill or competence of multiple individuals**, **Aptness ensures that knowledge arises from intellectual virtue, which may be distributed among several agents**
