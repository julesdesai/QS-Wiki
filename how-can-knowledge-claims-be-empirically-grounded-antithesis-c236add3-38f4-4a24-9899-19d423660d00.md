# How can knowledge claims be empirically grounded?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c236add3-38f4-4a24-9899-19d423660d00
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Coherence Theory](pragmatic-coherence-theory-synthesis-0a69366a-ab49-4b15-a3e1-b772859c47c7.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a belief system is wholly coherent yet totally disconnected from empirical evidence.**, **In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that knowledge must involve more than mere coherence of beliefs.**, **A belief coheres within a system without needing any empirical grounding, undermining the connection between knowledge and the reality it describes.**
