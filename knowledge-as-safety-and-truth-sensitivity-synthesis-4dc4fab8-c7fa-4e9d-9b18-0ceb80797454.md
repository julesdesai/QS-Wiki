# Knowledge as Safety and Truth Sensitivity

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is safe from being false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the person believes it, it is true, and their belief is safe from easily being false**
