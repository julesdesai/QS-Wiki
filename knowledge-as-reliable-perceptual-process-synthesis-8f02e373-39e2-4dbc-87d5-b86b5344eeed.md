# Knowledge as Reliable Perceptual Process

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge exist without inferential justification?](can-knowledge-exist-without-inferential-justification-antithesis-ba83cee5-5b0e-4536-81f9-4f3f00ce2190.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is produced by a reliable perceptual process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is produced by a reliable perceptual process**
