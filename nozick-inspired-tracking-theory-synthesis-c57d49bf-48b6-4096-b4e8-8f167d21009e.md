## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it, and if the proposition were true, the person would believe it**, **The belief has to track the truth through both counterfactual and factual scenarios**
