# Safety Condition for Knowledge

**Parent:** [Do all knowledge claims require true beliefs from properly functioning faculties?](do-all-knowledge-claims-require-true-beliefs-from-properly-functioning-faculties-antithesis-8da5fe30-b794-4be6-bcf8-b61b395f3d4b.md)

## Content

**For a belief to constitute knowledge, it must be safe**, **A belief is safe if, in nearby possible worlds where the belief is held, it is not false**, **A belief meeting this criterion constitutes knowledge if it is true**
