# Can infinite justification ever justify actions based on knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 8f3d8ecb-640b-4d1b-8cdb-421eb9a99225
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualist Infinitism](contextualist-infinitism-synthesis-74ebcf03-3de8-4e28-950c-dc2eefc9dc13.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that knowledge justifies actions.**, **If the justification is infinite, decision-making is delayed indefinitely due to never reaching full justification.**, **It is implausible that actionable knowledge requires an unfinished justification process.**
