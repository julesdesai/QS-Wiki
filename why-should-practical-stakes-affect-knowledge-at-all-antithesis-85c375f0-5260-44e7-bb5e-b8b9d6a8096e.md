# Why should practical stakes affect knowledge at all?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 85c375f0-5260-44e7-bb5e-b8b9d6a8096e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Subject-Sensitive Invariantism](subject-sensitive-invariantism-synthesis-687e35ad-b9eb-49bd-a730-d8bc9269ea3a.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which two people believe the same true proposition, but one has more at risk if they are wrong. Intuitively, we would not say the one with more at risk has less knowledge.**, **The principle that knowledge standards should remain unaffected by practical stakes contradicts the idea that practical stakes affect knowledge standards.**
