# Can a reliable process based on false circumstances lead to knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c8d8f18b-d87f-4c01-a184-4d1104892a1d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Evidentialist Reliabilism](evidentialist-reliabilism-synthesis-b2fd7651-b8ba-4bbd-aa9b-2c3c304a468b.md)

## Content

**Imagine a scenario where a person forms a true belief through a reliable process that assumes false external circumstances.**, **It is implausible that knowledge can be claimed when the reliability of the process depends on false assumptions.**
