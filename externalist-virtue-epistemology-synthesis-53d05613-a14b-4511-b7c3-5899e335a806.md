## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person exercises intellectual virtues in forming the belief**, **The truth of a belief contributes to knowledge only if there is an appropriate external relation between the belief and the fact**, **An appropriate external relation is one where the belief is causally or reliably connected to the fact**, **Intellectual virtues include traits like open-mindedness and intellectual courage**
