**Consider a scenario where teachers attempt to teach students universal standards of knowledge and belief justification. Intuitively, teachers should be able to convey standards that apply across various contexts.**, **If the standards for justification and knowledge vary depending on conversational context, it may hinder the teaching of universally applicable epistemic standards.**
