# Does pragmatic contextualism risk conflating belief with mere pragmatic success?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2a42d8ac-cfe1-45d2-8463-cdf2c6dc54af
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Contextualism](pragmatic-contextualism-synthesis-eb83c85f-dd41-4bfc-8e5a-553d55565568.md)

## Content

**A person may believe in something that produces favorable practical outcomes in a context without having adequate justification for the belief.**, **Without sufficient justification, pragmatic success alone is insufficient for traditional notions of knowledge.**, **There needs to be a distinction between a belief that leads to pragmatic success and one suitably justified to hold the status of knowledge.**
