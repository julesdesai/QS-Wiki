# Is a self-evident foundational belief necessary for justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2de37408-f227-4b4c-ac7a-a1e2e265b000
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundationalist Coherentism](foundationalist-coherentism-synthesis-1575945f-cea5-4275-8c31-c7cabab6c71a.md)

## Content

**Suppose a case where a person has a set of coherent beliefs, but none is self-evident. Intuitively, we might say they have justification for those beliefs despite the lack of a self-evident foundational belief.**, **It is implausible that all justified beliefs must rely on a self-evident foundational belief.**
