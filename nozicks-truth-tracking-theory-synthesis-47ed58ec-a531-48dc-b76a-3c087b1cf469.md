## Content

**A person knows a proposition if their belief tracks the truth**, **A belief tracks the truth if, were the proposition false, the person would not believe it**, **A belief tracks the truth if, were the proposition true, the person would believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**
