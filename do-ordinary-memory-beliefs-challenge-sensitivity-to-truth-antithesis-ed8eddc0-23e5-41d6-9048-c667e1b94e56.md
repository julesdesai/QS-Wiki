# Do ordinary memory beliefs challenge sensitivity to truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ed8eddc0-23e5-41d6-9048-c667e1b94e56
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Nozick's Conditional Entailment](nozicks-conditional-entailment-synthesis-db761f27-9caf-47b5-bb68-5ac892feba1b.md)

## Content

**Consider the scenario where someone remembers where they parked their car.**, **If the memory belief is true, they have knowledge, but there's often no sensitivity where if it were false, they would not believe it.**, **It is implausible that such a mechanism for sensitivity aptly covers the basis typical of memory beliefs as genuinely supporting knowledge.**
