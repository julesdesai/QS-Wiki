# Does belief coherence always imply consistency with reality?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a935523d-ff1e-409b-9605-bad3d3d785bd
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatism with Coherentism](pragmatism-with-coherentism-synthesis-7996efa8-d25e-4d54-acc0-6469819e4077.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person's coherent belief system still conflicts with external evidence. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that despite its coherence, this belief system does not count as knowledge.**, **There is a plausible principle that knowledge must align with reality, and mere internal coherence does not guarantee such alignment.**
