# Justified True Belief with Reliability

**Parent:** [Is safety condition sufficient without considering method reliability?](is-safety-condition-sufficient-without-considering-method-reliability-antithesis-11488082-9a4e-4caf-961b-75184c252d26.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in all nearby possible worlds where the person believes the proposition, the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the method of belief formation is reliable**
