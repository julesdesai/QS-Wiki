# How do time delays in perception fit with naive realism?

## Content

**Consider a case where we see a star that is actually no longer in existence due to the travel time of light. Intuitively, we cannot be directly acquainted with an object that no longer exists.**, **If perceptual experiences involve time delays, then acquaintance cannot be directly with the present existence of objects.**
