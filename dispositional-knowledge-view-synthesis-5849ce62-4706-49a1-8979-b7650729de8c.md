**Parent:** [Is belief always necessary for knowledge?](is-belief-always-necessary-for-knowledge-antithesis-816fbfa5-3c54-46a1-9fca-52e207accc69.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have a disposition to behave in ways that demonstrate implicit understanding of the proposition, the proposition is true, and the disposition arises from a reliable cognitive process**
