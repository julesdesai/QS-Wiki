**Parent:** [Can we know something without the inclination to act on or assert it?](can-we-know-something-without-the-inclination-to-act-on-or-assert-it-antithesis-0e743df0-98da-49a7-8f05-e086e949a1ca.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they are disposed to act on the proposition**, **A person knows a proposition if the proposition is true, they have justification for it, and are disposed to act on it**
