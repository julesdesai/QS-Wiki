## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for the proposition**, **The relevance of factors affecting justification can vary depending on the subject's practical situation**, **The truth condition of knowledge remains unaffected by context**
