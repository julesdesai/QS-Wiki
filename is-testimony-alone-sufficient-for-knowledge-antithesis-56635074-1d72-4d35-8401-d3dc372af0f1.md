# Is testimony alone sufficient for knowledge?

## Content

**Suppose a person accepts a proposition as true solely based on another's testimony without any further evidence or reasoning.**, **Merely relying on testimony without personal intellectual activity does not exercise one's own cognitive virtues.**, **Knowledge in virtue epistemology tends to require personal engagement in intellectual practice, not passive acceptance.**
