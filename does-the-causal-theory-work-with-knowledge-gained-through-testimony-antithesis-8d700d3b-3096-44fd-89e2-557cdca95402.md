# Does the causal theory work with knowledge gained through testimony?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 8d700d3b-3096-44fd-89e2-557cdca95402
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Causal Theory of Knowledge](causal-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-dc61c008-27ed-4921-863e-badc9b961db6.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where an individual gains knowledge through testimony, where the chain of causation involves the truth bearer but is not directly accessible to the hearer; intuitively this seems like knowledge.**, **It is implausible to maintain that each link in the testimonial chain must be directly understood by the receiver for it to count as knowledge.**
