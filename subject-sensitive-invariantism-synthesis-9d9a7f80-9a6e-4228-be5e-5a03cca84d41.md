## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the subject's practical interests are invariantly considered in determining the standards for knowledge**, **Practical stakes affect the level of justification required for a subject to know, but do not affect the objective standards for knowledge**
