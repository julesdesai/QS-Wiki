# Does coherentist phenomenalism adequately ensure objectivity?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d8dd0c4a-a2af-46e9-b00d-d7b71b979ffd
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentist Phenomenalism](coherentist-phenomenalism-synthesis-7f881170-8b02-4aad-92a8-2bed5a4b8a3c.md)

## Content

**Coherentist phenomenalism emphasizes subjective coherence rather than objective truth.**, **A key aspect of knowledge is its capacity to connect with an objective reality independent of individual belief systems.**, **Without a way to ensure beliefs relate to actual states of the world, coherentist phenomenalism could fail to secure objective knowledge.**
