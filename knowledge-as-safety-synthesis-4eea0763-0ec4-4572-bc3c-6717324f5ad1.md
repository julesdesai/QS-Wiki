# Knowledge as Safety

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can someone have knowledge if their belief is true by luck rather than intellectual virtue?](can-someone-have-knowledge-if-their-belief-is-true-by-luck-rather-than-intellectual-virtue-antithesis-d98803d0-5b58-4bb3-8e81-7d33e1ee1de0.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is safe, meaning that in nearby possible worlds where they believe it, it is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is safe**
