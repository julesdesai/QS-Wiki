# Truth-Tracking Theory

**Parent:** [Can justified beliefs fail to constitute knowledge if they are false?](can-justified-beliefs-fail-to-constitute-knowledge-if-they-are-false-antithesis-3429f620-2b4c-455d-8480-ece30b883c2a.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief changes in accordance with the truth across nearby possible situations**, **For a person to know a proposition, their belief must track the truth by being formed through a reliable cognitive process**
