## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief results from distributed intellectual virtues in a cooperative group**, **Distributed intellectual virtues are character traits collectively held by group members leading to true beliefs**
