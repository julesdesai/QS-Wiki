## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they can eliminate all relevant alternatives where the proposition is false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and they can eliminate all relevant alternatives**
