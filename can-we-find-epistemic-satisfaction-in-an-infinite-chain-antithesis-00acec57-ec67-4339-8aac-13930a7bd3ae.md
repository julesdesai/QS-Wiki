# Can we find epistemic satisfaction in an infinite chain?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 00acec57-ec67-4339-8aac-13930a7bd3ae
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism](infinitism-synthesis-c0072d0f-5658-4115-88b4-8385b90f0067.md)

## Content

**Consider a situation where a learner seeks to understand a complex topic and is given an infinite series of justifications. Intuitively, the learner requires a stopping point to achieve understanding and satisfaction.**, **It is implausible that learners find value in never-ending explanations, indicating practical stopping points are essential for genuine understanding.**
