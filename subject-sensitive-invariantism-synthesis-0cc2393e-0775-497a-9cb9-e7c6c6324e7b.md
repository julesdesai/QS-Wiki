## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person has sufficient stakes or interests in the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification criteria are satisfied invariantly**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, use invariant criteria, the proposition is true, and the stakes justify the belief**
