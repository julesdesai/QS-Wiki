## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief be infallible relative to the given epistemic context**, **Infallibility is defined as impossibility of being false given epistemic conditions and information**
