# Does externalism adequately consider the Gettier problem?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 4292befd-6614-4f60-933d-46dade55271d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalism](externalism-synthesis-9fb6627e-b63e-4d81-a81a-128eb1da4172.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where an individual forms a belief that is true and appears justified due to external conditions, yet the truth emerges from accidental factors unrelated to the justification process. Intuitively, we would judge that the presence of such accidental factors indicates the absence of knowledge.**, **Cases where true belief and external justification coincide through coincidence do not fulfill the intuitive grasp of knowing.**
