## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their cognitive performance is instrumental in acquiring the true belief**, **Cognitive performance involves skillful and successful use of intellectual abilities**
