# Can rare coincidences undermine truth-tracking as knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b29e2b9f-ee09-4e61-8aae-016a22272a6d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Truth-Tracking Theory](truth-tracking-theory-synthesis-44ea7417-270f-4c93-876d-f98b6754dd68.md)

## Content

**A person might believe a true proposition due to an unrelated event, much like coincidentally winning a lottery ticket with no influence or understanding.**, **The occurrence of truth due to coincidence without tracking poses a problem for relying solely on truth-tracking.**, **This illustrates a case where a belief aligns with truth without the mechanisms needed for tracking, challenging its sufficiency for knowledge.**
