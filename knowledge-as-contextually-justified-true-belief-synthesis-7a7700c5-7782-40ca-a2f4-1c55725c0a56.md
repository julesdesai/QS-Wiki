## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief is justified considering the context**, **Justification is determined by the standards and evidence applicable in the relevant context**
