## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition corresponds to reality**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they hold a justified belief in the true proposition corresponding to reality**
