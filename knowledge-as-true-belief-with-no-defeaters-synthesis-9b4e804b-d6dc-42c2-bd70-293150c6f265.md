# Knowledge as True Belief with No Defeaters

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does the clairvoyance case challenge justified true belief as knowledge?](does-the-clairvoyance-case-challenge-justified-true-belief-as-knowledge-antithesis-bb4d2cb0-e151-4756-aa85-6a0d4b4112e0.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there be no defeaters for their belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there are no defeaters for their belief**
