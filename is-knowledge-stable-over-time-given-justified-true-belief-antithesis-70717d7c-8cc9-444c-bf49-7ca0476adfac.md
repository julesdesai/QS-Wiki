# Is knowledge stable over time given justified true belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 70717d7c-8cc9-444c-bf49-7ca0476adfac
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism Revised](coherentism-revised-synthesis-de283b0a-5fd8-4f4c-bf89-d22a34799ca8.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a scientific theory, once justified and true, becomes discredited and replaced. Intuitively, we would judge that while the theory was once considered knowledge, it no longer counts as such.**, **It is implausible that knowledge, as mere justified true belief, does not account for the dynamic and revisable nature of justification over time.**
