# Virtue Epistemology with Environmental Luck Exclusion

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Do intellectual virtues mischaracterize accidental knowledge?](do-intellectual-virtues-mischaracterize-accidental-knowledge-antithesis-32f5f3ae-127b-4237-b0f0-41aa840e998c.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, their belief must be the result of an exercise of their intellectual virtues**, **An intellectual virtue is a trait that reliably yields true beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it must not be true due to environmental luck, meaning that the truth is not a result of factors external to the person's control or understanding**
