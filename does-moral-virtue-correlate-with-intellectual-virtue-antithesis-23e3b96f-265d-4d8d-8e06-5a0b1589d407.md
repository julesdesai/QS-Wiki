# Does moral virtue correlate with intellectual virtue?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 23e3b96f-265d-4d8d-8e06-5a0b1589d407
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-cfcf3dee-48b2-4ff8-855e-aba5061f4a51.md)

## Content

**It is implausible to assume that possessing moral virtues, such as honesty or integrity, necessarily correlates with possessing intellectual virtues like perceptiveness or acumen.**, **Consider a scenario where an honest person is often credulous to manipulation because they trust authority figures too readily. Intuitively, we might judge that moral virtue does not correlate with achieving true beliefs.**, **The lack of correlation between moral and intellectual virtues suggests that reliance solely on intellectual virtues for knowledge assessment is problematic.**
