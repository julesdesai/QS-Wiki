## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **A belief produced by a reliable process must be such that in most nearby possible worlds where the belief is formed, it remains true**, **A reliable cognitive process safeguards against arriving at false beliefs in similar situations**
