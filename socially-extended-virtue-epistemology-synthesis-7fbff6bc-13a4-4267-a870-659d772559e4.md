# Socially Extended Virtue Epistemology

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [What about knowledge through testimony?](what-about-knowledge-through-testimony-antithesis-bde690a7-f611-45a9-8b46-2e531ff59588.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief to be generated by an intellectual virtue or skill, either individually or socially**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is generated by an intellectual virtue or skill, either individually or socially**
