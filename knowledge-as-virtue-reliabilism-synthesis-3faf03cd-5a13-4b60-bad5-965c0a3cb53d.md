**Parent:** [Can luck conflict with reliable processes in producing knowledge?](can-luck-conflict-with-reliable-processes-in-producing-knowledge-antithesis-f48093f2-0787-4222-81c0-83f5baf7e6bc.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the reliability of the cognitive process is due to the intellectual virtues of the person**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process, the proposition is true, and the reliability is due to their intellectual virtues**
