# Could Infinitism dilute the significance of individual reasons?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 4f42c5b0-acc1-4aa1-b878-933800267f09
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism](infinitism-synthesis-db00e7c1-6a13-4ca5-8f09-6fae7cd29e0f.md)

## Content

**Consider a situation where reasons extend indefinitely, each contributing only minimal supportive value. Intuitively, this would dilute the significance of any individual reason.**, **It is implausible that justification maintains its strength when comprised of an overwhelming number of individually weak reasons.**
