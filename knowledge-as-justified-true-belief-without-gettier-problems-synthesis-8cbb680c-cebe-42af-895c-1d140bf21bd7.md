# Knowledge as Justified True Belief (Without Gettier Problems)

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 8cbb680c-cebe-42af-895c-1d140bf21bd7
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does sufficient evidence always yield true knowledge?](does-sufficient-evidence-always-yield-true-knowledge-antithesis-1c42cbe3-4108-43dc-a20a-42492f3ec6b9.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief in the proposition is justified**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have a justified belief in the proposition and the proposition is true**
