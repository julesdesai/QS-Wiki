# Knowledge as Reliably Produced Beliefs with Causal Connection

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Do Gettier problems undermine reliability theory?](do-gettier-problems-undermine-reliability-theory-antithesis-abc06ba5-bffc-4931-82be-9189a768ae50.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is a causal connection between the truth of the proposition and the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process, and there is a causal connection between the truth and belief**
