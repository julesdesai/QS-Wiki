# Knowledge as Justified True Belief (JTB)

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** eadf5333-fecb-4d49-9420-b4eed4ece1a3
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does contextualism undermine the normative role of knowledge?](does-contextualism-undermine-the-normative-role-of-knowledge-antithesis-6a6df525-ed48-423b-a811-8bef992be55b.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person has justification for the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is justified, true, and the person believes it**
