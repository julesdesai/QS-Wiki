# Can pragmatic coherence justify contradictory beliefs?

## Content

**Consider a scenario where two individuals have mutually coherent but directly contradictory beliefs.**, **In such a scenario, intuitively we would question the pragmatic justification of mutually exclusive beliefs.**, **If pragmatic coherence justifies beliefs that are contradictory, it challenges the idea of coherence as a basis for knowledge.**
