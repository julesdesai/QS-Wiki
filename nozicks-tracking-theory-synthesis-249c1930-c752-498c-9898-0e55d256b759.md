## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were true, the person would believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief tracks the truth in nearby possible worlds**
