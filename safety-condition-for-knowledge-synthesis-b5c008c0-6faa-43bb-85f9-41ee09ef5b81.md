# Safety Condition for Knowledge

## Content

**Knowledge requires an evidential connection to the truth**, **A belief is knowledge if it is supported by sufficient evidence**, **The evidence must be appropriately tied to the truth of the proposition**, **A belief is knowledge if it could not easily be false**, **The belief must be true in all nearby possible worlds where the conditions producing the belief remain constant**
