# Is there a unified way to adjudicate between conflicting contextual systems?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9ddce8cb-8daa-4234-8072-ce0799d65fe2
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualist Coherentism](contextualist-coherentism-synthesis-156207e7-3c9d-49a2-8bb7-7097c1d027cd.md)

## Content

**Imagine two scientific paradigms that offer different coherent systems for understanding the same phenomena, like geocentrism and heliocentrism in astronomy.**, **These systems align with their respective contextual information and justify conflicting beliefs.**, **If justification standards vary based on context, it becomes difficult to determine which system is more justified without a method for cross-contextual evaluation.**
