# Is it always clear when luck plays a role in forming a belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f021f143-34fb-470a-b84e-cf3829237cf0
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [No-Luck View](no-luck-view-synthesis-bccfbb90-e542-414d-b962-c3799a2855c9.md)

## Content

**There are cases where it is indeterminate whether luck is involved in the formation of a belief.**, **Consider a person obtaining information from a source that is typically reliable but occasionally disseminates misinformation unknowingly. It is unclear if luck is at play if the information turns out to be true.**, **Ambiguities in our assessment of luck-involvement demonstrate that the strict exclusion of luck may be impractical in evaluating knowledge claims.**
