# How can reliabilism handle environmental influences?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9222fd44-baf9-4bb9-8cb8-90b3c4584928
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism](reliabilism-synthesis-b7a77c41-f711-4239-97c6-124b43e5d847.md)

## Content

**Imagine a case where environmental factors, like being in a deceit-prone environment, affect a person's belief-formation processes. Intuitively, such beliefs seem less secure or trustworthy as knowledge.**, **Environmental influences may lead a process that generally produces true beliefs to create false ones; thus, the reliability of the process is insufficiently insulated from such influences.**
