## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **Knowledge requires the belief to be safe from error in similar situations**, **A belief is safe if, in nearby possible situations where the person believes the proposition, it is not false**
