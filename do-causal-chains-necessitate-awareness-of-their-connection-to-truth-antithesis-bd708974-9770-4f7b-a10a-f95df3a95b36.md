# Do causal chains necessitate awareness of their connection to truth?

**Terminal:** No

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that knowing a causal connection exists enhances the status of a belief as knowledge.**, **Consider a botanist who correctly identifies a plant species intuitively but can't explain the basis. Intuitively, we may doubt the identification as knowledge given the unrecognized causal chain.**
