## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have justification and the proposition is the best explanation of the evidence**, **The best explanation of the evidence usually leads to the truth**
