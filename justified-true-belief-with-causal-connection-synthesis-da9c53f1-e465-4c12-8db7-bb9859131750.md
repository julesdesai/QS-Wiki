## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is an appropriate causal connection between the fact that the proposition is true and the person’s belief in the proposition**
