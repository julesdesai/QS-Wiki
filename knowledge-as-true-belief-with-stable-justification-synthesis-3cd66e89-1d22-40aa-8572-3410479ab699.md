## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, the proposition must be close enough to the truth to be considered true for practical purposes**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief in the proposition is justified through stable and unchanging conditions**
