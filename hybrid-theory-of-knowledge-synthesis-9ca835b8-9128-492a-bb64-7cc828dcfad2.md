# Hybrid Theory of Knowledge

**Parent:** [Does the causal theory accommodate self-knowledge?](does-the-causal-theory-accommodate-self-knowledge-antithesis-60f0e9a8-e99a-427f-ab10-dfa0f150b9ca.md)

## Content

**A person knows a proposition if their belief is caused in the right way by the fact it is true or if it arises from direct introspection**, **The causal relationship must reliably connect the belief with the truth of the proposition**, **The proposition being true is a necessary condition for it to be known**, **Direct introspection is sufficient for self-knowledge**, **A belief’s usefulness or practicality is not required for it to qualify as knowledge**
