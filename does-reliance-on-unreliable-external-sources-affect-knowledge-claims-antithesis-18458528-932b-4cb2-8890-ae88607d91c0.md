# Does reliance on unreliable external sources affect knowledge claims?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 18458528-932b-4cb2-8890-ae88607d91c0
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Process Reliabilism](process-reliabilism-synthesis-963ad99c-e2e5-4a20-9800-229a3470b5c5.md)

## Content

**Suppose an individual forms a true belief based on information from usually unreliable external sources, and this information coincidentally turns out to be true. Intuitively, a belief formed in such a way cannot be considered knowledge.**
