# Does reliance solely on truth and belief ignore the complex reliability conditions?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 83dbdd1e-d36a-4c0c-a023-d682918e2de3
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalism with Reliable Beliefs](externalism-with-reliable-beliefs-synthesis-44f393ff-3a5c-4b93-8788-7ab032b1badd.md)

## Content

**Assume a media analyst believes a government policy will succeed based on past evidence that is accurate.**, **Following the belief that the policy is successful, the success arises due to unforeseen and unrelated market changes rather than the original evidence.**, **Even with a true and arrived belief, intuitively the analyst lacks full knowledge as initial cognitive reliability did not bear the causal weight for truth.**
