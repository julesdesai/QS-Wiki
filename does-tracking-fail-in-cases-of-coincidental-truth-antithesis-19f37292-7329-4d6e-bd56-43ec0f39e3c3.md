# Does tracking fail in cases of coincidental truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 19f37292-7329-4d6e-bd56-43ec0f39e3c3
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Nozick's Tracking Theory](nozicks-tracking-theory-synthesis-56c7bb47-47e8-422c-831e-e887399874aa.md)

## Content

**Suppose someone believes a proposition due to false but convincing evidence and the proposition happens to be true for unrelated reasons. Intuitively, we would not consider this knowledge.**, **In a nearby possible world where the proposition were false, the person would still believe it because of the convincing evidence, failing the tracking condition.**
