## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief meets the epistemic standards set by the context**, **Epistemic standards vary depending on the context of the knowledge claim**
