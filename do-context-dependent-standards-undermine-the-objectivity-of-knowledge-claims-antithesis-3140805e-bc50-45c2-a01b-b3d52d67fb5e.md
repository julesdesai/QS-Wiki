# Do context-dependent standards undermine the objectivity of knowledge claims?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3140805e-bc50-45c2-a01b-b3d52d67fb5e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism](contextualism-synthesis-605b5e27-4923-4bc2-b3cf-30db710f9ba8.md)

## Content

**Knowledge is true belief that aligns with contextually variable standards of justification.**, **Objectivity requires that the justification of knowledge claims does not vary with personal or situational contexts.**, **If justification depends on context, then objectivity in knowledge claims may be compromised.**
