# What happens when action-guiding beliefs are misguided?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** bb29eca0-b648-425e-a9e2-210cf2d2a419
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Coherence Theory](pragmatic-coherence-theory-synthesis-0a69366a-ab49-4b15-a3e1-b772859c47c7.md)

## Content

**Suppose action-guiding beliefs are based on incorrect assumptions or false information.**, **A belief being pragmatically justified due to coherence with misplaced action-guiding beliefs shows a flaw in the coherence criterion.**, **Pragmatic coherence should consider the correctness of action-guiding beliefs to truly justify knowledge.**
