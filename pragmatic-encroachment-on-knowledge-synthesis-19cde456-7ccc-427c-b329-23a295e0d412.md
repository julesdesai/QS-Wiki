## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the stakes in the situation do not undermine the reliability of the cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the stakes in the situation do not undermine the reliability of the cognitive process**
