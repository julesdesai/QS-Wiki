**Parent:** [Can a true belief that is safely from error still lack knowledge?](can-a-true-belief-that-is-safely-from-error-still-lack-knowledge-antithesis-449e041b-6962-4ae1-ad6a-ae5705e17049.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**
