# Is seeming justification too subjective to reliably indicate knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d8245a76-1f8d-400d-9fd3-f9abe07eff0c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Phenomenal Conservatism: Seeming Justification](phenomenal-conservatism-seeming-justification-synthesis-de77a591-8397-48c7-98a8-42a761bdf13a.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where cultural background causes a proposition to seem true to an individual, but the proposition is false. Intuitively, such subjective influences undermine the reliability of seeming justification as a standard for knowledge.**
