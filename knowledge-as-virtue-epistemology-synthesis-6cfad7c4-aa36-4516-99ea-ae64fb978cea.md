# Knowledge as Virtue Epistemology

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does misleading evidence negate knowledge despite a true belief?](does-misleading-evidence-negate-knowledge-despite-a-true-belief-antithesis-dd46d542-b65d-45a8-a873-4bd16504e457.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, and the belief arises from intellectual virtue**
