# Is infinite regress a viable framework for justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 75680743-7940-4f49-8494-15534724c20e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundationalism with Infinite Background](foundationalism-with-infinite-background-synthesis-b6351c4d-7dab-447a-b3c7-52e8418919cd.md)

## Content

**Suppose that each belief needs justification from another belief, forming an infinite regress.**, **An infinite regress of justification does not provide a secure foundation for any belief.**, **Without a secure foundation for beliefs, the justification required for knowledge is undermined.**
