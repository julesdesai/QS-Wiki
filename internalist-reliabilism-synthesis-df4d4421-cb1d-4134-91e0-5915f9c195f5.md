# Internalist Reliabilism

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Can a reliable but unknown process still yield knowledge?](can-a-reliable-but-unknown-process-still-yield-knowledge-antithesis-795638fc-7522-4fff-b9e3-fd841cbba4df.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person has internal access to the reliability of the process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the person has internal access to the reliability of the process**
