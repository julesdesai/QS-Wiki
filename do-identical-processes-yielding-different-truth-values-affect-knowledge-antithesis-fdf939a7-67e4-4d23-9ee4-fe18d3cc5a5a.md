# Do identical processes yielding different truth values affect knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** fdf939a7-67e4-4d23-9ee4-fe18d3cc5a5a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Process Reliabilism](process-reliabilism-synthesis-963ad99c-e2e5-4a20-9800-229a3470b5c5.md)

## Content

**Suppose two individuals use identical cognitive processes to form beliefs, but one results in a true belief and the other in a false belief. It is questionable whether the reliability of the process truly guarantees knowledge in such inconsistent outcomes.**
