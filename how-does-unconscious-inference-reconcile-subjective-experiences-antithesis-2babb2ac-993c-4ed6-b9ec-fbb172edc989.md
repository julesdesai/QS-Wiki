## Content

**Unconscious inference does not account for the subjectivity of personal experience, where two individuals may perceive the same reality differently through similar unconscious processes, challenging universality.**, **Given divergent subjective experiences, the reliability and consistency of knowledge through unconscious inference become doubtful, undermining consistent knowledge application.**
