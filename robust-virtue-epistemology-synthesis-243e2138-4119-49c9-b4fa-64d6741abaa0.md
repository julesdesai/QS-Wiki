**Parent:** [Can beliefs formed by luck reflect intellectual virtue?](can-beliefs-formed-by-luck-reflect-intellectual-virtue-antithesis-e033315a-e018-4586-bd7d-c828ed9ba62e.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed through the exercise of a robust intellectual virtue that ensures the avoidance of luck**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the belief is formed through the exercise of a robust intellectual virtue, and the proposition is true**
