## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **The justification for a belief involves the exercise of intellectual virtues**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification arises from intellectual virtues**
