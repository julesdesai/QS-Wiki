# Virtue Epistemology with Coherence Requirement

**Parent:** [Is the sufficiency condition too strong if intellectual virtue leads to contradictory beliefs?](is-the-sufficiency-condition-too-strong-if-intellectual-virtue-leads-to-contradictory-beliefs-antithesis-04e2567e-13ef-4a3d-9e30-1f1b4f58c4dc.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed through intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres with the person's other beliefs formed through intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is formed through intellectual virtue, and the belief coheres with the person's other beliefs formed through intellectual virtue**
