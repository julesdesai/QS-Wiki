## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief-forming process is dependably truth-conducive across a wide range of cases**, **A process is dependably truth-conducive if it generally leads to true beliefs in diverse conditions**
