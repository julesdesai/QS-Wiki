# Can moderate standards justify all knowledge claims?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 012da9b8-b1b0-4a63-bea0-e1bdc7547622
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Moderate Contextualism Summary](moderate-contextualism-summary-synthesis-550a9ee8-c8d3-41b5-bd1e-c079e9d505d4.md)

## Content

**Suppose a situation in which a person has a justified belief based on unreliable evidence. Intuitively, this challenges whether moderate standards of justification suffice for knowledge, as the belief may not be sufficiently supported.**, **It is implausible that all justified true beliefs meet the moderate standards for knowing a proposition due to the variability in the quality of evidence.**
