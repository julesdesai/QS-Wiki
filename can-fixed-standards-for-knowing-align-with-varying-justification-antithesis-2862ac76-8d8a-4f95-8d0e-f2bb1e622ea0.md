# Can fixed standards for knowing align with varying justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2862ac76-8d8a-4f95-8d0e-f2bb1e622ea0
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

## Content

**Consider a situation where two people in different contexts believe the same true proposition with different levels of justification; intuitively, we would judge there is a discrepancy in knowledge status.**, **A plausible principle suggests that the standards for knowing should align with the standards for justification.**, **The idea of fixed standards for knowing yet variable justification creates potential inconsistencies in determining knowledge.**
