# Can we distinguish appearance from reality in perception?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c9cb905a-26a4-4a8c-a5d1-08559c0b90b8
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Disjunctivism](disjunctivism-synthesis-15b36dc9-362d-489d-8156-bddc0dd6b522.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person sees a stick in water that appears bent, but the stick is straight. Intuitively, one cannot easily distinguish whether the perception presents reality or a mere appearance.**, **If perception cannot reliably distinguish between reality and mere appearance, then the claim that perception either presents direct reality or a mere appearance becomes problematic.**
