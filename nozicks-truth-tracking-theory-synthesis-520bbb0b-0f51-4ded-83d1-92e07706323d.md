# Nozick's Truth-Tracking Theory

**Parent:** [Is knowledge available even with causal interruptions?](is-knowledge-available-even-with-causal-interruptions-antithesis-9078d702-4c2a-4850-ad92-7e7f1be12c8d.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief tracks the truth across possible worlds**, **A belief tracks the truth if, in nearby possible worlds where the proposition is true, the person believes it, and if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**
