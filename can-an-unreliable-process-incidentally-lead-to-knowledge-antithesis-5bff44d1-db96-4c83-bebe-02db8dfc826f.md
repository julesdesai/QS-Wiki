# Can an unreliable process incidentally lead to knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5bff44d1-db96-4c83-bebe-02db8dfc826f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Process Reliabilism](process-reliabilism-synthesis-963ad99c-e2e5-4a20-9800-229a3470b5c5.md)

## Content

**Imagine an unreliable cognitive process that by chance produces a true belief about a proposition. It seems improbable to consider this as knowledge even if the belief happens to be true.**
