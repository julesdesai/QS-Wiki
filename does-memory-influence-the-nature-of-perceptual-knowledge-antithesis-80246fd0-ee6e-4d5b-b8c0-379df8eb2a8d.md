# Does memory influence the nature of perceptual knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 80246fd0-ee6e-4d5b-b8c0-379df8eb2a8d
**Depth:** 2
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Phenomenalism](phenomenalism-thesis-2daa98ba-8f8c-4200-a8b1-c2a336a65d2f.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person recalls an event inaccurately due to faulty memory influenced by past experiences. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that their knowledge of the event is flawed even if based on a perceptual experience.**, **There is a plausible principle that knowledge relying solely on perceptual experiences cannot account for distortions caused by memory limitations.**
