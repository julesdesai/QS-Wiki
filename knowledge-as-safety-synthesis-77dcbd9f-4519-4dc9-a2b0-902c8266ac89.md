# Knowledge as Safety

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 77dcbd9f-4519-4dc9-a2b0-902c8266ac89
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does sensitivity fail to account for expert intuition?](does-sensitivity-fail-to-account-for-expert-intuition-antithesis-bd1a8051-8139-4185-b11e-565f74ead0f6.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in nearly all nearby possible worlds where the person believes the proposition, the proposition is true**, **The belief in the proposition should be safe in the sense that it would not easily be false if believed**
