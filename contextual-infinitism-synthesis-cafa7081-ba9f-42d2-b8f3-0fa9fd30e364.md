## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that justification involves an infinite chain of reasons contextualized to specific situations**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the infinite chain of reasons is contextually relevant to the belief**
