## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their cognitive faculties function appropriately**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have a reliable method for achieving truth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their cognitive faculties function appropriately, they have a reliable method for the proposition, and the proposition is true**
