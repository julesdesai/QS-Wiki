# Does externalism undermine the value of knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d5182241-3ae1-435b-a078-183701ceec82
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalism](externalism-synthesis-5b44df35-6663-4278-91dc-65b3fcbf870c.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that knowledge, as valuable as it is, should be equated with merely true beliefs formed by reliable processes, discounting any internal insight or understanding.**, **Merely reliable cognitive processes disconnect knowledge from the active intellectual virtues traditionally associated with it.**
