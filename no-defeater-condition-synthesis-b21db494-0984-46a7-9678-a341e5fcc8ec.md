# No Defeater Condition

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can a belief remain despite misleading evidence and still count as knowledge?](can-a-belief-remain-despite-misleading-evidence-and-still-count-as-knowledge-antithesis-71b35ecd-7a03-4b1c-99e9-99c2243cde80.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there is no evidence which would count as a defeater to their belief**
