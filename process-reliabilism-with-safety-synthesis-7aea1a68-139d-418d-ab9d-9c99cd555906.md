## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **A reliable cognitive process has a high probability of producing true beliefs and avoids producing reliably false beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**
