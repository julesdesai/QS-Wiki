**Parent:** [Is it problematic for contextualism if it leads to relativistic knowledge truths?](is-it-problematic-for-contextualism-if-it-leads-to-relativistic-knowledge-truths-antithesis-63012785-213e-4a22-8426-af4c4f7f6eef.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the level of justification required is constant across all contexts**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is justified to a fixed standard**
