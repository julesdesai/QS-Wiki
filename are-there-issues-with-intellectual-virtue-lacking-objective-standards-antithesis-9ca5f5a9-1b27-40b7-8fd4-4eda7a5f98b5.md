# Are there issues with intellectual virtue lacking objective standards?

## Content

**Suppose intellectual virtue is defined subjectively or culturally, potentially leading to conflicting knowledge claims. Intuitively, this lack of objective standards challenges the consistency of knowledge.**, **A plausible principle is that knowledge requires objective criteria for evaluation, which might be inconsistent with subjective conceptions of virtue.**
