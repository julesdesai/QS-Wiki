## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that belief arises from integrated intellectual competences**, **For a proposition to count as knowledge, integrated competences should be synergistic with each other**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that integrated competences lead to belief and truth**
