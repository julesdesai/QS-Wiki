## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that standards for knowledge are relative to the individual's own epistemic framework rather than external context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their personal standards for knowledge are met**
