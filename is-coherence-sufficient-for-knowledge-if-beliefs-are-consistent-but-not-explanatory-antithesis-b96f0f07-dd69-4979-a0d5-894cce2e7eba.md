# Is coherence sufficient for knowledge if beliefs are consistent but not explanatory?

## Content

**Suppose a person has a set of beliefs that are logically coherent but fail to explain the underlying phenomena they pertain to.**, **Intuitively, coherence without explanatory power seems inadequate for claiming knowledge, as knowledge implies some understanding of why things are the way they are.**
