# Does counterfactual reasoning affect knowledge claims?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1574dbf9-6b38-4ce9-8138-66b720abe4d8
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Knowledge as Justified True Belief](knowledge-as-justified-true-belief-synthesis-62a63598-400f-4dfa-a0fa-a27f5f076869.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person, Frank, believes a proposition based on evidence that would be misleading in most possible situations. Frank's belief turns out true only because of this specific, rare situation. Intuitively, his belief lacks knowledge even if it is true.**, **Intuitively, knowledge involves beliefs that would remain true in a range of possible scenarios, unlike justified true belief which may falter under such scrutiny.**
