## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in most nearby possible worlds where they believe the proposition, the belief remains true**, **The conditional safety accounts for variations among possible worlds to determine knowledge without strict dependence on closeness**
