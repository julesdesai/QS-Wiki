## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in nearby possible worlds, the belief is not false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the belief is true, and in nearby possible worlds, the belief is not false**
