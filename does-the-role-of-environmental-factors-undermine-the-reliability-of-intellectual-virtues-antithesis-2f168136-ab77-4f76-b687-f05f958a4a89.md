# Does the role of environmental factors undermine the reliability of intellectual virtues?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2f168136-ab77-4f76-b687-f05f958a4a89
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-72984f03-1ce3-46fd-9fd6-13203e340ace.md)

## Content

**In environments where misinformation is prevalent, even those with intellectual virtues may frequently form false beliefs. Intuitively, the reliability of these virtues as knowledge producers is questionable in such contexts.**, **The presence of misleading external factors can impede the ability of intellectual virtues to reliably produce true beliefs.**
