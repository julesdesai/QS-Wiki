# Nozick's Tracking Theory of Knowledge

**Parent:** [Do all true beliefs produced by reliable processes constitute knowledge?](do-all-true-beliefs-produced-by-reliable-processes-constitute-knowledge-antithesis-30a6f3a9-4101-401b-8a27-13b6f09307a4.md)

## Content

**A belief qualifies as knowledge if it tracks the truth across relevant situations**, **A belief tracks the truth if, in the closest possible worlds where the belief is true, the person believes it**, **A belief tracks the truth if, in the closest possible worlds where the belief is false, the person does not believe it**, **Knowledge requires a belief to be both true and sensitive to the truth**
