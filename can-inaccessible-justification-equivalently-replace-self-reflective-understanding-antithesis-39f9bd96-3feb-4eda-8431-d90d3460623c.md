# Can inaccessible justification equivalently replace self-reflective understanding?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 39f9bd96-3feb-4eda-8431-d90d3460623c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalist Justification with Causal Connection](externalist-justification-with-causal-connection-synthesis-0c02280c-1b97-4965-a35c-599f5b7ce400.md)

## Content

**Imagine a scenario where someone's moral belief is true and causally connected but arises from an inexplicable flash of insight. Intuitively, it feels counterintuitive to classify this as knowledge without self-reflective understanding.**, **It is implausible to equate inaccessible justification with the reflective understanding needed for genuine knowledge.**
