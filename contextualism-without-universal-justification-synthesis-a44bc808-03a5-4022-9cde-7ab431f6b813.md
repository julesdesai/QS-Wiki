## Content

**Knowledge depends on the context of the knower**, **A person knows a proposition if they believe the proposition and meet the contextual standards of justification**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **The role of justification is determined contextually, thus avoiding claims of its universality**
