## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their method of forming the belief would yield a true belief if the proposition were false**, **The method must be such that it produces true beliefs in the actual world and various close possible worlds**
