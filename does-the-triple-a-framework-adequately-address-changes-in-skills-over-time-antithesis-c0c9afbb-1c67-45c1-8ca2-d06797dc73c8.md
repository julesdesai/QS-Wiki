# Does the Triple-A framework adequately address changes in skills over time?

## Content

**Consider an expert who loses their proficiency over time but forms a belief relying on outdated skills leading to accidentally accurate results. The outdated adroitness produces a true belief by chance.**, **In such a scenario, the temporal skill mismatch makes it difficult to ascertain if the belief fulfills aptness as an adroit belief.**
