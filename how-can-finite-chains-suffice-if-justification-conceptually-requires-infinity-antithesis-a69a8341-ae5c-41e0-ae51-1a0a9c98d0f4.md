# How can finite chains suffice if justification conceptually requires infinity?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a69a8341-ae5c-41e0-ae51-1a0a9c98d0f4
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Infinitism](pragmatic-infinitism-synthesis-f73c0505-1eaa-4768-8143-11a2f8c9ca92.md)

## Content

**The finite chains we supposedly use in practice appear insufficient if justification conceptually demands an infinite chain.**, **A finite chain cannot logically satisfy the requirement for an infinite chain.**, **The concept of a finite chain of reasons fulfilling an infinite requirement is logically contradictory.**, **Accepting finite chains undermines the conceptual demand for infinite justification chains.**
