# Knowledge as Acceptance

**Depth:** 3

**Parent:** [Does the necessity of belief exclude certain types of subconscious knowledge?](does-the-necessity-of-belief-exclude-certain-types-of-subconscious-knowledge-antithesis-90c7489b-1152-465f-92db-943d72f21021.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they accept the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the acceptance has practical consequences or usefulness**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they accept the proposition, the proposition is true, and the acceptance has practical consequences or usefulness**
