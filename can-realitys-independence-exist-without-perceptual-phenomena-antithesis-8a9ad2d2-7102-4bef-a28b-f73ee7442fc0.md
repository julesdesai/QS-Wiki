# Can reality's independence exist without perceptual phenomena?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 8a9ad2d2-7102-4bef-a28b-f73ee7442fc0
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Critical Realism](critical-realism-synthesis-1578ad3b-b599-423e-ae15-73d8fd806714.md)

## Content

**The assertion of reality's independent existence posits reality as detachable from perception**, **If perception indirectly accesses this independent reality, questions arise about whether unperceived entities concretely exist**, **This leads to a metaphysical query about the status of unperceived objects and their relation to known reality**
