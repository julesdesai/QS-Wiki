# Can group dynamics affect the reliability of individual cognitive virtues?

**Node ID:** 871b8d0b-20ae-4d98-8288-5f95755cca09
**Depth:** 4

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology With Virtuous Belief Formation](virtue-epistemology-with-virtuous-belief-formation-synthesis-53b228ae-7464-400d-be01-037da588b5a0.md)

## Content

**Consider an example where an individual’s reliable truth-seeking method is distorted when operating within a highly biased group setting, leading them to hold false beliefs.**, **The group pressure or consensus causes the individual’s normally reliable methods to malfunction in this context.**, **Intuitively, these beliefs don’t qualify as knowledge as the intellectual virtues are compromised by the group dynamics.**
