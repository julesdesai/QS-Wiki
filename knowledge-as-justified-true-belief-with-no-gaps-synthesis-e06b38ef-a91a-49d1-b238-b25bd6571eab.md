## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is not based on any false lemmas**, **For a belief to count as knowledge, all essential information and context must be considered**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, their belief is not based on any false lemmas, and all essential context is included**
