# Can subjective and objective aspects of reality be truly distinguished?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5c741255-e9b3-42d7-a07d-094f3bbe6bca
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Dual Aspect Theory](dual-aspect-theory-synthesis-e0a4859a-34ba-4bc9-9368-89fce3ac2e21.md)

## Content

**Objective aspects are aspects of reality that exist independently of any observer's perception or experience.**, **Subjective aspects are aspects of reality that depend on an observer's perception or consciousness.**, **It is implausible that any aspect of reality can be completely independent of all observers, as our access to reality is always mediated through perception and cognition.**
