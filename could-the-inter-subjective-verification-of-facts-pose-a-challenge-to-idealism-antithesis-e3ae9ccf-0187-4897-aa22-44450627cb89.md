# Could the inter-subjective verification of facts pose a challenge to idealism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e3ae9ccf-0187-4897-aa22-44450627cb89
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Idealism](idealism-synthesis-6a8ea4b4-8cf6-44ff-98fb-cf08805f781d.md)

## Content

**Scientific experiments and observations are repeatable and verifiable by multiple independent observers. Intuitively, this inter-subjective verification supports the idea of an objective reality beyond individual mental experiences.**, **The ability of multiple observers to independently verify the same facts challenges the notion that knowledge is solely constituted by mental stimuli and constructs.**
