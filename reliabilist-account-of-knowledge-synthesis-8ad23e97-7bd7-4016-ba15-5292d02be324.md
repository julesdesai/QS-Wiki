## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief was formed through a reliable cognitive process**, **A reliable cognitive process is one that produces true beliefs in a high percentage of cases**
