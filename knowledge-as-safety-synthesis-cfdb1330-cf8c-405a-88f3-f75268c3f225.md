## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, it would be very unlikely for the person to believe it**, **The belief in the proposition should remain safe from error in nearby possible worlds**
