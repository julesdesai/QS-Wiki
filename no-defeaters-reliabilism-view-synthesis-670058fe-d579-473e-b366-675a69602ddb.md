## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **Knowledge requires that the belief is the result of a reliable cognitive process**, **A reliable cognitive process, rather than the total absence of defeaters, secures the justification status of belief**
