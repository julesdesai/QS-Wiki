# Knowledge as Bounded Foundational Coherence

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 24d1edce-f080-4d8c-b4bb-9642634154b0
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can beliefs actually cohere infinitely?](can-beliefs-actually-cohere-infinitely-antithesis-1ff18354-c421-4276-8b08-406021142a60.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is part of a finite coherent system**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the coherent system contains foundational beliefs that do not require further justification**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is part of a finite coherent system with foundational beliefs**
