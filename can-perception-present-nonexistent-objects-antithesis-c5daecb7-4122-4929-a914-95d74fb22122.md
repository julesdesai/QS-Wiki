# Can perception present nonexistent objects?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c5daecb7-4122-4929-a914-95d74fb22122
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Intentionalism about Perception](intentionalism-about-perception-synthesis-61a99e6e-ccdd-4f9c-a128-c382d15947b5.md)

## Content

**Perception can involve hallucinations where no real objects are present.**, **In hallucinations, individuals can still seem to perceive objects and their properties.**, **If perception directly presents objects, then hallucinations should not occur without real objects.**
