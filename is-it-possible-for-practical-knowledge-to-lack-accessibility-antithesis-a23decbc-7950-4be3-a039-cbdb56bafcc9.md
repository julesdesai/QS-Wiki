# Is it possible for practical knowledge to lack accessibility?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a23decbc-7950-4be3-a039-cbdb56bafcc9
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Hybrid Internalism](hybrid-internalism-synthesis-3d9ce0d9-6ba2-4665-a054-ed06708d6ee0.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that practical knowledge requires actionable understanding, which implies knowing not just that something is true, but how to use that truth. This principle together with the idea that justification must always be accessible implies a potential contradiction when a source is reliable but the user cannot access why it is reliable.**
