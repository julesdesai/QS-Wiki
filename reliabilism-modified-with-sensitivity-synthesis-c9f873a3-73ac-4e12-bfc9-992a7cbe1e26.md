# Reliabilism Modified with sensitivity

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Are defeaters always identifiable?](are-defeaters-always-identifiable-antithesis-7f7f14be-9e5e-465b-9e65-a33449998a7f.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **The justification for a belief is reliable if it would not lead to false beliefs in similar circumstances**, **Knowledge requires sensitivity to falsehood, such that the belief would not be held if the proposition were false**
