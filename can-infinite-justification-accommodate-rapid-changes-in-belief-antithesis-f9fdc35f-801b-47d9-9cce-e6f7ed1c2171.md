# Can infinite justification accommodate rapid changes in belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f9fdc35f-801b-47d9-9cce-e6f7ed1c2171
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Dynamic Infinitism Summary](dynamic-infinitism-summary-synthesis-65698d6e-d9bc-439d-bd28-3a216caa0bfd.md)

## Content

**In real-life situations where beliefs need quick updating, infinite justification provides no clear temporal stopping point, so quick belief shifts might not fit neatly into this framework.**, **In cases such as sudden emergencies, there isn't time for linear infinite justification to catch up with immediate required belief changes.**, **This suggests a disconnect between infinite justification and practical approaches to rapidly changing knowledge requirements.**
