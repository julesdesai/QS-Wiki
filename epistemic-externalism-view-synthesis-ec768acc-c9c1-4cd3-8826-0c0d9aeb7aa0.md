## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is not necessary for them to have internal access to justification**, **What matters is that the belief is formed by a reliable external process**
