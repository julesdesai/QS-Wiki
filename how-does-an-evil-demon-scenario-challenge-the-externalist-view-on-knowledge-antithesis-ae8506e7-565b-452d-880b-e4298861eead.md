# How does an evil demon scenario challenge the externalist view on knowledge?

## Content

**Imagine a scenario where an evil demon manipulates experiences so that they are systematically misleading, yet still results in reliable beliefs. Intuitively, we might judge that such beliefs do not constitute knowledge.**, **In a demon scenario, the reliability of a process could be an illusion, thereby challenging the externalist condition for knowledge.**
