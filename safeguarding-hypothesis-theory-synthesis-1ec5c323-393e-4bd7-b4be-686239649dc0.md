## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person maintains true beliefs across a range of situations and variations**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person's belief is safe from error in nearby possible scenarios**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that these safeguarding conditions hold**
