## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **There is a minimum threshold of standards for knowing a proposition across all contexts**, **Justification must meet this threshold while allowing room for contextual variability**
