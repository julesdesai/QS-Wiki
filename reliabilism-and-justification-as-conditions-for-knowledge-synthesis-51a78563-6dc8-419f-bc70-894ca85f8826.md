# Reliabilism and Justification as Conditions for Knowledge

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief in the proposition be produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, justification of the belief offers an additional source of epistemic support that is not negated by reliability**
