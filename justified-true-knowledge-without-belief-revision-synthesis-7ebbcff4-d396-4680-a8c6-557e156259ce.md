## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is permissible for them to lack belief in the proposition**, **A person knows a proposition if it is true, they have justification for it, and the proposition is optionally believed or not**
