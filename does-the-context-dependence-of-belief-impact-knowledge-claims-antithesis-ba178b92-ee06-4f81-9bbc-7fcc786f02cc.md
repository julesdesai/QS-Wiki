# Does the context-dependence of belief impact knowledge claims?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ba178b92-ee06-4f81-9bbc-7fcc786f02cc
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Truth-Tracking Theory](truth-tracking-theory-synthesis-1814de97-222c-437f-bd26-a9ce4edff4f7.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which the truth of a proposition varies significantly across different contexts, and a person's belief changes according to these variations. Intuitively, we may find knowledge claims suspect if the belief is too context-dependent.**, **It is plausible to argue that the context-dependence of belief potentially weakens the stability required for sustaining knowledge across different scenarios.**
