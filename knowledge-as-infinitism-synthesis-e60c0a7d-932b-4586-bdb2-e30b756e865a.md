# Knowledge as Infinitism

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** e60c0a7d-932b-4586-bdb2-e30b756e865a
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does sufficient evidence always yield true knowledge?](does-sufficient-evidence-always-yield-true-knowledge-antithesis-1c42cbe3-4108-43dc-a20a-42492f3ec6b9.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is an infinite non-repeating chain of reasons backing the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is true and supported by an infinite non-repeating chain of reasons**
