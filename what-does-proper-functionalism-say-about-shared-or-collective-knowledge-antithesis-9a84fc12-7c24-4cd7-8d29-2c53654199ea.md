# What does proper functionalism say about shared or collective knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9a84fc12-7c24-4cd7-8d29-2c53654199ea
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Proper Functionalism](proper-functionalism-synthesis-56ac2890-cc65-4132-90ba-c28a7f97b59c.md)

## Content

**Knowledge can be formed by a group collaboration even if individual cognitive faculties are not solely reliable.**, **Group knowledge implies that shared beliefs mediated by group dynamics challenge the notion of individual cognitive faculties functioning independently.**, **Proper functionalism might struggle to explain how collective knowledge is acquired without individual proper function.**
