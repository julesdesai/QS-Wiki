# No-Luck View

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** bccfbb90-e542-414d-b962-c3799a2855c9
**Depth:** 3
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can someone know something true by coincidence?](can-someone-know-something-true-by-coincidence-antithesis-87e30f73-82dc-4718-aff3-08a34e3d09c5.md)

## Content

**Knowledge cannot result from epistemic luck**, **Epistemic luck occurs when a belief is true because of factors unrelated to the reasons for the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, no part of their belief can be true due to luck**, **For a person to know a proposition, the means of arriving at the belief must be reliable**
