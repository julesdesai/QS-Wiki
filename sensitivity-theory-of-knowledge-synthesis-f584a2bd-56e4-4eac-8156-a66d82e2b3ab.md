## Content

**A person knows a proposition if their belief would not be held if the proposition were false**, **A belief is sensitive if, in the closest possible world where the proposition is false, the person does not believe it**, **Knowledge requires that the belief be true**, **Knowledge requires the ability for belief revision in the face of contrary evidence**
