# Coherentist Theory of Knowledge

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is a causal connection always necessary for knowledge?](is-a-causal-connection-always-necessary-for-knowledge-antithesis-a201eca0-02ba-4a5d-b1fc-bd6fa401625a.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their beliefs cohere with a larger system of beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their beliefs cohere with a larger system of beliefs**
