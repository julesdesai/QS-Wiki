# Is knowledge without awareness compatible with proper functionalism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9645324b-e3d3-4ab5-bd30-a202719a82e3
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Proper Functionalism](proper-functionalism-synthesis-56ac2890-cc65-4132-90ba-c28a7f97b59c.md)

## Content

**A person might acquire a true belief through functioning cognitive faculties but remain entirely unaware of the belief.**, **If awareness of a belief is not required for knowledge, this raises questions about proper functionalism's conditions for knowledge without conscious acceptance of the belief.**, **Proper functionalism may overlook nuances where awareness and acceptance of a true belief diverge.**
