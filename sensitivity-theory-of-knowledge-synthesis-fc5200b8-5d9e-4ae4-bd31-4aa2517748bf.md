## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that were the proposition not true, the person would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is true, believed, and is sensitive to the truth in the outlined way**
