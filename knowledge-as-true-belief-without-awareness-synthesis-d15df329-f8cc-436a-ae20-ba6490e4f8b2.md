## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is a result of reliable cognitive processes**, **Conscious awareness of the role of these cognitive processes is not necessary for acquiring knowledge**
