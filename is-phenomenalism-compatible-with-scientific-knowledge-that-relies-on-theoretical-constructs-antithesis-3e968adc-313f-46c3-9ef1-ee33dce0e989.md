# Is phenomenalism compatible with scientific knowledge that relies on theoretical constructs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3e968adc-313f-46c3-9ef1-ee33dce0e989
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Perceptual Baseline Phenomenalism](perceptual-baseline-phenomenalism-synthesis-c5e3ac41-9c0b-44e1-9f51-d5e2e022393a.md)

## Content

**Many scientific concepts, such as atoms or black holes, are understood through theoretical models not directly perceptible.**, **In a scenario where observers accept scientific models based on empirical data without direct perception, the baseline of common perceptual experiences is bypassed.**, **It is implausible to claim objective knowledge of such scientific constructs if they do not align with common perceptual experiences.**
