# Is the variability of standards problematic for communication?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0cb317a4-5045-44cc-9b88-d46135d2cef7
**Depth:** 2
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism](contextualism-thesis-de4edc71-c75a-4fcd-afbe-4333fb44b1b5.md)

## Content

**Effective communication relies on shared understanding of terms such as "knowledge."**, **Suppose in one context a person communicates they know something, but in another context they do not. Intuitively, this variability would create confusion.**, **The variability of knowledge standards can disrupt mutual understanding in communication.**
