# Is incidental truth sufficient for causally grounded knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 68ad73b7-0119-4662-a9a6-da88e0ccb063
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Causal Theory of Knowledge](causal-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-916d423a-1723-4911-b4ab-5dfda1d6916a.md)

## Content

**Suppose Mary believes her sibling is calling because she often calls at that hour; her sibling calls by coincidence unrelated to this pattern. It is implausible that Mary knows her sibling will call since her belief is incidentally true without a substantive causal link.**
