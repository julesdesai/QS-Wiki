# Is the distinction between first-order and second-order virtues justified?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3ce2de87-4e1a-4828-aa8d-b26fdf31f297
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Meta-Virtue Epistemology](meta-virtue-epistemology-synthesis-b3bd54fd-8ed8-46f5-92c6-aa5fa4222616.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that virtues are unified and should not be divided into orders. This principle together with the absence of distinct functioning leads to the conclusion that meta-virtues may not hold a unique role in epistemology.**
