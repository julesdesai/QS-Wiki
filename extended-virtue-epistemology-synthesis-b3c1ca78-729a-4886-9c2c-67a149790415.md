## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief arises from a combination of intellectual virtue and external resources**, **An intellectual virtue is a cognitive faculty or character trait that contributes to the formation of true beliefs**, **External resources can contribute to knowledge when they are integrated into a person’s cognitive processes**
