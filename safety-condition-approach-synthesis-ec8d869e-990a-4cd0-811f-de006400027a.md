## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief could not easily have been false in close possible worlds**, **The belief is safe if it holds in similar scenarios without being false**
