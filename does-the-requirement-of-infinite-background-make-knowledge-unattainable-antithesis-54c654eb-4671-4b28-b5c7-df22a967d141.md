# Does the requirement of infinite background make knowledge unattainable?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 54c654eb-4671-4b28-b5c7-df22a967d141
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundationalism with Infinite Background](foundationalism-with-infinite-background-synthesis-b6351c4d-7dab-447a-b3c7-52e8418919cd.md)

## Content

**An infinite background supporting structure is not practically attainable by finite human agents.**, **If knowledge requires justification through an infinite supporting structure, then human beliefs cannot be truly justified.**, **If human beliefs cannot be truly justified, then humans cannot possess knowledge.**
