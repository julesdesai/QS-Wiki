# Knowledge as Non-defeated Justified True Belief

**Parent:** [Can we know a self-verifying delusion under the sensitivity theory?](can-we-know-a-self-verifying-delusion-under-the-sensitivity-theory-antithesis-c88f5e45-3962-44ea-92b9-cd6c8658a359.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that no overriding or undermining defeaters exist**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, they have justification for believing it, and no defeaters exist**
