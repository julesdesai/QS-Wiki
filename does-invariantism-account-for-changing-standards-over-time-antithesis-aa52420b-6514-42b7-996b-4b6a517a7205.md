# Does invariantism account for changing standards over time?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** aa52420b-6514-42b7-996b-4b6a517a7205
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Invariantism with Fixed Standards](invariantism-with-fixed-standards-synthesis-2877eecc-8388-4e01-b87d-ffe7f20b2619.md)

## Content

**As science progresses, what was once known may later be revised or rejected.**, **Consider historical beliefs, such as the Earth being flat, which were once regarded as knowledge.**, **If the standards for knowledge are truly invariant, once something is known, it should remain known, conflicting with the progressive refinement of knowledge across history.**
