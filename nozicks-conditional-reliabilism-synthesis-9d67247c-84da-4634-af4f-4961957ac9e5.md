## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have a true belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, their belief must track the truth across close possible worlds**, **A belief tracks the truth if in the nearest possible worlds where the proposition is false, the person would not believe it**, **A belief tracks the truth if in the nearest possible worlds where the proposition is true, the person would believe it**
