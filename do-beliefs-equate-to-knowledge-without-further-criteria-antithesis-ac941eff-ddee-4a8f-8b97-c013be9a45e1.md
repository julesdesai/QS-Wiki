# Do beliefs equate to knowledge without further criteria?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ac941eff-ddee-4a8f-8b97-c013be9a45e1
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Proper Function Theory](proper-function-theory-synthesis-ea617099-7596-484d-aca6-1b318b0033ac.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person believes a proposition merely because it is told to them by an untrustworthy source, and it happens to be true. Intuitively, this seems not to constitute knowledge.**, **There is a plausible principle that knowledge should involve more than true belief alone, such as justification or evidence.**
