# Knowledge as Safety

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the person were to believe it, the proposition would not easily be false**, **The belief in the proposition should be safe in the sense that in most nearby possible worlds where the person believes the proposition, it is true**
