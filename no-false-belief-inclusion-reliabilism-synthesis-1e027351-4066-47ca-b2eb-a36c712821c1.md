## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a belief formed by a reliable cognitive process to constitute knowledge, it is required that the belief is true in the specific instance**
