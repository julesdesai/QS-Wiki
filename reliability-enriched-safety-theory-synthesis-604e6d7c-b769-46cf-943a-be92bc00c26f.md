## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, the belief must be formed by a reliable process**, **A reliable process produces mostly true beliefs across many cases**, **A belief is safe if in nearby worlds, where the belief is formed by the same process, it remains true**, **Knowledge excludes beliefs formed through accidental discovery or luck**
