# Knowledge as Truth-Tracking

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does contextual epistemology accommodate objective knowledge?](does-contextual-epistemology-accommodate-objective-knowledge-antithesis-0c9aad31-e595-47ad-afae-2c1400f17e41.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, their belief must track the truth through relevant alternative situations**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief tracks the truth**
