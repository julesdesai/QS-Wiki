# No Luck Justification

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is knowledge infallible or open to luck?](is-knowledge-infallible-or-open-to-luck-antithesis-d659639d-f649-4eb6-a94d-ff82a6da857d.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified without reliance on luck**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is justified without reliance on luck**
