# Knowledge as Non-Accidentally True Belief

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is non-accidentally true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **Knowledge is a true belief that is not true merely by accident**
