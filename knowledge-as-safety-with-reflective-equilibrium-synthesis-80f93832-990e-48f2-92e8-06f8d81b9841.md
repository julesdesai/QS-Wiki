## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief could not have easily been false given the method of belief formation**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is achieved through reaching a reflective equilibrium between intuitive judgments and principles**
