## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief in the proposition is justified, true, and they believe it**
