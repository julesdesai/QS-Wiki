## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief results from the proper functioning of cognitive faculties**, **Cognitive faculties must be operating in a suitable environment for producing true beliefs**
