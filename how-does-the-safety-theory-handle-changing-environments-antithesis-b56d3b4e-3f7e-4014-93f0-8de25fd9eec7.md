# How does the Safety Theory handle changing environments?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b56d3b4e-3f7e-4014-93f0-8de25fd9eec7
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Safety Theory of Knowledge](safety-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-8e9332b1-e101-417c-8080-59c95e445ed8.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person comes to have a true belief based on experience in a stable environment, but the environment changes such that similar propositions would easily become false. Intuitively we might judge that the person no longer has knowledge of new propositions in the changed environment.**, **The requirement that a belief would not easily be false in similar situations implies some level of environmental consistency. Sudden changes in the environment challenge the stability requisite in the theory.**
