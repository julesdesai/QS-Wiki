# Is having a belief alone sufficient for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2963a349-f382-4973-89e5-1223e7fd1b9c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Access Internalism](access-internalism-synthesis-1ebb0a7e-8cd6-40ff-bf0f-a16161232f85.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where a person holds a belief due to a misunderstanding or false premise. Intuitively, it seems implausible to claim that this person possesses knowledge if their understanding is flawed.**, **It is implausible that holding a belief due to a misunderstanding satisfies the conditions for knowledge.**
