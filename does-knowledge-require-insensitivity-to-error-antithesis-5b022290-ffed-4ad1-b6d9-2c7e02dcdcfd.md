# Does knowledge require insensitivity to error?

**Node ID:** 5b022290-ffed-4ad1-b6d9-2c7e02dcdcfd

**Parent:** [Knowledge as Sensitivity Theory](knowledge-as-sensitivity-theory-synthesis-cfe82d73-3c24-4801-8611-fbe2443e064c.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person believes a proposition that is true but remains highly confident in their belief even when presented with counter-evidence.**, **If a person incorrectly refuses to abandon a belief even with contrary evidence, their belief lacks the flexibility expected in knowing, suggesting knowledge requires some sensitivity to potential error.**
