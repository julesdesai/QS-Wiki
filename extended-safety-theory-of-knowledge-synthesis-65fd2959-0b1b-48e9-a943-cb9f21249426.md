## Content

**A belief is safe if in most nearby possible worlds where the belief is formed in the same way, it is true**, **A person knows a proposition if they believe it and their belief is safe**, **A person knows a proposition if the proposition is true**, **Safety requires that the method of forming the belief would not easily lead to a false belief**, **A belief remains safe if any defeaters are neutralized by additional information**, **Knowledge requires that a belief withstands potential defeaters through further supporting evidence**
