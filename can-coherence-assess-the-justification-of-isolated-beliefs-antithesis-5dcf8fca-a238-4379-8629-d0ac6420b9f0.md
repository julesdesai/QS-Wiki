# Can coherence assess the justification of isolated beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5dcf8fca-a238-4379-8629-d0ac6420b9f0
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Adaptive Coherentism](adaptive-coherentism-synthesis-f17f0588-6de8-4c0d-a6e6-63e09dfee45f.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a single belief is isolated but true. Intuitively, we would judge that this belief might still be justified.**, **There is a plausible principle that some beliefs can be justified independently of their coherence with other beliefs. This principle together with the coherence requirement suggests that coherence might not capture all justified beliefs.**
