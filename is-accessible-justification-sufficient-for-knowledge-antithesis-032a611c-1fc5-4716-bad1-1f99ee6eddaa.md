# Is accessible justification sufficient for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 032a611c-1fc5-4716-bad1-1f99ee6eddaa
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualist Internalism](contextualist-internalism-synthesis-130e5ac9-b512-486c-b0ba-4156d7ff0e7d.md)

## Content

**A case in which a person is in a situation of dreaming that they are sitting on a beach provides them with justification that is true by the context of dreaming.**, **In such a case, the person would not have knowledge since dreaming scenarios are not reliable processes for knowing propositions about the external world.**
