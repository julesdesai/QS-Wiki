# Do different domains require different standards of reliability?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b3621c71-b872-453f-b2ab-598c7d41a509
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism](reliabilism-synthesis-41e7a61f-ad45-42cd-8dd7-ba40ca519ba9.md)

## Content

**Standards of reliability vary across different domains, such as science versus everyday reasoning.**, **Imagine a belief formed by a reliable process in one domain but not considered reliable in another.**, **If domain-specific standards affect judgments of reliability, uniform application of reliabilism becomes problematic.**
