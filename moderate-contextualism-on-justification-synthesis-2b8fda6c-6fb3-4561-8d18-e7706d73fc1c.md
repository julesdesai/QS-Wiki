## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief be justified to a degree that varies with context but adheres to minimum rational standards**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification meets context-specific but minimum rational standards**
