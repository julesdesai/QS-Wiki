## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is supported by foundational beliefs that are intellectually accessible**, **Intellectual accessibility requires the person to understand and articulate reasons for their foundational beliefs**
