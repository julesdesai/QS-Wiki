# Is it possible to avoid circularity in an infinite structure of coherence?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9d9aaf5a-ffad-45b7-9a4a-efe5d153db2a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitist Coherentism](infinitist-coherentism-synthesis-c961c647-ce7e-4676-a333-4ee929ef8887.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a coherent system of beliefs, each supported by the other, like parts of a circle. Intuitively, this interconnectedness seems susceptible to circular reasoning.**, **It is implausible to claim that structuring beliefs into an infinite, coherent cycle evades the problem of circular justification, as the same beliefs may repeatedly rely on each other.**
