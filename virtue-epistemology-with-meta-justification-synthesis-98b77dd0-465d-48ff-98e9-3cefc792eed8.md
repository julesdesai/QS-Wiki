## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is a justified basis for thinking the process leading to the belief is reliable**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief arises from an intellectual virtue or competence with meta-justification for its use**
