## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **Knowledge requires that the proposition could not easily have been false given the method of belief formation**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the proposition could not easily have been false**
