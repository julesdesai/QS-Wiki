## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is formed safely**, **A belief is safe if, in nearly all nearby possible worlds where the person forms the belief in the same way, the belief is true**
