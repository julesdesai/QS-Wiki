# Does naturalized epistemology adequately address skepticism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** caebdfc0-3fb8-4d6a-994e-c2722b3d834f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Naturalized Epistemology](naturalized-epistemology-synthesis-99d5c2d6-f993-4109-8adb-f973b72c0335.md)

## Content

**Consider the skeptical scenario that all our empirical observations may be systematically misleading. Intuitively, naturalized processes cannot account for knowledge in such a situation.**, **There is a plausible principle that epistemology must offer a response to radical skepticism beyond empirical justification.**, **If justification consists in processes studied by natural sciences, it lacks resources to counter radical skepticism.**
