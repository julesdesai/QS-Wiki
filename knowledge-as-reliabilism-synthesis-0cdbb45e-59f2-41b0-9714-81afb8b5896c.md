## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief was formed through a reliable process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief was produced by a reliable process ensuring its truth**
