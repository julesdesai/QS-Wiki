# Do sense-data provide a satisfactory solution to the problem of perception?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 105f0024-4c9c-4737-bd8a-ec7444722f9d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Sense-Datum Theory](sense-datum-theory-synthesis-4d28daea-3a89-4e3c-8daf-1b28135e59bc.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which someone sees a stick submerged in water that appears bent. Intuitively, either we have to say the sense-data correctly represent the bent appearance or that sense-data mislead our perception of the actual stick, neither of which satisfactorily explains our accurate understanding of the stick being straight.**
