# Does the notion of basic beliefs imply dogmatism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** de448dcf-742a-4a42-8f5b-776ea81b4682
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundationalist Coherentism](foundationalist-coherentism-synthesis-b8a8c906-b541-4af2-8e95-f0f08da1d9c7.md)

## Content

**Basic beliefs are those taken to be self-justifying and requiring no further justification.**, **To accept certain beliefs as basic without question resembles a form of dogmatic acceptance without considering the necessity for external validation.**, **Critics of foundationalism argue that this dogmatic acceptance undermines the critical scrutiny needed in establishing sound justification for beliefs.**
