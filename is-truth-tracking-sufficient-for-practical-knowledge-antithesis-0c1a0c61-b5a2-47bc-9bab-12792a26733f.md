# Is truth-tracking sufficient for practical knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0c1a0c61-b5a2-47bc-9bab-12792a26733f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Epistemic Externalism with Nozick's Tracking Theory](epistemic-externalism-with-nozicks-tracking-theory-synthesis-99a4169f-6bac-4b5a-b46b-52fe3916b903.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where an agent is unaware of the reliability of their belief-forming method, but the method does track truth.**, **Practical knowledge requires not just truth-tracking, but also actionable awareness of justification to successfully guide action.**, **Truth-tracking alone doesn't ensure the agent has sufficient grounds for action without awareness of reliability.**
