# Are subjective experiences reliable for assessing knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0f8c8d4e-5656-411b-950e-c69d8d8cae7d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatism](pragmatism-synthesis-08f9dcda-197c-4176-877f-438b2d186f9d.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which an individual has a subjective experience of being abducted by aliens. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that subjective experience alone is insufficient to assess this as knowledge.**, **It is implausible that subjective experiences, which can vary widely between individuals, contribute consistently and reliably to the practical assessment of knowledge.**
