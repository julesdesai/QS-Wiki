# Can intellectual virtues alone ensure reliability?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 38797153-c0fa-47a1-ba1f-c2d53c948194
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology Summary](virtue-epistemology-summary-synthesis-460b628b-7c65-45e3-9b14-3e9060c40e11.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that mere possession of intellectual virtues ensures a reliable belief-forming process, as virtues may operate under mistaken premises or misinformation.**, **Consider a scenario in which a researcher uses intellectual courage to pursue a controversial theory based on outdated or flawed data. Intuitively, despite their courage, the belief lacks reliability.**
