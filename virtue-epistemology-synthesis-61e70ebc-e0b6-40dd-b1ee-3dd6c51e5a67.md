## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief results from intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief is true, results from intellectual virtue, and they believe it**
