## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification involves both foundational beliefs and coherence with the entire belief system**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification follows from both foundational beliefs and a coherent belief system**
