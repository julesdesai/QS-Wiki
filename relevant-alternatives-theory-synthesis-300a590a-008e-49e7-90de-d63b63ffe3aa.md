## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they can rule out all relevant alternatives in which the proposition is false**, **An alternative is relevant if it represents a plausible scenario where the proposition could be false**
