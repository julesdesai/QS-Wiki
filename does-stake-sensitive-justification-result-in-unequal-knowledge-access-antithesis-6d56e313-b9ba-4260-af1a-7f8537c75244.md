# Does stake-sensitive justification result in unequal knowledge access?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 6d56e313-b9ba-4260-af1a-7f8537c75244
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Subject-Sensitive Invariantism Summary](subject-sensitive-invariantism-summary-synthesis-519a57d4-27a1-46ac-ac38-c130e30ad088.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that individuals with the same evidence and belief have unequal access to knowledge purely based on their stakes.**, **Suppose a high-stakes situation makes a belief less justified, while a low-stakes identical scenario does not. Intuitively, this creates an unjust discrepancy in knowledge access.**
