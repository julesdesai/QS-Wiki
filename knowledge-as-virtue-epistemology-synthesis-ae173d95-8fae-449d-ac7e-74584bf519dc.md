# Knowledge as Virtue Epistemology

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** ae173d95-8fae-449d-ac7e-74584bf519dc
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does mere pragmatic benefit suffice for knowledge?](does-mere-pragmatic-benefit-suffice-for-knowledge-antithesis-34603a96-31f4-403a-894d-91d4675c4f9c.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is the result of a reliable cognitive virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is the result of a reliable cognitive virtue**
