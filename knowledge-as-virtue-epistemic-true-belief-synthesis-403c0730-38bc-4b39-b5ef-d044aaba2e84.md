# Knowledge as Virtue-Epistemic True Belief

**Parent:** [Can a lack of false propositions really guarantee knowledge?](can-a-lack-of-false-propositions-really-guarantee-knowledge-antithesis-64ee371b-cc2f-4cd6-9702-3d4cfcdc5d17.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from intellectual virtues, such as careful reasoning and open-mindedness**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief arises from intellectual virtues**
