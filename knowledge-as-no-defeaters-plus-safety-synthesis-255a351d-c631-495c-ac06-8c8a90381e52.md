# Knowledge as No Defeaters Plus Safety

**Parent:** [Does safety adequately handle Gettier-style problems?](does-safety-adequately-handle-gettier-style-problems-antithesis-06bfc29b-5c78-4686-8acf-2530012253c7.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief has no defeating evidence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and no defeaters are present and the belief holds safely**
