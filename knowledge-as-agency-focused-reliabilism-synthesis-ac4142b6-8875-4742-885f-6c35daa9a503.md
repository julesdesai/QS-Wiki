## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process actively endorsed by the agent**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief results from a process that the agent has validated as reliable**
