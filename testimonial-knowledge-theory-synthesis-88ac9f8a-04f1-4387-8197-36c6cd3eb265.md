## Content

**A person can know a proposition through testimony if they have no reason to doubt the testifier**, **The proposition must be true for the acquisition of knowledge**, **The testifier must have a reliable cognitive process leading to the believed proposition**, **Knowledge through testimony does not require the receiver to assess the truth of the proposition directly**
