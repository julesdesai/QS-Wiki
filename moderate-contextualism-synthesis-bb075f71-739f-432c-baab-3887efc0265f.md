## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the standards of knowledge are moderately flexible within a narrowed context range**, **For minor shifts in context, the proposition remains known if it meets the relevant standards**
