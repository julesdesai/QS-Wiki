# Knowledge as a Reliable Process

**Parent:** [Can substantial inductive reasoning provide a basis for knowledge without false lemmas?](can-substantial-inductive-reasoning-provide-a-basis-for-knowledge-without-false-lemmas-antithesis-87998354-5f7c-43a0-9a73-ba95cf22d095.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief-forming process is reliable**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing it through a reliable process, and the proposition is true**
