# Knowledge as Justified True Belief Plus No Defeaters

**Parent:** [Can sensitivity adequately distinguish between knowledge and true belief?](can-sensitivity-adequately-distinguish-between-knowledge-and-true-belief-antithesis-5e59cb7d-e33e-4913-88b6-9e742056251d.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that there are no defeaters present for the person's justification**
