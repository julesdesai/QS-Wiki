## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification appropriate to their situation for believing the proposition**, **The essential standards for knowledge remain the same, but practical circumstances may affect what counts as justification**
