## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **Knowledge requires that the belief is produced by a cognitive process that reliably leads to truth**, **A reliable process is one that is likely to produce true beliefs over false ones**
