# How can direct perception explain perceptual constancies?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c9f60549-a68e-4924-a8ed-dd621882537e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Naïve Realism Summary](naïve-realism-summary-synthesis-ad05d8f4-c9d2-4024-8001-bf520360cf02.md)

## Content

**Perceptual constancies occur when objects are perceived as unchanging despite changes in sensory input, as when a white object appears white regardless of lighting.**, **If perception is direct awareness, changes in sensory input should alter perceived properties of objects.**, **Perceptual constancies imply a stabilization process at work, challenging the notion of direct perception.**
