# How does infinitism apply to simple empirical beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 27945be9-d250-4336-8ac8-f70ba6577ae6
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism View](infinitism-view-synthesis-bddda30f-213c-4b95-bb22-45afdb1e886a.md)

## Content

**Empirical beliefs are beliefs based on sensory experience and are often considered justifiable through direct observation or evidence.**, **An infinite chain of reasoning for simple empirical beliefs conflicts with the straightforward nature of sensory justification that employs finite and concrete evidence.**, **This disconnect raises difficulties in applying infinitism to common experiences that do not naturally involve infinite justifications.**
