## Content

**Knowledge requires a reliable connection to the truth**, **A person knows a proposition if the proposition is true and their belief tracks the truth across possible situations**, **A belief tracks the truth if in the closest possible worlds where the proposition is false, the person does not believe it**, **A person knows a proposition if it is true and their belief is sensitive and would not be held if false**
