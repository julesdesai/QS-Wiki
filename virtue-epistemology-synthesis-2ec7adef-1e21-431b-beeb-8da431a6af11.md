## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is formed by intellectual virtue**, **Intellectual virtues are cognitive faculties that reliably yield true beliefs**, **Awareness of the process forming the belief is not necessary for knowledge**
