# How does this theory account for memory-based knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 27e05414-76f5-49f3-b5e3-f6c0ff5d7a13
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Nozick’s Truth-Tracking Theory](nozicks-truth-tracking-theory-synthesis-5a62ad60-20de-4329-b52b-21f59b8da1c1.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person remembers a true proposition from a reliable source, yet if prompted to reconsider, they might not believe it if they doubt their memory in close possible worlds. Intuitively, we would want to say the person has knowledge based on their reliable memory even though their belief might fail to track truth under hypothetical skeptical scrutiny.**
