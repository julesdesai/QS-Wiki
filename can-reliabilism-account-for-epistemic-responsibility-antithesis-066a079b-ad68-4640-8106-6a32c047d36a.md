# Can reliabilism account for epistemic responsibility?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 066a079b-ad68-4640-8106-6a32c047d36a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism](reliabilism-synthesis-5d1929f3-45fe-4f02-bb7b-941a1c5017f0.md)

## Content

**Consider a belief formed reliably that leads to tension with other held beliefs. Intuitively, a responsible epistemic agent should resolve inconsistencies to ensure unified knowledge.**, **A plausible principle states that epistemic responsibility involves integrating new beliefs with existing knowledge, which reliability alone does not guarantee.**
