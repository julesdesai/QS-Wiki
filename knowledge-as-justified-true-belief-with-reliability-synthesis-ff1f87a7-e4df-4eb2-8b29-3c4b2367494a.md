## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is justified**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is true, they believe the proposition, their belief is justified, and their belief is formed by a reliable process**
