## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person sees a reflection in a mirror. The intuitive judgment might be that the person is not directly perceiving the external object but rather an indirect representation.**, **Indirect perception, such as seeing through a mirror, raises questions about whether perception is truly a causal interaction with external objects.**
