# Can knowledge be stable if it relies on inherently unstable perceptions?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 22b1ec32-5d1c-4909-b5a5-b31fcdd3d8c6
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Perceptual Baseline Phenomenalism](perceptual-baseline-phenomenalism-synthesis-c5e3ac41-9c0b-44e1-9f51-d5e2e022393a.md)

## Content

**External conditions, such as lighting or mood, can influence individual perceptual experiences.**, **A person's interpretation of a perceived object can change over time or with additional context.**, **It is implausible that knowledge can be stable if it is based on perceptions subject to such variability.**
