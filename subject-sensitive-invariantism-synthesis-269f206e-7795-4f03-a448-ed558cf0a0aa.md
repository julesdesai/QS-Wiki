## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, the standards for knowledge are constant across contexts but sensitive to the subject's practical interests**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **The subject's stakes affect whether they meet the standards for knowledge, but the standards themselves remain constant**
