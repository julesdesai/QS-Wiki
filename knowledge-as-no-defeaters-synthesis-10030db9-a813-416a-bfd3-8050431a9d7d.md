# Knowledge as No Defeaters

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 10030db9-a813-416a-bfd3-8050431a9d7d
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does inferential justification guarantee knowledge?](does-inferential-justification-guarantee-knowledge-antithesis-3b3cdbd4-f6cb-46bc-9f0b-a70f44eaaf94.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief has no defeaters, meaning no true propositions would undermine the justification if known**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief has no defeaters**
