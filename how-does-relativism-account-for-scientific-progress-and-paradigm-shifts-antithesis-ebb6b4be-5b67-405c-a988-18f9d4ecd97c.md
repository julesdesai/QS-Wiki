# How does relativism account for scientific progress and paradigm shifts?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ebb6b4be-5b67-405c-a988-18f9d4ecd97c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Relativism Summary](relativism-summary-synthesis-2665e075-79d4-4adb-9371-55cb5a5794f8.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which scientific paradigms shift based on accumulated evidence, as seen in the shift from Newtonian to quantum physics. Intuitively, this shift reflects objective progress rather than mere changes in frameworks.**, **A plausible principle is that scientific progress implies the existence of objective truths that transcend individual frameworks.**
