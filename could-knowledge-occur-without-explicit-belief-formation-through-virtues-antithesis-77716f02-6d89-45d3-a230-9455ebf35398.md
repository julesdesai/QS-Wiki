# Could knowledge occur without explicit belief formation through virtues?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 77716f02-6d89-45d3-a230-9455ebf35398
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-eab7dfb4-c128-4fbc-84f2-061ea66e30ee.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person gains knowledge through subconscious information processing, such as picking up a language accent without deliberate effort. Intuitively, we would judge that they have knowledge despite not exercising intellectual virtues explicitly.**, **Belief formation does not always involve conscious or explicit exercise of intellectual virtues.**
