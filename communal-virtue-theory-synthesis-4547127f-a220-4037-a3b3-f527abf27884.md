## Content

**For a group to know a proposition, it is necessary that they collectively exhibit communal intellectual virtues when believing the proposition**, **For a group to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a group to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed through collective exercise of communal intellectual virtues**, **For a group to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the group exhibits communal intellectual virtues, the proposition is true, and the belief is formed through collective exercise of communal intellectual virtues**
