**Parent:** [Does the necessity of truth for knowledge exclude justified false beliefs?](does-the-necessity-of-truth-for-knowledge-exclude-justified-false-beliefs-antithesis-2158f018-5f16-4c5c-82b5-833129cbcfd4.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief has a high probability of being true given the justification**, **Justification increases the likelihood of the truth of a belief**
