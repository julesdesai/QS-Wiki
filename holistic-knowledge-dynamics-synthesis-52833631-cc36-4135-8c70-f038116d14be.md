## Content

**For a group to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition as a dynamic system**, **For a group to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a group to know a proposition, it is necessary that belief is formed through dynamic interactions among group members**, **For a group to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the group dynamically believes the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is formed through dynamic interactions**
