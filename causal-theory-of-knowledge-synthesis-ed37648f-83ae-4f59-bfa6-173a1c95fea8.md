# Causal Theory of Knowledge

**Parent:** [Are there instances where safe belief fails to track truth, thereby undercutting knowledge?](are-there-instances-where-safe-belief-fails-to-track-truth-thereby-undercutting-knowledge-antithesis-cc10ca88-a610-4bf4-8525-f1843139cb71.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the truth of the proposition causes the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the truth causes the belief**
