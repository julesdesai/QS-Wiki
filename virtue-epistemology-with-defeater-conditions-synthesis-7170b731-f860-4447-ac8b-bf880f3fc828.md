## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief arises from an intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no defeaters present**, **An intellectual virtue is a character trait that reliably leads to true beliefs**
