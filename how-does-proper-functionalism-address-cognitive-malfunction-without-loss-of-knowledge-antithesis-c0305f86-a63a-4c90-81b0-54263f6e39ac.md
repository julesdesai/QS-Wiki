# How does proper functionalism address cognitive malfunction without loss of knowledge?

**Node ID:** c0305f86-a63a-4c90-81b0-54263f6e39ac
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Proper Functionalism](proper-functionalism-synthesis-56ac2890-cc65-4132-90ba-c28a7f97b59c.md)

## Content

**Cognitive faculties might malfunction temporarily but still produce true beliefs.**, **A true belief formed from malfunctioning faculties raises doubt about the process required by proper functionalism for knowledge.**, **If beliefs formed from malfunctioning faculties cannot be knowledge, this challenges the necessity of fully proper functioning for knowledge.**
