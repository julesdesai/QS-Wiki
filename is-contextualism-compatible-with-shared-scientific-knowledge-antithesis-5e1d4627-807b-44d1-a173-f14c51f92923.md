# Is contextualism compatible with shared scientific knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5e1d4627-807b-44d1-a173-f14c51f92923
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism about Knowledge](contextualism-about-knowledge-synthesis-3f771a9e-26d6-43c4-b604-a31f8bd9947c.md)

## Content

**Scientific knowledge is based on rigorously tested and universally applicable theories.**, **Contextualism suggests that these theories could lack knowledge status in contexts with different standards.**, **It is counterintuitive if scientific truths can lose their knowledge status simply because contextual standards differ.**
