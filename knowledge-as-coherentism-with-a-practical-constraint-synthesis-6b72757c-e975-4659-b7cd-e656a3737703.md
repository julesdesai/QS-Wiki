# Knowledge as Coherentism with a Practical Constraint

**Parent:** [Does coherence entail an overly demanding cognitive burden?](does-coherence-entail-an-overly-demanding-cognitive-burden-antithesis-5c258aa8-ddf3-4502-beb1-be4734526d46.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief coheres with other beliefs that are relevant to practical concerns**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief coheres with beliefs relevant to practical concerns**
