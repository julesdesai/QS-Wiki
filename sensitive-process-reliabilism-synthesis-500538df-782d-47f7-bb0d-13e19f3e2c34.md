## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is the result of a process that is sensitive to the context**, **A process is sensitive to the context if it adjusts its reliability based on the specific features of the situation**
