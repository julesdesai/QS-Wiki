# Knowledge as Justified True Belief plus No Defeaters

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can a person have knowledge even if their belief could easily have been false?](can-a-person-have-knowledge-even-if-their-belief-could-easily-have-been-false-antithesis-34f0ae64-3ed5-4368-aa19-7148b48057de.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no true propositions that would defeat their justification for the belief if known**
