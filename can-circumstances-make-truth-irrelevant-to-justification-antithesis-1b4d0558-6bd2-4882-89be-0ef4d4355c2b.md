# Can circumstances make truth irrelevant to justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1b4d0558-6bd2-4882-89be-0ef4d4355c2b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Evidentialist Theory of Knowledge](evidentialist-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-d871be87-85c4-4634-87bb-b00a8ec22277.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person's life-or-death decision relies on believing a falsehood due to all known evidence pointing to its truth. Intuitively, acting on such a belief could seem justified given the circumstances.**, **This indicates that justification could be context-dependent, questioning the universal necessity of truth in justified belief.**
