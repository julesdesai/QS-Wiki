## Content

**Robust knowledge involves true beliefs that hold across a range of similar situations**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **A person knows a proposition if their true belief holds up across a variety of similar scenarios**
