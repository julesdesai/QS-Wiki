# Does externalism imply that agents could have knowledge without awareness?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** fe86753d-6426-4e25-9c33-098db55e82f2
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Epistemic Externalism](epistemic-externalism-synthesis-a9c372ef-1ae7-460b-b26c-8a82dc6a1ee1.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a belief that aligns with external truth conditions, but the agent is completely unaware of this alignment.**, **Intuitively, we either have to say that the belief is knowledge or it is not.**, **If it is knowledge, awareness and reflection seem unnecessary, which conflicts with common consciousness-based understanding of knowledge.**, **If it is not knowledge, awareness and reflection seem essential, opposing external-only criteria.**
