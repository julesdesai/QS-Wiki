# Can we coherently separate practical and theoretical rationality?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 6e51008b-116e-4e36-ab96-f5c45d1d0325
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Encroachment](pragmatic-encroachment-synthesis-829538ce-ebfd-41f1-81ea-e2a3bdbbc235.md)

## Content

**Consider that practical and theoretical rationality are often viewed as addressing distinct domains, where practical rationality guides decision-making under risk, and theoretical rationality concerns belief formation based on evidence and truth. Suppose a person holds a belief that is theoretically rational but practically irrational due to high stakes; it is implausible to claim that the theoretical rationality of their belief is undermined by practical considerations.**
