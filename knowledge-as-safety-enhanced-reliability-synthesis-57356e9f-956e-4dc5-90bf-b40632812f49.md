# Knowledge as Safety-Enhanced Reliability

**Parent:** [Is reliability sufficient for knowledge without indefeasibility?](is-reliability-sufficient-for-knowledge-without-indefeasibility-antithesis-c62edd2d-0588-463b-b91e-c551664a833d.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is safe in the sense that it could not have easily been false**, **Safety requires that in nearby possible worlds, where similar conditions hold, the belief is reliably formed and true**
