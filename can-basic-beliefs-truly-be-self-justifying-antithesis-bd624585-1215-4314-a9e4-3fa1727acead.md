# Can basic beliefs truly be self-justifying?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** bd624585-1215-4314-a9e4-3fa1727acead
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundationalism with Basic Belief Justification](foundationalism-with-basic-belief-justification-synthesis-36983d87-c369-411c-807c-6c854afc89c3.md)

## Content

**Consider a belief that appears to be self-justifying, such as "I exist." This belief seems true to the believer simply through reflection.**, **It is implausible to assert that reflecting on one's own existence is enough to self-justify the belief without needing further evidence or reasoning.**
