# Can a reliable process be affected by external interference?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 711929bc-7068-4c16-b0d6-0ef3ea3076c1
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilist Theory of Knowledge](reliabilist-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-e6fc2437-0f22-487d-91cb-ba903a4e1b1f.md)

## Content

**A reliable process for forming beliefs about temperature involves a functioning thermostat.**, **Suppose external interference alters the thermostat without observable sign, leading to a false belief about room temperature.**, **Knowledge seems unjustified because external interference affected a normally reliable process.**
