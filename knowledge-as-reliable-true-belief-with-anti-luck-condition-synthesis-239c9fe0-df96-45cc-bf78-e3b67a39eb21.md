# Knowledge as Reliable True Belief with Anti-Luck Condition

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is reliability sufficient for knowledge in cases of luck or coincidence?](is-reliability-sufficient-for-knowledge-in-cases-of-luck-or-coincidence-antithesis-a678f0b4-fd03-4265-a1b3-314b42ea80da.md)

## Content

**Knowledge is a reliably formed true belief if it is not formed by mere luck**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief is formed through a reliable process and not due to luck**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **A reliable belief excludes knowledge when it occurs through luck**
