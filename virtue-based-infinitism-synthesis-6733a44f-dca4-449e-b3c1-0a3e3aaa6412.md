# Virtue-Based Infinitism

**Parent:** [Does infinite justification conflate reason-giving and reason-seeking?](does-infinite-justification-conflate-reason-giving-and-reason-seeking-antithesis-c2cb607c-4ba1-4569-ab3e-c1cbe8e0fff1.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that justification is facilitated by an endless chain of virtuous reasons**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and justification is facilitated by an endless chain of virtuous reasons**
