# Do changing environments affect reliability?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f819f0c5-d7fd-4ae0-beba-2f923ca08b8b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliability Theory of Knowledge](reliability-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-971ec002-e8db-455e-806a-7b81feaf9154.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a reliable cognitive process operates in an environment in which it is reliable, but the environment changes, affecting reliability. Even with justified belief and truth, the altered reliability questions the stability of knowledge.**, **A shift in environmental factors that change reliability can cause a once-reliable process to become unreliable, affecting the status of the knowledge claim.**
