## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in most nearby possible worlds where the person believes the proposition, it is true**, **A belief is safe if it is true in most nearby possible worlds where the person holds the belief**
