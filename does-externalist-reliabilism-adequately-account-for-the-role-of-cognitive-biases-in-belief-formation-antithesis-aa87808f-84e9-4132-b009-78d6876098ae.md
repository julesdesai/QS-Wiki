# Does externalist reliabilism adequately account for the role of cognitive biases in belief formation?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** aa87808f-84e9-4132-b009-78d6876098ae
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalist Reliabilism](externalist-reliabilism-synthesis-a51e876d-808b-45d8-97dd-3bdec299c925.md)

## Content

**Cognitive biases like confirmation bias and anchoring can lead to the formation of beliefs through otherwise reliable processes. In such cases, the resulting beliefs may be systematically false.**, **It is implausible to consider beliefs that are formed under the influence of strong cognitive biases as justified, even if they result from typically reliable processes.**
