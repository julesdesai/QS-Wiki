# Knowledge and Good Enough Safety

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** f51fb90e-3e3a-4626-b74e-bdc15a980dc5
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge tolerate minor possible errors?](can-knowledge-tolerate-minor-possible-errors-antithesis-765e1370-a9ad-4add-8dc9-63505b238ded.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is safe from error in most nearby possible worlds**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is safe from error in most nearby possible worlds**
