# Is practical usefulness necessary for every kind of propositional knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2b08d6ed-d726-4eab-8d62-06d41209d629
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Knowledge as Reliable Pragmatism](knowledge-as-reliable-pragmatism-synthesis-601e2ec8-4358-4137-9d1e-243ee7e2906f.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that scientific knowledge, like knowing facts about quantum mechanics, often lacks immediate practical usefulness for the knower. This principle indicates that practical usefulness might not be necessary for all kinds of knowledge.**
