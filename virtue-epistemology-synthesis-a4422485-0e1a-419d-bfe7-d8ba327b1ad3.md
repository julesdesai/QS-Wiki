**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is the result of intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is true and the belief results from an exercise of intellectual virtue**
