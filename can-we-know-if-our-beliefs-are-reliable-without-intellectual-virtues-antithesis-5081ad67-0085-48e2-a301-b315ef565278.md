# Can we know if our beliefs are reliable without intellectual virtues?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5081ad67-0085-48e2-a301-b315ef565278
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Expanded Virtue Reliabilism](expanded-virtue-reliabilism-synthesis-be1f6636-aeca-4898-8b76-a37fb9c31f3b.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person lives in a society where beliefs are shaped by cultural norms that are generally reliable, but the person lacks critical intellectual virtues themselves. In such a scenario, intuitively, we would judge that they might not possess knowledge of their beliefs' reliability despite conforming to them.**, **For a reliable method to truly influence belief, personal intellectual engagement is necessary, which is missing in conformity without intellectual virtues.**
