# Does foundationalism allow for evolving knowledge systems?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 8547a77a-608b-428c-b29c-776badf69b77
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundationalist Knowledge Summary](foundationalist-knowledge-summary-synthesis-d6291bb5-6e86-4606-a555-64e99394a6d4.md)

## Content

**Suppose an individual's understanding of the foundations of their knowledge changes over time. Intuitively, foundationalism seems inflexible in accommodating such changes fluidly within the justification structure.**, **It is implausible that foundationalism can account for dynamic, evolving systems of knowledge without modification to its core principles.**
