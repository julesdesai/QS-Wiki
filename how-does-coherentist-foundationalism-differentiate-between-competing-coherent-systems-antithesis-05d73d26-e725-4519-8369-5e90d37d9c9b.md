# How does coherentist foundationalism differentiate between competing coherent systems?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 05d73d26-e725-4519-8369-5e90d37d9c9b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentist Foundationalism](coherentist-foundationalism-synthesis-d82a768f-5fd5-4bf6-9304-6b054d40d9c7.md)

## Content

**Suppose two distinct but internally coherent belief systems exist, each denying the other's truth. Intuitively, there must be a method to determine which, if either, represents knowledge.**, **It is implausible that merely coherence within a system suffices to distinguish true knowledge from error.**
