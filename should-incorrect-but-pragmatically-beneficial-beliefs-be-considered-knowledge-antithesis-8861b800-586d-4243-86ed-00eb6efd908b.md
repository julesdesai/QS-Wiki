# Should incorrect but pragmatically beneficial beliefs be considered knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 8861b800-586d-4243-86ed-00eb6efd908b
**Depth:** 2
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Theory of Knowledge](pragmatic-theory-of-knowledge-thesis-1b010c5d-a3a9-40c5-8a7c-81c5cfc02c7c.md)

## Content

**There are cases where a person’s false belief results in beneficial practical outcomes.**, **A belief must be true to align with the concept of traditional knowledge.**, **Equating false beliefs with knowledge based solely on practical benefits undermines the requirement of truth in knowledge.**
