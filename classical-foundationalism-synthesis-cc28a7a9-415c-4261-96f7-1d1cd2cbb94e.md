## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that it is supported by basic beliefs or is incorrigible**, **Basic beliefs do not require further justification and serve as the foundation for other knowledge**
