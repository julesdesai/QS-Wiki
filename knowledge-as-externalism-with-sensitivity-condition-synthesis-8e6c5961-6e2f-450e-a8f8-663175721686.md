## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is sensitive to truth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, some conditions other than consciousness provide the justification for the belief, and the belief is sensitive to truth**
