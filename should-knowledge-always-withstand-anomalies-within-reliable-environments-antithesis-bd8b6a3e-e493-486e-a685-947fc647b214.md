## Content

**Suppose we have a case where beliefs are formed through reliable processes, yet anomalies affecting result cannot be completely eliminated. Intuitively, exposure to anomalies weakens claims to knowledge.**, **It is implausible for knowledge to adhere solely to reliable processes when anomalies persistently disrupt conditions.**
