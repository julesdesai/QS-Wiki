# No-Luck Reliability

**Parent:** [Do Gettier Problems undermine the sufficiency of reliable processes for knowledge?](do-gettier-problems-undermine-the-sufficiency-of-reliable-processes-for-knowledge-antithesis-f6115513-d8f0-4886-a6ba-fa5c60a0b727.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief, although produced by a reliable process, excludes significant elements of luck or coincidence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is produced by a reliable process void of luck**
