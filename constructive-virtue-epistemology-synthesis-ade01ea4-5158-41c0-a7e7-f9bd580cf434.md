## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person believes the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief arises from expansive cognitive virtues**, **Constructive outcomes, rather than truth alone, justify belief as knowledge**, **An expansive cognitive virtue is a trait that constructs coherent and useful beliefs**, **Knowledge involves constructing beliefs that coherently contribute to one's epistemic system**
