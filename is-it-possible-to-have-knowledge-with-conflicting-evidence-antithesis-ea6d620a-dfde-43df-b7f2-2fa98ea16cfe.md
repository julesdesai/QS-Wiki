# Is it possible to have knowledge with conflicting evidence?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ea6d620a-dfde-43df-b7f2-2fa98ea16cfe
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Evidentialist Reliabilism](evidentialist-reliabilism-synthesis-b2fd7651-b8ba-4bbd-aa9b-2c3c304a468b.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person holds a belief based on reliable evidence but also has strong opposing evidence that they choose to ignore.**, **Intuitively, it seems that the presence of neglected strong opposing evidence precludes knowledge, even if their belief-formation process was reliable.**
