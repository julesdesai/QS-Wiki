# Can two identical processes lead to different results regarding truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 4d02b55d-b67c-4987-8ebc-8486169245ef
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Reliabilism](virtue-reliabilism-synthesis-fa07ee15-e57c-4439-9a6e-88138086c5f8.md)

## Content

**Two cognitive processes that are typically reliable could, under identical circumstances, yield different beliefs about the same proposition.**, **This variation challenges the notion that reliability alone ensures the production of true beliefs consistently, thereby impacting the claim of knowledge.**
