## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief's truth is robust across a variety of complex dependencies**, **The robustness condition ensures that in both nearby and more distant possible worlds, the belief remains true regardless of complex dependencies**
