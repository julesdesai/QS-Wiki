## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is safe**, **A belief is safe if in most nearby possible worlds where the person believes the proposition, the proposition is true**
