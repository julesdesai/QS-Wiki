# Is dispositional ability sufficient for genuine belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** afb4c4a7-abef-49a8-9f8a-c2a8aacdd73c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Dispositional Account of Knowledge](dispositional-account-of-knowledge-synthesis-697a3559-6974-4820-bdde-37c51f6f9430.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a person has a dispositional ability to form true beliefs but forms a belief accidentally or through faulty reasoning. Intuitively, we either have to say that the person knows the proposition or that they don't due to lack of genuine belief formation. If we say they know, it challenges our understanding of genuine belief. If we say they don't, it suggests the dispositional ability is not sufficient for genuine belief**
