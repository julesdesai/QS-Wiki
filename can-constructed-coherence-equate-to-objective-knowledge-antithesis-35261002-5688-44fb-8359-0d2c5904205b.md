# Can constructed coherence equate to objective knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 35261002-5688-44fb-8359-0d2c5904205b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitist Coherentism](infinitist-coherentism-synthesis-c961c647-ce7e-4676-a333-4ee929ef8887.md)

## Content

**A coherent belief system can be constructed around misinformation or false assumptions. Intuitively, this does not seem to equate to knowledge because it lacks truth.**, **There is a plausible principle that objective knowledge must align with reality outside of one’s internal belief system, which constructed coherence might fail to achieve.**
