# Is expert-induced constancy controlling knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** fbe3fe14-b80b-4cfb-bd6a-038c0c78ecc7
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Nozick's Tracking Theory](nozicks-tracking-theory-synthesis-f1663159-56f4-4f6f-b53a-d60dc9dbfcb7.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where experts universally believe a fact true within a discipline despite potential disproof under unlikely circumstances. Intuitively, trusting expert consensus imbues a non-sensitive belief with consistency indicating knowledge.**, **There is a plausible principle that expertise and consensus grant epistemic authority, possessing knowledge independently of tracking hypothetical falsifications.**
