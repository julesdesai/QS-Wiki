# Does coherence provide a reliable path to truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f0d90d4e-1732-4657-a709-6a97ea6d462c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Temporal Coherentism](temporal-coherentism-synthesis-f4dfebfd-2f02-4b67-a5dd-1a5ab136482a.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that knowledge should be reliably connected to the truth. This principle, together with the claim that coherence does not guarantee truth due to potential systematic misinformation, entails that coherence alone fails to reliably establish knowledge.**
