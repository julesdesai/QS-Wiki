# Can foundational and coherent justification co-exist without contradiction?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** fddab338-35da-41a3-bf0b-4eb4b6f9829f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Unified Coherentism and Foundationalism (Foundherentism)](unified-coherentism-and-foundationalism-foundherentism-synthesis-d6bc8fa5-d0e8-4de3-ac09-102778002877.md)

## Content

**A belief system based on foundationalism implies that justification comes from foundational beliefs themselves**, **A belief system based on coherentism implies that justification comes from the interrelatedness of beliefs within the system**, **The claim that a belief requires both foundational and coherent justification gives rise to a potential conflict in their sources of justification**
