## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that practical concerns related to the person's circumstances do not affect whether the belief could have been false**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the true belief not be too easily lost given the person's practical environment**
