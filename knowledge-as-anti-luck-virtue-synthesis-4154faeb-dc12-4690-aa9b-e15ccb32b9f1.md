## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have a reliable belief-forming process that excludes luck**, **A reliable belief-forming process that leads to knowledge largely excludes luck in truth acquisition**
