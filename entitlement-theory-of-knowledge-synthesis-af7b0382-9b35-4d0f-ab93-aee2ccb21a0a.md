## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they are entitled to hold the belief owing to their epistemic environment**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and they are entitled to hold the belief owing to their epistemic environment**
