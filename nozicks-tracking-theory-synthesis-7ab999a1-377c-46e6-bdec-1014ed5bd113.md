## Content

**A belief constitutes knowledge if it tracks the truth**, **A belief tracks the truth if in nearby possible worlds, if the proposition were false, the believer would not believe it**, **If the belief is true in the actual world, the person knows the proposition**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief changes according to the truth in nearby possible worlds**
