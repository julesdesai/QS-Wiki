# Does everyone have equal access to intellectual virtues?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 434027de-2161-47ee-9e0a-c990e6f92b4d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-ffa55650-0795-4012-9f33-14517d86090e.md)

## Content

**Cognitive abilities can vary greatly among individuals due to factors like education and environment.**, **A belief formed by someone without access to intellectual development might not arise from what would be traditionally considered cognitive virtues.**, **This variance suggests that access to knowledge might unjustly favor those with more developed cognitive traits.**
