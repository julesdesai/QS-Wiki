## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief coheres with a relevant set of beliefs in some context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief coheres with their set of beliefs in a context**
