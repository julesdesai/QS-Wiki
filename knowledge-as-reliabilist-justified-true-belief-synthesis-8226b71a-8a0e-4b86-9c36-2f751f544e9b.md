# Knowledge as Reliabilist Justified True Belief

**Parent:** [Can a person know something without explicit awareness of their justification?](can-a-person-know-something-without-explicit-awareness-of-their-justification-antithesis-02ed5657-a21d-42e6-ade6-2567f6a56782.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A person has justification for a belief if it is produced by a reliable process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is true, the person believes it, and the belief was produced by a reliable process**
