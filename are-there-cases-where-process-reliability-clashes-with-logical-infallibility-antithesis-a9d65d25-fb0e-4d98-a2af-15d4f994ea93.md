# Are there cases where process reliability clashes with logical infallibility?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a9d65d25-fb0e-4d98-a2af-15d4f994ea93
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Process Reliabilism](process-reliabilism-synthesis-383ba069-e0f1-4291-bdc7-eacefc701040.md)

## Content

**Consider a process regarded as reliable because it frequently yields truth but occasionally contradicts logical reasoning. Intuitively, when contradictions arise, reliability alone doesn’t safeguard the belief's truth.**, **Logical infallibility presents a stronger claim for belief than reliability of process, hence suggesting a gap between logical consistency and mere reliability in defining knowledge.**
