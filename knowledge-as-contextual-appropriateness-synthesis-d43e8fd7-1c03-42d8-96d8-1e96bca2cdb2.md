## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief fits the appropriate context of inquiry**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief fits the appropriate context of inquiry and the proposition is true**
