# Is belief reliability always correlated with proper functioning?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 509da37a-bfa6-4f13-96df-8396e72b5e06
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Proper Functionalism](proper-functionalism-synthesis-60a6689e-3dfa-4e29-ad7b-61cc33b34e1b.md)

## Content

**Instances exist where belief reliability does not directly correlate with cognitive proper functioning.**, **External factors, such as luck or fortuitous circumstances, might ensure belief accuracy.**, **If belief reliability can be decoupled from proper functioning, then knowledge might not depend solely on such functioning.**
