# Is perception's influence on mental representations always reliable for achieving knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a1c691ff-ba61-4dd1-bc02-8908827b4570
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Epistemic Representationalism](epistemic-representationalism-synthesis-c86e8f4e-5440-4a21-8e04-4337bf022fd8.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which someone's perception is distorted, yet they arrive at a true belief. Intuitively, we either have to say that the person has knowledge despite defying the influence of perception, or reject that they have knowledge due to the flawed perception.**
