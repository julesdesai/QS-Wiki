# How does Foundationalist Coherentism differentiate between true belief systems and misleading coherence?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1661bfb9-5e48-4cd4-913f-1559a1eb7602
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundationalist Coherentism](foundationalist-coherentism-synthesis-b8a8c906-b541-4af2-8e95-f0f08da1d9c7.md)

## Content

**A system of beliefs might be coherent without accurately representing reality.**, **Coherence alone does not prevent a set of beliefs from being systematically misleading or false.**, **Ensuring that coherence can differentiate truth from misleading beliefs requires an additional criterion external to coherence itself, which Foundationalist Coherentism might not adequately provide.**
