**Parent:** [Does sensitivity impose unrealistic demands for future knowledge?](does-sensitivity-impose-unrealistic-demands-for-future-knowledge-antithesis-868ae0a4-1f54-4ad0-91c7-23f4fe6eee19.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process, and the proposition is true**
