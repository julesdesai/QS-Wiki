# Does the reliability of cognitive faculties suffice for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9ce6a439-a52e-46e6-b016-5be59360a829
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-7601e066-11d5-4f0c-8c42-44a4cc1f650d.md)

## Content

**A person uses a reliable cognitive faculty, such as vision, to form the belief that it is noon, but due to an anomalous circumstance, it is actually midnight. This belief is not a case of knowledge.**, **A case in which a cognitive faculty is reliable does not guarantee that every belief it produces is true.**
