# Is shifting standards for justification practical?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 91aeeec2-3cd2-4570-83bd-2295c14cf8db
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Moderate Contextualism Summary](moderate-contextualism-summary-synthesis-54895291-30c8-42cf-8322-baea34575bd1.md)

## Content

**Imagine a legal case where standards of justification shift during trial phases, leading to different judgments at different times despite the same evidence. Intuitively, this results in an impractical and volatile assertion of knowledge.**, **Consistency in justifying standards is critical for applications like law or science, as unstable standards could undermine factual conclusions.**
