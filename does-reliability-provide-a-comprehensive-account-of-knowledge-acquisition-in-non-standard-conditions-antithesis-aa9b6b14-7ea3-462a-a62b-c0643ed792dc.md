# Does reliability provide a comprehensive account of knowledge acquisition in non-standard conditions?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** aa9b6b14-7ea3-462a-a62b-c0643ed792dc
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism about Knowledge](reliabilism-about-knowledge-synthesis-c84f45b2-357a-4b29-be66-6a09ffb029e2.md)

## Content

**Imagine astronauts relying on a series of technologically advanced, reliable processes for communication, which work differently in space due to novel variables. Intuitively, what counts as knowledge may differ**, **It is implausible that reliability accounts capture knowledge where conventional processes behave unpredictably in non-standard conditions**
