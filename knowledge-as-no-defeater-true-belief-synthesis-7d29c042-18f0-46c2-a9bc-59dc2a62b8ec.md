# Knowledge as No Defeater True Belief

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 7d29c042-18f0-46c2-a9bc-59dc2a62b8ec
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is it possible to have false knowledge?](is-it-possible-to-have-false-knowledge-antithesis-82911aba-c101-49fd-a5d8-516c263513dc.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there be no defeaters present**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and no defeaters are present**
