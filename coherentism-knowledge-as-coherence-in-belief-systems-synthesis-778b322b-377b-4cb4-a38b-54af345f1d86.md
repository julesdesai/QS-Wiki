## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that justification for the belief involves the coherence of a system of beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe a proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification involves the coherence of a system of beliefs**
