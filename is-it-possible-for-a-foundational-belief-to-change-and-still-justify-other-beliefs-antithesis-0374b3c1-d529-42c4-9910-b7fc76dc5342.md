# Is it possible for a foundational belief to change and still justify other beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0374b3c1-d529-42c4-9910-b7fc76dc5342
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundationalist Knowledge Summary](foundationalist-knowledge-summary-synthesis-d6291bb5-6e86-4606-a555-64e99394a6d4.md)

## Content

**Suppose a foundational belief held by someone changes over time due to new evidence. Intuitively, one would question whether beliefs based on it remain justified.**, **It is implausible that a foundational belief can provide a stable basis for justification if it is subject to change.**
