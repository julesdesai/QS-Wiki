# Is knowledge possible if tracking overlooks relevant alternatives?

## Content

**In some contexts, relevant alternatives to the truth must be considered when evaluating if a belief tracks truth, yet tracking theory might incorrectly grant knowledge without accounting for these alternatives.**, **This raises the intuition that knowledge isn't present because awareness of relevant alternative possibilities, which tracking might ignore, contributes significantly to forming genuine knowledge.**
