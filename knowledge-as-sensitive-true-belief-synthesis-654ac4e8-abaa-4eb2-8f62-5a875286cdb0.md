## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **Sensitivity is assessed by considering whether in nearby possible worlds where the proposition is false, the person does not believe it**
