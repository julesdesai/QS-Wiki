# Nozick's Truth-Tracking Theory

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Can knowledge be coincidental rather than reliable?](can-knowledge-be-coincidental-rather-than-reliable-antithesis-3b79eebc-8e55-4eb4-85f4-615933c72d81.md)

## Content

**Knowledge is a true belief that tracks truth across possible worlds**, **A person knows a proposition if, in nearby possible worlds where the proposition is false, the person does not believe it**, **A person knows a proposition if, in nearby possible worlds where the proposition is true, the person believes it**, **The belief must hold in the actual world and track the truth in these possible worlds for it to be knowledge**
