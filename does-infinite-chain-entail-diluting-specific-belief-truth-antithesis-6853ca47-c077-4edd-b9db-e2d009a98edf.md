# Does infinite chain entail diluting specific belief truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 6853ca47-c077-4edd-b9db-e2d009a98edf
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherent Infinitism](coherent-infinitism-synthesis-f2c99927-f862-4ee8-8b5b-2fefd2717ee2.md)

## Content

**A plausible principle is that as a chain of justification extends infinitely, the direct relevance of each reason to the original belief diminishes progressively. This principle together with the requirement for connectedness in justification entails that a coherent chain weakens its connection to the specific proposition being justified.**
