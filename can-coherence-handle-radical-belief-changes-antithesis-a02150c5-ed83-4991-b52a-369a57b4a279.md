# Can coherence handle radical belief changes?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a02150c5-ed83-4991-b52a-369a57b4a279
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism](coherentism-synthesis-fe89b418-bb8b-4164-8874-fa4a88946376.md)

## Content

**Consider a person who converts from one religion to another, bringing about a radical shift in beliefs.**, **Intuitively, such shifts might not be mediated by coherence but rather by external factors like experiences or new evidence.**, **The concept of an entirely coherent belief system struggles to account for sudden, dramatic changes in belief.**
