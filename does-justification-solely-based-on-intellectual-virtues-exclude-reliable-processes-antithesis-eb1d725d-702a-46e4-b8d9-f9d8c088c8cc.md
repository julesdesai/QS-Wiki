# Does justification solely based on intellectual virtues exclude reliable processes?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** eb1d725d-702a-46e4-b8d9-f9d8c088c8cc
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-ad239dac-f508-456d-984a-1344bc3c3ad8.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have cases where knowledge is attained through reliable cognitive processes but lacking identified intellectual virtues. Intuitively, the knowledge seems justified.**, **There is a plausible principle that reliable cognitive processes can independently constitute justification, without necessarily involving intellectual virtues.**
