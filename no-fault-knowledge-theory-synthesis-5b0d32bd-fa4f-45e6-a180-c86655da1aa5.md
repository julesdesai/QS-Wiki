# No-Fault Knowledge Theory

**Parent:** [Can luck fulfill the conditions for knowledge?](can-luck-fulfill-the-conditions-for-knowledge-antithesis-8fc3e1c3-d854-47b0-b2a5-6b79f9683ed5.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief involves no cognitive errors**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief involves no cognitive errors**
