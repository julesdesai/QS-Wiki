## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the person believes the proposition, it could not easily be false**, **Safety rather than sensitivity addresses the likelihood of misleading information affecting belief**
