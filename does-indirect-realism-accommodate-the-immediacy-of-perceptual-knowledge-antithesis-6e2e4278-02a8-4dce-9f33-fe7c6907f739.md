# Does indirect realism accommodate the immediacy of perceptual knowledge?

## Content

**The immediacy of perceiving something like a flash of lightning suggests direct knowledge acquisition without mediation.**, **Indirect realism, with its emphasis on interpretative processes and mental representations, implies a delay in knowledge acquisition.**, **There is a tension between the immediacy of some perceptual knowledge and the mediated nature of knowledge proposed by indirect realism.**
