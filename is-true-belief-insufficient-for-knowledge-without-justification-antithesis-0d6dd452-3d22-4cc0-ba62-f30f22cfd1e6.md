# Is true belief insufficient for knowledge without justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0d6dd452-3d22-4cc0-ba62-f30f22cfd1e6

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-e399f75f-498c-40d7-978e-5c4c6418f273.md)

## Content
