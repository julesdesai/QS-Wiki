## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief in the proposition is not the result of luck**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their cognitive process actively connects belief with truth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the anti-luck condition is met**
