# Does truth need to play a direct causal role in knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3a627f78-cef9-47ef-bf9a-9c9efed727c9
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Causal Theory of Knowledge](causal-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-916d423a-1723-4911-b4ab-5dfda1d6916a.md)

## Content

**Imagine a situation where Alex believes his friend will attend a party due to past habits and indeed the friend attends, though this time unexpectedly for unrelated reasons. The truth of the friend’s attendance plays no causal role in forming Alex's belief. Thus, it seems implausible for Alex to have knowledge under these conditions.**
