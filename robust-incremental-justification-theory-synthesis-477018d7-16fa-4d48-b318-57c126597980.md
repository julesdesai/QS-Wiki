# Robust Incremental Justification Theory

**Parent:** [Can knowledge arise from accidental truths without incremental justification?](can-knowledge-arise-from-accidental-truths-without-incremental-justification-antithesis-efba4244-d13e-4c12-b804-f2c3fb1c10b8.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief robustly justifies the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief robustly justifies the proposition**
