# Is Reliable Process Theory too externalist for first-person access?

## Content

**A plausible principle is that first-person knowledge requires more than just process reliability; it requires an understanding or awareness of that reliability.**, **Suppose a person forms a true belief using an internally opaque algorithm. Intuitively, we may doubt their knowledge despite external reliability due to their lack of understanding or awareness.**
