# Can knowledge exist with undiscovered defeaters?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 61baf1c8-0dc0-4f3a-9706-ec6553845d92
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [No defeaters theory of knowledge](no-defeaters-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-293021ef-516a-4641-9c48-087d1223e339.md)

## Content

**Imagine a scenario where a person believes a proposition based on good evidence, making it true. However, there's an unknown fact, which if discovered, would undermine the belief. This scenario suggests that knowledge might require more than just true belief without any known defeaters**, **In cases where the person remains unaware of such facts, it is intuitive to suggest that true belief alone might not constitute knowledge**
