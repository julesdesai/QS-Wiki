# What role does selective attention play in direct knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 35305117-a2fd-4f2e-b8df-89ee8bdbb6d9
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Direct Realism](direct-realism-synthesis-63f38132-cda9-42dc-9c71-38670cc01e77.md)

## Content

**Selective attention is the process by which a person focuses on certain stimuli in the environment while ignoring others, such as hearing one's name in a noisy room.**, **In scenarios of selective attention, perception of the environment is filtered and not comprehensive, indicating that attention may mediate what is directly perceived.**, **If attention filters perception, then perception may not provide immediate and direct knowledge of all external objects.**
