## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief depends counterfactually on the truth of the proposition in nearby possible worlds**, **Counterfactual dependence means that a belief would not exist if the proposition were false in similar worlds**
