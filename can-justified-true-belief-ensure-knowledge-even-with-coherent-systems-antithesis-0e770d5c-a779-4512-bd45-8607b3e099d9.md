# Can justified true belief ensure knowledge even with coherent systems?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0e770d5c-a779-4512-bd45-8607b3e099d9
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism focuses on systematized justification](coherentism-focuses-on-systematized-justification-synthesis-825f84fd-be44-4c37-9d4c-4b4f5d772a40.md)

## Content

**A case where a person's belief system is coherent and the beliefs are true due to sheer chance is not a case in which the person knows those beliefs.**, **It is plausible to claim that knowledge requires not just coherence and truth but reliable truth-tracking. Coherent systems do not guarantee reliability.**
