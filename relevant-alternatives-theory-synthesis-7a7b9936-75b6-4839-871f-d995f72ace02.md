# Relevant Alternatives Theory

**Parent:** [Can beliefs based on misleading evidence undermine safety?](can-beliefs-based-on-misleading-evidence-undermine-safety-antithesis-39f32f01-6cdf-499a-a4e2-794b1a5dcf37.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they can rule out relevant alternatives where the proposition is false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and they can rule out relevant alternatives**
