# Can someone know something if they do not believe it?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f2d759ae-3df8-44e8-b858-b67cda63f448
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Invariantism with Fixed Standards](invariantism-with-fixed-standards-synthesis-2877eecc-8388-4e01-b87d-ffe7f20b2619.md)

## Content

**Suppose someone habitually asserts a proposition is false but acknowledges its truth upon reflection.**, **It seems possible to know something even if one does not actively maintain the belief or constantly affirm it.**, **Invariantism with the condition that a person knows a proposition if they believe it would incorrectly disqualify this knowledge.**
