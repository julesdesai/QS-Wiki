## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed through intellectual virtue**, **An intellectual virtue involves the integration of reliable cognitive abilities with a conscientious disposition toward achieving truth**
