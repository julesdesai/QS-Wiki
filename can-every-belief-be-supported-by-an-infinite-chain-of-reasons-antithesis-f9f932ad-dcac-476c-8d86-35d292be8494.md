# Can every belief be supported by an infinite chain of reasons?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f9f932ad-dcac-476c-8d86-35d292be8494
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism with Non-arbitrary Reasons](infinitism-with-non-arbitrary-reasons-synthesis-11967ecc-f2b8-44cb-a381-b7e79afac297.md)

## Content

**Beliefs are often based on immediate perception or experience, not on an infinite chain of reasons.**, **Practical beliefs, such as those used in daily life, typically do not rely on an infinite chain of reasons.**, **Requiring an infinite chain for all beliefs is impractical and unlikely to be satisfied universally.**
