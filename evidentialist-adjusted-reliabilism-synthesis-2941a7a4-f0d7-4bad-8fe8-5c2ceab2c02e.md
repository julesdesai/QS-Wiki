## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have additional evidence linking their belief to its truth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, their belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process, and they have additional evidence linking their belief to its truth**
