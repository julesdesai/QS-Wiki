## Content

**Knowledge depends on the context of the knower**, **A person knows a proposition if they have sufficient justification specific to their context**, **Different epistemic contexts alter what counts as sufficient justification**, **It is not possible for a person to simultaneously know and not know the same proposition in the same context**
