# How do Gettier cases challenge the claim that knowledge is justified true belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 540067de-f10a-4e7f-96de-29c411925754
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Phenomenal Conservatism: Seeming Justification](phenomenal-conservatism-seeming-justification-synthesis-de77a591-8397-48c7-98a8-42a761bdf13a.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where an individual believes a proposition because of a justified but false belief, yet the proposition is accidentally true. In this scenario, intuitively, we would not say the individual has knowledge.**, **A belief can be justified and true by coincidence, but intuitive judgment suggests this does not constitute knowledge.**
