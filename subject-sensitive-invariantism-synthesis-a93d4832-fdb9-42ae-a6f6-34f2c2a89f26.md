# Subject-Sensitive Invariantism

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** a93d4832-fdb9-42ae-a6f6-34f2c2a89f26
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Are contextual factors relevant to knowledge?](are-contextual-factors-relevant-to-knowledge-antithesis-478fe1c2-2f80-4357-8e1e-953b970457b4.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified in a way that is sensitive to the practical stakes for the subject**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification is sensitive to the practical stakes for the subject**
