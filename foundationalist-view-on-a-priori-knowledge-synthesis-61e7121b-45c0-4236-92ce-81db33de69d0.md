# Foundationalist View on A Priori Knowledge

**Parent:** [Does external evidence always denote a necessary condition for knowledge?](does-external-evidence-always-denote-a-necessary-condition-for-knowledge-antithesis-c01aebf0-6a8c-49b6-bc9d-b942426fa6fb.md)

## Content

**For a person to know an a priori proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know an a priori proposition, it is necessary that they hold foundational beliefs supporting the proposition**, **For a person to know an a priori proposition, it is sufficient that it is true and supported by foundational beliefs**
