# Does the absence of a defeater guarantee justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 63824f87-b6a0-4295-9113-bce8a5375d3d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [No Defeaters Theory](no-defeaters-theory-synthesis-e6eeb208-311d-4d09-a784-60d3e95f478f.md)

## Content

**A belief might be unjustified even if there is no defeating information available.**, **Having no defeater for one's justification does not necessarily imply the presence of adequate justification.**, **It is conceivable that someone holds a belief due to poor reasoning yet avoids exposure to any defeating information.**
