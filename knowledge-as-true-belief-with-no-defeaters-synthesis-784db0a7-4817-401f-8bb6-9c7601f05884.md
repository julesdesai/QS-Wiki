# Knowledge as True Belief with No Defeaters

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can false beliefs formed by reliable processes still count as knowledge?](can-false-beliefs-formed-by-reliable-processes-still-count-as-knowledge-antithesis-4dc63b1a-d122-4aad-b224-2df010d9f777.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no defeaters for the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is true and there are no defeaters**
