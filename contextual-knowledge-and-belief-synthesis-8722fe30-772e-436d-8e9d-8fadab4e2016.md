# Contextual Knowledge and Belief

**Parent:** [Are judgement-based actions plausible without belief?](are-judgement-based-actions-plausible-without-belief-antithesis-a7fb0a9a-1b20-45a1-96dd-3462ab6fc717.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for the proposition**, **Belief is contextually required depending on the practical stakes for acting on the knowledge**, **A person knows a proposition if it is true, they have justification, and belief is required only in high-stakes contexts**
