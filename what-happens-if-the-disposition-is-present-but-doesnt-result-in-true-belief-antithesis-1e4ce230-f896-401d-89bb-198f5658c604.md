# What happens if the disposition is present but doesn't result in true belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1e4ce230-f896-401d-89bb-198f5658c604
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Dispositional Theory of Knowledge](dispositional-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-1fd28b95-7886-486d-be0c-ab1f241be193.md)

## Content

**Suppose there is a person who has a disposition to believe a particular proposition when it is true, but due to nervousness, they regularly fail to form a belief or form incorrect beliefs under pressure.**, **Intuitively, we would be reluctant to say that this person knows the proposition despite having the right disposition.**
