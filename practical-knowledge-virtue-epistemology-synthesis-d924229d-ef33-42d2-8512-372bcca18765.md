## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have practical competence in acting on the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary their competence results from intellectual virtue**, **An intellectual virtue is a trait that systematically leads to successful action based on truth**
