# Can foundational beliefs alone prevent isolation?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f5090a04-aa0e-450d-b704-c6d2c98491ee
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Minimal Foundational Coherentism Summary](minimal-foundational-coherentism-summary-synthesis-0e7aa745-4942-4fbb-a96c-6043bbe35e5d.md)

## Content

**Suppose a coherent belief system is only linked to foundational beliefs but disregards new evidence. Intuitively, such a system could still be isolated from reality, as it does not adapt to changing circumstances.**, **The possibility of disregarding new evidence questions whether foundational beliefs truly prevent isolation if they do not ensure adaptability and responsiveness.**
