## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their cognitive faculties functioning properly have produced the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their cognitive faculties functioning properly have produced the belief, the proposition is true, and they believe the proposition**
