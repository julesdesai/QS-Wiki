## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is formed by a reliable causal process that usually yields true beliefs**, **The process must filter out the influence of misleading evidence**
