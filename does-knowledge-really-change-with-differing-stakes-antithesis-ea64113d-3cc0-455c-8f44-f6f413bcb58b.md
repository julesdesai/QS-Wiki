# Does knowledge really change with differing stakes?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ea64113d-3cc0-455c-8f44-f6f413bcb58b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Subject-Sensitive Invariantism](subject-sensitive-invariantism-synthesis-687e35ad-b9eb-49bd-a730-d8bc9269ea3a.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a situation where two people have the same evidence for a proposition, but face different consequences if they're wrong. Intuitively, we might doubt that their level of knowledge should be different.**, **There is a plausible principle that evidence, not stakes, should determine knowledge.**
