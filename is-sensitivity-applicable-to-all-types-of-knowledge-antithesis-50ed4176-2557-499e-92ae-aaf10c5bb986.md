# Is sensitivity applicable to all types of knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 50ed4176-2557-499e-92ae-aaf10c5bb986
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Sensitivity Theory of Knowledge](sensitivity-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-ba84f32c-948c-4607-9039-09527bd88c50.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where mathematical knowledge is involved, such as knowing that 2+2=4, where the falsity of the proposition is not conceivable. In such a case, intuitively, the sensitivity condition cannot be applied because the proposition cannot be false.**
