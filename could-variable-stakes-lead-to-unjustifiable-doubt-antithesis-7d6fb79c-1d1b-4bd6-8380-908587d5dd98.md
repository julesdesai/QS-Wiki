# Could variable stakes lead to unjustifiable doubt?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7d6fb79c-1d1b-4bd6-8380-908587d5dd98
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Subject-Sensitive Invariantism](subject-sensitive-invariantism-synthesis-2eef6ba9-ea95-4aea-9a96-5c60688dde0e.md)

## Content

**Consider a situation where rising stakes cause an individual to doubt previously held knowledge. Intuitively, a change in stakes should not retroactively impact well-founded knowledge.**, **If stakes influence knowledge, then individuals in high-stakes situations could fall into undue skepticism, which might be unwarranted.**
