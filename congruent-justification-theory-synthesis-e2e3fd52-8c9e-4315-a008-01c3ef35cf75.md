## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their justification aligns naturally with the truth of the proposition**, **Alignment refers to a congruence between the mode of justification and the obtaining of the truth**
