# Infinitism in Epistemic Justification

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **Justification for a belief involves an infinite chain of reasons**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have an infinite chain of justifying reasons, and the proposition is true**
