# Can one possess knowledge under extreme environmental distortion?

## Content

**Imagine an individual in an artificial environment where sensory data completely misleads, yet they form a true belief by chance. Intuitively, this situation seems inadequate for genuine knowledge.**, **There is a plausible principle that knowledge requires an environment that does not distort cognitive processing. The suggestion that knowledge can exist with faulty environmental interaction raises a contradiction.**
