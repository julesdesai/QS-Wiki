# Can a person know something based on unreliable but true evidence?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7f204503-d88a-4499-a59e-32f13f2bc0e8
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Evidentialist Reliabilism](evidentialist-reliabilism-synthesis-b2fd7651-b8ba-4bbd-aa9b-2c3c304a468b.md)

## Content

**A person can have a true belief that is produced by an unreliable process, such as guessing correctly.**, **In such a case, intuitively, it seems incorrect to say this person knows the proposition despite having true belief.**
