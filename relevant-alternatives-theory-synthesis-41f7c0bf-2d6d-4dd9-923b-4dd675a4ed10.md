## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief in the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they can rule out relevant alternatives to the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have a true belief and can dismiss relevant competing hypotheses**
