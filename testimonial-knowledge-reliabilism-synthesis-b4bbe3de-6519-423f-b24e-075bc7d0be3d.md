## Content

**For a person to know a proposition from testimony, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition from testimony, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition from testimony, it is necessary that the testimony comes from a reliable source**, **For a person to know a proposition from testimony, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the testimony comes from a reliable source**
