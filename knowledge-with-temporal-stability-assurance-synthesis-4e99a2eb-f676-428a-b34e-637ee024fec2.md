**Parent:** [Could virtuously formed true beliefs lack knowledge status if the environment shifts post-formation?](could-virtuously-formed-true-beliefs-lack-knowledge-status-if-the-environment-shifts-post-formation-antithesis-28f20e7f-59e7-4765-9875-ba7e22c93776.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from intellectual virtue or competence**, **For a proposition to count as knowledge, it is necessary that post-formation environmental conditions do not undermine the reliability of the belief formation upon reevaluation**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief arises from intellectual virtue or competence, and post-formation environmental conditions confirm the knowledge process**
