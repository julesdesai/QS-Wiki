# Can we truly have critical realism if perceptual experiences do not directly correspond to reality?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 47f94243-742a-4bef-849f-5d011991fabb
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Critical Realism](critical-realism-synthesis-a95d91b3-a4d2-4740-8b59-c24b26663299.md)

## Content

**If perceptual experiences are indirect, they may not correspond accurately to reality.**, **If perceptual experiences do not correspond accurately to reality, this challenges the assertion that they offer any form of access to external objects.**, **Without correspondence to reality, the critical realism claim that perception provides access to external objects is undermined.**
