# Knowledge as Virtue with Understanding

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** f704e775-3de6-4e34-9e37-ecc82b7dd57a
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can a virtue without understanding grant knowledge?](can-a-virtue-without-understanding-grant-knowledge-antithesis-f22b7d78-95ae-4223-9020-72b61b40c0ac.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from an intellectual virtue of the person**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the intellectual virtue involves understanding**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief arises from an intellectual virtue of the person, and the intellectual virtue involves understanding**
