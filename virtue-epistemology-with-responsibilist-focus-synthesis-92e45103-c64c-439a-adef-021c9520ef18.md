## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is a result of intellectual virtues characterized by responsibility and conscientiousness**, **For intellectual virtues to inform knowledge, they must involve responsible inquiry practices**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief results from responsible and conscientious intellectual virtues**
