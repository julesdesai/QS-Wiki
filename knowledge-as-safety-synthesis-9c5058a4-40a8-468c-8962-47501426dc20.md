## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief could not easily have been false in a similar situation**, **The safety condition ensures knowledge stability across varying contexts**
