## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **The criteria for relevant alternatives vary with the context of the attributor of the knowledge claim**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that in contextually relevant conditions, they believe the proposition, have justification, and the proposition is true**
