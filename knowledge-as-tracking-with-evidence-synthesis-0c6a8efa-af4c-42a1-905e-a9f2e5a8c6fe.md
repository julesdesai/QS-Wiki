# Knowledge as Tracking with Evidence

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 0c6a8efa-af4c-42a1-905e-a9f2e5a8c6fe
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Could knowledge require more than belief, truth, and tracking?](could-knowledge-require-more-than-belief-truth-and-tracking-antithesis-1c523b13-2a25-4889-b296-480a1817b4da.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief tracks the truth across possible scenarios**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have evidence supporting the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, their belief tracks the truth across possible scenarios, and they have evidence supporting the belief**
