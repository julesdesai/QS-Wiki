# Are mediated perceptions excluded under Naïve Realism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** db99eb08-dbbf-4df6-9f97-2ecfe8943776
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Naïve Realism](naïve-realism-synthesis-960c5650-a545-46a7-9f9f-61ad44274751.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case where a person uses eyeglasses to see clearly. Intuitively, the perception in this case is mediated by the glass lens, which implies that not all perceptions are direct.**, **There is a plausible principle that if perception were always direct, no external medium should alter the visual input.**
