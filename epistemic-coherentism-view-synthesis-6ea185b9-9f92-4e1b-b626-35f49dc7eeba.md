## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief coheres with a system of beliefs**, **A coherent belief system is logically consistent and mutually supportive**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief coheres with their system of beliefs**
