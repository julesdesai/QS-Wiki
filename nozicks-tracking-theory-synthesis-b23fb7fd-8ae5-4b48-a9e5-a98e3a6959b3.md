## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a proposition to be known, if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **For a proposition to be known, if the proposition were true, the person would believe it**
