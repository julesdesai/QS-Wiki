# Knowledge as Relevant Alternatives Theory

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they can rule out relevant alternative propositions**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that among seriously considered possibilities, only true alternatives remain**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and they have ruled out every relevant alternative**
