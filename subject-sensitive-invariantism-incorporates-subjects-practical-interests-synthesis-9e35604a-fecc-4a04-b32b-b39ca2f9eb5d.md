# Subject-Sensitive Invariantism incorporates subject's practical interests

**Parent:** [Is the context an unreliable guide for determining standards of knowledge?](is-the-context-an-unreliable-guide-for-determining-standards-of-knowledge-antithesis-2df8fbfd-fdd0-454c-9d92-6010057ea607.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the standards of knowledge consider the subject’s practical interests**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the standards incorporating subject’s practical interests are met**
