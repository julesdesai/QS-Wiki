# Nozick's Conditional Theory of Knowledge

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can someone know something based on unchanged belief?](can-someone-know-something-based-on-unchanged-belief-antithesis-ee97dd0d-b6f3-4c48-b7d9-5e336447f6d3.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, if the proposition were true, the person would believe it**
