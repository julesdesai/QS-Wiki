## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person exercises intellectual virtues in forming the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **A person knows a proposition if the proposition is true, they believe it, and they have justification for it**, **Intellectual virtues include traits like open-mindedness and intellectual courage**
