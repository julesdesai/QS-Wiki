## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they would not believe it if it were false in contexts similar to the actual context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe it, it is true, and they would not believe it if false in similar contexts**
