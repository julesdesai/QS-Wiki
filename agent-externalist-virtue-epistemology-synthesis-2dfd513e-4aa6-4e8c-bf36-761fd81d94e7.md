# Agent-Externalist Virtue Epistemology

**Parent:** [Should intellectual virtue always lead to truth?](should-intellectual-virtue-always-lead-to-truth-antithesis-2612182e-b45f-40cb-bba3-bebcd4434974.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the truth of the belief to be dependent on factors outside the agent's control**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the truth of the belief is dependent on factors outside the agent's control**
