## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief arises from a reliable cognitive process**, **Cognitive process reliability does not vary with context**
