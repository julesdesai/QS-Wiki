**Parent:** [How can finite beings possess an infinite chain of justified beliefs?](how-can-finite-beings-possess-an-infinite-chain-of-justified-beliefs-antithesis-d533e9a3-ed0d-44e2-8aa6-6ca462a42d68.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres with a coherent finite system of justified beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that each belief is justified by another belief within a finite system**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief coheres with a coherent finite system of justified beliefs**
