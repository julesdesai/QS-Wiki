## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in nearly all nearby possible worlds where the person believes the proposition, the proposition is true**, **A person knows a proposition if they believe the proposition is true**, **Close possible worlds are ones that are almost identical to the actual world**, **A person knows a proposition if the proposition is true**
