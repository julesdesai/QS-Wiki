## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from reliable epistemic dependability**, **Epistemic dependability is the consistent ability to filter true from false beliefs**
