## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their cognitive faculties are functioning properly**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief results from both intellectual virtue and properly functioning faculties designed for truth acquisition**
