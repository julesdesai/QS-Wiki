## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary their belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief is produced reliably and meets the other conditions**
