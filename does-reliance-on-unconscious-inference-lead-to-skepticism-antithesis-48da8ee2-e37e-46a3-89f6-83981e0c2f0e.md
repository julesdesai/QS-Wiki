# Does reliance on unconscious inference lead to skepticism?

## Content

**Unconscious inference suggests that much of our perception could be influenced by cognitive processes outside of our awareness, which can lead to skeptical doubts about the veracity of our perceptions.**, **If our perceptions are shaped significantly by unconscious processes, it becomes challenging to distinguish between when we truly perceive external reality and when we do not, potentially leading to radical skepticism.**
