# Relevant alternatives theory

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they can rule out all relevant alternatives**, **The relevance of alternatives is determined by the epistemic situation, not the conversational context or stakes**
