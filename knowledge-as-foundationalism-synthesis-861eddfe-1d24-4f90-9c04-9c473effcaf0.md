## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is either self-justified or rests on foundational beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is either directly justified by foundational beliefs or is self-justified**
