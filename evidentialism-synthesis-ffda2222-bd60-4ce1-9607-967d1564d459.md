## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have sufficient evidence for the proposition**, **The sufficiency of evidence depends on the context and nature of the proposition**
