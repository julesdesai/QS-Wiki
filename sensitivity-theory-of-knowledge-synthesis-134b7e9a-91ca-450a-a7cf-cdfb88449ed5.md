## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, their belief must be sensitive to the truth of the proposition**, **A belief is sensitive if, were the proposition false, the person would not believe it**
