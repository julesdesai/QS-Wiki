# Knowledge as Social-Ly Modified Reliable Process

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises through a reliable cognitive process that may include social influences without being entirely dependent on them**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is formed through a reliable process, the proposition is true, and the belief is held**
