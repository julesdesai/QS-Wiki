# Knowledge as True Belief from Credible Authority

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge originating from testimony require one's intellectual virtue?](can-knowledge-originating-from-testimony-require-ones-intellectual-virtue-antithesis-9fd84217-4e84-4643-9478-e78ad6129922.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed through trusting a credible authority without requiring intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is formed through trusting a credible authority**
