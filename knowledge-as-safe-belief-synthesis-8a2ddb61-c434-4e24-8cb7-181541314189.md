# Knowledge as Safe Belief

**Parent:** [Can knowledge arise from subconscious processes that do not involve intellectual virtues?](can-knowledge-arise-from-subconscious-processes-that-do-not-involve-intellectual-virtues-antithesis-a80b4c26-e89c-40f1-8639-9c26c75e6baf.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is safe from error**, **A belief is safe from error if, in nearby possible situations, the belief would only be held if it were true**
