## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from the exercise of pragmatic intellectual virtues**, **Pragmatic intellectual virtues are character traits that contribute to achieving practical goals and truth**
