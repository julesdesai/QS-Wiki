# Virtue epistemology with anti-chance safeguards

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief results from an intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that chance does not play a significant role in making the belief true**
