## Content

**Knowledge is true belief arising from the exercise of intellectual ability**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief arises from the exercise of intellectual ability**, **A person knows a proposition if they believe it, the proposition is true, and their belief arises from the exercise of intellectual ability**
