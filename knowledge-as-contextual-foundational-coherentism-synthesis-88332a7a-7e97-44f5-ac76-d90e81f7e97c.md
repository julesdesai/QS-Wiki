# Knowledge as Contextual Foundational Coherentism

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres with a system of interconnected beliefs within a context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is at least one foundational empirical belief supporting the coherence within the context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief coheres with a system of interconnected beliefs within a context, and there is at least one foundational empirical belief supporting the coherence within the context**
