## Content

**Knowledge requires that the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **A belief counts as knowledge if the process leading to the belief has a high probability of producing true beliefs**, **The reliability of the cognitive process is assessed independently of the concept of knowledge**, **The belief must be true for it to count as knowledge**
