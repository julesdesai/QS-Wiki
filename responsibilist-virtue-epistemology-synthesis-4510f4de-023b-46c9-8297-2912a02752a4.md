# Responsibilist Virtue Epistemology

**Parent:** [Does lucky epistemic success qualify as knowledge?](does-lucky-epistemic-success-qualify-as-knowledge-antithesis-7f5023ad-db1f-4821-b4d0-17e8e9452470.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed through the exercise of intellectual character virtues**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is formed through the exercise of intellectual character virtues**
