# Can someone be virtuous yet still ignorant?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e1ad6333-5341-41ce-84a6-565a7fdf3f41
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-cfcf3dee-48b2-4ff8-855e-aba5061f4a51.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a person exercises intellectual virtues like open-mindedness and thorough examination, but they live in a community that systematically distorts information. Intuitively, we either have to say that they do not know any truths about the external world, or that their exercise of virtue is ineffective in acquiring knowledge.**, **A person can exercise intellectual virtues and still remain ignorant due to external misinformation beyond their control.**
