# Can coherentism account for radically different belief systems being justified?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3cce3402-e445-45b5-be8e-013a71d62973
**Depth:** 2
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism](coherentism-thesis-368b08e4-1a7c-4e5d-96a8-85ade01bb443.md)

## Content

**Two individuals could hold entirely different belief systems that are each coherent internally but contradict each other fundamentally.**, **Intuitively, both systems cannot be equally justified if they lead to opposite conclusions about the same proposition.**
