# Knowledge with Causal Connection

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 16c44eb3-54e3-4382-9de6-c82de4f32dbf
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is tracking truth necessary for knowledge?](is-tracking-truth-necessary-for-knowledge-antithesis-29ee8705-f7cd-4e14-a87d-98e3c5047c7a.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there be an appropriate causal connection between the fact that makes the proposition true and the person's belief in the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there is an appropriate causal connection to the belief**
