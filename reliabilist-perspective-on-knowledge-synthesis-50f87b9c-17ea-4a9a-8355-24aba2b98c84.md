## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, their belief must be a result of a reliable process**, **A process is reliable if it tends to produce true beliefs over false beliefs**, **For a belief to be knowledge, it is not necessary to trace the specific causal chain**, **A person knows a proposition if they believe the proposition is true and it is produced by a reliable process**
