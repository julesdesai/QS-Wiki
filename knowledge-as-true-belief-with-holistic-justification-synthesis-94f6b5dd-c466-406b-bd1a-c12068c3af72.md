## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is justified through the coherence of the entire set of beliefs**, **Holistic justification requires agreement between the belief and the network of all other beliefs**
