# Knowledge as Reliable Indicator

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 123c09e2-0385-4018-b3db-a325fab5d359
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is belief itself necessary for knowledge?](is-belief-itself-necessary-for-knowledge-antithesis-2584e6cd-c474-4f03-a979-97b28083a2f4.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in nearly all nearby possible worlds where the person is in a similar situation, the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have justification for believing the proposition, the proposition is true, and in nearly all nearby possible worlds where they are in a similar situation, the proposition is true**
