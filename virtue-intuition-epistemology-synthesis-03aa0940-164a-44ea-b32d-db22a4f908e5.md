## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their true belief is the result of an intellectual virtue or a rational intuition**, **A rational intuition is distinct from a gut feeling and involves a reflective understanding**
