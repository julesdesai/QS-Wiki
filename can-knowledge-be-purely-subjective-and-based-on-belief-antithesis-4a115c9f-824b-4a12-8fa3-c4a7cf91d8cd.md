# Can knowledge be purely subjective and based on belief?

**Node ID:** 4a115c9f-824b-4a12-8fa3-c4a7cf91d8cd

**Terminal:** No

## Content

**It is implausible that believing something automatically confers knowledge upon an individual.**, **A case in which a person believes they will win the lottery does not mean they know they will win just because it serves their personal utility.**
