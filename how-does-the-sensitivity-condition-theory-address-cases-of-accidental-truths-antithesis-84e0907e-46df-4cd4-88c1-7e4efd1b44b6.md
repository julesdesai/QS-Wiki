# How does the Sensitivity Condition Theory address cases of accidental truths?

**Terminal:** No

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person believes a true proposition, but their belief could easily have been false due to luck or coincidence.**, **Intuitively, a person does not seem to know a proposition if their belief is only accidentally true, even if it is sensitive.**, **A belief that turns out to be true by accident lacks the necessary underpinning for knowledge.**
