# Does possession of intellectual virtue guarantee knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2599c2b3-55b4-4e46-8e9b-981dd88e353f
**Depth:** 2
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-thesis-bdb11636-09c0-49d0-87da-f8f93d023ea2.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case where an individual possesses intellectual virtue but believes something false.**, **Intuitively, having intellectual virtue does not ensure that the individual knows the proposition if the belief is false.**
