# What about knowledge of one’s own mental states?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5bbc1455-2a00-46bd-aed7-d78acf595445
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Conceptual Empiricism](conceptual-empiricism-synthesis-7b58485d-3ea5-4513-ba7e-4d4d19b630bf.md)

## Content

**A person can know that they are in pain or happy without those concepts arising from perceptual experiences.**, **The concept of being in pain or being happy does not necessarily derive from perceptual experiences.**, **Knowledge of one’s own mental states does not always require an application of concepts generated by perceptual experiences.**
