# Knowledge as Externalist Justification

**Parent:** [Do intellectual virtues accommodate the evolving nature of scientific knowledge?](do-intellectual-virtues-accommodate-the-evolving-nature-of-scientific-knowledge-antithesis-3d06220e-f26a-4388-8813-f9e2039d5ebf.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their justification depends on factors external to their own mind**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, the proposition is true, and their justification depends on factors external to their own mind**
