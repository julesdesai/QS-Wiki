# Can knowledge be threatened by process reliability variability?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c4fbbbad-8b13-407f-b8de-359f1b1ae183
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Process Reliabilism](process-reliabilism-synthesis-963ad99c-e2e5-4a20-9800-229a3470b5c5.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where a process is generally reliable but sometimes yields flawed results due to occasional environmental influences. When true beliefs are formed by this unreliable variance, we might intuitively say these cannot constitute knowledge.**
