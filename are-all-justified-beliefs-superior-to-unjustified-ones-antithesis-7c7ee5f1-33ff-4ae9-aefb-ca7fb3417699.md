# Are all justified beliefs superior to unjustified ones?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7c7ee5f1-33ff-4ae9-aefb-ca7fb3417699
**Depth:** 2
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundationalism](foundationalism-thesis-fd868af7-578e-400a-bb27-265f88ced5ee.md)

## Content

**A person may hold a justified belief that turns out to be false, such as believing a commonly accepted scientific theory that later gets disproven.**, **It is plausible that sometimes an unjustified belief, coinciding with truth by chance, may offer correct knowledge.**
