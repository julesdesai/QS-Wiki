# Is contextualism circular in justifying its standards?

**Parent:** [Contextualist approach to knowledge](contextualist-approach-to-knowledge-synthesis-a36ecad8-b4cc-4b9e-8a50-84a5c5d3051f.md)

## Content

**Justifying justification standards through context seems to rely on those same standards.**, **The contextualist approach might end up in circular reasoning if it has to justify why a context is relevant using contextual standards.**, **The model risks relying on its own presuppositions without independent grounding for why certain contexts deserve specific standards.**
