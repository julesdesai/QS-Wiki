## Content

**For a person or animal to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by cognitive faculties naturally selected for their truth-conducive outcomes**, **For cognitive faculties to be truth-conducive, it is necessary that they perform successfully in the environment they evolved in**, **For a person or animal to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is true and their belief stems from naturally evolved truth-conducive cognitive faculties**
