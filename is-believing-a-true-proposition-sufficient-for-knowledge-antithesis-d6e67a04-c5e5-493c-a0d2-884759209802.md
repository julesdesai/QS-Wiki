# Is believing a true proposition sufficient for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d6e67a04-c5e5-493c-a0d2-884759209802
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatism: Practical Consequences](pragmatism-practical-consequences-synthesis-0f8107eb-2a4a-42d8-a5a9-2983c41a2844.md)

## Content

**A person might hold a belief that is true by coincidence without any supporting evidence.**, **It is implausible that merely believing in a true proposition without evidence or justification constitutes knowledge.**
