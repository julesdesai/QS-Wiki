## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for their belief to be such that the probability of it easily being false is minimized significantly**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the likelihood of the belief being false is elevated to a very high level**
