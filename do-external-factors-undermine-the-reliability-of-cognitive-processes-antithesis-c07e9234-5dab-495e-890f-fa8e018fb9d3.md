# Do external factors undermine the reliability of cognitive processes?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c07e9234-5dab-495e-890f-fa8e018fb9d3
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilist Theory of Knowledge](reliabilist-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-fec1f444-460d-4835-9da9-5fd5405f3ea1.md)

## Content

**Suppose an individual forms a true belief based on a cognitive process that tends to produce true beliefs in normal conditions. If the surroundings unexpectedly and temporarily change, making the process unreliable, intuitively we would judge that the individual lacks knowledge while the process is unreliable.**, **The reliable operation of cognitive processes might depend on external conditions that are not always in control of the believer.**
