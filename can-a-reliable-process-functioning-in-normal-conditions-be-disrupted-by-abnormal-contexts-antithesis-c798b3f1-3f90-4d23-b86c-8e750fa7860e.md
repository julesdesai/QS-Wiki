# Can a reliable process functioning in normal conditions be disrupted by abnormal contexts?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c798b3f1-3f90-4d23-b86c-8e750fa7860e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalist View](externalist-view-synthesis-75028f40-4ef7-4feb-a21d-1e6a75d787a6.md)

## Content

**A reliable process under normal conditions might not function the same in abnormal or manipulated contexts. Intuitively, this suggests that knowledge can't rely solely on process reliability absent context consideration.**, **Abnormal contexts can undermine otherwise reliable processes, making intuitive claims about knowledge uncertain.**
