# Can differing frameworks lead to truth relativism?

**Node Type:** antithesis

**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Epistemic Relativism with Justification Relative to Frameworks](epistemic-relativism-with-justification-relative-to-frameworks-synthesis-ad42e55b-b997-4355-ba09-4958e4df6c61.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where person A believes the earth is flat due to their epistemic framework, while person B believes in a spherical earth based on scientific evidence. Intuitively, we would judge that person B's belief corresponds more closely to the truth.**, **There is a plausible principle that truth is not relative and should be a constant across frameworks. This principle together with changing frameworks suggests that truth relativism undermines the universality of truth.**
