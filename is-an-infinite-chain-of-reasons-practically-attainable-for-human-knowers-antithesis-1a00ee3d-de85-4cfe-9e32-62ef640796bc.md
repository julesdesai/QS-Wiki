# Is an infinite chain of reasons practically attainable for human knowers?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1a00ee3d-de85-4cfe-9e32-62ef640796bc
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitist Epistemology](infinitist-epistemology-synthesis-6da6e9b6-0ddd-4346-9ef8-d27afad7386e.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that human cognitive capacities can handle an infinite chain of reasons.**, **Consider a scenario in which someone attempts to provide an infinite series of supporting reasons for their belief in everyday life. Intuitively, we would judge that such a task is impossible to complete within our finite lifespan.**
