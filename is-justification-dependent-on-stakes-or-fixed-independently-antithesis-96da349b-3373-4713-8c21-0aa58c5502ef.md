# Is justification dependent on stakes or fixed independently?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 96da349b-3373-4713-8c21-0aa58c5502ef
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Subject-Sensitive Invariantism Summary](subject-sensitive-invariantism-summary-synthesis-519a57d4-27a1-46ac-ac38-c130e30ad088.md)

## Content

**A plausible principle is that justification should be determined by evidence and belief quality alone, independent of practical stakes.**, **Suppose we have a case in which a person P has a justified true belief with low stakes, and another person Q with the same evidence has high stakes. Intuitively, both P and Q should have the same level of justification for their belief.**
