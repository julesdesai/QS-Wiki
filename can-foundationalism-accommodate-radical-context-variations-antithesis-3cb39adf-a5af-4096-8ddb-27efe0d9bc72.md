# Can foundationalism accommodate radical context variations?

**Node ID:** 3cb39adf-a5af-4096-8ddb-27efe0d9bc72
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualist Foundationalism](contextualist-foundationalism-synthesis-b757e054-5c50-4073-a4ee-c1ee757cbdbb.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person believes a friend is trustworthy because they were once reliable. In a new context where the friend acts deceitfully, foundational beliefs must adjust significantly.**, **If foundational beliefs can vary with different contexts and evidence, consistency of knowledge claims becomes questionable.**, **A case in which context changes too drastically can make foundationalist claims seem arbitrary or contradictory.**
