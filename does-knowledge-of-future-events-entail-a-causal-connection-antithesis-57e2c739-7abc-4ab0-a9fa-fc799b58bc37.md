# Does knowledge of future events entail a causal connection?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 57e2c739-7abc-4ab0-a9fa-fc799b58bc37
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Causal Theory of Knowledge](causal-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-9ece6694-b5cf-4143-897d-321cf0165994.md)

## Content

**Consider person P confidently predicts it will snow tomorrow based on evidence today, yet the truth of tomorrow's snow is not causally connected to P's current belief.**, **Knowledge of future events anticipated from evidence today lacks a causal path from future truth to present belief.**
