# Knowledge as Tracking the Truth

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** a1575fac-f0df-4f36-92ec-6352a5e8bb55
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge exist without belief?](can-knowledge-exist-without-belief-antithesis-ea8db8e1-997e-4909-9a9c-210bf3726279.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition is true, the person believes it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is true, if the proposition were false, they would not believe it, and if it is true, they believe it**
