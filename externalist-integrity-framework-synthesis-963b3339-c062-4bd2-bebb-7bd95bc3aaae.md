## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is produced by a reliable process with safeguarding mechanisms external to the person’s belief system**, **Belief formation processes are relevant but do not require direct awareness by the individual**, **Safeguarding mechanisms are necessary to ensure self-deception does not affect reliability**
