## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, their belief must be supported by evidence that is considered high-quality within the specific context**, **A person’s belief must be true for them to have knowledge**, **The context determines what counts as high-quality evidence and thus justification for belief**, **Evidence must be comprehensive, reliable, and contextually appropriate to ensure the truth of belief**
