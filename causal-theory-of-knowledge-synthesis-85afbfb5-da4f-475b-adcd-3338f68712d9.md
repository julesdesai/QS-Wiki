## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person believes the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is causally connected to the facts in the right way**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is both true and causally connected to the facts**
