# Is it possible for context to demand impractical standards for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3e80cad2-ac73-4587-8020-65128de480bd
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Moderate Contextualism](moderate-contextualism-synthesis-a2419722-fa3b-4f65-af9b-d94e8c32837e.md)

## Content

**Imagine a context in which a person is expected to meet impossibly high evidential standards to claim knowledge of a simple fact. Intuitively, this context seems unreasonable and suggests that not all context-related standards are appropriate for assessing knowledge.**
