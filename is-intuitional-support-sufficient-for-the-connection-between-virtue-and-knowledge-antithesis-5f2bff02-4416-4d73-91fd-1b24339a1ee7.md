# Is intuitional support sufficient for the connection between virtue and knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5f2bff02-4416-4d73-91fd-1b24339a1ee7
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology with Defeasibility](virtue-epistemology-with-defeasibility-synthesis-802a6edd-3a94-41b9-8765-bb5c975aae31.md)

## Content

**Consider a belief formed via intellectual virtue that isn't supported by intuitional or empirical evidence. This belief, despite being virtuous, lacks formal justification as knowledge.**, **Intuition alone is insufficient to assert a causal connection between intellectual virtue and knowledge without empirical support.**
