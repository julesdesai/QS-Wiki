# Does the justification of a belief require a reliable process in all cases?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c5d42094-9310-439f-88ae-30fca5dc3a3d

**Parent:** [Knowledge as Reliable Pragmatism](knowledge-as-reliable-pragmatism-synthesis-601e2ec8-4358-4137-9d1e-243ee7e2906f.md)

## Content

**Consider an individual who forms a true belief through divine inspiration with no identifiable reliable process. Intuitively, such a belief could still be considered knowledge, suggesting reliability may not always be necessary for justification.**
