# Does pragmatic encroachment lead to an untenable relativity in knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 984d8d66-7024-446b-8886-0c43a6c8d9c5
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Encroachment](pragmatic-encroachment-synthesis-829538ce-ebfd-41f1-81ea-e2a3bdbbc235.md)

## Content

**Imagine two equally situated individuals with the same evidence and belief, but differing in their stakes due to personal priorities or values. It is contradictory for one individual's knowledge status to change merely because of personal stakes, as this introduces an unwanted relativism to the concept of knowledge.**
