## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there be no defeaters for their belief**, **A defeater is information that would undermine the justification for the belief were it to be obtained**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and no defeaters for the belief exist**
