# Does the tracking theory handle inferential knowledge correctly?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c4d03c85-cd95-4b89-a71e-c48acda7b342
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Tracking Theory of Knowledge](tracking-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-6d302593-f2de-4e10-8195-85a314e81400.md)

## Content

**Consider someone inferring a true proposition based on other known propositions which they accidentally correctly believe.**, **In this situation, the inferential knowledge does not necessarily track the truth across situations because it relies on the accidental correctness of the premise beliefs.**, **This questions whether truth-tracking adequately explains inferential knowledge.**
