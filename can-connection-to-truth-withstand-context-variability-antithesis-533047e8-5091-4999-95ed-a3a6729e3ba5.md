# Can connection to truth withstand context variability?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 533047e8-5091-4999-95ed-a3a6729e3ba5
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextual Justificationism](contextual-justificationism-synthesis-1b60e9a8-d801-480b-8adb-e726a7af0440.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that truth is absolute and cannot vary with context, posing a challenge for a theory that embeds truth in context-sensitive justification.**, **If truth remains constant, then knowledge claims based on varying contexts become suspect, as they could contradict an absolute understanding of truth.**
