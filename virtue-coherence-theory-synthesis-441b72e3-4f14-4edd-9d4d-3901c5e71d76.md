## Content

**Knowledge is true belief arising from a coherent set of intellectual virtues**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **Coherence of virtues implies they support and do not contradict each other**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe it, the proposition is true, and their coherent set of intellectual virtues leads to the belief**
