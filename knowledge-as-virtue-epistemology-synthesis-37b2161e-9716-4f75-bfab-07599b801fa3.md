# Knowledge as Virtue Epistemology

**Parent:** [What happens if foundational beliefs are inconsistent with each other?](what-happens-if-foundational-beliefs-are-inconsistent-with-each-other-antithesis-c8b176ad-8528-4b30-bae3-570a1102466f.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from an intellectual virtue or ability**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief arises from intellectual virtue or ability**
