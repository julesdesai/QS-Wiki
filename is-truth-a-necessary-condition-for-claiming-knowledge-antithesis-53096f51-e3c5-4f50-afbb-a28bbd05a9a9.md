# Is truth a necessary condition for claiming knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 53096f51-e3c5-4f50-afbb-a28bbd05a9a9
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Infinitism](pragmatic-infinitism-synthesis-f73c0505-1eaa-4768-8143-11a2f8c9ca92.md)

## Content

**Suppose someone claims to know that a specific historical event happened, but it is later discovered to be false.**, **This introduces cases where belief and justification were present, but truth was absent, challenging the necessity of truth for knowledge.**, **Truth as a necessary condition fails to account for justified beliefs that are retrospectively perceived as knowledge.**, **If truth were necessary, all justified false beliefs would automatically exclude knowledge claims, which conflicts with how knowledge claims operate in life.**
