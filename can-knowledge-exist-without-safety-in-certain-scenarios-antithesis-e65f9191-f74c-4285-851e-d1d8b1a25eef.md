# Can knowledge exist without safety in certain scenarios?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e65f9191-f74c-4285-851e-d1d8b1a25eef
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Safety Theory of Knowledge](safety-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-a0cd1fa5-c1bb-4980-9053-18fe84acbd7d.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person believes a true proposition due to random chance. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that they do not know the proposition despite its truth.**, **It is implausible that a belief gained purely by chance should be considered safe.**
