**For knowledge, the belief must be formed through a reliable process**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief is produced by a process that reliably leads to truth**, **The reliability of the process is more important than the specific evidence available**, **A belief formed through a reliable process constitutes knowledge even if external factors are present**
