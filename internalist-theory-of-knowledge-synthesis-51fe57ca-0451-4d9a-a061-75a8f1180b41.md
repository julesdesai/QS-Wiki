## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, they must believe the proposition**, **A person must have internal access to the justification of their belief for it to count as knowledge**, **A person’s belief must be true for it to qualify as knowledge**, **For knowledge, awareness of the reliability of the justification is necessary**
