## Content

**Knowledge depends on the context of the knower**, **A person knows a proposition if they believe it and have justification based on evidence for believing it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **There exist fixed epistemic thresholds that ensure consistency irrespective of context variation**
