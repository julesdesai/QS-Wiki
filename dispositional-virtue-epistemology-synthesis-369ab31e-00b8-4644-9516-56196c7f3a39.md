## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their true belief is produced by a competence habit**, **A competence habit is an ingrained ability that consistently produces true beliefs even if it is not strictly an intellectual virtue**
