# Can knowledge exist without truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d1985c62-cffe-44c9-9ed4-788c1a2de814
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualist Theory of Knowledge](contextualist-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-4769a94f-2567-4d65-9377-c3f1aea872f2.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that knowledge could exist without a proposition being true.**, **There is a plausible principle that truth is an essential component of knowledge. This principle together with the contextualist view entails that contexts can change a necessary feature of knowledge.**
