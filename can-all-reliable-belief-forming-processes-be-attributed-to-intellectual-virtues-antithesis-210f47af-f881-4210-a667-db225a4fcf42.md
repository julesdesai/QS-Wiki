# Can all reliable belief-forming processes be attributed to intellectual virtues?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 210f47af-f881-4210-a667-db225a4fcf42
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology Summary](virtue-epistemology-summary-synthesis-460b628b-7c65-45e3-9b14-3e9060c40e11.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that all reliable belief-forming processes are due to intellectual virtues, as some processes, like natural instincts or intuitive reactions, might lead to true beliefs without virtues.**, **Consider a scenario in which someone instinctively reacts to a dangerous situation correctly without conscious reasoning or consideration of virtues. Intuitively, the reliability seems independent of intellectual virtues.**
