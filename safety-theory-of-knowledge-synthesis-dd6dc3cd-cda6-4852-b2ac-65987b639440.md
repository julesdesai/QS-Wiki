## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person's belief could not easily have been false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person's belief is held true in nearby possible scenarios**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief is true and is safely held true**
