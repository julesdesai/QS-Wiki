# Does the theory adequately address counterfactuals?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 041a6a5c-df15-4b98-8813-e6763d3bd647
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Nozick's Truth-Tracking Theory](nozicks-truth-tracking-theory-synthesis-1a215329-c53d-4dbe-9509-6b517d1bb8ad.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where a person, owing to their cognitive limitations, cannot access sufficient information regarding possible worlds where their belief would be false. We might still feel they possess knowledge if their belief is consistently correct in their actual world.**, **It is implausible that knowledge could require sensitivity to all counterfactual scenarios, especially when improbable.**
