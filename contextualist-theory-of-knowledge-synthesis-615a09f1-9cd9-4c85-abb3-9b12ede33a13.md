## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, the standards for knowledge may vary with context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they meet the contextually appropriate standards for belief, justification, and truth**
