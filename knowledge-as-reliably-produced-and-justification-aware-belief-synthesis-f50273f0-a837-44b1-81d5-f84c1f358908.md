# Knowledge as Reliably Produced and Justification-Aware Belief

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** f50273f0-a837-44b1-81d5-f84c1f358908
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does knowledge require conscious awareness of reliability?](does-knowledge-require-conscious-awareness-of-reliability-antithesis-498c3262-41ff-40ba-9e1c-a678d02828a5.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from a reliable process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have an awareness of the justification for their belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief arises from a reliable process, and they are aware of the justification for their belief**
