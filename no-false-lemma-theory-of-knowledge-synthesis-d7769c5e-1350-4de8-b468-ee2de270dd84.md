# No-False-Lemma Theory of Knowledge

**Parent:** [Do defeaters only concern truth or also other aspects of belief?](do-defeaters-only-concern-truth-or-also-other-aspects-of-belief-antithesis-e5501aaf-7829-4c9c-a750-7220de67c746.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their justification for the belief does not depend on any false lemmas**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their justification does not depend on any false lemmas**
