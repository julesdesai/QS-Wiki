# Is it necessary that a knower understand how their belief is formed to truly know?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 03b21bee-778b-43d6-8dc3-ed26d61f34f8
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Moderate Externalism Summary](moderate-externalism-summary-synthesis-b4668bae-162e-4191-89bc-89b702818630.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that for knowledge, one must grasp how a belief is formed. This is undermined by the view that a person can know without understanding the formation of their belief.**
