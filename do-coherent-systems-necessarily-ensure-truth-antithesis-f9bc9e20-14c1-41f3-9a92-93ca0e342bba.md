# Do coherent systems necessarily ensure truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f9bc9e20-14c1-41f3-9a92-93ca0e342bba
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalist Coherentism](externalist-coherentism-synthesis-f4ef4378-ebfc-4a41-8845-189c2c8e0f20.md)

## Content

**A logically coherent system of beliefs may still contain false propositions.**, **Coherence does not entail correspondence with reality, as two opposing systems may each be coherent internally but contradict externally verifiable truths.**, **The presence of coherence within a system doesn't inherently filter out all error.**
