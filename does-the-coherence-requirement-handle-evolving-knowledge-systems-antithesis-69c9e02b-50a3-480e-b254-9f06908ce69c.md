# Does the coherence requirement handle evolving knowledge systems?

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a scientist revises her belief system due to new evidence, causing temporary incoherence. Intuitively, her knowledge doesn't disappear during this transition period.**, **The assumption that coherence is needed for knowledge struggles with cases where belief systems naturally undergo change and temporarily lack coherence.**
