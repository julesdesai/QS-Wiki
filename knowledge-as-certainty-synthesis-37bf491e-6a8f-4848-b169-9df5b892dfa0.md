# Knowledge as Certainty

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person's belief is based on evidence that guarantees the truth of the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the true proposition based on evidence that guarantees the truth of the proposition**
