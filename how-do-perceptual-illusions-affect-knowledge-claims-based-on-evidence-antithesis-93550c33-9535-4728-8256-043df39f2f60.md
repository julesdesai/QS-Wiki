# How do perceptual illusions affect knowledge claims based on evidence?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 93550c33-9535-4728-8256-043df39f2f60
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Evidentialist Theory of Knowledge](evidentialist-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-1034ea09-f6fc-4c03-a110-072015f4c221.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a person's belief is formed through perception affected by an illusion.**, **Intuitively, we would say they lack knowledge if their perception is deceptive, despite having what seems like evidence.**, **This indicates that evidence subject to distortion or error challenges its sufficiency for knowledge.**
