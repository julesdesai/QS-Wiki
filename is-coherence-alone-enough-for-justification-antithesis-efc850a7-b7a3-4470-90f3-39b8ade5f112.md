# Is coherence alone enough for justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** efc850a7-b7a3-4470-90f3-39b8ade5f112
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitist Coherentism](infinitist-coherentism-synthesis-c961c647-ce7e-4676-a333-4ee929ef8887.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where an individual's beliefs are coherent based on paranoid delusions. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that the beliefs are not justified as they are not based on reality.**, **There is a plausible principle that beliefs should be based on some form of empirical or evidential support, which pure coherence lacks.**
