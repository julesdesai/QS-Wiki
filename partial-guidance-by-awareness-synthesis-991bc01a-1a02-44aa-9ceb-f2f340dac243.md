# Partial guidance by awareness

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can expertise lead to knowledge without conscious justification?](can-expertise-lead-to-knowledge-without-conscious-justification-antithesis-e9acb82b-9c50-486c-9587-e3ea3453fe9a.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief is guided by some form of awareness, which need not be complete or conscious**, **For expertise-based knowledge, automatic cognitive processes suffused with past awareness can suffice**
