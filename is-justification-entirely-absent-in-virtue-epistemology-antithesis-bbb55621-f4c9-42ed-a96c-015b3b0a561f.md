# Is justification entirely absent in virtue epistemology?

**Node ID:** bbb55621-f4c9-42ed-a96c-015b3b0a561f

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-0303bf31-8e93-428e-bc89-30c6b91ae71e.md)

## Content

**A plausible principle is that knowledge requires both truth and justification in addition to belief.**, **Intellectual virtues involve traits like courage and attentiveness but do not directly involve justification for holding a belief.**, **A case where a person holds a true belief through intellectual courage but without justification challenges its recognition as knowledge.**
