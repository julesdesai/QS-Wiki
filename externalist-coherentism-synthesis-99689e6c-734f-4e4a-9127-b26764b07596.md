# Externalist Coherentism

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification involves a coherent set of beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the justification to include information about the external world influencing the beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, there exists a coherent set of beliefs justifying the belief, and the justification is externally informed**
