# Is knowledge dependent on practical considerations and beneficial outcomes?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 20d3d4f4-a5dd-433c-a94d-aceb4f3cef0a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Theory of Knowledge](pragmatic-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-c88c0bac-fed4-4271-b530-0511020766f8.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person believes a false proposition that proves beneficial when acted upon. Intuitively, we would judge that the person does not have knowledge of the proposition.**, **There is a plausible principle that knowledge requires truth. This principle together with the belief being false entails that there is no knowledge.**
