# Is truth a sufficient condition for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 73a5cbd9-45e2-4917-96a9-314441e02e41
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundationalism with Pragmatic Grounds](foundationalism-with-pragmatic-grounds-synthesis-35d5e861-b788-4e52-a02b-4488aba3b8d2.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person holds a true belief based on pure guesswork. In such a scenario, intuitively, we would judge that the person does not possess knowledge.**, **A plausible principle states that knowledge requires more than mere accidental truth. This principle together with forming a true belief by chance indicates insufficiency of truth alone for knowledge.**
