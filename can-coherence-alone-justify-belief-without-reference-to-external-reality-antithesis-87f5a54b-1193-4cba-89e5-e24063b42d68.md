# Can coherence alone justify belief without reference to external reality?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 87f5a54b-1193-4cba-89e5-e24063b42d68
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextual Coherentism with Evidential Relevance](contextual-coherentism-with-evidential-relevance-synthesis-fae3d112-80bb-4914-96ee-f5c765695147.md)

## Content

**A belief can be coherent with a system of beliefs in which all beliefs are false.**, **If a coherent system of beliefs can consist entirely of false beliefs, coherence alone cannot justify a belief as knowledge.**
