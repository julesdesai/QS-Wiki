## Content

**A person knows a proposition if their belief is causally connected to the fact that the proposition is true**, **For a causal connection to exist, the truth of the proposition must bring about the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**
