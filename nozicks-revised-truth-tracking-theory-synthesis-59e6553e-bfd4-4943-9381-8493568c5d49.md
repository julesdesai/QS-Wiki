## Content

**A person knows a proposition if they believe it and the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, if the proposition were false and it could be false, they would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, if the proposition were true and it could be otherwise, they would believe it**, **Knowledge requires belief that tracks the truth across relevant possible worlds where the truth is conventionally contingent**
