## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief to be produced by an intellectual virtue**, **It is necessary for the intellectual virtue to be confirmed by contextually relevant information**
