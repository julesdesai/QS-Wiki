# Infallibilist Knowledge Criterion

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** f023368d-e693-4607-95a1-95304dca2448
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Could misleading but unrelevant alternatives undermine knowledge?](could-misleading-but-unrelevant-alternatives-undermine-knowledge-antithesis-6d0969b4-e84f-47ff-935c-8a219d3d7bf7.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that no possible evidence undermines the proposition's truth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and no possible evidence undermines the proposition's truth**
