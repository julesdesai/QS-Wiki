# Can one have knowledge without cognitive ability?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c98cd188-766f-411a-a058-1ed96ba2f36c

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-b6aa4282-ad09-4174-b191-6cfd3fcbdf83.md)

## Content
