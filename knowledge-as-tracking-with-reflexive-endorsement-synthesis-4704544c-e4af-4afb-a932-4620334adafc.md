## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief tracks the truth across close possible worlds**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they can endorse the reliability of the tracking process reflexively**
