## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification for the belief is accessible to the person**, **Justification must rely on reasons or evidence that are available upon reflection**
