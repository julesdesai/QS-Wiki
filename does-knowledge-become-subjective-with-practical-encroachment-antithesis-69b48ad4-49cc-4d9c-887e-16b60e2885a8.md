# Does knowledge become subjective with practical encroachment?

## Content

**Imagine two historians both independently verifying a historic date with the same sources. For one, confirming it is inconsequential, but for the other, it prevents a career-damaging publication.**, **If higher stakes alter what counts as known, then knowledge risks becoming subjective, conflicting with its typical understanding as objective.**
