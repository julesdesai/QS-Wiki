## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a process that enhances its reliability through additional verification**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is produced by an enhancement-driven reliable process**
