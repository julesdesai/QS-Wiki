# Knowledge as True Belief Plus Justification and Reliability

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Can intellectual virtue lead to false beliefs?](can-intellectual-virtue-lead-to-false-beliefs-antithesis-c1624732-2c8d-4c2b-b1cb-c346581e3e65.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified and arises from a reliable process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is justified and arises from a reliable process**
