# Does an infinite series of reasons obviate the need for foundational beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e3c86939-0094-4d66-be4f-7664659f8b81
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism Summary](infinitism-summary-synthesis-17c5b8c2-c7de-43ec-bf90-6dedae4ad235.md)

## Content

**The idea of foundational beliefs is that they provide a stopping point in the chain of justification.**, **Without a foundational belief, it seems that each belief demands further justification ad infinitum.**, **Intuitively, it seems that an infinite regress without foundational beliefs fails to offer the stability sought in epistemological justification.**
