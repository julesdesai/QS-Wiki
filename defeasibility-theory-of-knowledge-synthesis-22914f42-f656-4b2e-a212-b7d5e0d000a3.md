# Defeasibility Theory of Knowledge

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 22914f42-f656-4b2e-a212-b7d5e0d000a3
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge arise from coincidental truths?](can-knowledge-arise-from-coincidental-truths-antithesis-4661736a-1cd2-4cf8-b044-9b2d22128b89.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that no defeater exists which, if known, would undermine the justification**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and no defeater exists**
