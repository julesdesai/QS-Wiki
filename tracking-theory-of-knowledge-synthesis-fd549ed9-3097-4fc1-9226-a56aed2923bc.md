# Tracking Theory of Knowledge

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief tracks the truth across nearby possible worlds**, **A belief tracks the truth if in the closest worlds where the proposition is false, the person does not believe it, and in the closest worlds where the proposition is true, the person believes it**
