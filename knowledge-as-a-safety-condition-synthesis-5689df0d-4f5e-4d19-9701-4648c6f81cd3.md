## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that it could not easily be false given the method of belief formation**, **The safety condition is invariant across different contexts**
