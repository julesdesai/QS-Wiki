# Robust Defeasibility Theory

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no defeaters present, whether detectable or undetectable**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there are no defeaters present, whether detectable or undetectable**
