# Knowledge as Safe Belief

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Can knowledge be acquired without manifesting intellectual virtue?](can-knowledge-be-acquired-without-manifesting-intellectual-virtue-antithesis-fcd5c8bb-6dd7-47fd-a9b2-07de44d75611.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief could not have easily been false in similar circumstances**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief could not have easily been false in similar circumstances**
