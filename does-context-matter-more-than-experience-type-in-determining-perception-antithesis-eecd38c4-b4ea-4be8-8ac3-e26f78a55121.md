# Does context matter more than experience type in determining perception?

**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Disjunctivism](disjunctivism-synthesis-e62747dd-0f26-4379-9406-3d7f6fb883b9.md)

## Content

**Consider two scenarios: seeing a hologram mistaken for an object and seeing an object clearly in distorted light. Intuitively, context more than experience type explains whether perception connects to reality.**, **A plausible principle is that contextual cues often determine our understanding of perception, rather than the inherent nature of the perceptual experience itself. This questions the different nature of perception in veridical and hallucinatory experiences.**
