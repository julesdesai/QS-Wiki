## Content

**Knowledge is true belief that arises from intellectual virtue that balances the promotion of truth with the avoidance of error**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief arises from intellectual virtue that balances the promotion of truth with error avoidance**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe it, the proposition is true, and their intellectual virtue balances truth promotion with error avoidance**
