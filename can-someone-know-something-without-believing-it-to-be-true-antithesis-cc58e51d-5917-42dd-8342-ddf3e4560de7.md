# Can someone know something without believing it to be true?

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that knowledge requires belief, as one cannot know what they do not at least believe.**, **Consider a scenario in which an individual is presented with evidence but remains skeptical, lacking genuine belief in the truth of a proposition. Intuitively, without belief, we hesitate to say they know the proposition.**
