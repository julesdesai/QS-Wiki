# Does human cognitive limitation preclude infinite justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 4eb00a46-f146-4821-91fe-70c3d902bd46
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualist Infinitism](contextualist-infinitism-synthesis-74ebcf03-3de8-4e28-950c-dc2eefc9dc13.md)

## Content

**Humans have cognitive limitations that prevent managing infinite information.**, **If justification requires handling infinite reasons, then human capacity falls short.**, **It is implausible that knowledge demands which exceed human capability can be met.**
