# What if knowledge requires more than a reliable process?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d8a98c75-3862-4020-b59c-47738a9f03dd
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalism to knowledge with reliable cognitive process](externalism-to-knowledge-with-reliable-cognitive-process-synthesis-e78416a8-ed0b-42d6-b63b-0cc8fec7b97b.md)

## Content

**Consider a situation where a person's belief is formed by a reliable process, but they also rely on superstitions as part of their reasoning.**, **Despite the reliability, the involvement of irrational elements suggests we might refuse to classify the belief as knowledge.**, **This raises the question of whether further rational scrutiny is necessary beyond process reliability.**
