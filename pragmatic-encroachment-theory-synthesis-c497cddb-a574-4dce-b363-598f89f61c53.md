## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the practical interests of the person are compatible with the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, it is true, and their practical interests are compatible with the belief**
