## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from intellectual virtue**, **For a proposition to count as knowledge, it is necessary that the intellectual virtues be pragmatically applicable**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that intellectual virtues pragmatically justify the belief's truth**
