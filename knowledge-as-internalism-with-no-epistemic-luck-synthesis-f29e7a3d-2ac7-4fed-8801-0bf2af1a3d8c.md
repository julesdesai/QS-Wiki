## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is justified by internal factors**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their justified belief rules out epistemic luck due to internal reflection**
