# How does pragmatic encroachment handle epistemic luck?

**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Encroachment with Influence of Practical Stakes](pragmatic-encroachment-with-influence-of-practical-stakes-synthesis-1e83d295-b88f-4064-889e-097b4fb938ca.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person correctly guesses the winner of a horse race due to luck alone, without any supporting evidence, and has high practical stakes involved. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that the person does not know who the winner is despite the outcome.**, **Practical stakes influencing the threshold for knowledge could mistakenly attribute knowledge to cases involving epistemic luck, which undermines the connection between knowledge and justified belief.**
