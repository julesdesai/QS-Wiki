## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were not true, the person would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition is true, the person believes it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is true, and if the proposition were not true, the person would not believe it, and if the proposition is true, the person believes it**
