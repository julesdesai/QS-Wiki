## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person's belief is formed under appropriate contextual standards**, **Contextual standards vary depending on the particular epistemic situation of the individual**
