# Can causal connections be malformed yet still achieve true belief?

**Node ID:** f7514e3e-cab0-457d-acd0-0d19dc5c7981

**Terminal:** No

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which someone believes it is going to snow due to feeling cold, and it does snow, but the cold was due to malfunctioning air conditioning.**, **Intuitively, we would judge that the belief’s connection to truth was malformed, questioning its knowledge status.**
