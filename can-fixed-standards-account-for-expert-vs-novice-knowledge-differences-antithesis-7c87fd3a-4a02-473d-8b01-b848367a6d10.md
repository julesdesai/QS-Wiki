# Can fixed standards account for expert vs. novice knowledge differences?

## Content

**Experts and novices both believe true propositions, yet we intuitively ascribe knowledge more confidently to experts.**, **A principle suggests specialized expertise contributes to knowing a proposition beyond true belief.**, **If standards are invariant, everyone believing a true proposition equally knows it, which doesn't align with our intuitive distinctions.**
