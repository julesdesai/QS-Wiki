# Does the distinction between reliability of process and truth of belief raise issues for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9ff5ca20-cafa-4f98-9be6-ae472fadcde4
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology Summary](virtue-epistemology-summary-synthesis-ebb76c32-727a-4eba-8f42-21c2ee3c5622.md)

## Content

**A belief can be produced by a highly reliable process, yet be false due to atypical factors. This suggests that reliability of process and truth conditions together may not fully capture knowledge.**
