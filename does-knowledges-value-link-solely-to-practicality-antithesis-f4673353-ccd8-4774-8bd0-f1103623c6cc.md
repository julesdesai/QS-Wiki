# Does knowledge's value link solely to practicality?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f4673353-ccd8-4774-8bd0-f1103623c6cc
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Theory of Knowledge](pragmatic-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-af5a1410-77c2-4ceb-b390-2ced8876b488.md)

## Content

**Consider a literature scholar who understands a complex narrative structure in classical literature that holds intellectual value but no direct practical application. Intuitively, this understanding is valuable in and of itself.**, **The value of knowledge is multifaceted and not merely determined by its practical aspects; it can also include elements like aesthetic appreciation and intellectual stimulation.**
