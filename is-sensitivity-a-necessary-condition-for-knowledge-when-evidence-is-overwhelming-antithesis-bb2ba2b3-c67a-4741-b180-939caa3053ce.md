# Is sensitivity a necessary condition for knowledge when evidence is overwhelming?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** bb2ba2b3-c67a-4741-b180-939caa3053ce
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Process Reliabilism with Sensitivity](process-reliabilism-with-sensitivity-synthesis-3b7ba929-b462-4672-9048-c64e1338414e.md)

## Content

**Imagine a case where a person has seen overwhelming evidence for a scientific fact, like observing countless experiments consistently supporting the fact's truth. Intuitively, the person has knowledge of this fact.**, **If the fact were false, hypothetically due to some massive deception, the person would still believe it, challenging the need for sensitivity in regards to overwhelming evidence.**
