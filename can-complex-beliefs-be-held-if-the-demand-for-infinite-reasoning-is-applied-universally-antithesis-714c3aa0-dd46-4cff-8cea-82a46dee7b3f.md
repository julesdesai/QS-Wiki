# Can complex beliefs be held if the demand for infinite reasoning is applied universally?

**Node ID:** 714c3aa0-dd46-4cff-8cea-82a46dee7b3f

**Terminal:** No

## Content

**Complex beliefs, such as scientific theories, are typically grounded in finite reasoning, yet demanding infinite reasons for them makes their justification and acceptance implausible.**, **Applying the demand for infinite reasoning universally invalidates most complex beliefs that are currently justified through finite explanation.**
