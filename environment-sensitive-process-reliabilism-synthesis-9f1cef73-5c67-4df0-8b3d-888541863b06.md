# Environment-Sensitive Process Reliabilism

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is reliability contextual?](is-reliability-contextual-antithesis-7447168a-a4de-4bfc-93d1-1788f1ef7a3f.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a cognitive process reliably adapted to the specific environment**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is produced by a cognitive process reliably adapted to the specific environment**
