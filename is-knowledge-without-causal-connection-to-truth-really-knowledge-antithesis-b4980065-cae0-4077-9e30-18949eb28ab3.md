# Is knowledge without causal connection to truth really knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b4980065-cae0-4077-9e30-18949eb28ab3
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-b6aa4282-ad09-4174-b191-6cfd3fcbdf83.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person believes a true proposition without any causal link to the truth of that proposition**, **Intuitively, if a belief is true without being causally linked to the truth, it might seem disconnected and not solid knowledge**, **There is a plausible principle that knowing a proposition involves being causally connected to its truth**
