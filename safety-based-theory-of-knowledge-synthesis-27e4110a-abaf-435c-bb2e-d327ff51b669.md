## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief is safe**, **A belief is safe if in nearby possible worlds where the person believes the proposition, the proposition is not false**
