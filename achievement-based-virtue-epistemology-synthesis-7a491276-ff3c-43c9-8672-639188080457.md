## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is an epistemic achievement**, **Epistemic achievement requires that the belief is both true and attributable to the person's intellectual virtues**, **A true belief attributable to the exercise of intellectual virtues constitutes knowledge**, **A person knows a proposition if they believe it**
