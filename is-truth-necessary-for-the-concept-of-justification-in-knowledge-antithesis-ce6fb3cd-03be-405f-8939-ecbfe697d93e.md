# Is truth necessary for the concept of justification in knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ce6fb3cd-03be-405f-8939-ecbfe697d93e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Neo-Mooreanism](neo-mooreanism-synthesis-aeb63ebc-6515-4a29-8322-7a2c352d1501.md)

## Content

**It is possible for someone to be justified in believing a false proposition based on reliable evidence. Intuitively, the justification seems valid regardless of the truth of the proposition.**, **Justification can exist independently of the truth value of a belief, suggesting that justification does not inherently require truth.**
