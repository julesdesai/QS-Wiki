## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief safely tracks the truth**, **A belief safely tracks the truth if it could not easily have been false given the method of forming the belief**
