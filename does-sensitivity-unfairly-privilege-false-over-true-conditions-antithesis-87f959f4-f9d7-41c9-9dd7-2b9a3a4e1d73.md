## Content

**Imagine assessing if knowledge demands understanding in practical, truth-supporting environments, like a math teacher proven in students' concepts with consistent correct results. Intuitively, this demonstrates the teacher's knowledge.**, **Sensitivity suggests if under hypothetical false conditions, relying on such ideas could lead to consistent albeit wrong teaching due to biased tests undermines the teacher’s justified belief claim solely based on sensitivity.**
