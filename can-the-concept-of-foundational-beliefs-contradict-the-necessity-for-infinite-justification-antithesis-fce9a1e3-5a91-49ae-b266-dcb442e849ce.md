# Can the concept of foundational beliefs contradict the necessity for infinite justification?

**Node ID:** fce9a1e3-5a91-49ae-b266-dcb442e849ce

## Content

**A foundational belief is one that does not rely on further beliefs for its justification.**, **An infinite chain of reasons demands no terminus in justification.**, **A system that equally requires foundational beliefs and infinite justification could be seen as self-contradictory.**
