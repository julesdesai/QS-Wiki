# Can pragmatic concerns override truth in determining knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5df634af-2947-4830-ac3e-a28a0b0bbf38
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Encroachment Theory](pragmatic-encroachment-theory-synthesis-48f209b4-b71c-4b9b-814c-8c2a5af0c840.md)

## Content

**Imagine a scenario where a person S is convinced under anxiety about missing a flight, even though there is an accurate flight time displayed and confirmed. Intuitively, S does not know the flight time if decisions are made based on anxiety rather than truth.**, **There is a plausible principle that knowledge requires aligning one's belief with truth rather than with pragmatic concerns or emotional states.**, **It seems implausible that pragmatic concerns can override truth when determining whether a belief constitutes knowledge.**
