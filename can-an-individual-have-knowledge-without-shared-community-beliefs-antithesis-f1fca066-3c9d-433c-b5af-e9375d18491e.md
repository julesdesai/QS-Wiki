# Can an individual have knowledge without shared community beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f1fca066-3c9d-433c-b5af-e9375d18491e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism with Shared Justification](coherentism-with-shared-justification-synthesis-efbed405-50ec-4920-aeba-74abec1bb76e.md)

## Content

**A person may have a belief justified by their own experiences or reasoning independent of any community input.**, **If justification requires a coherent web of beliefs shared within a community, personal justification not shared by a community does not constitute knowledge.**
