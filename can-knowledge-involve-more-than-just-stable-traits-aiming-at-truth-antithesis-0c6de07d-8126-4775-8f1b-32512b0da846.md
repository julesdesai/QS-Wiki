# Can knowledge involve more than just stable traits aiming at truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0c6de07d-8126-4775-8f1b-32512b0da846
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-91237602-868c-4a49-88c0-4e168ec082b8.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person uses a reliable but entirely unconscious process to arrive at a true belief.**, **In such a scenario, intuitively we might judge that the person has knowledge, even if the process does not involve intellectual virtues.**
