# Does lucky true belief equate to knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 4ffbb375-fe2b-4757-bd09-cce0f453a9d9
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Refined Virtue Epistemology](refined-virtue-epistemology-synthesis-ed8e6ab8-d432-4955-9aec-982a4b148be7.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where someone's true belief arises from sheer luck instead of intellectual virtue. We would intuitively judge that this true belief does not amount to knowledge.**, **A plausible principle asserts that knowledge requires belief to be the result of a reliable method, aligning with the necessity of intellectual virtue.**
