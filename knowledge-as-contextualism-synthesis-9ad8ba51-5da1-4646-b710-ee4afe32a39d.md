## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **The standards for justification vary according to the context of the individual**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification meets the contextual standard**
