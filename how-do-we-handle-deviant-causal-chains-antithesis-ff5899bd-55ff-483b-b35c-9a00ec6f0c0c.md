# How do we handle deviant causal chains?

**Parent:** [Causal Theory of Knowledge](causal-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-f882c843-046b-4ab6-afcf-19932fafd538.md)

## Content

**Imagine a case in which a belief is formed through an unusual causal chain that strays from the typical path of justification, yet still leads to a true belief. Intuitively, this is not knowledge.**, **Consider a scenario where a rockslide causes both the leaving of the house and the belief about not being hit by a rock, but without any perceived connection. Intuitively, this does not seem like knowledge despite the causal link.**
