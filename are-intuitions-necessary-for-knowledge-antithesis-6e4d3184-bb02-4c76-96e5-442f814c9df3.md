# Are intuitions necessary for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 6e4d3184-bb02-4c76-96e5-442f814c9df3
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism](reliabilism-synthesis-27039b77-724d-4113-8b7b-b992ae688652.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person has strong intuitions about a proposition being true, but lacks any reliable method to justify it.**, **In such a case, we may judge intuitively that the person lacks knowledge due to the absence of a reliable cognitive process.**, **It is plausible that intuition alone is insufficient for establishing reliable cognitive processes.**
