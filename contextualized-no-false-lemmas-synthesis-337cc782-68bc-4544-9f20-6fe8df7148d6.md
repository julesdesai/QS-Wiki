## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that any false belief not affect the core inference of their belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and any false beliefs are irrelevant to the core inference**
