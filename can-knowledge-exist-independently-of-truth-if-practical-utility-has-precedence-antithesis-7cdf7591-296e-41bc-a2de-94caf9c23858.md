# Can knowledge exist independently of truth if practical utility has precedence?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7cdf7591-296e-41bc-a2de-94caf9c23858
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [True Belief as Primary in Knowledge](true-belief-as-primary-in-knowledge-synthesis-f83d0a87-6cde-4f50-a75a-0024fde50cce.md)

## Content

**The practical utility of a belief can sometimes be more crucial than its truth in decision-making scenarios.**, **In scenarios where a false belief leads to the best outcome, these beliefs might be seen as knowledge for practical purposes.**, **Conflating truth with primary importance in all situations disregards contexts where false beliefs still lead to successful results.**
