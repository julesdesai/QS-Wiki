## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is an appropriate causal connection between the person's belief and the fact that makes the proposition true**, **Causal connections provide a link between belief and the justification for the belief**
