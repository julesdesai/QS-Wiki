# Is it possible for truth-tracking to fail while knowledge persists?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 08699ebf-b3bc-418e-a3e6-b935569983be
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Truth-Tracking Theory](truth-tracking-theory-synthesis-3c1e7956-3dab-458a-877c-dcdbcd3fe2cd.md)

## Content

**Consider a situation where a person loses belief in a true proposition in nearby possible worlds due to slight environmental changes that don't pertain to the truth of the proposition itself. In this case, intuitively, one might judge that the person still knows the proposition in the actual world.**, **There is a plausible principle asserting that slight irrelevant variations in circumstances shouldn't necessarily preclude knowledge of a proposition.**
