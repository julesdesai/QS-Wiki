# What happens when reasons in the chain are irrelevant or misleading?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7e634bc6-595c-4e6d-8d7e-55ac34297efe
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitist Epistemology](infinitist-epistemology-synthesis-6da6e9b6-0ddd-4346-9ef8-d27afad7386e.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which someone has an infinite chain of reasons, but many of these reasons are irrelevant or misleading in context. Intuitively, we would judge this does not constitute knowledge.**, **It is plausible to state that for a chain of reasons to result in knowledge, the reasons must be relevant and contribute to the truth of the belief.**
