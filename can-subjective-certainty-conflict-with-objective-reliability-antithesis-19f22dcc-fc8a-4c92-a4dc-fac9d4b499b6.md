# Can subjective certainty conflict with objective reliability?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 19f22dcc-fc8a-4c92-a4dc-fac9d4b499b6
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilist View](reliabilist-view-synthesis-9b4935ff-e1a0-4d23-aeca-7dfa716ad7fb.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where an individual feels extremely confident in their belief due to a misleading but convincing fake news article. Intuitively, such confidence does not amount to knowledge, despite the person's belief being true by chance.**, **Confidence based on misleadingly acquired information contradicts the idea that a reliable process is sufficient for knowledge, highlighting the difference between subjective assurance and reliable belief production.**
