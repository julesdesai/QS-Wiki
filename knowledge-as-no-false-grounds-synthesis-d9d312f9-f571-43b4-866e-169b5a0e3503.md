## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the evidence or reasons for the belief do not include any falsehoods**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their evidence or reasons do not include any falsehoods**
