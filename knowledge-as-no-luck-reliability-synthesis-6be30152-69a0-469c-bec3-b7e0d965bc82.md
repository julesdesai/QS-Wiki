# Knowledge as No-Luck Reliability

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is a reliably produced true belief sufficient for knowledge?](is-a-reliably-produced-true-belief-sufficient-for-knowledge-antithesis-cd95196a-2226-468c-b188-dad174a60a90.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A belief qualifies as knowledge if it is produced by a reliable cognitive process and is not the result of luck**, **A reliable process consistently produces true beliefs over false ones**
