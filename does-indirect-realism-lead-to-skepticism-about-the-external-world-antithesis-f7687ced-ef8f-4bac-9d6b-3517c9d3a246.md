# Does indirect realism lead to skepticism about the external world?

## Content

**Skepticism about the external world is the doubt about whether we can know anything beyond our mental perceptions.**, **If sensory data mediates knowledge, there is a possibility that the external world might be entirely different from what is perceived, leading to philosophical skepticism.**
