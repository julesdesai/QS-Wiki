# Can knowledge arise from an intellectual virtue without being true belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 786649a0-c0bb-4422-94a5-685c99cc72d5
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-ffa55650-0795-4012-9f33-14517d86090e.md)

## Content

**A person can have a belief produced by intellectual virtue that happens to be false.**, **Knowledge is usually considered justified true belief, so a false belief cannot be knowledge.**, **An intellectual virtue being a reliable cognitive trait does not guarantee the infallibility of the belief it produces.**
