# Do safety conditions suffice for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 164fa220-35c6-48bf-962e-5742622ba52d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Safety Theory](safety-theory-synthesis-3968e05f-d744-409d-bfbb-59e5b6ab2a0b.md)

## Content

**There is a scenario in which a person holds a true belief that is safe because it is true in most nearby possible worlds.**, **Intuitively, we would judge that this belief does not constitute knowledge because it is formed on a lucky guess.**, **If forming a belief on a lucky guess does not constitute knowledge, then safety conditions are not sufficient for knowledge.**
