## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief be defeasibly warranted by appropriate epistemic context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief-forming process is dependable within its contextual framework**
