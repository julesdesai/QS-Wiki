## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed via a reliable process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is formed via a reliable process**
