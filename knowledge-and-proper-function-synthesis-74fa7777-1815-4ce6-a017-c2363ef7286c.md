# Knowledge and Proper Function

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is safety a sufficient condition for knowledge?](is-safety-a-sufficient-condition-for-knowledge-antithesis-c9dcb86f-cfc3-4373-838d-a9d395cfbb87.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a cognitive faculty functioning properly in a suitable environment**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is produced by a cognitive faculty functioning properly in a suitable environment**
