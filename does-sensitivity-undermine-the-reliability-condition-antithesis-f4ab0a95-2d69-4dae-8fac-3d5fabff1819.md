# Does sensitivity undermine the reliability condition?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f4ab0a95-2d69-4dae-8fac-3d5fabff1819
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Process Reliabilism with Sensitivity](process-reliabilism-with-sensitivity-synthesis-3b7ba929-b462-4672-9048-c64e1338414e.md)

## Content

**Consider a knowledge claim that meets both true belief and reliable process conditions easily, like an astronomer predicting an eclipse based on verified models. Intuitively, this is accepted as knowledge.**, **Suppose sensitivity requires the astronomer would not believe the eclipse would happen if their models were falsely predicting it, yet given their confidence in the models, they might, which challenges reliance purely on sensitivity.**
