# Knowledge from Reliable Sources

**Parent:** [Can beliefs founded on false premises still constitute knowledge if true?](can-beliefs-founded-on-false-premises-still-constitute-knowledge-if-true-antithesis-26f81ee6-42fa-4f1f-ac22-473728f8cbb3.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief originates from reliable sources or methods**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief comes from reliable sources or methods**
