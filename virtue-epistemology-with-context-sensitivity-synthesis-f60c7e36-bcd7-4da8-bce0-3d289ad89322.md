## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they possess intellectual virtues aligning with contextual demands**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their intellectual virtues meet context-sensitive standards**
