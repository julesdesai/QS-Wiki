## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **The sufficiency of knowing a proposition includes having practical stakes in the truth of the proposition that affect whether belief conditions are met**
