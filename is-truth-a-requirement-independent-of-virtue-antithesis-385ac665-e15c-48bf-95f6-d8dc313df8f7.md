# Is truth a requirement independent of virtue?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 385ac665-e15c-48bf-95f6-d8dc313df8f7
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-35af9163-7530-4447-9474-ccb688222939.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that truth is a requirement for knowledge, independent of the exercise of intellectual virtue. This principle suggests that someone might employ virtue but still end up with false beliefs.**, **The disconnection between virtue and the guarantee of truth questions whether knowledge should fundamentally hinge on the former.**
