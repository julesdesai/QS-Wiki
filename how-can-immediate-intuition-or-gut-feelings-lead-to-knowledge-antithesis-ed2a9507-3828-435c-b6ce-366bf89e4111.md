# How can immediate intuition or gut feelings lead to knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ed2a9507-3828-435c-b6ce-366bf89e4111
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-4f2fdbe9-b5ae-4003-9ce0-28573a7cd0c4.md)

## Content

**Immediate intuitions or gut feelings sometimes result in true beliefs, such as quickly judging someone's trustworthiness, without involving intellectual virtues**, **These intuitive judgments challenge the view that only beliefs produced by intellectual virtues can be considered knowledge**
