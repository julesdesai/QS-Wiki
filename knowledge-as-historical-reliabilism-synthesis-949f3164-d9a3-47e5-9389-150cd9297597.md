## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A belief qualifies as knowledge if it is produced by a cognitive process that was reliable at the time of belief formation**, **A cognitive process is reliable if it consistently produces true beliefs over false ones at the time of the belief's production**
