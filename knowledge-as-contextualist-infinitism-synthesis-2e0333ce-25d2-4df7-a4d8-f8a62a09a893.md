## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres with an infinite chain of justifying beliefs which is contextual to what counts as sufficient justification**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief coheres with a contextually infinite chain of beliefs**
