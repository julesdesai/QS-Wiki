## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is cognitively accessible to them**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is non-accidentally true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is cognitively accessible, non-accidentally true, and the proposition is true**
