# Does knowledge require reconciling subjective perceptions with an objective reality?

## Content

**Knowledge traditionally involves justified true belief, suggesting that one can know something without necessarily reconciling differing perspectives.**, **It is plausible to hold that subjective perceptions can be a source of knowledge in their own right, independent of objective reality.**, **In some cases, subjective perceptions may align directly with objective reality without requiring an active reconciliation process.**
