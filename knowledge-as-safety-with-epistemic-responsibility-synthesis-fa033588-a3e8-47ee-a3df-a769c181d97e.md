# Knowledge as Safety with Epistemic Responsibility

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** fa033588-a3e8-47ee-a3df-a769c181d97e
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does safe belief always lead to knowledge?](does-safe-belief-always-lead-to-knowledge-antithesis-7ac6b1ca-935e-4d27-bc7b-fb080d34f922.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief could not easily have been false and is formed with epistemic responsibility**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is safe, and the believer is epistemically responsible in forming the belief**
