## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have comprehensive justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their comprehensive justification is not reliant solely on communal consensus**
