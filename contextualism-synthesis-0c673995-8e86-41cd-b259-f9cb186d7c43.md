## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **The standards for knowing a proposition can vary with different contexts**, **In everyday contexts, less stringent standards might apply than in philosophical contexts**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe it, the proposition is true, and the standards for knowledge in the context are met**
