# Does foundationalism account for evolving knowledge bases?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** bce341bb-7f40-43b4-a2a7-5b162f2269ac
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundationalism with Basic Beliefs](foundationalism-with-basic-beliefs-synthesis-db3b35bf-6f35-4ce4-82b6-698bdde0b345.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that beliefs should update with new information. This suggests foundational beliefs may not remain basic over time.**, **Consider a situation where scientific advancements challenge previous basic beliefs. Intuitively, these updates imply foundational beliefs can be revisited.**, **It is implausible that foundational beliefs remain unchanged when confronted with strong, contrary evidence.**
