# How do infinite justifications maintain existential relevance over time and context?

## Content

**Suppose a belief is justified by indefinitely expanding reasons with minimal current relevance. Intuitively, reasons must be contextually relevant to provide knowledge value at a given point in time.**, **In situations where time or context shifts, infinite justifications may fail to maintain their epistemic worth, challenging temporal stability of the knowledge claimed.**
