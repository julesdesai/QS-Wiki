# Is it possible that justified true belief fails to account for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** da3ae94c-497e-47c4-b533-9a20c848358b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Access Internalism](access-internalism-synthesis-1ebb0a7e-8cd6-40ff-bf0f-a16161232f85.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where a person has a justified true belief but the justification is based on false or misleading evidence. Intuitively, such situations challenge the notion that justified true belief is sufficient for knowledge because justification seems faulty.**, **A justified true belief based on false or misleading evidence suggests deficiencies in the idea that such belief suffices for knowledge.**
