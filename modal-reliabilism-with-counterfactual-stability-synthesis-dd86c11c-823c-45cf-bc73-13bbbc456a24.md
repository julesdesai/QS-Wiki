# Modal Reliabilism with Counterfactual Stability

**Parent:** [Can knowledge stem from a malfunctioning but reliable process?](can-knowledge-stem-from-a-malfunctioning-but-reliable-process-antithesis-0e652f7d-2939-4ca5-8131-447763e4b9ce.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief to be produced by a process that would produce true beliefs in nearby possible worlds**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is produced by a process that would produce true beliefs in nearby possible worlds**
