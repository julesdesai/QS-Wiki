# Knowledge as Justified True Belief

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** c9fd14b3-02ec-4837-bf56-dfd505f2021a
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can belief be true without knowledge?](can-belief-be-true-without-knowledge-antithesis-655d7a8e-1741-4ddf-85c7-a393d2dd8421.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is justified by a stable method that reliably leads to truth**, **Stable and reliable justification is distinguished from mere pragmatic usefulness**
