# Can knowledge ever arise from unreliable processes?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d75f753f-60fb-4f3d-b397-e024d650c1bb
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism](reliabilism-synthesis-27039b77-724d-4113-8b7b-b992ae688652.md)

## Content

**A person could come to a true belief via an unreliable method such as lucky guessing or sheer coincidence.**, **In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that the person does not have knowledge, despite the belief being true.**
