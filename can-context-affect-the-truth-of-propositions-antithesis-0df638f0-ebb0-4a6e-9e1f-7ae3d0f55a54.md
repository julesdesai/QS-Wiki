# Can context affect the truth of propositions?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0df638f0-ebb0-4a6e-9e1f-7ae3d0f55a54
**Depth:** 2
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism](contextualism-thesis-de4edc71-c75a-4fcd-afbe-4333fb44b1b5.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that truth is absolute and does not vary between contexts.**, **If truth is absolute, the truth of a proposition cannot depend on context.**, **The context of the knower cannot influence the truth of a proposition.**
