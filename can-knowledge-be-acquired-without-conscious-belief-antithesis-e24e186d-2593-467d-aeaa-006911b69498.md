# Can knowledge be acquired without conscious belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e24e186d-2593-467d-aeaa-006911b69498
**Depth:** 2
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundationalism](foundationalism-thesis-fd868af7-578e-400a-bb27-265f88ced5ee.md)

## Content

**A case in which someone acts with skill or expertise, such as a musician performing, is not a case in which they consciously believe every aspect of their performance.**, **It is implausible to suggest that all knowledge requires a direct belief in the specific propositions involved.**
