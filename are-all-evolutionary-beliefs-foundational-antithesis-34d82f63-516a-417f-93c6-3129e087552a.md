# Are all evolutionary beliefs foundational?

**Parent:** [Evolutionary Foundationalism](evolutionary-foundationalism-synthesis-4b200bf3-94a7-4a07-af03-f4d0ca848734.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that all beliefs arising from evolutionary processes are foundational. Complex societal beliefs, which evolve culturally rather than biologically, may lack foundational status despite providing survival benefits.**
