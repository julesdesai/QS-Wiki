**Parent:** [Does contextual variation undermine the stability of knowledge claims?](does-contextual-variation-undermine-the-stability-of-knowledge-claims-antithesis-f894aac9-7878-490b-b5fe-e18b612bebb9.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification for the belief is invariant across all contexts**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification for the belief is invariant across all contexts**
