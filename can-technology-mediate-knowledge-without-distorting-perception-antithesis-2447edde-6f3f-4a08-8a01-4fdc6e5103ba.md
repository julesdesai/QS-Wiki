# Can technology mediate knowledge without distorting perception?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2447edde-6f3f-4a08-8a01-4fdc6e5103ba
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Indirect Realism with Acknowledgment of Mediation](indirect-realism-with-acknowledgment-of-mediation-synthesis-e56d95ce-eeee-446c-93a6-a21b3a4bd4d7.md)

## Content

**Technological mediation can introduce an error despite being generally reliable.**, **Suppose a person perceives through a screen that misrepresents colors. Intuitively, we would judge that their knowledge of colors in the world is flawed.**, **There is a plausible principle that for knowledge to be accurate, perception must correlate directly with the property perceived without distortion.**
