# Is the concept of an infinite chain of justifications meaningful?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5404f59e-10f3-4b42-9d38-eafc251cbce3
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism](infinitism-synthesis-dd7098a7-016a-4f9d-9b36-944f2be4f1f4.md)

## Content

**An infinite chain implies an endless process that lacks a final step or completion.**, **For a chain of reasoning to support a belief, it must be accessible or interpretable by a cognitive agent.**, **An infinite non-repeating chain of justifications defies actualization in any practical scenario or instance of reasoning.**
