## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is the result of the person's intellectual virtues**, **Insights derived from reasonable false beliefs can contribute to the advancement of knowledge**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition and that the belief is true, resulting from the exercise of intellectual virtues with supportive, insightful false beliefs**
