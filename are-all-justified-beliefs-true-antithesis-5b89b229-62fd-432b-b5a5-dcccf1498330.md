# Are all justified beliefs true?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5b89b229-62fd-432b-b5a5-dcccf1498330
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Holism with Infinite Justification](holism-with-infinite-justification-synthesis-5f38dde0-d181-4939-9f1a-56e147cd5ef5.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person has a justified belief that is not true due to false evidence.**, **In such a scenario, not all justified beliefs are true.**, **Thus, justified beliefs are not necessarily true.**
