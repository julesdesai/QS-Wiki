## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **The justification required for knowledge depends on the subject's practical interests**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification standard driven by their practical interests is met**
