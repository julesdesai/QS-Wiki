# Knowledge as Substantial Justified True Belief

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief depends on an epistemically substantial reason**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is justified, and it depends on an epistemically substantial reason**
