# Are all practical consequences relevant to knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 59cf223d-1cf9-4bf6-8b55-4fbeff3dd449
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Direct Realism](pragmatic-direct-realism-synthesis-4173acef-5348-4f34-a95b-b6ba164e9e84.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person P has a practical consequence from a perception, but it's irrelevant to the main knowledge claim, such as perceiving an apple and then dropping a pencil. Intuitively, the practical consequence should relate directly to the knowledge claim.**, **Practical consequences unrelated to the perception in question should not determine the existence of knowledge.**
