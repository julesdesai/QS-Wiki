## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification consists of the coherence of the belief with a broader system of beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification consists of the coherence of the belief with a broader system of beliefs**
