**Parent:** [Does the truth condition alone ensure knowledge?](does-the-truth-condition-alone-ensure-knowledge-antithesis-eafb1843-c5bc-4843-9d15-ec72f4eccb21.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief tracks the truth across relevant possible situations**, **Tracking involves having beliefs that would be true if the proposition were false in certain nearby scenarios**
