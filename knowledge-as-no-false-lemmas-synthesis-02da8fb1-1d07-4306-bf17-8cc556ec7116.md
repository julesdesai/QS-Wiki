# Knowledge as No False Lemmas

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Could a belief be safe from error but still fail to qualify as knowledge?](could-a-belief-be-safe-from-error-but-still-fail-to-qualify-as-knowledge-antithesis-71536931-4478-49cf-9c2e-ed41971578f7.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that no false belief in the inference chain is used to justify the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and no false belief in the inference chain is used to justify the belief**
