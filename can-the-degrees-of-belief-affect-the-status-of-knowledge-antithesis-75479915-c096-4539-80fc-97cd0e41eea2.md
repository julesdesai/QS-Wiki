# Can the degrees of belief affect the status of knowledge?

**Terminal:** No

## Content

**Consider a case in which a person has a very low degree of belief in a true proposition, due to conflicting evidence but believes it for practical purposes. Intuitively, it seems they lack full knowledge because their confidence is unstable despite it being true.**
