# Does the context of the knower really determine knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 07ef70c7-71d0-4244-92c3-b5258783b466
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism with Defeat Condition](contextualism-with-defeat-condition-synthesis-644e1e82-6b43-472b-8e60-41eaf47756ee.md)

## Content

**In a scenario where an individual has a true belief supported by evidence but lacking specific contextual information, intuitively we might still judge that they possess knowledge.**, **It is implausible that knowledge could be fully determined by the context of the knower alone, given the variability and subjectivity of personal contexts.**
