# Is a sensitive belief always indicative of reliability?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0d3f66e0-1463-48f9-9f58-946a57941258
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Sensitivity Condition View](sensitivity-condition-view-synthesis-f14f93ca-735d-497d-b9de-d52fabd7ef03.md)

## Content

**There is a situation where a belief is sensitive, meaning it would change if the proposition were false, but it is formed through unreliable means.**, **Intuitively, we would say this is not knowledge due to the lack of reliability, despite the sensitivity.**
