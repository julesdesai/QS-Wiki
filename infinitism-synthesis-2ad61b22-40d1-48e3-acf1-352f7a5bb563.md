## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to have justification, it is necessary that justification consists of infinite, non-repeating reasons**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary they have justification for believing it**
