# Does prior probability influence the reliability of a belief-forming process?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 70f96ee5-0635-40f8-8acd-30137d9e4496
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism bases knowledge on reliable processes](reliabilism-bases-knowledge-on-reliable-processes-synthesis-e8668809-0baa-4531-bd9d-063ab7893b2b.md)

## Content

**A belief-forming process that is generally reliable may produce a true belief about a highly improbable event.**, **Intuitively, the improbability of the event leads us to doubt the process's reliability in this instance, thus challenging the status of the belief as knowledge.**
