# Knowledge as Virtuous Responsiveness

**Parent:** [Is knowledge possible in cases of cognitive luck?](is-knowledge-possible-in-cases-of-cognitive-luck-antithesis-7ba09d20-acff-4ad5-83f1-487bbe6b5d87.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they respond to their evidential environment with intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is the result of a virtuous response**
