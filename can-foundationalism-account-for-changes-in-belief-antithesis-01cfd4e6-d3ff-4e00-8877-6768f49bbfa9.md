# Can foundationalism account for changes in belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 01cfd4e6-d3ff-4e00-8877-6768f49bbfa9
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundationalism in Epistemology](foundationalism-in-epistemology-synthesis-4c27bb26-6321-4022-8e66-a85ea5d939d9.md)

## Content

**Humans frequently revise their beliefs in light of new evidence or reasoning.**, **If foundational beliefs are revised, they might not have been truly self-justified.**, **The need to revise foundational beliefs suggests they may not serve as an immutable foundation.**
