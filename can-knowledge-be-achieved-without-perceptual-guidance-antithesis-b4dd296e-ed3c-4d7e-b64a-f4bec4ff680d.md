# Can knowledge be achieved without perceptual guidance?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b4dd296e-ed3c-4d7e-b64a-f4bec4ff680d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Memory-Integrated Phenomenalism](memory-integrated-phenomenalism-synthesis-20b6ad46-c9b1-41b8-87a0-7619b03b72c5.md)

## Content

**Suppose a scientist predicts the existence of a planet solely based on mathematical models without any direct perceptual data. Intuitively, this prediction, later confirmed by observation, represents knowledge achieved without initial perceptual guidance.**, **It is implausible that all knowledge originates solely in perception if theoretical predictions can count as knowledge before empirical verification.**
