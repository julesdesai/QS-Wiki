# Are moderate standards robust enough across diverse scenarios?

## Content

**Consider a scenario where moderate standards of justification might permit a belief in a conspiracy theory due to lack of complete evidence. Intuitively, moderate standards may fail to address extreme cases adequately.**, **It is plausible to think that stringent standards of justification are required in certain contexts to ensure the reliability of knowledge.**
