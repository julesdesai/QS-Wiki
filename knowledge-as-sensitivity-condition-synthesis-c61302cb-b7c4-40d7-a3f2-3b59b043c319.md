## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in the nearest possible world where the proposition is false, the person does not believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and in the nearest possible world where it is false, they do not believe it**
