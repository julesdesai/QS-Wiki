# Virtue Epistemology with Pragmatic Benefits

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief be formed through intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that believing the proposition has pragmatic utility**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is formed through intellectual virtue, and it has pragmatic utility**
