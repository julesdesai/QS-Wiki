# How do we evaluate knowledge in cases of necessary truths?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 01e6138d-f9fa-4423-8f17-1562398cb784
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Nozick's Truth Tracking Theory](nozicks-truth-tracking-theory-synthesis-6290274e-e060-4b88-85f5-f721b81423aa.md)

## Content

**In the case of a necessary truth, if a person believes it and the proposition is true, then by necessity they would still believe it if it were false, which creates a contradiction.**, **It seems implausible that knowledge would permit such an inconsistency with necessary truths.**
