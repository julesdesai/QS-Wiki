# Does knowledge require belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 8bc47eb2-94a7-4a0f-ac08-b59c6fd066e6
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Dynamic Infinitism Summary](dynamic-infinitism-summary-synthesis-65698d6e-d9bc-439d-bd28-3a216caa0bfd.md)

## Content

**An intuitive example might involve an individual who does not believe a proposition due to doubt or lack of understanding but nonetheless knows it to be true due to reliable evidence, showing belief is not necessary for knowledge.**, **Consider a scenario where a person has overlooked evidence but unknowingly acts in a justified way, suggesting knowledge does not strictly require personal belief.**, **In cases where individuals dismiss their accurate judgments due to low personal confidence, they may still functionally use knowledge, challenging the belief requirement for knowledge.**
