## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they can distinguish the proposition from relevant alternatives**, **The capacity to rule out relevant alternatives is sufficient for knowledge**
