# How does the view cope with irrelevant context?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2151f680-4e97-4efd-879a-2dd2f39f4e1f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualist Knowledge Summary](contextualist-knowledge-summary-synthesis-c38eaf7b-9feb-49d3-8375-2d19895c61fa.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which an irrelevant context is deemed pertinent to a knowledge claim. Intuitively, allowing such contexts to modify justification requirements seems problematic.**, **A scenario where irrelevant contexts impact the required justification could permit knowledge claims based on improper grounds, confusing genuine epistemic reasoning.**
