# No-defeater theory of knowledge

**Parent:** [Does knowledge of future events entail a causal connection?](does-knowledge-of-future-events-entail-a-causal-connection-antithesis-57e2c739-7abc-4ab0-a9fa-fc799b58bc37.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no defeaters for the belief in the proposition**, **A defeater is a reason that would undermine the justification for the belief**
