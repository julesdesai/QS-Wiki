# Could context undermine the universality of intellectual virtue?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e84a8640-df31-445e-8281-0839939bedbf
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalist Virtue Contextualism](externalist-virtue-contextualism-synthesis-69e2f760-46ad-465f-a31e-67e150de071d.md)

## Content

**Intellectual virtue is context-dependent and evaluated by the standards of the situation suggests variability in what counts as virtue.**, **Universal intellectual virtues are traits or excellences of the mind that are applicable across all contexts.**, **If context undermines universality, then intellectual virtues might not consistently guide judgments or actions across different situations.**
