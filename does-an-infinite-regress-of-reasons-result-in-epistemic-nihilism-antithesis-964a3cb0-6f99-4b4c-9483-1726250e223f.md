# Does an infinite regress of reasons result in epistemic nihilism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 964a3cb0-6f99-4b4c-9483-1726250e223f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism Summary](infinitism-summary-synthesis-17c5b8c2-c7de-43ec-bf90-6dedae4ad235.md)

## Content

**Epistemic nihilism holds that knowledge or justified beliefs are not attainable.**, **If every belief requires another, endlessly, this might lead to the conclusion that our beliefs aren't justified at all.**, **An infinite regress system could unintentionally support an epistemic nihilist position rather than opposing it.**
