# Can Infinitism provide closure on any belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2df77bbc-2f77-4d4c-94b2-35d6cd78678a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism](infinitism-synthesis-db00e7c1-6a13-4ca5-8f09-6fae7cd29e0f.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where a person continuously finds new reasons without ever concluding the justification process. Intuitively, this prevents closure on any belief.**, **It is implausible that Infinitism allows for epistemic closure, as the infinite nature of reasons precludes the possibility of concluding the inquiry into any belief.**
