## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it, except in contexts where practical stakes are low**, **The belief must be sensitive to the truth in contexts that matter practically for the person**
