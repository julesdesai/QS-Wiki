## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief is formed through a responsible use of intellectual virtues**, **Epistemic responsibility involves deliberate and conscious closure of information relevant to the belief**
