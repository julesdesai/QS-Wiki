## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that practical interests affect the standards of knowing**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that these practical interests be adequately accounted for in the justification of the belief**
