# How does perceptual knowledge challenge a strict causal link?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 230b63c2-a9bf-4477-b2e6-a02e25a5c1e0
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Causal Theory of Knowledge](causal-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-9ece6694-b5cf-4143-897d-321cf0165994.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person P sees a blue sky and believes that the sky is blue. The visual experience is a direct perception that does not clearly delineate a causal chain between the belief and the truth that the sky is blue.**, **Perception can generate true beliefs without a transparent causal chain that links the belief and the truth.**
