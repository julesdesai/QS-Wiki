# Does context truly vary justification standards?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c148a303-2642-447d-877a-7eda9cc48296
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism: Contextual Standards](contextualism-contextual-standards-synthesis-864b880e-c24e-4de1-9edf-fbd584c75f88.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a situation where two people have the same evidence for a proposition but are in different contexts that supposedly alter the standards of justification. Intuitively, it seems implausible that one has knowledge while the other does not under identical evidence.**
