## Content

**Suppose there is a principle that justification for a belief must be more rigorous than simply applied environmental reliability.**, **If justification suffices solely on environment-based reliability, then stricter standards applied in practice might highlight inadequacies in the environment's reliability when assessing justification.**, **It is plausible to think that relying merely on environmental reliability for justification could lead to inadequate assessments in varying contexts.**
