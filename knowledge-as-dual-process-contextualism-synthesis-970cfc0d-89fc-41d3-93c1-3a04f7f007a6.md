## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have justification for believing it through either an inferential process or non-inferential perception**, **Justification can arise from foundational beliefs validated by contextual factors and non-inferential perceptual beliefs**
