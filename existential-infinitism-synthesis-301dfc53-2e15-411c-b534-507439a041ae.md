## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient for an infinite chain of reasons to exist in principle, even if not all are explicitly known or experienced**, **For practical purposes, only a finite segment of the chain needs to be accessible**
