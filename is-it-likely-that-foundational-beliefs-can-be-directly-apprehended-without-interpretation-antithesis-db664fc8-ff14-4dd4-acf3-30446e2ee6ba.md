# Is it likely that foundational beliefs can be directly apprehended without interpretation?

**Terminal:** No

## Content

**Suppose an individual claims to directly apprehend a foundational belief with no interpretation. It is implausible that any belief exists completely free from interpretive frameworks.**, **A plausible principle states that interpretation plays an inevitable role in shaping even seemingly foundational beliefs.**
