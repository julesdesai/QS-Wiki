# Knowledge as Coherent True Belief with Externalist Justification

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is coherency within a belief system always sufficient to guarantee truth?](is-coherency-within-a-belief-system-always-sufficient-to-guarantee-truth-antithesis-8fc78b00-e520-4fb6-8aeb-e769a58c39af.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres with the person's entire belief system**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person has an external justification for the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief coheres with the person's entire belief system, and the person has an external justification for the belief**
