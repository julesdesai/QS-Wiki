# How does Contextualist Coherentism address the problem of infinite regress in justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 37a7b9fd-87ad-4f17-8a36-9d89ff470736
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualist Coherentism](contextualist-coherentism-synthesis-156207e7-3c9d-49a2-8bb7-7097c1d027cd.md)

## Content

**Consider the belief that a justification must itself be justified by another coherent belief within a context.**, **Each subsequent justification could require further justification, leading to an infinite regress.**, **It is implausible that justification can be constantly deferred without resolution.**
