# Credit-based Virtue Epistemology

**Parent:** [Do accidental true beliefs from intellectual virtue qualify as knowledge?](do-accidental-true-beliefs-from-intellectual-virtue-qualify-as-knowledge-antithesis-3cd1c816-13e9-488a-a6ae-83b33fbb959c.md)

## Content

**Knowledge is a belief for which a person deserves credit**, **Credit is granted when true belief results primarily from one's intellectual virtues**, **Intellectual virtues include qualities like open-mindedness and attentiveness**, **A true belief must occur primarily due to one's intellectual virtues**, **An accidental true belief does not grant credit and thus does not qualify as knowledge**
