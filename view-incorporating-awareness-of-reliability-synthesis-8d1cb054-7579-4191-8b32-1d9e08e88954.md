## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief in the proposition results from a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they are aware of the reliability of the cognitive process used**, **A reliable cognitive process is one that consistently produces true beliefs**
