# Can one have evidence and still lack belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 23009197-98b5-40b0-83c4-b2d353b2a515
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Evidentialism Summary](evidentialism-summary-synthesis-dea02623-290c-44c3-896b-2f967081eb1b.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person encounters a great deal of compelling evidence for a certain conclusion but remains unconvinced, intuitively suggesting they possess evidence without holding the corresponding belief.**, **A person might acknowledge the persuasiveness of evidence yet consciously refrain from forming a belief, indicating that possession of evidence does not automatically result in belief.**
