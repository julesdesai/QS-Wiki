## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for their belief to be safe and formed through epistemic virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, their belief is safe from being false, and they possess the epistemic virtue in forming that belief**
