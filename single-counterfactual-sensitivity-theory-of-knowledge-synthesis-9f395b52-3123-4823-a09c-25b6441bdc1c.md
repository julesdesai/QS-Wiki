## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in the closest possible world where they believe the proposition, it is not false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and in the closest possible world where they believe it, it is not false**
