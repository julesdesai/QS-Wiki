# Knowledge as True Belief with Internalist Justification

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** ab3c97e0-4961-42d3-804e-bd937240dc7d
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can belief be true without knowledge?](can-belief-be-true-without-knowledge-antithesis-655d7a8e-1741-4ddf-85c7-a393d2dd8421.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is justified by factors internal to their mental states**, **Internal factors may include introspective access to evidence or reasons**
