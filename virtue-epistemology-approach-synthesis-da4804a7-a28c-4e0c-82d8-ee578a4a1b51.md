## Content

**A person knows a proposition if they believe it and their belief results from an intellectual virtue**, **An intellectual virtue is a character trait that systematically leads to the acquisition of true beliefs**, **Virtue epistemology emphasizes the agent's cognitive character in the actual world**, **Knowledge is seen as a success resulting from the agent's intellectual virtues rather than hypothetical situations**
