# What is the role of cognitive biases in shaping 'seemings'?

## Content

**It is implausible that biases such as confirmation bias do not influence the way things seem true to a person. Since biases can distort 'seemings', naïve reliance on them for justification without critical scrutiny could lead to erroneous beliefs being held as knowledge.**
