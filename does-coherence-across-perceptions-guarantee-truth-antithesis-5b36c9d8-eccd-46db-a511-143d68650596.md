# Does coherence across perceptions guarantee truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5b36c9d8-eccd-46db-a511-143d68650596
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherent Phenomenalism](coherent-phenomenalism-synthesis-762c00d9-ee32-4648-8d8e-67643edf20ba.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a person’s perceptions of a dream are consistent every night. Intuitively, we either have to say that knowledge of the dream's content is genuine or that consistency does not ensure truth. If we say knowledge of the dream is genuine, then such content is accepted as real. If we say consistency does not ensure truth, then knowledge cannot be based merely on coherence.**
