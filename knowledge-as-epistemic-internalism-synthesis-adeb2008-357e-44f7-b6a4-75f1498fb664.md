## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the person has access to the justification for their belief**, **Justification must be available to the conscious awareness of the person**
