# Can someone have a justified true belief without knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 495ed798-5ec0-4fcd-940a-760bda6f1f35
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Tracking Theory of Knowledge](tracking-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-34b45a3c-9960-48d7-bb61-82e72864432f.md)

## Content

**A person can have a justified true belief in a proposition without having knowledge of that proposition if their belief does not track the truth.**, **Consider a scenario where a person believes a proposition based on strong evidence and the proposition is true, but the belief would not have been held if the proposition were false. In such cases, intuitively we would say the person lacks knowledge despite having justified true belief.**
