# Is every true belief formed by intellectual virtue knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 27d837c5-f65f-4457-8cde-108fedfe5c04
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-7601e066-11d5-4f0c-8c42-44a4cc1f650d.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person has a true belief based on intellectual virtues but does not have justification for why the belief is true. Intuitively, this is not knowledge.**, **Justification is required for true belief to constitute knowledge beyond mere intellectual virtue.**
