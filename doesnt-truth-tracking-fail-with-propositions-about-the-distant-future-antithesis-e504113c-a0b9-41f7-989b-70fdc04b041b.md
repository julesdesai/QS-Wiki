# Doesn't truth-tracking fail with propositions about the distant future?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e504113c-a0b9-41f7-989b-70fdc04b041b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Information-Tracking Theory](information-tracking-theory-synthesis-990127f2-7220-489f-9c29-17860a8cc351.md)

## Content

**Consider a proposition about a distant future event that a person currently believes.**, **If the belief about the future turns out true, this doesn't mean the person tracked the truth, since it's impossible to check future scenarios.**
