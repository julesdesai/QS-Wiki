# Justified True Belief Plus No False Lemmas

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 3fe5df6d-e2d1-4ef6-8f58-3d434dbc1802
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Do contextual standards always suffice for knowledge?](do-contextual-standards-always-suffice-for-knowledge-antithesis-281adcae-fc87-43d9-94ad-144d65b76db3.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is not inferred from any false lemmas**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is justified, and not based on false lemmas**
