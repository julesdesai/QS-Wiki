# Does the theory imply issues with cumulative knowledge?

## Content

**A person might know a set of facts in one context but lack knowledge if those same facts are considered in combination with additional facts or alternatives in another context.**, **As knowledge builds upon prior knowledge, if previous knowledge claims are context-dependent, it might challenge the concept of cumulative or foundational knowledge used in scientific or historical inquiry.**
