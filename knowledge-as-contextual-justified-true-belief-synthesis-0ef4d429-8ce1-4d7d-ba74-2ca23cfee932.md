# Knowledge as Contextual Justified True Belief

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 0ef4d429-8ce1-4d7d-ba74-2ca23cfee932
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does truth always need virtue?](does-truth-always-need-virtue-antithesis-bca5c496-e717-405b-9a82-213c055d7e38.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is assessed and held true within relevant contexts**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is valid within relevant contexts**
