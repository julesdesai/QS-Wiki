# Can reliably produced beliefs still be false?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 6f23d475-6725-407a-add6-3227bc0a031a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism](reliabilism-synthesis-41e7a61f-ad45-42cd-8dd7-ba40ca519ba9.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that beliefs can be reliably produced yet still be false, as a reliable process does not guarantee infallibility.**, **A belief produced by a reliable process may be true generally but becomes an exception in a particular case.**, **If a belief can be false even when produced by a reliable process, then reliability alone cannot suffice for knowledge.**
