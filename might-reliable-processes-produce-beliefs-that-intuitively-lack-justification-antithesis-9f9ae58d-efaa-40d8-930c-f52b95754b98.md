# Might reliable processes produce beliefs that intuitively lack justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9f9ae58d-efaa-40d8-930c-f52b95754b98
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Process Reliabilism with Evidence Sensitivity](process-reliabilism-with-evidence-sensitivity-synthesis-e3e15d29-f0bc-4d87-b0aa-fbeab510c604.md)

## Content

**Imagine a case where a belief is produced by a reliable process but does not seem justified because it ignores some available evidence. Intuitively, we would not classify the belief as knowledge.**, **Even reliable processes may fail to account for all available evidence, which can undermine justification.**
