# Can justification be non-inferential and still be internal?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a9b74af0-ac3a-4c6c-ac8f-fbfdf78ecb41
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Minimalist Internalism](minimalist-internalism-synthesis-c1aff540-8ee4-4dca-921e-260ac4a2c333.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that if justification requires reflective access, then such access must be inferential in nature.**, **If reflective access is inferential, then minimal reflective access does not provide true internal justification.**, **Non-inferential justification contradicts the requirement for reflective access to justification.**
