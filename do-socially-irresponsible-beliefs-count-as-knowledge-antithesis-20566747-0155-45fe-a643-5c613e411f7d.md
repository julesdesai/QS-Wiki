# Do socially irresponsible beliefs count as knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 20566747-0155-45fe-a643-5c613e411f7d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Responsibilist Virtue Epistemology](responsibilist-virtue-epistemology-synthesis-d65854c6-8623-4bd4-aed3-f2a4bfae69c9.md)

## Content

**There are beliefs that might be true and responsibly held individually but are socially irresponsible.**, **Such beliefs may arise from ignoring social or ethical responsibilities, yet remain true.**, **It is questionable whether socially irresponsible beliefs can be considered knowledge.**
