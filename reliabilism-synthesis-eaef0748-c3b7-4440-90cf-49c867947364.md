## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, their belief must be produced by a reliable process**, **A reliable process is one that produces a high ratio of true to false beliefs**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief stems from a process that typically leads to truth**, **Knowledge depends on the dependability of the process used to form the belief**
