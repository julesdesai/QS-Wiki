# Can coherentism distinguish between true coherence and clever fabrication?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 54356275-dedc-42a8-a4ce-5e801d5e69c8
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism View](coherentism-view-synthesis-4dcf0483-85cc-4ed6-8a5e-86aaec2b7626.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a belief system that is completely fabricated yet internally coherent, such as an elaborate fictional world. Intuitively, we would judge that such a system lacks truth even if it is coherent.**, **Therefore, relying on coherence alone to justify beliefs may result in endorsing beliefs that are false but cleverly fabricated.**
