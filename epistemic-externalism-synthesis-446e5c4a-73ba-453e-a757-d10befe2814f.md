## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person has access to reliable processes or circumstances that justify the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is the result of reliable processes**
