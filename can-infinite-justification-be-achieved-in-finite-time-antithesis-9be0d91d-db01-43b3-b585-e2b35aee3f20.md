# Can infinite justification be achieved in finite time?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9be0d91d-db01-43b3-b585-e2b35aee3f20
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Recursive Infinitism Summary](recursive-infinitism-summary-synthesis-e60eeb81-9314-4a67-a7c8-de308e762c47.md)

## Content

**If knowledge requires infinite justification, then the process for obtaining justification would require an infinite amount of time.**, **Human cognitive capacities and lifespans are finite, making it practically impossible for anyone to complete an infinite process.**, **If a requirement for knowledge cannot be practically satisfied by any human, then it is implausible to claim that knowledge requires such a condition.**
