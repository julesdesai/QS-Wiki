# Does knowledge really require belief to track truth across all possible worlds?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 6c2c08d6-a7bf-4b37-879a-198d178b86dd
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Truth-Tracking View](truth-tracking-view-synthesis-ffb314b5-26cd-4e37-96a0-e81b3c75b895.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person forms a true belief by sheer luck, without tracking the truth. Intuitively, we might still be inclined to say the person knows the truth in this case.**, **Consider a scenario where a belief would change according to a truth change in an irrelevant scenario, casting doubt on whether truth-tracking can reliably indicate knowledge in practical terms.**
