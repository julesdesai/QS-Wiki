# Can unreliable traits ever lead to knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 24653452-ab73-4394-b41b-000731d64442
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-5dae303e-e392-4dd3-a9fb-c518398dc86e.md)

## Content

**Consider a person who normally forms beliefs through a generally unreliable cognitive process but happens to form a true belief on a particular occasion.**, **Intuitively, it seems possible that the person still possesses knowledge, even though the process was not based on intellectual virtue.**
