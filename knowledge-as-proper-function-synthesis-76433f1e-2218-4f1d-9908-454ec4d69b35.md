# Knowledge as Proper Function

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge exist without a causal connection?](can-knowledge-exist-without-a-causal-connection-antithesis-e0215c8f-4759-4cf1-8d78-e374570ba693.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their cognitive faculties are functioning properly**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, the proposition is true, and their cognitive faculties are functioning properly**
