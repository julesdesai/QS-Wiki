# Can coherence alone ensure the justification of beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** cf956f2f-90dd-44dc-8744-c1906089b5f2
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitist Coherentism](infinitist-coherentism-synthesis-2a4efbd0-0f1f-4a01-bfa6-1ecf016eb1e0.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which multiple beliefs form a coherent set but lack any correspondence to reality. Intuitively, we either have to say that coherence is sufficient for justification or that it is not. If we say coherence is sufficient, then we must accept possibly false beliefs as justified. If we say coherence is not sufficient, then coherence alone cannot justify beliefs.**
