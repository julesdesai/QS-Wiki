## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief corresponds to an objective list of methods generating truth**, **The methods on the list are not tied to a specific environment**
