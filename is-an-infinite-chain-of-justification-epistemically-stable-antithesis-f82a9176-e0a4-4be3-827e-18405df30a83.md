# Is an infinite chain of justification epistemically stable?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f82a9176-e0a4-4be3-827e-18405df30a83
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitist Coherentism](infinitist-coherentism-synthesis-2a4efbd0-0f1f-4a01-bfa6-1ecf016eb1e0.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which an infinite chain of reasons provides belief support. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that the chain's reliance on infinite links makes it prone to fragility.**, **A belief system reliant on infinite links can easily collapse if coherence is disrupted at any point, questioning its stability.**
