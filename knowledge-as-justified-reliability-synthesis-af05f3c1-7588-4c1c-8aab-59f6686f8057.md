# Knowledge as Justified Reliability

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** af05f3c1-7588-4c1c-8aab-59f6686f8057
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Should reliability accommodate diverse epistemic values beyond truth?](should-reliability-accommodate-diverse-epistemic-values-beyond-truth-antithesis-e9759d9f-a275-4c27-8f8e-5240e6d8391b.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief aligns with epistemically justified values such as thoroughness or precision**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process, and the belief aligns with epistemically justified values**
