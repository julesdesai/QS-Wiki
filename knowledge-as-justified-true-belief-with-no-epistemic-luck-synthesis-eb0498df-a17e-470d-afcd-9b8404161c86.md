# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with No Epistemic Luck

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is a justified true belief with a reliable process always knowledge if it can still be lucky?](is-a-justified-true-belief-with-a-reliable-process-always-knowledge-if-it-can-still-be-lucky-antithesis-7640583e-86ea-4b10-b548-6285270a37a9.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed through a reliable process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the truth of the belief is not a result of epistemic luck**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is formed through a reliable process, and the belief's truth is not due to epistemic luck**
