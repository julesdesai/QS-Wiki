# No False Lemmas

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 2934d344-8a53-442b-84b6-c3d373f3ade4
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does knowledge require more than just truth and belief?](does-knowledge-require-more-than-just-truth-and-belief-antithesis-23ea7134-c321-49ba-bb9d-828c4c84aaf8.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, the belief in the proposition must not be based on any false assumptions or lemmas**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is based on no false assumptions**
