## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, there must be an appropriate causal connection between the belief and the truth of the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition and there is a causal connection between the belief and the truth**
