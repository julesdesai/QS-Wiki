# Knowledge as Hierarchical Reliabilism

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Are all types of cognitive processes equally reliable in producing knowledge?](are-all-types-of-cognitive-processes-equally-reliable-in-producing-knowledge-antithesis-9b01a7b8-fe9f-4da5-a56f-f047ee244d4a.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a hierarchically reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is produced by a hierarchically reliable cognitive process**
