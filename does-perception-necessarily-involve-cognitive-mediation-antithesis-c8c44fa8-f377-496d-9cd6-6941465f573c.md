# Does perception necessarily involve cognitive mediation?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c8c44fa8-f377-496d-9cd6-6941465f573c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Critical Realism](critical-realism-synthesis-04d59a43-df5d-4ff1-bc30-b363f66f9a03.md)

## Content

**A plausible concept is that perception, at its base level, is simply sensory input that does not require thought or cognition.**, **Consider an infant who reacts to bright colors without having the capacity for cognitive processing. In this scenario, intuitively, the infant perceives without interpretative cognition.**
