# Is coherence sufficient for justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e9256c4a-d3ba-4ad0-9edc-e6ee0ebbf372
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism](coherentism-synthesis-fe89b418-bb8b-4164-8874-fa4a88946376.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that a belief system can be internally coherent and yet entirely disconnected from reality.**, **A coherent set of beliefs among a cult who share false perceptions is an example of such a system.**, **Being coherent does not necessarily entail that the beliefs are true or correspond to the external world.**
