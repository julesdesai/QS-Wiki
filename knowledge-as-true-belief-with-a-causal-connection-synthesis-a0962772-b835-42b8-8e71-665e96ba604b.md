## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief to be justified**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is an appropriate causal connection between the belief and the truth of the proposition**
