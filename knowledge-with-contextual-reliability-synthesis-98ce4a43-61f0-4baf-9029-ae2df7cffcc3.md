## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, the reliability of the belief-forming process must be contextually appropriate**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition and the belief is formed through a contextually appropriate reliable process**
