# Can an infinite regress of reasons lead to skepticism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** cadec786-d3bf-4f4c-9819-1859d3dcfaac
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism with Finite Justifications](infinitism-with-finite-justifications-synthesis-69ad7e03-7f20-4a4f-85ef-ebc999d85fe8.md)

## Content

**Suppose we accept that every justification requires further justification. This can lead to an infinite regress, which can be employed to argue that no belief is truly justified, leading to skepticism.**, **An infinite regress of justification seems to conflict with the notion of having knowledge if such a regress implies that no ultimate point of justification is ever reached.**
