# Can knowledge exist without the belief being justified in traditional terms?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 20cbcb3a-93b0-420f-be22-d4ed61f3f487
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalist View](externalist-view-synthesis-75028f40-4ef7-4feb-a21d-1e6a75d787a6.md)

## Content

**A belief being produced by a reliable process does not ensure that it is justified in the traditional sense.**, **Justification in the traditional sense involves having reasons or evidence accessible to the person. If this is not met, intuitively one might doubt the presence of knowledge.**
