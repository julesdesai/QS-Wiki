# Safety Condition in Knowledge

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge be undermined by Gettier-like cases?](can-knowledge-be-undermined-by-gettier-like-cases-antithesis-f715423c-b5da-4f25-854c-9a6ab5e17613.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief could not easily have been false**, **A belief is safe if in nearly all nearby possible worlds where the person believes the proposition, the proposition is true**
