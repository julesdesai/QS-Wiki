# Safety-Based Theory of Knowledge

**Parent:** [Does knowledge require more than truth-tracking to address skepticism?](does-knowledge-require-more-than-truth-tracking-to-address-skepticism-antithesis-71b8e39a-c6be-4e46-81f9-079d635b090c.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is safe, meaning in most nearby possible worlds where they believe it, the proposition is not false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is safe**
