# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with Contextual Justification

**Parent:** [Does the context of justification affect the status of knowledge?](does-the-context-of-justification-affect-the-status-of-knowledge-antithesis-4ae0589d-c9b2-455a-a48a-570ff242119f.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **The context determines the level of justification required for knowing a proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have contextually appropriate justification, and the proposition is true**
