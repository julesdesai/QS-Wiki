# Knowledge as Reliabilist Epistemology

**Parent:** [Can a reliable but non-virtuous source still provide knowledge?](can-a-reliable-but-non-virtuous-source-still-provide-knowledge-antithesis-42b430ff-5f89-4dae-83eb-644c14fab4d1.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is produced by a process that reliably leads to true beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is produced by a process that reliably leads to true beliefs**
