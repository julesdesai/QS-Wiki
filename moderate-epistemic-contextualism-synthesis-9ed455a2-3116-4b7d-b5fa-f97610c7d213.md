## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the epistemic standards are not subject to trivial contextual variations**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and stable epistemic standards are met**
