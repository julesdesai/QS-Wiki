## Content

**Knowledge requires a belief to track the truth across nearby possible worlds**, **A belief is considered knowledge if it is true in the actual world and would be true in similar scenarios where the proposition remains true**, **A belief is not considered knowledge if it would be false in nearby possible worlds where the proposition is false**
