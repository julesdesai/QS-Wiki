# Is reliability the same across all domains of knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e5874084-5c4e-43c6-822e-405ee7b77e1b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism Summary](reliabilism-summary-synthesis-2bc1a227-81a9-4aef-a155-aa259d89de26.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that a process which is reliable in one domain (such as arithmetic) is equally reliable in another (such as moral reasoning).**, **If the reliability of a cognitive process differs across domains, then the reliability criterion for knowledge may produce inconsistencies in what is accepted as knowledge.**
