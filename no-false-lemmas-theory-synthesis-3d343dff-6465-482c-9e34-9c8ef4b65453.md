# No False Lemmas Theory

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their justification does not include any false beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, they have justification for believing it, and their justification does not include any false beliefs**
