# Is private perceptual experience necessary for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 598912ed-5019-42ef-8946-af77deef3f11
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Phenomenalism with Reliance on Testimony](phenomenalism-with-reliance-on-testimony-synthesis-6d144448-4d6b-4388-8669-d0f45e936182.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person P never personally witnesses any external object and relies exclusively on the testimony of others. Intuitively, we would judge that P's knowledge is lacking a direct perceptual foundation.**, **It is implausible that knowledge can be entirely founded on testimony without any personal perceptual experience.**
