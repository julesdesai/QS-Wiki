## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is produced by a reliable process external to the person’s belief system and precludes self-deception**, **Belief formation processes are relevant but do not require direct awareness by the individual**, **Reliability is maintained by excluding any self-deceptive influences**
