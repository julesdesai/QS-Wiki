## Content

**Knowledge is true belief produced by a mechanism that reliably generates true beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is produced by a reliable mechanism**, **A person knows a proposition if they believe it, the proposition is true, and their belief is produced by a reliable mechanism**
