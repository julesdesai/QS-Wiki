**Parent:** [How can an infinite regress of reasons be practically implemented?](how-can-an-infinite-regress-of-reasons-be-practically-implemented-antithesis-3517cdab-d29d-4953-9cb8-555707e48cf2.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified by a potentially infinite chain of reasons, but practically finite**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is justified by a potentially infinite chain of reasons, but practically finite**
