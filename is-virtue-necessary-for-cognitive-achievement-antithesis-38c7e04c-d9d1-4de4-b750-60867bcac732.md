# Is virtue necessary for cognitive achievement?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 38c7e04c-d9d1-4de4-b750-60867bcac732
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-35af9163-7530-4447-9474-ccb688222939.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where someone demonstrates high intellectual capability, achieving significant understanding without deliberate cultivation of intellectual virtue. Intuitively, we would judge that cognitive achievement can be independent of actively adopted virtue.**, **If cognitive achievements occur naturally, it challenges the necessity of virtue for knowledge as cognitive achievement.**
