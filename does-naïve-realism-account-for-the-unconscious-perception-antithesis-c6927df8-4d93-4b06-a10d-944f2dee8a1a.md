## Content

**There is an empirical fact that subliminal stimuli can influence people's thoughts and behavior without them being consciously aware of these stimuli.**, **A plausible principle is that if perception operates as a straightforward presentation, conscious awareness should always accompany perception. This principle together with unconscious perception challenges straightforward presentation.**
