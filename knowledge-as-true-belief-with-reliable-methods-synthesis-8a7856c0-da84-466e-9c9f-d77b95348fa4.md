## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person's belief is formed through reliable methods**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is true and the belief is formed through reliable methods**
