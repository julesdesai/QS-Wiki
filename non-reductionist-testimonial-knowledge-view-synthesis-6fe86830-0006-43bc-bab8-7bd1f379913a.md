## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For testimonial knowledge, it is necessary that the person receives information from a reliable source and has no defeaters**, **The reliability of the source and absence of reasons to doubt supports knowledge**
