## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed by a reliable process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the reliability of the process is periodically reassessed and updated to maintain its accuracy**, **A person knows a proposition if they believe it, the proposition is true, and their belief is formed by a continuously evaluated reliable process**, **A reliable process is one that produces true beliefs more often than false ones**
