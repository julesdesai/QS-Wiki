## Content

**For an agent to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For an agent to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For an agent to know a proposition, their belief must result from a process functioning to reliably produce true beliefs**, **An epistemic process has a function if it typically leads to the acquisition of true beliefs**
