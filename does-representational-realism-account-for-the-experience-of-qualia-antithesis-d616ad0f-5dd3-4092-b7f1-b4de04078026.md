# Does Representational Realism account for the experience of qualia?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d616ad0f-5dd3-4092-b7f1-b4de04078026
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Representational Realism](representational-realism-synthesis-2653bf9f-214b-48a1-b757-9a49097d2459.md)

## Content

**Qualia refer to the subjective, qualitative aspects of conscious experiences, such as the redness of an apple.**, **Representational Realism must explain how mental representations account for these subjective experiences.**, **It is implausible that purely objective mental representations fully capture subjective qualitative experiences.**
