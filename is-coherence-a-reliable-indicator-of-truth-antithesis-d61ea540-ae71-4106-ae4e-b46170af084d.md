# Is coherence a reliable indicator of truth?

**Node ID:** d61ea540-ae71-4106-ae4e-b46170af084d

**Parent:** [Coherentism with Justified Belief](coherentism-with-justified-belief-synthesis-01e10c3b-52a8-4097-b02c-3f010fc56355.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which beliefs are coherently formed in a community that structurally lacks access to relevant information. Intuitively, coherence does not ensure these beliefs are true.**, **There is a plausible principle that the reliability of a belief is partly dependent on factors beyond internal coherence, such as access to evidence.**
