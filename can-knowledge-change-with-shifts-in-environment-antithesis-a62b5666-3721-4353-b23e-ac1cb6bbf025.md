# Can knowledge change with shifts in environment?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a62b5666-3721-4353-b23e-ac1cb6bbf025
**Depth:** 2
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Internalism](internalism-thesis-ada01deb-da91-4734-b57d-93c13fa13538.md)

## Content

**Suppose a scientist believes she has internal justification for knowing that a particular plant species does not exist in the area.**, **Later, without any change in her internal state, an invasive species establishes itself locally. Intuitively, what she "knows" seems compromised, as knowledge depends on the external reality it tracks.**
