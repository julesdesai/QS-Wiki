# How does reliabilism handle epistemic circularity?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 676834d3-2240-4d66-bcf6-f17d285189cf
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism Summary](reliabilism-summary-synthesis-c54e9ff1-39e1-4224-b3be-b0979177f411.md)

## Content

**A situation arises when a person needs to justify the reliability of a belief-forming process by using that very process to form a belief about its reliability. Intuitively, this involves epistemic circularity, which seems problematic.**, **Reliabilism permits processes to justify themselves, allowing epistemic circularity, which is traditionally considered a flaw in justifying beliefs.**
