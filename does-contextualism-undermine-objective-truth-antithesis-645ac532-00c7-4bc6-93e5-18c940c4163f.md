# Does Contextualism Undermine Objective Truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 645ac532-00c7-4bc6-93e5-18c940c4163f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism](contextualism-synthesis-6d1ded2e-2e81-4f10-b6c8-db699d1664cc.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that knowledge requires a standard of truth that transcends individual contexts, experiences, or circumstances.**, **If knowledge is truly dependent on context, as contextualism suggests, it challenges the idea of an objective truth that is accessible regardless of context.**, **It is implausible to hold that knowledge can be both context-dependent and universally applicable across all contexts without contradiction.**
