## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **Reliability is defined by a high truth-to-error ratio in the process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, their belief is produced by a reliable process, and the proposition is true**
