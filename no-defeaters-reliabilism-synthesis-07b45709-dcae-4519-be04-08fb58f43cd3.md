## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is the product of a reliable cognitive process**, **A belief formed by a reliable cognitive process is likely to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no defeaters for the belief**
