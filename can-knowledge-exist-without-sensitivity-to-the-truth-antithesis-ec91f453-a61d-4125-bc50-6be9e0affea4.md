# Can knowledge exist without sensitivity to the truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ec91f453-a61d-4125-bc50-6be9e0affea4
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Nozick's Truth-Tracking Theory](nozicks-truth-tracking-theory-synthesis-1a215329-c53d-4dbe-9509-6b517d1bb8ad.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where person A believes a true proposition based on wishful thinking, with no regard for evidence. Intuitively, we would judge that A does not have knowledge, even though the belief tracks the truth.**, **It is implausible that merely adjusting one's belief by accident or coincidence leads to true knowledge rather than simply a true belief.**
