# Does Process Reliabilism fail when the evidence is misleading yet the belief is true?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e4274bb6-8ee8-46f8-bd34-f0152618be5f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Process Reliabilism with Evidence Sensitivity](process-reliabilism-with-evidence-sensitivity-synthesis-e3e15d29-f0bc-4d87-b0aa-fbeab510c604.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where misleading evidence is present but the belief produced is true and formed by a generally reliable process. Intuitively, we would judge that the belief should not constitute knowledge due to reliance on misleading evidence.**, **Knowledge requires that the belief-forming process respond properly to evidence, even when that evidence is misleading.**
