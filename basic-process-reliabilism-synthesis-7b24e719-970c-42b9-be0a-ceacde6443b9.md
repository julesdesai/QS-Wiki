# Basic Process Reliabilism

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does knowledge require subjective certainty?](does-knowledge-require-subjective-certainty-antithesis-af355500-103b-4503-a5a6-e0c1c4925c06.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief to be produced by a process that is reliable at a fundamental level**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is produced by a fundamentally reliable process**
