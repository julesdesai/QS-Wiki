## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person has some internal justification for the belief even if the process is reliable**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is produced by a reliable process and the person has some awareness of this reliability**
