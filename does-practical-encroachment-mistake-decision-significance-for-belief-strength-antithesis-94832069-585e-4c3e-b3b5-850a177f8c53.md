# Does practical encroachment mistake decision significance for belief strength?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 94832069-585e-4c3e-b3b5-850a177f8c53
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Encroachment Theory](pragmatic-encroachment-theory-synthesis-66e6d98a-2899-4b80-b05b-15066f046ddd.md)

## Content

**Consider a driver deciding whether to fill up with gas. Knowing the tank level is sufficient for a short trip, they feel confident.**, **If a longer trip is planned, the stakes rise considerably, and hesitation ensues.**, **It is mistaken for the increased decision significance to affect the strength of their belief in the gas level.**
