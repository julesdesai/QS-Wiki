## Content

**Knowledge is true belief for which the believer deserves credit**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief is credited to their own intellectual virtue and not luck**, **A belief is not knowledge if most of the credit for its truth belongs to luck**, **A person knows a proposition if they believe it and deserve credit due to the role of their intellectual virtue in sustaining the belief's truth**
