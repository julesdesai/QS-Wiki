# Virtue Epistemic Coherentism

**Parent:** [Can a false proposition be part of a coherent belief system?](can-a-false-proposition-be-part-of-a-coherent-belief-system-antithesis-bdb6f3f2-ad95-4d9e-a087-d44dfdd47bd2.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief arises from intellectual virtue**, **For an intellectual virtue, the belief must coherently fit in the believer's system**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that it is believed truly and virtuously in a coherent system**
