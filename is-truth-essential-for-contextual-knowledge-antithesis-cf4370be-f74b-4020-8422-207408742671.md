# Is truth essential for contextual knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** cf4370be-f74b-4020-8422-207408742671
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualist Foundationalism](contextualist-foundationalism-synthesis-b0409110-d832-4fdb-9fe3-2e01026d1f88.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where a person has a belief that is contextually justified but false. Intuitively, we might judge that the person has knowledge if context support seems wholly adequate.**, **There is a plausible principle that knowledge requires truth. This principle with the case challenges the necessity of truth for knowledge.**
