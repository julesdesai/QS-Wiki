# How does constructivist coherentism deal with the challenge of radical skepticism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 150aa459-0889-4f1d-80c7-46174f2f62b0
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Constructivist Coherentism](constructivist-coherentism-synthesis-df66a247-3331-4fc3-9810-0103a6210363.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that a response to radical skepticism should show how beliefs can be reliably tied to external reality instead of merely being a network of internal consistency.**, **Consider a scenario in which a consistent but internally isolated belief system prevails, which radical skepticism claims is sufficient for truth. In such a scenario, intuitively this seems to undermine attempts to anchor beliefs to external evidence and reality, thus not addressing skepticism effectively.**
