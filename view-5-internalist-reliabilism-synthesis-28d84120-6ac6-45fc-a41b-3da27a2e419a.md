## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed by a reliable cognitive process and the person is aware of the reliability**, **For knowledge, a person should hold some internal justification that their belief is reliable**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is formed by a reliable process, and they are aware of the reliability**
