## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed by a reliable process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief could not easily have been false in nearby possible worlds**, **A person knows a proposition if they believe it, the proposition is true, and the belief is formed by a reliable process that ensures the belief would not easily be false**, **A reliable process is one that produces true beliefs more often than false ones**
