# How does the theory handle subconscious perception?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9741a4c6-74cf-4ea7-bfc8-283b25c7dd49
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Naive Realism](naive-realism-synthesis-a575a970-7874-461b-b9b5-9de679546681.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where a person subliminally perceives an object, influencing their behavior without conscious awareness. Intuitively, they have some form of perception without direct experience being consciously constitutive.**, **If perception can occur without conscious awareness, then perception does not necessarily amount to direct acquaintance as naive realism stipulates.**
