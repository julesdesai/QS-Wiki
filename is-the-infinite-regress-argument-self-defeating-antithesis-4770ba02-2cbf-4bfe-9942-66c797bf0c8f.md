# Is the infinite regress argument self-defeating?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 4770ba02-2cbf-4bfe-9942-66c797bf0c8f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism in Epistemic Justification](infinitism-in-epistemic-justification-synthesis-7e8bc411-aa66-4d88-bdc9-2fb4f4857304.md)

## Content

**Suppose a chain of infinite reasons is required for justification. Intuitively, this requirement suggests that each reason lacks sufficient strength alone, which undermines the entire justification.**, **If each individual reason in a chain is insufficient, then a belief justified by infinitely insufficient reasons appears unjustified.**
