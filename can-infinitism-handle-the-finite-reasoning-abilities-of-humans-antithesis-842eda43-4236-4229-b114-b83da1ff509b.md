# Can Infinitism handle the finite reasoning abilities of humans?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 842eda43-4236-4229-b114-b83da1ff509b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism](infinitism-synthesis-dd7098a7-016a-4f9d-9b36-944f2be4f1f4.md)

## Content

**Human cognitive capacities are finite and limited in their ability to grasp or construct infinite chains of reasoning.**, **Beliefs require justification in a way that is accessible to human reason, given human cognitive limitations.**, **It is implausible that finite human minds can possess or comprehend infinite, non-repeating chains of justification.**
