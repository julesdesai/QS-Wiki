## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have a justified belief in the proposition**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the person to believe the proposition**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have a justified, true belief in the proposition**
