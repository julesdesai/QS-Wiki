## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for their belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have a reliably justified belief in the proposition, and their justification would be revised or retracted in light of reliable counter-evidence**
