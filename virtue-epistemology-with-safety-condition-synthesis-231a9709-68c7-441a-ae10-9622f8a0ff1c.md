## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief results from the exercise of intellectual virtue**, **It is necessary for knowledge that, had the person believed the proposition through their intellectual virtues, the proposition would not easily have been false**
