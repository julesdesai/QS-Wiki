**Parent:** [Does optional belief diminish the emotive power of knowing?](does-optional-belief-diminish-the-emotive-power-of-knowing-antithesis-2f300dd3-6062-46cc-bca4-21e66162ca57.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their justification serves an epistemic role analogous to belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is not necessary for them to believe the proposition**, **A person knows a proposition if it is true and its epistemic role is fulfilled by justification**
