## Content

**Knowledge requires having true belief undefeated by counter-evidence**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief results from a reliable cognitive process leading to true belief**, **A belief formed by a reliable process retains its status as knowledge unless defeaters occur in the operational process**, **Misleading evidence post-formation does not itself constitute a defeater if the process remains reliable**
