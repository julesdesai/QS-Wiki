## Content

**Knowledge is justified true belief produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **A cognitive process is reliable if it tends to produce true beliefs**, **A process is reliable internally if it can be evaluated based on introspective access by the person**, **For a person to know a proposition, the person must believe the proposition, the proposition must be true, the belief must result from a reliable cognitive process**
