# Knowledge as Coherent System of True Beliefs

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 225e4aaa-1f41-4769-a43f-cd4b28fcd980
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is truth an independent requirement for knowledge?](is-truth-an-independent-requirement-for-knowledge-antithesis-fbfddc21-b95a-4615-adaf-dc4d5b0ded9e.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres with a system of other true beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, it is true, and coheres with a system of other true beliefs**
