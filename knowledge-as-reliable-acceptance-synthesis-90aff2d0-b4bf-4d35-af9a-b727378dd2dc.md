# Knowledge as Reliable Acceptance

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 90aff2d0-b4bf-4d35-af9a-b727378dd2dc
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does practical consequence guarantee knowledge?](does-practical-consequence-guarantee-knowledge-antithesis-59173d23-ea12-45ff-96f2-f0102508e8be.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they accept the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the acceptance is produced by a reliable process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they accept the proposition, the proposition is true, and the acceptance is produced by a reliable process**
