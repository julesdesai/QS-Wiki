# What if accessible justification leads to infinite regress?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a1a9f044-0d25-4f93-b6c5-d12227150e27
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Hybrid Internalism](hybrid-internalism-synthesis-3d9ce0d9-6ba2-4665-a054-ed06708d6ee0.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that every justification can be fully accessed without leading to further questioning. For accessible justification to be complete, each level of justification should itself be justifiable. This introduces an infinite regress problem because each justification would require another justification ad infinitum.**
