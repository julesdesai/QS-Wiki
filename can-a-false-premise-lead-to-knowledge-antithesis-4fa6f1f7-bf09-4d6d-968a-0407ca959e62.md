# Can a false premise lead to knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 4fa6f1f7-bf09-4d6d-968a-0407ca959e62
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Responsibilist Virtue Epistemology](responsibilist-virtue-epistemology-synthesis-d65854c6-8623-4bd4-aed3-f2a4bfae69c9.md)

## Content

**A belief might be responsibly formed from a false premise but result in a true conclusion.**, **The belief's truth might seem disconnected from the reliability of the underlying reasoning.**, **It is debatable whether this constitutes genuine knowledge.**
