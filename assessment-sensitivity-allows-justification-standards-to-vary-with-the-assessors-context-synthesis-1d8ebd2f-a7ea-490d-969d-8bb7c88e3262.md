# Assessment sensitivity allows justification standards to vary with the assessor's context

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Do different domains require differing standards of justification?](do-different-domains-require-differing-standards-of-justification-antithesis-02bf57ab-a9a3-438b-97ec-0058dbd31ca4.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the standard of justification is determined by the context of the assessor**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and meet the justification standard set by the assessor's context**
