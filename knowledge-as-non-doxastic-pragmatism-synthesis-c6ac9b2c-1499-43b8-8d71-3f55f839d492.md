## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they pragmatically accept the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the pragmatic acceptance aids in achieving one's goals or solving practical problems**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they pragmatically accept the proposition, the proposition is true, and the pragmatic acceptance aids in goal achievement or problem-solving**
