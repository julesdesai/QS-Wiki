# Do subjective elements in belief construction compromise the potential for objective knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 6a054bea-4569-457a-823d-0b3527f3b56b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Constructivist Coherentism](constructivist-coherentism-synthesis-df66a247-3331-4fc3-9810-0103a6210363.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that subjective elements embedded in belief construction can always align with the dictates of objective reality, potentially leading to discrepancies.**, **Suppose we have a case in which subjective preferences or biases significantly shape the belief construction process. Intuitively, such a process seems unreliable as a source of objective knowledge due to its susceptibility to personal bias.**
