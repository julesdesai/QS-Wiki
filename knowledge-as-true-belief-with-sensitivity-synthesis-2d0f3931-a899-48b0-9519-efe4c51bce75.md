# Knowledge as True Belief with Sensitivity

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 2d0f3931-a899-48b0-9519-efe4c51bce75
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can belief be true without knowledge?](can-belief-be-true-without-knowledge-antithesis-655d7a8e-1741-4ddf-85c7-a393d2dd8421.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is sensitive to the truth of the proposition**, **Sensitivity means that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**
