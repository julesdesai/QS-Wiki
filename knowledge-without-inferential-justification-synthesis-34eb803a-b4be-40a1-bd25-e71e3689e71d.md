## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified**, **The justification of a belief does not necessarily involve inferential processes, allowing for non-inferential justification through direct intuition**
