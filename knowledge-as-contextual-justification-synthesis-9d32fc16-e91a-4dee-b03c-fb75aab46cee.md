# Knowledge as Contextual Justification

**Parent:** [Is knowledge possible through non-reflective processes?](is-knowledge-possible-through-non-reflective-processes-antithesis-39a7c622-c865-4489-ae13-d8c217d7bed2.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is justified given the specific contextual factors at play**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is contextually justified**
