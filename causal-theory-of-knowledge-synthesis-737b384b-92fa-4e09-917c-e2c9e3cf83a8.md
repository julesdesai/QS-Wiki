## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief is causally connected to the fact it is true**, **The causal connection should be of the right sort such that it reliably leads to true beliefs**
