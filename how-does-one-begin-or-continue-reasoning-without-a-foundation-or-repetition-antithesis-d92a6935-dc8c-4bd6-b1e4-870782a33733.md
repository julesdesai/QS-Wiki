# How does one begin or continue reasoning without a foundation or repetition?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d92a6935-dc8c-4bd6-b1e4-870782a33733
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism](infinitism-synthesis-fd5e1271-f2df-4333-a434-50b9d95350fd.md)

## Content

**Reasoning typically begins with foundational beliefs and uses repetitions or similar elements for coherence, which contradicts an infinite non-repeating chain of reasons.**, **If an infinite series cannot repeat, starting or continuing reasoning without foundational or recurring elements becomes implausible.**, **An infinite chain of reasons without repetition poses a challenge because real reasoning practices involve recurring principles or beliefs, which this requirement negates.**
