# Does pragmatic justification conflate practical benefits and truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e2579389-c7b0-490a-8008-33ecba314259
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Justification Theory](pragmatic-justification-theory-synthesis-838d6a9b-2af0-4e99-882f-62ebc82bed50.md)

## Content

**Consider a belief that yields beneficial practical results but lacks a reliable truth basis. Intuitively, practical benefits should not replace truth as a criterion for knowledge.**, **Equating practical outcomes with truth can lead beliefs that are beneficial but untrue to be wrongly accepted as knowledge.**
