# Does differing reliability across domains impact knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2423f0e3-49a3-4fef-abf1-b7b66a8e36b3
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilist Theory of Knowledge](reliabilist-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-fec1f444-460d-4835-9da9-5fd5405f3ea1.md)

## Content

**Consider a person whose cognitive process is reliable for mathematics but not for literature interpretation. If asked about a literary metaphor while using the same cognitive process, intuitively, they might lack knowledge despite the reliability in a different domain.**, **A cognitive process reliably producing true beliefs in one domain doesn't ensure similar outcomes across different domains of knowledge.**
