# Do private experiences count as knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0e22187e-2228-4f64-88f1-1d382855837a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Inter-subjective Phenomenalism](inter-subjective-phenomenalism-synthesis-c00af758-5073-4931-9585-2f560dddd897.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person experiences a vivid dream. In such a scenario, intuitively, the person knows their subjective experience of dreaming, even though it does not involve any inter-subjective agreement or external verification.**, **It is implausible that knowledge of personal experiences, such as dreams or internal thoughts, requires inter-subjective verification.**
