# Is context the primary determinant of justified belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9cf9a6e1-6ccd-4624-b86b-1735b4ea18c4
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Epistemic Contextualism Summary](epistemic-contextualism-summary-synthesis-463c65ed-13fd-4c3b-8fbb-fc52a847cb71.md)

## Content

**There is a principle that justification of beliefs primarily stems from evidence.**, **If context is necessary beyond evidence for justified belief, this makes evidence seem insufficient on its own, which is implausible.**
