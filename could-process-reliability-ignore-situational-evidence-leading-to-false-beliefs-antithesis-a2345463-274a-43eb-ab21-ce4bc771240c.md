# Could process reliability ignore situational evidence leading to false beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a2345463-274a-43eb-ab21-ce4bc771240c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Process Reliabilism with Evidence Sensitivity](process-reliabilism-with-evidence-sensitivity-synthesis-e3e15d29-f0bc-4d87-b0aa-fbeab510c604.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a belief is produced by a process that is generally reliable but ignores a significant piece of evidence due to situational factors. Intuitively, we would judge the belief does not constitute knowledge.**, **A reliable process that generally produces true beliefs when evidence is present can still miss situational evidence that is critical for correctly forming beliefs in certain cases.**
