# Can pragmatic justification be used to justify false beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 92b0aaeb-c1f8-493b-9579-58fcbb4f8dab
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Justification Theory](pragmatic-justification-theory-synthesis-838d6a9b-2af0-4e99-882f-62ebc82bed50.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person believes a false proposition because it leads to better outcomes in their life. In such a scenario, intuitively, this person seems pragmatically justified in their belief.**, **A belief that is pragmatically justified, if false, should not be considered knowledge by most definitions.**
