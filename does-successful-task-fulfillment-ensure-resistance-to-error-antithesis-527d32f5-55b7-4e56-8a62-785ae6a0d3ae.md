# Does successful task fulfillment ensure resistance to error?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 527d32f5-55b7-4e56-8a62-785ae6a0d3ae
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Epistemic Functionalism with Cognitive Task Fulfillment](epistemic-functionalism-with-cognitive-task-fulfillment-synthesis-3edd3165-e9fd-4e05-81df-6b63dbbb89eb.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where a person uses a reliable method to navigate but is unaware that their tool is malfunctioning half the time. Intuitively, this uncertainty undermines their knowledge of their location even when they reach their destination.**, **It seems implausible to view task fulfillment in such a scenario as knowledge due to vulnerability to error.**
