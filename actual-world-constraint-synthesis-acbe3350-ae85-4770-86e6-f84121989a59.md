## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief must not be false in the actual world**, **A belief is safe if it is true in the actual world and in most nearby possible worlds where it is held**
