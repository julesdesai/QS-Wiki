# Does the reliability of a subconscious process imply truth without exception?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 97b396a0-c8c7-4099-88d1-00ac1f53213b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [No False Grounds Theory](no-false-grounds-theory-synthesis-37dfac32-afee-4ea8-9a67-23e7efbc2507.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that all reliable subconscious processes are free from false premises.**, **Consider a scenario where a subconscious process is generally reliable but happens to introduce a false premise in a particular instance. Intuitively, such cases undermine the notion that reliability alone ensures absence of false premises.**
