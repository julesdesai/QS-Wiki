# Is the sufficient condition of evidence too demanding for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 8a78b7f4-0210-4697-88ca-711a3a21558f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Evidentialist Theory of Knowledge](evidentialist-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-5b3e9103-4910-4e24-af7b-eaff612fb6ae.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that for every instance of knowledge, the evidential basis must be immune to any possible inaccuracies or coincidental truths.**, **Consider a scenario in which a medical diagnosis based on comprehensive tests turns out correct, yet one of the tests was faulty. Intuitively, the doctor is still said to know the diagnosis.**
