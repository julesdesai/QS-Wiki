# Can Infinitism accommodate the closure principle?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ced05163-cdbf-4346-8230-d06664f95b31
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism](infinitism-synthesis-dd7098a7-016a-4f9d-9b36-944f2be4f1f4.md)

## Content

**The closure principle states that if a person knows a proposition and knows that this proposition entails another, they also know the entailed proposition.**, **Infinite chains of justification may disrupt or complicate the closure relationship between linked propositions.**, **Handling closure becomes problematic when infinite aspects are introduced, since the entailment process must maintain continuity through infinite steps.**
