## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **Knowledge results from consistently applied cognitive competences**, **Cognitive competences are stable abilities that reliably produce true beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have a true belief resulting from consistent competence**
