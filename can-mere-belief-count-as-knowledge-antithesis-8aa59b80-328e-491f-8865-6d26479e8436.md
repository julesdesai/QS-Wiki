# Can mere belief count as knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 8aa59b80-328e-491f-8865-6d26479e8436
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitist Coherentism](infinitist-coherentism-synthesis-c961c647-ce7e-4676-a333-4ee929ef8887.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a person believes a true proposition without any awareness or consideration of supporting reasons. Intuitively, this does not seem to be a case of knowledge.**, **It is implausible that mere belief alone, even if true, without a process of verification or justification, should qualify as knowledge.**
