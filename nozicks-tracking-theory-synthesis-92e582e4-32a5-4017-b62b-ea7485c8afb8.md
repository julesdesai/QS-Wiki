## Content

**A person knows a proposition if their belief tracks the truth across possible worlds**, **A belief tracks the truth if in nearby possible worlds, if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **Knowledge allows for sensitivity to truth conditions in other scenarios**, **Accessible justification is not a requirement for this process**
