## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **It is necessary that the reliability of the cognitive process involves the agent exercising intellectual virtue**, **For a belief to count as knowledge, the truth must not be due to luck**
