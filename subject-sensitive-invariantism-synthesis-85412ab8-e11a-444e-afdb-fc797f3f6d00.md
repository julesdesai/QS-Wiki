## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the knowledge depends on personal stakes and practical interests of the subject, maintaining consistent standards within this framework**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and personal stakes do not override the truth conditions**
