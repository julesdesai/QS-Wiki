# How do dreams and illusions influence the reliability of cognitive faculties?

## Content

**Consider scenarios where dreams, mistaken for reality, lead someone to form true beliefs. Intuitively, these beliefs aren't viewed as knowledge because they originate from unreliable processes.**, **Dreams and illusions that cause genuine belief undermining reliability question whether such processes adhere to a design plan yielding knowledge.**
