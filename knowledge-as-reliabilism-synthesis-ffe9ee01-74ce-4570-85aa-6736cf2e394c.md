# Knowledge as Reliabilism

**Parent:** [Do all coherent systems have equal epistemic value?](do-all-coherent-systems-have-equal-epistemic-value-antithesis-a39cb3d6-e367-4e47-99b4-34f2cf19a827.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process that generally leads to true beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process that generally leads to true beliefs**
