# Does the absence of justification render belief arbitrary?

**Parent:** [Externalism with Reliabilism](externalism-with-reliabilism-synthesis-c10e5c74-1dbc-479d-ba5e-469176c5d537.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that holding a belief without any accessible justification does not render the belief arbitrary.**, **Consider a scenario where an individual forms a belief through an arbitrary or random method that accidentally yields a true belief. Intuitively, such beliefs do not qualify as knowledge.**
