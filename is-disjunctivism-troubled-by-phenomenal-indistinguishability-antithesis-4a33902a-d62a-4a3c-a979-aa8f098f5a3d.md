# Is disjunctivism troubled by phenomenal indistinguishability?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 4a33902a-d62a-4a3c-a979-aa8f098f5a3d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Disjunctivism Summary](disjunctivism-summary-synthesis-72a215c5-4d26-4e6e-86ab-383aabdf6290.md)

## Content

**Consider two cases: one involving genuine perception of a cat and another a hallucination of the same cat with no differences in appearance. Intuitively, both seem phenomenally indistinguishable, bringing into question whether perception can be directly linked to external reality as disjunctivism suggests.**
