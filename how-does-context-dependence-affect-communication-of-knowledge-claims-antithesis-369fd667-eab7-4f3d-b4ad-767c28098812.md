# How does context-dependence affect communication of knowledge claims?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 369fd667-eab7-4f3d-b4ad-767c28098812
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualist Theory](contextualist-theory-synthesis-57f2b09b-3c03-4991-b6b3-1cfd3022a37a.md)

## Content

**Suppose two people are conversing about a scientific theory, where one uses a high standard for knowledge and the other a low standard, leading to miscommunication. Intuitively, effective communication depends on shared standards, which are compromised by context-dependence.**, **A communicative principle suggests that for effective knowledge transmission, discourse requires common standards. Context-dependence challenges shared understanding and effective communication in discourse.**
