# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with Anti-Luck Condition

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 05a85094-3da3-44f6-abba-834ca53f35e2
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is luck sometimes involved in knowledge?](is-luck-sometimes-involved-in-knowledge-antithesis-aef14230-14b1-48b2-95f1-87181b6ae403.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the formation of the belief does not involve luck**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for it, the proposition is true, and the formation of the belief does not involve luck**
