**Parent:** [Can an intellectually virtuous belief be justified without resulting in knowledge?](can-an-intellectually-virtuous-belief-be-justified-without-resulting-in-knowledge-antithesis-c23e48be-dc76-40fa-ad95-7a9201a2c441.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is a result of a reliable exercise of intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief results from a reliable exercise of intellectual virtue**
