# No-False-Belief View

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge ever be unsafe and still be knowledge?](can-knowledge-ever-be-unsafe-and-still-be-knowledge-antithesis-fbda2b6a-43b0-4131-8b52-de09eca812d3.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that none of their auxiliary beliefs relevant to the proposition are false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and none of their auxiliary beliefs relevant to the proposition are false**
