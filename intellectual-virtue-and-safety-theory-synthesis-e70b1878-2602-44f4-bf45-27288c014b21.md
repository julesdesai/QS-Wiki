## Content

**Knowledge is true belief arising from intellectual virtue and satisfies the safety condition**, **An intellectual virtue is a trait that reliably leads to true beliefs**, **The safety condition requires that in nearly all nearby possible worlds where one's belief is formed in the same way, the belief remains true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it must be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, they must believe it**, **Beliefs resulting from intellectual virtues and satisfying the safety condition are justified**
