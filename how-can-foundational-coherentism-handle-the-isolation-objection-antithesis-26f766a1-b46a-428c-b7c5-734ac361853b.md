# How can Foundational Coherentism handle the Isolation Objection?

**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a system of beliefs is internally coherent yet completely detached from empirical reality. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that the beliefs are unjustified due to lack of connection to the world.**, **A plausible principle is that justified beliefs must bear some relation to the actual world. This principle together with the scenario entails that cohering with a system of beliefs is insufficient for justification if the beliefs don't connect with the world.**
