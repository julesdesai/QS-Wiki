## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the cognitive faculties producing the belief function properly in a suitable environment according to a design plan**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief results from properly functioning faculties in the appropriate environment**
