# Can changes in intention alter knowledge attribution?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 161cf351-7393-40cb-ac6d-72d4f6a6bf13
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Encroachment with Influence of Practical Stakes](pragmatic-encroachment-with-influence-of-practical-stakes-synthesis-1e83d295-b88f-4064-889e-097b4fb938ca.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where someone fully intends to travel abroad based on knowing passport requirements, then suddenly changes intention to cancel the trip. Intuitively, the switch in intention should not affect whether the person knows the passport requirements.**, **It is implausible that changes in personal intent or practical stakes affecting those intentions should influence knowledge attribution concerning static facts.**
