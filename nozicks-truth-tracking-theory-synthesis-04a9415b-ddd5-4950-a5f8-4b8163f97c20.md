## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief tracks the truth**, **Truth-tracking involves being sensitive to both the proposition being true and it being false**
