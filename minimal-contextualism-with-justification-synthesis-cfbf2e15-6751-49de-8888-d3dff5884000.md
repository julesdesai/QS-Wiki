## Content

**Knowledge minimally depends on the context of the knower**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **The variability of knowledge standards is managed to avoid communication disruption**
