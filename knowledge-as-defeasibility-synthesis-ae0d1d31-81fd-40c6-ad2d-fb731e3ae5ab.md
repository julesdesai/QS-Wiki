# Knowledge as Defeasibility

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Does truth alone adequately distinguish justified belief from knowledge in misleading environments?](does-truth-alone-adequately-distinguish-justified-belief-from-knowledge-in-misleading-environments-antithesis-ceae63fd-e009-4272-9302-9d3ac2eba879.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there be no defeaters, or overriding evidence, that undermine their justification**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for the proposition which is undefeated, and the proposition is true**
