# Are stakes-sensitive beliefs still genuine knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 37bb3b31-c169-480c-853c-a321477380b8
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Encroachment Theory](pragmatic-encroachment-theory-synthesis-66e6d98a-2899-4b80-b05b-15066f046ddd.md)

## Content

**Suppose a graduate student writes a paper, knowing the historical date of an event with high confidence under normal low-stake conditions.**, **Discovering that a financial grant depends on the accuracy of this date introduces high stakes.**, **It’s counterintuitive to think that her knowledge of the date could evaporate merely due to this added importance.**
