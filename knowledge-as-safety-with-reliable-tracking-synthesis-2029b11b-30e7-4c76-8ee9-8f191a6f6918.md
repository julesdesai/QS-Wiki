# Knowledge as Safety with Reliable Tracking

**Parent:** [Does the condition of belief safety conflict with our intuitions about ordinary knowledge?](does-the-condition-of-belief-safety-conflict-with-our-intuitions-about-ordinary-knowledge-antithesis-39c463ff-3638-450a-88ad-d300eca57fc2.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief to reliably track the truth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief reliably tracks the truth**
