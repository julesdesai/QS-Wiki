# Is knowledge possible without access to a broader coherent system?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** bc16c2fa-4189-4c1e-b466-ae5220d01914
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Fallibilist Coherentism](fallibilist-coherentism-synthesis-f085a715-bc4b-46dd-bc93-6874997728cd.md)

## Content

**A person in isolation with limited access to information and unable to verify coherence beyond their immediate beliefs may lack knowledge.**, **Intuitively, without the ability to connect individual beliefs to a larger system, the truth of isolated beliefs seems insufficient for claiming knowledge.**
