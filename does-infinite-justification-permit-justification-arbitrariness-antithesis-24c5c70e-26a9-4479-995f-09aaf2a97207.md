# Does infinite justification permit justification arbitrariness?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 24c5c70e-26a9-4479-995f-09aaf2a97207
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualist Infinitism](contextualist-infinitism-synthesis-74ebcf03-3de8-4e28-950c-dc2eefc9dc13.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where the next reason in an infinite chain is chosen arbitrarily.**, **In such a scenario, intuitively the chain's justificatory power is compromised by randomness.**, **It is implausible that arbitrary selections within a chain can provide solid knowledge justification.**
