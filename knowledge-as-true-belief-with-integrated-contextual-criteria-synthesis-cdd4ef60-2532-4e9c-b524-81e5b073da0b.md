## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is justified through an integration of pragmatic and epistemic criteria within context**, **Both pragmatic and epistemic factors are necessary for full justification**
