# Knowledge as Understanding

**Parent:** [Can beliefs unsupported by reasons or understanding still be knowledge?](can-beliefs-unsupported-by-reasons-or-understanding-still-be-knowledge-antithesis-dae3fdd6-b41e-461a-8166-5f5951e8e0f3.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person understands the proposition and the context that makes it true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and they have the understanding of both the proposition and the context that makes it true**
