# Are functional outcomes definitive for knowledge of future experiences?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ac974504-4cb9-4a17-a91c-680fa531c187
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Functional Phenomenalism](functional-phenomenalism-synthesis-2d380001-bba7-40c1-89a8-df1e50353485.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person predicts rain based on weather patterns but the weather remains sunny. Intuitively, we would judge that knowledge about future weather goes beyond functional prediction when the outcome differs.**, **It is implausible that functional predictability captures all future contingencies and their role in knowledge.**
