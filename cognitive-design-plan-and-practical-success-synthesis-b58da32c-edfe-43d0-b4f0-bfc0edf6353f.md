## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that actions according to a cognitive design plan are successful**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their cognitive faculties are functioning properly**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is true, actions following a cognitive design plan are successful, and their cognitive faculties are functioning properly**
