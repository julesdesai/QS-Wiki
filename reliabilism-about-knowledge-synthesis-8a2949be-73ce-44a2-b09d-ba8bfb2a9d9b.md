## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person's belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **A cognitive process is reliable if it produces a high proportion of true beliefs over false ones**
