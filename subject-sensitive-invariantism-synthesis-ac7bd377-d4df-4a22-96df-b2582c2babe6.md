## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **The standards for justification depend on the subject's practical interests but remain objectively defined**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief aligns with their practical interests, is justified under uniform criteria, and the proposition is true**
