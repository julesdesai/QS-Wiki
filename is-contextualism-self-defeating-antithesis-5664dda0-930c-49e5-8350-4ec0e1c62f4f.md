# Is contextualism self-defeating?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5664dda0-930c-49e5-8350-4ec0e1c62f4f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism](contextualism-synthesis-c0d03a39-c775-403c-87e2-d078dab04731.md)

## Content

**If contextualism claims that all knowledge depends on context, then this claim itself must depend on a particular context.**, **This means that the truth of contextualism could be context-dependent, leading to a self-defeating position if it denies its own universal applicability.**, **The intuition is that a theory of knowledge should not undermine its own validity.**
