# Can environmental factors override intellectual virtues?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 53fc139e-caa0-48f6-a1b3-cd16d18bca7a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-f60d76d9-bfb9-483c-b262-fa52975172be.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person exercises intellectual virtues by seeking varied perspectives, but misinformation is overwhelmingly prevalent in their environment.**, **In such a scenario, they might still end up with false beliefs, indicating that contextual factors can override the influence of intellectual virtues.**
