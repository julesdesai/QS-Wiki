## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person claims to know they are in pain solely through introspection, without external verification. Intuitively, one can know such mental states directly without tracking external truths across scenarios.**, **According to tracking theory, knowledge requires belief to track truth across possible scenarios. Introspective knowledge appears to lack external scenario tracking, suggesting an exception to the theory's criteria for knowledge.**
