## Content

**Knowledge is true belief that results from a cognitive process with a variety of successful outcomes**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief results from a cognitive process with many successful outcomes**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe it, the proposition is true, and the cognitive process has a variety of successful outcomes**
