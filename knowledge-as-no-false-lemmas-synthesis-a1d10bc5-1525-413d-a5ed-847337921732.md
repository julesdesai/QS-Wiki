# Knowledge as No False Lemmas

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Are intellectual virtues always necessary for knowledge?](are-intellectual-virtues-always-necessary-for-knowledge-antithesis-81c8583a-7f60-4574-a314-22739450a569.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that no false propositions are used in the reasoning process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, the proposition is true, and no false propositions are used in the reasoning process**
