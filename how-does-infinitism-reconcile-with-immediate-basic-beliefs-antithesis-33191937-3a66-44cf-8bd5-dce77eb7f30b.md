# How does infinitism reconcile with immediate basic beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 33191937-3a66-44cf-8bd5-dce77eb7f30b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism in Epistemic Justification](infinitism-in-epistemic-justification-synthesis-7e8bc411-aa66-4d88-bdc9-2fb4f4857304.md)

## Content

**Immediate basic beliefs are intuitive starting points not requiring further justification, which contradicts the concept of an infinite regress of reasons.**, **The existence of immediate, self-evident truths suggests that not all justification requires an infinite chain of reasons.**
