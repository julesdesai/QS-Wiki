## Content

**Knowledge is justified true belief that is undefeated by any defeater**, **A person knows a proposition if they have no true defeater for their belief**, **A defeater is any true proposition that undermines the justification of the belief**, **For a justified true belief to be knowledge, no relevant defeater must be present**
