# Knowledge as No False Grounds

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge exist in environments of pervasive deception?](can-knowledge-exist-in-environments-of-pervasive-deception-antithesis-1a424b41-186d-46c4-a987-134559e42fa2.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person's basis for the belief does not include any falsehoods**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their basis for the belief does not include any falsehoods**
