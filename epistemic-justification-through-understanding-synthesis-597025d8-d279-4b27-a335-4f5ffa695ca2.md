## Content

**Knowledge is true belief that arises from intellectual virtue which reliably avoids error**, **Knowledge requires epistemic justification through understanding**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief arises from an intellectually virtuous process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for their epistemic justification to include understanding of the truth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have both epistemic justification through understanding and their belief arises from virtuous reliability**
