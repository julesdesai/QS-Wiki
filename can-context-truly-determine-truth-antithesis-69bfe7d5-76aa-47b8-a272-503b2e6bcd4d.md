# Can context truly determine truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 69bfe7d5-76aa-47b8-a272-503b2e6bcd4d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualist Coherentism](contextualist-coherentism-synthesis-598dc05f-03d6-4aeb-b0db-fa7173390f96.md)

## Content

**In a scientific discussion, the context holds that objective facts determine truth, not social or cultural context. This implies that in some domains, truth is not subject to context.**, **If truth is not context dependent in areas like mathematics or empirical science, then the claim that truth universally depends on context is challenged.**
