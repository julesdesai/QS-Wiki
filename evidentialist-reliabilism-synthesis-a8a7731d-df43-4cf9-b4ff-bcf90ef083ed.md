## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief results from a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is supported by adequate evidence**, **A reliable process and evidence together constitute knowledge**
