## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres with a consistent and comprehensive system of beliefs**, **For some foundational beliefs to exist that do not depend on coherence with other beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that foundational beliefs cohere with other beliefs and together support the proposition**
