# Knowledge as Safe Belief

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is inferential knowledge vulnerable to epistemic luck?](is-inferential-knowledge-vulnerable-to-epistemic-luck-antithesis-95404427-203e-4599-99a3-7043a369cf0c.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is safe, meaning it would not easily be false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is safe**
