# Knowledge as Inferential Causal Theory

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** e5441697-33dd-449a-93b9-caff9225be7b
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can beliefs caused by inferential processes be knowledge?](can-beliefs-caused-by-inferential-processes-be-knowledge-antithesis-54e2de14-a400-46cb-8322-3ba4cac60be5.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is caused by an appropriate inferential process linking to the true proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the belief is true, and the belief is caused by an appropriate inferential process linking to the true proposition**
