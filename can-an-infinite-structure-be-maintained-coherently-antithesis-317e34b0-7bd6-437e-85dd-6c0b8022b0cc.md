# Can an infinite structure be maintained coherently?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 317e34b0-7bd6-437e-85dd-6c0b8022b0cc
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitist Coherentism](infinitist-coherentism-synthesis-2a4efbd0-0f1f-4a01-bfa6-1ecf016eb1e0.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that coherence requires maintaining consistent relations among propositions. This principle together with the need for infinite connections implies that maintaining coherence in an infinite structure is impractical.**, **It is implausible that coherent relations can persist without contradiction in an infinite web of beliefs.**
