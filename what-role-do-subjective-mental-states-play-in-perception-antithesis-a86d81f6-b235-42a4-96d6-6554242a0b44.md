# What role do subjective mental states play in perception?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a86d81f6-b235-42a4-96d6-6554242a0b44
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Intersubjective Phenomenalism](intersubjective-phenomenalism-synthesis-0a89d160-2bee-479f-b8d3-046de6da5ef6.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that subjective mental states, such as emotions and biases, do not influence perception.**, **Suppose we have a case in which individuals perceive the same event but interpret it differently due to differing emotional states. Intuitively, we either have to say that their agreement is necessary for knowledge or that individual perception can suffice. If we say agreement is necessary, then knowledge may be unjustly denied when perception is clear to an individual.**
