## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A person knows a proposition if they would not believe it if it were false**, **This sensitivity condition ensures that the belief is responsive to the truth**
