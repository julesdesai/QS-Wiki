# Does the requirement of unconscious inference threaten autonomy?

**Parent:** [Indirect Realism with a Role for Unconscious Inference](indirect-realism-with-a-role-for-unconscious-inference-synthesis-78a86651-c747-4945-9c1d-0df1bc77c499.md)

## Content

**If knowing is heavily dependent on unconscious inference, it implies that an individual's autonomy in forming knowledge is reduced, since cognitive processes operate independently of direct control.**, **There is a plausible principle that essential human autonomy relies on conscious awareness and control, which is undermined if knowledge formation is reliant on unconscious processes.**
