## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed by an intellectual virtue**, **An intellectual virtue is a reliable cognitive character trait that produces true beliefs in a wide range of situations**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is formed by an intellectual virtue**
