## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have objective justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their justification stands scrutiny outside communal agreement**
