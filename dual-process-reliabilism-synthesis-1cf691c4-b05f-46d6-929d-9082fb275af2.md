**Parent:** [Can memory's fallibility challenge the sufficiency of reliable processes for knowledge?](can-memorys-fallibility-challenge-the-sufficiency-of-reliable-processes-for-knowledge-antithesis-f97b1ca9-6c1c-4eac-a3eb-3505d1e80e09.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that both initial and recall processes are reliable**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and both initial and recall processes are reliable**
