# Can erroneous causal links provide a basis for supposed knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** bfa36602-dd3d-4715-89ac-9307dfa0d2d0
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Causal Theory of Knowledge](causal-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-916d423a-1723-4911-b4ab-5dfda1d6916a.md)

## Content

**Imagine a case where Karen believes her train will arrive on time because she misread the schedule, and coincidentally, the train does arrive on time. Her belief is causally linked to a wrong fact, suggesting an uprooted connection between true knowledge and causal correctness.**
