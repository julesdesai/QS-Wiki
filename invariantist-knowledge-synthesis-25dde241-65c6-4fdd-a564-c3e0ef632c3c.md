**Parent:** [Does contextualism undermine the normative role of knowledge?](does-contextualism-undermine-the-normative-role-of-knowledge-antithesis-6a6df525-ed48-423b-a811-8bef992be55b.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified by stable standards**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is justified by stable standards**
