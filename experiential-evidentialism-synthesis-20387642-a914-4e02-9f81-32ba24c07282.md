## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their direct experience suffices as evidence for certain kinds of propositions without additional evidence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief in the proposition is appropriately aligned with their experiential evidence**
