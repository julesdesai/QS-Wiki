# Is a true belief sufficient for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 36ceeed4-95ed-4121-85c0-b642c339b3d8
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Moderate Contextualism](moderate-contextualism-synthesis-a2419722-fa3b-4f65-af9b-d94e8c32837e.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person believes a proposition due to mere guesswork, and it turns out to be true. Intuitively, we would judge that the person does not know the proposition, indicating that true belief alone is not sufficient for knowledge.**
