## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief was formed through a process that would not easily produce a false belief in similar situations**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief was formed through a process that would not easily produce a false belief in similar situations**
