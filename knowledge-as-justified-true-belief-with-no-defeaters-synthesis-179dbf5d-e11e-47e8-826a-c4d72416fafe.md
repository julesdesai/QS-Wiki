# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with No Defeaters

**Parent:** [Is intellectual virtue too demanding for ordinary knowledge claims?](is-intellectual-virtue-too-demanding-for-ordinary-knowledge-claims-antithesis-6a0a8b27-6737-40ef-b86a-b1037145826a.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no defeaters for the justification**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, they have justification, and there are no defeaters**
