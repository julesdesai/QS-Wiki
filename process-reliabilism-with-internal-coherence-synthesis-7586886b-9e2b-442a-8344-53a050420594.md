## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed by a reliable process**, **A person knows a proposition if they can internally coherently explain why the process is reliable**, **A reliable process is one that produces true beliefs more often than false**, **Justification involves both reliability and internal coherence of belief formation**
