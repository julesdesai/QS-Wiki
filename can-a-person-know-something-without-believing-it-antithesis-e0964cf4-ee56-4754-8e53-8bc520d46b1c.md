# Can a person know something without believing it?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e0964cf4-ee56-4754-8e53-8bc520d46b1c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism without Belief](reliabilism-without-belief-synthesis-5ad6402e-0bf8-4ba4-9e47-6461ce29b289.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person is presented with substantial evidence for a proposition but remains skeptical or agnostic, never forming a belief either way. Intuitively, we would judge that this person does not know the proposition, despite the evidence reliably supporting it.**
