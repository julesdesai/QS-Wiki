# Is scientific knowledge purely perceptual?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9a85cd9f-75c9-4116-817b-8af23804677f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Inference-Based Phenomenalism](inference-based-phenomenalism-synthesis-3c5287e4-c850-454f-b68f-cef5a0c2976c.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which scientists derive a theory based on experiments that cannot be directly perceived but inferred. Intuitively, they have knowledge of the theory.**, **It is implausible that all scientific knowledge relies solely on perceptual experiences.**
