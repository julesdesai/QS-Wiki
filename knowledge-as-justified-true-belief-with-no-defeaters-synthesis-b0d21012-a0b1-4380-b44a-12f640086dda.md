## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is justified**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no defeaters to their justified belief in the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, their belief is justified, and there are no defeaters**
