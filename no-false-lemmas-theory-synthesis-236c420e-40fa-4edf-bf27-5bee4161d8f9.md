## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person believes the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is not derived from any false premises or lemmas**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that these no false lemmas conditions hold**
