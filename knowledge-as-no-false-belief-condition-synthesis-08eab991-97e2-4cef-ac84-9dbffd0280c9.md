# Knowledge as No-False-Belief Condition

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 08eab991-97e2-4cef-ac84-9dbffd0280c9
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can sincere belief equate to knowledge?](can-sincere-belief-equate-to-knowledge-antithesis-3d797309-fad9-47de-80b7-610f057ba50d.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, the proposition is true, and none of their beliefs about related propositions are false**
