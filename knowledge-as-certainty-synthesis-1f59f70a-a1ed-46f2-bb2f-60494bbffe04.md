# Knowledge as Certainty

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person's belief in the proposition is certain, meaning it is impossible for them to be wrong**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the person believes it, the proposition is true, and the belief is certain**
