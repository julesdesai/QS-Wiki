# No-False-Belief Theory

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 7576f949-9ea2-4d57-8c3d-be17dc972fae
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can Deductive Inferences Create Knowledge?](can-deductive-inferences-create-knowledge-antithesis-68d49a8e-08d2-40dd-b76b-bc024341d185.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief was not based on any false propositions**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe it, it is true, and the belief was not dependent on any false propositions**
