# How can one be said to 'have' an infinite set of reasons?

**Node ID:** 45e37f43-6c10-4cbb-bc83-44ffbe36985e

**Terminal:** No

## Content

**Having reasons typically implies possessing something that is finite and complete.**, **The concept of an infinite regress suggests a never-completed task.**, **Claiming to have such infinite reasons seems at odds with our usual concept of possession.**
