# Do changing stakes make knowledge subjective?

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Encroachment with Influence of Practical Stakes](pragmatic-encroachment-with-influence-of-practical-stakes-synthesis-1e83d295-b88f-4064-889e-097b4fb938ca.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that knowledge aims to be an objective stance toward truth. This principle suggests that personal or situational factors should not render knowledge subjective.**, **Suppose we have a case in which what counts as knowledge changes based on a person's financial stakes. Intuitively, this subjectivity undermines the objectivity of knowledge.**
