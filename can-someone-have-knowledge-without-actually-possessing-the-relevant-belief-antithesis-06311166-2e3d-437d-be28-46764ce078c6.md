# Can someone have knowledge without actually possessing the relevant belief?

## Content

**Suppose someone has direct access to a truth due to some reliable ability but never actually forms the belief. This challenges the requirement of belief for knowledge.**, **Consider a savant with intuitive insights into mathematical truths without consciously believing them. This person might be said to know, even absent explicit belief.**
