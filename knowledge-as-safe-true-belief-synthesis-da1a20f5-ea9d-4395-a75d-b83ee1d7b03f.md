# Knowledge as Safe True Belief

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Is causal connection sufficient for knowledge if error possibilities exist?](is-causal-connection-sufficient-for-knowledge-if-error-possibilities-exist-antithesis-cbe6906d-4a41-435a-a04b-e5b380c7099e.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is safely formed, meaning that in nearby possible worlds, the belief would also be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have a safely formed true belief**
