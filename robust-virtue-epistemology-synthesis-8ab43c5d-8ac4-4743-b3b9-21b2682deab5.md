## Content

**Knowledge is attained when a belief results from intellectual virtues in a non-deceptive environment**, **Intellectual virtues are personal qualities that lead to true beliefs in reliable environments**, **A person knows a proposition if and only if their intellectual virtues operate in an environment that usually produces true beliefs**, **The reliability of the environment is a necessary condition for a person to know a proposition**
