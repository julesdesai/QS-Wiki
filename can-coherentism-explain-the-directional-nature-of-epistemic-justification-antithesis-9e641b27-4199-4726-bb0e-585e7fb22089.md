# Can coherentism explain the directional nature of epistemic justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9e641b27-4199-4726-bb0e-585e7fb22089
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism Summary](coherentism-summary-synthesis-7bdcae0a-f633-4c38-9857-77d6ba49435c.md)

## Content

**Suppose the belief system of a person is coherent, yet their beliefs mainly arise from flawed reasoning. Intuitively, we would judge that the source or direction of justification matters beyond coherence alone. Coherence without tracing beliefs back to reliable sources seems insufficient for justification.**
