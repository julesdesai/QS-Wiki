# Is perception sufficient for knowing the structure of reality?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0e8a4eeb-bafe-4192-8e36-39c596ca7cea
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Epistemic Structural Realism](epistemic-structural-realism-synthesis-363c9a38-22ba-473c-9125-53ba3b8ce920.md)

## Content

**A case in which a person sees a Müller-Lyer illusion but perceives the lines as being of different lengths is a case where perception does not capture true relational structure.**, **In the case of optical illusions, the relational structure perceived is misleading, challenging the idea that perception reliably reveals structure.**, **If perception can misrepresent relations, then it is insufficient for knowing the structure of reality.**
