# Does the dependence on context allow for knowledge without justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c20c2b6d-3418-4d97-89cf-5e878e1a2d2a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Contextualism Summary](pragmatic-contextualism-summary-synthesis-cadd0bfa-2179-4efb-90ce-cbf918dbd894.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where due to a particular context, a person claims knowledge based solely on belief with no evidence. Intuitively, this does not constitute true knowledge.**, **It is implausible for contextual dependence to allow claims of knowledge without proper justification.**
