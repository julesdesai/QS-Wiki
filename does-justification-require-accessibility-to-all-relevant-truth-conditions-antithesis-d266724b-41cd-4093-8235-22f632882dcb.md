# Does justification require accessibility to all relevant truth conditions?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d266724b-41cd-4093-8235-22f632882dcb
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [No-False-Lemma View](no-false-lemma-view-synthesis-d417d5a7-7b9d-4d6b-8a89-c64775b0283f.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person holds a true belief justified by evidence, yet they lack awareness of important truth conditions that pertain to the belief. Intuitively, the person's ignorance seems to undermine their claim to knowledge.**, **There is a plausible principle that knowledge demands awareness of conditions relevant to the truth of the belief. This demands a holistic understanding of truth conditions which goes beyond absence of false grounds.**
