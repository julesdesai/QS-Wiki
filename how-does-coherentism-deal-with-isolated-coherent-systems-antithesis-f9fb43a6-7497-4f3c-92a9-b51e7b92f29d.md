# How does coherentism deal with isolated coherent systems?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f9fb43a6-7497-4f3c-92a9-b51e7b92f29d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentist Knowledge Summary](coherentist-knowledge-summary-synthesis-1fa3c944-9677-491a-8edd-ea154ce998c7.md)

## Content

**Imagine a case in which an individual's beliefs form a coherent system isolated from empirical reality. Intuitively, such isolation questions the justification for these beliefs.**, **It is implausible to suppose that isolation from empirical checks maintains robust justification. Coherence alone, without external connection, might not suffice for reliable knowledge.**
