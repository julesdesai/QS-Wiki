## Content

**There are cases where individuals have beliefs caused by facts that are accidentally true, like a person believing there is a dog in the yard based on barking, but the barking comes from a speaker and there is coincidentally a dog hidden from view.**, **Intuitively, having a belief caused by an unrelated or coincidental truth does not seem to qualify as knowledge.**
