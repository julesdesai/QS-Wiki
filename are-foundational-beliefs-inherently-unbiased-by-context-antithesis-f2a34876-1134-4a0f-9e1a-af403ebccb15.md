# Are foundational beliefs inherently unbiased by context?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f2a34876-1134-4a0f-9e1a-af403ebccb15
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualist Foundationalism](contextualist-foundationalism-synthesis-b0409110-d832-4fdb-9fe3-2e01026d1f88.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which foundational beliefs are formed under strong contextual influence that skews their justification. Intuitively, these foundational beliefs might not appear justified.**, **It is implausible that foundational beliefs are devoid of bias introduced by context. This suggests foundational beliefs might be unjustified if overly context-dependent.**
