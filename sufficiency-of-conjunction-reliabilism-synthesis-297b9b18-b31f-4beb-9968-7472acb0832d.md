## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a belief to constitute knowledge, it is sufficient that the belief is true, the individual believes it, and the belief is formed by a reliable cognitive process**
