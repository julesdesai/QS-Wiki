# Contextualism in Knowledge

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief meets the relevant epistemic standards of the context**, **Epistemic standards can vary depending on the context and affect whether true belief amounts to knowledge**
