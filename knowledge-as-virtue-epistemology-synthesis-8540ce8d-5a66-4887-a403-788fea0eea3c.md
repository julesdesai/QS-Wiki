# Knowledge as Virtue Epistemology

**Parent:** [Does belief alone suffice for claiming knowledge?](does-belief-alone-suffice-for-claiming-knowledge-antithesis-71e50173-731f-4112-851b-77b0c0ad6b11.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification for their belief incorporates intellectual virtues like open-mindedness and intellectual courage**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification for their belief involves intellectual virtues**
