## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a belief to be knowledge, it is necessary that the belief was formed by a reliable process**, **A process is reliable if it tends to produce true beliefs over false ones**
