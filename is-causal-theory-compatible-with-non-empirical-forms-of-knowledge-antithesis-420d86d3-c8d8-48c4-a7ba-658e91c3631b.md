# Is causal theory compatible with non-empirical forms of knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 420d86d3-c8d8-48c4-a7ba-658e91c3631b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Causal Theory of Knowledge](causal-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-dc61c008-27ed-4921-863e-badc9b961db6.md)

## Content

**Non-empirical knowledge, like that of logical truths, lacks a causal origin related to the belief but is still intuitively knowledge.**, **It is implausible that entirely non-empirical knowledge can be explained by causal influences, since they do not track facts in the traditional causal sense.**
