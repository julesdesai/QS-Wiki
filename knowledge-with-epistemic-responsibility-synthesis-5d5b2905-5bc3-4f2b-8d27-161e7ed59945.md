# Knowledge with Epistemic Responsibility

**Parent:** [Can implicit biases lead to knowledge with epistemic luck exclusion?](can-implicit-biases-lead-to-knowledge-with-epistemic-luck-exclusion-antithesis-8ab778c0-a0e2-4439-ab06-f9c953d46172.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is not true merely due to epistemic luck**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed with epistemic responsibility**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, their belief is not true merely due to epistemic luck, and their belief is formed with epistemic responsibility**
