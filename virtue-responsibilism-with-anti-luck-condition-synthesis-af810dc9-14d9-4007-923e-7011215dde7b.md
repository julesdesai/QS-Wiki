## Content

**Knowledge is true belief that arises from intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief arises from intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is not formed by luck**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe it, the proposition is true, their intellectual virtue is involved, and their belief is not formed by luck**
