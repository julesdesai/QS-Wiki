# Can a person know a false belief if it is useful or practical?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f4555d7d-bb89-4fb2-86c5-c15101145ce3
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Instrumentalism with Externalist Justification](instrumentalism-with-externalist-justification-synthesis-69903d30-4d37-4781-884e-3e9ef74484e3.md)

## Content

**A belief is useful or practical if it helps achieve desired outcomes in some situations**, **A belief can be useful or practical even if it is false, such as a placebo belief that improves health outcomes through psychological effects**, **If a belief is false, then it cannot be considered knowledge even if it is useful or practical in achieving outcomes**
