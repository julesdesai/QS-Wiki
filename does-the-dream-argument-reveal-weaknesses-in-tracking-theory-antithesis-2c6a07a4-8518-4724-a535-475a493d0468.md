# Does the dream argument reveal weaknesses in tracking theory?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2c6a07a4-8518-4724-a535-475a493d0468
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Nozick's Tracking Theory](nozicks-tracking-theory-synthesis-56c7bb47-47e8-422c-831e-e887399874aa.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person believes they are awake when they are dreaming but the belief is coincidentally true when they wake up and still maintain the belief. Intuitively, we would not consider this knowledge.**, **During the time they are asleep, if they were not dreaming, they would not believe they are dreaming, so the requirement that the belief tracks truth across possible worlds is not met.**
