# Can an unreliable information source lead to coherent and justified beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 29d6743e-f726-4827-bdd4-18741acd2f03
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilist Coherentism](reliabilist-coherentism-synthesis-2c1f8a9c-d9de-4c66-ad9a-56c41dc7c349.md)

## Content

**An unreliable source can, by chance, produce information that fits into a coherent belief system, leading to an illusion of justification.**, **A coherent process based on unreliable sources may yield beliefs that seem justified due to internal consistency, despite external unreliability.**
