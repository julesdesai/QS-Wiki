# Can knowledge arise from malfunctioning cognitive faculties?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 8fa4638b-3082-4825-b9e9-ab7e54e9dfda
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Proper Functionalist Knowledge Summary](proper-functionalist-knowledge-summary-synthesis-ea5a08e3-f799-4ced-bdad-582e7b9aaf66.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person believes it is raining because of a brain malfunction, yet it is truly raining. Intuitively, we would judge this person does not know it is raining despite having a true belief.**, **It's plausible to hold that a belief formed due to malfunctioning cognitive faculties lacks justification, even if true.**
