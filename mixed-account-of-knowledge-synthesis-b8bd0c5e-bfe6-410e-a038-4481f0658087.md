**Parent:** [Can reliance on intellectual virtue exclude justified true beliefs?](can-reliance-on-intellectual-virtue-exclude-justified-true-beliefs-antithesis-87672439-7295-4908-93c3-19fb7e177ec5.md)

## Content

**Knowledge is true belief that is either justified through intellectual virtue or supported by reliable cognitive processes**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is either justified through intellectual virtue or supported by reliable cognitive processes**, **A person knows a proposition if they believe it, the proposition is true, and their belief is either justified through intellectual virtue or supported by reliable cognitive processes**
