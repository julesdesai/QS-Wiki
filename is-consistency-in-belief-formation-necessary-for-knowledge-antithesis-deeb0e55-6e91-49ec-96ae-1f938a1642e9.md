# Is consistency in belief formation necessary for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** deeb0e55-6e91-49ec-96ae-1f938a1642e9
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-7601e066-11d5-4f0c-8c42-44a4cc1f650d.md)

## Content

**A person uses reliable cognitive faculties that sometimes fail due to inconsistencies, leading to true beliefs only sporadically. Intuitively, these sporadic true beliefs do not equate to knowledge.**, **Consistency in reliable cognitive operations is necessary to attribute knowledge to true beliefs.**
