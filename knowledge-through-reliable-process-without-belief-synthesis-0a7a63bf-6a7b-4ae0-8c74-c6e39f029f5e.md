## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition results from a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, explicit belief is not required**, **A person knows a proposition if it is true, results from a reliable process, and belief is optional**
