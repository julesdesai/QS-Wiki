# Does contextuality undermine universal standards of justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f5b44218-d3fe-4b38-bc95-2c03df223d89
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextual Foundationalism](contextual-foundationalism-synthesis-7feed83d-0716-412e-8434-831a7f45f379.md)

## Content

**Universal standards of justification provide a consistent framework for evaluating truth claims across different contexts.**, **There is a plausible principle that contextually based justification systems allow for relativism, where truth becomes a matter of perspective rather than objective assessment.**, **Relativism undermines the possibility of objective knowledge, posing a threat to the notion of knowledge as universally recognizable truth.**
