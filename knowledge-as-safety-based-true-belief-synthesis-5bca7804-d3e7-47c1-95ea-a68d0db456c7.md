# Knowledge as Safety-Based True Belief

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 5bca7804-d3e7-47c1-95ea-a68d0db456c7
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge be gained from a source that is typically unreliable but accidentally correct?](can-knowledge-be-gained-from-a-source-that-is-typically-unreliable-but-accidentally-correct-antithesis-0d943ab7-7799-4f2b-a45d-5a5aa31fee66.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in nearby possible worlds where the person believes the proposition, the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is safe across nearby possible worlds, and the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**
