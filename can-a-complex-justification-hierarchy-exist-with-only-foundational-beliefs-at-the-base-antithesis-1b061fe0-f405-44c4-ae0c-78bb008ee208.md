# Can a complex justification hierarchy exist with only foundational beliefs at the base?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1b061fe0-f405-44c4-ae0c-78bb008ee208
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundationalist Knowledge Summary](foundationalist-knowledge-summary-synthesis-d6291bb5-6e86-4606-a555-64e99394a6d4.md)

## Content

**Suppose an individual's web of beliefs all try to rest upon a few simple foundational beliefs. It intuitively seems unlikely that complex knowledge structures can be fully justified in this manner.**, **A principle of coherentism suggests that mutual support between beliefs contributes more effectively to justification than isolated foundational supports.**
