# Is coherence necessary for justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 292ee675-bdee-40be-9b2e-9ffc890bb4cd

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliable Coherentism](reliable-coherentism-synthesis-cd562728-46bb-4ccf-9e19-2bbd570f1b0e.md)

## Content

**A belief might be justified based purely on empirical evidence without reference to coherence, like a doctor's diagnosis based solely on test results.**, **Justification can exist independently of coherence, challenging the view that coherence is a necessary condition for justification.**
