# Does the variability of justification affect knowledge stability?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1b63cafa-42e6-4e5f-b7a8-da1d7b7be4ae
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [The Contextualist View of Knowledge](the-contextualist-view-of-knowledge-synthesis-744b190b-128c-4187-8edf-0a707cd161d1.md)

## Content

**The contextualist view suggests that justification standards vary across contexts.**, **Consider a person who knows a proposition under low justification standards in one context.**, **In another context with higher standards, the same person might not know the same proposition, leading to instability in knowledge claims.**
