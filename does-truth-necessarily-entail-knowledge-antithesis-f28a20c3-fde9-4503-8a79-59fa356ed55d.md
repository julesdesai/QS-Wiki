# Does truth necessarily entail knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f28a20c3-fde9-4503-8a79-59fa356ed55d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-05879bdc-4686-45ec-8d5f-c387922414d1.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that a true belief alone always constitutes knowledge. This suggests that while reliability in gaining true beliefs might be necessary, it is not sufficient for knowing a proposition.**
