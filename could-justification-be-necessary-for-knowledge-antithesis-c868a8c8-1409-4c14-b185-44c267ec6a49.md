# Could justification be necessary for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c868a8c8-1409-4c14-b185-44c267ec6a49
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-a1d061ab-4ce5-4544-95e5-a72c751db8d0.md)

## Content

**Suppose a case where a person acquires a true belief purely by accident, without any sort of justification, and the belief is in fact true.**, **There is an intuition that without justification, the true belief in such a case might not qualify as knowledge, as it feels more like a lucky guess.**
