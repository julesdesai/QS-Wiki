# Knowledge as Sensitivity with Stable Environments

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Can sensitivity address beliefs formed through malfunctioning devices?](can-sensitivity-address-beliefs-formed-through-malfunctioning-devices-antithesis-b5bf612c-2d02-4e1a-bef7-28a0e388ff39.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **The belief in the proposition should be sensitive to the truth of the proposition in a counterfactual sense**, **The environment in which the belief is formed should be stable and not prone to causing false beliefs**
