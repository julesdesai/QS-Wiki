## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief would not easily be false in similar situations**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed through epistemic virtues such as careful reflection or investigation**
