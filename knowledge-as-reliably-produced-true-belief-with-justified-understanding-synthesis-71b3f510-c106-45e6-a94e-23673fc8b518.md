# Knowledge as Reliably Produced True Belief with Justified Understanding

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can a belief produced by a reliable but misunderstood process count as knowledge?](can-a-belief-produced-by-a-reliable-but-misunderstood-process-count-as-knowledge-antithesis-8e2dc796-a26a-429e-baf0-954de472ea3f.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person has a justified understanding of why the belief is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process, and the person has a justified understanding of why it is true**
