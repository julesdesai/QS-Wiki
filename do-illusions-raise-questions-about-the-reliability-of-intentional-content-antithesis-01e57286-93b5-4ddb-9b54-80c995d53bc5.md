# Do illusions raise questions about the reliability of intentional content?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 01e57286-93b5-4ddb-9b54-80c995d53bc5
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Intentionalism Summary](intentionalism-summary-synthesis-3678fe22-b4a0-4fa7-aad8-5dfa98b1a709.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a stick partially submerged in water appears bent. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that perception represents the stick as bent, though it is not.**, **If changes in perception are changes in intentional content, then the unchanging stick's perception as bent contradicts its actual state, questioning the reliability of intentional content.**
