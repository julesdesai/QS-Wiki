## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification was adequate given the historical context of the inquiry**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believed the proposition, the proposition was true, and it was justified adequately in the historical context in which the proposition was considered**
