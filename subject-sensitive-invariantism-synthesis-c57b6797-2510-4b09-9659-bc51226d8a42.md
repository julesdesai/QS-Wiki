## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for the proposition**, **The standards for justification and belief are invariant as to conversational context but vary with subject’s practical interests**, **Belief is sensitive to the practical importance of being right**
