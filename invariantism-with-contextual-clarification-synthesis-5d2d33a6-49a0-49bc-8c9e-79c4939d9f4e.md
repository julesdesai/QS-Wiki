## Content

**Knowledge does not depend on the context of the knower**, **A person knows a proposition if the proposition is true**, **The standards for knowing a proposition are fixed and do not vary with different contexts**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have evidence supporting the proposition**, **The amount of evidence needed for justification is consistent and context-independent**, **Clear criteria define what counts as sufficient evidence**
