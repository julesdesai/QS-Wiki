# Relevant Alternatives Theory of Knowledge

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person can rule out relevant alternatives**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the person believes the proposition, it is true, and the person can rule out relevant alternatives**
