# Reliabilist Theory of Knowledge

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge be attributed without direct causation?](can-knowledge-be-attributed-without-direct-causation-antithesis-e742d100-cafb-4286-880f-928761b33e2b.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **Knowledge requires the belief to be reliably produced**, **A reliable cognitive process produces true beliefs more often than not**
