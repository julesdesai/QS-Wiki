## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification for the belief consists of a coherent set of mutually supportive reasons**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief forms a part of a coherent system**
