# Is it possible for someone to lack justification yet still fail to know?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5ed6ce75-1d47-4267-9756-4d72d0450aae
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Safety Theory of Knowledge](safety-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-92f8baec-50d4-4810-a26f-7675d59c578a.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a person holds a belief that is true and safe, but for which they have no justification. Intuitively, we would not ascribe knowledge to this person.**, **It is implausible that knowledge can exist in the absence of justification, as justification is a traditional component of knowledge.**
