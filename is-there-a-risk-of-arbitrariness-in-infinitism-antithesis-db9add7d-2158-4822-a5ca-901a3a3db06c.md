# Is there a risk of arbitrariness in Infinitism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** db9add7d-2158-4822-a5ca-901a3a3db06c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism](infinitism-synthesis-db00e7c1-6a13-4ca5-8f09-6fae7cd29e0f.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that justification requires some standard to discriminate between valid and invalid reasons. Without such a standard, infinite reasons risk becoming arbitrary, lacking genuine justificatory power.**, **It is implausible that a sequence of reasons without a coherent or consistent evaluative standard could provide meaningful justification.**
