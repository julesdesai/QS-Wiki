## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have sufficient justification for the most relevant context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have relevant-context justification, the proposition is true, and they believe it**, **Relevant contexts are determined by objective criteria common to scientific inquiry**
