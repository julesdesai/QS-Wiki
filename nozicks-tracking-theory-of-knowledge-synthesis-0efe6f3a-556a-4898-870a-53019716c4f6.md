## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **A person knows a proposition if, were it false, they would not believe it**, **A person knows a proposition if, were it true, they would believe it**
