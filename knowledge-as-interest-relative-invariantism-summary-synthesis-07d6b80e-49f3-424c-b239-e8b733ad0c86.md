## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the importance of the proposition to the individual plays a role in justification**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification appropriately considers the individual's interests**
