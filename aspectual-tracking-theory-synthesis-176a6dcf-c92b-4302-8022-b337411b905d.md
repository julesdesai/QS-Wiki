## Content

**Knowledge requires that one's belief about each aspect of a proposition tracks the truth of that aspect**, **A person's belief about an aspect of a proposition tracks the truth if, in relevant scenarios, they would not believe that aspect if it were false**, **A person's belief about an aspect of a proposition tracks the truth if, in relevant scenarios, they do believe that aspect when it is true**
