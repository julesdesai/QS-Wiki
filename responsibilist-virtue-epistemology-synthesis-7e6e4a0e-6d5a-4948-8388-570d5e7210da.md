## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **Intellectual virtues are defined as character traits involving the responsible management of one's cognitive faculties**, **For a person to know a proposition, their belief must arise from intellectual responsibility as facilitated by intellectual virtues**
