# Are all responsible beliefs knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 044a8463-00d8-4fae-a25b-4fd6464f58c2
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Responsibilist Virtue Epistemology](responsibilist-virtue-epistemology-synthesis-d65854c6-8623-4bd4-aed3-f2a4bfae69c9.md)

## Content

**A person may responsibly hold beliefs that are ultimately false.**, **Responsible commitment to a belief does not ensure its truth.**, **It is doubtful whether responsible yet false beliefs qualify as knowledge.**
