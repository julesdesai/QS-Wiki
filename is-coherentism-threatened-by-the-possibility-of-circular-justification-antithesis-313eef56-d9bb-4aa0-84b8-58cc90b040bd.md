# Is coherentism threatened by the possibility of circular justification?

## Content

**It is plausible that coherentism often relies on circular reasoning, since beliefs are justified by other beliefs within the same system.**, **Circular reasoning is typically seen as a form of invalid reasoning that fails to give genuine justification.**, **If coherentism is inherently circular, then it might only provide apparent rather than genuine knowledge.**
