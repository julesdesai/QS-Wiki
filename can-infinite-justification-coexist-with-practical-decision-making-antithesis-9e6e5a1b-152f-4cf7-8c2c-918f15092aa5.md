# Can infinite justification coexist with practical decision-making?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9e6e5a1b-152f-4cf7-8c2c-918f15092aa5
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextual Infinitism](contextual-infinitism-synthesis-969d15cb-7035-4449-8e21-a18b5558491f.md)

## Content

**Practical decision-making often requires prompt action based on available reasons and evidence**, **It is implausible to delay decisions until an infinite chain of reasons is justified due to the urgency of most decisions**, **A scenario in which one must await infinite justification before acting would lead to paralysis in practical decision-making**
