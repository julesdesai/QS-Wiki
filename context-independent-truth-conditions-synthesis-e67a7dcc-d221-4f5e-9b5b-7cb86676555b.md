## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **The truth conditions for "know" do not vary with context**, **Differences in context affect only the pragmatic aspects of asserting knowledge**
