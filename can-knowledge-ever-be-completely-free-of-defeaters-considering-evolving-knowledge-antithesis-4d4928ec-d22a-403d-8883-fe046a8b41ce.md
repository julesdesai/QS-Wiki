# Can knowledge ever be completely free of defeaters considering evolving knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 4d4928ec-d22a-403d-8883-fe046a8b41ce
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Internalist Defeasibility View](internalist-defeasibility-view-synthesis-fd143e9a-1c88-44de-a8f1-4ab4ab5bc60e.md)

## Content

**Imagine a scenario where scientific advances reveal new information that acts as a defeater to past justified beliefs. Intuitively, this suggests that knowledge is always susceptible to future defeaters.**, **If knowledge must withstand all possible future defeaters, it would imply that current knowledge can never be truly complete.**, **The demand for knowledge to be free of all possible future defeaters may make the concept of knowledge overly limited.**
