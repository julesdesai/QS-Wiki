# Do cases of induced beliefs, like brainwashing, conform to causal theory?

## Content

**Suppose a person is brainwashed into forming specific beliefs about historical facts.**, **Brainwashing can cause a direct causal connection between belief and fact without the belief being formed in a trustworthy manner.**, **A belief can be causally connected to a fact yet lack justification if formed through coercive or manipulative processes.**
