# Are logical truths known only through perception?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2b8aba67-9d1c-4804-9085-4e8571f326d5
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Inference-Based Phenomenalism](inference-based-phenomenalism-synthesis-3c5287e4-c850-454f-b68f-cef5a0c2976c.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a person knows that if all humans are mortal and Socrates is a human, then Socrates is mortal. Intuitively, this knowledge is based on logical reasoning, not perception.**, **It is implausible that logical knowledge is dependent on perceptual experiences.**
