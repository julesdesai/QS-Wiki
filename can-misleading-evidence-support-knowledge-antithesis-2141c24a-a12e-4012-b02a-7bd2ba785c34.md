# Can misleading evidence support knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2141c24a-a12e-4012-b02a-7bd2ba785c34
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Responsibilist Virtue Epistemology](responsibilist-virtue-epistemology-synthesis-d65854c6-8623-4bd4-aed3-f2a4bfae69c9.md)

## Content

**A person may responsibly commit to a belief based on misleading but genuine evidence.**, **Such evidence might coincidentally lead to a true belief.**, **It is contentious whether such a belief should be considered knowledge.**
