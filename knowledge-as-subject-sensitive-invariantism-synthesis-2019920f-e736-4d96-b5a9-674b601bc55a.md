## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **The standards for knowing a proposition are fixed and do not vary with contexts**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is sensitive to the subject's stakes**
