# How can we address changing truths in tracking theory?

**Parent:** [Tracking Theory](tracking-theory-synthesis-0bad637f-a0d8-4d67-8d05-73b0a7027f5a.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a belief tracks the truth, but the truth changes and the belief adjusts slowly. Intuitively, the person lacks knowledge during the transition.**, **It is implausible that knowledge resides in a belief that fails to promptly align with changing truths.**
