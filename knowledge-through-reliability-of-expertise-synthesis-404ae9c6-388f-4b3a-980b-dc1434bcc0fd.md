**Parent:** [How can trust be justified if expertise is inaccessible?](how-can-trust-be-justified-if-expertise-is-inaccessible-antithesis-93de80a3-abf6-4d45-9763-f2805e62d00e.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they hold justified trust in the reliability of credible expertise over time**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition and that belief is based on justified trust in the reliability of credible expertise over time**
