# How do we judge hidden but reliable sources?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c4adcd44-a2bc-4518-98a8-4c3433f14ca4
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Hybrid Internalism](hybrid-internalism-synthesis-3d9ce0d9-6ba2-4665-a054-ed06708d6ee0.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person relies on a secretively reliable source, such as a randomly chosen guess out of a factually accurate database they cannot directly verify. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that the person does not truly know the information because they cannot understand or access why the source is reliable.**
