# Do external circumstances challenge invariant standards for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 59ac8f0b-f82d-40d8-846c-ae330d7cce17
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Subject-Sensitive Invariantism](subject-sensitive-invariantism-synthesis-ef027452-1300-45f1-9d7c-023fdb6cd119.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person is under threat and needs to ascertain the location of a nearby safehouse. The urgency alters the sufficiency of evidence they accept. Intuitively, this suggests that external circumstances can impinge on what we count as knowledge, clashing with constant standards.**
