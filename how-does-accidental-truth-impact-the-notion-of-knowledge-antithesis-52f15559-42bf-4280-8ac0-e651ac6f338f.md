# How does accidental truth impact the notion of knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 52f15559-42bf-4280-8ac0-e651ac6f338f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [No-False-Lemma Theory](no-false-lemma-theory-synthesis-5710d9f4-02e8-4ed3-aa41-f8ff2a235ea5.md)

## Content

**Suppose an individual believes a fact due to a lucky guess that turns out to be true.**, **The truth of the believed proposition arises independently of any solid justification or reasoning.**, **Such scenarios trigger intuitions against labeling the belief as knowledge, questioning the sufficiency of true belief.**
