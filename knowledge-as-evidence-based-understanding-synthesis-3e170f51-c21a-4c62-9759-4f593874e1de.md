# Knowledge as Evidence-Based Understanding

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 3e170f51-c21a-4c62-9759-4f593874e1de
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is knowledge possible without understanding the reasons behind a belief?](is-knowledge-possible-without-understanding-the-reasons-behind-a-belief-antithesis-9fd206fc-2bbc-44dc-8818-fef334206c55.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition based on sufficient evidence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, they have sufficient evidence for the belief, and they understand the evidence**
