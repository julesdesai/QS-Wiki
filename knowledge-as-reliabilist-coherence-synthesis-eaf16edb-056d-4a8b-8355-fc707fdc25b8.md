**Parent:** [Are intrinsically justified basic beliefs truly necessary for knowledge?](are-intrinsically-justified-basic-beliefs-truly-necessary-for-knowledge-antithesis-a3b36ea7-1826-4da0-a57b-77389fbc1375.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres with a system of interconnected beliefs that are produced by reliable cognitive processes**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief coheres with a system of interconnected beliefs produced by reliable processes**
