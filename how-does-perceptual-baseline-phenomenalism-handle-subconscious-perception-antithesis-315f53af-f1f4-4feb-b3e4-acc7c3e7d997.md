## Content

**Individuals can have subconscious perceptual experiences that influence their knowledge without their conscious awareness.**, **A scenario where someone subconsciously hears a tune but cannot consciously recall it challenges the baseline requirement for conscious acknowledgment.**, **It is questionable whether subconscious experiences can be part of a common baseline for knowledge if they are not universally accessible.**
