# Does survival ensure knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 35c3fb33-cb6f-472d-95db-06d11cb0d58f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Evolutionary Foundationalism](evolutionary-foundationalism-synthesis-4b200bf3-94a7-4a07-af03-f4d0ca848734.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that the mechanisms ensuring survival do not necessarily ensure the acquisition of true knowledge. A person may survive by holding false but pragmatically beneficial beliefs, which suggests that survival-oriented foundations do not guarantee knowledge.**
