# Is it necessary for perception to play a central role in the accuracy of mental representations?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c3701bcd-64dd-4c2f-8b41-0f0aaecb8588
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Epistemic Representationalism](epistemic-representationalism-synthesis-c86e8f4e-5440-4a21-8e04-4337bf022fd8.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that mental representations could be accurate independently of perception. This principle together with cases of mathematical or logical knowledge, which do not rely on sensory perception, entails that perception is not always necessary for accurate mental representation.**
