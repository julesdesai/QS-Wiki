## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that justification is objective and doesn't rely on contextual variation**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification meets a fixed high standard of evidence**
