# Does the phenomenology of perceptual experience conflict with intentionalism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ad7b4730-d5b8-4c21-9cd8-b75657d81c8b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Intentionalism Summary](intentionalism-summary-synthesis-3678fe22-b4a0-4fa7-aad8-5dfa98b1a709.md)

## Content

**Consider a case in which a person perceives a vivid and colorful sunset. Intuitively, we either have to say that the richness of the experience is captured entirely by intentional content or that something beyond content contributes to the experience.**, **If intentional content alone captures perceptual experience, this conflicts with the rich phenomenological aspects that might not be representational in nature.**
