## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a proposition to be known, in nearby worlds where the person believes the proposition, it must still be true**
