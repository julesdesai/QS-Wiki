# What if perceptual experiences lead to systematically false beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a6289cec-18ae-499e-89a7-c9f4967a331b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Phenomenal Experience Consistency](phenomenal-experience-consistency-synthesis-dff6895e-5f7e-47e4-9fd7-5b1463da077a.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person with color blindness perceives all green objects as gray. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that their belief about the color is consistently false.**, **It is implausible that knowledge derived from consistent false beliefs is reliable.**
