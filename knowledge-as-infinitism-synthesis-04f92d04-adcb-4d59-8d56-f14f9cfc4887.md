## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is supported by an infinite chain of reasons**, **Each reason in the chain is supported by further reasons without endpoint**
