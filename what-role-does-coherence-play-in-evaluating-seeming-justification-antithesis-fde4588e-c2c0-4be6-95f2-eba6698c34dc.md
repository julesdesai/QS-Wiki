# What role does coherence play in evaluating seeming justification?

**Node ID:** fde4588e-c2c0-4be6-95f2-eba6698c34dc
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Phenomenal Conservatism: Seeming Justification](phenomenal-conservatism-seeming-justification-synthesis-de77a591-8397-48c7-98a8-42a761bdf13a.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person's belief network includes several coherent propositions that support one another and appear justified due to seeming. If an incoherent belief also seems true, intuitive judgment suggests coherence is crucial, challenging the sufficiency of mere seeming justification.**
