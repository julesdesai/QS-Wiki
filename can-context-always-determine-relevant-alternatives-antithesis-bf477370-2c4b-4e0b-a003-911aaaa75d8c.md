# Can context always determine relevant alternatives?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** bf477370-2c4b-4e0b-a003-911aaaa75d8c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Relevant Alternatives Theory](relevant-alternatives-theory-synthesis-d7f52b7c-07b8-44a2-8804-2022a32ed27e.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that context can always determine which alternatives are relevant.**, **Consider a scenario where different people have inconsistent intuitions about which alternatives are relevant. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that context cannot determine relevance conclusively.**
