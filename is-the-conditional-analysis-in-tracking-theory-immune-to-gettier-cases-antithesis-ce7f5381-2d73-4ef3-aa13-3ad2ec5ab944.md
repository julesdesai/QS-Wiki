# Is the conditional analysis in tracking theory immune to Gettier cases?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ce7f5381-2d73-4ef3-aa13-3ad2ec5ab944
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Nozick's Tracking Theory](nozicks-tracking-theory-synthesis-56c7bb47-47e8-422c-831e-e887399874aa.md)

## Content

**In a Gettier case where someone derives a true belief with justified reasoning but only through a lucky error, we intuitively judge that the person does not know the proposition.**, **Although the belief is true and the reasoning is justified, the belief tracks the truth due to the error and not because of proper epistemic tracking.**
