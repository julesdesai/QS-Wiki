# Non-Accidental Truth Theory of Knowledge

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** b39ec8b2-5b25-46b3-b1c4-b0ec0c5ef303
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is it possible for a belief to be accidentally true yet unsafe?](is-it-possible-for-a-belief-to-be-accidentally-true-yet-unsafe-antithesis-fc246e79-b40e-4dd8-9000-f3f4af94b8f9.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the truth of the proposition is not accidental relative to the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the truth of the proposition is not accidental relative to the belief**
