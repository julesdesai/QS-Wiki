## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have a justified belief in the proposition**, **A belief is justified if it seems true to the person, barring defeaters**, **Defeaters are reasons to doubt the belief given new evidence or arguments**
