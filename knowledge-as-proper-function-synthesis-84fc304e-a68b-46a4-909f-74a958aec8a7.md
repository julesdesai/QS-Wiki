# Knowledge as Proper Function

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does sensitivity handle propositions known a priori?](does-sensitivity-handle-propositions-known-a-priori-antithesis-b9508911-68b4-4dfb-9a6b-e6052fa59766.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the cognitive faculties producing the belief are functioning properly**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the cognitive faculties are aimed at truth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, their cognitive faculties are functioning properly, and are aimed at truth**
