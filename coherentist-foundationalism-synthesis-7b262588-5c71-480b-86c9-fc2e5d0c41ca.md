## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification involves foundational beliefs coherently supporting each other**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and foundational beliefs coherently support each other**
