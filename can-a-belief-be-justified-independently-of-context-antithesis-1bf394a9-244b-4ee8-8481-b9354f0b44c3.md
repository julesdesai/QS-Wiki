# Can a belief be justified independently of context?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1bf394a9-244b-4ee8-8481-b9354f0b44c3
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualist Foundationalism](contextualist-foundationalism-synthesis-b0409110-d832-4fdb-9fe3-2e01026d1f88.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a situation where a person’s belief remains compelling regardless of any contextual considerations. Intuitively, this belief might still appear justified.**, **There is a plausible principle that beliefs can be justified purely on internal coherence. This principle together with the scenario suggests justification may exist independently of context.**
