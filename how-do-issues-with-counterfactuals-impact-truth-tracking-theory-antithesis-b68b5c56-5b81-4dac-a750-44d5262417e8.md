# How do issues with counterfactuals impact truth-tracking theory?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b68b5c56-5b81-4dac-a750-44d5262417e8
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Truth-Tracking Theory](truth-tracking-theory-synthesis-44ea7417-270f-4c93-876d-f98b6754dd68.md)

## Content

**Counterfactuals are hypothetical statements about what could happen but did not; their interpretation can impact beliefs about what is truly tracked.**, **Different interpretations of counterfactual scenarios might lead people to different conclusions about whether one's belief follows truth in all scenarios.**, **The reliance on counterfactual sensitivity for knowledge under truth-tracking complicates accounting for diverse logical interpretations.**
