## Content

**Knowledge is true belief that arises from intellectual virtue without involving epistemic luck**, **An intellectual virtue is a trait that reliably leads to true beliefs**, **Epistemic luck is when a belief is true only due to chance circumstances**, **For a person to know a proposition, it must be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, they must believe it**, **Beliefs resulting from intellectual virtues without epistemic luck are justified**
