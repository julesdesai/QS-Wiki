## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, their belief would not be held if the proposition were false**, **A person knows a proposition if it is true, they believe it, and their belief is sensitive to the truth**
