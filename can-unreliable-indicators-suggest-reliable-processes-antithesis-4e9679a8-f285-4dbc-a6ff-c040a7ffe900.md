# Can unreliable indicators suggest reliable processes?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 4e9679a8-f285-4dbc-a6ff-c040a7ffe900
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism](reliabilism-synthesis-41e7a61f-ad45-42cd-8dd7-ba40ca519ba9.md)

## Content

**An indicator is reliable if it consistently signals true beliefs.**, **In a scenario where unreliable indicators are present, a process might seem reliable but is not.**, **If unreliable indicators can mislead one into assuming a process's reliability, then reliability judgments might be flawed.**
