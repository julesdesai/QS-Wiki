# Does foundationalism inevitably lead to circular justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b5917f18-cac8-474e-bf9e-2dc48c133aeb
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundationalism with Infinite Background](foundationalism-with-infinite-background-synthesis-b6351c4d-7dab-447a-b3c7-52e8418919cd.md)

## Content

**Foundationalism posits foundational beliefs that do not require further justification.**, **If these foundational beliefs are justified by an infinite background, then at some point, justification may circle back to initial beliefs.**, **Circular justification cannot provide secure foundations for knowledge.**
