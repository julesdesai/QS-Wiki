## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres with the person's other beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is true and coheres with the person's other justified beliefs**
