**Parent:** [When does a reliable process fail due to environmental shifts?](when-does-a-reliable-process-fail-due-to-environmental-shifts-antithesis-9e42920c-ef78-4070-8c12-f1d9b12a0a77.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, and the belief is true in a nearby possible world**
