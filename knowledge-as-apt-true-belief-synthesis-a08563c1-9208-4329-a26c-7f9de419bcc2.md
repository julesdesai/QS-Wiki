# Knowledge as Apt True Belief

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** a08563c1-9208-4329-a26c-7f9de419bcc2
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does knowledge always require conscious effort?](does-knowledge-always-require-conscious-effort-antithesis-693711fd-27aa-4975-9065-486e8f6fba82.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed in an apt way, where aptness means the belief is accurate because of the competence of the believer**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is formed in an apt way**
