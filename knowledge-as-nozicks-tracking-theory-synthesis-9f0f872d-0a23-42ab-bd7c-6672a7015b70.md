## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a belief to constitute knowledge, if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **For a belief to constitute knowledge, if the proposition were true, the person would believe it in nearby possible worlds**
