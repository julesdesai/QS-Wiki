# Can someone have knowledge of a proposition if the proposition lacks practical value to them?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 64446fea-ebb4-4d90-a316-6c5b46bb7307
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Knowledge as Reliable Pragmatism](knowledge-as-reliable-pragmatism-synthesis-601e2ec8-4358-4137-9d1e-243ee7e2906f.md)

## Content

**Consider an astronomer who discovers a distant star's properties that have no conceivable impact on practical life. Intuitively, this astronomer knows the proposition regarding the star, although it lacks practical consequences or usefulness.**
