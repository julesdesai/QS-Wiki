# Does memory functioning as a reliable process always provide knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 4c865a67-c314-4e42-b378-dc654cab411a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Process Reliabilism](process-reliabilism-synthesis-963ad99c-e2e5-4a20-9800-229a3470b5c5.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person formed a true belief in the past based on a reliable process but now forgets the process and only remembers the belief. Intuitively, this person cannot be said to know the proposition if they cannot recall the reliability of the original process.**
