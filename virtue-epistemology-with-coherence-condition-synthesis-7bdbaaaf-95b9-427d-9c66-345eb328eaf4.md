## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed through intellectual virtue**, **Intellectual virtue encompasses cognitive faculties functioning reliably and coherently with other justified beliefs**
