# Can belief alone suffice for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 69b138ab-1571-4532-a694-8371f3be389f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology Foundationalism](virtue-epistemology-foundationalism-synthesis-a32c22e3-fd99-46f1-bd06-3346824cbc70.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that knowledge requires more than mere belief. This principle together with the idea that a person knows a proposition if they believe it entails that belief alone would be sufficient for knowledge, which contradicts the principle that knowledge requires more than belief.**
