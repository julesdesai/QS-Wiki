# Does justification depend on the reliability of evidence sources?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ce20ea26-41b9-4a95-9205-06054002c883
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Evidentialism](evidentialism-synthesis-fd1a4d8b-5b56-45a2-920f-de86623a4098.md)

## Content

**Consider a situation where a person believes their car is in good condition based on a fraudulent mechanic's report. Intuitively, we would question whether the belief is justified, given the unreliability of the source.**, **The principle of evidential reliability suggests that justification should depend on the reliability of evidence sources, creating tension with the view that any available evidence suffices for justification.**
