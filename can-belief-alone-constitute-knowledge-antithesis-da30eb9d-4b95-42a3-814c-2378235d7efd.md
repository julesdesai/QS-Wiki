# Can belief alone constitute knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** da30eb9d-4b95-42a3-814c-2378235d7efd
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Holism with Infinite Justification](holism-with-infinite-justification-synthesis-5f38dde0-d181-4939-9f1a-56e147cd5ef5.md)

## Content

**A person can believe a false proposition.**, **A belief does not guarantee that the proposition is true.**, **Therefore, belief alone cannot constitute knowledge.**
