# Is it possible for humans to maintain infinite chains of reasoning?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ccaa2d0a-099d-460a-bff0-892f06fef595
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology Infinitism](virtue-epistemology-infinitism-synthesis-700b1be0-40ec-4861-9f40-25460b6be3e1.md)

## Content

**Cognitive limitations in humans restrict their ability to grasp and process infinite chains of reasoning.**, **There is a plausible principle that humans can only maintain a finite number of beliefs and reasons at any time.**, **This principle, combined with the notion of justification as an infinite chain of reasons, suggests that humans cannot achieve justification.**
