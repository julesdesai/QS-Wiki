# Does the context-sensitivity of knowledge affect its connection to reality?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 48a019f4-7e06-4f05-8768-1c11ddcc01f5
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Disjunctivism](disjunctivism-synthesis-90a3baa8-9102-43d8-b9cc-d77493986c7c.md)

## Content

**Imagine a person who knows a fact in one context but loses that knowledge in another due to changing standards. Intuitively, we suspect that knowledge may vary, which challenges the idea of a stable genuine connection to reality.**, **The sensitivity of knowledge to different contexts could imply that a genuine connection to reality fluctuates, which may be problematic for maintaining the truth of a proposition.**
