## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from the context-specific exercise of intellectual virtues**, **Intellectual virtues can vary in their manifestation depending on situational factors while still reliably producing true beliefs**
