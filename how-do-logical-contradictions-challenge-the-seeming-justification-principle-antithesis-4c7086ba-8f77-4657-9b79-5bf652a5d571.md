# How do logical contradictions challenge the seeming justification principle?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 4c7086ba-8f77-4657-9b79-5bf652a5d571
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Phenomenal Conservatism: Seeming Justification](phenomenal-conservatism-seeming-justification-synthesis-de77a591-8397-48c7-98a8-42a761bdf13a.md)

## Content

**Imagine a person who holds two justified beliefs that are logically contradictory without being aware of it. This implies that seeming justification can lead to inconsistency, which intuitively suggests a failure to adequately account for knowledge.**
