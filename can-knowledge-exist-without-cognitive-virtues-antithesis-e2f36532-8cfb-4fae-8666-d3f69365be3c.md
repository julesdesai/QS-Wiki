# Can knowledge exist without cognitive virtues?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e2f36532-8cfb-4fae-8666-d3f69365be3c

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-51f9ed1e-8cbb-4e95-8090-3e1317ce9636.md)

## Content

**A person with a cognitive defect may still form beliefs that are true.**, **A person who consistently guesses correctly does not possess a proper intellectual character trait.**, **A scenario where one's true belief arises from luck rather than cognitive virtue challenges the idea that such virtue is necessary for knowledge.**
