# How do memory distortions affect the reliability of knowledge?

**Node ID:** 7ed0d1d9-5bf9-4a71-ba38-7297381a7fbc
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Revised Phenomenalism Allowing Memory Role](revised-phenomenalism-allowing-memory-role-synthesis-5fb5abbe-6adb-4855-a476-25c8a91d11a9.md)

## Content

**Memory is subject to distortion and reconstruction over time. This fact together with the reliance on memory-influenced perceptual experiences for knowledge threatens the reliability of such knowledge claims.**, **Consider a case where a person recalls an event from years ago, and their memory includes significant embellishments not present in the actual occurrence. Intuitively, we would say that their knowledge is not accurate if based on this distorted memory-influenced perception.**
