## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief tracks the truth in near possible worlds**, **If the proposition were true in a slightly different situation, the person would still believe it**, **If the proposition were not true in a slightly different situation, the person would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is sensitive to the truth**
