## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the process by which they form the belief is reliable**, **A reliable process is one that produces a high proportion of true beliefs over false ones**
