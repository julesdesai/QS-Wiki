## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person believes the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from an intellectual virtue or cognitive faculty that reliably aims at truth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is true, the person believes it, and it arises from a reliable cognitive faculty**
