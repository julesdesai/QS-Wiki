# Knowledge as Virtue Epistemology

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** ed0fcb79-60b6-40fc-aef4-b7e1a9be4a18
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does foundationalism handle skepticism adequately?](does-foundationalism-handle-skepticism-adequately-antithesis-326bc370-ef4d-4e25-85e2-856d027db984.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is the result of exercising intellectual virtues**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief results from the exercise of intellectual virtues**
