# Can knowledge be claimed with arbitrary belief coinciding with truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 23ed7fb7-39f1-4446-814d-9f30829fc55c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Proper Function Theory](proper-function-theory-synthesis-45579dd6-32de-43f3-ae7a-76b3bb7de09b.md)

## Content

**Consider a person randomly guessing a number and, by chance, the number is correct. Intuitively, we would hesitate to say that the person knows the number.**, **The principle that knowledge requires more than accidental alignment of belief and truth suggests a need for justification beyond mere chance.**
