## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is formed in a way that would not easily lead to falsehood under similar conditions**, **A safe belief is one that could not easily have been false given the way it was formed**
