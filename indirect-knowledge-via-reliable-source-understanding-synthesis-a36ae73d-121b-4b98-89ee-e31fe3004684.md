## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary to understand the reliability of the external source from which the information is derived**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and they understand the reliability of the external source from which it is derived**
