## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their justification involves a coherent set of beliefs formed by reliable processes**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their coherent belief system is formed by reliable processes**
