## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in nearly all nearby possible worlds where the person believes the proposition in the same way, the proposition is true**, **The belief's safety ensures that it could not easily have been false**
