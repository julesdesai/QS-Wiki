# Do standards for knowledge vary consistently across contexts?

## Content

**Consider a scenario where the standards for knowledge change drastically from one context to another, resulting in a person knowing a proposition in one context but not knowing it in another.**, **In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that such radical shifts may undermine the general reliability of knowledge.**
