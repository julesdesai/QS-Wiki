# Are all reliable processes sufficient for justified belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7160f3c6-b4a0-48eb-ae78-fd2e70a244dd
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilist Externalism Summary](reliabilist-externalism-summary-synthesis-3207924c-3d5f-4b8a-8088-8161a66f3954.md)

## Content

**Some reliable processes, such as reading a horoscope, may result in a true belief, but intuitively will not grant knowledge because they seem arbitrary rather than epistemically valuable.**, **It is implausible that all reliable processes are epistemically equivalent in granting justification.**
