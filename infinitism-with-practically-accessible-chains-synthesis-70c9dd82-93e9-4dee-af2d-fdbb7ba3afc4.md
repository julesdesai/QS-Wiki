## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that justification involves a chain of consistent reasons that is potentially infinite but practically accessible**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there exists a potentially infinite but practically accessible chain of consistent reasons justifying the belief**
