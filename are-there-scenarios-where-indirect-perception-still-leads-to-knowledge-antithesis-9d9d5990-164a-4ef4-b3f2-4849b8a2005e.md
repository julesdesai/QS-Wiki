# Are there scenarios where indirect perception still leads to knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9d9d5990-164a-4ef4-b3f2-4849b8a2005e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextual Direct Realism](contextual-direct-realism-synthesis-9053ea14-9a99-4649-aa95-1bc3b223c980.md)

## Content

**Scientific instruments mediate our perceptions, providing indirect data that enrich our understanding of phenomena.**, **In some cases, knowledge derived indirectly via technology is as reliable, if not more so, than direct perception.**, **It is implausible that knowledge should only be acknowledged when directly perceived, even when indirect data is crucial and accurate.**
