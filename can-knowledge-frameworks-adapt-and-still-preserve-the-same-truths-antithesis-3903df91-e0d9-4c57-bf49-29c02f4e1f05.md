# Can knowledge frameworks adapt and still preserve the same truths?

## Content

**A change in cognitive frameworks often leads to reevaluation or revision of knowledge claims, questioning whether truths can remain the same**, **Consider historical paradigm shifts where frameworks changed but underlying truths eventually remained consistent after reinterpretation, implying truth as independent of framework changes**, **It is implausible that changing frameworks should always lead to different truths if those truths are robust and independent of cognitive frames**
