## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the person believes the proposition, then the proposition is true in nearly all nearby possible worlds**, **A belief is safe if it does not easily turn out false in nearby possible worlds**
