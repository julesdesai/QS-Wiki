## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is not necessary that they explicitly believe the proposition if their actions consistently reflect an understanding**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **Justification depends on the practical stakes and interests of the subject but remains consistent across similar circumstances**, **Knowledge is sufficient if a person's actions are aligned with true propositions and are informed by practical justifications**
