# Knowledge as Virtue-Based Acceptance

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** f0370d04-468f-4cff-8e4a-2a620f0fcc85
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Are there cases in which acceptance is insufficient for knowledge?](are-there-cases-in-which-acceptance-is-insufficient-for-knowledge-antithesis-3c4d036e-39c9-4f83-9677-42e8290df6d5.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they accept the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the acceptance results from intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they accept the proposition, the proposition is true, and the acceptance results from intellectual virtue**
