# Can someone know without intellectual virtue?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 82dab4ef-bb73-418e-a180-4b627583656c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Refined Virtue Epistemology](refined-virtue-epistemology-synthesis-ed8e6ab8-d432-4955-9aec-982a4b148be7.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that knowledge can be had without the presence of an intellectual virtue guiding the belief formation process.**, **Suppose we have a case in which a person has a true belief due to a random stroke of luck, without any intellectual virtue involved. Intuitively, we have to say that this is not a case of knowledge even though the proposition believed is true.**
