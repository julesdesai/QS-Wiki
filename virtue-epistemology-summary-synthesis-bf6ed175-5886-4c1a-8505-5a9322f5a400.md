## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief in the proposition results from the exercise of intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that it is true, believed, and the belief results from an intellectual virtue**
