# Can the tracking theory account for knowledge in shared environments?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** af1c23b0-9e3e-4be7-89b4-d322c15c4a07
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Tracking Theory of Knowledge](tracking-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-dda35d00-5b10-4342-b443-f197350806e5.md)

## Content

**Consider a group of people who collectively gather evidence and form beliefs. Intuitively, we treat their shared, collaborative conclusions as knowledge.**, **There is a plausible principle that collective knowledge can emerge from shared evidence and agreement among individuals. This principle with shared environments implies group knowledge challenges individual tracking requirements.**
