# Is a design plan always aimed at truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d323bb82-a898-427b-8951-8402c3d330c4
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Proper Functionalism](proper-functionalism-synthesis-252aa5eb-1857-4367-af60-24f7afe1c9be.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person's cognitive faculties are shaped by evolutionary processes that prioritize survival over truth. Intuitively, it seems plausible to say they might still arrive at true beliefs without the faculties being aimed at truth.**, **Evolutionary theory suggests cognitive faculties could be aimed at survival rather than truth, suggesting a design plan might not always aim at truth, yet knowledge could still be possible.**
