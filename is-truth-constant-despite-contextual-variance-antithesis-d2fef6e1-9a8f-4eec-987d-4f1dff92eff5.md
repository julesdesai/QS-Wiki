# Is truth constant despite contextual variance?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d2fef6e1-9a8f-4eec-987d-4f1dff92eff5
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextual Justificationism](contextual-justificationism-synthesis-1b60e9a8-d801-480b-8adb-e726a7af0440.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a situation where what counts as justifiable evidence differs between contexts but the truth of the proposition remains constant; this implies a tension with the notion that truth is unaffected by context.**, **It is implausible that truth as a component of knowledge can be context-dependent while maintaining consistency across evaluations.**
