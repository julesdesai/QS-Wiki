# Does perception always reveal an object's essence?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ee0f3008-134c-4cb0-b2d7-653bc6702e54
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Phenomenological Perspective](phenomenological-perspective-synthesis-c5a75a88-a263-494b-bef9-8c1e426bd706.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that superficial observations, such as seeing a person from afar and only noting their clothing, grasp the essence of who that person is.**, **The direct grasping of essence would require depth of perception that is not always present in casual or fleeting perceptual acts.**
