## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from the exercise of intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is true and results from the exercise of intellectual virtue**
