# Can one possess intellectual virtues without knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 63446a65-3dfe-4067-9272-c99d99e6dbb0
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-f60d76d9-bfb9-483c-b262-fa52975172be.md)

## Content

**Consider someone who diligently applies critical thinking, a recognized intellectual virtue, but operates under a deep-seated misconception.**, **Despite exercising an intellectual virtue, this person’s beliefs remain false, showing that one can possess intellectual virtues without constituting knowledge.**
