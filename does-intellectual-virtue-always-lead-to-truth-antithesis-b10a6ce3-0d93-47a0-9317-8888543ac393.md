# Does intellectual virtue always lead to truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b10a6ce3-0d93-47a0-9317-8888543ac393
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-f60d76d9-bfb9-483c-b262-fa52975172be.md)

## Content

**There are cases where an individual possesses an intellectual virtue, such as open-mindedness, but still holds a false belief due to misleading evidence.**, **If a belief resulting from an intellectual virtue can be false, then having an intellectual virtue does not necessarily guarantee the truth.**
