# Can Representational Realism address the problem of change blindness?

## Content

**Change blindness is the failure to detect changes in a visual scene, even when they occur right in front of an observer.**, **This failure indicates that mental representations might not accurately capture every change in external reality.**, **Intuitively, if such changes go unrepresented, knowledge based on such mental representations is incomplete.**
