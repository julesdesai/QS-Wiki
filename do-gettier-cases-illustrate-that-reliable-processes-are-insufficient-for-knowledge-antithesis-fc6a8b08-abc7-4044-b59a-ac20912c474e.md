# Do Gettier cases illustrate that reliable processes are insufficient for knowledge?

## Content

**In some scenarios, an individual's belief is both true and produced by a reliable process, yet we intuitively judge that they do not have knowledge.**, **Gettier cases demonstrate situations where all conditions for justified true belief are met, yet knowledge is not present, suggesting that reliability alone may not be sufficient.**
