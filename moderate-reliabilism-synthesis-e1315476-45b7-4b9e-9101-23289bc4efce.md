## Content

**A belief is knowledge if it is produced by a moderately reliable cognitive process**, **Moderate reliability means the process is generally reliable in typical contexts, with some tolerance for failure in atypical conditions**, **The truth of a belief is a necessary condition for it to be considered knowledge**, **The adaptive reliability of the process accounts for various contexts without strict uniformity**
