# Can coherence alone determine foundational truths?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** de1f946a-cc7d-40b1-9a27-a06fadee7447
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentist Foundationalism](coherentist-foundationalism-synthesis-815fa62f-5e40-4962-8349-ea400fc3f5ce.md)

## Content

**Imagine a scenario in which foundational beliefs are established by coherence with other beliefs but lack any intrinsic evidential value or independent verification. Intuitively, we would judge that these beliefs might not be trustworthy as foundational truths.**, **It is implausible that foundational truths require validation only through coherence with other beliefs, as foundational truths often need self-evidence or intrinsic certainty.**
