# Is the requirement of practical utility overly restrictive for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a3d15aac-9979-48d1-b570-d4ecc06ab8b5
**Depth:** 4

**Parent:** [Revised Pragmatic Theory of Knowledge](revised-pragmatic-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-d7b209ba-b523-44b3-ad9a-c228c53c8771.md)

## Content

**Many forms of knowledge, especially academic or artistic, do not have immediate practical applications.**, **Fields like poetry or pure mathematics offer insights considered knowledge without practical utility.**, **A stringent requirement for practicality potentially narrows the scope of recognized knowledge excessively.**
