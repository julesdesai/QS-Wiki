# Knowledge as Environment-Sensitive Reliabilism

**Parent:** [Can overgeneralization undermine knowledge claims in reliabilism?](can-overgeneralization-undermine-knowledge-claims-in-reliabilism-antithesis-ccc16e04-0270-4ca4-8581-a4c3c721cc2f.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed through a reliable process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief-forming process accounts for environmental conditions**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is formed through an environment-sensitive reliable process**
