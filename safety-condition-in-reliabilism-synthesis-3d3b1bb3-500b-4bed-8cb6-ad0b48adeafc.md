**Parent:** [Can two equally reliable processes deliver different epistemic statuses?](can-two-equally-reliable-processes-deliver-different-epistemic-statuses-antithesis-b4db1f3c-623a-47d9-bae2-a9b0631ce5c0.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a process that would not easily deliver a false belief in similar circumstances**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is produced by a process that would not easily deliver a false belief in similar circumstances**
