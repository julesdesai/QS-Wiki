# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with No Defeaters

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** a8030307-fa61-4ab6-a2d0-5724a5fad0fe
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge be undermined by defeating evidence that one ignores?](can-knowledge-be-undermined-by-defeating-evidence-that-one-ignores-antithesis-63165ec4-3b37-4af5-a48f-eeb5628d959a.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for their belief to be justified and have no defeating evidence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have a justified true belief with no defeating evidence**
