## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, the standards for knowledge are invariant and do not change across contexts**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **The standards for knowledge apply uniformly to all contexts regardless of any conversational shifts**
