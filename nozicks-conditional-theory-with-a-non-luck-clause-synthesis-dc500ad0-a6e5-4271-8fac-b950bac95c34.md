## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the true belief is not due to luck**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it, and the belief is not a matter of luck**
