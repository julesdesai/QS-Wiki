# Integrative Reliabilism with Intuition Sensitivity

**Parent:** [Does intuitive understanding of reliable processes affect whether one has knowledge?](does-intuitive-understanding-of-reliable-processes-affect-whether-one-has-knowledge-antithesis-6176761f-42c1-42c5-a364-6152a63efb19.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed through a reliable process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have some intuitive grasp of the reliability of the process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is formed through a reliable process, and they have some intuitive grasp of the reliability of the process**
