# Are there unrecognized reliable processes?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 26544f3f-9f6a-4258-a0d6-f1686767b5eb
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilist Theory of Knowledge](reliabilist-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-b0906775-f1f5-4edb-9c31-9ab274e2992b.md)

## Content

**It is implausible for someone to know something through a reliable process if they are entirely unaware that the process is reliable, due to self-deception or ignorance.**, **Suppose a person habitually forms beliefs about the weather based on their intuition and these beliefs are nearly always correct, yet they think they are just guessing. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that the person lacks knowledge.**
