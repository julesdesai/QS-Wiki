## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **A belief formed by a reliable cognitive process is likely to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have evidence supporting the belief**
