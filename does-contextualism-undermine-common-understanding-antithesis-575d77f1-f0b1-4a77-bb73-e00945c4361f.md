# Does contextualism undermine common understanding?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 575d77f1-f0b1-4a77-bb73-e00945c4361f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism](contextualism-synthesis-ed8715f4-7106-486f-8992-736e64ae52d2.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which different people apply different standards and thus have different knowledge claims about the same everyday fact. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that mutual understanding is compromised.**, **It is implausible for everyday communication to thrive if knowledge claims were heavily context-dependent thereby impeding common understanding.**
