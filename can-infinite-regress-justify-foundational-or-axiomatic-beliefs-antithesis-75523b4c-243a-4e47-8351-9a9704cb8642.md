# Can infinite regress justify foundational or axiomatic beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 75523b4c-243a-4e47-8351-9a9704cb8642
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism Summary](infinitism-summary-synthesis-a48a630f-d4e8-4dc2-b25a-518a64dbd1fb.md)

## Content

**Axiomatic beliefs are typically held without reasons or are self-evident, contradicting the need for an infinite regress of reasons that the view demands.**, **For foundational beliefs, infinite regress would undermine their self-evident nature, which typically requires no further justification.**
