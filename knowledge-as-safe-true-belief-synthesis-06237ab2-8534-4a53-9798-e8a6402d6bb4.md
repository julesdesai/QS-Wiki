# Knowledge as Safe True Belief

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 06237ab2-8534-4a53-9798-e8a6402d6bb4
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can unreliable cognitive processes yield knowledge?](can-unreliable-cognitive-processes-yield-knowledge-antithesis-d920738e-eb08-40a6-8e02-fab2653dfcd2.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief be produced in a way that would not easily have been false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is produced in a way that would not easily have been false**
