# Knowledge as Anti-Luck Virtue Epistemology

**Parent:** [Is safety sufficient for knowledge given cases where safety stems from luck?](is-safety-sufficient-for-knowledge-given-cases-where-safety-stems-from-luck-antithesis-3009a5cd-0f85-4065-9bfa-06261baf97e0.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is produced by a cognitive ability or intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for their belief to not easily be false**, **It is sufficient for a person to know a proposition if they believe it, it is true, their belief is safe, and is produced by their intellectual virtue**
