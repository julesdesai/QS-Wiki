## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed through the exercise of an intellectual virtue or faculty**, **An intellectual virtue is a stable disposition that reliably leads to true beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe in the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief results from an intellectual virtue**
