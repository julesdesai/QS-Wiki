# Knowledge as Apt Belief Within Proper Environments

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, their belief must be apt**, **A person's belief is apt if it is true because of their intellectual competence**, **The truth of the belief must stem from the believer's skill or competence**, **Apt beliefs must be formed within environments conducive to the demonstration of competence**, **The environment must not obstruct or interfere with the competence's expression**
