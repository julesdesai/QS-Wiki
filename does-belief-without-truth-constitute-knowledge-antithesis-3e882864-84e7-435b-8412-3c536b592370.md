# Does belief without truth constitute knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3e882864-84e7-435b-8412-3c536b592370
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Finite Infinitism Summary](finite-infinitism-summary-synthesis-cf1727db-af05-4f36-9758-13f464b5dd32.md)

## Content

**Suppose we consider a belief where a person genuinely believes a false proposition. Intuitively, we wouldn't say the person knows the proposition.**, **The concept of true belief aligns strongly with conventional definitions of knowledge. Without truth, belief alone seems insufficient to constitute knowledge.**
