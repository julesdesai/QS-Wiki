# Sensitivity Theory of Knowledge

**Parent:** [Is belief tracking truth against counterexamples?](is-belief-tracking-truth-against-counterexamples-antithesis-c48c943b-e721-4965-9d3d-4fb2a7c447ad.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person's belief in a proposition to qualify as knowledge, the belief must be sensitive to truth in nearby possible worlds**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is sensitive to truth in nearby possible worlds**
