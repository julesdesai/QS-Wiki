## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their conviction in the belief is weighted more heavily when the evidence is lacking or memory is strong**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief in the proposition involves an appropriate degree of conviction relative to the available evidence and confidence weight**
