## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have a belief that tracks the truth across close possible worlds**, **If the proposition were not true, the person would not believe it, and if it were true, they would believe it**
