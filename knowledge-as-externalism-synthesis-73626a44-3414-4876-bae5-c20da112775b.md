# Knowledge as Externalism

**Parent:** [Do all coherent systems have equal epistemic value?](do-all-coherent-systems-have-equal-epistemic-value-antithesis-a39cb3d6-e367-4e47-99b4-34f2cf19a827.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief has a connection to the truth that is external and not fully accessible to the believer's conscious reflection**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there is a connection to the truth that is external to and not fully accessible by the believer's conscious reflection**
