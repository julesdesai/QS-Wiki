# Does indirect realism adequately account for the subjective variation in perception?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7119c44a-1a2f-465d-98c3-71673e1a344d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Indirect Realism Maintained Through Inference](indirect-realism-maintained-through-inference-synthesis-ffd82d7b-be0f-4614-a603-c896b6b1b7df.md)

## Content

**Perceptual experiences vary significantly between individuals due to subjective factors like mood, health, and perspective.**, **Inference derived from these variable experiences cannot guarantee uniformity in knowledge of external objects.**, **The disparity in individual perceptions challenges the claim that systematic inference can yield reliable and objective knowledge of an external world.**
