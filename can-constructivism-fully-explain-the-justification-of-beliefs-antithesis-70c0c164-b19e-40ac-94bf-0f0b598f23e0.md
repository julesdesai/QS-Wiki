# Can constructivism fully explain the justification of beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 70c0c164-b19e-40ac-94bf-0f0b598f23e0
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Constructivism](constructivism-synthesis-c5cc0372-1259-4a9f-927f-8a96c3c904c0.md)

## Content

**A justified belief traditionally requires a grounding in empirical evidence or logical reasoning.**, **Constructivism suggests that justification may derive from the personal construction of the proposition.**, **In situations where constructed knowledge lacks empirical grounding, the sufficiency of justification remains questionable.**
