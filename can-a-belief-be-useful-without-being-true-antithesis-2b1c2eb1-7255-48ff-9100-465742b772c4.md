# Can a belief be useful without being true?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2b1c2eb1-7255-48ff-9100-465742b772c4
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Revised Pragmatic Theory of Knowledge with Justification](revised-pragmatic-theory-of-knowledge-with-justification-synthesis-570c8b94-2cc9-4f79-b923-9eacb64ddf74.md)

## Content

**A belief that it is beneficial to drink a certain type of tea for health reasons may be useful if it encourages healthy habits, even if the belief is false.**, **Intuitively, a belief that is false cannot be knowledge, even if it is pragmatically useful.**
