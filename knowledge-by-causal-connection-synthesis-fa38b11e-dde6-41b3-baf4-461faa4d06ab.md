**Parent:** [Is virtuous belief formation necessary if mere true belief suffices for practical success?](is-virtuous-belief-formation-necessary-if-mere-true-belief-suffices-for-practical-success-antithesis-bc5a05f7-5f18-456e-b249-9fe07c7ef428.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is a causal connection between the fact the proposition states and the person's belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there is a causal connection between the fact the proposition states and the person's belief**
