# Nozick's Truth-Tracking Theory

**Parent:** [Does the causal theory handle knowledge from non-perceptual sources?](does-the-causal-theory-handle-knowledge-from-non-perceptual-sources-antithesis-d8ea0603-6fef-4104-b9c7-e1d615f96114.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were true, the person would believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and they track the truth of the proposition**
