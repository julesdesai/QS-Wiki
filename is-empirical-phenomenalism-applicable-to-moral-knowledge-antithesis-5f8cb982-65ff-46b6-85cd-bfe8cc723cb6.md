# Is empirical phenomenalism applicable to moral knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5f8cb982-65ff-46b6-85cd-bfe8cc723cb6

**Parent:** [Empirical Phenomenalism](empirical-phenomenalism-synthesis-0f019261-788c-4093-9ea8-d1a3828e8b76.md)

## Content

**Moral knowledge often involves principles that are not derived from direct sensory experiences.**, **If moral judgments cannot be empirically verified, then they challenge the view that all knowledge is grounded in sensory experiences.**
