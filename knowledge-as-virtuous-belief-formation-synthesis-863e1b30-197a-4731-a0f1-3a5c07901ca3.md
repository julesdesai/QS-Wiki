**Parent:** [Is it possible for perfectly justified true beliefs to be mere coincidences, not knowledge?](is-it-possible-for-perfectly-justified-true-beliefs-to-be-mere-coincidences-not-knowledge-antithesis-9182fd12-031f-47df-b2a0-d84e12c62c47.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed by intellectual virtues**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is formed by intellectual virtues**
