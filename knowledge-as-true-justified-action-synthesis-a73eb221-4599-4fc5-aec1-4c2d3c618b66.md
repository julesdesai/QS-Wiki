# Knowledge as True Justified Action

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** a73eb221-4599-4fc5-aec1-4c2d3c618b66
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is belief really necessary for knowledge?](is-belief-really-necessary-for-knowledge-antithesis-fb4382c1-7a89-47d6-8a1b-1731bfbdde65.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition be pragmatically endorsed through action**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have a justification for acting on the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is true, pragmatically endorsed through action, and justified through action**
