# Are there limitations to applying truth-tracking broadly?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c96660d1-a4fb-4f92-9c3d-fe084ffa0795
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Nozick's Truth-Tracking Theory](nozicks-truth-tracking-theory-synthesis-1a215329-c53d-4dbe-9509-6b517d1bb8ad.md)

## Content

**Suppose every individual in a community holds a true belief due to societal norms without personal reflection, resulting in truth-tracking. Intuitively, societal-induced beliefs might lack the individual understanding we associate with knowledge.**, **There is a plausible principle that personal engagement and understanding should factor into what qualifies as knowledge over straightforward truth-tracking.**
