# Knowledge as Infinitist Coherentism

**Parent:** [Can coherentism deal with the problem of circularity?](can-coherentism-deal-with-the-problem-of-circularity-antithesis-37323b11-1ff6-4878-a2d7-2dbcee985489.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres with an infinite chain of justifying beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief coheres with an infinite chain of justifying beliefs**
