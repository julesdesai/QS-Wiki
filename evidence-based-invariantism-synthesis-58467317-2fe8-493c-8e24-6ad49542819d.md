## Content

**Knowledge depends on the quality of evidence available to the knower**, **A person knows a proposition if the proposition is true**, **Standards for knowing a proposition are determined by objective availability of evidence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **Justification relies on the presence of sufficient evidence, which remains fixed regardless of context**
