## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed through dependable interaction with the environment**, **Dependable interaction ensures that beliefs generally correspond to reality**
