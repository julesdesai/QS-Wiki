## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that fixed and universal standards for knowledge are met**, **The standards for knowledge do not change regardless of context or subject matter**
