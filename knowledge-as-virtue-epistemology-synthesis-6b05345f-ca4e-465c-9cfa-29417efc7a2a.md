# Knowledge as Virtue Epistemology

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 6b05345f-ca4e-465c-9cfa-29417efc7a2a
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is safe belief reliable belief?](is-safe-belief-reliable-belief-antithesis-8488d7a4-a657-409a-958c-b8f7b48841d0.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief be formed through an exercise of intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief results from the exercise of intellectual virtue**
