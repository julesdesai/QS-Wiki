# How can foundational beliefs coexist with infinite chains?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c2f09c5e-640f-4f94-ac84-8a49d3f40fdd
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundational Infinitism](foundational-infinitism-synthesis-8fb4ffc2-abd6-406c-b0e9-89dab8b3bfd6.md)

## Content

**Foundational beliefs imply that some beliefs do not require further justification, which seems inconsistent with the requirement for an infinite chain of justifications.**, **If a foundational belief is self-justified, the demand for an infinite chain of reasons becomes redundant.**, **Infinite chains necessitate that every belief relies on another for justification, undermining any concept of a belief being foundational.**
