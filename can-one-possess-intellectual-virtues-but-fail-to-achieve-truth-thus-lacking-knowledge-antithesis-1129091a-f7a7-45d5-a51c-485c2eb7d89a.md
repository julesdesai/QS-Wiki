# Can one possess intellectual virtues but fail to achieve truth, thus lacking knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1129091a-f7a7-45d5-a51c-485c2eb7d89a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-91237602-868c-4a49-88c0-4e168ec082b8.md)

## Content

**There are cases where a person approaches problems with exemplary intellectual virtues but consistently encounters misleading circumstances.**, **Such cases might provide a true aim but fail to yield true beliefs, challenging the sufficiency of intellectual virtues for knowledge.**
