# Can perceptual illusions undermine the correspondence of perception to external objects?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 37eba0f3-7ec8-4ec3-9b53-10faee4266dc
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Direct Realism with Strong Correspondence](direct-realism-with-strong-correspondence-synthesis-28884d49-4ced-45da-83a0-528139a02f57.md)

## Content

**There are cases where perceptual experiences are systematically deceptive, such as mirages where a distant object appears to be present when it is not.**, **In such cases, intuitively, the perceptual experiences do not correspond to external objects.**, **If perceptual experiences do not always correspond to external objects, the claim that they do may be challenged.**
