## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief is produced by cognitive faculties functioning properly**, **Proper functioning is determined by a design plan successfully aimed at truth**
