## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they can rule out relevant alternatives where the proposition is false**, **Knowledge requires the ability to distinguish the proposition from relevant false alternatives**
