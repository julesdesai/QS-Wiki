## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no defeaters present**, **A defeater is a true proposition that, if known, would lead the person to no longer hold the belief**
