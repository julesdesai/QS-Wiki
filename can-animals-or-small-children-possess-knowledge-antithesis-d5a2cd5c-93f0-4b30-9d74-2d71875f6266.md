# Can animals or small children possess knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d5a2cd5c-93f0-4b30-9d74-2d71875f6266
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Internalist Reliabilism Summary](internalist-reliabilism-summary-synthesis-afd99030-664a-4a62-9431-6f8bc1e36717.md)

## Content

**Animals and small children often hold true beliefs formed by reliable cognitive processes.**, **They typically lack the internal access to the reliability of these processes required by internalist reliabilism.**, **This raises the question of whether internalist reliabilism can account for their apparent knowledge.**
