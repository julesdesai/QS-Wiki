# Can misleading evidence challenge the safety in knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7ced8b04-3530-4c7e-a22b-e261dc53cfdd
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Safety-Based Theory of Knowledge](safety-based-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-1cacd25a-9a70-4642-920d-232b8ef6dbd3.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person sees a shadow and believes it is a bear when it is actually a rock.**, **In nearby possible worlds where a bear is blocking the rock, the belief remains false due to misleading cues.**, **Thus, relying on misleading evidence might call into question if they truly know.**
