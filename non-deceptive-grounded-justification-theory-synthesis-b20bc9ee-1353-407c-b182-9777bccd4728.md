# Non-Deceptive Grounded Justification Theory

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person's belief is not formed under deceptive conditions**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their justification is independently verified**
