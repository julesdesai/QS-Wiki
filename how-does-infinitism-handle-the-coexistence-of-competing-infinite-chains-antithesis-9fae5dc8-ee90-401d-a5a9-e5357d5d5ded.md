# How does infinitism handle the coexistence of competing infinite chains?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9fae5dc8-ee90-401d-a5a9-e5357d5d5ded
**Depth:** 2
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Knowledge as Infinitism](knowledge-as-infinitism-thesis-1ccb10a4-65b9-4ca2-911c-7c3a5e2f3c55.md)

## Content

**It is conceivable that for any proposition, multiple infinite chains of reasons could exist, some potentially leading to contradictory conclusions.**, **If contradictory conclusions can each be justified by separate, infinitely long chains, then infinite chains cannot provide a decisive criterion for knowledge.**
