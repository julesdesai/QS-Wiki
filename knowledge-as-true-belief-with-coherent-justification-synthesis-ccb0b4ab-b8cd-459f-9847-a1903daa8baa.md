# Knowledge as True Belief with Coherent Justification

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** ccb0b4ab-b8cd-459f-9847-a1903daa8baa
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge exist in isolation from social practices?](can-knowledge-exist-in-isolation-from-social-practices-antithesis-859cbc96-354d-4c83-978c-0e9296d62321.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is required that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief cohere with a system of justified beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief coheres with a system of justified beliefs**
