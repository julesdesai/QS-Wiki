# Knowledge with True Method Justification

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** a87bdb4b-0b3c-4b10-99d0-cbe1375ca090
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [What if manipulation leads to justified beliefs?](what-if-manipulation-leads-to-justified-beliefs-antithesis-fcda31cf-0de1-4468-9bbb-e1d9d579e203.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified by a reliable method**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification method is not the result of manipulation**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is justified by a reliable method not resulting from manipulation**
