# Knowledge with a Two-Tiered Reliability Condition

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** ef12ef91-de86-4837-85a6-984ecb3483ae
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does practical usefulness always accompany reliable knowledge formation?](does-practical-usefulness-always-accompany-reliable-knowledge-formation-antithesis-25cbfa72-0ddd-4856-a880-1fa3a4ce96dd.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed through a first-order reliable process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is justified by a second-order reliability check**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, their belief is formed through a first-order reliable process, and justified by a second-order reliability check**
