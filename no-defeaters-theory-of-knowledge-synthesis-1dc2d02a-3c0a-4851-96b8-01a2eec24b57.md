## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no defeaters present**, **A defeater is information or evidence that would undermine or contradict the justification for a belief**
