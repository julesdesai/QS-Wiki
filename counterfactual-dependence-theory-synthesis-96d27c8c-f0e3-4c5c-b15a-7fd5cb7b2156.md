## Content

**Knowledge depends on counterfactual dependence to the truth**, **A person knows a proposition if, in nearby possible worlds, they would hold the belief only if it were true**, **A person knows a proposition if, in nearby possible worlds, they would not hold the belief if it were false**, **Counterfactual dependence requires that changes in truth status affect the belief status across close possible scenarios**
