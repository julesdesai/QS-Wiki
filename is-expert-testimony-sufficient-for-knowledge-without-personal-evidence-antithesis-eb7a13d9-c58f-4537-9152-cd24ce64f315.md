# Is expert testimony sufficient for knowledge without personal evidence?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** eb7a13d9-c58f-4537-9152-cd24ce64f315
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Evidentialist Theory of Knowledge](evidentialist-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-1034ea09-f6fc-4c03-a110-072015f4c221.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which someone believes a scientific claim solely based on expert testimony, without direct evidence.**, **Intuitively, we might judge that person possesses knowledge due to trust in expertise.**, **This questions the need for personal evidence to justify knowledge claims.**
