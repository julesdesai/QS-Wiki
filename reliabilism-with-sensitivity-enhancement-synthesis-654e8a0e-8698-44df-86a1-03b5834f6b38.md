## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief-forming process is reliable and sensitive to true conditions**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person would not believe the proposition if it were false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that reliable and sensitive belief-forming processes are in place**
