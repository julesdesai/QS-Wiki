# Do infinite chains of reasons lead to skepticism rather than knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** dd7f07f1-1dab-489e-9a4a-4f3f4b29313e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism](infinitism-synthesis-d655bb23-a2c0-4c5f-b0d3-18cd986ab587.md)

## Content

**An infinite regress of reasons threatens to devolve into skepticism, as we can never have complete or final justification.**, **If each link in an infinite chain must contribute to knowledge, then no knowledge can be secured because the process is never complete.**, **Consider a scenario in which a person attempts to justify their belief endlessly without reaching a conclusion, intuitively such a process seems not to yield knowledge.**
