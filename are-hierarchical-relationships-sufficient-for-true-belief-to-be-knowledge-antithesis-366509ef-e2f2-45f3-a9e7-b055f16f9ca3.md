# Are hierarchical relationships sufficient for true belief to be knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 366509ef-e2f2-45f3-a9e7-b055f16f9ca3
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Structural Foundationalism](structural-foundationalism-synthesis-037c32e2-5bf4-4b6c-88a6-0e0659b44b9e.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a belief is true and fits within a hierarchical structure, but lacking evidence or reason for the truth. Intuitively, we either have to say that it is not knowledge or it fails the sufficiency of structure alone.**, **There is a plausible principle that evidence, beyond structure, is required for true belief to qualify as knowledge.**
