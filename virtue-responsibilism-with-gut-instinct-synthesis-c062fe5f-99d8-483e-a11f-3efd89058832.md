# Virtue Responsibilism with Gut Instinct

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Are reliable cognitive processes necessary for all knowledge?](are-reliable-cognitive-processes-necessary-for-all-knowledge-antithesis-6b9067bf-3b12-4c69-a441-fb7f3df9001d.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief to be formed through intellectual virtue that can include gut instincts**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is formed through an intellectual virtue that may involve gut feeling**
