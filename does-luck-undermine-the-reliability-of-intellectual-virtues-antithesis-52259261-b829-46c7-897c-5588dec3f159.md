# Does luck undermine the reliability of intellectual virtues?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 52259261-b829-46c7-897c-5588dec3f159
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-43f1bd52-38ff-4c3b-a3d7-408481a71c52.md)

## Content

**Imagine a situation where a person discovers a bank has altered their schedule, confirming their belief about an impending shift based on a lucky guess rather than observation. Here, intuitive judgment views the belief as true yet not resulting from intellectual virtue.**, **Beliefs coinciding with truth through luck rather than reliable intellectual processes lack the hallmark of knowledge.**
