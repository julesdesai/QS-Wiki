# No False Grounds Condition on Knowledge

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Is true belief without safety condition sufficient for knowledge?](is-true-belief-without-safety-condition-sufficient-for-knowledge-antithesis-d1b4fe7d-c0f9-47ae-b261-c1c2c7bd4dfe.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that none of their reasons for belief depend on falsehoods**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, the proposition is true, and there are no falsehoods in their grounds for belief**
