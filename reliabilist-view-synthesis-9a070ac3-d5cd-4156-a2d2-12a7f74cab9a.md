## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **A reliable process of forming beliefs, if it consistently leads to true beliefs, can suffice for knowledge**, **Justification is a matter of the reliability of the belief-forming process**
