# Reliabilist-Influenced Evidentialism

**Parent:** [Are there situations where evidence is unnecessary but knowledge still exists?](are-there-situations-where-evidence-is-unnecessary-but-knowledge-still-exists-antithesis-9449747f-c010-493c-8054-f339a0c4526b.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is produced through a reliable cognitive process which itself suffices as evidence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief in the proposition involves an appropriate degree of conviction based on the reliability of the process**
