# Could our knowledge of appearances transcend the subjective to gain intersubjective validity?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9ca24220-75ee-4c80-9433-3b8895a9bd7c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Transcendental Idealism](transcendental-idealism-synthesis-de14e49b-50e4-409a-b85a-983076811c68.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have cases in which different observers consistently report the same phenomena under varying conditions. Intuitively, this intersubjective agreement suggests that knowledge of appearances can transcend individual subjectivity, challenging the notion that such knowledge is strictly conditioned by subjective cognitive structures.**
