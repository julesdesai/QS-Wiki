# Does the transfer of knowledge through testimony conflict with the definition of intellectual virtues?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 90295895-5926-4f70-ab0e-ab22bf5dbff3
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-72984f03-1ce3-46fd-9fd6-13203e340ace.md)

## Content

**Suppose knowledge is acquired through testimony from a reliable informant, and the recipient lacks intellectual virtues. Intuitively, we still consider the recipient's true belief as knowledge.**, **The transfer of knowledge through testimony challenges the idea that knowledge must always stem from personal intellectual virtues.**
