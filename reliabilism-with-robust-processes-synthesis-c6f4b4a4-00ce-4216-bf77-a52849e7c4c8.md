## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know, it is necessary that their belief results from a reliable cognitive process**, **Reliable cognitive processes can produce beliefs that are fallible**
