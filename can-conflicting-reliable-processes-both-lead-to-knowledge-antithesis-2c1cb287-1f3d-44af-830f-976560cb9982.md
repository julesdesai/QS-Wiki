# Can conflicting reliable processes both lead to knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2c1cb287-1f3d-44af-830f-976560cb9982
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism](reliabilism-synthesis-f6f2f982-fa0f-404e-a509-24417604aa1e.md)

## Content

**Suppose two individuals use different but reliable processes to arrive at contradictory beliefs about the same proposition.**, **If both processes are reliable, it leads to the problematic conclusion that contradictory beliefs could count as knowledge.**
