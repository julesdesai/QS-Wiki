# Can belief in a false proposition imply knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 891a57c6-7701-4c58-aff7-4885eaa860b1
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Tracking Theory of Knowledge](tracking-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-6d302593-f2de-4e10-8195-85a314e81400.md)

## Content

**If a person knows a proposition, then they must believe it.**, **Suppose there is a person who believes a proposition that is actually false.**, **It is implausible to suggest that believing a false proposition could count as knowledge, since knowledge implies truth.**
