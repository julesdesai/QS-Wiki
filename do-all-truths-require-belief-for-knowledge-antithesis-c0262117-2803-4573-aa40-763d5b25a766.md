# Do all truths require belief for knowledge?

## Content

**Consider propositions such as mathematical truths that are true regardless of belief. It is implausible that such truths require individual belief to be known.**, **A case in which a person learns a mathematical truth from evidence without prior belief illustrates that knowledge does not always require prior belief.**
