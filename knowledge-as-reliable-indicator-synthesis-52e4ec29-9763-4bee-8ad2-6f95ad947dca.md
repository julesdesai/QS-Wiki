## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their method of belief formation reliably produces true belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the person believes the proposition, it is true, and their method of belief formation reliably produces true belief**
