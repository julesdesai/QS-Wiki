# Is coherence sufficient for belief justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 799798cc-b7ac-410b-b49a-3e327d60ffdb
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Fallibilist Coherentism](fallibilist-coherentism-synthesis-f085a715-bc4b-46dd-bc93-6874997728cd.md)

## Content

**Suppose there is a coherent system of belief that is completely detached from reality and based on false premises.**, **Intuitively, coherence alone seems inadequate for justification if the beliefs are not also reliably connected to the truth or external reality.**
