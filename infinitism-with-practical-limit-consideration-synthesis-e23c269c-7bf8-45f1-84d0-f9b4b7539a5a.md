## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that justification involves infinite chains of reasons approximated practically**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there is a practically attainable approximation of an infinite, non-repeating chain of consistent reasons justifying the belief**
