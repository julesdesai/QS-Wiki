# Does coherence alone suffice for justification?

**Parent:** [Coherentism Summary](coherentism-summary-synthesis-4ac849ed-bd0f-4658-91df-2567162ccdab.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that a set of highly coherent beliefs can ensure justification if they are completely detached from any empirical reality**, **Suppose there is a case where an individual creates a highly plausible and coherent fictional narrative. Intuitively, the coherence of the narrative seems insufficient to claim knowledge unless it relates back to some observational or empirical data**
