# Is knowledge possible through passive observation?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** fa5e99b1-6867-4716-a69a-e39b21e26dc1
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-5eebb8d4-de96-4ab7-90ca-fa4ae98a5219.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person forms beliefs through passive observation of environmental cues without active engagement or intellectual virtue.**, **In this case, intuitively, we would judge that these beliefs can constitute knowledge because of the consistent reliability of passive observation.**
