## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is sensitive to the truth**, **Belief sensitivity requires that if the proposition were false, the person would not hold the belief**
