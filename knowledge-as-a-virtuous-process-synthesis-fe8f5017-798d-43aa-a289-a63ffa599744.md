## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A belief qualifies as knowledge if it results from a cognitive process that is not only reliable but also exhibits intellectual virtues**, **A cognitive process exhibits intellectual virtues if it includes aspects like open-mindedness, intellectual courage, and intellectual thoroughness**
