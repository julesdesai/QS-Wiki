## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is not based on any false assumptions**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing it, it is true, and it is not based on any false assumptions**
