## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres with a foundational system of beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the foundational system contains basic beliefs that are themselves justified without further inference**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief coheres with a foundational belief system, and the foundational system contains justified basic beliefs**
