# Knowledge as No Defeaters Reliabilism

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** f9ed0408-ba26-40ba-aab0-df7eaeccfc6b
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can reliable processes yield false beliefs?](can-reliable-processes-yield-false-beliefs-antithesis-01506b31-8422-43e9-8c49-ddecd7b10bdc.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no true propositions, known by the person, that would defeat their justification for the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is produced by a reliable process, and there are no known defeaters**
