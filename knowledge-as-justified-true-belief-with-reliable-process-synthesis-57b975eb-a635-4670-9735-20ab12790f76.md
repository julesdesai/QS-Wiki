## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief is justified and formed through a reliable process**, **For a belief to constitute knowledge, it is necessary that the belief is justified**
