# Does exercising virtue guarantee knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d8c70e5a-2531-4417-8b07-bd61dcf70166
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-35af9163-7530-4447-9474-ccb688222939.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that merely exercising intellectual virtue guarantees knowledge, as external factors can influence the truth of the propositions one believes.**, **Suppose we have a case where someone exercises intellectual virtue but happens to base their conclusions on a premise that is false; intuitively, exercise of virtue in this case would not lead to knowledge.**
