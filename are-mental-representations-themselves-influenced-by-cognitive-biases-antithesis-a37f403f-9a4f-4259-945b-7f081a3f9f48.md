# Are mental representations themselves influenced by cognitive biases?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a37f403f-9a4f-4259-945b-7f081a3f9f48
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Representationalism](representationalism-synthesis-e49b01dd-7eac-432b-990b-a606f4616c7d.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person perceives events differently due to the influence of prior beliefs, such as political biases affecting perceptions of a debate.**, **Intuitively, such biases suggest that mental representations are subject to internal distortions.**, **This implies that the accuracy of mental representations in conveying external truths may be compromised by cognitive biases.**
