# What if the skill is externally derived rather than internally generated?

## Content

**Suppose a person uses a basic algorithm to predict outcomes at work and form beliefs about future trends accurately. The beliefs are based on this algorithm's skill rather than the individual’s.**, **The beliefs are accurate thanks to the algorithm’s skill, raising the question of whether beliefs are less attuned by individual adroitness when external aids are involved.**
