## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in nearly all nearby possible worlds where they believe the proposition via any reliable method, it is true**, **Reliability across multiple methods ensures true belief avoids being coincidental**
