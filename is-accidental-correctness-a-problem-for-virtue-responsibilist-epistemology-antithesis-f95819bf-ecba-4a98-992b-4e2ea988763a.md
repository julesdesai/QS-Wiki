# Is accidental correctness a problem for virtue-responsibilist epistemology?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f95819bf-ecba-4a98-992b-4e2ea988763a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue-Responsibilist Epistemology](virtue-responsibilist-epistemology-synthesis-ad635627-9cca-4d73-9dab-ad2a12c77e90.md)

## Content

**Suppose there is a scenario where an intellectually virtuous person predicts a future event accurately, yet the truth of the prediction is purely accidental; it seems that virtue did not contribute to the knowledge.**, **It is implausible that purely accidental correctness results in knowledge, even when intellectual virtue is present.**
