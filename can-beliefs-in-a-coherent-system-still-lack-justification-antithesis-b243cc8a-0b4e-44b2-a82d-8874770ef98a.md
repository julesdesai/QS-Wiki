# Can beliefs in a coherent system still lack justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b243cc8a-0b4e-44b2-a82d-8874770ef98a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism with finite justification](coherentism-with-finite-justification-synthesis-b896ee94-3397-4ff0-b92e-2df5df1929c9.md)

## Content

**A coherent system of beliefs can include beliefs that are unrelated to evidence.**, **If a belief is not related to evidence, it seems unjustified.**, **Therefore, a coherent system of beliefs could still ever bear unjustified beliefs.**
