# Is Infinitism at odds with common intuitions about justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7fbf83ca-699f-498f-ae69-9152f525d8ee
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism](infinitism-synthesis-db00e7c1-6a13-4ca5-8f09-6fae7cd29e0f.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person seeks justification for the belief that it is raining, using weather reports as evidence. Intuitively, sufficient justification does not require an infinite chain of reasons.**, **It is implausible that common-sense beliefs about justification align with Infinitism's requirement for an infinite chain of reasons.**
