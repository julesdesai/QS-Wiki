## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **The standards for justification change depending on the context of the knowledge claim**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient if they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification meets the contextually determined standards**
