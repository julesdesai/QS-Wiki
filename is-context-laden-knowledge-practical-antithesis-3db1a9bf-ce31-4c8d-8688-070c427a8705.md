# Is context-laden knowledge practical?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3db1a9bf-ce31-4c8d-8688-070c427a8705
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism](contextualism-synthesis-ed8715f4-7106-486f-8992-736e64ae52d2.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where decisions are made based on varying contextual knowledge claims. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that practical decision-making becomes unreliable.**, **It is implausible that practical knowledge claims necessary for decision-making should hinge on subjective context rather than objective fact.**
