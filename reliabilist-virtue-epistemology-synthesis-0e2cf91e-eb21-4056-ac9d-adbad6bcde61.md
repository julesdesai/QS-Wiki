## Content

**Knowledge is a true belief arising from a reliable process**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process, such as perception or memory**, **For a belief to constitute knowledge, it must originate from a process that generally yields true beliefs**, **Knowledge requires the truth to be creditable to the reliability of the process, not necessarily to individual cognitive abilities**
