# Can external environmental factors undermine the anti-luck condition?

**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology with Anti-Luck Condition](virtue-epistemology-with-anti-luck-condition-synthesis-d2e613e4-56dd-4edb-a19a-a70d714eba0b.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a skilled driver always checks their mirrors before changing lanes and forms the belief that it is safe to proceed, but a car is approaching in the blind spot unseen due to glare, making the decision seem luck-based. Intuitively, this situation involves luck not accounted for by intellectual virtues.**, **Factors such as environmental changes can introduce luck in beliefs, challenging the notion that virtues are sufficient to exclude epistemic luck.**
