# Is stake-influenced knowledge an inconsistent theory?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** fa4e5358-6c1a-425a-98e6-65cf1ba96816
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Subject-Sensitive Invariantism Summary](subject-sensitive-invariantism-summary-synthesis-519a57d4-27a1-46ac-ac38-c130e30ad088.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that a theoretically consistent view allows practical considerations to alter knowledge attribution.**, **Suppose we have a case in which belief truth and justification are identical, but knowledge attribution shifts with stakes. Intuitively, this inconsistency questions the soundness of such a theory.**
