# Knowledge with Reliable Perceptual Justification

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can someone have perceptual justification without knowledge?](can-someone-have-perceptual-justification-without-knowledge-antithesis-b7b2ba14-70eb-4ec0-ac28-7f8d6522dc91.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have perceptual justification that is reliable and inferential justification**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and they have reliable perceptual or inferential justification**
