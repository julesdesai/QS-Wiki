# Knowledge as Consistently Trust-Based True Belief

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 82dc268b-ad73-4433-bb88-d7f77d325f6b
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does momentary trust equate to stable knowledge?](does-momentary-trust-equate-to-stable-knowledge-antithesis-c58bd050-f4f9-49d6-8ae1-75d1d50eee04.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from consistently evaluated trust between knower and source**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief arises from consistently evaluated trust between knower and source**
