**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a belief to be safe, it must be true not only in most nearby possible worlds but also in worlds where relevant environmental conditions are similar**, **A belief is safe only if it accounts for variations in environmental conditions across possible worlds**
