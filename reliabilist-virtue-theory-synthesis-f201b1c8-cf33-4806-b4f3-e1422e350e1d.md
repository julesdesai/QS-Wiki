## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is the result of a process that could be deemed virtuous in a significant majority of similar cases**, **A virtuous process is one that yields reliable outcomes over time**
