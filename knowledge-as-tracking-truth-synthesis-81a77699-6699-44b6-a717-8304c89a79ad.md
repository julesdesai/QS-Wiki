# Knowledge as Tracking Truth

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Are intellectual virtues context-dependent for knowledge?](are-intellectual-virtues-context-dependent-for-knowledge-antithesis-6477b3aa-dc2c-4c78-9fd9-6e37e4353b21.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they can track the truth across close possible worlds**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and they can track the truth across close possible worlds**
