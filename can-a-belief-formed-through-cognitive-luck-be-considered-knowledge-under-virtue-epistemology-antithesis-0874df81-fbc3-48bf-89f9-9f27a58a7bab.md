# Can a belief formed through cognitive luck be considered knowledge under virtue epistemology?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0874df81-fbc3-48bf-89f9-9f27a58a7bab
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-72984f03-1ce3-46fd-9fd6-13203e340ace.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person forms a true belief due to cognitive luck, like guessing correctly. In this case, intuitively, we would not count this belief as knowledge.**, **A belief that is a result of cognitive luck is not a product of an intellectual virtue, and thus it is implausible to consider such beliefs as knowledge.**
