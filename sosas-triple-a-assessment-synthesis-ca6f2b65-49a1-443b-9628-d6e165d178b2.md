## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is apt, meaning it is accurate because it was adroitly acquired**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief's accuracy is due to the exercise of intellectual skill**
