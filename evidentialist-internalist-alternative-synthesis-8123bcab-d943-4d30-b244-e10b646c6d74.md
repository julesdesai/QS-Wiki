## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have evidence accessible to them**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that this evidence constitutes justification for the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary and sufficient that they have accessible justification and the proposition is true**
