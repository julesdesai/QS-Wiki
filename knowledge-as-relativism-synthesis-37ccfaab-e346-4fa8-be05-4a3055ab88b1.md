## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that in the context, the standards for knowledge are met according to personal cognitive perspectives**, **The standards for knowledge can vary depending on individual perspectives, not just conversational contexts**
