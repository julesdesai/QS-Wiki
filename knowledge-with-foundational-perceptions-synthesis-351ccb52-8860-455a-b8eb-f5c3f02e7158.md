## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief-forming process is reliable**, **For basic beliefs, coherence with a system may not be necessary**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that if non-basic, the belief coheres with a system of interconnected beliefs, and they believe the proposition, which is true, and the belief-forming process is reliable**
