## Content

**Knowledge is true belief that approximately arises from intellectual virtue which reliably avoids error**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be approximately true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief arises from intellectual virtue that reliably avoids error**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe it, the proposition is approximately true, and their intellectual virtue reliably avoids error**
