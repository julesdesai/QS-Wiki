# Safety-Based Theory of Knowledge

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Can unreliable processes sometimes lead to true beliefs?](can-unreliable-processes-sometimes-lead-to-true-beliefs-antithesis-8541caae-a29c-43f5-a545-1ca18b3ac2e6.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **Knowledge requires that the belief could not easily have been false in similar circumstances**, **A belief is safe if in most nearby possible worlds where the person forms that belief in the same way, it is true**
