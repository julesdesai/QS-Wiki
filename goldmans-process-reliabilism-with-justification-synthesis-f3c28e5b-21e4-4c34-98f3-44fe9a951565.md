## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have a true belief**, **A belief is justified if it is produced by a reliable process**, **A person knows a proposition if they believe it, the proposition is true, and the belief is justified**, **A reliable process is one that tends to produce a preponderance of true over false beliefs**
