## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is the result of the person's intellectual virtues**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, it is true, and the belief is a result of intellectual virtues**
