## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **The standards for knowledge vary with context, affecting whether a person knows a proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and they meet the relevant contextual standards for knowledge**
