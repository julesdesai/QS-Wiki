## Content

**A belief constitutes knowledge if it is true in nearly all nearby possible worlds**, **Knowledge requires beliefs that are safe from error in similar scenarios**, **Intellectual virtues contribute by making it more likely that beliefs remain true in varying conditions**, **Truth is not necessary to be directly creditable to the knower; rather, the belief must be safe from falsity**
