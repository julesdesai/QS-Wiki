# Do practical stakes affect the objective nature of knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 494cf09b-6dbc-4503-93fc-986ab066a8fa
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Encroachment Theory](pragmatic-encroachment-theory-synthesis-48f209b4-b71c-4b9b-814c-8c2a5af0c840.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person T has a belief based on strong evidence that a bridge is safe to cross. The practical stakes for T involve just taking a leisurely stroll. Intuitively, T knows the bridge is safe.**, **Suppose person U has the same belief and evidence but must cross the bridge to escape danger. Intuitively, U also knows the bridge is safe.**, **It is implausible that the objective nature of knowledge can shift merely because the practical stakes for T and U differ.**
