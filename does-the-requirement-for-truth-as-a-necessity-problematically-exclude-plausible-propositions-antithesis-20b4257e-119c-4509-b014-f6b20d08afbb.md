# Does the requirement for truth as a necessity problematically exclude plausible propositions?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 20b4257e-119c-4509-b014-f6b20d08afbb
**Depth:** 4

## Content

**Consider a proposition that is very likely but not yet proven as true, such as future scientific discoveries. Intuitively, the proposition might still be regarded as worthy of belief.**, **There is a plausible principle that some knowledge can be probabilistic rather than strictly true, suggesting necessity for actual truth may exclude significant knowledge claims.**
