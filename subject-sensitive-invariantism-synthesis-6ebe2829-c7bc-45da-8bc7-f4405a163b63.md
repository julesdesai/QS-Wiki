## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **The level of justification required for knowledge is partially dependent on the subject's own stakes in the truth of the proposition**, **Higher stakes necessitate more robust justification for knowledge**
