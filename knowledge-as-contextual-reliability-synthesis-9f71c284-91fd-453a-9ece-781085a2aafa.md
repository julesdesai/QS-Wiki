# Knowledge as Contextual Reliability

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 9f71c284-91fd-453a-9ece-781085a2aafa
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is reliability alone sufficient for knowledge?](is-reliability-alone-sufficient-for-knowledge-antithesis-5ddbe75f-bd2a-47df-b261-ee7f3840eba8.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is the result of a reliable cognitive process**, **The reliability of a cognitive process is contextually dependent on the situation and environment of the believer**
