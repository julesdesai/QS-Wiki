## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **A person knows a proposition if, when the proposition is true, they believe it**, **A person knows a proposition if, when the proposition is false, they do not believe it**
