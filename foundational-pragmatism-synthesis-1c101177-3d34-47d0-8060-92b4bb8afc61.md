## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification involves a pragmatic chain of reasons beginning with action-oriented foundational beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there exists a pragmatic chain beginning with action-oriented foundational beliefs justifying the proposition**
