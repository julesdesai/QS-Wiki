# Can a design plan always guarantee justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 674dcaac-fe04-43a3-ac4a-3d88742cf29b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Proper Function Theory](proper-function-theory-synthesis-ea617099-7596-484d-aca6-1b318b0033ac.md)

## Content

**Suppose an individual is part of a community where cognitive faculties systematically produce false beliefs due to flawed cultural norms. In such a scenario, these faculties are functioning according to the design plan but fail to deliver justification.**, **It is implausible that merely following a design plan guarantees genuine justification if the design plan itself is fundamentally flawed.**
