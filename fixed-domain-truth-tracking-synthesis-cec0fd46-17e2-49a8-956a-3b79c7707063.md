## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person's belief tracks truth within a fixed domain of considerations**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the fixed domain remains consistent across possible situations**
