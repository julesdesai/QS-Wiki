# Knowledge as Reliable Process Generated True Belief

**Parent:** [Are there cases where justified true belief is not sufficient for knowledge?](are-there-cases-where-justified-true-belief-is-not-sufficient-for-knowledge-antithesis-a90b56be-0539-4ee9-aa3b-8f1d7e39bdfd.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is produced by a reliable process**
