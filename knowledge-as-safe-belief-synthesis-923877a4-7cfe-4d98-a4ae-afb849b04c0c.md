# Knowledge as Safe Belief

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 923877a4-7cfe-4d98-a4ae-afb849b04c0c
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does intellectual virtue always guarantee truth?](does-intellectual-virtue-always-guarantee-truth-antithesis-764d80ad-f845-4d95-8c4e-b46d6ed3961e.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief was formed in a way that could not have easily been false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief was formed safely**
