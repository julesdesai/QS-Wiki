## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is not necessary for them to explicitly believe the proposition**, **Implicit valuation of the proposition informs a person’s worldview even without explicit belief**
