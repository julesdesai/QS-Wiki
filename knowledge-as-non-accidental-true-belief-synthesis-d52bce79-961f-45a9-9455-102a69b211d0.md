# Knowledge as Non-Accidental True Belief

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** d52bce79-961f-45a9-9455-102a69b211d0
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is it possible for a person to know something that is accidentally true?](is-it-possible-for-a-person-to-know-something-that-is-accidentally-true-antithesis-03b855db-676a-4e06-a275-82b46aa837b8.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is not true merely by accident**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is not true merely by accident**
