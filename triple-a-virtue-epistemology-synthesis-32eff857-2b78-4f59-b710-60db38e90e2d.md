# Triple-A Virtue Epistemology

**Parent:** [Is there an epistemic gap between belief and intellectual virtues?](is-there-an-epistemic-gap-between-belief-and-intellectual-virtues-antithesis-e6529912-b33a-4831-8725-454cf05eb93f.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from cognitive aptness**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief arises from cognitive aptness**
