**Parent:** [Is a true belief with an incorrect causal story still knowledge?](is-a-true-belief-with-an-incorrect-causal-story-still-knowledge-antithesis-358e193a-2708-4157-9da9-99a142654c6a.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is the result of properly functioning cognitive faculties**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is the result of properly functioning cognitive faculties**
