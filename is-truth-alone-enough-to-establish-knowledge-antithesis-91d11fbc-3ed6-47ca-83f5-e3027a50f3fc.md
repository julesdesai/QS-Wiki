# Is truth alone enough to establish knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 91d11fbc-3ed6-47ca-83f5-e3027a50f3fc
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism Without Memory Constraints](infinitism-without-memory-constraints-synthesis-df5a4028-0f5d-4102-8a53-7b53596c5281.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person unknowingly flips a coin and happens to guess the correct outcome purely by chance. Intuitively, although the person's belief is true, we would not claim they have knowledge of the outcome.**, **It is implausible that merely true beliefs qualify as knowledge without justification or understanding.**
