**Parent:** [Can a belief cohere with a fragment that is isolated but faulty?](can-a-belief-cohere-with-a-fragment-that-is-isolated-but-faulty-antithesis-6770cdbd-2eb5-4dea-b611-983e7566138f.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified by its role in a coherent system of beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are external factors that connect the belief to the truth of the proposition**
