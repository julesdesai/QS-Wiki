# Are beliefs about unobservable entities sensitive?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ff104ed0-d855-47bc-9e06-8603d7405d37
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Knowledge as Sensitivity](knowledge-as-sensitivity-synthesis-a361f947-df1a-4345-a4d3-ea9ac7712828.md)

## Content

**Consider a scientist who believes in the existence of neutrinos based on indirect evidence from experiments.**, **The belief in neutrinos remains true because the evidence supports their existence, but their existence cannot be observed directly.**, **Given the lack of direct observation, sensitivity seems challenged as the belief might persist were the entities not truly existent.**
