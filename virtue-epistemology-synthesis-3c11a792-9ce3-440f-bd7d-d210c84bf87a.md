## Content

**A person knows a proposition if their belief is the result of the exercise of intellectual virtues**, **Intellectual virtues include traits like open-mindedness, intellectual courage, and intellectual humility**, **Knowledge requires that a person's belief be formed through these virtuous cognitive processes**, **The virtuous formation of belief ensures a strong link between belief and truth**
