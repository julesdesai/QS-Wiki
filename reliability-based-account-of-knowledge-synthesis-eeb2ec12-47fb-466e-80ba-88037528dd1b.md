## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed through a reliable method**, **The method is considered reliable if it consistently leads to true beliefs across various situations**
