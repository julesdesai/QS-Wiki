# Is contextualism susceptible to the problem of subjectivity?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b996a3ae-9579-477a-b979-736f8d1c086f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism Summary](contextualism-summary-synthesis-970b1696-c212-4dd0-be78-254d7b0cf7ce.md)

## Content

**If knowledge justification depends entirely on a context, then subjective judgment plays a crucial role in setting the context. It is implausible that subjective judgment alone should determine objectivity in knowledge acquisition.**
