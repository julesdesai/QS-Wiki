## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is a result of intellectual virtue or ability**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief fits coherently within a web of other beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, their belief results from intellectual virtue, and it fits coherently within a web of other beliefs**
