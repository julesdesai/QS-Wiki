# Do unknown cognitive designs challenge knowledge judgment?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3e729055-5fa7-4ffd-8e0f-bf0094a4f53a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Proper Functionalist Knowledge Summary](proper-functionalist-knowledge-summary-synthesis-ea5a08e3-f799-4ced-bdad-582e7b9aaf66.md)

## Content

**Consider an individual who holds true beliefs, yet their cognitive faculties are of unknown or undetermined origin. Intuitively, we might struggle to judge these beliefs as knowledge.**, **It's plausible that knowing the origin and design of cognitive faculties aids in justifying beliefs.**
