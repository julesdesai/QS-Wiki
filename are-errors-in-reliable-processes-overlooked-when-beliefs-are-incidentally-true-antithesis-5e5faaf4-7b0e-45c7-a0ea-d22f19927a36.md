# Are errors in reliable processes overlooked when beliefs are incidentally true?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5e5faaf4-7b0e-45c7-a0ea-d22f19927a36
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalism with Reliable Beliefs](externalism-with-reliable-beliefs-synthesis-44f393ff-3a5c-4b93-8788-7ab032b1badd.md)

## Content

**Consider an investor using reliable financial models to make predictions about stock investments.**, **A technical error misrepresents data, yet the investor’s stock prediction turns out accurate due to an unrelated factor.**, **The belief’s truth emerges dependently on errors not accounted for in the reliable process, casting doubt on whether it constitutes knowledge.**
