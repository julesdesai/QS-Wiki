# Knowledge by Externalist Justification

**Parent:** [Is having strong conviction enough for knowledge if adequate reasons are absent?](is-having-strong-conviction-enough-for-knowledge-if-adequate-reasons-are-absent-antithesis-c1c0e5e4-968f-4c85-872a-df7c54b5e9c7.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified by factors external to the individual's mental states**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is justified by factors external to the individual's mental states**
