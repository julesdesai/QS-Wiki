# Knowledge as Causal Coherentism

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Does coherence allow for knowledge without causal connection?](does-coherence-allow-for-knowledge-without-causal-connection-antithesis-0cd0dfc3-46a7-46d2-9eee-7b32b52621de.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for their belief to be causally related to the proposition in question**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for their belief to cohere with other justified beliefs they possess**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, their belief is causally related to the proposition, and their belief coheres with their other justified beliefs**
