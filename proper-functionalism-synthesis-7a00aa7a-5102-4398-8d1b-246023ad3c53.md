## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their cognitive faculties function properly**, **Properly functioning cognitive faculties are those that are operating in accordance with a design plan aimed at truth**
