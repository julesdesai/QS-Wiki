## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition with intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is a result of a reliable cognitive faculty**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition with intellectual virtue, the proposition is true, and the belief results from a reliable cognitive faculty**
