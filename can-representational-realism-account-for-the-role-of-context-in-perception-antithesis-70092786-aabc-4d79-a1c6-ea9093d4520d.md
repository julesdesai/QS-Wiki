# Can Representational Realism account for the role of context in perception?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 70092786-aabc-4d79-a1c6-ea9093d4520d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Representational Realism](representational-realism-synthesis-2653bf9f-214b-48a1-b757-9a49097d2459.md)

## Content

**The same object can be perceived differently depending on the context, such as lighting or distance.**, **Even with different perceptions, the mental representation must still correspond to the same objective reality.**, **It is plausible that differing mental representations in varying contexts challenge the idea that knowledge requires a single accurate correspondence.**
