## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in nearby possible worlds, the belief could not have easily been false**, **Nearby possible worlds are those that are similar to the actual world**
