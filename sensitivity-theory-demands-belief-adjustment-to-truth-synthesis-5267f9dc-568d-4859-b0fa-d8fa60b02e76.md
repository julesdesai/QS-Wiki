# Sensitivity Theory demands belief adjustment to truth

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Is contextualism's account of knowledge too permissive?](is-contextualisms-account-of-knowledge-too-permissive-antithesis-7456cfe5-aede-4ddc-b7f4-afb2bae292b4.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they would not believe it if it were false**, **Sensitivity requires the belief to track the truth conditionally**
