# Knowledge as Social Virtue Reliant

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Is testimony reliant on another's virtue sufficient for knowledge?](is-testimony-reliant-on-anothers-virtue-sufficient-for-knowledge-antithesis-1c816eb0-1dfc-4642-bb52-bc342f6474f3.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief indirectly relies on a community's intellectual virtues**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief indirectly relies on a community's intellectual virtues**
