## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **When a person knows a proposition based on memory, it is either because the original justification endures or because the memory belief is supported directly by the fact**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their knowledge comes from either enduring justification or direct support by the fact**
