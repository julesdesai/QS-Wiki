# Is knowledge purely a construct of the mind?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a4962909-eae6-48de-bde4-ea1cc4dbb15d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Idealism](idealism-synthesis-53ec409c-4a9b-4261-be64-9dda9f92eb94.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where two people perceive a tree. One person sees it as a tree, while the other, due to an altered mental construct, sees it as a giant birdhouse. Intuitively, we would judge that the tree exists independently of these perceptions.**, **It is implausible that the existence of the tree as an object outside of perception is solely a construct of the mind.**
