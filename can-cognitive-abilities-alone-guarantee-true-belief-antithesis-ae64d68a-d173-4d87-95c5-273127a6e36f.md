# Can cognitive abilities alone guarantee true belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ae64d68a-d173-4d87-95c5-273127a6e36f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-b6aa4282-ad09-4174-b191-6cfd3fcbdf83.md)

## Content

**It is possible for a person to have strong cognitive abilities and yet form false beliefs due to misleading information**, **There is a plausible principle that valuable knowledge should reliably lead to truth. This principle together with the possibility of false beliefs formed with strong cognitive abilities challenges whether intellectual virtue alone is sufficient for knowledge**
