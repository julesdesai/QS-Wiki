# Is belief revision adequately accounted for in this theory?

**Parent:** [Nozick's Conditional Theory of Knowledge](nozicks-conditional-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-ff394d52-e522-4d27-8272-d4808dc162bd.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person revises their belief upon receiving new evidence, but the initial belief was true by luck rather than method. In such cases, intuitively, this lacks knowledge coherence.**, **A plausible principle states belief revision upon new evidence should be compatible with knowledge retention, yet luck devalues this coherence.**
