**Parent:** [Is coherence relevant for knowledge if empirical grounding suffices?](is-coherence-relevant-for-knowledge-if-empirical-grounding-suffices-antithesis-144441b0-3f13-4824-b97e-45109c4c84ee.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is grounded in empirical evidence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is grounded in empirical evidence**
