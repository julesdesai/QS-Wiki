# Knowledge as Virtuous Coherence

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** a77fa575-967d-4139-acbc-19c240d5bbd0
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is knowledge merely true belief?](is-knowledge-merely-true-belief-antithesis-5eac6ff0-9d61-4fd8-832a-f55645fe3776.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is a result of the exercise of intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres with other justified beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is produced by intellectual virtue, and the belief coheres with other justified beliefs**
