# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with No Defeaters

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Can reliabilism account for informed guesswork leading to truth?](can-reliabilism-account-for-informed-guesswork-leading-to-truth-antithesis-e3322615-4203-4135-aff1-1d38e380b19b.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary there are no defeaters to this justified belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, they have justification for the belief, and no defeaters are present**
