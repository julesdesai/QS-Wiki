## Content

**Knowledge does not transmit through a chain of believers unless each link in the chain is itself a reliable process of belief formation**, **For a person to know a proposition through testimony, it is necessary that the proposition is known by the testifier**, **For a reliable transmission of knowledge, each person's belief must independently be produced by a reliable cognitive process**
