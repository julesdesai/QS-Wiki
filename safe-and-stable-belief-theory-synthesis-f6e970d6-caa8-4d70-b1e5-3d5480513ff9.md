# Safe and Stable Belief Theory

**Parent:** [Can one know something when beliefs are true by mere luck?](can-one-know-something-when-beliefs-are-true-by-mere-luck-antithesis-f06b381a-966d-49cd-ac2a-9ec5073e6293.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is stable across close possible worlds**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is safe from being false in nearby possible worlds**
