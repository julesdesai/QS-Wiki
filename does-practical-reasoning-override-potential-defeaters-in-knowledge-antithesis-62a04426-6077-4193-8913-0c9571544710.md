# Does practical reasoning override potential defeaters in knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 62a04426-6077-4193-8913-0c9571544710
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Defeasibility Theory](defeasibility-theory-synthesis-bec695a8-d154-4145-af72-ab6869088e07.md)

## Content

**Suppose a pilot justifiably believes their navigation knowledge is correct and lands the plane safely despite receiving a later-known incorrect weather report. Intuitively, knowledge of safe landing procedure remains intact.**, **There is a plausible principle that pragmatic results, like safe outcomes, reinforce that knowledge holds even when potential paper-based defeaters could theoretically apply. Results verifying belief's correctness neutralize speculative defeaters.**
