# Knowledge as Foundationalist Truth-Tracking

**Parent:** [Is there a problem with multiple, equally coherent systems?](is-there-a-problem-with-multiple-equally-coherent-systems-antithesis-7b3805a9-70de-4aa6-9c31-ecc5cbf898d2.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief tracks the truth as part of a foundational belief system**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief tracks the truth as part of a foundational belief system**
