## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that a veridical perception relates directly to the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that direct relations justify the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is true and justified by direct veridical perception**
