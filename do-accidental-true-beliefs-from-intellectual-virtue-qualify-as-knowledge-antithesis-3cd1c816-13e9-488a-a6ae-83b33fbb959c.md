# Do accidental true beliefs from intellectual virtue qualify as knowledge?

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person guesses correctly that a coin will land heads based solely on attentive focus on an irrelevant detail.**, **Intuitively, the correct guess based on attentiveness does not seem to qualify as knowledge because it is accidental.**
