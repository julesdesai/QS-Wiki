# Is the concept of infinite background coherent within finite human epistemic practices?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e13bf003-51d1-4bb1-90ab-539db3ad22b8
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundationalism with Infinite Background](foundationalism-with-infinite-background-synthesis-b6351c4d-7dab-447a-b3c7-52e8418919cd.md)

## Content

**The representational and cognitive capacities of humans are finite.**, **If knowledge requires infinite background structures, those structures exceed human cognitive capacities.**, **Beliefs relying on structures beyond cognitive capacity are not fully accessible or understandable, challenging their role in providing justification.**
