## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that justification involves an infinite chain of reasons that are mutually coherent**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there exists an infinite, coherent chain of reasons justifying the belief**
