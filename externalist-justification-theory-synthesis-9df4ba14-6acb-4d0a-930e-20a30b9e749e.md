**Parent:** [Can knowledge be purely subjective if it depends on context?](can-knowledge-be-purely-subjective-if-it-depends-on-context-antithesis-cd0bc44b-bf26-4de9-9bd4-3feb96025048.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified through factors external to the person's mental states**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is justified through factors external to the person's mental states**
