# What about the reliability of perception when cognitive biases influence it?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** cd049a1a-1c4e-48f7-8b8a-1376d37f71ce
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Direct Realism with Strong Correspondence](direct-realism-with-strong-correspondence-synthesis-28884d49-4ced-45da-83a0-528139a02f57.md)

## Content

**Cognitive biases can alter perception, such as seeing a harmless figure as a threat in low light due to fear.**, **In these cases, perception might not reliably correspond to the external reality that it purports to represent.**, **If cognitive biases influence perception, they may challenge the reliability needed for perceptual correspondence.**
