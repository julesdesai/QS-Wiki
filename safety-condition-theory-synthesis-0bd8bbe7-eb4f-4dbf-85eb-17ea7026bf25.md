## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in nearby possible worlds where the person believes the proposition, it is not false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is true and the belief is safe from error in nearby possible worlds**
