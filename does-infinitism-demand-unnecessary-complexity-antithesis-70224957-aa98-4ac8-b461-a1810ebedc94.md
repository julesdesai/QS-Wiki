# Does Infinitism demand unnecessary complexity?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 70224957-aa98-4ac8-b461-a1810ebedc94
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism](infinitism-synthesis-db00e7c1-6a13-4ca5-8f09-6fae7cd29e0f.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that epistemic theories should not demand unnecessary complexity without adequate justification. The requirement of infinite reasons introduces excessive complexity.**, **It is implausible that complex justification demands contribute more to understanding or knowledge acquisition than simpler models.**
