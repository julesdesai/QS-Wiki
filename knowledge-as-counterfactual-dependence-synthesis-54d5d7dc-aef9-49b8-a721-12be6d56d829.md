# Knowledge as Counterfactual Dependence

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Does knowledge require conscious reflection on cognitive virtues?](does-knowledge-require-conscious-reflection-on-cognitive-virtues-antithesis-0be39fbe-e3b2-459d-aab4-ffcc017009b1.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the truth of their belief counterfactually depends on the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the truth of their belief counterfactually depends on the proposition**
