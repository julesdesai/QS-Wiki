## Content

**Knowledge depends on belief formed through a reliable process**, **A process is reliable if it yields a high proportion of true beliefs**, **A person knows a proposition if it is true, formed through such a process, and sensitive to counterfactuals**, **A belief is sensitive to counterfactuals if, were the proposition false, the belief would not be held**
