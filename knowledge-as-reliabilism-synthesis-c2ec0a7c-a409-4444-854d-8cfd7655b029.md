## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief was produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is true and was produced by a reliable cognitive process**
