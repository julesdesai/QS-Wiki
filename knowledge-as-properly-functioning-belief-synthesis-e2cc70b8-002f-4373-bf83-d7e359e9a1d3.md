## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, their belief must result from properly functioning cognitive faculties**, **Proper cognitive function involves faculties operating in accordance with a design plan successfully aimed at truth**, **The design plan must be successfully targeted at truth in the person's environment**, **Proper functioning excludes knowledge from being a result of mere chance**
