# Knowledge as Reliabilism

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** eb26ebdc-34d3-4243-9e40-0f21b39f3afe
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does sufficient evidence always yield true knowledge?](does-sufficient-evidence-always-yield-true-knowledge-antithesis-1c42cbe3-4108-43dc-a20a-42492f3ec6b9.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is true and produced by a reliable cognitive process**
