## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **Non-epistemic factors like practical interests can affect whether a belief counts as knowledge**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and non-epistemic factors do not preclude it from being knowledge**
