# Is the definition of epistemic virtues too narrow?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 638f644d-06cb-4790-8212-6c2e327ada7f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-8faa0d3d-af6f-4054-8090-da1e790293ff.md)

## Content

**There are traits such as creativity that contribute to knowledge but are not explicitly aimed at reliably forming true beliefs.**, **A trait like creativity may lead to new insights and thus knowledge, but lacks the straightforward connection to reliability that the definition of epistemic virtue demands.**, **The narrow focus on reliability may exclude valuable traits that contribute to knowledge acquisition in less predictable ways.**
