# Can a belief be justified if cognitive faculty design is irrelevant?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 31f0f28f-eac2-427b-9b0c-af79d2928e54
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Proper Functionalist Knowledge Summary](proper-functionalist-knowledge-summary-synthesis-ea5a08e3-f799-4ced-bdad-582e7b9aaf66.md)

## Content

**Imagine a person raised without social interactions forms true beliefs about social norms through isolated but perfectly accurate cognitive mechanisms. Intuitively, it seems these beliefs might lack proper justification.**, **The design of cognitive faculties to produce true beliefs might not account for every context in knowledge formation.**
