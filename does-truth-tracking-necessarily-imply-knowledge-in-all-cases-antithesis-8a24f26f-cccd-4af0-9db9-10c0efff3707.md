# Does truth-tracking necessarily imply knowledge in all cases?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 8a24f26f-cccd-4af0-9db9-10c0efff3707
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Process Reliabilism with Nozick's Condition](process-reliabilism-with-nozicks-condition-synthesis-5d350bc0-f5e6-4b33-b9c5-feeee7d84d21.md)

## Content

**A case exists where a person’s belief tracks the truth through a miraculously rare event preserving the truth. Intuitively, we doubt that this event constitutes knowing.**, **It is implausible that truth-tracking alone, without examining the source of reliability, establishes knowledge.**
