# Knowledge as Coherentism

**Parent:** [Can pragmatic considerations justify beliefs without foundations?](can-pragmatic-considerations-justify-beliefs-without-foundations-antithesis-7108c57c-7d85-43cb-941f-b3f87c456f02.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief coheres with a system of interconnected beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief coheres with a system of interconnected beliefs**
