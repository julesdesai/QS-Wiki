# Can context influence the truth conditions of knowledge claims?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ac9cdb27-cd95-4a50-b14c-3e5e31a39a20
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism in Epistemology](contextualism-in-epistemology-synthesis-f4c5fe35-58d0-4960-a84a-81d2bc69d122.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case where a person knows a fact under one context but that knowledge is questioned or rejected under a stricter context. Intuitively, if knowledge is allowed to fluctuate so, its connection to stable truth conditions becomes doubtful.**, **It is implausible that the truth conditions for knowledge can be so flexible that they shift with context while still maintaining philosophical rigor.**
