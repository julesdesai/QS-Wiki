# Can foundationalism account for complex theoretical beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d859e8e1-cdef-4111-80fa-0e2c6041bd33
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundationalism: Basic Beliefs](foundationalism-basic-beliefs-synthesis-fa10f2ed-646f-4b84-85d3-ce2c6a58459d.md)

## Content

**Complex theoretical beliefs, such as advanced scientific theories, often rely on extensive interrelated propositions.**, **These beliefs are not solely based on basic beliefs that are self-evident or evident to the senses.**, **If foundationalism claims that all justification must ultimately trace back to basic beliefs, complex beliefs pose a challenge.**, **The dependence of complex beliefs on chains of reasoning suggests a limitation in foundationalism's ability to justify them purely through basic beliefs.**
