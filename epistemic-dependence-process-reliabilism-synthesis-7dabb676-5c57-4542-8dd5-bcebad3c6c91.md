## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a process that reliably produces true beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief includes epistemic dependence on human cognitive processes**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is produced by a reliable process, and there is epistemic dependence on human cognition**
