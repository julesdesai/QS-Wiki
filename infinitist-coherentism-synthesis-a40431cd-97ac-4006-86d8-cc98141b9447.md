## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the coordination of reasons forms an infinite web of mutual support**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there exists an infinite web providing mutual support justifying the proposition**
