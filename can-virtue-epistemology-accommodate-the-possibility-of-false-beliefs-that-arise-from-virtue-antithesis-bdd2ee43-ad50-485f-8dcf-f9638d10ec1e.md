# Can virtue epistemology accommodate the possibility of false beliefs that arise from virtue?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** bdd2ee43-ad50-485f-8dcf-f9638d10ec1e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-deb477f8-4a11-4db0-ba96-00a9c4a7815c.md)

## Content

**A person exercising an intellectual virtue such as thorough research may still end up with a false belief due to misleading evidence, which challenges the certainty of linking virtue to knowledge.**, **Imagine a historian using reliable methods but drawing on an artifact later proven to be a forgery; the intellectual virtue is present, but the belief is false.**
