# Can knowledge ascriptions be the same across all contexts?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 97d76429-0b3b-4561-9ed0-4c2f00d67a89
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism](contextualism-synthesis-bf708b38-e467-425c-8744-5d490abcb982.md)

## Content

**It is plausible that some fundamental truth conditions remain constant across all contexts of knowledge ascription.**, **If there are constant truth conditions, then the variability of truth conditions with context becomes questionable.**, **The observable consistency in knowledge judgments across different contexts suggests that truth conditions might not vary with context.**
