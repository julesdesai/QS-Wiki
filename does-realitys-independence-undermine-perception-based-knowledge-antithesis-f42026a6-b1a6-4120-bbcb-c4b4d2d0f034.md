# Does reality's independence undermine perception-based knowledge?

## Content

**Reality exists independent of perception implies that perception may not accurately reflect reality**, **If perception is indirect, then there is a risk of distortion or misinterpretation of reality through perception**, **The interpretative nature of knowledge suggests that cognitive processes may introduce biases or errors in understanding reality**
