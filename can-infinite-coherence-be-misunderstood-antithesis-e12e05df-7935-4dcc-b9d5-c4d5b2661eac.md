# Can infinite coherence be misunderstood?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e12e05df-7935-4dcc-b9d5-c4d5b2661eac
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherent Infinitism](coherent-infinitism-synthesis-f2c99927-f862-4ee8-8b5b-2fefd2717ee2.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where partial, finite coherency among beliefs appears to provide sufficient justification. Intuitively, one might judge that coherence need not be infinite to deliver adequate justification for knowledge.**, **It is implausible that an infinite number of coherent beliefs is necessary if a coherent subset could fulfill the role of justification.**
