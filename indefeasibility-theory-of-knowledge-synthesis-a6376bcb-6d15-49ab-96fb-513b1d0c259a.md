## Content

**A belief is knowledge if it is justified and true and could not be defeated by uncovering further information**, **Knowledge requires that there is no true information that, if learned, would lead the person to give up their belief**, **Defeasibility involves considering potential evidence not actually possessed**, **Indefeasibility ensures the stability of knowledge despite new revelations**
