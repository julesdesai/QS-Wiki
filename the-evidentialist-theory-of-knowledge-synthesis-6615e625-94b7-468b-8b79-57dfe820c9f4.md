## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is based on adequate evidence**, **Adequate evidence is defined by its ability to justify the belief sufficiently**
