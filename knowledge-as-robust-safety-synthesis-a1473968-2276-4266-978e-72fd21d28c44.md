## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the person's belief is true and would remain true despite plausible counterfactual alternatives**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed in a way that excludes significant epistemic luck through a robust method**
