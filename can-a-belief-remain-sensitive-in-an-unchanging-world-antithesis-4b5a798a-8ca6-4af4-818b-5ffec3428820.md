# Can a belief remain sensitive in an unchanging world?

**Node ID:** 4b5a798a-8ca6-4af4-818b-5ffec3428820

## Content

**In a world where the facts of a proposition do not change, a person’s belief would not change either.**, **In such a scenario, it is implausible to claim that the person lacks knowledge when the belief and truth remain aligned.**
