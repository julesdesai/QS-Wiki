# Knowledge as Epistemic Externalism

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** ffd33737-2795-455f-85dc-b35da9dab4b4
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is a reliably produced true belief always knowledge?](is-a-reliably-produced-true-belief-always-knowledge-antithesis-9cdd6933-6d26-43f8-b7ac-232fa7bbd7fc.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is justified through factors external to the individual's subjective awareness**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is justified via external factors**
