# Does contextualism diminish the reliability of common sense knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b43af0ae-542e-41d2-9c30-b19af1ee88c4
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism about Knowledge](contextualism-about-knowledge-synthesis-3f771a9e-26d6-43c4-b604-a31f8bd9947c.md)

## Content

**Common sense knowledge suggests certain beliefs are inherently justified and true across contexts.**, **Contextualism implies these beliefs could be unjustified in contexts with differing standards.**, **It is implausible that common sense knowledge, known for its reliability, could lose justification through changing contexts.**
