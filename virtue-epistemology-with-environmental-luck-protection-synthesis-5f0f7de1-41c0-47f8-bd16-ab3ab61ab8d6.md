## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary to exclude knowledge claims tainted by environmental luck**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief emerges from both intellectual virtue and is insulated from luck regarding truth**
