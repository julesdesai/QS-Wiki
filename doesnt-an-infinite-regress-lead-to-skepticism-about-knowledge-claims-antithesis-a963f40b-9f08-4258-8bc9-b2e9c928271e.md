# Doesn't an infinite regress lead to skepticism about knowledge claims?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a963f40b-9f08-4258-8bc9-b2e9c928271e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism Summary](infinitism-summary-synthesis-a48a630f-d4e8-4dc2-b25a-518a64dbd1fb.md)

## Content

**If every belief demands an infinite chain of reasons, then no belief is ever fully justified, leading to a form of skepticism that denies genuine knowledge.**, **The insistence on an infinite chain of reasons for justification makes it impossible to ever conclusively satisfy the requirements for knowledge.**
