# Is non-inferential justification coherent under this view?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1af1fd27-f1d1-4483-9107-54122d502dfd
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism with Foundational Anchors](infinitism-with-foundational-anchors-synthesis-6b525f84-03a5-4482-aa47-2329492f1b5f.md)

## Content

**Non-inferential justification claims some beliefs do not require further justification from other beliefs.**, **If a belief is non-inferentially justified, then the belief is not part of an infinite chain of reasons.**, **The notion of infinite justification seems incompatible with the existence of non-inferentially justified beliefs, creating tension within the theory.**
