## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, their belief in that proposition must be produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **A reliable cognitive process is one that generally leads to true beliefs**, **Knowledge is not dependent on the individual knowing all underlying facts**, **A person's reasoning can include false steps as long as the belief is obtained through a reliable method**
