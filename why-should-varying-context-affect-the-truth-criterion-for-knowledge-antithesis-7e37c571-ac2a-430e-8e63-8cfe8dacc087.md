# Why should varying context affect the truth criterion for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7e37c571-ac2a-430e-8e63-8cfe8dacc087
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualist Theory of Knowledge](contextualist-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-4769a94f-2567-4d65-9377-c3f1aea872f2.md)

## Content

**The truth of a proposition is considered an objective feature, not typically influenced by contextual factors. If contexts alter what is truth for knowledge, it suggests a potential relativism that may undermine objective truth.**
