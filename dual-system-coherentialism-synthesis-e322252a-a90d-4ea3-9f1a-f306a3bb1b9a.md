# Dual-System Coherentialism

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres with a primary system of beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is assessed by a secondary system for simplicity**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief coheres with a primary system, and passes secondary assessment**
