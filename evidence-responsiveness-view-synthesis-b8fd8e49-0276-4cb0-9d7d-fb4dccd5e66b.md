## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the person's belief responds adequately to available evidence**, **Evidence responsiveness requires the belief to change in light of new or differing evidence**
