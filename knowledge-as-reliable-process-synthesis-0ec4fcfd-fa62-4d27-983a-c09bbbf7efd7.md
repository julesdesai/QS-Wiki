## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a cognitive process that is reliable**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the true proposition produced by a reliable process**
