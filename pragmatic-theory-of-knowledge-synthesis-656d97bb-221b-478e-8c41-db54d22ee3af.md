## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **A person knows a proposition if acting on the belief leads to successful outcomes**, **The truth of a proposition is corroborated by the practical effectiveness of actions based on it**
