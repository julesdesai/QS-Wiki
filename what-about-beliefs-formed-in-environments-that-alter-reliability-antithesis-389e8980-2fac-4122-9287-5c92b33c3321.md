# What about beliefs formed in environments that alter reliability?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 389e8980-2fac-4122-9287-5c92b33c3321
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Process Reliabilism](process-reliabilism-synthesis-383ba069-e0f1-4291-bdc7-eacefc701040.md)

## Content

**Consider someone with a consistently reliable way of telling time using a clock, which is later unknowingly tampered with and now provides false times. Intuitively, in this scenario, even though the process was initially reliable, changes in environment affect this status.**, **It is implausible that external changes affecting a process' reliability can suddenly shift the knowledge status of all resulting beliefs. This challenges reliability’s sufficiency for defining belief as knowledge.**
