# Does the Tracking Theory account for knowledge about necessary truths?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 8665fe85-0bd6-48ac-a972-a9cb238cab74
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Nozick's Tracking Theory of Knowledge](nozicks-tracking-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-3fc68b12-ca29-40d7-8341-51e40d71c5e5.md)

## Content

**Consider the belief that 2+2=4, which is a necessary truth. A person believes this proposition truly.**, **Even if in some impossible world 2+2 did not equal 4, the person would still believe it because it is grounded in logical necessity rather than contingent fact.**, **In any slightly different situation, say with minor variations in method of arithmetical calculation, the belief that 2+2=4 would still be held.**
