## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief was produced by a reliable process**, **Reliability is defined as a tendency to produce a high ratio of true beliefs over false ones**
