# Can contextual coherentism address the problem of circularity?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2f1f8c81-35cb-4393-a36c-f51d42404dda
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualist Coherentism](contextualist-coherentism-synthesis-2211d0a5-4c05-4516-984a-e004555aaa45.md)

## Content

**Coherence involves beliefs supporting one another in a system.**, **Systems that derive justification from mutual support can be circular.**, **Circularity in supporting one belief with another that relies on the former is problematic, as it may not lead to genuine justification.**
