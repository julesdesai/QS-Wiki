## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **The standards of justification can appropriately depend on the practical stakes, which remain constant within a context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification standards accounting for practical stakes are met**
