# Are practical stakes a reliable guide for knowledge attribution?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0c929629-bbd8-4616-adc1-cafec1e6f6ba
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Encroachment Summary](pragmatic-encroachment-summary-synthesis-e897bf05-5a21-418d-ae35-b563f51919c6.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a student with excellent evidence for a test question hesitates to say they know the answer due to the high importance of the exam. Intuitively, strong prior evidence should be adequate for knowledge despite external pressures.**, **It is implausible for knowledge attribution to rest significantly on variable practical stakes when it contradicts an individual's quality of evidence.**
