# Can the theory properly address propositions involving necessary truths?

**Parent:** [Nozick's Conditional Theory of Knowledge](nozicks-conditional-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-ff394d52-e522-4d27-8272-d4808dc162bd.md)

## Content

**Consider the belief in a necessary truth, like mathematical axioms, where belief would hold even if propositions were false due to their necessity. This suggests a limitation in Nozick's conditions addressing necessary truths.**, **If a belief remains constant regardless of potential falsehood, applying conditional and reliability constraints becomes inadequate.**
