# Is inference a sufficient bridge between experience and knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e4c64a97-fca3-41dc-add3-262e58b06bc5
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Indirect Realism Maintained Through Inference](indirect-realism-maintained-through-inference-synthesis-ffd82d7b-be0f-4614-a603-c896b6b1b7df.md)

## Content

**If knowledge of external objects is mediated by inference from perceptual experiences, then the quality and validity of inference become critical.**, **Inference relies on cognitive processes that may introduce biases or errors unrelated to the reliability of perceptual experience itself.**, **In cases where perceptual veracity is in question, the sufficiency of inference to establish knowledge is undermined.**
