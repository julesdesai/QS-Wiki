## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief involves both intellectual virtues and supplementary cognitive faculties to mitigate biases**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief involves intellectual virtues alongside cognitive faculties that mitigate biases**
