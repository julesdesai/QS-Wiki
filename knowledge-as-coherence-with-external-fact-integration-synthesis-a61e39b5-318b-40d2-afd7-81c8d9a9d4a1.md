# Knowledge as Coherence with External Fact Integration

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** a61e39b5-318b-40d2-afd7-81c8d9a9d4a1
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does coherence with beliefs overlook the role of external facts?](does-coherence-with-beliefs-overlook-the-role-of-external-facts-antithesis-3cf4e61b-fbc8-4162-89b3-d727523fada6.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres with a system of interconnected beliefs and is justified by the context in which it is held**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief be connected to external facts about the world**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief coheres with a system of interconnected beliefs, is justified by the context in which it is held, and is connected to external facts about the world**
