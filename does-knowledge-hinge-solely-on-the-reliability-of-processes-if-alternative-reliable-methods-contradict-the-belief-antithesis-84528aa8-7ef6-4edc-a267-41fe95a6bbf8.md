# Does knowledge hinge solely on the reliability of processes if alternative reliable methods contradict the belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 84528aa8-7ef6-4edc-a267-41fe95a6bbf8
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalism with Reliable Processes](externalism-with-reliable-processes-synthesis-89e590de-768c-4370-a7df-27a5b4f5eaa5.md)

## Content

**Assume a person forms a true belief based on one scientifically endorsed method, but another equally reliable method yields a contradictory outcome at the same time.**, **Intuitively, if both methods are reliable, the tension between their outcomes makes it hard to claim unequivocal knowledge via either method.**, **Competing reliable approaches that suggest contradictory beliefs put into question singular reliance on reliability for knowing.**
