# Are non-discriminable experiences fundamentally different?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3f2787b8-d0e4-4a85-8803-f0813ab736f3
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Disjunctivism Summary](disjunctivism-summary-synthesis-72a215c5-4d26-4e6e-86ab-383aabdf6290.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that an experience of a person looking at a green apple in good light is fundamentally different from a qualitatively identical experience induced in a virtual reality. If such experiences are fundamentally similar, then disjunctivism's claim of a direct relation in the former but not the latter case is problematic.**
