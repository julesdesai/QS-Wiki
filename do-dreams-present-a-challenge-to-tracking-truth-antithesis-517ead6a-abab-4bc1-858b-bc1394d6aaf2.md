# Do dreams present a challenge to tracking truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 517ead6a-abab-4bc1-858b-bc1394d6aaf2
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Tracking Theory of Knowledge](tracking-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-c5c60481-0513-43f2-8ed6-61505f94790c.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a person dreams they are doing something, and upon waking, it is true they did it.**, **In such cases, the person’s belief while dreaming does not track reality, despite its truth.**, **Intuitively, dreaming of a scenario that happens to be true does not yield knowledge of that scenario.**
