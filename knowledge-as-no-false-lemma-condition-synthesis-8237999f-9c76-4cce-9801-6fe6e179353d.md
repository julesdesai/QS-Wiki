# Knowledge as No-False-Lemma Condition

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does memory reliability affect knowledge?](does-memory-reliability-affect-knowledge-antithesis-6fa3d5dd-b070-4cab-80e8-6200b6de1b47.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person's justification for believing does not include any falsehoods**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their justification does not include any falsehoods**
