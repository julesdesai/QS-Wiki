# Can foundationalism allow for infinite support?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 8b2ac3f6-3fec-4ded-abfa-1f6e3fc0a5b7
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundationalism with Infinite Support](foundationalism-with-infinite-support-synthesis-a4f6a799-0125-4c36-8d78-ba3ae0077937.md)

## Content

**If justification involves foundational beliefs supporting infinite reasons, then there must be an infinite chain of support.**, **An infinite chain of support contradicts the foundational nature of beliefs, which implies a stopping point in the justificatory chain.**
