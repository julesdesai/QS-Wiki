# Is knowledge robust to justification length variation?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 8581a358-6d8a-4f23-8be3-4613ec20e30a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualist Infinitism](contextualist-infinitism-synthesis-74ebcf03-3de8-4e28-950c-dc2eefc9dc13.md)

## Content

**A person cannot feasibly differentiate knowledge claims based on infinitesimally varied justification lengths.**, **If a minute difference in justification length affects knowledge status, it seems knowledge lacks robustness.**, **It is implausible that true knowledge depends on finely tuned justification length that can't be feasibly discerned.**
