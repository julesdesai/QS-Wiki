# Knowledge as Sensitivity-Based True Belief

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge be gained from a source that is typically unreliable but accidentally correct?](can-knowledge-be-gained-from-a-source-that-is-typically-unreliable-but-accidentally-correct-antithesis-0d943ab7-7799-4f2b-a45d-5a5aa31fee66.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is sensitive to the truth, and the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**
