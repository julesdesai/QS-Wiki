## Content

**Consider a theoretical scenario where a coherent network of beliefs is constructed entirely on false assumptions. Intuitively, merely being coherent should not be sufficient for justification if it lacks correspondence with reality.**, **If a coherent network can exist in opposition to reality, coherence alone might be insufficient for true justification.**
