# Does indirect realism explain perceptual change or development?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 423f13ca-375a-451b-ade2-fea8cc1f44af
**Depth:** 2
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Indirect Realism](indirect-realism-thesis-685db4d3-6670-4245-a0b7-dd17f1d7a4dc.md)

## Content

**Perception can change over time, such as developing night vision or new perceptual skills.**, **If perception is merely a mediator, it should account for perceptual development.**, **The lack of focus on perceptual change does not address how representations evolve while maintaining a match with reality.**
