# Is belief justified based on unknown conditions?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 691af89f-4fcc-4617-b589-7747a13eb5d2
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalism](externalism-synthesis-9fb6627e-b63e-4d81-a81a-128eb1da4172.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person holds a true belief based on a process they are entirely unaware of, such as a rare and random neural process that leads to truth. Intuitively, we would judge that such a belief is not well justified because the knower has no understanding or awareness of the process.**, **A belief justified based solely on unknown external conditions lacks a connection to the believer's rational evaluation of the belief.**
