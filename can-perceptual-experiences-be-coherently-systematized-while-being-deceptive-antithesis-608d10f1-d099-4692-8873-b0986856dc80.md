# Can perceptual experiences be coherently systematized while being deceptive?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 608d10f1-d099-4692-8873-b0986856dc80
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Scientific Phenomenalism](scientific-phenomenalism-synthesis-ce8a34ca-bcf7-45c1-b3c8-1c3da9267ceb.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where someone has consistent perceptual experiences of a bent stick in water.**, **The proposition that the stick is bent contributes to the systematization of these experiences.**, **However, we know intuitively that the stick is not actually bent, questioning the coherence as a basis for knowledge.**
