# Knowledge as Infinitism

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 3
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does the causal requirement overlook abstract knowledge?](does-the-causal-requirement-overlook-abstract-knowledge-antithesis-1139f3dd-345b-4e2b-bfaf-31d882645754.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification for the belief consists of an infinite series of non-repeating reasons**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification consists of an infinite series of non-repeating reasons**
