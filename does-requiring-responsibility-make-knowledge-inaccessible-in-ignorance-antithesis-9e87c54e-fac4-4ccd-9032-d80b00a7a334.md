# Does requiring responsibility make knowledge inaccessible in ignorance?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9e87c54e-fac4-4ccd-9032-d80b00a7a334
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Mixed Virtue Epistemology](mixed-virtue-epistemology-synthesis-9b1d3c06-dd01-4be5-8e49-871c85befc1a.md)

## Content

**Imagine someone deductively reaches a true belief but remains unaware of a crucial fallacy in their reasoning, due to ignorance. Intuitively, we hesitate to deny them knowledge.**, **There is a concern that requiring full responsibility might unduly restrict knowledge in cases where ignorance or error does not affect the truth or reliability of the belief.**
