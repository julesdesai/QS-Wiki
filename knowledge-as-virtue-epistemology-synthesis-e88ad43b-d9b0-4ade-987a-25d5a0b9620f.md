## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for their belief to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed via intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have a true belief formed through intellectual virtue**
