# Knowledge as Virtue Epistemology

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person exercises intellectual virtue in forming the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and they exercise intellectual virtue in forming the belief**
