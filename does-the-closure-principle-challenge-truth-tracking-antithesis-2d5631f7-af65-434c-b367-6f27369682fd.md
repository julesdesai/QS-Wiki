# Does the closure principle challenge truth-tracking?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2d5631f7-af65-434c-b367-6f27369682fd
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Nozick's Truth-Tracking Theory](nozicks-truth-tracking-theory-synthesis-3749df9e-e568-414c-88ec-cc6b23580530.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that if a person knows a proposition and also knows that this proposition entails another, they must know the entailed proposition. This principle together with the idea that a person’s belief must track the truth can lead to a contradiction.**, **Suppose a person knows a proposition and that this proposition entails another true proposition. Intuitively, the person should know the entailed proposition, yet the tracking condition may not be satisfied.**
