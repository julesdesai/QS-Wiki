# Can relevant alternatives change over time and impact past knowledge claims?

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which technological or scientific advancements introduce new relevant alternatives to a previously established knowledge claim. Intuitively we would judge that the original knowledge claim should remain valid, even if new alternatives were not considered.**, **The notion that past knowledge depends on future discovery introduces instability to knowledge claims.**
