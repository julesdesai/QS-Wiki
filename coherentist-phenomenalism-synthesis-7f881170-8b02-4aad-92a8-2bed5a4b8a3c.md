## Content

**Knowledge is of perceptual experiences that fit coherently within a larger system of beliefs**, **A person can know propositions that are coherently related to their perceptual experiences**, **External objects and historical facts can be known if they coherently fit into an existing set of perceptual experiences and beliefs**
