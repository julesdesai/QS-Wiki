# Is the concept of truth-tracking too coarse-grained for particular cases?

**Depth:** 4

**Parent:** [Epistemic Externalism with Nozick's Tracking Theory](epistemic-externalism-with-nozicks-tracking-theory-synthesis-99a4169f-6bac-4b5a-b46b-52fe3916b903.md)

## Content

**Imagine a person watching a fake video of a real event, believing the event happened. In reality, the event did happen, but not as depicted.**, **The belief superficially tracks the truth—if event didn’t happen, belief wouldn't exist.**, **Truth-tracking fails when specific details or misleading evidential sources lead to a misplaced sense of truth.**
