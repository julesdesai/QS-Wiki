# How can illusions demonstrate issues with direct realism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a34c890c-20fe-4711-b173-c91c06d14dc5
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextual Direct Realism](contextual-direct-realism-synthesis-9053ea14-9a99-4649-aa95-1bc3b223c980.md)

## Content

**An optical illusion might lead one to directly perceive something that does not match reality.**, **Believing one has knowledge from perceiving an illusion conflicts with the notion of knowledge aligning with reality.**, **It is implausible for knowledge claims to arise from perceptions that derive from recognized illusions.**
