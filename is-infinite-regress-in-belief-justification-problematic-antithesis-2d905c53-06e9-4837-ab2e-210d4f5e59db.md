# Is infinite regress in belief justification problematic?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2d905c53-06e9-4837-ab2e-210d4f5e59db
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherent Infinitism](coherent-infinitism-synthesis-f2c99927-f862-4ee8-8b5b-2fefd2717ee2.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that an infinite regress can provide final justification or closure needed for knowledge.**, **Suppose that each reason in a justification chain requires further support. Intuitively, if the chain requires infinite reasons, it can never settle on a final grounding reason, thereby constantly deferring justification.**
