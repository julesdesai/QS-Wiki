## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is reliably produced by traits of the person**, **The reliability of epistemic traits is determined by their tendency to produce true beliefs more often than false ones**
