# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with No Defeaters

**Parent:** [Can justified true belief with reliabilism adequately address Gettier cases?](can-justified-true-belief-with-reliabilism-adequately-address-gettier-cases-antithesis-cf084e2d-eec9-40a0-af52-f0068c965922.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no available truths that would defeat the justification**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process, and no available truths would defeat the justification**
