# Does reliabilism accommodate the social aspect of knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3e40b38b-147b-4276-95a6-055732004a17
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism](reliabilism-synthesis-5d1929f3-45fe-4f02-bb7b-941a1c5017f0.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person forms a belief through a reliable process that is nevertheless based on misleading social testimony, corrected by community consensus. Intuitively, social correction contributes to knowledge beyond individual reliable belief formation.**, **Consider scenarios where social dynamics play a crucial role in validating knowledge, suggesting community aspects can't be reduced to reliability.**
