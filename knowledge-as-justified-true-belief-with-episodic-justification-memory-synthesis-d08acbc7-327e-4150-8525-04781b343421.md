# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with Episodic Justification Memory

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** d08acbc7-327e-4150-8525-04781b343421
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is memory retention inconsistent with awareness demand?](is-memory-retention-inconsistent-with-awareness-demand-antithesis-61d674f7-88a0-41c6-8b4a-f598c1c98c5a.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, they must have episodic memory of having once been aware of their justification**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, and have episodic memory of previous justification awareness in conjunction with the proposition being true**
