**Parent:** [Does absence of belief affect confidence in knowledge claims?](does-absence-of-belief-affect-confidence-in-knowledge-claims-antithesis-70194e7c-384b-4129-8ad4-c276c0463cd8.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their justification comes from a reliable process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is not necessary that they have a firm belief in the proposition**, **A person knows a proposition if it is true, reliably justified, and held with a tentative belief**
