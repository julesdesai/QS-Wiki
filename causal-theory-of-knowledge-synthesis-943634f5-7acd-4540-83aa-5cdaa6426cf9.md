## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief is caused by a fact about the world**, **The causal link must appropriately connect the belief to the fact**
