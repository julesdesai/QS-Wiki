# How does phenomenalism handle shared hallucinations?

**Parent:** [Phenomenalism suggests that physical objects are logical constructions of sensory experiences.](phenomenalism-suggests-that-physical-objects-are-logical-constructions-of-sensory-experiences-synthesis-70c8baf6-067c-48d6-b521-64b5ff284db6.md)

## Content

**Suppose two people claim to see a pink elephant in the room, but the elephant is not physically present. Intuitively, this suggests that sensory experiences can be misleading about the existence of physical objects.**, **If multiple people can have shared sensory experiences that do not correspond to an actual object, this complicates the idea that objects are merely logical constructions of sensory experiences.**
