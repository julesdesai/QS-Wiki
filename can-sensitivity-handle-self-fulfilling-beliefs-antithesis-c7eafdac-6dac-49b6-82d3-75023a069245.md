# Can sensitivity handle self-fulfilling beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c7eafdac-6dac-49b6-82d3-75023a069245
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Knowledge as Sensitivity](knowledge-as-sensitivity-synthesis-a361f947-df1a-4345-a4d3-ea9ac7712828.md)

## Content

**Imagine a scenario in which a person believes they will succeed in a task, and their belief contributes to their success.**, **Their belief is true given the eventual success, caused partly by the belief itself.**, **The problem of evaluating sensitivity here arises since the belief might remain abstinent were the circumstances different.**
