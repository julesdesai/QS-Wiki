## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres with the person's entire belief system**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the coherent system includes stable background beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief coheres with the person's entire belief system, and the coherent system includes stable background beliefs**
