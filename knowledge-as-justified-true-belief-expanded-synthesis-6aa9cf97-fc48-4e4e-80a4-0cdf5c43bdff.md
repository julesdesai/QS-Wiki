# Knowledge as Justified True Belief Expanded

**Parent:** [Is propositional knowledge the only form of knowledge?](is-propositional-knowledge-the-only-form-of-knowledge-antithesis-1ae7570b-ba2b-48ca-b9e9-d4dd74917b5a.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person has justification for their belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is justified**
