# Is intellectual virtue alone sufficient for justification in knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2ef1e7e5-e6b7-4706-8316-775383975d88
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology Summary](virtue-epistemology-summary-synthesis-ebb76c32-727a-4eba-8f42-21c2ee3c5622.md)

## Content

**Consider a person who consistently uses intellectual virtues but arrives at false beliefs. Intuitively, the application of intellectual virtues alone does not ensure the truth of a belief, thus questioning whether virtue alone is sufficient for knowledge.**
