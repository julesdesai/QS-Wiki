# Can skill acquisition bypass intellectual virtues?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 10ca815e-eeb7-4f3d-8dfb-cc8eadfd9114
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology with Skills](virtue-epistemology-with-skills-synthesis-458da717-0537-4f5a-8a52-aec5d63f6438.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where someone learns a skill through passive repetition without deploying critical thinking or reflection. Intuitively, we would judge the learned skill as rote rather than knowledge enriched by intellectual virtues.**, **It is implausible that all skill acquisition inherently calls for engagement of intellectual virtues, as many skills are mastered via routine practice without reflective input.**
