# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with No Defeaters

**Parent:** [Does knowledge as safety explain cases where belief is formed from unreliable sources?](does-knowledge-as-safety-explain-cases-where-belief-is-formed-from-unreliable-sources-antithesis-dadc6a4c-97ac-4f43-9299-bb736bcd70b4.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person has justification for the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there be no defeaters for the justification of their belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, they have justification for the belief, and there are no defeaters for the justification**
