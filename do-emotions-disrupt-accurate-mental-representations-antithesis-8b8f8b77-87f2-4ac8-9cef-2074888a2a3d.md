# Do emotions disrupt accurate mental representations?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 8b8f8b77-87f2-4ac8-9cef-2074888a2a3d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Representationalism](representationalism-synthesis-c6ad7a19-c6b2-4a71-b6f5-a8020ca1b42c.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where two people witness the same event but have different emotional reactions that color their mental representations. Intuitively, we might say one representation is influenced by emotion, challenging its accuracy.**, **There is a plausible principle that emotions can interfere with accurate mental representations. This principle implies that emotional influence might lead to knowledge being questioned, even if representation aims at accuracy.**
