# Is all true belief knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 504a73dc-3dd1-4156-a0d0-01dfdfc4fa4e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Grounding Knowledge in Evidence](grounding-knowledge-in-evidence-synthesis-000c43a5-6d20-4fdc-9093-7ec5ca2a4c8d.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that all true beliefs constitute knowledge, even if they are true.**, **Consider a scenario where someone randomly guesses the correct answer to a question without evidence. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that the guess does not constitute knowledge.**
