# Are unwarranted leaps void of knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** dae6e814-95ea-47b4-9bc0-8af91b08409e
**Depth:** 2
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [No False Lemmas Theory](no-false-lemmas-theory-thesis-a96b2c39-d876-40fc-94b2-391826bfefda.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person leaps from a justified belief to another closely related, yet unwarranted belief that is true. Intuitively, such a leap doesn't seem to represent knowledge.**, **Unwarranted ideological leaps, even if they result in true beliefs, fall short of constituting knowledge due to the lack of appropriate justification.**
