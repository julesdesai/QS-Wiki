## Content

**Knowledge does not depend on the context of the knower**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief in the proposition is sensitive to the truth of the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **The standards for knowing a proposition are fixed and do not vary with different contexts**
