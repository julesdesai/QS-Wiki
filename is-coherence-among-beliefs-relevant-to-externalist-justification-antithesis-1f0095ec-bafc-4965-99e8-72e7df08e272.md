# Is coherence among beliefs relevant to externalist justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1f0095ec-bafc-4965-99e8-72e7df08e272
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalism](externalism-synthesis-9fb6627e-b63e-4d81-a81a-128eb1da4172.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that coherence among one's beliefs contributes significantly to the overall justification of those beliefs. This principle suggests that achieving consistency and harmony among beliefs is central to their justification.**, **A perspective that overemphasizes external conditions while ignoring the coherence requirement of beliefs risks overlooking how beliefs support and justify each other internally.**
