# Can perceptual errors be fully explained by naive realism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f3292787-d414-4558-a5e5-e08d0e773560
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Naive Realism](naive-realism-synthesis-a575a970-7874-461b-b9b5-9de679546681.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person perceives a mirage on the road ahead, thinking it is water. Intuitively, there is no water to be directly acquainted with.**, **If experiences can mislead about the external reality they are supposed to directly represent, then perception cannot be fully explained by direct acquaintance.**
