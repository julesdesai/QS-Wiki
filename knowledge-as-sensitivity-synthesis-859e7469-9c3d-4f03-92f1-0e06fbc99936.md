# Knowledge as Sensitivity

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is belief in a true proposition with justification always knowledge?](is-belief-in-a-true-proposition-with-justification-always-knowledge-antithesis-da23c170-d852-4b82-8d86-fb67391a55fd.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, if the proposition were false they would not believe it, and the proposition is true**
