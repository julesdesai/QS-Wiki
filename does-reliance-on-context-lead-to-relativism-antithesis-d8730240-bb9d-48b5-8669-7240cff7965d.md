# Does reliance on context lead to relativism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d8730240-bb9d-48b5-8669-7240cff7965d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism with Reliabilism](contextualism-with-reliabilism-synthesis-c7c3d2a5-9a25-4e3f-9e1f-65010441e024.md)

## Content

**If knowledge depends on the context of the knower, then knowledge becomes subject to personal or cultural perspectives, leading to epistemic relativism.**, **Epistemic relativism suggests that there is no objective truth, only differing perspectives, which challenges the traditional notion of objective knowledge.**
