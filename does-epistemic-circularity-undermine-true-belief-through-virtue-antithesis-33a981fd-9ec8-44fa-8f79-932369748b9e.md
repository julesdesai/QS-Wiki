**Consider a case where a person's intellectual virtue relies on a belief system that is self-referential or circular. Intuitively, such circularity raises doubts about forming genuine knowledge.**, **There is a plausible concern that epistemic circularity undermines the reliability of intellectual virtue in achieving true belief.**
