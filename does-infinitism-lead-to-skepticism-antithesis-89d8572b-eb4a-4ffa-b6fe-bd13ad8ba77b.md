# Does infinitism lead to skepticism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 89d8572b-eb4a-4ffa-b6fe-bd13ad8ba77b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism View](infinitism-view-synthesis-bddda30f-213c-4b95-bb22-45afdb1e886a.md)

## Content

**Skepticism is the philosophical position that doubts the possibility of acquiring genuine knowledge.**, **If beliefs require an infinite chain of reasons that is practically never achievable, then knowledge may be deemed unattainable, promoting a skeptical outlook.**, **Infinitism's requirement could inadvertently strip beliefs of justification, feeding into skeptical arguments about the impossibility of knowledge.**
