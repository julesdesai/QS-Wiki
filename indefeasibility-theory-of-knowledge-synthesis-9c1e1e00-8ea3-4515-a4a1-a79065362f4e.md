## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification for the belief is indefeasible**, **Indefeasibility means that new evidence cannot overturn the justification**
