# Could someone know a proposition based on luck?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b35268bb-5469-4285-b3ac-f3b085b04f10
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Sensitivity Theory of Knowledge](sensitivity-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-ba84f32c-948c-4607-9039-09527bd88c50.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a person believes a true proposition due to sheer luck, and their belief was not formed through a reliable method.**, **Intuitively, we would say that the person does not know the proposition because knowledge cannot be gained through luck alone.**
