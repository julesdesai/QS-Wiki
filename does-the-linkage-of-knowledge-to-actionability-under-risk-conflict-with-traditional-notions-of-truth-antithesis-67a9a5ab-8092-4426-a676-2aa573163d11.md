# Does the linkage of knowledge to actionability under risk conflict with traditional notions of truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 67a9a5ab-8092-4426-a676-2aa573163d11
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Encroachment](pragmatic-encroachment-synthesis-829538ce-ebfd-41f1-81ea-e2a3bdbbc235.md)

## Content

**Suppose that a truth is considered actionable without risk in low-stakes situations but not in high-stakes ones due to potential consequences. It is implausible for the truth condition of knowledge to depend on situational risk factors, as traditional accounts of knowledge prioritize truth as an objective matter, not an action-based calculation.**
