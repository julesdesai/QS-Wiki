# How do defeaters relate to context in defining knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 074243de-d116-4e74-9f5d-3ba8cd75f2e9
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Internalist Defeasibility View](internalist-defeasibility-view-synthesis-fd143e9a-1c88-44de-a8f1-4ab4ab5bc60e.md)

## Content

**Suppose a situation in which what counts as a defeater shifts based on the social or conceptual context. Intuitively, this shift suggests that our understanding of defeaters and therefore knowledge is context-dependent.**, **If defeaters change based on context, the internalist requirement for defeaters in knowledge may lack consistency across different contexts.**, **This context dependence poses a challenge for a universal and objective internalist definition for knowledge requiring no defeaters.**
