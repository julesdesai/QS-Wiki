# Does all evidence equal strong evidence?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 88ba418b-794b-4c9c-bfe0-7bc76cc92d01
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Knowledge as Evidentialism](knowledge-as-evidentialism-synthesis-d084f707-0b95-4668-9830-11979f2fc460.md)

## Content

**Consider a person who believes they left their keys on the kitchen counter because they remember doing so hazily.**, **Their belief is based on evidence from memory, but the recollection is weak, failing to qualify as strong evidence.**, **Even if the belief is true, the weakness of the evidence challenges whether the belief constitutes knowledge.**
