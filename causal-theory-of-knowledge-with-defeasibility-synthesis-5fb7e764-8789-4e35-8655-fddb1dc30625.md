## Content

**Knowledge is a true belief causally connected to the fact it represents that can withstand challenges**, **For a belief to constitute knowledge, it must be true and causally linked to the fact in such a way as to avoid defeaters**, **A defeater is information that would disrupt or undermine the causal link if known**
