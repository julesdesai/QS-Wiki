## Content

**Consider a situation where a person believes that the lights are on because their eyes perceive it as so. However, the possibility of an advanced hologram being used creates doubt about the truth of their belief. Intuitively, error possibilities often challenge whether the belief constitutes knowledge.**, **There is a plausible principle that acknowledges alternative possibilities can undermine claims to knowledge, suggesting that knowledge may require ruling out certain errors.**
