# Does the concept of an infinite chain of reasons lead to skepticism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e584d5d4-4861-402f-95ee-e31224a7a9b1
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism in Epistemology](infinitism-in-epistemology-synthesis-3dfd51b6-00d8-4b2a-a651-5af96def07b3.md)

## Content

**If every belief requires an infinite number of reasons to be justified, then no belief can ever be fully justified.**, **Without a fully justified belief, knowledge becomes impossible, thus leading to skepticism about our ability to truly know anything.**, **An epistemic model that requires conditions that are impossible to fulfill in principle seems to disallow knowledge entirely.**
