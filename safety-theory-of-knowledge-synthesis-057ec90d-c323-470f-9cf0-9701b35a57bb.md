## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they would not easily believe a false proposition**, **The truth of a proposition must generate a condition where the belief could not easily be false**
