**Parent:** [Is accidental or coincidental alignment with truth enough to violate tracking sufficiency?](is-accidental-or-coincidental-alignment-with-truth-enough-to-violate-tracking-sufficiency-antithesis-570f771f-c97b-4863-b35c-e162c498ff68.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief in the proposition results from the exercise of intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person's cognitive abilities reliably lead to true beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that these virtue-based conditions hold**
