# Can true belief alone constitute knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b8b9fc7e-cb92-4bfe-ae76-37e970beb93b
**Depth:** 2
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundationalism](foundationalism-thesis-fd868af7-578e-400a-bb27-265f88ced5ee.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person guesses correctly about a future event; intuitively, we would judge that this does not count as knowledge even if their belief is true.**, **There is a plausible principle that knowledge requires more than just true belief, such as justification or evidence.**
