# Can functionalism explain the phenomenon of mental state inversion?

**Parent:** [Functionalism](functionalism-synthesis-2d0849d8-6d8d-49c5-822f-95e70d5e82e6.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which two individuals have mental states with identical causal roles but experience them differently, such as one seeing red where the other sees green. Intuitively, we would judge that their knowledge about colors differs, even if their mental states functionally are the same.**
