# Knowledge as Safety and Virtue

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Does sensitivity fail to account for expert intuition?](does-sensitivity-fail-to-account-for-expert-intuition-antithesis-bd1a8051-8139-4185-b11e-565f74ead0f6.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is both safe and results from intellectual virtue**, **Safety involves not easily believing a false proposition, and virtue involves reliably leading to truth**
