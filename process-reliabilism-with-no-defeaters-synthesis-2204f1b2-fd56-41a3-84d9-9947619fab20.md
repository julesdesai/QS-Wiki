## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, what counts as justification is determined by the reliability of the process used to arrive at the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is true, the belief in the proposition was formed by a reliable process, and there are no defeaters**
