## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief be produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, their belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process, and some conditions other than consciousness provide justification for the belief**
