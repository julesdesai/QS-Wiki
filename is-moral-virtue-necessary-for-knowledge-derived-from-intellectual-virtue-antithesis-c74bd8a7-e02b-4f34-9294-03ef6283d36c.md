# Is moral virtue necessary for knowledge derived from intellectual virtue?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c74bd8a7-e02b-4f34-9294-03ef6283d36c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-7601e066-11d5-4f0c-8c42-44a4cc1f650d.md)

## Content

**Consider a person with intellectual virtues who regularly forms true beliefs but lacks moral virtues, such as honesty. Intuitively, these beliefs may not be known if moral considerations are relevant.**, **The absence of moral virtue may undermine the status of a true belief as knowledge, even when intellectual virtues are present.**
