## Content

**A person's belief is reliable if it is formed through a consistently truth-conducive process**, **To know a proposition, a person must believe the proposition**, **To know a proposition, the proposition must be true**, **A person's belief is safe if in nearby possible worlds where their belief is formed in the same way and is formed through a reliable process, it is still true**
