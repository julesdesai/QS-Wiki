# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with Reliability as a Safeguard

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Could a process be reliable but not intellectually virtuous?](could-a-process-be-reliable-but-not-intellectually-virtuous-antithesis-b3fdf88e-0d0c-4bf0-a51c-46a898422458.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified and the justification is safeguarded by reliability**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is justified, and the justification is safeguarded by reliability**
