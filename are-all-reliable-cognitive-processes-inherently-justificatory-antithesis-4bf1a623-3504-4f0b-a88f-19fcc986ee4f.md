# Are all reliable cognitive processes inherently justificatory?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 4bf1a623-3504-4f0b-a88f-19fcc986ee4f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalism with Reliance on External Factors](externalism-with-reliance-on-external-factors-synthesis-94d45e40-e833-458e-8cba-8623e44201d6.md)

## Content

**Suppose there exists a reliable cognitive process that systematically excludes minority perspectives, leading to biased beliefs. Intuitively, such a process would not be justificatory.**, **The mere reliability of cognitive processes does not necessarily entail justification if these processes fail broader standards of fairness or comprehensiveness.**
