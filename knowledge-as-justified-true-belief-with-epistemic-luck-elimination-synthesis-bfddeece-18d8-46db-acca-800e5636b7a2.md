# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with Epistemic Luck Elimination

**Parent:** [Does the view account for Gettier-style scenarios adequately?](does-the-view-account-for-gettier-style-scenarios-adequately-antithesis-b06d68e3-d26f-4dfa-941e-30dcc930b8fe.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification is not undermined by epistemic luck**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their justification is free from epistemic luck**
