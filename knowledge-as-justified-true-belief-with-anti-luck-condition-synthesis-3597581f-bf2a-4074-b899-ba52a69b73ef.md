# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with Anti-Luck Condition

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is intellectual virtue sufficient for knowledge in all cases?](is-intellectual-virtue-sufficient-for-knowledge-in-all-cases-antithesis-725b8101-815e-40b7-a1eb-0ca49bf38743.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is not true purely by luck**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, they have justification for the belief, and the truth is not a result of sheer luck**
