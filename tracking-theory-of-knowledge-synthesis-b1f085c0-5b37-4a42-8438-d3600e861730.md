**Parent:** [Can false defeaters wrongly undermine knowledge?](can-false-defeaters-wrongly-undermine-knowledge-antithesis-4babf768-f42b-480b-a491-706f21851f93.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in nearby possible worlds in which the proposition is true, the person believes it**
