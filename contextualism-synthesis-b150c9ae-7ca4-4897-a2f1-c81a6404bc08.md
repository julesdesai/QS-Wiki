## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief to be justified according to the context-sensitive standards of knowledge**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is justified according to context-sensitive standards**
