# Can knowledge be fully accessible internally?

**Parent:** [Hybrid Externalism Summary](hybrid-externalism-summary-synthesis-2250092f-2630-4da2-af9c-09ed6c4e9cfe.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person believes a proposition based on a reliable process that they cannot cognitively recognize. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge they lack knowledge.**, **Suppose we have a case where a person cannot access reasons for their belief, except that it is derived from a reliable method. Intuitively, we would judge they lack understanding of why the belief is true.**
