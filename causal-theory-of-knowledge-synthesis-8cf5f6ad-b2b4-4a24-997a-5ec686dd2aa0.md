## Content

**Knowledge is a true belief caused in a reliable way by the fact that makes it true**, **There must be a causal connection between the fact and the person's belief**, **A person knows a proposition if the belief is formed by a causal mechanism linking to the truth of the proposition**, **For a belief to be considered knowledge, accidental or lucky truths without causal connection are excluded**
