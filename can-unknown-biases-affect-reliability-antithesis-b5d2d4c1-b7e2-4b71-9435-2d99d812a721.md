# Can unknown biases affect reliability?

## Content

**Suppose a person has subconscious biases that affect their cognitive process, leading it to become unreliable without their awareness. This hidden unreliability can question whether their beliefs truly qualify as knowledge.**, **A cognitive process tainted by unseen biases does not ensure the reliability needed to produce knowledge.**
