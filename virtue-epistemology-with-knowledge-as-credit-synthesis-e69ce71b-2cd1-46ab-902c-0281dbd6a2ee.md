**Parent:** [Are there cases where justified true belief aligns with virtue epistemology yet lacks knowledge?](are-there-cases-where-justified-true-belief-aligns-with-virtue-epistemology-yet-lacks-knowledge-antithesis-2160a711-cd28-4ab1-8001-912f47c2eb55.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed through intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they receive credit for arriving at the true belief through their intellectual virtues**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is formed through intellectual virtue, and they receive credit for their belief**
