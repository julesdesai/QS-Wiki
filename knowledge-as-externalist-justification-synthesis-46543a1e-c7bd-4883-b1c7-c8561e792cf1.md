## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary they have an external justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process or has external justification**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient they believe the proposition, have an external justification for believing the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**
