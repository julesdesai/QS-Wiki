# What happens if someone knows without being aware they are tracking the truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ce75763a-c5ed-4841-9913-8d32f698270e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Tracking Theory of Knowledge](tracking-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-dda35d00-5b10-4342-b443-f197350806e5.md)

## Content

**Imagine a person who consistently forms true beliefs based on instinct rather than conscious reasoning. Intuitively, we might still attribute knowledge to them even if they are unaware of tracking the truth.**, **There is a plausible principle that knowledge does not always require conscious deliberation or awareness of truth-tracking. This principle combined with instinctually true beliefs challenges the necessity of awareness for knowledge.**
