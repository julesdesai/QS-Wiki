# Does the lack of access to justification impact our understanding of knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** dee32481-4d03-48ee-9579-1f0a0899f17c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Disjunctivism](disjunctivism-synthesis-90a3baa8-9102-43d8-b9cc-d77493986c7c.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person believes it will rain because they have a reliable source informing them, but they cannot recall this source, thereby lacking access to their justification for this belief. Intuitively, we question whether they truly know it will rain.**, **In situations where a person lacks access to the justification for their belief, it might seem implausible to claim that a genuine connection to reality is maintained.**
