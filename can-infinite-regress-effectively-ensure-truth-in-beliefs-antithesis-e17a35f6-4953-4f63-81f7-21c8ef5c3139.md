# Can infinite regress effectively ensure truth in beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e17a35f6-4953-4f63-81f7-21c8ef5c3139
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Finite Infinitism Summary](finite-infinitism-summary-synthesis-cf1727db-af05-4f36-9758-13f464b5dd32.md)

## Content

**Consider a situation where the belief's justification potentially extends infinitely. It is not guaranteed that these reasons will eventually lead to the truth.**, **Infinite justificatory potential does not necessarily impose constraints on false beliefs. Without a truth direction, infinite regress alone cannot ensure correctness.**
