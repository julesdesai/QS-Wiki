## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is required that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified through personal evidence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is justified through personal evidence**
