# How can we trust our perceptual experiences?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 544b9c64-26f1-4d2f-a54b-3e43cabe1218
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Disjunctivism](disjunctivism-synthesis-15b36dc9-362d-489d-8156-bddc0dd6b522.md)

## Content

**Imagine scenarios where two people perceive the same event differently, such as a magic trick that looks real to one but not the other. Intuitively, this raises doubts about the reliability of perception in presenting direct reality.**, **If perception can yield vastly different experiences in similar scenarios, then claiming perception presents direct reality or mere appearance seems insufficient to explain such differences.**
