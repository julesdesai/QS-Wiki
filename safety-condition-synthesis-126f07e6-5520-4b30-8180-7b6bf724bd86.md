# Safety Condition

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 126f07e6-5520-4b30-8180-7b6bf724bd86
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does knowledge require more than just truth and belief?](does-knowledge-require-more-than-just-truth-and-belief-antithesis-23ea7134-c321-49ba-bb9d-828c4c84aaf8.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in nearby possible worlds where the person has the same belief process, the belief remains true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is safe**
