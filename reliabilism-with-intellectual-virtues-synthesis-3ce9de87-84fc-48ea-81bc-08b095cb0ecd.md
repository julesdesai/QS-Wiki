**Parent:** [Is it possible to possess knowledge without conscious virtue?](is-it-possible-to-possess-knowledge-without-conscious-virtue-antithesis-2a4059bb-fab1-4561-bcc9-16f5b2f88060.md)

## Content

**Knowledge is true belief that is reliably produced**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief arises from a reliable process**, **Intellectual virtue is a factor that can enhance the reliability of belief formation but is not strictly necessary for knowledge**
