## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief tracks the truth across nearby possible worlds**, **Truth-tracking involves a belief being true when the proposition is true and not being held when the proposition is false**
