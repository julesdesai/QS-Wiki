# Can justification exist without a true belief leading to knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 025997db-b1c2-4dde-8f63-f6ab7d172e4f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Neo-Mooreanism](neo-mooreanism-synthesis-aeb63ebc-6515-4a29-8322-7a2c352d1501.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where Smith believes his friend owns a Ford based on reliable evidence, but unbeknownst to him, the friend just sold it. Intuitively, Smith's belief is justified but not knowledge since the belief is false.**, **A belief can be justified without being true, and hence not lead to knowledge, showing a gap between justification and knowledge.**
