## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief has not been legitimately challenged**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that default justification is present unless successfully challenged**
