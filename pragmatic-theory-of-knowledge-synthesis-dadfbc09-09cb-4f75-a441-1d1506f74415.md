## Content

**Knowledge is true belief that serves practical purposes**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief serves a practical purpose**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the belief serves a practical purpose, and the proposition is true**
