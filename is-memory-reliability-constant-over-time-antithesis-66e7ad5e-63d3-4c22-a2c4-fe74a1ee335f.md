# Is memory reliability constant over time?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 66e7ad5e-63d3-4c22-a2c4-fe74a1ee335f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilist Theory of Knowledge](reliabilist-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-fec1f444-460d-4835-9da9-5fd5405f3ea1.md)

## Content

**Suppose someone's cognitive process for memory is reliable at time A, making a true recall. At time B, due to stress or aging, this process becomes unreliable, casting doubt on earlier knowledge claims. Intuitively, stability of reliability is critical to sustained knowledge.**, **Shifts in reliability of cognitive processes over time challenge the sustained validity of former knowledge claims.**
