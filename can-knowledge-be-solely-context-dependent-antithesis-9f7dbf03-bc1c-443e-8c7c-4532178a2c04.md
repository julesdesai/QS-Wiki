# Can knowledge be solely context-dependent?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9f7dbf03-bc1c-443e-8c7c-4532178a2c04
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Truth-Conditional Contextualism](truth-conditional-contextualism-synthesis-531a396e-31ae-4fe8-8636-7f56a6debb5b.md)

## Content

**A situation where a person knows a proposition as true in one context but not another defies the intuition that knowledge should be stable across contexts.**, **If knowledge varies with each minor change in context, it can lead to contradictions about what a person knows at any time.**
