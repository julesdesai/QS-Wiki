## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is justification for the belief that is appropriate to the context**, **Different contexts may call for different forms of justification**
