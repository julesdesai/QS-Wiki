# Are epistemic virtues sufficient for knowledge in all contexts?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d985aee2-d354-474e-8035-3d289dbf979f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism with Virtue Epistemology](contextualism-with-virtue-epistemology-synthesis-4d017c8b-6b5b-48d7-b8c7-737c6c80044f.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person employs epistemic virtues perfectly but fails to know under high epistemic standards. Intuitively, we would question whether virtue is sufficient for knowledge.**, **There is a plausible principle that epistemic virtues should be sufficient for knowledge regardless of contextual standards.**
