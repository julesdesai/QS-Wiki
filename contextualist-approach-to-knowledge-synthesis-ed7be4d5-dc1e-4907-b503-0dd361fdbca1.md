## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person's belief to qualify as knowledge, the conditions for what counts as knowledge may vary with different contexts**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they meet the contextual standards for belief, justification, and truth in their situation**
