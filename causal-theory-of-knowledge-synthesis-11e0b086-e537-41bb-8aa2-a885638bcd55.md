# Causal Theory of Knowledge

## Content

**Knowledge is justified true belief**, **A person knows a proposition if there is an appropriate causal connection between the belief and the truth of the proposition**, **The causal connection can be direct or indirect through perception, memory, or testimony**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief to be true**
