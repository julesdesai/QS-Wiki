# Does coherentism handle disconnected sub-networks of beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 632737b9-9043-40aa-95a1-3acf6f746d4b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism with Infinite Justificatory Network](coherentism-with-infinite-justificatory-network-synthesis-c0ed2adb-3371-4bf3-b36f-58a215809fa4.md)

## Content

**A network of beliefs could consist of sub-networks that are coherent internally but completely disconnected from each other, making coherence unhelpful for overall justification.**, **In such disconnected networks, each belief is only justified in isolation from the whole, undermining general belief coherence.**
