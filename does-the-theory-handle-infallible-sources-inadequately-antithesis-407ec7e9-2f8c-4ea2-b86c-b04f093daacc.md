# Does the theory handle infallible sources inadequately?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 407ec7e9-2f8c-4ea2-b86c-b04f093daacc
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Tracking Theory of Knowledge](tracking-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-6d302593-f2de-4e10-8195-85a314e81400.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person believes a true proposition based on an infallible source, such as a trustworthy scientific instrument.**, **Since the source is infallible, the belief would hold even in counterfactual situations, making truth-tracking irrelevant.**, **This raises a concern that the tracking condition does not address certain types of knowledge that do not rely on the person’s belief-adjustment.**
