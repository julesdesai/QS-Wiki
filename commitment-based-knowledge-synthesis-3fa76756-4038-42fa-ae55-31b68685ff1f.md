## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they are committed to the truth of the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the cognitive faculties producing the belief to function properly in an appropriate environment**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they are committed to the truth of the proposition, the proposition is true, and the cognitive faculties producing the belief function properly in an appropriate environment**
