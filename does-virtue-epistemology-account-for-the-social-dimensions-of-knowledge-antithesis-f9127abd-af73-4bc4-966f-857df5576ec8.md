# Does virtue epistemology account for the social dimensions of knowledge?

## Content

**Consider knowledge that arises in collaborative projects where no single individual's cognitive virtues are responsible for the outcome.**, **In such scenarios, the attribution of knowledge cannot be fully explained by individual cognitive virtues, suggesting a limitation in this epistemic view.**
