## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person uses intellectual virtues to arrive at the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient if their belief results from the exercise of intellectual virtues**
