# Can contextualism address the issue of future knowledge changes?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1f144dd2-779c-450c-8f74-c7f2dcc23b6e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualist approach to knowledge](contextualist-approach-to-knowledge-synthesis-a36ecad8-b4cc-4b9e-8a50-84a5c5d3051f.md)

## Content

**Knowledge claims could change over time as contexts shift and standards evolve.**, **Contextualism does not account for how such changes affect the stability of knowledge claims.**, **This raises concerns about the reliability of knowledge over time in fluctuating contexts.**
