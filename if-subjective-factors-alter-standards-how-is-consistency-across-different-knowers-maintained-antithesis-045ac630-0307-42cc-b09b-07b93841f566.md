## Content

**Consider a scenario in which Charles and Dana both believe the same true proposition, but Charles feels confident while Dana feels doubtful. Intuitively, this suggests Charles knows it while Dana does not, yet this violates the consistency of fixed standards.**, **A principle that identical epistemic situations should yield identical results seems undermined if subjective factors alter outcomes.**
