# Knowledge Requiring Causal Connection

**Parent:** [Does knowledge require more than just reliable truth-conducive processes?](does-knowledge-require-more-than-just-reliable-truth-conducive-processes-antithesis-f7e7f319-70cf-4d67-b4d3-f14933b28fc6.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is a causal connection between the fact and their belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their true belief is causally connected to the fact**
