# Can phenomenalism account for the objectivity of scientific knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c0ac7490-81c6-4e3c-9118-15bebd247ecc
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Phenomenalism with Appeal to Memory](phenomenalism-with-appeal-to-memory-synthesis-2b7704c1-89ef-4cc3-a1d9-427b8175dc3f.md)

## Content

**Scientific knowledge aims to describe objective truths about the world that exist independently of individual perceptual experiences.**, **If knowledge is solely based on perceptual experiences and memory, it challenges the possibility of objective scientific knowledge which relies on consistency across observers.**
