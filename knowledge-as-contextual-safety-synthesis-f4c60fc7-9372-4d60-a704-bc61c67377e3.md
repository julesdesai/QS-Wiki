# Knowledge as Contextual Safety

**Parent:** [Is it possible for intellectually virtuous actions to not result in knowledge?](is-it-possible-for-intellectually-virtuous-actions-to-not-result-in-knowledge-antithesis-8c1a6acd-ea83-4ba7-ae2a-40ee77cb2dca.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed safely given the specific context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the person believes the proposition, the belief is true, and the belief's formation is contextually safe**
