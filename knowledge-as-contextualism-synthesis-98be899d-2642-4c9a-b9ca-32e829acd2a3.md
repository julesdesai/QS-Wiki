# Knowledge as Contextualism

**Parent:** [Can knowledge exist without sensitivity to falsehood?](can-knowledge-exist-without-sensitivity-to-falsehood-antithesis-67beba41-6967-4533-9640-2c69b7dff6a3.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the standards for knowledge in the person's context are met**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, the proposition is true, and the context standards are met**
