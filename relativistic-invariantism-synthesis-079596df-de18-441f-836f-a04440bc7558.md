## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that justification standards allow for relative cultural or situational interpretation**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and they meet justification standards which permit cultural or situational considerations**
