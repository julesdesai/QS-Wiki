## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief arises from the exercise of intellectual virtues**, **Intellectual virtues are character traits that reliably lead to true beliefs in a wide range of situations**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief results from intellectual virtues**
