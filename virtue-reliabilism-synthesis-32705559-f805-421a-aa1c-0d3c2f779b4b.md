## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the reliable cognitive process involves intellectual virtue or skill on the part of the person**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient if it is the result of a reliable process executed with intellectual virtue**
