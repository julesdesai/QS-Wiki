## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the standards of justification are invariant but sensitive to the person's practical interests**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their practical interests dictate the appropriate level of justification**
