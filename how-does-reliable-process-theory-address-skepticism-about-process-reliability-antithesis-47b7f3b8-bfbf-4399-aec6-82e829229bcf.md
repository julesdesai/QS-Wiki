# How does Reliable Process Theory address skepticism about process reliability?

**Depth:** 4

## Content

**It is implausible that individuals always have access to the reliability of their belief-forming processes.**, **A plausible principle is that for a belief to be known, the believer must have some access to its justification. Therefore, if one cannot assess process reliability, reliable process theory might be undermined.**
