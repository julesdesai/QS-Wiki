# Knowledge as Safe Belief

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can a true belief with justification become knowledge through unreliable processes?](can-a-true-belief-with-justification-become-knowledge-through-unreliable-processes-antithesis-4f530dec-c27c-4481-b19f-6c3644113712.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed in a way that would not easily be false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is formed in a way that would not easily be false**
