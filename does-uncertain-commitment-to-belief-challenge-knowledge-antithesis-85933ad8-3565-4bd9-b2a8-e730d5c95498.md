# Does uncertain commitment to belief challenge knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 85933ad8-3565-4bd9-b2a8-e730d5c95498

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [No-False-Lemma Theory](no-false-lemma-theory-synthesis-5710d9f4-02e8-4ed3-aa41-f8ff2a235ea5.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person holds a belief but with great uncertainty and lack of confidence.**, **In this case, societal intuition often resists viewing this belief as knowledge, due to the absence of strong commitment.**, **The lack of conviction in the belief presents a challenge to the concept of it constituting knowledge.**
