## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person has the competence to discern true propositions from false ones**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the competence leads to true beliefs in relevant contexts**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that these competence-based conditions hold**
