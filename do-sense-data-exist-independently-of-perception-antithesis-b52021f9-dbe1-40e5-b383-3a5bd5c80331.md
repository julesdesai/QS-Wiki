# Do sense data exist independently of perception?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b52021f9-dbe1-40e5-b383-3a5bd5c80331
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Indirect Realism](indirect-realism-synthesis-6f280b3f-d71e-41be-89e6-75cad68b6820.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that sense data exist independently of perception because they seem inherently tied to individual experiences.**, **The reliance on sense data presupposes an objective connection to reality, which may not exist if sense data are subjective.**
