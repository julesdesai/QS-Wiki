# Is coherence sufficient for distinguishing truth from error?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 4c27c030-22f4-48cc-a9a0-c0dee4429372
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism Summary](coherentism-summary-synthesis-189c934d-40a0-41d9-b70b-1a7a0a9cc7c3.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that knowledge requires the ability to distinguish truth from error. This principle together with the existence of a coherent belief system that includes errors suggests that coherence is not sufficient to discern truth from error.**
