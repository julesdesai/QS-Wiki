# Knowledge as Virtue Epistemology

**Parent:** [Does sensitivity overlook the role of accidental belief?](does-sensitivity-overlook-the-role-of-accidental-belief-antithesis-2090d03e-d466-46e2-964a-e94fdbeb423c.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief arises from intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief arises from intellectual virtue**
