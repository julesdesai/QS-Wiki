# Knowledge as Virtue Epistemology

**Node ID:** ca16f550-2686-41fd-9cc5-c1e3abdc4320
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can justification be practically infinite?](can-justification-be-practically-infinite-antithesis-ced39643-38f1-4b57-83f5-f5120ff65cfd.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief arises from intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief arises from intellectual virtue**
