# Knowledge as Safety Condition

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 95413db3-3d38-43eb-b071-9cc37d005d68
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is the causal connection requirement too strict?](is-the-causal-connection-requirement-too-strict-antithesis-bedb2417-a847-490c-b3aa-eb1f5b10288e.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in nearby possible scenarios where the person believes the proposition, it remains true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and in nearby possible scenarios where the person believes the proposition, it remains true**
