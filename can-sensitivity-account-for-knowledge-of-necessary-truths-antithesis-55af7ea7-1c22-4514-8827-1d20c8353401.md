# Can sensitivity account for knowledge of necessary truths?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 55af7ea7-1c22-4514-8827-1d20c8353401
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Knowledge as Sensitivity](knowledge-as-sensitivity-synthesis-a361f947-df1a-4345-a4d3-ea9ac7712828.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person knows a mathematical truth, such as 2+2=4.**, **It is not possible for the proposition 2+2=4 to be false, as it is necessarily true.**, **In a scenario where a proposition cannot be false, we cannot evaluate whether a belief would remain unchanged if it were false.**, **Knowledge of necessary truths challenges the requirement for sensitivity as they cannot be otherwise.**
