# Epistemic Dependability with Environmental Normalcy

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is stable and reliable belief always sufficient for knowledge?](is-stable-and-reliable-belief-always-sufficient-for-knowledge-antithesis-85d3b63d-2e5c-4ca2-a71d-55133a8b9eb1.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the surrounding circumstances are normal and conducive to truth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief depends on a stable and reliable relation to the truth and the surrounding circumstances are normal and conducive to truth**
