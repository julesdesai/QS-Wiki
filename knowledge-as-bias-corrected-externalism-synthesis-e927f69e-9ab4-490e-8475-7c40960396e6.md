**Parent:** [Do cognitive biases affect the reliability of belief formation?](do-cognitive-biases-affect-the-reliability-of-belief-formation-antithesis-a7c6fdce-5c11-4df4-88f7-def96570acc6.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed by a reliable process corrected for biases**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is formed by a reliable process corrected for biases**
