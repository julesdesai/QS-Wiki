# Are we too reliant on unlikely scenarios for safety?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 169c8f9b-7523-4221-b43a-8e3e0374ad95
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [The Safety Condition](the-safety-condition-synthesis-1c250b1c-da7f-4cab-a10a-178f18915234.md)

## Content

**A belief is considered safe only if it could not easily have been false.**, **In an unlikely but possible world where different factors could easily make the belief false, the belief should not be considered knowledge.**, **Depending on unlikely scenarios to determine safety seems counterintuitive and overly restrictive**
