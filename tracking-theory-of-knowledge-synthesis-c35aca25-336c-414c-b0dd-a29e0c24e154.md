# Tracking Theory of Knowledge

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is truth necessary for knowledge in all cases?](is-truth-necessary-for-knowledge-in-all-cases-antithesis-b3f8c497-d2a8-4056-8a6b-d20cc56cdf41.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were true, the person would believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief tracks the truth of the proposition across relevant nearby possible worlds**
