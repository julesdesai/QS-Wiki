## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the standard of justification varies based on factors related to the subject such as stakes and practical interests**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and meet the variable justificatory standard**
