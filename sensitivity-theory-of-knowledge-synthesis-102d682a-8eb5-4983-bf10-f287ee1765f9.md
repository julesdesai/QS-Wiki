# Sensitivity Theory of Knowledge

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 3
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can outdated evidence limit knowledge?](can-outdated-evidence-limit-knowledge-antithesis-8317706e-1d23-4d4f-8489-44d31a1e311d.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **This counterfactual sensitivity ensures that the belief tracks the truth**
