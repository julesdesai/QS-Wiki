# Does Infinitism lead to skepticism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 52525299-b858-4c1a-bdf6-aed44f500dea
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism](infinitism-synthesis-db00e7c1-6a13-4ca5-8f09-6fae7cd29e0f.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a person cannot provide infinite reasons to justify their belief. Intuitively, we have to say that their belief is unjustified or unknowable.**, **This scenario suggests that if Infinitism is correct, it would lead to a form of skepticism where most, if not all, beliefs are unjustified.**
