# Can beliefs be justified without considering cognitive biases and errors in perception?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ccf2e72f-977f-4966-bcdb-d90c7b1d0d3e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Phenomenal Conservatism](phenomenal-conservatism-synthesis-177872be-9e69-40ef-b8fe-02aefba4e5af.md)

## Content

**Human perception is subject to cognitive biases and errors such as confirmation bias, where people see what they expect to see.**, **If justification relies solely on perceptual experiences, it might ignore these cognitive flaws, undermining the reliability of the justification.**
