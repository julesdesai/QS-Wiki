# Can knowledge be attributed independently of truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1ca6d63d-1d3c-498b-ae2e-849c14be09c0
**Depth:** 4

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology Summary](virtue-epistemology-summary-synthesis-10eec969-c917-4d6d-bc9d-ac9f02dc3f98.md)

## Content

**There are instances where a person believes a proposition due to strong evidence or intellectual virtue, yet the proposition is false.**, **A belief formed by a reliable process that fails due to misleading evidence may seem justified yet may still result in falsehoods.**, **A belief formed through cognitive abilities can still be false, suggesting that virtue alone isn't sufficient for knowledge.**
