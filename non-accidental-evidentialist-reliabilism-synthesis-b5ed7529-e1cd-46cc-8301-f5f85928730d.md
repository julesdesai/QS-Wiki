# Non-accidental Evidentialist Reliabilism

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** b5ed7529-e1cd-46cc-8301-f5f85928730d
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge be accidental but reliable?](can-knowledge-be-accidental-but-reliable-antithesis-06c5d028-4383-4273-a116-b0f5e58513ef.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is produced by a reliable process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the truth of the belief is not accidental**, **The non-accidentality condition requires that the belief's truth be connected in a non-coincidental way to the believer's cognitive faculties**
