## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from the exercise of epistemic virtues or abilities**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is true, they believe it, and the belief arises from the exercise of epistemic virtues or abilities**
