## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have access to evidence supporting the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief in the proposition withstands rational evaluation under the evidence available**
