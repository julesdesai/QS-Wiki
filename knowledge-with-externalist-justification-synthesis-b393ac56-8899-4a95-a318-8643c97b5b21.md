# Knowledge with Externalist Justification

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can infants or animals possess knowledge without intellectual virtue?](can-infants-or-animals-possess-knowledge-without-intellectual-virtue-antithesis-df0759b5-3ece-4280-8326-bb6520727668.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is externally justified by factors outside the person's awareness**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is externally justified by factors outside the person's awareness**
