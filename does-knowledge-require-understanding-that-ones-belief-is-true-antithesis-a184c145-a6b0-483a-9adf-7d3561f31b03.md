# Does knowledge require understanding that one's belief is true?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a184c145-a6b0-483a-9adf-7d3561f31b03
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-5b73def0-7386-47a1-9b12-ba7bdc34b62a.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a person has a true belief but lacks understanding of why it is true. Intuitively, we might question whether they genuinely have knowledge without grasping the basis of the truth.**, **The requirement for a person’s belief to result from their own intellectual capabilities suggests a connection to understanding, challenging whether true beliefs alone suffice for knowledge.**
