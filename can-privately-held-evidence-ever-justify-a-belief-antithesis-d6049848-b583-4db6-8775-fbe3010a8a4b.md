# Can privately held evidence ever justify a belief?

**Parent:** [Evidentialism with Objective Evidence](evidentialism-with-objective-evidence-synthesis-54d609bf-9930-4ddb-a0d6-5d54abfff05d.md)

## Content

**In a case where evidence is based on personal experience or testimony, it is plausible to consider a belief justified even if it is not publicly available.**, **Consider a scenario where someone sees a red car while alone and firmly believes it, intuitively we would say they possess knowledge although the evidence is private.**
