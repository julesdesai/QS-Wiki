# Can foundational beliefs be self-justified without contradiction?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 19378b84-a35d-4f6d-aa02-415887ef2258
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism with Foundational Beliefs](infinitism-with-foundational-beliefs-synthesis-d8cdd5a2-d709-4e37-9648-d85b66458099.md)

## Content

**Consider foundational beliefs that exist without justification. Intuitively, if they justify infinite others, they contradict the need for self-justification.**, **It is implausible that a belief serves as both foundational and non-foundational if a chain of unlimited justification is involved.**
