## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is independently justified by empirical evidence without reliance on coherence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is independently justified by empirical evidence**
[END]

This list represents a few significant variations that remain connected to the original view while addressing the objection regarding the necessity of coherence.
