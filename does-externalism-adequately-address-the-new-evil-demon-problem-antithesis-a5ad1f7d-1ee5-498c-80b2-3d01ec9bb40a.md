# Does externalism adequately address the new evil demon problem?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a5ad1f7d-1ee5-498c-80b2-3d01ec9bb40a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalism](externalism-synthesis-5b44df35-6663-4278-91dc-65b3fcbf870c.md)

## Content

**Consider a person placed in a demon-controlled world where all their perceptual beliefs are false due to an external manipulation. Intuitively, we would judge their beliefs as unjustified though they were formed reliably given the constraints of their experience.**, **Justification involving external conditions conflicts with such intuitions as reliable process-based knowledge under these circumstances would mislead us regarding their justificatory status.**
