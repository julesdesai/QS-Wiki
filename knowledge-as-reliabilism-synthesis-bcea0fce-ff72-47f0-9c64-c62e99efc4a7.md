# Knowledge as Reliabilism

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** bcea0fce-ff72-47f0-9c64-c62e99efc4a7
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does having a justified true belief guarantee knowledge?](does-having-a-justified-true-belief-guarantee-knowledge-antithesis-bcdbda99-0279-4dde-9549-d8f6697f4117.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed through a reliable method**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is formed through a reliable method**
