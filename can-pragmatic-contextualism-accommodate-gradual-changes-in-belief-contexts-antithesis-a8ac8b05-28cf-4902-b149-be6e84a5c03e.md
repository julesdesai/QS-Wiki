# Can pragmatic contextualism accommodate gradual changes in belief contexts?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a8ac8b05-28cf-4902-b149-be6e84a5c03e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Contextualism](pragmatic-contextualism-synthesis-eb83c85f-dd41-4bfc-8e5a-553d55565568.md)

## Content

**Belief contexts may change gradually, altering the criteria for practical success without clear points of transition.**, **The fluid nature of context changes complicates determining when a belief achieves or loses the status of knowledge.**, **Gradual shifts suggest that knowledge cannot strictly depend on practical success, as transitional periods may not offer clear success markers.**
