## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that some beliefs are basic and self-justified**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that other beliefs are justified by basic beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is true and derived from basic justified beliefs**
