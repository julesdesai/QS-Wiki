# How does virtue epistemology handle scenarios of epistemic luck?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9a918155-5211-4d69-a7dd-542ce2bb9939
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-db8e4632-83fc-4e14-a2b9-f64cf1d5bb50.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person guesses the answer to a complex question and happens to be correct; their cognitive process was not a reliable indicator of truth.**, **In this case, the reliability of cognitive virtues is challenged, since knowledge should not be attributed merely due to luck or chance.**
