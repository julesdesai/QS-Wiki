## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in nearby possible worlds, if the person believed the proposition, it would be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe it, the proposition is true, and if they were to believe it in nearby possible worlds, it would be true**
