# Does every perceptual error stem from interpretation?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f68fd7f3-d5e6-4fa2-9fa8-5fdf8aa1c83f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Critical Realism](critical-realism-synthesis-04d59a43-df5d-4ff1-bc30-b363f66f9a03.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person sees a stick in water and thinks it is bent due to refraction. In this scenario, intuitively, the error does not stem from interpretation but from the physical properties of light.**, **It is plausible that many visual errors result from physical phenomena rather than misinterpretation.**
