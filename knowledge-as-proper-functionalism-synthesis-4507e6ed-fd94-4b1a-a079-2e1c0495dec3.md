# Knowledge as Proper Functionalism

**Parent:** [Does luck completely undermine knowledge?](does-luck-completely-undermine-knowledge-antithesis-d08962f2-e559-4fea-9f37-fe6fc324092e.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief to be produced by properly functioning cognitive faculties**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is produced by properly functioning cognitive faculties**
