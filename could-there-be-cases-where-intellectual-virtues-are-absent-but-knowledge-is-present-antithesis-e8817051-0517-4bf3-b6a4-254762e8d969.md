# Could there be cases where intellectual virtues are absent, but knowledge is present?

## Content

**Suppose a person stumbles upon the truth by mere luck without relying on intellectual virtues like open-mindedness or intellectual courage. Intuitively, this person has knowledge.**, **A case involving sheer guesswork that turns out to be true does not involve intellectual virtues, yet seems to be an instance of knowledge.**
