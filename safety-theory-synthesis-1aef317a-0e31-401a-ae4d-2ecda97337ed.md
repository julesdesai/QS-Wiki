## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false in nearby possible worlds, they would not believe it**, **A belief is safe if it is true in all nearby possible worlds where the person believes it**
