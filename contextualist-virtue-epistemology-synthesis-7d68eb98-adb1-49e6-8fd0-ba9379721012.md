## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief is the result of intellectual virtue in context-appropriate conditions**, **An intellectual virtue is a character trait that leads one to form true beliefs in typical contexts**, **Context-appropriate conditions are circumstances where the relevant virtues are effective**
