## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is produced by a reliably functioning cognitive process external to the person’s deliberate control**, **Belief formation processes are relevant but do not require direct awareness by the individual**, **Self-deceptive processes must operate outside reliable cognitive mechanisms**
