# Is Coherentism vulnerable to the problem of competing coherent systems?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** cbf9c0ad-cffa-4dc4-a014-72bdd9d54d84
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism about Knowledge](coherentism-about-knowledge-synthesis-39987724-1a23-4547-a925-9e75bbe8b421.md)

## Content

**There could be multiple coherent systems that support contradictory beliefs.**, **If a person can hold two coherent but contradictory systems, it is unclear which, if any, provides genuine knowledge.**, **Intuitively, having competing systems suggests that coherence might not be a sufficient criterion for determining knowledge.**
