# How can knowledge be differentiated from mere true belief if not through intellectual virtue?

**Node ID:** 9100750c-5962-48ea-ae38-27be25426ed2

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-4f2fdbe9-b5ae-4003-9ce0-28573a7cd0c4.md)

## Content

**A person might form a true belief based on a reliable source like a news forecast, which is a source of testimonial knowledge rather than an intellectual virtue**, **In cases of testimonial knowledge, the reliability of the source rather than the individual's intellectual virtue is responsible for the truth of the belief**
