## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification rests ultimately on basic beliefs that are informed by external factors**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is justified by a foundation of basic beliefs influenced by external factors**
