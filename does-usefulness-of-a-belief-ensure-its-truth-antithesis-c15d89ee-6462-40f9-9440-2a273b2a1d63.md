# Does usefulness of a belief ensure its truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c15d89ee-6462-40f9-9440-2a273b2a1d63
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Instrumentalism with Externalist Justification](instrumentalism-with-externalist-justification-synthesis-69903d30-4d37-4781-884e-3e9ef74484e3.md)

## Content

**A belief being useful does not guarantee its truth, as false beliefs can lead to beneficial effects, similar to morale-boosting falsehoods in leadership**, **Truth involves the correspondence of a belief to reality, which is independent of the belief's usefulness or practical outcomes**, **Knowledge requires truth, which cannot be assured solely by the usefulness of a belief**
