# What about cases of cognitive malfunction?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** fd06af75-3040-4157-85ba-b185e871651d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Internalist Reliabilism Summary](internalist-reliabilism-summary-synthesis-afd99030-664a-4a62-9431-6f8bc1e36717.md)

## Content

**Suppose a normally reliable cognitive process malfunctions, leading a person to hold a true belief by accident.**, **Internalist reliabilism suggests they know the proposition because it's true and internally justified.**, **However, the intuitive lack of knowledge in this malfunction scenario challenges the view's validity.**
