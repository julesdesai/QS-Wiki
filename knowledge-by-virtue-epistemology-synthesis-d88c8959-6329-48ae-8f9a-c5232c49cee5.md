# Knowledge by Virtue Epistemology

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Can practical consequences suffice for knowledge on their own?](can-practical-consequences-suffice-for-knowledge-on-their-own-antithesis-268389fb-ca13-44d8-9776-f1f95565a979.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is a result of exercising intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief results from the exercise of intellectual virtue**
