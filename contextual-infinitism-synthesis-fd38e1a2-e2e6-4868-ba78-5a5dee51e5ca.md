## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the degree to which the belief needs to be supported by reasons depends on the context**, **For ordinary contexts, it is sufficient for a belief to have finite support**
