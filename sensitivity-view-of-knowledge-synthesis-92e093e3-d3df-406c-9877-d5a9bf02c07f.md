# Sensitivity View of Knowledge

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can safety alone account for luck?](can-safety-alone-account-for-luck-antithesis-d9e2b1e6-7309-4264-b27b-f560faabeb6a.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A belief is sensitive if in the nearest possible world where the proposition is false, the person does not believe it**, **A belief is justified if it is sensitive**, **To know, a person's justified true belief must not be true purely by luck**
