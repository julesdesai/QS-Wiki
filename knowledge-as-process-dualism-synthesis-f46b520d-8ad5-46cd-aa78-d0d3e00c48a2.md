# Knowledge as Process Dualism

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Is reliability sufficient for knowledge without indefeasibility?](is-reliability-sufficient-for-knowledge-without-indefeasibility-antithesis-c62edd2d-0588-463b-b91e-c551664a833d.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is formed both by a reliable cognitive process and a secondary process providing additional confirmation**, **The secondary process must independently support the reliability of the primary process**
