# Does reliabilism neglect the value of justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5073139e-d163-443b-b8fc-3e8437a23e75
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism Summary](reliabilism-summary-synthesis-c54e9ff1-39e1-4224-b3be-b0979177f411.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person consistently forms true beliefs due to a reliable but undiscriminating process, such as a magic 8-ball that coincidentally functions well. Intuitively, the lack of evidential basis suggests these beliefs aren't truly justified.**, **The sheer reliability without further evidential or value consideration diminishes the depth of genuine justification.**
