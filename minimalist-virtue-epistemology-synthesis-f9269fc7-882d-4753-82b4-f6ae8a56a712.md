**Parent:** [Is it possible to possess knowledge without conscious virtue?](is-it-possible-to-possess-knowledge-without-conscious-virtue-antithesis-2a4059bb-fab1-4561-bcc9-16f5b2f88060.md)

## Content

**Knowledge is true belief enhanced by, but not dependent on, intellectual virtues**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **Intellectual virtues add value to the belief but are not strictly necessary for it to be knowledge**, **The belief is considered knowledge even in the absence of intellectual virtues due to its truth and justification**
