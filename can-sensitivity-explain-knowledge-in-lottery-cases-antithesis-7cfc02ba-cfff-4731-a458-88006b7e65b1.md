# Can sensitivity explain knowledge in lottery cases?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7cfc02ba-cfff-4731-a458-88006b7e65b1
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Knowledge as Sensitivity](knowledge-as-sensitivity-synthesis-a361f947-df1a-4345-a4d3-ea9ac7712828.md)

## Content

**Consider a person who believes their ticket will not win a large and fair lottery based on the overwhelming odds.**, **Even when the belief is true, the sensitivity condition implies they lack knowledge, as they would retain the belief even if it were false.**, **The mismatch between common intuition that they lack knowledge and the true belief questions the adequacy of sensitivity.**
