## Content

**Some aspects of knowledge depend on context, but objective truths remain constant**, **A person knows a proposition if the proposition is true**, **The standards for knowing a proposition vary with different contexts**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **Justification standards can vary with context without affecting truth**
