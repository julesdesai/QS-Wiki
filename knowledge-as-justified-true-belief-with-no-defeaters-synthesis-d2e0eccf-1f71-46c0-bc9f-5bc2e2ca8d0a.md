## Content

**Knowledge is a justified true belief with no defeaters present**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no defeaters undermining their justification**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is justified**
