# Does safety secure against Gettier cases?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3894521a-07af-4fbb-9b71-a8b623c56254
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Safety Theory](safety-theory-synthesis-3968e05f-d744-409d-bfbb-59e5b6ab2a0b.md)

## Content

**In a Gettier case, an agent has a justified true belief that is accidentally true due to unexpected external factors.**, **Intuitively, such beliefs are considered not to be knowledge despite being safe as they are true in nearby possible worlds.**, **If safety does not prevent Gettier cases, then it is insufficient for turning true belief into knowledge.**
