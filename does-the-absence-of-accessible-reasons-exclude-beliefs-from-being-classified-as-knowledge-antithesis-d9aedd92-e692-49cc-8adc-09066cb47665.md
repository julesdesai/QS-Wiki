**Imagine a person who correctly believes a particular fact without any ability to retrieve or comprehend how the belief arose. Intuitively, we would say this person lacks knowledge since accessible reasons are typically crucial for knowledge classification.**, **A principle suggests that knowledge must involve reasons or justifications that a knower can access.**
