## Content

**Consider a case where a mathematician intuits the solution to a complex problem without going through all the steps of proof. Intuitively, the mathematician may be said to know the solution before rigorously evidencing it.**, **A person may have a strong intuitive belief about a friend’s emotional state without explicit evidence, and in circumstances, this intuitive understanding is often referred to as knowledge.**
