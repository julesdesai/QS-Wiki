## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **The standards for knowing a proposition depend on the context of the attribution**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they meet the contextually set standards for knowing the proposition**
