# Does reliable belief formation contradict accessibility requirements for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e21bd3c3-9ae8-4f2f-ab77-8b529c7a8684
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Access Internalism](access-internalism-synthesis-1ebb0a7e-8cd6-40ff-bf0f-a16161232f85.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which an individual is unable to access the reliability of their belief formation process due to lack of cognitive awareness. Intuitively, we either have to accept that this person can still know the proposition, conflicting with access requirements, or deny knowledge, which questions reliance on reliability without accessibility.**
