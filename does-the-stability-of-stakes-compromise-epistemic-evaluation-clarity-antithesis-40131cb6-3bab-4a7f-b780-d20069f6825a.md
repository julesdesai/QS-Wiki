## Content

**Suppose a professor maintains that stakes should remain stable for clarity when teaching students about knowledge. Intuitively, this could obscure the practical application of knowledge in real-world scenarios where stakes are dynamic.**, **In examining real-life decision-making, stakes often fluctuate, making the imposition of stable stakes during evaluation potentially misleading.**
