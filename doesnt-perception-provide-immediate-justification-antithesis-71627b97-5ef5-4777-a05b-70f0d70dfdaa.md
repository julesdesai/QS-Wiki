# Doesn't perception provide immediate justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 71627b97-5ef5-4777-a05b-70f0d70dfdaa
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism](infinitism-synthesis-21450005-9ac1-4d45-9121-350dbebcb2cf.md)

## Content

**Perception is commonly considered to give immediate and direct justification for beliefs.**, **If perception already provides immediate justification, then an infinite series of reasons is unnecessary.**, **Therefore, reliance on perception and immediate justification challenges the need for an infinite chain of reasons.**
