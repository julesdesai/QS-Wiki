## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, the process by which they come to the belief must be reliable in producing true beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification, the proposition is true, and the belief is formed through a reliable process**
