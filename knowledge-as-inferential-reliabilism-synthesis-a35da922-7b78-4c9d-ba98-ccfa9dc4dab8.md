# Knowledge as Inferential Reliabilism

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** a35da922-7b78-4c9d-ba98-ccfa9dc4dab8
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does memory constitute a reliable cognitive process?](does-memory-constitute-a-reliable-cognitive-process-antithesis-f72c7b4f-b29f-4bbf-9679-0f9c83188640.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a reliable inferential process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is produced by a reliable inferential process**
