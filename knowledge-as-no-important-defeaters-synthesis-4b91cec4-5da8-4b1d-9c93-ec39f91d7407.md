# Knowledge as No Important Defeaters

**Parent:** [Does the presence of all possible defeaters unfairly deny knowledge?](does-the-presence-of-all-possible-defeaters-unfairly-deny-knowledge-antithesis-f6cdd006-f720-4bd0-a581-dfa77ab2b01f.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there be no important defeaters for the person's justification**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there are no important defeaters for the person's justification**
