# Is truth alone sufficient for knowledge regardless of belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** abd76f8f-85d6-431c-aedf-1492afdfc953
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism with Bounded Rationality](infinitism-with-bounded-rationality-synthesis-6de35f9a-9231-4ac2-b1a8-a1ab9be932b6.md)

## Content

**Knowledge has traditionally involved the coherence between belief and truth.**, **If a person knows a proposition if it is true, regardless of belief, then there is no subjective component in knowing.**, **Truth alone cannot account for knowledge because it divorces the knower's recognition or awareness of the truth.**
