# Does contextualism undermine the universality and reliability of knowledge?

**Node ID:** ce35a4e1-5c87-4c84-9794-b7ae179b3b1f

## Content

**A plausible principle is that knowledge should be reliable and universal to be useful and meaningful. This principle seems at odds with the changing standards of knowledge, which suggest a lack of universality and reliability.**
