## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief would not easily be false in similar scenarios**, **Safety is about the stability of truth in close possibilities**
