## Content

**For a belief to constitute knowledge, it must not be true due to luck**, **If a belief is true in the actual world, it must be true in nearby possible worlds**, **Unlucky true beliefs do not count as knowledge**, **Safety is assessed based on how easily a belief could have been false**
