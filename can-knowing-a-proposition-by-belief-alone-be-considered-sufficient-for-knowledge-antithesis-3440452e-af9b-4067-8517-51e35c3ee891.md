# Can knowing a proposition by belief alone be considered sufficient for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3440452e-af9b-4067-8517-51e35c3ee891
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Practical Infinitism Summary](practical-infinitism-summary-synthesis-d4cb8c2e-484f-490e-8d3c-323f91446649.md)

## Content

**Believing a proposition does not ensure consideration of alternative possibilities or evidence supporting the belief.**, **Simply believing a proposition without additional justification methods does not fulfill criteria for knowledge.**
