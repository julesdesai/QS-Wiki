# Can someone lack intellectual virtues yet still know a proposition?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** cac4795c-bbf4-4335-91e8-70b3d2b2bede
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-72984f03-1ce3-46fd-9fd6-13203e340ace.md)

## Content

**Picture a scenario where a person knows a proposition because they received a highly credible report from an expert, but this person's cognitive processes lack intellectual virtues. Intuitively, we might still consider this person to know the proposition.**, **It is plausible for an individual to acquire knowledge from credible sources without demonstrating personal intellectual virtues.**
