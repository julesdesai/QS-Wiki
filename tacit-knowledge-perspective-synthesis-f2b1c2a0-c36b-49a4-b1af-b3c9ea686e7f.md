## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that it be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their implicit understanding guides appropriate action concerning the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is true and their understanding is reflected in actions without requiring explicit belief**
