# Does the Safety Condition for knowledge fail in lottery scenarios?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ecc31652-6980-4699-b8e3-304d84ef49f8
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Safety Condition View](safety-condition-view-synthesis-739cc2e2-5111-4640-9af6-f9324f258620.md)

## Content

**Consider a lottery scenario where a person believes their ticket will not win based on the high odds of losing. Intuitively, we judge that they do not know this despite having a true belief produced by a reliable process.**, **In a nearby possible world, there could be a different outcome in the lottery where this belief is false. This suggests the belief is not safe as it could easily be false.**
