# Does indirect realism allow for veridical perceptions?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c9038f71-96b5-4fa8-ba1b-aea78ee4bc81
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Indirect Realism](indirect-realism-synthesis-801f6a7d-c909-434a-bdd9-effe8ebd5964.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that veridical perception requires a true correspondence between mental representations and external reality. This principle together with the mediation of perception by sensory representations entails a difficulty in verifying when perceptions are veridical.**
