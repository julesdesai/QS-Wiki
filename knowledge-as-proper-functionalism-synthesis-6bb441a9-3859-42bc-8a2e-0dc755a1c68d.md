## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the cognitive faculties are operating properly in a suitable environment**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that properly functioning faculties in a suitable environment yield a true belief**
