# Are intellectual virtues consistent across diverse contexts?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 92a6ea3c-4fbb-4420-8e43-c9339d2b8bbf
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Safe Intellectual Virtue Epistemology](safe-intellectual-virtue-epistemology-synthesis-f848b9b7-8a1e-463c-8e96-66e43c041eac.md)

## Content

**Knowledge is true belief arising from intellectual virtue.**, **An intellectual virtue in one context may fail to produce true beliefs in another context due to differing conditions.**, **If virtues cannot consistently yield true beliefs across contexts, their effectiveness in defining knowledge is limited.**
