# Can a belief formed by a reliable process without coherence constitute knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5e681772-3ec4-46e0-9180-84ae6eb48da7
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism with Empirical Check](coherentism-with-empirical-check-synthesis-72695d27-5d80-452b-8845-9bf078f88484.md)

## Content

**A person believes a proposition because it was formed by a reliable cognitive process like perception or memory.**, **The proposition is true and the process is reliable, lending justification to the belief.**, **Without coherence with a system of beliefs, the true belief from a reliable process seems to be knowledge.**
