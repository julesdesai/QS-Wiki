# Knowledge as Safe True Belief

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 1fe6e8ae-7103-470f-9c15-e7bf658e93dd
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can belief be true without knowledge?](can-belief-be-true-without-knowledge-antithesis-655d7a8e-1741-4ddf-85c7-a393d2dd8421.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief could not easily have been false**, **Safety is achieved if there are no close possibilities in which the belief is false**
