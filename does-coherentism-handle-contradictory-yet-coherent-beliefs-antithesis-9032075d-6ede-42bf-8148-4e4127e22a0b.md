# Does coherentism handle contradictory yet coherent beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9032075d-6ede-42bf-8148-4e4127e22a0b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism Summary](coherentism-summary-synthesis-14364920-f24e-48ed-b988-c7d3265113e2.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which an individual holds contradictory beliefs because each belief is justified by separate, coherent belief systems, like holding religious views that contradict scientific explanatory models. Intuitively, holding two coherent yet contradictory systems of belief raises doubts about the sufficiency of coherence in terms of truth and justification.**, **It is implausible that justification through coherence alone can be enough when it permits the coexistence of contradictory beliefs supported by different coherent systems.**
