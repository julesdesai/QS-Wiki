# Virtue Epistemology with Reliabilist Component

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief results from the exercise of intellectual virtue**, **For a belief resulting from the exercise of intellectual virtue to lead to knowledge, it is necessary that the virtue reliably produces true beliefs**
