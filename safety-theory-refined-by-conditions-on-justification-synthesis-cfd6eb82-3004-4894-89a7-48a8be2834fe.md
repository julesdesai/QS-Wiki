**For a person to know a proposition, the belief must not be easily false**, **A belief is safe if in nearby possible worlds where the person forms the belief in the same manner, the belief remains true despite minor perceptual changes**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief is true in a wide range of similar and nearby situations with stable justifications**, **Knowledge remains unaffected by perceptual inaccuracies under minor circumstantial changes**
