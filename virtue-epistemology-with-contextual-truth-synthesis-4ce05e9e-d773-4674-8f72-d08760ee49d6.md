## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true within the context of the current available evidence**, **For a person to know a proposition, their belief must be the result of an exercise of their intellectual virtues**, **An intellectual virtue is a trait that reliably yields true beliefs**, **Truth is considered relative to the context of present knowledge**
