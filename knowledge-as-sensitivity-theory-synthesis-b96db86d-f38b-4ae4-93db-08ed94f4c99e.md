# Knowledge as Sensitivity Theory

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does the potential for mistake invalidate tracking?](does-the-potential-for-mistake-invalidate-tracking-antithesis-799d8d7d-8650-4926-8196-c727e39368ab.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it in nearby possible worlds**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the person believes the proposition, it is true, and the person would not believe it if it were false in nearby possible worlds**
