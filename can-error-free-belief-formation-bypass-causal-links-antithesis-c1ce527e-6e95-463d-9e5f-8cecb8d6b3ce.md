# Can error-free belief formation bypass causal links?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c1ce527e-6e95-463d-9e5f-8cecb8d6b3ce
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Causal theory of knowledge](causal-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-cf478e0f-32fe-4979-ba4a-203ad9c242c7.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person forms beliefs in an error-free manner due to an extraordinary cognitive ability, avoiding common errors without a causal link. Intuitively, these beliefs can still constitute knowledge.**, **Suppose a savant accurately computes large numbers in their mind without external tools. These true beliefs about the sums do not require causal interactions with external mathematical aids.**
