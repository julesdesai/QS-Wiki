# Knowledge as Outcome of Overall Systematic Reliability

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 40566440-20c3-4a6f-afee-18e7bea207c2
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does cognitive malfunction invalidate all knowledge claims?](does-cognitive-malfunction-invalidate-all-knowledge-claims-antithesis-d09a58cb-9082-45c5-8337-5356bc649156.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is an outcome of a globally reliable cognitive system, even if some parts temporarily malfunction**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is an outcome of a globally reliable cognitive system**
