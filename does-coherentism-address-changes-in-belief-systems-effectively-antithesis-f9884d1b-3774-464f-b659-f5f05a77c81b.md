# Does Coherentism address changes in belief systems effectively?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f9884d1b-3774-464f-b659-f5f05a77c81b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism about Knowledge](coherentism-about-knowledge-synthesis-39987724-1a23-4547-a925-9e75bbe8b421.md)

## Content

**Belief systems can be dynamically updated, potentially affecting coherence.**, **In a case where new evidence requires significant changes to a coherent belief system, past coherence seems unhelpful for current justification.**, **The prospect of major revisions challenges the stability and reliability of coherence as a measure of knowledge.**
