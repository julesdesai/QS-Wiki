# Can knowledge be truly independent of any accessible justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 8b9a0026-8de1-4ff9-94d4-a5ba95fc47ee
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilist Externalism Summary](reliabilist-externalism-summary-synthesis-3207924c-3d5f-4b8a-8088-8161a66f3954.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person correctly predicts an earthquake through gut feeling; intuitively, without understanding or providing evidence, this prediction lacks epistemic legitimacy.**, **The absence of accessible justification in such cases creates a situation where the agent knows only accidentally, questioning the sufficiency of reliability for actual knowledge.**
