# Knowledge as True Belief with Causal Connection

**Parent:** [Are knowledge claims sensitive to changes in context that challenge justified true belief?](are-knowledge-claims-sensitive-to-changes-in-context-that-challenge-justified-true-belief-antithesis-aff056ef-c516-4872-9475-bc29e3080778.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is a causal connection between the truth of the proposition and the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there is a causal connection between the proposition and the belief**
