# Can foundational beliefs be genuinely independent of further justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ffa3027d-33fe-46c0-92d6-589d307054b5
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundationalism with Infinite Background](foundationalism-with-infinite-background-synthesis-b6351c4d-7dab-447a-b3c7-52e8418919cd.md)

## Content

**A foundational belief is supposed to be justified without referencing other beliefs.**, **The potential need for an infinite background calls into question the independence of foundational beliefs.**, **If foundational beliefs are not independent, they fail to provide a proper basis for justification.**
