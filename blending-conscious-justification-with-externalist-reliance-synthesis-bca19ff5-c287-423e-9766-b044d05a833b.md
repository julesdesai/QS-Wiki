# Blending Conscious Justification with Externalist Reliance

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have access to justifying reasons through conscious reflection**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief is additionally grounded by external factors independent of conscious reflection**
