# Can circularity justify beliefs under coherentism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1561cc70-3319-437e-adde-009a61bb963f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism in Justified Belief](coherentism-in-justified-belief-synthesis-5c04c2a6-2653-47f8-81ab-a9d276d37a24.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person justifies a belief A by belief B, belief B by belief C, and belief C by belief A, forming a circular justification. Intuitively, circular reasoning appears to undermine genuine epistemic justification.**, **It is implausible to claim that circularity alone can justify beliefs, as it doesn't provide new reasons for belief beyond those already assumed.**
