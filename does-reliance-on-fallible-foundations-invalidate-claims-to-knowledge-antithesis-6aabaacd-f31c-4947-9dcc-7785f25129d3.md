# Does reliance on fallible foundations invalidate claims to knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 6aabaacd-f31c-4947-9dcc-7785f25129d3
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Revised Foundationalism with Fallible Foundations](revised-foundationalism-with-fallible-foundations-synthesis-eea8b3fa-1186-4e04-a7da-360421394f6f.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a person holds a true belief that is justified by a foundational belief that could have been false due to its fallible nature. Intuitively, we must question whether such a foundation provides sufficient support for genuine knowledge.**, **It is implausible that a belief based on a fallible foundation can possess the certainty typically associated with knowledge.**
