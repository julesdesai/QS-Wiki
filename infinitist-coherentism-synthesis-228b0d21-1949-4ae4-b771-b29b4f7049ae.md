## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that justification involves an infinite coherent regress of reasons**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is part of an infinite coherent regress of reasons**
