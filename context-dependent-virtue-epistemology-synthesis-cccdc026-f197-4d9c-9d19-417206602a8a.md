## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from context-sensitive intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is not easily dismissed when examined through different professional lenses**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief arises from context-sensitive intellectual virtue, and the belief withstands varied evaluative methods**
