# Does reliability matter in cases of self-knowledge?

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that self-knowledge should be directly accessible rather than inferred from a reliable process.**, **Suppose a person accurately believes in their own emotional state through reliable self-evaluation but cannot articulate or introspectively access that emotion. Intuitively, this suggests a gap in self-knowledge.**
