## Content

**A person knows a proposition if their belief tracks the truth across possible worlds and is produced by a reliable process**, **A person's belief tracks the truth if it is true when the proposition is true**, **A person's belief is false when the proposition is false**, **A reliable belief-forming process is one that generally produces true beliefs**
