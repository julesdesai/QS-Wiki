# What about false beliefs that have an infinite justificatory chain?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7a84ba67-0036-4451-a101-453cfc7a74e3
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitist Epistemology](infinitist-epistemology-synthesis-6da6e9b6-0ddd-4346-9ef8-d27afad7386e.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where a person believes a false proposition with infinite supporting reasons that are incorrect. Intuitively, we would not judge that this person has knowledge.**, **It is plausible that for knowledge to occur, the supporting reasons must themselves be true or reliable, not just infinite.**
