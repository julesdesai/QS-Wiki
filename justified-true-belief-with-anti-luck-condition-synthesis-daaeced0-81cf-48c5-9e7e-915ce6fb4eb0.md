# Justified True Belief with Anti-Luck Condition

**Parent:** [Does the Safety Condition allow for knowledge in lotteries?](does-the-safety-condition-allow-for-knowledge-in-lotteries-antithesis-7731035f-d378-4880-97f8-62863f5a8a0d.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for their belief to be justified**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief to not be true merely due to luck**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is justified, and the belief is not true merely due to luck**
