# Is all testimonial knowledge independent of additional evidence or verification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5adea618-4e88-4990-8d4a-ce2a6a996ae2
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Testimonial Justification Theory](testimonial-justification-theory-synthesis-a0572f9e-ea0a-45f6-9bf1-a16e71038692.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which an individual relies solely on another's testimony for knowledge without seeking additional evidence. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that their confidence in the knowledge is unwarranted without independent verification.**, **There is a plausible principle that knowledge often requires corroboration or supporting evidence beyond initial claims. This principle suggests that testimony alone might not suffice for justified knowledge in every case.**
