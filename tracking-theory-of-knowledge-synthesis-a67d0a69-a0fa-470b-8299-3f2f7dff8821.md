# Tracking Theory of Knowledge

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does sensitivity account for perceptual knowledge?](does-sensitivity-account-for-perceptual-knowledge-antithesis-aa27d832-f0ee-4174-a0b7-c75bc1a70407.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief tracks the truth across close possible worlds where the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief tracks the truth in close possible worlds**
