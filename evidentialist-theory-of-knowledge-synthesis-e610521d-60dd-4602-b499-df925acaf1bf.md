## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person’s belief is supported by adequate evidence**, **Adequate evidence is evidence that makes the truth of the belief probable**
