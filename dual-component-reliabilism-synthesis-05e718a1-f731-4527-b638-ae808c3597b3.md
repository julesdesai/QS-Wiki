## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **A belief formed by a reliable cognitive process has a high probability of resulting in a true belief**, **For justification, reliability and some additional cognitive component like second-order reflection are necessary**
