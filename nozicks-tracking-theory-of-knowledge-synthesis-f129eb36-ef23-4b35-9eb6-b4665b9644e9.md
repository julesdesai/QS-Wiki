# Nozick’s Tracking Theory of Knowledge

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does false evidence ever justify true belief?](does-false-evidence-ever-justify-true-belief-antithesis-3a224599-fca2-4fc1-8dfb-5502adfc9093.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief tracks the truth of the proposition across nearby possible worlds**, **A belief tracks truth if it would not be held in the absence of the proposition being true**
