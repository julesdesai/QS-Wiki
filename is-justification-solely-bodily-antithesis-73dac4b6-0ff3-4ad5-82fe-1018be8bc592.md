# Is justification solely bodily?

**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Embodied Cognition with Reliance on Bodily Skills](embodied-cognition-with-reliance-on-bodily-skills-synthesis-e9d28d24-22c1-4ccc-a9a1-a716398534d0.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that all cues for justifying beliefs are bodily, as some justifications rely on abstract reasoning or data not linked to bodily sensation.**, **Consider a scenario where someone justifies their belief in climate change through statistics and logical analysis, rather than physical interaction with climate phenomena. Intuitively, this justification is not mediated by bodily engagement.**
