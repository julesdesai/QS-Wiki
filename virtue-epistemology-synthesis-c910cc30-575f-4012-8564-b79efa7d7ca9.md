## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief arises from an intellectual virtue or competence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, it is true, and their belief arises from an intellectual virtue or competence**
