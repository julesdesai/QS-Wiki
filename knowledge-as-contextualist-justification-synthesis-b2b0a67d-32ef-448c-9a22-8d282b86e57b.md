**Parent:** [Is the concept of intellectual virtue universally applicable?](is-the-concept-of-intellectual-virtue-universally-applicable-antithesis-c0a8c657-4326-4520-b145-6964f1e8fffc.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified according to context-sensitive standards**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is justified according to context-sensitive standards**
