# No Defeaters Theory

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 6e65bf26-2a65-4756-b85e-44b3268f730f
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can an unreliable but accurate belief still be knowledge?](can-an-unreliable-but-accurate-belief-still-be-knowledge-antithesis-db1304fd-1ec8-4399-8162-fdb22fe54626.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there be no defeaters for the belief, meaning no true propositions that would undermine it if known**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there are no defeaters for the belief**
