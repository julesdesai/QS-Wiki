**Parent:** [Does knowledge survive false but generally reliable heuristics?](does-knowledge-survive-false-but-generally-reliable-heuristics-antithesis-4b5f2700-f169-4f29-b28d-9f980f6277e6.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed in a manner responsive to intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is formed in a manner responsive to intellectual virtue**
