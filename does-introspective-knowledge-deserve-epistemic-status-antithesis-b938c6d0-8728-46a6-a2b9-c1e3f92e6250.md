# Does introspective knowledge deserve epistemic status?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b938c6d0-8728-46a6-a2b9-c1e3f92e6250
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Verificationism](verificationism-synthesis-742a7e84-42a2-4d13-8c35-af2e03d93250.md)

## Content

**Introspective knowledge, such as knowing you are in pain, cannot be empirically verified by others but is intuitively seen as true and meaningful**, **If knowledge requires empirical testing through perception, then introspective knowledge would not constitute knowledge**, **It is implausible to exclude introspective knowledge as it forms a fundamental aspect of personal awareness**
