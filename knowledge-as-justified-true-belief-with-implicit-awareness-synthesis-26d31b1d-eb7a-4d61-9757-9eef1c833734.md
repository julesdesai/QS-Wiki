# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with Implicit Awareness

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, implicit awareness of justification is sufficient for knowledge**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification, and the proposition is true**
