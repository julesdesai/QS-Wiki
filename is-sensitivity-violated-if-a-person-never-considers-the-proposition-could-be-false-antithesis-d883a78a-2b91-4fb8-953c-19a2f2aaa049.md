# Is sensitivity violated if a person never considers the proposition could be false?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d883a78a-2b91-4fb8-953c-19a2f2aaa049
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Sensitivity Theory](sensitivity-theory-synthesis-68f75ad1-a4e1-40d8-ae80-33e70d924463.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a person is so strongly convinced by authority that they never entertain the possibility that the proposition they believe could be false. Intuitively, we would have to say they lack true knowledge due to dogmatic belief, not sensitivity to falsity.**
