# Knowledge as Foundational Empiricism with Coherence

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Is coherence with false beliefs still sufficient for knowledge?](is-coherence-with-false-beliefs-still-sufficient-for-knowledge-antithesis-95fad0fd-bf61-44f3-8587-001e84b25e57.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres with a system of interconnected beliefs grounded in empirical observation**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are foundational beliefs based on empirical evidence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief coheres with a system of interconnected beliefs grounded in empirical observation, and there are foundational beliefs based on empirical evidence**
