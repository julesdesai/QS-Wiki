## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the explanation contrasts appropriately with relevant alternatives**, **Knowledge is understood as a relation between a person, a proposition, and a set of contrasting propositions**
