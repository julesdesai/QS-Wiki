# How does disagreement among experts challenge intellectual virtue?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 38e8746d-56a2-41c7-a81f-6a23b8c6869c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-43f1bd52-38ff-4c3b-a3d7-408481a71c52.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where two equally qualified climate scientists with access to the same data disagree about future climate patterns, each believing their conclusion through a virtuous process. Each scientist's process fulfills conditions of intellectual virtue, yet both cannot possess knowledge if one belief is false.**, **When intellectual virtue leads to conflicting truths, it raises questions about its role in reliably connecting beliefs with truth.**
