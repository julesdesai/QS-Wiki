## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified by social processes like testimony or peer consensus**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is true and justified through collective social endorsement**
