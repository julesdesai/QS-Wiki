# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with Epistemic Virtues

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Does pragmatic utility conflict with epistemic responsibility?](does-pragmatic-utility-conflict-with-epistemic-responsibility-antithesis-dc313be8-9820-4c45-9737-536456f6b6fd.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **Epistemic virtues such as open-mindedness and intellectual honesty reinforce justification but are not necessary components of knowledge**
