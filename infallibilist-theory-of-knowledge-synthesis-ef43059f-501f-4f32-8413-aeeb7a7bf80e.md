# Infallibilist Theory of Knowledge

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge exist without a causal connection?](can-knowledge-exist-without-a-causal-connection-antithesis-d768a0da-ae80-4042-b4e4-64a232ae3d01.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief could not possibly be false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person have certainty about the proposition**
