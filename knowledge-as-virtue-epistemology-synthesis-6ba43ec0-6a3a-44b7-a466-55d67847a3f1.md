# Knowledge as Virtue Epistemology

**Parent:** [Could a socially validated belief system be inherently flawed?](could-a-socially-validated-belief-system-be-inherently-flawed-antithesis-3202aa6b-5ec9-494f-9358-bc9ac4348de5.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is the result of a virtuous intellectual process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is the result of a virtuous intellectual process**
