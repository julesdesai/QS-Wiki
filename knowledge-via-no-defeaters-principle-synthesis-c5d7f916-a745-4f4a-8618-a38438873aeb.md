## Content

**Knowledge requires that there are no defeaters for the person's belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **A person knows a proposition if there are no situations or evidence that would undermine the truth or justification of their belief**
