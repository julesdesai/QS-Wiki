## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is justified by an infinite chain of reasons**, **The justification of a belief takes into account the epistemic context, including environment and background information**
