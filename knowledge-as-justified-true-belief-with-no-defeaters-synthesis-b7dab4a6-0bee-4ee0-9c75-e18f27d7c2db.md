# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with No Defeaters

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** b7dab4a6-0bee-4ee0-9c75-e18f27d7c2db
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can everyone exercise intellectual virtue equally?](can-everyone-exercise-intellectual-virtue-equally-antithesis-fb0a05f7-3770-41d6-8641-98db70e7db82.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no defeaters to their belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, they have justification for believing the proposition, and there are no defeaters to their belief**
