# Can a belief be safe but not stem from an intellectual virtue due to cognitive errors?

**Node ID:** 11e7cc7b-6120-42bb-b01f-fef7e9076e5c

## Content

**A belief is safe if it could not easily have been false.**, **An individual might form a belief through a cognitive error or bias, which does not qualify as an intellectual virtue, even if the belief is safe.**
