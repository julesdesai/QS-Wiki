# Does contextualism collapse into relativism about knowledge?

**Depth:** 4

**Parent:** [Contextualism Summary](contextualism-summary-synthesis-970b1696-c212-4dd0-be78-254d7b0cf7ce.md)

## Content

**Suppose each context establishes its own standards that justify whether a person knows something. Intuitively, if such contextual standards fluctuate enough, it could suggest a relativistic view where no proposition is universally known.**
