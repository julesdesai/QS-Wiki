# Can environmental changes affect the reliability of a cognitive process?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ccb35616-6a53-418a-8d53-55468a38ea3d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilist Theory of Knowledge](reliabilist-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-fec1f444-460d-4835-9da9-5fd5405f3ea1.md)

## Content

**Imagine an individual whose cognitive process is reliable in a quiet room but unreliable in noisy settings. If their belief about a proposition is formed amidst surrounding noise, intuitively, we may doubt it as knowledge.**, **Environmental factors can influence the reliability of cognitive processes, posing challenges to determining when knowledge is present.**
