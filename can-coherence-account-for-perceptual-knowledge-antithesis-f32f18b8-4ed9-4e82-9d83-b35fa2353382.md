# Can coherence account for perceptual knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f32f18b8-4ed9-4e82-9d83-b35fa2353382
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism: System of Beliefs](coherentism-system-of-beliefs-synthesis-446f5d76-49b9-4fbc-a49a-1aaf884652f2.md)

## Content

**Perceptual beliefs often seem justified regardless of their fit within a larger belief system.**, **A person can perceive something and know it without consulting other beliefs.**, **Coherence with a belief system seems unnecessary for perceptual knowledge.**, **Therefore, an account of knowledge emphasizing coherence may fail to account for perceptual knowledge.**
