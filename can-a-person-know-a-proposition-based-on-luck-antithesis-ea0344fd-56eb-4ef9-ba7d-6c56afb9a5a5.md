# Can a person know a proposition based on luck?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ea0344fd-56eb-4ef9-ba7d-6c56afb9a5a5
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Responsibilist Virtue Epistemology](responsibilist-virtue-epistemology-synthesis-d65854c6-8623-4bd4-aed3-f2a4bfae69c9.md)

## Content

**There are cases where a person forms a true belief purely due to luck.**, **In such cases, the person lacks intellectual responsibility for their belief.**, **It seems counterintuitive to claim such a person knows the proposition.**
