# Semantic Cohesion in Knowledge

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is semantically cohesive**, **A belief is semantically cohesive if it would not encounter paradoxes in determining its truth value**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is semantically cohesive**
