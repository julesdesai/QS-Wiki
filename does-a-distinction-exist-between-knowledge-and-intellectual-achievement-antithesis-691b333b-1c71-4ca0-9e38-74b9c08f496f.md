# Does a distinction exist between knowledge and intellectual achievement?

**Node ID:** 691b333b-1c71-4ca0-9e38-74b9c08f496f

**Terminal:** No

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which an individual achieves a true belief through intellectual effort, but the belief turns out to be coincidentally true.**, **Intuitively, we might attribute intellectual achievement without knowledge due to the coincidental nature of the truth.**
