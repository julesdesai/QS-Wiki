## Content

**There is a plausible principle that holds knowledge must remain open to defeasibility under new evidence. This principle, juxtaposed with a static focus on reliability, raises concerns about the dynamism of knowledge.**, **In scenarios where reliability resists acknowledging contrary information, intuitively, the flexibility necessary for knowledge appears compromised.**
