# Can intellectual virtues coexist with false beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** aeae6e2e-041b-4034-8b57-9932771e1f9c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-83c48565-5b77-4330-ac3e-7b5984f59b6e.md)

## Content

**Consider a situation where a person exercises intellectual virtues yet forms a false belief because consistent evidence and sound reasoning pointed to that falsehood. Intuitively, we would say that having intellectual virtues cannot exclude the formation of false beliefs.**, **A plausible principle is that false beliefs are incompatible with knowledge. Thus, intellectual virtues alone are inadequate to account for knowledge if they can lead to false beliefs.**
