## Content

**Knowledge depends on the sensitivity of the belief to truth**, **A belief is sensitive if, in a nearby possible world where the proposition is false, the person does not believe it**, **A person knows a proposition if the proposition is true, the person believes the proposition, and the belief is sensitive**
