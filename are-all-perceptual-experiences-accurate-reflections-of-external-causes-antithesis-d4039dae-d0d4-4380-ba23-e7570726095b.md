# Are all perceptual experiences accurate reflections of external causes?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d4039dae-d0d4-4380-ba23-e7570726095b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Indirect Realism](indirect-realism-synthesis-197f3805-c8e1-415f-87ee-9ac547e71c62.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which an individual sees a mirage of water on a road on a hot day. Intuitively, either the mirage accurately represents external reality, or it does not. If we say the mirage represents reality accurately, then this conflicts with our knowledge that there's no actual water. If we say it does not, then perceptual experiences may not accurately reflect external causes.**
