## Content

**A belief is justified if supported by evidence that increases the probability of the belief being true**, **Knowledge is a true belief that is backed by evidence that makes the belief highly probable**, **The persuasive strength of evidence is judged by its probabilistic support for the proposition**, **A person knows a proposition if they have evidence that warrants the belief and makes it highly probable**
