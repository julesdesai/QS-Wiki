# Is reliabilism threatened by clairvoyance cases?

## Content

**Consider a hypothetical scenario in which an individual, Clair, has the innate and reliable ability to form true beliefs about future events without using any normal inference or evidence.**, **Reliabilism would classify these beliefs as justified knowledge, yet intuitively, we may argue Clair lacks genuine knowledge or proper justification due to lack of understanding.**
