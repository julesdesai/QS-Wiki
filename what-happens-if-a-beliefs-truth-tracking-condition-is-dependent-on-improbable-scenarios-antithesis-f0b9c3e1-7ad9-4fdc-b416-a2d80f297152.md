# What happens if a belief's truth-tracking condition is dependent on improbable scenarios?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f0b9c3e1-7ad9-4fdc-b416-a2d80f297152
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Truth-tracking theory of knowledge](truth-tracking-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-bf2c0c4d-aad3-4dd0-ab9e-ac7c51e27bd7.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where an investor believes a stock will rise based on reliable trends, yet an unexpected global event suddenly devalues it, revealing the belief didn’t track truth in this improbable scenario.**, **It is implausible to reject such an investment belief as knowledge due to a condition based on highly improbable scenarios.**
