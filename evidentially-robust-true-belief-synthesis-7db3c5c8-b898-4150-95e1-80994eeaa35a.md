**Parent:** [Does justification derived from consensus only suffice as knowledge independently?](does-justification-derived-from-consensus-only-suffice-as-knowledge-independently-antithesis-46f3768d-e28d-4cf3-b94c-0a6f73512bfa.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification consists of robust evidence independently of community consensus**
