# Is cross-modal integration always seamless in direct experience?

**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which someone hears a sound on their left but sees a visual stimulus on their right. Intuitively, the individual must choose which modality to trust for accurate knowledge.**, **This scenario indicates that direct experiences from different senses may not automatically integrate into a coherent perception of the environment.**, **Discrepancies in cross-modal perception suggest that integration may not be as natural or direct as proposed.**
