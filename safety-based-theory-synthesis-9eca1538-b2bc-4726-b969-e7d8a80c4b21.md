## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in most nearby possible worlds where the person believes the proposition, the proposition is true**, **The belief does not need to track truth in all hypothetical scenarios**
