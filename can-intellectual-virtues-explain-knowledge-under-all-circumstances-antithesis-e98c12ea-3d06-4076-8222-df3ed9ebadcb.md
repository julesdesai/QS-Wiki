# Can intellectual virtues explain knowledge under all circumstances?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e98c12ea-3d06-4076-8222-df3ed9ebadcb
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue epistemology focusing on intellectual virtues](virtue-epistemology-focusing-on-intellectual-virtues-synthesis-f3a48039-3cf5-452a-9c79-9ac1b05ad9d2.md)

## Content

**Imagine a case where a person forms a true belief based on reliable but completely external mechanisms, such as a computer vendor selling perfectly functioning devices under peculiar circumstances.**, **In this situation, the person knows the device will work yet did not arrive at this belief through intellectual virtue, questioning the universality of virtues in acquiring knowledge.**
