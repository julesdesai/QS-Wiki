# How does indirect realism handle perceptual learning and adaptation?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0c396a7c-c4be-4a99-aebe-66fb7973989c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Indirect Realism with Contextual Perception](indirect-realism-with-contextual-perception-synthesis-77e2d877-90d5-4b41-9238-09aba0491b6a.md)

## Content

**Perceptual learning, such as recognizing new types of faces, occurs without explicit changes in mental representations.**, **Indirect realism posits that knowledge acquisition is mediated by pre-existing mental representations that should guide perception.**, **The adaptability of perception in response to learning challenges the fixed nature of representation implicit in indirect realism.**
