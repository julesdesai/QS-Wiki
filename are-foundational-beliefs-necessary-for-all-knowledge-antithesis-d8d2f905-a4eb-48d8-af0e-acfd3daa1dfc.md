# Are foundational beliefs necessary for all knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d8d2f905-a4eb-48d8-af0e-acfd3daa1dfc
**Depth:** 2
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundationalism](foundationalism-thesis-fd868af7-578e-400a-bb27-265f88ced5ee.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case where people hold beliefs based on empirical evidence; intuitively, we recognize this evidence-based belief might not require reference to foundational beliefs.**, **It is implausible that all knowledge originates from a set of indubitable beliefs.**
