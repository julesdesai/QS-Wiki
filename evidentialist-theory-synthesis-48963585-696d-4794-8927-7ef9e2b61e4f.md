## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a belief to count as knowledge, it is necessary that it is based on adequate evidence**, **Adequate evidence consists of reliable and relevant grounds that support the truth of a belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is based on adequate evidence**
