# Is ignorance of internal justification a problem for reliable belief formation?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** eb3f500e-4dac-4461-a990-2ae3e296b51c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalism With Reliable Belief Formation](externalism-with-reliable-belief-formation-synthesis-2a34c494-e349-4b40-95af-530f8f0219c4.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which an individual forms a belief through a reliable process but lacks internal justification because they are unaware of why the process is reliable. Intuitively, we would think this person does not truly know the proposition.**, **It is implausible that knowledge can be attributed if the person possesses no internal understanding or justification for their belief.**
