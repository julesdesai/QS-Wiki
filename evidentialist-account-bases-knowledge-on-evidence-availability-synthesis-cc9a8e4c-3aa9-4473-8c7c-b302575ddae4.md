# Evidentialist account bases knowledge on evidence availability

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have adequate evidence for believing the proposition**, **Adequate evidence is required regardless of contextual variations**
