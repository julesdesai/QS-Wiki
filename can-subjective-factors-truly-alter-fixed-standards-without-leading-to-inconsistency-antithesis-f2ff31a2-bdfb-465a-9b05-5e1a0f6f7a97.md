# Can subjective factors truly alter fixed standards without leading to inconsistency?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f2ff31a2-bdfb-465a-9b05-5e1a0f6f7a97
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Subject-Sensitive Invariantism Summary](subject-sensitive-invariantism-summary-synthesis-fa9170ce-e58d-4311-9c0f-f4410b4d69a4.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which Bob believes the bridge is safe based on his engineering knowledge, but he feels anxious crossing it. Intuitively, it seems inconsistent to claim his knowledge depends on his anxiety if standards are fixed.**, **It is implausible that subjective factors can alter something that is invariant.**
