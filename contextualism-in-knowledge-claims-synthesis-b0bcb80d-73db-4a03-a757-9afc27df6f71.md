## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **The standards for what counts as 'knowing' a proposition vary with the context in which the knowledge claim is made**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they meet the contextually appropriate standards for believing the proposition and the proposition is true**
