# Is personal bias a problem for idealism?

**Parent:** [Idealism](idealism-synthesis-53ec409c-4a9b-4261-be64-9dda9f92eb94.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person holds a belief that all dogs are friendly due to their past experiences. In such a scenario, intuitively we would not judge this as true knowledge because it ignores contrary evidence.**, **It is implausible that personal biases derived from mental constructs do not affect perceptions and alleged knowledge.**
