# Does the truth condition adequately account for the role of context in knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 11a0bd99-8fbb-45cb-aef1-28dd39e5d2eb
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology Summary](virtue-epistemology-summary-synthesis-ebb76c32-727a-4eba-8f42-21c2ee3c5622.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person holds a belief that is true in one context but false in another, such as the belief that someone is at home. The varying truth relative to context suggests that mere truth may not account for the complexity of knowledge.**
