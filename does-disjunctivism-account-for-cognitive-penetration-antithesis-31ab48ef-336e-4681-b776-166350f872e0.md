# Does disjunctivism account for cognitive penetration?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 31ab48ef-336e-4681-b776-166350f872e0
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Disjunctivism](disjunctivism-synthesis-19882849-157e-4dc6-b31d-66173a7c79c0.md)

## Content

**Suppose an art critic perceives a painting differently than a layperson due to cognitive influences. Intuitively, the experience is shaped by prior knowledge which suggests that the experience is not wholly direct or sufficient for new knowledge.**, **It is implausible that perceptual experiences are isolated from cognitive states, suggesting knowledge may arise from more than immediate perceptual content.**
