# Can inaccessible reliable processes grant knowledge?

## Content

**Consider a situation where a person possesses tacit knowledge via deeply ingrained skills that consistently lead to true beliefs.**, **The person's process is highly reliable but not consciously accessible or articulated by them.**, **This raises the issue of whether accessible justification is truly necessary if reliability alone seems sufficient.**
