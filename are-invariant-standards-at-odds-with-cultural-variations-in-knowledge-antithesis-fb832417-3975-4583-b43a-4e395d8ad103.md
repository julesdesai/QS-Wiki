# Are invariant standards at odds with cultural variations in knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** fb832417-3975-4583-b43a-4e395d8ad103

**Terminal:** No

## Content

**There are cultural differences in what is considered knowing or wise. Intuitively, these reflect genuine epistemic variations, suggesting different standards.**, **A plausible principle is that knowledge norms are culturally mediated, impacting public knowledge practices.**, **If standards are invariant, cultural distinctions should not manifest, which contradicts anthropological findings.**
