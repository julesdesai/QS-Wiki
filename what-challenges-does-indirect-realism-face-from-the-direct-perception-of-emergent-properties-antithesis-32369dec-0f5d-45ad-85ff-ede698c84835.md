# What challenges does indirect realism face from the direct perception of emergent properties?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 32369dec-0f5d-45ad-85ff-ede698c84835
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Indirect Realism](indirect-realism-synthesis-801f6a7d-c909-434a-bdd9-effe8ebd5964.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a person perceives emergent properties, like the holistic experience of watching a movie. Intuitively, this suggests perception does more than just represent individual sensory inputs.**, **There is a plausible principle that emergence creates new realities not reducible to representations of parts. This principle together with emergent properties implies indirect realism might fail to account for complex perceptions.**
