**Parent:** [Is a belief derived from an unreliable witness ever considered knowledge?](is-a-belief-derived-from-an-unreliable-witness-ever-considered-knowledge-antithesis-90103902-42f1-4ffa-bce3-fbd9b715845c.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is an appropriate causal connection between their belief and the fact**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there is an appropriate causal connection between their belief and the fact**
