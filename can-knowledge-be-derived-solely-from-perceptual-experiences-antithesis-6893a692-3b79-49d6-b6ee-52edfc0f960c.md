# Can knowledge be derived solely from perceptual experiences?

**Node ID:** 6893a692-3b79-49d6-b6ee-52edfc0f960c

**Parent:** [Phenomenalism Summary](phenomenalism-summary-synthesis-ab29b70f-4a42-463b-b303-b38517d8aa22.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person derives a mathematical truth, like 2+2=4, without any particular perceptual experience. Intuitively, we would judge that the person knows this proposition even though it is not derived from perceptual experience.**
