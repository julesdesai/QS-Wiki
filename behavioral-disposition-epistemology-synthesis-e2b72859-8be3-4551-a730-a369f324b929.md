## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have a disposition to act in truth-conducive ways**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their disposition is systematically linked to successful outcomes**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their behavioral tendencies align with true states of affairs and lead to positive adaptations**
