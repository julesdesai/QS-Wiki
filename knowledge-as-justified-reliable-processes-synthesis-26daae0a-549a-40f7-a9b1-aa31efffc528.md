# Knowledge as Justified Reliable Processes

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is reliability without awareness sufficient for knowledge?](is-reliability-without-awareness-sufficient-for-knowledge-antithesis-e7f1fc86-af1a-44e7-98ef-018fa7653f03.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have justification and the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **A reliable cognitive process is one that produces a high proportion of true beliefs over false ones**
