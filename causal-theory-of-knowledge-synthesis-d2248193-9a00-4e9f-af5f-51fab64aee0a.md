# Causal theory of knowledge

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is a causal connection between the belief and the truth of the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there is a causal connection between the belief and the truth of the proposition**
