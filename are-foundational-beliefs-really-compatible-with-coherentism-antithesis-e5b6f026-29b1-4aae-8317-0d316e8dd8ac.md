# Are foundational beliefs really compatible with coherentism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e5b6f026-29b1-4aae-8317-0d316e8dd8ac
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentist Foundationalism](coherentist-foundationalism-synthesis-815fa62f-5e40-4962-8349-ea400fc3f5ce.md)

## Content

**Suppose a foundational belief needs to rest on coherence with other beliefs to be justified. If that's the case, it is not purely foundational since it relies on coherence.**, **There is a plausible principle that a foundational belief, by definition, should not require justification from other beliefs to maintain foundational status.**
