**Parent:** [Can someone know a proposition if there are contexts where their belief does not hold?](can-someone-know-a-proposition-if-there-are-contexts-where-their-belief-does-not-hold-antithesis-916d7694-0543-452d-a3ae-1c120b9c82cd.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief be justified in a context-independent manner**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is context-independently justified**
