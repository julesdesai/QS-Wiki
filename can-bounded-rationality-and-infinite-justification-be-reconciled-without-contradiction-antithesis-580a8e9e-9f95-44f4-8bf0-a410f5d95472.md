# Can bounded rationality and infinite justification be reconciled without contradiction?

**Node ID:** 580a8e9e-9f95-44f4-8bf0-a410f5d95472

## Content

**Bounded rationality posits that individuals have limited cognitive resources and rely on simplified strategies.**, **Infinite justification demands endlessly extending reasoning chains that theoretically exceed cognitive capacities.**, **Reconciliation efforts would need to address the contradiction between infinite theoretical demands and finite practical capacities.**
