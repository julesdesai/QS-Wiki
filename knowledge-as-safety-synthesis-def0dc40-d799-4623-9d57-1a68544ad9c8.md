## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in nearby possible worlds, if they believe the proposition, it is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the person believes the proposition, the proposition is true, they have justification for the belief, and if they believe it in similar situations, it is true**
