## Content

**There is a plausible principle that traits counted as intellectual virtues should consistently lead to epistemic goods like truth. However, identifying such traits precisely can be difficult and contentious.**, **Suppose we have a dispute about whether curiosity or open-mindedness qualifies as an intellectual virtue. Intuitively, this dispute reveals challenges in assessing which virtues reliably produce knowledge.**
