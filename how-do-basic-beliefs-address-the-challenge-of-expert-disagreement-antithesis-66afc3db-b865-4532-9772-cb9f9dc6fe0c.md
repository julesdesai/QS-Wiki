# How do basic beliefs address the challenge of expert disagreement?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 66afc3db-b865-4532-9772-cb9f9dc6fe0c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundationalism in Epistemology](foundationalism-in-epistemology-synthesis-4c27bb26-6321-4022-8e66-a85ea5d939d9.md)

## Content

**In many domains, expert disagreement occurs despite access to the same evidence.**, **If experts disagree, then the evidence supporting basic beliefs might not be self-justifying.**, **Basic beliefs formed on contested evidence cannot be universally foundational.**
