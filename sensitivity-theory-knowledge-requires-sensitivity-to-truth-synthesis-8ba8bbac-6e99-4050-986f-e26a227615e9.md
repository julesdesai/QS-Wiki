# Sensitivity Theory: Knowledge requires sensitivity to truth

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can the causal theory handle Gettier-style cases effectively?](can-the-causal-theory-handle-gettier-style-cases-effectively-antithesis-032b6ca3-d66a-4fd4-8b43-5aa9931f4133.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **A belief is sensitive if, in the nearest possible world where the proposition is false, the belief is not held**
