# Does reliability accommodate the subjective aspect of justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b90ef729-64f3-40ed-a432-d51aceaa4a00
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism](reliabilism-synthesis-f6f2f982-fa0f-404e-a509-24417604aa1e.md)

## Content

**Consider a person who feels confident in an intuitive but unreliable process that coincidentally produces a true belief.**, **Reliability fails to address the subjective feeling of justification, which can conflict with the process's objective reliability.**
