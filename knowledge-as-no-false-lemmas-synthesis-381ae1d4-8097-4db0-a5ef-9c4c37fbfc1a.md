# Knowledge as No False Lemmas

**Parent:** [Can practical considerations obscure the truth condition for knowledge?](can-practical-considerations-obscure-the-truth-condition-for-knowledge-antithesis-64e4e818-ac63-451e-9f21-67af4e1d5b87.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no false lemmas or intermediary false propositions leading to the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there are no false lemmas leading to the belief**
