# Can a false belief based on virtues be considered knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1c7517f7-b516-4e56-99f9-7a708d52760f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Knowledge as Virtuous True Belief](knowledge-as-virtuous-true-belief-synthesis-0cc7c39c-3c22-4b7d-8311-bdde6f1e8698.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a person employing all proper intellectual virtues to arrive at a belief. If this belief turns out to be false due to misleading evidence, it seems counterintuitive to label their rigorous belief as knowledge.**, **Even if a belief is the product of intellectual virtues, if the belief is false, it cannot be considered knowledge.**
