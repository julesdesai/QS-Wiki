## Content

**Knowledge is not dependent on context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **A person knows a proposition if the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **Justification involves external factors beyond the mental state of the knower**
