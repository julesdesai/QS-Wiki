# Is practical reasoning undermined by infinitism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7176c165-5cae-49ed-8801-273804527d19
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundational Infinitism](foundational-infinitism-synthesis-8fb4ffc2-abd6-406c-b0e9-89dab8b3bfd6.md)

## Content

**Practical reasoning typically requires reaching conclusions or decisions efficiently, while infinitely regressing justifications stall this process entirely.**, **Reliance on infinite chains makes decision-making, which is often time-sensitive, impossible to execute effectively.**, **Finite beings with limited time cannot reason through infinite regress, rendering infinitism impractical for real-world reasoning.**
