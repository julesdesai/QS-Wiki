**Parent:** [Is accidental or coincidental alignment with truth enough to violate tracking sufficiency?](is-accidental-or-coincidental-alignment-with-truth-enough-to-violate-tracking-sufficiency-antithesis-570f771f-c97b-4863-b35c-e162c498ff68.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person believes the proposition as a result of a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the cognitive process is not dependent on chance alignment with truth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that these reliability conditions hold**
