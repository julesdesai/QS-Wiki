# Knowledge in a Reliably Changing Process

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** f3739ab8-1f13-4772-aa60-14c2e00239c5
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is antecedent reliability necessary for knowledge?](is-antecedent-reliability-necessary-for-knowledge-antithesis-733bf45c-ae06-4742-a1fa-61fd13a4f39c.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is caused by a process that is becoming increasingly reliable**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is caused by a process that is becoming increasingly reliable**
