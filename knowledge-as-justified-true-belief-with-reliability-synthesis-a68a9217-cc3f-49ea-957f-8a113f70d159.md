## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is justified**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is true, the person believes the proposition, and their belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**
