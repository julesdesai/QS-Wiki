## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they possess a skill or ability underpinned by understanding of the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is true and demonstrated through skillful performance without explicit belief**
