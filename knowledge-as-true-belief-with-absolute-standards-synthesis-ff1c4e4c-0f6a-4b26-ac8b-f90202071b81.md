# Knowledge as True Belief with Absolute Standards

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** ff1c4e4c-0f6a-4b26-ac8b-f90202071b81
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge exist in inconsistent contexts?](can-knowledge-exist-in-inconsistent-contexts-antithesis-654b2a0a-d243-4202-835c-4d41f43a6934.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification for the belief adheres to absolute standards**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification for the belief adheres to absolute standards**
