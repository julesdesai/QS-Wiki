## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, justification involves relative standards that do not affect the objectivity of truth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they meet the standards of justification relevant to their context but maintain the proposition's objective truth**
