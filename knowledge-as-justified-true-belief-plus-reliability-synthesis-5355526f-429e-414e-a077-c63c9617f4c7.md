# Knowledge as Justified True Belief Plus Reliability

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Is ruling out relevant alternatives truly sufficient for knowledge?](is-ruling-out-relevant-alternatives-truly-sufficient-for-knowledge-antithesis-d81c1ce7-7da1-4745-903b-860dd50775c7.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the process by which they arrived at the belief is reliable**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing it, the proposition is true, and the process of arriving at the belief is reliable**
