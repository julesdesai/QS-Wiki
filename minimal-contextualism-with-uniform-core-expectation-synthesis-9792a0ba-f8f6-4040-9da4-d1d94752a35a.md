## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief meets both a core, invariant standard of justification and contextual standards within reason**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief meets both a core invariant and reasonable contextual standards**
