## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they can rule out relevant alternatives**, **An alternative is relevant if it poses a genuine epistemic challenge in the situation**
