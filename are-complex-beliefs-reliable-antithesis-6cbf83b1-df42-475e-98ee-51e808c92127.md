# Are complex beliefs reliable?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 6cbf83b1-df42-475e-98ee-51e808c92127
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliability Theory of Knowledge](reliability-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-971ec002-e8db-455e-806a-7b81feaf9154.md)

## Content

**Consider a belief that is complex and derived from multiple cognitive processes, some of which are reliable and others are not. This complexity makes it unclear whether the belief can be said to be reliably produced.**, **The interaction of reliable and unreliable processes in forming a complex belief raises doubts about whether it fully counts as knowledge.**
