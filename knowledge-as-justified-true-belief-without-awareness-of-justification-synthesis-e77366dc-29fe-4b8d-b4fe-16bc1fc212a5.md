# Knowledge as Justified True Belief without Awareness of Justification

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** e77366dc-29fe-4b8d-b4fe-16bc1fc212a5
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is full awareness possible for all kinds of knowledge?](is-full-awareness-possible-for-all-kinds-of-knowledge-antithesis-f8af264b-c6ab-456c-9f7d-9dac093ce7f1.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficent that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, and the proposition is true**
