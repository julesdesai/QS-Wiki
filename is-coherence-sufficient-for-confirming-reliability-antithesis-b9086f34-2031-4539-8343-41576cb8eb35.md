# Is coherence sufficient for confirming reliability?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b9086f34-2031-4539-8343-41576cb8eb35
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilist Coherentism](reliabilist-coherentism-synthesis-d6c1c642-66ac-40b0-82ca-187cf7250017.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a set of coherent beliefs is derived from a fictional narrative, like a novel. Intuitively, the coherence of the story's beliefs does not confirm their reliability in representing actual truths.**, **It is implausible that coherence alone can suffice to confirm the reliability of a belief-generating system without empirical validation.**
