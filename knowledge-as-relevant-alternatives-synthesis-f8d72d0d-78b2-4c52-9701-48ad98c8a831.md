## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A belief qualifies as knowledge if the believer can rule out relevant alternatives where the belief might be false**, **Relevant alternatives theory considers the effect of environmental variability on belief**
