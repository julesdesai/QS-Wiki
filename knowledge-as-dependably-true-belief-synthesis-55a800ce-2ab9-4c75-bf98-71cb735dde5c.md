## Content

**Knowledge is a true belief that is dependably true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief reliably remains true despite potential changes**
