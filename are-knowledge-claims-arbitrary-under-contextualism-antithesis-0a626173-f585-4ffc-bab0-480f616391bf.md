# Are knowledge claims arbitrary under contextualism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0a626173-f585-4ffc-bab0-480f616391bf
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [The Contextualist View of Knowledge](the-contextualist-view-of-knowledge-synthesis-744b190b-128c-4187-8edf-0a707cd161d1.md)

## Content

**Knowledge claims are impacted by the standards for justification in a given context.**, **With multiple potential contexts, these claims may become arbitrary and lack uniformity.**, **This undermines the consistency needed for clear communication about what people know.**
