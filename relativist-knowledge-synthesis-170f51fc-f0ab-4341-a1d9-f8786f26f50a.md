## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **The truth of the knowledge-claim is relative to the standards accepted by the assessor**, **The assessor's context determines the justification level for knowledge, maintaining consistency within that context**
