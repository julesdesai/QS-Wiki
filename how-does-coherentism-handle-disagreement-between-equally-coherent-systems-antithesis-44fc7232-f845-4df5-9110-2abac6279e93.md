# How does coherentism handle disagreement between equally coherent systems?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 44fc7232-f845-4df5-9110-2abac6279e93
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism: System of Beliefs](coherentism-system-of-beliefs-synthesis-446f5d76-49b9-4fbc-a49a-1aaf884652f2.md)

## Content

**Two observers can have equally coherent but conflicting belief systems.**, **Only one of the conflicting systems, or neither, can correspond to reality.**, **Knowledge requires correspondence with reality, not just coherence.**, **Thus, coherence cannot resolve which system, if any, constitutes knowledge.**
