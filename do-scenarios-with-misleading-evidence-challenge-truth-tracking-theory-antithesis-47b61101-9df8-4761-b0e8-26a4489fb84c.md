# Do scenarios with misleading evidence challenge truth-tracking theory?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 47b61101-9df8-4761-b0e8-26a4489fb84c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Truth-Tracking Theory](truth-tracking-theory-synthesis-44ea7417-270f-4c93-876d-f98b6754dd68.md)

## Content

**In a situation where someone believes a proposition due to misleading evidence, but the proposition is true, they do not necessarily know it.**, **If a belief is formed correctly through reliable evidence, yet misleading evidence is present, this complicates whether the truth is truly tracked.**, **This raises questions about whether tracking a true proposition consistently across scenarios completely accounts for knowledge.**
