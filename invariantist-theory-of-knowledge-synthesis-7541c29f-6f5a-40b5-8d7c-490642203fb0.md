## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **The standards for justification remain constant regardless of the context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have uniform justification, and the proposition is true**
