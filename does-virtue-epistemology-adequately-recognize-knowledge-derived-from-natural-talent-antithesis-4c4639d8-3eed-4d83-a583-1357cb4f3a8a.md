# Does virtue epistemology adequately recognize knowledge derived from natural talent?

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a person with exceptional innate reasoning ability forms a true belief without consciously deploying cognitive virtues. Intuitively, this may be seen as a case of knowledge.**, **Natural talent in cognition might independently achieve true beliefs, distinguishing its methodology from learned or deliberately practiced virtues like thoroughness or perseverance.**
