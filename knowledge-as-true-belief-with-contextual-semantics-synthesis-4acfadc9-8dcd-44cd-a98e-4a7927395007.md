## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is justified by semantic consistency across contexts**, **Contextual semantics requires that the meaning of the proposition is consistent across different situations**
