# Does the model overlook the role of environment?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3643e2a0-af75-4067-a8fa-3c5aee43349e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-cfcf3dee-48b2-4ff8-855e-aba5061f4a51.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person has a character trait that reliably leads to truth, but only because of a supportive environment that continually adjusts misleading information. Intuitively, we might judge that the trait itself is not sufficient for the trait to be virtuous or knowledge to occur.**, **There is evidence that environmental conditions greatly impact the reliability of cognitive processes.**, **It is implausible to regard an intellectual virtue as independent of one's environment, since such independence disregards the contingent nature of reliability tied to surroundings.**
