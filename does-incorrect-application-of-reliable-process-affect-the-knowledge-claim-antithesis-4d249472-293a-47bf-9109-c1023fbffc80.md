# Does incorrect application of reliable process affect the knowledge claim?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 4d249472-293a-47bf-9109-c1023fbffc80
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalism with Reliable Beliefs](externalism-with-reliable-beliefs-synthesis-44f393ff-3a5c-4b93-8788-7ab032b1badd.md)

## Content

**Consider a person who is normally a reliable witness of historical facts and believes a certain historical event occurred on a specific date.**, **This belief was formed using a flawed edition of a history book that contained the right date by a printing mistake.**, **In this case, the belief was true and arrived by a normally reliable process, but intuitively this process was applied incorrectly, and thus it seems the person lacks knowledge.**
