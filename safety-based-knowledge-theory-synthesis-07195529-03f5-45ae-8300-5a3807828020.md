## Content

**Knowledge requires safety**, **A person knows a proposition if, in nearby possible worlds where the belief is held, the belief remains true**, **Safety condition permits knowledge if, in similar circumstances, the belief is not easily mistaken**, **Knowledge does not necessitate tracking every counterfactual to avoid the condition where false beliefs are sometimes involved due to peculiar circumstances**
