# What about the issue of epistemic circularity in reliabilism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 69c476f9-dbb0-468f-b4d8-fc1cc95531dd
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism](reliabilism-synthesis-a405882b-2e95-4de6-9817-4516092396fb.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person believes their process for forming beliefs is reliable because it has consistently produced true beliefs in the past**, **However, to claim a process is reliable because it has been seems to involve circular reasoning, assuming the trustworthiness of the process in its own evaluation**, **Reliabilism may allow this form of evaluation, raising concerns over whether it can robustly support justified belief or knowledge claims**
