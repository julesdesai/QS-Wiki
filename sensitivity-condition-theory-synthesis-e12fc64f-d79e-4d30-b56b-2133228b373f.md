## Content

**For a belief to constitute knowledge, it must be sensitive to truth**, **A belief is sensitive if, if it were false, the subject would not believe it**, **The cognitive faculties must aim at producing true beliefs**, **A belief satisfying the sensitivity condition constitutes knowledge if it is true**
