## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres with the person's system of beliefs**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for there to exist an appropriate external condition making the belief non-accidental**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief coheres with their system of beliefs, and an appropriate external condition exists making the belief non-accidental**
