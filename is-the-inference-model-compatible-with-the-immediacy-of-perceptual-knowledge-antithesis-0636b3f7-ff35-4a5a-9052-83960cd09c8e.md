# Is the inference model compatible with the immediacy of perceptual knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0636b3f7-ff35-4a5a-9052-83960cd09c8e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Indirect Realism Maintained Through Inference](indirect-realism-maintained-through-inference-synthesis-ffd82d7b-be0f-4614-a603-c896b6b1b7df.md)

## Content

**Perceptual knowledge is often characterized as immediate and seamless, directly apprehending external objects.**, **Inferring the existence of external objects necessitates cognitive processing that disrupts the immediacy of this knowledge.**, **The requirement for inference suggests an intervening mechanism that distances immediate perception from genuine knowledge.**
