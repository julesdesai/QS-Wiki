## Content

**The standards for knowing vary according to context**, **In some contexts, knowledge does not require the absence of doubt**, **A person knows a proposition if they believe it**, **A person knows a proposition if the proposition is true**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief does not rely on any false premises**
