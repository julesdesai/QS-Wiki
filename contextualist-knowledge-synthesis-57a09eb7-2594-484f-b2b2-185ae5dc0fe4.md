# Contextualist Knowledge

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, the standards for justification may vary depending on the context in which the claim is evaluated**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that in the particular context, they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and contextually appropriate justification exists**
