# Does practical success imply epistemic success?

## Content

**Imagine a person who succeeds by chance while following a factually incorrect belief. Intuitively, their practical success does not translate into epistemic success because the belief is false.**, **In epistemology, true justified belief is considered as successful knowledge, differentiating from mere practical success.**
