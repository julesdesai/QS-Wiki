## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their cognitive faculties are functioning properly in their appropriate environment**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief arises from cognitive faculties functioning properly in the appropriate environment**
