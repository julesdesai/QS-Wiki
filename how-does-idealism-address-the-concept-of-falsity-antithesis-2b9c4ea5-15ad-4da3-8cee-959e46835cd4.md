# How does idealism address the concept of falsity?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2b9c4ea5-15ad-4da3-8cee-959e46835cd4
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Idealism](idealism-synthesis-6a8ea4b4-8cf6-44ff-98fb-cf08805f781d.md)

## Content

**Suppose someone falsely believes that there is gold in a silver mine based on a misapprehension. Intuitively, for this belief to be false, there must be an objective aspect of reality that contradicts the mental construct.**, **False beliefs indicate the presence of an objective truth to which mental phenomena must correspond, contradicting the view that reality is entirely mental.**
