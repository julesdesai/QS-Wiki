## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person uniquely trusts a belief-supporting mechanism that functions reliably under stable conditions**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is constantly true in an unchanging environment with reliable belief-support structures**
