# Knowledge as Virtue Epistemology

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Does knowledge turn into non-knowledge with context changes?](does-knowledge-turn-into-non-knowledge-with-context-changes-antithesis-f5930031-badb-4a65-9123-417abf6dc3d1.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is the result of intellectual virtue**
