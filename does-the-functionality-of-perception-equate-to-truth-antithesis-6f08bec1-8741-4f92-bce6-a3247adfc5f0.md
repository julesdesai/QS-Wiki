## Content

**Suppose we have a case where a person's perception of a quicksand pit as solid is immediately actionable if they're carrying a weight. Intuitively, believing in functionality does not ensure the truth about the actual danger.**, **A plausible principle suggests that perceived functionality may not amount to factual knowledge.
