# Knowledge as Coherence with Contextual Justification

**Parent:** [Is coherence vulnerable to unjustified beliefs in the system?](is-coherence-vulnerable-to-unjustified-beliefs-in-the-system-antithesis-d215f0ae-33ac-4d9f-b2a7-dbcc9ab7f52b.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres with a system of connected beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification for foundational beliefs is contextually appropriate**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief coheres with a system of connected beliefs, and the justification for foundational beliefs is contextually appropriate**
