# Knowledge as Direct Acquaintance

**Parent:** [Does inferential justification cover all types of knowledge?](does-inferential-justification-cover-all-types-of-knowledge-antithesis-fb2a2ebd-d343-47da-8d93-b6f2b30ff6b9.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they are directly acquainted with the truth of the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and they are directly acquainted with the truth of the proposition**
