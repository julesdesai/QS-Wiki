## Content

**Knowledge can be based on factors external to the subject's consciousness**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is reliably connected to the truth in the external world**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is connected to the truth by an external reliable process**
