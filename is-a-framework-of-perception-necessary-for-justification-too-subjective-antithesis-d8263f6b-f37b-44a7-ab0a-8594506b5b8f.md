# Is a framework of perception necessary for justification too subjective?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d8263f6b-f37b-44a7-ab0a-8594506b5b8f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Critical Realism with Indirect Perception](critical-realism-with-indirect-perception-synthesis-e835ac77-9565-4215-bec3-25c08b025df9.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that justification requires access to objective criteria not heavily influenced by subjective frameworks. This principle, together with the concept that knowledge involves perception justified within a subjective framework, entails that such justification may be overly subjective to support knowledge.**
