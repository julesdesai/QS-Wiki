# Do high practical stakes demand more evidence for knowledge?

## Content

**Suppose two jurors have identical evidence about a defendant’s guilt, but one juror has significant personal risk about the trial's outcome.**, **The idea that increased stakes could force the juror to require more evidence to know, despite identical epistemic situations, seems problematic.**
