# Do we truly experience reality as it is, or do interpretations play a role?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 725351cf-ef55-4d22-aa1f-f08a6b5cd3a6
**Depth:** 2
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Direct Realism](direct-realism-thesis-65da2b06-0c60-4e66-b428-771d2431fe55.md)

## Content

**The brain processes sensory input before we become aware of it, suggesting interpretation precedes perception.**, **Cognitive psychology provides evidence that prior experience and expectations can shape perception.**, **If perceptions were direct, interpretations and processing would not alter the experience of reality.**
