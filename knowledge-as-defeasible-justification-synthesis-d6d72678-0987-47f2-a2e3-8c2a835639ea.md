# Knowledge as Defeasible Justification

**Parent:** [Can consistency in cognitive processes question the sufficiency of reliability?](can-consistency-in-cognitive-processes-question-the-sufficiency-of-reliability-antithesis-2050cb25-3d27-443a-a7df-98047b94cc49.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that no defeaters exist for the justification**, **A defeater is information that would undermine the justification if it were known**
