## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief arises from a reliable cognitive process**, **A reliable cognitive process is one that produces true beliefs in a majority of instances**
