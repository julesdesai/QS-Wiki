# Knowledge as Fallibilism

**Parent:** [Can someone know something even with misleading defeaters?](can-someone-know-something-even-with-misleading-defeaters-antithesis-81061988-546d-4bba-8c23-d1b245fdeeac.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their justification for the belief be reliable, even if fallible**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification is reliable**
