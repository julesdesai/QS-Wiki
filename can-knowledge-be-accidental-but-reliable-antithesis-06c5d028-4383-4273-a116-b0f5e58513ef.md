# Can knowledge be accidental but reliable?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 06c5d028-4383-4273-a116-b0f5e58513ef
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Evidentialist Reliabilism](evidentialist-reliabilism-synthesis-b2fd7651-b8ba-4bbd-aa9b-2c3c304a468b.md)

## Content

**Consider a situation where a person's reliable process of belief formation results in a true belief, but only accidentally so.**, **Intuitively, it appears that accidental truths should not be regarded as knowledge, even if resulting from a reliable process.**
