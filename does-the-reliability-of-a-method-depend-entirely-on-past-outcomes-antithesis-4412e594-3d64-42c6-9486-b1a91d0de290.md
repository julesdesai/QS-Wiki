# Does the reliability of a method depend entirely on past outcomes?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 4412e594-3d64-42c6-9486-b1a91d0de290
**Depth:** 4

**Parent:** [Reliabilist Theory of Knowledge](reliabilist-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-b27c6d4b-ac60-4e95-8268-38e201ff9a4c.md)

## Content
