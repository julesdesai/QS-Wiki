# Is it possible to have knowledge without justified belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e8c2d4e9-a251-42a6-af5d-712279b4b2aa
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Subject-Sensitive Invariantism](subject-sensitive-invariantism-synthesis-ef027452-1300-45f1-9d7c-023fdb6cd119.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case where a person accurately guesses the outcome of a complex scientific experiment without any understanding or evidence. Even though the proposition is true and they believe it, intuitively, we would not consider this as knowledge due to the lack of justification.**
