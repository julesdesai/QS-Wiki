# Knowledge as Virtue-Adjusted Reliabilism

**Parent:** [What happens when intellectual virtue results in false belief?](what-happens-when-intellectual-virtue-results-in-false-belief-antithesis-233de30e-b80a-46a9-aecb-f61fefae72ad.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is the result of a process that is both reliable and exhibits intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is the result of a process that is both reliable and exhibits intellectual virtue**
