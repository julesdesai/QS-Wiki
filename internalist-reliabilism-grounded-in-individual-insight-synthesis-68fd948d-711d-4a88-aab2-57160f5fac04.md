## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is based on the person's own insight or understanding**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have personal insight, and the proposition is true**
