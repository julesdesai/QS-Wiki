# How does Representational Realism address the problem of the infinite regress of representations?

## Content

**If knowledge involves mental representations of external reality, each representation might require another representation to be understood.**, **This leads to a potential infinite regress where each representation depends on another to validate it.**, **An infinite regress challenges the possibility of ever achieving accurate mental representations of reality.**
