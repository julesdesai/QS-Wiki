# Does appealing to infinite background structures lead to skepticism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 13e20586-24ad-418d-9f13-0e5a006dd683
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundationalism with Infinite Background](foundationalism-with-infinite-background-synthesis-b6351c4d-7dab-447a-b3c7-52e8418919cd.md)

## Content

**A belief may require infinite support, which is not accessible to finite agents.**, **If beliefs are not justified without infinite support, skepticism arises as beliefs remain unjustified.**, **Unjustified beliefs do not qualify as knowledge, fostering skepticism about what we know.**
