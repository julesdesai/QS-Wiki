# Does the phenomenon of cognitive penetration impact the notion of immediate perceptual knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 21d3a268-7c8c-4bdc-b98a-30f34086edcc
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Direct Realism](direct-realism-synthesis-2e1488cf-3c3f-4cc1-b449-47fe37a86624.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a person's emotions color their perception, such as fear making someone see ambiguous shadows as threats. Intuitively, we either have to say that perception is influenced by cognition or that it remains purely direct. If we say perception is influenced by cognition, then perception is not entirely immediate or direct.**
