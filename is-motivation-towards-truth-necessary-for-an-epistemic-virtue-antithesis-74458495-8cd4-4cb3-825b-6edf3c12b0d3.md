# Is motivation towards truth necessary for an epistemic virtue?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 74458495-8cd4-4cb3-825b-6edf3c12b0d3
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-91237602-868c-4a49-88c0-4e168ec082b8.md)

## Content

**Individuals might engage in practices leading to truth not out of a desire for truth, but for other reasons like curiosity or habit.**, **These scenarios raise the question of whether motivation towards truth is essential for classifying something as an intellectual virtue.**
