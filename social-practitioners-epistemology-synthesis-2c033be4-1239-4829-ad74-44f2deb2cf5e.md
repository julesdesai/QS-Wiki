## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from socially informed intellectual virtues**, **For a proposition to count as knowledge, it is necessary that intellectual virtues integrate feedback from social contexts**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the intellectual virtues incorporate socially relevant information**
