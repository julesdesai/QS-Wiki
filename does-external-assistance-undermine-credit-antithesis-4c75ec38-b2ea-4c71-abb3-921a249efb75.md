# Does external assistance undermine credit?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 4c75ec38-b2ea-4c71-abb3-921a249efb75
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Credit Theory of Knowledge](credit-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-f65ebe79-7fd3-4c3c-9fb3-cd47f9656422.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which an agent arrives at a true belief with significant help from another person. Intuitively, we either have to say that the agent deserves credit for knowing, or that they do not. If we say they deserve credit, then the role of intellectual virtues is undermined. If we say they do not, then external factors play a major role in attribution.**
