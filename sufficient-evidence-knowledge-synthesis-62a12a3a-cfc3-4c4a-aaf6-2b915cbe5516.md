# Sufficient Evidence Knowledge

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Does ignorance of potential defeaters imply knowledge?](does-ignorance-of-potential-defeaters-imply-knowledge-antithesis-a3c00434-0882-434b-832f-47c2c459bdc1.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they possess sufficient evidence that precludes any defeaters**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and they possess sufficient evidence that precludes any defeaters**
