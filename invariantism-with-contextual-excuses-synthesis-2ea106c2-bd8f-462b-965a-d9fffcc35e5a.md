## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the standards of knowledge are met across all contexts**, **For a justified belief in one context not being knowledge in another, it is excusable due to contextual differences**
