## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they use intellectual virtues leading to true beliefs**, **Intellectual virtues include cognitive skills and abilities exercised in the right context**, **The execution of intellectual virtues is sensitive to context, affecting knowledge claims**
