## Content

**A person knows a proposition if their belief is sensitive to the truth across possible situations**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **Belief sensitivity requires that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**
