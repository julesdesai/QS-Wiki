## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief not only arises from a reliable cognitive process but would not have been held if false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief both arises from a reliable cognitive process and would not have been held if false**
