## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is safe, meaning it could not easily have been false under similar circumstances**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief satisfies a reliable method of determining truth, ensuring safety**
