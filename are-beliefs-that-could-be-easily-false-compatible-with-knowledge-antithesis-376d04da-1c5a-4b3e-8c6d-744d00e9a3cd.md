# Are beliefs that could be easily false compatible with knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 376d04da-1c5a-4b3e-8c6d-744d00e9a3cd
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Sensitivity Theory of Knowledge](sensitivity-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-a0fb85c6-725f-41c5-8b26-cba28f135f18.md)

## Content

**Consider a case in which a person holds a belief that is true but could easily have been false under slightly different circumstances**, **Intuitively, we would say that the belief does not constitute knowledge because it lacks stability due to its sensitivity to changes**
