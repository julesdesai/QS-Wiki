# Knowledge with No Defeaters

**Node ID:** 40337701-7301-4793-85b6-95f7b2aabced
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can environmental factors negate perceptual justification?](can-environmental-factors-negate-perceptual-justification-antithesis-6bd1175a-8dfd-418b-b44e-6cfbd19ed007.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no defeating conditions for their belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and no defeating conditions exist for their belief**
