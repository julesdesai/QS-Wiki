# Relevant Alternatives Theory

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can Deductive Inferences Create Knowledge?](can-deductive-inferences-create-knowledge-antithesis-68d49a8e-08d2-40dd-b76b-bc024341d185.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A person knows a proposition if they can rule out all relevant alternatives where the proposition is false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe it, the proposition is true, and all relevant alternatives are ruled out**
