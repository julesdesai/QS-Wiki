## Content

**A case in which different interpretive contexts drastically alter what counts as a foundational belief illustrates variability. Intuitively, this suggests foundational beliefs might lack genuine security.**, **It is implausible to claim that mere contextual agreement renders foundational beliefs genuinely secure when they can vary so widely.**
