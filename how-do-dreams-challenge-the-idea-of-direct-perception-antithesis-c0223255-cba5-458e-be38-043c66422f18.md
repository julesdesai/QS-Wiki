# How do dreams challenge the idea of direct perception?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c0223255-cba5-458e-be38-043c66422f18
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Direct Realism](direct-realism-synthesis-63f38132-cda9-42dc-9c71-38670cc01e77.md)

## Content

**Dreams provide experiences that appear real but lack corresponding external objects.**, **Intuition suggests that if one is dreaming, there is no direct perception of reality involved.**, **If dreaming can simulate perception without direct access to external reality, then immediate knowledge of external objects might not always be certain.**
