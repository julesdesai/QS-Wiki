# Is external condition alignment sufficient for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 846ab6af-5ca6-49d8-afbf-27116c52c047
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Epistemic Externalism](epistemic-externalism-synthesis-a9c372ef-1ae7-460b-b26c-8a82dc6a1ee1.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a belief that aligns with external conditions for truth, but those conditions are too remote or obscure for the agent to grasp.**, **Intuitively, we either have to say that this is knowledge or that it is not knowledge.**, **If we claim it is knowledge, we ignore the role of an agent being connected to their belief through accessible justification.**, **If we deny it is knowledge, then simply aligning with external truth conditions is insufficient for knowledge.**
