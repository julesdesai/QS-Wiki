## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a belief to constitute knowledge, it must track the truth across nearby possible worlds**, **For a belief to track the truth, if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **For a belief to track the truth, if the proposition were true, the person would believe it**
