## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief results from exercising intellectual virtues**, **Intellectual virtues are qualities like open-mindedness and intellectual courage that reliably lead to true beliefs**
