# Can coherence justify beliefs without considering alternative systems?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a1b14c82-7585-4587-b0a0-5f017defef6c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentist Knowledge Summary](coherentist-knowledge-summary-synthesis-1fa3c944-9677-491a-8edd-ea154ce998c7.md)

## Content

**Suppose we consider a scenario where multiple competing yet coherent systems exist. Intuitively, having multiple coherent systems complicates justification.**, **It is implausible that coherence in one system provides justification without addressing other potential systems. This suggests coherence alone may not offer robust grounds without comparative evaluation.**
