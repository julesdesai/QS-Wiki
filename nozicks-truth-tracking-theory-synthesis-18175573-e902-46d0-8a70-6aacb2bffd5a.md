## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **Knowledge requires the belief to track the truth across close possible worlds**, **A belief tracks the truth if, were the proposition true, the person would believe it, and if the proposition were false, they would not believe it**
