# How does externalism handle radical skepticism regarding external conditions?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a25416ae-d3ed-4161-ae85-d0c8af4d1122
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Epistemic Externalism](epistemic-externalism-synthesis-a9c372ef-1ae7-460b-b26c-8a82dc6a1ee1.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where external conditions themselves are unreliable or unknowable, such as in a skeptical thought experiment.**, **In such a scenario, intuitively, we tend not to regard beliefs as knowledge because the criteria for external truth conditions may not be met.**, **There is a plausible principle that knowledge should withstand radical skepticism, ensuring conditions for truth are verifiable.**
