## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for their disposition toward the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have a knowledge-supportive disposition toward the proposition**, **A person knows a proposition if the proposition is true, they have justification for their disposition, and a knowledge-supportive disposition toward it**
