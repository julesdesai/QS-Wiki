# Can justification be derived solely from coherence without external validation?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 4a5373e8-cc64-4e1f-afa3-6f54593fc755
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism in Justified Belief](coherentism-in-justified-belief-synthesis-5c04c2a6-2653-47f8-81ab-a9d276d37a24.md)

## Content

**Consider a belief system regarding a fictional world meticulously developed over years, with internal logic and coherence. Intuitively, beliefs about this world are not epistemically justified concerning reality, despite their coherence.**, **It is implausible that beliefs can be justified solely through internal coherence without correspondence to an external criterion or reality.**
