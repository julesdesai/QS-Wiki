# Does Infinitism negate foundational beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d068ed58-a8c2-4ed6-a57e-93140cc409a6
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism](infinitism-synthesis-db00e7c1-6a13-4ca5-8f09-6fae7cd29e0f.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a belief that is self-evident, such as "I am conscious." Intuitively, we can claim this belief is justified without recourse to further reasons.**, **It is implausible that foundational beliefs require an infinite chain of reasons, as this would deny the existence of any foundational or self-evident beliefs.**
