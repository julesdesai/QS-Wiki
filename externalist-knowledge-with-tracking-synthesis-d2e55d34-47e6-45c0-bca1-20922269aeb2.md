# Externalist Knowledge with Tracking

**Parent:** [Can a belief be justified if unknowingly defeated?](can-a-belief-be-justified-if-unknowingly-defeated-antithesis-a282a1f0-5a1b-4ae3-bd01-015e9ef354af.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief reliably tracks the truth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A belief reliably tracks the truth if it would not be held as true in scenarios where the proposition is false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, their belief reliably tracks the truth, and the proposition is true**
