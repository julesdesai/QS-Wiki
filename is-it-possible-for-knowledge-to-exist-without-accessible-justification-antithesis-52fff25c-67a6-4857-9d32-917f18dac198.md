# Is it possible for knowledge to exist without accessible justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 52fff25c-67a6-4857-9d32-917f18dac198
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Modified Internalism with External Reliability](modified-internalism-with-external-reliability-synthesis-5d3adb72-ce8d-4976-b1d3-5d6a6f159cf7.md)

## Content

**A situation could arise where a person believes a proposition because an expert told them it is true, but they do not understand the domain at all.**, **In such a situation, the person would still believe the proposition and it could be true.**, **The justification might be reliable because of the expert's credibility, but the individual does not have accessible justification for their belief.**
