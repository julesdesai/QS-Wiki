# Does the reliability of inferential connections from perceptions guarantee knowledge of external objects?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f547ddbe-b4c6-4108-a01e-fcdb572e7c31
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Phenomenalism with Inference](phenomenalism-with-inference-synthesis-5c9b476a-f87e-418f-98f3-b4842f3fdc21.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that reliable inferential connections are necessary for knowledge of external objects. This principle together with the possibility of deceptive environments entails that inferential connections from perceptions may not always guarantee knowledge.**
