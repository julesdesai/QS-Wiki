## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, their justification must meet a high and unwavering standard**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their justification meets this unwavering standard**
