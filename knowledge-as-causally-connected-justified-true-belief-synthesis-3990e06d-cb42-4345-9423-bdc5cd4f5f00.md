## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is causally connected to the fact in a way that bypasses cognitive biases**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there is a causal connection bypassing cognitive biases**
