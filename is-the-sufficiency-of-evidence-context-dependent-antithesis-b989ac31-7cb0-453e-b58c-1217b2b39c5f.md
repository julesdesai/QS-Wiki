# Is the sufficiency of evidence context-dependent?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b989ac31-7cb0-453e-b58c-1217b2b39c5f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Evidentialist Theory of Knowledge](evidentialist-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-d871be87-85c4-4634-87bb-b00a8ec22277.md)

## Content

**Imagine a scenario where minimal evidence leads to a justified belief in a low-stakes situation but not in a high-stakes one. In such scenarios, intuitively, the sufficiency of evidence could vary by context.**, **This highlights the possibility that what counts as adequate evidence might depend on situational factors, questioning its strict necessity.**
