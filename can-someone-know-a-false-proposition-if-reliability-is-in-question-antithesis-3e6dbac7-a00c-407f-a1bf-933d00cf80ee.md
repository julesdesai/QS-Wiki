# Can someone know a false proposition if reliability is in question?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3e6dbac7-a00c-407f-a1bf-933d00cf80ee
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism](reliabilism-synthesis-4db07ebc-4cb3-402b-b335-2b01121c3e1a.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person believes a false proposition because their belief was formed by what is normally a reliable process. Intuitively, one might feel this person knows the proposition since their process is reliable.**, **There is a plausible principle that knowing a false proposition is not possible, regardless of the reliability of the process**
