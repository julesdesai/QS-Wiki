## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the standards for knowing are met in the context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, it is true, and the relevant contextual standards for knowledge are met**
