# Knowledge as No-Defeat Reliabilism

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** b9fc4a8f-3cd0-4362-9c07-f3b616dcc857
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can an unreliable witness ever provide knowledge?](can-an-unreliable-witness-ever-provide-knowledge-antithesis-158850ab-e8d7-4604-b133-2e4965ea0b2a.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no true propositions that, if added to the person's beliefs, would defeat their justification for the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and no true propositions, if added to their beliefs, would defeat their justification for the proposition**
