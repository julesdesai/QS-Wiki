# Can Coherentism solve the problem of empirical grounding?

**Node ID:** be695c2f-a7d8-4c58-8b33-8bdff0abe194

## Content

**A purely coherent system does not inherently provide empirical content, as coherence is an internal relation.**, **In a scenario where a person’s beliefs are coherent but disconnected from empirical evidence, such beliefs seem intuitively unjustified.**, **Without empirical grounding, a coherent system appears incapable of adequately dealing with knowledge claims about the external world.**
