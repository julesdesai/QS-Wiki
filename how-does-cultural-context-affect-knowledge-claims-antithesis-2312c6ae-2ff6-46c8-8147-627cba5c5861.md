# How does cultural context affect knowledge claims?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2312c6ae-2ff6-46c8-8147-627cba5c5861
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualist Coherentism](contextualist-coherentism-synthesis-598dc05f-03d6-4aeb-b0db-fa7173390f96.md)

## Content

**If a group believes a proposition purely because it coheres with their cultural context, yet the proposition is false, their belief appears unjustified.**, **Cultural or social context alone may not justify beliefs or determine truth, hence challenging a context-dependent view of truth and coherence.**
