## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the person were to believe the proposition, then the proposition would not be false**, **The belief in the proposition should be such that in nearby possible worlds where the person believes it, the proposition is true**
