# Is it plausible for justification standards to vary without inconsistency?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9ee865da-c231-4cfb-b0b0-35e0531756d6
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism with Justification](contextualism-with-justification-synthesis-0a61842b-49a8-480a-aa14-aeb970bf43b5.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have an example where justification standards differ in two separate contexts for the same evidence supporting the same proposition. Intuitively, we would expect consistent application of justification standards for the same evidence.**, **It is implausible for justification standards to vary without creating logical inconsistencies across contexts.**
