# How would the view handle discrepancies in contextual perceptions during cooperative tasks?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e57c4ba2-c1f7-4aeb-9c65-734f70eb9848
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextual Direct Realism](contextual-direct-realism-synthesis-9053ea14-9a99-4649-aa95-1bc3b223c980.md)

## Content

**In cooperative tasks, individuals must mesh differing perceptions to complete a common goal.**, **Conflicting perceptions of the same context can lead to disputes about what is known or unknowable.**, **It is implausible for knowledge claims to be context-dependent if multiple valid interpretations arise in collaborative situations.**
