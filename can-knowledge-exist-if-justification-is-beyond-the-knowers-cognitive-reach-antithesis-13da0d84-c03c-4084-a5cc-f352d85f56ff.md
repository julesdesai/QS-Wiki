# Can knowledge exist if justification is beyond the knower’s cognitive reach?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 13da0d84-c03c-4084-a5cc-f352d85f56ff
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Moderate Externalism Summary](moderate-externalism-summary-synthesis-b4668bae-162e-4191-89bc-89b702818630.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that someone could claim to know something if they have no cognitive access whatsoever to the justification process for their belief.**, **A situation where a person's belief is justified externally but inaccessible internally prompts intuitively the judgment that the person lacks knowledge.**
