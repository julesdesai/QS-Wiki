## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, the belief must be the result of exercising intellectual virtue**, **Intellectual virtue is a quality that reliably leads to true beliefs**, **Knowledge does not require absence of false intermediates as long as virtuous traits are exercised**, **The focus is on the character of the believer rather than the justification process**
