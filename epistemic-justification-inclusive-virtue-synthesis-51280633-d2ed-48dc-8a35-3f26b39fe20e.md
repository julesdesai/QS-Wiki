## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for their belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is both the result of an intellectual virtue and is justified**, **An intellectual virtue must include an awareness of one's cognitive biases**
