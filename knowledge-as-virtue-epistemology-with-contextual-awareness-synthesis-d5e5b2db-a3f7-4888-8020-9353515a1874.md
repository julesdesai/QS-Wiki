# Knowledge as Virtue Epistemology with Contextual Awareness

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief to result from the person's exercising their intellectual virtues**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for their intellectual virtues to account for the context of belief formation**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief results from exercising intellectual virtues that consider the context**
