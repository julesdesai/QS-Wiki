# Do all intellectual virtues contribute to knowledge equally?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e935d87f-4df4-4a56-95ef-a9a0e330f693
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue epistemology focusing on intellectual virtues](virtue-epistemology-focusing-on-intellectual-virtues-synthesis-d559d8c1-587e-4ccf-9e44-8233679e40bf.md)

## Content

**Consider the virtues of intellectual curiosity and intellectual humility contributing differently to forming true beliefs. Intuitively, some virtues are more crucial to knowledge.**, **The varying impact of different virtues questions whether all intellectual virtues are equally tied to the concept of knowledge.**
