# Can virtue epistemology adequately explain testimonial knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 85bcba0f-7b1b-4cf0-8b8b-390c5c4f2323
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-db8e4632-83fc-4e14-a2b9-f64cf1d5bb50.md)

## Content

**In a case where an individual gains knowledge from testimony, the reliability of the testifier’s cognitive virtues affects the truth of the information, rather than the listener’s virtues.**, **The requirement of a listener's cognitive virtues seems unnecessary to hold testimonial knowledge, questioning whether knowledge arises from their virtues.**
