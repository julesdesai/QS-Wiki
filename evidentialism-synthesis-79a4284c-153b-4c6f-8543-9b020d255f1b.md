## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have evidence that justifies their belief**, **The belief must be based on evidence strong enough to elevate the belief to likely truth**
