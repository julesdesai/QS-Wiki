# Does justified true belief necessitate infinitely motivated support?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 140f0116-7f09-42b0-93e4-775392d81f63
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism Without Memory Constraints](infinitism-without-memory-constraints-synthesis-df5a4028-0f5d-4102-8a53-7b53596c5281.md)

## Content

**Suppose we consider a belief in a mathematical theorem backed by infinite steps of reasoning not accessible to the believer. Intuitively, the believer lacks knowledge due to inability to fully grasp the justification.**, **Justification that requires infinite motivation may not be humanly accessible, making it impractical as a basis for knowledge.**
