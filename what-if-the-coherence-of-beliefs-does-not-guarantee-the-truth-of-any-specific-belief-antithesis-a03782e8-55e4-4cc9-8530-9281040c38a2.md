# What if the coherence of beliefs does not guarantee the truth of any specific belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a03782e8-55e4-4cc9-8530-9281040c38a2
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Partial Correspondence Coherentism Summary](partial-correspondence-coherentism-summary-synthesis-73282c9a-d730-43f4-a933-7c2ffa7c9bab.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which two people hold perfectly coherent yet contradictory belief systems about a state of affairs.**, **Intuitively, coherence does not help us determine which belief system corresponds to reality.**, **If coherence alone does not differentiate between true and false belief systems, then it cannot serve as a reliable basis for knowledge.**
