# Is contextual coherentism subject to the problem of arbitrariness in context selection?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3eb13e41-0ea5-483c-9545-9c6afe8cfe3e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualist Coherentism](contextualist-coherentism-synthesis-2211d0a5-4c05-4516-984a-e004555aaa45.md)

## Content

**Justification depends on the context in which the belief is held.**, **There can be numerous different contexts for any belief, sometimes with conflicting coherence systems.**, **Without a clear criterion for choosing among contexts, contextual coherentism risks becoming arbitrary.**
