# Is externalism vulnerable to circularity in process reliance?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3d8c73af-38c5-4e9b-9d3c-573806884a61
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalism](externalism-synthesis-5b44df35-6663-4278-91dc-65b3fcbf870c.md)

## Content

**Suppose one verifies that their cognitive processes are reliable by using those same processes. It is implausible to believe that one obtains a justified assurance in this manner.**, **The dependence of process reliability for knowledge can lead to a circular reliance preventing the formation of independently justifiable knowledge.**
