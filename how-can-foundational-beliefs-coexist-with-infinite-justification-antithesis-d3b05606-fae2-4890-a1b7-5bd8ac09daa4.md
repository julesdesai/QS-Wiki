# How can foundational beliefs coexist with infinite justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d3b05606-fae2-4890-a1b7-5bd8ac09daa4
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism with Foundational Beliefs](infinitism-with-foundational-beliefs-synthesis-d8cdd5a2-d709-4e37-9648-d85b66458099.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a foundational belief serves as the basis for further beliefs. Intuitively, a foundational belief should not require further justification.**, **Suppose a belief requires an infinite chain of justification. Intuitively, such a chain contradicts the notion of having a foundational belief that does not require justification.**
