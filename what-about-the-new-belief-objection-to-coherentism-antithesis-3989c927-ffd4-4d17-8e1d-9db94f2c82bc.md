# What about the new belief objection to coherentism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3989c927-ffd4-4d17-8e1d-9db94f2c82bc
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism in Epistemology](coherentism-in-epistemology-synthesis-03124e37-be18-4868-b2cf-e370b5186105.md)

## Content

**When a new belief is introduced, it might initially lack coherence due to not being integrated into the existing web of beliefs.**, **Consider a scientist who encounters unexpected experimental data that challenges their existing theories.**, **Intuitively, the new belief might have epistemic value even if it initially disrupts coherence, suggesting that coherence is not the only path to justification.**
