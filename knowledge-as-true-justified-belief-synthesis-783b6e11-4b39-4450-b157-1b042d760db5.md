# Knowledge as True Justified Belief

**Parent:** [Are there counterexamples where true belief lacks intrinsic knowledge value?](are-there-counterexamples-where-true-belief-lacks-intrinsic-knowledge-value-antithesis-19337da1-49c4-408b-9953-a52518abf5b8.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **True belief is valuable when it is accompanied by justification, providing a grounding for its significance**
