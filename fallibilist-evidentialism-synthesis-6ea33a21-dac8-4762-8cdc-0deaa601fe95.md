# Fallibilist Evidentialism

**Parent:** [Are conditions of belief certainty required for knowledge?](are-conditions-of-belief-certainty-required-for-knowledge-antithesis-3a0fd1ac-4c57-4282-9e74-dddc82bf9ecd.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have access to evidence supporting the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is not required that their belief in the proposition involves full certainty based on the evidence**
