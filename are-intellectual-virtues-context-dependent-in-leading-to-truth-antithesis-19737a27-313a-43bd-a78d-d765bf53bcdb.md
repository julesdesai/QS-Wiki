# Are intellectual virtues context-dependent in leading to truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 19737a27-313a-43bd-a78d-d765bf53bcdb
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-f60d76d9-bfb9-483c-b262-fa52975172be.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where the cultural or social context pressures an individual to form beliefs that align with majority views despite conflicting evidence.**, **If one's intellectual virtues conform to cultural pressures rather than objective truth-seeking, it indicates that intellectual virtues might be context-dependent in their effectiveness.**
