# Contextualist Alternative to Foundationalism

**Parent:** [Can inconsistent beliefs still lead to knowledge?](can-inconsistent-beliefs-still-lead-to-knowledge-antithesis-18758a0a-7370-4ff1-b7a6-0a9a5d47116f.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified according to the standards of the context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is justified according to the standards of the context**
