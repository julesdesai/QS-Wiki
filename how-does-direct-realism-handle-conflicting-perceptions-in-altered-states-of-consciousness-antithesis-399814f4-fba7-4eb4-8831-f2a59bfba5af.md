# How does direct realism handle conflicting perceptions in altered states of consciousness?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 399814f4-fba7-4eb4-8831-f2a59bfba5af
**Depth:** 2
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Direct Realism](direct-realism-thesis-65da2b06-0c60-4e66-b428-771d2431fe55.md)

## Content

**Under the influence of drugs, individuals perceive the world differently, questioning the directness of perception.**, **Altered states can radically alter apparent reality, suggesting a divergence from direct perception.**, **If perception were truly direct, such external modifications should not produce highly subjective experiences.**
