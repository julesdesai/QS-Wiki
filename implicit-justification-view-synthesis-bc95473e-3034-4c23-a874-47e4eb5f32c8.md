# Implicit Justification View

**Parent:** [Is it possible to have justified true beliefs without active awareness?](is-it-possible-to-have-justified-true-beliefs-without-active-awareness-antithesis-90142569-8f4a-4da0-b10e-efb82cad6de6.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief be implicitly justified by an underlying cognitive mechanism**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that this underlying mechanism reliably produces true beliefs without active conscious awareness**
