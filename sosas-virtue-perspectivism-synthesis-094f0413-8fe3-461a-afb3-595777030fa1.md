## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief manifests intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is apt, meaning it is accurate and adroit**, **For a person to have knowledge, it is sufficient that their belief is apt and perspectivally justified, accounting for reflective endorsement**
