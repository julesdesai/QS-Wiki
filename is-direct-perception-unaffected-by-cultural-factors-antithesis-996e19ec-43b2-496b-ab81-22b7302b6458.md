# Is direct perception unaffected by cultural factors?

**Node ID:** 996e19ec-43b2-496b-ab81-22b7302b6458

**Parent:** [Direct Realism](direct-realism-synthesis-dd7d3552-f5e8-48f4-9a89-f17b9eced87a.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that subjective experiences are independent elements that do not alter direct perception of reality because cultural background informs perceptual interpretation.**, **Consider a scenario in which individuals from different cultures perceive facial expressions in ways influenced by cultural norms. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that cultural factors mediate perception.**
