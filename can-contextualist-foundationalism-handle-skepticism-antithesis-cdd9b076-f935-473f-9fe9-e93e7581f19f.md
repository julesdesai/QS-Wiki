# Can contextualist foundationalism handle skepticism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** cdd9b076-f935-473f-9fe9-e93e7581f19f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualist Foundationalism](contextualist-foundationalism-synthesis-b757e054-5c50-4073-a4ee-c1ee757cbdbb.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person doubts the reliability of memory in context. Intuitively, knowledge claims about past events become difficult to support without solid foundational beliefs.**, **A person knows a proposition if the context supports the belief; however, this doesn't guard against skeptical challenges undermining the reliability of foundational beliefs.**, **Foundational beliefs can vary with different contexts and evidence; skepticism can exploit this variance to destabilize knowledge claims.**
