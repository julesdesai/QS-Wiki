## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have good justification for accepting the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, cognitive integration into one's web of beliefs is sufficient without specific belief**, **A person knows a proposition if it is true, justified, and integrated into cognitive framework**
