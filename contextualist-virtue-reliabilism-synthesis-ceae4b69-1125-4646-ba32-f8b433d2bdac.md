## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is the result of a cognitive process shaped by intellectual virtues**, **An intellectual virtue is reliable in a context if it tends to produce true beliefs in that context**
