# Can a belief be virtuous and still lack truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7b19f2f4-7e3e-4f40-a553-dad7617a4cf8
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

## Content

**Suppose someone is virtuous in forming a belief, exercising open-mindedness and careful reasoning, but the belief turns out false. Intuitively, this does not appear as knowledge.**, **A virtuous method alone cannot convert a belief into knowledge without the element of truth.**
