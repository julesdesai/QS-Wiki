## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that, in all nearby possible worlds, any method consistently leads to the true belief**, **Comprehensive safety covers all conceivable reliable methods**
