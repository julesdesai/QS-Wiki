# Can infinitism avoid circularity while providing endless justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 381584fb-6283-429c-b8e0-da067e58fdb9
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism View](infinitism-view-synthesis-bddda30f-213c-4b95-bb22-45afdb1e886a.md)

## Content

**Circular reasoning involves justifying a belief by itself or by another belief that depends on it.**, **An infinite chain that avoids circularity never concludes nor grounds its foundational justification.**, **If each step in the chain requires a previous step for its justification, the absence of a conclusive ground might prevent any justified belief.**
