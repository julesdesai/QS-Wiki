# Knowledge as Defeasibility Condition

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** e9b8f4ed-7cde-4285-9bda-c77271657d79
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge exist without a reliable process?](can-knowledge-exist-without-a-reliable-process-antithesis-72735ff6-39e2-45d6-8e79-fcf072f18ff4.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that no defeaters undermine their justification for the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and no defeaters undermine their justification**
