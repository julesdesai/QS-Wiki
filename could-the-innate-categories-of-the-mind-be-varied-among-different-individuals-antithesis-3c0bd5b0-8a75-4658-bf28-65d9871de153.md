# Could the innate categories of the mind be varied among different individuals?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3c0bd5b0-8a75-4658-bf28-65d9871de153
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Transcendental Idealism](transcendental-idealism-synthesis-de14e49b-50e4-409a-b85a-983076811c68.md)

## Content

**It is conceivable that individuals have different innate cognitive structures based on varying biological or environmental factors. Intuitively, if these structures differ, then perceptual experiences structured by them would also differ, challenging the view that perception is universally structured by fixed innate categories.**
