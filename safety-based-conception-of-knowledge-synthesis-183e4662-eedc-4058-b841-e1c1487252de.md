# Safety-Based Conception of Knowledge

## Content

**A person knows a proposition if their belief is safe from error in nearby possible situations**, **Safety requires that in most nearby possible worlds where the person believes the proposition, it is true**, **The proposition being true is a necessary condition for it to be known**, **Knowledge requires that a belief not easily be false if held true**
