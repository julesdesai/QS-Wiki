## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed by an intellectual virtue**, **An intellectual virtue is a trait that reliably leads to true beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their reliable cognitive process is an act of intellectual virtue**
