## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they could not easily have been wrong in similar scenarios**, **A belief is safe if, in most near worlds where the person forms the belief in the same way, it is true**
