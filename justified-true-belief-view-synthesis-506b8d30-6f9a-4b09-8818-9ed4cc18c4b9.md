## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **A person knows a proposition if and only if it is true, they have justification for it, and believe it**
