## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have evidence that justifies the belief in the proposition**, **Evidence sufficient for justification must be truth-conducive and relevant to the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and they have adequate evidence justifying the belief**
