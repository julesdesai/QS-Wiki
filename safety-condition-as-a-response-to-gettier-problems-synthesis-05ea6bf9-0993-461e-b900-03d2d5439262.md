**Parent:** [Do Gettier cases undermine counterfactual dependence?](do-gettier-cases-undermine-counterfactual-dependence-antithesis-c1a7e2e5-e59b-4505-b639-da1542cef951.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief would not easily be false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief would not easily be false**
