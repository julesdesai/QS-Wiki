# Can Infinitism address the regress problem without circularity?

**Node Type:** antithesis

**Depth:** 4

## Content

**The regress problem in epistemology pertains to the requirement of reasons for justified beliefs that could lead to infinite regress.**, **Infinitism proposes infinite chains of justification to avoid foundational or circular reasoning.**, **Whether these infinite chains can genuinely escape being conceptually circular remains contestable.**
