# Is contextualism compatible with scientific knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d3709d27-06fc-45e5-8d05-83cb9ee7ef7b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualist Theory of Knowledge](contextualist-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-56747199-fb08-45b9-8206-960c0717486b.md)

## Content

**Scientific knowledge often relies on stable and replicable standards across different contexts.**, **If the justification standards shift with context, scientific findings might be inconsistently validated.**, **It is problematic for a theory of knowledge to allow scientific discoveries to depend on variable contexts.**
