# Can there be an infinite chain of reasons in practice?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a6db586b-c5a6-43fc-b706-19984ef27dde
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism View](infinitism-view-synthesis-bddda30f-213c-4b95-bb22-45afdb1e886a.md)

## Content

**An infinite series is a series with no end point and extends endlessly.**, **Human cognitive capacities are finite and thus cannot comprehend or produce an infinite series in practice.**, **If justification requires an infinite chain of reasons, then no belief can actually be justified due to human cognitive limitations.**
