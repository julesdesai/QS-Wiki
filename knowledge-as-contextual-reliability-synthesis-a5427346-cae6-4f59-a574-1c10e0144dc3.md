# Knowledge as Contextual Reliability

**Parent:** [How does immediate perceptual knowledge fit with reliability?](how-does-immediate-perceptual-knowledge-fit-with-reliability-antithesis-67bada4f-ada1-462b-bdac-e18709825836.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed by processes that are reliable in contexts similar to the present one**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is formed by processes that are reliable in contexts similar to the present one**
