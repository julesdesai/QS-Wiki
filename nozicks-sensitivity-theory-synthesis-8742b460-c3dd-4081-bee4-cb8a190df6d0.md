**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is sensitive**, **A belief is sensitive if, in the closest possible world wherein the proposition is false, the person does not believe it**
