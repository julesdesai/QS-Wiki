## Content

**Knowledge is the ability to distinguish truth from relevant alternatives**, **A belief is justified if it accounts for all relevant alternatives**, **For a person to know a proposition, it must be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, they must believe it**, **A relevant alternative is one that could realistically be confused with the truth in a given context**, **To know, a person's belief must eliminate all relevant alternatives**
