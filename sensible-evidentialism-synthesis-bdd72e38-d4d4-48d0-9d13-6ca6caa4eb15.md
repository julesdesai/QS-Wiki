## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A belief qualifies as knowledge if it is supported by adequate evidence**, **Adequate evidence consists of reasons or data that make a belief highly likely to be true**.
