# Does sensitivity account for knowledge involving gradual evidence accumulation?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** bd8e9d65-ad68-4216-aa17-de40201cc9ea
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Sensitivity Theory](sensitivity-theory-synthesis-3d534a12-140c-4311-b926-a2f37f38e945.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person gradually gathers evidence about an investment's potential return and eventually believes it will be profitable. This gradual belief formation is sensitive to accumulating evidence.**, **There is a plausible principle that gradual evidence-based beliefs reflecting long-term accumulation might not be fully captured by sensitivity alone, especially regarding complex ventures or predictions.**
