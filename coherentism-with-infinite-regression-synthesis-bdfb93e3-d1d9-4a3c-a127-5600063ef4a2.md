## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified within a coherent system of beliefs**, **For a coherent system of beliefs, it is sufficient that the beliefs are mutually supportive and infinitely regress**
