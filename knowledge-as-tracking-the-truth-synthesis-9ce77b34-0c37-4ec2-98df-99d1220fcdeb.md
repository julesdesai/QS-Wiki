# Knowledge as Tracking the Truth

**Parent:** [Can sensitivity to truth withstand the lottery paradox?](can-sensitivity-to-truth-withstand-the-lottery-paradox-antithesis-5c0da2d4-9662-495f-a1c5-310e3a2e9f5f.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it, and if the proposition were true, the person would believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and they track the truth of the proposition by believing it when true and not believing it when false**
