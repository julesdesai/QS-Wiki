# How does infinitism account for basic beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 95fe11ec-67f9-4524-b327-7840824476f9
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism View](infinitism-view-synthesis-bddda30f-213c-4b95-bb22-45afdb1e886a.md)

## Content

**Basic beliefs are those that do not require justification from other beliefs to be considered justified.**, **An infinite chain of reasons denies the existence of basic beliefs since each belief must be justified by another belief.**, **If no belief can be basic, then all beliefs require infinite justification, which might make forming any basic level of knowledge practically impossible.**
