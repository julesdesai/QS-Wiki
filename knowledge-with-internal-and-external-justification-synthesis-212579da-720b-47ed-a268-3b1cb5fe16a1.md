# Knowledge with Internal and External Justification

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does the exclusion of external factors undermine safe belief?](does-the-exclusion-of-external-factors-undermine-safe-belief-antithesis-f3a14cf6-2289-4baf-b526-579ffedf2eb5.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have internal justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have external justification such as reliability of the source for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have both internal and external justification for believing the proposition, believe the proposition, and the proposition is true**
