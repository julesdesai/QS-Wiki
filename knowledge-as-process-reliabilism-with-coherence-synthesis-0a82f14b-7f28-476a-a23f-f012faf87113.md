## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief arises from a reliable process and fits coherently within their overall belief system**, **A belief is coherent if it is consistent and mutually supportive with other beliefs**
