## Content

**Knowledge involves a causal connection between the truth of a proposition and the belief**, **Causation can involve multiple intermediaries as long as the belief is ultimately rooted in the truth**, **A belief formed through a causal chain from the truth to the believer can still qualify as knowledge even with misleading aspects**, **The direct cause of belief need not be purely factual, provided an indirect connection exists**
