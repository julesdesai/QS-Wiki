## Content

**To know a proposition, a person must believe the proposition**, **To know a proposition, the proposition must be true**, **To know a proposition, a belief must result from a process that is reliable in a specific context and justified by contextual evidence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief results from a reliable and contextually justified process**
