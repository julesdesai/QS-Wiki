## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is the result of a reliable process in that instance**, **A reliable process is defined as one that produces true beliefs in a given context most of the time**
