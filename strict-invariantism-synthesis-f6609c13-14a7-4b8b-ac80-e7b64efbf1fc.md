## Content

**Knowledge is determined by unchanging standards**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **Practical stakes do not influence the standards of knowledge**
