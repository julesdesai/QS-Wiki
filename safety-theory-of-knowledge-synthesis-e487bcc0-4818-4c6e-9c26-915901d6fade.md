## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief be formed safely in the sense that if the belief were false, the person would not hold it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no defeaters present**, **A belief is safe if it is true not just in the actual world but in nearby possible worlds**
