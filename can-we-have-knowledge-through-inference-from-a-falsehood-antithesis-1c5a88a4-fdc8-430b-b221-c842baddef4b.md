# Can we have knowledge through inference from a falsehood?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1c5a88a4-fdc8-430b-b221-c842baddef4b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Nozick’s Truth-Tracking Theory](nozicks-truth-tracking-theory-synthesis-5a62ad60-20de-4329-b52b-21f59b8da1c1.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person holds a true belief as a result of a valid inferential process, but their reasoning involved a false auxiliary premise. Intuitively, one might judge that the person does not possess knowledge because the basis of their belief includes a falsehood, despite satisfying truth-tracking conditions elsewhere.**
