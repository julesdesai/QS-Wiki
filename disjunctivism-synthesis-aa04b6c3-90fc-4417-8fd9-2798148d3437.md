## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is grounded in a direct awareness of the fact**, **Direct awareness allows for an immediate connection between the belief and the truth of the proposition**
