# Can a belief be both coherent and false?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d5d451d7-b4fb-4b6f-8e84-236d2a8c2af7
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherence Theory of Knowledge](coherence-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-c64df5c7-cb02-4465-9b04-2cdc00a6f3a6.md)

## Content

**A belief system can be internally consistent and well-supported, yet still be based on false premises.**, **A belief that the Earth is the center of the universe could logically cohere with a set of other beliefs held in the past, yet this belief is false.**
