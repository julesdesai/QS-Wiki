## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is the result of an epistemic virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is true and a result of epistemic virtue**
