# Sensitivity and Reliability Combination Theory

**Parent:** [Are there situations where belief tracks truth through chance rather than knowledge?](are-there-situations-where-belief-tracks-truth-through-chance-rather-than-knowledge-antithesis-d24661da-aec0-49fc-831d-53c44deca460.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, they would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief was formed through a reliable process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, if the proposition were false, they would not believe it, and the belief was formed through a reliable process**
