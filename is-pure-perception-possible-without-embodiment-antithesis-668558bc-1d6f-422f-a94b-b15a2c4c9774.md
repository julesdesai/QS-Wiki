# Is pure perception possible without embodiment?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 668558bc-1d6f-422f-a94b-b15a2c4c9774
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Embodied Cognition with Sensory Integration](embodied-cognition-with-sensory-integration-synthesis-1b01035b-41e7-4892-81fb-893d75aaae6e.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which someone is engaged in pure mathematical perception, such as visualizing geometric forms in their mind without physical interaction. Intuitively, we ought to say that this is perception without embodiment.**, **It is implausible that all perceptual experiences require physical embodiment.**
