# Knowledge as True Belief with Objective Justification

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** f2174e5e-118a-4c8f-a757-304d9966ef1a
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge exist in inconsistent contexts?](can-knowledge-exist-in-inconsistent-contexts-antithesis-654b2a0a-d243-4202-835c-4d41f43a6934.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification for the belief meets objective standards**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification for the belief meets objective standards**
