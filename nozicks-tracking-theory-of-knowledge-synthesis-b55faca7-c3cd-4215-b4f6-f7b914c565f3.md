# Nozick's Tracking Theory of Knowledge

**Parent:** [Are all knowledgeable beliefs necessarily sensitive to counterfactual scenarios?](are-all-knowledgeable-beliefs-necessarily-sensitive-to-counterfactual-scenarios-antithesis-e3563d1c-93c4-47c4-be85-bb2960868ea8.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were true, the person would believe it**, **This bidirectional tracking binds the belief to the truth**
