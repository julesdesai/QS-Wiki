## Content

**A person knows a proposition if there is an appropriate causal connection between the fact and the belief**, **For a proposition to be known, the causal relationship involves the truth of the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**
