# Causal Theory of Knowledge with Contextual Influence

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Does knowledge reduce simply to belief and truth?](does-knowledge-reduce-simply-to-belief-and-truth-antithesis-ffc74545-c688-472f-92e2-2a7932a67367.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is a causal connection between the belief and the truth of the proposition**, **Contextual factors influence the nature of the required causal connection**
