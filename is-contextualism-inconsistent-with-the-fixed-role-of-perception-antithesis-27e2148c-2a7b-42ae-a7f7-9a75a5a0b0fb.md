# Is contextualism inconsistent with the fixed role of perception?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 27e2148c-2a7b-42ae-a7f7-9a75a5a0b0fb
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism Summary](contextualism-summary-synthesis-970b1696-c212-4dd0-be78-254d7b0cf7ce.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where perceptions lead to different knowledge claims in different contexts based on different standards. If perception as a basis for knowledge is fixed, contextually varying standards should not change what is known through perception itself.**
