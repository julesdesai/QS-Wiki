# Is infinite justification necessary for true belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 32ab07b0-4c17-4181-a943-7871dd759bc9

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherent Infinitism](coherent-infinitism-synthesis-f2c99927-f862-4ee8-8b5b-2fefd2717ee2.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person has a justified true belief based on a finite series of strong reasons. Intuitively, we would judge that such beliefs still count as knowledge without requiring infinite justification.**, **It is implausible that infinite justification is necessary when a robust finite justification suffices for conferring knowledge.**
