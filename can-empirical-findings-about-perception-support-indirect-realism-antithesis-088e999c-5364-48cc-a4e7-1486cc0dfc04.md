# Can empirical findings about perception support indirect realism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 088e999c-5364-48cc-a4e7-1486cc0dfc04
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Selective Direct Realism](selective-direct-realism-synthesis-744f6327-3f79-490f-be88-a0aa209da42b.md)

## Content

**Empirical findings in psychology suggest that what we perceive is heavily mediated by cognitive processes, such as memory and expectation.**, **If perception is mediated by internal cognitive processes, it does not directly relate to unaltered foundational parts of reality.**, **If perception is influenced by internal cognitive states and changes across conditions, it may not provide stable perceptions required for knowledge.**
