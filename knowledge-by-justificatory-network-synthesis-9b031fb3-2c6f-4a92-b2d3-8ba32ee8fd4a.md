## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is part of a network that contains awareness of justification**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justificatory network supports the belief even without immediate awareness**
