## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that justification involves an infinite, coherent set of mutually supportive reasons without a meta-justification**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and justify their belief with an infinite, coherent set of mutually supportive reasons**
