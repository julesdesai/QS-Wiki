# Knowledge as Foundationalism

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge exist without true belief?](can-knowledge-exist-without-true-belief-antithesis-4959cdf0-91dc-4676-9c56-b8c43486051e.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is either basic or derived from basic beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is either basic or derived from basic beliefs**
