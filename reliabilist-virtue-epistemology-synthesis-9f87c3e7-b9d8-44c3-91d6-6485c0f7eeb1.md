## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their true belief arises from a reliable process, which can include intellectual virtues**, **A reliable process is one that consistently yields true beliefs, whether through internal cognitive faculties or through external aids**
