# Does non-reductive physicalism contend with the normativity of knowledge?

**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

## Content

**Knowing entails an ability to assess reasons and evidence, which seems to require more than a physical or mental state.**, **Consider a case where a computer processes data accurately without comprehending the reasons or evidence behind its outputs.**, **If humans' knowledge were like this, it would miss the normative dimension of assessing and weighing reasons.**
