# At what point does future evidence invalidate an initial justified true belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d7208acc-22e4-4479-8f07-2963e60d18a9
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Defeasibility Theory](defeasibility-theory-synthesis-f4ee4d58-3624-4a6a-b28c-5ecaca4ef4ed.md)

## Content

**Consider the following: a person believes a true proposition with solid evidence, but subsequently new evidence arises that contradicts their initial justification.**, **We might intuitively argue that later evidence overrides the truth and justification of the initial belief, challenging the notion of persistent knowledge.**
