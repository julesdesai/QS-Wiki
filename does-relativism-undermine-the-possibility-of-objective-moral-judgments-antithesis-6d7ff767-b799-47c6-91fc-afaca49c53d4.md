# Does relativism undermine the possibility of objective moral judgments?

## Content

**Consider a case in which one cultural framework condones practices considered morally wrong by another framework. Intuitively, we might feel there should be an objective standard to adjudicate between these views.**, **There is a plausible principle that moral judgments require an objective basis to avoid relativism leading to moral paralysis.**
