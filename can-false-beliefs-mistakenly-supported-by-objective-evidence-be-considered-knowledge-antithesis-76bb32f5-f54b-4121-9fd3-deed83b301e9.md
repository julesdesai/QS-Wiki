# Can false beliefs mistakenly supported by objective evidence be considered knowledge?

**Node ID:** 76bb32f5-f54b-4121-9fd3-deed83b301e9

## Content

**A belief could be justified by objective evidence and yet be false, leading to the intuition that it cannot be knowledge.**, **Consider a case where a scientific consensus is based on available evidence but turns out to be incorrect, intuitively we might hesitate to consider such beliefs as knowledge.**
