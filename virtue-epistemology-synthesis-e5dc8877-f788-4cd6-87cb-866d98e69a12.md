## Content

**A person knows a proposition if they believe it**, **A person knows a proposition if the proposition is true**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief results from an exercise of intellectual virtue**, **Intellectual virtue involves having cognitive qualities like open-mindedness, intellectual courage, and intellectual humility**, **For a proposition to be known, it is sufficient that the belief is true and arises from an exercise of intellectual virtue**
