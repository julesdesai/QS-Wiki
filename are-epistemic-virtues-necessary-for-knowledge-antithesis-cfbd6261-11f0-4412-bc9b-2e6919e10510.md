# Are epistemic virtues necessary for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** cfbd6261-11f0-4412-bc9b-2e6919e10510
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-8faa0d3d-af6f-4054-8090-da1e790293ff.md)

## Content

**In practice, people often have knowledge without possessing or applying epistemic virtues.**, **Imagine a scenario where someone accidentally overhears a true and justified claim while not exhibiting any particular epistemic virtue. Intuitively, this can still count as knowledge.**, **The necessity of epistemic virtues for knowledge could be too demanding, excluding many ordinary cases of knowledge.**
