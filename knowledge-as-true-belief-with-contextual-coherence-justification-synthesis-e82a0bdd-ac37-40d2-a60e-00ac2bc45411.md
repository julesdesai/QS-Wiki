## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is justified through coherence within a specific context**, **Coherence among beliefs in context ensures stronger justification than pragmatic usefulness alone**
