**Parent:** [Can reliability be context-dependent, affecting its sufficiency for knowledge?](can-reliability-be-context-dependent-affecting-its-sufficiency-for-knowledge-antithesis-1636ee57-b8ac-4fa3-a1a7-05f8d6034825.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient if they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process appropriate to the context of the belief-forming situation**
