## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, knowledge attributions depend on the context of the speaker and the relevant alternatives considered**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe it, the proposition is true, and the belief meets the standards of the context in which knowledge is attributed**
