# Does conversational context unduly influence knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 16bd4feb-8e06-4c6b-9552-3c9c603c7382
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [The Contextualist View of Knowledge](the-contextualist-view-of-knowledge-synthesis-744b190b-128c-4187-8edf-0a707cd161d1.md)

## Content

**The contextualist view states that conversational context determines the justification needed for knowledge.**, **This implies that knowledge can be influenced by irrelevant conversational factors rather than factual reality.**, **Such influence could undermine the reliability of knowledge claims.**
