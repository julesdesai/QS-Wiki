# Sensitivity-Based Theory of Knowledge

**Parent:** [Can unreliable processes sometimes lead to true beliefs?](can-unreliable-processes-sometimes-lead-to-true-beliefs-antithesis-8541caae-a29c-43f5-a545-1ca18b3ac2e6.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **Knowledge requires that the belief would not be held if the proposition were false**, **A belief is sensitive if in the nearest possible world where the proposition is false, the person does not believe it**
