# Knowledge as True Belief Mediated by Cognitive Integration

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 8dee821a-49c0-4ab7-a5e5-afe92f4c12bb
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can communal support grant knowledge to false propositions?](can-communal-support-grant-knowledge-to-false-propositions-antithesis-9d02b983-d87b-47ef-bf8e-c22e7356799d.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is integrated into a coherent network of beliefs through personal cognitive effort**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is integrated into a coherent network of beliefs through personal cognitive effort**
