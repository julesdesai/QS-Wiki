# Knowledge as Reliably Connected True Belief

**Parent:** [Could net positive epistemic customs override insufficient causal links for knowledge?](could-net-positive-epistemic-customs-override-insufficient-causal-links-for-knowledge-antithesis-c352e95a-a0c0-4e04-b318-c56c762161ec.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is reliably connected to the truth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is reliably connected to the truth**
