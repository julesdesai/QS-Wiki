# Moderate Invariantism with Justification

**Parent:** [Should truth be the sole criterion for knowledge?](should-truth-be-the-sole-criterion-for-knowledge-antithesis-7f11f9e1-3a76-43c2-9f85-fa37a7b52295.md)

## Content

**Knowledge is dependent on stable criteria and not on individual practical interests**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **Practical stakes do not affect the application of knowledge standards**
