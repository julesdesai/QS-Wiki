# Can knowledge exist without awareness of its justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0592fb7a-cc76-4184-b652-0fc10c21e470
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalism with Reliabilism](externalism-with-reliabilism-synthesis-c10e5c74-1dbc-479d-ba5e-469176c5d537.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that an individual can know a proposition without being aware of any justification for that proposition.**, **Consider a scenario in which a person believes a proposition is true due to a lucky guess, and the proposition is true. In such a scenario, intuitively we would not say that the person knows the proposition.**
