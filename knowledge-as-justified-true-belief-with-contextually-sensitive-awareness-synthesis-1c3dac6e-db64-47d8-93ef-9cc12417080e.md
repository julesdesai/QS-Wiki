# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with Contextually Sensitive Awareness

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 1c3dac6e-db64-47d8-93ef-9cc12417080e
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is memory retention inconsistent with awareness demand?](is-memory-retention-inconsistent-with-awareness-demand-antithesis-61d674f7-88a0-41c6-8b4a-f598c1c98c5a.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, they must be aware of their justification only when the context demands it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, and are aware of their justification when contextually required in conjunction with the proposition being true**
