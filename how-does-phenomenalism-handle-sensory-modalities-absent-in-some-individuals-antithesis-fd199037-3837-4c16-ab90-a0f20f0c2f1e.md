# How does phenomenalism handle sensory modalities absent in some individuals?

**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Phenomenalism with Cross-Modal Coherence](phenomenalism-with-cross-modal-coherence-synthesis-83c3e50e-d940-4bde-b547-d7163870772c.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person is congenitally blind and cannot access visual experiences. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that cross-modal perception cannot be based on consistent phenomenal experiences aligning across senses.**, **A person who lacks one or more senses, such as vision, demonstrates a case where perceptual experiences are not coherent across different sensory modalities.**
