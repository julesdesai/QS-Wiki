# Can non-foundational beliefs be justified without foundational support?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** bd32477f-ce3b-4667-a5ec-85c22c75d410
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundationalist Coherentism](foundationalist-coherentism-synthesis-1575945f-cea5-4275-8c31-c7cabab6c71a.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where a belief is justified by its coherence with the system but is not directly supported by a foundational belief. Intuitively, this belief still appears justified.**, **It is implausible that every justified belief must receive support from a foundational belief rather than coherence within the system.**
