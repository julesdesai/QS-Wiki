# Knowledge as Contextualist Justified True Belief

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition in a given context, it is necessary that their justification meets the context-specific standards**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, they have justification, and it meets the context-specific standards**
