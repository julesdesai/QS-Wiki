# Safety Theory of Knowledge

**Depth:** 3
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge result from accidental success?](can-knowledge-result-from-accidental-success-antithesis-ce176e3f-50f1-4e0f-ae9b-22eae21c1c96.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, the belief must not be easily false**, **A belief is safe if in nearby possible worlds where the person forms the belief in the same manner, the belief is not false**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief is true in a wide range of similar and nearby situations**, **Knowledge is immune to being easily false under minor changes in circumstances**
