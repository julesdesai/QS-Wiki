## Content

**The standards for knowing vary, potentially making communication about knowledge unclear or impractical across different contexts.**, **Suppose two individuals from different contexts discuss a fact and one claims knowledge while the other doubts due to differing standards. Intuitively, this potential for misunderstanding poses a challenge for effective communication about knowledge.**
