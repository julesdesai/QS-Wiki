## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed through an intellectual virtue, a reliable cognitive ability**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they exercise an intellectual virtue in believing the proposition, and the proposition is true because of the virtue**
