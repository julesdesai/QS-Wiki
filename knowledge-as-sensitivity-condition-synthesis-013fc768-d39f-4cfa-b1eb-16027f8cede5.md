## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is such that if the proposition were false, they would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the above conditions hold and they believe the truth due to proper sensitivity**
