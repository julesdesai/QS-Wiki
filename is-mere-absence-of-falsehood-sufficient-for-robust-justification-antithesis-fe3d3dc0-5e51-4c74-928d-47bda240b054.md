# Is mere absence of falsehood sufficient for robust justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** fe3d3dc0-5e51-4c74-928d-47bda240b054
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [No-False-Lemma View](no-false-lemma-view-synthesis-d417d5a7-7b9d-4d6b-8a89-c64775b0283f.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person holds a true belief justified mainly by excluding falsehoods yet having minimal engagement with truth. Intuitively, this limited engagement with truth challenges the depth of knowledge claimed.**, **It is implausible that knowledge can be claimed through mere avoidance of falsehood without substantial grounding in deeper truth engagement.**
