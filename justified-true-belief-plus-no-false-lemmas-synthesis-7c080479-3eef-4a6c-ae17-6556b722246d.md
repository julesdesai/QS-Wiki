# Justified True Belief Plus No False Lemmas

**Parent:** [What about cases where a belief is safe but not true?](what-about-cases-where-a-belief-is-safe-but-not-true-antithesis-0da1d1aa-0a1a-44c7-8a75-3c5f544d9c63.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is justified**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is not based on any false assumption**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, their belief is justified, and it is not based on any false assumption**
