# What about scenarios where process reliability is contingent on misleading information?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** aeb014ed-11c1-4458-bb4b-f68a5fa97f13
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism about Knowledge](reliabilism-about-knowledge-synthesis-c84f45b2-357a-4b29-be66-6a09ffb029e2.md)

## Content

**Consider an intelligence analyst forming beliefs through reliable computational models, but those models are based on intentionally flawed data. Intuitively, this undermines the notion of true knowledge**, **It is implausible for cognitive processes to be deemed reliable when their outputs depend on a foundation of misinformation or deceit**
