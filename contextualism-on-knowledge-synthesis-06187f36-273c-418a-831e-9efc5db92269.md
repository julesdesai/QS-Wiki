## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is justified according to the standards of the given context**, **Knowledge standards vary depending on the context in which knowledge is ascribed**
