# Does infinite justification undermine practical decision-making?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** daafc65e-af78-419d-86e6-1c33f694d4d6
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherent Infinitism Summary](coherent-infinitism-summary-synthesis-eca6bdeb-868b-4e1e-9a63-23b9880f21ff.md)

## Content

**Practical decision-making typically relies on finite and manageable sets of reasons to guide action.**, **An infinite network of interrelated reasons might paralyze decision-making as it requires endless analysis.**, **Being unable to conclude which reasons are sufficient complicates or prevents practical decisions.**
