# Can truth-tracking handle Gettier cases?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7bc52486-566a-475c-9fbd-ae2e2be60696
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Nozick's Truth-Tracking Theory](nozicks-truth-tracking-theory-synthesis-1b212c88-0c4c-4dd5-88f4-3ea31a716dd5.md)

## Content

**Consider a case in which a person's belief is justified and true due to coincidental reasons, a Gettier case. In such a case, intuitively, the person lacks knowledge even though they might meet truth-tracking conditions.**, **A plausible principle is that truth-tracking conditions should rule out Gettier cases as knowledge. This principle, together with typical Gettier scenarios, entails tension with using truth-tracking as the sole condition.**
