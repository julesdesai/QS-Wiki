## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief to be justified based on the individual's practical situation**, **The practical situation includes the subject's interests and potential consequences affecting them**
