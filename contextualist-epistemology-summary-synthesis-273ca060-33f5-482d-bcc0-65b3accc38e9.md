## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for what counts as knowledge to vary according to context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition and the proposition is true within the contextually appropriate standards of knowledge**
