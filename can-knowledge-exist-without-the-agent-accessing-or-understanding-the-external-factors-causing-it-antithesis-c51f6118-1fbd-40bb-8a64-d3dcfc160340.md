# Can knowledge exist without the agent accessing or understanding the external factors causing it?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c51f6118-1fbd-40bb-8a64-d3dcfc160340
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Epistemic Externalism](epistemic-externalism-synthesis-a9c372ef-1ae7-460b-b26c-8a82dc6a1ee1.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where an agent holds a true belief due to external conditions they do not comprehend.**, **In this scenario, intuitively, we would judge that the agent lacks knowledge because they do not understand the cause of their belief's truth.**, **It is implausible to claim that an agent possesses knowledge when they cannot access or understand the external factors that make their belief true.**
