# Hybrid Externalism with Anticipative Defeasibility

**Parent:** [Can knowledge withstand future defeaters?](can-knowledge-withstand-future-defeaters-antithesis-abb05472-a192-4ada-a4e4-21b428b7d702.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is based on external conditions anticipated to remain reliable**, **The reliability must withstand foreseeable future evidence or reasons**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is based on external anticipated conditions that withstand future defeaters**
