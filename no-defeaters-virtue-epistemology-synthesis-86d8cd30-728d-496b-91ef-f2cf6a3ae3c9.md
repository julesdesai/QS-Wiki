# No Defeaters Virtue Epistemology

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 86d8cd30-728d-496b-91ef-f2cf6a3ae3c9
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is intellectual virtue always sufficient for knowledge?](is-intellectual-virtue-always-sufficient-for-knowledge-antithesis-25b7674a-ef1a-41ef-86fb-f40e42d9c239.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is a result of intellectual virtue or ability**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, their belief results from intellectual virtue, and there are no defeaters undermining their justification**
