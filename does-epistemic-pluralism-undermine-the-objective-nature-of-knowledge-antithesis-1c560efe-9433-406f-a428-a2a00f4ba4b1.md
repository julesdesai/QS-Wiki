# Does epistemic pluralism undermine the objective nature of knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1c560efe-9433-406f-a428-a2a00f4ba4b1

## Content

**Multiple standards for knowledge suggest that what is known can vary between systems, implying subjectivity.**, **If knowledge is subjective to the epistemic system applied, it may not be universally valid.**, **The idea of objective truth may be challenged if different systems can hold conflicting knowledge claims that are equally valid.**
