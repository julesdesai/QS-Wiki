## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief safely tracks truth**, **A belief safely tracks truth if in nearly identical circumstances the belief would not be false**
