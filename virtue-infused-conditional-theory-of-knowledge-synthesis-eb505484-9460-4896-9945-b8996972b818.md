# Virtue-Infused Conditional Theory of Knowledge

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief would not be held if the proposition were false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that belief formation is guided by intellectual virtues**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the truth of the proposition to create a conditional reliability in belief**
