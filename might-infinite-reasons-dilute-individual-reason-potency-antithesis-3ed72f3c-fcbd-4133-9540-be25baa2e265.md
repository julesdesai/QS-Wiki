# Might infinite reasons dilute individual reason potency?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3ed72f3c-fcbd-4133-9540-be25baa2e265
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherent Infinitism](coherent-infinitism-synthesis-f2c99927-f862-4ee8-8b5b-2fefd2717ee2.md)

## Content

**A plausible principle posits that the longer the chain of reasons, the less potent each reason becomes in justifying the original belief. This principle suggests that the added complexity of infinite reasoning waters down rather than strengthens justification.**
