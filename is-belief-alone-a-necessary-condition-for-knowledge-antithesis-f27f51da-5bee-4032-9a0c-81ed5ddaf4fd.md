# Is belief alone a necessary condition for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f27f51da-5bee-4032-9a0c-81ed5ddaf4fd
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Experiential Foundationalism](experiential-foundationalism-synthesis-fd2d94ab-5595-4b29-a3f8-2962ee0c4e3c.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person has a true belief formed by accident or chance without any real understanding. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that belief alone is not a necessary condition for knowledge.**
