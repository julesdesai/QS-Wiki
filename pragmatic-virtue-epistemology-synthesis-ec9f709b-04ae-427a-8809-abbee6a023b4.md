## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief to be formed through intellectual virtue with practical adequacy**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the virtue is practically adequate**
