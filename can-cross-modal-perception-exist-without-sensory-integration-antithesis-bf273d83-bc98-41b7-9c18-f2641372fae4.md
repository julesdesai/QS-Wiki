# Can cross-modal perception exist without sensory integration?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** bf273d83-bc98-41b7-9c18-f2641372fae4
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Embodied Cognition with Sensory Integration](embodied-cognition-with-sensory-integration-synthesis-1b01035b-41e7-4892-81fb-893d75aaae6e.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which an individual can perceive both an image and a sound simultaneously without integrating them through physical interaction. In such a scenario, intuitively it seems possible for the individual to experience these perceptions separately yet simultaneously.**, **It is implausible that cross-modal perception is solely explained by the body's integration of sensory inputs.**
