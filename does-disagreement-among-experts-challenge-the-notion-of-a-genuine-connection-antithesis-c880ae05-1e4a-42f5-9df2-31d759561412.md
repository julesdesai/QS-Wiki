# Does disagreement among experts challenge the notion of a genuine connection?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c880ae05-1e4a-42f5-9df2-31d759561412
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Disjunctivism](disjunctivism-synthesis-90a3baa8-9102-43d8-b9cc-d77493986c7c.md)

## Content

**Consider two experts who observe the same data and reach different conclusions. Intuitively, this situation challenges our trust in one of them having a genuine connection to reality.**, **When disagreements persist among those with seemingly equal access to facts, it raises doubt about the idea that genuine connections reliably lead to truth.**
