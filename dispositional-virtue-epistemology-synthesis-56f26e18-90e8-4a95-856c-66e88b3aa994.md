## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief in the proposition is formed by exercising a cognitive disposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is true and the belief is formed by exercising a cognitive disposition**
