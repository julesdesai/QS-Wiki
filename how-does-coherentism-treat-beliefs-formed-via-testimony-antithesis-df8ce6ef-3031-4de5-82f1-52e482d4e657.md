## Content

**Consider a case where a person forms a belief based on reliable testimony, which is common in acquiring knowledge. Intuitively, such beliefs seem justified despite not necessarily cohering with one's existing beliefs.**, **A plausible principle suggests that testimonial beliefs can have direct justification, independent of coherence with other beliefs.**
