# Reliabilist Theory of Knowledge

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Does coherence provide genuine justification for knowledge?](does-coherence-provide-genuine-justification-for-knowledge-antithesis-670d7ec4-fda9-4694-9e6d-22f43ecf80e3.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief was produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief was produced by a reliable cognitive process**
