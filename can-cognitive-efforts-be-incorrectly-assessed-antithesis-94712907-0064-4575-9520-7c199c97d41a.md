# Can cognitive efforts be incorrectly assessed?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 94712907-0064-4575-9520-7c199c97d41a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Responsibilist Virtue Epistemology](responsibilist-virtue-epistemology-synthesis-d65854c6-8623-4bd4-aed3-f2a4bfae69c9.md)

## Content

**Assessment of cognitive efforts can be subjective or influenced by biases.**, **A person might appear responsible based on incorrect evaluations of their cognitive efforts.**, **This subjectivity casts doubt on the objectivity of responsible beliefs as knowledge.**
