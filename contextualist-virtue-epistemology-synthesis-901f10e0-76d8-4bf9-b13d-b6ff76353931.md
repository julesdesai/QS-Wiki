## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from intellectual virtue**, **For a proposition to count as knowledge, it is necessary that intellectual virtues are sensitive to the context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient for the belief, truth, and intellectual virtue to be contextually sensitive**
