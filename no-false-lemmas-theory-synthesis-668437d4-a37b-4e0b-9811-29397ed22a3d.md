# No False Lemmas Theory

**Parent:** [Can one have justified true belief without knowing?](can-one-have-justified-true-belief-without-knowing-antithesis-3d43fe4c-f885-419a-9b5c-b9e77c044e5a.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person has justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification does not involve any false lemmas**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification that does not involve false lemmas, and the proposition is true**
