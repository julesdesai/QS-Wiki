# Can infinitism justify basic foundational beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** cb9ed9b0-ffef-4a6f-8cb3-d32885d9011f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Finite Infinitism](finite-infinitism-synthesis-ec56b28f-9687-4f6e-b141-48dc2e075636.md)

## Content

**Suppose there are foundational beliefs that seem self-evident and require no further reasoning, like "I exist". Intuitively, these need no infinite justification.**, **If foundational beliefs provide basic knowledge without potentially infinite reasons, then infinite justification is not always necessary.**, **There is a plausible principle that foundational beliefs stand without requiring infinite reasons, challenging infinite justification requirements.**
