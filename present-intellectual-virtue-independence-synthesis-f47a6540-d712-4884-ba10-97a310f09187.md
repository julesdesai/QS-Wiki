# Present-Intellectual Virtue Independence

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition was acquired through intellectual virtue at some point**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief would not easily have been false in similar situations**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the proposition was acquired through intellectual virtue at some point, and the belief would not easily have been false in similar situations**
