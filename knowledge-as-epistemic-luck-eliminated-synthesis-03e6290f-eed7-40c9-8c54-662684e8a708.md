# Knowledge as Epistemic Luck Eliminated

**Parent:** [Can intellectual virtues lead to false beliefs alongside true beliefs?](can-intellectual-virtues-lead-to-false-beliefs-alongside-true-beliefs-antithesis-9d2c080d-1796-44ff-a074-6b59244016dd.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that epistemic luck be eliminated from the process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and epistemic luck is absent from how they arrive at the belief**
