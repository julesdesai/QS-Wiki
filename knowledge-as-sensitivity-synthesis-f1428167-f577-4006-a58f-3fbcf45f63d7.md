# Knowledge as Sensitivity

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** f1428167-f577-4006-a58f-3fbcf45f63d7
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does sufficient evidence always yield true knowledge?](does-sufficient-evidence-always-yield-true-knowledge-antithesis-1c42cbe3-4108-43dc-a20a-42492f3ec6b9.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is true and if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**
