# Does long-term stability in perception assure knowledge amid environmental changes?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ba26ab13-b623-4335-84d2-8f991d753fae
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Phenomenal Stability View](phenomenal-stability-view-synthesis-fa414e6c-0cf2-4c6a-a8f2-9164813ceb3c.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where someone consistently perceives a mountain that eventually erodes away, yet stable perceptions over time still describe an unchanged mountain. Intuitively, ecological or environmental changes challenge the idea that stability alone can ensure knowledge of physical objects.**, **It is implausible to claim that continual, stable perceptions provide knowledge when external reality has changed incontrovertibly, revealing potential disconnects between perception and knowledge.**
