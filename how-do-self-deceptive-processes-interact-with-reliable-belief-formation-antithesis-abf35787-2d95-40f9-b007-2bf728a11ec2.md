# How do self-deceptive processes interact with reliable belief formation?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** abf35787-2d95-40f9-b007-2bf728a11ec2
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalist View](externalist-view-synthesis-75028f40-4ef7-4feb-a21d-1e6a75d787a6.md)

## Content

**If belief formation processes include self-deceptive mechanisms that occasionally produce true beliefs, one might intuitively doubt the presence of genuine knowledge.**, **Reliability should not include self-deceptive elements, as they compromise the integrity of true knowledge.**
