# Is it possible for all beliefs to be equally dependent on infinite chains?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9566e2da-15e4-4b8b-94d7-8af7414e64f0
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism and Non-Foundational Justification](infinitism-and-non-foundational-justification-synthesis-6eeef37c-3f39-4c5c-91c9-b90fbd483157.md)

## Content

**Consider a hierarchy of beliefs in which some are more central or critical to a person's belief system than others. Intuitively, we would differentiate their justificatory needs.**, **It is implausible that all beliefs equally require infinite chains for justification, rather than allowing some to play more foundational roles.**
