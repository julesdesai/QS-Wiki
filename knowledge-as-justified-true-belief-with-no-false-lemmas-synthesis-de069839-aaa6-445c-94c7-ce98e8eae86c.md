# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with No False Lemmas

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that this justification does not depend on any false premises**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the person believes the proposition, the proposition is true, the person has justification for the belief, and this justification is based on true premises**
