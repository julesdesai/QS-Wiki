## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **Knowledge requires the belief to track the truth across nearby possible worlds**, **If the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**
