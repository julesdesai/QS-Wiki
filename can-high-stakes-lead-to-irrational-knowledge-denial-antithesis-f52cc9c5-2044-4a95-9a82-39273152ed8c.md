# Can high stakes lead to irrational knowledge denial?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f52cc9c5-2044-4a95-9a82-39273152ed8c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Encroachment Summary](pragmatic-encroachment-summary-synthesis-e897bf05-5a21-418d-ae35-b563f51919c6.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person has strong evidence for a true belief but faces significant practical consequences if they are wrong. Intuitively, we might hesitate to attribute knowledge to the person, not because of a lack of evidence, but due to the increased stakes.**, **It is implausible to suggest that external factors unrelated to the truth of a proposition should affect the attribution of knowledge to someone.**
