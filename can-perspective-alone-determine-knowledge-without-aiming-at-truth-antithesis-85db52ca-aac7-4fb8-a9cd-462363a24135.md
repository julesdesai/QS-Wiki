# Can perspective alone determine knowledge without aiming at truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 85db52ca-aac7-4fb8-a9cd-462363a24135
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Relativism about Knowledge](relativism-about-knowledge-synthesis-db569ddc-fb91-431d-962c-b8c6e0bc3b22.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person believes a false proposition from their perspective. Intuitively, one would argue they do not possess knowledge since their belief does not align with truth.**, **If knowledge depends only on the evaluator's perspective, then aiming at truth is not necessary, which seems counterintuitive.**
