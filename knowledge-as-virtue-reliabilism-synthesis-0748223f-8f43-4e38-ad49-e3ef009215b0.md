# Knowledge as Virtue Reliabilism

**Parent:** [Can externalism handle epistemic defeat?](can-externalism-handle-epistemic-defeat-antithesis-0a21df38-8101-4005-b64a-24030966941f.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification includes virtue-reliant processes safeguarding against defeat**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification involves virtue-reliant processes accessible through reliable means**
