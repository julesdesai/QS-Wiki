# How is infinite justification practically achievable?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 249ccf88-8c52-463d-8381-f4105d828ed3
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Finite Infinitism Summary](finite-infinitism-summary-synthesis-cf1727db-af05-4f36-9758-13f464b5dd32.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person has a justified belief. In such a scenario, intuitively, we would judge that the person must be able to cite reasons for their belief.**, **A reasoning process that is potentially infinite requires that one could, in principle, endlessly prolong the process of giving reasons. It seems unfeasible that individuals can afford their time and resources to undergo such potentially infinite reasoning.**
