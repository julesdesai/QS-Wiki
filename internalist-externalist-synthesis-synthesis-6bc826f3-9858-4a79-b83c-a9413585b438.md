## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is produced by a reliable process external to the person's belief system and they have some awareness of this process**, **Belief formation processes are relevant and partial awareness by the individual is required for knowledge**
