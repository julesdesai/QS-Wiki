## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in nearly all nearby possible worlds where their belief is formed in the same way, the belief remains true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, their belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process, and the belief is true in nearly all nearby possible worlds where it is formed in the same way**
