# Can false beliefs ever lead to knowledge through properly functioning faculties?

**Node ID:** 0f772245-840d-4b79-b3c5-885a3fb1acaa
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

## Content

**Suppose there is a case in which a properly functioning cognitive faculty produces a false belief due to misleading information, and yet, through further reflection, the person arrives at a true belief. Intuitively, the latter true belief could still count as knowledge.**
