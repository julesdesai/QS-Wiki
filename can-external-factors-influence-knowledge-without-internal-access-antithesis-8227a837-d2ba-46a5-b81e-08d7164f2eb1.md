# Can external factors influence knowledge without internal access?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 8227a837-d2ba-46a5-b81e-08d7164f2eb1
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Internalist Reliabilism Summary](internalist-reliabilism-summary-synthesis-afd99030-664a-4a62-9431-6f8bc1e36717.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person's belief is true and formed by a reliable process unknown to them.**, **Intuitively, it seems this person could still possess knowledge despite lacking internal access to process reliability.**, **This suggests that external factors might play a role in knowledge without requiring internal awareness.**
