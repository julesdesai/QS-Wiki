## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have a tracking belief-forming process**, **A tracking belief-forming process is one where if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it, and if the proposition were true, the person would believe it across nearby possible worlds**
