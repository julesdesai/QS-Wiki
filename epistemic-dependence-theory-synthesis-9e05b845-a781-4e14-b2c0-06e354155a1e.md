## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief depends on factors sufficiently connected to the truth of the proposition**, **The connection must be sufficiently direct and relevant**
