# Two-Tier Justification Model

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does fallible conscious access undermine justification?](does-fallible-conscious-access-undermine-justification-antithesis-151dc1ea-6068-4984-a488-d76371127df8.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they possess both consciously accessible reasons and a secondary level of non-conscious justification**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that these dual grounds for justification are present**
