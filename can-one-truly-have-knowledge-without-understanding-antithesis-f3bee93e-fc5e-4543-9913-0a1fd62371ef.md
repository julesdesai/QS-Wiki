# Can one truly have knowledge without understanding?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f3bee93e-fc5e-4543-9913-0a1fd62371ef
**Depth:** 2
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalism](externalism-thesis-b63a2d55-95dc-4341-9aea-4bf66dde78a4.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person memorizes a set of facts without understanding them and one fact happens to be true; it seems counterintuitive to say they know that fact**, **Intuitively, understanding appears to be a key component of what it means to have knowledge beyond mere true belief**
