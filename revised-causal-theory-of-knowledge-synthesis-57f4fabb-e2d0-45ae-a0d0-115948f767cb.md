# Revised Causal Theory of Knowledge

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 57f4fabb-e2d0-45ae-a0d0-115948f767cb
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can we know false propositions?](can-we-know-false-propositions-antithesis-1cd3b74c-8ef4-49ae-8c34-7896ecb9bf69.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the causal chain connecting the belief and the fact is operative**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the causal chain connecting the belief and the fact is operative**
