## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if they believe the proposition, then the proposition is not easily false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and if they believe the proposition, then it is not easily false**
