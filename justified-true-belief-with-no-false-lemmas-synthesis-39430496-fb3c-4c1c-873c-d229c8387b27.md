# Justified True Belief with No False Lemmas

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is justified**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief does not rely on any false assumptions**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, their belief is justified, and their belief does not rely on any false assumptions**
