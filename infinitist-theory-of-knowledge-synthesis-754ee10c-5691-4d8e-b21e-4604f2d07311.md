## Content

**A person knows a proposition if they have an infinite chain of non-repeating evidence or reasons**, **Each reason in the chain supports the belief in the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **A person knows a proposition if the proposition is true**
