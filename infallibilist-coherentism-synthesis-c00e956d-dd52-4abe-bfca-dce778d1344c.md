# Infallibilist Coherentism

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres with a consistent and comprehensive system of beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the coherence excludes any possibility of false belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief coheres with their cognitive system, and the coherence excludes any possibility of false belief**
