# Is the subjunctive conditional central to tracking flawed?

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that subjunctive conditionals used in evaluating belief-tracking are prone to ambiguity and misinterpretation. This principle leads to potential misunderstandings about when a belief truly tracks the truth.**, **Consider a scenario in which two subjunctive interpretations lead to different conclusions about a belief's status. Intuitively, reliance on such conditionals seems dubious for securing knowledge.**
