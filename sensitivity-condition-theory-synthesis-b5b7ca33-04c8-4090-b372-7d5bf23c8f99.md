## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person's belief would change**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and if the proposition were false, their belief would change**
