# Does coherence guarantee truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e7724a5d-d7ec-4169-a84a-05e2a2d84b2e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Coherence Theory](virtue-coherence-theory-synthesis-a8b2112a-831e-4091-afd7-72f8c8e37bcf.md)

## Content

**A person has a coherent set of beliefs, but one belief is that their twin brother is alive despite evidence that he died years ago.**, **Intuitively, even if the beliefs are coherent, the false belief about the brother's status means not all of their beliefs are true.**
