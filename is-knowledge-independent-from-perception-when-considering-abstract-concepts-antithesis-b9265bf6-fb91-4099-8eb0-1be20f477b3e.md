# Is knowledge independent from perception when considering abstract concepts?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b9265bf6-fb91-4099-8eb0-1be20f477b3e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Direct Realism with Strong Correspondence](direct-realism-with-strong-correspondence-synthesis-28884d49-4ced-45da-83a0-528139a02f57.md)

## Content

**Abstract concepts like mathematical truths are known without direct perceptual experiences, such as knowing that 2+2=4.**, **These concepts are known to correspond to reality despite lacking perceptual experience of them.**, **If abstract knowledge can be independent, knowledge in general may not be wholly dependent on perceptual experience.**
