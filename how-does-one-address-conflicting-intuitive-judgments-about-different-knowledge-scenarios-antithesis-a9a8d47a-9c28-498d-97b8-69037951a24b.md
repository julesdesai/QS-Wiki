# How does one address conflicting intuitive judgments about different knowledge scenarios?

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that knowledge requires consistency in application of virtue doctrines, contrasting with intuitive judgments across diverse scenarios.**, **Consider two scenarios: one where knowledge arises directly from virtue, another where truth isn’t connected to virtue yet seems intuitively like knowledge. This contrast challenges consistent doctrine application without modifying virtue requirements.**
