# Knowledge as Truth-Tracking

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 9fcb0107-85ea-42b4-bc2e-4458b1e3f7e6
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does sensitivity handle necessary truths?](does-sensitivity-handle-necessary-truths-antithesis-09b5606d-f565-429f-86a0-18882939fc51.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in nearby worlds where the proposition is false, they do not believe the proposition, and in nearby worlds where the proposition is true, they believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they satisfy these necessary conditions**
