## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief forms part of a coherent noetic structure**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and some conditions other than consciousness provide the justification for the belief within a coherent noetic structure**
