# Does possessing evidence guarantee the truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d6837c28-811a-4aa9-92b2-26b1dabd5c91
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Evidentialist Reliabilism Summary](evidentialist-reliabilism-summary-synthesis-f38be777-625f-41ad-8d84-ebe55a9ee5fa.md)

## Content

**Suppose a competent doctor diagnoses a patient using all available evidence but arrives at a rare misdiagnosis due to an atypical presentation. Intuitively, despite having sufficient evidence, the doctor’s belief is false, challenging the notion that possessing evidence ensures truth.**, **It is implausible that possessing evidence always guarantees the truth of a belief when faced with complex, atypical cases.**
