# What if agreement is based on erroneous perceptions?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** dc79f35b-c6d9-4ba2-9046-43718831e7e8
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Intersubjective Phenomenalism](intersubjective-phenomenalism-synthesis-0a89d160-2bee-479f-b8d3-046de6da5ef6.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which observers agree on a perception of a mirage as a body of water. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that their agreement does not amount to true knowledge.**, **The possibility of mass hallucination demonstrates that intersubjective agreement can occur even with misperceptions.**
