## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition and the proposition is true**, **The belief must cohere with a universal network of foundational beliefs applicable across varying contexts**
