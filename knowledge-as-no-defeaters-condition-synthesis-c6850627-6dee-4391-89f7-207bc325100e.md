# Knowledge as No-Defeaters Condition

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** c6850627-6dee-4391-89f7-207bc325100e
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is proper cognitive function essential for knowledge?](is-proper-cognitive-function-essential-for-knowledge-antithesis-d726946e-bb24-4e4a-b3a5-72f921f3647e.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their justification is undefeated by any contradictory evidence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification, the proposition is true, and their justification is undefeated**
