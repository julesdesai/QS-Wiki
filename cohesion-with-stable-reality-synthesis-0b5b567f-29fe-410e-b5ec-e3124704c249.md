# Cohesion with Stable Reality

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres with an unchanging aspect of reality**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief coheres with an unchanging aspect of reality**
