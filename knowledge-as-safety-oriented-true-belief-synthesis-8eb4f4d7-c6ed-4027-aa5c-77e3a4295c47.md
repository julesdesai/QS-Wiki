# Knowledge as Safety-Oriented True Belief

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in similar conditions, the person does not easily form a false belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and they do not easily form a false belief in similar conditions**
