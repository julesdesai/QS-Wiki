# How can we address cases of cognitive virtue misuse leading to knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 6a15929a-feee-4c30-9919-23990e0f1589
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-ad239dac-f508-456d-984a-1344bc3c3ad8.md)

## Content

**Imagine a situation where a cognitive virtue is used inappropriately or dishonestly resulting in a true belief. Intuitively, the person does not seem to possess genuine knowledge.**, **It is implausible that cognitive virtues alone, without correct application or ethical considerations, can always lead to knowledge.**
