# How does perceptual baseline phenomenalism accommodate non-human perceptions?

**Parent:** [Perceptual Baseline Phenomenalism](perceptual-baseline-phenomenalism-synthesis-c5e3ac41-9c0b-44e1-9f51-d5e2e022393a.md)

## Content

**Animals and artificial intelligence systems can have perceptual experiences that differ from humans.**, **A scenario where different species interpret sensory input in ways unique to their physiological makeup challenges human-centric perceptual baselines.**, **It is implausible to establish a common perceptual baseline that excludes these non-human perspectives.**
