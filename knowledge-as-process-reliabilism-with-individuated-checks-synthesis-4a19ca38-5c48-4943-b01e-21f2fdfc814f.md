## Content

**To know a proposition, it is necessary that any process involved in belief formation includes individuation checks**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and any belief is formed through a process with individuation checks**
