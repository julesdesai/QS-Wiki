## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have evidence for the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their evidence is sourced from reliable informants**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, they have evidence from reliable sources, and the proposition is true**
