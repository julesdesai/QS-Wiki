# Is infinitism straightforwardly compatible with intuitive accounts of virtue?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d8645869-0fef-40a8-81be-f887d789cc58
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology Infinitism](virtue-epistemology-infinitism-synthesis-700b1be0-40ec-4861-9f40-25460b6be3e1.md)

## Content

**Intellectual virtues typically emphasize qualities conducive to good intellectual practices, such as curiosity and intellectual humility.**, **Suppose a virtue-based epistemology appreciates reliable cognitive faculties over maintaining infinite justificatory chains.**, **It is implausible for infinitism to naturally align with our intuitive understanding of intellectual virtues.**
