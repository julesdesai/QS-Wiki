# Are changes in context liable to affect the truth-value of beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a9cf49a2-aa27-4884-9680-aeebbb21ea46
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualist Approach to Coherence](contextualist-approach-to-coherence-synthesis-974832c4-5139-4ec9-9891-da5bb6a159f3.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that the truth-value of a belief should fluctuate merely due to a change in context.**, **Consider a scenario in which a belief is justified and true in one context but loses justification and truth in another because of a shift in external circumstances.**, **In such cases, intuitively, we would judge that truth should not be contextually variable.**
