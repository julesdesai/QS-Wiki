## Content

**Some contextual features matter in the attribution of knowledge, but this does not lead to variation in standards**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief in the proposition is safe from error in nearby possible worlds**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **The standards for knowing a proposition remain constant across different contexts**
