## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is safely formed**, **For a belief to be safely formed, it must not easily be false in nearby possible worlds**
