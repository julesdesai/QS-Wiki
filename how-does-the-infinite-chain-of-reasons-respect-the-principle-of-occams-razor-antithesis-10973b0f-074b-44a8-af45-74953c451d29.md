# How does the infinite chain of reasons respect the principle of Occam's Razor?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 10973b0f-074b-44a8-af45-74953c451d29
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism](infinitism-synthesis-c0072d0f-5658-4115-88b4-8385b90f0067.md)

## Content

**Occam's Razor suggests that among competing hypotheses, the one with the fewest assumptions should be selected. An infinite series introduces endless assumptions, violating this principle.**, **In practical applications, simpler explanations with fewer assumptions often prove more effective and reliable.**
