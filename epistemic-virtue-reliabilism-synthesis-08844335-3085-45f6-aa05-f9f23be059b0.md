## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief results from a reliable cognitive process governed by epistemic virtues**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition truly and their belief results from a reliable cognitive process governed by epistemic virtues**
