# Reliable External Conditions with Internal Rationale

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 512d20a8-548e-4750-ab61-3b0f06f800d9
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does intuition alone determine knowledge?](does-intuition-alone-determine-knowledge-antithesis-e05abd3e-5b2e-41c3-b124-b6b28c1094b3.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is supported by external conditions which shield it from defeasibility due to future evidence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have some internal rationale for why the belief is true**
