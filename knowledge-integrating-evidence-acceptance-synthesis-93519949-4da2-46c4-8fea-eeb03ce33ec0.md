# Knowledge Integrating Evidence Acceptance

**Parent:** [Can beliefs formed under duress be knowledge?](can-beliefs-formed-under-duress-be-knowledge-antithesis-fe972929-e5e4-4829-b660-58e13f0c6ac0.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief formation process to incorporate reliable evidence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is not necessary for the belief formation to exclude situational or psychological pressure**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief formation process incorporates reliable evidence**
