# What role do scientific entities play if reality is only sensory data?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a7c5a7db-92af-46aa-8078-e5dd1c454607
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Phenomenalism Summary](phenomenalism-summary-synthesis-6256cdee-fde6-4d81-b9e8-d1897bfcfd04.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which scientific instruments detect electrons, which are not directly perceivable by human senses. Intuitively, we would judge that electrons are part of reality despite not being part of direct sensory experience.**, **It is plausible that parts of reality, such as scientific entities, exist beyond direct sensory data. This challenges the claim that reality consists entirely of sensory data.**
