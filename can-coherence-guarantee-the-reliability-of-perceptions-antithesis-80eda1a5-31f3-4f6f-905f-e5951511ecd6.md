# Can coherence guarantee the reliability of perceptions?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 80eda1a5-31f3-4f6f-905f-e5951511ecd6
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Instrumental Coherence Theory](instrumental-coherence-theory-synthesis-188d4652-e220-47fa-9e2f-da98cf583190.md)

## Content

**In a case where an instrument is faulty but consistently aligns with direct perception due to matching errors, coherence would occur, yet the perceptions are unreliable. Such scenarios suggest that coherence does not guarantee the reliability of mediated observation.**
