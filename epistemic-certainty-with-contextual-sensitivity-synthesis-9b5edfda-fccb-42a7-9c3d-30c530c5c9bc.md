# Epistemic Certainty with Contextual Sensitivity

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 9b5edfda-fccb-42a7-9c3d-30c530c5c9bc
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does inability to rule out a possibility defeat knowledge?](does-inability-to-rule-out-a-possibility-defeat-knowledge-antithesis-5c66a0e5-0ff8-4ea6-b39d-c96692835558.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they rule out alternative possibilities made contextually salient**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, the proposition is true, and they rule out contextually salient alternative possibilities**
