## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification involves an infinite chain of reasons each validated for logical soundness**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and each reason in the infinite chain is validated for logical soundness**
