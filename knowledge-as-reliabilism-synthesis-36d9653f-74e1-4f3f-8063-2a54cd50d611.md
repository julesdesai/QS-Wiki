## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a belief to be justified, it must result from a reliable cognitive process**, **If a reliable process leads to a true belief, the person has knowledge**
