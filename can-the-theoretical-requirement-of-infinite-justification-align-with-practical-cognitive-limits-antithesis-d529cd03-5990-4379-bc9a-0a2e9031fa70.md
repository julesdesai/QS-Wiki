# Can the theoretical requirement of infinite justification align with practical cognitive limits?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d529cd03-5990-4379-bc9a-0a2e9031fa70
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism with Bounded Rationality](infinitism-with-bounded-rationality-synthesis-6de35f9a-9231-4ac2-b1a8-a1ab9be932b6.md)

## Content

**Justification requiring infinite reasons entails an infinite regress of reasons needed for any belief.**, **Given bounded rationality, human beings cannot manage or comprehend infinite chains of reasoning in practice.**, **The requirement for infinite justification seems to conflict with known cognitive limitations of human beings.**
