# Knowledge as Success from Ability

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge be had even if cognitive faculties malfunction?](can-knowledge-be-had-even-if-cognitive-faculties-malfunction-antithesis-5406c47a-307a-4ee3-8688-5eae173006f2.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is successful because of the person's cognitive abilities**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is successful due to the person's cognitive abilities**
