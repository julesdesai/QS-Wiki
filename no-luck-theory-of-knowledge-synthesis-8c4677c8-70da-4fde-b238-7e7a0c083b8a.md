## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, their belief must not be true merely by chance**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is not accidentally true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is both true and reliably produced**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the reliability of the belief formation process excludes accidental truths**
