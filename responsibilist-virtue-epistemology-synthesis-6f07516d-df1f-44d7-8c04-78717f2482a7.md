## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from the exercise of intellectual virtues**, **Intellectual virtues include both the reliable acquisition of true beliefs and a responsible commitment to evidence and reason**
