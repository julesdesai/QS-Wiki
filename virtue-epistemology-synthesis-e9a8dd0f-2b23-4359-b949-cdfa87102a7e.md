## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for their belief to result from an intellectual virtue or reliable cognitive ability**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief results from an intellectual virtue**
