# Knowledge as Tracking

**Parent:** [Can sensitivity account for knowledge of necessary truths?](can-sensitivity-account-for-knowledge-of-necessary-truths-antithesis-5bbe5b5e-eef8-436c-90e5-a480dffc0a55.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief tracks the truth**, **For a person to perceive a proposition as knowledge, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, they would not believe it**
