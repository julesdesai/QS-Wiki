## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition that is sensitive to the context of inquiry**, **For a person to know a proposition, contextual factors must dictate the robustness of the justification for it to count as knowledge**
