# Knowledge as Contextual Justification

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 68665ab8-0bdf-4826-8fe1-cfdda3c59775
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is knowledge possible with independent verification?](is-knowledge-possible-with-independent-verification-antithesis-f223c99a-be54-464a-9f94-139390c635b1.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification for the belief is adequate given the context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification for the belief is adequate given the context**
