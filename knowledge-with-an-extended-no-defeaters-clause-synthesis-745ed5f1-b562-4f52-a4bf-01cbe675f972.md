# Knowledge with an Extended No-Defeaters Clause

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Can environmental factors be hidden defeaters?](can-environmental-factors-be-hidden-defeaters-antithesis-fb91fca3-3051-4755-a1a8-92c00644b377.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no defeaters caused by recent, relevant changes in the environment**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there are no defeaters, caused by relevant environmental changes, for the belief**
