## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, their belief must be apt**, **A person's belief is apt if it is true because of their intellectual competence**, **The truth of the belief must stem from the believer's skill or competence**, **Apt beliefs require at least contextually supporting conditions for competence to be effective**, **Contextual factors must not impede the exercise of intellectual competence**
