**Parent:** [Are there scenarios where a reliable process yields non-knowledge?](are-there-scenarios-where-a-reliable-process-yields-non-knowledge-antithesis-bf1f3d95-b663-4219-a4c4-a6ba51e6b91e.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is the result of an intellectual virtue or competence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief results from an intellectual virtue or competence**
