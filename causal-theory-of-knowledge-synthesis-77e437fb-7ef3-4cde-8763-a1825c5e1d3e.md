# Causal Theory of Knowledge

**Parent:** [Can a true belief lack intellectual virtue?](can-a-true-belief-lack-intellectual-virtue-antithesis-23d054b2-4c48-440d-bb46-f85f17165e50.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is an appropriate causal connection between the belief and the fact it represents**, **An appropriate causal connection is one where the fact causes the person to believe the proposition**
