## Content

**Knowledge is true belief that arises from intellectual virtue which reliably avoids error**, **Knowledge requires reflective access to the genesis of one's belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief arises from intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have reflective access to why their intellectual virtue is reliable**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe it, the proposition is true, and they have reflective access to why their virtue reliably avoids error**
