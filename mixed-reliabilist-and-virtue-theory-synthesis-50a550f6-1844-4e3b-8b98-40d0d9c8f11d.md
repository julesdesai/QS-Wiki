## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their true belief arises from a mixture of reliable processes and intellectual virtues**, **A reliable process is one that consistently produces true beliefs, which can involve both internal intellectual virtues and external aids**
