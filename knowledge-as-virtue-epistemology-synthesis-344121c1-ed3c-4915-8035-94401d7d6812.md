# Knowledge as Virtue Epistemology

**Parent:** [Can technological mediators of beliefs challenge reliabilism?](can-technological-mediators-of-beliefs-challenge-reliabilism-antithesis-b948f88d-68dd-41e1-854e-e43a5305ae2f.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is the result of exercising intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief results from the exercise of intellectual virtue**
