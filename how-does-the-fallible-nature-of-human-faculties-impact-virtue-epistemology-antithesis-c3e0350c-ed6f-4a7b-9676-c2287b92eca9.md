# How does the fallible nature of human faculties impact virtue epistemology?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c3e0350c-ed6f-4a7b-9676-c2287b92eca9
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-72984f03-1ce3-46fd-9fd6-13203e340ace.md)

## Content

**Human cognitive faculties are naturally fallible and can produce false beliefs. Assuming knowledge must result from the infallible exercise of intellectual virtue is implausible.**, **Given human fallibility, the expectation that intellectual virtues always reliably produce true beliefs is questionable.**
