# Are causal links sufficient for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0a4548e3-a9f6-451b-949a-edf7370c8a5f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Causal Theory of Knowledge](causal-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-83958a5b-d735-4d00-9b23-ae4f36a61779.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where a person forms a belief through a causal chain that also leads to a false belief, such as misinterpreting evidence.**, **Within the same causal framework, the truth of one belief does not guarantee immunity from error in another linked belief.**, **A coherent scenario emerges where causally linked beliefs do not result uniformly in knowledge, challenging sufficiency.**
