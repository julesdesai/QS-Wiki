# Knowledge with Non-Defeated Fallible Justification

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** e8ac045a-236f-4338-9d4e-00494152cd70
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge exist with fallible evidence?](can-knowledge-exist-with-fallible-evidence-antithesis-9dbf0afc-b8fc-48a6-bc77-1e15da282da2.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their justification is not defeated by further evidence**
