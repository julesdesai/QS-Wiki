# Does inter-subjective agreement ensure accuracy?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** de5f6fd6-c710-48a7-a3f0-68f25abde673
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Inter-subjective Phenomenalism](inter-subjective-phenomenalism-synthesis-c00af758-5073-4931-9585-2f560dddd897.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that knowledge should reliably represent reality. This principle with requiring inter-subjective agreement implies the potential for shared inaccuracies.**, **Imagine a historical case where a group collectively misinterprets a natural event. Intuitively, their agreement on the event does not ensure accurate knowledge.**
