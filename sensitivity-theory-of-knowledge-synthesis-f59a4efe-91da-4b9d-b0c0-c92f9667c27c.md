## Content

**A person knows a proposition if they believe it and it is true**, **A belief is sensitive if, in the nearest possible world where the proposition is false, the person does not believe it**, **Sensitivity requires that if the proposition were false, the belief would not be held**, **Sensitivity is a condition on knowledge that mitigates issues arising from environmental changes**
