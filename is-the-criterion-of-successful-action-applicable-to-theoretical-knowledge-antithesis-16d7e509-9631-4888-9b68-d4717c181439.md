# Is the criterion of successful action applicable to theoretical knowledge?

## Content

**Consider the case of knowledge claims in purely theoretical fields, such as mathematics, where practical consequences are not directly applicable. Intuitively, such knowledge should not be dependent on pragmatic utility**, **The truth of highly abstract theoretical knowledge should not be determined by pragmatic outcomes since those fields do not always aim for practical applications**
