# Can perception exist without interpretation?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0e5e7504-485d-4d8e-8354-94a69c47c00b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Dual Aspect Theory](dual-aspect-theory-synthesis-e0a4859a-34ba-4bc9-9368-89fce3ac2e21.md)

## Content

**Perception is the process of receiving sensory information from the external world.**, **Interpretation refers to the assigning of meaning or understanding to the perceived sensory inputs.**, **There can be cases, such as simple organism responses to stimuli, where perception occurs without an appreciable level of interpretation.**
