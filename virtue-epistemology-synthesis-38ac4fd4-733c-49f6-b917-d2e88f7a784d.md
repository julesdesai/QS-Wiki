## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person believes the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person's belief is a result of intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that these intellectual virtue conditions hold**
