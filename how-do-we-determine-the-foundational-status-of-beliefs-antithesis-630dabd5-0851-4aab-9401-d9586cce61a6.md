# How do we determine the foundational status of beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 630dabd5-0851-4aab-9401-d9586cce61a6
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology Foundationalism](virtue-epistemology-foundationalism-synthesis-a32c22e3-fd99-46f1-bd06-3346824cbc70.md)

## Content

**Foundational beliefs are those which do not require justification from other beliefs. It is implausible that we can identify foundational beliefs without some form of justification, as this would lead to an arbitrary selection of foundational beliefs. Therefore, a method is required to non-arbitrarily determine which beliefs are foundational, challenging the sufficiency of intellectual virtues alone.**
