# Knowledge as Contextual Coherence

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can beliefs actually cohere infinitely?](can-beliefs-actually-cohere-infinitely-antithesis-1ff18354-c421-4276-8b08-406021142a60.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres with other beliefs in a contextually relevant way**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that these contextual beliefs are justified**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief coheres with contextually justified beliefs**
