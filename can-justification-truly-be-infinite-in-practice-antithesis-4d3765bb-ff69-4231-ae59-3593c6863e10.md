# Can justification truly be infinite in practice?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 4d3765bb-ff69-4231-ae59-3593c6863e10
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherent Infinitism](coherent-infinitism-synthesis-f2c99927-f862-4ee8-8b5b-2fefd2717ee2.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that any human cognitive process can handle an actual infinite chain of reasons.**, **Suppose a person attempts to justify their belief by providing reasons. Intuitively, if the process never ends, one might feel that no final justification is attained.**
