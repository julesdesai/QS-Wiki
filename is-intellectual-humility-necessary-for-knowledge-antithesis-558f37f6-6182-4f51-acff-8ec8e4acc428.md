# Is intellectual humility necessary for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 558f37f6-6182-4f51-acff-8ec8e4acc428
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Responsibilist Virtue Epistemology](responsibilist-virtue-epistemology-synthesis-d65854c6-8623-4bd4-aed3-f2a4bfae69c9.md)

## Content

**A person might responsibly believe a true proposition but lack intellectual humility.**, **Without acknowledging the limits of their knowledge, their cognitive responsibility is incomplete.**, **This raises doubts about whether such beliefs are truly knowledge.**
