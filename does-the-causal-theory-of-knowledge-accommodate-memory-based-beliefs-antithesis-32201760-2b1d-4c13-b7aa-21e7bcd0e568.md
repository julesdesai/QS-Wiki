# Does the causal theory of knowledge accommodate memory-based beliefs?

## Content

**Suppose a person believes they once visited Paris based on memory, though their current experiences have no evident causal link to the past event, complicating the direct causal requirement.**, **Memory appears problematic for direct causal theory since potency of recollection might overshadow the current absence of causal influx from fact to belief.**
