# Can safety accommodate complex modal realities?

## Content

**In modal epistemology, beliefs are evaluated against a complex structure of possible worlds that vary in accessibility and distance.**, **The concept of "nearness" of worlds may vary significantly, leading to potential variance in what is considered a safe belief.**, **If safety cannot adequately handle complex modal realities, it may be limited as a condition for knowledge.**
