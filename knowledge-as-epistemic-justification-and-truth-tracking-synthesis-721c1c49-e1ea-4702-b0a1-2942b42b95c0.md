## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have epistemic justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief tracks the truth across relevant scenarios**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, they have epistemic justification for the belief, and the belief tracks the truth across relevant scenarios**
