## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief to be produced by cognitive faculties functioning properly in an appropriate environment**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is produced by cognitive faculties functioning properly in an appropriate environment**
