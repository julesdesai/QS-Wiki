# Knowledge as No False Lemmas Justified True Belief

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** e8724d99-aa9f-4ab9-b59f-b670bdf6c737
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is Gettier-style certainty required for knowledge?](is-gettier-style-certainty-required-for-knowledge-antithesis-908ac538-8fe9-4a76-9e64-6a3cd362f534.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that none of their justifications depend on false lemmas**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, the proposition is true, and none of their justifications depend on false lemmas**
