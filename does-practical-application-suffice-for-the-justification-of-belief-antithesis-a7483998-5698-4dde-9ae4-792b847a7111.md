# Does practical application suffice for the justification of belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a7483998-5698-4dde-9ae4-792b847a7111
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatism Summary](pragmatism-summary-synthesis-d0b80c94-4161-403a-9bb9-e4c173237700.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a belief about medicinal treatment is formed based on anecdotal success rather than scientific evidence. Intuitively, it seems that such a belief is not justifiably held, despite its practical success in certain cases.**, **It is implausible that practical application without proper evidential support provides sufficient justification for belief.**
