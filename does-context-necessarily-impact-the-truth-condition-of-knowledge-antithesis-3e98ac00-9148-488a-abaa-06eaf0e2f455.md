# Does context necessarily impact the truth condition of knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3e98ac00-9148-488a-abaa-06eaf0e2f455
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Moderate Contextualism](moderate-contextualism-synthesis-a2419722-fa3b-4f65-af9b-d94e8c32837e.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person knows a proposition based on evidence now but loses all evidence later while the proposition remains true. Intuitively, we judge that context changes should not negate past knowledge if the proposition remains true.**
