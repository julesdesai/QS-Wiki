# Knowledge as Epistemic Internalism

**Parent:** [Can justified true belief be insufficient for knowledge?](can-justified-true-belief-be-insufficient-for-knowledge-antithesis-d3c4d331-db64-48fc-8fb2-c013676f98ab.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person has access to the justification that supports the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the person can access the justification**
