# Invariantism with Gettier Problem Accommodation

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 832d0bb1-f2e6-44eb-8e47-52b6c109678f
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge be both justified and false?](can-knowledge-be-both-justified-and-false-antithesis-c8c367ec-2e4a-471b-9f8a-9baf5e498dba.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, the proposition is true, and there are no Gettier-type problems or defeaters**
