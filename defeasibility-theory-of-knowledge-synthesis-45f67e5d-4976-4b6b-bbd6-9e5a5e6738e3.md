## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no true propositions that would undermine their justification if learned**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification, the proposition is true, and no defeaters exist**
