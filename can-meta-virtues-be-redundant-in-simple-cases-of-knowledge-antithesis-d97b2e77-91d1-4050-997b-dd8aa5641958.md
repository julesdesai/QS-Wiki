# Can meta-virtues be redundant in simple cases of knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d97b2e77-91d1-4050-997b-dd8aa5641958
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Meta-Virtue Epistemology](meta-virtue-epistemology-synthesis-b3bd54fd-8ed8-46f5-92c6-aa5fa4222616.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a child learns basic arithmetic truths through rote memorization, without any awareness of their learning methods. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that the child knows these truths, suggesting meta-virtues are redundant.**
