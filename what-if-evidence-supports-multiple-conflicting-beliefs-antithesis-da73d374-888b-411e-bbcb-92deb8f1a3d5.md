# What if evidence supports multiple, conflicting beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** da73d374-888b-411e-bbcb-92deb8f1a3d5
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Evidentialist Theory of Knowledge](evidentialist-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-79c62cab-dbc0-4dae-ad91-54d5bcae1188.md)

## Content

**A juror receives equally compelling evidence for two opposing testimonies but must decide on a verdict. Intuitively, we might judge this scenario does not provide clear knowledge.**, **Where evidence supports conflicting beliefs, making a justified true belief impossible, questions the reliability of evidence in providing knowledge.**
