# Can bodily engagement alone justify belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** bba90ad8-2463-40b3-8cb1-c1232ac4237a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Embodied Cognition with Reliance on Bodily Skills](embodied-cognition-with-reliance-on-bodily-skills-synthesis-e9d28d24-22c1-4ccc-a9a1-a716398534d0.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that all bodily engagements provide justification, as some interactions may be misleading or erroneous.**, **Consider a scenario in which a person touching ice with numbed hands gains the belief that it is hot due to an unusual heat sensation. Intuitively, this interaction does not justify the belief that ice is hot.**
