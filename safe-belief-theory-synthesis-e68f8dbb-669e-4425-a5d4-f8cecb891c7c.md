## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed in a safe manner**, **A belief is safe if it would be true in similar situations**, **Safety requires that the belief is not based on misleading evidence in likely scenarios**
