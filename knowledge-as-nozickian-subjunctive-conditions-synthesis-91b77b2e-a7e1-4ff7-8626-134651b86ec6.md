# Knowledge as Nozickian Subjunctive Conditions

**Parent:** [Is knowledge dependent on the method of belief formation?](is-knowledge-dependent-on-the-method-of-belief-formation-antithesis-4a8f2d1b-5092-4af0-9ca0-00e35590d76e.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were true, the person would believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the subjunctive conditions are met**
