## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **The standards for justification depend on the epistemic standards personally adopted by the individual**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they personally believe the proposition, have personally appropriate justification, and the proposition is true**
