# Does bounded rationality permit rational stopping points in infinite justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b20db3fd-e8f4-4803-972d-11535f01e333
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism with Bounded Rationality](infinitism-with-bounded-rationality-synthesis-6de35f9a-9231-4ac2-b1a8-a1ab9be932b6.md)

## Content

**Rational agents are said to use bounded rationality to stop the chain rationally.**, **Bounded rationality relies on heuristics or other practical considerations, which may not align with purely rational stopping in infinite chains.**, **If stopping is permitted by bounded rationality, then infinite justification weakens since practical constraints override infinite reasoning.**
