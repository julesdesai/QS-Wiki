# Knowledge as Virtue Epistemology

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 6d99a9fb-0502-4463-95ec-9e45b839a6fa
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge depend on false beliefs?](can-knowledge-depend-on-false-beliefs-antithesis-e894dc83-2473-4968-ad29-cb1eccccb362.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is the result of an intellectual virtue or competence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is the result of an intellectual virtue or competence**
