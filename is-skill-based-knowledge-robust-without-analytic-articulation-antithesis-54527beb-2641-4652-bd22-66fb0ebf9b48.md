# Is skill-based knowledge robust without analytic articulation?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 54527beb-2641-4652-bd22-66fb0ebf9b48
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology with Skills](virtue-epistemology-with-skills-synthesis-458da717-0537-4f5a-8a52-aec5d63f6438.md)

## Content

**Consider a situation where an individual can effectively perform a task but cannot explain how they do it due to lack of analytical reflection. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that while skill is present, absence of analytical articulation questions its status as knowledge.**, **There is a plausible principle that understanding includes the ability to analyze or explain. This principle together with the exercise of skill without explanation suggests a lack of understanding typically associated with knowledge.**
