# Can foundational anchors sufficiently differentiate between arbitrary stopping points and justified beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 447a15a7-6e87-4d79-bab8-78a446ab1dd5
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism with Foundational Anchors](infinitism-with-foundational-anchors-synthesis-6b525f84-03a5-4482-aa47-2329492f1b5f.md)

## Content

**Without clear criteria, any belief might be arbitrarily claimed to be non-inferentially justified.**, **If a belief is arbitrarily selected as foundational, it may lack the epistemic grounding expected of non-inferentially justified beliefs.**, **This arbitrariness threatens to undermine the stability of the justification such beliefs are purported to offer.**
