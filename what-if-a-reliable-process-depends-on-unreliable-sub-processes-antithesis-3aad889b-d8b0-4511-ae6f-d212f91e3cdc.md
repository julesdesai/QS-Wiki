# What if a reliable process depends on unreliable sub-processes?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3aad889b-d8b0-4511-ae6f-d212f91e3cdc
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Reliabilism](virtue-reliabilism-synthesis-fa07ee15-e57c-4439-9a6e-88138086c5f8.md)

## Content

**A reliable cognitive process could be composed of sub-processes that are not reliably truth-conducive.**, **If an overall process is reliable but its components are not, this might complicate the notion of the entire process consistently producing knowledge.**
