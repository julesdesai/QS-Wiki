# Reliabilism with No Defeaters

**Parent:** [Is certainty without defeaters a sufficient condition for knowledge?](is-certainty-without-defeaters-a-sufficient-condition-for-knowledge-antithesis-f1dd284b-144f-47da-8b24-ce1f8581d5a2.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is formed by a reliable cognitive process, the proposition is true, and no defeaters are present for the belief**
