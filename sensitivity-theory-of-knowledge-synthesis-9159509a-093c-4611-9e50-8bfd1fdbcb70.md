## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, their belief must be sensitive to the truth**, **A belief is sensitive if, in the nearest possible world where the proposition is false, the person does not believe it**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief would not be held if the proposition were false in a nearby world**, **Knowledge implies that a true belief would change in response to a falsehood in a relevantly similar situation**
