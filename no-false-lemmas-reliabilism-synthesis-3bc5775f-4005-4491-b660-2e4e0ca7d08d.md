**Parent:** [Can we trust all outcomes of reliable processes for knowledge claims?](can-we-trust-all-outcomes-of-reliable-processes-for-knowledge-claims-antithesis-df8156e6-14b9-476b-a9a6-a31b0e08c022.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **A reliable cognitive process must not involve any false lemmas, or premises, in the reasoning that leads to the belief**, **A belief formed by a reliable cognitive process has a high probability of resulting in a true belief**
