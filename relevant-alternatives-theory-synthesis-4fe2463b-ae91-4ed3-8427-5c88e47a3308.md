## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have a true belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they are able to rule out relevant alternatives**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have a true belief and relevant alternatives are ruled out**
