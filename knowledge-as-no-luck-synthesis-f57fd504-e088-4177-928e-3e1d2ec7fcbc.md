# Knowledge as No Luck

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** f57fd504-e088-4177-928e-3e1d2ec7fcbc
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does sensitivity fail in skeptical scenarios?](does-sensitivity-fail-in-skeptical-scenarios-antithesis-285a8263-cdf1-4354-8ba5-5b65572023f2.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is not true merely by luck**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is not true merely by luck**
