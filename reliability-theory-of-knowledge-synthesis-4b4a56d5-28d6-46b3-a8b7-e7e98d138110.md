## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they justify the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition be true**, **A belief is reliable if it is formed by a process that usually produces true beliefs**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief is formed by a reliable process and the proposition is true**
