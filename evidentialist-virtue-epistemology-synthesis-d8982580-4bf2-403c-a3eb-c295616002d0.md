## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is supported by adequate evidence**, **Evidence is information that increases the likelihood that a belief is true**
