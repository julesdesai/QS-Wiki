# Knowledge as Justification Truth and Absence of Defeaters

**Parent:** [Does evidence one is unaware of affect knowledge possession?](does-evidence-one-is-unaware-of-affect-knowledge-possession-antithesis-b5bf46f1-9129-49b4-9a60-73eed889910d.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is justification for the person's belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have justification for their belief, the proposition is true, and there is no defeating evidence available to the person**
