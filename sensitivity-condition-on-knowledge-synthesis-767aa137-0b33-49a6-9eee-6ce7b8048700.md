# Sensitivity Condition on Knowledge

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 767aa137-0b33-49a6-9eee-6ce7b8048700
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can an unsafe belief ever constitute knowledge?](can-an-unsafe-belief-ever-constitute-knowledge-antithesis-cfe5ad0d-16aa-46a8-91a2-dbdfaa87d224.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief would not have been held were the proposition false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is sensitive to the truth**
