## Content

**Consider a scenario where each reason given for a belief is subject to infinite further questioning. Intuitively, this continual regression undermines certainty in any knowledge claim.**, **A plausible principle suggests certainty requires a stopping point, yet infinite regression fails to provide such epistemic grounding.**
