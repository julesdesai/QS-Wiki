# Does sensitivity theory make skeptical scenarios too easy to dismiss?

**Terminal:** No

## Content

**In skeptical scenarios where systematic deception is possible, beliefs might fail sensitivity despite being based on strong evidence, which challenges the common sense conviction that we have knowledge in such contexts.**, **If sensitivity is required, it seems to make otherwise ordinary knowledge questionably absent due to potential for deception.**
