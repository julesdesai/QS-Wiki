## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true within their own epistemic framework**, **The truth of a proposition can vary between different epistemic frameworks**, **The standards for knowledge are determined by the individual's framework and not by context**
