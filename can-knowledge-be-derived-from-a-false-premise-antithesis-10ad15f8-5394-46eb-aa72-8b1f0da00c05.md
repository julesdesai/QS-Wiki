# Can knowledge be derived from a false premise?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 10ad15f8-5394-46eb-aa72-8b1f0da00c05
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology Summary](virtue-epistemology-summary-synthesis-ebb76c32-727a-4eba-8f42-21c2ee3c5622.md)

## Content
