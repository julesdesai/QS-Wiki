# Is truth alone sufficient for knowledge in truth-tracking theory?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b15c4aba-b5ca-4e98-8451-c18e4108060b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Truth-Tracking Theory](truth-tracking-theory-synthesis-44ea7417-270f-4c93-876d-f98b6754dd68.md)

## Content

**A proposition being true does not necessarily lead to knowledge if the person does not believe it.**, **A person can know a proposition only if they have a belief in that proposition.**, **If a proposition is true but no one believes it or tracks the truth across possible scenarios, this poses a problem for claiming it's known.**
