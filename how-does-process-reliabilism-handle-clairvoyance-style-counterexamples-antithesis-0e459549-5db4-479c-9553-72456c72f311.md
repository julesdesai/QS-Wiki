# How does process reliabilism handle clairvoyance-style counterexamples?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0e459549-5db4-479c-9553-72456c72f311
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Process Reliabilism](process-reliabilism-synthesis-0b742bab-1646-43b8-9078-24258a3aaaf7.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person, Claire, has a clairvoyant power to accurately predict future events without any prior evidence or reasoning. Claire forms a belief about the next state leader through her clairvoyance, which is true and consistently reliable. Intuitively, we would judge that Claire does not know who the next leader is.**, **Clairvoyance qualifies as a reliable process due to its consistent truth-tracking.**, **The cognitive disconnect between the believer and the 'source' of the knowledge calls into question whether a reliable process alone suffices for knowledge.**
