## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is supported by adequate evidence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief is true and justified by evidence that veridically supports the truth of the proposition**
