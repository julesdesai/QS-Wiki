# Knowledge as Reflective Equilibrium

**Parent:** [If a belief formed through faulty reasoning is correct by chance, is it knowledge?](if-a-belief-formed-through-faulty-reasoning-is-correct-by-chance-is-it-knowledge-antithesis-8aa7695d-4a28-443e-85c9-903a0f201784.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres with other beliefs in a reflective equilibrium**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief coheres with other beliefs in a reflective equilibrium**
