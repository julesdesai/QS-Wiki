# Knowledge through Reliance on Justification

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Can a person have knowledge if they learn unconsciously?](can-a-person-have-knowledge-if-they-learn-unconsciously-antithesis-71f1e47c-ae9a-4f24-84fc-53916ef55d3f.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief relies on justification, whether conscious or unconscious**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their reliance on justification, even if not consciously aware, informs the belief**
