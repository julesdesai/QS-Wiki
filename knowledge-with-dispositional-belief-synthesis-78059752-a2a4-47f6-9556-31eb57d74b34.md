# Knowledge with Dispositional Belief

**Parent:** [Are judgement-based actions plausible without belief?](are-judgement-based-actions-plausible-without-belief-antithesis-a7fb0a9a-1b20-45a1-96dd-3462ab6fc717.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for the proposition**, **For a person to act on a judgement, it is necessary to have a dispositional belief in the proposition**, **A dispositional belief is a recurrent but not continuous belief in the truth of the proposition**
