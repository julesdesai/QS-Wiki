# Do practical considerations make justification contingent?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7dd13a59-1c83-45fa-94d5-bdb3a16c6a3c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Theory of Justification](pragmatic-theory-of-justification-synthesis-cd0f39d2-c604-4de4-bb81-1900aa8a1a8f.md)

## Content

**Suppose a case where justification for a belief in a dangerous situation is successful due to survival, yet the same justification would fail in non-threatening contexts. This indicates practical justification is context-dependent.**, **Consider if someone uses belief justified by prayer leading to practical success, the contingency of this success on factors like luck makes the justification unstable.**
