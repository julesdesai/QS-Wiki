## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the cognitive faculties reliably produce true beliefs**, **Cognitive faculties are reliable if they have been evolutionarily selected for their tendency to produce true beliefs**
