# No Defeaters Account of Knowledge

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Do Gettier-style cases undermine the connection between reliability and knowledge?](do-gettier-style-cases-undermine-the-connection-between-reliability-and-knowledge-antithesis-357972cb-5bc1-4ca5-abc5-d5d58301d87c.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there exist no defeaters, or overriding reasons, that would undermine the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there exist no defeaters**
