# Knowledge with Sensitivity Condition Refined

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief would be different if the proposition were false, except in cases of reliance on institutional knowledge**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the belief exhibits conditional sensitivity, and the proposition is true**
