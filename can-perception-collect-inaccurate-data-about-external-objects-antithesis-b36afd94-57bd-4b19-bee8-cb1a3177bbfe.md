# Can perception collect inaccurate data about external objects?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b36afd94-57bd-4b19-bee8-cb1a3177bbfe
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Empiricism Summary](empiricism-summary-synthesis-4e15833e-fa6c-416c-a78e-6563a82d7628.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person sees a mirage in the desert and perceives it to be water. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that perception has collected inaccurate data about external objects.**, **Perceptual experiences can be deceptive or misleading, resulting in incorrect data about external objects.**
