# Can idealism adequately explain the existence of unperceived objects?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c7c8a63c-699a-4155-8c51-f3fecae1ded5
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Idealism](idealism-synthesis-6a8ea4b4-8cf6-44ff-98fb-cf08805f781d.md)

## Content

**Consider the existence of distant celestial bodies like stars that exist and undergo changes even if they are not perceived by anyone. The intuitive understanding is that these stars do not rely on mental constructs to exist.**, **The existence and behavior of distant, unperceived objects suggest a reality that is not merely constituted by perceptual experiences or mental phenomena.**
