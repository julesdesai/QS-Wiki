# Can a belief formed through luck not undermine but actually contribute to the acquisition of knowledge?

**Terminal:** No

## Content

**In some scenarios, luck might not only fail to undermine but actually contribute to knowledge acquisition.**, **Consider a scientist stumbling upon an accidental but correct hypothesis through flawed logic that subsequently serves as the basis for successful testing and validation, resulting in knowledge despite its lucky origin.**, **The eventual confirmation of a truth through sound processes validates the role of initial luck as a catalyst rather than an impediment to knowledge.**
