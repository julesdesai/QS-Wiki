# Is belief sufficient for knowledge regardless of evidence or truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e793c5aa-59b1-4964-9916-2ac79072f4c0
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [True Belief as Primary in Knowledge](true-belief-as-primary-in-knowledge-synthesis-f83d0a87-6cde-4f50-a75a-0024fde50cce.md)

## Content

**Believing a proposition does not necessarily mean having knowledge of it if the belief lacks grounding in evidence.**, **In cases where a belief is purely based on conjecture, it might be true but not known due to lack of justification.**, **A belief that is true by coincidence doesn't fulfill the proper criteria for knowledge without supporting evidence.**
