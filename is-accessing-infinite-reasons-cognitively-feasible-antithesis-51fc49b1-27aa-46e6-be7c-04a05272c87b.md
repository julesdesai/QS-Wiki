# Is accessing infinite reasons cognitively feasible?

## Content

Intuitively, accessing an infinite array of reasons seems cognitively impossible.**, **There is a plausible principle that beliefs should be justified in a cognitively feasible manner, challenging the practicality of an infinite chain requirement.**
