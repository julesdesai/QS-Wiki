# Knowledge as justified true belief with defeaters

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Can isolated reliable beliefs challenge the necessity of reliability?](can-isolated-reliable-beliefs-challenge-the-necessity-of-reliability-antithesis-9659120c-b252-41aa-af58-edf226c8bbfa.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have justified true belief and no defeaters are present**
