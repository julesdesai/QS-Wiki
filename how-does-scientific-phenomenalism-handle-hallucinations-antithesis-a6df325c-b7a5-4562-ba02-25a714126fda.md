# How does scientific phenomenalism handle hallucinations?

**Parent:** [Scientific Phenomenalism](scientific-phenomenalism-synthesis-ce8a34ca-bcf7-45c1-b3c8-1c3da9267ceb.md)

## Content

**Imagine a person experiences a vivid hallucination of an elephant in a room where there is none.**, **The coherent systematization of these experiences suggests the presence of an elephant.**, **However, the absence of the elephant in reality shows that the knowledge claim is flawed.**
