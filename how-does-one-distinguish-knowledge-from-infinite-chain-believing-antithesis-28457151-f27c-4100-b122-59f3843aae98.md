# How does one distinguish knowledge from infinite chain believing?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 28457151-f27c-4100-b122-59f3843aae98
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism with Conceptual Infinite Regress](infinitism-with-conceptual-infinite-regress-synthesis-14e25e58-6ba7-452c-ac18-aba278058c50.md)

## Content

**Knowledge is traditionally distinguished from mere belief by the quality of justification.**, **An infinite regress of reasons may blur the line between justified belief and mere belief.**, **If both knowledge and infinite chain believing involve infinite reasons, it becomes hard to distinguish one from the other.**
