# Are epistemic virtues resistant to contextual manipulation?

**Node Type:** antithesis

**Depth:** 4

**Parent:** [Contextualism with Virtue Epistemology](contextualism-with-virtue-epistemology-synthesis-4d017c8b-6b5b-48d7-b8c7-737c6c80044f.md)

## Content

**If epistemic virtues are sensitive to context, rather than constant, their role in knowledge attribution becomes unstable. Intuitively, stable epistemic virtues should be resilient to contextual changes.**
