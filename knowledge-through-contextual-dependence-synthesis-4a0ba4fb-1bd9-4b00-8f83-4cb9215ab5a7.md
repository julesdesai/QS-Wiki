## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A belief qualifies as knowledge if it is formed by a reliable process, taking into consideration the context in which the belief is formed**, **Contextual dependency means the standards for reliability may vary based on situational factors**
