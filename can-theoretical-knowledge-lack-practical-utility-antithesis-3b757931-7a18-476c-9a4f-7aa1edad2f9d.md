# Can theoretical knowledge lack practical utility?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3b757931-7a18-476c-9a4f-7aa1edad2f9d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Theory of Knowledge](pragmatic-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-af5a1410-77c2-4ceb-b390-2ced8876b488.md)

## Content

**Suppose a physicist has detailed knowledge of quantum mechanics’ theoretical aspects, which do not directly aid in solving practical problems. Intuitively, this knowledge still enriches the field of physics despite lacking immediate utility.**, **It is implausible that knowledge loses its value when detached from practical concerns, as disciplines like mathematics and philosophy are inherently theoretical.**
