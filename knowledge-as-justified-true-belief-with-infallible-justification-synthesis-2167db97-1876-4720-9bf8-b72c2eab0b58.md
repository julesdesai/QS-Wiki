# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with Infallible Justification

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can a justified true belief be undermined by the Gettier problem?](can-a-justified-true-belief-be-undermined-by-the-gettier-problem-antithesis-97b72373-ac55-475e-a76e-cb9069a57d02.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their justification be infallible**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their justification is infallible**
