## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief results from cognitive faculties functioning properly in an environment conducive to truth**, **Proper functioning involves faculties operating according to a design plan aimed at truth**
