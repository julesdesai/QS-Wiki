# Can knowledge occur from non-designed truth-producing mechanisms?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 38c6d4ae-8342-448d-a57c-e31190db9ff3
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Proper Functionalism With Proper Functioning Belief Mechanisms](proper-functionalism-with-proper-functioning-belief-mechanisms-synthesis-d742806a-8086-4d38-b926-69c7d212e234.md)

## Content

**Suppose there is an artificial intelligence system designed to entertain rather than to produce truths, yet it accidentally generates a true belief. Intuitively, if users rely on this truth and consider it knowledge, it challenges the necessity of a design aimed at truth.**, **Knowledge can arise without a mechanism designed specifically to produce truth.**
