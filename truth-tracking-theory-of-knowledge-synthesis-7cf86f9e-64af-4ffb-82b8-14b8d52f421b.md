# Truth-Tracking Theory of Knowledge

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 7cf86f9e-64af-4ffb-82b8-14b8d52f421b
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can someone have knowledge without a belief?](can-someone-have-knowledge-without-a-belief-antithesis-894b9500-aa17-4738-8b92-defabb598044.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were true, the person would believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is true, that if it were false the person would not believe it, and that if it were true the person would believe it**
