# Can knowledge extend beyond future perceptual predictions?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 056dfedb-e4ca-4ac2-b099-b47d074c337e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Phenomenalist Instrumentalism](phenomenalist-instrumentalism-synthesis-4537874c-c8f8-46da-9468-0a115148189b.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person knows they have a headache but does not make predictions about future perceptual experiences. Intuitively, they possess knowledge despite the absence of predictive utility.**, **A person can have knowledge related to past events that do not entail predictions about future perceptual experiences, challenging the limitation to future predictions.**
