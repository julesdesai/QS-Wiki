## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A belief qualifies as knowledge if it was formed during a period when the process was reliable**, **A process is reliable if it consistently produces more true beliefs than false during that period**
