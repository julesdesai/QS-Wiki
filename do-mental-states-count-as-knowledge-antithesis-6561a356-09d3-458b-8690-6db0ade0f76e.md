# Do mental states count as knowledge?

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person feels pain and knows they are in pain. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that this knowledge is not derived from perception but from introspection.**, **There is a plausible principle that introspective knowledge is distinct from perceptual knowledge.**
