**Parent:** [Can true belief constitute knowledge without justification on foundational beliefs?](can-true-belief-constitute-knowledge-without-justification-on-foundational-beliefs-antithesis-a5165248-8eaa-497a-8800-ff12d62fc794.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified according to the standards of the context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is justified within the context**
