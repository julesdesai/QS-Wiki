## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for them to effectively remember the proposition**, **A person knows a proposition if the proposition is true, they have justification for it, and they effectively remember it**
