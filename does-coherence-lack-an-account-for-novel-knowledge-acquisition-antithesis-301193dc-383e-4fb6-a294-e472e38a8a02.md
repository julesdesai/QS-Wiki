# Does coherence lack an account for novel knowledge acquisition?

## Content

**Suppose a person suddenly acquires a new piece of true information that contradicts their current web of beliefs, such as unexpected scientific evidence. Intuitively, this new belief should be justified if it's reliably sourced.**, **Without a foundational belief system, coherentism fails to clearly explain how new, truth-tracking beliefs are incorporated into an existing web when they initially lack coherence.**
