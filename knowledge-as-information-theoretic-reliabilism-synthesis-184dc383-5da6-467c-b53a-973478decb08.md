# Knowledge as Information-Theoretic Reliabilism

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed by a process that reliably channels true information**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have a true belief that is reliably informed by a trustworthy process**
