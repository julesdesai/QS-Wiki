# Does pragmatic encroachment negate intellectual standards for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 49dec519-9d12-4126-91ba-dead298dbee3
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Encroachment Theory](pragmatic-encroachment-theory-synthesis-48f209b4-b71c-4b9b-814c-8c2a5af0c840.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where person V believes a medical treatment will work based on thorough scientific evidence, with low personal stakes. Intuitively, V knows the treatment will work.**, **In a similar situation with high stakes, such as life-or-death consequences, person W also believes based on the same scientific evidence. Intuitively, W knows the treatment will work.**, **It is implausible that pragmatic considerations should permit fluctuating intellectual standards for what qualifies as knowledge.**
