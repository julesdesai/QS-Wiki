## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is the result of intellectual virtues sensitive to context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they hold a true belief formed by intellectually virtuous practices contextually appropriate**
