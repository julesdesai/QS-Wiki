# Does the existence of abstract objects challenge perceptual dependency?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5cd00137-b8db-4821-9562-01f01f90dcd4
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Phenomenalism Summary](phenomenalism-summary-synthesis-ab29b70f-4a42-463b-b303-b38517d8aa22.md)

## Content

**Suppose there are mathematical entities like numbers that are widely argued to exist abstractly. Intuitively, these objects do not depend on perceptual experience to exist, which suggests that not all objects of knowledge rely on perception.**
