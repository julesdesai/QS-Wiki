# What about knowledge in cases of extreme cognitive deficits?

**Depth:** 4

**Parent:** [Virtue epistemology focusing on intellectual virtues](virtue-epistemology-focusing-on-intellectual-virtues-synthesis-d559d8c1-587e-4ccf-9e44-8233679e40bf.md)

## Content

**Consider a person with severe cognitive impairments who accidentally forms a true and justified belief. Intuitively, this can still be considered knowledge.**, **In such cases, the absence of intellectual virtues like open-mindedness and intellectual courage does not seem to preclude knowledge.**
