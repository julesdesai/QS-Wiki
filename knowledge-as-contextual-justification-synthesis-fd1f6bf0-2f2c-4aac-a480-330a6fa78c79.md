**Parent:** [Can the presence of error in a belief's evidence chain annul knowledge?](can-the-presence-of-error-in-a-beliefs-evidence-chain-annul-knowledge-antithesis-d65c77f9-70e7-4a34-9de6-99a8c17c7ed6.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief's justification is appropriate to the context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief's justification is appropriate to the context**
