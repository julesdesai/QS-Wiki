## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition be true**, **Justification for a belief is provided by processes that are sensitive to the truth**, **A belief is sensitive if, had the proposition been false, the person would not have believed it**
