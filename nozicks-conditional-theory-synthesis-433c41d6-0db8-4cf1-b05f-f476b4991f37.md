# Nozick's Conditional Theory

**Parent:** [Can causal connection account for deduced knowledge?](can-causal-connection-account-for-deduced-knowledge-antithesis-9d6a2f86-6334-4990-a52d-68c1ddaff04e.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were true, the person would believe it**
