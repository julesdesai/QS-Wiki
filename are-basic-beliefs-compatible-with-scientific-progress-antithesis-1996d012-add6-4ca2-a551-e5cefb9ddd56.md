# Are basic beliefs compatible with scientific progress?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1996d012-add6-4ca2-a551-e5cefb9ddd56
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundationalism in Epistemology](foundationalism-in-epistemology-synthesis-4c27bb26-6321-4022-8e66-a85ea5d939d9.md)

## Content

**Scientific progress relies on revising beliefs based on new findings and evidence.**, **If basic beliefs are fixed, then they may conflict with the evolving nature of scientific understanding.**, **A system of knowledge with fixed foundational beliefs may not accommodate the flexible epistemic approach required in science.**
