## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **The standards for intellectual virtue must be stable across contexts**, **A person knows a proposition if they have a true belief resulting from stable intellectual virtue**
