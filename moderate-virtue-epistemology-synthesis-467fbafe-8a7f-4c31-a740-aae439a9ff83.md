## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that a minimal form of intellectual virtue is present**, **Minimal intellectual virtue does not require systematicity but does require some reliable belief-forming processes**
