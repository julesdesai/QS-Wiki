## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, their belief must be the result of a reliable process**, **A reliable process is one that generally leads to a high ratio of true beliefs over false ones**
