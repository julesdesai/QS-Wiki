# Can knowledge be fully explained without accessible justification?

**Node ID:** b2209789-1c07-443d-b2c8-4d646158db95

**Parent:** [Contextualism about Justification](contextualism-about-justification-synthesis-1dd27965-c99f-4b62-ae25-50f61d9bceba.md)

## Content

**A belief is regarded as knowledge due to contextual factors despite the absence of accessible justification to the believer.**, **It is plausible that knowledge requires some conscious access to justification, as it allows the knower to substantiate their claims when challenged, maintaining the traditional value we associate with knowledge.**
