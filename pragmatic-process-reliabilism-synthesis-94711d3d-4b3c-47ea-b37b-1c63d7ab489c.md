**Parent:** [Does the requirement for truth make the theory circular?](does-the-requirement-for-truth-make-the-theory-circular-antithesis-299b43ec-7644-4535-b71a-56492167a44f.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a cognitive process that is instrumentally valuable for achieving practical goals**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is produced by a pragmatically valuable cognitive process**
