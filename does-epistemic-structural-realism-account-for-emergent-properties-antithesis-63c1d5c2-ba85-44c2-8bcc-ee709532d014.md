# Does epistemic structural realism account for emergent properties?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 63c1d5c2-ba85-44c2-8bcc-ee709532d014
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Epistemic Structural Realism](epistemic-structural-realism-synthesis-4cc8fed2-62df-47a3-af1a-0c0e49fc23b5.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where the structure of water is understood through its molecular configuration, yet the emergent property of 'wetness' is not captured by mere structure. Intuitively, knowledge of a substance should include these emergent features to be fully comprehensive.**, **If emergent properties do not conform to structural understanding alone, then a purely structural account of knowledge might be regarded as incomplete.**
