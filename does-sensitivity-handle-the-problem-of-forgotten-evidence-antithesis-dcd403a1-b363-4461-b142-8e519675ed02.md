# Does sensitivity handle the problem of forgotten evidence?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** dcd403a1-b363-4461-b142-8e519675ed02
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Sensitivity Theory](sensitivity-theory-synthesis-c636dae2-8d6f-478b-827f-65895cd50362.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person knows a historical fact based solely on evidence they once had but have since forgotten.**, **The forgotten evidence means if the fact were false, they would not have any evidence to support disbelief.**, **The sensitivity condition implies the person does not know the fact without current evidence, challenging our intuition that they retain knowledge based on past evidence.**
