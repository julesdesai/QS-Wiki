# Can one have justified beliefs without knowing the justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** faddc508-901d-4ecc-8d32-749e01dd0c58
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Modified Internalism with External Reliability](modified-internalism-with-external-reliability-synthesis-5d3adb72-ce8d-4976-b1d3-5d6a6f159cf7.md)

## Content

**Imagine a patient who believes a medical diagnosis is correct due to a doctor’s authority but cannot understand the medical rationale.**, **The diagnosis is indeed correct, and the doctor's method is reliable.**, **The lack of understanding challenges the view that knowledge requires the knower to have accessible justification.**
