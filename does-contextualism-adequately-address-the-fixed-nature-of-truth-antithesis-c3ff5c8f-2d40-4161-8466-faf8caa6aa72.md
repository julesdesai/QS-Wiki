# Does contextualism adequately address the fixed nature of truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c3ff5c8f-2d40-4161-8466-faf8caa6aa72
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism](contextualism-synthesis-876f0403-5dcc-4837-9485-46808d567bde.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where two people disagree about a factual claim due to differing contexts but the truth remains constant. Intuitively, knowledge claims should align with the fixed nature of truth rather than fluctuate with context.**, **It is implausible that truth, as a constant, allows for variable knowledge claims about the same fact.**
