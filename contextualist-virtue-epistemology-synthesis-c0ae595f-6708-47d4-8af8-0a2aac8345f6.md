## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, their belief must result from the exercise of intellectual virtues recognized within the context**, **An intellectual virtue is a trait that is contextually defined and reliably yields true beliefs within specific contexts**
