# Truth-Tracking Virtue Epistemology

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 11f9e8ce-31b4-479e-91e3-a19fbb847e02
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Are all responsible beliefs knowledge?](are-all-responsible-beliefs-knowledge-antithesis-044a8463-00d8-4fae-a25b-4fd6464f58c2.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have intellectual responsibility in forming beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief tracks the truth across nearby possibilities**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have intellectual responsibility, the proposition is true, and their belief tracks the truth across nearby possibilities**
