## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is causally connected to the belief**, **Causal connection requires that the belief originates from a pattern of cause leading to true belief**
