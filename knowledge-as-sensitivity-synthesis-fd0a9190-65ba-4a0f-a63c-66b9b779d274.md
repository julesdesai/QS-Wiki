# Knowledge as Sensitivity

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** fd0a9190-65ba-4a0f-a63c-66b9b779d274
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is truth undermined by undiscovered defeaters?](is-truth-undermined-by-undiscovered-defeaters-antithesis-0b005bc2-92bf-45a0-bf33-2f43be0c07b0.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief would not be held if the proposition were false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification, the proposition is true, and the belief is sensitive to the truth of the proposition**
