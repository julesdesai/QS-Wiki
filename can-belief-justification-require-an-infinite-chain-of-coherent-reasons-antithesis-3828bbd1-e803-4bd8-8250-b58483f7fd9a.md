# Can belief justification require an infinite chain of coherent reasons?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3828bbd1-e803-4bd8-8250-b58483f7fd9a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitist Coherentism](infinitist-coherentism-synthesis-2a4efbd0-0f1f-4a01-bfa6-1ecf016eb1e0.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that mental resources are finite in any human being. This principle together with the idea that beliefs require an infinite chain of reasons entails that finite beings cannot hold justified beliefs.**, **It is implausible that beliefs requiring an infinite chain can be justified given human cognitive limitations.**
