# Can the safety criterion accommodate conflicting expert opinions?

**Node Type:** antithesis

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Safety Theory of Knowledge](safety-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-ddb0d759-5c3e-4616-85ba-00e34fffe012.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person gains a belief on a scientific fact by reading a reputable article only for contrary findings to emerge from other reputable scientific sources. Intuitively, recognition of uncertainty makes the belief's knowledge status dubious.**, **When conflicting expertise leads to opposing conclusions, the belief's safety is compromised due to potential falseness in the contingent proximity of other expert judgments.**
