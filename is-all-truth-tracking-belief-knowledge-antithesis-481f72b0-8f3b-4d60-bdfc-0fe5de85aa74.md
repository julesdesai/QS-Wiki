# Is all truth-tracking belief knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 481f72b0-8f3b-4d60-bdfc-0fe5de85aa74
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism as a Knowledge Theory](reliabilism-as-a-knowledge-theory-synthesis-b5bdc6be-6410-4aae-a2f1-e20391888e99.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person forms a belief by a reliable process that accidentally tracks the truth due to coincidental factors. Intuitively, we would judge that this person does not possess knowledge.**, **There is a plausible principle that coincidental truth-tracking is insufficient for knowledge.**
