# Can foundational beliefs coexist with the requirement of an infinite chain of reasons for justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 58aa408c-e2c2-437a-8ad8-61ac2ba9e6e7
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism with Foundational Beliefs](infinitism-with-foundational-beliefs-synthesis-4273f090-f358-492e-9e7c-904c2a039ab2.md)

## Content

**A belief can be foundational if it does not require further justification.**, **A belief as part of an infinite chain must have further reasons for its justification.**, **It is implausible for a belief to be both foundational and part of an infinite series needing additional justification.**
