## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person has justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary there is no 'Gettier problem', meaning no justified true belief in a way that coincidentally leads to truth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, they are justified, and there is no Gettier problem**
