# Knowledge as Reliabilist Justified True Belief

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 9cd5c618-6d82-4ab6-9978-94b9c0f5faff
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can a person have justified true belief and still fail to know?](can-a-person-have-justified-true-belief-and-still-fail-to-know-antithesis-dffa1d22-1dcb-4f6a-8efc-dd675a731849.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person has justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief be produced by a reliable process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, they have justification for believing the proposition, and the belief is produced by a reliable process**
