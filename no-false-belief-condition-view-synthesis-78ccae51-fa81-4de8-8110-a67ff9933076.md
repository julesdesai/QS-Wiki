## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person not hold any false beliefs that entail the proposition**, **Avoidance of false beliefs ensures reliability in belief formation processes**
