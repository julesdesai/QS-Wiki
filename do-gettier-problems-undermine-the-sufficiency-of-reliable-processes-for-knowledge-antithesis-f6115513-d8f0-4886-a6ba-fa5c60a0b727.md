# Do Gettier Problems undermine the sufficiency of reliable processes for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f6115513-d8f0-4886-a6ba-fa5c60a0b727
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalist View](externalist-view-synthesis-75028f40-4ef7-4feb-a21d-1e6a75d787a6.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where a reliable process produces a true belief through luck or coincidence. Intuitively, one might judge that this is not robust knowledge.**, **Gettier-type scenarios show that reliability alone is insufficient for eliminating all cases of apparent knowledge.**
