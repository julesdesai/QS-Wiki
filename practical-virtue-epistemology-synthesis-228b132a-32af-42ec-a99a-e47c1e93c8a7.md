## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their true belief stems from a practically virtuous action**, **A practically virtuous action is one that aligns with intellectual virtues in context but need not involve the exercise of intellectual virtues themselves**
