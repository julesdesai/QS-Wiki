# Can a belief be justified if it coheres but lacks empirical foundation?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 27bdd7be-5350-4d0a-9b74-c648d5dadfd8
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherent Foundationalism](coherent-foundationalism-synthesis-d7e34eeb-53ce-49ff-840a-2989c3d20d02.md)

## Content

**A belief that fits into a coherent system of beliefs might lack empirical evidence.**, **A belief without empirical evidence may not be justified even if it coheres with other beliefs.**, **It is implausible that coherence alone without empirical support can justify a belief.**
