# Can knowledge exist without intellectual virtue?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a6539d52-3a30-4548-9d7b-e2b362fd5ce1
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-5eebb8d4-de96-4ab7-90ca-fa4ae98a5219.md)

## Content

**It is plausible that a person can form true beliefs purely by luck rather than by intellectual virtue.**, **A scenario in which a person guesses an answer correctly without any basis shows that the belief formation was not due to the exercise of intellectual virtue.**
