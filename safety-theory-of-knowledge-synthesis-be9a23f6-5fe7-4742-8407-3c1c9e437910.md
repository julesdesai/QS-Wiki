## Content

**A person knows a proposition if it is true, they believe it, and their belief could not easily have been false**, **For a belief to qualify as knowledge, it must be safe from error in nearby possible situations**, **The belief must be true across similar scenarios to count as knowledge**, **Knowledge requires that in most similar situations, the belief would still be true**
