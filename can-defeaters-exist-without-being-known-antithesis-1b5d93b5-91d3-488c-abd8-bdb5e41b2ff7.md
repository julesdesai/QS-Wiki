# Can defeaters exist without being known?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1b5d93b5-91d3-488c-abd8-bdb5e41b2ff7
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [No Defeaters Theory](no-defeaters-theory-synthesis-e6eeb208-311d-4d09-a784-60d3e95f478f.md)

## Content

**Defeaters are defined as information that would undermine justification if available.**, **There may be unknown factors that count as defeaters, even if unavailable to the believer.**, **The mere possibility of such unknown factors challenges the idea that one can truly know without defeaters.**
