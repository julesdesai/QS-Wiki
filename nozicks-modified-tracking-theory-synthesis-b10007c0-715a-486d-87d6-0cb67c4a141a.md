## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief tracks the truth in nearby possible scenarios**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they would not believe the proposition if it were false in nearby scenarios**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief continuously tracks the truth in nearby possible scenarios, the proposition is true, and they would not believe it if it were false in nearby scenarios**
