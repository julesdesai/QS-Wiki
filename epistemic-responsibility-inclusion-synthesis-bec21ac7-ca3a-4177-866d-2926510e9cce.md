## Content

**A person knows a proposition if they have epistemic responsibility for the formation of their belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **A belief is safe if in nearby possible worlds where the belief is held responsibly, it remains true**
