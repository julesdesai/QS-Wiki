## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief in the proposition is reliably formed**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **The justification for the belief can include external factors not accessible to the believer**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief is reliably formed and the proposition is true**
