# Does the presence of intellectual virtue ensure belief truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 98d79709-0685-4f76-92c2-6245516fcad3
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-ffa55650-0795-4012-9f33-14517d86090e.md)

## Content

**A belief can be the result of an intellectual virtue and still be mistaken if it derives from misleading evidence.**, **Intuitively, the mere presence of virtue does not guarantee belief truth due to potential external factors.**, **If truth is essential to knowledge, virtues alone are insufficient for ensuring the accuracy of beliefs.**
