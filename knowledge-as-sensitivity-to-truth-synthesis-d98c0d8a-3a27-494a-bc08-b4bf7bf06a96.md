# Knowledge as Sensitivity to Truth

**Parent:** [Is there a causal connection between belief and fact in cases of shared knowledge?](is-there-a-causal-connection-between-belief-and-fact-in-cases-of-shared-knowledge-antithesis-91c80d17-918f-40bc-a174-05404eb6b998.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief would not be held if the proposition were false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and they would not believe the proposition if it were false**
