# Knowledge as Safe Process Reliabilism

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is the result of a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief could not easily have been false**, **A cognitive process is reliable if it yields a high ratio of true to false beliefs in the long run**
