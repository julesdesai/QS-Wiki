# Does truth-tracking disregard the role of understanding in knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e136bc5f-0d86-44ce-b2cb-11ee822c1384
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Nozick's Truth-Tracking Theory](nozicks-truth-tracking-theory-synthesis-1b212c88-0c4c-4dd5-88f4-3ea31a716dd5.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person gains a superficial truth-tracking of a fact without deeper comprehension. In such a scenario, intuitively, the person lacks full knowledge due to absence of understanding.**, **It is implausible that truth-tracking alone without engagement with understanding encapsulates the full rubric of knowledge.**
