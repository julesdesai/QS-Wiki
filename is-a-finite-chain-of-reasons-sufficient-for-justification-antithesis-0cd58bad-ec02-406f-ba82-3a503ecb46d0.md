# Is a finite chain of reasons sufficient for justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0cd58bad-ec02-406f-ba82-3a503ecb46d0
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism with a finite chain of reasons](reliabilism-with-a-finite-chain-of-reasons-synthesis-a3f7a107-c974-4a8e-a7f2-d20f4743454a.md)

## Content

**A finite chain of reasons might lead to an arbitrary stopping point in the justification process.**, **Without clear termination criteria, such a chain might leave the status of justified true belief unresolved.**, **If the endpoint of justification is arbitrary, it could compromise the robustness required for true knowledge.**
