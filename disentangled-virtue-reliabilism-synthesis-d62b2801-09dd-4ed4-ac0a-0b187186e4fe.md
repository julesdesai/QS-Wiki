## Content

**Knowledge is true belief formed through distinct, but complementary, roles of virtue and reliability**, **Intellectual virtues include character traits like open-mindedness and fairness**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **Reliability ensures the correctness of the belief**, **Intellectual virtues guide the process of belief formation without necessarily guaranteeing truth**
