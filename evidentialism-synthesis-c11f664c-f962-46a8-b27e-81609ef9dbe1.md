## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have adequate evidence for it**, **Adequate evidence consists of information that sufficiently supports the truth of the proposition**
