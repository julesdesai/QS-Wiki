# Can basic beliefs ever lead to unjustified complex beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 6cf3c4fc-136a-4124-a0ce-d2dd01cc8d7a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundationalism with Pragmatic Grounds](foundationalism-with-pragmatic-grounds-synthesis-35d5e861-b788-4e52-a02b-4488aba3b8d2.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that complex beliefs can only be justified if derived from justified basic beliefs. This principle together with deriving complex beliefs from unjustified basic beliefs entails unjustified acceptance of complex beliefs.**, **Consider a scenario where a complex belief about the world is inferred from basic beliefs formed through misinformation. Intuitively, we would judge that the complex belief is unjustified.**
