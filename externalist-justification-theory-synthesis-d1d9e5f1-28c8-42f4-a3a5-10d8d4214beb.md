## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified by factors external to the person's cognitive perspective**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, and the belief is justified by external factors, and the proposition is true**
