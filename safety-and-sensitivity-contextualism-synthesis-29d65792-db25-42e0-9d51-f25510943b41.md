## Content

**Knowledge depends on the context of the knower**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief in the proposition is both safe and sensitive to truth across nearby possible worlds**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **The standards for knowing a proposition vary with different contexts**, **Sensitivity requires that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**
