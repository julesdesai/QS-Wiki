# Knowledge as Reliable True Belief with Anti-luck Condition

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 9c3494c1-4f6d-42b5-8a87-53cde7d52ad4
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can reliable processes produce knowledge without true belief?](can-reliable-processes-produce-knowledge-without-true-belief-antithesis-9330ea70-458b-45fc-b9ab-b22a58294b6c.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the true belief is not due to epistemic luck which negates knowledge**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process, and the belief is not due to negating epistemic luck**
