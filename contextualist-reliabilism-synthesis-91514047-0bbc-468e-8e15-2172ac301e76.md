## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed by a reliable process**, **A person knows a proposition if they believe it**, **A person knows a proposition if the proposition is true**, **A reliable process is one that produces true beliefs more often than false ones**, **The reliability of a process depends on the context in which the belief is formed**
