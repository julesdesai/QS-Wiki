# Is knowledge limited to perceptual relations?

**Node ID:** ca1f5d87-03f7-4810-8c1c-98bc0ee2a197
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Epistemic Structuralism](epistemic-structuralism-synthesis-7fb538c7-d6b1-4a5d-b302-4f53eea0d56a.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that knowledge can be derived from testimony and not solely from individual perceptual experiences**, **Suppose a child learns about historical facts from teachers and books without direct perceptual experience of those events. Intuitively, we would judge that the child has knowledge of historical events through testimony, suggesting that knowledge is not limited to personal perceptual structures**
