# Knowledge as True Belief plus Safety

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Can knowledge exist without explicit acknowledgment of intellectual virtue?](can-knowledge-exist-without-explicit-acknowledgment-of-intellectual-virtue-antithesis-d197a4e5-4a01-46bb-bbef-0f42fcedc425.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is safe**, **For a belief to be safe, in nearby possible worlds where a person believes the proposition, the proposition is also true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is safe**
