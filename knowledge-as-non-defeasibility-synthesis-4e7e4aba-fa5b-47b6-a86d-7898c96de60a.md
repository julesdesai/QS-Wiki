# Knowledge as Non-Defeasibility

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 4e7e4aba-fa5b-47b6-a86d-7898c96de60a
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is it possible to have knowledge without believing the proposition?](is-it-possible-to-have-knowledge-without-believing-the-proposition-antithesis-e612195e-a180-47ea-bf01-13a356181a54.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that no defeaters exist for the belief in the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and no defeaters exist for the belief in the proposition**
