# Is it possible for an infinite chain of reasons to cause a regress problem?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 04350ad7-e92e-4669-a343-7b67d931aca5
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism with Foundational Beliefs](infinitism-with-foundational-beliefs-synthesis-4273f090-f358-492e-9e7c-904c2a039ab2.md)

## Content

**An infinite chain of reasons can lead to a regress problem where the justification for each belief depends on another belief ad infinitum.**, **In a scenario where each belief requires an additional belief for justification, we may never reach a point where justification is complete.**, **It is implausible that an infinite regress can serve as a sufficient basis for the justification of any belief.**
