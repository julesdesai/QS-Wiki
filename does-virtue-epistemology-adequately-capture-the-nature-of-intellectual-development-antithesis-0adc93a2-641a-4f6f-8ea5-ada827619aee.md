# Does virtue epistemology adequately capture the nature of intellectual development?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0adc93a2-641a-4f6f-8ea5-ada827619aee
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology View](virtue-epistemology-view-synthesis-31f81092-b7b3-48dd-9f17-87af421f7694.md)

## Content

**A person developing intellectual virtues might still form true beliefs early on through less reliable methods.**, **The process of cultivating intellectual virtues suggests variability in what is considered a reliable lead to truth.**, **Recognizing true beliefs as knowledge throughout intellectual development challenges the fixed application of virtue epistemology.**
