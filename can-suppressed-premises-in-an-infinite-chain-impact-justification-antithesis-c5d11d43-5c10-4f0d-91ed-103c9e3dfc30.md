**Suppose we have an infinite chain where some premises are unstated or presumed. Intuitively, we need to account for these premises to maintain the chain's integrity.**, **It is implausible to sustain justification when crucial links in an infinite support chain are missing or suppressed, as it undermines the continuity of the chain.**
