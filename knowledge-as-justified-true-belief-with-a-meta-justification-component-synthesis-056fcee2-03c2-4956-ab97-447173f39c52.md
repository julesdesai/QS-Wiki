# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with a Meta-Justification Component

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 056fcee2-03c2-4956-ab97-447173f39c52
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does knowledge require reflective endorsement of reliability?](does-knowledge-require-reflective-endorsement-of-reliability-antithesis-ba29d8e7-056a-4bfb-a256-7afd3e8aa2ff.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification that includes an understanding of why the belief-forming process is reliable**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification that includes understanding of reliability, and the proposition is true**
