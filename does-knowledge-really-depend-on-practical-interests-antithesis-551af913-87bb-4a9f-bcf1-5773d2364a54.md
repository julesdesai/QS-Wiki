# Does knowledge really depend on practical interests?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 551af913-87bb-4a9f-bcf1-5773d2364a54
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Subject-Sensitive Invariantism](subject-sensitive-invariantism-synthesis-687e35ad-b9eb-49bd-a730-d8bc9269ea3a.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that knowledge should remain stable across different contexts. This principle together with knowledge depending on practical interests entails that knowledge is unstable.**, **Practical interests can vary greatly between individuals without affecting the truth of a proposition, so it's implausible that they should affect knowledge.**
