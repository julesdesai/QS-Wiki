# Can knowledge persist without conscious access to evidence?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9e60b804-b624-40d2-998f-98b1c897cb5e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Evidentialist Reliabilism](evidentialist-reliabilism-synthesis-b2fd7651-b8ba-4bbd-aa9b-2c3c304a468b.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person has a reliable belief-forming process and a true belief, yet lacks conscious access to the evidence that underpins the belief.**, **Intuitively, the absence of conscious access to evidence seems to undermine the claim to knowledge, even if the belief is true and reliably formed.**
