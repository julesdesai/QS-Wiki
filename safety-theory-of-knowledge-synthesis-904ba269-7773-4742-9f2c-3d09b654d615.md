## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief could not easily have been false in similar situations**, **A belief is safe if in nearly all similar scenarios where the belief is held, it is true**
