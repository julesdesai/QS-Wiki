# Could critical assessment of perception still fail to provide knowledge of external objects?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** eaec6353-129c-4968-af4b-86cf3e3ebcb1
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Critical Realism](critical-realism-synthesis-a95d91b3-a4d2-4740-8b59-c24b26663299.md)

## Content

**If perceptual experiences can be wrong, critical assessment does not guarantee accurate knowledge.**, **Critical assessment requires criteria to differentiate true from false perceptions, but these can themselves be flawed.**, **Thus, critical assessment may not increase the reliability of knowing about external objects.**
