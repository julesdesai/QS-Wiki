# Are epistemic burdens on different individuals consistent?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** cf73b4a5-0df4-4d03-8cd6-ad24bf5211a3
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Invariantism for Knowledge Assessment](invariantism-for-knowledge-assessment-synthesis-0fb1590d-9ce0-4644-a80f-68ae37bc3da5.md)

## Content

**Consider the abilities and resources available to a novice and an expert in discerning whether a rare species is present in an area. Intuitively, the novice requires stricter scrutiny compared to the expert to claim knowledge.**, **It is implausible that the standards for knowing a proposition remain constant across individuals of varying expertise and abilities.**
