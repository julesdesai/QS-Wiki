# Safety Theory of Knowledge

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, their belief must be safe with respect to the truth of the proposition**, **A belief is safe if, in nearby possible worlds where the person believes the proposition in the same way, the proposition is not false**
