## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief has a reliable causal connection to the truth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is caused by the truth and the proposition is true**
