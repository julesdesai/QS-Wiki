# Can the belief condition alone ensure knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 6c7634c9-46fc-4538-b4af-d34743fc8c9e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Truth-Conditional Contextualism](truth-conditional-contextualism-synthesis-531a396e-31ae-4fe8-8636-7f56a6debb5b.md)

## Content

**A case in which a person firmly believes a false proposition is not a case in which that person possesses knowledge.**, **A case in which a person merely believes a true proposition without justification or understanding is intuitively not a case of knowledge.**
