# Knowledge as Sensitivity

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does belief based on reasoning from true premises always amount to knowledge?](does-belief-based-on-reasoning-from-true-premises-always-amount-to-knowledge-antithesis-a3257e0d-df5f-48ef-a3ce-853125175dac.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief in the proposition would not occur if the proposition were false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief in the proposition would not occur if the proposition were false**
