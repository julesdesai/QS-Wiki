# Does over-reliance on external aids undermine knowledge claims?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 58e24744-33d9-481b-a61a-13411a4cd67f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-5b73def0-7386-47a1-9b12-ba7bdc34b62a.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person's true belief heavily depends on external aids, like technology, rather than their own intellectual capabilities. Intuitively, we may doubt their claim to knowledge as it is heavily reliant on external support.**, **A true belief heavily reliant on external aids challenges the requirement for a person's belief to result from their own intellectual capabilities.**
