# Is having true belief alone enough for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** bc8a1f64-9472-40a5-9930-468b6a83cd08
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Evidentialism Summary](evidentialism-summary-synthesis-dea02623-290c-44c3-896b-2f967081eb1b.md)

## Content

**A case where a person guesses the outcome of a coin flip correctly would lead us to intuitively judge they have true belief but not knowledge.**, **There exist scenarios where individuals hold beliefs that happen to be true yet are formed on the basis of flawed methods, suggesting that true belief alone is insufficient for knowledge.**
