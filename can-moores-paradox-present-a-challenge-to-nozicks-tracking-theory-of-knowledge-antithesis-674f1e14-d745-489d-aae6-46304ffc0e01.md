# Can Moore's Paradox present a challenge to Nozick's Tracking Theory of knowledge?

**Terminal:** No

## Content

**Moore's Paradox involves a person asserting "It is raining, but I don't believe it is raining", which suggests a belief issue but whose content can be true.**, **Tracking conditions do not address the paradoxical nature of believing a proposition while potentially holding an equally false belief about the belief itself.**, **Moore's Paradox reveals that knowledge involves more than belief tracking truth, as it concerns coherence between belief and self-awareness.**
