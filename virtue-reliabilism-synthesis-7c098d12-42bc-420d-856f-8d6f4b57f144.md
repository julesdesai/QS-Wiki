## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is an exercise of intellectual virtue**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is an exercise of intellectual virtue**
