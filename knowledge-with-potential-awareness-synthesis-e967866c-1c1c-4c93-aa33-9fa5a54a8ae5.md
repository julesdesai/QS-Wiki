# Knowledge with Potential Awareness

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they are potentially aware of the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is non-accidentally true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they are potentially aware of the belief, the belief is non-accidentally true, and the proposition is true**
