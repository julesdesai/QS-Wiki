# Sensitivity Condition: Belief Sensitivity to Truth

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 83618d0f-2aed-4f21-9737-8030e31d9271
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is self-deceptive belief causally connected?](is-self-deceptive-belief-causally-connected-antithesis-f6163fdd-c1bb-43b6-a1ab-6ed65bfc4bc8.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **Knowledge requires that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **A person's belief is sensitive if it would not be held if the proposition were not true**
