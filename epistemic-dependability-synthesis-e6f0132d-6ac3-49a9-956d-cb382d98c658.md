## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **Knowledge requires that the belief is formed through dependable epistemic faculties**, **Epistemic faculties are dependable if they operate consistently under normal conditions and are not prone to systematic error**
