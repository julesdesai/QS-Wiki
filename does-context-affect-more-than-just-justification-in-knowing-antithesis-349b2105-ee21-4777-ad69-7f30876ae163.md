# Does context affect more than just justification in knowing?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 349b2105-ee21-4777-ad69-7f30876ae163
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Moderate Contextualism: Contexts Influence Justification, Not Knowledge](moderate-contextualism-contexts-influence-justification-not-knowledge-synthesis-be3d790f-5519-485d-8f0d-32864e4a3e9e.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case where the context involves differing consequences of being wrong about a belief; intuitively, we would judge that this affects whether one knows, not just how justified one is.**, **There is a plausible principle that context also influences what counts as knowledge, not merely justification.**, **Claiming that only justification and not knowledge standards vary with context ignores the broader effects of context.**
