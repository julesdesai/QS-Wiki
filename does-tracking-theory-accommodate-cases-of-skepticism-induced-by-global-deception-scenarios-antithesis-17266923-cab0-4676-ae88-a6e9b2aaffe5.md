# Does tracking theory accommodate cases of skepticism induced by global deception scenarios?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 17266923-cab0-4676-ae88-a6e9b2aaffe5
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Tracking Theory of Knowledge](tracking-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-d50e12e5-c945-480c-9bf6-a71471f277b2.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person is in a simulated reality like the Matrix, unaware of their actual environment. Intuitively, all ordinary knowledge claims about their environment should be false because their beliefs do not track external truths.**, **Suppose a theory posits truth-tracking across scenarios for knowledge acquisition. In global deception scenarios, even reliable observations fail to track truth, challenging the theory's capacity to account for genuine knowledge in ordinary circumstances.**
