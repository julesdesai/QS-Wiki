# Knowledge as Causal Connection

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 1fb195eb-8dde-4a6a-9d95-734492667101
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can belief be formed independently of truth?](can-belief-be-formed-independently-of-truth-antithesis-2414c1e5-898c-45ae-8e81-b4623f326263.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is causally connected to the fact the proposition represents**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is causally connected to the fact the proposition represents**
