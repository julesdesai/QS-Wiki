# Dual-Level Justificatory Knowledge

**Parent:** [Is awareness over-emphasized in knowing?](is-awareness-over-emphasized-in-knowing-antithesis-b24a8258-467e-4d9d-a843-3eca90142801.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and have either a conscious or a non-conscious justificatory process supporting their belief**
