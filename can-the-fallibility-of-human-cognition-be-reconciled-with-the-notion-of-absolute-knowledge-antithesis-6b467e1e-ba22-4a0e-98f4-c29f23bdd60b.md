# Can the fallibility of human cognition be reconciled with the notion of absolute knowledge?

**Node ID:** 6b467e1e-ba22-4a0e-98f4-c29f23bdd60b
**Depth:** 4

## Content

**It is implausible that human cognition, inherently fallible, can yield absolute, infallible knowledge, questioning the adequacy of justified true belief to account for human error and deception.**
