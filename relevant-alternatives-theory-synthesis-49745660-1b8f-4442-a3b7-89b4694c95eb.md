## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary to be able to rule out relevant alternatives where the proposition would be false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient to believe the proposition and be able to rule out relevant alternatives**
