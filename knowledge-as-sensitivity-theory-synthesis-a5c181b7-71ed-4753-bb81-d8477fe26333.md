# Knowledge as Sensitivity Theory

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** a5c181b7-71ed-4753-bb81-d8477fe26333
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can false beliefs ever track truth?](can-false-beliefs-ever-track-truth-antithesis-c664d870-cd01-4ed9-84bc-a093fe69399a.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the person meets all the necessary conditions, where their belief is formed through a reliable process sensitive to the truth**
