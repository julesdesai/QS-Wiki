# Are animals capable of knowledge under this theory?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 8d6e5bb8-9295-4fc5-9b36-9612b37d6669
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Nozick’s Truth-Tracking Theory](nozicks-truth-tracking-theory-synthesis-5a62ad60-20de-4329-b52b-21f59b8da1c1.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which an animal tracks environmental truths instinctively but without conscious belief or reasoning. Intuitively, it seems the animal can still be said to have knowledge of its environment, raising questions about whether conscious belief is a necessary component of knowledge even if the animal satisfies truth-tracking conditions in a non-cognitive way.**
