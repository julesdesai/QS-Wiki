## Content

**Knowledge is primarily determined by factors external to the individual's mental states**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief in the proposition is produced by a reliable belief-forming process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **The standards for knowing a proposition involve the reliability of the belief-forming process, not the context of the individual**
