## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a proposition to be known, the belief must be true in nearby possible worlds**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe it and it remains true in similar scenarios without being affected by environmental luck**
