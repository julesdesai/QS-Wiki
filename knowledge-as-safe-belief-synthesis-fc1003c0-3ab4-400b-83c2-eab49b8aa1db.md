# Knowledge as Safe Belief

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** fc1003c0-3ab4-400b-83c2-eab49b8aa1db
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can someone exhibit intellectual virtues without knowledge?](can-someone-exhibit-intellectual-virtues-without-knowledge-antithesis-d43ce30c-d689-4908-ab5f-6fcd26b5b4a3.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief could not easily have been false**, **A belief is safe if in nearby possible worlds where the person believes the proposition, it is true**
