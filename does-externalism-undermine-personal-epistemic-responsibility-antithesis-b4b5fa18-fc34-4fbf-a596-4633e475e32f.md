# Does externalism undermine personal epistemic responsibility?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b4b5fa18-fc34-4fbf-a596-4633e475e32f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalism](externalism-synthesis-9fb6627e-b63e-4d81-a81a-128eb1da4172.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that individuals are epistemically responsible for their beliefs only if they have control or influence over the justification of those beliefs.**, **If knowledge and justification depend only on external factors beyond a believer's control or influence, it is implausible that the believer is fulfilling their epistemic duties.**
