**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **A person tracks the truth if, were the proposition false, they would not believe it**, **A person tracks the truth if, were the proposition true, they would believe it**
