# Do logically inferred beliefs that truth-track via reliable processes always equate to knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ce028de9-5b7e-4488-845b-848c8bbc49ba
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Process Reliabilism with Nozick's Condition](process-reliabilism-with-nozicks-condition-synthesis-5d350bc0-f5e6-4b33-b9c5-feeee7d84d21.md)

## Content

**A person infers a complex belief logically from several assumptions that are reliably formed but not themselves known or tested. Intuitively, it is not clear that mere logical deduction assures knowledge.**, **It is implausible that reliable logical deductions founded on unverified premises constitute knowledge.**
