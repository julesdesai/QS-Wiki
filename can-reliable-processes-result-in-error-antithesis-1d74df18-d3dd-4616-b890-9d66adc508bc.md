# Can reliable processes result in error?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1d74df18-d3dd-4616-b890-9d66adc508bc
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology Summary](virtue-epistemology-summary-synthesis-10eec969-c917-4d6d-bc9d-ac9f02dc3f98.md)

## Content

**Consider a situation where a person's belief is formed through a generally reliable cognitive process but, due to unusual circumstances, the belief is false.**, **A belief process can be virtuous yet yield an incorrect conclusion due to unforeseen variables.**, **The reliability of a process doesn't ensure infallibility, challenging the link between reliable processes and knowledge.**
