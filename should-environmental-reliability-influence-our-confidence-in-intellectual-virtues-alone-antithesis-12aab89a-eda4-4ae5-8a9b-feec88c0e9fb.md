# Should environmental reliability influence our confidence in intellectual virtues alone?

## Content

**There is an intuition, given by certain cases, that intellectual virtue should not suffice for knowledge if the environment is generally misleading, even if not demonstrably so in the instance.**, **In environments where genuine feedback is obscured, the role of virtue seems compromised, undermining its sufficiency for knowledge acquisition.**
