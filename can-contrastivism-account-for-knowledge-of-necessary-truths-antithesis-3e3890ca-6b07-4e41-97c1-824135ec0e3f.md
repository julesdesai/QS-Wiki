# Can contrastivism account for knowledge of necessary truths?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3e3890ca-6b07-4e41-97c1-824135ec0e3f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contrastivism](contrastivism-synthesis-b472000e-958d-4f0b-baf2-3d82da5c8cf3.md)

## Content

**Necessary truths, by definition, do not allow for alternatives, yet people claim to know necessary truths, such as mathematical axioms and logical tautologies.**, **If knowing involves contrasting with alternatives, then knowing necessary truths should be impossible, but common intuitions affirm that we know necessary truths regardless.**
