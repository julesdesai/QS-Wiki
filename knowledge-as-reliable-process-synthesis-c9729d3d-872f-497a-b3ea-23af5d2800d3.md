# Knowledge as Reliable Process

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** c9729d3d-872f-497a-b3ea-23af5d2800d3
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is reflexive self-assurance sufficient for knowledge?](is-reflexive-self-assurance-sufficient-for-knowledge-antithesis-306708d8-a25e-4610-bf6d-dd92e9ec05fc.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified by a reliable process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is justified by a reliable process**
