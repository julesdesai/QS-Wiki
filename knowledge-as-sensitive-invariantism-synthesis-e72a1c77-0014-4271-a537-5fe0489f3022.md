# Knowledge as Sensitive Invariantism

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** e72a1c77-0014-4271-a537-5fe0489f3022
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can coherence ever override truth?](can-coherence-ever-override-truth-antithesis-0ff1b178-16c3-444c-807e-72a9d217ea5f.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person would not believe the proposition if it were false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, the proposition is true, and they would not believe it if it were false**
