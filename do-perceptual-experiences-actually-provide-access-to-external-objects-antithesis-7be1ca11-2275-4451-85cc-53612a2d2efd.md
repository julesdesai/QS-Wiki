# Do perceptual experiences actually provide access to external objects?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7be1ca11-2275-4451-85cc-53612a2d2efd
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Critical Realism](critical-realism-synthesis-a95d91b3-a4d2-4740-8b59-c24b26663299.md)

## Content

**Perceptual experiences could be systematically deceiving, as in scenarios involving perfect hallucinations.**, **In cases of perfect hallucinations, perceptual experiences do not provide access to external objects.**, **If perceptual experiences do not provide access to external objects, then they cannot be considered reliable sources for knowledge of external objects.**
