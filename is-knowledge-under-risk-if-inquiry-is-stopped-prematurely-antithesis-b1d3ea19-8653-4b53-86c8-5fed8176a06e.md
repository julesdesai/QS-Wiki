# Is knowledge under risk if inquiry is stopped prematurely?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b1d3ea19-8653-4b53-86c8-5fed8176a06e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Finite Infinitism](finite-infinitism-synthesis-ec56b28f-9687-4f6e-b141-48dc2e075636.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person stops inquiry based on practical limitations before satisfying the infinite justification requirement. Intuitively, this halts potential acquisition of knowledge.**, **There is a plausible principle that premature end to inquiry can result in incomplete justification, undermining claims to knowledge.**, **Halting inquiry due to practical purposes contradicts the infinite justification requirement necessary for proper knowledge.**
