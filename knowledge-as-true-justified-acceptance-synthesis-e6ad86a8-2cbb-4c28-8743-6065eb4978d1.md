# Knowledge as True Justified Acceptance

**Parent:** [Can a useful but erroneous belief qualify as knowledge?](can-a-useful-but-erroneous-belief-qualify-as-knowledge-antithesis-f229eac6-d9d3-40fc-8e63-e8ac933f8b51.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary to accept it as true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the acceptance be justified**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they accept the proposition as true, the proposition is true, and the acceptance is justified**
