# Moderate Externalist Conception of Knowledge

**Parent:** [Can knowledge withstand all possible contrary evidence due to external factors?](can-knowledge-withstand-all-possible-contrary-evidence-due-to-external-factors-antithesis-7f84d6ac-872e-4a0d-9e06-2704de2cf981.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is justified by external factors that remain stable despite ordinary contrary evidence**
