## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no true propositions that would undermine their justification for believing it**, **The belief in the proposition should have no defeaters that make the justification inadequate**
