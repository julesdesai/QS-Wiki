# Knowledge as Defeasibility Model

**Parent:** [What happens if intellectual virtue leads to conflicting beliefs?](what-happens-if-intellectual-virtue-leads-to-conflicting-beliefs-antithesis-0e69376f-5b36-4440-86e2-e79a6958291e.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their justification is undefeated by any true proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their justification is undefeated by any true proposition**
