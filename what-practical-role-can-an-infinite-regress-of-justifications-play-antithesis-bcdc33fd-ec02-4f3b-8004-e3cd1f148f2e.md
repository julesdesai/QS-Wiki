# What practical role can an infinite regress of justifications play?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** bcdc33fd-ec02-4f3b-8004-e3cd1f148f2e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism Summary](infinitism-summary-synthesis-17c5b8c2-c7de-43ec-bf90-6dedae4ad235.md)

## Content

**For a theory of justification to be useful, it must apply to actual reasoning and decision-making practices.**, **An infinite regress entails a level of complexity that seems unmanageable in practical terms.**, **It is implausible that a practically effective justification could rely on an unattainable infinite justification process.**
