# Can we ever complete the infinite chain of reasons required for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** cce3f4d2-9de7-46b5-adf7-8bd7d9903f6e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism](infinitism-synthesis-fd5e1271-f2df-4333-a434-50b9d95350fd.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that any human being can have an actual infinite chain of reasons in mind because human cognitive capacities are finite.**, **An infinite chain of reasons defies practical verification because individuals cannot verify or examine an infinite sequence.**, **If the chain of reasons must be infinite, then any claim to knowledge seems unattainable because we cannot practically or finitely create or understand such a chain.**
