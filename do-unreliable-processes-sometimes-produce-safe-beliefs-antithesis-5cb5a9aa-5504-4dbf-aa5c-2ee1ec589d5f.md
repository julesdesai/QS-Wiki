# Do unreliable processes sometimes produce safe beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5cb5a9aa-5504-4dbf-aa5c-2ee1ec589d5f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Safety Condition View](safety-condition-view-synthesis-739cc2e2-5111-4640-9af6-f9324f258620.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person forms a belief about the outcome of a sports game by listening to a highly exaggerating commentator. Intuitively, the process is unreliable in forming true beliefs.**, **In nearby possible worlds, the belief remains true based on coincidental outcomes, suggesting safety of the belief despite originating from an unreliable process.**
