# Do intellectual virtues necessarily involve motivational components?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 4b440fbb-458c-4e7b-8ed4-073fa313714b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology With Virtuous Belief Formation](virtue-epistemology-with-virtuous-belief-formation-synthesis-53b228ae-7464-400d-be01-037da588b5a0.md)

## Content

**Consider a scholar who pursues truth not for its own sake but for financial gain, yet reliably arrives at true beliefs through sophisticated methods.**, **The belief is the product of cognitive achievement with correct outcomes, but the underlying motivation is misaligned with epistemic virtues.**, **Intuitively, when motivations are non-epistemic, we question if attainment should be considered knowledge despite truth and reliability.**
