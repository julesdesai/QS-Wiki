# What about the role of interpretation in perception?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e2a8fd6f-206a-4411-a8b5-d54f0c2639f8
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Naïve Realism](naïve-realism-synthesis-25d0b7e3-6f66-46c3-a45d-bb41c1dbe70b.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where unclear images or figures are perceived, such as the famous "duck-rabbit" illustration that can be seen as either. Intuitively we would judge that interpretation significantly influences perception, which complicates the idea of direct perception without intermediaries.**
