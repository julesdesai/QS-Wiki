# Does sensitivity guarantee that one truly knows?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 014affee-1e98-480a-b18c-6916527fe89a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Sensitivity Theory of Knowledge](sensitivity-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-ba84f32c-948c-4607-9039-09527bd88c50.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that knowledge requires a reliable process of belief formation. This principle together with a case where a belief is sensitive but formed through an unreliable process entails that the belief may not qualify as knowledge.**
