# Knowledge as Defeasibility-Based True Belief

**Depth:** 3

**Parent:** [Does the view handle Gettier cases of justified true belief with no knowledge?](does-the-view-handle-gettier-cases-of-justified-true-belief-with-no-knowledge-antithesis-98ab2526-9941-4cab-b217-e2e937b9b360.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief could not be defused by any additional true proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief could not be defeated by any further true proposition**
