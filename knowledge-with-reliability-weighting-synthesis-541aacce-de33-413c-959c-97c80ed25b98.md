# Knowledge with reliability weighting

**Parent:** [Does implicit trust in evidence count as a defeater?](does-implicit-trust-in-evidence-count-as-a-defeater-antithesis-e7c8ada3-b098-4691-8c69-89d716c66e81.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief stems from a process weighted toward reliable outcomes**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief stems from a process weighted toward reliable outcomes**
