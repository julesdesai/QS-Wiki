## Content

**Knowledge does not depend on the context of the knower**, **A person knows a proposition if the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **Context influences the level of justification needed but not whether someone knows**
