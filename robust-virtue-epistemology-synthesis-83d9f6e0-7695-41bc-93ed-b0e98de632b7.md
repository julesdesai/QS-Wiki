## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed through the exercise of intellectual virtue**, **An intellectual virtue involves a reliable cognitive process informed by an understanding of why the proposition is true**
