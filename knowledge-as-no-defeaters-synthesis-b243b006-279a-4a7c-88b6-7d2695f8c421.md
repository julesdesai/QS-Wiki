## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, there must be no defeaters present that undermine the belief**, **A defeater is information that would nullify justification for the belief**
