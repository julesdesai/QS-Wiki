## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it must be the case that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it must be the case that if the proposition is true, the person believes it**, **A belief tracks the truth across nearby possible scenarios**
