# How does contextualism address the challenge of novice knowers?

## Content

**Imagine a novice in a field who claims to know a concept based on a limited understanding contextualized by simplified discussions. Intuitively, this scenario raises questions on whether the contextual ease improperly elevates novice claims to knowledge.**, **Contextualism suggests knowledge claims are altered by conversational scope, potentially inflating novice comprehension beyond their actual epistemic status.**
