## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is an appropriate causal connection between the belief and the fact that makes the proposition true**, **The belief must be causally dependent on the truth of the proposition**
