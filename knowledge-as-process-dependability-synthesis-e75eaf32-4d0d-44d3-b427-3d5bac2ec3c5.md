# Knowledge as Process Dependability

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Does knowledge require more than belief in nearby possible worlds?](does-knowledge-require-more-than-belief-in-nearby-possible-worlds-antithesis-6d2d87a7-dd41-4971-81f4-adf0d5b97a22.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the process forming the belief consistently leads to true beliefs across a range of scenarios**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is consistently produced by a dependable process**
