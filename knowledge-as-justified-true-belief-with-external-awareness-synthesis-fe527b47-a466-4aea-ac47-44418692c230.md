## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **Awareness can be derived from external factors rather than internal consciousness for knowledge**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification, and the proposition is true**
