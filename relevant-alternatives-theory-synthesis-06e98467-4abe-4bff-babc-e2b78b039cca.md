## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they can rule out all relevant alternatives to the proposition**, **Relevance of alternatives does not vary with different contexts**
