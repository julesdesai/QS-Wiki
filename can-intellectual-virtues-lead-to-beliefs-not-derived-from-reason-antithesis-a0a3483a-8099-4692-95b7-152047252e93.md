# Can intellectual virtues lead to beliefs not derived from reason?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a0a3483a-8099-4692-95b7-152047252e93
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-f60d76d9-bfb9-483c-b262-fa52975172be.md)

## Content

**Imagine someone with the intellectual virtue of creativity, which helps them construct plausible but entirely speculative theories.**, **These theories might sometimes coincidentally align with the truth without being based on evidence or reason, making it questionable whether they constitute knowledge.**
