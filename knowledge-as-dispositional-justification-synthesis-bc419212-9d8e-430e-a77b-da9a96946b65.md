# Knowledge as Dispositional Justification

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does knowledge require immediate awareness of justification?](does-knowledge-require-immediate-awareness-of-justification-antithesis-2249f6ec-85c8-42af-b9bc-4c287a81c220.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is underpinned by dispositional awareness of the justification for believing**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that, if prompted, they could become aware of the justification for their belief**
