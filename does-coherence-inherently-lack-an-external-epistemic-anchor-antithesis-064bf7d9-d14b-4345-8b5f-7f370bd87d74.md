# Does coherence inherently lack an external epistemic anchor?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 064bf7d9-d14b-4345-8b5f-7f370bd87d74
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitist Coherentism](infinitist-coherentism-synthesis-c961c647-ce7e-4676-a333-4ee929ef8887.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that coherence in isolation provides an external anchor or foundation for knowledge, as it depends on internal consistency rather than external validation.**, **Consider a tightly knit community with shared beliefs that are coherent among members but disconnected from reality. Intuitively, we would judge these shared beliefs to lack knowledge due to missing external reference.**
