# Does Nozick's account handle Lotteries?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ab333b65-a561-4a6d-b99b-dcb818ac1d74
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Nozick's Conditional Reliabilism](nozicks-conditional-reliabilism-synthesis-29b3c3e0-b3ff-4b45-a994-893f15be46b2.md)

## Content

**Consider a person who holds a lottery ticket in a fair lottery and believes they will lose. While their belief is statistically justified, intuitively this is not knowledge.**, **In Nozick's view, the belief that they will lose tracks the truth if they would continue to hold it as winning is extremely unlikely, but this certainty might not track in conditions where rare events occur.**, **Relying on statistical improbabilities for belief-tracking might unjustly elevate statistical predictions to the status of knowledge.**
