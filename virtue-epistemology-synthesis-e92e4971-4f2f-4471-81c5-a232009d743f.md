## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they possess intellectual virtues that help in forming the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is formed through intellectual virtues**
