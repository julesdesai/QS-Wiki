# Is coherentism a viable alternative to causal grounding?

## Content

**Suppose a coherent system of beliefs is formed without external causal input, like a purely mathematical system. Intuitively, this system can be internally justified without external causes.**, **There is a plausible principle that internal coherence can justify belief systems independently of external causal inputs, challenging the necessity of causal grounding.**
