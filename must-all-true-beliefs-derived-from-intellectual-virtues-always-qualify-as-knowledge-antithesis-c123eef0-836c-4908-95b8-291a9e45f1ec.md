# Must all true beliefs derived from intellectual virtues always qualify as knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c123eef0-836c-4908-95b8-291a9e45f1ec

**Parent:** [Knowledge as Virtue Epistemology](knowledge-as-virtue-epistemology-synthesis-d19acabb-013f-4278-8a52-84aea003e41a.md)

## Content

**Suppose a student who exercises their intellectual virtues derives a mathematically true hypothesis, yet lacks the proper conceptual understanding of the hypothesis.**, **True belief derived from intellectual virtues without conceptual understanding does not constitute knowledge.**
