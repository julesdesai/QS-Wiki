## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief to be grounded in foundational beliefs that are justified independently of context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is grounded in independently justified foundational beliefs**
