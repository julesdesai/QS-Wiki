# Can reliability alone account for all cases of knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5822ffe8-a0b6-4b63-a5e4-a127b3d68094
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism](reliabilism-synthesis-4db07ebc-4cb3-402b-b335-2b01121c3e1a.md)

## Content

**Imagine a situation where a person uses a reliable process but has no understanding of why their belief is true. Intuitively, we might hesitate to say they have knowledge because they lack insight or understanding**, **It is implausible that mere reliability, devoid of any insight or comprehension, suffices for knowledge**
