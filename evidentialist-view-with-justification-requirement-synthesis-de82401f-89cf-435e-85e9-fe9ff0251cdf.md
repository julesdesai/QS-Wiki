# Evidentialist View with Justification Requirement

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does conviction necessarily align with sufficient evidence for knowledge?](does-conviction-necessarily-align-with-sufficient-evidence-for-knowledge-antithesis-0188fa32-7373-45e1-b730-cf996c26a996.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have access to evidence supporting the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief in the proposition is appropriately justified based on the evidence**
