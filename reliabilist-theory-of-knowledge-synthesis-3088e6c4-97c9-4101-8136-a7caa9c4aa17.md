## Content

**A person knows a proposition if their belief in the proposition is reliably formed**, **The reliability of a belief is determined by the process through which it is formed**, **For a belief to be reliable, it must be formed by a process that consistently leads to truth**, **The reliability of the belief formation process is independent of the individual's awareness of its reliability**
