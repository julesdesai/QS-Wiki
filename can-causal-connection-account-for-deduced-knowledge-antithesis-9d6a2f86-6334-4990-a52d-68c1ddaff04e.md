# Can causal connection account for deduced knowledge?

**Parent:** [Causal Theory of Knowledge](causal-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-9ece6694-b5cf-4143-897d-321cf0165994.md)

## Content

**Imagine person P deduces the truth of proposition Q from proposition P, which P knows is true. The deduction does not involve a causal connection between Q's truth and P's belief in Q, questioning the necessity of such a link in deduction.**, **Deductive reasoning provides knowledge without directly involving causation.**
