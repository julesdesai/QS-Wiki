# Does intersubjective agreement reliably lead to truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 230b862b-5376-46ba-b496-9948acc9428b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Intersubjective Phenomenalism](intersubjective-phenomenalism-synthesis-0a89d160-2bee-479f-b8d3-046de6da5ef6.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that knowledge requires correspondence to objective reality. This principle together with the idea that knowledge arises only from intersubjective agreement entails that intersubjective agreement must always correspond to objective reality.**, **It is implausible that intersubjective agreement, without objective verification, guarantees the truth of a proposition.**
