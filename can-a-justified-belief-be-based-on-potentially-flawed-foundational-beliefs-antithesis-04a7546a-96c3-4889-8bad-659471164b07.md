# Can a justified belief be based on potentially flawed foundational beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 04a7546a-96c3-4889-8bad-659471164b07
**Depth:** 2
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundationalism](foundationalism-thesis-fd868af7-578e-400a-bb27-265f88ced5ee.md)

## Content

**A case in which a person believes a proposition due to a foundational belief that is later proven false is not a case in which their belief remains justified.**, **There is a plausible principle that justification should depend on the reliability and truth of foundational beliefs.**
