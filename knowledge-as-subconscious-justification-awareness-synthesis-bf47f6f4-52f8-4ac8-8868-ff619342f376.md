## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have subconscious awareness of the justification**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient if their subconscious awareness of the justification is accurate and reliable**
