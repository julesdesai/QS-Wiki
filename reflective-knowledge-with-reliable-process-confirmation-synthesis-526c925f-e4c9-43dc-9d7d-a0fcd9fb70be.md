## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief results from a reliable process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person can confirm the reliability of the process leading to their belief**
