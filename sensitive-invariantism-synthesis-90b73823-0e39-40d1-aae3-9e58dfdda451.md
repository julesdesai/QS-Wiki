## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief to be reasonably justified given practical concerns**, **Practical concerns include stakes but not as a determiner of context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have reasonable justification, and the proposition is true**
