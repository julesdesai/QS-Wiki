# Embodied Knowledge View

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 958c409b-bd42-4d1b-a74a-eab238a57a99
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is belief always necessary for knowledge?](is-belief-always-necessary-for-knowledge-antithesis-816fbfa5-3c54-46a1-9fca-52e207accc69.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their bodily orientations or actions are aligned with the proposition in a way that does not require explicit belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is true and the individual's bodily states align with it in a meaningful way**
