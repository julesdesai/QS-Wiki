## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have evidence justifying their belief**, **The quality and sufficiency of evidence is judged within the context of inquiry**, **What counts as justifying evidence can change with the context**
