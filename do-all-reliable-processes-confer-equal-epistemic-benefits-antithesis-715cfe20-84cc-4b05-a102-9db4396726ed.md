# Do all reliable processes confer equal epistemic benefits?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 715cfe20-84cc-4b05-a102-9db4396726ed
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism](reliabilism-synthesis-41e7a61f-ad45-42cd-8dd7-ba40ca519ba9.md)

## Content

**Different reliable processes have varying degrees of reliability.**, **Consider a process that is reliable in most contexts but fails in critical ones.**, **If not all reliable processes confer equal benefits, then merely being reliable is insufficient to consistently confer knowledge.**
