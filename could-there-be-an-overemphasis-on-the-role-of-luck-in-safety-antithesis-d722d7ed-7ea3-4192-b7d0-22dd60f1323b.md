# Could there be an overemphasis on the role of luck in safety?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d722d7ed-7ea3-4192-b7d0-22dd60f1323b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [The Safety Condition](the-safety-condition-synthesis-1c250b1c-da7f-4cab-a10a-178f18915234.md)

## Content

**Luck might affect whether a belief is true or false.**, **A belief can be both safely held and occasionally true due to luck rather than reliable processes.**, **If luck influences the status of belief without affecting its safety, then safety might not adequately address the role of luck in knowledge**
