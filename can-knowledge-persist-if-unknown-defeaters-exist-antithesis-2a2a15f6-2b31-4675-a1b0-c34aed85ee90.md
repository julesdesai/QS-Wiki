# Can knowledge persist if unknown defeaters exist?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2a2a15f6-2b31-4675-a1b0-c34aed85ee90
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology with Defeasibility](virtue-epistemology-with-defeasibility-synthesis-802a6edd-3a94-41b9-8765-bb5c975aae31.md)

## Content

**Imagine a situation where an individual holds a true belief born of intellectual virtue, but there are unknown defeaters that, if known, would undermine the belief. This intuitively questions whether the person really knows the proposition.**, **For genuine knowledge, the absence of any defeaters must coincide with the awareness and consideration of potential contrary evidence.**
