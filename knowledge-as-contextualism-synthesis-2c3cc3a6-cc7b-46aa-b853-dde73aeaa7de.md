## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, the standards for 'knowing' vary with different contexts**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they meet the contextually determined standards for justification and the proposition is true**
