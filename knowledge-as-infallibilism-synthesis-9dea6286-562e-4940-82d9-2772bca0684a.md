## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there be no possible situation in which they believe the proposition and it is false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and they cannot be mistaken**
