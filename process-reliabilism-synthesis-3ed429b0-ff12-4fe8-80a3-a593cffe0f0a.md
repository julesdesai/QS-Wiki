## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed via a cognitive process that reliably produces true beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is not necessary that they consciously endorse the belief**
