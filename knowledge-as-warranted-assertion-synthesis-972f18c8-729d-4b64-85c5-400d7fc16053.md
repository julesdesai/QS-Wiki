# Knowledge as warranted assertion

**Parent:** [Does contextualism lead to confusion in everyday knowledge claims?](does-contextualism-lead-to-confusion-in-everyday-knowledge-claims-antithesis-5e70fc87-ee77-4bb7-be5e-a7b23e591da7.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they can warrant their assertion of the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and they can warrant their assertion**
