## Content

**For a belief to constitute knowledge, it must be produced by cognitive faculties that are functioning reliably in their current context**, **Reliably functioning cognitive faculties are those that can adjust and respond to present environmental demands effectively**, **The cognitive faculties must consistently aim at true beliefs given present challenges**, **A belief formed in these conditions constitutes knowledge if it is true**
