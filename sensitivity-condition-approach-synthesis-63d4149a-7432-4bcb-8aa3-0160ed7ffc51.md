## Content

**Knowledge is a true belief sensitive to the truth across possible worlds**, **A belief is sensitive if, in the closest possible worlds where the proposition is false, the person does not believe it**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief is both true and sensitive**, **For a belief to be considered knowledge, cases where a belief is insensitive and true by luck are excluded**
