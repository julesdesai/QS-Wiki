# Can emotions, which influence perceptions, be included in the baseline?

**Node ID:** 25d1a6d8-12d2-46fb-a37d-21a2efe26564

## Content

**Emotional states can significantly alter an individual's perception of events or objects.**, **Consider a case where a person perceives a neutral facial expression as hostile when in a negative emotional state.**, **If emotions can alter perceptions, the stability and universality of a perceptual baseline are questionable.**
