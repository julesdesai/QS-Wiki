# Knowledge as Inferential Justification

**Parent:** [Is the requirement for truth too stringent for practical knowledge claims?](is-the-requirement-for-truth-too-stringent-for-practical-knowledge-claims-antithesis-bb72843a-da43-4c5d-a125-36ace2d2fb63.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification involves inference from other justified beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and it is justified by inferential beliefs**
