# Empirical Virtue Epistemology

**Parent:** [Can a belief from intellectual virtues align with truth solely by coincidence?](can-a-belief-from-intellectual-virtues-align-with-truth-solely-by-coincidence-antithesis-f06fff93-d31a-4caa-8752-ed9142596bd9.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is a result of the person's intellectual virtues**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief corresponds to empirical observations and experiences**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is a result of the person's intellectual virtues, and the belief corresponds to empirical observations and experiences**
