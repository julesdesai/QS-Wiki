## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is justified through reliance on trustworthy social institutions and practices**, **Social institutions and practices provide a framework for verifying the truth and justification of beliefs**
