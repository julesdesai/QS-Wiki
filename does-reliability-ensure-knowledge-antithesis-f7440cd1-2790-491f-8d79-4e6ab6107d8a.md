# Does reliability ensure knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f7440cd1-2790-491f-8d79-4e6ab6107d8a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-d7a894af-29e5-42fe-8e5e-957e2009566d.md)

## Content

**Consider a person who frequently uses a horoscope to predict future events, and by chance, the predictions are often accurate, leading to true beliefs.**, **The reliance on a horoscope is not a reliable method, yet it may produce true beliefs through coincidence, suggesting reliability alone does not ensure knowledge.**
