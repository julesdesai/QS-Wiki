# Is truth a necessary condition for knowledge within contextual infinitism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 41a7931b-b153-480b-a01e-979e829a2c41
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextual Infinitism](contextual-infinitism-synthesis-969d15cb-7035-4449-8e21-a18b5558491f.md)

## Content

**Truth is traditionally considered a necessary component of knowledge**, **A person can know a proposition if the proposition is true, but knowledge includes further conditions like justification**, **It is implausible to argue for knowledge if the belief is not based on truth, as false beliefs cannot form the basis of genuine knowledge**
