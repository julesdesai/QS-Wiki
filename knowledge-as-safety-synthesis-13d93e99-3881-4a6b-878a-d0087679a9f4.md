## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the person believes the proposition, then the belief is not easily false**, **The belief in the proposition should be safe from error in nearby possible worlds**
