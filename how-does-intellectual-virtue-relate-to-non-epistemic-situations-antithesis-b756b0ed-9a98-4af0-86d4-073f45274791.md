# How does intellectual virtue relate to non-epistemic situations?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b756b0ed-9a98-4af0-86d4-073f45274791
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue epistemology view on knowledge](virtue-epistemology-view-on-knowledge-synthesis-e49b38ea-0e37-4416-ac67-ff35516a157e.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person demonstrates intellectual virtues in non-epistemic situations. Intuitively, their virtues alone do not confer epistemic status or knowledge.**, **It is implausible that intellectual virtues, without engagement in epistemic endeavors, lead to knowledge.**
