## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the person were to believe the proposition, it would not easily be false**, **This requirement on safety ensures that the belief holds in nearby possible worlds where the belief is formed in a similar manner**
