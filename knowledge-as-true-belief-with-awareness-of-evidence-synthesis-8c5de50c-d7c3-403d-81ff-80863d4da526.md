# Knowledge as True Belief with Awareness of Evidence

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 8c5de50c-d7c3-403d-81ff-80863d4da526
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can beliefs formed under ignorance be knowledge?](can-beliefs-formed-under-ignorance-be-knowledge-antithesis-4cbff494-18b9-4dd9-8d1f-f9e387388bad.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they are aware of relevant evidence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, the proposition is true, and they are aware of relevant evidence**
