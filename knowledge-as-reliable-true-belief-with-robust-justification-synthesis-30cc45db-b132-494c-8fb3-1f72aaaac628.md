# Knowledge as Reliable True Belief with Robust Justification

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is caused by a reliable process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their justification be robust against deception scenarios**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is caused by a reliable process, and their justification is robust against deception scenarios**
