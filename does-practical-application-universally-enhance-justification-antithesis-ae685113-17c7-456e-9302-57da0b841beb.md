# Does practical application universally enhance justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ae685113-17c7-456e-9302-57da0b841beb
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatism: Practical Consequences](pragmatism-practical-consequences-synthesis-0f8107eb-2a4a-42d8-a5a9-2983c41a2844.md)

## Content

**Practical success may follow from various factors including chance, unrelated to belief justification.**, **It is implausible to universally claim such outcomes enhance justification without direct relevance to the belief itself.**
