# Can non-virtuous agents possess knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ffedde0e-35b1-4b62-9aec-28d804ddc957
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-05879bdc-4686-45ec-8d5f-c387922414d1.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where artificial intelligence forms true beliefs based on algorithmic processes without intellectual virtues. Intuitively, we would judge that such an AI may still possess knowledge, even in the absence of intellectual virtues as typically understood for humans.**
