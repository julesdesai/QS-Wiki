# Does knowledge depend on reliable traits alone?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 80de3bbd-8610-4583-8e38-36b34c54a26f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-05879bdc-4686-45ec-8d5f-c387922414d1.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that a person can acquire knowledge through a one-time insightful guess that does not rely on a stable trait. This principle suggests that occasionally true insights can amount to knowledge without being the result of a reliable intellectual virtue.**
