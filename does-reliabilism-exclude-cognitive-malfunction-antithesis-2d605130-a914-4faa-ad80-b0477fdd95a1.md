# Does Reliabilism exclude cognitive malfunction?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2d605130-a914-4faa-ad80-b0477fdd95a1
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism](reliabilism-synthesis-e69ee86a-1c0a-4d0e-840e-f25c67fd8e9a.md)

## Content

**A scenario in which a person forms a true belief because of a cognitive malfunction or error, such as having a lucky neural glitch that produces a true belief, seems intuitively problematic to classify as knowledge.**, **The fact that a process can produce a true belief due to error or malfunction challenges the notion that reliability alone suffices for knowledge.**
