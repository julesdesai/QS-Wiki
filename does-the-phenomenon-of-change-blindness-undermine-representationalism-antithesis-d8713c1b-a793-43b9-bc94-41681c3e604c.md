# Does the phenomenon of change blindness undermine representationalism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d8713c1b-a793-43b9-bc94-41681c3e604c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Representationalism Summary](representationalism-summary-synthesis-d2db7ebf-cdb9-4791-9194-360bca10efaf.md)

## Content

**Change blindness shows a failure to detect changes in a visual stimulus.**, **The failure indicates a gap between representations and perceptual reality.**, **If changes go unnoticed, it undermines the reliability of representations in conveying true knowledge.**
