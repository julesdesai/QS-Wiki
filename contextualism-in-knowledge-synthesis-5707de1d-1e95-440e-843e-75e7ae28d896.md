## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief meets the contextual standards of justification**, **A person knows a proposition if it is true, they believe it, and the justification meets contextual standards**
