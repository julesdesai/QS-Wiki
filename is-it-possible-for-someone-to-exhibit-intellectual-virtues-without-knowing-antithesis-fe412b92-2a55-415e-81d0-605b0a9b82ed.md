# Is it possible for someone to exhibit intellectual virtues without knowing?

## Content

**Imagine a person who is diligent, open-minded, and intellectually courageous in investigating a false proposition. Intuitively, this person does not have knowledge.**, **A scenario where a person's inquiry is thorough and virtuous but results in a false belief challenges the connection between intellectual virtues and knowledge.**
