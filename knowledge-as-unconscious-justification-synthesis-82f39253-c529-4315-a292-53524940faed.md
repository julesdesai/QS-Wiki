## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief is guided by unconscious realization of justification**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their unconscious grasp of justification plays a role in forming the belief**
