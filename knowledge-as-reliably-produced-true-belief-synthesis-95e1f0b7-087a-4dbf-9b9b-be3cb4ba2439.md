# Knowledge as Reliably Produced True Belief

**Parent:** [Is the synchronization of proper function with truth often accidental?](is-the-synchronization-of-proper-function-with-truth-often-accidental-antithesis-6ca1d30f-990f-40cf-8679-c140797352a0.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by cognitive faculties that are reliably truth-conducive**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is produced by cognitive faculties that are reliably truth-conducive**
