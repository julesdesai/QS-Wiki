## Content

**Suppose someone knows a fact within a particular context but, when recalling it later in a different context, fails to meet the new justification standards. Intuitively, this raises questions about continuity in knowledge.**, **Knowledge claims based on memory that depend on initial and present contexts being concordant seem vulnerable to unjustified shifts.**
