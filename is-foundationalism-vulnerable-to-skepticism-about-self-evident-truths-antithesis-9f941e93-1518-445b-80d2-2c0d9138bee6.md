# Is foundationalism vulnerable to skepticism about self-evident truths?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9f941e93-1518-445b-80d2-2c0d9138bee6
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundationalism: Basic Beliefs](foundationalism-basic-beliefs-synthesis-fa10f2ed-646f-4b84-85d3-ce2c6a58459d.md)

## Content

**Skepticism about self-evident truths questions the existence of any propositions that are immediately known without justification.**, **Some claim that what one person sees as self-evident, another might question as needing further evidence.**, **Foundationalism relies on self-evident beliefs as a basis for knowledge and justification.**, **If skepticism about self-evident truths is valid, foundationalism lacks a secure starting point for justification.**
