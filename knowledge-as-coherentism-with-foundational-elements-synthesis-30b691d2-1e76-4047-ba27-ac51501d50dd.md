# Knowledge as Coherentism with Foundational Elements

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is coherence consistent with closure principles?](is-coherence-consistent-with-closure-principles-antithesis-e20d119f-eb08-4a64-b848-f5ed81c4189a.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is supported by foundational beliefs in addition to cohering with a system of interconnected beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is supported by foundational beliefs while cohering with a system of interconnected beliefs**
