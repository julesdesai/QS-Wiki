# How can direct realism account for perceptual illusions?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 84234749-a3ca-468f-a1b1-42fbeccf950e
**Depth:** 2
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Direct Realism](direct-realism-thesis-65da2b06-0c60-4e66-b428-771d2431fe55.md)

## Content

**In perceptual illusions, individuals perceive something that does not correspond to reality.**, **A scenario like a bent stick in water shows that perception can be misleading.**, **Intuitively, direct perception would not allow for systematic illusions if perception were purely direct.**
