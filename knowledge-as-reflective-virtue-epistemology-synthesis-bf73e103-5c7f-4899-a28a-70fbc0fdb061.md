## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief to be produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they reflectively endorse the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process, and they reflectively endorse the belief**
