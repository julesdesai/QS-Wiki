## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a proposition to be known, it must have a high probability of being true**, **A belief is knowledge if formed through intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition has a high probability of being true, and their belief is formed through intellectual virtue**
