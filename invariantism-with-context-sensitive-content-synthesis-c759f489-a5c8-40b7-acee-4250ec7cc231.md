## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that justification is determined by invariant standards**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification meets invariant standards**, **The content of the proposition a person knows can vary with context**
