# Can luck have a role in virtuous knowledge acquisition?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** cb1b2dc8-c404-4287-8619-c05dd7afcb09
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Responsibilist Virtue Epistemology](responsibilist-virtue-epistemology-synthesis-23f2e823-6756-4d05-9cb6-b00f2dd07c18.md)

## Content

**Consider a situation where an accidental encounter with new evidence leads someone to abandon a false belief and adopt a true one due to their open-mindedness, a virtue. Here, we question if luck played a role alongside virtues in the acquisition of knowledge.**, **There can be cases where the role of luck in aligning with virtues leads to the discovery of the truth, questioning the exclusivity of character virtues in the responsible process of knowledge acquisition.**
