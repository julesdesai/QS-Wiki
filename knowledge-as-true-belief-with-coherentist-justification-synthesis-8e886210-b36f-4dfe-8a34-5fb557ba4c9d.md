## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief coheres with a system of interconnected beliefs**, **The coherence of beliefs is determined by consistency, logical support, and integration within the system**
