# Infinitism Allows Endless Justification

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified through an endless chain of reasons, each supported by a further reason**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is justified by an endless chain of reasons**
