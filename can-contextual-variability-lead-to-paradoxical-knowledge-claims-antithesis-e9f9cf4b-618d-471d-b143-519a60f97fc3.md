# Can contextual variability lead to paradoxical knowledge claims?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e9f9cf4b-618d-471d-b143-519a60f97fc3
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [The Contextualist View of Knowledge](the-contextualist-view-of-knowledge-synthesis-744b190b-128c-4187-8edf-0a707cd161d1.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person knows a proposition in a context with relaxed standards.**, **In a context with stringent standards, they may lack knowledge of the same proposition.**, **This variability can result in paradoxical situations where both knowledge and lack of knowledge are claimed depending on context.**
