## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief to be such that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it in relevant contexts**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief meets the sensitivity condition within the context**
