# Invariantism with Standard Justification

**Parent:** [Can varying justifications lead to contradictory knowledge claims?](can-varying-justifications-lead-to-contradictory-knowledge-claims-antithesis-331429b7-0c92-4119-9f3b-bd1f946dc257.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification that meets a fixed standard**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and they have justification meeting the fixed standard**
