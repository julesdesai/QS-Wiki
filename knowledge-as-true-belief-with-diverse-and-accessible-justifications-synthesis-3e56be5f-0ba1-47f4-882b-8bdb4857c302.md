## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are diverse forms of justification for the belief**, **These diverse justifications must be accessible to people in a variety of contexts**
