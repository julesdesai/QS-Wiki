## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is justified by evidence available to their cognitive perspective**, **Only evidence that the agent is aware of can justify a belief**
