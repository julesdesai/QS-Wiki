# How does the truth-tracking view handle cases of forgotten knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 508afa66-7e83-4e24-8dad-51089228130d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Truth-Tracking View](truth-tracking-view-synthesis-ffb314b5-26cd-4e37-96a0-e81b3c75b895.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case where a person knew something true at an earlier time but later forgets the belief, yet it remains true in all possible worlds. Intuitively, we might still consider this person to have once had knowledge despite their beliefs not tracking the truth presently.**, **It is implausible to reject past knowledge based on its current failure to track truth, as this does not account for the temporal nature of how knowledge is held.**
