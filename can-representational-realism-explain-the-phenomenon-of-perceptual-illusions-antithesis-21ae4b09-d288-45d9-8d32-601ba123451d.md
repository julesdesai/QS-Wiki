# Can Representational Realism explain the phenomenon of perceptual illusions?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 21ae4b09-d288-45d9-8d32-601ba123451d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Representational Realism](representational-realism-synthesis-2653bf9f-214b-48a1-b757-9a49097d2459.md)

## Content

**Perceptual illusions occur when what is perceived is not what is present in reality, like the Müller-Lyer illusion with lines of the same length appearing different.**, **Illusions demonstrate that mental representations can fail to correspond accurately to the real world.**, **Intuitively, illusions should count against the idea that we possess knowledge through accurate correspondence.**
