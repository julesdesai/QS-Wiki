# Does reliability encompass all relevant circumstances?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2f10cdda-2ba1-406a-98fe-4d1322908284
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism as a Knowledge Theory](reliabilism-as-a-knowledge-theory-synthesis-b5bdc6be-6410-4aae-a2f1-e20391888e99.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that cognitive process reliability accounts for all possible misleading environmental conditions.**, **Consider a scenario in which a highly reliable process fails under a specific, unusual condition that the individual cannot anticipate. Intuitively, we would judge that a belief produced in this unusual condition does not equate to knowledge.**
