# Is the design plan concept too vague to determine proper function?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 67c6e8b7-062a-4ee8-b9ed-cd70375893e6
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Proper Function Theory](proper-function-theory-synthesis-eb8cad20-b2ac-4ebe-8b63-c23141145814.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that the concept of a design plan can be precisely articulated in terms of cognitive faculties.**, **Consider a scenario where different observers disagree on what the design plan for a cognitive faculty should be. Intuitively, this disagreement suggests that the design plan is too vague to reliably determine proper function.**
