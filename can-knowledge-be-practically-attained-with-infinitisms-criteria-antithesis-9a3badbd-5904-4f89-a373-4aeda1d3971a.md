# Can knowledge be practically attained with Infinitism's criteria?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9a3badbd-5904-4f89-a373-4aeda1d3971a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism](infinitism-synthesis-db00e7c1-6a13-4ca5-8f09-6fae7cd29e0f.md)

## Content

**A belief is a practical guide for action if it is justified under reasonable conditions. It is implausible for a belief to be a practical guide if it requires infinite reasons for justification, given human limits in processing information.**, **There is a plausible principle that knowledge is useful for life and decision-making. This principle together with the infinite requirement suggests that Infinitism might render knowledge impractical or inaccessible.**
