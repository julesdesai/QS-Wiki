# Does accidental causation pose a problem for the causal theory?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 70c7cfba-f0dc-46f2-84a8-d318c6e6eba8
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

## Content

**Suppose a person sees a fake news report about a celebrity being in town and forms a true belief, but the celebrity actually is in town by coincidence.**, **Intuitively, we would judge that coincidental causation does not provide knowledge even though the belief is true.**
