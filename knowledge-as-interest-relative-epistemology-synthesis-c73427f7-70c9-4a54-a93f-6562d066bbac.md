## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **The determination of knowledge depends on factors related to the interests and relevant alternatives of the subject**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief meets the interest-relative standards**
