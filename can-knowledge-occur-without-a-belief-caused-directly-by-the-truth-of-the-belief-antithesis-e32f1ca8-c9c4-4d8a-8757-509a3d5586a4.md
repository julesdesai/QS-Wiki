# Can knowledge occur without a belief caused directly by the truth of the belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e32f1ca8-c9c4-4d8a-8757-509a3d5586a4
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Causal Theory of Knowledge](causal-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-41ded06c-6d0f-456a-b47d-d275c7e4054e.md)

## Content

**Imagine a case where a person believes a true proposition based on strong evidence from an authoritative source, yet the truth of the proposition is unrelated to how they came to believe it.**, **In this case, it intuitively appears that the person possesses knowledge even though their belief was not caused directly by the truth of the belief.**
