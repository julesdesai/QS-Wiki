# Is contextual knowledge reliable across different settings?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 739d47af-82cd-4df4-a6fc-4f17ef28c866
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualist Theory of Knowledge](contextualist-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-b7b03c88-6aa4-4b47-9f59-bc5548cc994c.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person knows something in one context but is confronted with a different context that alters the knowledge claim. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that if knowledge fails to hold across contexts, it lacks reliability and credibility in one's cognitive framework.**
