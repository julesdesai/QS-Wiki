# Knowledge as Contextual Coherentism with Minimal Skepticism

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 5c0314ae-fa28-4874-8f11-7c11f46ab6b8
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is coherence alone enough to counter skepticism?](is-coherence-alone-enough-to-counter-skepticism-antithesis-7fbd472e-d07b-482d-9832-e8a02ed6762b.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres with a system of beliefs that is justified given the context**, **The system of beliefs that is justified given the context accepts minimal forms of skepticism to safeguard against complete doubt**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief coheres with a contextually justified system of beliefs with minimal skepticism**
