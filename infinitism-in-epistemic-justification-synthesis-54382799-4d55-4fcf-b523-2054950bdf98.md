## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **Justification for a belief consists of an infinite series of non-repeating reasons**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, it is true, and an infinite chain of reasons justifies their belief**
