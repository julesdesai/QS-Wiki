# Can devices truly extend perception without distortion?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 157fddf7-a62c-45f2-a284-f53d10ed4a2e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Critical Direct Realism](critical-direct-realism-synthesis-6462aad1-66cc-4ce3-ad99-1e3d9c26fcac.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person uses a telescope that alters color perception slightly, but still magnifies distant objects. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that the perception is distorted, not merely extended.**, **It is implausible that any device can enhance perception without altering it in some minimal way.**
