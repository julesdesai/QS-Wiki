## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed through an external process influenced by intellectual virtues**, **It is not necessary for a person to be consciously aware of the intellectual virtues involved in belief formation**
