## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is justified**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **The standards for justification in knowing a proposition vary with the context of the attributor of knowledge**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that context-dependent justification, belief, and truth criteria are met**
