# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with Contextual Analysis

**Parent:** [Could external factors negate knowledge despite reliable processes?](could-external-factors-negate-knowledge-despite-reliable-processes-antithesis-106f73f4-ea59-490f-a5db-65737c310f53.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is evaluated within the context of a generally stable environment**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is evaluated within the context of a generally stable environment**
