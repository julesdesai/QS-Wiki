# What if subjective experiences disrupt rather than fit within belief networks?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2d64e5b1-485e-442b-8787-00adf92b7a83
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism](coherentism-synthesis-47013a86-4e6f-4466-86d9-11bbc22ccdaf.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case where a person experiences a phantom limb sensation, feeling pain in a limb that does not exist. Intuitively, we might judge that subjective experiences do not always fit coherently within existing belief systems.**, **It is implausible that all subjective experiences can be neatly integrated into pre-existing beliefs without sacrificing the accuracy or authenticity of the experience.**
