# Can belief without explicit awareness be credited as knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 003d8cd1-1a07-449a-9c3c-5e10fc89af6e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitist Coherentism](infinitist-coherentism-synthesis-c961c647-ce7e-4676-a333-4ee929ef8887.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that knowledge requires some level of conscious awareness of why a belief is held. Belief alone may lack this reflective aspect necessary for knowledge.**, **Consider an individual who accurately predicts a future event due to a gut feeling, which turns out correct. Intuitively, we would not credit this as knowledge because the belief lacks reasoned understanding.**
