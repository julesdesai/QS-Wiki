## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is the result of exercising intellectual virtue**, **Intellectual virtues are cognitive faculties like perception, memory, and inference**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person believes the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe it, it is true, and their belief results from exercising intellectual virtue**
