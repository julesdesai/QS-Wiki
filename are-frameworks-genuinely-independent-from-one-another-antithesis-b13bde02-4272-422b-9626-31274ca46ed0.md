# Are frameworks genuinely independent from one another?

## Content

**It is implausible that epistemic frameworks are entirely independent since individuals frequently interact and share knowledge.**, **Consider a case where a scientist uses both empirical data and religious belief to justify claims about the natural world. Intuitively, such integration suggests that frameworks are not completely separate and can be influenced by external factors.**
