# Justified True Belief with Safety

**Parent:** [Can knowledge exist independently of psychological states like belief?](can-knowledge-exist-independently-of-psychological-states-like-belief-antithesis-69cf23b6-f03e-42f4-9eff-8461c27c5f03.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that, in nearby possible worlds, similar beliefs would not be false**, **A person knows a proposition if it is true, they believe it, they have justification for believing it, and similar beliefs would not be false in nearby possible worlds**
