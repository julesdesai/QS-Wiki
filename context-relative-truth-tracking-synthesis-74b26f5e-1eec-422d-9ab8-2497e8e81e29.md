**Parent:** [Does the context-dependence of belief impact knowledge claims?](does-the-context-dependence-of-belief-impact-knowledge-claims-antithesis-ba178b92-ee06-4f81-9bbc-7fcc786f02cc.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition in a context, it is necessary that the person’s belief changes in response to truth changes relevant to that context only**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief's context-relativity does not undermine knowledge**
