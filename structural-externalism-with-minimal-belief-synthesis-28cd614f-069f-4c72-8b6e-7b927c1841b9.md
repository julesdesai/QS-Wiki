# Structural Externalism with Minimal Belief

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Can knowledge be purely external with no internal state of belief?](can-knowledge-be-purely-external-with-no-internal-state-of-belief-antithesis-21cacb0d-0e6a-4bd4-952e-35058a916323.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for the proposition**, **A minimal degree of belief is necessary for a person to form knowledge of a proposition**, **A person knows a proposition if it is true, they have justification for it, and hold at least a minimal degree of belief in it**
