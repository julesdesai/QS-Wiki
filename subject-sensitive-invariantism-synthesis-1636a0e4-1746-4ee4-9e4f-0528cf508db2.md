## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person possesses a level of justification relevant to the importance of the proposition to the subject**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is true, they believe the proposition, and they possess relevant justification for the proposition's importance**
