# Can false foundational beliefs invalidate knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 8b9a841d-6a22-4398-98f5-d2b9538cb920
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Epistemic Foundationalism](epistemic-foundationalism-synthesis-e90eff9d-4b30-41a2-b4e3-4bbc38385542.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which foundational beliefs are actually false. Intuitively, we would judge that any belief based on these foundational beliefs could not amount to knowledge.**, **It is implausible that knowledge can be based on false foundational beliefs, because they undermine the truth requirement of knowledge.**
