# Is bodily integration necessary for successful sensory perception?

**Node Type:** antithesis

**Parent:** [Embodied Cognition with Sensory Integration](embodied-cognition-with-sensory-integration-synthesis-1b01035b-41e7-4892-81fb-893d75aaae6e.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which blind individuals use echolocation to perceive their surroundings without relying on other bodily senses. Intuitively, this suggests that successful sensory perception can occur without significant bodily integration.**
