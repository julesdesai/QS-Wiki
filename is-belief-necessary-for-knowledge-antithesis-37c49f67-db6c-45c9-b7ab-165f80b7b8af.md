# Is belief necessary for knowledge?

## Content

**One might imagine a scenario where a person acts as if they know something to be true without forming a belief at all**, **A person might display knowledge-level behavior in response to accurate information without holding an explicit belief in that information**, **If belief is not always present, it challenges the claim that belief is necessary for knowledge**
