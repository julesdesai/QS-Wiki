# Might knowledge require more than just evidence and truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 01274825-654a-4bea-96e6-05bc01702de3
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism with Evidence Requirement](contextualism-with-evidence-requirement-synthesis-8260fba2-b1af-49c2-a4cf-403608671469.md)

## Content

**Suppose a scenario where an individual has true belief and adequate evidence, but their belief is based on luck. Intuitively, it seems they do not have knowledge.**, **There is a plausible principle that having knowledge requires, beyond evidence and truth, some factor that eliminates luck or accidentality from the belief.**
