# Can true knowledge result from unsuccessful actions?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c4f47f8e-e820-437f-ad48-3e378d3fffbc
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Verifactive Pragmatism](verifactive-pragmatism-synthesis-4eab892d-8662-4622-835b-cae1a39a2d29.md)

## Content

**Imagine a scientist who believes in the existence of a subatomic particle based on theory, but cannot yet perform experiments to verify its existence due to technological limits. Their belief may still be true despite the lack of practical verification through actions.**, **Truth can exist independently of practical success, as theoretical insights might be knowledge even before practical verification is possible.**
