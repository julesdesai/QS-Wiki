# Knowledge as Safe Belief

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Do very reliable processes always produce true beliefs?](do-very-reliable-processes-always-produce-true-beliefs-antithesis-50c6b04a-f7f2-40bc-9c06-8a29cad3aa0c.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed in a way that it could not easily have been false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief could not easily have been false**
