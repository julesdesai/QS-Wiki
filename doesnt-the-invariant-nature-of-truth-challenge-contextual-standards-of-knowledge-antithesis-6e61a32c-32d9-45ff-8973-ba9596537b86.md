# Doesn't the invariant nature of truth challenge contextual standards of knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 6e61a32c-32d9-45ff-8973-ba9596537b86
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism in Epistemology](contextualism-in-epistemology-synthesis-cec2b7f9-8a52-4ab7-9dd4-6f75547db097.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that truth is objective and does not change based on context. This principle together with the idea that the standards for what constitutes knowledge change based on the context entails a conflict.**
