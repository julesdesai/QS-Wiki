## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **Knowledge requires the belief to be reliably produced in the context in which it's held**, **A reliable cognitive process is one that produces true beliefs more often than not within a specific context**
