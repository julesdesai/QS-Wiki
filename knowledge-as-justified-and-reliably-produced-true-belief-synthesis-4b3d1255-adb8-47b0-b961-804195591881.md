## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a reliable belief-forming process**
