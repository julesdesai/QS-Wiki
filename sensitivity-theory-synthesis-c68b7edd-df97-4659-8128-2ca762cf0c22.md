## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe that proposition**, **A belief is sensitive if it varies with the truth of the proposition across possible worlds**
