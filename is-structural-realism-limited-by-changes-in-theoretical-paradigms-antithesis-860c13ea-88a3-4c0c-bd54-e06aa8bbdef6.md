# Is structural realism limited by changes in theoretical paradigms?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 860c13ea-88a3-4c0c-bd54-e06aa8bbdef6
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Epistemic Structural Realism](epistemic-structural-realism-synthesis-4cc8fed2-62df-47a3-af1a-0c0e49fc23b5.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where the structure of scientific theories changes over time, such as the shift from Newtonian mechanics to quantum mechanics. Intuitively, structural reality might be seen as temporary and contingent on the development of knowledge disciplines.**, **A plausible principle is that knowledge should remain consistent across paradigm shifts to be reliable and lasting, which structural realism might struggle to assure.**
