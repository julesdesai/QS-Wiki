## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in nearly all nearby possible worlds where they infer the belief from justified reasoning, it is true**, **Inferential justification ensures that the belief is grounded in sound reasoning rather than coincidental truth**
