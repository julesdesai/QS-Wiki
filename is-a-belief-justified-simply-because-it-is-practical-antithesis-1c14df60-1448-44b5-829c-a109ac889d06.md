# Is a belief justified simply because it is practical?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1c14df60-1448-44b5-829c-a109ac889d06
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Revised Pragmatic Theory of Knowledge with Justification](revised-pragmatic-theory-of-knowledge-with-justification-synthesis-570c8b94-2cc9-4f79-b923-9eacb64ddf74.md)

## Content

**A person may believe that taking vitamin supplements is essential for health without scientific evidence; the belief may be practical as it encourages health awareness but is not justified by facts.**, **Intuitively, the practical nature of a belief does not alone provide justification for it.**
