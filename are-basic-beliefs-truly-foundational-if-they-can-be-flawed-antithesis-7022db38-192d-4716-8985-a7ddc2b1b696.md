# Are basic beliefs truly foundational if they can be flawed?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7022db38-192d-4716-8985-a7ddc2b1b696
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Moderate Foundationalism](moderate-foundationalism-synthesis-83772ba8-e93f-47f7-a15a-9e5a39b9dc9d.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a basic belief is formed based on sensory experience but due to an unusual condition, this is inaccurate. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that the belief lacks proper justification.**, **If a basic belief can be flawed, then it may not provide sufficient justification for non-basic beliefs.**
