## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is justified by reliably grounded reasons**, **Reliable grounding excludes justifications that are frequently unreliable such as cultural traditions**
