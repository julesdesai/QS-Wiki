# Is inaccessible justification genuinely justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 8ea9545d-1944-4072-a9da-8ffe48df2e59
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Proper Function Theory](proper-function-theory-synthesis-45579dd6-32de-43f3-ae7a-76b3bb7de09b.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person's belief is supported by evidence unavailable to their awareness. Intuitively, this raises doubts about whether this qualifies as justifying their belief.**, **A plausible principle is that justification requires some level of the individual's understanding or awareness, contradicting the idea that justification can be entirely inaccessible.**
