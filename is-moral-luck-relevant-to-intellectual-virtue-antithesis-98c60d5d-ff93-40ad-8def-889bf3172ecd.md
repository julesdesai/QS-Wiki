# Is moral luck relevant to intellectual virtue?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 98c60d5d-ff93-40ad-8def-889bf3172ecd
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Integrated Virtue-Responsibilism](integrated-virtue-responsibilism-synthesis-06dcde70-c679-48b5-94ca-324e3064105a.md)

## Content

**One might form a true belief through the exercise of intellectual virtues but only due to a lucky stroke, suggesting virtue may play a lesser role than luck.**, **The role of intellectual virtues is undermined if true beliefs arise from luck rather than consistent application of virtue.**
