# Does pragmatic encroachment imply variable knowledge across contexts?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 12110deb-dd42-47a3-853a-5767157fc577
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Encroachment Theory](pragmatic-encroachment-theory-synthesis-66e6d98a-2899-4b80-b05b-15066f046ddd.md)

## Content

**Consider a scientist who presents evidence in a lab about a chemical reaction. The stakes of being wrong are minor within the research environment.**, **However, if the same chemist presents the findings to a government body, the consequences are substantial.**, **It is odd for the same evidence to variously confer or not confer knowledge based on external practical factors.**
