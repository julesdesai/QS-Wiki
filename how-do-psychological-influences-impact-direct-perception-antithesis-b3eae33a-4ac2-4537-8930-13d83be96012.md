# How do psychological influences impact direct perception?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b3eae33a-4ac2-4537-8930-13d83be96012
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Direct Realism](direct-realism-synthesis-63f38132-cda9-42dc-9c71-38670cc01e77.md)

## Content

**Psychological state, such as mood or mental health, can influence perception, for example, seeing the world as a darker place when feeling sad.**, **In cases where psychological state alters perception, what is perceived may not reflect external reality accurately.**, **If psychological states influence perception, then it casts doubt on the idea that perception provides immediate knowledge of external objects.**
