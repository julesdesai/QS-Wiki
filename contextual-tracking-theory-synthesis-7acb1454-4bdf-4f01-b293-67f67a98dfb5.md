**Parent:** [Does the concept of false belief challenge the necessity of tracking conditions?](does-the-concept-of-false-belief-challenge-the-necessity-of-tracking-conditions-antithesis-e032c042-f80a-42ab-a059-fa084c802081.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person would not believe the proposition if it were false, given a relevant context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person would believe the proposition if it were true, given a relevant context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that these contextual tracking conditions hold**
