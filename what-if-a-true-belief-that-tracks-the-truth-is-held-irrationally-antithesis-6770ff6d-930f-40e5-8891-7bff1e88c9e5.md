# What if a true belief that tracks the truth is held irrationally?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 6770ff6d-930f-40e5-8891-7bff1e88c9e5
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Truth-Tracking Theory](truth-tracking-theory-synthesis-1814de97-222c-437f-bd26-a9ce4edff4f7.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case where a person holds a true belief that tracks truth across possible situations, but the belief is formed through irrational or illogical reasoning. Intuitively, we might question whether this belief truly constitutes knowledge.**, **It is implausible that knowledge can arise from beliefs that are not formed through rational means.**
