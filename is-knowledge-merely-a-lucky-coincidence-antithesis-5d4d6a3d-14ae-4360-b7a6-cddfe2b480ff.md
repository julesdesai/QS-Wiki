# Is knowledge merely a lucky coincidence?

**Node ID:** 5d4d6a3d-14ae-4360-b7a6-cddfe2b480ff
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology With Virtuous Belief Formation](virtue-epistemology-with-virtuous-belief-formation-synthesis-53b228ae-7464-400d-be01-037da588b5a0.md)

## Content

**A case in which a person forms a true belief about a friend’s opinion by guessing randomly based on no information is a case in which the person’s belief accidentally aligns with reality.**, **In this case, the belief was not produced by an intellectual virtue, as it was purely guesswork.**, **Intuitively, this does not seem like a case of knowledge, although the belief is true.**
