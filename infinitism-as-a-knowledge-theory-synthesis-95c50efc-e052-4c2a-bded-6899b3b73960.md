**Parent:** [Is self-evidence a reliable criterion for basic beliefs?](is-self-evidence-a-reliable-criterion-for-basic-beliefs-antithesis-81fcc8fa-82ba-4012-98a6-410ed03f95c0.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification consists of an infinite, non-repeating chain of reasons**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is justified by an infinite chain of reasons**
