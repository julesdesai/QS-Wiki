# Causal Theory of Knowledge

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 9e55cdd8-3715-432b-9aa9-c2e4289d4e77
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Do all justified true beliefs constitute knowledge?](do-all-justified-true-beliefs-constitute-knowledge-antithesis-1e0c7ec2-5bf5-4109-8863-2ef3e05e4d9e.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is a causal connection between the belief and the truth of the proposition**
