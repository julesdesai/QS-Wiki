# Knowledge as No False Lemmas

**Parent:** [Is truth always necessary for knowledge if reliability is emphasized?](is-truth-always-necessary-for-knowledge-if-reliability-is-emphasized-antithesis-be3019f3-d8a9-4b31-bc34-3ad0bf069892.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is not based on any false lemmas**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, they have justification for believing it and there are no false lemmas**
