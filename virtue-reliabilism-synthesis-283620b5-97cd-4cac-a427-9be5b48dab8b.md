## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief originates in a reliable cognitive process**, **Reliability of the process ensures that in most situations the process leads to true beliefs**
