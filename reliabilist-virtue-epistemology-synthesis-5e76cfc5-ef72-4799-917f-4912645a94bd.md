# Reliabilist Virtue Epistemology

**Parent:** [Does intellectual virtue always lead to knowledge?](does-intellectual-virtue-always-lead-to-knowledge-antithesis-2780d505-c1ef-4c00-91a9-22b002766986.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief results from a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief results from both intellectual virtue and a reliable cognitive process**
