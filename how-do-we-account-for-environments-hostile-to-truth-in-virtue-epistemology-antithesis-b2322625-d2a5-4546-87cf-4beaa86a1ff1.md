# How do we account for environments hostile to truth in virtue epistemology?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b2322625-d2a5-4546-87cf-4beaa86a1ff1
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology View](virtue-epistemology-view-synthesis-31f81092-b7b3-48dd-9f17-87af421f7694.md)

## Content

**Intellectual virtues may not lead to truth in environments systematically designed to deceive or obscure truth.**, **A person can exercise intellectual virtues in a context where reliability is compromised, leading to false beliefs.**, **Even highly virtuous individuals can be misled when surrounded by unreliable sources of information.**
