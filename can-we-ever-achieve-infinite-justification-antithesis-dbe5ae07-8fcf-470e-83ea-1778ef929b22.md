# Can we ever achieve infinite justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** dbe5ae07-8fcf-470e-83ea-1778ef929b22
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Holism with Infinite Justification](holism-with-infinite-justification-synthesis-5f38dde0-d181-4939-9f1a-56e147cd5ef5.md)

## Content

**Humans have finite cognitive capacities.**, **Infinite justification requires an endless chain of reasoning that humans cannot complete.**, **Therefore, infinite justification is implausible for human knowledge.**
