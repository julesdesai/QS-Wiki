# Knowledge as Safety

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 133895c7-d391-4536-92ad-eac5778d0ce5
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is a reliably produced true belief always knowledge?](is-a-reliably-produced-true-belief-always-knowledge-antithesis-9cdd6933-6d26-43f8-b7ac-232fa7bbd7fc.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief could not have easily been false given slight changes in circumstances**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is safe from being false**
