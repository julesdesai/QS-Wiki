# Does the absence of defeaters ensure knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 4c665592-215f-4734-959a-01fc8419c0d9
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [No defeaters theory of knowledge](no-defeaters-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-293021ef-516a-4641-9c48-087d1223e339.md)

## Content

**Consider a situation where a person believes in a proposition that is consistent with all known evidence and is true. Yet, there exists an undiscovered truth that, if revealed, would provide a counter-reason to disbelieve. Such unknown truths raise doubt whether absence of defeaters genuinely ensures knowledge.**, **Intuitively, one might argue the unknown wouldn't change existing true belief, yet this challenges the depth of understanding in knowledge.**
