## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief could not easily have been false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is true in nearby possible worlds**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed in such a way that it is reliably true across similar circumstances**, **Safety in belief formation ensures the truth of the belief in all nearby possible situations**
