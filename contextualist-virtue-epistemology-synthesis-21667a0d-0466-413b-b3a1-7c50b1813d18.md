## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the knowledge depends on either contextual standards involving epistemic virtues or reliable processes**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they satisfy contextual standards involving either epistemic virtues or reliable processes along with belief and truth**
