# What if reliable processes sometimes produce false beliefs?

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism-based Theory of Knowledge](reliabilism-based-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-0f3a79b0-0db7-4d73-8a16-c6724011fb93.md)

## Content

**A reliable cognitive process is defined as one that usually produces true beliefs, but not always.**, **There exist cases where a generally reliable process leads to a false belief, suggesting reliability is not a sufficient condition for knowledge.**
