# Is the truth condition sufficient to determine knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a42b71b8-a8a2-4fc3-b119-a1d2d2be6fb2
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilist View](reliabilist-view-synthesis-e6df444a-c8a0-46a3-91c3-0432faee253d.md)

## Content

**A belief might be true by coincidence rather than by virtue of being formed from a reliable process, which intuitively undermines the notion of knowledge.**, **Suppose someone predicts the exact result of a roulette wheel by chance, which calls into doubt the truth condition as enough for knowledge.**
