# Does infinitism conflict with epistemic responsibility?

**Node Type:** antithesis

**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

## Content

**Epistemic responsibility involves providing reasons for one’s beliefs that can be assessed and communicated.**, **If justification for a belief involves an infinite chain, it becomes impossible to fully communicate or assess this justification responsively.**, **If the requirement of infinite justification undermines epistemic responsibility, then it challenges the concept of justified true belief.**
