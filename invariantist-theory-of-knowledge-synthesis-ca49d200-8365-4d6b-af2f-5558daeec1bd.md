# Invariantist Theory of Knowledge

**Parent:** [Can one justify a false proposition within a context and still achieve knowledge?](can-one-justify-a-false-proposition-within-a-context-and-still-achieve-knowledge-antithesis-2812888b-18c6-461c-9296-242e64fdca1d.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person's belief meets an objective standard of justification**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief meets the objective standard of justification**
