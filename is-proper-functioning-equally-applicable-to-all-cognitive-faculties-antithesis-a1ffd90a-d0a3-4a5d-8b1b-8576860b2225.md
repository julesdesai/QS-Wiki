# Is proper functioning equally applicable to all cognitive faculties?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a1ffd90a-d0a3-4a5d-8b1b-8576860b2225
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Proper Functionalism](proper-functionalism-synthesis-60a6689e-3dfa-4e29-ad7b-61cc33b34e1b.md)

## Content

**Different cognitive faculties may require distinct forms of proper functioning suited to diverse purposes.**, **Some faculties might produce knowledge without operating in the same manner as others.**, **If contrasted faculties can both produce reliable beliefs, then a single metric of proper functioning might be inadequate.**
