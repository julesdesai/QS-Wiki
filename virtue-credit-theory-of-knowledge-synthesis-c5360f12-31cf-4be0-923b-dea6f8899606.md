## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the person deserves credit for arriving at the truth**, **Deserving credit involves applying intellectual virtues in the epistemic process**
