# Reliabilism with Justificatory Support

**Parent:** [Does reliability exclude other conditions for knowledge?](does-reliability-exclude-other-conditions-for-knowledge-antithesis-c33b8b6f-bb82-4e9b-b0c0-55d683d3bd73.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is justificatory support for their belief in addition to reliability**
