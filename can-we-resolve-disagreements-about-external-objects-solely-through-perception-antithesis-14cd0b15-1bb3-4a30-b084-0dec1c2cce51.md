# Can we resolve disagreements about external objects solely through perception?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 14cd0b15-1bb3-4a30-b084-0dec1c2cce51
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Direct Realism with Strong Correspondence](direct-realism-with-strong-correspondence-synthesis-28884d49-4ced-45da-83a0-528139a02f57.md)

## Content

**Disagreements about external objects may arise, such as the shape of an object when viewed from different angles, which affect perception.**, **If perceptual experiences can lead to disagreements without clear resolution through perception alone, the dependence of perceptual knowledge on direct perception is questioned.**, **Solving disagreements requires more than perception, questioning if knowledge solely depends on perceptual experiences.**
