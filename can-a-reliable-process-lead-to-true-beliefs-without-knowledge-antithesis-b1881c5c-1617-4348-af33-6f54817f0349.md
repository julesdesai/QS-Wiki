# Can a reliable process lead to true beliefs without knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b1881c5c-1617-4348-af33-6f54817f0349
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism](reliabilism-synthesis-f6f2f982-fa0f-404e-a509-24417604aa1e.md)

## Content

**A case in which a person forms a true belief through a reliable process due to a lucky guess is not a case in which the person has knowledge.**, **A reliable process can produce a true belief by luck, which undermines the claim that all true beliefs from reliable processes count as knowledge.**
