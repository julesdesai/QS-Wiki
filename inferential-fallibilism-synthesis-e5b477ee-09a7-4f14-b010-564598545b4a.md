## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is based on a reliable inference process**, **Knowledge does not require infallibly high standards, only reliable belief formation**
