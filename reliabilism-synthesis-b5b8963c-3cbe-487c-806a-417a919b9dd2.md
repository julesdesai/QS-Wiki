## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief in the proposition is produced by a reliable process**, **A reliable process is a belief-generating mechanism that generally yields true beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief in that proposition is produced by a reliable process**
