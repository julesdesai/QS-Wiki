## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that justification comes from factors external to subjective perspective**, **Such external factors include reliable indicators of truth beyond clarity of intellectual virtues**
