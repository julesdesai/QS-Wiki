# Can variability in perceptual experiences affect the universality of representations?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** dba23c32-e3d3-4947-ad3a-364b26f358e1
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Representationalism](representationalism-synthesis-e49b01dd-7eac-432b-990b-a606f4616c7d.md)

## Content

**Suppose we observe a situation where different people perceive the same object differently due to cultural or personal interpretations.**, **Intuitively, this suggests that perceptions can be subjective and vary across different observers.**, **This indicates a challenge to the idea that perceptual experiences universally provide reliable representations of external entities.**
