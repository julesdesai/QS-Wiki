# Can Coherentism explain the truth of beliefs without reference to reality?

**Terminal:** No

## Content

**A coherent system might include beliefs that are internally consistent yet entirely disconnected from reality.**, **A scenario where a person holds a coherent set of beliefs about a fictional world intuitively suggests these beliefs cannot constitute knowledge.**, **Truth in Coherentism being restricted to coherence makes it implausible for truth to be achieved without correspondence to external reality.**
