# Safety Theory: Knowledge requires safety from error

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** da438a68-936f-4b4f-abda-fee4352d4a1f
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can the causal theory handle Gettier-style cases effectively?](can-the-causal-theory-handle-gettier-style-cases-effectively-antithesis-032b6ca3-d66a-4fd4-8b43-5aa9931f4133.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief could not have easily been false in nearby possible worlds**, **A belief is safe if in nearby possible worlds where the belief is held, it is not false**
