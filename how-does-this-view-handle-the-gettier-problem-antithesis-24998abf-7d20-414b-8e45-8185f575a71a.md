# How does this view handle the Gettier problem?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 24998abf-7d20-414b-8e45-8185f575a71a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Revised Pragmatic Theory of Knowledge](revised-pragmatic-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-d7b209ba-b523-44b3-ad9a-c228c53c8771.md)

## Content

**In Gettier cases, a person has justified true belief without knowledge due to luck.**, **The pragmatic component might not prevent these counterexamples as practical benefit can still occur by coincidence.**, **Therefore, true belief with practical benefit alone may not resolve the Gettier problem effectively.**
