# Is the coherence of the whole chain necessary for individual beliefs to be justified?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 4ab02149-0312-4a61-865d-8f50122d591f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism and Non-Foundational Justification](infinitism-and-non-foundational-justification-synthesis-6eeef37c-3f39-4c5c-91c9-b90fbd483157.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where individual beliefs are justified by their place in the chain but the whole chain itself is incoherent. Intuitively, such individual beliefs should not be justified.**, **It is implausible that an incoherent infinite chain can justify the beliefs it purportedly supports, given the incoherence undermines the integrity of justification.**
