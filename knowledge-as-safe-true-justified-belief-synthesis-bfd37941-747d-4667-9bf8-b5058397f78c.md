# Knowledge as Safe True Justified Belief

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** bfd37941-747d-4667-9bf8-b5058397f78c
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is truth necessary for safe belief?](is-truth-necessary-for-safe-belief-antithesis-798a97ab-8956-413b-a3a0-2b25b73b339d.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief could not easily have been false due to justifying factors**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is justified by factors that prevent it from easily being false**
