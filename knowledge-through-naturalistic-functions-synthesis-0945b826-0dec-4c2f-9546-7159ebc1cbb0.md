# Knowledge Through Naturalistic Functions

**Parent:** [Can animals possess knowledge without intellectual virtue?](can-animals-possess-knowledge-without-intellectual-virtue-antithesis-70846c0e-efd8-4f70-ae77-86b03162afd0.md)

## Content

**For an entity to know a proposition, it is necessary that the entity believes the proposition**, **For an entity to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For an entity to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief serves a natural function that contributes to the survival or thriving of the entity**, **For an entity to know a proposition, it is sufficient that it believes the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief serves a survival function**
