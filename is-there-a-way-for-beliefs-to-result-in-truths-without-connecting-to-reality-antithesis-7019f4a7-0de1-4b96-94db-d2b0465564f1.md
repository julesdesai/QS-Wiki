# Is there a way for beliefs to result in truths without connecting to reality?

## Content

**Suppose a skilled chess player predicts their opponent's moves based purely on intuition, without analyzing the board, and wins the match. Intuitively, some might feel the player exhibits knowledge of their opponent's strategy.**, **If intuitive beliefs can result in truths without analysis or reality connection, it challenges whether a genuine connection is necessary for ensuring proposition truth.**
