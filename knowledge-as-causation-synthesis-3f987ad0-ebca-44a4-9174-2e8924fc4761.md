**Knowledge is a true belief causally connected to the fact that makes it true**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief is produced by an appropriate causal connection to the truth of that proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**
