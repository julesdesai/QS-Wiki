# Does coherence guarantee truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 21c6191b-9f94-44df-a529-b764465d4c60
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitist Coherentism](infinitist-coherentism-synthesis-c961c647-ce7e-4676-a333-4ee929ef8887.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a person's beliefs form a coherent system, such as a well-crafted but fictional narrative. Intuitively, we would not say the person knows the fictional claims to be true.**, **A plausible principle is that coherence does not entail truth, as coherence can occur within false systems like conspiracy theories.**
