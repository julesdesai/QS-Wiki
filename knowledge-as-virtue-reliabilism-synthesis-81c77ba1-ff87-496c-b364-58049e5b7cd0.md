**Parent:** [Are reliable processes context-dependent?](are-reliable-processes-context-dependent-antithesis-8ba046e2-d2b8-4821-84f9-8ce739977fa8.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process intrinsically linked to intellectual virtues**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is produced by a reliable process connected to intellectual virtues**
