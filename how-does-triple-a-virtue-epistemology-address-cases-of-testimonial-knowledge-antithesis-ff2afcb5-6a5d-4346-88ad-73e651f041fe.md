# How does Triple-A Virtue Epistemology address cases of testimonial knowledge?

**Terminal:** No

## Content

**Consider a case where an individual accepts a true belief based on another person's reliable testimony. The hearer acquires the true belief accurately through testimony rather than their own skill.**, **Intuitively, relying on someone else’s skill, rather than one’s own, seems insufficient for knowledge by virtue of its skill-based requirements.**
