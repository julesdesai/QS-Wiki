# Can indirect realism explain perceptual error correction?

## Content

**In case of perceptual errors, such as mistaking a rope for a snake, individuals can correct their beliefs upon closer inspection.**, **If knowledge relies on indirect perception, then correcting errors suggests an ability to bypass or adjust sensory data, which challenges the view of indirect realism.**
