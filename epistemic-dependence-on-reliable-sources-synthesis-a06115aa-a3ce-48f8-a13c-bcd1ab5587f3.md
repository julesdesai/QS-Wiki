## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, the proposition must be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, they must believe the proposition**, **Their belief must originate from a source or informant that is itself reliable**, **Reliability entails that in most cases, the source does not lead one to false beliefs in similar conditions**
