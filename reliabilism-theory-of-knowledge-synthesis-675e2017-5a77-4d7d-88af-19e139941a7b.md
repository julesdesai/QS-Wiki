**Parent:** [Does contextual justification vary too greatly?](does-contextual-justification-vary-too-greatly-antithesis-8ef44404-b310-43dc-a26a-5fd31d6bc877.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is a product of a reliable cognitive process**
