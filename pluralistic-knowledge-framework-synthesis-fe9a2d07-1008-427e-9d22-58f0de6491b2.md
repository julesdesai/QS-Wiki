## Content

**Knowledge is a true belief formed through multiple pathways, including intellectual virtues and reliable methods**, **Intellectual virtues include character traits like open-mindedness and fairness**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief arises from any combination of reliable methods or virtuous character traits**
