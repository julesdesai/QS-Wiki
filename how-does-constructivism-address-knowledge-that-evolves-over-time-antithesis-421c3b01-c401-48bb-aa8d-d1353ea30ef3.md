# How does constructivism address knowledge that evolves over time?

## Content

**Scientific theories often evolve as new evidence becomes available.**, **Constructivism could imply that previous knowledge was inaccurately constructed if it no longer corresponds to reality.**, **This raises questions about the stability and reliability of constructively acquired knowledge in the face of evolving truth.**
