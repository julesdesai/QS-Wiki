# Can any belief truly be self-evident?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2f830af2-3fe3-4da7-b34b-1e7ee3401a43
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundationalist Coherentism](foundationalist-coherentism-synthesis-1575945f-cea5-4275-8c31-c7cabab6c71a.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that any belief can be truly self-evident since all beliefs could be subject to doubt and reconsideration.**, **In a case where a supposed self-evident belief is questioned and refuted, intuitively, it loses its foundational status. This suggests doubt about the existence of genuine self-evident beliefs.**
