# Knowledge as Justification, Truth, and Belief

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Does sensitivity accommodate deeply entrenched cultural beliefs?](does-sensitivity-accommodate-deeply-entrenched-cultural-beliefs-antithesis-2476263b-99ea-4c99-a88b-5953081a4054.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person has justified belief in the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have justified belief in the proposition, and that the proposition is true**
