# Knowledge with a Reliabilist Twist

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Can environmental factors negate perceptual justification?](can-environmental-factors-negate-perceptual-justification-antithesis-6bd1175a-8dfd-418b-b44e-6cfbd19ed007.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief be formed by a reliable process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is formed by a reliable process**
