## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have a disposition to behave in ways that reflect knowledge of the truth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is true and they exhibit behavior consistent with understanding and reliable disposition**
