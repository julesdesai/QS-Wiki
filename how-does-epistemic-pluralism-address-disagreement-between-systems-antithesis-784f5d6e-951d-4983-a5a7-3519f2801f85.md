# How does epistemic pluralism address disagreement between systems?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 784f5d6e-951d-4983-a5a7-3519f2801f85
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Epistemic Pluralism](epistemic-pluralism-synthesis-81e4116f-f2b8-419c-97e5-1ca5c482c2fd.md)

## Content

**When two epistemic systems disagree on whether a belief is known, resolving the disagreement requires privileging one system over the other.**, **The lack of inter-system adjudication mechanisms might suggest that disagreements persist without resolution.**, **Persistent disagreements between systems could challenge the efficacy of epistemic inquiry itself.**
