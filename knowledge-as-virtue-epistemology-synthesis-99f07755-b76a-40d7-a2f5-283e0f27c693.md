## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person's true belief in the proposition is primarily due to their intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their true belief is primarily due to their intellectual virtue**
