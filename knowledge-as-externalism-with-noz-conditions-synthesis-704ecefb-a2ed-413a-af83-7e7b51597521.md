## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is non-accidentally true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and some external conditions ensure the belief's non-accidental truth**
