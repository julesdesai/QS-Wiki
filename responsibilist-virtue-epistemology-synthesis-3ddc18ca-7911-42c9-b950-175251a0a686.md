## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed through intellectual character traits**, **These intellectual character traits include open-mindedness, intellectual courage, and intellectual humility**
