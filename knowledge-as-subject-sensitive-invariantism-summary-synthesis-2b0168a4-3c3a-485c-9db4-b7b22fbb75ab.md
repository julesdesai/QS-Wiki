## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that subjective factors of the subject can influence the fixed standards of knowledge**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the fixed standards of knowledge are met considering the subject's circumstances**
