# Infinitism with Non-Circularity Assurance

**Parent:** [How do we prevent circularity in an infinite chain of reasons?](how-do-we-prevent-circularity-in-an-infinite-chain-of-reasons-antithesis-03b0c39a-01b4-42d5-b9db-b21545ee9581.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that justification involves an infinite, non-repeating, and explicitly non-circular chain of reasons**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there exists an infinite, non-repeating, and explicitly non-circular chain of reasons justifying the belief**
