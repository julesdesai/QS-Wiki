# Can knowledge exist even if the belief is not modifiable by the truth status?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** dd2ca75c-8029-43f0-beb3-5b3b8e311834
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Truth-Tracking Theory](truth-tracking-theory-synthesis-50ed68ac-8c7e-4392-b286-f68915df73b2.md)

## Content

**Imagine a situation where a person, Henry, maintains a belief regardless of evidence or truth status. Intuitively, such rigidity seems incompatible with knowledge.**, **There is a plausible principle that knowledge requires a belief subject to revision or abandonment in light of evidence. If Henry's belief is impervious to evidence, it lacks the flexibility needed for knowledge.**
