# Can intellectual virtues mislead us?

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person uses reliable truth-aiming abilities in a misleading environment and forms a false belief.**, **In this scenario, despite employing intellectual virtues, the belief does not amount to knowledge, suggesting virtues can sometimes mislead.**
