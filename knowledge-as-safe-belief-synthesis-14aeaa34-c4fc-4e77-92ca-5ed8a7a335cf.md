# Knowledge as Safe Belief

**Parent:** [Can a belief be justified if based on unreliable sources of information?](can-a-belief-be-justified-if-based-on-unreliable-sources-of-information-antithesis-3b8be787-4dc5-49b7-9cd6-9f34edce4ab0.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief could not have easily been false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the person believes the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief could not have easily been false**
