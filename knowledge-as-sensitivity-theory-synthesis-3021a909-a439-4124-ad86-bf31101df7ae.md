## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A person's belief in a proposition is sensitive if they would not believe it if it were false**, **Sensitivity depends on the method of belief formation aligning with truth when false**
