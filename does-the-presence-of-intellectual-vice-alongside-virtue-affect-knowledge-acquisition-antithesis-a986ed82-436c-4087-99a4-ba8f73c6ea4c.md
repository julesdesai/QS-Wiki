# Does the presence of intellectual vice alongside virtue affect knowledge acquisition?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a986ed82-436c-4087-99a4-ba8f73c6ea4c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-43f1bd52-38ff-4c3b-a3d7-408481a71c52.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which someone with a well-developed sense of empathy often manipulates truth to persuade others. A listener might form a true belief based on this communication act, but it raises intuitions against equating such beliefs with knowledge.**, **Intellectual vices can coexist with virtues, complicating the identification of when virtues genuinely lead to true knowledge.**
