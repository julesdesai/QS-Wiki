# Is virtue epistemology challenged by the notion of cognitive enhancement?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d3fb03c6-6b72-4e8d-8e26-812c299aa2f1
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-1adc9ad4-a545-4f75-9435-0709a79c765f.md)

## Content

**Consider a case involving someone using pharmacological enhancers that improve cognitive performance and lead to true beliefs; intuitively, these substances question the notion of 'natural' intellectual virtues.**, **If reliability of an intellectual virtue is augmented or artificially produced, apprehensions about modifying core facets challenge current frameworks.**, **Truth credit assigned to enhanced or artificial cognitive abilities raises uncertainties in delineating which cognitive abilities account for genuine knowledge.**
