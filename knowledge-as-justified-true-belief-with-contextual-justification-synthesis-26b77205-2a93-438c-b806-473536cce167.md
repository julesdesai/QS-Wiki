# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with Contextual Justification

**Parent:** [Does justification depend on the environmental reliability?](does-justification-depend-on-the-environmental-reliability-antithesis-22ebf457-1f4f-45a4-af24-2abc1969eefa.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification is appropriate to the context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification is contextually appropriate**
