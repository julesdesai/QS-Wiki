## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **Knowledge requires the belief to be reliably produced**, **A reliable cognitive process produces true beliefs more often than not**, **For a process to reliably produce knowledge, the process must causally track the truth of the proposition in question**
