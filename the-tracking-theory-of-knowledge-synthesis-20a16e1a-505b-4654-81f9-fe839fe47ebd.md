# The Tracking Theory of Knowledge

**Parent:** [Is it possible for someone to have knowledge without belief in a proposition?](is-it-possible-for-someone-to-have-knowledge-without-belief-in-a-proposition-antithesis-e5d34122-24e8-4032-abcb-07bb4a52b632.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person would not believe the proposition if it were false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person would believe the proposition if it were true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that these tracking conditions hold**
