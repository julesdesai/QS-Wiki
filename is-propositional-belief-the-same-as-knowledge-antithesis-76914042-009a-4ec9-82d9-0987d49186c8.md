# Is propositional belief the same as knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 76914042-009a-4ec9-82d9-0987d49186c8
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology: Intellectual Virtues](virtue-epistemology-intellectual-virtues-synthesis-1d322156-3f22-49a0-99a5-f932d24b0e66.md)

## Content

**In a scenario where belief in a proposition arises from irrational sources, it seems intuitive that such belief does not equate to knowledge.**, **A plausible principle is that knowledge differs from mere belief in requiring rational grounding, suggesting propositional belief might fall short of knowledge.**
