# Knowledge with Latent Justification Awareness

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is guided by latent awareness of the justification for believing**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that latent awareness could become active if required**
