## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it, and if it were true, the person would believe it**
