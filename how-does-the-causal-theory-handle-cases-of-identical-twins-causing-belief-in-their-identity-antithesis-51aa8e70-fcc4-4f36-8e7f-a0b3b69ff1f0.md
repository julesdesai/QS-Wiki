# How does the causal theory handle cases of identical twins causing belief in their identity?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 51aa8e70-fcc4-4f36-8e7f-a0b3b69ff1f0
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Goldman's Causal Theory](goldmans-causal-theory-synthesis-55eaa724-2453-46d2-8e70-b1e77bdcb753.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which identical twins are mistaken for each other due to their appearance.**, **A belief formed about one twin based on a causal interaction with the other twin lacks a causal connection to the specific factual identity of each twin.**, **A belief that lacks a direct causal link to the fact itself calls into question the adequacy of the causal theory.**
