# Is constant belief a requirement for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c49309ee-d471-4b15-bb14-a7797ff100ef
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infallibility Plus Temporary Suspension](infallibility-plus-temporary-suspension-synthesis-9f8686c4-37d1-4e26-82f7-4e1ee44ea4fc.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that knowledge requires continuous belief in a true proposition. This principle together with the notion of temporary suspension of belief entails that a person loses knowledge during suspension, challenging the idea that such suspension does not negate previous knowledge.**
