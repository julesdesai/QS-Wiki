# Do foundational beliefs require justification themselves?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 37448087-2635-4814-8fb2-843123eef661
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism with Foundational Beliefs](infinitism-with-foundational-beliefs-synthesis-4273f090-f358-492e-9e7c-904c2a039ab2.md)

## Content

**A belief is foundational in a system if it requires no further justification.**, **In some scenarios, intuition suggests even foundational beliefs should have some form of justification or grounding.**, **It is possible to challenge foundational beliefs by questioning what implicitly or explicitly justifies them, even if not overtly stated.**
