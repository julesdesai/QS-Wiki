# Do perceptual experiences have unique epistemic value?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 06963043-e6e5-4b9c-b445-f6fc0e3f8763
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism](coherentism-synthesis-fe89b418-bb8b-4164-8874-fa4a88946376.md)

## Content

**Perceptual experiences provide a direct link to external reality in a way that beliefs do not.**, **Consider a scenario where a person sees a tree; this direct experience has an epistemic status that is independent of their belief system.**, **It is implausible to assimilate the unique nature of perceptions wholly under coherence of beliefs.**
