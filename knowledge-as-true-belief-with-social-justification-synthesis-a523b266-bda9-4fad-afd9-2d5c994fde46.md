# Knowledge as True Belief with Social Justification

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** a523b266-bda9-4fad-afd9-2d5c994fde46
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does implicit justification suffice for knowledge?](does-implicit-justification-suffice-for-knowledge-antithesis-79a718a6-34aa-4748-8074-93b659dda91d.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified through social processes and shared knowledge**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is justified through social processes**
