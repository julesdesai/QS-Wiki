## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, their belief must be supported by adequate evidence available through the exercise of intellectual virtues**, **Intellectual virtues include the competence to gather and evaluate evidence effectively**
