## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the standards for knowledge are met in the context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is true and the relevant contextual standards are met**
