## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in similar circumstances the belief would not easily be false**, **A causal connection is not required for a person to have knowledge**
