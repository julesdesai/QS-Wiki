# Is tracking a sufficient condition for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 52015faa-e7f3-4ec6-b4e6-817f1af6e821
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Tracking Theory](tracking-theory-synthesis-0bad637f-a0d8-4d67-8d05-73b0a7027f5a.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that knowledge requires something more than consistent truth tracking, such as justification or evidence.**, **If a belief coincidentally tracks the truth purely by luck, it is implausible this alone constitutes knowledge.**
