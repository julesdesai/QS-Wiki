## Content

**For a belief to constitute knowledge, it must be produced by cognitive faculties functioning according to their present-day purposes**, **Present-day purposes of cognitive faculties are defined by the demands of the current environment and societal expectations**, **The cognitive faculties must aim directly at forming true beliefs under current conditions**, **A belief formed in these conditions constitutes knowledge if it is true**
