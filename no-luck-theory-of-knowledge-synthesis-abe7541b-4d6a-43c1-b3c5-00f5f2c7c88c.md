# No-Luck Theory of Knowledge

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can someone have a justified true belief without knowledge?](can-someone-have-a-justified-true-belief-without-knowledge-antithesis-495ed798-5ec0-4fcd-940a-760bda6f1f35.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is not true merely by luck**, **The belief not being true by luck means the truth of the belief is not accidental relative to the justification**
