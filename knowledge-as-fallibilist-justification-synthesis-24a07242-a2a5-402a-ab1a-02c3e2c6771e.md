## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A person can have knowledge even if their justification does not guarantee truth**, **Justification requires a high degree of reliability, but not certainty, in belief formation**
