# Is the sensory data collected by perception always reliable?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f54f41df-1778-4db1-b19f-cbad586afc50
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Empiricism Summary](empiricism-summary-synthesis-4e15833e-fa6c-416c-a78e-6563a82d7628.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that perception can be distorted by factors such as illness, fatigue, or hallucinations. This principle together with instances where sensory data is unreliable entails that perception does not always collect reliable data about external objects.**
