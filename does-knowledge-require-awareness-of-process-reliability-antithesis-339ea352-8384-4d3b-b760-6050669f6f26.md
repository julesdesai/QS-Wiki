# Does knowledge require awareness of process reliability?

**Node ID:** 339ea352-8384-4d3b-b760-6050669f6f26

**Terminal:** No

## Content

**Intuitively, one might argue that knowing requires awareness of how reliable a belief-forming process is, contradicting an external fact requirement.**, **A person could follow a reliable process unknowingly, leading to a belief, yet intuitively feel they lack knowledge without awareness of reliability.**
