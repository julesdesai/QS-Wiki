# Can epistemic responsibility be maintained with variable standards?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b1ab5d0b-a336-4bc4-a8e5-1109c0a0a068
**Depth:** 2
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism](contextualism-thesis-de4edc71-c75a-4fcd-afbe-4333fb44b1b5.md)

## Content

**Responsibility in epistemic practices involves knowing when to assert knowledge.**, **Consider a case where asserting knowledge depends on different contexts, leading to variable assertions. Intuitively, this variability may undermine epistemic responsibility.**, **Variable standards challenge the maintenance of consistent epistemic responsibility.**
