# How can one distinguish between intellectual virtue and sheer luck?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 6d6e3a01-d6be-411e-abab-7b656e38bf25
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-7601e066-11d5-4f0c-8c42-44a4cc1f650d.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person forms a true belief due to sheer luck while using reliable cognitive faculties. Intuitively, this does not constitute knowledge.**, **Reliability of cognitive faculties does not distinguish between truth arrived at by intellectual virtue and truth arrived at by luck.**
