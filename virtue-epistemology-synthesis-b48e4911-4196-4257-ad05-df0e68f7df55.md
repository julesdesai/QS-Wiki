# Virtue Epistemology

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief results from an intellectual virtue**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is true, they believe it, and their belief results from an intellectual virtue**
