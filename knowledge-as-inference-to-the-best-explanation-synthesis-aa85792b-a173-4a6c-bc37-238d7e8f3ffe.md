## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is part of the best explanation for a set of empirical observations**, **A coherent network of beliefs is required to form the best explanation**
