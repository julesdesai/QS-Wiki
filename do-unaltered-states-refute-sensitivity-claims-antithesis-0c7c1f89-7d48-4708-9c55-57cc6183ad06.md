# Do unaltered states refute sensitivity claims?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0c7c1f89-7d48-4708-9c55-57cc6183ad06
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Nozick's Tracking Theory](nozicks-tracking-theory-synthesis-f1663159-56f4-4f6f-b53a-d60dc9dbfcb7.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where external conditions that might invalidate a belief are never present to affect the belief. Intuitively, if knowledge required sensitivity to these never-manifesting conditions, legitimate knowledge might be wrongly discredited.**, **It is implausible to impose present epistemic judgments based on hypothetical but inexistent threat scenarios unrelated to current evidence validation.**
