## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief coheres with a system of interconnected justified beliefs**, **A system of beliefs is coherent if its elements logically support each other without contradictions**
