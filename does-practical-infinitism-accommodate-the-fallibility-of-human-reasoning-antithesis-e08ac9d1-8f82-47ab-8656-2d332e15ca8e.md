# Does practical infinitism accommodate the fallibility of human reasoning?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e08ac9d1-8f82-47ab-8656-2d332e15ca8e

**Terminal:** No

## Content

**Humans are susceptible to errors in reasoning, which can undermine the entire basis for justified true belief with practically adequate justification.**, **If all reasoning must be perfect for knowledge, practically adequate justification may be overly demanding.**
