# Can foundational beliefs be truly evident without inference?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 34af5dae-de52-4a3c-a600-82ae26a706c8
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Evidentialist Foundationalism](evidentialist-foundationalism-synthesis-8074c848-6a3d-411d-b5c9-e6a07bd8fe19.md)

## Content

**Foundational beliefs are those that do not derive their justification from other beliefs.**, **A belief cannot be evident if it requires inference from other beliefs to be justified.**, **A belief is either justified inferentially through other beliefs or it lacks justification.**
