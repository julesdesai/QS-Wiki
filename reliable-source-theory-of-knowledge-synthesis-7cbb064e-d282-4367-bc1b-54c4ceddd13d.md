# Reliable Source Theory of Knowledge

**Terminal:** No

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed based on a reliable process or source**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is formed based on a reliable process or source**
