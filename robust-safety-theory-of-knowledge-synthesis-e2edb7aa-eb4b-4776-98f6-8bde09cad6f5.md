## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is not easily false**, **A belief is robustly safe if in all nearby possible worlds with no drastic, unanticipated changes, the belief remains true**, **A person knows a proposition if the belief is true in a majority of comparable and stable situations**, **Knowledge is resistant to being easily false provided that no dramatic, unforeseen alterations occur**
