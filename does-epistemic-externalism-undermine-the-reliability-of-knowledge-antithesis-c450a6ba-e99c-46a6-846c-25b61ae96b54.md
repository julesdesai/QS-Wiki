# Does epistemic externalism undermine the reliability of knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c450a6ba-e99c-46a6-846c-25b61ae96b54
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Epistemic Externalism](epistemic-externalism-synthesis-da0a1d28-64c8-4b8c-8417-38bce9b3007e.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person believes they have reliable knowledge based on an external factor that is actually misleading. Intuitively, we would judge that such knowledge is not reliable.**, **Knowledge relies only on external factors can result in misleading beliefs being classified as knowledge.**, **It is implausible that knowledge should be considered reliable if based on external but misleading factors.**
