# Does logic hold meaning without empirical testing?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 54db38b3-e5b6-41f7-ba3f-4d29cdbdfa5b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Verificationism](verificationism-synthesis-742a7e84-42a2-4d13-8c35-af2e03d93250.md)

## Content

**Mathematical truths such as "2+2=4" are not subject to empirical testing or perception but are universally accepted as meaningful**, **If a statement only has meaning if it can be empirically verified, then mathematical truths would lack meaning**, **It is implausible to assert that mathematical truths are meaningless as they form the basis for many rational arguments**
