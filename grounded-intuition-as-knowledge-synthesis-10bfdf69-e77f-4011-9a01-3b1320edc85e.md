## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, sufficient justification can be provided by grounded intuitions**, **Grounded intuitions are formed by a history of justified experiences, without requiring conscious awareness**
