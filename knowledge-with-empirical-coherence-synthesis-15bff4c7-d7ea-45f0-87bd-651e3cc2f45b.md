## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres with empirically verifiable beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that foundational beliefs are empirically verifiable**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief coheres with empirical verification, and foundational beliefs are empirically verifiable**
