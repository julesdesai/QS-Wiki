## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is linked to truth through a reliable cognitive process**, **The reliability must be linked in such a way that it generally leads to true beliefs in most cases**
