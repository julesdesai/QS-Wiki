# Knowledge as True Belief with No Defeaters

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 4cb1165e-3981-4b53-b766-45e44f864de0
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is awareness of justification always accessible?](is-awareness-of-justification-always-accessible-antithesis-b4e70aeb-eed2-4170-9ae3-02aab5af96c3.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that no defeaters for their belief exist**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and no defeaters for their belief exist**
