# Is reliable source knowledge different from justified belief knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e1d4b76f-e2a4-48d5-9cc4-1252963ec3f3
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Hybrid Internalism](hybrid-internalism-synthesis-3d9ce0d9-6ba2-4665-a054-ed06708d6ee0.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a person believes something coming from a historically reliable source, but they cannot access the basis of this reliability anymore due to changed circumstances. Intuitively, we either have to say that they still have knowledge due to past reliability or acknowledge that knowledge requires updated justification. If they have knowledge, it suggests reliability suffices without current verification.**
