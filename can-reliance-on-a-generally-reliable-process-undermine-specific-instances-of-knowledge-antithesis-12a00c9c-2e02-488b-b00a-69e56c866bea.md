# Can reliance on a generally reliable process undermine specific instances of knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 12a00c9c-2e02-488b-b00a-69e56c866bea
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalism with Reliable Beliefs](externalism-with-reliable-beliefs-synthesis-44f393ff-3a5c-4b93-8788-7ab032b1badd.md)

## Content

**Consider a person who frequently uses a news channel with a high rate of accuracy to believe in new political developments.**, **During a breaking news event, the channel misreports a detail that is essential to understanding the story’s true context, yet the report aligns with what happens by chance.**, **Believing what the channel reports, the person ascribed truth to their belief, but given the accidental correctness of the report, intuitively, it feels they lack true knowledge.**
