# Knowledge with a Sensitivity Clause

**Parent:** [Can background knowledge serve as an unnoticed defeater?](can-background-knowledge-serve-as-an-unnoticed-defeater-antithesis-3867448a-6c06-4fa2-8bd5-5be6f9ad5ec0.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is sensitive, meaning they would not believe it if it were false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is sensitive**
