# Can cognitive faculties produce knowledge in unsuitable environments?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7872a3ce-afb5-4752-9258-f755e1181343
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Proper Function Theory](proper-function-theory-synthesis-7d89ec66-0836-44e3-8613-d999cc659dfb.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where an individual is using cognitive faculties designed for one environment but is currently in a drastically different environment, such as being underwater with normal breathing faculties. Intuitively, we may judge that they could still know things even if their faculties are not specifically designed for this environment.**
