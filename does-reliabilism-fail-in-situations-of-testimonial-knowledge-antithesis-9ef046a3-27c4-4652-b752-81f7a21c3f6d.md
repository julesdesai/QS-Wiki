# Does reliabilism fail in situations of testimonial knowledge?

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person believes a proposition based on testimony from an unreliable source that happens to be correct this time. Intuitively, we would judge that the person lacks knowledge.**, **The belief based on testimony is true, but the review process of the source for reliability reveals unreliability.**
