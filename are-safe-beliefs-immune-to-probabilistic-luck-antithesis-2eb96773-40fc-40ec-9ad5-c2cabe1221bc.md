# Are safe beliefs immune to probabilistic luck?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2eb96773-40fc-40ec-9ad5-c2cabe1221bc
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Safety Theory](safety-theory-synthesis-3968e05f-d744-409d-bfbb-59e5b6ab2a0b.md)

## Content

**Probabilistic luck occurs when a belief turns out to be true merely by chance despite low odds of being true.**, **A belief could remain true in most nearby possible worlds but only by luck in those worlds.**, **If a safe belief is true by luck, it challenges the notion that safety robustly guards against epistemic luck.**
