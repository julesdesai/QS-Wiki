# Can knowledge be undermined by failing the sensitivity condition across close possibilities?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 26e84018-c869-4ce9-a2e4-e1c63829c314
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Tracking Theory of Knowledge](tracking-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-34b45a3c-9960-48d7-bb61-82e72864432f.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where a person's belief in a true proposition is insensitive to certain nearby false scenarios because the method used to form the belief would not distinguish between the true and the false in these close scenarios.**, **In such cases, intuitively we might judge that the person's belief does not amount to knowledge despite meeting the basic criteria for tracking because the belief fails to genuinely reflect the actual world over close alternatives.**
