## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have evidence for the proposition**, **A person knows a proposition if the belief is formed by a reliable process and is supported by evidence**, **A reliable process is one that produces true beliefs more often than false ones**, **A person knows a proposition if the proposition is true**
