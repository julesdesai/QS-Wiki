## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed in a way that reliably tracks the truth within their context**, **The reliability of belief formation takes into account epistemic context, including environment and background information**
