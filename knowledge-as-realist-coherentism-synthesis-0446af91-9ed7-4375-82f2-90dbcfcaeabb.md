## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres with a set of justified beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that some foundational beliefs must directly connect to reality**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief coheres with a set of justified beliefs, and foundational beliefs have a direct connection to reality**
