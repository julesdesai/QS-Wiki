# Maintained Evidence Condition for Knowledge

**Parent:** [Does the tracking theory account for changes in belief over time?](does-the-tracking-theory-account-for-changes-in-belief-over-time-antithesis-dabbe19d-7b63-4dba-81f9-a720bcbc6563.md)

## Content

**Knowledge requires that one's belief tracks the truth**, **A person's belief tracks the truth if, in relevant scenarios, they would not believe it if it were false**, **A person's belief tracks the truth if, in relevant scenarios, they do believe it when it is true**, **For belief to remain knowledge, the evidence must remain reliable over time**, **Once the reliability of evidence is compromised, the belief may no longer be considered knowledge**
