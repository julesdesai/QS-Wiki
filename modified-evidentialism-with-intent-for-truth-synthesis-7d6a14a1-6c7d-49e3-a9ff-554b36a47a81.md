# Modified Evidentialism with Intent for Truth

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Does evidence from an unwitting source count towards knowledge?](does-evidence-from-an-unwitting-source-count-towards-knowledge-antithesis-b32cadd1-6416-42da-9db6-d082f9f4eacc.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the evidence is provided with an intent for truth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the evidence is provided with an intent for truth**
