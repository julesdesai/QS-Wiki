# How does indirect realism account for hallucinations?

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Indirect Realism](indirect-realism-synthesis-10d28774-b321-4c8b-8fd8-c00c96dc97dd.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person hallucinates a pink elephant and perceives it as real. Indirect realism implies this hallucination is perceived through mental representations similar to real objects, thus possibly equating hallucinations with reality.**
