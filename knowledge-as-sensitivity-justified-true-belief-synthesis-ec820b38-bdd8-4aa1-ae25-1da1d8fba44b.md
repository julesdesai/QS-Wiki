# Knowledge as Sensitivity Justified True Belief

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** ec820b38-bdd8-4aa1-ae25-1da1d8fba44b
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does indirect evidence undermine knowledge?](does-indirect-evidence-undermine-knowledge-antithesis-f7f7f079-48d8-4c96-9cd0-163c90ba4b0f.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that, had the proposition been false, they would not have believed it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, the proposition is true, and had the proposition been false, they would not have believed it**
