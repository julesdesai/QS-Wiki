## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres with a consistent and comprehensive system of beliefs**, **For justification to be provided by an infinite series of non-repeating reasons**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that infinite coherence among reasons supports the belief in the truth of the proposition**
