## Content

**Knowledge arises when belief is formed by an exemplar intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **An exemplar virtue is one that unfailingly leads to true beliefs when exercised**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe it, the proposition is true, and their belief is formed by an exemplar intellectual virtue**
