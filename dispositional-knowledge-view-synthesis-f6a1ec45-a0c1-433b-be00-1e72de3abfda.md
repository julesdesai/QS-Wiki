## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is guided by a dispositional awareness of the justification for believing**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the dispositional awareness influences their belief formation even if not actively present at each moment**
