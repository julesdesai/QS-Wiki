## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that this belief would not easily be false in nearby possible worlds**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is safe from likely error despite fallible justification**
