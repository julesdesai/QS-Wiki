# What role do false beliefs play in assessing cognitive faculties?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 6c8f42d7-3653-4e3b-8186-07fded87397c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Proper Functionalism Summary](proper-functionalism-summary-synthesis-5b044e3f-97d9-4386-9c2f-bd333212a4a8.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person forms many true beliefs and just one false belief that fits their design plan. The presence of a systematically false belief seems to question whether the cognitive faculties are functioning properly.**, **A design plan that allows for systematic false belief formation makes it implausible to consider the resulting true beliefs as knowledge.**
