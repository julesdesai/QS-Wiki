## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **Justification is an endless chain of non-repeating, inferential reasons**, **Knowledge does not require complete access to all justificatory reasons**
