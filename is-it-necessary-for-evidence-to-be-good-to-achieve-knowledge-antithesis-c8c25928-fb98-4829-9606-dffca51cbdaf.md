# Is it necessary for evidence to be good to achieve knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c8c25928-fb98-4829-9606-dffca51cbdaf
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Evidentialist Reliabilism](evidentialist-reliabilism-synthesis-b2fd7651-b8ba-4bbd-aa9b-2c3c304a468b.md)

## Content

**Suppose a reliable process produces a true belief, yet the belief is based on poor or insufficient evidence.**, **It is implausible to assert that knowledge is obtained when the belief is based on bad evidential grounds, despite the reliability of the process.**
