## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief maintains stability across various situations or environments**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief-forming process avoids systematic error in diverse environments**
