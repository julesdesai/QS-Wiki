# Are intellectual character traits sufficient for all kinds of knowledge?

## Content

**Consider a case in which a mathematician uses a complex theorem without full understanding of its proof, achieving correct results purely based on trust in other experts.**, **In such a scenario, knowledge appears present despite the absence of an individual's display of cognitive virtues or intellectual character traits.**
