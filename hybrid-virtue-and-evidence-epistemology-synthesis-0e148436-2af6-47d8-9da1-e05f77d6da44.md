## Content

**Knowledge is true belief arising from intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is also necessary that their belief is supported by evidence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **The combination of intellectual virtue and evidence renders belief as knowledge**
