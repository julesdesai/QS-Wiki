## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification for belief meets a fixed, context-independent standard**, **The standards for knowledge do not depend on the context of the belief**
