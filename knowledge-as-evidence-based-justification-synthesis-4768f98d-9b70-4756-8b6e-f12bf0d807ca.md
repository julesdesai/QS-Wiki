## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have a justification for the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their justification for believing the proposition precludes false lemmas and the proposition is true**
