## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief coheres with a contextually appropriate standard of justification**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief coheres with a contextually appropriate standard of justification**
