## Content

**Justification involves having an infinite series of non-repeating reasons within some epistemic contexts**, **In certain contexts, justification does not require an infinite series of reasons and relies on practical consensus**, **Knowledge requires beliefs to be justified by an infinite chain of reasons in contexts where infinite series are relevant**, **Each reason in the chain provides support for the subsequent reason where infinite series apply**
