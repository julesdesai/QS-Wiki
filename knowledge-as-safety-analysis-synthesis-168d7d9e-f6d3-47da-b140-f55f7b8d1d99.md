**Parent:** [Is reliability alone sufficient for knowledge in all cases?](is-reliability-alone-sufficient-for-knowledge-in-all-cases-antithesis-668f24a2-f474-44f4-850e-a6a712899748.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief could not easily be false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief could not easily be false**
