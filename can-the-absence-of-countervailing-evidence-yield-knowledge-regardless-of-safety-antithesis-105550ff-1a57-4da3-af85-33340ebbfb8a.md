# Can the absence of countervailing evidence yield knowledge regardless of safety?

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person believes a proposition true and lacks any evidence to the contrary. Intuitively, this absence alone should not suffice for knowledge.**, **It is implausible that knowledge can depend solely on the absence of contravening evidence without positive support.**
