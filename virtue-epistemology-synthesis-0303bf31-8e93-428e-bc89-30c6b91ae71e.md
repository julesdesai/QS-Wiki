## Content

**Knowledge is a belief arising from intellectual virtue**, **Intellectual virtues are qualities like open-mindedness, intellectual courage, and attentiveness**, **A belief must be true for it to be considered knowledge**, **The practicality or usefulness of a belief is not required for it to be considered knowledge**
