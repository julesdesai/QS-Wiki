# Can a person know without awareness of potential defeaters?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7f1332ac-7e59-4700-ba4e-f8264c60643c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism with Defeat Condition](contextualism-with-defeat-condition-synthesis-644e1e82-6b43-472b-8e60-41eaf47756ee.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a person believes a proposition that is true, justified, and contextually appropriate but remains unaware of a potential defeater not immediately evident.**, **Intuitively, we might still consider this belief as knowledge, suggesting undefeated defeaters may not always be necessary for knowledge.**
