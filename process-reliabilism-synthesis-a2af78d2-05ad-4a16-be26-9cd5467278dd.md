## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a process that usually yields true beliefs**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person believes the proposition**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is produced by a process that usually yields true beliefs, the proposition is true, and the person believes it**
