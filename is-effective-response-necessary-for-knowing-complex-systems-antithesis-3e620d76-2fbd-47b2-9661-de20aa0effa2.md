# Is effective response necessary for knowing complex systems?

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Direct Realism](pragmatic-direct-realism-synthesis-4173acef-5348-4f34-a95b-b6ba164e9e84.md)

## Content

**A person studying climate change might have a deep theoretical understanding but is unable to predict short-term weather due to inherent system complexities. Intuitively, their inability to provide consistent effective responses does not negate their knowledge.**, **Theoretical knowledge about complex systems may exist independently of practical effectiveness, highlighting limitations of expecting effective responses as a knowledge criterion.**
