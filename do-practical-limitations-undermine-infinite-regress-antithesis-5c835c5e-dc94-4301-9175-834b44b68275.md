# Do practical limitations undermine infinite regress?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5c835c5e-dc94-4301-9175-834b44b68275
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitist Coherentism](infinitist-coherentism-synthesis-c961c647-ce7e-4676-a333-4ee929ef8887.md)

## Content

**Consider practical human cognitive limitations in maintaining an infinite regress of reasons. Intuitively, we would judge that these limitations prevent genuine infinite justification.**, **A plausible principle is that justification must be pragmatically achievable by human thinkers, which an infinite regress seems to contradict.**
