# How does reliance on pragmatic success weigh against factual inaccuracies in knowledge attribution?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e5a56d64-39be-4079-8843-dbd6da4afc15
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatism with Reliabilism](pragmatism-with-reliabilism-synthesis-504b62f9-b2fa-4831-be00-0daf59bd2488.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person uses an outdated map for navigation but successfully reaches a destination through instinct. Intuitively, reaching the destination does not overcome the map's inaccuracy to constitute knowledge.**, **The principle that factual accuracy is central to knowledge seems overlooked if pragmatic success alone suffices.**
