## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, there must be a causal connection between the fact that makes the proposition true and the person's belief in that proposition**, **A belief is justified if it is based on a reliable causal chain**, **A false lemma cannot be causally connected to a true proposition in a way that justifies belief in that proposition**
