## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have a belief in the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is apt and successful due to cognitive skill**, **A person knows a proposition if and only if their belief is true because of their intellectual competence**
