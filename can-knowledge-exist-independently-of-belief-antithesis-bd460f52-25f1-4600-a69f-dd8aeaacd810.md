# Can knowledge exist independently of belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** bd460f52-25f1-4600-a69f-dd8aeaacd810
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Knowledge as Virtuous True Belief](knowledge-as-virtuous-true-belief-synthesis-0cc7c39c-3c22-4b7d-8311-bdde6f1e8698.md)

## Content

**Consider a case in which a person has all the evidence for a true proposition presented to them, yet they remain agnostic about the truth of the proposition due to lack of belief. Intuitively, the person could still be said to know this proposition.**, **Knowledge can be attributed to an individual even if they do not actively hold a belief in the proposition known.**
