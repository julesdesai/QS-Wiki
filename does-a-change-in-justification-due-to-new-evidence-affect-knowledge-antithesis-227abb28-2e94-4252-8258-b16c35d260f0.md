# Does a change in justification due to new evidence affect knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 227abb28-2e94-4252-8258-b16c35d260f0
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [No-False-Grounds Theory](no-false-grounds-theory-synthesis-00c0ff61-646c-4d63-9780-b055a1d82c9f.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where someone believes a fact based on outdated statistical data that has since been significantly revised, but their belief remains true through other unrelated improvements.**, **This situation suggests that knowledge should adapt with new information, highlighting the dynamic relationship between belief justification and evolving evidence.**, **If justification is static and becomes irrelevant, the status of the belief as knowledge is cast into doubt.**
