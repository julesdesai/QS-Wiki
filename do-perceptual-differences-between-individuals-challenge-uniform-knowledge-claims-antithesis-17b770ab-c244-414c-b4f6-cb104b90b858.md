# Do perceptual differences between individuals challenge uniform knowledge claims?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 17b770ab-c244-414c-b4f6-cb104b90b858
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Critical Direct Realism](critical-direct-realism-synthesis-6d68b442-c014-46c1-8b6c-ef16694b0a4c.md)

## Content

**Individuals with different sensory capacities, such as those who are colorblind, experience different perceptions from the same stimuli.**, **If cognitive processing depends on subjective experiences, direct perception may lead to divergent knowledge claims about the same object.**, **Knowledge claims that vary due to differing perceptions challenge the notion of universally valid direct perception.**
