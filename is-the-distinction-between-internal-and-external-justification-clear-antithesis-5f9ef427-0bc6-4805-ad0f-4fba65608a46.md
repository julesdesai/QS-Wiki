# Is the distinction between internal and external justification clear?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5f9ef427-0bc6-4805-ad0f-4fba65608a46
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Internalist Reliabilism Summary](internalist-reliabilism-summary-synthesis-afd99030-664a-4a62-9431-6f8bc1e36717.md)

## Content

**Consider a belief held with internal access to its formation process yet influenced by unseen external factors.**, **The distinction between internally justified reliable processes and external influence can be vague or overlapping.**, **This ambiguity complicates how internalist reliabilism defines knowledge.**
