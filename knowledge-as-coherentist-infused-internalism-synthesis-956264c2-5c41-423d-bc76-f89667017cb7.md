## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification involves an internally accessible coherent set of beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there exists an internally accessible coherent set of beliefs justifying the belief**
