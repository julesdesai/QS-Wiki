# Strong Foundationalist Knowledge

**Parent:** [What if all beliefs in a coherent system are false except one?](what-if-all-beliefs-in-a-coherent-system-are-false-except-one-antithesis-a3726f71-f65b-4abe-9ea6-5e3abc95c8f5.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is based on a foundational set of non-inferentially justified beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is based on a foundational set of non-inferentially justified beliefs**
