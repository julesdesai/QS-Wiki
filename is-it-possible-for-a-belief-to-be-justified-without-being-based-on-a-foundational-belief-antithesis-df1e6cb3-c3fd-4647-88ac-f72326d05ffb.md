# Is it possible for a belief to be justified without being based on a foundational belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** df1e6cb3-c3fd-4647-88ac-f72326d05ffb
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundationalism Summary](foundationalism-summary-synthesis-c35a9476-dc46-42db-bda2-17c735809ae7.md)

## Content

**Consider a case of a scientist who develops a justified theory based on a complex set of observations and models, none of which are self-evident or empirically certain on their own. Intuitively, the theory can still be justified.**
