## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, the person's belief continuously recognizes propositions through reliable mechanisms**, **For a person to know a proposition, these mechanisms must adjust the belief in accordance to truth changes**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient if both recognition and adjustment processes are operational**
