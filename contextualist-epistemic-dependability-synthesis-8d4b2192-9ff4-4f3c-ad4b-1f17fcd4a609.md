## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief depends on a contextually appropriate, stable, reliable relation to the truth and appropriate justification according to the context**
