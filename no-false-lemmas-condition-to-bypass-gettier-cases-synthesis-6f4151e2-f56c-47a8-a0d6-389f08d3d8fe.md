# No False Lemmas condition to bypass Gettier cases

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person's belief does not rely on any false assumptions**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief does not rely on any false assumptions**
