## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the person were to believe the proposition in nearby possible worlds, the proposition would not be false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have justification for believing the proposition**
