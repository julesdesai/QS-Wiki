# Can dispositional abilities account for subconscious knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** cac8a9e0-9ba8-44e7-8bc8-07a8edad18e9
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Dispositional Account of Knowledge](dispositional-account-of-knowledge-synthesis-697a3559-6974-4820-bdde-37c51f6f9430.md)

## Content

**A case in which an individual possesses subconscious knowledge, as in knowing how to stay safe in an emergency due to ingrained instincts, but they never consciously formed a true belief about the actions involved, challenges the assertion that all knowledge requires dispositional abilities**, **It is implausible that subconscious knowledge depends solely on the ability to consciously form true beliefs, since such knowledge often bypasses conscious reflection**
