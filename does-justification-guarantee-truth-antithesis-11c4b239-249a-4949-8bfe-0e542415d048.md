# Does justification guarantee truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 11c4b239-249a-4949-8bfe-0e542415d048
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Knowledge as Virtue Epistemology](knowledge-as-virtue-epistemology-synthesis-02df61d2-9c7e-4ff7-b545-6d28025dd3a5.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that one can be justified in believing a proposition that turns out to be false.**, **This principle together with the view that justification is necessary for knowledge implies that one could have knowledge even with some false beliefs, which is counterintuitive.**
