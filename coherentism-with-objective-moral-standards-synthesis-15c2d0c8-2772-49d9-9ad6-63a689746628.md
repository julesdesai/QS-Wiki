# Coherentism with Objective Moral Standards

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 15c2d0c8-2772-49d9-9ad6-63a689746628
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is internal consistency enough to justify moral knowledge?](is-internal-consistency-enough-to-justify-moral-knowledge-antithesis-a60b0ce9-331c-4817-b65e-767f9187695a.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres with a comprehensive system of beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief aligns with objective moral standards**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief coheres with a comprehensive system of beliefs, and aligns with objective moral standards**
