# Invariantism with Strict Justification Standards

**Parent:** [Can context shift patterns of justified belief into knowledge?](can-context-shift-patterns-of-justified-belief-into-knowledge-antithesis-bd002401-3a3b-4fb2-8b75-9bb59fd8959e.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **The standards for justification are constant and do not vary across different contexts**
