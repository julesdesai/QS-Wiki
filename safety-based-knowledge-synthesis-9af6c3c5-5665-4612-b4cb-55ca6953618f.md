## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief could not have easily been false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe it, it is true, and their belief is safely true in nearby possible worlds**
