## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed by a reliable cognitive process**, **A belief is safe if in nearby possible worlds where the person believes the proposition in the same way, the proposition is not false**
