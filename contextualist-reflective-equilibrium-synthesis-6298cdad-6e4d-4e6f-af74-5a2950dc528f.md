# Contextualist Reflective Equilibrium

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 6298cdad-6e4d-4e6f-af74-5a2950dc528f
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does justification require infallibility?](does-justification-require-infallibility-antithesis-0eaece6c-5713-47c2-8d94-88db2471775f.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification of belief varies with the context and contributes to reflective equilibrium**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification varies contextually with such equilibrium**
