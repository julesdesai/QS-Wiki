# Knowledge as Sensitivity

**Parent:** [Can knowledge be a product of an accidental alignment of belief and truth?](can-knowledge-be-a-product-of-an-accidental-alignment-of-belief-and-truth-antithesis-039d15bc-4ef5-4372-ae6c-a6223a937a85.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief would not be held if the proposition were false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief would not be held if the proposition were false**
