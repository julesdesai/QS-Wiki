## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is a clear and straightforward justification for the belief**, **Justification through simple and direct evidence can suffice**
