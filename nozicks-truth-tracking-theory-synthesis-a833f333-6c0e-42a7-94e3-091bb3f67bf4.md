## Content

**Knowledge requires one's belief to track the truth through counterfactual situations**, **A belief tracks truth if, in nearby situations where the proposition is false, the belief would not be held**, **In nearby situations where the proposition is true, the belief would be held**, **Knowledge is preserved if skeptical doubts do not disrupt the truth-tracking conditions**
