## Content

**A person knows a proposition if there is an appropriate causal connection between the proposition and the person's belief in it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the causal connection eliminates the possibility of misunderstanding**
