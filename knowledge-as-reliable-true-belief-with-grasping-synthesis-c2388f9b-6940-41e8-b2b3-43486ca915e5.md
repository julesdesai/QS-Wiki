## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief results from a reliable process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they grasp the modal conditions that secure the truth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, their belief results from a reliable process, and they grasp the modal conditions that secure the truth**
