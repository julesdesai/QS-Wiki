## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **Beliefs are justified by how they appear to the subject when cross-checked with additional inputs**, **If something seems true to a subject and is cross-checked against other reliable propositions, they have prima facie justification for believing it**
