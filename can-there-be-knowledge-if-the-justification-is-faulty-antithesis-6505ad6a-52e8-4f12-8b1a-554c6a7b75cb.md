# Can there be knowledge if the justification is faulty?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 6505ad6a-52e8-4f12-8b1a-554c6a7b75cb
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [No-Defeaters Theory of Knowledge](no-defeaters-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-0be8e529-9437-4b4e-96a3-c585180c1210.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person believes a true proposition due to a logical fallacy in their reasoning, with no defeaters present. Intuitively, we would not count this belief as knowledge.**, **Faulty justification undermines the integrity of belief even in the absence of defeaters, suggesting it cannot constitute knowledge.**
