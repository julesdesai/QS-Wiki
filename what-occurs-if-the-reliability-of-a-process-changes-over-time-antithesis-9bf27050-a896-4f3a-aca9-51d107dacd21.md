**Consider a process that was once reliable, but due to changes over time, becomes unreliable. Intuitively, the knowledge basis from that process is threatened retroactively.**, **Changes in context-sensitive factors affecting reliability pose challenges to whether past 'knowledge' formed through such processes retains its status.**
