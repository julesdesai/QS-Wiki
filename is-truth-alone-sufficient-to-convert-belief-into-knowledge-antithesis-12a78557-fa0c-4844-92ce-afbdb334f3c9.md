# Is truth alone sufficient to convert belief into knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 12a78557-fa0c-4844-92ce-afbdb334f3c9
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue epistemology view on knowledge](virtue-epistemology-view-on-knowledge-synthesis-e49b38ea-0e37-4416-ac67-ff35516a157e.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that truth alone, without any other condition such as justification or proper functioning faculties, converts belief into knowledge.**, **Consider a scenario where a person holds a true belief based on misinformation; intuitively, this does not seem to be a case of knowledge.**
