# Knowledge as Proper Functionalism

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Is safe belief reliable belief?](is-safe-belief-reliable-belief-antithesis-8488d7a4-a657-409a-958c-b8f7b48841d0.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the cognitive faculties involved in forming the belief are functioning properly**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the cognitive faculties used were functioning properly**
