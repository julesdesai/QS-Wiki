## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified in a way that does not rely solely on internal mental states**, **Externalist justification incorporates factors outside an individual's mental states to justify a belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is justified in a way that includes external factors**
