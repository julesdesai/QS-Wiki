## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have an epistemic virtue in forming the belief**, **A person knows a proposition if it is true, they believe it, and the belief is formed through epistemic virtue**
