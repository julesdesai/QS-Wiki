## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **True belief must be produced by an external factor that reliably leads to truth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their cognitive faculties reliably produce true beliefs**
