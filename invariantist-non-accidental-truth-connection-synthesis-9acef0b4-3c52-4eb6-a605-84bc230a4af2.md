# Invariantist Non-Accidental Truth Connection

**Parent:** [Can justified true belief always be sufficient for knowledge?](can-justified-true-belief-always-be-sufficient-for-knowledge-antithesis-cded4c1e-75ed-4e0b-8992-377a8d124108.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, justification must ensure the truth is not an accidental relationship between the belief and the fact**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and justification ensures the truth is relevantly connected**
