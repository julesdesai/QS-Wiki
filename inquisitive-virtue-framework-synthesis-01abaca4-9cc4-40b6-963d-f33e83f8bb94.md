## Content

**Intellectual virtues are defined by an inquisitive attitude towards seeking truth**, **The exercise of intellectual virtues does not ensure true beliefs in all cases**, **A commitment to intellectual virtues can be found even in mistaken beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief was formed through the diligent application of intellectual virtues**
