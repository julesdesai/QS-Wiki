## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief results from a reliable cognitive process**, **A belief results from a cognitive process stemming from intellectual virtues such as open-mindedness and intellectual honesty**
