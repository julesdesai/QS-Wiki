## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, the belief-forming process must be reliable**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief withstands scrutiny against communal disagreement**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the belief is formed through a reliable process, and the belief withstands scrutiny against communal disagreement**
