# Can knowledge be attributed to an agent when intellectual virtues fail?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 415f1626-699e-4c28-8acc-b52693917aea
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-7601e066-11d5-4f0c-8c42-44a4cc1f650d.md)

## Content

**If intellectual virtues lead to systematic errors in belief formation, such as perceptual illusions, the resulting beliefs are not knowledge despite their source.**, **Knowledge cannot be based on intellectual virtues if these virtues systematically lead to false beliefs.**
