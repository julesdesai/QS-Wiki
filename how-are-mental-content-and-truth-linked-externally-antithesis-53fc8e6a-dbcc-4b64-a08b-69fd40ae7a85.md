# How are mental content and truth linked externally?

**Node Type:** antithesis

**Depth:** 4

**Parent:** [Externalism About Mental Content](externalism-about-mental-content-synthesis-10b86dc5-14ba-470e-8399-957c36b317dc.md)

## Content

**Mental content is supposed to be aligned with external truth conditions.**, **External truth conditions can be unknown or misrepresented.**, **If misalignment with truth conditions is possible, mental content's dependency on factors outside one's cognitive access is undermined.**
