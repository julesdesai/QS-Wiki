## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person believes the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief in the proposition is formed through a reliable process**, **A reliable process is one that produces a high proportion of true beliefs**
