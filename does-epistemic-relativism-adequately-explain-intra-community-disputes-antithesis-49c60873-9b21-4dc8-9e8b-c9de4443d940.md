# Does epistemic relativism adequately explain intra-community disputes?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 49c60873-9b21-4dc8-9e8b-c9de4443d940
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Epistemic Relativism](epistemic-relativism-synthesis-8a36f430-fbe7-4356-93bb-efe42975c670.md)

## Content

**Consider two individuals within the same epistemic community who disagree about a proposition T despite following the same norms.**, **Epistemic relativism holds that community standards determine knowledge, yet the existence of disputes suggests that these standards are not uniformly applied or interpreted.**, **Such internal variations indicate that knowledge claims might not neatly conform to community norms, complicating the relativist position.**
