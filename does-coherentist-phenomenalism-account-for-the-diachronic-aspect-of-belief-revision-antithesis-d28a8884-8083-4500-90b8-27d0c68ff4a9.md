# Does coherentist phenomenalism account for the diachronic aspect of belief revision?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d28a8884-8083-4500-90b8-27d0c68ff4a9
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentist Phenomenalism](coherentist-phenomenalism-synthesis-7f881170-8b02-4aad-92a8-2bed5a4b8a3c.md)

## Content

**It is plausible that knowledge claims are tested and revised over time based on new experiences and evidence.**, **Coherentist phenomenalism emphasizes synchronic coherence at a particular time rather than diachronic consistency over time.**, **Without incorporating the role of new experiences in modifying beliefs, coherentist phenomenalism may overlook important dynamics of knowledge acquisition.**
