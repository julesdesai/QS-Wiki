# Does knowledge require understanding, not just truth-tracking?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 6ee2d2a8-27d0-48cd-9d2d-749b2e144750
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Information-Tracking Theory](information-tracking-theory-synthesis-990127f2-7220-489f-9c29-17860a8cc351.md)

## Content

**Knowledge involves more than just having true beliefs; it also requires understanding why the belief is true.**, **Understanding involves the ability to explain or make predictions based on the belief, which cannot be captured solely by truth-tracking.**
