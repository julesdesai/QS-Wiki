# Is evidence the only foundation for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1909a5c7-fabb-4dae-ab14-c97550572fec
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Grounding Knowledge in Evidence](grounding-knowledge-in-evidence-synthesis-000c43a5-6d20-4fdc-9093-7ec5ca2a4c8d.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that evidence is the sole foundation for all types of knowledge.**, **Suppose we have a case of a person who has intuitive insight into a mathematical truth without supporting evidence. Intuitively, we either have to say that the person has knowledge or that they do not. If we say they have knowledge, then intuitive insight can also be a foundation for knowledge. If we say they do not have knowledge, then evidence is indeed the sole foundation, which seems restrictive.**
