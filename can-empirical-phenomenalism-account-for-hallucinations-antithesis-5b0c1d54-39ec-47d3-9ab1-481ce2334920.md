# Can empirical phenomenalism account for hallucinations?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5b0c1d54-39ec-47d3-9ab1-481ce2334920
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Empirical Phenomenalism](empirical-phenomenalism-synthesis-0f019261-788c-4093-9ea8-d1a3828e8b76.md)

## Content

**In cases of hallucination, sensory experiences occur without any corresponding external objects being present.**, **If sensory experiences can exist without corresponding external objects, then empirical verification through perception is unreliable.**
