# Does context-based foundationalism resist empirical challenges?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b08e0e45-96ce-4b0e-90ef-0fb77930da95
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualist Foundationalism](contextualist-foundationalism-synthesis-b757e054-5c50-4073-a4ee-c1ee757cbdbb.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case where a scientist believes in a theory based on initial experimental context; empirical evidence from a different setting contradicts the theory.**, **Changes in empirical evidence are not adequately accounted for if beliefs are too closely tied to fluctuating contexts.**, **Empirical challenges necessitate foundational beliefs that transcend their immediate contextual justification.**
