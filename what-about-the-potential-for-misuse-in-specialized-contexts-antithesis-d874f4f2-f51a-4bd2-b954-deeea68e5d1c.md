## Content

**Suppose we have a scenario where specialized contexts, like legal or scientific settings, set incredibly high justification standards. Intuitively, we would judge this could lead to intentional misuse to obscure or deny straightforward knowledge.**, **A case in which context is used to manipulate or obstruct knowledge claims challenges the fairness and neutrality advocated by epistemic inquiry.**
