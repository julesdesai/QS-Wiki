# Knowledge as True Justified Acceptance

**Parent:** [Is a belief's practical usefulness sufficient for knowledge?](is-a-beliefs-practical-usefulness-sufficient-for-knowledge-antithesis-1f7cf1a1-e299-4fb0-bde3-7c9f8afcc704.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary to accept it as true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary to have justification for accepting the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they accept the proposition as true, the proposition is true, and they have justification for accepting the proposition**
