## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is a causal connection between the truth of the proposition and the person's belief in it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that there is a causal connection between the proposition's truth and their belief in it and that the proposition is true**
