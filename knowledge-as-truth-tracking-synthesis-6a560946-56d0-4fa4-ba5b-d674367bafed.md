# Knowledge as Truth-Tracking

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 6a560946-56d0-4fa4-ba5b-d674367bafed
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Do all types of knowledge require causation?](do-all-types-of-knowledge-require-causation-antithesis-caa2b824-f88b-4656-81da-a350a92145da.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief tracks the truth across possible scenarios**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief tracks the truth across possible scenarios**
