# Is action-based knowledge without conscious belief plausible?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7873b502-4cab-42b5-a281-a920734a5722
**Depth:** 4

**Parent:** [Contextualism with Dispositional Belief](contextualism-with-dispositional-belief-synthesis-1dbb9d87-3561-45d5-9c36-839374bee7ec.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person regularly saves money because they feel it's a good habit, not because they consciously believe in saving for the future.**, **Intuitively, we would not consider this person to know the importance of saving for the future if they act without conscious belief about its importance.**
