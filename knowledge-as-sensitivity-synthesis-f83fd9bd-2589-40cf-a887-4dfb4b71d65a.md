# Knowledge as Sensitivity

**Parent:** [Can false beliefs formed by reliable processes obscure the path to knowledge?](can-false-beliefs-formed-by-reliable-processes-obscure-the-path-to-knowledge-antithesis-2b4a3f1a-0a41-48b1-b43b-128e352fde95.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, they would not believe it**, **A person knows a proposition if it is true, they believe it, and their belief is sensitive to the truth of the proposition**
