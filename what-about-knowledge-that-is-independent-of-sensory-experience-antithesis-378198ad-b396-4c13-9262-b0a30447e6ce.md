# What about knowledge that is independent of sensory experience?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 378198ad-b396-4c13-9262-b0a30447e6ce
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Empiricism Summary](empiricism-summary-synthesis-4e15833e-fa6c-416c-a78e-6563a82d7628.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that introspective knowledge, such as understanding one's own thoughts or emotions, does not originate from sensory experience. This principle together with the direct access one has to their own mental states entails that some knowledge is independent of sensory experience.**
