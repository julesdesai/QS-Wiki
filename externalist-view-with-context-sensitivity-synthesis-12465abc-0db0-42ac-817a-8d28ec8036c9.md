## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have a true belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed through a reliable method**, **The reliability of the method must be contextually connected to the truth of the belief**, **The reliability of the method can vary based on contextual factors influencing the belief**
