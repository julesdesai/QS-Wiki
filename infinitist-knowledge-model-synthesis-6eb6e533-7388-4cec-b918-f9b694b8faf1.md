## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that there is an infinite, non-repeating chain of justifications**, **Each justification in the chain must be supported by a further justification**
