# Knowledge as Tracking

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief tracks the truth across close possible worlds**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief tracks the truth across close possible worlds**
