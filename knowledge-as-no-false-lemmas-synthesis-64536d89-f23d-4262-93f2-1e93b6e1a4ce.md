## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a belief to constitute knowledge, no false proposition can be an essential part of the reasoning leading to that belief**, **A belief resulting from a reliable process with no reliance on false lemmas qualifies as knowledge**
