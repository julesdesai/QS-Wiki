# Does the fake barn scenario challenge the tracking theory?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 687fa28f-2634-4462-8e57-e91283735c4f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Epistemic Externalism with Nozick's Tracking Theory](epistemic-externalism-with-nozicks-tracking-theory-synthesis-99a4169f-6bac-4b5a-b46b-52fe3916b903.md)

## Content

**In a scenario where a person is in fake barn country where there are many fake barns, if the person sees a real barn and believes it is a barn, intuitively we judge this belief as knowledge.**, **A belief about a barn in fake barn country tracks the truth if the belief wouldn't be held if it were false, and would be held if it were true.**, **It is counterintuitive to claim knowledge without the person discerning the fakes from the real barns.**
