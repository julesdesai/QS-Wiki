# Knowledge as Relevant Alternatives Theory

**Parent:** [Can contextual standards lead to skeptical outcomes?](can-contextual-standards-lead-to-skeptical-outcomes-antithesis-3bddeb8c-6fc8-43fc-96d2-60d6e8036974.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they can rule out all relevant alternatives to the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and they can rule out all relevant alternatives**
