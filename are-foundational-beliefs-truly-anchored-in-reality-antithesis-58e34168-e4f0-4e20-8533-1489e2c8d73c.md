# Are foundational beliefs truly anchored in reality?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 58e34168-e4f0-4e20-8533-1489e2c8d73c
**Depth:** 4

**Parent:** [Minimal Foundational Coherentism Summary](minimal-foundational-coherentism-summary-synthesis-0e7aa745-4942-4fbb-a96c-6043bbe35e5d.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a foundational belief is formed based on misleading evidence, which aligns coincidentally with reality. Intuitively, this belief does not seem reliably anchored in reality.**, **If foundational beliefs can be coincidentally true by misleading evidence, then they may not genuinely correspond to reality on a reliable basis.**
