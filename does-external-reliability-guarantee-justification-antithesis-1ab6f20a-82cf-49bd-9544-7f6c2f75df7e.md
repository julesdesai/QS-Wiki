# Does external reliability guarantee justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1ab6f20a-82cf-49bd-9544-7f6c2f75df7e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalist Coherentism](externalist-coherentism-synthesis-f4ef4378-ebfc-4a41-8845-189c2c8e0f20.md)

## Content

**A reliable belief-forming process can produce false beliefs if it operates under unusual or deceptive conditions.**, **A process might be generally reliable but fail in specific instances, leading to unjustified beliefs.**, **Reliability does not account for internal coherence, and a reliable process could still support incoherent beliefs.**
