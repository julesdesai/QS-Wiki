## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the method used to form the belief is reliably truth-producing**, **A method is reliably truth-producing if it yields a high ratio of true beliefs to false ones in the actual world**
