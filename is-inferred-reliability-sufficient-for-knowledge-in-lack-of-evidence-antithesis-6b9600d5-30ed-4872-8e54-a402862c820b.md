# Is inferred reliability sufficient for knowledge in lack of evidence?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 6b9600d5-30ed-4872-8e54-a402862c820b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Process Reliabilism](process-reliabilism-synthesis-73062a2b-1357-42ba-b901-d397b26dce96.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person believes their processes are reliable solely based on inference and not on direct evidence of past success. Intuitively, we might question if they truly have knowledge.**, **Imagine someone who assumes all of their cognitive processes are generally reliable due to cultural or classroom assumptions without real-world testing. We would claim that this assumption lacks intuitive knowledge support.**
