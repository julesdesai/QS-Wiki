## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **The standards for justification are sensitive to the practical interests of the person making the knowledge claim**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have practically appropriate justification, and the proposition is true**
