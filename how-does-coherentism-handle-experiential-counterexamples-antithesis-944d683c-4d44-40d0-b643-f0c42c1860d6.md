# How does coherentism handle experiential counterexamples?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 944d683c-4d44-40d0-b643-f0c42c1860d6
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Integrated Coherentism](integrated-coherentism-synthesis-026ba0da-a1c0-49d0-b737-4a448975b203.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where someone perceives a red apple but incorporates it into a belief system in which red apples do not exist as a result of an ingrained bias. Intuitively, the fact that the justified belief system contradicts direct experience seems incorrect.**, **A plausible principle suggests that direct experiential evidence should resist being overruled by mere coherence. This principle implies that coherentism could potentially marginalize or ignore first-hand experiences.**
