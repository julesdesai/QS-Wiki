# Enhanced Virtue Reliabilism with Error Avoidance

**Depth:** 3
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does possession of intellectual virtue guarantee knowledge?](does-possession-of-intellectual-virtue-guarantee-knowledge-antithesis-2599c2b3-55b4-4e46-8e9b-981dd88e353f.md)

## Content

**Knowledge is true belief that arises from intellectual virtue which reliably avoids error**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief arises from intellectual virtue that reliably avoids error**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe it, the proposition is true, and their intellectual virtue reliably avoids error**
