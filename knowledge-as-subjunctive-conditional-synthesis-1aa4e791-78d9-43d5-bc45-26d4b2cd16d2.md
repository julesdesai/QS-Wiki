# Knowledge as Subjunctive Conditional

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 1aa4e791-78d9-43d5-bc45-26d4b2cd16d2
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge be undermined by defeating evidence that one ignores?](can-knowledge-be-undermined-by-defeating-evidence-that-one-ignores-antithesis-63165ec4-3b37-4af5-a48f-eeb5628d959a.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, they would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and they would not believe it if false**
