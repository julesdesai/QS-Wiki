# Can a belief be safe but not the result of an intellectual virtue?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 473cf988-9e16-4145-a86f-a381b5ba5f71
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology with Safety Condition](virtue-epistemology-with-safety-condition-synthesis-2582b817-bed7-4f55-af09-985f5a9b310f.md)

## Content

**A belief can be safe if it could not easily have been false.**, **A belief that is safe might still not arise from an intellectual virtue if it is formed by luck or coincidence.**
