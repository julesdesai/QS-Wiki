## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have foundational justification for believing the proposition**, **Foundational justification involves basic beliefs that do not require further justification**, **Non-basic beliefs derive justification from their coherence with these basic beliefs**, **For a proposition to be known, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**
