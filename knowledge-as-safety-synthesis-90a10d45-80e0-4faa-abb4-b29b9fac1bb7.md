## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief could not easily have been false in similar circumstances**, **A person knows a proposition if it is true, they believe it, and their belief is safe from error in nearby possible worlds**
