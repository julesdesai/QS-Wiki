# Can true belief without understanding provide knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 743cfd94-dca0-4d6a-a357-a2a2cdbec843
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Access Internalism](access-internalism-synthesis-1ebb0a7e-8cd6-40ff-bf0f-a16161232f85.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person believes a true proposition by sheer luck without understanding why it’s true. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that the person lacks knowledge as their belief lacks foundation in understanding.**, **A person believing a true proposition without understanding why illustrates that true belief alone does not provide knowledge.**
