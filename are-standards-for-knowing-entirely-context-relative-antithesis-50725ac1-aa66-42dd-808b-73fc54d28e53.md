# Are standards for knowing entirely context-relative?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 50725ac1-aa66-42dd-808b-73fc54d28e53
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism with Defeat Condition](contextualism-with-defeat-condition-synthesis-644e1e82-6b43-472b-8e60-41eaf47756ee.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that knowledge requires a certain level of justification that does not solely depend on context.**, **This principle together with the idea of context-relative standards entails that some contexts may incorrectly recognize or deny knowledge.**
