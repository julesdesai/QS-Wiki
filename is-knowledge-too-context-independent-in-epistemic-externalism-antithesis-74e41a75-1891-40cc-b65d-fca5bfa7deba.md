# Is knowledge too context-independent in epistemic externalism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 74e41a75-1891-40cc-b65d-fca5bfa7deba
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Epistemic Externalism](epistemic-externalism-synthesis-da0a1d28-64c8-4b8c-8417-38bce9b3007e.md)

## Content

**In a case where different contexts require varying levels of evidence for the same belief, intuitively, we would judge that knowledge standards should vary with context.**, **The idea that standards for knowing are independent of context contradicts our intuitive understanding of varying evidence requirements in different situations.**, **It is implausible that knowledge standards do not change with the context if our intuitions suggest otherwise.**
