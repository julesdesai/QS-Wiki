# Can probability affect the reliability of a process for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 767a7210-b03a-4a35-b8f4-55fa4c6f922f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalist View](externalist-view-synthesis-75028f40-4ef7-4feb-a21d-1e6a75d787a6.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a belief is formed through a process that is reliable only at a lower probability threshold. Intuitively, we might judge such belief as lacking the certainty required for knowledge.**, **Higher probability of reliability might be necessary for true knowledge, as low-probability processes can undermine belief justification.**
