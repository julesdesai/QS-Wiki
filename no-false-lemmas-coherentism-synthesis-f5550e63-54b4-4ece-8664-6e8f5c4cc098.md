# No-False-Lemmas Coherentism

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification involves a coherent set of beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification does not rely on any false propositions**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief fits coherently within a system of beliefs, and the justification does not involve any false propositions**
