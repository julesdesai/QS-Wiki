# Is reliability measurable in all contexts?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7e8c2d83-9296-4919-a5e9-aa95c9e0f1d2
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism](reliabilism-synthesis-4db07ebc-4cb3-402b-b335-2b01121c3e1a.md)

## Content

**Consider a belief formed by a process that seems reliable but lacks a clear metric for reliability. Intuitively, this creates doubt about whether the belief constitutes knowledge**, **There is a plausible principle that some measure of reliability must be necessary to determine knowledge, and it is implausible to claim knowledge without such a measure**
