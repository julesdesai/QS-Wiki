# Necessity of True Belief and Justification

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary but not sufficient that they have justification for believing it**, **Knowledge-occurring conditions do not require multiple justifications**
