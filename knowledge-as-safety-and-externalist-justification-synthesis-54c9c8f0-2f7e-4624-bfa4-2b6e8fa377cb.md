## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for their belief to be safe and justified through external factors**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, their belief is safe from being false, and there is external justification for the belief**
