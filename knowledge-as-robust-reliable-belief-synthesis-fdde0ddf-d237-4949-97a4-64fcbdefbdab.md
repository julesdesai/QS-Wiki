# Knowledge as Robust Reliable Belief

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can a belief be considered knowledge if the person's understanding is flawed?](can-a-belief-be-considered-knowledge-if-the-persons-understanding-is-flawed-antithesis-e0a03798-600f-44ea-b2ab-c915987621d7.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief results from a highly reliable process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition and the proposition is true and their belief results from a highly reliable process**
