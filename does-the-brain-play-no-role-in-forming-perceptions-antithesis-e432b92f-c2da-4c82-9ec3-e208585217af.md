# Does the brain play no role in forming perceptions?

## Content

**Scientific evidence shows that brain processes significantly influence our perception, such as through the cerebral cortex's role in processing sensory input, suggesting that mental representations mediate perceptions. Intuitively, these processes cannot be bypassed or ignored in explaining perceptual experience.**, **A case in which optical illusions persist despite awareness of their deceptive nature suggests that perception is influenced by cognitive processes, indicating implicit mental representational activity.**
