# Pragmatic Epistemology: Knowledge in Service to Practicality

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that justification is practically adequate**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and justification works for practical purposes**
