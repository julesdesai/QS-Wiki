## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is not based on any false lemmas**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that all relevant evidence is considered for the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is justified, is not based on any false lemmas, and all relevant evidence is considered**
