# Knowledge as Apt Belief

**Parent:** [What if a person accidentally forms a true belief without exercising intellectual virtues?](what-if-a-person-accidentally-forms-a-true-belief-without-exercising-intellectual-virtues-antithesis-5aa0e914-fdb9-451f-953a-97f299cfebe0.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief manifests the exercise of an intellectual virtue**, **A belief manifests an intellectual virtue if the virtue is the primary factor in why the belief is both true and believed**
