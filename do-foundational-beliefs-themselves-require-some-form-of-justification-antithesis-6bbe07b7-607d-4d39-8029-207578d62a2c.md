# Do foundational beliefs themselves require some form of justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 6bbe07b7-607d-4d39-8029-207578d62a2c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundationalism Summary](foundationalism-summary-synthesis-254febae-17e3-437c-93f6-f73426d75660.md)

## Content

**Imagine an individual who holds certain foundational beliefs mainly because of societal indoctrination. Intuitively, it seems questionable whether these should count as adequately justified in a manner that confers knowledge.**, **This perspective urges reconsideration of whether foundational beliefs are genuinely exempt from needing justification.**
