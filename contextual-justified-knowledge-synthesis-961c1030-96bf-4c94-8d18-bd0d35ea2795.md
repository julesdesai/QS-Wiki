# Contextual Justified Knowledge

**Parent:** [Are scenarios where one doesn't believe but knows implausible?](are-scenarios-where-one-doesnt-believe-but-knows-implausible-antithesis-03027e82-de27-483a-88d4-6e316e988a50.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, justification may contextually suffice without belief if evidence overwhelmingly suggests truth**, **Knowledge arises in contexts where justification strongly supports and suggests belief independent of actual belief**
