## Content

**There is a plausible principle that epistemological principles should be universally applicable.**, **Consider an epistemological theory that requires adjustments based on specific knower contexts. Intuitively, such variability suggests limited universality.**, **Context dependence challenges the universal applicability of epistemological principles.**
