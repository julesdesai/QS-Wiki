# Can an unrecognized deficit in stakes awareness negate knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis

**Parent:** [Subject-Sensitive Invariantism Summary](subject-sensitive-invariantism-summary-synthesis-1a7f6ba4-b667-492e-9754-ad3a76a8b666.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person believes a proposition with true belief and justifiable evidence but overlooks potential high stakes they fail to recognize. Intuitively, their ignorance of these stakes should not impede their claim to knowledge if the standard is truly invariant.**, **It is implausible that a deficit in awareness of potential stakes can negate a knowledge claim if the standards for knowing remain constant across contexts.**
