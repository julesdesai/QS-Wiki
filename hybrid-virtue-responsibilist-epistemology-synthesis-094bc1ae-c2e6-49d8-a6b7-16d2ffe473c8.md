## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief results from both intellectual virtue and responsible epistemic practices**, **An intellectual virtue is a character trait that tends to form true beliefs**, **Responsible epistemic practices involve critical reflection and evidence-checking**
