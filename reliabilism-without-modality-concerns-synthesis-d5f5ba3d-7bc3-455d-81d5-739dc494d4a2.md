## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief is reliably produced**, **A belief is reliably produced if it is formed by a process that generally yields true beliefs**
