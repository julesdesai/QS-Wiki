## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief could not have easily been false**, **The belief could not have easily been false if, in nearby possible worlds, the belief continues to be true**
