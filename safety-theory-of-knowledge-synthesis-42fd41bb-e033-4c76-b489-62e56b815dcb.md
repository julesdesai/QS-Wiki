## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in nearby possible worlds where the person believes the proposition, it is not false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, it is true, and in nearby possible worlds where they believe it, it is not false**
