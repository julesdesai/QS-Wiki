# Can justification be derived solely from experiences?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 28c8c363-ac8b-4f64-8059-5663a4a7aece
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Experiential Foundationalism](experiential-foundationalism-synthesis-fd2d94ab-5595-4b29-a3f8-2962ee0c4e3c.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that justification can be influenced by factors other than direct experiences. This principle together with the notion that justification is solely derived from foundational experiences entails that other sources of justification are ignored.**
