## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is produced by a reliable process external to the person’s belief system without including self-deceptive mechanisms**, **Belief formation processes are relevant but do not require direct awareness by the individual**, **Reliable processes must exclude any self-deceptive elements**
