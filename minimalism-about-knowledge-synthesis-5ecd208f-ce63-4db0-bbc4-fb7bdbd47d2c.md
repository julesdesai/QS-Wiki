# Minimalism about Knowledge

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can context deliver inconsistent knowledge over time?](can-context-deliver-inconsistent-knowledge-over-time-antithesis-b03738c8-2be4-43ee-9d91-8c91def177e8.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that it is not reasonable doubt that the proposition is false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and it is not reasonable to doubt the proposition is false**
