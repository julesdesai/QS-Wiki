## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief be justified regardless of context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief in the proposition is true and justified**
