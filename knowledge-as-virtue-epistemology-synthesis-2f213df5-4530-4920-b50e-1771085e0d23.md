## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition through an intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is true, they believe the proposition, and their belief arises from an intellectual virtue**
