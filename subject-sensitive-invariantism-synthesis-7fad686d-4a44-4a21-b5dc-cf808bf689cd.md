## Content

**Knowledge does not depend on the context of the knowledge ascription but may depend on the subject's practical interests**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief is sensitive to the truth of the proposition**, **The subject's practical interests may influence whether they know a proposition**
