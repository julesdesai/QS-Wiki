# What if someone has justified true belief without relying on any external indicators?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3751fe33-dded-4e07-a498-96b26c8eb8ef
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Epistemic Externalism about Justification](epistemic-externalism-about-justification-synthesis-ef2753e9-b07e-44a0-bb88-245ae31dae48.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person intuitively knows their mother's voice among a crowd due to a strong personal connection, without relying on external indicators or evidence. Intuitively, they posses knowledge even though external justification is absent.**, **It is implausible that all justified true beliefs require external indicators, as some knowledge might be inherently internal or intuitive.**
