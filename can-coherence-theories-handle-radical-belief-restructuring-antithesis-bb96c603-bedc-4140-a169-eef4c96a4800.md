# Can coherence theories handle radical belief restructuring?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** bb96c603-bedc-4140-a169-eef4c96a4800
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Temporal Coherentism](temporal-coherentism-synthesis-f4dfebfd-2f02-4b67-a5dd-1a5ab136482a.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person radically restructures their beliefs periodically, each time forming a coherent system that is inconsistent with the previous ones. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that coherence over time does not necessarily establish knowledge if beliefs radically change.**
