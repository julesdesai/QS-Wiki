# Knowledge as Virtue Epistemology

**Parent:** [Does the sufficiency condition for knowledge encompass all possibilities?](does-the-sufficiency-condition-for-knowledge-encompass-all-possibilities-antithesis-635c0c27-b643-4e69-aa0f-d38417234e7c.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from the intellectual virtues of the believer**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief arises from intellectual virtue**
