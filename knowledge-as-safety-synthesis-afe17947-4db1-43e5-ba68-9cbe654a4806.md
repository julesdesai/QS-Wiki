## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief could not easily have been false under similar circumstances**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief could not easily have been false under similar circumstances**
