# Knowledge as Coherent Understanding

**Parent:** [Are there cases where understanding does not require practical consequences for knowledge?](are-there-cases-where-understanding-does-not-require-practical-consequences-for-knowledge-antithesis-1b207d30-c2d8-44b6-a60f-ebe232eb32ef.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they understand the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the understanding coheres with the rest of their beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they understand the proposition, the proposition is true, and it coheres with the rest of their beliefs**
