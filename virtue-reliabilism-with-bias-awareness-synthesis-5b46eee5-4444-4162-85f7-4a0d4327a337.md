## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is reliably produced through intellectual virtues and awareness of potential biases**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is a result of intellectual virtues, and the person is aware of potential biases**
