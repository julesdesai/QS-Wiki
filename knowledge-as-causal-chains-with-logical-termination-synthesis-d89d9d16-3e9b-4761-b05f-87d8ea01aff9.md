# Knowledge as Causal Chains with Logical Termination

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** d89d9d16-3e9b-4761-b05f-87d8ea01aff9
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [How do we handle circular causal chains?](how-do-we-handle-circular-causal-chains-antithesis-a4494b34-3974-4654-ad89-378fecde3c3a.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is causally connected to the fact that makes the proposition true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is causally connected to the fact that makes the proposition true**, **Causal chains terminate in logical truths which prevent circular chains**
