# Is it possible for a useful belief to fail as knowledge if threatened by future evidence?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 809035cb-3c5d-4571-a586-f5e80087885c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatism with Coherentism](pragmatism-with-coherentism-synthesis-7996efa8-d25e-4d54-acc0-6469819e4077.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a belief is currently useful but susceptible to being disproven by new evidence. Intuitively, we would judge that its current usefulness does not ensure it remains an instance of knowledge.**, **A plausible principle is that knowledge should be resistant to future evidence that can reveal a belief as false. This principle together with the potential fallibility of useful beliefs entails a deficiency in defining knowledge.**
