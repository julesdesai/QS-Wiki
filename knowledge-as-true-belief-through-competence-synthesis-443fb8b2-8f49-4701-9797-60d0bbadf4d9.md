# Knowledge as True Belief through Competence

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Can knowledge exist without belief?](can-knowledge-exist-without-belief-antithesis-3cc4dbf0-111a-4a37-9961-ae29f3fef0b8.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief in the proposition arises from competence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief in the proposition arises from competence and the proposition is true**
