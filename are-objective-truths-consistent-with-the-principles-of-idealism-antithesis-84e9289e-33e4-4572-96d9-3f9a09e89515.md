# Are objective truths consistent with the principles of idealism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 84e9289e-33e4-4572-96d9-3f9a09e89515
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Idealism](idealism-synthesis-6a8ea4b4-8cf6-44ff-98fb-cf08805f781d.md)

## Content

**Objective truths, such as historical facts, remain true regardless of current perception or mental constructs. Intuitively, this independence suggests a reality not entirely mental.**, **The enduring nature of objective truths across different mental constructs challenges the idea that knowledge is constituted solely by mental phenomena.**
