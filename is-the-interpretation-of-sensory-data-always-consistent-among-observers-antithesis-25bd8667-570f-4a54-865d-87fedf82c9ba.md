# Is the interpretation of sensory data always consistent among observers?

**Node ID:** 25bd8667-570f-4a54-865d-87fedf82c9ba

**Parent:** [Intersubjective Phenomenalism](intersubjective-phenomenalism-synthesis-0a89d160-2bee-479f-b8d3-046de6da5ef6.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which multiple observers perceive an ambiguous image differently due to their prior experiences. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that their agreement is not guaranteed.**, **A case in which people perceive colors differently due to varying conditions yet reach intersubjective agreement is a case in which agreement could be mistaken or superficial.**
