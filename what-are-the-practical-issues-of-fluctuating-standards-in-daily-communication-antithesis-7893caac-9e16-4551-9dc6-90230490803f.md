# What are the practical issues of fluctuating standards in daily communication?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7893caac-9e16-4551-9dc6-90230490803f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualist Theory of Knowledge](contextualist-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-c8c66eb7-99be-427a-b717-b1ecb74e1290.md)

## Content

**In day-to-day communication, people require consistent standards to understand and evaluate knowledge claims.**, **If the standards for what qualifies as knowledge change from one conversational context to another, it leads to confusion and misunderstandings among interlocutors.**, **A theory that permits significant variation in standards might struggle to provide stable criteria for interpreting and resolving disputes about knowledge in ordinary conversations.**
