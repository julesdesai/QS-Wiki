# Knowledge as Coherence with Externalist Justification

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification involves a coherent set of beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the coherent set of beliefs is externally verified**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there exists a coherent set of beliefs externally verified**
