# Can a belief be justified by insufficient evidence?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 273a8560-8c0b-40d7-88ae-56f380f71c00
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Evidentialism](evidentialism-synthesis-bfc3ee3a-740c-4ee5-bb7b-12c5efc1c03c.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person believes their friend will arrive because they read about it in a non-credible tabloid. Intuitively, we would judge that this belief is not justified due to the lack of credible evidence.**, **There is a plausible principle that credible evidence is required for justified belief. This principle, together with the case where an unreliable source is used, suggests that insufficient or non-credible evidence cannot justify a belief.**
