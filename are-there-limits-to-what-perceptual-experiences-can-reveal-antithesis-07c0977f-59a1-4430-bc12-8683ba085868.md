# Are there limits to what perceptual experiences can reveal?

**Node ID:** 07c0977f-59a1-4430-bc12-8683ba085868
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Intentionalism about Perception](intentionalism-about-perception-synthesis-61a99e6e-ccdd-4f9c-a128-c382d15947b5.md)

## Content

**There are properties of objects, such as subatomic structures, that cannot be directly perceived.**, **If perception aims at presenting all properties of objects, there should be no limit to what is perceived.**, **Some properties of objects must be inferred rather than directly perceived.**
