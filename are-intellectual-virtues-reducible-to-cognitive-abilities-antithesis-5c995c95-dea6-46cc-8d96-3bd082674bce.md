# Are intellectual virtues reducible to cognitive abilities?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5c995c95-dea6-46cc-8d96-3bd082674bce
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-0303bf31-8e93-428e-bc89-30c6b91ae71e.md)

## Content

**A plausible principle is that not all intellectual virtues, such as persevering inquiry, are strictly cognitive abilities.**, **Viewing intellectual virtues as cognitive skills alone could dismiss their broader epistemic significance in gaining knowledge.**
