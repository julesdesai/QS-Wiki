# Does the disjunction between truth and belief undermine knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 556fcd3f-1551-4402-b172-1cdf1de10a77
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Revised Virtue Epistemology](revised-virtue-epistemology-synthesis-e97d218b-557b-43e8-afcd-587c60b67e41.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that a mere disjunction between the truth of a proposition and belief in it satisfies the conditions for knowledge.**, **Knowledge requires an integration where truth and belief simultaneously and reliably arise from intellectual virtue.**
