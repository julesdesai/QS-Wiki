## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the cognitive faculties producing the belief function properly in an appropriate environment**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, that the proposition is true, and that the belief is produced by properly functioning cognitive faculties in an appropriate environment**
