# Infinitist Justification

**Parent:** [Does foundationalism account for knowledge from testimony?](does-foundationalism-account-for-knowledge-from-testimony-antithesis-1a86775d-9bc9-4a91-aac4-9b4d5024bab9.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their justification consists of an infinite chain of reasons where each reason is supported by another reason**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their justification consists of an infinite chain of reasons**
