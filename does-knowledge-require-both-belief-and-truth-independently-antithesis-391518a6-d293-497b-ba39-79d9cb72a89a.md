# Does knowledge require both belief and truth independently?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 391518a6-d293-497b-ba39-79d9cb72a89a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalist Coherentism](externalist-coherentism-synthesis-f4ef4378-ebfc-4a41-8845-189c2c8e0f20.md)

## Content

**Having a true belief is not sufficient for knowledge if belief is not formed reliably.**, **An individual can have a justified belief that aligns with the truth without being aware of the truth of the proposition.**, **Belief and truth need not independently lead to knowledge if the relationship between them is contingent.**
