# Knowledge as Causal Chains with External Termination

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [How do we handle circular causal chains?](how-do-we-handle-circular-causal-chains-antithesis-a4494b34-3974-4654-ad89-378fecde3c3a.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is causally connected to the fact that makes the proposition true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is causally connected to the fact that makes the proposition true**, **Causal chains terminate in external reality that confirms the truth of propositions, avoiding circularity**
