# Is practical success always a fair measure for the truth of a belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 97a33008-3c6b-46ac-a037-92d11c65513e

**Parent:** [Pragmatism](pragmatism-synthesis-c3735471-f546-42ec-8e21-f2451431b4e8.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where practical success is achieved through manipulation or deceit based on a belief. Intuitively, we question whether such a belief is genuinely true**, **A belief leading to successful deceit or manipulation challenges the notion that truth can always be determined by experiential outcomes**
