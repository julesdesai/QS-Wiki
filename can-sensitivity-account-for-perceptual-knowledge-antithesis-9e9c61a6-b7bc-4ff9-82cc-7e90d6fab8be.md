# Can sensitivity account for perceptual knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9e9c61a6-b7bc-4ff9-82cc-7e90d6fab8be
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Sensitivity Theory](sensitivity-theory-synthesis-c636dae2-8d6f-478b-827f-65895cd50362.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person sees a red apple in good lighting and believes it is red, and it is in fact red.**, **In a nearby possible world where the lighting is bad, the apple might appear green, leading the person to believe it is not red.**, **The sensitivity condition suggests the belief is not knowledge since it wouldn't track the truth if conditions were slightly altered, although we intuitively deem the belief to be knowledge.**
