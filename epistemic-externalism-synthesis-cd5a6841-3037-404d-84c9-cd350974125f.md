# Epistemic Externalism

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it need not be necessary for the person to have introspective access to the reasons for belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is formed by a process connected to the truth of the proposition**
