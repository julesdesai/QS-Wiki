## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified by evidence, whether direct or inferential**, **The role of evidence is interpreted within the framework of contextual factors such as environment and background information**
