# Are intellectual virtues exclusively character traits?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1521e9b0-a3d1-4780-bb50-8a0ecfbd0462
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-f60d76d9-bfb9-483c-b262-fa52975172be.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that suggests some cognitive abilities, like quick logical reasoning, contribute to knowledge acquisition without necessarily constituting character traits.**, **If cognitive abilities like these can lead to knowledge without being character traits, then the definition of intellectual virtues may be overly narrow if confined strictly to character traits.**
