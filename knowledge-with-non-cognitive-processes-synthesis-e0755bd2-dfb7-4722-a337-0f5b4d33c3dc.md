## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief has practical consequences or usefulness**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their non-cognitive processes or faculties function properly**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief has practical consequences or usefulness, and their non-cognitive processes or faculties are functioning properly**
