## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary and sufficient that there is an infinite chain of reasons supporting the belief**, **The chain of reasons does not terminate and continually provides justification**
