# Do intellectual virtues necessarily guarantee truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b1c40f19-82fc-4f90-8ecb-e39252ef3dc4
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue epistemology focusing on intellectual virtues](virtue-epistemology-focusing-on-intellectual-virtues-synthesis-d559d8c1-587e-4ccf-9e44-8233679e40bf.md)

## Content

**Suppose someone displays intellectual virtues like open-mindedness and intellectual courage yet arrives at a false belief. Intuitively, they do not possess knowledge.**, **Exercising intellectual virtues does not necessarily entail reaching the truth, hence challenging the view's necessity for virtue.**
