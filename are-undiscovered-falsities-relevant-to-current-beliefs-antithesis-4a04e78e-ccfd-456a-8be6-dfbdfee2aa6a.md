# Are undiscovered falsities relevant to current beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 4a04e78e-ccfd-456a-8be6-dfbdfee2aa6a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [No defeaters theory of knowledge](no-defeaters-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-293021ef-516a-4641-9c48-087d1223e339.md)

## Content

**Consider a case in which a person's belief in a true proposition acknowledges only presently available evidence, yet further facts exist that could invalidate this belief if known. This raises an issue regarding whether lack of falsifying evidence supports the concept of knowledge.**, **Intuitively, knowledge seems to require that all relevant evidence can withstand scrutiny against newly uncovered information.**
