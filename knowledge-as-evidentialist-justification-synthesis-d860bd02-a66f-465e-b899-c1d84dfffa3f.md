## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary they have evidence for the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have sufficient evidence for the belief and meet the other conditions**
