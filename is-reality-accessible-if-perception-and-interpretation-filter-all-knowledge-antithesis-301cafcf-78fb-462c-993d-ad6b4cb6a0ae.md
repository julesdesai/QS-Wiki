# Is reality accessible if perception and interpretation filter all knowledge?

## Content

**A plausible intuition suggests that if all knowledge is mediated through perception and interpretation, reality itself may remain inaccessible, filtered and altered by these processes**, **Consider a scenario where individuals have entirely different perceptions of a social situation, suggesting that interpretation layers may obscure any direct knowledge of reality**, **It is implausible to believe that interpretations, influenced by perceptual limitations and cognitive biases, can provide an unmediated access to reality**
