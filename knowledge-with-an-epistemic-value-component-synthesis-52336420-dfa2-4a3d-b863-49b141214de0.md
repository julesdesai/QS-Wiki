## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A belief qualifies as knowledge if it is produced by a reliable cognitive process that also enhances an epistemically valuable goal**, **An epistemically valuable goal involves acquiring cognitive achievements beyond the mere truth, such as understanding or insight**
