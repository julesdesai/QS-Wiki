# Can Bayesian updates lead to incorrect conclusions?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 60668287-fab6-4623-901a-2f0e6cab01d5
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Bayesian Epistemology](bayesian-epistemology-synthesis-4576807d-29a3-407d-a965-1c884989f7fe.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where different, conflicting pieces of evidence support a high probability for multiple incompatible beliefs. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that Bayesian updates could lead to incorrect or unjustified beliefs.**, **It is implausible that probabilistic reasoning can always ensure rational belief adjustment if the evidence can support conflicting outcomes.**
