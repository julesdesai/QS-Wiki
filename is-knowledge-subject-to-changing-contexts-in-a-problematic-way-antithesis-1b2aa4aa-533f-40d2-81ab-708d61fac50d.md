# Is knowledge subject to changing contexts in a problematic way?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1b2aa4aa-533f-40d2-81ab-708d61fac50d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Encroachment](pragmatic-encroachment-synthesis-829538ce-ebfd-41f1-81ea-e2a3bdbbc235.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where a person holds a belief that is true in one context due to low stakes, but later in a different context with higher stakes, the same true belief is no longer considered knowledge. Intuitively, it is troubling if the truth status of knowledge can shift based merely on contextual factors unrelated to the original evidence or truth condition.**
