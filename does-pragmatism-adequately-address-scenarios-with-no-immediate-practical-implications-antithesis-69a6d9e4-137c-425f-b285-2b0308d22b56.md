# Does pragmatism adequately address scenarios with no immediate practical implications?

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person gains knowledge about distant galaxies with no immediate practical implication here on Earth; intuitively, such knowledge is valuable despite a lack of direct practical outcomes.**, **A plausible principle is that assessing knowledge solely through practical implications undermines the potential value of theoretical or abstract knowledge.**
