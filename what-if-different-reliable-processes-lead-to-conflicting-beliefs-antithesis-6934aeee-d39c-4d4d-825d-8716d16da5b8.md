# What if different reliable processes lead to conflicting beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 6934aeee-d39c-4d4d-825d-8716d16da5b8
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalism with Reliabilism](externalism-with-reliabilism-synthesis-979d7449-6923-4b84-9ee7-21896810f66d.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case where two reliable cognitive processes produce conflicting beliefs. Intuitively, we either have to say that both beliefs are justified or that one is not**, **It is implausible that both conflicting beliefs can be justified simultaneously if knowledge is to be coherent and non-contradictory**
