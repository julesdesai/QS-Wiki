**Parent:** [Can someone possess knowledge without employing intellectual virtue?](can-someone-possess-knowledge-without-employing-intellectual-virtue-antithesis-e0dbf8c3-4ee4-4ad1-a4bc-68743ae711cc.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their true belief arises from a method successful in producing true beliefs**, **A method successful in producing true beliefs is evaluated based on its track record of yielding true outcomes rather than its employment of intellectual virtues**
