# Can all aspects of reality be accessed or known without perception?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 6e63afdb-4567-4cb5-bcd1-4c52f42889b4
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Dual Aspect Theory](dual-aspect-theory-synthesis-e0a4859a-34ba-4bc9-9368-89fce3ac2e21.md)

## Content

**Perception is one of the primary means by which humans engage with and learn about reality.**, **There are theoretical objects like numbers and abstract entities that do not require perceptual experiences to be known or understood.**, **It is implausible to claim that every aspect of reality must be perceived to be considered a part of reality.**
