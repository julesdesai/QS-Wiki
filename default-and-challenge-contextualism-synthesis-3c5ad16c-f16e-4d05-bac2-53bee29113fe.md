## Content

**A person knows a proposition by default unless challenged**, **For a person to know a proposition, their belief must withstand reasonable challenges**, **A person knows a proposition if they believe it, and the belief withstands reasonable challenges and is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief withstands reasonable challenges**
