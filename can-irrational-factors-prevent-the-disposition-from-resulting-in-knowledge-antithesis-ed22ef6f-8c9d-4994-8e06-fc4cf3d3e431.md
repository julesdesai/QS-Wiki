# Can irrational factors prevent the disposition from resulting in knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ed22ef6f-8c9d-4994-8e06-fc4cf3d3e431
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Dispositional Theory of Knowledge](dispositional-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-1fd28b95-7886-486d-be0c-ab1f241be193.md)

## Content

**Consider a situation where a person has a disposition to believe correctly about astrology due to strong cultural influences rather than factual reasoning.**, **It is implausible that this constitutes knowledge even if the disposition sometimes results in true belief.**
