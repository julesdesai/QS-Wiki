# Is a defeater's availability sufficient to challenge knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 65f41fa1-4f1c-4197-b25f-45d272b9fcd3
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [No Defeaters Theory](no-defeaters-theory-synthesis-e6eeb208-311d-4d09-a784-60d3e95f478f.md)

## Content

**A defeater is information that would undermine justification if available.**, **A piece of information being theoretically available does not guarantee its impact on an individual's justification.**, **Assuming availability of a defeater implies undermining may neglect practical contexts in which individuals operate.**
