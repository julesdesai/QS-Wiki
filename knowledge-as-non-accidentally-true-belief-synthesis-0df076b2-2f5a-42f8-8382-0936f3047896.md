# Knowledge as Non-Accidentally True Belief

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can the safety condition account for knowledge with no false lemmas?](can-the-safety-condition-account-for-knowledge-with-no-false-lemmas-antithesis-a5c0d1b8-ee42-4ea6-b99d-ba9f29d224f4.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is not accidentally true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is non-accidentally true**
