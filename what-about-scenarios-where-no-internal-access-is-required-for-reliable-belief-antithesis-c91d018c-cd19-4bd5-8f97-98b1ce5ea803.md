# What about scenarios where no internal access is required for reliable belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c91d018c-cd19-4bd5-8f97-98b1ce5ea803
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Internalist Justification Theory](internalist-justification-theory-synthesis-60885d02-ba8c-49f7-a8ac-6cdf9db1e5a8.md)

## Content

**Consider a person who forms a belief about the weather based on a reliable weather app without understanding the app’s methodology.**, **The app always provides accurate weather forecasts, thereby justifying the belief externally.**, **The user trusts the app blindly without having internal access to the reasons or data for the forecast accuracy.**
