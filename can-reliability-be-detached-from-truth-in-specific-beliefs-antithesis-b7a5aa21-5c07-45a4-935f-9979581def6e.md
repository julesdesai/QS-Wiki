# Can reliability be detached from truth in specific beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b7a5aa21-5c07-45a4-935f-9979581def6e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism](reliabilism-synthesis-4db07ebc-4cb3-402b-b335-2b01121c3e1a.md)

## Content

**Imagine a situation where a belief formed by a highly reliable process turns out false due to unusual circumstances. Intuitively, we would question whether reliability alone can uphold the notion of knowledge here**, **There is a plausible principle suggesting that without truth, regardless of reliability, knowledge cannot be claimed**
