# What about direct realism's challenge to mediation by intentional content?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 33c26aa4-360f-4032-9c53-88ddd1e91627
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Intentionalism Summary](intentionalism-summary-synthesis-3678fe22-b4a0-4fa7-aad8-5dfa98b1a709.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that perception could be direct contact with reality without any representational content. This principle together with the requirement for intentional content in perception challenges the mediation role of intentional content.**, **Consider a scenario where a person perceives a tree directly without any representational content. Intuitively, this suggests that intentional content might not be necessary for perception.**
