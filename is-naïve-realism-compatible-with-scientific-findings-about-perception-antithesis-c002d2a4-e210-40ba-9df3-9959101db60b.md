# Is naïve realism compatible with scientific findings about perception?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c002d2a4-e210-40ba-9df3-9959101db60b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Naïve Realism Summary](naïve-realism-summary-synthesis-ad05d8f4-c9d2-4024-8001-bf520360cf02.md)

## Content

**Scientific findings suggest that perception involves neural processes and brain activities that mediate between external objects and perceptual experience.**, **Direct awareness entails no mediation or processing between perceiver and perceived object.**, **Scientific evidence for mediation challenges the idea that perception is a direct awareness of external objects.**
