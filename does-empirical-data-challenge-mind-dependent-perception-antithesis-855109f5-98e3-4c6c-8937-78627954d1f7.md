# Does empirical data challenge mind-dependent perception?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 855109f5-98e3-4c6c-8937-78627954d1f7
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Empirical Idealism](empirical-idealism-synthesis-57e1bbec-13ac-4c5c-a0b5-84a9cd66f614.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where multiple independent observers record the same data about a natural event without interaction or sharing perceptions.**, **In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that this data corresponds to an objective reality independent of the mind.**, **It is implausible that the shared empirical data results solely from independent mental constructions.**
