# Could practical limitations refute infinite conceptual regress?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 338fe253-7f82-4cbf-bae8-d5add9cf65b3
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism with Conceptual Infinite Regress](infinitism-with-conceptual-infinite-regress-synthesis-14e25e58-6ba7-452c-ac18-aba278058c50.md)

## Content

**Humans inherently face practical cognitive and temporal limitations.**, **Generating or comprehending an infinite series of justifications may be beyond these limitations.**, **This seems to contrast with the claim that justification is an infinite conceptual regress.**
