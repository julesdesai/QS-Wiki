# Is knowledge too dynamic under contextualism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** dd9e05e8-7427-43b4-a74d-d4e7550a8c1d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [The Contextualist View of Knowledge](the-contextualist-view-of-knowledge-synthesis-744b190b-128c-4187-8edf-0a707cd161d1.md)

## Content

**The contextualist view allows justification standards to vary, causing knowledge to change across contexts.**, **Such dynamism suggests that people's knowledge is not fixed but fluctuates, causing potential confusion.**, **The concept of stable, reliable knowledge could be threatened by this variability.**
