## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, their belief must result from intellectual virtue**, **An intellectual virtue is a cognitive ability or competence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have a true belief resulting from intellectual virtue, and their belief would not be held if the proposition were false**
