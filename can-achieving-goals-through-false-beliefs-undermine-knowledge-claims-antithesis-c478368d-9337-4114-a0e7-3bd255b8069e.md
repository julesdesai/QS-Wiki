# Can achieving goals through false beliefs undermine knowledge claims?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c478368d-9337-4114-a0e7-3bd255b8069e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Coherentism](pragmatic-coherentism-synthesis-37d6c6a9-68e2-4800-8f97-9338457a8af1.md)

## Content

**Knowledge involves true beliefs coherent with an individual's objectives. However, achieving goals through false beliefs challenges the validity of such knowledge claims, as coherence with objectives does not guarantee the truth.**, **Consider a case in which a person uses a false belief to successfully achieve a goal. Intuitively, we would judge that while the practical success aligns with their objectives, the belief's falsity suggests a lack of genuine knowledge.**
