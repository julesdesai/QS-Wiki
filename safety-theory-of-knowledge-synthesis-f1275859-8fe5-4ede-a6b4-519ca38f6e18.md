# Safety Theory of Knowledge

**Parent:** [Are all truths knowable if sensitivity is required?](are-all-truths-knowable-if-sensitivity-is-required-antithesis-00d656b9-ca9b-447b-b260-2d3728365a90.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in nearby possible worlds where the person believes the proposition, the proposition is not false**
