## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief could not have easily been false in similar situations**, **A belief is safe if in most nearby possible worlds where the belief is held under similar circumstances, it is true**
