# Is a reliable process compatible with radical skepticism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 06063db1-e4f6-4b29-b7e8-f9d2e3f3ce76
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilist Theory of Knowledge](reliabilist-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-e6fc2437-0f22-487d-91cb-ba903a4e1b1f.md)

## Content

**A skeptic doubts the external world but relies on perception which is generally a reliable process.**, **Reliabilism suggests perception should yield knowledge if it produces true beliefs consistently.**, **Radical skepticism challenges whether perceptual processes can ever be justified as reliable knowledge sources.**
