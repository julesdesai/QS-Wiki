## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is reliably formed**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in nearby possible worlds where the person uses the same method to form the belief, the belief is true**
