# Knowledge as Dependable Reliability

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [What if intellectual virtue leads to redundancy?](what-if-intellectual-virtue-leads-to-redundancy-antithesis-ef4aa31c-15ab-40c7-a5ec-0b616480c24f.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief formation depends on reliably dependable cognitive mechanisms**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief formation depends on reliably dependable cognitive mechanisms**
