# No False Grounds Theory

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief does not rest on any false grounds**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is true and not based on any false premises**
