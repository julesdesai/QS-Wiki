## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief could not easily have been false**, **A belief is safe if in most nearby possible worlds where the person holds the belief, the proposition is true**
