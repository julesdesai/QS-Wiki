# Does hidden justification undermine knowledge claims?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b76bfe18-d072-463f-8096-c211c161e99d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Disjunctivist Theory of Knowledge](disjunctivist-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-eb3c40bf-59e8-47a3-98db-de4d9568dbcc.md)

## Content

**Suppose a scientist discovers a vaccine's effectiveness through sound methods that others cannot verify due to hidden lab notes. Intuitively, we might question the wide acceptance of knowledge if justification remains hidden, suggesting that accessible justification supports the dissemination of knowledge claims.**, **There is a plausible principle that widespread knowledge claims require justification accessible to some extent, to ensure reliability and verifiability.**
