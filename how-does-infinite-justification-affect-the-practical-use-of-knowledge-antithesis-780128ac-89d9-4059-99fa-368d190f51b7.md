# How does infinite justification affect the practical use of knowledge?

## Content

**Consider practical applications of knowledge requiring contemplation of infinite justification complexities. Intuitively, knowledge loses practical applicability if bound by infinitum.**, **There is a plausible principle that for knowledge to be useful, justification should be efficient. Infinite justification isn't practically efficient.**
