# Holistic Justification for Knowledge

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is justified by a holistic examination of evidence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is true, they believe the proposition, and their justification arises from a holistic examination of evidence**
