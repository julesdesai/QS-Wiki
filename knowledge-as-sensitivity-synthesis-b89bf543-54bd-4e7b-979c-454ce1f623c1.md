# Knowledge as Sensitivity

**Parent:** [Does a reliable process necessarily coincide with truth?](does-a-reliable-process-necessarily-coincide-with-truth-antithesis-43d1e6bf-4693-4c43-84ff-f9ef8c388fc3.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief would not be held if the proposition were false**
