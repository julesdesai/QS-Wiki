# How does externalism handle the issue of changing justification conditions?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d6b46176-e5c4-48c1-b1c9-b4fc7b864d86
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalism](externalism-synthesis-9fb6627e-b63e-4d81-a81a-128eb1da4172.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person's belief is justified by certain external conditions that change without their knowledge, leading to the same belief under different conditions. Intuitively, we would judge that awareness of changing justifications impacts the evaluation of knowledge status.**, **In situations where justification conditions change independently of the believer's recognition, an issue arises as to whether stable knowledge persists.**
