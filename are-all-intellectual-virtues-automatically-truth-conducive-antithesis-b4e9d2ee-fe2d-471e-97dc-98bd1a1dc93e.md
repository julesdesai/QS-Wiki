# Are all intellectual virtues automatically truth-conducive?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b4e9d2ee-fe2d-471e-97dc-98bd1a1dc93e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Anti-Luck Virtue Epistemology](anti-luck-virtue-epistemology-synthesis-fe5301ab-5101-4f93-ba1f-e195247489de.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that all recognized intellectual virtues are inherently truth-conducive. Some virtues may encourage productive habits without necessarily ensuring truth.**, **Suppose we acknowledge that certain intellectual virtues, such as creativity, do not directly aim at truth. Intuitively, we still regard beliefs arising from these virtues as beneficial, though they may not amount to knowledge.**
