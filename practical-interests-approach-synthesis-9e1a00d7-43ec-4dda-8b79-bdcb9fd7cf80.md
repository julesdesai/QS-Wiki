**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief aligns with appropriate practical interests**, **Appropriate practical interests are those where the cost of being wrong exceeds a certain threshold**
