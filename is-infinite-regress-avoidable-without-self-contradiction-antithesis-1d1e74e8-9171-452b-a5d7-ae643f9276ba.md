# Is infinite regress avoidable without self-contradiction?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1d1e74e8-9171-452b-a5d7-ae643f9276ba
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundationalism in Epistemology](foundationalism-in-epistemology-synthesis-4c27bb26-6321-4022-8e66-a85ea5d939d9.md)

## Content

**Avoiding an infinite regress in justification requires terminating at basic beliefs.**, **If basic beliefs are self-justified, they must not require external justification.**, **The notion of self-justification is problematic if it leads to a self-referential loop, which is a form of contradiction.**
