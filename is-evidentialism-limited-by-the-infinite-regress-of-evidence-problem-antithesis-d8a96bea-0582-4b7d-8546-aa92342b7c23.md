# Is Evidentialism limited by the infinite regress of evidence problem?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d8a96bea-0582-4b7d-8546-aa92342b7c23
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Evidentialism Summary](evidentialism-summary-synthesis-a76d832e-447a-4168-b9be-6c867a1a7af1.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that all beliefs require evidence, as this leads to an infinite regress where each piece of evidence itself needs further evidence. This logical implication challenges the viability of evidentialism by suggesting that ultimate justification cannot depend solely on evidence.**
