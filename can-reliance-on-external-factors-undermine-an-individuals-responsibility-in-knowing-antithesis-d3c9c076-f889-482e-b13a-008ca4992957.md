# Can reliance on external factors undermine an individual's responsibility in knowing?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d3c9c076-f889-482e-b13a-008ca4992957
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalism with Reliance on External Factors](externalism-with-reliance-on-external-factors-synthesis-94d45e40-e833-458e-8cba-8623e44201d6.md)

## Content

**Consider a case in which a person acquires a true belief through reliable external processes without any effort or understanding of these processes. Intuitively, we would question their epistemic responsibility for the belief.**, **A plausible principle suggests that knowing includes some level of personal accountability, which reliance on external factors weakens if not erases.**
