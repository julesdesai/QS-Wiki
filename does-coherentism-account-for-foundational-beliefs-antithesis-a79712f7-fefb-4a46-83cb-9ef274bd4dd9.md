# Does coherentism account for foundational beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a79712f7-fefb-4a46-83cb-9ef274bd4dd9
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentist Knowledge Summary](coherentist-knowledge-summary-synthesis-1fa3c944-9677-491a-8edd-ea154ce998c7.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which foundational beliefs are unjustified but support other beliefs in a coherent system. Intuitively, the system’s justification seems unstable without independently justified foundations.**, **A plausible principle is that foundational beliefs require independent justification for overall coherence to offer robust grounds. Without it, coherence might inadequately provide justification.**
