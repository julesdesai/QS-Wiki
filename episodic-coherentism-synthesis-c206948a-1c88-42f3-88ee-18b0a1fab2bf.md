# Episodic Coherentism

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [What if coherence changes over time?](what-if-coherence-changes-over-time-antithesis-f858018a-c6c3-4de5-a24c-121afa6f4ac2.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres internally and externally during distinct, coherent episodes**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief coheres internally and externally during distinct, coherent episodes**
