# How does the view handle cases of changing contexts?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9e79c5fd-bc23-481e-aedb-a80e12d9229d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualist Knowledge Summary](contextualist-knowledge-summary-synthesis-c38eaf7b-9feb-49d3-8375-2d19895c61fa.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which the epistemic context shifts rapidly. Intuitively, we either have to say that knowledge is gained or lost with each shift. If we say knowledge is gained or lost too easily, it undermines the stability of knowledge claims.**, **A case in which a context changes should not lead to an unstable epistemic landscape. Having rapidly changing knowledge claims challenges this stability.**
