# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with Firsthand Evidence Supplementation

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 50ebca17-7024-4330-bd73-12f5a8fc159a
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can trust replace firsthand evidence?](can-trust-replace-firsthand-evidence-antithesis-b3dceada-0923-43e3-abaf-ac8cdce8b50f.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for their belief**, **Justification includes a component of firsthand evidence supplementing credible expertise**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is true, the person believes it, and the belief is justified by a combination of credible expertise and firsthand evidence**
