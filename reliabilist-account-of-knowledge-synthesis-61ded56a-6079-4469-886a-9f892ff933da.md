## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the method by which the belief is formed is reliable**, **A method is reliable if it produces a high proportion of true beliefs in similar situations**
