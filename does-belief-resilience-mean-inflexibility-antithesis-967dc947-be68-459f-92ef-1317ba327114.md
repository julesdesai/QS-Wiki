# Does belief resilience mean inflexibility?

**Node ID:** 967dc947-be68-459f-92ef-1317ba327114

**Terminal:** No

## Content

**A belief system deemed resilient might resist change, even when new and compelling evidence should alter those beliefs.**, **If resilience leads to inflexibility, this would limit the corrective function of new evidence, undermining the notion of true knowledge.**
