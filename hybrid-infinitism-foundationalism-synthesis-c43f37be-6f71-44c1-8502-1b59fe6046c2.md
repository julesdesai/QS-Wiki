## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification is infinite but rooted in foundational beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification is an infinite series grounded in foundational beliefs**
