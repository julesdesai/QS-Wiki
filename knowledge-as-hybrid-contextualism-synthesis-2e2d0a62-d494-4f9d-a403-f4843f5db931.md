## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified through either direct evidence or an inferential process**, **The justification of a belief involves foundational beliefs validated by contextual factors, such as environment and background information**
