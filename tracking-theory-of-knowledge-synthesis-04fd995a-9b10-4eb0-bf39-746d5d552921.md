## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person’s belief tracks the truth across nearby possible worlds**, **A belief tracks the truth if it tends to be held when the proposition is true and not held when it is false**
