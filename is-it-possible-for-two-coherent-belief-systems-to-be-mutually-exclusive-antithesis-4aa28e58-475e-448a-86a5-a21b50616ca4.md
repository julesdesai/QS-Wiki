# Is it possible for two coherent belief systems to be mutually exclusive?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 4aa28e58-475e-448a-86a5-a21b50616ca4
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Partial Correspondence Coherentism Summary](partial-correspondence-coherentism-summary-synthesis-73282c9a-d730-43f4-a933-7c2ffa7c9bab.md)

## Content

**Imagine two individuals with mutually exclusive yet coherent belief systems regarding the same event.**, **In such a scenario, neither belief system necessarily corresponds to reality because coherence does not resolve their exclusivity.**, **If two coherent belief systems can be mutually exclusive, the insistence on coherence as a component of knowledge may not reliably anchor beliefs in reality.**
