# Does contextualism face issues with universal knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f1b47873-98de-45d1-9dae-19238ffd6df9
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism about Knowledge](contextualism-about-knowledge-synthesis-3f771a9e-26d6-43c4-b604-a31f8bd9947c.md)

## Content

**People often assert knowledge claims that seem to hold universally across different situations.**, **Contextualism implies that such claims could fail to hold in contexts with different justification standards.**, **It is implausible that universal knowledge claims could be invalidated simply by changing contexts.**
