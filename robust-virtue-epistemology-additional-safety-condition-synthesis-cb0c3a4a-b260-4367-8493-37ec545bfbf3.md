# Robust Virtue Epistemology: Additional Safety Condition

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is the result of the person's intellectual virtues**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if they were to believe the proposition in similar circumstances, the belief would not easily be false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief results from the exercise of intellectual virtues, and the belief is safe**
