# Is knowledge really possible under Infinitist Coherentism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ecb486f5-8078-4f34-baae-fe10e5303f91
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitist Coherentism](infinitist-coherentism-synthesis-2a4efbd0-0f1f-4a01-bfa6-1ecf016eb1e0.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a belief is supported by an infinite but non-terminating chain of reasons. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that each belief in the chain lacks final justification.**, **It is implausible that an infinitely regressive chain of reasoning can sufficiently provide knowledge due to the lack of a terminal justification point.**
