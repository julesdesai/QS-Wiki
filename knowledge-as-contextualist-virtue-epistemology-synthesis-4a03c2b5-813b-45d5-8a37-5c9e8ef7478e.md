## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the standards of knowing are met in the given context**, **The standards of knowing are determined by the epistemic virtues relevant to the context**
