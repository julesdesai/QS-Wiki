# Is the process external or internal to the agent forming the belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 59e8aa69-1308-4642-bb48-beea0ff0e48e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilist View](reliabilist-view-synthesis-9b4935ff-e1a0-4d23-aeca-7dfa716ad7fb.md)

## Content

**A belief formed internally by sheer intuition aligns with eventual external validation through reliable means. Intuitively, it seems internal processes might diverge from external reliability standards, questioning knowledge legitimacy.**, **Internal intuitive beliefs subsequently validated externally illustrate the disconnection between personal belief formation and external processes of reliability, casting doubt on the entirety of reliability as a criterion.**
