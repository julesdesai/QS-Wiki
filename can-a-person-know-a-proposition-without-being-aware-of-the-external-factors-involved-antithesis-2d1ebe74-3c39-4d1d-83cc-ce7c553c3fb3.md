# Can a person know a proposition without being aware of the external factors involved?

**Node ID:** 2d1ebe74-3c39-4d1d-83cc-ce7c553c3fb3

## Content

**It is implausible that a person can have knowledge of a proposition if they are not aware of the external factors that constitute their justification.**, **Suppose a person believes a proposition based on a reliable cognitive process unbeknownst to them, intuitively we might question whether they truly "know" the proposition.**
