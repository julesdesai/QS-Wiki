# How do we gain knowledge of moral facts?

**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Conceptual Empiricism](conceptual-empiricism-synthesis-7b58485d-3ea5-4513-ba7e-4d4d19b630bf.md)

## Content

**Moral truths such as "murder is wrong" seem to be known without reliance on perceptual experiences.**, **A person knows the proposition "murder is wrong" in ways that do not pertain directly to concepts derived from perceptual experiences.**, **External moral facts can be known without the application of concepts generated by perceptual experiences.**
