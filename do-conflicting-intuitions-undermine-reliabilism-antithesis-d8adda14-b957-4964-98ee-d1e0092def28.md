# Do conflicting intuitions undermine reliabilism?

**Parent:** [Externalism with Reliabilism](externalism-with-reliabilism-synthesis-c10e5c74-1dbc-479d-ba5e-469176c5d537.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that if intuitions in clear cases conflict with a theoretical view, then the view is threatened. This principle together with the intuition that reliability sometimes fails to indicate knowledge entails that reliabilism is challenged.**
