## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the individual has a cognitive disposition towards the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief could not easily have been false in nearby possible worlds**
