**Parent:** [Is the subjective feeling of certitude sufficient for knowledge?](is-the-subjective-feeling-of-certitude-sufficient-for-knowledge-antithesis-c7f25586-8ab4-4288-b6df-e8f07b84508f.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief be produced by a cognitive process that is generally reliable**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is produced by a cognitive process that is generally reliable**
