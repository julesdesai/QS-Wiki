## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the truth of knowledge claims is relative to a specific framework or individual perspective**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient if within their own perspective, the proposition is true and believed**
