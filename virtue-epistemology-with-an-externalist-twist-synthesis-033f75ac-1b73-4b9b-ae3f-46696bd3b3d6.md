## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed through a cognitive ability or intellectual virtue**, **It is sufficient for knowledge if the belief is formed through a reliable cognitive ability, whether or not the agent is aware of this reliability**
