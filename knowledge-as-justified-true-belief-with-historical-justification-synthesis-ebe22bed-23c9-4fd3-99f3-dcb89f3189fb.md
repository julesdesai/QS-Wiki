# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with Historical Justification

**Parent:** [Can memory confound the causal basis for knowledge?](can-memory-confound-the-causal-basis-for-knowledge-antithesis-e50a710f-215c-48e0-8a65-d543e5b45f58.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is historical justification for the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there is historical justification for the belief**
