# Does coherentism adequately address the certainty objection?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 42f5c6a1-0e2c-4708-b843-a5c4179adc21
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism in Epistemology](coherentism-in-epistemology-synthesis-03124e37-be18-4868-b2cf-e370b5186105.md)

## Content

**A coherent system might include beliefs with varying degrees of certainty and mutual support.**, **Consider a set of beliefs where some are speculative but coherent with more certain beliefs.**, **It is plausible that coherent speculation does not provide the same degree of justification as more certain beliefs, challenging the sufficiency of coherence for justification.**
