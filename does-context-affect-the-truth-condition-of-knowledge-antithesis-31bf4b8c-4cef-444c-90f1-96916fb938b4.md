# Does context affect the truth condition of knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 31bf4b8c-4cef-444c-90f1-96916fb938b4
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [The Contextualist View of Knowledge](the-contextualist-view-of-knowledge-synthesis-744b190b-128c-4187-8edf-0a707cd161d1.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true.**, **The contextualist view implies that truth conditions could be perceived differently in various contexts.**, **This challenges the objectivity and independence of truth from conversational context.**
