## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from reliable cognitive faculties**, **Cognitive faculties are reliable when they consistently produce true beliefs more often than not**
