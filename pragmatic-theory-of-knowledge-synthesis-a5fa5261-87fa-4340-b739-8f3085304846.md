## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that acting on the belief leads to practical success given the person’s goals**, **Practical success involves achieving the desired outcome without unintended negative consequences**
