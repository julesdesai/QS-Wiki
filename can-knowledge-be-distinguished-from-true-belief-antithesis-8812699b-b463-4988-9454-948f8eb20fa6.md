# Can knowledge be distinguished from true belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 8812699b-b463-4988-9454-948f8eb20fa6
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-35af9163-7530-4447-9474-ccb688222939.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person forms a belief that is true due to happenstance rather than intellectual work. Intuitively, we would judge such belief as lacking the characteristics of knowledge, despite being true.**, **A plausible principle is that knowledge differs from mere true belief, which raises the question of whether the exercise of intellectual virtue truly differentiates knowledge.**
