# Virtue Epistemology with Anti-Luck Condition

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is the result of intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is not true merely by luck**
