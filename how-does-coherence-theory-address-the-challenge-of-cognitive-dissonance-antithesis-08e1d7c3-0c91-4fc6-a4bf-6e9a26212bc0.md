# How does coherence theory address the challenge of cognitive dissonance?

**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism without Infinite Regress](coherentism-without-infinite-regress-synthesis-1802290e-23e3-475c-be0d-a870496d2311.md)

## Content

**Consider a person experiencing cognitive dissonance, holding both a belief and a contradicting belief coherently. Intuitively, cognitive dissonance indicates a failure of coherent justification because incompatible beliefs persist.**, **There is a plausible principle that effective coherence should eliminate contradictory beliefs, yet cognitive dissonance shows this is not guaranteed.**, **The persistence of cognitive dissonance questions the reliability of coherence as a measure of justified belief.**
