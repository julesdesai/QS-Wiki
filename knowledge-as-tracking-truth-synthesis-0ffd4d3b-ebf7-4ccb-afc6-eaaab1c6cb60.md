## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief tracks the truth across relevant circumstances**, **A belief tracks the truth if it changes according to the truth value in different situations**
