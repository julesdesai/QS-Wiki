## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from the intellectual virtues of the believer**, **Intellectual virtues include qualities like conscientiousness and open-mindedness**
