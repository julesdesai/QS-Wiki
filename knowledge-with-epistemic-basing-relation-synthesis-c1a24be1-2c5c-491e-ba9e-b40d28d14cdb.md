# Knowledge with Epistemic Basing Relation

**Parent:** [Is knowledge possible with indirect causal links?](is-knowledge-possible-with-indirect-causal-links-antithesis-dc1e02a7-c991-41d5-83e8-fffb9dd5dfd5.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief be appropriately based on reliable evidence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is appropriately based on reliable evidence**
