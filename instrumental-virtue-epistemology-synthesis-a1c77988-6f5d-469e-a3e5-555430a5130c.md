# Instrumental Virtue Epistemology

**Parent:** [Can knowledge be reduced to intellectual virtue?](can-knowledge-be-reduced-to-intellectual-virtue-antithesis-5ac721c0-b17d-468c-80cd-51013afcbd4b.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is facilitated by an intellectual virtue or ability with instrumental aid**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is facilitated by an intellectual virtue or ability with instrumental aid**
