# Knowledge as Virtue Epistemology

**Parent:** [Can a reliable process be defeated by better information?](can-a-reliable-process-be-defeated-by-better-information-antithesis-42ece46f-fd5c-4034-8fe4-97dbe73e4c63.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is produced by intellectual virtue**
