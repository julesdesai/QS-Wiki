# Knowledge as Properly Justified Belief

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they are properly justified in their belief**, **A belief is properly justified if it is based on evidence that makes the belief more likely than not**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and they are properly justified in their belief**
