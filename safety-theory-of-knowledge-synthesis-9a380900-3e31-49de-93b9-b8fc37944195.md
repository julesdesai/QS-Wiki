## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in nearby possible worlds, if they believe the proposition, then it is true**, **This ensures the belief is safe from error**
