## Content

**Knowledge is a relation between the knower, a proposition, and a universal set of alternatives**, **A person knows a proposition if the proposition is true in all possible contexts of relevant alternatives**, **A person knows a proposition if they believe it when all alternatives acknowledge the proposition as true**, **The context influences which alternatives are relevant, but does not alter the universal truth required for knowledge**
