## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have a true belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have a true belief and the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**
