## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they possess unified justification that applies across contexts**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have unified-context justification, the proposition is true, and they believe it**
