# Can the presence of unknown defeaters negate knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1d9eb0c8-ba0b-4f2e-b87c-64b7a61b99fc
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Process Reliabilism with Defeaters](process-reliabilism-with-defeaters-synthesis-8f2dc61a-89b5-47d0-8b68-b09b8bc0bb2a.md)

## Content

**It is plausible that someone may have a belief that is true and formed by a reliable process without being aware of existing counter-evidence.**, **If they lack awareness of defeaters, intuitively, it seems inappropriate to deny their claim to the knowledge.**
