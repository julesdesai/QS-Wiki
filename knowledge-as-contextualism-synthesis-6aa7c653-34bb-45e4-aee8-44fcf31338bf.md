## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the standards for knowledge are met, as determined by the context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief meets the contextual standards of knowledge and the proposition is true**
