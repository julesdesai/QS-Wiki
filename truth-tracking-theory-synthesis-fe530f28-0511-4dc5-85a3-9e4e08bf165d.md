## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they would not believe the proposition if it were false**, **A person's belief tracks the truth if it varies with the truth or falsity of the proposition**
