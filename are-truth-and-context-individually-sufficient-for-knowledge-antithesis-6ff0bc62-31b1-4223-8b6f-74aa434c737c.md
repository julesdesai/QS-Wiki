# Are truth and context individually sufficient for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 6ff0bc62-31b1-4223-8b6f-74aa434c737c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism with Defeat Condition](contextualism-with-defeat-condition-synthesis-644e1e82-6b43-472b-8e60-41eaf47756ee.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a person believes a proposition that happens to be true, but their belief is held in a context of unreliable information sources.**, **It is implausible that simply because a belief is true and contextually situated, the belief constitutes knowledge.**
