# Infinitist Justification

**Parent:** [Can foundational justifications vary with context to always suffice for knowledge?](can-foundational-justifications-vary-with-context-to-always-suffice-for-knowledge-antithesis-dc0aa0f7-7e2f-4415-91ce-7d0414a37be8.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is supported by an infinite chain of justifications**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is supported by an infinite chain of justifications**
