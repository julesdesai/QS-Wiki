# Knowledge as Proper Function

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is tracking truth necessary for knowledge?](is-tracking-truth-necessary-for-knowledge-antithesis-29ee8705-f7cd-4e14-a87d-98e3c5047c7a.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their cognitive faculties function properly in an environment for which they were designed**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their cognitive faculties are functioning properly**
