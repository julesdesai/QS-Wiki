# How can infinite chains of reasons fit within contextually coherent frameworks?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5c19b156-4554-496d-9672-d0221506019e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextual Infinitism](contextual-infinitism-synthesis-969d15cb-7035-4449-8e21-a18b5558491f.md)

## Content

**A contextually coherent framework is one that remains stable and consistent within a humanly manageable context**, **It is implausible to assume that an infinite chain of reasons can be managed and maintained in a finite human context**, **The demand for a contextually coherent framework seems incompatible with the idea of an infinite chain that requires constant updating and revision**
