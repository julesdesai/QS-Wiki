## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a belief to be reliable, it must be produced by a cognitive process that usually yields true beliefs**, **Intellectual virtues enhance the reliability of belief-formation processes**
