# Does sensitivity account for counterfactual situations adequately?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a1bf062a-ed47-4244-961b-26b5836211e3
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Sensitivity Theory of Knowledge](sensitivity-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-ba84f32c-948c-4607-9039-09527bd88c50.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person's belief remains unchanged even when a proposition is slightly altered or contextual factors differ only slightly. In such a scenario, intuitively, sensitivity may fail as minor changes should not intuitively affect knowledge.**
