# Can conflicting coherent systems both represent knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 8d38b87a-e41c-42d8-8f54-3c9b08d5c054
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualist Coherentism](contextualist-coherentism-synthesis-598dc05f-03d6-4aeb-b0db-fa7173390f96.md)

## Content

**Consider two individuals with coherent belief systems about the origin of the universe, one religious and one scientific. Both systems are internally coherent but conflict with each other.**, **If coherence alone could determine knowledge, both conflicting belief systems would equally represent knowledge, which is paradoxical.**
