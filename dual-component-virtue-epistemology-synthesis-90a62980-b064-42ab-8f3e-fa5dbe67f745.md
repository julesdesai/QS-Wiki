# Dual Component Virtue Epistemology

**Parent:** [Can knowledge be acquired without intellectual virtue?](can-knowledge-be-acquired-without-intellectual-virtue-antithesis-fa13d8b8-f80c-4fee-a64e-df36d69d8cc0.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that it is either the result of intellectual virtue or a reliable process**, **Intellectual virtue and reliability are distinct but compatible sources of knowledge**
