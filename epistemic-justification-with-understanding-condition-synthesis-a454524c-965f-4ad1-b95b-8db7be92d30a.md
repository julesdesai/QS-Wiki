# Epistemic Justification with Understanding Condition

**Parent:** [Can epistemic luck be entirely eliminated in cases of knowledge?](can-epistemic-luck-be-entirely-eliminated-in-cases-of-knowledge-antithesis-ffe010f4-46f7-40af-9534-9408ed3f9881.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is arrived at through understanding**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is justified, and the belief is arrived at through understanding**
