## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have internal access to their cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary and sufficient that their belief is formed by a reliable cognitive process and they have introspective awareness of this process**
