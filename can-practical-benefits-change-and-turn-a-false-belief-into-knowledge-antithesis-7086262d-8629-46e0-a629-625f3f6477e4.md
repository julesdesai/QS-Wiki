# Can practical benefits change and turn a false belief into knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7086262d-8629-46e0-a629-625f3f6477e4
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Revised Pragmatic Theory of Knowledge](revised-pragmatic-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-d7b209ba-b523-44b3-ad9a-c228c53c8771.md)

## Content

**A belief might initially seem useful and practical, but new information may change its perceived utility.**, **The perceived practicality of a belief could fluctuate with changing circumstances.**, **If practical benefits dictate knowledge, this introduces instability in what is considered knowledge over time.**
