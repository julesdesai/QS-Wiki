## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief-forming process is deemed reliable within a contextual framework**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is formed in a way reliably deemed appropriate by contextual standards**
