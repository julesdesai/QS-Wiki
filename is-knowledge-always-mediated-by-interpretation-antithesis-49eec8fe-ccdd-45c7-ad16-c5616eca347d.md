# Is knowledge always mediated by interpretation?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 49eec8fe-ccdd-45c7-ad16-c5616eca347d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Critical Realism](critical-realism-synthesis-04d59a43-df5d-4ff1-bc30-b363f66f9a03.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where a person intuitively knows it is cold by feeling the temperature on their skin. In this case, intuitively, this knowledge arises directly from perception without cognitive reinterpretation.**, **It seems plausible that not all knowledge must be processed through interpretative faculties, particularly immediate physical sensations.**
