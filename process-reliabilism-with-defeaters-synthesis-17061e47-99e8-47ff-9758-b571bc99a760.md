## Content

**A belief constitutes knowledge if it is produced by a reliable process and there are no defeating truths**, **Defeaters are truths that, if possessed, would undermine the warrant of a belief**, **A reliable process is one that generally produces true beliefs**, **A person knows a proposition if the proposition is true**, **A person knows a proposition if they believe it**
