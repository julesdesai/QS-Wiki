## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in nearly all nearby possible worlds where they believe the proposition, it is true regardless of the method used**, **The independence from specific belief-forming methods ensures cognitive safety**
