# Knowledge with a Fallible Process Condition

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** b2129265-434a-49ba-b29a-223b2a9bb376
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is the sufficiency condition too demanding?](is-the-sufficiency-condition-too-demanding-antithesis-285d118a-2730-4998-851e-17d70ca92afa.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is justified even if fallibly so**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is fallibly justified**
