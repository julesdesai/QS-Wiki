## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is justified internally through consistency with other beliefs they hold**, **Internal justification refers to coherence between the belief in question and other beliefs**
