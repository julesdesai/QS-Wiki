# Knowledge as Virtue Epistemology

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 8b768b7c-9d43-4ef4-954c-2dbd7536bee9
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can justified true belief be insufficient for knowledge?](can-justified-true-belief-be-insufficient-for-knowledge-antithesis-d3c4d331-db64-48fc-8fb2-c013676f98ab.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief manifests intellectual virtues**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief results from intellectual virtues**
