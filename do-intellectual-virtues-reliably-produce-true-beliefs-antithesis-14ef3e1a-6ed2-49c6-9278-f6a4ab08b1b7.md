# Do intellectual virtues reliably produce true beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 14ef3e1a-6ed2-49c6-9278-f6a4ab08b1b7
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-b6aa4282-ad09-4174-b191-6cfd3fcbdf83.md)

## Content

**There are cases where individuals with developed intellectual virtues hold persistent false beliefs due to cultural or situational biases**, **It is implausible that intellectual virtues are always sufficient to guarantee true beliefs given the influence of extraneous factors**
