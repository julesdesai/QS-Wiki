# Is non-conscious knowledge truly knowledge?

**Node ID:** f1e2bd7a-2a9f-4aa5-a658-4d9be8058e08
**Depth:** 4

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology Approach](virtue-epistemology-approach-synthesis-b14f243d-0108-46b0-abbb-b26bd31de791.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where a person gains true beliefs through virtue without any conscious awareness of them. Intuitively, it feels incorrect to classify this as actual knowledge due to the absence of conscious recognition.**, **Knowledge usually implies a degree of conscious reflection or awareness by the individual possessing it.**
