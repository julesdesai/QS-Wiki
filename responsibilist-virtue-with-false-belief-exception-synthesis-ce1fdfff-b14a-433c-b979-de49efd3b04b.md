## Content

**Knowledge is true belief formed through the exercise of intellectual virtue acknowledging exceptions**, **Intellectual virtues include traits like open-mindedness and fairness**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **Beliefs formed from intellectual virtues are accepted, but those that result in false beliefs are not counted as knowledge**
