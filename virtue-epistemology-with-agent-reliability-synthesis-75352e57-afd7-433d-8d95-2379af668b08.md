## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the agent reliably forms true beliefs in similar contexts**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief arises from an intellectual virtue or competence and the agent exhibits reliability**
