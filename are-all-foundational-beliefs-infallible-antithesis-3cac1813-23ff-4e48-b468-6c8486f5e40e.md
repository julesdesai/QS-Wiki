# Are all foundational beliefs infallible?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3cac1813-23ff-4e48-b468-6c8486f5e40e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundationalism with Reliable Basic Beliefs](foundationalism-with-reliable-basic-beliefs-synthesis-62bc80a9-ef75-4d49-9578-a29890370bd2.md)

## Content

**A foundational belief is one that does not derive justification from other beliefs.**, **It is implausible that a foundational belief could be always immune to error.**, **If foundational beliefs can be fallible, then relying solely on them for knowledge could lead to incorrect conclusions.**
