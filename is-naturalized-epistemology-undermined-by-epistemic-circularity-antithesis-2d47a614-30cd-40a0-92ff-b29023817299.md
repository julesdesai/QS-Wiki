# Is naturalized epistemology undermined by epistemic circularity?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2d47a614-30cd-40a0-92ff-b29023817299
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Naturalized Epistemology](naturalized-epistemology-synthesis-99d5c2d6-f993-4109-8adb-f973b72c0335.md)

## Content

**Suppose that justification consists in processes verified by empirical science, and empirical science itself relies on epistemic principles requiring justification. Intuitively, this circularity undermines confidence in such justification.**, **There is a plausible principle that a justified method cannot rely on its own results for validation.**, **Naturalized epistemology potentially falls into epistemic circularity by relying on empirical science to justify epistemological claims.**
