# Does non-conscious coherence satisfy the demands of justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 8fc64779-76d8-4cb5-8205-c13d43085a4d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism with Non-Conscious Elements](coherentism-with-non-conscious-elements-synthesis-025108b6-66b5-4784-9945-6373af308ffa.md)

## Content

**Consider a belief that is justified merely because it coheres with a larger system of non-conscious beliefs. This seems counterintuitive to our understanding of justification, which typically involves conscious evaluation.**, **There is a plausible principle that holds that non-conscious coherence by itself does not satisfy the requirements of justification for a belief.**
