# Can personal conviction alone establish knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 16f6af4a-eaeb-43ea-9d51-07679c775a0f

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person feels profound certainty about a belief like feeling loved, with no external evidence. Intuitively, we might judge they have personal knowledge.**, **Situations where personal conviction seems to establish knowledge question the necessity of external evidence for establishing truth.**
