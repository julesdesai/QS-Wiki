# Can subconscious processes accurately contribute to justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9adcd6a8-4bb1-466c-aa37-454b914762c3
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism](infinitism-synthesis-d655bb23-a2c0-4c5f-b0d3-18cd986ab587.md)

## Content

**Subconscious processes are difficult to verify or assess for their rationality or contribution to knowledge.**, **Suppose subconscious processes contribute to justification, intuitively it seems we cannot be aware of their appropriateness or role.**, **If we cannot access or evaluate subconscious processes, then they might not suffice for meaningful justification in a knowledge claim.**
