# Do intellectual virtues guarantee the coherence of beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5204d5fa-44dd-4987-a2b3-865dcb572503
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology Coherentism](virtue-epistemology-coherentism-synthesis-fa375fb7-6e26-497f-b63d-bbaec5305ff8.md)

## Content

**It is possible to exercise intellectual virtues like intellectual courage and arrive at a disjointed set of beliefs.**, **Having intellectual virtues in belief formation does not automatically ensure the coherence of those beliefs.**, **Suppose a person acts with intellectual open-mindedness and explores diverse perspectives, potentially leading to a more fragmented set of beliefs rather than a coherent one.**
