## Content

**Knowledge requires having true belief undefeated by counter-evidence**, **A person knows a proposition if and only if their belief is true in the context of justification at the time of realization**, **Justification for a belief is determined by context-specific standards**, **Misleading evidence may alter the context but does not necessarily defeat knowledge unless it changes the justification in that context**
