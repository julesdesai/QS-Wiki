## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified by factors external to the individual's mental states**, **Justification does not require the believer to be aware of the factors providing justification**
