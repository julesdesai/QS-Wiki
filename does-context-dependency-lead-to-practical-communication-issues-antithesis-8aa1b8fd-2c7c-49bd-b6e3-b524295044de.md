# Does context-dependency lead to practical communication issues?

**Node Type:** antithesis

**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism With Context-Dependent Justification Standards](contextualism-with-context-dependent-justification-standards-synthesis-a8126985-f485-4c51-b545-16d8a583ea1e.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person uses a technical context to claim knowledge but shifts to a casual context to avoid responsibility. Intuitively, such behavior suggests misuse of context-dependent standards leading to communication difficulties and misunderstandings.**, **There is a plausible principle that effective communication requires stable concepts of knowledge that do not shift with contexts. This principle together with context-dependent justification undermines communicative clarity.**
