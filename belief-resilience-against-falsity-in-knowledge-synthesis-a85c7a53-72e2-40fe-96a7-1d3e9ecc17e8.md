# Belief Resilience Against Falsity in Knowledge

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Could justified false beliefs highlight a flaw in the necessity of truth for knowledge?](could-justified-false-beliefs-highlight-a-flaw-in-the-necessity-of-truth-for-knowledge-antithesis-c5ef612d-3e88-4c19-8604-332c16411fb5.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is resilient against being proven false in practical circumstances**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition with resilient support, and practical outcomes support the belief**
