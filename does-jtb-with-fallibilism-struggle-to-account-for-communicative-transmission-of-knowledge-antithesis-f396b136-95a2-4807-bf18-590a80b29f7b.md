## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person communicates a belief based on fallible justification that happens to be true. Intuitively, the recipient might question the validity of receiving knowledge due to the fallibility involved.**, **There is a plausible principle that knowledge transmission through communication should maintain a certain level of reliability to ensure the integrity of knowledge sharing.**
