## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is an appropriate causal connection between the proposition and the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is true and causally connected to the fact it represents**
