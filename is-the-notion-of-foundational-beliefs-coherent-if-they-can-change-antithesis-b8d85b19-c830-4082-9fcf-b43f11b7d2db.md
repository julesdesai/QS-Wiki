# Is the notion of foundational beliefs coherent if they can change?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b8d85b19-c830-4082-9fcf-b43f11b7d2db
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualist Foundationalism](contextualist-foundationalism-synthesis-9835edc1-dd3b-4e9d-81fa-8a065de25b95.md)

## Content

**A belief that is foundational and stable should not vary with context if it is to provide a genuine foundation for knowledge. It is implausible to argue that a changing belief still serves as a robust foundation.**
