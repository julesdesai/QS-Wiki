## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they adhere to justification standards that are relativized but invariant**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and they adhere to justification standards that are relativized but invariant**
