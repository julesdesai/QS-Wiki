# Is the role of intellectual virtues in belief formation well-defined?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2b909bb0-8243-4493-9c25-f34ced2812e0
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology Foundationalism](virtue-epistemology-foundationalism-synthesis-a32c22e3-fd99-46f1-bd06-3346824cbc70.md)

## Content

**Suppose a belief is formed by relying on what one takes to be an intellectual virtue. Intuitively, there is ambiguity in how intellectual virtues are defined and categorized. Without a clear delineation, it is implausible to rely on them as foundational to knowledge, as this undermines the reliability and consistency attributed to them.**
