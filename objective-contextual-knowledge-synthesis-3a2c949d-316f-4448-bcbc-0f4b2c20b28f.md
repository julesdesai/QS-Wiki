# Objective Contextual Knowledge

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 3a2c949d-316f-4448-bcbc-0f4b2c20b28f
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does contextual justification challenge universal truth?](does-contextual-justification-challenge-universal-truth-antithesis-a0a4bd52-ed0a-4322-9dec-e60438e63398.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification is both appropriate to the context and subjected to objective scrutiny**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification is both appropriate to the context and subjected to objective scrutiny**
