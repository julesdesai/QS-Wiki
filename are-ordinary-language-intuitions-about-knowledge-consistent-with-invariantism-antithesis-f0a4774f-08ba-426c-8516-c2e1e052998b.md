# Are ordinary language intuitions about knowledge consistent with invariantism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f0a4774f-08ba-426c-8516-c2e1e052998b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Minimalist Invariantism Summary](minimalist-invariantism-summary-synthesis-54e077e3-2135-4360-975c-7f7ee003ff29.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where ordinary language suggests a person knows something in casual conversation but not in more formal evaluations like academic testing.**, **Intuitively, this discrepancy suggests that actual knowledge attribution varies with context and that invariantism conflicts with common usage.**
