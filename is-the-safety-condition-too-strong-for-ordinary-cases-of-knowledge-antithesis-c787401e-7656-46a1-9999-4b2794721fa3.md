# Is the Safety Condition too strong for ordinary cases of knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c787401e-7656-46a1-9999-4b2794721fa3
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Safety Condition View](safety-condition-view-synthesis-739cc2e2-5111-4640-9af6-f9324f258620.md)

## Content

**A person knows that the sun will rise tomorrow, relying on everyday inductive reasoning. Intuitively, this is considered knowledge even though it is based on past observations.**, **In nearby possible worlds, unforeseen astronomical events could prevent sunrise, suggesting the belief is not safe as it could easily be false in such scenarios.**
