## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, their belief must result from a significant intellectual virtue**, **A significant intellectual virtue is a cognitive ability or competence that is robustly connected to truth in a wide range of contexts**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have a true belief resulting from a significant intellectual virtue**
