# Could internal representations still be necessary for perception?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 528c96f2-452e-48a6-a281-498a0d922db0
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Ecological Direct Perception Theory](ecological-direct-perception-theory-synthesis-73031ffe-0106-4b3d-af38-bdf2623ababa.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that complex environments require internal models to integrate sensory data. This principle together with the claim that brain processes involve prediction and error correction entails that internal representations might still be necessary for accurate perception.**
