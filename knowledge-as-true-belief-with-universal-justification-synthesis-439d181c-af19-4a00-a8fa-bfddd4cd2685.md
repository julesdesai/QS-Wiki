# Knowledge as True Belief with Universal Justification

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 439d181c-af19-4a00-a8fa-bfddd4cd2685
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Do different contexts yield contradictory knowledge states?](do-different-contexts-yield-contradictory-knowledge-states-antithesis-17ef9714-feb5-4a46-8772-c44ecbf08087.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification for the belief holds universally across all contexts**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification for the belief holds universally across all contexts**
