# Knowledge as No-Luck Justification

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is justified**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that any justification they have for the belief is not a result of luck**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, their belief is justified, and the justification is not a result of luck**
