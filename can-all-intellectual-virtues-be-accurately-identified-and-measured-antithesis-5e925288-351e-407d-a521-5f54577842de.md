# Can all intellectual virtues be accurately identified and measured?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5e925288-351e-407d-a521-5f54577842de
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-c68e0504-3343-450c-a9ae-cebbb34ec2ad.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that we can identify and quantify all the various aspects of intellectual virtue given human diversity and contextual variability, suggesting challenges in applying the concept consistently.**, **Consider a scenario where different cultures prioritize different cognitive skills as virtues. In such a scenario, intuitively, identifying a universal list of virtues encompassing all knowledge cases seems problematic, challenging the claim that intellectual virtue suffices for knowledge universally**
