## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary to accept it as true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the acceptance be carefully considered**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they accept the proposition as true, the proposition is true, and the acceptance is carefully considered**
