# Virtue Epistemology with Stability Component

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 47da3f26-1c25-48d3-af91-dd5913643f7f
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge survive radical changes in justification?](can-knowledge-survive-radical-changes-in-justification-antithesis-75cca677-1fe3-4fc2-9a13-58433081266a.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is produced by an intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their justification remains stable**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, the justification remains stable, the proposition is true, and their belief is produced by an intellectual virtue**
