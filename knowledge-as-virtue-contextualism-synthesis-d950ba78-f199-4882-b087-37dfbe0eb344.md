## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief arises from an epistemic virtue in the relevant context**, **Epistemic virtue is defined as a quality that reliably leads to truth across similar contexts**
