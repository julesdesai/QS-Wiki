## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres with a system of beliefs**, **For a belief to be known, it is necessary that it aligns with empirical evidence that simplifies its integration**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, it coheres with a system of beliefs, and is supported by empirical evidence**
