# Credit Theory of Knowledge

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does true belief from intellectual virtue ensure knowledge?](does-true-belief-from-intellectual-virtue-ensure-knowledge-antithesis-5829b941-4312-442c-b3ab-b0568961fed0.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is a result of intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the truth of the belief is significantly creditable to the person's intellectual efforts and abilities**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the truth is significantly creditable to the person's intellectual efforts**
