# Are afterimages compatible with experience constituting objects?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** bcbddb64-717f-48f1-bb3b-f1a9cffd622e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Naive Realism](naive-realism-synthesis-a575a970-7874-461b-b9b5-9de679546681.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person looks at the sun and then sees an afterimage when closing their eyes. Intuitively, there is no external object directly accounting for the experience of the afterimage.**, **If experiences like afterimages occur without external objects, then the claim that objects themselves constitute the experience is challenged.**
