# Can constructed knowledge be reliable?

## Content

**Suppose people use different mental operations to construct knowledge from the same perception.**, **Intuitively, varied mental operations could lead to different constructed knowledge outputs.**, **If different people construct different knowledge from the same perceptual input, it is doubtful whether such knowledge is universally reliable.**
