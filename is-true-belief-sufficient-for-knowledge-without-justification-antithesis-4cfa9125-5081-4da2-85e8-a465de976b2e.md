# Is true belief sufficient for knowledge without justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 4cfa9125-5081-4da2-85e8-a465de976b2e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatism with Practical Consequences](pragmatism-with-practical-consequences-synthesis-9fee2762-7d04-464a-9533-5a40ce1aa186.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where someone correctly guesses the outcome of a lottery without any evidence or reason. Intuitively, their true belief is not regarded as knowledge because it lacks justification.**, **Epistemic justification requires more than just the truth of a belief; it demands reasons or evidence that connect belief with truth.**
