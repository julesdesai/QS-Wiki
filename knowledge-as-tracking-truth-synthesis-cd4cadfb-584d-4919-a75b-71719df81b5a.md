# Knowledge as Tracking Truth

**Parent:** [Is circularity in reliability a challenge to externalism?](is-circularity-in-reliability-a-challenge-to-externalism-antithesis-e7f557ed-d278-4988-b106-94fb98f2ddd2.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person would not believe the proposition if it were false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and they would not believe it if it were false**
