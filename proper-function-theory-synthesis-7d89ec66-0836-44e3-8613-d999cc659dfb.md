## Content

**For a belief to constitute knowledge, it must be produced by cognitive faculties that are functioning properly**, **Properly functioning cognitive faculties are those that operate in an environment for which they were designed**, **The cognitive faculties must aim at producing true beliefs**, **A belief formed in these conditions constitutes knowledge if it is true**
