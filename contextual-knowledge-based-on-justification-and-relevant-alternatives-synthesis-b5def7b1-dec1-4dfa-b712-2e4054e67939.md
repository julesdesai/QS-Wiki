# Contextual knowledge based on justification and relevant alternatives

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** b5def7b1-dec1-4dfa-b712-2e4054e67939
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is belief alone enough without justification?](is-belief-alone-enough-without-justification-antithesis-c934b0e7-c97e-4fe9-a36c-456f6e1a299e.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient to have justified true belief and rule out relevant alternatives**, **The context determines which alternatives are relevant and which require justification**
