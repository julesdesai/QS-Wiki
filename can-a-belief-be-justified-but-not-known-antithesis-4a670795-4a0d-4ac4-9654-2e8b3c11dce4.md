# Can a belief be justified but not known?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 4a670795-4a0d-4ac4-9654-2e8b3c11dce4
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualist Coherentism](contextualist-coherentism-synthesis-598dc05f-03d6-4aeb-b0db-fa7173390f96.md)

## Content

**A case in which a person sincerely believes a false historical claim, such as that humans and dinosaurs coexisted, and this belief coheres with their overall system of beliefs is one where the belief is justified but not known.**, **A justified belief that is false cannot constitute knowledge, even if it fits into a coherent system.**
