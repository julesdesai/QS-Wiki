# Can knowledge be acquired without exercising intellectual virtues?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0b7a9134-8de9-4ff9-b66d-77c07e0e7792
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-fbdf23c1-0af0-48fb-9b70-516c394a97b2.md)

## Content

**A person may acquire true beliefs by lucky guesses or accidental correctness, thus not exercising any intellectual virtues.**, **Knowledge can still be attributed to a person even if they do not intentionally deploy any intellectual virtue when arriving at a true belief.**
