## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that all relevant alternatives to the proposition being true have been ruled out**, **What counts as a relevant alternative is determined by pragmatic and epistemic contextual factors**
