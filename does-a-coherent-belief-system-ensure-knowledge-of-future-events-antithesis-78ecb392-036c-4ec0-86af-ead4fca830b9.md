# Does a coherent belief system ensure knowledge of future events?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 78ecb392-036c-4ec0-86af-ead4fca830b9
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism with Pragmatic Justification](coherentism-with-pragmatic-justification-synthesis-aef723d7-2be0-4b42-a946-2acb73c17357.md)

## Content

**A person can have a coherent belief system that is accurate up to the present but fails to account for future events that might invalidate current beliefs.**, **If knowledge requires enduring truth, then knowledge of future events is insecure within coherentism.**
