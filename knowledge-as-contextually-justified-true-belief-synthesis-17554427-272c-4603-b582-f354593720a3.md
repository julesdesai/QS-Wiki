# Knowledge as Contextually Justified True Belief

**Parent:** [Can knowledge be attributed when trust is based on social or cultural bias?](can-knowledge-be-attributed-when-trust-is-based-on-social-or-cultural-bias-antithesis-2341fa54-9d0f-453b-97f9-a69311faeef1.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is contextually justified**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is contextually justified**
