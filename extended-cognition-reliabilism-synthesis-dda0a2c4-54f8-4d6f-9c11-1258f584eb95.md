## Content

**A belief counts as knowledge if it is the result of a reliable cognitive process that extends beyond the individual**, **Reliable cognitive processes can include those that are social or communal in nature**, **Knowledge does not require the individual to have insight or understanding of the cognitive process if it is extended**
