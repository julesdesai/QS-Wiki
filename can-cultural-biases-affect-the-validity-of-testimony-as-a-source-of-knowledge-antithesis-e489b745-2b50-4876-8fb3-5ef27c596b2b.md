# Can cultural biases affect the validity of testimony as a source of knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e489b745-2b50-4876-8fb3-5ef27c596b2b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Testimonial Justification Theory](testimonial-justification-theory-synthesis-a0572f9e-ea0a-45f6-9bf1-a16e71038692.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which testimony is influenced by cultural bias and another in which testimony remains neutral. Intuitively, we would judge that knowledge gained from culturally biased testimony might be questionable compared to neutral testimony.**, **There is a plausible principle that biases can distort information and affect the credibility of testimony. This principle suggests that knowledge justified by culturally biased testimony is potentially compromised.**
