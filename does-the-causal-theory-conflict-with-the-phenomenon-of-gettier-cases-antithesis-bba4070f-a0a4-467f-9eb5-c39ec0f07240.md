# Does the causal theory conflict with the phenomenon of Gettier cases?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** bba4070f-a0a4-467f-9eb5-c39ec0f07240
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Causal Theory of Knowledge](causal-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-cee9090d-2e34-47f2-b4ee-a59054387264.md)

## Content

**Suppose an individual forms a belief that they will meet a friend based on seeing a distinctive car that friend drives, but unknown to the individual, the friend was already intending to visit. The belief is true, but causally, it was misinformed by the presence of another similar car. Intuition suggests the individual lacks knowledge despite the truth of the belief.**, **A Gettier case challenges the causal link because the truth of the belief did not cause the belief to be true in the expected manner.**
