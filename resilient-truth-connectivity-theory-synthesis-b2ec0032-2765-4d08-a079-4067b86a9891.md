## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, the truth and belief connectivity must remain resilient through short-term external variations**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person would not believe the proposition if it were false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that resilient connectivity and belief-satisfaction conditions hold**
