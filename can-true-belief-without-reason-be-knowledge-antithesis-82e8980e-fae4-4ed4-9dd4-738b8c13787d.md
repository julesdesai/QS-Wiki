# Can true belief without reason be knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 82e8980e-fae4-4ed4-9dd4-738b8c13787d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-7b73a914-ea0c-4f4b-b314-725a0c878daa.md)

## Content

**Consider a person who has an unexplained true belief based purely on a hunch or whim. Intuitively, we would assert that this does not meet the standards of genuine knowledge**, **It is implausible for knowledge to consist solely of true belief without an accompanying reasoned justification**, **Knowledge traditionally encompasses justified true belief, where the justification serves as a bridge between belief and truth**
