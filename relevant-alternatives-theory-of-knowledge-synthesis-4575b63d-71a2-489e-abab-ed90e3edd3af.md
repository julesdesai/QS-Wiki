## Content

**A person knows a proposition if the proposition is true and they believe it**, **Knowledge requires the ability to distinguish the truth from relevant alternatives**, **Relevant alternatives are those which could realistically be confused with the truth**, **If only irrelevant alternatives are available, knowing does not require awareness of them**
