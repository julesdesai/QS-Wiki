# Knowledge as Sensitivity to Truth

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** a7dec324-2f36-421a-b26a-1ccf8e72042b
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does truth alone adequately distinguish justified belief from knowledge in misleading environments?](does-truth-alone-adequately-distinguish-justified-belief-from-knowledge-in-misleading-environments-antithesis-ceae63fd-e009-4272-9302-9d3ac2eba879.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, the proposition is true, and that if the proposition were false, they would not believe it**
