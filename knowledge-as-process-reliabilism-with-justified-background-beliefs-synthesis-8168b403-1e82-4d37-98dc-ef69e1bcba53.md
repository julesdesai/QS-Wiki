# Knowledge as Process Reliabilism with Justified Background Beliefs

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is the result of a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the believer has justified background beliefs that support the belief**
