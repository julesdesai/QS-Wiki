# Knowledge as No False Lemmas

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Can knowledge be undermined by the possibility of misleading evidence?](can-knowledge-be-undermined-by-the-possibility-of-misleading-evidence-antithesis-96e63b54-af01-4211-821e-cfd6bda4d4bc.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is not based on any false assumption or lemma**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is not derived from any false premise**
