# Does Coherentism risk circular justification among beliefs?

## Content

**In a coherent system, beliefs mutually support each other, but this might lead to a circular justification problem.**, **A system where a belief is supported by another that eventually loops back to support the original belief suggests no independent grounding for justification.**, **A circularly justified belief seems intuitively unjustified, questioning whether coherence alone can guarantee justification.**
