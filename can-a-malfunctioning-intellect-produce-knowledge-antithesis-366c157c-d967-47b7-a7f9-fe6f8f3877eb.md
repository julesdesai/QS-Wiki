# Can a malfunctioning intellect produce knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 366c157c-d967-47b7-a7f9-fe6f8f3877eb
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue epistemology view on knowledge](virtue-epistemology-view-on-knowledge-synthesis-e49b38ea-0e37-4416-ac67-ff35516a157e.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case where a person's intellectual faculties are impaired, yet they form a true belief. Intuitively, we either have to say this is knowledge or it is not. If we say it is knowledge, then functioning faculties are not necessary. If we say it is not knowledge, then malfunctioning faculties cannot produce knowledge.**
