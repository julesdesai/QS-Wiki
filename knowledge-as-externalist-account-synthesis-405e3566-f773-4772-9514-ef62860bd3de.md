## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is a reliable link between the belief and the truth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, it is true, and there is a reliable link between the belief and the truth**
