## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is an appropriate causal connection between the belief and the fact**, **The causal connection must be such that it ensures the belief is formed in a truth-oriented manner**
