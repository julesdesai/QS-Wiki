# Can accidental truths be considered genuine knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e07add0f-a58b-43ac-9152-93930f78639a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Nozick's Truth-Tracking Theory](nozicks-truth-tracking-theory-synthesis-1a215329-c53d-4dbe-9509-6b517d1bb8ad.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person forms a belief by guessing, which turns out to be true due to sheer luck. Intuitively, this belief does not amount to knowledge because it was not formed via any truth-sensitive process.**, **There exists a plausible principle that beliefs formed solely by luck, though true, do not constitute knowledge.**
