# Do practical stakes overwhelm evidential reasons for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 64bf5b8d-0308-48f5-b33e-c80f5b0bd37c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Encroachment with Influence of Practical Stakes](pragmatic-encroachment-with-influence-of-practical-stakes-synthesis-1e83d295-b88f-4064-889e-097b4fb938ca.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a scientist has overwhelming evidence that a theory is correct, but stands to lose funding or credibility if found wrong. In such a scenario, intuitively, we would judge that the scientist's knowledge remains intact despite practical pressures.**, **It is implausible that practical stakes can override strong evidential reasons for knowledge, as knowledge should primarily rest on evidence and truth.**
