# Are foundational beliefs universally reliable?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e1328294-fae1-465a-8df5-d499530ec827
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Evolutionary Foundationalism](evolutionary-foundationalism-synthesis-4b200bf3-94a7-4a07-af03-f4d0ca848734.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a foundational belief that has been selected for survival, such as an instinctive fear of snakes. Intuitively, this fear may not always correspond to the presence of danger. Thus, it is questionable whether such foundational beliefs are universally reliable in producing true beliefs.**
