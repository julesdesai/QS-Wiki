# Is truth necessary if cognitive and character virtues are present?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d633d4b4-a27e-4a99-928e-7513bbaa91d5
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Responsibilist Virtue Epistemology](responsibilist-virtue-epistemology-synthesis-23f2e823-6756-4d05-9cb6-b00f2dd07c18.md)

## Content

**Suppose there is a hypothetical world where cognitive and character virtues are perfectly manifested, yet the beliefs formed are false due to deception or misinformation. We might question if knowledge can be contingent merely upon virtue without requiring truth.**, **There is an intuition that virtues can independently contribute to justified belief without necessarily ensuring the truth of that belief, challenging the necessity of truth in knowledge.**
