# Knowledge as No False Lemmas

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can a belief track the truth in inherently unknowable cases?](can-a-belief-track-the-truth-in-inherently-unknowable-cases-antithesis-7daa33b8-fbe0-48fd-a1c1-7685be6b43cc.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person has justification for their belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification for the belief does not rely on any false assumptions**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification that does not rely on false assumptions, and the proposition is true**
