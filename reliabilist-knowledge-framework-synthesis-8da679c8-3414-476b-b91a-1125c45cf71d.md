## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **Reliability of a process is determined by its tendency to produce true beliefs**
