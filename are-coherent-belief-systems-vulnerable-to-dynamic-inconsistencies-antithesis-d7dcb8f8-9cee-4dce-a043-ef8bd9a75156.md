# Are coherent belief systems vulnerable to dynamic inconsistencies?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d7dcb8f8-9cee-4dce-a043-ef8bd9a75156
**Depth:** 2
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism](coherentism-thesis-368b08e4-1a7c-4e5d-96a8-85ade01bb443.md)

## Content

**A belief system can be coherent at one time and incoherent at another due to shifts in belief or new information.**, **This suggests that coherence is a dynamic property that might not provide stable justification or knowledge over time.**
