# Could pragmatic encroachment undermine the universality of knowledge claims?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7dfef88b-35f2-4a1e-a4a6-f4562eb95402
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Encroachment](pragmatic-encroachment-synthesis-829538ce-ebfd-41f1-81ea-e2a3bdbbc235.md)

## Content

**Imagine an academic subject where experts assess knowledge based on evidence and methodical inquiry. If pragmatic encroachment is introduced, it is conceivable that knowledge claims would vary based on individual or cultural stakes, thus eroding the universality and objectivity typically ascribed to knowledge.**
