## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief results from the exercise of intellectual virtue**
