## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A person is justified in believing a proposition if, in most nearby possible worlds where they believe it, it is true**, **Safety ensures the belief is not easily false in similar circumstances**
