# Is representation in perception possible without embodiment?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** effba012-e9ba-4680-97dd-5a2fafc42c02
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Embodied Cognition with Sensory Integration](embodied-cognition-with-sensory-integration-synthesis-1b01035b-41e7-4892-81fb-893d75aaae6e.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that perceptual representation involves mapping sensory inputs onto mental constructs. This principle together with instances where individuals construct mental representations without physical interaction suggests that perception can involve representation without embodiment.**
