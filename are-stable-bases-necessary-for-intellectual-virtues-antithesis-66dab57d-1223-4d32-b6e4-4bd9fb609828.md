# Are stable bases necessary for intellectual virtues?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 66dab57d-1223-4d32-b6e4-4bd9fb609828
**Depth:** 4

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology Knowledge Summary](virtue-epistemology-knowledge-summary-synthesis-93cb2e65-9f1c-412f-9271-07ea69b7164c.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that all intellectual virtues provide a stable basis since some virtues can be context-dependent.**, **A virtue may lead to knowledge in one context but not in another, questioning the need for a stable basis.**
