# Does phenomenological realism account for unconscious knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 44af9743-786b-46eb-b528-d4c383774eb0
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Phenomenological Realism](phenomenological-realism-synthesis-f972efdb-cd3c-4df6-b5d4-a15ff27a6c64.md)

## Content

**Consider a skilled pianist who can play complex pieces without consciously thinking about each note they are going to play. In such a scenario, the pianist intuitively possesses knowledge of the music without engaging in direct, conscious perceptual experience.**, **Knowledge can be encoded and utilized unconsciously in skill-based activities which do not always involve conscious perception as experience.**
