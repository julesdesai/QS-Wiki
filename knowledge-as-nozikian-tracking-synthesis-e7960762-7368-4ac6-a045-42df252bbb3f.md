# Knowledge as Nozikian Tracking

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** e7960762-7368-4ac6-a045-42df252bbb3f
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does adding a defeater remove knowledge?](does-adding-a-defeater-remove-knowledge-antithesis-4bf3ffe7-5579-499f-ac84-9588834167a6.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition is true, the person believes it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief tracks the truth both ways**
