## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have some degree of cognitive endorsement for the proposition**, **A person knows a proposition if it is true, they have justification for it, and have some degree of cognitive endorsement without full belief**
