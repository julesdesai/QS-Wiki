# Can Reliable Process Theory explain knowledge in new belief contexts?

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which an individual uses a new technology to form beliefs, such as AI predictions. The process is unfamiliar, yet surprisingly accurate. Intuitively, we question if these beliefs constitute knowledge despite lacking familiarity.**, **New technology may form reliable beliefs, but the lack of historical reliability challenges the immediate recognition of knowledge.**
