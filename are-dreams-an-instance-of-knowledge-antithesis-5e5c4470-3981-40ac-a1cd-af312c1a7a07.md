# Are dreams an instance of knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5e5c4470-3981-40ac-a1cd-af312c1a7a07
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Idealism](idealism-synthesis-53ec409c-4a9b-4261-be64-9dda9f92eb94.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person dreams vividly about flying. In such a scenario, intuitively we would not judge their experience of flying as knowledge of flight.**, **There is a plausible principle that knowledge requires an correspondence with objective reality. This principle together with dreams being private mental constructs entails that dreams do not constitute knowledge.**
