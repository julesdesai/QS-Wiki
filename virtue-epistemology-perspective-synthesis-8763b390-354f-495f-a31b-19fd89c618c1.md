## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief to be justified**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from an intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is justified, and the belief arises from an intellectual virtue**
