# How can reliability account for intuitive differences in knowledge between individuals?

## Content

**Consider two individuals with the same reliable processes but differing intuitive access to information, leading to different beliefs.**, **Intuitively, one person's belief might be seen as knowledge while the other's is not, challenging the sufficiency of reliability for knowledge.**
