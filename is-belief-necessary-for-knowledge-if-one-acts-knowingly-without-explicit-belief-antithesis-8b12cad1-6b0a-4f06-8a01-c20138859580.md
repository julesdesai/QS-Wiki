# Is belief necessary for knowledge if one acts knowingly without explicit belief?

**Terminal:** No

## Content

**Suppose someone navigates their home in the dark, seemingly “knowing” where everything is without actively thinking about it. Intuitively, we judge that they have knowledge of their environment.**, **There are instances where individuals demonstrate knowledge in their actions without holding explicit beliefs about every movement, questioning the necessity of belief for knowledge.**
