# Is perceived consistency affected by subjective interpretation?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 6efec100-96a4-4035-af95-cc2f2ede1c1d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Phenomenal Realism with Consistency Conditions](phenomenal-realism-with-consistency-conditions-synthesis-05f9226f-1221-4391-b2ae-790270c7a451.md)

## Content

**Consider the case where two different individuals perceive the same phenomenon under the same conditions but interpret it differently due to personal biases or cultural background. Intuitively, both individuals can be said to possess knowledge of the phenomenon despite the lack of perceived consistency.**, **It is implausible that objective phenomenal consistency across different persons' experiences is necessary for knowledge because individual differences in interpretation do not necessarily invalidate the truth of what is known.**
