# Can genetic luck challenge the causal theory?

**Node ID:** 6cd82a01-92dd-493f-95c0-e2a6b7f55a30

**Parent:** [Causal Theory of Knowledge](causal-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-dc61c008-27ed-4921-863e-badc9b961db6.md)

## Content

**Imagine a case where someone's genetic predisposition allows them to form a true belief without an understandable causative chain, intuitively we might regard this as mere luck and not knowledge.**, **There seems to be a missing explanatory element required for knowledge that cannot be provided solely by a causal story originating in genetic factors.**
