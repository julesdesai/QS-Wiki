**Parent:** [Can luck undermine knowledge derived from cognitive virtues?](can-luck-undermine-knowledge-derived-from-cognitive-virtues-antithesis-2433456f-e1e6-438d-af04-f3c2276c85c2.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief derives from their intellectual efforts and abilities rather than luck**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief derives from their intellectual efforts and abilities**
