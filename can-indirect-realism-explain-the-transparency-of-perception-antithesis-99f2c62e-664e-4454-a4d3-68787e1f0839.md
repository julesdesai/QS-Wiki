# Can indirect realism explain the transparency of perception?

## Content

**Perceptual transparency occurs when we are focused on the external world rather than our own mental states.**, **Indirect realism emphasizes mental representations, which suggests we should be more conscious of these internal states.**, **The transparency of perception indicates a disconnect between the focus on mental representations and how perception is typically experienced.**
