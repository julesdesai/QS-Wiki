## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person's belief in the proposition is formed through an intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person has an understanding of the relevant evidence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is true, the belief is formed through an intellectual virtue, and the person understands the relevant evidence**
