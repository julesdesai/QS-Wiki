# How does Indirect Realism handle the knowledge of abstract concepts?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d54fa4f7-0e66-4d13-ada7-caa7cfc97056
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Indirect Realism Summary](indirect-realism-summary-synthesis-1b3cd8c8-92a3-4a52-be84-8ec951a45683.md)

## Content

**Abstract concepts, like mathematical entities, lack a direct external object.**, **The principle holds that knowledge involves mental representations corresponding to external objects.**, **If abstract knowledge cannot depend on external objects, then indirect realism might struggle to explain how we know abstract concepts.**
