# How does contextualism address the problem of forgotten contexts and their influence?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9c7b5c54-569a-4f87-a441-771244027973
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualist Coherentism](contextualist-coherentism-synthesis-156207e7-3c9d-49a2-8bb7-7097c1d027cd.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person at time t1 believes information based on contextual coherence with surrounding beliefs.**, **Over time, at time t2, the person has forgotten the specific context at t1 but retains the belief.**, **It seems implausible that a belief remains justified without the contextual knowledge that originally justified it.**
