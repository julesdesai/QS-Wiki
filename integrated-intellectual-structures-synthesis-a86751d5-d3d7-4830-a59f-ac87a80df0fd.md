## Content

**For a group to know a proposition, it is necessary that they use integrated intellectual structures to form a belief about the proposition**, **For a group to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a group to know a proposition, it is necessary that integrated intellectual structures contribute to belief formation**, **For a group to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the group uses integrated intellectual structures to believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and integrated intellectual structures contribute to belief formation**
