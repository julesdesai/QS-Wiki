# Knowledge as Non-Doxastic Reliabilism

**Parent:** [Are beliefs arising from intellectual recklessness still knowledge?](are-beliefs-arising-from-intellectual-recklessness-still-knowledge-antithesis-51fd8f26-572f-4606-8a34-9d8115c5e7fe.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they are in a position to assent to the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief forming process is reliable**, **Reliability pertains to consistent correctness across similar propositions**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they are in a position to assent to the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief forming process is reliable**
