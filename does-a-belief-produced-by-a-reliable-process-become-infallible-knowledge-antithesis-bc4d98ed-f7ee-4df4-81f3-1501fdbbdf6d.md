# Does a belief produced by a reliable process become infallible knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** bc4d98ed-f7ee-4df4-81f3-1501fdbbdf6d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalist View](externalist-view-synthesis-75028f40-4ef7-4feb-a21d-1e6a75d787a6.md)

## Content

**Even reliable processes can occasionally produce false beliefs; thus, reliability does not imply infallibility. Intuitively, one might recognize a need for a stricter criterion for knowledge.**, **Infallibility is not inherent in reliable processes, so additional criteria may be necessary for knowledge.**
