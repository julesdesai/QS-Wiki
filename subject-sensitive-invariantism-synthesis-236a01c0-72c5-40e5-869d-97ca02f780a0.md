## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A person's practical interests can influence whether they know a proposition without changing the standards for knowing**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief aligns with their practical interests**
