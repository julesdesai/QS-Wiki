# Can knowledge be absent when a belief tracks truth causally disconnected from reality?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f78eb427-4cb0-42de-9545-7cf1e67bcfba
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Tracking Theory of Knowledge](tracking-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-34b45a3c-9960-48d7-bb61-82e72864432f.md)

## Content

**A situation could exist where a belief about a true proposition tracks the truth despite being formed by a process disconnected causally from the facts, challenging intuitions about knowledge.**, **In such cases, intuitively the belief appears not to be genuine knowledge because the truth-tracking connection is coincidental rather than informative.**
