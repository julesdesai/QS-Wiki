# How do we account for the subjective differences in perception if we perceive the world directly?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e5a1825a-7d6a-4dbd-8322-e65405fb1529
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Naïve Realism](naïve-realism-synthesis-25d0b7e3-6f66-46c3-a45d-bb41c1dbe70b.md)

## Content

**Suppose two people view the same sunset but describe it using different terms or perceive the colors differently due to variations in visual systems. Intuitively, we are prompted to conclude that perception is influenced by subjective or physiological factors, challenging the direct perception claim.**
