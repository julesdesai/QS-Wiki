# Knowledge as No-False-Lemma Justified True Belief

**Parent:** [Is external justification vulnerable to cases of misguided information processing?](is-external-justification-vulnerable-to-cases-of-misguided-information-processing-antithesis-343b05c3-7e08-45c0-bf18-8170633fb2a8.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification does not rely on any false lemmas**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification does not rely on any false lemmas**
