# Does self-doubt make process reliability insufficient for knowledge?

## Content

**Consider a case where a person doubts their reliable cognitive processes despite forming true beliefs, questioning their own memory or perception frequently. We intuitively recognize this self-doubt as indicative of a lack of knowledge.**, **Imagine someone systematically doubts their reliable process due to lack of confidence or misinformation about mental health issues. Intuitively, the fact of reliable belief doesn't compensate for the knowledge they doubt their own knowledge possession.**
