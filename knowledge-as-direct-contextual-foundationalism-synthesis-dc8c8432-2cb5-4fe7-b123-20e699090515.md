**Parent:** [Is inferential justification redundant if direct evidence suffices?](is-inferential-justification-redundant-if-direct-evidence-suffices-antithesis-39947c63-57e5-4a6b-924e-0068851479b1.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified via foundational beliefs derived from direct evidence**, **These foundational beliefs are validated by contextual factors, such as environment and background information**
