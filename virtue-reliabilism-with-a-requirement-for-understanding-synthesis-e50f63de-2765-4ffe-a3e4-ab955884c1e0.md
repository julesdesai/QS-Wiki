**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person exercises intellectual virtues in forming the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person’s belief is both true and formed through a reliable process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person has an understanding of the proposition and how it relates to other known propositions**, **Intellectual virtues include traits like open-mindedness and intellectual courage**
