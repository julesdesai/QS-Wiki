# Can a belief result from an intellectual virtue and still fail to be knowledge due to external factors?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** caee64a7-5faf-4bf3-af67-88d1af101c82
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-91237602-868c-4a49-88c0-4e168ec082b8.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where someone forms a belief through careful reasoning, an intellectual virtue, but the belief is false due to misleading evidence beyond their control.**, **This suggests that intellectual virtues alone may not suffice for knowledge, as external truth conditions must also be met.**
