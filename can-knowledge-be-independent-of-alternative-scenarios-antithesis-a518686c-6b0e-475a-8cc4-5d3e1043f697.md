# Can knowledge be independent of alternative scenarios?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a518686c-6b0e-475a-8cc4-5d3e1043f697
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Nozick's Tracking Theory](nozicks-tracking-theory-synthesis-f1663159-56f4-4f6f-b53a-d60dc9dbfcb7.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario involving a historical fact that is true and believed by a person who cannot conceive of it being false because there are no meaningful alternatives. Intuitively, we would judge that the person knows the historical fact without evaluating if belief would change under false conditions.**, **It is implausible to demand knowledge require considering alternative factual scenarios in situations where alternatives are inconceivable or irrelevant.**
