# Does relativism respect the intuitive distinction between belief and truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3bba47aa-cbd6-408a-a427-7b49e3c79347
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Relativism Summary](relativism-summary-synthesis-2665e075-79d4-4adb-9371-55cb5a5794f8.md)

## Content

**Consider a case in which a belief is well-supported but false within a certain framework. Intuitively, truth should be determined independently of belief in such cases.**, **It is implausible that the truth value of propositions is entirely determined by the acceptance within one's framework.**
