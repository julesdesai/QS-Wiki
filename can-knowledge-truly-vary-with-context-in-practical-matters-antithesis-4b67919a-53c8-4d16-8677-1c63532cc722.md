# Can knowledge truly vary with context in practical matters?

**Parent:** [Contextualism with Dispositional Belief](contextualism-with-dispositional-belief-synthesis-1dbb9d87-3561-45d5-9c36-839374bee7ec.md)

## Content

**Consider a chef in a kitchen who senses when a dish needs more salt without conscious deliberation based on contextual cues.**, **If their belief about the needed salt varies by different kitchen setups, it raises doubt about whether context allows for stable foundational knowledge that can be relied upon.**
