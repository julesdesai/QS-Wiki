# Is external reliability adequate for justifying knowledge?

## Content

**There can be a case where a person has a true belief caused by an external source, but they lack any informational clue or evidence of its reliability.**, **Intuitively, having no awareness or evidence of this reliability challenges the idea that the person actually knows the proposition.**
