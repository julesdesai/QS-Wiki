# Can perceptual error systematically undermine knowledge claims?

## Content

**Consider a scientist using an instrument calibrated incorrectly but unaware of the error, they've grounded their belief in this faulty perception which mirrors a direct connection. They have justified beliefs but not verifiable knowledge.**, **Systematic perceptual errors show that a reliable causal basis rather than mere perception itself may underpin knowledge claims.**
