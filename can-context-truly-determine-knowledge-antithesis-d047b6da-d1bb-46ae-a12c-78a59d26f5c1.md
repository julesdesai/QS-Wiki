# Can context truly determine knowledge?

**Node ID:** d047b6da-d1bb-46ae-a12c-78a59d26f5c1

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism Summary](contextualism-summary-synthesis-70123d28-f52a-42a2-8d85-6f92ac2f5bd1.md)

## Content

**In a scenario where a person is asked a question in a relaxed setting and then in a high-stakes environment, intuitively the person may still hold the same knowledge despite the change in stakes.**, **It is implausible that the same belief could be justified in one context but not in another solely based on situational factors.**
