# Does varying epistemic environments affect agency credit?

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which an individual accurately predicts weather events in an area with little known data. Intuitively, the same prediction in a data-rich environment calls for different credit due to the variance in information availability.**, **Credit may unjustifiably change based on the context rather than the agent's epistemic efforts.**
