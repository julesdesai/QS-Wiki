# Isn't idealism challenged by the occurrence of involuntary perceptions?

**Node ID:** b43504d5-13b1-41b8-9cdb-81233ab2aa08
**Depth:** 4

**Parent:** [Idealism](idealism-synthesis-6a8ea4b4-8cf6-44ff-98fb-cf08805f781d.md)

## Content

**Involuntary perceptions, such as hunger or pain, occur without conscious mental constructs or intention. Intuitively, these experiences suggest aspects of reality imposed on or experienced by the mind.**, **The occurrence of involuntary perceptions implies a form of reality not entirely dependent on mental constructs or perceptual decisions.**
