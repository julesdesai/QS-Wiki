# Knowledge as True Belief Plus Warrant

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is an additional warrant justifying the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have true belief plus warrant**
