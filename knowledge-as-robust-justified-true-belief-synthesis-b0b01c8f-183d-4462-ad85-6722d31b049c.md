## Content

**Knowledge is a justified true belief that is robust**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief is justified, true, and robust against counterexamples**
