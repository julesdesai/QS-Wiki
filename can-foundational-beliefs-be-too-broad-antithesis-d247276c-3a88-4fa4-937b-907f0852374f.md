# Can foundational beliefs be too broad?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d247276c-3a88-4fa4-937b-907f0852374f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Evolutionary Foundationalism](evolutionary-foundationalism-synthesis-4b200bf3-94a7-4a07-af03-f4d0ca848734.md)

## Content

**Consider the broad foundational belief that one must avoid danger. While true, it is not informative enough to guide specific actions or constitute knowledge about particular threats. Intuitively, foundational beliefs may lack the specificity needed for knowledge.**
