# Can a lack of intellectual meta-virtue lead to knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** aefdb424-486f-4d71-9086-53162dfcdb02
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Meta-Virtue Epistemology](meta-virtue-epistemology-synthesis-b3bd54fd-8ed8-46f5-92c6-aa5fa4222616.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a person uses reliable methods for belief formation without understanding why these methods work. Intuitively, either we have to say that this person knows things or that they do not. If we say they know, then meta-virtues are not necessary. If we say they do not know, then it contradicts the reliable belief formation.**
