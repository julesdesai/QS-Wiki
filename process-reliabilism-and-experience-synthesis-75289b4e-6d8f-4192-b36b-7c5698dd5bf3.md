**Parent:** [Can knowledge be purely instinctive?](can-knowledge-be-purely-instinctive-antithesis-c4e8e322-b6ab-454b-ad2e-988a730c30a6.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed by a reliable process**, **A process is reliable if, based on prior experience, it consistently produces true beliefs**
