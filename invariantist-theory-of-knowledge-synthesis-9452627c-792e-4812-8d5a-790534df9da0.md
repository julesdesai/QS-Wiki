## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the standards for knowledge are fixed and do not vary by context**, **The standards for knowledge apply uniformly across different contexts and subject matters**
