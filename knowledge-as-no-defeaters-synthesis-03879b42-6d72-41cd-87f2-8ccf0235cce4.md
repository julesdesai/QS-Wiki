# Knowledge as No Defeaters

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A belief is justified if there are no defeaters that the person should be aware of**, **The absence of defeaters for a true belief provides knowledge**
