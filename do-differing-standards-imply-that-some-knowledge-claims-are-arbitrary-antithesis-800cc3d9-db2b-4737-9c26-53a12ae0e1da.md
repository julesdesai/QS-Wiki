# Do differing standards imply that some knowledge claims are arbitrary?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 800cc3d9-db2b-4737-9c26-53a12ae0e1da
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Epistemic Pluralism](epistemic-pluralism-synthesis-81e4116f-f2b8-419c-97e5-1ca5c482c2fd.md)

## Content

**If standards can vary widely between different systems, some claims to knowledge could be made arbitrarily.**, **Arbitrary knowledge claims might lack sufficient grounding in evidence or reason, undermining their validity.**, **The legitimacy of knowledge claims could be sacrosanct only within a particular system, not universally.**
