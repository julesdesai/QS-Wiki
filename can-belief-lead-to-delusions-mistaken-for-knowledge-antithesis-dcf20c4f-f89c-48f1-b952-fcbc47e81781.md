# Can belief lead to delusions mistaken for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** dcf20c4f-f89c-48f1-b952-fcbc47e81781
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherent Infinitism](coherent-infinitism-synthesis-f2c99927-f862-4ee8-8b5b-2fefd2717ee2.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which someone vividly believes they communicate with aliens due to persuasive subjective experiences. Intuitively, despite their belief, this does not constitute knowledge in the absence of external validation.**, **It is implausible to classify mere belief, especially of contested or unverifiable claims, as equivalent to knowledge without robust justification.**
