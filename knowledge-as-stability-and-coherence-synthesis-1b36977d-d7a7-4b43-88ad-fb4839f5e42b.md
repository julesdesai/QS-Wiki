# Knowledge as Stability and Coherence

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Does coherence offer knowledge when future experiences invalidate current beliefs?](does-coherence-offer-knowledge-when-future-experiences-invalidate-current-beliefs-antithesis-495273ec-9ce5-42e2-b3fe-6e9c5b11317b.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres with an interconnected and stable system of beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the coherence is resistant to potential future evidence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief coheres with a stable system of interconnected beliefs, and the coherence is resistant to potential future evidence**
