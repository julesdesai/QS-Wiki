# Does cognitive bias undermine the reliabilist claim?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c431aa1d-2421-4c9a-a9dc-f0249b8238fd
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilist Theory of Knowledge](reliabilist-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-b27c6d4b-ac60-4e95-8268-38e201ff9a4c.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person forms a belief through a method believed to be reliable, but cognitive biases affect the belief formation process.**, **It is implausible that a method affected by cognitive biases can always be deemed reliable.**
