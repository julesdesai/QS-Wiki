## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is justified through social interaction and discourse**, **Social interaction and discourse are mechanisms that can distribute intellectual virtues across individuals**
