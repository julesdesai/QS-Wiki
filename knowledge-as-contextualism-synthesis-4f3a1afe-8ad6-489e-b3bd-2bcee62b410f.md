## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the truth conditions for knowing vary with the context of the attributor's assertion of knowledge**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the context-specific standards for knowledge are met**
