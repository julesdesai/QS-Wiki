# Could contextualism lead to epistemic relativism?

## Content

**Epistemic relativism is the view that truth and justification are relative to frameworks or perspectives.**, **Contextualism with dynamic standards implies that truth and belief justification depend on varying contexts.**, **Allowing context to govern truth and justification can lead to relativism by varying with personal or cultural perspectives.**
