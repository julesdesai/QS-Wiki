# Knowledge as Safety Plus Justification

**Parent:** [Is safety sufficient for knowledge given cases where safety stems from luck?](is-safety-sufficient-for-knowledge-given-cases-where-safety-stems-from-luck-antithesis-3009a5cd-0f85-4065-9bfa-06261baf97e0.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for their belief to be justified**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for their belief to not easily be false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, their belief is justified, and their belief is safe from being false**
