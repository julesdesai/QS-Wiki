## Content

**Knowledge is true belief arising from reflective intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief arises from reflective intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe it, the proposition is true, and their reflective intellectual virtue produces the belief**
