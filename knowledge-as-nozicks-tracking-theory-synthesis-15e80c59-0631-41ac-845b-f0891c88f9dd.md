# Knowledge as Nozick's Tracking Theory

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Does a person always have justification for true beliefs?](does-a-person-always-have-justification-for-true-beliefs-antithesis-0d02727b-165b-4bb9-a779-b4e42fe81f28.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A person is justified in believing a proposition if their belief tracks the truth across close possible worlds**, **Justification involves sensitivity to the truth such that, in nearby situations where the proposition is false, the person would not believe it**
