# Can knowledge depend on the stakes for an individual?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a403f769-073c-4f85-acb9-cdc230c735cd
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Subject-Sensitive Invariantism](subject-sensitive-invariantism-synthesis-2eef6ba9-ea95-4aea-9a96-5c60688dde0e.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person, Alex, is faced with a choice to invest a small or large amount of money based on the belief that a company will succeed. The stakes of the decision affect Alex's epistemic evaluation. Intuitively, it seems uncomfortable to say that Alex's knowledge of the company's success changes because of the stake size.**, **If knowledge depends on personal stakes, it potentially makes knowledge too subjective, undermining the objective nature that is generally attributed to knowledge.**
