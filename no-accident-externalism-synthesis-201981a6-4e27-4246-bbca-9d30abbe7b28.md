# No Accident Externalism

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can a true belief be considered knowledge if it results from a deceptive scenario?](can-a-true-belief-be-considered-knowledge-if-it-results-from-a-deceptive-scenario-antithesis-9ad5c176-b81e-4849-9b3e-d3f1837ca67d.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their true belief is not accidentally true**, **A true belief is not accidentally true if it arises through a belief-forming process that could not easily have produced a false belief**
