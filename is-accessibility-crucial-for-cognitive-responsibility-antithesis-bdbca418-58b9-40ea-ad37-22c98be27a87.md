# Is accessibility crucial for cognitive responsibility?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** bdbca418-58b9-40ea-ad37-22c98be27a87
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-a1d061ab-4ce5-4544-95e5-a72c751db8d0.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person can never access their own reasons or justification for their beliefs and acts purely on conviction.**, **It is plausible to consider that without accessibility to justification, the individual lacks cognitive responsibility or control, which could threaten the claim of knowledge.**
