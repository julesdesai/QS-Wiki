# Knowledge as a Hybrid of Intellectual Ability and External Reliabilism

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief results from intellectual virtue or a reliable belief-forming process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief results from intellectual virtue or a reliable belief-forming process**
