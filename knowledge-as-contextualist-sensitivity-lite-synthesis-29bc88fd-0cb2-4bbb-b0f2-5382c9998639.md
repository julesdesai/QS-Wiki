## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in the context, the person would not believe the proposition were it false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and in the context, the person would not believe the proposition if it were false**
