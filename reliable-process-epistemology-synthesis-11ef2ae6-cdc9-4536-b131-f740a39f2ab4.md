## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed through cognitive processes that are generally reliable**, **Reliability of cognitive processes does not require advanced intellectual virtues**
