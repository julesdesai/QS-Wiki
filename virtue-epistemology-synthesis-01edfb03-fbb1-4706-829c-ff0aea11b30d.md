## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from an intellectual virtue or skill**, **The intellectual virtue links the belief to the truth**
