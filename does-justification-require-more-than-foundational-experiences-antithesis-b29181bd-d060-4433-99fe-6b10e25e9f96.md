# Does justification require more than foundational experiences?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b29181bd-d060-4433-99fe-6b10e25e9f96
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Experiential Foundationalism](experiential-foundationalism-synthesis-fd2d94ab-5595-4b29-a3f8-2962ee0c4e3c.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that all justified beliefs rest solely on direct experiential foundational beliefs.**, **Consider a scenario where a person believes a complex scientific theory based on foundational experiences and expert testimony. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge their belief is justified even if it doesn't rest solely on foundational experiences.**
