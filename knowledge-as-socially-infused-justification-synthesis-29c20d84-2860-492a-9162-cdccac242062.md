# Knowledge as Socially Infused Justification

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 29c20d84-2860-492a-9162-cdccac242062
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does intellectual virtue always guarantee truth?](does-intellectual-virtue-always-guarantee-truth-antithesis-764d80ad-f845-4d95-8c4e-b46d6ed3961e.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed through socially supported ways of knowing**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is formed through socially supported ways of knowing**
