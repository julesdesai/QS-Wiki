# Does contextualism lead to cultural relativism in knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 4d3db403-3722-4d1f-a073-a3b59f2d6c93
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualist Theory of Knowledge](contextualist-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-56747199-fb08-45b9-8206-960c0717486b.md)

## Content

**Cultural relativism suggests knowledge varies between cultures based on differing contexts.**, **If each culture's context defines knowledge, universal claims become culturally bound.**, **It is implausible for knowledge to be fully subject to cultural variability.**
