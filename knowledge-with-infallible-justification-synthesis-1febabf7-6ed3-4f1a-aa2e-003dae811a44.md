## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have infallible justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have an infallible belief that the proposition is true**
