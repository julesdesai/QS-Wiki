# Does the Tracking Theory account for counterfactual skepticism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 65add613-06a2-47e5-ab4b-cdc628e6fe15
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Nozick's Tracking Theory of Knowledge](nozicks-tracking-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-3fc68b12-ca29-40d7-8341-51e40d71c5e5.md)

## Content

**Consider the belief that the sun will rise tomorrow, a belief held true by astronomical evidence. Intuitively, we attribute knowledge to such natural certainties.**, **If contrary facts (like an astronomical catastrophe) emerged, the person's belief would change due to lack of prior evidence.**, **In slightly different conditions, such as minor shifts in Earth's rotation, the belief remains due to robustness against small changes in physical understanding.**
