# How does Representational Realism handle the phenomenon of inattentional blindness?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 28838942-ff2a-4994-b0b8-137f43b92603
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Representational Realism](representational-realism-synthesis-2653bf9f-214b-48a1-b757-9a49097d2459.md)

## Content

**Inattentional blindness occurs when a person fails to perceive an unexpected stimulus in plain view due to attention focused elsewhere.**, **The unregistered stimulus suggests no mental representation is formed, despite the objective presence of the stimulus.**, **If knowledge depends on forming accurate mental representations, this phenomenon challenges the completeness of perceptual knowledge.**
