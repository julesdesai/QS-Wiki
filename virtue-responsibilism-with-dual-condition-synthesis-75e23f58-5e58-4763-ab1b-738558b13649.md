## Content

**Knowledge is true belief that arises from intellectual virtue which is reliable and responsibly aligned with cognitive goals**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is responsibly aligned with cognitive goals**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe it, the proposition is true, and their intellectual virtue aligns with cognitive goals**
