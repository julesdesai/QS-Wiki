# Does practical success guarantee knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 097da921-7355-44d5-8ae1-e7e7936e8d5a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-4f2fdbe9-b5ae-4003-9ce0-28573a7cd0c4.md)

## Content
