## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person has no false beliefs relevant to the proposition**, **The belief in the proposition should not be surrounded by relevant false beliefs**
