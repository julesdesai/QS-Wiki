# Knowledge as a Justified True Belief with No Defeaters

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 5f069086-23e0-4009-bafc-34600fba8c99
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is the connection between truth and belief too weak?](is-the-connection-between-truth-and-belief-too-weak-antithesis-ed76e4d0-5936-4cb1-80c4-1d1fa69fbc05.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no defeaters for the person’s justification**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the person believes the proposition, it is true, the person has justification for believing it, and there are no defeaters for the person’s justification**
