## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for their belief to be justified**, **The degree of justification required does not change with context, but may vary based on the subject's circumstances**
