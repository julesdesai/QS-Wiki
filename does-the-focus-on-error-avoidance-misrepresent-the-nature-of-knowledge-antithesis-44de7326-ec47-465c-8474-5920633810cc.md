# Does the focus on error avoidance misrepresent the nature of knowledge?

**Node ID:** 44de7326-ec47-465c-8474-5920633810cc
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

## Content

**Consider a case in which avoiding error does not contribute to understanding or comprehension. In such a case, intuitively we would judge that mere error avoidance is insufficient for genuine knowledge.**, **There is a plausible principle that knowledge involves a positive grasp of truth, not just avoiding falsehoods, suggesting mere error avoidance is inadequate.**
