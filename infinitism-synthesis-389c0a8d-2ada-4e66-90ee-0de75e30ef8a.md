## Content

**Beliefs can be justified by an infinite chain of non-repeating reasons**, **There is no foundational belief that halts the need for further justification**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have an infinite chain of justification for the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, the proposition must be true**
