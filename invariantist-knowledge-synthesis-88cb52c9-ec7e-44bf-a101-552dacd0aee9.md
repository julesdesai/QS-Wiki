## Content

**Knowledge does not depend on the context of the knower**, **A person knows a proposition if the proposition is true**, **The standards for knowing a proposition are fixed and do not vary across different contexts**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have evidence supporting the proposition**, **The amount of evidence needed for justification is constant and not context-dependent**
