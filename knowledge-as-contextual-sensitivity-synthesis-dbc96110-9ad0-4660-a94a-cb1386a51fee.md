# Knowledge as Contextual Sensitivity

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does sensitivity accommodate deeply entrenched cultural beliefs?](does-sensitivity-accommodate-deeply-entrenched-cultural-beliefs-antithesis-2476263b-99ea-4c99-a88b-5953081a4054.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it, given the relevant context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it, given the relevant context**
