# Reliabilist Addendum to Coherentism

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification involves a coherent set of beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed through a reliable process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief fits coherently within a system of beliefs, and the belief is formed through a reliable process**
