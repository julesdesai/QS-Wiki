## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed through the exercise of intellectual virtue**, **Intellectual virtue can entail an understanding of the reliability of the process without personal verification**
