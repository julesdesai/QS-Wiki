**Parent:** [Is infinite justification reconcilable with epistemic responsibility?](is-infinite-justification-reconcilable-with-epistemic-responsibility-antithesis-c623f98a-c361-414e-8ebc-9c851264cb56.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification involves an infinite chain of reasons that are pragmatically accessible**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there exists a pragmatically accessible infinite chain of reasons justifying the belief**
