# Does direct perception require prior knowledge?

**Node ID:** 6f759ece-12c8-4ada-b20b-a321e7b6ecb1

**Parent:** [Critical Direct Realism](critical-direct-realism-synthesis-3a3a192c-9b76-44ad-9069-1225c99b4079.md)

## Content

**In some instances, understanding a proposition may require prior knowledge or context that is not available through direct perception alone.**, **Direct perception does not account for the necessity of background knowledge in interpreting and understanding complex propositions.**, **A person may perceive an object yet fail to know it due to a lack of necessary contextual understanding.**
