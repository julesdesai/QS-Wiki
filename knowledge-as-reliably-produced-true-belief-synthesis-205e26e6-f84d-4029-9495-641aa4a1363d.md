# Knowledge as Reliably Produced True Belief

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 205e26e6-f84d-4029-9495-641aa4a1363d
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is requiring no false assumptions too restrictive?](is-requiring-no-false-assumptions-too-restrictive-antithesis-f0490ef5-c4c8-4e9d-8132-c127640f3589.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is produced by a reliable method**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is produced by a reliable method**
