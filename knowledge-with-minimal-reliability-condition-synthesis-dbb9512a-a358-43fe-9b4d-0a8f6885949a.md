# Knowledge with Minimal Reliability Condition

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** dbb9512a-a358-43fe-9b4d-0a8f6885949a
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is the sufficiency condition too demanding?](is-the-sufficiency-condition-too-demanding-antithesis-285d118a-2730-4998-851e-17d70ca92afa.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that belief formation uses a minimally reliable process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and belief formation uses a minimally reliable process**
