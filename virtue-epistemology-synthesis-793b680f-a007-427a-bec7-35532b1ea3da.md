## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have a true belief arising from intellectual virtue which aims at truth**
