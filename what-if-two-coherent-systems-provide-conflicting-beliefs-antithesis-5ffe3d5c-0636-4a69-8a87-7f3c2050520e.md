# What if two coherent systems provide conflicting beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5ffe3d5c-0636-4a69-8a87-7f3c2050520e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitist Coherentism](infinitist-coherentism-synthesis-a30e3b29-79f2-4d20-a375-1e170fe170c4.md)

## Content

**Two separate systems of beliefs can each be coherent while supporting contradictory claims.**, **Coherence alone does not guarantee truth, as coherent systems might internally justify false beliefs.**, **Without foundational beliefs, there is no objective standard to resolve contradictions between different coherent systems.**
