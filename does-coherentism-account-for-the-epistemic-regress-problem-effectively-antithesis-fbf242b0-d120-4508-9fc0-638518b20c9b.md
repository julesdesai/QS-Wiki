# Does coherentism account for the epistemic regress problem effectively?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** fbf242b0-d120-4508-9fc0-638518b20c9b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism in Epistemology](coherentism-in-epistemology-synthesis-03124e37-be18-4868-b2cf-e370b5186105.md)

## Content

**Justification within coherentism arises from the mutual support of beliefs within the system.**, **In theory, this could lead to an infinite regress, requiring an endless chain of supporting beliefs.**, **It is implausible that justification can be maintained with an infinite regress, suggesting a need for some foundational beliefs or stopping point.**
