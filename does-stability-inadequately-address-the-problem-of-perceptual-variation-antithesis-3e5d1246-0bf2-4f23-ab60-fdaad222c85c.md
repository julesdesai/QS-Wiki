# Does stability inadequately address the problem of perceptual variation?

## Content

**Imagine a musician who consistently perceives middle C but measures pitch variation due to a detuning instrument across performances. Intuitively, stability is insufficient for knowledge in domains where perceptual variation naturally occurs and must be corrected for.**, **It is plausible that perceptual variation can introduce errors that stability alone cannot account for, necessitating additional criteria for ensuring knowledge.**
