## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in nearly all nearby possible worlds where the person continues to believe the proposition, the proposition remains true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is safe in the relevant manner**
