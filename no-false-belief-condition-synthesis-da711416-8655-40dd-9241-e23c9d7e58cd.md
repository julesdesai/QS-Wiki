# No-False-Belief Condition

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** da711416-8655-40dd-9241-e23c9d7e58cd
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is intellectual virtue always necessary for knowledge?](is-intellectual-virtue-always-necessary-for-knowledge-antithesis-c2509a3c-081d-47fb-9b14-6d67b23e9ba9.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person's belief system contains no false beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief system contains no false beliefs**
