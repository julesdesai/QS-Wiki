## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that pragmatic factors influence the standard of justification**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and meet the justification standard influenced by pragmatic factors**
