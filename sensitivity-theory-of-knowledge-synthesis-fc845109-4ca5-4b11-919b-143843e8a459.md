## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **If the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **Knowledge requires belief to be sensitive to the truth of the proposition**
