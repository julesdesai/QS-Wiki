# Reliabilism emphasizes reliable belief-forming processes

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** daf7f30c-325c-420c-83c4-e87c6f2b8bf0
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does having a true proposition guarantee knowledge?](does-having-a-true-proposition-guarantee-knowledge-antithesis-d5c13129-b0c9-4491-8e2b-1a919a121fa1.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **A reliable cognitive process is one that generally produces true beliefs**
