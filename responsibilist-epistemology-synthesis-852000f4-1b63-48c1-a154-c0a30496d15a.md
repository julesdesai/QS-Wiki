# Responsibilist Epistemology

**Parent:** [Is it an issue if a belief is deemed knowledge despite lacking appropriate internal justification?](is-it-an-issue-if-a-belief-is-deemed-knowledge-despite-lacking-appropriate-internal-justification-antithesis-b5e83fd6-fc3a-441f-9fa8-94a7096f17fd.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed through the exercise of intellectual responsibility**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is formed through the exercise of intellectual responsibility**
