# Does coherentism lead to circular justification?

**Node ID:** 78aeed25-b398-488c-aff0-7ad10713863d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

## Content

**In a system of beliefs where each belief is justified by another, justification could potentially become circular.**, **Circularity in justification undermines the notion of independent support for beliefs.**, **A coherent set could unintentionally support beliefs through such circularity.**
