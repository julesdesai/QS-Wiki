# Could practical interests unjustifiably shift knowledge standards?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d86ff107-aeef-4364-92a9-be41cd47bebe
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Subject-Sensitive Invariantism](subject-sensitive-invariantism-synthesis-687e35ad-b9eb-49bd-a730-d8bc9269ea3a.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that temporary and situational practical interests could justifiably affect the stable nature of knowledge.**, **Suppose that knowledge standards can shift based on practical relevance. This contradicts the idea that knowledge should be objective and stable.**
