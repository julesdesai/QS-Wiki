# Can sensory perception errors undermine sensitivity?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ac51f0d8-6f8d-42c8-84b5-70775de6d2b9
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Sensitivity Theory](sensitivity-theory-synthesis-68f75ad1-a4e1-40d8-ae80-33e70d924463.md)

## Content

**Suppose there’s a case where a person sees a red object and thus believes it is red. When the lighting changes, the object would appear differently, but they continue to believe it is red based solely on initial perception. Intuitively, knowledge seems undermined by the failure to adjust beliefs with changes in perception.**
