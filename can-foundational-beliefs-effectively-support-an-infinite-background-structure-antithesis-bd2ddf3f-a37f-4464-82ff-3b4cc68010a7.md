# Can foundational beliefs effectively support an infinite background structure?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** bd2ddf3f-a37f-4464-82ff-3b4cc68010a7
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundationalism with Infinite Background](foundationalism-with-infinite-background-synthesis-b6351c4d-7dab-447a-b3c7-52e8418919cd.md)

## Content

**Foundational beliefs are supposed to be basic and self-justified.**, **An infinite background structure supporting these beliefs implies that they are justified by additional reasons.**, **If foundational beliefs require further justification, they are not truly foundational and undermine the foundationalist structure.**
