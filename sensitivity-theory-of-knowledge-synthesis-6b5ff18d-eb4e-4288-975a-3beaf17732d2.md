## Content

**Knowledge is a true belief that changes in response to the truth**, **A belief is sensitive if, in the closest possible world where it is false, the person does not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it must be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, they must believe it**, **A belief is justified if it is sensitive**, **To know, a person's belief must not hold if the proposition were false**
