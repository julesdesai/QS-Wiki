# Is it possible to have a reliable process producing mostly false beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e7a13aef-a270-415e-937c-0b138663b8a5
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism](reliabilism-synthesis-f6f2f982-fa0f-404e-a509-24417604aa1e.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person's process for forming beliefs is reliable in an unusual setting but produces predominantly false beliefs in most other settings.**, **A process being reliable in specific settings but unreliable in others challenges the notion that it universally produces knowledge.**
