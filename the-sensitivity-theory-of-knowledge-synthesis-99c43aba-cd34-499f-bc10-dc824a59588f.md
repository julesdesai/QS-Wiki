## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person does not rely on chance**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that these sensitivity conditions hold**
