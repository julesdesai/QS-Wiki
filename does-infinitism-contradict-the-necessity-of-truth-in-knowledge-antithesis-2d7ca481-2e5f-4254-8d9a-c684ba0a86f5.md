# Does infinitism contradict the necessity of truth in knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2d7ca481-2e5f-4254-8d9a-c684ba0a86f5
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism in Epistemic Justification](infinitism-in-epistemic-justification-synthesis-7e8bc411-aa66-4d88-bdc9-2fb4f4857304.md)

## Content

**If a person’s belief must be true for them to have knowledge, an infinite chain of reasons does not directly ensure the truth of the final belief.**, **An infinite regress of reasons without a definitive connection to the truth does not sufficiently ground the belief in reality.**
