# Knowledge as Multi-Valued Reliability

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Should reliability accommodate diverse epistemic values beyond truth?](should-reliability-accommodate-diverse-epistemic-values-beyond-truth-antithesis-e9759d9f-a275-4c27-8f8e-5240e6d8391b.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is the result of a process that is reliable across multiple epistemic values such as speed and depth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is the result of a process that is reliable across multiple epistemic values**
