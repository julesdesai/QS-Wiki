# Knowledge as Innate Truth-Tracking

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Does reliabilism ignore the value problem of knowledge?](does-reliabilism-ignore-the-value-problem-of-knowledge-antithesis-3c8a8533-63ba-49a7-8171-5d00b9a7f5c4.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A belief qualifies as knowledge if the cognitive process generating it tracks the truth in a more demanding way than reliability alone**, **Truth-tracking involves a process whereby the belief would not have been held if the proposition were false**
