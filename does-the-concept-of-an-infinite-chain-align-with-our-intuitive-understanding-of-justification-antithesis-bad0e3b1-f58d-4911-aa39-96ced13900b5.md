# Does the concept of an infinite chain align with our intuitive understanding of justification?

**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism with An Emphasis on the Chain of Justifications](infinitism-with-an-emphasis-on-the-chain-of-justifications-synthesis-fbcbcc46-9003-42db-8878-9fc62d8799d4.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person can only ever add more unknown reasons to support their knowledge claim. Intuitively, we would judge this does not improve their justified belief.**, **Intelligent reasoning suggests that additions to our chain of justifications should enhance clarity and understanding. This contradicts the idea of infinite chains which remain conceptually unclear.**
