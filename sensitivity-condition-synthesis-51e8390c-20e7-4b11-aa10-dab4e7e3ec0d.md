# Sensitivity Condition

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that, if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **A belief is sensitive if it fails when falsity obtains**
