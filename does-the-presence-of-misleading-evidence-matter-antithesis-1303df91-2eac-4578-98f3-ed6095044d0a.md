# Does the presence of misleading evidence matter?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1303df91-2eac-4578-98f3-ed6095044d0a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Tracking Theory of Knowledge](tracking-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-c5c60481-0513-43f2-8ed6-61505f94790c.md)

## Content

**Consider a case in which a person has true belief based on selective evidence, ignoring contrary misleading evidence.**, **The existence of misleading evidence challenging the belief would imply unreliable tracking.**, **Intuitively, knowledge appears undermined if it rests on disregarded or unexamined evidence.**
