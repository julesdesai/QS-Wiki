# Can justifying standards change without changing knowledge status?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2ef7d058-10a2-4c79-a40b-c9f30d24deea
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Moderate Contextualism Summary](moderate-contextualism-summary-synthesis-54895291-30c8-42cf-8322-baea34575bd1.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where the standards of justification are raised due to unexpected scrutiny, yet the original evidence remains strong and convincing. Intuitively, the knower's status should not alter just due to shifting standards.**, **Suppose at a relaxed standard, a shallow investigation suffices, but under intense scrutiny, a thorough investigation is required. In both cases, the proposition remains true, intuitively suggesting that knowledge does not fluctuate with standards.**
