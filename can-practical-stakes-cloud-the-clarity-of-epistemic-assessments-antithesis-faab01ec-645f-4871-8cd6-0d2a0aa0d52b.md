# Can practical stakes cloud the clarity of epistemic assessments?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** faab01ec-645f-4871-8cd6-0d2a0aa0d52b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Subject-Sensitive Invariantism](subject-sensitive-invariantism-synthesis-a38d2af6-3ec4-421c-a558-c594d0cbc8b7.md)

## Content

**High stakes may influence emotional states, such as fear or anxiety, leading to impaired assessment of what one knows. Intuitively, knowledge should depend on clear and objective evaluation rather than fluctuate with emotion-driven stake assessment.**, **It is plausible that emotional responses to stakes obscure true epistemic conditions, challenging the claim that stakes determine knowledge.**
