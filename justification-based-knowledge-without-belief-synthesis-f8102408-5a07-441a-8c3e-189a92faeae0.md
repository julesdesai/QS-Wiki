## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, optional belief in it enhances the plausibility of knowledge**, **A person knows a proposition if it is true, they have justification for it, and they optionally believe it if it aligns with their justified evidence**
