# What if the chain of reasons includes faulty or false beliefs?

**Node ID:** d01620f9-ec15-4c5d-b73c-833e4a63abb6

## Content

**In an endless chain, the inclusion of a false belief would intuitively contaminate and invalidate the justification of subsequent beliefs.**, **A principle of epistemic purity suggests a justified belief cannot rely on false premises, so endless chains risk justifying beliefs on faulty grounds.**
