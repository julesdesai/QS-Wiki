## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their justification does not depend on any false beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their true belief is based on no false grounds**
