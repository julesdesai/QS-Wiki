# Knowledge as Safety

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 721622e3-7a62-4fda-9362-9d89157e4cbe
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does sufficient evidence always yield true knowledge?](does-sufficient-evidence-always-yield-true-knowledge-antithesis-1c42cbe3-4108-43dc-a20a-42492f3ec6b9.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the person were to believe the proposition, it would not easily have been false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, it is true, and it would not easily have been false if believed**
