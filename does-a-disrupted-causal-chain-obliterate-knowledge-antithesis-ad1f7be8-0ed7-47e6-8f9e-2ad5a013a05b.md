# Does a disrupted causal chain obliterate knowledge?

## Content

**Suppose a news interruption distorts a broadcast about a natural disaster, yet a person deduces a true outcome about the disaster's impact from fragmented information.**, **Intuitively, the disruption challenges a continuous causal chain yet the belief remains true, complicating the concept of knowledge.**
