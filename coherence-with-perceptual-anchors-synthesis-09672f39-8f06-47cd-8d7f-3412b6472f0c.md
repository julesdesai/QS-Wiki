## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For perceptual beliefs, it is necessary to be justified by direct perceptual inputs serving as anchors**, **For a person to know a non-perceptual proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and it coheres with a network of interrelated justified beliefs**
