# Is subjective cognitive construction applicable to shared objective knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ef5c4f85-e3f5-47ce-84cd-0cf062f19518
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Constructivism](constructivism-synthesis-c5cc0372-1259-4a9f-927f-8a96c3c904c0.md)

## Content

**Scientific knowledge is often considered objective and shared across different observers.**, **This objective nature seems to conflict with constructivist claims that knowledge is subjectively constructed.**, **If knowledge is primarily subjective, it is unclear how multiple individuals can share and agree upon the same scientific truths.**
