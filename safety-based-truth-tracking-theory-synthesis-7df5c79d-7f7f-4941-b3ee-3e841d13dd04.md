## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief is safe**, **A belief is safe if it could not easily have been false in similar situations**, **Knowledge of an entailed proposition follows if the initial proposition is safely believed and the entailment is recognized**
