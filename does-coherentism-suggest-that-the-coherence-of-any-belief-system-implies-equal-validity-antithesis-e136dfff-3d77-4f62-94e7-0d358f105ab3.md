# Does coherentism suggest that the coherence of any belief system implies equal validity?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e136dfff-3d77-4f62-94e7-0d358f105ab3
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism](coherentism-synthesis-56ae39cf-eba6-4a99-aaa4-0dbe8a5aac85.md)

## Content

**If coherence is the only measure, all coherent belief systems might appear equally valid regardless of underlying truth.**, **Equating validity with coherence alone disregards the need for alignment with objective reality.**, **In coherentism, the idea that all coherent systems are considered valid clashes with the notion of objective truth.**
