# Is reliabilism sufficient for knowledge in cases of belief without reflection?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** fda54539-a0ff-4e17-901a-76be0bff7c96
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism](reliabilism-synthesis-5d1929f3-45fe-4f02-bb7b-941a1c5017f0.md)

## Content

**Suppose someone forms a belief through a reliable process without awareness of the process, such as credence based on statistical correlation. Intuitively, without reflection or understanding, the person lacks genuine knowledge.**, **It is implausible that a belief formed without reflection or understanding constitutes knowledge, even if produced reliably.**
