# Does process reliabilism adequately accommodate the Gettier problem?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a10168f9-027f-4453-95fb-286ece6696f1
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Process Reliabilism](process-reliabilism-synthesis-0b742bab-1646-43b8-9078-24258a3aaaf7.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person, Sam, forms a belief that a barn exists while driving in a region full of fake barns, and his belief happens to be about the only real barn in that area. Intuitively, we would judge that Sam does not truly know there is a barn, despite having a justified true belief.**, **The belief was formed through a reliable process, as Sam’s visual perception generally produces true beliefs.**, **This situation challenges the notion that knowledge is simply a belief formed through a reliable process.**
