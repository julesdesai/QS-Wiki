# Can a belief be falsely reinforced by truth-tracking over numerous possible worlds?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f7e4e453-4683-4ee0-a385-3a289cad7fba
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Knowledge as Truth-Tracking](knowledge-as-truth-tracking-synthesis-2c16302d-4d7f-4dee-a7ac-cd2c9d6b658d.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where a false belief is repeatedly reinforced due to a misleading consideration of nearby possible worlds. Intuitively, this seems incompatible with knowledge acquisition.**, **Reinforcement of falsehoods through partial truth-tracking undermines the idea that such tracking is sufficient for knowledge.**
