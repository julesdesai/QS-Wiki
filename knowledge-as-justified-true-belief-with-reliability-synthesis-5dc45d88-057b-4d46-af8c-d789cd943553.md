# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with Reliability

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Can justified false beliefs undermine the condition of objective justification?](can-justified-false-beliefs-undermine-the-condition-of-objective-justification-antithesis-015676cb-e51f-4c39-add0-dc2daa16bc84.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, the proposition is true and the belief-forming process is reliable**
