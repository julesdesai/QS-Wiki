# Practical and Environmental Reliabilism

**Parent:** [Can non-cognitive functions fulfill the role of knowing?](can-non-cognitive-functions-fulfill-the-role-of-knowing-antithesis-93d81bac-f2e8-4bc8-bbc6-423ab7bba502.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief has practical consequences or usefulness**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person's environment reliably causes true beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief has practical consequences or usefulness, and their environment reliably causes true beliefs**
