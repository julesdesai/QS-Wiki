## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified**, **Contextual factors such as environment and background information can contribute to justification, but are not required if justification is achieved through direct intuition**
