# Do intellectual virtues always lead to knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3c5a9360-1cd7-444f-936a-7469b98f1398
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology with Reliance on Intellectual Virtues](virtue-epistemology-with-reliance-on-intellectual-virtues-synthesis-e0dc7230-c9bb-46c6-a298-46d61cd678d8.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person has well-formed intellectual virtues but falls into systematic error due to misinformation. In such a scenario, it seems they fail to acquire knowledge despite their virtues.**, **There is a plausible principle that possessing intellectual virtues does not guarantee avoidance of all epistemic errors, which challenges the direct link between virtues and knowledge.**
