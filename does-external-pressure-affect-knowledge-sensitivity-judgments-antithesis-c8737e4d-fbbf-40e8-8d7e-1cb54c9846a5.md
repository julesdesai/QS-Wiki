# Does external pressure affect knowledge sensitivity judgments?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c8737e4d-fbbf-40e8-8d7e-1cb54c9846a5
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Sensitivity Theory of Knowledge](sensitivity-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-102d682a-8eb5-4983-bf10-f287ee1765f9.md)

## Content

**Imagine a well-informed journalist who believes a politician is corrupt based on evidence, yet would still believe it under government pressure to do so. However, this seems like knowledge due to the strong basis of evidence.**, **The sensitivity model fails to accommodate beliefs influenced by external rather than evidential factors.**
