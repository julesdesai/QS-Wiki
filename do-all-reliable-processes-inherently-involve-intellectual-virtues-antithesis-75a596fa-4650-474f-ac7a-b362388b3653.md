# Do all reliable processes inherently involve intellectual virtues?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 75a596fa-4650-474f-ac7a-b362388b3653
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Mixed Virtue Epistemology](mixed-virtue-epistemology-synthesis-9b1d3c06-dd01-4be5-8e49-871c85befc1a.md)

## Content

**Suppose a case where a machine-perfect method produces true beliefs, such as a highly advanced AI giving predictions. Intuitively, the operator using these results still might not have knowledge if no intellectual contribution is involved.**, **It is questionable whether the reliability of purely mechanistic processes ought to be equated with intellectual virtues necessary for knowledge, prompting a reevaluation of what contributes to virtue.**
