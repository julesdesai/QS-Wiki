# Knowledge as Virtue Epistemology

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief to be produced by intellectual virtues of the person**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is produced by the intellectual virtues of the person**
