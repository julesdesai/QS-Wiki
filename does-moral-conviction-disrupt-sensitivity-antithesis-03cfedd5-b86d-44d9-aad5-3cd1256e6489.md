# Does moral conviction disrupt sensitivity?

**Parent:** [Sensitive belief theory of knowledge](sensitive-belief-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-a04ea135-b51c-4804-8a79-c6b4af4a4910.md)

## Content

**Consider a person who holds a moral principle and believes it true while another person equally believes the opposite moral principle true. If neither would alter their belief under different circumstances, intuitively, neither person's moral belief satisfies sensitivity claims of knowing.**, **Stable personal conviction in moral beliefs, irrespective of actual truth or change, reveals a complexity in aligning moral knowledge with sensitivity conditions.**
