## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification that meets a fixed moderate standard across contexts**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and they meet a fixed moderate standard of justification**
