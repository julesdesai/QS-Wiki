# Can we truly align mental frameworks with reality?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** cfadf6d2-f52a-4f43-9a83-daeb40df9252
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Idealism](idealism-synthesis-53ec409c-4a9b-4261-be64-9dda9f92eb94.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a person believes their mental framework aligns perfectly with reality, but they are delusional. Intuitively, we either have to say that they know the reality or are mistaken. If we say they know, then illusions are equivalent to reality. If we say they are mistaken, then mental alignment is insufficient for knowledge.**
