## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they exercise cognitive skills, such as reasoning and observation, effectively**, **Cognitive skills must be reliable regardless of the environment**
