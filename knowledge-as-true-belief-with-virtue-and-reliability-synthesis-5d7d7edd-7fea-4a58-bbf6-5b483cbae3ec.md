# Knowledge as True Belief with Virtue and Reliability

## Content

**Knowledge is true belief arising from a combination of intellectual virtue and reliability**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief is both virtuously formed and produced by a reliable process**, **A belief is not knowledge if its truth is due to luck, even with some intellectual virtue**, **A person knows a proposition if they believe it and the combination of virtue and reliability eliminates luck in the belief's truth**
