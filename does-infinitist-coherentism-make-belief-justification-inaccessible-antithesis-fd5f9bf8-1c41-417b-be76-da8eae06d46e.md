# Does Infinitist Coherentism make belief justification inaccessible?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** fd5f9bf8-1c41-417b-be76-da8eae06d46e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitist Coherentism](infinitist-coherentism-synthesis-2a4efbd0-0f1f-4a01-bfa6-1ecf016eb1e0.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person attempts to justify their belief by tracing an infinite chain of reasons. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that practical belief justification becomes inaccessible and never complete.**, **A situation where belief justification cannot be accessed or completed negates the practical value of having justified beliefs.**
