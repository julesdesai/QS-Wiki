# Knowledge as Reliably Formed True Belief

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 0cc97f6c-c10d-42c2-8570-705707b5be1d
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does knowledge require more than just intellectual virtue?](does-knowledge-require-more-than-just-intellectual-virtue-antithesis-644ab88f-c33f-45c9-8aad-39933b7713d2.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief be produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process, and the proposition is true**
