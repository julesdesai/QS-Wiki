# Does contextualism lead to skepticism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** eff91f85-f97c-4f9e-afb0-ee074580486f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism](contextualism-synthesis-ed8715f4-7106-486f-8992-736e64ae52d2.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which standards for knowledge are set extremely high in all applicable contexts. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that nobody can ever know anything, which seems overly skeptical.**, **It is implausible that knowledge could be determined by arbitrarily high contextual standards making all knowledge claims fail.**
