# Can belief alone confer knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7dbc1306-fa72-485e-bad5-fb5411be0eee
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism Without Memory Constraints](infinitism-without-memory-constraints-synthesis-df5a4028-0f5d-4102-8a53-7b53596c5281.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a person sincerely believes a false proposition due to misinformation. Intuitively, one would not claim the person knows the proposition.**, **There is a plausible principle that knowledge requires factual accuracy. This principle together with belief suggests that belief alone is insufficient for knowledge.**
