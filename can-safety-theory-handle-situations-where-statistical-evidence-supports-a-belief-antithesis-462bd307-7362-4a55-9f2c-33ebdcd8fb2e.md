# Can Safety Theory handle situations where statistical evidence supports a belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 462bd307-7362-4a55-9f2c-33ebdcd8fb2e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Safety Theory](safety-theory-synthesis-2fb86082-7528-4d1d-a3e0-8c751a94ae5c.md)

## Content

**Consider a person who believes a proposition based on high statistical probability, like believing a lottery ticket will lose**, **Even though the belief is true in most possible worlds, and hence safe, we intuitively do not say the person knows the ticket will lose while it remains uncertain**
