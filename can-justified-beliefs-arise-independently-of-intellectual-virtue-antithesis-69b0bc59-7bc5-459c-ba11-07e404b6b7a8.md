# Can justified beliefs arise independently of intellectual virtue?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 69b0bc59-7bc5-459c-ba11-07e404b6b7a8
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-ffa55650-0795-4012-9f33-14517d86090e.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where a true belief is formed through strong evidence and reasoning, yet without reliance on specific cognitive character traits.**, **Intuitively, this belief seems justified even if it does not arise from an intellectual virtue.**, **If justification requires intellectual virtue, many beliefs formed through evidence and reasoning might be deemed unjustified.**
