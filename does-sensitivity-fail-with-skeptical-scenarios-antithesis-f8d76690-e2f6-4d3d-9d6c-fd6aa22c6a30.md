# Does sensitivity fail with skeptical scenarios?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f8d76690-e2f6-4d3d-9d6c-fd6aa22c6a30
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Sensitivity Theory](sensitivity-theory-synthesis-c636dae2-8d6f-478b-827f-65895cd50362.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person believes the external world exists based on perceptual evidence.**, **In a skeptical scenario where the external world does not exist, the person would still believe it does due to deception, like in a brain-in-a-vat scenario.**, **Since the belief does not track the truth in this nearby possible world, sensitivity implies it is not knowledge, opposing our common sense understanding of perceptual knowledge.**
