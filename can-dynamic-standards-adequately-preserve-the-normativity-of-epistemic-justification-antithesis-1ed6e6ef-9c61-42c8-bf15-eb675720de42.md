# Can dynamic standards adequately preserve the normativity of epistemic justification?

**Node ID:** 1ed6e6ef-9c61-42c8-bf15-eb675720de42

## Content

**Normativity in epistemology refers to the rightness or wrongness of belief justification.**, **Dynamic standards fluctuate with context, potentially leading to varying evaluations of justification.**, **If normativity fluctuates, it becomes difficult to consistently assess the strength of justification.**
