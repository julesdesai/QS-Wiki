# Does circularity threaten the foundation of process reliabilism?

**Parent:** [Process Reliabilism](process-reliabilism-synthesis-094c2ddc-bd9c-46c2-87b1-e486aaff8cb4.md)

## Content

**A cognitive process is described as reliable if it produces more true beliefs than false ones. However, judging its reliability requires a prior standard, which assumes knowledge of truth and error.**, **Suppose we must assess a process without access to whether its output beliefs are true or false. Intuitively, such assessment would lack grounding if based solely on the process itself.**
