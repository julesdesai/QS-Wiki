# Knowledge as No-Defeater Justified True Belief

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Does coherence dilute the truth requirement in knowledge?](does-coherence-dilute-the-truth-requirement-in-knowledge-antithesis-adcf73da-b31e-4ae6-aa9e-e1cc65cb4e80.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no known defeaters that undermine the justification**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there are no known defeaters that undermine the justification**
