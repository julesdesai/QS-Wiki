# How does contextualism handle situations of conflicting contexts?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** bde36622-12fc-4d3b-a324-da1bf8533279
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualist Theory of Knowledge](contextualist-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-56747199-fb08-45b9-8206-960c0717486b.md)

## Content

**Conflicting contexts can arise when different standards are applied by different groups or individuals.**, **If conflicting contexts provide contradictory knowledge claims, determining which context prevails becomes problematic.**, **It is implausible that knowledge could be disrupted by mere shifts in perspective.**
