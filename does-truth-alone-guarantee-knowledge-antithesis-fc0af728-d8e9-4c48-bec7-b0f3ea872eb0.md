# Does truth alone guarantee knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** fc0af728-d8e9-4c48-bec7-b0f3ea872eb0
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatism about Knowledge](pragmatism-about-knowledge-synthesis-d6358f85-abbd-4b14-8f12-af989e14cf1c.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a person believes a proposition that is true but has no justification for that belief, like winning a random lottery by guessing the numbers. Intuitively, we would judge that a true belief without justification does not amount to knowledge.**, **There is a plausible principle that knowledge requires justification or support beyond mere truth. This principle suggests that truth alone does not guarantee knowledge.**
