# Does knowing require understanding?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 6924797f-f714-41c4-8f46-f34b44026bb8
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Disjunctivism](disjunctivism-synthesis-90a3baa8-9102-43d8-b9cc-d77493986c7c.md)

## Content

**Imagine a student who has memorized the multiplication table but does not understand why 2 times 2 is 4. We might hesitate to say they truly know the answer.**, **If knowledge requires understanding, then the lack of understanding undermines the assumption that purely believing results from a genuine connection to reality.**
