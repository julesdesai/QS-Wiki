# How does lack of evidence for alternative scenarios challenge sensitivity?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2d2e8649-3836-4bc3-9fd3-37d7cc93a267
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Sensitivity Theory](sensitivity-theory-synthesis-68f75ad1-a4e1-40d8-ae80-33e70d924463.md)

## Content

**A person believes a proposition about the weather based on today's forecast. If the forecast were wrong, and they had no access to other sources, they would still believe it. Intuitively, knowledge seems absent when counterfactuals involve no evidence reassessment.**
