## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief results from intellectual virtue**, **Intellectual virtue involves reliable cognitive processes**, **Intellectual virtue requires the intellectual character of the believer to influence reliably forming the belief**
