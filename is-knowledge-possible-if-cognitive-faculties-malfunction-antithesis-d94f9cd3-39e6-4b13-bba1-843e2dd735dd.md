# Is knowledge possible if cognitive faculties malfunction?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d94f9cd3-39e6-4b13-bba1-843e2dd735dd
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Proper Function Theory](proper-function-theory-synthesis-7d89ec66-0836-44e3-8613-d999cc659dfb.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person's cognitive faculties are malfunctioning due to a temporary illness. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that the person could still form a true belief.**, **It is plausible that a person can still hold knowledge even if their cognitive faculties are temporarily malfunctioning.**
