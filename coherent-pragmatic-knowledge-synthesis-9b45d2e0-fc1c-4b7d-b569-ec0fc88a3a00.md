## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have an attitude towards the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the attitude coheres with the person's other beliefs and actions**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have an attitude towards the proposition, the proposition is true, and the attitude coheres with the person's other beliefs and actions**
