## Content

**For a belief to constitute knowledge, it is necessary that the belief is true**, **For a belief to constitute knowledge, it is necessary that there is an appropriate causal connection between the truth of the proposition and the belief in it**, **An appropriate causal connection involves the fact of the matter causing the belief**
