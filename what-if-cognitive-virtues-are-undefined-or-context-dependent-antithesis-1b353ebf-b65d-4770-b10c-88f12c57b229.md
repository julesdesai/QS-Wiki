# What if cognitive virtues are undefined or context-dependent?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1b353ebf-b65d-4770-b10c-88f12c57b229
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-51f9ed1e-8cbb-4e95-8090-3e1317ce9636.md)

## Content

**The concept of a cognitive virtue may lack a clear and universally applicable definition.**, **A case in which the same behavior is considered a virtue in one context but not in another can undermine the notion of consistent intellectual traits.**, **Ambiguity in defining 'cognitive virtue' can lead to uncertainty about what constitutes knowledge.**
