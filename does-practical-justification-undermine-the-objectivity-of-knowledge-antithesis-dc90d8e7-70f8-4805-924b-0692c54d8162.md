# Does practical justification undermine the objectivity of knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** dc90d8e7-70f8-4805-924b-0692c54d8162
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Theory of Justification](pragmatic-theory-of-justification-synthesis-cd0f39d2-c604-4de4-bb81-1900aa8a1a8f.md)

## Content

**An instance where a belief is justified by personal practical success, but not by objective evidence, raises concerns about the subjectivity of knowledge.**, **Suppose a person uses a belief that was justified by successfully winning a game, the lack of universal applicability of this justification questions the reliability of practical considerations for knowledge.**
