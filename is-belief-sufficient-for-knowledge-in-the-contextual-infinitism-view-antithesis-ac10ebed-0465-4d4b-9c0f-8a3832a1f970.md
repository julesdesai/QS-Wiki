# Is belief sufficient for knowledge in the contextual infinitism view?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ac10ebed-0465-4d4b-9c0f-8a3832a1f970
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextual Infinitism](contextual-infinitism-synthesis-969d15cb-7035-4449-8e21-a18b5558491f.md)

## Content

**A person can believe a proposition yet lack sufficient justification or truth, which are traditionally required components for knowledge**, **It is implausible that belief alone should suffice for knowledge since belief can be based on false or unjustified grounds**, **Consider a case where someone believes a proposition purely by chance, without any supporting evidence or true background, demonstrating that belief itself is not enough for knowledge**
