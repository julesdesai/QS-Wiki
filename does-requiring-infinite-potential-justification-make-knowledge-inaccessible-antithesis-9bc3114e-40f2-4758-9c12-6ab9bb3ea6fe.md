# Does requiring infinite potential justification make knowledge inaccessible?

**Parent:** [Finite Infinitism Summary](finite-infinitism-summary-synthesis-cf1727db-af05-4f36-9758-13f464b5dd32.md)

## Content

**If knowledge requires potentially infinite justification, it seems that few can ever achieve knowledge, as human efforts to justify beliefs wouldn't attempt infinite length.**, **An accessible concept of knowledge must account for human capabilities. This implies adaptability to finite structures of belief and justification.**
