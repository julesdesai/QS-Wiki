## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **It is necessary that were the proposition false, the person would not believe it**, **It is necessary that the belief corresponds to the truth in nearby possible worlds**
