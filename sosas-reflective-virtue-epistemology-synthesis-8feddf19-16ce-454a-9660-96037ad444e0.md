## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief arises from an intellectual virtue or competence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is conscious reflection or awareness of reliance on intellectual virtues**
