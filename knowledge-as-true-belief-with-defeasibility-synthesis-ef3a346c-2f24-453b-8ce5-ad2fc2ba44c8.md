# Knowledge as True Belief with Defeasibility

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** ef3a346c-2f24-453b-8ce5-ad2fc2ba44c8
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does social support ensure justification?](does-social-support-ensure-justification-antithesis-3768c889-a9b2-4d81-91a8-caa25942daf5.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that no relevant defeaters undermine the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and no relevant defeaters undermine the belief**
