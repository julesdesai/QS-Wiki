# Can reliabilism account for cases of environmental luck?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 831e43ed-aec1-4877-9d47-7a2506e632bb
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism](reliabilism-synthesis-5d1929f3-45fe-4f02-bb7b-941a1c5017f0.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person forms a belief in a truth-conducive environment, but unknown changes make the environment misleading, though an external factor makes the belief true. Intuitively, the belief should not qualify as knowledge due to environmental deception.**, **A reliable process requires stable environments, so a process influenced by environmental luck lacks reliability.**
