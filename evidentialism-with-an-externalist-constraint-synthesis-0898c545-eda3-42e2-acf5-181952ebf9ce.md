## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification based on evidence for the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the circumstances of the belief formation are reliable**
