## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed by the intellectual virtues of the believer**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is formed by intellectual virtues**
