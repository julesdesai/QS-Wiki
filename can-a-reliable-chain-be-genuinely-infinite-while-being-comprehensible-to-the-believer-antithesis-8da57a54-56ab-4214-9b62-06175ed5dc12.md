# Can a reliable chain be genuinely infinite while being comprehensible to the believer?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 8da57a54-56ab-4214-9b62-06175ed5dc12
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilist Infinitism](reliabilist-infinitism-synthesis-31746681-ada4-44ae-aa39-47d03ad8abbb.md)

## Content

**Justification for a belief involves an infinite chain of reliable belief-forming processes.**, **For justification to be meaningful, it must be comprehensible and assessable by the believer, which conflicts with the concept of an infinite chain.**
