## Content

**A person knows a proposition if their belief tracks the truth across possible situations**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **If the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **If the proposition were true, the person would believe it**
