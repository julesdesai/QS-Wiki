# Is functionalism too permissive about what constitutes knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis

**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Functionalism](functionalism-synthesis-2d0849d8-6d8d-49c5-822f-95e70d5e82e6.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that knowledge requires some degree of truth sensitivity, not merely functional correspondence. This principle suggests that functionalism might mistakenly grant knowledge status to cases where mental states functionally align with reality purely by chance.**
