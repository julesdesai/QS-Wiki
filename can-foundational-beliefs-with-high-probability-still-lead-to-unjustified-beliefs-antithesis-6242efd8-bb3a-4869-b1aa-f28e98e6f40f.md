# Can foundational beliefs with high probability still lead to unjustified beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 6242efd8-bb3a-4869-b1aa-f28e98e6f40f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Probabilistic Foundationalism](probabilistic-foundationalism-synthesis-4f173a54-3943-4ddb-a2cd-a6bb34479be2.md)

## Content

**There are scenarios in which a belief based on high-probability foundational beliefs might still be unjustified, such as when crucial evidence has been overlooked or dismissed.**, **Intuitively, if key evidence is ignored, the resulting belief should not be considered justified, irrespective of the probability of the foundational beliefs.**
