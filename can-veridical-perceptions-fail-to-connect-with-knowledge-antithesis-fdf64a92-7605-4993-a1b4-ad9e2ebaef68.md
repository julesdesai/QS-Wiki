# Can veridical perceptions fail to connect with knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** fdf64a92-7605-4993-a1b4-ad9e2ebaef68
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Disjunctivism Summary](disjunctivism-summary-synthesis-e9744a1e-a377-4989-9c30-b08459f11a98.md)

## Content

**Consider a person who has a veridical perceptual experience that matches reality but lacks the concept or ability to recognize what they are perceiving. Intuitively, they would not acquire knowledge of the proposition related to their experience.**, **A veridical perceptual experience that does not lead to knowledge challenges the idea that such an experience inherently provides direct knowledge of reality.**
