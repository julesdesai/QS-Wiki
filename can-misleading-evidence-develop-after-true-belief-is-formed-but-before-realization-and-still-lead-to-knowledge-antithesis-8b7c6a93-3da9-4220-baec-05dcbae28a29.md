# Can misleading evidence develop after true belief is formed but before realization, and still lead to knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 8b7c6a93-3da9-4220-baec-05dcbae28a29
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Defeasibility Theory](defeasibility-theory-synthesis-f4ee4d58-3624-4a6a-b28c-5ecaca4ef4ed.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person believes a proposition to be true based on strong evidence and later receives misleading evidence that makes them abandon their belief.**, **Intuitively, we might judge that the presence of misleading evidence does not convert true belief into knowledge if the belief is abandoned, even if initial belief was accurate.**
