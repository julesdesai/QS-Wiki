## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person can rule out all relevant alternatives to the proposition**, **Relevant alternatives are determined by the situation, not the evaluative context**
