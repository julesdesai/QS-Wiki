# Knowledge as Non-Accidental Reliability

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 3162f6f9-36ba-4d5d-abf8-6469fc1bf3a5
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is reliability alone sufficient for knowledge?](is-reliability-alone-sufficient-for-knowledge-antithesis-39939579-96f9-405e-9cf0-8dec2d5a4bb2.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the truth of the belief is not accidental relative to the cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the truth of the belief is not accidental relative to the cognitive process**
