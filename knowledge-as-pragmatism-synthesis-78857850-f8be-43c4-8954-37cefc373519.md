## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief leads to successful practical outcomes**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is true and leads to successful practical actions**
