# Contextualist Reliabilism

**Parent:** [Does reliability ensure truth?](does-reliability-ensure-truth-antithesis-5e191f2f-3d3a-4c96-a0e3-3889ee29b787.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from a reliable cognitive process appropriate to the context**, **The level of reliability required for knowledge depends on the context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief arises from a reliable cognitive process appropriate to the context**
