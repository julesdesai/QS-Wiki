# Knowledge as Critical Virtue Epistemology

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Does reflective endorsement always coincide with knowledge?](does-reflective-endorsement-always-coincide-with-knowledge-antithesis-5c5b4ad4-8a4d-44f9-95d0-f30cf55b7dab.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief to be produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the person to critically engage with and evaluate the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process, and they critically engage with and evaluate the belief**
