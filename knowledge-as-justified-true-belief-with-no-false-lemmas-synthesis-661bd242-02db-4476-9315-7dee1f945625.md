# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with No False Lemmas

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person has justification that includes no false steps**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their justification contains no false steps**
