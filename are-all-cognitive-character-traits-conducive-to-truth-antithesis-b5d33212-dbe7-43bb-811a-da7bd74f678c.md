# Are all cognitive character traits conducive to truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b5d33212-dbe7-43bb-811a-da7bd74f678c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-ffa55650-0795-4012-9f33-14517d86090e.md)

## Content

**Certain cognitive traits, while virtues, may lead to partiality or distortion, such as loyalty in assessing a friend's ability.**, **These traits might be reliable in social contexts but not in producing truth.**, **If such traits are considered intellectual virtues, they could mislead rather than enlighten.**
