# Can beliefs be safe but still lack justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** cd07d585-3e1a-440e-b296-222297ff5b30
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Safety Condition View](safety-condition-view-synthesis-739cc2e2-5111-4640-9af6-f9324f258620.md)

## Content

**A person randomly guesses an answer on a multiple-choice test and the answer happens to be correct. Intuitively, this case does not constitute knowledge due to lack of justification.**, **In nearby possible worlds where their random guess was incorrect, the belief would still be safe since the answer options remain consistent, yet lacks the justification component of knowledge.**
