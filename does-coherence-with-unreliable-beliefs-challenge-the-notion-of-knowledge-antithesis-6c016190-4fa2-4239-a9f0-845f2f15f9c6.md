# Does coherence with unreliable beliefs challenge the notion of knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 6c016190-4fa2-4239-a9f0-845f2f15f9c6
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentist Reliability Summary](coherentist-reliability-summary-synthesis-49034568-193c-4a88-8ae7-b062de898be5.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person believes a proposition that fits consistently within their belief system, yet many of their other beliefs are based on unreliable sources, like rumors or fantasies. Intuitively, this person's belief does not constitute knowledge.**
