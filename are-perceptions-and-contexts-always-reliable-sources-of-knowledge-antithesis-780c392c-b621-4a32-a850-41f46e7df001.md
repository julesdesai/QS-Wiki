# Are perceptions and contexts always reliable sources of knowledge?

**Parent:** [Contextual Phenomenalism](contextual-phenomenalism-synthesis-7d8a3858-3738-47ab-85b6-1a71b73ecaf5.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a mirage is perceived as an oasis; intuitively, the knowledge claim based on this perception is false.**, **There is a plausible principle that perceptions can often be deceptive and therefore cannot always be trusted as foundations of knowledge.**, **A case where misleading contexts lead to incorrect interpretations suggests that context fails to guarantee reliable knowledge.**
