# Knowledge as No False Lemmas

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge exist without justification in some cases?](can-knowledge-exist-without-justification-in-some-cases-antithesis-aae4a280-8a2f-4153-b5d7-f49a57506e74.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have a justified true belief and no part of the justification relies on a false lemma**
