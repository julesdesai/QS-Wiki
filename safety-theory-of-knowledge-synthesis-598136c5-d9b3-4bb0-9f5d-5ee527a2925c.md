## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief would not have been formed if the proposition were false**, **A belief is safe if in nearby possible worlds where the person forms the belief in the same way, the belief is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is true and safe**
