# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with No Defeaters

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 7991b5fd-e06d-4205-8e15-5e4368980dd4
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does sensitivity apply to historical knowledge?](does-sensitivity-apply-to-historical-knowledge-antithesis-c9db9915-fb78-495a-ba15-be6c1c43c7b5.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no true propositions that, if believed, would defeat their justification**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, the proposition is true, and there are no true propositions that, if believed, would defeat their justification**
