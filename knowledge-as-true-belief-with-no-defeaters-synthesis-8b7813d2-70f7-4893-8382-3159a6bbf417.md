# Knowledge as True Belief with No Defeaters

**Parent:** [Does causal connection ensure knowledge in scenarios of accidental truths?](does-causal-connection-ensure-knowledge-in-scenarios-of-accidental-truths-antithesis-dfce8bf9-20b8-4b39-80c0-eca07c8ff49c.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no defeaters for the person's belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there are no defeaters for the person's belief**
