# Can knowledge exist without intellectual virtue?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5557c3e5-f80f-49a7-a90f-7d7cc67dd99b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-d7a894af-29e5-42fe-8e5e-957e2009566d.md)

## Content

**Suppose a child, without any developed intellectual virtues, correctly identifies an object as a chair, simply because they were told so moments before.**, **This implies that knowledge can exist even in the absence of intellectual virtues, challenging their necessity.**
