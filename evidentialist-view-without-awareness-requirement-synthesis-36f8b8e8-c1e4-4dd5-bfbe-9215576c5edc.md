# Evidentialist View without Awareness Requirement

**Parent:** [Is it possible to know something without being aware of having supporting evidence?](is-it-possible-to-know-something-without-being-aware-of-having-supporting-evidence-antithesis-32117718-ecf5-4a38-b134-00a0efbaa59b.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is evidence supporting the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is not necessary that they are aware of having the evidence**
