## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief tracks truth across close possible worlds**, **In close possible worlds where the proposition is false, the person would not believe the proposition**
