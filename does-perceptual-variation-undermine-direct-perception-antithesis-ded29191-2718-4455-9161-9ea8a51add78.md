# Does perceptual variation undermine direct perception?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ded29191-2718-4455-9161-9ea8a51add78
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Naïve Realism Summary](naïve-realism-summary-synthesis-ad05d8f4-c9d2-4024-8001-bf520360cf02.md)

## Content

**Perceptual variation involves changes in how objects are perceived based on context, such as temperature affecting how one perceives water as hot or cold.**, **If perception is direct awareness of objects, then these variations should not occur in perception without corresponding variations in the objects themselves.**, **Perceptual variation implies perception is not merely direct but influenced by contextual factors.**
