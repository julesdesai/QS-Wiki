## Content

**Memory can alter or affect perceptions by blending past experiences with present ones, leading to a distorted perception of an event.**, **Since memory is imperfect, relying on it for critical evaluation of perceptions introduces potential inaccuracies.**, **This possibility raises concerns about the reliability of direct realism when memories influence perceptions.**
