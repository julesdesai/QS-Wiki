## Content

**Truth is not essential for the display of intellectual virtue**, **An intellectual virtue is a commendable character trait that enhances belief formation**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **The exercise of intellectual virtue can be valuable independently of the truth of a belief**, **A person's belief can result from intellectual virtues even if the belief is false**
