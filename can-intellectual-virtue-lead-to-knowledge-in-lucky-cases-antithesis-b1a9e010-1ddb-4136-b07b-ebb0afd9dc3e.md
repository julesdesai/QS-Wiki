# Can intellectual virtue lead to knowledge in lucky cases?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b1a9e010-1ddb-4136-b07b-ebb0afd9dc3e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-ffa55650-0795-4012-9f33-14517d86090e.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person gains a true belief through guesswork, which requires no intellectual virtue.**, **Intuitively, this scenario does not match the conceptual understanding of knowledge as justified true belief.**, **If intellectual virtue leads to knowledge even in lucky cases, it might grant knowledge to beliefs gained through mere guessing.**
