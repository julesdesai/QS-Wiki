# What about cases where knowledge arises from testimony without direct perceptual experience or memory support?

**Node ID:** d6855ee9-2729-463e-95c4-ec820c167217

## Content

**Individuals often gain knowledge via testimony when they lack perception or direct memories of events**, **Consider someone knowing about a distant event through news articles or reports, without any personal perceptual experience involved**, **Relying on perception and memory alone would challenge the credibility of testimonial knowledge in such cases**
