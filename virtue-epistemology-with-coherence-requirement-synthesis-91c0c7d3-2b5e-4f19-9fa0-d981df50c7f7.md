## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is coherent with other beliefs held**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief arises from an intellectual virtue or competence and is coherent**
