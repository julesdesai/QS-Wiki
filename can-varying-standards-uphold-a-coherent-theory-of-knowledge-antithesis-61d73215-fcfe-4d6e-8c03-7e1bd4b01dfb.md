**Knowledge depends on the context of the knower, which leads to varying standards, potentially challenging the coherence of a unified theory of knowledge.**, **Consider scenarios where different contexts lead to contradictions about whether someone knows a proposition. Intuitively, a coherent theory should avoid such contradictions, suggesting an issue with varying standards.**
