**Parent:** [Do unforeseen defeaters nullify the sufficiency of justified true belief?](do-unforeseen-defeaters-nullify-the-sufficiency-of-justified-true-belief-antithesis-a24247c1-c5c4-46ba-9d12-ad5e8333a4af.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is safe**, **A belief is safe if in nearby possible worlds where the person forms the belief in the same way, it remains true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief is true and safe**
