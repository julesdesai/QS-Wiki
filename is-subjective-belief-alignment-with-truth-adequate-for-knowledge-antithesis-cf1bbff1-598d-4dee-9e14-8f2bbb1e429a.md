# Is subjective belief alignment with truth adequate for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** cf1bbff1-598d-4dee-9e14-8f2bbb1e429a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundational Infinitism](foundational-infinitism-synthesis-8fb4ffc2-abd6-406c-b0e9-89dab8b3bfd6.md)

## Content

**Aligning belief with truth is essential for knowledge, yet infinite justification chains do not ensure alignment with truth reliably.**, **A person might perceive an infinite sequence of reasons as valid but still arrive at a false belief, casting doubt on truth being a byproduct of infinite chains.**, **Infinite chains may include truths at every step, but they do not necessarily culminate in renowned truths without some finite verification.**
