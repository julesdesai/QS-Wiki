# Epistemic Dependence on Reliable Sources

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Is testimony a counterexample to intellectual virtue?](is-testimony-a-counterexample-to-intellectual-virtue-antithesis-b10c09e6-76d3-44d1-8045-92325b67b71f.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief to be based on either intellectual virtues or epistemic reliance on reliable sources**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is based on either intellectual virtues or epistemic reliance on reliable sources**
