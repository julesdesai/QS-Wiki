## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief in the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, then they would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have a true belief that is sensitive to the truth**
