## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is part of a coherent system of beliefs**, **A coherent system is one where beliefs mutually support each other**
