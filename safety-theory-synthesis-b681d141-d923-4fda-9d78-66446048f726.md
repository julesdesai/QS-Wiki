## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief could not easily have been false**, **A belief is safe if in most nearby possible worlds where the person believes the proposition, it is true**
