**Parent:** [Is it possible for someone to know a false proposition?](is-it-possible-for-someone-to-know-a-false-proposition-antithesis-37e65bd4-6aae-4c7a-af06-a1a2a1336008.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a proposition to be known, the person could not easily have believed it if it were false**
