# Does the theory overlook the role of external verification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0a266025-6320-44c6-8518-203350fd2bf9
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Credit Theory of Knowledge](credit-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-193f50ad-c71a-4e26-bf9b-21d5bbf78043.md)

## Content

**Consider a role where independent verification is essential for a belief to be knowledge due to external evidence evaluation, as in scientific experimentation. The individual's cognitive attempt may be inadequate without external methods confirming the result.**, **It is implausible that knowledge always relies solely on cognitive credit without considering independent verification as part of forming true belief.**
