# Is there a conflict between usefulness and truth in perception?

**Parent:** [Perceptual Pragmatism Summary](perceptual-pragmatism-summary-synthesis-6450a6b1-86fa-4b81-99b4-df7ae7bc6aea.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person finds the idea of luck as a guiding factor in life useful and actionable. Intuitively, we would judge that this perception does not constitute true knowledge about how life decisions should be made.**, **There is a plausible principle that a perception can be useful yet not true. This principle raises questions about how truth is determined when different perceptions guide actions.**
