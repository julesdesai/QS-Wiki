# Knowledge as Reflective Externalism

**Parent:** [Does externalism overlook inferential responsibility?](does-externalism-overlook-inferential-responsibility-antithesis-7659a82a-9e81-43c9-8435-f71605ef0bbd.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification relies on reliable external factors assessed through conscious reflection**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification involves reliable external factors assessed through conscious reflection**
