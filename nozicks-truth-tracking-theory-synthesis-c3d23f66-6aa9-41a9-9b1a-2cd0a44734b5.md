## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief would not change when the truth value of the proposition remains the same**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief would change when the truth value of the proposition changes**
