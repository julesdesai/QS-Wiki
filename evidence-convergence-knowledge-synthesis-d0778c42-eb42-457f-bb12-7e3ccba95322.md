## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they converge towards belief as the evidence strengthens**, **A person gains knowledge as justified evidence increasingly aligns with potential belief in a true proposition**
