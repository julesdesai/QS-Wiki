## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that justification involves coherence with foundational empirical beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and it fits within a coherent and foundationally grounded system of beliefs**
