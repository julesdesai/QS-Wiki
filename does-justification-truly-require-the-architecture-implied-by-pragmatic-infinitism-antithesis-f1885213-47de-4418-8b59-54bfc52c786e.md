# Does justification truly require the architecture implied by pragmatic infinitism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f1885213-47de-4418-8b59-54bfc52c786e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Infinitism](pragmatic-infinitism-synthesis-f73c0505-1eaa-4768-8143-11a2f8c9ca92.md)

## Content

**The requirement for an infinite regression of justification conflicts with typical instances where individuals feel justified through immediate and contextually grounded reasoning.**, **Consider someone justifying their belief in a scientific fact based on trust in the scientific community, without infinite justifications.**, **In this situation, finite justification suffices for practical knowledge, contradicting the infinite chain requirement.**, **Real-life justification rarely reflects the infinite regress that pragmatic infinitism suggests.**
