# Evidential Justification Adequacy

**Parent:** [Does contextualism overlook the role of evidence in knowledge?](does-contextualism-overlook-the-role-of-evidence-in-knowledge-antithesis-b7ede8be-7526-4bd5-af99-399c93ea704d.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that justification reaches an adequate level of evidential support**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there is adequate evidential justification**
