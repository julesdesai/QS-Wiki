# Can justification be subjective?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ec6054e5-2874-4476-a62b-ca0f6061ab02
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism with Pragmatic Constraint](infinitism-with-pragmatic-constraint-synthesis-44356aca-8ef0-4341-a1b2-865cb5949b96.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where two individuals have different reasons for belief due to personal perspectives and experiences. Intuitively, justification of a belief may vary subjectively between individuals.**, **It is implausible that justification can be entirely objective given the diversity of individual experiences and reasoning processes.**
