# How does cognitive penetration affect perception?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 21105b81-3f50-4e5e-bff9-631840734049
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Intentionalism about Perception](intentionalism-about-perception-synthesis-61a99e6e-ccdd-4f9c-a128-c382d15947b5.md)

## Content

**Perception can be influenced by one's beliefs, desires, or background knowledge, a phenomenon known as cognitive penetration.**, **If perception is solely about presenting properties of objects, it should not change based on cognitive states.**, **Cognitive penetration suggests that perceptual content is not entirely determined by the external world.**
