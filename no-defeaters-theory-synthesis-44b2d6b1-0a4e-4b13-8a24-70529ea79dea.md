## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no defeaters for their belief**, **A defeater is any truth that would invalidate the justification for the belief if the person were aware of it**
