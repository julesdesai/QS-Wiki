## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the standards for knowledge are met universally**, **The standards for knowledge do not vary depending on the conversational context or stakes involved**
