# Can the truth-tracking requirement be too demanding for everyday knowledge claims?

## Content

**In a case where a person believes a bridge is safe because it has never collapsed, but still may collapse due to unforeseen stress, the belief doesn’t track the truth across all stress scenarios.**, **It is implausible for everyday safety knowledge claims to meet such an extensive truth-tracking demand.**
