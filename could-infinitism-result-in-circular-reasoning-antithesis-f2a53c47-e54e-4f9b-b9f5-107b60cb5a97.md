# Could Infinitism result in circular reasoning?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f2a53c47-e54e-4f9b-b9f5-107b60cb5a97
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism](infinitism-synthesis-db00e7c1-6a13-4ca5-8f09-6fae7cd29e0f.md)

## Content

**An infinite chain of reasons, if not well-defined, risks repeating the same reasons. It is implausible that infinite chains lacking novelty or distinctiveness genuinely contribute to justification.**, **Consider a case where reasons circle back to earlier points in the chain. Intuitively, this circularity undermines the claim to genuine justification.**
