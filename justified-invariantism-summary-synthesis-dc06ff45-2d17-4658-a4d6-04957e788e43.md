## Content

**Knowledge is independent of the context of the knower**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **The standards for knowing a proposition are fixed and do not vary with different contexts**
