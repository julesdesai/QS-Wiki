## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have evidence for the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their true belief arises from adequate evidence**
