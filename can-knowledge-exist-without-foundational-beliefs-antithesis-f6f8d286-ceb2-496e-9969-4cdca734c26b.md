# Can knowledge exist without foundational beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f6f8d286-ceb2-496e-9969-4cdca734c26b

**Parent:** [Structural Foundationalism](structural-foundationalism-synthesis-037c32e2-5bf4-4b6c-88a6-0e0659b44b9e.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a person knows a proposition through experiential learning without any foundational beliefs. Intuitively, we must say that the person possesses knowledge.**, **It is implausible that knowledge necessarily springs from foundational beliefs.**
