# How can slight differences in context significantly affect knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 32dd3f58-ce03-4d6c-be65-11802df49432
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Moderate Contextualism](moderate-contextualism-synthesis-a2419722-fa3b-4f65-af9b-d94e8c32837e.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a minimal change in context, such as a person's mood, affects their apparent knowledge of a proposition. Intuitively, it seems implausible that such minor context changes should affect whether they know the proposition.**
