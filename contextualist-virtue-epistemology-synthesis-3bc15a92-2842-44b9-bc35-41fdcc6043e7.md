## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from intellectual virtues tailored to specific contexts**, **For a proposition to count as knowledge, the intellectual virtues must take into account potential deceptive elements in the environment**, **For knowledge, the intellectual virtues must be refined to detect and counteract deception in various contexts**
