## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified by strong evidence or reasoning**, **Epistemic luck is minimized by the strength of the justification, ensuring the belief is likely true**
