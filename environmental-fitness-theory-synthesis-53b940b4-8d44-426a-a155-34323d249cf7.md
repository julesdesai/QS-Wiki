## Content

**For a belief to constitute knowledge, it must be produced by cognitive faculties that are fit for the current environment**, **Fitness for the current environment means faculties must be suited to present ecological and social surroundings**, **The cognitive faculties must aim to maximize true beliefs within the context of their environmental fitness**, **A belief formed in these conditions constitutes knowledge if it is true**
