# Knowledge and Anti-Luck Condition

**Parent:** [Should the notion of safety replace traditional justification?](should-the-notion-of-safety-replace-traditional-justification-antithesis-d2fcb141-2947-4da4-a40f-aa34e2d535af.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is not formed by luck in the actual world or nearby possible worlds**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief satisfies an anti-luck condition beyond standard justification**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief satisfies both an anti-luck condition and justification**
