## Content

**Knowledge is true belief that arises from the agent's own intellectual virtue that reliably avoids error**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief arises from their own intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe it, the proposition is true, and their belief arises from their own intellectual virtue**
