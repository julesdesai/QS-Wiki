# Knowledge as No False Evidence Condition

**Parent:** [Does knowledge of self-evident propositions conform to the sensitivity condition?](does-knowledge-of-self-evident-propositions-conform-to-the-sensitivity-condition-antithesis-d02605e1-0959-4b71-944b-94c79a57a19e.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is not based on any false evidence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is based on no false evidence**
