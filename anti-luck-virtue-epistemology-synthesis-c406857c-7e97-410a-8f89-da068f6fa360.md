## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed primarily through the exercise of intellectual virtue with minimal influence from epistemic luck**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is virtuously formed with epistemic luck ruled out**
