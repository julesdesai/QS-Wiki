## Content

**Suppose the justification of any belief within an interconnected web requires reference to another belief within the same web. Intuitively, we would judge that this might lead to circular reasoning in trying to validate beliefs.**, **Circular reasoning is commonly considered a flaw in logical argumentation, suggesting coherentist foundationalism risks circularity in its justification process.**
