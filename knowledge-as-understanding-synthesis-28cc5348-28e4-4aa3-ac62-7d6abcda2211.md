# Knowledge as Understanding

**Parent:** [Is absence of defeaters alone sufficient for knowledge?](is-absence-of-defeaters-alone-sufficient-for-knowledge-antithesis-8db38c9b-782d-4da8-8fe8-f1185e31cd3c.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have an understanding of why the proposition is true**, **It is sufficient for a person to know a proposition that they believe the proposition, that the proposition is true, and that they have an understanding of why it is true**
