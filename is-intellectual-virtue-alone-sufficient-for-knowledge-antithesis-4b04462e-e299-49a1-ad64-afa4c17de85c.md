# Is intellectual virtue alone sufficient for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 4b04462e-e299-49a1-ad64-afa4c17de85c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology with Defeasibility](virtue-epistemology-with-defeasibility-synthesis-802a6edd-3a94-41b9-8765-bb5c975aae31.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where a person has a true belief arising from intellectual virtue but lacks any understanding of the reasoning behind their belief, suggesting they do not have knowledge.**, **Intellectual virtue may not be sufficient if understanding the reasoning for a belief is necessary for knowledge.**
