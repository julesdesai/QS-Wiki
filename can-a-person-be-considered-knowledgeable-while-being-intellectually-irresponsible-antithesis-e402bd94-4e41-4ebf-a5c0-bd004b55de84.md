# Can a person be considered knowledgeable while being intellectually irresponsible?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e402bd94-4e41-4ebf-a5c0-bd004b55de84
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Mixed Virtue Epistemology](mixed-virtue-epistemology-synthesis-9b1d3c06-dd01-4be5-8e49-871c85befc1a.md)

## Content

**Imagine that a person holds a true belief based on prejudiced or biased reasoning, which they are unaware of. Intuitively, this does not seem to qualify as knowledge.**, **There is an observable distinction between true beliefs formed via unbiased, responsible reasoning and those formed irresponsibly, suggesting knowledge requires responsibility.**
