# Can a reliable skill ensure the connection between belief and truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 69a73e7e-2b31-42da-93e3-42a3abdc9727
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-43f1bd52-38ff-4c3b-a3d7-408481a71c52.md)

## Content

**Consider a skill of navigating through intuition rather than knowledge, such as a poker player's instinctive bluff success that relies on subtle cues. Intuitively, this skill leads to true belief, yet we hesitate to classify it as intellectual virtue due to the lack of knowledge about the cues themselves.**, **Truths inferred from such instinctual skills may not clearly reflect intellectual virtues because they operate without articulated understanding or knowledge.**
