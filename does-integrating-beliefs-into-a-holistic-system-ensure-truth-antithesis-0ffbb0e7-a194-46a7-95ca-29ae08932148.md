# Does integrating beliefs into a holistic system ensure truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0ffbb0e7-a194-46a7-95ca-29ae08932148
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Holism with Infinite Justification](holism-with-infinite-justification-synthesis-5f38dde0-d181-4939-9f1a-56e147cd5ef5.md)

## Content

**A holistic system could be internally coherent but entirely detached from reality.**, **Integration alone does not guarantee that the beliefs correspond to reality.**, **Therefore, holistic integration does not ensure truth.**
