# Non-Lucky Virtue Epistemology

## Content

**Knowledge is true belief arising from intellectual virtue without reliance on luck**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief is a result of intellectual virtue, excluding lucky instances**, **A person knows a proposition if they have justification for believing it that is not merely lucky**, **A person knows a proposition if the proposition is true and the truth is reached through non-lucky means**
