## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have a true belief**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief is sensitive**, **A belief is sensitive if, were the proposition false, the person would not believe it**, **Sensitivity ensures that the method of forming the belief would not lead to a false belief in similar situations**
