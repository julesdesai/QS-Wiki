# Does meta-cognition distract from direct epistemic goals?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9a4823e9-a533-4895-a8ea-8dbc88c0ad92
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Meta-Virtue Epistemology](meta-virtue-epistemology-synthesis-b3bd54fd-8ed8-46f5-92c6-aa5fa4222616.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person becomes so focused on meta-cognitive reflection that they neglect the direct application of their intellectual virtues, leading to less effective belief formation. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that this focus on meta-cognition detracts from knowledge.**
