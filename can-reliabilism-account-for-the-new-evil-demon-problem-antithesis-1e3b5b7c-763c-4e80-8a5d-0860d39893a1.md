## Content

**Consider a scenario where an individual is subjected to an evil demon that manipulates their sensory inputs to produce false beliefs, yet their processes remain reliable relative to their environment.**, **In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that the individual lacks knowledge despite their beliefs being the product of processes that have been reliable in the past.**
