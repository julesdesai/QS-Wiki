## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were not true, they would not believe it**, **Sensitive belief is a belief that would not be held if it were false**
