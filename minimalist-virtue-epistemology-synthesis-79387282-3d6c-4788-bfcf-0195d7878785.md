## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief is the result of an exercise of their intellectual virtues and formed in a non-accidental manner**
