## Content

**Knowledge requires a belief that counterfactually depends on the truth of the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief is dependent on the proposition being true across nearby possible worlds**
