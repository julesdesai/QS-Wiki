## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is an appropriate causal connection between the fact that makes the proposition true and the person's belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is true, the person believes the proposition, and there is an appropriate causal connection**
