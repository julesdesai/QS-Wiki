# Do practical considerations improperly prioritize subjective over objective justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 313e67bc-ea3e-42c2-916d-9bbaeb24f23b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Encroachment Theory](pragmatic-encroachment-theory-synthesis-48f209b4-b71c-4b9b-814c-8c2a5af0c840.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where person Z is sure of a safe shortcut based only on a reliable map. Intuitively, Z knows of the shortcut's safety.**, **Suppose, however, that the stakes for another person A using the same map involve a high-risk decision. Intuitively, the existence of high stakes does not intuitively change the knowledge derived from the map.**, **It is implausible that practical considerations should prioritize subjective stakes over the objective justification given by evidence.**
