## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have an implicit understanding of the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their understanding is demonstrated through consistent behavior**, **Implicit understanding does not require explicit beliefs**, **A person's implicit understanding of a proposition is sufficient for them to know it if it reliably guides successful action**
