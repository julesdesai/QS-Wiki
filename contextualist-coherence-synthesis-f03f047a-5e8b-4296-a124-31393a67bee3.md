# Contextualist Coherence

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification involves a coherence of beliefs that take into account contextual factors**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief fits coherently within a system of beliefs considering context**
