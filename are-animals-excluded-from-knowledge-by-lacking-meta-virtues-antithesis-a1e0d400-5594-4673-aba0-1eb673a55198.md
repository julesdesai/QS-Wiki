# Are animals excluded from knowledge by lacking meta-virtues?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a1e0d400-5594-4673-aba0-1eb673a55198
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Meta-Virtue Epistemology](meta-virtue-epistemology-synthesis-b3bd54fd-8ed8-46f5-92c6-aa5fa4222616.md)

## Content

**A plausible principle is that non-human animals can have knowledge of their environments. This principle together with their lack of intellectual meta-virtues entails that such meta-virtues cannot be a requirement for knowledge.**
