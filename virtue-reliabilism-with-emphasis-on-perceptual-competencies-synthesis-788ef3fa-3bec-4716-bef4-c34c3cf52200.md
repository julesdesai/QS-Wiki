## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief arises from a reliable intellectual competence, including perceptual competencies**, **Perceptual competencies are considered intellectual virtues in this framework**
