# Knowledge as No-False-Lemma Reliability

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is reliability sufficient for knowledge in the face of justified false beliefs?](is-reliability-sufficient-for-knowledge-in-the-face-of-justified-false-beliefs-antithesis-a447f343-d4e0-4f6b-b399-30b8d57b5741.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is based on a reliable process, and the belief does not rely on any false lemmas**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that no belief components include reliance on justified false beliefs**
