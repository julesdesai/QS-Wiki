# Knowledge as Sensitivity in Close Worlds Only

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** e9358d92-c321-428d-9d4f-4e15a12fd028
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does tracking require too much about nearby worlds?](does-tracking-require-too-much-about-nearby-worlds-antithesis-69212bb2-6d5d-491b-9f64-1179440896e5.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false in a nearby possible world, the person would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the person believes the proposition, it is true, and the person would not believe it in a nearby world where it is false**
