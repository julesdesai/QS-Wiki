# Knowledge as True Belief with an Anti-Luck Condition

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** a1e9e06d-d523-4383-b2ab-9b5670fe26df
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge be gained from a source that is typically unreliable but accidentally correct?](can-knowledge-be-gained-from-a-source-that-is-typically-unreliable-but-accidentally-correct-antithesis-0d943ab7-7799-4f2b-a45d-5a5aa31fee66.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is not true merely by luck**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is not true merely by luck, and the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**
