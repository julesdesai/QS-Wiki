# Knowledge via Dependable Justification

**Parent:** [Can environmental factors negate perceptual justification?](can-environmental-factors-negate-perceptual-justification-antithesis-6bd1175a-8dfd-418b-b44e-6cfbd19ed007.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification that is robust against error**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their justification is robust against error**
