# Are safety conditions overly demanding in practice?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** bd456aeb-8a6f-4521-9e2d-c59676356422
**Depth:** 4

**Parent:** [The Safety Condition](the-safety-condition-synthesis-1c250b1c-da7f-4cab-a10a-178f18915234.md)

## Content
