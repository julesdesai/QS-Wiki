## Content

**Consider a situation where a new instrument is used without known reliability. Intuitively, one might be skeptical about the validity of perceptions it produces.**, **A principle exists that suggests perception validity depends on a well-established track record of accuracy, not just assumed reliability.**, **Validity hinges on evidence beyond theoretical reliability, including practical demonstration.**
