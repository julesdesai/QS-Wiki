# What role do virtues play when external factors dictate true belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b1605986-bb93-4743-bcf8-10b33cfc2999
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue epistemology focusing on intellectual virtues](virtue-epistemology-focusing-on-intellectual-virtues-synthesis-f3a48039-3cf5-452a-9c79-9ac1b05ad9d2.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person consistently tells a truth due to being in a controlled environment with no external verification, like a simulated reality.**, **The truthfulness of the belief isn’t due to their intellectual virtue but due to the setup of their environment, raising doubt on virtues' roles in true belief acquisition.**
