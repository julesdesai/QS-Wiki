## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed by a cognitive process that is reliable in the specific context**, **A belief formed by a contextually reliable cognitive process is likely to be true in that context**
