# Knowledge as Ability Epistemology

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Does knowledge require a conscious rational reflection on every belief?](does-knowledge-require-a-conscious-rational-reflection-on-every-belief-antithesis-053b12f8-14f0-4711-99b6-677e5c33a92d.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is a result of cognitive ability**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief results from cognitive ability**
