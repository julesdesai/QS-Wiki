## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have a stable, reliable relation to the truth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is integrated into a coherent network of beliefs**
