# Can justification truly exist in an infinite network without collapsing into circularity?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5e19267c-548f-43be-b02d-b5a5101128be
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism with Infinite Justificatory Network](coherentism-with-infinite-justificatory-network-synthesis-c0ed2adb-3371-4bf3-b36f-58a215809fa4.md)

## Content

**If justification is attained through a network where beliefs support each other indefinitely, there must be a starting point to the network, failing which it becomes circular.**, **In an infinite justificatory network, every belief is justified by another belief indefinitely, but this could lead to an endless regress unless an arbitrary, unjustified starting point is assumed.**
