**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **The sensitivity condition handles complex dependencies by ensuring beliefs respond appropriately to variations in conditions**
