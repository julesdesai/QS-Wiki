**Parent:** [Can a reliable process still lead to false beliefs?](can-a-reliable-process-still-lead-to-false-beliefs-antithesis-4726a174-577e-4865-8caf-9e32a8741bcd.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A person knows a proposition if in nearby possible worlds where the proposition were false, they would not believe it**, **A person knows a proposition if in nearby worlds where the proposition is true, they would believe it**
