# How do design flaws in faculties impact knowledge claims?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 02c0a7e8-f63b-438d-b75f-6a1ba97a28f2
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Proper Function Theory](proper-function-theory-synthesis-eb8cad20-b2ac-4ebe-8b63-c23141145814.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person has a cognitive faculty with a design flaw that consistently produces true beliefs in a stable environment. Intuitively, we would question whether these true beliefs constitute knowledge if based on flawed faculties.**, **It is implausible that a flawed design, even if it yields true beliefs, conveys the same level of reliability associated with knowledge.**
