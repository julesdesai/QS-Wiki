# Can one ascribe knowledge if a belief was formed through manipulation yet is true?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 01910c48-dbdc-4f9f-bb62-e1f65012773a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalism with Reliable Processes](externalism-with-reliable-processes-synthesis-89e590de-768c-4370-a7df-27a5b4f5eaa5.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where a person believes a false conspiracy theory made true by a deceptive manipulation of facts by a malicious actor.**, **If the belief coincidentally coincides with reality merely due to manipulation, it seems incorrect to claim the believer possesses true knowledge.**, **The manipulation aspect questions whether the criteria for knowledge are sufficiently met.**
