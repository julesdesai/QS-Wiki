# Can knowledge exist with a non-truth aimed design plan?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0fe66b8f-7a2e-4147-bb11-933a77e9c18b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Proper Functionalism](proper-functionalism-synthesis-252aa5eb-1857-4367-af60-24f7afe1c9be.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where cognitive faculties result from a design plan aimed at social cohesion rather than truth, yet individuals hold true beliefs. Intuitively, they might still possess knowledge.**, **Suppose societal or cultural pressures shape cognitive faculties towards objectives other than truth. This implies that faculties need not be aimed at truth for true beliefs to still be considered knowledge.**
