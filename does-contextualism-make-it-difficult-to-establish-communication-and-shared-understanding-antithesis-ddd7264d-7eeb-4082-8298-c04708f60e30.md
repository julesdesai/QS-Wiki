# Does contextualism make it difficult to establish communication and shared understanding?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ddd7264d-7eeb-4082-8298-c04708f60e30
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism about Knowledge](contextualism-about-knowledge-synthesis-b64cba08-2a7b-44b8-b81d-d7241d8be6e0.md)

## Content

**Suppose two people, A and B, are in a conversation but are in different epistemic contexts with different standards for knowledge. Intuitively, we might have to say that their use of "know" could refer to different states accordingly, leading to potential misunderstandings and communication breakdowns.**
