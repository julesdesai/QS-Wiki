# Relativism about knowledge claims

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true in a relative sense based on a particular perspective**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified according to standards set by specific epistemic communities**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true in a relative sense, and the belief is justified according to standards set by specific epistemic communities**
