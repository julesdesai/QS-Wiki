# What is the role of context in affirming intellectual virtue's impact on belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a3c901b5-424b-4dd3-a322-405e53f71c01
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-83c48565-5b77-4330-ac3e-7b5984f59b6e.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which the context or environment significantly influences whether an intellectual virtue leads to a true belief. Certain contexts might render a reliable intellectual virtue ineffective in producing knowledge. Intuitively, context thus has a significant role in whether a belief formed via intellectual virtue qualifies as knowledge.**, **There is a plausible principle that all-encompassing claims about the role of intellectual virtues in forming knowledge should account for differences in context and environment.**
