# How does sensitivity account for self-evident truths?

**Node ID:** 0b857650-8947-4446-be24-be09f83351d3
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Sensitivity Theory of Knowledge](sensitivity-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-fa7f9900-da54-4309-8d89-06019053aee3.md)

## Content

**Self-evident truths, such as simple logical principles or mathematics, inherently lack false possible worlds, yet are known despite potential absence of sensitivity.**, **It seems implausible to suggest that sensitivity plays a role in acknowledging self-evident truths, contradicting intuitive sense of certain knowledge.**
