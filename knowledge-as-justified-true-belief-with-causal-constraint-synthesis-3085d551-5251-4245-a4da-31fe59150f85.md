# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with Causal Constraint

**Parent:** [Is a true belief with an incorrect causal story still knowledge?](is-a-true-belief-with-an-incorrect-causal-story-still-knowledge-antithesis-358e193a-2708-4157-9da9-99a142654c6a.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there be a causal connection between the reason for the belief and the fact**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification, the proposition is true, and there is a causal connection between the reason for the belief and the fact**
