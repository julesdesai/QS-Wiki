# Is belief a necessary part of knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d4aca58b-b3dd-49b7-92eb-971dc430e599
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Probabilistic Foundationalism](probabilistic-foundationalism-synthesis-4f173a54-3943-4ddb-a2cd-a6bb34479be2.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which someone has a true belief, yet does not consciously acknowledge or consider it, such as in cases of implicit knowledge. Intuitively, knowledge could still be present, challenging the need for explicit belief.**, **It is implausible to claim that explicit belief is always necessary for knowledge when knowledge can sometimes operate implicitly.**
