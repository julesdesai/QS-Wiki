## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief is formed through a reliable process and no defeating evidence is present**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that no evidence exists that undermines the belief**
