# Can everyday knowledge be lost in complex scenarios where truth-tracking is satisfied?

## Content

**In complex scenarios, one might satisfy the truth-tracking conditions by accident, not through reliable methods, leading to intuitions that we lack true knowledge due to the precarious nature of belief formation in these contexts.**, **Consider a scenario where a person guesses correctly, and their belief accidentally meets the tracking conditions but is not formed through a cognitive faculty reliable in other contexts, indicating a lack of genuine knowledge.**
