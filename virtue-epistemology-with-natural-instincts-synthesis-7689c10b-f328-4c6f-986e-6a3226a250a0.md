## Content

**Knowledge involves the manifestation of epistemic virtues or competences, which involves achieving truth through the exercise of intellectual virtues**, **Animals and young children can possess epistemic virtues in non-reflective ways, relying on natural instincts**, **For someone to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief arises from their epistemic virtues or competences and that the proposition is true**
