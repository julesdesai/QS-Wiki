# Can someone know something with a false basis for their evidence?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** dc940999-1dbc-4515-af6c-b44b9fc6b079
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Evidentialist Reliabilism](evidentialist-reliabilism-synthesis-b2fd7651-b8ba-4bbd-aa9b-2c3c304a468b.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person believes a true proposition because their evidence, although leading to the true belief, is itself false.**, **It is implausible that someone can truly know something when the basis of their evidence is false, even if the conclusion is true.**
