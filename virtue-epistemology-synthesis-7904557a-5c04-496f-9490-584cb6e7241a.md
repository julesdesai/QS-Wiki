## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person believes the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from the intellectual virtues of the person**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is both true and the result of intellectual virtues**
