# Knowledge as Safe Truth

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can one have knowledge if similar situations yield mostly false beliefs?](can-one-have-knowledge-if-similar-situations-yield-mostly-false-beliefs-antithesis-fdde1603-3842-4d31-bcf5-2ee1b6de973b.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief-forming process would not easily lead them to a false belief in similar situations**, **A belief-forming process is safe if it leads to true beliefs in most nearby possible worlds**
