**Parent:** [Does process reliabilism handle the generality problem effectively?](does-process-reliabilism-handle-the-generality-problem-effectively-antithesis-c10499e1-3741-44b2-8981-8785bb07cc25.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the context determines which process is considered reliable for forming the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is produced by a process deemed reliable in the context**
