# Can someone possess knowledge without satisfying all conditions of Virtue Epistemology?

## Content

**In some cases, a person can fortuitously stumble upon the truth by chance without exercising intellectual virtues like carefulness or open-mindedness.**, **A case in which a detective forms a correct conclusion by a lucky guess without evidence or virtue challenges the claim that knowledge requires intellectual virtues.**, **It is implausible that merely believing a proposition and having it turn out to be true by luck constitutes knowledge.**
