# Knowledge from Stable Environments

**Parent:** [Can an unreliable intermediary affect knowledge?](can-an-unreliable-intermediary-affect-knowledge-antithesis-f1024517-5727-4904-9f2e-13dddb0abb38.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed in an environment that is stable and reliable**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is formed in a stable and reliable environment**
