# How can Direct Realism account for perceptual illusions?

**Parent:** [Direct Realism](direct-realism-synthesis-63f38132-cda9-42dc-9c71-38670cc01e77.md)

## Content

**Perceptual illusions are cases where there is a discrepancy between perception and reality.**, **In a scenario where a stick appears bent in water, intuitively, perception seems distorted.**, **If perception is distorted in illusions, then perception cannot provide immediate and direct knowledge of the external object.**
