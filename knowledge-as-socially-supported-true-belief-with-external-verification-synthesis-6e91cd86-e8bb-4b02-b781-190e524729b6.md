# Knowledge as Socially Supported True Belief With External Verification

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can social practices lead to inconsistent beliefs?](can-social-practices-lead-to-inconsistent-beliefs-antithesis-7f53fbfc-59d5-4cb8-bde1-cfc0211ed6a6.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is required that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is supported by social knowledge practices and verified externally**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is supported by social knowledge practices and verified externally**
