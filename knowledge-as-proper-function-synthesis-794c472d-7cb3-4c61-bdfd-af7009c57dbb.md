# Knowledge as Proper Function

**Parent:** [Can partial reliability suffice for knowledge acquisition?](can-partial-reliability-suffice-for-knowledge-acquisition-antithesis-da5f7bdf-f296-46bc-ab0a-242b68f2721b.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the cognitive faculties producing the belief are functioning properly in a suitable environment**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the cognitive faculties are functioning properly in a suitable environment**
