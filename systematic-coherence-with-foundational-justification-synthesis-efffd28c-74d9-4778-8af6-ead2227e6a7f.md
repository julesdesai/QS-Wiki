## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that foundational beliefs provide a base that enhances systematic coherence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, foundational beliefs are justified, and the belief coheres robustly with their cognitive system**
