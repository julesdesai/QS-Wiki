# Can we know anything by basing our beliefs on an infinite series?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b7aa6e73-c6b4-4d63-b67b-d19586abd49d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism Summary](infinitism-summary-synthesis-17c5b8c2-c7de-43ec-bf90-6dedae4ad235.md)

## Content

**Knowledge requires that our beliefs be justified to some satisfactory degree.**, **If reasons for a belief extend infinitely, there seems to be no point at which beliefs are suitably justified.**, **It seems implausible to assert that one can have knowledge if the justification process never completes.**
