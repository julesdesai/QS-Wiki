# Nozick's Truth-Tracking Theory

**Parent:** [Are non-causal beliefs impossible to know?](are-non-causal-beliefs-impossible-to-know-antithesis-9e3aac44-e4f5-4e72-9efb-f1a641419d5b.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, they would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were true, they would believe it**
