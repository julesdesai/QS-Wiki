## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition seems true to the person in an appropriate way**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the proposition seems true to the person in an appropriate way**
