# Does knowledge depend solely on practical consequences?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 93726a22-8cc5-428d-8742-cf1e60a53a50
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatism Summary](pragmatism-summary-synthesis-b07cedb4-abb7-4db7-b910-98d4ce99ba6e.md)

## Content

**There are cases where a belief leads to useful outcomes but is still false, challenging the idea that practical consequences determine knowledge.**, **In a scenario where a person believes it will rain and takes an umbrella, thus staying dry, intuitively whether this constitutes knowledge does not solely depend on the usefulness of staying dry.**
