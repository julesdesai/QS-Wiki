## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, and the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief coheres with a stable and context-independent network of foundational beliefs**
