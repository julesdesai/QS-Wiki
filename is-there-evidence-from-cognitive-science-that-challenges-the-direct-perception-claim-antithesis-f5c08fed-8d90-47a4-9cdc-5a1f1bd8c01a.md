# Is there evidence from cognitive science that challenges the direct perception claim?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f5c08fed-8d90-47a4-9cdc-5a1f1bd8c01a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Naïve Realism](naïve-realism-synthesis-25d0b7e3-6f66-46c3-a45d-bb41c1dbe70b.md)

## Content

**Cognitive science shows that the brain processes and interprets sensory data before we become consciously aware of our perceptions. It is implausible that this intermediate processing aligns with the claim of direct perception.**
