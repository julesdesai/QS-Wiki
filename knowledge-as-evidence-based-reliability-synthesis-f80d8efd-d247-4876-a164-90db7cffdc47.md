# Knowledge as Evidence-Based Reliability

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can sensitivity handle cases of necessary truths?](can-sensitivity-handle-cases-of-necessary-truths-antithesis-23fb67eb-7e2a-4ada-b890-a6e5dcead6bb.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief stems from reliable evidence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe it, the proposition is true, and the belief is based on reliable evidence**
