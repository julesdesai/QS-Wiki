**Parent:** [Is it possible for a reliable process to produce false beliefs, and how does that affect knowledge claims?](is-it-possible-for-a-reliable-process-to-produce-false-beliefs-and-how-does-that-affect-knowledge-claims-antithesis-6a287592-61bc-4719-87ca-ebceda825955.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they could not easily have believed the proposition without it being true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and they could not easily have believed it without it being true**
