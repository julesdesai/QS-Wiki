# Does direct realism conflict with scientific findings on perception?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e11690b2-a625-4b77-bf15-3a257c57a7b3
**Depth:** 2
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Direct Realism](direct-realism-thesis-65da2b06-0c60-4e66-b428-771d2431fe55.md)

## Content

**Experimental evidence shows perception is often unreliable and subject to error, challenging its directness.**, **Scientific models often depict perception as a constructive process, not merely a reception of external information.**, **Direct realism struggles to accommodate findings where perception is modulated by internal cognitive systems.**
