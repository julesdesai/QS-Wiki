## Content

**Intellectual virtues are character traits that consistently aim at true beliefs**, **The primary value of intellectual virtues lies in the pursuit of truth**, **A person can possess intellectual virtues even if they occasionally form false beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient for the belief to be reliably produced by intellectual virtues even if not infallible**
