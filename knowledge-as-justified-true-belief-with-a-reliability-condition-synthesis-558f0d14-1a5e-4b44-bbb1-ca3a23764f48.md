# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with a Reliability Condition

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 558f0d14-1a5e-4b44-bbb1-ca3a23764f48
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can we know via indirect causation?](can-we-know-via-indirect-causation-antithesis-1ddc8938-7927-45b3-ac9d-a6baee3498de.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification rests on a reliable process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, the justification rests on a reliable process, and the proposition is true**
