## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a skeptical argument challenges knowledge claims in a strict context but fails in a relaxed context. Intuitively, we would judge this inconsistency problematic in addressing skepticism coherently.**, **It is implausible that varying contexts should influence the fundamental challenge posed by skepticism, as it questions the possibility of any knowledge.**
