# Does contextualism result in epistemic relativism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 359bb509-e061-4353-83ce-22d06dd4337e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Knowledge as Contextualism](knowledge-as-contextualism-synthesis-34a646f3-6bed-4d6b-8194-a00ffa60b5bb.md)

## Content

**Contextualism holds that justification depends on contextual factors, leading to the idea that knowledge is relative to the context.**, **If knowledge depends on individual contexts, then it implies epistemic relativism, where the truth-value of knowledge claims varies with context-specific conditions.**
