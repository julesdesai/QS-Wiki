# Nozick's Truth-tracking Theory

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition is true, the person believes it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have a true belief that would not be held if the proposition were false and would be held if the proposition is true**
