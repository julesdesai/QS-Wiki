## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive virtue or ability**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and some reliable cognitive virtue or ability of the person is responsible for the belief**
