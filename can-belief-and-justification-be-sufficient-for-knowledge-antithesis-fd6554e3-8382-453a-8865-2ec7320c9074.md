# Can belief and justification be sufficient for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** fd6554e3-8382-453a-8865-2ec7320c9074
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Relativistic Contextualism with Stable Core](relativistic-contextualism-with-stable-core-synthesis-3b7ed9ae-6709-4913-87ed-5986155c06e2.md)

## Content

**Simply believing a proposition and having justification for it does not necessarily amount to knowledge, as there may be cases where the belief is true for irrelevant reasons.**, **A scenario can be imagined where a justified belief is true but only due to luck, thus not constituting genuine knowledge.**
