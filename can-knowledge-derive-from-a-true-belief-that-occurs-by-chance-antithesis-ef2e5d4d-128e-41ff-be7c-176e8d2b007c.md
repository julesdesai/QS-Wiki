# Can knowledge derive from a true belief that occurs by chance?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ef2e5d4d-128e-41ff-be7c-176e8d2b007c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-7601e066-11d5-4f0c-8c42-44a4cc1f650d.md)

## Content

**Intuitively, if a person forms a true belief because of a reliable cognitive process but only by chance, it is not knowledge.**, **A belief that is true purely due to chance rather than intellectual virtue is not considered knowledge.**
