# Knowledge with Indefeasibility Clause

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can a belief be considered knowledge if it is based on unreliable sources?](can-a-belief-be-considered-knowledge-if-it-is-based-on-unreliable-sources-antithesis-5edb91a9-89a7-44d2-8adc-594298c2f9c3.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is indefeasible, meaning no false information could undermine it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is indefeasible**
