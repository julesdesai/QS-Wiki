# Does contextualism explain knowledge in ordinary language adequately?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a6fabc0b-55d9-4081-8005-a1d2dca99db6
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism](contextualism-synthesis-c0d03a39-c775-403c-87e2-d078dab04731.md)

## Content

**Consider scenarios where people casually claim to know everyday propositions without specifying context.**, **Contextualism implies that these claims require specifying context, which is often absent in ordinary discourse.**, **This suggests that contextualism may not align well with common-sense usage and understanding of knowledge in everyday language.**
