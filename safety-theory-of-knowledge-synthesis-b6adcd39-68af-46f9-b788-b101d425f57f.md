## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the person believes the proposition, then in nearly all nearby possible worlds where they believe it, the proposition is true**, **This safety condition ensures that the belief reliably indicates the truth**
