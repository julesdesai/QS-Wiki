## Content

**Knowledge is true belief that arises from intellectual virtue which reliably avoids error**, **Knowledge requires that the belief is accompanied by understanding**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief arises from intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is accompanied by understanding**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe it, the proposition is true, it arises from intellectual virtue, and is accompanied by understanding**
