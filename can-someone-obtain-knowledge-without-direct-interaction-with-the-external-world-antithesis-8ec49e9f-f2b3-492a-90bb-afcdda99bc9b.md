# Can someone obtain knowledge without direct interaction with the external world?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 8ec49e9f-f2b3-492a-90bb-afcdda99bc9b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Epistemic Representationalism](epistemic-representationalism-synthesis-c86e8f4e-5440-4a21-8e04-4337bf022fd8.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that knowledge can be acquired through internal reasoning or intuition, independent of direct perception. This principle together with mathematical or introspective knowledge entails that direct interaction with the world is not always necessary for knowledge.**
