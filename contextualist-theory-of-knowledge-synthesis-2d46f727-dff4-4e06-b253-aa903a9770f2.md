## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **The criteria for justification vary with the context of the knowledge claim**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they meet the contextually relevant standards for justification, the proposition is true, and they believe it**
