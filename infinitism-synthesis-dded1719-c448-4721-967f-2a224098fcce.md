## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have an infinite and non-repeating chain of reasons for the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there is an infinite and non-repeating chain of reasons for the belief**
