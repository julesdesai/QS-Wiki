# Does knowledge require infallibility?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** fff2eeb5-208a-431f-a0b6-ba1593353d9b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Knowledge First Epistemology](knowledge-first-epistemology-synthesis-5ffe7f85-d642-4212-8020-2dc118f603c3.md)

## Content

**If a person can have a justified true belief that turns out to be false due to unforeseen factors, intuitively this suggests knowledge might require certainty beyond justification and truth.**, **This observation implies that some levels of fallibility may not satisfy our notion of what counts as knowledge.**
