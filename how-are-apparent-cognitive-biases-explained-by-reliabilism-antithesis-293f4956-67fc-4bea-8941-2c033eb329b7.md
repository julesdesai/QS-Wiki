## Content

**A reliable process is one that generally produces true beliefs over false ones.**, **Cognitive biases can lead to systematic errors in judgment and decision-making.**, **If biases frequently lead to errors, labeling the resulting true beliefs as knowledge goes against intuitive judgments about reliability.**
