# Are pure sensory experiences sufficient for mathematics and abstract reasoning?

**Consider how mathematical truths, like 2+2=4, are not derived from sensory experience but rather from abstract reasoning. It intuitively suggests that abstract concepts can't be reduced to sensory experiences**, **There is a plausible principle that abstract reasoning and knowledge of mathematical truths require cognitive capabilities beyond sensory experiences**
