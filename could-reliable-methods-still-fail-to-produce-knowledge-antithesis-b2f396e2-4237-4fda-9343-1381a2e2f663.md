# Could reliable methods still fail to produce knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b2f396e2-4237-4fda-9343-1381a2e2f663
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Knowledge First Epistemology](knowledge-first-epistemology-synthesis-5ffe7f85-d642-4212-8020-2dc118f603c3.md)

## Content

**Suppose there exists a scenario where a person uses a reliable method to acquire a belief, yet the result is false through no fault of the person or method.**, **Intuitively, one might question whether the belief constituted as knowledge, given the unexpected error in the reliable method.**
