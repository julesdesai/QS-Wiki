**Parent:** [Does the variability of justification undermine stable knowledge?](does-the-variability-of-justification-undermine-stable-knowledge-antithesis-dcc77cd8-1e3a-4b66-aa8f-211b3e42315e.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief tracks the truth across possible scenarios**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief tracks the truth**
