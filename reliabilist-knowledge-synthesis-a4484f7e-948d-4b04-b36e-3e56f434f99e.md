# Reliabilist Knowledge

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Does sensitivity undermine the closure of knowledge?](does-sensitivity-undermine-the-closure-of-knowledge-antithesis-8a563b7c-5038-4acc-8df5-c2ac9e6ffafe.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is produced by a reliable process**
