# How does coherentism address common sense beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** bfaf561e-e862-469a-b2f1-b75d14cfe907
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism with Justified Belief](coherentism-with-justified-belief-synthesis-01e10c3b-52a8-4097-b02c-3f010fc56355.md)

## Content

**Common sense beliefs, such as the existence of an external world, are often seen as justified without the need for coherence with other beliefs. Intuitively, these beliefs are justified immediately through experience.**, **It is implausible that a coherent system alone suffices for justifying beliefs typically held as common sense.**
