# Knowledge as reliabilism without the belief condition

**Parent:** [Can knowledge exist without belief?](can-knowledge-exist-without-belief-antithesis-bd1b31cc-1aab-483a-96e6-d7dac50f12a8.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief-forming process is reliable**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is true and formed through a reliable process**
