## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief could not easily have been false given the method of belief formation**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed through a reliable cognitive process**
