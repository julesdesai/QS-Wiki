## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief-forming faculties are functioning properly**, **Proper functioning is determined by a design plan successfully aimed at truth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief results from properly functioning cognitive faculties**
