# Does contextualism create problems for legal standards of knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2ba97f27-967f-4e96-a0be-f07f93e12d59
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism about Knowledge](contextualism-about-knowledge-synthesis-3f771a9e-26d6-43c4-b604-a31f8bd9947c.md)

## Content

**In a legal context, the standard for knowledge is often fixed and must be clearly defined to ensure justice.**, **Contextualism implies that these legal standards could vary dramatically with context, leading to inconsistencies.**, **In a legal framework, it is problematic if knowledge and justification can differ so greatly depending on context.**
