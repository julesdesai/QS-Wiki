# What about knowledge from testimony without direct causal links?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 867babe3-613e-4973-8d85-0b83246f6ec7
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Causal Theory of Knowledge](causal-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-2f0f13d0-725f-4ae5-ba67-2c976061582b.md)

## Content

**Knowledge acquired through testimony relies on trust and justification rather than direct causal links to the facts.**, **If a historian knows about an event from documented evidence, their belief is justified and true, though lacking a causal connection to the original event.**
