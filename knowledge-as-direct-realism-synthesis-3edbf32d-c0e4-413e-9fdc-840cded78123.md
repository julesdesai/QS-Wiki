## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified**, **The justification of a belief can be achieved through direct interaction with the world without the need for inferential processes**
