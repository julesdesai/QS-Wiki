# Knowledge and Certainty

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they are certain of their belief**, **A person knows a proposition if it is true, they believe it, and they are certain of their belief**
