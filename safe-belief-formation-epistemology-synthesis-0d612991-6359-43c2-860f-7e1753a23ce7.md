## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that believing it safely would lead to a true belief in similar circumstances**, **Safety in belief entails that in nearby possible worlds, the belief remains true**
