## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is true in nearby possible worlds with similar processes**, **Belief formation processes are relevant but do not require direct awareness by the individual**, **A belief is sensitive if, in cases where the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**
