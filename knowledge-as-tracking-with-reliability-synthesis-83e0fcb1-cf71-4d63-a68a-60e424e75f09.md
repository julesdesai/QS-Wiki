# Knowledge as Tracking with Reliability

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 83e0fcb1-cf71-4d63-a68a-60e424e75f09
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Could knowledge require more than belief, truth, and tracking?](could-knowledge-require-more-than-belief-truth-and-tracking-antithesis-1c523b13-2a25-4889-b296-480a1817b4da.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief tracks the truth across possible scenarios**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the process by which the belief is formed is reliable**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, their belief tracks the truth across possible scenarios, and the process by which the belief is formed is reliable**
