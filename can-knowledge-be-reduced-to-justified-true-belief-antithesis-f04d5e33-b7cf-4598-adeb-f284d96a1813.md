# Can knowledge be reduced to justified true belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f04d5e33-b7cf-4598-adeb-f284d96a1813
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology: Intellectual Virtues](virtue-epistemology-intellectual-virtues-synthesis-1d322156-3f22-49a0-99a5-f932d24b0e66.md)

## Content

**A scenario exists in which a person has a justified true belief due to a reliable evidence but lacks awareness of their evidence, which intuitively suggests they do not have knowledge.**, **A plausible principle is that knowing requires an awareness of relevant evidence, thus showing that justified true belief without awareness is incomplete for knowledge.**
