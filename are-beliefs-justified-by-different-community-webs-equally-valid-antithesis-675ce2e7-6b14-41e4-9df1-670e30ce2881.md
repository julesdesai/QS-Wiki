# Are beliefs justified by different community webs equally valid?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 675ce2e7-6b14-41e4-9df1-670e30ce2881
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism with Shared Justification](coherentism-with-shared-justification-synthesis-efbed405-50ec-4920-aeba-74abec1bb76e.md)

## Content

**Two different communities can have coherent webs of beliefs that provide conflicting justifications for the same proposition.**, **Justification by a coherent community web leads to relativism, where conflicting beliefs from different communities are equally justified, challenging the universality of knowledge.**
