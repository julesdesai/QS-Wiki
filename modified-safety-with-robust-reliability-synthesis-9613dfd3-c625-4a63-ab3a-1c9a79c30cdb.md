## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed by a method that remains reliable across a wide range of possible scenarios**, **The reliability condition demands that even under variations, the belief method continues to produce true beliefs in nearly all close possible worlds**
