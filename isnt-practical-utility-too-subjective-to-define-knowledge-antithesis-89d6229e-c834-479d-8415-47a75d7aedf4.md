# Isn't practical utility too subjective to define knowledge?

## Content

**The notion of what is practical or beneficial varies greatly between individuals and contexts.**, **A belief that is practical for one person may not be for another, challenging the universality of the knowledge claim.**, **Subjective utility assessments might lead to disagreements about what constitutes knowledge in different situations.**
