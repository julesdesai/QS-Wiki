# Knowledge as Externalism with Degree-Adjusted Reliability

**Parent:** [Does the reliability condition account for degrees of belief?](does-the-reliability-condition-account-for-degrees-of-belief-antithesis-90313bb9-b6b5-4d54-99d0-8c91cbdd9a34.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed by a reliable process sensitive to degrees of belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, some conditions other than consciousness provide the justification for the belief, and the belief is formed by a reliable process sensitive to degrees of belief**
