## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that no defeaters exist that would undermine the belief**, **Defeaters are conditions that would justify retracting the belief**
