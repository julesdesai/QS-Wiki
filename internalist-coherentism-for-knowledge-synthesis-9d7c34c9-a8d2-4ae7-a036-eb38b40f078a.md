## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person's beliefs cohere with one another**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that coherence of beliefs provides justification**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is true and the belief coheres with the person's other beliefs**
