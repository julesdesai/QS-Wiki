# Knowledge as Safety Condition

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 14ed7731-0813-432c-b823-044ae653cfea
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is belief alone sufficient for knowledge acquisition?](is-belief-alone-sufficient-for-knowledge-acquisition-antithesis-57c4e93b-2e95-4316-94ae-38da19ea4c16.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief would not easily have been false in nearby possible worlds**
