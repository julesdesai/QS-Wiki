# How does this theory address cases of necessary truths?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** cd6b940e-7933-40e5-a098-8afe7f7ecc1d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Sensitivity Theory of Knowledge](sensitivity-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-102d682a-8eb5-4983-bf10-f287ee1765f9.md)

## Content

**Consider the belief that 2+2=4, which a person believes because they learned it in school. Even if it were not true, they couldn't believe otherwise because they cannot conceive of '2+2' equalling something different. Intuitively, the person still knows this mathematical truth.**, **Knowledge of necessary truths seems insensitive to counterfactual considerations by their nature.**
