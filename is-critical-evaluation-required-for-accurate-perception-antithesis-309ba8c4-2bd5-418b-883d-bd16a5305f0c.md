# Is critical evaluation required for accurate perception?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 309ba8c4-2bd5-418b-883d-bd16a5305f0c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Critical Realism](critical-realism-synthesis-04d59a43-df5d-4ff1-bc30-b363f66f9a03.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person accurately perceives a tree outside a window simply by looking at it. In this scenario, intuitively, no cognitive critical evaluation takes place to ascertain the nature of the tree.**, **A plausible principle is that some perceptions accurately reflect reality without critical evaluation, particularly in clear and unambiguous contexts.**
