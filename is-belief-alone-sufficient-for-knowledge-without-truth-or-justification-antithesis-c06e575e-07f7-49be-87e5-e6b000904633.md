# Is belief alone sufficient for knowledge without truth or justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c06e575e-07f7-49be-87e5-e6b000904633
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Instrumentalism with Externalist Justification](instrumentalism-with-externalist-justification-synthesis-69903d30-4d37-4781-884e-3e9ef74484e3.md)

## Content

**Believing a proposition does not necessarily make it true or known**, **People can believe propositions that are false or unjustified, such as conspiracy theories which are widely believed but lack evidence or truth**, **A proposition must be true and have some form of justification to be considered knowledge**
