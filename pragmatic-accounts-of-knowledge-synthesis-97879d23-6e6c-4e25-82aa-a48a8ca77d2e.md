## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A belief qualifies as knowledge if it reliably leads to practical success**, **Practical success is the achievement of one's goals based on beliefs**.
