## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief serves a practical function or results in a successful action, which counts as a form of evidential support**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief in the proposition involves an appropriate degree of conviction based on its practical efficacy**
