# Can deeply ingrained biases affect sensitivity to false propositions?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 416f156d-32e8-4ca0-9490-23a30a5fb409
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Sensitivity Theory](sensitivity-theory-synthesis-68f75ad1-a4e1-40d8-ae80-33e70d924463.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person believes a proposition influenced by a strong cultural bias. If the proposition turns false, their bias ensures they still hold it. Intuitively, deeply rooted biases prevent the necessary sensitivity required for knowledge.**
