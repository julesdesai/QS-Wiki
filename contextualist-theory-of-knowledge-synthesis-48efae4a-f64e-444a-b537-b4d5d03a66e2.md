## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the standards for knowledge in that context are satisfied**, **The standards for knowledge can vary depending on contextual factors such as the level of scrutiny or stakes involved**
