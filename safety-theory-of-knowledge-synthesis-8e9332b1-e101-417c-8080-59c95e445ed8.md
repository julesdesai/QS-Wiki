# Safety Theory of Knowledge

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 3
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does justified belief always exclude false lemmas?](does-justified-belief-always-exclude-false-lemmas-antithesis-9286d5ef-4072-41a6-ad7b-5f8f2fec0fb8.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief would not easily be false in similar situations**
