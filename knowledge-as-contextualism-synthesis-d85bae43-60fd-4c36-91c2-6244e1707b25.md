## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **The standards for knowledge depend on the context of the knower**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the contextually appropriate standards for knowledge are met**
