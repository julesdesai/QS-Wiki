# Does the truth of a proposition guarantee knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 8c68e207-784c-4f48-a3e4-ac7717aae482
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Access Internalism](access-internalism-synthesis-1ebb0a7e-8cd6-40ff-bf0f-a16161232f85.md)

## Content

**Imagine a person randomly guesses the answer to a complex question and their guess happens to be correct. In such a scenario, intuitively, we would not say that the person has knowledge even though the proposition is true.**, **Correctly guessing an answer is a situation where truth alone does not provide knowledge.**
