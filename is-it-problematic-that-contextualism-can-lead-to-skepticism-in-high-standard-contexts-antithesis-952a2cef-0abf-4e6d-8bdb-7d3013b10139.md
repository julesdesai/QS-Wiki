## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person P is in a high-standard context requiring certainty to claim knowledge. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that P knows very few, if any, propositions.**, **Knowledge attributions depending on the context of the ascriber may lead to skepticism in contexts with high standards for knowledge.**
