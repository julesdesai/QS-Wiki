## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified by internal factors accessible to the person**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have a justified, true belief based on internal reasons**
