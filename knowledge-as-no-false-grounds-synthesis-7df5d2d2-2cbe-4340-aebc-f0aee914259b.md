# Knowledge as No False Grounds

**Parent:** [How do Gettier cases challenge the necessity of causal connections?](how-do-gettier-cases-challenge-the-necessity-of-causal-connections-antithesis-4e1dcd27-4ecf-4e23-89a9-744f53287632.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that none of the grounds on which they base their belief are false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, the proposition is true, and none of the grounds on which they base their belief are false**
