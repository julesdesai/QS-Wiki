## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed by a reliable process**, **A reliable process is one that produces true beliefs more often than false ones**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification beyond the reliable process, such as understanding the context of the belief**
