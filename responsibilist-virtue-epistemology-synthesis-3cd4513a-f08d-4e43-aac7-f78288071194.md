## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their true belief results from the exercise of intellectual character virtues**, **Intellectual character virtues include traits like open-mindedness and intellectual courage**
