# Knowledge with a No-Overriding-Defeaters Clause

**Parent:** [Can not knowing about the existence of a defeater preserve knowledge?](can-not-knowing-about-the-existence-of-a-defeater-preserve-knowledge-antithesis-f0eb447b-1b7e-4192-850e-50ee1339c02c.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no overriding defeaters for the belief at the time of assessment**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there are no overriding defeaters for the belief at the time of assessment**
