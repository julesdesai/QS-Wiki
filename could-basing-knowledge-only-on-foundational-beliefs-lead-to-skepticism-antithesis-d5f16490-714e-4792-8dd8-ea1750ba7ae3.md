# Could basing knowledge only on foundational beliefs lead to skepticism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d5f16490-714e-4792-8dd8-ea1750ba7ae3
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundationalism with Pragmatic Grounds](foundationalism-with-pragmatic-grounds-synthesis-35d5e861-b788-4e52-a02b-4488aba3b8d2.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that utilizing a narrow range of foundational beliefs might leave numerous questions unanswered, leading to skepticism. This principle together with reliance on foundational beliefs without exploring broader inquiries can result in limited understanding.**, **Consider a scenario where individuals restrict themselves to foundational beliefs without probing further. Intuitively, such restriction seems likely to diminish overall knowledge and lead to skepticism about many unknowns.**
