## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the person believes the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is formed through a reliable cognitive process, and the reliability is appropriate for the current epistemic context**
