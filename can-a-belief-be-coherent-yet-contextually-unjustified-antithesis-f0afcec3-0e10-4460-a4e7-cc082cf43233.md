# Can a belief be coherent yet contextually unjustified?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f0afcec3-0e10-4460-a4e7-cc082cf43233
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualist Coherentism](contextualist-coherentism-synthesis-156207e7-3c9d-49a2-8bb7-7097c1d027cd.md)

## Content

**Suppose an individual believes they are being watched because their neighbor always closes their curtains when seen. This belief is coherent with the individual's other paranoid beliefs.**, **However, without evidence or contextual confirmation, the belief seems unjustified even if coherent with a paranoid system.**
