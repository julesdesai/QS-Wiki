## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they can rule out relevant alternative situations where the proposition is not true**, **Belief tracking is not required for all conceivable scenarios, only relevant ones**
