## Content

**There are cases where testimonies that were initially reliable ceased to be so after new evidence emerged. Intuitively, this suggests challenges in establishing the reliability required for knowledge.**, **Suppose testimony concerns a cultural custom from antiquity that is unsupported by archaeological evidence. Intuitively, the uncertainty of historical accuracy questions the reliability of such testimony.**
