## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is not based on any false lemmas**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is based on complete and relevant information**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is justified, the belief is not based on any false lemmas, and the belief is based on complete and relevant information**
