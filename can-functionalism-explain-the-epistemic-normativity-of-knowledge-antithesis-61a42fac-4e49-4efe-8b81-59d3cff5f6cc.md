# Can functionalism explain the epistemic normativity of knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 61a42fac-4e49-4efe-8b81-59d3cff5f6cc
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Functionalism](functionalism-synthesis-2d0849d8-6d8d-49c5-822f-95e70d5e82e6.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case where two individuals both reach a conclusion due to identical functional processes, yet one violates epistemic norms. Intuitively, we either have to say that knowledge is normatively indifferent or that epistemic norms differentiate knowledge, challenging the reduction of knowledge to functional roles.**
