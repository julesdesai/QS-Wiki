# Can isolated true beliefs amount to knowledge when disconnected from broader reliable sets?

**Node ID:** 807634b3-73ac-4369-82da-d97fd53d07a3

**Terminal:** No

## Content

**Consider a case where a person holds a true belief that does not connect with any other belief they hold. The belief is isolated. Intuitively, one might judge that this belief does not constitute knowledge because it lacks broader coherence.**
