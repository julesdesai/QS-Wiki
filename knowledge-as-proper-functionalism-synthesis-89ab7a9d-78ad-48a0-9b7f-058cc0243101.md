## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A belief amounts to knowledge when it results from cognitive faculties functioning properly in an appropriate environment**, **The proper function refers to the reliable operation of cognitive processes designed to produce true beliefs**
