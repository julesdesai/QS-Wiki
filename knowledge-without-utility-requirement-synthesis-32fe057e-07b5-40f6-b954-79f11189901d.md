# Knowledge without Utility Requirement

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 32fe057e-07b5-40f6-b954-79f11189901d
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge be useless?](can-knowledge-be-useless-antithesis-8342035c-9026-400f-a9c5-75934a284046.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is not the result of manipulation**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is not the result of manipulation**
