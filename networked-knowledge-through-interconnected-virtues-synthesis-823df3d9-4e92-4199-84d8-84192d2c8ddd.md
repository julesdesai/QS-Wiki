## Content

**For a network to know a proposition, it is necessary that the network believes the proposition**, **For a network to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a network to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed through interconnected intellectual virtues**, **For a network to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is formed through interconnected intellectual virtues**
