## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in nearly all nearby possible worlds where they form the belief based on evidence, it is true**, **Caution in belief formation prevents accidental truths from being considered knowledge**
