## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief remains true in nearby possible worlds**, **A belief is considered safe if it would not easily have been false**
