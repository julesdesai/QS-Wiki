# Does contextualism lead to skepticism in everyday knowledge claims?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5c1f7fee-5569-4a89-b1f4-6ea9fc410231
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualist approach to knowledge](contextualist-approach-to-knowledge-synthesis-a36ecad8-b4cc-4b9e-8a50-84a5c5d3051f.md)

## Content

**In everyday contexts, people claim to know things without rigorous justification standards.**, **Contextualism implies that lowering standards in some contexts makes knowledge easier to claim.**, **This flexibility potentially leads to a skeptical view that challenges simple everyday knowledge claims.**, **The relaxed standards in some contexts question the reliability of everyday claimed knowledge.**
