# Is context too subjective to be reliable in knowledge assessment?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 06d3a1d3-f59e-4d0a-8720-9d670b2e7a36
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism](contextualism-synthesis-876f0403-5dcc-4837-9485-46808d567bde.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where what counts as relevant contextual factors is subject to individual interpretation. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that knowledge becomes highly subjective and inconsistent.**, **It is implausible that knowledge, which aims at truth, should be reliant on subjective contextual interpretations.**
