## Content

**A person knows a proposition if they have internally accessible justification for their belief**, **The person's justification must adequately support the belief in the proposition's truth**, **The proposition being true is a necessary condition for it to be known**, **A belief's causal connection to the truth is not required for it to qualify as knowledge**
