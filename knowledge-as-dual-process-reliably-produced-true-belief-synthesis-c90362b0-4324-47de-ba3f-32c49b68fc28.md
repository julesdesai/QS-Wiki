## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that a secondary cognitive process confirms the reliability of the primary process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process, and a secondary cognitive process confirms the reliability of the primary process**
