# Is knowledge without a precise causal link plausible?

## Content

**An individual may have a true belief through means that appear magical or non-causal, such as clairvoyance, which undermines the notion of a clear causal chain.**, **While the belief might be true and consistently reliable, the absence of a verifiable causal link seems to exclude it from being knowledge.**
