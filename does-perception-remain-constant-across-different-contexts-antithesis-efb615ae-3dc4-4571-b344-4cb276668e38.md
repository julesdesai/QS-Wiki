# Does perception remain constant across different contexts?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** efb615ae-3dc4-4571-b344-4cb276668e38
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Direct Realism](direct-realism-synthesis-dd7d3552-f5e8-48f4-9a89-f17b9eced87a.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that perception remains constant across different contexts because context-dependence affects the observation.**, **Consider a scenario in which the same sound is perceived differently in a concert hall and a busy street. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that perception is influenced by context.**
