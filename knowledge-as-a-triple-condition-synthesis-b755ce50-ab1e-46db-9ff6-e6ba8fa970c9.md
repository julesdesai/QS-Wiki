## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for it, and the proposition is true**
