# Can knowledge exist if justification comes from irrelevant evidence?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3b24caa6-fdd0-4d75-95c8-0cc1f804b63e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [No-Defeaters Theory of Knowledge](no-defeaters-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-0be8e529-9437-4b4e-96a3-c585180c1210.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person holds a true belief due to justification from irrelevant or unrelated evidence, yet no defeaters exist. Intuitively, we would judge this is not knowledge.**, **Relevance of evidence to justification is crucial for knowledge irrespective of the presence of defeaters, suggesting irrelevant justification fails to secure knowledge.**
