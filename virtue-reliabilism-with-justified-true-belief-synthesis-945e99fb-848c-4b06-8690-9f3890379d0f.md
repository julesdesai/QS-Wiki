## Content

**Knowledge is justified true belief supported by intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is guided by intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they hold a justified true belief and their intellectual virtue plays a guiding role**
