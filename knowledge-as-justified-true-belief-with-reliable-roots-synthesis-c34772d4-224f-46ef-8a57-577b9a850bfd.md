## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for their belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their justification involves awareness of the reliability of the cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have a justified true belief with awareness of the reliability of the cognitive process**
