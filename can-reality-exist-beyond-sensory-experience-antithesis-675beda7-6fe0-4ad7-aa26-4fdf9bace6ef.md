# Can reality exist beyond sensory experience?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 675beda7-6fe0-4ad7-aa26-4fdf9bace6ef
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Phenomenalism](phenomenalism-synthesis-628ba6c3-810c-40bb-af25-92e29ab1c990.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that says reality could exist independently of human perception. This principle together with the idea that reality is composed of external objects entails the possibility of reality existing beyond sensory experiences.**, **If sensory experiences are dependent on physical conditions like light or sound waves, then external objects might maintain existence independently of whether or not they are perceived.**
