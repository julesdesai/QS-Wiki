# Knowledge as Responsibly Justified True Belief

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 7a8d2afb-46ca-4fd8-aad2-98edd0d3ace3
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Are all responsible beliefs knowledge?](are-all-responsible-beliefs-knowledge-antithesis-044a8463-00d8-4fae-a25b-4fd6464f58c2.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have epistemic justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their justification is the result of intellectual responsibility**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have epistemic justification, the proposition is true, and their justification results from intellectual responsibility**
