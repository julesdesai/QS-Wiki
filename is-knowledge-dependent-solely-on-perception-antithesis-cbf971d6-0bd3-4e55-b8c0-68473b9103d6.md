# Is knowledge dependent solely on perception?

## Content

**A plausible principle is that knowledge can be attained purely through rational reflection without any perceptual input.**, **Consider a scenario where a mathematician arrives at a new theorem through logical deduction while blindfolded with ear plugs. In this scenario, intuitively, the mathematician has knowledge of the theorem despite no sensory perception involvement.**
