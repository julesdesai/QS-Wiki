# Is knowledge simply about belief coherence?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9462777d-7910-4388-9407-c5f0600a8d8c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Coherence Theory](pragmatic-coherence-theory-synthesis-0a69366a-ab49-4b15-a3e1-b772859c47c7.md)

## Content

**A person can have a coherent set of beliefs without those beliefs being true about the actual world.**, **A person can hold a false belief that logically coheres with their other beliefs.**, **If beliefs are pragmatically justified merely through coherence with action-guiding beliefs, then false or misguided actions could still seem justified.**
