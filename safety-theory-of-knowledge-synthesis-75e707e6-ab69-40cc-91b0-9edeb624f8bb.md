## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief in the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if a person believes a proposition, then the belief could not have easily been false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have a true belief that is safely true**
