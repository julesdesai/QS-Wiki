# Do certain reliable processes ignore pivotal evidence while forming beliefs?

**Parent:** [Process Reliabilism with Evidence Sensitivity](process-reliabilism-with-evidence-sensitivity-synthesis-e3e15d29-f0bc-4d87-b0aa-fbeab510c604.md)

## Content

**Suppose a situation where the process producing a belief is reliable except when critical evidence is overlooked. Intuitively, such overlooked evidence suggests the resulting belief is not knowledge.**, **A reliable process can produce beliefs that fail to capture knowledge by neglecting crucial evidence even if it's generally effective.**
