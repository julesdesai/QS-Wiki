## Content

**A belief constitutes knowledge if it rests on sufficient evidence**, **Sufficient evidence must be appropriately linked to the truth of the belief**, **Knowledge requires that evidence is not undermined by any true defeater**, **Defeasibility ensures that accidental truths do not constitute knowledge**
