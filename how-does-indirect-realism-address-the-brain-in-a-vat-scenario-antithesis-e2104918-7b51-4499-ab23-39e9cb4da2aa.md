# How does indirect realism address the brain-in-a-vat scenario?

**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Indirect Realism with Contextual Perception](indirect-realism-with-contextual-perception-synthesis-77e2d877-90d5-4b41-9238-09aba0491b6a.md)

## Content

**The brain-in-a-vat scenario illustrates that one's mental representations, despite being internally coherent, can be completely disconnected from external reality.**, **In the brain-in-a-vat scenario, mental representations may correspond to a constructed reality but not to the actual external world.**, **Indirect realism implies knowledge requires mental representations to correspond to reality, but this requirement is challenged if reality can be entirely manufactured artificially.**
