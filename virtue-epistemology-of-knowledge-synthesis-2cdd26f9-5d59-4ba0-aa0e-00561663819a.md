# Virtue Epistemology of Knowledge

**Parent:** [Does truth condition alone suffice to validate a reliable process?](does-truth-condition-alone-suffice-to-validate-a-reliable-process-antithesis-b83376a5-8a96-4003-914f-fbf0bee04e86.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief results from the exercise of their intellectual virtues**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for the belief, the proposition is true, and the belief results from the exercise of their intellectual virtues**
