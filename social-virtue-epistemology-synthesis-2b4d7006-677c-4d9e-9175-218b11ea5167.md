## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a group to know a proposition, it is necessary that the group's belief in the proposition is formed through a set of intellectual virtues distributed among group members**, **For a group to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is true and the belief is formed through distributed intellectual virtues within the group**
