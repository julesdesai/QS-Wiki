## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **Knowledge cannot be analyzed in simpler terms like belief or justification**, **Knowledge itself is the fundamental epistemic concept**
