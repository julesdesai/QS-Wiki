# Knowledge as No Defeaters

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 15ace245-1d1a-456b-b006-03ec74c986dc
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is sensitivity compatible with memory-based beliefs?](is-sensitivity-compatible-with-memory-based-beliefs-antithesis-b1690e3e-0ccd-42a0-892b-cc94d3da4c7c.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there be no true propositions which if added would lead them to withhold belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there are no defeaters**
