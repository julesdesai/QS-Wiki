## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that justification is supported by communal practices or validated personal verification for empirical science**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification aligns with communal or personal epistemic norms**
