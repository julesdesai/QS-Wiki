## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **Knowledge is belief that tracks the truth across nearby possible worlds**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief is true in the actual world and if it were false, then they would not believe it**
