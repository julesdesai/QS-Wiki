# Do unobservable infinite chains undermine justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 34838c9c-9bd5-4459-b3d3-821168f41ee8
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitist Coherentism](infinitist-coherentism-synthesis-2a4efbd0-0f1f-4a01-bfa6-1ecf016eb1e0.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which the reasons justifying a belief are part of an infinite chain that is unobservable. Intuitively, we either have to say that unobservable chains can justify beliefs or that they cannot. If we say they can, then such justification lacks transparency. If we say they cannot, then unobtainable parts of the chain fail to provide justification.**
