## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For knowledge, it is necessary that in the contextually relevant possible worlds the person believes the proposition in a similar manner and it is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and in the contextually relevant worlds the belief is true**
