# Can a belief be justified without coherence or internalist reason?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5a7901fa-0ae7-468b-a159-aebb7a0addc4
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Hybrid Coherentist-Internalist View](hybrid-coherentist-internalist-view-synthesis-ffa44d27-7377-4f6d-8451-515975b92108.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that a belief can be justified solely based on coherence or internal reasons if it is isolated from empirical evidence.**, **Consider a belief held by a person in isolation from any external input. Intuitively, we would judge that such a belief lacks adequate justification even though it might cohere with other beliefs or have internal reasons.**
