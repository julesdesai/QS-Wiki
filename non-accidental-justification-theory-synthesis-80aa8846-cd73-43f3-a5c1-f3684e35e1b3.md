**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief's truth is not a result of accidental factors**, **A belief is non-accidental if its truth results from reliable methods of belief formation**
