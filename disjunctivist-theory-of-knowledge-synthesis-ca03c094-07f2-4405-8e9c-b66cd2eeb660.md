# Disjunctivist Theory of Knowledge

**Parent:** [Can a person have knowledge without practical consequences?](can-a-person-have-knowledge-without-practical-consequences-antithesis-fa568c8c-23a0-45cd-a112-d9befcff1747.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their experience provides direct contact with the fact**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their experience provides direct contact with the fact**
