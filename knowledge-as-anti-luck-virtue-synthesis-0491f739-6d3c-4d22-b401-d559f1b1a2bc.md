# Knowledge as Anti-Luck Virtue

**Parent:** [Can luck contribute to knowledge despite the exercise of intellectual virtue?](can-luck-contribute-to-knowledge-despite-the-exercise-of-intellectual-virtue-antithesis-74c102bd-47af-4e40-b9cd-7b321ca10009.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed by intellectual virtue and is free from the influence of luck**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is formed by intellectual virtue without luck**
