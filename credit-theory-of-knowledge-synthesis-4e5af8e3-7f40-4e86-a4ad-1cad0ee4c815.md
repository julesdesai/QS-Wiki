## Content

**Knowledge is true belief for which credit is attributable to the believer's intellectual abilities**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is reached through an exercise of their intellectual abilities**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe it, the proposition is true, and their intellectual abilities are predominantly responsible for the true belief**
