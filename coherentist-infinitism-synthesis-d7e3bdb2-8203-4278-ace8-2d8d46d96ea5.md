# Coherentist Infinitism

**Parent:** [Does Infinitism overlook the epistemic role of coherence?](does-infinitism-overlook-the-epistemic-role-of-coherence-antithesis-78f04973-aea0-4013-b400-00921cc52dbd.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is supported by an infinite chain of reasons**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief system is coherent**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is supported by an infinite chain of reasons, and the belief system is coherent**
