## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief seems correct without counter-evidence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that seeming provides justification**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is true and the belief seems correct**
