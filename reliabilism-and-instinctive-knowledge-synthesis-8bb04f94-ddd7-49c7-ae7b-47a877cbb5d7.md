# Reliabilism and Instinctive Knowledge

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 8bb04f94-ddd7-49c7-ae7b-47a877cbb5d7
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge be purely instinctive?](can-knowledge-be-purely-instinctive-antithesis-c4e8e322-b6ab-454b-ad2e-988a730c30a6.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief results from a reliable cognitive process**, **Instinctive behavior can be considered a reliable cognitive process if it generally leads to true beliefs**
