# Does perception necessarily involve knowing if reality is inherently subjective?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7fb15fcd-09f2-42bc-9953-37c406f38658
**Depth:** 2
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Direct Realism](direct-realism-thesis-65da2b06-0c60-4e66-b428-771d2431fe55.md)

## Content

**Subjective aspects of perception, such as color experiences varying by individual, suggest perception is not a direct apprehension of objective features.**, **Cultural and linguistic factors can influence perception, implying it contains subjective components.**, **Intuitively, if it were direct, such variability should not exist given an objective reality to perceive.**
