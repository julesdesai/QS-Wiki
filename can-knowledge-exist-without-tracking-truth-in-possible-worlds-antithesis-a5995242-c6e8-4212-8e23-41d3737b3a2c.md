# Can knowledge exist without tracking truth in possible worlds?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a5995242-c6e8-4212-8e23-41d3737b3a2c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Truth-Tracking Theory](truth-tracking-theory-synthesis-c14e1fed-7ecf-480a-90a0-7623f2270956.md)

## Content

**It is possible for a person to believe a proposition in the actual world because it is true, but this belief might not track the truth in nearby possible worlds.**, **A person might still believe a false proposition in some possible worlds, yet still be considered to have knowledge in the actual world.**
