# Are cognitive limits essential to the concept of justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 11edfb23-1c0a-40bf-9892-c377db8de8ef
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism Without Memory Constraints](infinitism-without-memory-constraints-synthesis-df5a4028-0f5d-4102-8a53-7b53596c5281.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person attempts to justify their beliefs through limitless steps beyond their cognitive grasp. Intuitively, justification seems unsound without consideration of cognitive limits.**, **It is implausible that justification divorced from cognitive reality can sustain the concept of knowledge.**
