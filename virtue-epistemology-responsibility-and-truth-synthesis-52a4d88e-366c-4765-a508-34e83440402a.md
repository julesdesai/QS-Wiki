## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed through epistemically responsible methods**, **A belief is formed through epistemically responsible methods if it results from intellectual virtues like open-mindedness and attentiveness**
