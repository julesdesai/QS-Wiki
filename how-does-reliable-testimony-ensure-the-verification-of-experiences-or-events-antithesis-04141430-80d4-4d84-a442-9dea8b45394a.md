# How does reliable testimony ensure the verification of experiences or events?

## Content

**Consider a situation where someone reports an encounter with a rare phenomenon, but no others can verify it through testimony. Intuitively, this raises skepticism about the reliability of such knowledge.**, **Suppose multiple conflicting testimonies exist about the weather on a particular day, with no empirical data to resolve the differences. Intuitively, the reliability of such testimony is questionable for knowledge.**
