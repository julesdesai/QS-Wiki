# Can someone know just by meeting their own stakes?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3a2a1151-2d8d-4e9a-9c34-8a76031fafa5
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Subject-Sensitive Invariantism](subject-sensitive-invariantism-synthesis-a38d2af6-3ec4-421c-a558-c594d0cbc8b7.md)

## Content

**Consider a person with low personal stakes in believing a true proposition, while another person with higher stakes doubts it. Intuitively, the truth-value of a proposition should not depend on individual stakes but rather on independent factors like evidence and reality.**, **It is plausible to assert that knowledge should remain constant irrespective of individual stake variations, suggesting that truth and belief suffice for knowledge irrespective of personal stakes.**
