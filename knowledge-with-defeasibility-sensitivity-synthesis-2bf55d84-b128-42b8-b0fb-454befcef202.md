# Knowledge with Defeasibility Sensitivity

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 2bf55d84-b128-42b8-b0fb-454befcef202
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can justified true belief be knowledge without defeating conditions?](can-justified-true-belief-be-knowledge-without-defeating-conditions-antithesis-77753db4-e84c-456d-81f6-289f7c98c2c5.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they are sensitive to potential defeaters**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, the proposition is true, and they are sensitive to potential defeaters**
