# Knowledge as Minimal Coherentism

**Parent:** [Is it possible for a true belief to be isolated yet still count as knowledge?](is-it-possible-for-a-true-belief-to-be-isolated-yet-still-count-as-knowledge-antithesis-b3044c47-647a-4fb1-8b65-bb731269ec25.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief has minimal coherence with some other beliefs held by the person**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief has minimal coherence with some other beliefs**
