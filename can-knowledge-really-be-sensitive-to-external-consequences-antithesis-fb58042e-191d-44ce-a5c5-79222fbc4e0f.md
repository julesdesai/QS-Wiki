# Can knowledge really be sensitive to external consequences?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** fb58042e-191d-44ce-a5c5-79222fbc4e0f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Encroachment Theory](pragmatic-encroachment-theory-synthesis-66e6d98a-2899-4b80-b05b-15066f046ddd.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person, C, knows that a bridge is safe to cross based on solid engineering evidence. The stakes are low because crossing the bridge saves only a few minutes.**, **Imagine the stakes become very high because crossing becomes essential to saving someone’s life.**, **It seems strange that C's knowledge about the bridge's safety would change with the importance of the act of crossing, even though the evidence remains the same.**
