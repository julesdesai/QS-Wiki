# Knowledge as Reliability

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can beliefs supported by misleading evidence still constitute knowledge?](can-beliefs-supported-by-misleading-evidence-still-constitute-knowledge-antithesis-8aefb9fe-516b-4af9-bda0-b4db8a8bd9bb.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have a true belief formed by a reliable process**
