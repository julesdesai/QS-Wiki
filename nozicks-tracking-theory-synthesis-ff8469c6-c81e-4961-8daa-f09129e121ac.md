## Content

**A person knows a proposition if their belief tracks the truth both in reality and in close counterfactual situations**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **The belief must not result from cognitive malfunction, even if it occasionally leads to true beliefs**
