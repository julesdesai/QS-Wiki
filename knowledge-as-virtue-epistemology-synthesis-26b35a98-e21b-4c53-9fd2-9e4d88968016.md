## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A person is justified in believing a proposition if and only if their belief arises from intellectual virtues**, **Intellectual virtues are character traits that reliably lead to true beliefs**
