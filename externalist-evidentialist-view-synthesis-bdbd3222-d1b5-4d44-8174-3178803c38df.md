# Externalist Evidentialist View

**Parent:** [Does relying on evidence always imply access to that evidence?](does-relying-on-evidence-always-imply-access-to-that-evidence-antithesis-4192957a-81e7-4eca-b81a-10cf7c654076.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there exists evidence supporting the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is not necessary that they have direct access to the evidence supporting the proposition**
