# Is coherence sufficient for justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 001caaf2-af8f-4b83-a473-992dbe3f0416
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism with Justified Belief](coherentism-with-justified-belief-synthesis-01e10c3b-52a8-4097-b02c-3f010fc56355.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which an individual's beliefs about astrology are internally coherent but entirely based on a pseudoscience. Intuitively, we would judge that these beliefs, while coherent, are not justified.**, **It is implausible that coherence alone suffices for a belief to be justified regardless of the truth or evidence of the beliefs.**
