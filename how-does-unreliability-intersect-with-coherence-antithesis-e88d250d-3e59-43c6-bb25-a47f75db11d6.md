# How does unreliability intersect with coherence?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e88d250d-3e59-43c6-bb25-a47f75db11d6
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilist Coherentism](reliabilist-coherentism-synthesis-d6c1c642-66ac-40b0-82ca-187cf7250017.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where an unreliable process leads to a coherent belief system. Intuitively, coherence alone does not rescue the system from being unjustified due to the unreliability of its source.**, **It is implausible that coherence can compensate for the foundational unreliability of a belief-generating process in terms of justification.**
