## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed through sound reasoning**, **Sound reasoning is reasoning that adheres to logical principles and is free from fallacies**
