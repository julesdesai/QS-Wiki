# Does justification require more than just reliability of processes?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e12097df-22ea-4b7e-a5b4-bc4bbd9971a8
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism bases knowledge on reliable processes](reliabilism-bases-knowledge-on-reliable-processes-synthesis-e8668809-0baa-4531-bd9d-063ab7893b2b.md)

## Content

**A reliable belief-forming process might lead to a belief that lacks proper justification due to factors such as luck or deception.**, **It is implausible to consider a belief justified if influenced by deceptive factors, even if the belief-forming process is generally reliable.**
