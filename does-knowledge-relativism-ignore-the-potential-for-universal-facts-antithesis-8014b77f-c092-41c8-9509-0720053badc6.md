# Does knowledge relativism ignore the potential for universal facts?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 8014b77f-c092-41c8-9509-0720053badc6
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Epistemic Relativism](epistemic-relativism-synthesis-8a36f430-fbe7-4356-93bb-efe42975c670.md)

## Content

**Suppose there are universal facts about the world, such as laws of physics, that remain constant across different epistemic frameworks.**, **Epistemic relativism implies that knowledge of such facts is contingent upon community norms.**, **If these facts hold universally, the relativist approach struggles to account for knowledge that transcends culturally or epistemically constructed boundaries.**
