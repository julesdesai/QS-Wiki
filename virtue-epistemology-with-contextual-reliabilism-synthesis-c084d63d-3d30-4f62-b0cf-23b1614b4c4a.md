# Virtue Epistemology with Contextual Reliabilism

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge result from unreliable virtue?](can-knowledge-result-from-unreliable-virtue-antithesis-198a5c93-5914-4059-8ade-edb3b7cbf293.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief to result from an intellectual virtue that is contextually reliable**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief results from an intellectual virtue that is contextually reliable**
