## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief aligns with the epistemic standards of the context**, **Epistemic standards vary with different contexts and affect what counts as knowledge**
