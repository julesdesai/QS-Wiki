# Can we have knowledge with safe beliefs in distant yet relevantly similar worlds?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a0bf028f-5837-40c5-b9a8-ed45085a69ad
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Safety Condition View](safety-condition-view-synthesis-739cc2e2-5111-4640-9af6-f9324f258620.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person has a safe belief that it is raining because they see water on the ground and people with umbrellas. Intuitively, we judge that seeing people with umbrellas often leads to true beliefs about rain.**, **There could be a distant possible world where it is a sunny day but people are still using umbrellas due to a cultural practice, disqualifying safety due to erroneous belief in distant scenarios.**
