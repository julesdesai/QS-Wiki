## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition when it is true and not believe it when it is false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe it, it is true, and their belief tracks the truth**
