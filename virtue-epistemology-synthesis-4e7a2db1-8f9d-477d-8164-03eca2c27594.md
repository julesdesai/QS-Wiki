## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person believes the proposition**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is a result of an intellectual virtue**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is true, they believe it, and their belief is a result of an intellectual virtue**
