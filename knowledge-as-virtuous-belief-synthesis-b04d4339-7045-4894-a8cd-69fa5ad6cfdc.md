# Knowledge as Virtuous Belief

**Parent:** [Does the safety condition account for all necessary conditions of knowledge?](does-the-safety-condition-account-for-all-necessary-conditions-of-knowledge-antithesis-617fb7c7-4174-42a0-b386-afb8b07ea6b8.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed through intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is formed through intellectual virtue**
