# Does Pragmatic Infinitism lead to skepticism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 94675225-b877-473c-a4b8-1a478aba8dc0
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Infinitism](pragmatic-infinitism-synthesis-954ef6f8-808a-47cf-95ad-717cbaeffb04.md)

## Content

**If justification requires an infinite chain of reasons, then in practice it may be impossible to achieve justified beliefs.**, **If justified beliefs are practically impossible, then it leads to a form of skepticism about knowledge.**
