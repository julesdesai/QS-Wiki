# Does virtue epistemology overemphasize the individual at the expense of the environment?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2290244a-dd42-4907-b24d-64d257996dbd
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-db8e4632-83fc-4e14-a2b9-f64cf1d5bb50.md)

## Content

**Consider situations where knowledge depends heavily on environmental factors rather than individual cognitive virtues, such as using reliable navigational tools.**, **This implies that external factors, outside of an individual's control, play a significant role in knowledge acquisition, challenging the necessity of individual virtues.**
