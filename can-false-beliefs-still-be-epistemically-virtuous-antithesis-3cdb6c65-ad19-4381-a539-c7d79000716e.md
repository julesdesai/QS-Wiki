# Can false beliefs still be epistemically virtuous?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3cdb6c65-ad19-4381-a539-c7d79000716e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology Summary](virtue-epistemology-summary-synthesis-10eec969-c917-4d6d-bc9d-ac9f02dc3f98.md)

## Content

**A person could maintain a belief through rigorous empirical investigation, yet the proposition in question is ultimately false.**, **The intellectual virtues employed in forming the belief do not prevent the possibility of falsehood.**, **Virtuous processes might lead to well-justified beliefs that fail to be true, complicating the virtue epistemology perspective.**
