## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person believes the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief arises from an instrumentally valuable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient if the belief promotes practical or theoretical goals effectively**, **An intellectual virtue is a cognitive faculty or character trait that reliably promotes valuable goals**, **Knowledge is linked to the instrumental value of belief-forming processes**
