# Can coherence be objective?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 99c60806-1673-4ed2-a524-b3ba8a54fc36
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherent Phenomenalism](coherent-phenomenalism-synthesis-2365a190-e3fc-4d37-a83b-defd0eadffa2.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have two scientists with coherent perceptual webs interpreting data differently due to subjective biases. Intuitively, coherence in perceptions does not guarantee objectivity in reaching knowledge about the natural world.**, **It is implausible that subjective interpretations, even when coherent, can be the sole basis for objective knowledge.**
