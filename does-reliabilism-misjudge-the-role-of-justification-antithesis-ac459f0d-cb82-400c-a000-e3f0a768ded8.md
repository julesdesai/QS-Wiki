# Does reliabilism misjudge the role of justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ac459f0d-cb82-400c-a000-e3f0a768ded8
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilist Theory of Knowledge](reliabilist-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-e6fc2437-0f22-487d-91cb-ba903a4e1b1f.md)

## Content

**An individual confidently believes in a particular stock's rise due to reading a reliable financial report.**, **The belief comes true but the rise was due to market manipulation unknown to the individual.**, **The belief was true but not truly justified because its formation relied on incomplete information.**
