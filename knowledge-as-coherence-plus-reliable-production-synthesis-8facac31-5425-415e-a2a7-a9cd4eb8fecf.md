# Knowledge as Coherence Plus Reliable Production

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief system is produced by a reliable process that ensures coherence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is coherently produced by a reliable process**
