# Knowledge as Reliabilism

**Parent:** [Could conflicting self-evident beliefs be possible?](could-conflicting-self-evident-beliefs-be-possible-antithesis-4fc3d138-1b43-46b3-9814-b25c3c896de2.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification is produced by a reliable cognitive process**
