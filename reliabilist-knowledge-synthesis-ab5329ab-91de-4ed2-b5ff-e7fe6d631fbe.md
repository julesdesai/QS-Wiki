# Reliabilist Knowledge

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** ab5329ab-91de-4ed2-b5ff-e7fe6d631fbe
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can genuine knowledge exist without coherence?](can-genuine-knowledge-exist-without-coherence-antithesis-fdd59509-530c-48b7-9b79-9501c9f30899.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is not necessary that the belief coheres with a system of beliefs**
