## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in nearby possible worlds, if the proposition were false, the belief would not arise**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief would not arise in nearby false scenarios**
