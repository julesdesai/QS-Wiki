## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief to result from intellectual virtue**, **Intellectual virtue involves the exercise of cognitive faculties in an excellent way that generally leads to truth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief results from intellectual virtue**
