# Do intellectual virtues necessitate infinite justification chains?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 973d8c5d-3845-487d-ac3c-6feb200d3dc3
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology Infinitism](virtue-epistemology-infinitism-synthesis-700b1be0-40ec-4861-9f40-25460b6be3e1.md)

## Content

**Intellectual virtues are often understood to involve reliability, open-mindedness, and insight rather than infinite chains of reasoning.**, **Suppose intellectual virtues require practicality and efficiency, which might conflict with the idea of maintaining infinite chains of reasons.**, **It is plausible that intellectual virtues support sufficiency of reasons rather than infinitude.**
