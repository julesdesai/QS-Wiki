# Does actual infinity matter for practical knowledge claims?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 072ceba0-5b53-47f4-83e6-5178e96e7a2d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Finite Infinitism Summary](finite-infinitism-summary-synthesis-cf1727db-af05-4f36-9758-13f464b5dd32.md)

## Content

**Consider the claim that knowledge requires potentially infinite justification. Here, it seems that real-world knowledge claims almost never involve such considerations.**, **There is a plausible principle that practical knowledge requires actionable beliefs. Actionable beliefs don't seem to require an infinite regress of reasons, making such justification impractical for knowledge.**
