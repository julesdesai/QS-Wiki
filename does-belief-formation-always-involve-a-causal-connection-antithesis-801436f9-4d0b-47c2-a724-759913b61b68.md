## Content

**Consider a situation where a person P arrives at a true belief randomly or by chance, without any causal process linking the belief to its truth. Intuitively, P does not have knowledge in this case.**, **Not all belief formation involves a discernible causal connection to the truth of the proposition, raising doubts about the necessity of such a process for knowledge.**
