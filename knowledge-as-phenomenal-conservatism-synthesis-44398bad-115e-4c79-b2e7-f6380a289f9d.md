## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that it seems to the person that the proposition is true, and it is indeed true**, **Seeming states can serve as a basis for knowledge if they are both true and compelling**
