**Parent:** [Does the requirement of truth compromise the hypothetical nature of chains?](does-the-requirement-of-truth-compromise-the-hypothetical-nature-of-chains-antithesis-c1e36a0f-1833-46a6-8b87-e8dd6f7960a8.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification involves a primary infinite, non-repeating chain of reasons where each link is plausible**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there exists a primary infinite, non-repeating chain of plausible reasons justifying the belief**
