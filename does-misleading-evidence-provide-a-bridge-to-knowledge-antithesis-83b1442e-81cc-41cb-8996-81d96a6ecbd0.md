# Does misleading evidence provide a bridge to knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 83b1442e-81cc-41cb-8996-81d96a6ecbd0
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Grounding Knowledge in Evidence](grounding-knowledge-in-evidence-synthesis-000c43a5-6d20-4fdc-9093-7ec5ca2a4c8d.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that misleading evidence constitutes a legitimate pathway to knowledge.**, **Consider a scenario where someone is led to believe a proposition by evidence that misrepresents the facts but incidentally leads to a true belief. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that the belief is not knowledge.**
