# Sensitivity Condition for Knowledge

## Content

**Knowledge requires an evidential connection to the truth**, **A belief is knowledge if it is supported by sufficient evidence**, **The evidence must be appropriately tied to the truth of the proposition**, **A belief is sensitive if it would not be held if it were false**, **For knowledge, if the proposition were false, the subject would not believe it**
