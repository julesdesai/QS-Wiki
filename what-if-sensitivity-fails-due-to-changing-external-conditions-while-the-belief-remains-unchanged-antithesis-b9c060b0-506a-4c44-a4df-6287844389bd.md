# What if sensitivity fails due to changing external conditions while the belief remains unchanged?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b9c060b0-506a-4c44-a4df-6287844389bd
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Sensitivity Condition on Knowledge](sensitivity-condition-on-knowledge-synthesis-bfa2da3c-dc32-483c-bef7-ea8100b18339.md)

## Content

**Imagine a storm that could have disrupted an outdoor clock while a person uses it to form a belief about the time. The storm dissipates quickly and the person does not know about it.**, **If the storm had disrupted the clock leading to incorrect time, the person would still believe the proposition under the false scenario. This scenario illustrates the failure of the sensitivity condition.**
