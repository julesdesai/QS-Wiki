## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a generally reliable process**, **A person's belief must be true for them to have knowledge**, **A generally reliable process is one that produces more true beliefs than false ones**, **The truth of the belief should not be coincidental with respect to the process**
