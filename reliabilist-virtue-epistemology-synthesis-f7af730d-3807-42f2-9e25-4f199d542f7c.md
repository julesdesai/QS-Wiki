## Content

**Knowledge is a true belief arising from reliable virtues**, **Intellectual virtues are reliable cognitive faculties, skills, or traits**, **The reliability of these intellectual virtues is sufficient for the formation of true beliefs**, **The belief must be true because of the agent’s reliable intellectual virtue**, **Moral considerations are not necessary for the reliability of intellectual virtues**
