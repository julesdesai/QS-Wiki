**Parent:** [Does the notion of a defeater adequately address all intuitions about knowledge?](does-the-notion-of-a-defeater-adequately-address-all-intuitions-about-knowledge-antithesis-c0eede94-0c41-4996-ad5f-52c8c3452695.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is the result of an epistemic virtue or skill**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is the result of an epistemic virtue or skill**
