## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the truth of their belief is not due to luck**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the truth is not due to luck, and the proposition is true**
