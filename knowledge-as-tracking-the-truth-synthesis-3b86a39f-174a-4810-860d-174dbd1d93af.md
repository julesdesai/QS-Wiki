# Knowledge as Tracking the Truth

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 3b86a39f-174a-4810-860d-174dbd1d93af
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does mere pragmatic benefit suffice for knowledge?](does-mere-pragmatic-benefit-suffice-for-knowledge-antithesis-34603a96-31f4-403a-894d-91d4675c4f9c.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were true, the person would believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and both if the proposition were false, they would not believe it, and if the proposition were true, they would believe it**
