**Parent:** [Does knowledge require more than just intellectual virtues and truth?](does-knowledge-require-more-than-just-intellectual-virtues-and-truth-antithesis-bfff7b28-fbe2-43f3-8b40-479e0269e409.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief result from both the person's intellectual virtues and their responsible reflection on available evidence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief results from intellectual virtues and responsible reflection on evidence**
