**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is a sufficient body of evidence supporting the belief**, **The evidence must confer a high probability upon the proposition**
