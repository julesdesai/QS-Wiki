**Parent:** [Is intellectual virtue always necessary for knowing simple truths?](is-intellectual-virtue-always-necessary-for-knowing-simple-truths-antithesis-7691a933-9ed4-4804-bdcc-95acb6a644b5.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification for the belief is supported by an infinite and non-repeating chain of reasons**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification is supported by an infinite and non-repeating chain of reasons**
