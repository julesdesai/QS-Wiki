## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is justified**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that potential defeaters involving social groups or context are considered**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the belief is justified, the proposition is true, and social contextual defeaters are absent**
