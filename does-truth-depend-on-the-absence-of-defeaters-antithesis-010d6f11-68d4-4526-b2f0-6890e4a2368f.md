# Does truth depend on the absence of defeaters?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 010d6f11-68d4-4526-b2f0-6890e4a2368f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [No Defeaters Theory](no-defeaters-theory-synthesis-e6eeb208-311d-4d09-a784-60d3e95f478f.md)

## Content

**Knowledge requires the truth of the proposition.**, **The truth of a proposition is independent of whether there are defeaters.**, **A proposition may be true even in cases where a defeater exists; knowledge, therefore, cannot solely depend on the absence of a defeater.**
