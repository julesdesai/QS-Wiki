# Knowledge with Contextual Reliability

**Parent:** [Does practical usefulness always accompany reliable knowledge formation?](does-practical-usefulness-always-accompany-reliable-knowledge-formation-antithesis-25cbfa72-0ddd-4856-a880-1fa3a4ce96dd.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed through a reliable process contextual to the knowledge area**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is formed through a reliable process contextual to the knowledge area**
