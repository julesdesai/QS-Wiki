# Knowledge as Context-Independent Justified True Belief

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** adec3de9-4d44-4d00-802c-e6ea8cbff36e
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does contextual justification challenge universal truth?](does-contextual-justification-challenge-universal-truth-antithesis-a0a4bd52-ed0a-4322-9dec-e60438e63398.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification for the belief holds universally rather than being context-dependent**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification for the belief holds universally**
