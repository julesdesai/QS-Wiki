# Knowledge as Contextualism

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the standards for knowing are determined by the context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the contextually appropriate standards for knowing are met**
