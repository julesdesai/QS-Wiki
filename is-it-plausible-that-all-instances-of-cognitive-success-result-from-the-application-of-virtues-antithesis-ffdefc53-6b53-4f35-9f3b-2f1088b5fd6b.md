# Is it plausible that all instances of cognitive success result from the application of virtues?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ffdefc53-6b53-4f35-9f3b-2f1088b5fd6b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-7681d6b8-18d3-4c19-95f1-d061b70d0c81.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that all cognitive successes are due to virtues when some cases result from intuition or insight without deliberate virtue application.**, **Cognitive virtues involve active engagement, which contrasts with passive instances where the correct belief may just "pop into mind" without conscious employment of virtues.**
