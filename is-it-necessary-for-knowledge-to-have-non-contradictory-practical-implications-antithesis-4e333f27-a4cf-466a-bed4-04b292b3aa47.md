# Is it necessary for knowledge to have non-contradictory practical implications?

## Content

**Suppose a person believes exercising daily is beneficial and pursues it, yet experiences joint deterioration due to a specific medical condition.**, **Here, a true belief leads to practical implications that contradict health outcomes, despite knowledge appearing genuine.**, **A plausible principle states that practical implications need not align perfectly with the truth of every practical belief.**
