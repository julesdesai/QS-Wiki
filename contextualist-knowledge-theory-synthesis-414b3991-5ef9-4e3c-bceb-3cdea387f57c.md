## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the standard of justification varies with context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have the proposition be true, and meet the contextually variable standard of justification**
