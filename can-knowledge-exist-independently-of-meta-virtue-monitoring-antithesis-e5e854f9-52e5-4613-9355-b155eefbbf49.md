# Can knowledge exist independently of meta-virtue monitoring?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e5e854f9-52e5-4613-9355-b155eefbbf49
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Meta-Virtue Epistemology](meta-virtue-epistemology-synthesis-b3bd54fd-8ed8-46f5-92c6-aa5fa4222616.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that knowledge is simply a matter of having a true belief obtained through reliable means. This principle together with a lack of meta-cognitive involvement entails that meta-virtues may be irrelevant to consider someone knowledgeable.**
