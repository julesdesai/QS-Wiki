# Knowledge as Minimalist Justification Theory

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 5e4c3538-fa43-48d8-8dcb-e5d5d2882fcc
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can false beliefs ever track truth?](can-false-beliefs-ever-track-truth-antithesis-c664d870-cd01-4ed9-84bc-a093fe69399a.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have some form of minimal justification or support**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, it is true, and possess minimal but adequate justification**
