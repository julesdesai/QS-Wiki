## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **Knowledge requires the belief to be produced by a reliable process**, **The reliable process must not yield the true belief due to a lucky guess**
