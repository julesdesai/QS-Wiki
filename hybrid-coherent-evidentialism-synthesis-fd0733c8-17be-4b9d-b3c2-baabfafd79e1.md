## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief to be bolstered by empirical evidence as well as coherence with other relevant beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is bolstered by empirical evidence, and the belief coheres with other relevant beliefs**
