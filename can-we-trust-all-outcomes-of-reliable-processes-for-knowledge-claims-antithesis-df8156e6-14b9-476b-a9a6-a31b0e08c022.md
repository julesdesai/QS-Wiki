# Can we trust all outcomes of reliable processes for knowledge claims?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** df8156e6-14b9-476b-a9a6-a31b0e08c022
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism](reliabilism-synthesis-27039b77-724d-4113-8b7b-b992ae688652.md)

## Content

**Suppose a reliable cognitive process generally results in true beliefs but occasionally produces false ones.**, **A belief formed during one of these false outcomes challenges whether the definition of reliability necessarily aligns with knowledge.**, **Intuitively, we might hesitate to claim knowledge when aware of such process fallibility.**
