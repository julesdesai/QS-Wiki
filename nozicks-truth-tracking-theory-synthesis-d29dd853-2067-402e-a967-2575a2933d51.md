## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief tracks the truth**, **A belief tracks the truth if it would not be held were it false and would be held were it true**
