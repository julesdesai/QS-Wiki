# Knowledge as Safety Reliabilism

**Parent:** [Can one have knowledge if similar situations yield mostly false beliefs?](can-one-have-knowledge-if-similar-situations-yield-mostly-false-beliefs-antithesis-fdde1603-3842-4d31-bcf5-2ee1b6de973b.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have a belief-forming process that leads to true beliefs and avoids easily leading to false beliefs**, **A belief-forming process is reliable if it does not easily produce false beliefs under similar conditions**
