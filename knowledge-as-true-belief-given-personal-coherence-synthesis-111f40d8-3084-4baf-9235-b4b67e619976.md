# Knowledge as True Belief Given Personal Coherence

**Parent:** [Can an individual defeater of social beliefs still possess knowledge?](can-an-individual-defeater-of-social-beliefs-still-possess-knowledge-antithesis-c06fddff-ad31-44e9-a1ad-fbbb6fbff251.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is required that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres with their total web of beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief coheres with their total web of beliefs**
