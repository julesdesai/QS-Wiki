# Knowledge as Contextual Coherence

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Can a belief system be coherent without foundational beliefs?](can-a-belief-system-be-coherent-without-foundational-beliefs-antithesis-7fa671d4-ad95-4c52-ad4d-f5a4c6293716.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief coheres with a relevant context of beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief coheres with a relevant context of beliefs**
