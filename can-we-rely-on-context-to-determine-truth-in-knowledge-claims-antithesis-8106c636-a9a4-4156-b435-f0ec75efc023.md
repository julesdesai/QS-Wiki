# Can we rely on context to determine truth in knowledge claims?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 8106c636-a9a4-4156-b435-f0ec75efc023
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism Summary](contextualism-summary-synthesis-d6d485b4-dc75-41a4-a1b0-f6bf593abba7.md)

## Content

**A person knows a proposition if the proposition is true presents difficulties if truth itself is context-dependent, which might make it elusive.**, **If truth varies with context, then it could lead to contradictory truths across different contexts.**
