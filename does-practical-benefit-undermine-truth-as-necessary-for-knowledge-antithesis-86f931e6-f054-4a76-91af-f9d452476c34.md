# Does practical benefit undermine truth as necessary for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 86f931e6-f054-4a76-91af-f9d452476c34
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatism Summary](pragmatism-summary-synthesis-ddc2fd27-140a-48fe-b697-40a3c8c11bfc.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person believes a false proposition but finds practical success in acting on that belief. This scenario shows that practical benefit does not guarantee truth.**, **There is a plausible principle that knowledge requires truth. This principle together with the presence of false beliefs that are beneficial entails that practical benefit alone cannot constitute knowledge.**
