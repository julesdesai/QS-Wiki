# Knowledge as Reliable Process

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is sensitivity a necessary condition for knowledge formed by intuition?](is-sensitivity-a-necessary-condition-for-knowledge-formed-by-intuition-antithesis-09b58b51-ab25-4271-ad9d-6a5d78f54772.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief results from a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief results from a reliable cognitive process**
