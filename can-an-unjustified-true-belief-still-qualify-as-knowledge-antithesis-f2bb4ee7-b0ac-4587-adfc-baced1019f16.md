# Can an unjustified true belief still qualify as knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f2bb4ee7-b0ac-4587-adfc-baced1019f16
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Phenomenal Conservatism: Seeming Justification](phenomenal-conservatism-seeming-justification-synthesis-de77a591-8397-48c7-98a8-42a761bdf13a.md)

## Content

**Imagine a case where someone guesses a lottery number and wins. The person believes they would win; their belief turns out true. However, intuitively, we wouldn't say they had knowledge they would win, indicating that true belief without justification doesn't count as knowledge.**
