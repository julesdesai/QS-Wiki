# How can contextualism explain mutual understanding across different contexts?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0e43f4f9-95cf-46ad-bc57-53d3b26445ec
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism about Knowledge](contextualism-about-knowledge-synthesis-3f771a9e-26d6-43c4-b604-a31f8bd9947c.md)

## Content

**Individuals from different contexts often seem to understand and communicate knowledge effectively.**, **If contextualism is correct, differing standards in each context should complicate or even impede this understanding.**, **It is counterintuitive that mutual understanding operates seamlessly across contexts if contextual standards are so variable.**
