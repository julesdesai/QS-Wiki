## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief remains safe given the specific context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief contextually remains safe from being false**
