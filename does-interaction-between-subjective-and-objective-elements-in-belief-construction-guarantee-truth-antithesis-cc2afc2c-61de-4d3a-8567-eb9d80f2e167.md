# Does interaction between subjective and objective elements in belief construction guarantee truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** cc2afc2c-61de-4d3a-8567-eb9d80f2e167
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Constructivist Coherentism](constructivist-coherentism-synthesis-df66a247-3331-4fc3-9810-0103a6210363.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case where subjective construction of beliefs skews significantly from objective evidence but the resultant belief system remains coherent. Intuitively, such a belief system cannot guarantee truth, as skewed subjective interpretations fail to grasp objective reality fully.**, **It is implausible to assume that coherence between subjective and objective elements by itself guarantees truth, as distortions consistently introduced by subjective elements can lead to systemic misunderstanding.**
