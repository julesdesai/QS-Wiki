# How does Foundational Coherentism address the Problem of Circularity?

## Content

**Consider a belief system where each belief requires justification by reference to other beliefs, forming a circle. Intuitively, such circular justification seems problematic and does not genuinely secure justification.**, **A belief system that is coherent but circular seems to render the need for foundational empirical backing irrelevant, as coherence alone should not suffice for justification.**
