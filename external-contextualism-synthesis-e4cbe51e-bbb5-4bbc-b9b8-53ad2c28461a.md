## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is justified**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that relevant defeaters known to other parties are absent**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the belief is justified, the proposition is true, and relevant defeaters known to others are absent**
