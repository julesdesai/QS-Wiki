# What about justified beliefs that are false?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f4dd3fd6-0b3d-445a-b7d0-5674b2313783
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliable Coherentism](reliable-coherentism-synthesis-cd562728-46bb-4ccf-9e19-2bbd570f1b0e.md)

## Content

**There can be a belief that coheres with a system of beliefs but is actually false, such as believing that the earth is flat because it fits historical and cultural narratives.**, **A belief is justified if it coheres with a system of beliefs, but it can still be false, which challenges the equation of justification with coherence.**
