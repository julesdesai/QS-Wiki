# Does Sticky Coherentism collapse into circular reasoning?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** dd057f09-3a76-4274-9d76-09a9eb9034a8
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Sticky Coherentism](sticky-coherentism-synthesis-cfe693b5-fe7c-40d5-b39e-4879bfdf6ed9.md)

## Content

**In a coherent system, beliefs are justified by their relations to other beliefs, potentially leading to a situation where beliefs justify each other in a circular way.**, **If a belief is justified only by other beliefs that are, in turn, justified by the initial belief, then intuitive reasoning challenges the idea of genuine justification.**
