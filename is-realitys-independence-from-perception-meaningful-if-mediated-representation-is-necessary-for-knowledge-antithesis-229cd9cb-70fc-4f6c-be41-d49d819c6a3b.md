# Is reality's independence from perception meaningful if mediated representation is necessary for knowledge?

**Node ID:** 229cd9cb-70fc-4f6c-be41-d49d819c6a3b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

## Content

**Imagine individuals describe a room's color differently due to lighting. Intuitively, their varied perceptions illustrate the room's reality is known only through color representation.**, **The discrepancy in perception leaves open the question of what independent reality truly is.**, **If objective knowledge relies on mediated representation, then reality's independence becomes conceptually elusive.**
