# Does perception's dependence on sensory organ integrity challenge direct realism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f56c0580-2621-4309-a63e-612d1ca808ef
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Selective Direct Realism](selective-direct-realism-synthesis-744f6327-3f79-490f-be88-a0aa209da42b.md)

## Content

**Perception is dependent on the health and integrity of sensory organs, which can vary greatly, impacting perception quality.**, **With perception dependent on sensory organs, it doesn't directly relate to unaltered foundational parts of reality, but rather to physiological condition.**, **Variability and instability in sensory organ function across conditions mean perceptions can't ensure stability needed for reliable knowledge.**
