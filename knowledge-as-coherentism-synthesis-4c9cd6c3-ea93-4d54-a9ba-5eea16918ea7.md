**Parent:** [Are intuitions a viable form of basic beliefs?](are-intuitions-a-viable-form-of-basic-beliefs-antithesis-6551e222-4e97-4551-a094-9e4be707799b.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief coheres with the rest of their belief system**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief coheres with the rest of their belief system**
