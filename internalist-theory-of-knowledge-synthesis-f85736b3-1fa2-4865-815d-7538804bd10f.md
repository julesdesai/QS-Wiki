# Internalist Theory of Knowledge

**Parent:** [Are contextual standards subjective?](are-contextual-standards-subjective-antithesis-bb3d4607-5b66-4cb1-b0be-23443a97ab66.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that justification for the belief is accessible to the person**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and they have accessible justification for their belief**
