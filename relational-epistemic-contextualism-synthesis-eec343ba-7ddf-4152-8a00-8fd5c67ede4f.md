## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **The standards for knowledge are determined by the relational features between the knower and the proposition**, **A person has knowledge if their epistemic position provides adequate access to the truth of the proposition in relation to their cognitive environment**
