# Can perception occur without any sensory input?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0c7cab4b-2c59-4d43-99ae-aa67892b617f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Phenomenological Perspective](phenomenological-perspective-synthesis-c5a75a88-a263-494b-bef9-8c1e426bd706.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person has a vivid dream about their hometown without any current sensory input. Intuitively, they would still claim to be perceiving something despite the lack of present sensory engagement.**, **If perception is an experiential relation dependent on sensory input, it seems problematic to explain such experiences without it.**
