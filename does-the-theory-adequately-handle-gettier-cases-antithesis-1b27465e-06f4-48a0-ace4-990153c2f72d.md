# Does the theory adequately handle Gettier cases?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1b27465e-06f4-48a0-ace4-990153c2f72d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Nozick's Conditional Theory of Knowledge](nozicks-conditional-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-ff394d52-e522-4d27-8272-d4808dc162bd.md)

## Content

**Imagine a scenario in which a person forms a belief based on justified reasoning about a time, such as 10 AM, when looking at a stopped clock that happens to display 10 AM. In this scenario, intuitively, the person does not truly know that it is 10 AM despite the belief being true and justified.**, **A scenario in which a person has a justified true belief without knowing a fact suggests an inadequacy in knowledge definition.**
