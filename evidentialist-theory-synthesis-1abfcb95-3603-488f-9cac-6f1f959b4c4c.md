## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is based on adequate evidence**, **The belief must be proportioned to the evidence to ensure its reliability and truth**
