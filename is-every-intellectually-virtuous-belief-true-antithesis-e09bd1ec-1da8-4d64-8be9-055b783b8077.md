# Is every intellectually virtuous belief true?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e09bd1ec-1da8-4d64-8be9-055b783b8077
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-5dae303e-e392-4dd3-a9fb-c518398dc86e.md)

## Content

**A person might possess a trait that systematically leads to acquiring true beliefs, yet occasionally forms a false belief due to misinformation.**, **The existence of false beliefs formed by intellectually virtuous individuals challenges the claim that intellectual virtues systematically lead to true beliefs.**
