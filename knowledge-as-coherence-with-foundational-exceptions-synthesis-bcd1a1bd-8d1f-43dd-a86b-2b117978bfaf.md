## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres with an interconnected system of beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is external support for the belief's truth**, **It is necessary that foundational beliefs must be true for derived beliefs to count as knowledge**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief coheres with a system of interconnected beliefs, foundational beliefs are true, and there is external support for the belief's truth**
