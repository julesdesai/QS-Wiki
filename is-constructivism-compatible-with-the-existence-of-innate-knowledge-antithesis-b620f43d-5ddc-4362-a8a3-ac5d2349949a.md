# Is constructivism compatible with the existence of innate knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b620f43d-5ddc-4362-a8a3-ac5d2349949a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Constructivism](constructivism-synthesis-c5cc0372-1259-4a9f-927f-8a96c3c904c0.md)

## Content

**Some theories propose that certain knowledge is innate and does not require experiential learning.**, **This type of knowledge seems to exist prior to any interaction with the world or sensory experience.**, **Constructivism implies that all knowledge requires active construction, which conflicts with the concept of innate knowledge.**
