## Content

**Knowledge requires intellectual virtue in forming beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief arises from intellectual virtue rather than luck**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief results from the exercise of intellectual virtue**
