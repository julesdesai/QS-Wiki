**Parent:** [Is it possible to have justified false beliefs?](is-it-possible-to-have-justified-false-beliefs-antithesis-9f6c0e05-bf49-46c4-9d4f-dd3d5b5c9222.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, their belief must be produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **A person’s belief must be true for them to have knowledge**, **A person’s belief must be adequately justified for them to have knowledge**, **The reliability of the process must ensure that a person’s belief is highly unlikely to be false**, **The justification must be based on evidence sufficient to rule out error in normal circumstances**
