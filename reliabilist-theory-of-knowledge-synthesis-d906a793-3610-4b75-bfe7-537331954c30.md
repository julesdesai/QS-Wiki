## Content

**For a belief to constitute knowledge, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **A reliable cognitive process is one that produces a high ratio of true beliefs over false beliefs**, **Knowledge does not require the evidence for the belief to be non-coincidentally correct**
