# Knowledge as Justified True Belief Plus No Defeaters

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Can there be knowledge without truth under certain conditions?](can-there-be-knowledge-without-truth-under-certain-conditions-antithesis-f11c3dd8-2be1-489c-9be4-3f2c0cb8d08e.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their justification for the belief is not undermined by any true propositions**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there are no defeaters to the justification**
