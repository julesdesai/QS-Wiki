# What role does justification play in knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 4d9615e3-8f44-49a6-82f7-32956abe409a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue epistemology view on knowledge](virtue-epistemology-view-on-knowledge-synthesis-e49b38ea-0e37-4416-ac67-ff35516a157e.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that knowledge requires justification. This principle, together with the idea that knowledge involves only belief and truth, challenges the sufficiency of the latter two for knowledge.**, **Consider a scenario in which a person believes a true proposition based on a lucky guess; such a scenario is intuitively not a case of knowledge.**
