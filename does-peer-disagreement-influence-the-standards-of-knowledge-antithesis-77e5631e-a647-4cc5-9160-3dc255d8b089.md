# Does peer disagreement influence the standards of knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 77e5631e-a647-4cc5-9160-3dc255d8b089
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Subject-Sensitive Invariantism](subject-sensitive-invariantism-synthesis-ef027452-1300-45f1-9d7c-023fdb6cd119.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where several well-informed individuals are involved in a debate, but one member's belief is contrary to others. Intuitively, peer disagreement might suggest that the standards of knowledge are not constant since group consensus could impact individual knowledge claims.**
