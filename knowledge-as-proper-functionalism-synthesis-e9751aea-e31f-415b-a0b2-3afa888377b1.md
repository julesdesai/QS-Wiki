## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person's cognitive faculties are functioning properly**, **Proper cognitive functionality includes being in an environment suitable for those faculties**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the cognitive faculties are functioning properly in a suitable environment**
