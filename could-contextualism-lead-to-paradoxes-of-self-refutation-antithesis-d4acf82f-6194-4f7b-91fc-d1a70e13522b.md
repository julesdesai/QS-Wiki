# Could contextualism lead to paradoxes of self-refutation?

## Content

**A paradox arises if one can have knowledge of contextualism itself in a context that denies context-dependence.**, **If contextualism is itself context-dependent, it could negate its own claim in certain contexts.**, **It is logically implausible for a theory to invalidate itself in functioning contexts.**
