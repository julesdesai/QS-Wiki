# Is intellectual virtue distinct from moral virtue in this context?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 83abccb0-755d-49ab-a75b-05c4c846b1f7
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-8faa0d3d-af6f-4054-8090-da1e790293ff.md)

## Content

**There is an overlap between epistemic virtue and moral virtue which can obfuscate the precise role of epistemic virtues in knowledge.**, **Consider that honesty can be both a moral and epistemic virtue, but its manifestation might vary in non-epistemic contexts, thus affecting knowledge differently.**, **If the definition of epistemic virtues inherently involves moral dimensions, it complicates the distinct role they play purely in epistemic terms.**
