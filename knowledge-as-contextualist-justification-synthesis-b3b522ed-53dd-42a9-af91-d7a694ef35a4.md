## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **What counts as justification can vary depending on the context of the knowledge claim**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have justification according to the context and meet the other conditions**
