## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have a reliable belief-forming process leading to the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is produced by a reliable process and the proposition is true**
