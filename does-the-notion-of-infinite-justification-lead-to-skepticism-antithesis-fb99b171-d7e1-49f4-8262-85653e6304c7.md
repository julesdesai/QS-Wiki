# Does the notion of infinite justification lead to skepticism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** fb99b171-d7e1-49f4-8262-85653e6304c7
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Dynamic Infinitism Summary](dynamic-infinitism-summary-synthesis-65698d6e-d9bc-439d-bd28-3a216caa0bfd.md)

## Content

**If infinite justification is required, it might create a skeptical scenario where knowledge becomes unattainable because no finite justification suffices.**, **Suppose one demands infinite reasons to justify any knowledge claim; this makes it impractical to ever claim certain knowledge.**, **Skepticism arises when the demands of justification are set so high that any claim to knowledge becomes doubtful or impossible.**
