# Does indirect realism face challenges in explaining perceptual immediacy?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7823d61e-f6f3-4fd2-a680-ae23684ea3a4
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Indirect Realism](indirect-realism-synthesis-801f6a7d-c909-434a-bdd9-effe8ebd5964.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that perception is mediated by mental representations since perceiving the world feels immediate and direct.**, **Consider the scenario of seeing a tree and immediately knowing it's there, without awareness of any intermediate representation. Intuitively, this suggests a direct connection to reality, contrary to the indirect realism view.**
