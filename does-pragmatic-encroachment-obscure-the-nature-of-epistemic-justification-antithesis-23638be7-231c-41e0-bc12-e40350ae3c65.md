# Does pragmatic encroachment obscure the nature of epistemic justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 23638be7-231c-41e0-bc12-e40350ae3c65
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Encroachment](pragmatic-encroachment-synthesis-829538ce-ebfd-41f1-81ea-e2a3bdbbc235.md)

## Content

**Suppose that epistemic justification is altered by practical stakes, effectively blending epistemic and practical domains. It is implausible that the intrinsic quality of a justified belief shifts with varying stakes, as epistemic justification is traditionally viewed as independent of practical concerns.**
