# Does using devices entail an indirect interference with perception?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1c43779e-8958-41b8-8638-5ad0557e1c9c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Critical Direct Realism](critical-direct-realism-synthesis-6462aad1-66cc-4ce3-ad99-1e3d9c26fcac.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that any mediation in perception, whether digital or analog, results in indirect perception. This principle together with the use of any enhancement device entails that perception cannot remain wholly direct.**
