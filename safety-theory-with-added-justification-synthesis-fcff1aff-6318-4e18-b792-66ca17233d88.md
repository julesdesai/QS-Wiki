# Safety Theory with Added Justification

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can safe beliefs still be epistemically irresponsible?](can-safe-beliefs-still-be-epistemically-irresponsible-antithesis-b613fb88-327e-4deb-87e9-546822522777.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief could not easily have been false in nearby possible worlds**, **A belief is safe if it is true in most nearby possible worlds where it is held**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for the belief beyond its safety**
