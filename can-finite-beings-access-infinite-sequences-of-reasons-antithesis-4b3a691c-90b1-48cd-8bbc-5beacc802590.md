# Can finite beings access infinite sequences of reasons?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 4b3a691c-90b1-48cd-8bbc-5beacc802590
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism with Trustworthy Basis](infinitism-with-trustworthy-basis-synthesis-cdd09be4-e56b-4271-8690-c1707e7d65c1.md)

## Content

**Humans, as finite beings, have cognitively bounded limits making it implausible to access or identify infinite sequences of reasons.**, **If it is implausible for finite beings to access infinite sequences of reasons, then demanding such sequences for knowledge is unfeasible.**
