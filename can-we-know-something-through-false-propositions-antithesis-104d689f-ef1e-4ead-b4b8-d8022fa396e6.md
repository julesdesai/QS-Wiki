# Can we know something through false propositions?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 104d689f-ef1e-4ead-b4b8-d8022fa396e6
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Tracking Theory of Knowledge](tracking-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-6d302593-f2de-4e10-8195-85a314e81400.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where believing a false proposition leads someone to correctly believe a different true proposition.**, **A case could be a detective with a false belief that one suspect committed the crime leading them to gather evidence against the correct culprit.**, **In this scenario, the eventual true belief does not track the truth of the original false belief, questioning the sufficiency of truth-tracking for knowledge.**
