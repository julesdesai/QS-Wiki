# Can reliabilism account for justified false beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 629f576f-52ed-4e12-8f0a-d7f46098e6c5
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism](reliabilism-synthesis-5d1929f3-45fe-4f02-bb7b-941a1c5017f0.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a reliable process forms a belief that is false due to unusual circumstances. Intuitively, we would not say this belief constitutes knowledge.**, **There is a plausible principle that for a belief to count as knowledge, it must be true. This principle together with the scenario implies that the belief cannot be knowledge even if it is reliably formed.**
