## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres with an interconnected system of beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that its justification involves empirical evidence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief coheres with a system of interconnected beliefs, and its justification involves empirical evidence**
