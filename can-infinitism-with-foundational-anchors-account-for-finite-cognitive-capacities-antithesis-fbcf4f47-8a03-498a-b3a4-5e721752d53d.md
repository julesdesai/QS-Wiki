# Can infinitism with foundational anchors account for finite cognitive capacities?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** fbcf4f47-8a03-498a-b3a4-5e721752d53d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism with Foundational Anchors](infinitism-with-foundational-anchors-synthesis-6b525f84-03a5-4482-aa47-2329492f1b5f.md)

## Content

**Human beings have finite cognitive capacities and cannot process or hold infinite chains of reasons.**, **If justification requires an infinite chain of reasons, then most human beliefs are unjustified due to cognitive limitations.**, **A theory that implies most human beliefs cannot be justified seems implausible given ordinary human epistemic practices.**
