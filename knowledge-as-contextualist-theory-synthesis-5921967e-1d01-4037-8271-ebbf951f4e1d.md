## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the conditions for knowing vary with the context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, it is true, and the context-appropriate standards for justification are met**
