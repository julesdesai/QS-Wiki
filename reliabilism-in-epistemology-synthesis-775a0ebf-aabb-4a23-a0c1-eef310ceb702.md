## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **A belief is knowledge if it is the result of a cognitive process that reliably produces true beliefs**
