# Can a person's lack of awareness of evidence undermine their claim to knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b9eba7b9-0736-4aee-80f9-4e4861fa18d8
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Evidential Externalism](evidential-externalism-synthesis-1f3be30d-cfb3-46d2-9d1a-294e056d7ea0.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a person who holds a true belief but is completely unaware of any supporting evidence that exists externally. Intuitively, we either have to say that this person lacks knowledge or redefine what we mean by 'knowing'. If we claim they lack knowledge, then knowledge requires evidence awareness. If we redefine knowing, this conflicts with traditional notions of knowledge as requiring awareness.**
