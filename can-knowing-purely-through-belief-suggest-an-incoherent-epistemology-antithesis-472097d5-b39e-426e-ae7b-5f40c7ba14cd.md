# Can knowing purely through belief suggest an incoherent epistemology?

## Content

**Consider a principle that holds: It is plausible that true belief without justification lacks the features we commonly associate with knowledge, such as reliability and evidence.**, **Suppose we have a case in which a person believes a true proposition solely due to a lucky guess. Intuitively, we would judge that the person does not know the proposition.**
