# How does coherentism handle isolated foundational beliefs?

## Content

**A person believes that they exist, which is a foundational belief in their belief system.**, **This foundational belief does not require coherence with other beliefs to be justified.**, **If justification hinges on coherence, the status of isolated foundational beliefs becomes problematic.**
