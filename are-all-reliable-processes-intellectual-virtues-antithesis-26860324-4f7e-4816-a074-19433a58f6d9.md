# Are all reliable processes intellectual virtues?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 26860324-4f7e-4816-a074-19433a58f6d9
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology with Process Reliabilism](virtue-epistemology-with-process-reliabilism-synthesis-0b2ea367-af5a-4f0a-a73e-9afbc8cf5818.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a cognitive process reliably produces true beliefs but lacks moral or intellectual praiseworthiness. Intuitively, we would judge that such processes do not constitute intellectual virtues.**, **There is a plausible principle that intellectual virtues should encompass moral and intellectual excellence. This principle implies that reliable processes that lack these excellences may not be considered intellectual virtues.**
