# Does Foundational Infinitism make knowing anything impossible?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ad994bff-e6f9-4f75-aa1d-60c25eb7d4cc
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundational Infinitism](foundational-infinitism-synthesis-8fb4ffc2-abd6-406c-b0e9-89dab8b3bfd6.md)

## Content

**If every belief requires an infinite chain of justification, it is impossible for anyone to genuinely claim to know anything.**, **Human cognitive capacities are limited and cannot process or comprehend infinite justificatory chains.**, **The necessity of drawing from foundational beliefs conflicts with the demand for infinite reasons, as it suggests a finite starting point.**
