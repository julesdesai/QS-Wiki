# Knowledge as Virtue Epistemology

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Could a lucky true belief be considered knowledge under this view?](could-a-lucky-true-belief-be-considered-knowledge-under-this-view-antithesis-b32c221b-c0c8-42a8-ac5c-f29919ccb42f.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is attributable to the person's intellectual virtues**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is attributable to the person's intellectual virtues**
