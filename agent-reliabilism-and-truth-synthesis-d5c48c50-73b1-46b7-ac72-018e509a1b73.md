## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they are a reliable agent in forming beliefs**, **A reliable agent forms beliefs in ways that typically lead to truth**
