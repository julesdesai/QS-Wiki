# Knowledge as True Belief with Evidence

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** f798bdef-ba0b-48f6-9ced-3aa5e64e404f
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is reliability necessary for knowledge?](is-reliability-necessary-for-knowledge-antithesis-66ec3ca6-9267-4791-8b4d-33c702a1828c.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that sufficient evidence supports the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and sufficient evidence supports the belief**
