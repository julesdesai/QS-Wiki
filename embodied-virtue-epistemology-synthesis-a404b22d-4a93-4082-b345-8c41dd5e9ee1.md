## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief to be formed through intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is true and the belief is formed through intellectual virtue or reliable non-belief cognitive skills**
