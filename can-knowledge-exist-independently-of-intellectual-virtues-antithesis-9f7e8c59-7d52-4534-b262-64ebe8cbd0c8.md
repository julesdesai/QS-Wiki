# Can knowledge exist independently of intellectual virtues?

## Content

**In some instances, individuals can possess knowledge without consciously applying intellectual virtues; for example, when remembering a fact effortlessly.**, **Consider a scenario where someone accurately predicts the outcome of an event based on a hunch rather than deliberate virtuous reasoning.**, **If knowledge hinges solely on having a true belief and intellectual virtues, then intuitive cases of knowledge in everyday life go unexplained.**
