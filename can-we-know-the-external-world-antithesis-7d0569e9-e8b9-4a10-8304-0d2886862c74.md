# Can we know the external world?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7d0569e9-e8b9-4a10-8304-0d2886862c74
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherent Phenomenalism](coherent-phenomenalism-synthesis-2365a190-e3fc-4d37-a83b-defd0eadffa2.md)

## Content

**Imagine a scenario where a person has coherent dreams or imagination indistinguishable from reality. Intuitively, they do not have knowledge of an external world if all experiences are internal constructs.**, **There is a plausible principle that coherent experiences alone cannot be a reliable indicator of an external world's existence since they could be entirely self-generated.**
