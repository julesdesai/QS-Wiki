# Does reliance on internal reasons exclude external inputs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2a68c0de-a6a8-4d5d-9460-611449b24ca3
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Hybrid Coherentist-Internalist View](hybrid-coherentist-internalist-view-synthesis-ffa44d27-7377-4f6d-8451-515975b92108.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case where a person's justification relies solely on internal reasoning without considering external evidence. Intuitively, we would judge that such justification is incomplete.**, **To have complete justification, beliefs need to incorporate external inputs beyond purely internal reasons.**
