# Are practical consequences alone sufficient for justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e12bab50-2088-4a40-8a08-8258903fc312
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatism: Practical Consequences](pragmatism-practical-consequences-synthesis-0f8107eb-2a4a-42d8-a5a9-2983c41a2844.md)

## Content

**Two people may hold identical beliefs, yet only one experiences favorable practical outcomes.**, **Relying solely on practical outcomes makes it implausible to assert that both have justified beliefs.**
