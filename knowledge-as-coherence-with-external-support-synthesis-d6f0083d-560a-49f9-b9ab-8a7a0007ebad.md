# Knowledge as Coherence with External Support

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** d6f0083d-560a-49f9-b9ab-8a7a0007ebad
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is coherence sufficient for justification?](is-coherence-sufficient-for-justification-antithesis-5c7d481d-1d75-46e1-8506-30a24bfc06ca.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres with a justified system of beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the system of beliefs has external support**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief coheres with a justified system of beliefs that is externally supported**
