# No Accidental Truth Theory

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can deviant causal chains undermine knowledge from reliable processes?](can-deviant-causal-chains-undermine-knowledge-from-reliable-processes-antithesis-3ac0a0f7-465a-472d-9c6b-9dec7dd804c0.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the truth of the proposition is not incidental to the cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process, and the truth is not incidental**
