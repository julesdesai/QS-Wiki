## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that contextual parameters allow for its assertion as knowledge**, **Contextual parameters include the standards and norms of justification relevant to the situation**
