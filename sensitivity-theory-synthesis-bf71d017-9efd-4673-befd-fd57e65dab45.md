# Sensitivity Theory

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can an unreliable but accurate belief still be knowledge?](can-an-unreliable-but-accurate-belief-still-be-knowledge-antithesis-db1304fd-1ec8-4399-8162-fdb22fe54626.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is sensitive, meaning they would not believe it if it were false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is sensitive**
