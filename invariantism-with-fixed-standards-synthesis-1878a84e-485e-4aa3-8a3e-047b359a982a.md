## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person believes the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person has justification for believing the proposition**, **The standards for knowledge remain fixed and do not vary depending on context**
