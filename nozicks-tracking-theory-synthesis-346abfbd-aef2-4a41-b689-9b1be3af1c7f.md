## Content

**Knowledge is a true belief that corresponds to how the world would be in nearby possible scenarios**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **If the proposition were false, the person would not believe it in nearby possible worlds**, **If the proposition were true, the person would believe it in nearby possible worlds**
