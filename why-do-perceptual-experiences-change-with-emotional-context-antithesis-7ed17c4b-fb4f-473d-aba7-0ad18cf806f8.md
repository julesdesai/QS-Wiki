# Why do perceptual experiences change with emotional context?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7ed17c4b-fb4f-473d-aba7-0ad18cf806f8
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Direct Realism Summary](direct-realism-summary-synthesis-42a15467-8636-4e99-a9b6-42a7b3d54195.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which an individual's mood affects their perception, such as sadness making colors seem duller. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that emotional states influence perception, requiring some form of mental processing.**, **It is implausible that direct perception without mental representation could account for changes in perceptual experience due to internal, subjective states.**
