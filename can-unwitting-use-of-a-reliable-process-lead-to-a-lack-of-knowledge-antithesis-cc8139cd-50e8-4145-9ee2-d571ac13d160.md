# Can unwitting use of a reliable process lead to a lack of knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** cc8139cd-50e8-4145-9ee2-d571ac13d160
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalism with Reliable Beliefs](externalism-with-reliable-beliefs-synthesis-44f393ff-3a5c-4b93-8788-7ab032b1badd.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person believes a medical treatment is effective because it was endorsed by a normally reliable expert.**, **Unknown to the person, the expert’s endorsement was based on flawed research, yet the treatment is effective for reasons unrelated to the flawed research.**, **Although the belief is true and seemingly based on a reliable source, intuitively the person does not have knowledge about the treatment’s effectiveness.**
