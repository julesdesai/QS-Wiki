# How does the possibility of reliable cognitive processes affect justified belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** aea132ba-9fae-46f7-b8ad-fca44a084f9e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Phenomenal Conservatism: Seeming Justification](phenomenal-conservatism-seeming-justification-synthesis-de77a591-8397-48c7-98a8-42a761bdf13a.md)

## Content

**Consider a situation where a person forms a belief based on a reliable cognitive process, such as perception, even when it does not seem to them initially that the proposition is true. Intuitively, the reliability of the process could justify the belief despite lack of seeming justification.**
