# Can malfunctioning belief mechanisms produce knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d545fd10-4e52-4ac5-ae45-6508c1afe61a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Proper Functionalism With Proper Functioning Belief Mechanisms](proper-functionalism-with-proper-functioning-belief-mechanisms-synthesis-d742806a-8086-4d38-b926-69c7d212e234.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person believes a proposition due to a random electrical disturbance in the brain that occasionally causes reliable true beliefs. Intuitively, we would not judge this person's true belief as knowledge.**, **In this scenario, the cognitive mechanism is not functioning properly, yet it produces a true belief.**
