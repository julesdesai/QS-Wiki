## Content

**Knowledge consists of true belief appropriately reflective of one's cognitive abilities**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have a higher-order perspective on their belief-forming process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe it, the proposition is true, and they have a reflective perspective verifying their belief**
