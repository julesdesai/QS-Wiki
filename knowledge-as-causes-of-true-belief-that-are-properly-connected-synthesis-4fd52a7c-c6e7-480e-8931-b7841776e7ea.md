# Knowledge as Causes of True Belief That Are Properly Connected

**Parent:** [Does circular reasoning fulfill non-accidental causation requirements?](does-circular-reasoning-fulfill-non-accidental-causation-requirements-antithesis-bf310a67-8071-4209-a71b-42d3c3f8558a.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is caused by the truth of the proposition in a way that is properly connected**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is caused by the truth of the proposition in a way that is properly connected**
