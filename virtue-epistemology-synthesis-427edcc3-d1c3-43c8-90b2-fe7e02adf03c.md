## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from an intellectual virtue**, **An intellectual virtue is a trait or quality that reliably leads to the acquisition of truth**
