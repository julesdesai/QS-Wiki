## Content

**Knowledge requires a true belief arising from intellectual virtue**, **Knowledge also requires a separate justification condition not based on virtues**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is justified by a method beyond intellectual virtue**
