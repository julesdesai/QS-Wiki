**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from the exercise of intellectual virtue**, **An intellectual virtue is a trait that reliably leads to the formation of true beliefs**
