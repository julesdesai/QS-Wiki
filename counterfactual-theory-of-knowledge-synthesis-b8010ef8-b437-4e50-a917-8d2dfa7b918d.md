## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **Knowledge requires a sensitivity to the truth of the proposition such that the belief would change if the truth were different**
