# Knowledge as Safe Reliabilism

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 851204d1-f146-4163-a970-7254a6db5c5f
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does safe belief always lead to knowledge?](does-safe-belief-always-lead-to-knowledge-antithesis-7ac6b1ca-935e-4d27-bc7b-fb080d34f922.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed by a reliable process that ensures it could not easily have been false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is formed by a reliable, error-resistant process**
