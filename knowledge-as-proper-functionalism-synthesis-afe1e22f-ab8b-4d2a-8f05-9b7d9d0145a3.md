## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from a properly functioning cognitive faculty**, **Properly functioning cognitive faculties are those that are operating as designed under appropriate conditions**
