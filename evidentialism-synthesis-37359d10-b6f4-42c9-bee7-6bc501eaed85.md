## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have sufficient evidence for the truth of the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **The standards for knowledge are determined by the availability of evidence rather than context**
