## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that knowledge claims are evaluated according to the context of the person making the claim**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and knowledge claims are evaluated according to the context of the person making the claim**
