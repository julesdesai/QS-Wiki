## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person's belief in the proposition is formed through an intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person can reflectively endorse their belief formation process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is true, the belief is formed through an intellectual virtue, and the person can reflectively endorse their belief formation process**
