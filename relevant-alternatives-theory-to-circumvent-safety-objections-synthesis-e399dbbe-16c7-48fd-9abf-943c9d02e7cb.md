## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **A belief counts as knowledge only when a person can rule out all relevant alternatives where the proposition would be false**, **The concept of relevant alternatives ensures that knowledge is secure against epistemic luck**
