# Is the distinction between perception presenting reality or appearance always clear?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1a9712bd-4391-4161-9611-718c90d2d94d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Disjunctivism](disjunctivism-synthesis-15b36dc9-362d-489d-8156-bddc0dd6b522.md)

## Content

**In ambiguous images like the Necker cube, a person perceives both versions alternately over time. Intuitively, these alternating perceptions challenge the clear-cut distinction.**, **If perception allows for multiple interpretations without clear determination as reality or appearance, this challenges the simplification of perception as either clearly one or the other.**
