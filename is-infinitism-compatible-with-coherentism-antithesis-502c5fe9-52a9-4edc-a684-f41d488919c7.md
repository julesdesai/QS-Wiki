# Is Infinitism compatible with coherentism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 502c5fe9-52a9-4edc-a684-f41d488919c7
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism](infinitism-synthesis-db00e7c1-6a13-4ca5-8f09-6fae7cd29e0f.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that coherency among beliefs is a source of justification. This principle combined with Infinitism suggests potential tension, as infinite regress in itself does not guarantee coherence among the reasons.**, **Suppose a case where an infinite chain of reasons is disordered or inconsistent. Intuitively, such a chain would not be considered coherent, challenging the compatibility of Infinitism with coherentism.**
