## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that justification consists of an infinite chain of reasons**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that each reason is further supported ad infinitum**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is true and an infinite chain of reasons exists**
