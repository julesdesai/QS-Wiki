# How do hallucinations affect the justification provided by perceptual experiences?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** fabe02bd-0a98-4f29-9e31-41507ca98d1a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Phenomenal Conservatism](phenomenal-conservatism-synthesis-177872be-9e69-40ef-b8fe-02aefba4e5af.md)

## Content

**In cases of hallucination, a person might have vivid perceptual experiences that are not caused by any corresponding external reality.**, **These experiences are indistinguishable to the individual from genuine perceptions, challenging the notion that all perceptual experiences offer prima facie justification.**
