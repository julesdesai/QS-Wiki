## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, the belief must arise from intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe it and the belief arises from intellectual virtue, reliably leading to truth**
