## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **If the proposition were true, the person would believe it**, **The belief formation process is generally reliable even if sensitivity condition is not satisfied**
