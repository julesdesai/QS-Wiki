# Knowledge as Externalism

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** b563ff6f-9ed4-412d-8f25-83f111ad2299
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can partially justified beliefs lead to knowledge?](can-partially-justified-beliefs-lead-to-knowledge-antithesis-dab289a8-1bb9-4526-94c0-f7da587d0e11.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the external world plays a role in the justification**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the external world plays a role in the justification**
