## Content

**A person knows a proposition if the proposition is true and they believe it**, **A belief qualifies as knowledge if there is an appropriate causal connection between the belief and the fact that makes it true**, **This causal connection should account for how the belief is produced and sustained**, **Knowledge does not require counterfactual considerations about the truth of the proposition**
