# Disjunctivist Conception of Knowledge

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that when the belief is right it is because it presents the world correctly**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief presents the world correctly**
