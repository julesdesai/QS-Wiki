## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief could not easily have been false in nearby possible worlds**, **In addition to safety, a belief must be true because of an exercise of a cognitive ability to avoid epistemic luck**
