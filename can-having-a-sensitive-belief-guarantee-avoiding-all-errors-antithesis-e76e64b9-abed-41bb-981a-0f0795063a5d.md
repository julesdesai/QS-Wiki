# Can having a sensitive belief guarantee avoiding all errors?

**Parent:** [Nozick's Sensitivity Theory](nozicks-sensitivity-theory-synthesis-094644b1-cf24-4462-a6c6-f40dc1c2de1a.md)

## Content

**Sensitivity may not help in distinguishing knowledge from accidental truth.**, **Consider an archer who is consistently accurate but relies on the faulty view that making a circular motion guarantees hitting the target; failures in belief formation should disqualify it as knowledge even if hits occur in nearby worlds.**
