# Is truth a requirement for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** aa8dded2-c0d8-4ca1-baa7-34a0303d10fb
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-5dae303e-e392-4dd3-a9fb-c518398dc86e.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where a highly virtuous individual believes something that was true but has recently become false, yet the belief still guides successful action.**, **This supports the notion that truth might not be necessary for knowledge if effective action is maintained.**
