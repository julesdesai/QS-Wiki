# Does empirical phenomenalism account for the social aspect of knowledge acquisition?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1eec7578-9ce1-41b5-b858-3197f18adb4a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Empirical Phenomenalism](empirical-phenomenalism-synthesis-0f019261-788c-4093-9ea8-d1a3828e8b76.md)

## Content

**Many beliefs are justified through testimony from others rather than direct sensory experience.**, **If testimony involves knowledge that cannot be empirically verified solely through an individual's own perception, then empirical phenomenalism might not fully capture knowledge acquisition.**
