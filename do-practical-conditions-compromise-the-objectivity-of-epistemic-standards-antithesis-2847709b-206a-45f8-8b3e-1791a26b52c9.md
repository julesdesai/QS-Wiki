# Do practical conditions compromise the objectivity of epistemic standards?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2847709b-206a-45f8-8b3e-1791a26b52c9
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism with Sensitivity to Practical Conditions](contextualism-with-sensitivity-to-practical-conditions-synthesis-e44527bc-e319-461c-896c-272879d9a91c.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that practical conditions, such as personal stakes in an outcome, should determine epistemic standards for everyone, as this would introduce subjective variability not conducive to objective epistemic standards traditionally aspired to in the pursuit of knowledge.**
