## Content

**Consider if someone leaves a room and then returns later to find the furniture unchanged. Intuitively, we would believe the furniture continued to exist while unperceived.**, **The expectation of object permanence, such as a child believing their toy is still in the chest when they cannot see it, suggests an understanding of objects existing continuously beyond perception.**
