# Does the causal theory undermine the role of justification in knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5a6fe5f0-60e9-4259-8bfd-3d686de2e9c0
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Causal Theory of Knowledge](causal-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-dc61c008-27ed-4921-863e-badc9b961db6.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where someone has a true belief caused by a reliable mechanic but has no access to the justification provided by that mechanic, in which we would intuitively judge that they might not possess knowledge.**, **It is plausible to believe that knowledge requires the individual to access their justification, just as it requires a true belief.**
