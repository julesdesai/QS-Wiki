# Can external reliability negate the requirement of internal justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 193c5fab-75c4-47dd-80c6-af347f41394a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Process Reliabilism with Nozick's Condition](process-reliabilism-with-nozicks-condition-synthesis-5d350bc0-f5e6-4b33-b9c5-feeee7d84d21.md)

## Content

**In cases where a person forms beliefs through entirely external reliable processes with no personal understanding or justification, intuitively their beliefs appear bereft of knowledge.**, **It is implausible that complete detachment from internal justification, despite external reliability, provides true knowledge.**
