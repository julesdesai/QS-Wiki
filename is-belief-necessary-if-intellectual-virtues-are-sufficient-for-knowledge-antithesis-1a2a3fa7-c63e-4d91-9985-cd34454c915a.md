# Is belief necessary if intellectual virtues are sufficient for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1a2a3fa7-c63e-4d91-9985-cd34454c915a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue epistemology view on knowledge](virtue-epistemology-view-on-knowledge-synthesis-e49b38ea-0e37-4416-ac67-ff35516a157e.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that belief is necessary if the exercise of intellectual virtues suffices for knowledge.**, **Consider a scenario in which someone exercises intellectual virtues but does not form a belief; intuitively, this fails to qualify as knowledge, suggesting belief is necessary.**
