# Knowledge as Virtue Epistemology

**Parent:** [Is it possible to have knowledge despite counterfactual dependence on mistaken circumstances?](is-it-possible-to-have-knowledge-despite-counterfactual-dependence-on-mistaken-circumstances-antithesis-58c76c42-033c-403d-8de6-542275024f4f.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief be the result of an intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief results from an intellectual virtue**
