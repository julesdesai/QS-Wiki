# Knowledge as True Belief with Reliable Process and Contextual Hierarchy

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is formed through a reliable cognitive process**, **The justification format is hierarchically context-dependent when beliefs conflict**
