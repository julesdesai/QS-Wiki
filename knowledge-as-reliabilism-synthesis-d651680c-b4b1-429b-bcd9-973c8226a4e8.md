## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A person is justified in believing a proposition if and only if their belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **Reliable cognitive processes are those that consistently produce true beliefs**
