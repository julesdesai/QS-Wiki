## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief meets the standards of justification set by the conversational context**, **Conversational context determines the epistemic standards for knowledge attribution**
