# Can ignorance of methodology affect knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0cd31043-1eaf-46b5-b10d-9a3e69f4f265
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Tracking Theory of Knowledge](tracking-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-c5c60481-0513-43f2-8ed6-61505f94790c.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person believes a proposition is true but is unaware of the method to track its truth.**, **Even if the belief is accurate and true, ignorance of the method seems to undermine knowledge.**, **It is implausible to claim someone knows if they are unaware of how their belief tracks truth.**
