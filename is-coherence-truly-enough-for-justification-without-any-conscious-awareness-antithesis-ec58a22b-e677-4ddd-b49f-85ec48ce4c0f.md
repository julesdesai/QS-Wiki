# Is coherence truly enough for justification without any conscious awareness?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ec58a22b-e677-4ddd-b49f-85ec48ce4c0f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism with Non-Conscious Elements](coherentism-with-non-conscious-elements-synthesis-025108b6-66b5-4784-9945-6373af308ffa.md)

## Content

**Consider a system where beliefs are perfectly coherent but entirely beyond the conscious awareness of the person. There is a plausible principle that a system's coherence without conscious awareness is not sufficient for justification of individual beliefs.**, **It is implausible that a belief could be truly justified solely based on coherence with other non-conscious beliefs if there is no conscious awareness.**
