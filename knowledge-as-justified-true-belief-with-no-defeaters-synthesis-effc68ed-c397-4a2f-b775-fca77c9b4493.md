# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with No Defeaters

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** effc68ed-c397-4a2f-b775-fca77c9b4493
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Could someone possess knowledge without possessing intellectual virtues?](could-someone-possess-knowledge-without-possessing-intellectual-virtues-antithesis-625dd029-6fb1-49f8-9489-f9be194ec0d1.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have justification for their belief and there are no defeaters for their justification**, **A defeater is evidence or information that would undermine or override the justification**
