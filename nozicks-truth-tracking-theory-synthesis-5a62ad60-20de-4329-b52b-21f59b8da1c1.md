## Content

**A person knows a proposition if they believe it and the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, if the proposition were false, they would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, if the proposition were true, they would believe it**, **Knowledge requires belief that tracks the truth across close possible worlds**
