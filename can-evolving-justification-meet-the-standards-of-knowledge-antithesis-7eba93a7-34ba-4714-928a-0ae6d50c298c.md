# Can evolving justification meet the standards of knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7eba93a7-34ba-4714-928a-0ae6d50c298c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Dynamic Infinitism Summary](dynamic-infinitism-summary-synthesis-65698d6e-d9bc-439d-bd28-3a216caa0bfd.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where the justification for a belief is constantly evolving without ever solidifying; this lack of stability challenges the belief's status as knowledge.**, **In cases of rapidly changing justification, it becomes difficult to say with certainty that the belief is knowledge at any point in time.**, **Knowledge typically implies a fixed standard of justification, which constant evolution seems to undermine by providing a shifting rationale.**
