# Knowledge as Properly Accountable Reliability

**Parent:** [Is justification redundant if reliability is present?](is-justification-redundant-if-reliability-is-present-antithesis-9a82ffd6-cdbf-4e02-a9f8-02c15583c72c.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a reliable process and the person is aware of this reliability**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is produced by a reliable process of which they are aware**
