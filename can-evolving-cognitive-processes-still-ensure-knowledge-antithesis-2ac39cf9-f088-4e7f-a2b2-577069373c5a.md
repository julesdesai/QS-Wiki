# Can evolving cognitive processes still ensure knowledge?

## Content

**Consider that human cognition evolves, and what is reliable now may not have been reliable in the past or future. This evolutionary reliability raises questions about the consistency of what qualifies as knowledge.**, **The evolution of cognitive processes over time challenges the ability to consistently claim knowledge if past or future reliability differs.**
