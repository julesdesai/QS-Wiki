## Content

**Knowledge is true belief that arises from intellectual virtue and is also safe from luck**, **For a belief to be safe from luck, it must not be formed in a way that, given similar circumstances, it might easily have been false**, **A belief must be adroit, displaying intellectual skill**, **A belief must be apt, being accurate mainly due to intellectual virtue**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief is adroit, apt, and safe from luck**
