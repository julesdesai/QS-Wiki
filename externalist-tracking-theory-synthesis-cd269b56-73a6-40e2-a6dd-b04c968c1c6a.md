## Content

**Knowledge depends on tracking the truth**, **A belief tracks the truth if in all nearby possible worlds where the proposition is true, the person believes it, and in all nearby worlds where it is false, the person does not believe it**, **A person knows a proposition if the proposition is true, the person believes the proposition, and the belief tracks the truth**
