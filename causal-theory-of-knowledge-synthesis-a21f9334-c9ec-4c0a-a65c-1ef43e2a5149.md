## Content

**Knowledge involves having a belief that is causally connected to the truth**, **A belief is causally connected to the truth if the truth of a proposition is part of the explanation of why the person believes it**, **For a person or animal to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief is causally connected to the truth and the proposition is true**
