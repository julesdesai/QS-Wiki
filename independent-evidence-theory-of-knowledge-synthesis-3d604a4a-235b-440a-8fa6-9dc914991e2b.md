## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is supported by independent evidence**, **Independent evidence must be directly related to the claim and free from deceptive influences**
