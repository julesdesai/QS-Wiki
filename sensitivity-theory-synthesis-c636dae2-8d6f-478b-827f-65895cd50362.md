## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, they would not believe it**, **A belief is sensitive if it tracks the truth in nearby possible worlds**
