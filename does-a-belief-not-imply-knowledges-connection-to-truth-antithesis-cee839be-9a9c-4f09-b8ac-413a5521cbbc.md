# Does a belief not imply knowledge’s connection to truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** cee839be-9a9c-4f09-b8ac-413a5521cbbc
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalist Knowledge without Belief](externalist-knowledge-without-belief-synthesis-c20b0285-a7bd-4197-bc3d-7153e215190d.md)

## Content

**Suppose someone makes a statement they do not believe, and coincidentally it turns out to be true. Intuitively, this raises questions about whether truth alone suffices for knowledge if belief is absent.**, **It is implausible to assert that someone truly knows something if they simultaneously reject its truth by not believing in it.**
