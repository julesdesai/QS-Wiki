## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in nearly all nearby possible worlds where they form the belief based on their cognitive skills, it is true**, **Skill ensures accuracy in beliefs beyond mere chance concurrence**
