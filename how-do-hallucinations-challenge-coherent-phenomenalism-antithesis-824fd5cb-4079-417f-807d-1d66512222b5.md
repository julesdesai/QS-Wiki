# How do hallucinations challenge coherent phenomenalism?

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person experiences a vivid hallucination of a pink elephant. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that the person’s coherent perceptual experience does not correspond with reality and thus does not constitute knowledge of an external object.**
