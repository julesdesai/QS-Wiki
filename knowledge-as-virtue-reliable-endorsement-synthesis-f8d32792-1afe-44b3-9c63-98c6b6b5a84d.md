# Knowledge as Virtue Reliable Endorsement

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** f8d32792-1afe-44b3-9c63-98c6b6b5a84d
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Must a belief be simultaneously justified by reliability and reflection to constitute knowledge?](must-a-belief-be-simultaneously-justified-by-reliability-and-reflection-to-constitute-knowledge-antithesis-a0aa3ec6-a162-4e17-84d8-8e3c4d578f2a.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief to be produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have the capacity to reflectively endorse the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process, and they have the capacity to reflectively endorse the belief**
