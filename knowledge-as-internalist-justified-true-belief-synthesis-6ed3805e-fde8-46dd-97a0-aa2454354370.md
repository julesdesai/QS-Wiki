## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary they have internal justification for the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and they have internal justification for the belief**
