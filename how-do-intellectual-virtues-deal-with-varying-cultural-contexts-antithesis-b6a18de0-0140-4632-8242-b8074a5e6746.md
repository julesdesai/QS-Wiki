# How do intellectual virtues deal with varying cultural contexts?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b6a18de0-0140-4632-8242-b8074a5e6746
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-05879bdc-4686-45ec-8d5f-c387922414d1.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which an individual's intellectual virtues are defined by their specific cultural upbringing and norms. Intuitively, either intellectual virtues must be culturally invariant or they risk being non-universal. If they are culturally invariant, they might fail to capture legitimate differing knowledge capacities across cultures. If they are culturally variant, their objectivity and universality as criteria for knowledge are challenged.**
