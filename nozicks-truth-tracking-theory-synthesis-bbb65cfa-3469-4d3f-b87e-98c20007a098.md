## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, if the proposition were true, the person would believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that it tracks the truth across possible variations**
