# Internalist Justification Knowledge

**Parent:** [Can someone have knowledge without a belief?](can-someone-have-knowledge-without-a-belief-antithesis-894b9500-aa17-4738-8b92-defabb598044.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person has justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is true and the person has strong internal justification for the proposition**
