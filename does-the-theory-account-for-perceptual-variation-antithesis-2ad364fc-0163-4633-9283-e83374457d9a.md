# Does the theory account for perceptual variation?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2ad364fc-0163-4633-9283-e83374457d9a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Naive Realism](naive-realism-synthesis-a575a970-7874-461b-b9b5-9de679546681.md)

## Content

**Suppose two people see the same object, but one perceives it as red and another as orange under different lighting conditions.**, **If perception involves variation despite the same external object, then experience can differ without changes in the object itself, challenging the idea that perception is direct and unmediated.**
