# Nozick's Truth-Tracking Theory

**Parent:** [What about cases where a belief is safe but not true?](what-about-cases-where-a-belief-is-safe-but-not-true-antithesis-0da1d1aa-0a1a-44c7-8a75-3c5f544d9c63.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false the person would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition is true, the person believes it because it is true**
