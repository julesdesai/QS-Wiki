# Is it plausible that beliefs require infinite justification to be known?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 13a84199-f5c9-469c-bbcc-d748cacd55d0
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism](infinitism-synthesis-dd7098a7-016a-4f9d-9b36-944f2be4f1f4.md)

## Content

**Common-sense intuitions suggest that many justified beliefs are obtained from finite reasoning processes.**, **The idea that only infinite chains can justify knowledge conflicts with how knowledge is typically acquired and applied.**, **Many well-established areas of knowledge operate effectively without invoking infinite chains of evidence.**
