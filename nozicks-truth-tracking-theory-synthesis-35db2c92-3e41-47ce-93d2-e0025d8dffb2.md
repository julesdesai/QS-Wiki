## Content

**Knowledge involves having a true belief that tracks the truth across different possible scenarios**, **A person knows a proposition if, in the nearest possible world where the proposition is false, the person would not believe it**, **A person knows a proposition if, in the actual world, the person believes it when it is true**, **The belief must be sensitive to the truth and counterfactually dependent on it**
