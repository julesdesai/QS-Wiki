# Is subjective interpretation irrelevant to perception?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 14816ff2-6c27-4b44-967c-4255d79b2e6c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Naïve Realism](naïve-realism-synthesis-960c5650-a545-46a7-9f9f-61ad44274751.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case of two people observing the same abstract painting; one sees a landscape, while the other sees random shapes. Intuitively, the subjective interpretation impacts the knowledge derived from perception.**, **It is implausible that subjective experiences are completely distinct from knowledge of the external world when such variations exist.**
