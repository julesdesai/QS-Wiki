# Does expertise imply knowledge regardless of belief correctness?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f58969a4-f04b-44da-b086-a520380cb0fd
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-0020c014-7b91-4782-b27a-7c265359a5c7.md)

## Content

**Suppose an expert holds a false belief about a subject in which they have extensive knowledge.**, **An intellectual virtue should, analytically, contribute to the formation of true rather than false beliefs.**, **If expertise leads to false beliefs, it questions the role of intellectual character in defining knowledge.**, **Belief correctness is integral to knowledge, yet expertise or virtue without correctness fails to suffice for knowledge.**
