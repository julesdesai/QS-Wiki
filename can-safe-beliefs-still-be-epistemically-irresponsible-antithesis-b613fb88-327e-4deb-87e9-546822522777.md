# Can safe beliefs still be epistemically irresponsible?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b613fb88-327e-4deb-87e9-546822522777
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Safety Theory](safety-theory-synthesis-3968e05f-d744-409d-bfbb-59e5b6ab2a0b.md)

## Content

**Suppose an agent forms a belief irresponsibly, without considering evidence, and yet it is true in most nearby possible worlds.**, **Intuition might suggest this belief does not constitute knowledge due to the irresponsible method of belief formation.**, **If epistemically irresponsible methods prevent beliefs from constituting knowledge, then safety conditions do not capture all that's needed for knowledge.**
