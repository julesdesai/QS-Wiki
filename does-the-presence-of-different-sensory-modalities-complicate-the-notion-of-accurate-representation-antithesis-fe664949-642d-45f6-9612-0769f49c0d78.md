# Does the presence of different sensory modalities complicate the notion of accurate representation?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** fe664949-642d-45f6-9612-0769f49c0d78
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Representationalism](representationalism-synthesis-e49b01dd-7eac-432b-990b-a606f4616c7d.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which sight and sound provide conflicting information about the location of a sound-emitting object.**, **Intuitively, there is a question as to which sense provides the accurate representation of reality.**, **The coexistence of conflicting sensory information challenges the idea that perceptions provide univocal and reliable representations of external objects.**
