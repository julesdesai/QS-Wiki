# Is truth a necessary outcome of proper cognitive function?

**Parent:** [Proper Functionalism With Proper Functioning Belief Mechanisms](proper-functionalism-with-proper-functioning-belief-mechanisms-synthesis-d742806a-8086-4d38-b926-69c7d212e234.md)

## Content

**Consider a case in which a cognitive mechanism functions properly in all respects but results in a false belief due to misleading evidence. Intuitively, the true belief here seems absent not because of a malfunction but due to external factors.**, **Proper functioning does not guarantee true belief, yet knowledge may still be claimed if luck intervenes to correct the error.**
