# Can knowledge exist if false beliefs can hypothetically exist?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 111ea811-a7b0-4be8-a50f-4bacf122ac18
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Process Reliabilism with Sensitivity](process-reliabilism-with-sensitivity-synthesis-3b7ba929-b462-4672-9048-c64e1338414e.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person believes a proposition based on a reliable process, and that the proposition is true. Intuitively, we would judge that this person has knowledge.**, **Imagine if the person's belief were false instead of true; they would still believe it due to the reliability of the process. This raises doubt if sensitivity is needed for knowledge.**
