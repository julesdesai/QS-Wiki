# What happens if beliefs do not track truth across all relevant scenarios?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e4061e66-9bcc-4731-8c30-9a5feba90775
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Truth-Tracking Theory](truth-tracking-theory-synthesis-1814de97-222c-437f-bd26-a9ce4edff4f7.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a person believes a proposition, and the belief is true in their current environment but changes to false in subtly altered scenarios. Intuitively, we might either say that the person does not have knowledge because their belief does not track truth across similar scenarios or that they do have knowledge due to the belief being true in the actual scenario.**, **A belief that fails to remain true in nearby possible scenarios might indicate that the belief does not meet the conditions necessary for knowledge.**
