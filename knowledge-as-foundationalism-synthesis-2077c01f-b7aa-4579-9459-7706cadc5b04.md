## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is properly basic or inferred from properly basic beliefs**, **Properly basic beliefs are justified non-inferentially or are self-evident and incorrigible**
