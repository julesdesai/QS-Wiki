## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief coheres with other beliefs valid in some relevant contexts**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there are contexts where their belief coheres with other beliefs**
