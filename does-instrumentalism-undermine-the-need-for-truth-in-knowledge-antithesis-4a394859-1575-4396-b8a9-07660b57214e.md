# Does instrumentalism undermine the need for truth in knowledge?

**Node ID:** 4a394859-1575-4396-b8a9-07660b57214e

## Content

**Instrumentalism prioritizes the usefulness of beliefs for predictions or actions over their truth content**, **A belief can be instrumental without being true, such as scientific models that predict without fully representing reality**, **Knowledge is traditionally tied to truth; thus, instrumentalism may challenge the conventional understanding of knowledge needing truth**
