## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person has justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification be produced by a reliable process**
