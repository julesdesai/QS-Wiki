## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed through a methodologically sound process**, **The soundness of the method is tested against all available past and future evidence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is formed through a methodologically sound process that stands up to past and future evidence**
