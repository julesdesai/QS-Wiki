# Can infinite chains lead to circular justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a655f060-e615-4318-beea-1863010c7e93
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualist Infinitism](contextualist-infinitism-synthesis-74ebcf03-3de8-4e28-950c-dc2eefc9dc13.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where an infinite chain of reasons loops back on itself.**, **In such a scenario, intuitively the reasons would lose their justificatory power by becoming circular.**, **It is implausible that circular reasoning can provide a strong basis for justification.**
