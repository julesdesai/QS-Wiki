## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, what counts as justification is determined by the reliability of the process, as evaluated in the relevant context**, **The relevant context varies according to the domain of belief, adjusting reliability standards**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is true and the belief in the proposition was formed by a contextually reliable process**
