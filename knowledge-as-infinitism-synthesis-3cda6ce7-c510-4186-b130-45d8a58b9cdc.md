# Knowledge as Infinitism

**Parent:** [Could knowledge obtained through foundational beliefs be affected by external factors?](could-knowledge-obtained-through-foundational-beliefs-be-affected-by-external-factors-antithesis-40e65318-5ada-4db2-aeaf-061062b88a1c.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification for their belief consists of an infinite series of non-repeating reasons**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification consists of an infinite series of reasons**
