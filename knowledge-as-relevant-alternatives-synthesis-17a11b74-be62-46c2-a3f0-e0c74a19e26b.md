# Knowledge as Relevant Alternatives

**Parent:** [Does continuous context shifting affect the stability of knowledge?](does-continuous-context-shifting-affect-the-stability-of-knowledge-antithesis-e79a060e-6f0d-4665-808f-5c80e5c01f51.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person can rule out relevant alternatives to the proposition being false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and they can rule out all relevant alternatives**
