# Knowledge as Reliability with Error Margin

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** f5342735-3fb6-4610-87f5-e678e9b0c6eb
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [What if reliable processes do not yield consistent truth?](what-if-reliable-processes-do-not-yield-consistent-truth-antithesis-64364d87-423b-4883-b8ce-9ce350af4696.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from a reliable cognitive process allowing for an error margin**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief arises from a reliable cognitive process allowing for an error margin**
