# Knowledge as True Belief with Defeasibility

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no defeaters present to undermine the belief**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief in the proposition is true and there are no defeaters**
