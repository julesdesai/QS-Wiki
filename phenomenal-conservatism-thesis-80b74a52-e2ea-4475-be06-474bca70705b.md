# Phenomenal Conservatism

**Node Type:** thesis
**Node ID:** 80b74a52-e2ea-4475-be06-474bca70705b
**Depth:** 1
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [What is knowledge?](what-is-knowledge-question-308fcc82-4ce4-4be0-a8ca-588f405c6c60.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have an appearance or seeming in favor of the proposition**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the appearance is not defeated by contrary evidence**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have an appearance in favor, the proposition is true, and there is no contrary evidence**
