# Does the possibility of accidentally true beliefs pose a problem for the Tracking Theory?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d13af5fe-9374-4b37-b236-aea33e74813c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Nozick's Tracking Theory](nozicks-tracking-theory-synthesis-afb98a5d-d8c8-4475-9a7e-863f117c2239.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person forms a belief by guessing, and the belief happens to be true. Intuitively, the person does not have knowledge.**, **A guess might not change whether the belief is false, failing the condition for belief dependence on truth.**, **A guess's truth under varying conditions does not change the fact it is merely a guess and lacks justification beyond luck.**
