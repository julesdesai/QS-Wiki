# Knowledge as Causal Correspondence

**Parent:** [Does knowledge require external validation rather than just internal coherence?](does-knowledge-require-external-validation-rather-than-just-internal-coherence-antithesis-d59213ed-bda8-4c7a-bf2e-e8ae69dd70d7.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is a causal connection between the belief and the state of affairs that makes it true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there is a causal connection between the belief and the state of affairs that makes it true**
