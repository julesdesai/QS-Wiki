## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the truth of their belief is creditable to their cognitive abilities**, **Knowledge requires the successful application of one's cognitive abilities to achieve true belief**
