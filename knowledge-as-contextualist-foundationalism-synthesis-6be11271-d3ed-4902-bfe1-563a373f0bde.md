## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is supported by basic beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the standards for knowledge depend on the context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is supported by basic beliefs, and it meets the contextual standards**
