## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that justification involves membership in a coherent system of beliefs that remains stable across varying contexts**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the coherence of the belief system resists arbitrary restructuring across contexts**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and it fits within a contextually stable coherent system of beliefs**
