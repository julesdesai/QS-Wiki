# Can functionalism differentiate between true belief and knowledge?

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person has a belief that coincidentally aligns with reality due to functional roles. Intuitively, we would judge that true belief alone does not constitute knowledge unless there is an additional factor like justification, which functionalism may not sufficiently address.**
