# Process Reliabilism with Defeasibility Condition

**Parent:** [Does every belief from a reliable process avoid luck entirely?](does-every-belief-from-a-reliable-process-avoid-luck-entirely-antithesis-f6b3331c-64d7-4f72-89ca-2a1ab13ffaa8.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that any defeaters, or reasons to doubt, are absent**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is produced by a reliable process, and no defeaters are present**
