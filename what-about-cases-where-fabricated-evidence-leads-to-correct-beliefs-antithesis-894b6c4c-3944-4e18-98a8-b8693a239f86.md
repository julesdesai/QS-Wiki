# What about cases where fabricated evidence leads to correct beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 894b6c4c-3944-4e18-98a8-b8693a239f86
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Causal Theory of Knowledge](causal-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-336c94a4-e4b0-4a1c-b1db-218c9b8f6add.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where a person finds fabricated documents that, unbeknownst to forgers and accidentally reflect the truth. There seems to be an intuitive lack of knowledge despite an adequate causal connection to the fact.**, **The causal sequence leading to the belief is defective because its links to the fact are accidental rather than appropriate.**
