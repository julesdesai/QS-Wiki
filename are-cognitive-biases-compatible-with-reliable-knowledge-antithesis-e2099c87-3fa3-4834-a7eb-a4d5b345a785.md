# Are cognitive biases compatible with reliable knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e2099c87-3fa3-4834-a7eb-a4d5b345a785
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Knowledge as Reliabilism](knowledge-as-reliabilism-synthesis-e43dfc1f-e9e5-4ef5-90d3-e8588e1acc09.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person forms a belief about someone's character based on stereotypes rather than evidence, yet the belief is true. Intuitively, we would not consider this knowledge.**, **Knowledge cannot be acquired through a process influenced by cognitive bias, even if the belief formed is true because bias undermines the reliability of the cognitive process.**
