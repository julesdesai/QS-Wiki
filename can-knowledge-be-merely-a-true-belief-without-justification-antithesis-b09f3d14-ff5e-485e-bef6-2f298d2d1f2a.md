# Can knowledge be merely a true belief without justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b09f3d14-ff5e-485e-bef6-2f298d2d1f2a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism: Reliable Processes](reliabilism-reliable-processes-synthesis-f70f1f60-dbef-46bd-b415-215191e514dd.md)

## Content

**A case in which a person randomly guesses the answer to a multiple-choice question, their answer happens to be correct, and they believe the answer is not a case in which we would intuitively judge they have knowledge.**, **It is implausible that a belief resulting solely from a guess without any justification constitutes knowledge.**
