# Does Infinitism lead to skepticism about knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** bf9fd58e-0413-4ca0-a0ff-1233cc992d5b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism](infinitism-synthesis-dd7098a7-016a-4f9d-9b36-944f2be4f1f4.md)

## Content

**Skepticism involves doubting the possibility of knowledge.**, **If infinite justification is required for knowledge, then human knowledge becomes infinitely elusive.**, **This requirement could imply that nothing is ever truly known, leading towards a skeptical stance on the obtainability of knowledge.**
