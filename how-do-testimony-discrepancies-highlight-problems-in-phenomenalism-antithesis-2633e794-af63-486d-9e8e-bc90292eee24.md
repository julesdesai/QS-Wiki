# How do testimony discrepancies highlight problems in phenomenalism?

**Node Type:** antithesis

**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Phenomenalism with Reliance on Testimony](phenomenalism-with-reliance-on-testimony-synthesis-6d144448-4d6b-4388-8669-d0f45e936182.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which two people provide testimony about a shared lunch, but their accounts differ drastically regarding the menu. Intuitively, we would judge that such discrepancies present a problem for using testimony to establish perception-based knowledge.**, **There is a plausible principle that consistency in testimony is crucial for knowledge acquisition. This principle together with testimony discrepancies suggests weaknesses in a phenomenalism reliant on testimony.**
