# Does indirect realism imply an infinite regress of representations?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 29bee649-0a12-41cb-8e2e-9f7092503116
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Indirect Realism](indirect-realism-synthesis-0725136f-b8bb-483b-867f-86c96e5faac6.md)

## Content

**If knowledge involves indirect perception through mental representations, each representation might require another representation to be known.**, **An infinite series of representations would make actual knowledge impossible, calling into question the feasibility of indirect realism.**
