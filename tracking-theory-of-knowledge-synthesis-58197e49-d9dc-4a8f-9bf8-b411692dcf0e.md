## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have a belief that tracks the truth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have a belief that tracks the truth and the proposition is true**
