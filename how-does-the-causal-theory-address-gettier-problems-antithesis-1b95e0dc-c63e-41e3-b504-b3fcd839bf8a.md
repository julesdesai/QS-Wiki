## Content

**Gettier problems show cases where justified true belief is not sufficient for knowledge, and causal connections do not necessarily resolve these cases.**, **Imagine a situation where a person believes that a friend is in a garden because they are looking at a twin, accidentally leading to a correct belief with no proper causal link.**
