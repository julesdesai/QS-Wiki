## Content

**Suppose new evidence contradicts a previously pragmatically justified belief. Intuitively, reconsidering and revising beliefs in light of new evidence is necessary for maintaining true knowledge.**, **A belief's attachment to pragmatics rather than truth testability might undermine necessary belief revisions for truth alignment.**
