## Content

**Knowledge is a true belief that could not easily have been false**, **A belief is considered knowledge if it is true**, **A belief is knowledge if in nearly all nearby scenarios where the belief is held, it is true**, **The safeness of a belief does not involve tracking truth across all possible worlds**
