## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their beliefs form a coherent system**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **Basic beliefs need not be absolutely certain but must contribute to coherence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief system is coherent and the proposition is true**
