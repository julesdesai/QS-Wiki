## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the person were to believe the proposition in similar circumstances, the proposition would not be false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and in similar situations where the person believes the proposition, it is not false**
