## Content

**Knowledge is true belief arising from intellectual virtue and, independently, from safety**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief arises both from intellectual virtue and satisfies a safety condition**, **A person knows a proposition if they believe it, the proposition is true, and their belief arises from both intellectual virtue and a safety condition**
