## Content

**Knowledge requires that the truth of belief is creditable to the believer**, **A belief counts as knowledge if the believer has intellectual credit for the belief being true**, **The believer earns credit when their effort or cognitive abilities contribute to forming a true belief**, **The belief must be true either through effort exerted by the believer or via their cognitive abilities**
