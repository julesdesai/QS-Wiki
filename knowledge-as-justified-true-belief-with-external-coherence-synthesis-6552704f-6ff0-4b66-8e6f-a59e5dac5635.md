# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with External Coherence

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 6552704f-6ff0-4b66-8e6f-a59e5dac5635
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can internal coherence be subjective?](can-internal-coherence-be-subjective-antithesis-a66c1188-5988-430d-9d43-7fb5c72aef2e.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified by external coherence with reality**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is justified by external coherence**
