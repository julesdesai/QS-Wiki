# Can coherence alone ensure the truth of a belief within a context?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0b43af60-122b-4583-9c15-35cb0a31ed99
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualist Coherentism](contextualist-coherentism-synthesis-156207e7-3c9d-49a2-8bb7-7097c1d027cd.md)

## Content

**Consider a community where all members share the belief that a particular medical treatment is effective, based on coherence with various cultural beliefs.**, **Later, scientific evidence shows the treatment is ineffective. The belief, while coherent, is false.**, **Coherence alone does not guarantee truth, even when knowledge is defined as a true belief that coheres with a system.**
