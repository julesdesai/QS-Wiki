## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, their belief must result from the exercise of cross-culturally recognized intellectual virtues**, **An intellectual virtue is a trait universally recognized to reliably yield true beliefs across cultures**
