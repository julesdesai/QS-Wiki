# Can a person still be considered to have knowledge even if their belief does not track truth in certain scenarios?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 4784699e-2a9b-4c42-9251-e59346ae6a20
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Truth-Tracking Theory](truth-tracking-theory-synthesis-c14e1fed-7ecf-480a-90a0-7623f2270956.md)

## Content

**A person could have knowledge based on habitual or inertial belief that does not actively track truth in certain scenarios. Intuitively, we would still judge that this amounts to knowledge.**, **Certain beliefs may fulfill the conditions of being true and believed in the actual world, yet do not track truth without relinquishing their status as knowledge.**
