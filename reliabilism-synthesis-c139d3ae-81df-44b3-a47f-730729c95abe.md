## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief be produced by a reliable process**, **A reliable process is one that produces true beliefs more often than not**
