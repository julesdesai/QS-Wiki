# Can coherence alone avoid circularity in justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b01665c1-ae43-473c-888f-65505530f967
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Temporal Coherentism](temporal-coherentism-synthesis-f4dfebfd-2f02-4b67-a5dd-1a5ab136482a.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a person's beliefs form a coherent circular system, where each belief supports others but there is no independent grounding. Intuitively, we would have to say that this system is lacking in genuine justification. If we say that justification arises from coherence alone, then such systems would seem unjustified.**
