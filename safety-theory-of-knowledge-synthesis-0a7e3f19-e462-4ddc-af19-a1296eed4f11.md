## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in nearby possible worlds the belief remains true if formed in the same way**, **A person knows a proposition if the proposition is true and the belief is safe from being false in similar situations**, **A reliable process involves forming beliefs that are not easily false in nearby possible worlds**, **The safety condition ensures immunity to environmental luck**
