# How do we determine when cognitive faculties are truly dependably functioning?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 33a895e9-f8d8-460a-ac18-a67781574f91
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Epistemic Dependability View](epistemic-dependability-view-synthesis-57cd5008-8279-46a6-a04a-50cbd8dfd618.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case where cognitive faculties typically lead to truth but occasionally produce significant errors. Intuitively, discerning when dependability is sufficient for knowledge appears challenging.**, **It is implausible that a cognitive faculty with some instances of failure can always be considered dependably functioning. This creates uncertainty in identifying genuine knowledge.**
