# Is systematic inference itself vulnerable to skepticism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b8e456c1-bcee-4c3a-a664-fbbb4073ea01
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Indirect Realism Maintained Through Inference](indirect-realism-maintained-through-inference-synthesis-ffd82d7b-be0f-4614-a603-c896b6b1b7df.md)

## Content

**Systematic inference presupposes rules or principles that guide the process of inferring from perceptual experiences.**, **Skeptical challenges can question the justification for those inferential rules without appealing to perception directly.**, **If inference cannot justify its own foundational principles, then the reliability of perception established through it is questionable.**
