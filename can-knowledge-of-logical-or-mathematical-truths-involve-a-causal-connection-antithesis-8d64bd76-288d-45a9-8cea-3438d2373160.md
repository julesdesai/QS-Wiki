# Can knowledge of logical or mathematical truths involve a causal connection?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 8d64bd76-288d-45a9-8cea-3438d2373160
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Causal Theory of Knowledge](causal-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-9ece6694-b5cf-4143-897d-321cf0165994.md)

## Content

**For a person P to know that 2+2=4, their belief is true, yet it is difficult to posit a causal connection between the proposition's abstract truth and P's belief.**, **Knowledge of mathematical truths appears to lack causal connections inherent in physical processes.**
