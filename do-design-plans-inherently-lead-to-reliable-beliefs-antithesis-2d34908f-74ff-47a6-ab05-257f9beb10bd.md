# Do design plans inherently lead to reliable beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2d34908f-74ff-47a6-ab05-257f9beb10bd
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Proper Functionalism](proper-functionalism-synthesis-60a6689e-3dfa-4e29-ad7b-61cc33b34e1b.md)

## Content

**Design plans may not account for unforeseen environmental changes which affect belief reliability.**, **A belief produced by cognitive faculties functioning according to a design plan might still be false if the plan is unsuitable for current contexts.**, **Reliability in forming true beliefs may require adaptability beyond a fixed design plan.**
