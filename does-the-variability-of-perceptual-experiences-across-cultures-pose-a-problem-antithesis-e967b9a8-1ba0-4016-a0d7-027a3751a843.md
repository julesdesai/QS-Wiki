# Does the variability of perceptual experiences across cultures pose a problem?

**Node ID:** e967b9a8-1ba0-4016-a0d7-027a3751a843
**Depth:** 4

**Parent:** [Disjunctivism](disjunctivism-synthesis-15b36dc9-362d-489d-8156-bddc0dd6b522.md)

## Content

**Consider that the same visual scene can be perceived differently by individuals from different cultures due to varying cognitive processes and experiences. Intuitively, this suggests that perception might not exclusively present direct reality or mere appearance.**, **Cultural variations in perception imply that cognitive processes significantly shape perception, which complicates the clear division between direct perception and appearances.**
