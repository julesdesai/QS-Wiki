# Knowledge as Immediate Perception with Probabilistic Support

**Parent:** [Can Bayesian Epistemology account for perceptual knowledge?](can-bayesian-epistemology-account-for-perceptual-knowledge-antithesis-f1d4c445-9ced-4afb-8612-54029d2ff6fd.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is justified by either perception or secondary probabilistic support**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is justified by either perception or secondary probabilistic support**
