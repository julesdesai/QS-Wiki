# Do probabilistic outcomes affect credit attribution?

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person flips a fair coin, believes it will land heads, and it does. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that the person does not know it will land heads since the outcome was based on chance.**, **Knowledge seems not to result from the exercise of intellectual virtues if the belief is formed on purely probabilistic grounds.**
