## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief system coherently integrates the proposition with existing beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **A person knows a proposition if it is true, they coherently integrate the belief, and they believe it**
