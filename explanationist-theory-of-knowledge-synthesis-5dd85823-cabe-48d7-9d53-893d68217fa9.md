## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **Knowledge requires the best possible explanation for why the belief is true**, **The best explanation involves an understanding of causal or logical connections related to the proposition**
