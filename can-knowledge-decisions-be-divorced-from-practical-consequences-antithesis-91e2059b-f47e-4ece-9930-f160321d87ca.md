# Can knowledge decisions be divorced from practical consequences?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 91e2059b-f47e-4ece-9930-f160321d87ca
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Encroachment with Influence of Practical Stakes](pragmatic-encroachment-with-influence-of-practical-stakes-synthesis-1e83d295-b88f-4064-889e-097b4fb938ca.md)

## Content

**Consider a situation where a mountaineer must decide whether to continue climbing based on weather predictions. Intuitively, whether the mountaineer knows the predictions should be separate from the high practical stakes of the decision involved.**, **It is implausible that practical consequences alone should be the determining factor in whether a prediction is known, as knowledge should rely on evidence and accuracy of the prediction.**
