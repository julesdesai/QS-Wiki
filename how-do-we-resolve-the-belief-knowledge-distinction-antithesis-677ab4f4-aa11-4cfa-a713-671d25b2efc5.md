# How do we resolve the belief-knowledge distinction?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 677ab4f4-aa11-4cfa-a713-671d25b2efc5
**Depth:** 4

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-7b73a914-ea0c-4f4b-b314-725a0c878daa.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person holds a firm belief based on subjective perception without objective verification. Intuitively, this does not amount to knowledge**, **It is important to distinguish belief from knowledge by emphasizing objective verification and justification**, **Without objective support or reasoned justification, belief remains personal and subjective, lacking the broader grounding required for knowledge**
