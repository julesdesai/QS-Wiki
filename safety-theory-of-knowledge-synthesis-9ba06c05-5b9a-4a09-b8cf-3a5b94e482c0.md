## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in nearly all nearby situations where the person believes the proposition, the proposition is true**, **This safety condition prevents knowledge in scenarios prone to error**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief is true and safe**
