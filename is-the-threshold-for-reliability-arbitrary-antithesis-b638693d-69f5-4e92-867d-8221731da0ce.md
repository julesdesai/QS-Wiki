# Is the threshold for reliability arbitrary?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b638693d-69f5-4e92-867d-8221731da0ce
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilist Theory of Knowledge](reliabilist-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-b0906775-f1f5-4edb-9c31-9ab274e2992b.md)

## Content

**It is implausible to determine an exact threshold of frequency for a reliable process that definitively distinguishes between knowledge and mere true belief.**, **Suppose two belief-forming processes are 69% and 70% reliable, respectively. Intuitively, it is questionable whether only the latter should count as reliable in granting knowledge.**
