## Content

**Knowledge is a true belief that cannot be defeated by further true information**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **There must be no overriding or defeating evidence of true relevance that, if known, would undermine the belief**
