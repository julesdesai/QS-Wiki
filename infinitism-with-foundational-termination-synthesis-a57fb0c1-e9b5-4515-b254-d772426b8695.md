## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **Infinite chains of justification can terminate in foundational beliefs**, **Foundational beliefs need not form an infinite chain themselves**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the chain terminates in a foundational belief**
