# Can disjunctivism sufficiently address the causal role of perception?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 597d9ca1-0f93-4ba9-9d2a-f426f3c81a73
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Disjunctivism Summary](disjunctivism-summary-synthesis-6c9ba281-6911-4c85-9826-b83f47b211cf.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where a person perceives a bird through a window, and it causes them to believe that a bird is outside.**, **In a hallucination with the same belief formation, where no bird exists, the causal role of perception in forming beliefs seems undermined.**, **The causal role of perception in forming beliefs appears challenged if similar beliefs can be formed in both veridical perception and hallucination.**
