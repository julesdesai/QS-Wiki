# Is there a role for mental representation in perception?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 07dd734a-22c6-419f-852a-eba1cf993817
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Naïve Realism Summary](naïve-realism-summary-synthesis-ad05d8f4-c9d2-4024-8001-bf520360cf02.md)

## Content

**Cognitive science suggests that perception involves complex neurological processes that create internal representations of external objects.**, **Direct awareness of external objects would mean no representational intermediaries are involved in perception.**, **If perception involves internal representations, then perception cannot be purely direct.**
