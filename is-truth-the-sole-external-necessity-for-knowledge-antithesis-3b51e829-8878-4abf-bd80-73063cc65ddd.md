# Is truth the sole external necessity for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3b51e829-8878-4abf-bd80-73063cc65ddd
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Subject-Sensitive Invariantism Summary](subject-sensitive-invariantism-summary-synthesis-f39134a9-444f-41a7-8dde-6cf57fa28070.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case where someone randomly guesses that the number of candies in a jar is 50, and it turns out to be true. Intuitively, we would judge that the person does not know the number of candies, despite their belief being true.**, **It is implausible that mere truth, without reliable methodology or justification, can constitute knowledge.**
