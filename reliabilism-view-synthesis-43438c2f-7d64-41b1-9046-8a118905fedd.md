## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is produced by a reliable process**, **A person knows a proposition if it is true, they believe it, and it is produced by a reliable process**
