## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a proposition to be known, the belief must be grounded in evidence that resists relevant counter-evidence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition and the belief is grounded in evidence**
