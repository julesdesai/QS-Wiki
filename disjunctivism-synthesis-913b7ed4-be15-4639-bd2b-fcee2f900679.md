## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that perception provides a direct awareness of the truth of the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their perception links them directly to the fact**
