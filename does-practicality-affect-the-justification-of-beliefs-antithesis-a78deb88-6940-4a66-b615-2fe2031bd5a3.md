# Does practicality affect the justification of beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a78deb88-6940-4a66-b615-2fe2031bd5a3
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Evidentialist Theory of Knowledge](evidentialist-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-d871be87-85c4-4634-87bb-b00a8ec22277.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where acting on a belief has critical practical benefits despite a lack of sufficient evidence. In such a scenario, intuitively, we might find the belief pragmatically justified.**, **This raises the question of whether practical stakes could influence our view of what counts as justified belief, contrary to the idea that practicality is irrelevant to knowledge.**
