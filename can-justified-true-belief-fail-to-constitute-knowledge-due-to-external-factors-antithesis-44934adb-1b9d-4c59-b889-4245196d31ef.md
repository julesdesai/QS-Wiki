# Can justified true belief fail to constitute knowledge due to external factors?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 44934adb-1b9d-4c59-b889-4245196d31ef
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue epistemology focusing on intellectual virtues](virtue-epistemology-focusing-on-intellectual-virtues-synthesis-d559d8c1-587e-4ccf-9e44-8233679e40bf.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where external forces manipulate a person's belief such that it ends up being true and justified. Intuitively, we do not recognize this as knowledge.**, **In such situations where truth and justification stem from manipulation, the presence of intellectual virtues seems irrelevant.**
