## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a belief to be reliably justified, it is necessary that the cognitive process producing it is highly likely to lead to true beliefs**
