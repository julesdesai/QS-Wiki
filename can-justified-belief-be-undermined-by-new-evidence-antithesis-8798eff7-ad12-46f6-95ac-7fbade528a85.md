# Can justified belief be undermined by new evidence?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 8798eff7-ad12-46f6-95ac-7fbade528a85
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Evidentialism](evidentialism-synthesis-bfc3ee3a-740c-4ee5-bb7b-12c5efc1c03c.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a belief in the safety of a bridge is justified by an engineering report, but this is later contradicted by a new, more thorough report. Intuitively, the new evidence undermines the original justification.**, **There is a plausible principle that new evidence can override previous justification. This principle together with intuition about the case suggests that previous justifications can be revoked by new evidence.**
