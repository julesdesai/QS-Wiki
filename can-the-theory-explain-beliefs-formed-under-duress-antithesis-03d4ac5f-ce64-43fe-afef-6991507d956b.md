# Can the theory explain beliefs formed under duress?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 03d4ac5f-ce64-43fe-afef-6991507d956b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Nozick's Sensitivity Theory](nozicks-sensitivity-theory-synthesis-6e1ddae7-9a08-404e-ac91-e01a19448f49.md)

## Content

**Beliefs formed under duress might still be true, such as believing it will rain because someone is pressured to say so**, **Sensitivity requires that if a proposition were false, the belief wouldn’t be held**, **Duress challenges the reliability of sensitivity as the belief might persist regardless of truth**
