## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is reliably formed relative to the context of inquiry, not easily swayed by environmental factors**, **Contextual factors must be independently reliable and uninfluenced by manipulative circumstances**
