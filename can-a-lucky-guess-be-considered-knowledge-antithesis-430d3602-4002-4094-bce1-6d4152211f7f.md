# Can a lucky guess be considered knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 430d3602-4002-4094-bce1-6d4152211f7f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Process Reliabilism](process-reliabilism-synthesis-456c7378-2f2e-48dd-af81-ab7fd499203a.md)

## Content

**A reliable process is one that generally produces true beliefs over false ones.**, **It is possible for a belief to be true due to sheer luck rather than a reliable process.**, **A true belief that results from a lucky guess should intuitively not be considered knowledge.**
