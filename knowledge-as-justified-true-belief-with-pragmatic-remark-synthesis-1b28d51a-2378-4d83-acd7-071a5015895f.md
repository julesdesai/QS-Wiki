## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is justified**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary to consider the pragmatic consequences as a remark, but not as a requirement**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is justified**
