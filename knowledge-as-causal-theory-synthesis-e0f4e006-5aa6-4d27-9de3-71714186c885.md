## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, there must be an appropriate causal connection between the fact and the person's belief**, **An appropriate causal connection ensures the belief is not accidentally true**
