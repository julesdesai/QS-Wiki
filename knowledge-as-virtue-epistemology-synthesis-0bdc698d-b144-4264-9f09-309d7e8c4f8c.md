## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief results from an intellectual virtue**, **An intellectual virtue is a trait that reliably leads to true beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief results from an intellectual virtue**
