## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is part of a coherent system of beliefs**, **Coherence involves mutual support among beliefs in a system without contradictions**
