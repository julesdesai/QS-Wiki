## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is safe under relevantly similar circumstances in the context**, **Safety is defined as the belief being true in all nearby possible worlds given the contextual backdrop**
