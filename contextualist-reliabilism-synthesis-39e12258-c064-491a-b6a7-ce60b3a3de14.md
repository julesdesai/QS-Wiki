## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief results from a reliable cognitive process**, **The reliability of a cognitive process is assessed contextually depending on the standards in a given situation**, **Context determines the standards by which reliability is judged**
