## Content

**Knowledge requires a belief that is sensitive to the truth**, **A belief is sensitive if, in the nearest possible world where the proposition is false, the person does not believe it**, **A person knows a proposition if they believe it and it is true**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief that the proposition is true tracks truth reliably across possible scenarios**
