# Safety-Based Theory of Knowledge

**Parent:** [What happens to knowledge in skeptical scenarios?](what-happens-to-knowledge-in-skeptical-scenarios-antithesis-2a0c63fb-32fe-422b-ab0e-489114547631.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in nearby possible worlds where the person believes the proposition, the proposition is not false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is safe from error in nearby possible worlds**
