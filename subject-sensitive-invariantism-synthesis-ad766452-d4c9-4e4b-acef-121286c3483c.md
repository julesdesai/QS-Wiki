## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **The knowledge claim is affected by the practical stakes for the subject but not by general contextual factors**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justified belief, and the proposition is true, all being influenced by practical concerns**
