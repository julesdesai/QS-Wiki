# Knowledge as Coherent True Understanding

**Parent:** [Can a false belief yield knowledge if it has practical usefulness?](can-a-false-belief-yield-knowledge-if-it-has-practical-usefulness-antithesis-f127ab1b-6904-4c73-8a5a-8bbf26bbf922.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they understand the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their understanding coheres with their other justified beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they understand the proposition, the proposition is true, and their understanding coheres with their other justified beliefs**
