## Content

**Consider a case where a person witnesses a car accident, faints, and dreams about the accident, later believing it occurred due to the dream. An inappropriate causal chain undermines the resulting belief's epistemic status.**, **Deviant or indirect causal connections challenge the necessity of 'appropriate' causal links.**
