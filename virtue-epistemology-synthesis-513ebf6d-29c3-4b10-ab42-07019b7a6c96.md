## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief result from an intellectual virtue or skill**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief results from an intellectual virtue or skill**
