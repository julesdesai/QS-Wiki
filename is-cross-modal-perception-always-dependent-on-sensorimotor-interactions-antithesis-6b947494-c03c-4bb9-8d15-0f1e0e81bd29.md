# Is cross-modal perception always dependent on sensorimotor interactions?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 6b947494-c03c-4bb9-8d15-0f1e0e81bd29
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Enactivism with Sensorimotor Dependence](enactivism-with-sensorimotor-dependence-synthesis-21015160-c32a-4c0c-952b-d1e2dc59ec3e.md)

## Content

**There are cases in which people perceive music as having color, known as synesthesia, which occur without any direct sensorimotor dependency.**, **It is implausible to claim that cross-modal perception only arises from sensorimotor dependency when synesthetes experience it without typical sensorimotor interactions.**
