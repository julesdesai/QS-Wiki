## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition be practically useful in navigating the world**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their justification for the proposition has consistently led to successful actions**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that it is true and practically useful**
