## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief is the result of a reliable cognitive process**, **Reliability of a cognitive process is defined by its consistent production of true beliefs across different situations**
