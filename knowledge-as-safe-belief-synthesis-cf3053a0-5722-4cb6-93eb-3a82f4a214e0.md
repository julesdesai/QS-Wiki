# Knowledge as Safe Belief

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** cf3053a0-5722-4cb6-93eb-3a82f4a214e0
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can a belief be reliable yet fail to represent knowledge?](can-a-belief-be-reliable-yet-fail-to-represent-knowledge-antithesis-83e39e74-96fb-4741-b158-85aebbd8b8ef.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is safe in the sense that it could not have easily been false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is safe**
