## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person's belief in the proposition is formed by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is true and formed reliably**
