# Does contextualism undermine the objective nature of knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ed5a64e3-c956-4bf9-89ed-f61894bace78
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualist Theory of Knowledge](contextualist-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-bce49363-1810-4900-8ccf-1e421dbc62ca.md)

## Content

**Suppose the objectivity of knowledge requires that the truth conditions of knowledge claims are the same regardless of who is making the claim and when.**, **If knowledge is context-dependent, then the truth conditions for knowledge claims would vary with different situations and contexts.**, **It is implausible for an objective concept like knowledge to have truth conditions that vary, as this would undermine its objectivity.**
