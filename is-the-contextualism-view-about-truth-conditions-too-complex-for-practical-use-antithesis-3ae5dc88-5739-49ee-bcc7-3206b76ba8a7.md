# Is the contextualism view about truth conditions too complex for practical use?

**Node ID:** 3ae5dc88-5739-49ee-bcc7-3206b76ba8a7
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that practical application requires simpler models of truth conditions.**, **The requirement for different truth conditions across varying contexts complicates observable knowledge applications in everyday settings.**, **If truth conditions are too complex to be practically applied, then contextualism might fail to offer effective explanatory power.**
