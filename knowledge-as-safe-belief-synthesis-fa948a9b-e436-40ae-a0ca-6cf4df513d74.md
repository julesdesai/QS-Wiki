# Knowledge as Safe Belief

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is it possible to have knowledge without epistemic virtue?](is-it-possible-to-have-knowledge-without-epistemic-virtue-antithesis-db917be2-995b-4e0c-ac0c-599a8158d18c.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is safe from error in similar situations**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is safe from error in similar situations**
