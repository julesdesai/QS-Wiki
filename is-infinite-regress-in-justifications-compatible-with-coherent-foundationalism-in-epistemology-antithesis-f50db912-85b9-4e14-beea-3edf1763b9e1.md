# Is infinite regress in justifications compatible with coherent foundationalism in epistemology?

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a foundational belief receives continuous infinite regress without base justification. Intuitively, coherent doctrine demands a foundational stop to regress.**, **Coherent foundationalism posits that grounding beliefs should rest on basic, not infinitely regressing, justificatory reasons.**
