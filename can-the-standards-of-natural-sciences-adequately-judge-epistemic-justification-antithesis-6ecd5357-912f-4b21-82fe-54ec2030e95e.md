## Content

**Scientific standards typically require objective, empirical evidence or reproducibility which may not be applicable or available in all epistemic situations**, **Consider a situation in which a person has a deep moral conviction or an aesthetic appreciation which is justifiably held but does not conform to empirical scrutiny. Intuitively, the belief is justified although not empirically verifiable**
