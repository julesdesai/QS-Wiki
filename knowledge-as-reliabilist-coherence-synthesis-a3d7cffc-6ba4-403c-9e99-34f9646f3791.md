# Knowledge as Reliabilist Coherence

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [How does one handle conflicting coherent systems?](how-does-one-handle-conflicting-coherent-systems-antithesis-bc306f41-dba7-400f-aeea-ddf02ee0d148.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the reliable process leads to a coherent belief system**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief results from a reliably coherent process**
