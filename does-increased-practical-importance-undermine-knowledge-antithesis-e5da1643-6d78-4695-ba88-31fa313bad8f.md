# Does increased practical importance undermine knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e5da1643-6d78-4695-ba88-31fa313bad8f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Encroachment Theory](pragmatic-encroachment-theory-synthesis-66e6d98a-2899-4b80-b05b-15066f046ddd.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which an individual S knows that the train leaves at 5 PM, and a small mistake would not have severe consequences. Intuitively, we accept that S knows the time of departure.**, **Now consider a scenario where, if S misses the train, they miss an important job interview, making the stakes much higher. Intuitively, it seems odd to claim that S suddenly loses their knowledge about the departure time solely due to increased stakes.**
