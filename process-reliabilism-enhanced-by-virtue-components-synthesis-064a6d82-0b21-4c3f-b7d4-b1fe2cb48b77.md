## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed through a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is guided by an intellectual virtue**, **A belief counts as knowledge if it results from a reliable process involving intellectual virtues, even absent agent awareness**
