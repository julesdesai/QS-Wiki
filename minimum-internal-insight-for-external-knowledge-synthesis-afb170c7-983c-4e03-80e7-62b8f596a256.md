# Minimum Internal Insight for External Knowledge

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** afb170c7-983c-4e03-80e7-62b8f596a256
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does intuition alone determine knowledge?](does-intuition-alone-determine-knowledge-antithesis-e05abd3e-5b2e-41c3-b124-b6b28c1094b3.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is supported by reliable external conditions which shield it from defeasibility**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have a minimum internal insight into why these conditions support their belief**
