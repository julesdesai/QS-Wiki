# Can knowledge require an infinite series of reasons?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 574ed54a-7dd3-4a6b-a5bc-0ae8b870c958
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism](infinitism-synthesis-21450005-9ac1-4d45-9121-350dbebcb2cf.md)

## Content

**Knowledge typically requires a finite amount of cognitive effort to obtain.**, **A belief that requires an infinite amount of mental processing or reasons is beyond the cognitive capabilities of human beings.**, **If knowledge required an infinite series of reasons, it would be practically impossible for human beings to possess knowledge.**
