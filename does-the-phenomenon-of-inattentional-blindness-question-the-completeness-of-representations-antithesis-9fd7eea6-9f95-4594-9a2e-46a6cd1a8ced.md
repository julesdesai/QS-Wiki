# Does the phenomenon of inattentional blindness question the completeness of representations?

**Node ID:** 9fd7eea6-9f95-4594-9a2e-46a6cd1a8ced
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

## Content

**Inattentional blindness occurs when we fail to notice an unexpected stimulus in our visual field.**, **This suggests that not all elements of reality are captured in our mental representations.**, **If representations miss crucial details, it challenges their completeness and accuracy.**
