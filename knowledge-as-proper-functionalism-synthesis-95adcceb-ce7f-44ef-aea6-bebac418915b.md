## Content

**Knowledge is a true belief formed by cognitive faculties functioning properly in an appropriate environment**, **A person knows a proposition if their cognitive faculties are functioning properly and produce a true belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**
