## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is supported by strong evidence**, **Strong evidence must be readily available and directly linked to the proposition**
