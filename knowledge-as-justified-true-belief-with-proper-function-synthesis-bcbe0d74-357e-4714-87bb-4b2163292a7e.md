## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is supported by intuitively justified proper cognitive function in a suitable environment**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, they have justification for the belief, and the belief is supported by intuitively justified proper cognitive function in a suitable environment**
