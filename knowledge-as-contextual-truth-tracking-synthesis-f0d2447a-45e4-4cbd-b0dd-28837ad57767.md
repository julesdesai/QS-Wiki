## Content

**Knowledge is a true belief that tracks the truth across relevant contexts**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief would track the truth if the circumstances were different**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**
