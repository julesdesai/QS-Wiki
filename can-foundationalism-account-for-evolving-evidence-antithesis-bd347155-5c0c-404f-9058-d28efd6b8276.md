# Can foundationalism account for evolving evidence?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** bd347155-5c0c-404f-9058-d28efd6b8276
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Probabilistic Foundationalism](probabilistic-foundationalism-synthesis-4f173a54-3943-4ddb-a2cd-a6bb34479be2.md)

## Content

**In a dynamic environment where new evidence emerges, beliefs based on initially high-probability foundational beliefs may become less justified, making reliance on foundationalism problematic.**, **The intuition is that justification must account for evolving evidence, which foundationalism may inadequately address if it assumes static probabilities.**
