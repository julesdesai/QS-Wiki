# Does unrelated justification support knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 30a36081-783a-4e65-bb0e-a3664ce93567
**Depth:** 2
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [No False Lemmas Theory](no-false-lemmas-theory-thesis-a96b2c39-d876-40fc-94b2-391826bfefda.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where a person offers unrelated justification that coincides with their true belief. Intuitively, unrelated justification is inadequate grounds for knowledge.**, **Knowledge requires that justification explicitly support the specific truth of the proposition, not merely coincide with it.**
