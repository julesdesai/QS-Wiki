# Is knowledge possible when falsely believing in certain possibilities?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 82bfa113-a496-4ffa-872b-03f15a2a872f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Truth-tracking theory of knowledge](truth-tracking-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-bf2c0c4d-aad3-4dd0-ab9e-ac7c51e27bd7.md)

## Content

**Suppose an astronomer believes a star will be visible tonight based on clear skies, but a sudden unexpected cloud obscures it; this belief doesn't track the truth across all possible sky conditions.**, **It is implausible that such a belief qualifies as knowledge when potential false scenarios aren't considered.**
