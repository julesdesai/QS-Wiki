# Are intellectual meta-virtues necessary for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 596fbf90-6fbe-4590-bf7c-8f56ac166c28
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Meta-Virtue Epistemology](meta-virtue-epistemology-synthesis-b3bd54fd-8ed8-46f5-92c6-aa5fa4222616.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person consistently forms true beliefs using first-order intellectual virtues but has no reflective meta-awareness. In such a scenario, intuitively, we would judge that this person knows things even without meta-virtues.**
