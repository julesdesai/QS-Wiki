## Content

**Knowledge depends on the context of the knower**, **A person knows a proposition if the proposition is true**, **The standards for knowing a proposition are fixed and uniform across different contexts**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **Justification is evaluated based on fixed standards that do not vary with context**
