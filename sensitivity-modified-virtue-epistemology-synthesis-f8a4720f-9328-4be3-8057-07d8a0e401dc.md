## Content

**Knowledge is true belief arising from sensitivity to truth**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief is sensitive to the truth conditions of the proposition**, **A belief is sensitive if, in similar situations where the proposition were false, the person does not believe it**, **A person knows a proposition if they believe it because their sensitivity to truth minimizes the influence of luck**
