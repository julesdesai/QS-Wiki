# Is the disconnect between agent's mental states and external reality problematic for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 01a8be21-7a6e-4e8f-8095-9d5f6d94ecb1
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Epistemic Externalism](epistemic-externalism-synthesis-a9c372ef-1ae7-460b-b26c-8a82dc6a1ee1.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where an agent's beliefs align with external reality, but this alignment is unknown or irrelevant to them.**, **Intuitively, we would judge there is a disconnect between having true belief and having knowledge.**, **It is implausible that knowledge does not require some connection between an agent's mental states and the external world they aim to know.**
