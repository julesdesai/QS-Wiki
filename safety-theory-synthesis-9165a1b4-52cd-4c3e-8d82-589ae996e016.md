## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in nearby possible worlds where they believe the proposition, it is true**, **A safe belief is one where the belief remains true in nearby possible worlds**
