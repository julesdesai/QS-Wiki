## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed by a process reliable in the local context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is formed by a contextually reliable process**
