# How does the concept of stable context withstand rapid environmental changes?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2549572d-9fc9-4242-9b49-f31a10fa7482
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextual Direct Realism](contextual-direct-realism-synthesis-9053ea14-9a99-4649-aa95-1bc3b223c980.md)

## Content

**A stable context suggests an unchanging or consistent environment that supports direct perception.**, **In rapidly changing environments, perceptions fluctuate, undermining the notion of stability required for knowledge.**, **It is implausible that knowledge remains context-based when the context undergoes continuous and unpredictable change.**
