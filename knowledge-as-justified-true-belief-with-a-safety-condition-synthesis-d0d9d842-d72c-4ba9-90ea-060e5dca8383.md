# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with a Safety Condition

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** d0d9d842-d72c-4ba9-90ea-060e5dca8383
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can pragmatic encroachment challenge knowledge?](can-pragmatic-encroachment-challenge-knowledge-antithesis-c9cc5819-c4be-447e-820c-7d12293c8a4a.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in nearly all nearby possible worlds where the person believes the proposition, it is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, they have justification for believing the proposition, and in nearly all nearby possible worlds where they believe the proposition, it is true**
