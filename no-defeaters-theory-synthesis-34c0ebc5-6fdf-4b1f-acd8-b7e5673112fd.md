# No Defeaters Theory

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that no defeater is present**, **A defeater is an undermining or rebutting reason that, if known, would lead the person to cease to believe the proposition**
