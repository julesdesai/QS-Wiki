# Is it plausible to separate belief and truth in defining knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** da5b7630-0df5-4865-b287-ca87a1bbc6c2
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Temporal Coherentism](temporal-coherentism-synthesis-f4dfebfd-2f02-4b67-a5dd-1a5ab136482a.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that defining knowledge solely on separate criteria of belief and truth suffices. Intuitively, knowledge requires the integration of both belief and truth in a non-separable manner.**
