# Knowledge as Double-Check Reliability

**Parent:** [Does reliability ignore luck affecting knowledge?](does-reliability-ignore-luck-affecting-knowledge-antithesis-f5d70159-4fac-4e3c-9412-1462fd59b9fd.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that a second independent reliable check or method confirms the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have a true belief confirmed by two independent reliable processes**
