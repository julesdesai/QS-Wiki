# Does practical importance override truth in determining knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 84e70499-2df3-41b4-9e00-2a944535236c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Encroachment Theory](pragmatic-encroachment-theory-synthesis-66e6d98a-2899-4b80-b05b-15066f046ddd.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where an employee knows that their job evaluation will be positive based solely on verified performance metrics.**, **The importance of this evaluation for their career advancement is high, making the stakes significant.**, **It is implausible that this importance should override the objective truth regarding their performance in determining their knowledge of the evaluation outcome.**
