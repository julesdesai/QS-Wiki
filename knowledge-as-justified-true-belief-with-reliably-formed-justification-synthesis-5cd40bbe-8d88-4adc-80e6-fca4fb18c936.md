# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with Reliably Formed Justification

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can a person really have knowledge when beliefs are based on easily manipulated evidence?](can-a-person-really-have-knowledge-when-beliefs-are-based-on-easily-manipulated-evidence-antithesis-3b5954a4-12ee-47e5-a83f-16504f6252cd.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their justification be formed in a reliable manner**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification formed in a reliable manner, and the proposition is true**
