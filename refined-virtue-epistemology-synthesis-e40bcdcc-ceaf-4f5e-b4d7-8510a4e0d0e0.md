## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, their belief must result from a refined intellectual virtue**, **A refined intellectual virtue includes both cognitive competence and a commitment to the epistemic good**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have a true belief resulting from a refined intellectual virtue**
