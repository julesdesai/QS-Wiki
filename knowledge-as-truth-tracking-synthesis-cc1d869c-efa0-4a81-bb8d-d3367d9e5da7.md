**Parent:** [Is contextualism's reliance on context for justification standards too subjective?](is-contextualisms-reliance-on-context-for-justification-standards-too-subjective-antithesis-34b0ab40-30a9-4df7-8f1b-18ad2d3ff50a.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief tracks the truth across nearby possible situations**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief tracks the truth**
