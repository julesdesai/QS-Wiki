# No Defeaters Theory of Knowledge

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can a belief be safe and still not count as knowledge?](can-a-belief-be-safe-and-still-not-count-as-knowledge-antithesis-d0116cd2-2c7b-495c-a792-2eabd3a765e7.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no true propositions which would defeat their justification if they were known**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there are no true propositions which would defeat their justification if they were known**
