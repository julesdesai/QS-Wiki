## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is reliably acquired through testimony**, **Testimony is communication from others which typically leads to true beliefs**
