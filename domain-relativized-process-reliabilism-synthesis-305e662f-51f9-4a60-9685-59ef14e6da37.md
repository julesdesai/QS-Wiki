# Domain-Relativized Process Reliabilism

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Could there be different standards of reliability that vary across domains?](could-there-be-different-standards-of-reliability-that-vary-across-domains-antithesis-985e16f4-cda2-4ec9-b664-679b7b0ee380.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is generated by a process whose reliability is assessed relative to the standards of the domain**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is generated by a process whose reliability is assessed relative to the standards of the domain**
