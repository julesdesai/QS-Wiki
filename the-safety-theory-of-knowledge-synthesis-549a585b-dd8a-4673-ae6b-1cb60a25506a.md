## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person believes the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person could not easily have believed it falsely**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that these safety conditions hold**
