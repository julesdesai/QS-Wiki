# Knowledge as True Belief with Reliabilist Justification

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can pragmatic usefulness justify false beliefs?](can-pragmatic-usefulness-justify-false-beliefs-antithesis-8bebb152-d81d-4380-910b-ec3bc60a80ff.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is justified by a reliable cognitive process**, **A reliable cognitive process is one that consistently leads to true beliefs**
