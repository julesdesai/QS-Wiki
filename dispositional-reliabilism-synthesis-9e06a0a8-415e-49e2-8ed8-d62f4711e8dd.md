## Content

**A belief is knowledge if it is produced by a cognitive disposition that is both reliable and consciously accessible**, **A cognitive disposition is reliable if it tends to produce true beliefs over false ones in favorable conditions**, **Conscious accessibility means the believer can, at least potentially, access the disposition as part of their awareness**
