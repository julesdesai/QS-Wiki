## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person can rule out relevant alternatives where the proposition is false**, **An alternative is relevant if it cannot be easily dismissed based on the person's evidence**
