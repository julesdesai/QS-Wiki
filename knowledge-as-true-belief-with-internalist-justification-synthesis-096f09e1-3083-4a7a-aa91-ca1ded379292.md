# Knowledge as True Belief with Internalist Justification

**Parent:** [Does pragmatic utility conflict with epistemic responsibility?](does-pragmatic-utility-conflict-with-epistemic-responsibility-antithesis-dc313be8-9820-4c45-9737-536456f6b6fd.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have internalist justification for believing the proposition, where justification depends on internal states such as access to evidence**
