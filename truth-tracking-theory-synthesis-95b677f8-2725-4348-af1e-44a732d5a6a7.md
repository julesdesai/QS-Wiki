# Truth-Tracking Theory

**Parent:** [Are there contexts where knowledge standards should not change?](are-there-contexts-where-knowledge-standards-should-not-change-antithesis-51088dce-556b-4df5-abab-d2cad3c57797.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief tracks the truth across nearby possible worlds**, **A person's belief tracks the truth if it is formed and maintained in a way that aligns with the proposition being true in nearby worlds**
