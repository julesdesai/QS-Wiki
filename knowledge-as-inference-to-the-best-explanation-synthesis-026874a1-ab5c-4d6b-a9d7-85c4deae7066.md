## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified by being the best explanation of the evidence available**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres with the person's entire belief system**
