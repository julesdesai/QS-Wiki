# Is context truly stable when perceptions can be internally manipulated via mind-altering substances?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b3c9dc63-7787-452f-973b-537c05eb75ae
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextual Direct Realism](contextual-direct-realism-synthesis-9053ea14-9a99-4649-aa95-1bc3b223c980.md)

## Content

**Mind-altering substances such as hallucinogens can significantly alter an individual's perception of their environment.**, **Ingesting such a substance may create a context that feels stable to the individual, though the perception aligns poorly with reality.**, **It is implausible to claim stable context when the internal state of perception is susceptible to manipulation.**
