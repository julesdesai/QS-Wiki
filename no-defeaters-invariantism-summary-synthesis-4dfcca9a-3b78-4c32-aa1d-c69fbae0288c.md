## Content

**Knowledge is independent of the context of the knower**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no true propositions which, if known by the person, would defeat their justification for the knowledge claim**, **The standards for knowing a proposition are fixed and do not vary with different contexts**
