**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Could a justified true belief fail to be knowledge due to the nature of justification?](could-a-justified-true-belief-fail-to-be-knowledge-due-to-the-nature-of-justification-antithesis-50171c40-3c4f-4b98-b51f-b9a972121d29.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified by external factors such as reliability or truth-conduciveness**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is justified by external factors**
