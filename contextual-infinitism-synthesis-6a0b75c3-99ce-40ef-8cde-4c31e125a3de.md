## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that justification is embedded within an endless chain of reasons relevant to the context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and justification is embedded within an endless chain of contextually relevant reasons**
