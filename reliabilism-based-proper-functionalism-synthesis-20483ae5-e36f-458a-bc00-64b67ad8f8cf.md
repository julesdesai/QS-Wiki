## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the cognitive faculties are functioning properly**, **The cognitive faculties must be functioning reliably in producing true beliefs**, **Proper functioning includes design plan functioning in an appropriate environment**
