## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a reliable process such that in nearby possible worlds where the belief is formed, it is still true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is formed in a manner that remains true in nearby possible worlds**
