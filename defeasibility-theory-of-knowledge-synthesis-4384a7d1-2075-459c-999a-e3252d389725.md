## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that no defeaters for the belief exist**, **A defeater is any reason or evidence that would undermine the justification for holding the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and no defeaters exist**
