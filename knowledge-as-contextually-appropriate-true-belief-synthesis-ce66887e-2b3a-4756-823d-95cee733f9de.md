**Parent:** [Can the concept of "proper function" be too vague to ground knowledge?](can-the-concept-of-proper-function-be-too-vague-to-ground-knowledge-antithesis-de2db082-7d4b-4605-9e12-9431aca16b60.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed using an epistemic standard appropriate to the context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is formed using an epistemic standard appropriate to the context**
