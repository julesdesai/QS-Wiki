# Does reliable belief formation always ensure knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 91ae41b3-8605-465c-a03a-d99f299bfd49
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Process Reliabilism with Nozick's Condition](process-reliabilism-with-nozicks-condition-synthesis-5d350bc0-f5e6-4b33-b9c5-feeee7d84d21.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person forms a belief through a reliable process, yet the environment is manipulated so that the reliability was coincidentally maintained. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that the person does not have knowledge.**, **It is implausible that accidental reliability grants true knowledge.**
