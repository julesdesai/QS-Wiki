# What role should uncertainty and doubt play in evaluating the persistence of knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d94f9074-9f24-498e-a5b5-03197bbce637
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Defeasibility Theory](defeasibility-theory-synthesis-f4ee4d58-3624-4a6a-b28c-5ecaca4ef4ed.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a person maintains a true belief but simultaneously harbors some doubt due to unexplored counter-evidence.**, **Intuitively, we either have to say that the doubt nullifies the knowledge or that uncertainty does not impact the status of the truth of the belief.**
