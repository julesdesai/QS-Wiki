# Do moral virtues impact the assessment of intellectual virtues related to knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0b5d60ad-4f89-46fc-96ce-a70ae1f3a271
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-fbdf23c1-0af0-48fb-9b70-516c394a97b2.md)

## Content

**Individuals with questionable moral character can still possess knowledge through intellectual means, suggesting a disconnection between moral and intellectual virtues.**, **The moral character of a person does not necessarily affect their capacity for knowledge, challenging the idea that all virtues impact knowledge equally.**
