# Does knowledge require belief to be strong and unwavering?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f1018efb-1f58-4f1f-aafa-956a215baaaf
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Invariantist Knowledge Summary](invariantist-knowledge-summary-synthesis-7e971fef-e35c-4ec9-93e0-84ae5864f440.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person only weakly holds a belief that they express due to peer or situational pressure, yet the belief happens to be true and justified. Intuitively, it seems problematic to say this person truly knows the proposition.**, **A case in which knowledge requires unwavering belief seems more intuitively acceptable than one where weak belief results in knowledge.**
