## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in the relevant context, they would be accepted as knowing it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the context supports the belief as knowledge**
