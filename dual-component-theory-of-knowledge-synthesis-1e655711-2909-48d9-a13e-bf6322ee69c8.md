# Dual-Component Theory of Knowledge

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is reliabilism challenged by cognitive integration?](is-reliabilism-challenged-by-cognitive-integration-antithesis-dac6e395-d505-4b3f-a5b2-49aa50355d44.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed through a reliable process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have introspective awareness of how the belief was formed**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is formed through a reliable process, and they have introspective awareness of how the belief was formed**
