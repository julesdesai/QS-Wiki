**Parent:** [Can someone know a false proposition if it is grounded in a generally reliable process?](can-someone-know-a-false-proposition-if-it-is-grounded-in-a-generally-reliable-process-antithesis-97a0cace-0293-4e98-b19d-fe5cfc0c2f80.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were not true, the person would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified**
