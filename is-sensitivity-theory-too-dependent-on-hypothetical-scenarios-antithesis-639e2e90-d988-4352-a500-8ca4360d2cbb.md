# Is sensitivity theory too dependent on hypothetical scenarios?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 639e2e90-d988-4352-a500-8ca4360d2cbb
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Sensitivity Theory of Knowledge](sensitivity-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-fa7f9900-da54-4309-8d89-06019053aee3.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person holds a belief that is true and justified, but they lack the ability or opportunity to consider alternate possible worlds. It seems implausible that such a person is denied knowledge based only on their lack of engagement with hypothetical scenarios.**, **There is a plausible principle that knowledge does not require consideration of possible worlds but is based on the actual facts and evidence at hand.**
