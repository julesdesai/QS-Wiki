# Knowledge as Truth-Restricted Coherence

**Node ID:** 9c22bbec-9d2a-4f8c-83ec-a8c7eaad1058
**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Does coherentism allow false beliefs to contribute to knowledge due to their coherence with one another?](does-coherentism-allow-false-beliefs-to-contribute-to-knowledge-due-to-their-coherence-with-one-another-antithesis-520fbd8e-6d99-4ac4-81bc-551dd1bd3992.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief fits coherently with a network of beliefs that contain no false beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief fits coherently with a network of beliefs that contain no false beliefs**
