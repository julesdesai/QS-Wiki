# Can infinite chains establish a firm foundation for justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 6ffd8ec0-68b6-49d7-9bdf-92af519dfb78
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextual Infinitism](contextual-infinitism-synthesis-969d15cb-7035-4449-8e21-a18b5558491f.md)

## Content

**Justification traditionally requires a firm foundation to avoid infinite regress or circularity**, **It is implausible that an infinite chain can provide a foundation, as it implies a never-ending sequence rather than a conclusive basis**, **Suppose someone's belief justification leads endlessly without reaching a base; it lacks foundational support necessary for robust justification**
