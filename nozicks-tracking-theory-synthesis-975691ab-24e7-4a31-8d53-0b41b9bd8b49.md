## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that, if it were false, the person would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that, if it were true, the person would believe it**, **Tracking conditions help to manage complex dependencies by following the truth across various situations**
