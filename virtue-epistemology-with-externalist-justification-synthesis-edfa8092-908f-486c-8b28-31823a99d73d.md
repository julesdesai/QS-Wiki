## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is justified by external factors**, **External factors are conditions in the world that contribute to the truth of the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and external factors justify the belief**
