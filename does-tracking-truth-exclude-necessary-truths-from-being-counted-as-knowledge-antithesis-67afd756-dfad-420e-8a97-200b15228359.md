# Does tracking truth exclude necessary truths from being counted as knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 67afd756-dfad-420e-8a97-200b15228359
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Nozick’s Truth-Tracking Theory](nozicks-truth-tracking-theory-synthesis-5a62ad60-20de-4329-b52b-21f59b8da1c1.md)

## Content

**Consider a proposition that is necessarily true in all close possible worlds, such as a mathematical truth. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that the individual knows the proposition because it cannot be false in any possible world, yet the requirement that belief must covary with the truth seems vacuous because the truth could never be otherwise.**
