## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from intellectual faculties that are reliable over time**, **Intellectual faculties are abilities or processes that extend beyond stable character traits while still reliably leading to true beliefs**
