# How does the perspective variability of perception affect the view that properties we perceive are properties objects have?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 32ea7b5b-f585-4462-b301-886aa2b9119e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Naïve Realism](naïve-realism-synthesis-25d0b7e3-6f66-46c3-a45d-bb41c1dbe70b.md)

## Content

**When looking at a round table from an angle, it appears elliptical rather than circular. Intuitively, this variability in perceived shape suggests that perception is not a direct encounter with the object's real shape, challenging the perceived-proceeded property claim.**
