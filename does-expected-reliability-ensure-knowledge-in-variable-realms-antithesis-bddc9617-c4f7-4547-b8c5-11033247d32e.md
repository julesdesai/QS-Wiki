# Does expected reliability ensure knowledge in variable realms?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** bddc9617-c4f7-4547-b8c5-11033247d32e

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Proper Functionalist Knowledge Summary](proper-functionalist-knowledge-summary-synthesis-ea5a08e3-f799-4ced-bdad-582e7b9aaf66.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where an individual’s cognitive faculties are designed to be reliable in one setting but must operate in a radically different one, yet they form correct beliefs. Intuitively, these beliefs may not qualify as knowledge.**, **Reliability expectations might fall short in guaranteeing knowledge in diverse environments.**
