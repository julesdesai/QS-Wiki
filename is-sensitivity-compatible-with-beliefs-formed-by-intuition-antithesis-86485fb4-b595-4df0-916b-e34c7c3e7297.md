# Is sensitivity compatible with beliefs formed by intuition?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 86485fb4-b595-4df0-916b-e34c7c3e7297
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Sensitivity Theory](sensitivity-theory-synthesis-68f75ad1-a4e1-40d8-ae80-33e70d924463.md)

## Content

**Consider a person who intuitively believes a mathematical conjecture without proof and holds this belief. If the conjecture were false, they would unintentionally still believe it due to unwavering intuition. Intuitively, this seems incompatible with genuine knowledge which requires sensitivity.**
