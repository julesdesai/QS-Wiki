# Can external objects be known if they exist independently of all perception?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 82c438b2-27dc-4253-b4c6-8df7627a4438
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Critical Realism](critical-realism-synthesis-a95d91b3-a4d2-4740-8b59-c24b26663299.md)

## Content

**If external objects exist independently of perception, then there could be no perceptual evidence for their existence.**, **If there is no perceptual evidence for the existence of external objects, then we cannot know they exist.**, **Therefore, if external objects exist independently of perception, we cannot know they exist.**
