# Does evidence always justify belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c7b8f825-7238-4f9c-8727-a61042f483a4
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Evidentialism Summary](evidentialism-summary-synthesis-dea02623-290c-44c3-896b-2f967081eb1b.md)

## Content

**It is implausible to suggest that evidence must always lead to justification, given that individuals may assess the same evidence in divergent manners due to cognitive biases or misinformation.**, **Consider a case where two individuals interpret the same ambiguous evidence but reach opposite conclusions, indicating that evidence itself does not universally justify belief.**
