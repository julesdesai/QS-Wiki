## Content

**Knowledge depends on belief formed through a reliable process**, **A process is reliable if it yields a high proportion of true beliefs**, **A person knows a proposition if it is true, formed through such a process, and modally robust**, **A belief is modally robust if it holds only in worlds close to the actual world in which the proposition is true**
