# Can someone possess knowledge if they lack awareness of the truth-conducive process?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 369eac4e-3c4f-42cd-acef-cb45cca7ece4
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism: Reliable Processes](reliabilism-reliable-processes-synthesis-f70f1f60-dbef-46bd-b415-215191e514dd.md)

## Content

**Consider a case in which a person forms a belief through a process they do not understand and have no awareness of, yet the process happens to be truth-conducive. Intuitively, we would question whether this person truly has knowledge.**, **A plausible principle is that knowledge requires some awareness or understanding of the process that leads to the belief being justified.**
