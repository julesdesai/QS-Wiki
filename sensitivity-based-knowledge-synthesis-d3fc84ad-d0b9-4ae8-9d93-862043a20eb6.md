# Sensitivity-Based Knowledge

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it must be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it must be believed**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief is sensitive to the truth or falsity of the proposition**, **A belief is sensitive if, were the proposition false, the person would not have the belief**
