# Can shared beliefs always ensure truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c4be2fb8-817a-494a-9ec6-a40273830200
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualist Coherentism](contextualist-coherentism-synthesis-598dc05f-03d6-4aeb-b0db-fa7173390f96.md)

## Content

**In a community where everyone believes in the same myths without evidence, the coherence of their shared belief system does not equate to truth.**, **Consensus or coherence in a group does not guarantee that the beliefs are true or constitute knowledge.**
