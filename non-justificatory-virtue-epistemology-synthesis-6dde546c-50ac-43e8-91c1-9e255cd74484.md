## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is the result of an epistemic virtue without the requirement of traditional justification**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is the result of an epistemic virtue without the requirement of traditional justification**
