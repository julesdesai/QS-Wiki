## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief reliably leads to true outcomes in practice**, **A belief qualifies as knowledge if it is formed through a process that reliably produces true beliefs**, **Knowledge is assessed based on the reliability of the processes involved in forming the belief**
