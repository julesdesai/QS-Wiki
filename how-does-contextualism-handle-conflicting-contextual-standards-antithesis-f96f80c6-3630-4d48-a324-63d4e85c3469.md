# How does contextualism handle conflicting contextual standards?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f96f80c6-3630-4d48-a324-63d4e85c3469
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism](contextualism-synthesis-c0d03a39-c775-403c-87e2-d078dab04731.md)

## Content

**In situations with multiple overlapping contexts, conflicting contextual standards for justification might arise.**, **This could result in situations where a knowledge claim is simultaneously both justified and unjustified, which is paradoxical.**, **If contextual standards conflict, it is difficult to see how a coherent account of knowledge can be maintained.**
