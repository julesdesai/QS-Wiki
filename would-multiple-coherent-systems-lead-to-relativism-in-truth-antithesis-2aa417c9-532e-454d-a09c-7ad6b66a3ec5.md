# Would multiple coherent systems lead to relativism in truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2aa417c9-532e-454d-a09c-7ad6b66a3ec5
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentist Foundationalism](coherentist-foundationalism-synthesis-815fa62f-5e40-4962-8349-ea400fc3f5ce.md)

## Content

**Consider a case in which two people have entirely coherent belief systems that differ fundamentally in their conclusions about the world, like differing worldviews. Intuitively, we would judge that mere coherence does not lead to an objective criterion for truth if relativistic, contradictory truths can both be justified.**, **It is implausible that a coherentism allowing multiple coherent systems can effectively differentiate between what is objectively true and false.**
