# Does context-dependence lead to skepticism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 66034e8a-aaa8-4bbc-bd57-3b97a433c609
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [The Contextualist View of Knowledge](the-contextualist-view-of-knowledge-synthesis-744b190b-128c-4187-8edf-0a707cd161d1.md)

## Content

**If justification standards shift with context, then what is known in one context may not be in another.**, **This raises the possibility of universal skepticism, as certainty about knowing anything might dissolve across contexts.**, **The varying standards could contribute to a broader skeptical challenge.**
