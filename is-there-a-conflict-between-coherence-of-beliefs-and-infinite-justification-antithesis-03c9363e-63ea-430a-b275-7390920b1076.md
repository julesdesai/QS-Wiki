# Is there a conflict between coherence of beliefs and infinite justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 03c9363e-63ea-430a-b275-7390920b1076
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Finite Infinitism](finite-infinitism-synthesis-ec56b28f-9687-4f6e-b141-48dc2e075636.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where beliefs are coherent within a limited framework, but lack infinite justification. Intuitively, coherence without infinite justification still feels adequate for some claims to knowledge.**, **There is a plausible principle that the coherence theory of truth can satisfy justification requirements, conflicting with demands for infinite reasoning.**, **If coherence can suffice for practical justification, the need for infinite reasoning creates a tension with commonly accepted coherence principles.**
