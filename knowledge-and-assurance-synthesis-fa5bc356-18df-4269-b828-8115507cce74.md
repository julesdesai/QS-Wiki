## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A belief qualifies as knowledge if it provides assurance that it is true**, **Assurance is having a justified confidence that the belief is adequately supported**
