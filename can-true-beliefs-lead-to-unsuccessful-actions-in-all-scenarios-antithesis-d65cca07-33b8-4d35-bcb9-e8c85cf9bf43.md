# Can true beliefs lead to unsuccessful actions in all scenarios?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d65cca07-33b8-4d35-bcb9-e8c85cf9bf43
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Verifactive Pragmatism](verifactive-pragmatism-synthesis-4eab892d-8662-4622-835b-cae1a39a2d29.md)

## Content

**A well-prepared athlete believing in their methods could lose a race due to random external factors like weather changes, despite their belief being based on truth about training effectiveness. True beliefs can lead to failure due to factors beyond the truth of a belief itself.**, **Factors unrelated to the truth of a belief can cause unsuccessful actions, showing that practical success is not necessary for truth.**
