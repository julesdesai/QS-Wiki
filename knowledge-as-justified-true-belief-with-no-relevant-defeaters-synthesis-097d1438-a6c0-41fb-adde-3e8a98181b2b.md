# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with No Relevant Defeaters

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 097d1438-a6c0-41fb-adde-3e8a98181b2b
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does availability of alternative evidence affect knowledge?](does-availability-of-alternative-evidence-affect-knowledge-antithesis-336914e3-d1c0-4568-9dd5-c3b1bb9f17a4.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no relevant defeaters to their belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification, the proposition is true, and there are no relevant defeaters to the belief**
