# Knowledge as Virtuous Belief

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 77fb57a9-a44a-4b1a-b97c-5099ee058f6d
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does every true belief from a reliable process constitute knowledge?](does-every-true-belief-from-a-reliable-process-constitute-knowledge-antithesis-e3bb515f-5a40-4d7c-9062-1a404acde114.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed through a virtuous cognitive character**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is formed through a virtuous cognitive character**
