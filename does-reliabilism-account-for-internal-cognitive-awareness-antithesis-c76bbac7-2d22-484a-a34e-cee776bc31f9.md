# Does reliabilism account for internal cognitive awareness?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c76bbac7-2d22-484a-a34e-cee776bc31f9
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism Summary](reliabilism-summary-synthesis-c54e9ff1-39e1-4224-b3be-b0979177f411.md)

## Content

**Imagine a person who has a true belief due to a reliable subconscious process but has no awareness or insight into how their belief was formed. Intuitively, we may doubt that such a person truly knows the proposition, despite the reliability of their belief-forming process.**, **Knowledge seems to require some level of cognitive access or awareness that reliabilism does not necessitate.**
