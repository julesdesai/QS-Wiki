# What about knowledge that alters what is practical?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c8c356c8-8c9f-4641-abac-a1560844a689
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Revised Pragmatic Theory of Knowledge](revised-pragmatic-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-d7b209ba-b523-44b3-ad9a-c228c53c8771.md)

## Content

**Knowledge itself can change perceptions of what is practical and beneficial.**, **Learning a new fact can redirect one’s practical pursuits or redefine priorities.**, **Thus, defining knowledge based on pre-existing practical utility may neglect its transformative capacity.**
