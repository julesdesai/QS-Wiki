## Content

**A belief is safe if in most nearby possible worlds where the belief is formed in the same way, it is true**, **A person knows a proposition if they believe it and their belief is safe**, **A person knows a proposition if the proposition is true**, **Safety requires that the method of forming the belief would not easily lead to a false belief**, **The belief remains safe even in the presence of defeaters if the initial justification is intact**, **Defeaters do not nullify knowledge if the core justification withstands the misleading information**
