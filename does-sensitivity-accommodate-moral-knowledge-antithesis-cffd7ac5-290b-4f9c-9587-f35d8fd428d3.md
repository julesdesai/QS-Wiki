# Does sensitivity accommodate moral knowledge?

**Node ID:** cffd7ac5-290b-4f9c-9587-f35d8fd428d3
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Knowledge as Sensitivity](knowledge-as-sensitivity-synthesis-a361f947-df1a-4345-a4d3-ea9ac7712828.md)

## Content

**Consider a person who believes that stealing is wrong, which is a moral truth they consider to be universal.**, **The belief about the wrongness of stealing is held whether others agree or disagree with it.**, **The persistence of belief regardless of consensus complicates the assessment of sensitivity in moral knowledge.**
