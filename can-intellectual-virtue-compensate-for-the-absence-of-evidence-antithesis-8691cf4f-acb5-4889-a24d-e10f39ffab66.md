# Can intellectual virtue compensate for the absence of evidence?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 8691cf4f-acb5-4889-a24d-e10f39ffab66
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue-Responsibilist Epistemology](virtue-responsibilist-epistemology-synthesis-ad635627-9cca-4d73-9dab-ad2a12c77e90.md)

## Content

**A person has no evidence for a belief but holds the belief due to intellectual virtue; intuitively, we feel the lack of evidence undermines the claim to knowledge.**, **There is a plausible principle that even virtue-based beliefs lacking evidential support fail to meet the conditions for knowledge.**
