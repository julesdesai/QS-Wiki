## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification is adequate given a moderately flexible context of the inquiry**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and it is justified adequately in the moderately flexible context**
