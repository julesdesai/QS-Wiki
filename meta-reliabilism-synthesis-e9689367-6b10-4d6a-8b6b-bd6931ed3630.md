## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed by a reliable cognitive process**, **For reliability to confer knowledge, the person must have a reliable higher-order belief that their belief-forming process is reliable**
