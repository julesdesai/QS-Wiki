# Can intellectual virtue compensate for unreliable environments?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9bd16d3c-8ff0-4c64-883e-f3329d2ad5fa
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Safe Intellectual Virtue Epistemology](safe-intellectual-virtue-epistemology-synthesis-f848b9b7-8a1e-463c-8e96-66e43c041eac.md)

## Content

**A person knows a proposition if their belief arises from an intellectual virtue that ensures safety.**, **In a case where a person is in an intrinsically unreliable environment, even if their belief arises from intellectual virtue, it may still be unsafe.**, **If an unreliable environment undermines belief safety despite intellectual virtue, virtues alone may not secure knowledge.**
