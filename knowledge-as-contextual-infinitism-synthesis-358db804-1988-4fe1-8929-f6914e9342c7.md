# Knowledge as Contextual Infinitism

**Parent:** [Can knowledge be false even if it coheres with contextually justified beliefs?](can-knowledge-be-false-even-if-it-coheres-with-contextually-justified-beliefs-antithesis-1bb5f881-6553-4fb7-a83e-203093ea518c.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified by an infinite series of reasons considered within the context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is justified by an infinite series of contextually relevant reasons**
