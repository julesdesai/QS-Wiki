# Do brain-in-a-vat scenarios question direct knowledge via perception?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e86f5b59-d513-405f-be56-9136d5e17ac8
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Direct Realism](direct-realism-synthesis-2e1488cf-3c3f-4cc1-b449-47fe37a86624.md)

## Content

**Consider a thought experiment where a person is a brain in a vat, receiving simulated experiences indistinguishable from reality. Intuitively, we would judge that this person lacks direct access to the external world.**, **This scenario suggests that what one perceives can be disconnected from the actual external world, challenging the concept of direct realism.**
