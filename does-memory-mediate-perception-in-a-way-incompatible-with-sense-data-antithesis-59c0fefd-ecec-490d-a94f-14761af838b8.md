# Does memory mediate perception in a way incompatible with sense data?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 59c0fefd-ecec-490d-a94f-14761af838b8
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Indirect Realism](indirect-realism-synthesis-6f280b3f-d71e-41be-89e6-75cad68b6820.md)

## Content

**Memory can sometimes alter or fill in perceptual experiences, suggesting that what we perceive might integrate more than immediate sense data.**, **If sense data are supplemented by memory, their function as sole mediators of knowledge is challenged.**
