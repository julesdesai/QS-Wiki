## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **A person knows a proposition if they have access to information that connects their belief to the true world**, **This connection can be through reliable cognitive processes or another external link**
