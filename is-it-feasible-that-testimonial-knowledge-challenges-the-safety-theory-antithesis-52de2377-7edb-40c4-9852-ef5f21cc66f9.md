# Is it feasible that testimonial knowledge challenges the Safety Theory?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 52de2377-7edb-40c4-9852-ef5f21cc66f9
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Knowledge as Safety Theory](knowledge-as-safety-theory-synthesis-80153c33-42b8-4596-bd42-fa1a51acc2f7.md)

## Content

**Suppose individuals David and Eve rely on testimony that conveys true propositions. Intuitively, if the testimony is credible and consistent, we would judge both David and Eve possess knowledge.**, **However, if the testimonial source could easily have been deceptive or mistaken in alternative scenarios, meeting the safety condition becomes problematic, suggesting their knowledge claim is weakened.**
