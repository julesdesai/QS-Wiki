# Can knowledge truly arise from an infinite chain of reasons?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 416e991b-440c-4610-966d-b2cae60a5232
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism](infinitism-synthesis-d655bb23-a2c0-4c5f-b0d3-18cd986ab587.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that any human mind can process or grasp an infinite chain of reasons.**, **A conceivable infinite regress of reasons cannot be practically or cognitively completed by human individuals.**, **If justification requires an endless series of subconscious processes, then conscious awareness of justification becomes impossible.**
