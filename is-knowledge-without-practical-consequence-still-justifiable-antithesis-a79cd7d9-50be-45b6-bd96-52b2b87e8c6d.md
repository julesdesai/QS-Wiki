# Is knowledge without practical consequence still justifiable?

**Node ID:** a79cd7d9-50be-45b6-bd96-52b2b87e8c6d

## Content

**Imagine a historian who believes in a historical theory that is strongly supported by evidence but has no practical consequences today. Intuitively, this belief is still justified despite lacking practical impacts.**, **Justifiability of belief does not solely depend on practical consequences; it can also be firmly rooted in evidential support.**
