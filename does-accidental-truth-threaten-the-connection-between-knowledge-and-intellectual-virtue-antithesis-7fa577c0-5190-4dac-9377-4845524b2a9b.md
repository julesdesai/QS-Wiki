# Does accidental truth threaten the connection between knowledge and intellectual virtue?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7fa577c0-5190-4dac-9377-4845524b2a9b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilist Virtue Epistemology](reliabilist-virtue-epistemology-synthesis-a48fe91b-846b-4740-a801-397d1be1c53f.md)

## Content

**A person can believe a proposition that is true due to a coincidence unrelated to their intellectual virtue.**, **The occurrence of true beliefs that are accidentally true undermines the necessity of a reliable process for knowledge.**
