**Parent:** [How do we handle beliefs formed under deception yet safely believed?](how-do-we-handle-beliefs-formed-under-deception-yet-safely-believed-antithesis-7f0ab3a1-0347-4d87-ab55-e32f51929e58.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief would not easily be false in similar situations**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief would not be based on a falsehood in similar situations**
