# Do all true beliefs lead to practical benefits?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a82c7bb0-a52c-4da8-9445-2e524697e253
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatism Summary](pragmatism-summary-synthesis-ddc2fd27-140a-48fe-b697-40a3c8c11bfc.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where a true belief about an abstract mathematical truth yields no immediate practical benefit. Intuitively, this still counts as knowledge.**, **It is implausible to claim that all truths must have practical application to be considered knowledge, as knowledge can be pursued for its own sake.**
