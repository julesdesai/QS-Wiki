# Do Gettier cases undermine truth-tracking as necessary for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 555211fe-769d-49c3-8783-cbd5ef757874
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Truth-Tracking Theory](truth-tracking-theory-synthesis-c14e1fed-7ecf-480a-90a0-7623f2270956.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person arrives at a true belief through a justified false belief that is coincidentally true. In this scenario, intuitively we would judge that the person does not possess knowledge despite tracking truth in nearby possible worlds.**, **A true belief obtained through justified false beliefs can satisfy truth-tracking conditions without constituting knowledge.**
