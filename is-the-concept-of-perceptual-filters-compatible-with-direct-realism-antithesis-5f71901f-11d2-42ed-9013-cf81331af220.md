# Is the concept of perceptual filters compatible with direct realism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5f71901f-11d2-42ed-9013-cf81331af220
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Selective Direct Realism](selective-direct-realism-synthesis-744f6327-3f79-490f-be88-a0aa209da42b.md)

## Content

**Psychological research indicates humans use perceptual filters, like attention and focus, which shape what we perceive.**, **The use of perceptual filters means that perception does not relate to unaltered foundational parts of reality but is influenced by various factors.**, **Perceptual filters cause variations and instability over different conditions, questioning the stability criterion for knowledge.**
