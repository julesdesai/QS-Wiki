# Does proper functionalism rely too much on external environments?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f2e1d353-9b23-4ed0-911d-f451d8fab807
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Proper Functionalism](proper-functionalism-synthesis-56ac2890-cc65-4132-90ba-c28a7f97b59c.md)

## Content

**The reliability of cognitive faculties is dependent on the truth-conducive nature of the environment.**, **A belief could be considered knowledge if it is true, yet it may not have been formed in a truth-conducive environment.**, **Proper functionalism appears to overemphasize the role of external environmental conditions in determining knowledge.**
