# Can a person have knowledge if they do not realize the safety of their belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 983a8dcf-68a6-47de-a719-c2406df8d7fd
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Safety Theory of Knowledge](safety-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-8e9332b1-e101-417c-8080-59c95e445ed8.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person has a true belief that is safe in similar situations, but they are unaware of the reason why their belief is safe or true. Intuitively, we might judge they lack knowledge despite holding a true and safe belief.**, **Awareness of the factors that contribute to the truth and safety of a belief may be deemed necessary for genuine knowledge possession.**
