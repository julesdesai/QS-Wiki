# Could Infinitism imply redundancy in justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e33ed31a-b9b9-412d-9aec-985704e699c4
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism](infinitism-synthesis-dd7098a7-016a-4f9d-9b36-944f2be4f1f4.md)

## Content

**A non-repeating chain of infinite justification implies additional, potentially unnecessary layers of reasoning.**, **There are no mechanisms to distinguish between necessary and redundant components in an infinite chain of reasons.**, **The idea of redundancy runs counter to the principle of parsimony in philosophical explanation and reasoning.**
