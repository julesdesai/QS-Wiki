## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person has evidence that supports the belief in the truth of the proposition**, **The strength of evidence must be proportionate to the certainty required for knowledge**
