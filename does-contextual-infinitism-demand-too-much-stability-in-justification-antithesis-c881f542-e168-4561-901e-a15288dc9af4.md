# Does contextual infinitism demand too much stability in justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c881f542-e168-4561-901e-a15288dc9af4
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextual Infinitism](contextual-infinitism-synthesis-969d15cb-7035-4449-8e21-a18b5558491f.md)

## Content

**Justification requires stability to differentiate knowledge from mere belief**, **It is implausible that an infinite and constantly evolving chain of reasons can maintain the stability required for justification in knowledge**, **Consider a scenario where newly added reasons continuously alter the overall support for a belief; this would challenge the notion of stable justification**
