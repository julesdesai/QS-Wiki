## Content

**Knowledge consists of two components: the reliability of the method and the presence of intellectual virtue**, **Intellectual virtues are character traits that consistently lead to true beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **The method used to form the belief must be both reliable and virtuous**
