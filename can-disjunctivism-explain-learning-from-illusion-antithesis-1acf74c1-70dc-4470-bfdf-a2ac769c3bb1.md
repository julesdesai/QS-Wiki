# Can disjunctivism explain learning from illusion?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1acf74c1-70dc-4470-bfdf-a2ac769c3bb1
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Disjunctivism](disjunctivism-synthesis-19882849-157e-4dc6-b31d-66173a7c79c0.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which someone watches a magician create the illusion of pulling a rabbit from a hat. Intuitively, they learn about the illusion even though their perception is not veridical.**, **It is implausible that perceptual knowledge always involves direct relation to facts, as experiences can lead to knowledge through misperceptions.**
