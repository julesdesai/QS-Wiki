## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for the proposition**, **Knowledge requires reflexive access to one's own cognitive states**, **A person knows a proposition if it is true, they have justification for it, and possess reflexive access to their cognitive valuation of it**
