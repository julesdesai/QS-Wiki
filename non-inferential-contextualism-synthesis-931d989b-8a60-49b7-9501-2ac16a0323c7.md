## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified**, **Justification can be obtained through direct intuition or basic perceptual experiences without inferential processes, with contextual elements enriching but not obligatory for justification**
