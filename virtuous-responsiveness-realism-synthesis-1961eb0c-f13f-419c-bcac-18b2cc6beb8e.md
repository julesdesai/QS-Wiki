# Virtuous Responsiveness Realism

**Parent:** [Can true belief arising from unreliable processes qualify as knowledge?](can-true-belief-arising-from-unreliable-processes-qualify-as-knowledge-antithesis-7099db5e-7ea2-4f42-9e01-2584f3bd34c3.md)

## Content

**Knowledge is true belief formed by a cognitive agent's responsiveness to reality**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed by responsiveness to reality**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe it, the proposition is true, and their belief is formed by responsiveness to reality**
