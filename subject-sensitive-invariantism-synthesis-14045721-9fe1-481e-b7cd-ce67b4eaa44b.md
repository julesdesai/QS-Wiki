## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the subject's practical interests play a role in justification**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the subject's practical interests are appropriately accounted for in justification**
