# How can knowledge remain consistent when external factors are variable and unpredictable?

**Node ID:** fd320211-243c-441b-984f-46c812efd7b0
**Depth:** 4

**Parent:** [Externalism with Reliance on External Factors](externalism-with-reliance-on-external-factors-synthesis-94d45e40-e833-458e-8cba-8623e44201d6.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that knowledge can be maintained consistently when it is dependent on variable external factors that an individual cannot predict or control.**, **If justification is reliant on external reliability, then any fluctuation undermines the stability of the claimed knowledge.**
