## Content

**Knowledge is true belief that would not be held if the belief were false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is sensitive to truth**, **A person knows a proposition if they believe it, the proposition is true, and they would not believe it if it were false**
