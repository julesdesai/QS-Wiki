# Knowledge with Epistemic Justification

**Node ID:** 7c93a5ca-3b49-4148-8244-bdeb70c3d639
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is it possible to have unsafe knowledge?](is-it-possible-to-have-unsafe-knowledge-antithesis-501091cb-fbee-4ff2-bfae-075aaf9839ec.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is justified through strong evidence or reasoning**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief has practical consequences or usefulness**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is justified through strong evidence or reasoning, and the belief has practical consequences or usefulness**
