# What role does epistemic responsibility play in Proper Functionalism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 07bdad77-b566-4965-9ba9-5875e628d149
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Proper Functionalism](proper-functionalism-synthesis-17c9fccb-5f3e-4011-91c3-5213c66550ac.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that individuals are epistemically responsible for ensuring their faculties are functioning properly. This principle together with the notion of epistemic responsibility entails that proper function alone may not capture the full extent of what it means to know.**, **A case in which a person neglects their responsibility yet their faculties function properly might lead to justified but irresponsible beliefs. Intuitively, knowledge seems to require some degree of epistemic responsibility.**
