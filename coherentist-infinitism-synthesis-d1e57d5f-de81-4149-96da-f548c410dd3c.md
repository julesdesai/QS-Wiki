## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that justification for believing the proposition involves infinite and coherent reasons**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and justification involves infinite and coherent reasons**
