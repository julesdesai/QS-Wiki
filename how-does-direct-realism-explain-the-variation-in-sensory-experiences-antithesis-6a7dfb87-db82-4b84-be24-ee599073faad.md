# How does direct realism explain the variation in sensory experiences?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 6a7dfb87-db82-4b84-be24-ee599073faad
**Depth:** 2
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Direct Realism](direct-realism-thesis-65da2b06-0c60-4e66-b428-771d2431fe55.md)

## Content

**In a case where one person perceives an object as cold and another as warm, variability challenges direct perception.**, **Sensory experiences can differ across species, indicating perception does not directly reveal reality as it is.**, **These variations suggest perception is not an immediate access to reality but influenced by physiological factors.**
