# How do we define context?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1242e314-8726-4e2c-be55-9bdb54c597d4
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism](contextualism-synthesis-ed8715f4-7106-486f-8992-736e64ae52d2.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person is unsure which standards for knowledge are applicable. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that knowledge cannot depend on context since contexts might be ambiguous.**, **It is implausible that contextual factors without a clear definition can determine whether someone knows something.**
