# Nozick's Tracking Theory

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can a true belief lack intellectual virtue?](can-a-true-belief-lack-intellectual-virtue-antithesis-23d054b2-4c48-440d-bb46-f85f17165e50.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief tracks the truth across nearby possible worlds**, **Tracking the truth involves the belief being true in nearby possible worlds where the belief is held and being not held in worlds where it is false**
