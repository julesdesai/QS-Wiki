# Are Gettier cases a problem for the Safety Principle?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0f50a9f6-bc5f-4ba1-8f36-97acd5372404
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Knowledge with Safety Principle](knowledge-with-safety-principle-synthesis-36cb0228-2d5a-4aa3-87ba-01c1622dfdc5.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where someone holds a justified true belief that their friend owns a Ford but only because they misremember a recent conversation; coincidentally, the friend does buy a Ford afterwards. Intuitively, they do not know their friend owns a Ford.**, **Their belief, despite being true, does not track the truth as it relies on coincidental circumstances.**, **In nearby possible worlds, the belief could easily have been false if their friend hadn't bought a Ford.**
