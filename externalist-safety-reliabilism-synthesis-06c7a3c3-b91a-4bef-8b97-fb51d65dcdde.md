# Externalist Safety Reliabilism

**Parent:** [Is knowledge undermined by an underlying false assumption?](is-knowledge-undermined-by-an-underlying-false-assumption-antithesis-a8b1fece-2057-47cf-8532-335d477cd92d.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed by processes that consistently yield true beliefs in similar scenarios**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is formed by processes that consistently yield true beliefs in similar scenarios**
