# Can knowledge exist without justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 13a7cbe6-70d0-4be9-a802-404ad5752967
**Depth:** 2
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism](contextualism-thesis-de4edc71-c75a-4fcd-afbe-4333fb44b1b5.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that knowledge requires justification.**, **Consider a case in which a person believes a true proposition by mere coincidence. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that the person does not have knowledge.**, **Belief and truth together are insufficient for knowledge without justification.**
