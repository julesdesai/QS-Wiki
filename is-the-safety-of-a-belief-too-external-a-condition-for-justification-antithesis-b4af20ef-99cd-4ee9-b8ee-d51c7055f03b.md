# Is the safety of a belief too external a condition for justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b4af20ef-99cd-4ee9-b8ee-d51c7055f03b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Safety Condition Externalism Summary](safety-condition-externalism-summary-synthesis-29bd2257-ee31-4256-8506-f8628368ab8d.md)

## Content

**Imagine a scenario where a belief is true in nearby possible worlds, but the individual holding the belief misunderstands the evidence supporting it. Intuitively, we would say this person lacks justification for their belief.**, **It is plausible that justification requires a correct understanding of the evidence, not just the external condition that the belief remains true in nearby worlds.**
