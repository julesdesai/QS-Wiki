# Do intuitive examples of self-justifying beliefs sufficiently illustrate the process that halts regress?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** cee9219b-e2b6-4ec7-9dec-8324957e21bc
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundationalism with Basic Belief Justification](foundationalism-with-basic-belief-justification-synthesis-36983d87-c369-411c-807c-6c854afc89c3.md)

## Content

**Suppose we examine a belief such as "I am thinking" and view it as self-justifying, an intuitive example of a basic belief. Intuitively, it seems to halt an infinite regress.**, **It is implausible to assume that intuitive examples alone are comprehensive explanations for how foundationalism effectively halts regress without more rigorous theoretical grounding.**
