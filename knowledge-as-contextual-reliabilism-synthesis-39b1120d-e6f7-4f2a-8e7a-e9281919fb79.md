## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed through a reliable process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed in conditions where the process is reliable**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is formed through a context-sensitive reliable process**
