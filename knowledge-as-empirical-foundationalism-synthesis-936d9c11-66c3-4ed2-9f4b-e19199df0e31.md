**Parent:** [What happens when coherence with beliefs diverges from new empirical evidence?](what-happens-when-coherence-with-beliefs-diverges-from-new-empirical-evidence-antithesis-dc0b9419-cb5f-4000-a568-2af23e9ffe9b.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is grounded in empirical evidence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is grounded in empirical evidence**
