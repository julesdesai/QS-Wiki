# Is functionalism compatible with the possibility of zombies?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5c0a9594-085e-471d-a4f5-454a5234d5c6
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Functionalism](functionalism-synthesis-2d0849d8-6d8d-49c5-822f-95e70d5e82e6.md)

## Content

**Consider the thought experiment involving philosophical zombies, beings functionally identical to humans but lacking subjective experience. Intuitively, we would judge that zombies do not possess knowledge because they lack experiential awareness, challenging the idea that functional equivalence to humans results in knowledge.**
