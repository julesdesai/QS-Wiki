## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, what counts as justification is determined by the reliability of the process used to arrive at the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, the justification cannot be defeated by new evidence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is true, the belief is formed by a reliable process, and the justification cannot be defeated**
