# Justification and Truth Centric View

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** bfa41697-715b-40fb-970d-27d5c4934c69
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is belief always necessary for knowledge?](is-belief-always-necessary-for-knowledge-antithesis-9acf10fd-b00d-4a57-a3a8-e8fa501c2785.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have justification for believing the proposition and the proposition is true**, **For knowledge, it is not always necessary to hold a specific or explicit belief if the relevant justification and truth conditions are satisfied**
