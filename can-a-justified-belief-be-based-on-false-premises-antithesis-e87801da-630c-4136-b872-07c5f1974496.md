# Can a justified belief be based on false premises?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e87801da-630c-4136-b872-07c5f1974496
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [No-False-Lemma View](no-false-lemma-view-synthesis-d417d5a7-7b9d-4d6b-8a89-c64775b0283f.md)

## Content

**Suppose a belief is true and justified but initially relied on false premises that did not contribute to its truth. Intuitively, knowledge is not present even though truth and justification exist.**, **There is a plausible principle that a justified belief must not only be free of falsehood in its premises but also wholly transparent in its justification without any misleading basis.**
