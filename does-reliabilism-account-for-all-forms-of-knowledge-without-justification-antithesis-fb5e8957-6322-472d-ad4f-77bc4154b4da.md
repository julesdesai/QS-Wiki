# Does reliabilism account for all forms of knowledge without justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** fb5e8957-6322-472d-ad4f-77bc4154b4da
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilist Theory of Knowledge](reliabilist-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-b27c6d4b-ac60-4e95-8268-38e201ff9a4c.md)

## Content

**Consider an animal that seems to know where food is hidden because of instinctive behavior. It does not have a belief that was formed using a reliable method, or due to reasoning or justification.**, **It is implausible that such instinctive knowledge requires reliable belief formation processes.**
