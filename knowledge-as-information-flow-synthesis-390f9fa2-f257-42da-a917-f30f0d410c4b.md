## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there be a proper flow of information from the fact to the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, there is a proper flow of information from the fact to the belief, and the proposition is true**
