# Can knowledge vary arbitrarily with stakes?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 27c53c9e-d3e2-4b30-a152-fd6240e74221
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Encroachment Summary](pragmatic-encroachment-summary-synthesis-e897bf05-5a21-418d-ae35-b563f51919c6.md)

## Content

**A case in which a person believes, based on solid evidence, that a bridge is safe and knows this when taking a stroll across it may not be a case where they know it if they are about to drive a school bus full of children over it.**, **It is implausible that knowledge can vary simply due to varying practical stakes, as this leads to inconsistency in our judgments of scenarios with the same evidence.**
