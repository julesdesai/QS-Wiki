# Can falsified coherent beliefs still be justified?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** fb12184d-939a-40e9-84ae-e8d24edf77fb
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Hybrid Coherentist-Internalist View](hybrid-coherentist-internalist-view-synthesis-ffa44d27-7377-4f6d-8451-515975b92108.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a system of beliefs is coherent but later shown to be based on false premises. Intuitively, we would judge that such beliefs were not justified from the outset.**, **A belief is not justified if it is based on false premises, regardless of coherence or internal justification.**
