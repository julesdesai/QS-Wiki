# Knowledge as Sensitivity Condition

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge be undermined by defeating evidence that one ignores?](can-knowledge-be-undermined-by-defeating-evidence-that-one-ignores-antithesis-63165ec4-3b37-4af5-a48f-eeb5628d959a.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for their belief to be sensitive to truth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is sensitive to truth**
