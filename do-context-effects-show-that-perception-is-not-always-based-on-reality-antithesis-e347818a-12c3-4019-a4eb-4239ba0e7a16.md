# Do context effects show that perception is not always based on reality?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e347818a-12c3-4019-a4eb-4239ba0e7a16
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Representationalism Summary](representationalism-summary-synthesis-d2db7ebf-cdb9-4791-9194-360bca10efaf.md)

## Content

**Perception of stimuli can be altered based on surrounding context.**, **Context effects indicate that representations can change without any change in the actual stimulus.**, **This raises doubts about representations directly reflecting reality.**
