# Does the theory account for the 'Gettier Problem'?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 6b5331d1-cc7d-4122-bb05-b10b9a85df8a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Knowledge as Truth-Tracking](knowledge-as-truth-tracking-synthesis-2c16302d-4d7f-4dee-a7ac-cd2c9d6b658d.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person has a justified true belief due to coincidental factors, which is not knowledge according to the Gettier Problem. Intuitively, many would agree this is not genuine knowledge.**, **Gettier cases present justified true beliefs that fail to be knowledge, thus questioning the completeness of the truth-tracking theory for accounting such cases.**
