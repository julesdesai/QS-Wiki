# Knowledge as Causal Process Reliabilism

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 9970737a-2027-4a11-bdaf-f92ff66fdc02
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can a belief be both reliable and false?](can-a-belief-be-both-reliable-and-false-antithesis-cf898393-120c-4459-b740-c06bb6ff53e3.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is produced by a causal process that regularly results in true beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is produced by a causal process that reliably yields true beliefs**
