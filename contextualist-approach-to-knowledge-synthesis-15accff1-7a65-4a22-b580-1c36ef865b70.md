## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition relevant to the context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the level of justification meets the standards set by the context**, **A person knows a proposition if and only if the proposition is true, believed, and justified to the relevant context’s standards**
