# Knowledge as Safe Belief

**Parent:** [Can erroneous applications of virtues lead to knowledge?](can-erroneous-applications-of-virtues-lead-to-knowledge-antithesis-30e354e2-70d0-44a8-aa37-5f7500b41808.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed by a process that avoids error in similar situations**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is formed in a way that would not easily have been false**
