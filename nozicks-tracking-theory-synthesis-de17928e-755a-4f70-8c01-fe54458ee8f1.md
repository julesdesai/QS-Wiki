## Content

**Knowledge is true belief that tracks the truth across possible scenarios**, **A person knows a proposition if, in relevant possible worlds, if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **A person knows a proposition if, in relevant possible worlds where the proposition is true, the person believes it**, **Belief in a proposition must track its truth to count as knowledge**
