## Content

**For a person to know a mathematical truth, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a mathematical truth, it is necessary that they have a rational intuition of the truth**, **For a person to know a mathematical truth, it is sufficient that it is true and they have a rational intuition of it**
