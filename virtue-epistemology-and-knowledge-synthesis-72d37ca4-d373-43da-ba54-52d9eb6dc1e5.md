## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief results from intellectual virtues**, **An intellectual virtue is a trait that reliably leads to the formation of true beliefs**
