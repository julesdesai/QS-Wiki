# Can social factors undermine beliefs formed through reliable processes?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** cb47f258-d02e-460c-8817-3ab20a6f16ff
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Knowledge as Reliable Belief](knowledge-as-reliable-belief-synthesis-6bb1f4f4-efa6-4ffe-a0f7-a4c9c80938da.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which an individual's belief is based on reliable testimony but is later invalidated by their entire community’s contrasting views. Intuitively, we would judge that this communal contradiction undermines the individual’s claim to knowledge, suggesting reliability alone might not be sufficient.**
