# Knowledge as Contextualist Externalism

**Parent:** [Are Gettier-like cases a problem for externalism?](are-gettier-like-cases-a-problem-for-externalism-antithesis-66c258d1-f6f4-4edb-9efd-72281d8bf160.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed by a reliable process**, **For a belief to constitute knowledge, the standards of reliability may vary with context**, **For a person to know a proposition in a particular context, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is formed by a contextually adequate process**
