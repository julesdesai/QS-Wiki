## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief could not have been false in the actual world or any nearby possible worlds**, **A belief is safe if it is true in all nearby possible worlds where it is held**
