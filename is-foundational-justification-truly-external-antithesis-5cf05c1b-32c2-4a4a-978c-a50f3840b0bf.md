# Is foundational justification truly external?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5cf05c1b-32c2-4a4a-978c-a50f3840b0bf

**Parent:** [Externalist Foundationalism](externalist-foundationalism-synthesis-3e0224db-6723-4288-8a1b-8819cbac7570.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that foundational beliefs require justification rooted in experience or knowledge accessible to the individual. This principle together with external justification implies that foundational justification might require more than merely external inputs for validation.**
