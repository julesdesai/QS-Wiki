# How stable is knowledge that is continually reaffirmed?

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Sensitivity Theory of Knowledge](sensitivity-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-102d682a-8eb5-4983-bf10-f287ee1765f9.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person checks their thermometer frequently and believes it reads 20°C. If it changed momentarily, they’d still believe this having checked so many times. Yet, they are considered to know the temperature.**, **The consistency of evidence would suggest knowledge even in the face of brief false beliefs.**
