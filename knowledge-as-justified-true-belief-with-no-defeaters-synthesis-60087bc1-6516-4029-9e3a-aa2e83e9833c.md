# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with No Defeaters

**Parent:** [Does coincidence alongside the truth negate a higher epistemic standard for knowledge?](does-coincidence-alongside-the-truth-negate-a-higher-epistemic-standard-for-knowledge-antithesis-a81dcb34-e669-406e-81e9-09c890139b91.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no defeaters present that would undermine the justification**
