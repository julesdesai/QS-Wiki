# Can a lucky guess be considered knowledge under truth-tracking?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0d222ea2-6024-4d64-baf8-b41cfe9ffa27
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Information-Tracking Theory](information-tracking-theory-synthesis-990127f2-7220-489f-9c29-17860a8cc351.md)

## Content

**A person might guess a true proposition while lacking evidence and still meet the conditions of truth-tracking.**, **Intuitively, a lucky guess does not constitute knowledge, suggesting truth-tracking is inadequate.**
