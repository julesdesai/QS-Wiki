# Is it sufficient for knowledge if one is unaware of the mechanism making a process reliable?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0300f036-3a23-4e62-9ed7-fb20275d8443
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism about Knowledge](reliabilism-about-knowledge-synthesis-262d9553-ebaf-45bd-b20f-bae6bfba05f7.md)

## Content

**Consider a person using a computer algorithm to predict outcomes. They are unaware of the specifics of how the algorithm works but often produce true beliefs. Intuitively, we might question the attribution of knowledge if process understanding seems required.**, **Reliability focuses on general outcomes but does not address whether understanding the process is central to assigning knowledge.**
