# Does the role of expectations in perception challenge Direct Realism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7d36076e-3fec-4e14-8122-1dd475a36aff
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Direct Realism with Cross-Modal Integration](direct-realism-with-cross-modal-integration-synthesis-1a006bbb-612c-4e5a-ba7b-987e18fe12d2.md)

## Content

**In an experiment, participants expecting to see a certain object are more likely to perceive it even if it is absent. Intuitively, this suggests expectations shape perception.**, **This reliance on expectation implies that perception may not be a purely direct and objective relationship with external objects.**, **Perceptions influenced by expectations highlight that direct sensory perception is susceptible to cognitive influences.**
