# Does predictive success equate to knowledge?

**Parent:** [Proper Function Theory](proper-function-theory-synthesis-ea617099-7596-484d-aca6-1b318b0033ac.md)

## Content

**Imagine a person who accurately predicts future events using astrology, despite no scientific basis for astrology. Intuitively, their success does not constitute genuine knowledge.**, **There is a plausible principle that predictive success alone is insufficient for knowledge unless grounded in reliable methods.**
