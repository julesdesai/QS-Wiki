# Knowledge as True Reliable Belief and Usefulness

**Parent:** [Are truth and usefulness independently sufficient for knowledge?](are-truth-and-usefulness-independently-sufficient-for-knowledge-antithesis-ac8c6d8a-6cb7-4678-a47a-b3d76e3b5327.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary to BELIEVE the proposition true by means of a reliable process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is useful in practice**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is believed true by a reliable process, the proposition is true, and the belief has practical usefulness**
