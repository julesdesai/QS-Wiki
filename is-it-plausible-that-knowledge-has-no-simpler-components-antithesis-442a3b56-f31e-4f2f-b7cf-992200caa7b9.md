# Is it plausible that knowledge has no simpler components?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 442a3b56-f31e-4f2f-b7cf-992200caa7b9
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Knowledge First Epistemology](knowledge-first-epistemology-synthesis-5ffe7f85-d642-4212-8020-2dc118f603c3.md)

## Content

**The idea that knowledge is fundamental raises the issue of whether it can still be analyzed into simpler components without losing explanatory power.**, **This implies that if knowledge cannot be broken down into simpler components like belief or justification, this could problematize its analysis in other epistemic contexts.**
