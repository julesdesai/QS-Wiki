## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from an intellectual virtue or competence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the virtue shows stable truth-tracking over time**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief arises from a virtue with demonstrated stability in truth-tracking**
