# Epistemic Dependability with Defeasibility

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** e3b6325d-e239-4abf-87a1-0a7e695de506
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is stable and reliable belief always sufficient for knowledge?](is-stable-and-reliable-belief-always-sufficient-for-knowledge-antithesis-85d3b63d-2e5c-4ca2-a71d-55133a8b9eb1.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no defeaters present that would undermine the belief if known**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief depends on a stable and reliable relation to the truth and there are no defeaters present that would undermine the belief if known**
