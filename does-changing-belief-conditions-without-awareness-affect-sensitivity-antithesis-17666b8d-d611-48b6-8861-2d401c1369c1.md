# Does changing belief conditions without awareness affect sensitivity?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 17666b8d-d611-48b6-8861-2d401c1369c1
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Sensitivity Theory](sensitivity-theory-synthesis-68f75ad1-a4e1-40d8-ae80-33e70d924463.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case where a person believes a proposition because of stable environmental conditions. If these conditions subtly change, altering proposition truth, yet they hold the belief due to ignorance, this impacts sensitivity. Intuitively, they lack true knowledge.**
