## Content

**A person knows a proposition if they have a true belief formed by a reliable process**, **For a belief to be justified, no internal awareness of the reliability is required**, **Reliability alone can suffice for knowledge if a belief is true and reliably formed**, **A belief is externally justified if the process leading to the belief is reliable**
