# Does infinite justification lead to skepticism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 06d76117-dbf5-4bb3-b044-a7e36aed4afe
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism with Contextual Stops](infinitism-with-contextual-stops-synthesis-554d1150-ce74-450b-ad43-18770e414e5e.md)

## Content

**Justification involves infinite reasons, which might imply skepticism because no belief could ever be sufficiently justified.**, **If knowledge requires an unattainable standard, skepticism about knowledge claims could increase.**, **An infinite regress of reasons may leave no belief fully justified, leading to overall doubt about human knowledge.**
