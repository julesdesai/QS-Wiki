# Does collective knowledge invalidate individual cognitive credit?

**Node Type:** antithesis

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Credit Theory of Knowledge](credit-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-193f50ad-c71a-4e26-bf9b-21d5bbf78043.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where a scientific team collectively arrives at a true theory due to a complex collaborative process. No individual member can claim their cognitive ability solely resulted in the true belief.**, **Complex collaborative processes in science or other areas suggest that true beliefs can be considered knowledge even when not creditable to an individual's cognitive abilities.**
