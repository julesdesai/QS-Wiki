## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the standards of knowledge are matched to the subject's epistemic situation**, **The standards of knowledge do not change with different contexts outside the subject's situation**
