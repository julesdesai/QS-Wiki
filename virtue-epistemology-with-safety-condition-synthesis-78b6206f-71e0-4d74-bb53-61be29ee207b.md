## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is a result of intellectual virtue**, **An intellectual virtue is a trait that promotes forming beliefs safely, where safety means the belief could not easily have been false**
