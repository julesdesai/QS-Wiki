# Should practical stakes influence knowledge standards?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 560dc292-8717-45d6-b13e-522a4c47aee9
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Contextualism Summary](pragmatic-contextualism-summary-synthesis-cadd0bfa-2179-4efb-90ce-cbf918dbd894.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that practical stakes, such as potential personal consequences, should influence whether a person knows a proposition.**, **Consider two individuals with identical evidence and beliefs; if one faces higher practical stakes, intuitively both should equally know or not know the proposition.**
