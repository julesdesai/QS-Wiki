# Knowledge as Justified True Belief Plus No Defeaters

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Are there contexts where knowledge standards should not change?](are-there-contexts-where-knowledge-standards-should-not-change-antithesis-51088dce-556b-4df5-abab-d2cad3c57797.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no true defeaters for the belief in that proposition**
