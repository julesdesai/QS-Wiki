## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is justified through multiple complementary approaches**, **Complementary approaches include coherence, reliability, and pragmatic usefulness**
