## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have a high probability of being right, making their belief likely true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief holds a high probability of truth**
