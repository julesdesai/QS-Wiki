# Can knowledge withstand an infinite regress argument?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b8d570c5-caf5-4293-9a9c-34b4bb6b8f70
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualist Infinitism](contextualist-infinitism-synthesis-74ebcf03-3de8-4e28-950c-dc2eefc9dc13.md)

## Content

**Suppose each reason for a belief requires further justification without end.**, **Intuitively, this creates an infinite regress that undermines the possibility of final justification.**, **If final justification is elusive, then knowledge becomes unachievable.**
