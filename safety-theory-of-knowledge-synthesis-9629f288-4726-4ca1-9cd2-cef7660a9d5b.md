# Safety Theory of Knowledge

**Parent:** [Is causation always a reliable guide to knowledge?](is-causation-always-a-reliable-guide-to-knowledge-antithesis-7ffa6cfe-aad6-42e9-9423-0e442192da89.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in most nearby possible worlds where the person believes the proposition, it is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and in most nearby possible worlds where the person believes the proposition, it is true**
