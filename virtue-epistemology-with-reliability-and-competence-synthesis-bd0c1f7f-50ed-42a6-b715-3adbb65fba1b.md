## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief in the proposition results from a reliable cognitive process**, **A reliable cognitive process is one that consistently produces true beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they competently use their cognitive abilities in forming the belief**
