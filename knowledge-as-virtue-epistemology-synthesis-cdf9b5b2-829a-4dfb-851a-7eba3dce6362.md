## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is produced by intellectual virtues**, **An intellectual virtue is a trait that reliably leads to the acquisition of true beliefs**
