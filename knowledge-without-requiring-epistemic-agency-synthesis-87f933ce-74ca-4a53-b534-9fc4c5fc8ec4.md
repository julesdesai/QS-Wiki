# Knowledge without Requiring Epistemic Agency

**Parent:** [Does requiring awareness privilege epistemically advantaged agents?](does-requiring-awareness-privilege-epistemically-advantaged-agents-antithesis-1341f05a-c424-44bd-8852-53ae6b771d41.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief to be produced by a factive cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is produced by a factive cognitive process**
