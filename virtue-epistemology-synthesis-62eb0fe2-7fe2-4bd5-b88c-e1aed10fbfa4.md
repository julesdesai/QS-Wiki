## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is due to the person's intellectual virtues**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, it is true, and the belief is due to intellectual virtues**
