# Does the context-dependent evaluation of virtue account for practical inconsistencies?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 37cda5af-670a-4184-8f66-539b1b0d5f0a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalist Virtue Contextualism](externalist-virtue-contextualism-synthesis-69e2f760-46ad-465f-a31e-67e150de071d.md)

## Content

**If intellectual virtue is context-dependent, then virtues might be evaluated differently in action than in principle.**, **In practice, context-dependent virtue evaluation might result in actions not aligning with initially stated virtues or principles.**, **Practical inconsistencies might lead to doubts about the efficacy or reliability of context-dependent intellectual virtues.**
