## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their cognitive faculties are functioning properly**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **It is sufficient for a person to know a proposition that they believe it, their cognitive faculties function properly according to a design plan, and the faculties are aimed at truth**
