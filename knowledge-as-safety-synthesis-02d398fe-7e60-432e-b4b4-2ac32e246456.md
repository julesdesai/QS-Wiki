# Knowledge as Safety

**Parent:** [Can a reliable process lead to inaccurate beliefs in specific instances?](can-a-reliable-process-lead-to-inaccurate-beliefs-in-specific-instances-antithesis-25173870-280f-4d89-af58-aa5bfa24ff0d.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in nearby possible worlds where the person believes the proposition, it is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and in nearby possible worlds where the person believes the proposition, it is true**
