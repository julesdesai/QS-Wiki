## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person believes the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief has a high conditional probability given the person's evidence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that these probabilistic conditions hold**
