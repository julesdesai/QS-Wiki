## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they are responsive to reasons for or against the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the person's cognitive faculties are reliable and aligned with a disposition to respond accurately to the truth**
