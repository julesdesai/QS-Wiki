# Does epistemic relativism fail to account for cross-community communication?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d5bbac65-a13a-4050-80c8-dab372c47d8a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Epistemic Relativism](epistemic-relativism-synthesis-8a36f430-fbe7-4356-93bb-efe42975c670.md)

## Content

**Consider a situation where two individuals from different epistemic communities must communicate about a factual claim Q.**, **Each individual may hold different standards of what constitutes knowledge regarding Q due to their distinct community norms.**, **These varying standards can inhibit effective communication and mutual understanding if both individuals assert knowledge claims from their divergent frameworks.**
