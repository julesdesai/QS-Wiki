# Justified True Belief Plus No Defeaters

**Parent:** [Could someone unknowingly use an unreliable process yet still have knowledge by accident?](could-someone-unknowingly-use-an-unreliable-process-yet-still-have-knowledge-by-accident-antithesis-648b07a8-8ffc-43e3-8cf3-5d587e3e1aa4.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no defeaters for the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, they have justification for believing it, and there are no defeaters**
