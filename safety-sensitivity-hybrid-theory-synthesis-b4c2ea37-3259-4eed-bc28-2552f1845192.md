## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in nearby possible worlds where the person believes the proposition, it is not false**, **The belief is both sensitive and safe, integrating both conditions**
