# Knowledge with a Safety Condition

**Parent:** [Do Gettier cases undermine the sufficiency of the externalist condition?](do-gettier-cases-undermine-the-sufficiency-of-the-externalist-condition-antithesis-b706ad37-9bbc-462a-a69a-26cd04d34361.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is safe, meaning that in nearby possible worlds where the belief is held, it is not formed incorrectly**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is safe**
