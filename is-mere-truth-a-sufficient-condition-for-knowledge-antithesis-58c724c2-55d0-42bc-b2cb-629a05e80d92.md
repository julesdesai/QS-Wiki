# Is mere truth a sufficient condition for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 58c724c2-55d0-42bc-b2cb-629a05e80d92

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-a1d061ab-4ce5-4544-95e5-a72c751db8d0.md)

## Content

**Reflect on cases where beliefs are true merely due to chance or coincidence, such as guessing a lottery result that turns out accurate.**, **Intuitively, true beliefs arising from chance or luck do not satisfy the requirements of knowledge, as knowledge should be more robust.**
