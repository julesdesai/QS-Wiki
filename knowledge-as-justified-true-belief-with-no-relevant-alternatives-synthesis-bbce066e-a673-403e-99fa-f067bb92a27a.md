## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed through a reliable process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no relevant alternatives to the truth defeated by the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is formed through a reliable process, and there are no relevant alternatives to the truth**
