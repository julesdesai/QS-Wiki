## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have a belief-forming process that is both reliable and virtuous**, **A belief-forming process is virtuous if it is guided by an intellectual virtue that aims at the truth**
