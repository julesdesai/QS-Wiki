# Can the sensitivity condition of tracking theory handle Gettier cases appropriately?

**Node Type:** antithesis

**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

## Content

**Gettier cases present situations where a belief is true through fortuitous circumstances, challenging whether a belief indeed tracks truth, specifically when altered minor elements affect the belief in different worlds**, **Consider a case where someone believes the time on a stopped clock matches the true time by chance, raising questions about genuine belief tracking and the notion of sensitivity**, **In a Gettier scenario, a person may hold a justified true belief, but the belief's truth is based more on luck than on tracking, casting doubt on its classification as knowledge**
