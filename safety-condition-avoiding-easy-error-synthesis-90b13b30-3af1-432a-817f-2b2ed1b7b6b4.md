# Safety Condition: Avoiding Easy Error

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Is self-deceptive belief causally connected?](is-self-deceptive-belief-causally-connected-antithesis-f6163fdd-c1bb-43b6-a1ab-6ed65bfc4bc8.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **Knowledge requires that in near possible worlds where the person believes the proposition, the proposition remains true**, **A belief is safe if, in all nearby possible situations where the belief is held, it is true**
