## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that belief arises from intellectual competence**, **For a proposition to count as knowledge, it is necessary that the intellectual competences involve sensitivity to contextual features**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the intellectual competences attune to the contextual nuances**
