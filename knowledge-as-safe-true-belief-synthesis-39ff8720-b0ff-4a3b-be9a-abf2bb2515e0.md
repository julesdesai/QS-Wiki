# Knowledge as Safe True Belief

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 39ff8720-b0ff-4a3b-be9a-abf2bb2515e0
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is belief sufficient if it tracks truth without understanding?](is-belief-sufficient-if-it-tracks-truth-without-understanding-antithesis-9fb26d94-f2fb-4619-99f3-6414e4e581d3.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is safely true in actual and nearby scenarios**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is safely true in actual and nearby scenarios**
