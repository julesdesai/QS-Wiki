## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their cognitive faculties are appropriately integrated with their environment**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their cognitive faculties are appropriately integrated with their environment**
