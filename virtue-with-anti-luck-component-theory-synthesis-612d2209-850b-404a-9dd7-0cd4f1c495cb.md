## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed in an apt manner and is not subject to epistemic luck**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is formed in an apt manner, and the belief's truth is not due to luck**
