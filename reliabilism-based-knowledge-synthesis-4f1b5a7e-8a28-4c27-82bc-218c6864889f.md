# Reliabilism-Based Knowledge

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 4f1b5a7e-8a28-4c27-82bc-218c6864889f
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is true belief without knowledge possible?](is-true-belief-without-knowledge-possible-antithesis-8948ca37-b039-494b-811e-d21d5f36d893.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed through a reliable process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is formed through a reliable process**
