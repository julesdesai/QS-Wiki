# Are external objects just mental constructions?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 110bae52-b031-4ccb-90f0-97c8f391c9c2
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Constructivist Phenomenalism](constructivist-phenomenalism-synthesis-f704c8d8-fd4e-46dd-9dda-e9ccb460bf16.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that all perceptions are simply mental constructions with no objective reality.**, **Consider a scenario where two people perceive an external object in the same way independently. Intuitively, this suggests the object has an objective existence.**, **If external objects exist independently of mental constructs, they are not solely based on perceptions.**
