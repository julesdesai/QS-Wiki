## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have basic beliefs that do not require further justification**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have basic beliefs that support it, and the proposition is true**
