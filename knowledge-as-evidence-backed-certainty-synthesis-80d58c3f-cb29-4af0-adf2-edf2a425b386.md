## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, the evidence must be sufficiently strong to warrant high confidence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have strong evidence leading to near-certainty in the belief**
