## Content

**The reliability of justification is tied to the stability of the reasons supporting a belief**, **An infinite chain of reasons involves an ever-growing list of beliefs that could introduce instabilities**, **Ensuring each additional reason in an infinite chain supports the same level of justification is impractical**
