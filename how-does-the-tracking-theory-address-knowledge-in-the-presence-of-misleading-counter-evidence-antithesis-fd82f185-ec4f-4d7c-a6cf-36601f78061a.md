# How does the tracking theory address knowledge in the presence of misleading counter-evidence?

**Terminal:** No

## Content

**Consider a person who justifiably knows a fact but encounters misleading evidence that counters their belief. Intuitively, we might say they still retain knowledge.**, **It is implausible for knowledge to be wholly negated by misleading counter-evidence when original evidence remains robust.**
