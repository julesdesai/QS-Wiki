# Causal Reliability Epistemology

**Parent:** [Can non-virtuous agents possess knowledge?](can-non-virtuous-agents-possess-knowledge-antithesis-ffedde0e-35b1-4b62-9aec-28d804ddc957.md)

## Content

**For an agent to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For an agent to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For an agent to know a proposition, there must be a reliable causal connection between the belief and the state of affairs described**, **A reliable causal connection is one that consistently leads to true beliefs in similar environments**
