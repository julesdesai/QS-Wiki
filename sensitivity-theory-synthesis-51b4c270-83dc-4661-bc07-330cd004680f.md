## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a belief to count as knowledge, it must be sensitive**, **A belief is sensitive if, in the closest possible world where the proposition is false, the person does not believe it**
