# Can contextual variability in standards lead to epistemic relativism?

## Content

**In a scenario where two groups, A and B, have their distinct contextual standards for accepting knowledge**, **What is known in group A may not be recognized in group B due to these differing standards**, **If contextual variability allows for such disparity, it supports an epistemic relativism threatening universal standards**
