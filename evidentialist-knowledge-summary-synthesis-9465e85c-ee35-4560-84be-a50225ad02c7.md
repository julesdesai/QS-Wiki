## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have sufficient evidence for believing the proposition**, **Sufficient evidence is evidence that logically supports the truth of the proposition**
