## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **A reliable cognitive process consistently yields truth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the belief is true, and it is produced by a reliable cognitive process**
