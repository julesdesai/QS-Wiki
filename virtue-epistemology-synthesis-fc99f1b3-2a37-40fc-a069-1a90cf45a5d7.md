## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is a result of intellectual virtues**, **Intellectual virtues include traits like open-mindedness and intellectual courage**, **A belief resulting from intellectual virtues is likely to be aligned with achieving truth**
