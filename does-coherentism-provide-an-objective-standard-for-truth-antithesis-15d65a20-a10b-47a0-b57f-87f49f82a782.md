# Does Coherentism provide an objective standard for truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 15d65a20-a10b-47a0-b57f-87f49f82a782
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism about Knowledge](coherentism-about-knowledge-synthesis-39987724-1a23-4547-a925-9e75bbe8b421.md)

## Content

**Truth in Coherentism is defined relative to a system rather than an objective standard.**, **In a situation where different individuals hold differing but internally coherent belief systems, truth appears subjective and relative.**, **Without an objective standard of truth beyond coherence, the notion of truth risks becoming relativistic.**
