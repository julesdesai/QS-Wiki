# Does analyzing other concepts in terms of knowledge limit exploration?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f49eb6ce-edaf-4b9f-a4d2-8ca3acc7a89b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Knowledge First Epistemology](knowledge-first-epistemology-synthesis-5ffe7f85-d642-4212-8020-2dc118f603c3.md)

## Content

**Analyzing concepts such as belief and justification primarily through the lens of knowledge might restrict the full exploration of these concepts in their own right.**, **This potentially constrains the development of a varied and comprehensive epistemic framework.**
