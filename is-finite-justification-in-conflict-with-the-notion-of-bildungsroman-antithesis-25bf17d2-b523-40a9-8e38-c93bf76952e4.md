# Is finite justification in conflict with the notion of bildungsroman?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 25bf17d2-b523-40a9-8e38-c93bf76952e4
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism with Finite Justifications](infinitism-with-finite-justifications-synthesis-69ad7e03-7f20-4a4f-85ef-ebc999d85fe8.md)

## Content

**Consider a bildungsroman, which typically depicts the journey of a person attaining knowledge or understanding within finite constraints. It is implausible then for knowledge to require infinite justification, as such a narrative-based framework would not withstand this requirement.**, **A narrative of growth or understanding typically involves finite learning processes, suggesting a harmony with finite justification, yet creating tension with the need for infinite justification.**
