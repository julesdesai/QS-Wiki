## Content

**Knowledge requires a reliable connection to the truth**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief is sensitive to whether the proposition is true or false**, **A belief is sensitive if, were the proposition false, the person would not believe it**, **A person knows a proposition if it is true and they would not believe it if it were false**
