# Is virtue epistemology compatible with partial knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0dced546-b3f9-414a-8768-6f8b9458ed23
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology Summary](virtue-epistemology-summary-synthesis-df7f6b25-43d9-4bf2-9d94-240d68c6e5be.md)

## Content

**Consider a person who has a true belief about a complex topic but only understands a part of it. Intuitively, we might hesitate to say they wholly "know" the topic.**, **Knowing a complex subject requires more than piecemeal true beliefs; it calls into question whether partial knowledge arising from intellectual virtues counts as genuine knowledge.**
