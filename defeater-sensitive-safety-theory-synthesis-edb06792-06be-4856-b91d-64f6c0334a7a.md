# Defeater-sensitive Safety Theory

**Parent:** [Does the safety condition ignore defeated beliefs?](does-the-safety-condition-ignore-defeated-beliefs-antithesis-c3874ffa-126c-4c5a-abe9-9c8078558f91.md)

## Content

**A belief is safe if in most nearby possible worlds where the belief is formed in the same way, it is true**, **A person knows a proposition if they believe it and their belief is safe**, **A person knows a proposition if the proposition is true**, **Safety requires that the method of forming the belief would not easily lead to a false belief**, **Defeaters must be accounted for in assessing the safety of a belief**, **A belief is only safe if it remains true even when potential defeaters are addressed**
