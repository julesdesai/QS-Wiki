# Does indirect realism lead to skepticism about the external world?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 04ecfb5a-30e7-4db8-a9e5-6fe7be4cd85b
**Depth:** 2
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Indirect Realism](indirect-realism-thesis-685db4d3-6670-4245-a0b7-dd17f1d7a4dc.md)

## Content

**If knowledge is mediated by perception, then it becomes hard to verify the external world's true nature.**, **Perception might only represent an internal image, not the external object itself.**, **This uncertainty about the external world can lead to a skeptical view that we cannot truly know it.**
