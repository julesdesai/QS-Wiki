# Reliabilist Approach to Knowledge

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Is it possible to have unsafe knowledge?](is-it-possible-to-have-unsafe-knowledge-antithesis-501091cb-fbee-4ff2-bfae-075aaf9839ec.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief has practical consequences or usefulness**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process, and the belief has practical consequences or usefulness**
