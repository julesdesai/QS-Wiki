# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with Robust Justification

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is stable belief consistency a necessary condition for knowledge?](is-stable-belief-consistency-a-necessary-condition-for-knowledge-antithesis-582132ed-4090-4d0b-9f31-bc13cc31f834.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have robust justification for believing the proposition that resists minor counterevidence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the person believes the proposition, the proposition is true, and the person has robust justification for their belief**
