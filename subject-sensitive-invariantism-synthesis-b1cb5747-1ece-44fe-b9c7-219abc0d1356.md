## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **A person's knowledge is sensitive to practical interests and stakes**, **This practical sensitivity implies that what a person knows depends on the consequences for them of being wrong**
