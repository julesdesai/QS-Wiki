# Can irrational beliefs be considered knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0f2d92b7-84c2-4cd0-a40e-67c7e221463d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Subject-Sensitive Invariantism](subject-sensitive-invariantism-synthesis-ef027452-1300-45f1-9d7c-023fdb6cd119.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a person believes a proposition purely based on irrational reasoning, such as believing they will win a lottery against astronomical odds and the proposition turns out to be true. It is implausible that such a belief constitutes knowledge.**
