# Do hypothetical scenarios destabilize consistency criteria for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f8b607f9-dcb5-4662-98c2-e71ba2635eb7
**Depth:** 4

**Parent:** [Phenomenal Realism with Consistency Conditions](phenomenal-realism-with-consistency-conditions-synthesis-05f9226f-1221-4391-b2ae-790270c7a451.md)

## Content

**Consider hypothetical scenarios where individuals have knowledge despite hypothetical alterations in perception, such as in altered states of consciousness through dreaming. Intuitively, knowledge persists even in hypothetical deviations.**, **It is implausible that hypothetical alterations that undermine consistency have any bearing on actual knowledge states.**
