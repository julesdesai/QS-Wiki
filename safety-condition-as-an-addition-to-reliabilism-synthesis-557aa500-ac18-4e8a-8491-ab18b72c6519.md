## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief is formed by a reliable process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief could not easily have been false in nearby possible worlds**
