# Can perceptions shaped by mental constructs provide objective truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 94a0562c-9184-4d8e-822a-9c8a95efad3a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Idealism](idealism-synthesis-53ec409c-4a9b-4261-be64-9dda9f92eb94.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which two observers view an optical illusion, each perceiving a different image. Intuitively, we would judge that at least one of the perceptions does not correspond to reality.**, **There is a plausible principle that contradictions cannot both be true. This principle together with differing valid perceptions entails that perceptions alone cannot provide objective truth.**
