## Content

**Consider an epistemological approach that does not rely on distinguishing different types of perceptions.**, **Such an approach could still provide a basis for acquiring knowledge without needing to separate perceptions into fundamentally different categories.**, **This suggests that the necessity of distinguishing between veridical and non-veridical perceptions for resolving skepticism might be questioned.**
