## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is an appropriate causal connection between the fact and the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is true and there is an appropriate causal connection between the fact and the belief**
