## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief results from the exercise of intellectual virtue**, **For knowledge to occur, it is necessary that the truth of the belief is not due to epistemic luck**
