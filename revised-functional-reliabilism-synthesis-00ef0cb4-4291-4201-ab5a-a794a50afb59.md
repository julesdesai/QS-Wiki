## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **Knowledge depends on an individual's cognitive faculties being generally reliable in producing true beliefs**, **Cognitive faculties can still contribute to knowledge even if one fails, as long as the overall system generally produces true beliefs**
