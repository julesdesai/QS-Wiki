## Content

**A person knows a proposition if that belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to have reliable cognitive processes producing their belief, it is necessary for those processes to yield a high truth-to-falsehood ratio**, **It is irrelevant whether a specific causal connection exists in the case of each instance of knowledge**, **Beliefs produced by unreliable processes, regardless of causal connection, fail to constitute knowledge**
