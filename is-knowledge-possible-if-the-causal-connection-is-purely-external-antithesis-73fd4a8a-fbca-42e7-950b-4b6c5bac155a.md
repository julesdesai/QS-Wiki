# Is knowledge possible if the causal connection is purely external?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 73fd4a8a-fbca-42e7-950b-4b6c5bac155a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Causal Theory of Knowledge](causal-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-4b0c5095-8474-46c5-ad2b-26df0561fddc.md)

## Content

**Imagine a scenario where a person acquires a true belief through an external process, like being told by a random passerby who happens to be an expert.**, **Even if the belief is true and produced by a reliable source, the person's understanding and the external nature of causation complicate the notion of it being knowledge.**
