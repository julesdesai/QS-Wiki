## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if a person were to believe the proposition, the belief would not be easily false**, **This safety condition ensures that the belief is formed in a stable environment**
