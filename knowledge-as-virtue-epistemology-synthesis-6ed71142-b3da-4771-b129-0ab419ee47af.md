## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is the result of cognitive virtues or intellectual character**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief arises from a reliable cognitive process while excluding epistemic luck**
