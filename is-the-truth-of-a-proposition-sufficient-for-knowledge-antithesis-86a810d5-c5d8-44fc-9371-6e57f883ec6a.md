# Is the truth of a proposition sufficient for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 86a810d5-c5d8-44fc-9371-6e57f883ec6a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Embodied Cognition with Reliance on Bodily Skills](embodied-cognition-with-reliance-on-bodily-skills-synthesis-e9d28d24-22c1-4ccc-a9a1-a716398534d0.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person believes a false proposition due to a misperception but later, by chance, the proposition becomes true. Intuitively, we would not say the person originally had knowledge. The truth of a proposition becoming true does not retroactively grant knowledge.**
