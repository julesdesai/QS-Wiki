# Can a belief be safe in most nearby worlds but unsafe in the actual world?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5d22835f-b208-451f-a5da-80a907f9b495
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Safety Condition Theory](safety-condition-theory-synthesis-b5a72759-42a3-4d9b-b74c-df40c84bdc9d.md)

## Content

**A scenario can be imagined where a belief is true in most nearby possible worlds but false in the actual world. Intuitively, we judge that knowledge requires truth in the actual world, not just in nearby possible worlds.**, **The safety condition theory would permit knowledge in such a scenario, which conflicts with the principle that knowledge requires truth.**
