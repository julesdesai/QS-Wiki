# How can hallucinations challenge the requirement for direct perception?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7b6d5244-3a17-42ea-b202-03a6b727189d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextual Direct Realism](contextual-direct-realism-synthesis-9053ea14-9a99-4649-aa95-1bc3b223c980.md)

## Content

**During a hallucination, a person perceives propositions that do not correspond to reality.**, **In cases of hallucination, one might claim to perceive directly, yet does not acquire knowledge.**, **It is implausible that direct perception occurs when the external reality and perceived proposition diverge entirely.**
