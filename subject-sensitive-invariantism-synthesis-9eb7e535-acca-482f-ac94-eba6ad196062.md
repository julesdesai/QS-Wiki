## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person believes the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person's practical interests are relevant to whether the person has knowledge**, **The standards for knowledge are not context-dependent, but affected by the person's stakes or interests**
