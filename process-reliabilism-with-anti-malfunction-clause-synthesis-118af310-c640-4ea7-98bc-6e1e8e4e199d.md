## Content

**A person knows a proposition if the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process that is free from malfunction**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **Reliable cognitive processes must operate normally and must not produce beliefs due to anomalous errors**
