# Sensitivity Theory of Knowledge

**Parent:** [Do causal chains necessitate awareness of their connection to truth?](do-causal-chains-necessitate-awareness-of-their-connection-to-truth-antithesis-bd708974-9770-4f7b-a10a-f95df3a95b36.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief would not be held if the proposition were false**, **A belief is sensitive if in the nearest possible world where the proposition is false, the person does not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is true and sensitive**
