# Does truth-tracking handle Gettier-style cases?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 95961aaa-27b3-4987-99e6-afa3911d8403
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Nozickian Tracking Theory Summary](nozickian-tracking-theory-summary-synthesis-44760ad8-dc58-4f04-9662-a1508a0ab6d4.md)

## Content

**Consider a situation where a person's belief tracks truth and the proposition is true, yet the belief seems to be true by sheer coincidence, akin to a Gettier case. In such cases, intuitively we would judge that the person does not have knowledge.**, **There is a plausible principle that coincidence undermines the claim to knowledge even if the truth conditions are met. Therefore, tracking truth may not be sufficient for ensuring knowledge.**
