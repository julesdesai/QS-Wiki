## Content

**Knowledge is a belief grounded in intellectual virtue which reliably avoids error**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for their belief to have a high probability of being true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief arises from intellectual virtue that reliably avoids error**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe it, the belief has a high probability of being true, and their intellectual virtue reliably avoids error**
