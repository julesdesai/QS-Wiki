# Can a belief have infinite justification while still being coherent?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ed9f0f0b-6bba-4e37-b859-9c5f4df95c32
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherent Infinitism Summary](coherent-infinitism-summary-synthesis-eca6bdeb-868b-4e1e-9a63-23b9880f21ff.md)

## Content

**Justification through an infinite network of interrelated reasons might be too complex for the human mind to comprehend or handle.**, **Beliefs typically require a certain level of simplicity or manageability to be accessible or understood by human cognitive faculties.**, **Cognitive limitations imply that an actual infinite network of reasons provides no practical guidance for belief justification.**
