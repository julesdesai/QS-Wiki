## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is produced by a reliable process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief aligns with the contextually appropriate standards of rationality**
