## Content

**Knowledge is true belief arising from reliably functioning intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief arises from reliably functioning intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe it, the proposition is true, and their intellectual virtue functions reliably to produce the belief**
