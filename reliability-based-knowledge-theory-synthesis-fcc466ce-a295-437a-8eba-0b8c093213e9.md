## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a reliable method**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the method leads to true beliefs more often than not**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that these reliability-based conditions hold**
