## Content

**A belief is sensitive if the belief would not be held if the proposition were false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **A person's belief is safe if in nearby possible worlds where their belief is formed in the same way, it is sensitive and remains true**
