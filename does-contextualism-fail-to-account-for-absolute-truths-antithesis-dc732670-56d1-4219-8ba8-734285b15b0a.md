# Does contextualism fail to account for absolute truths?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** dc732670-56d1-4219-8ba8-734285b15b0a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism about Knowledge](contextualism-about-knowledge-synthesis-3f771a9e-26d6-43c4-b604-a31f8bd9947c.md)

## Content

**A proposition that is universally accepted as true across all contexts should remain true regardless of contextual variations.**, **If contextualism were correct, then such universally true propositions would vary in truth based on context.**, **It is implausible that universally accepted truths could vary in truth value simply due to contextual changes.**
