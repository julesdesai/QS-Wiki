# What about beliefs formed from incomplete evidence?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 93d729f7-6788-4749-8b3d-c3f0a39bca3f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Nozick's Sensitivity Theory](nozicks-sensitivity-theory-synthesis-6e1ddae7-9a08-404e-ac91-e01a19448f49.md)

## Content

**A person might form a belief based on incomplete evidence that happens to be true**, **According to the theory, sensitivity implies a connection between belief and the truth of the proposition**, **Incomplete evidence doesn’t always align with sensitivity, challenging the connection implied by the theory**
