## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is overlapping justification for the belief, but not necessarily multiple forms**, **Pragmatic consequences may contribute to the justification but are not mandatory**
