# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with Gettier Considerations

**Parent:** [Does knowledge require more than intellectual virtues for justification?](does-knowledge-require-more-than-intellectual-virtues-for-justification-antithesis-5b65bdb7-3201-4245-a581-cbb55e0fc540.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, and the proposition is true, provided there are no Gettier-type cases**
