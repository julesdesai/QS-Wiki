## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **The belief must be safe, meaning in nearly all nearby possible worlds where the belief is formed, the belief is true**
