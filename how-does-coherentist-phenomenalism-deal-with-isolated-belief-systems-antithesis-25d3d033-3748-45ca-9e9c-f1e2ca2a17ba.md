# How does coherentist phenomenalism deal with isolated belief systems?

**Parent:** [Coherentist Phenomenalism](coherentist-phenomenalism-synthesis-7f881170-8b02-4aad-92a8-2bed5a4b8a3c.md)

## Content

**Within coherentism, a completely isolated belief system could theoretically be internally consistent and coherent.**, **A belief system that is entirely disconnected from reality could still be coherent if no external facts challenge it.**, **It is implausible to accept knowledge claims from isolated belief systems with no checks against reality.**
