## Content

**For a belief to count as knowledge, the process generating the belief must be reliable**, **A reliable process is one that consistently produces true beliefs**, **The reliability of the process requires no coincidentally correct evidence for the belief**, **Knowledge is achieved when a belief is formed through a reliable method**
