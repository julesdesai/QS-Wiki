## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the justification involves an infinite, non-repeating chain of reasons**, **Justification involves an endless series of reasons where each reason supports the next**
