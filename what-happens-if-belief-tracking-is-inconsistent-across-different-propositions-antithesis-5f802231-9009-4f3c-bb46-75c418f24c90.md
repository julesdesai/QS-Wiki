# What happens if belief tracking is inconsistent across different propositions?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5f802231-9009-4f3c-bb46-75c418f24c90
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Truth-Tracking Theory](truth-tracking-theory-synthesis-1814de97-222c-437f-bd26-a9ce4edff4f7.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person's belief about one proposition accurately tracks truth across situations, but the same person's beliefs about other related propositions do not track truth consistently. Intuitively, this inconsistency may lead us to question their overall knowledge status.**, **It is implausible that knowledge can be considered stable if belief tracking is inconsistent across propositions that are interdependent.**
