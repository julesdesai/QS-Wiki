# Does Indirect Realism fail to address the external world's existence?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d929d0c1-d9f7-4308-965c-418e7e76987b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Indirect Realism Summary](indirect-realism-summary-synthesis-1b3cd8c8-92a3-4a52-be84-8ec951a45683.md)

## Content

**Indirect realism posits indirect perception through mental representations.**, **It is plausible that directly perceiving something provides stronger evidence for its existence than indirect perception.**, **If perception is always indirect, then one might doubt whether the external world exists at all.**
