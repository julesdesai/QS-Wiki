## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is supported by strong evidence**, **The evidence for belief must depend on factors external to the internal mental states of the individual**
