# Knowledge as No False Lemmas

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 2257d534-e678-41ae-9d28-04b6b1c895e3
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Are causally determined beliefs always knowledge?](are-causally-determined-beliefs-always-knowledge-antithesis-02061f88-fcba-47f9-a288-502ea16993ef.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is not inferred from any falsehoods**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is not inferred from any falsehoods**
