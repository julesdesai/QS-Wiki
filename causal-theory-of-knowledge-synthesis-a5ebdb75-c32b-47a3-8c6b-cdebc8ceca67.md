## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have a justified belief that is causally connected to the truth of the proposition**, **The causal connection between justification and truth must form an appropriate link for a belief to qualify as knowledge**
