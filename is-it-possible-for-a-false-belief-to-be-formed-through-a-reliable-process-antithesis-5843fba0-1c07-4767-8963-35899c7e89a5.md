# Is it possible for a false belief to be formed through a reliable process?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5843fba0-1c07-4767-8963-35899c7e89a5
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliable Process Theory of Knowledge](reliable-process-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-8a084bde-8e94-4b49-86d3-6339aef5c9e6.md)

## Content

**A process could be reliable in many relevant situations, but there might be specific instances where it produces false beliefs**, **There could be cases where the proportion of true beliefs to false ones is high, yet some specific false beliefs are still formed**, **The occurrence of even a single false belief challenges the notion that a reliable process necessarily leads to knowledge**
