# Can perception occur without direct objects to be aware of?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 6c71b60a-f4f1-49ac-95da-710ed6853849
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Naive Realism with Direct Cross-Modal Sensing](naive-realism-with-direct-cross-modal-sensing-synthesis-96f2c374-253a-4f49-a9c2-1ee8405679ee.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person hallucinates an object that is not present. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that the person is having a perceptual experience without direct awareness of an actual object.**, **It is implausible that an awareness of objects can be direct in the absence of actual objects to perceive.**
