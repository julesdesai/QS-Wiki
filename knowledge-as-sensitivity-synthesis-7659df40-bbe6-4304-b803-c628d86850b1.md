## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A belief qualifies as knowledge if it is sensitive, meaning if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **Sensitivity allows for consideration of environments altering belief reliability**
