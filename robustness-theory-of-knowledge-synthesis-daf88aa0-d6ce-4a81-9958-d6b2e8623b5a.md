## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person's belief is robust across various likely scenarios**, **The robustness condition ensures that across diverse likely situations, the belief remains true**
