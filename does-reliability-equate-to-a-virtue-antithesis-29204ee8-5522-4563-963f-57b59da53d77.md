# Does reliability equate to a virtue?

## Content

**An intellectually virtuous person with ail reliable cognitive abilities might still, occasionally form false beliefs through these faculties, as in cases of optical illusions.**, **This suggests reliability alone cannot be equated with virtue, since errors still occur, questioning the necessity of virtues in knowledge.**
