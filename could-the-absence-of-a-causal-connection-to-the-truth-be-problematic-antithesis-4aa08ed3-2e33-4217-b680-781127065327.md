# Could the absence of a causal connection to the truth be problematic?

## Content

**Reliabilism posits that knowledge does not require a causal connection to the truth.**, **In scenarios where there is no causal connection, it is counterintuitive to categorize the belief as true knowledge, as understanding typically involves tracking the truth.**
