# Modified Tracking Theory with Consistency Requirement

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [What about cases where contradictory beliefs track the truth?](what-about-cases-where-contradictory-beliefs-track-the-truth-antithesis-c6e66ba2-0402-439b-9a68-227ce98d4a0e.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were true, the person would believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief tracks the truth in the relevant manner, and there are no contradictory beliefs held**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that any tracked belief does not contradict another belief simultaneously held by the person**
