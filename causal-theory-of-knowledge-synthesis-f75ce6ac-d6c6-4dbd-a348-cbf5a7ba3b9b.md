## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have a belief in the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is a causal connection between the fact and the belief**, **A person knows a proposition if the proposition is true, and their belief is caused by the fact in an appropriate way**
