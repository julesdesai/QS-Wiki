## Content

**There is a plausible principle that some self-knowledge, gained through introspection, is immediate and not mediated by any perceptual or relational structures**, **Consider a person who instantly realizes they are anxious based purely on internal reflection. Intuitively, we would judge that they have immediate knowledge of their emotional state without reference to external perceptual structures**
