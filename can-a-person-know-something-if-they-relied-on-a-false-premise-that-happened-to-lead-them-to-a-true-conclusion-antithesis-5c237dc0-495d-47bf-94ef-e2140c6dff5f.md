# Can a person know something if they relied on a false premise that happened to lead them to a true conclusion?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5c237dc0-495d-47bf-94ef-e2140c6dff5f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [No-False-Leads Theory](no-false-leads-theory-synthesis-34d410a8-8e56-47be-a1d5-0817ba3be140.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person believes a true proposition because they deduced it based on a false premise.**, **Intuitively, the person does not know the true proposition even though it is true and they believe it.**
