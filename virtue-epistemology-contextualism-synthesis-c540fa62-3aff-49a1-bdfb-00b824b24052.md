## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is safe, meaning it would not be easily false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief results from the exercise of an intellectual virtue or competence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, their belief is safe, and their belief results from the exercise of an intellectual virtue or competence**
