# How can external justification reconcile false yet useful beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7eae669c-73ae-4747-888c-7235531fc423
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Instrumentalism with Externalist Justification](instrumentalism-with-externalist-justification-synthesis-69903d30-4d37-4781-884e-3e9ef74484e3.md)

## Content

**External justification implies a form of validation or evidence for a belief's truth**, **Some beliefs are practically useful but false, lacking the external justification necessary for their truth, like folkloric remedies that work due to placebo effects**, **A belief must be true and have external justification to align with the concept of knowledge**
