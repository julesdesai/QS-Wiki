# Can indirect realism accommodate perceptual knowledge in animals?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** cdf261d2-57fc-44df-bee5-cc3eee4f7aa2
**Depth:** 2
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Indirect Realism](indirect-realism-thesis-685db4d3-6670-4245-a0b7-dd17f1d7a4dc.md)

## Content

**Animals also acquire knowledge through perception as seen with a bat using echolocation.**, **If perception mediates knowledge, then even animal knowledge is indirectly mediated.**, **The ability of animals to navigate and survive suggests a more straightforward form of perception than indirect realism provides.**
