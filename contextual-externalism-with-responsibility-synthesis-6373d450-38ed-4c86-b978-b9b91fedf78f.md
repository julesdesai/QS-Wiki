# Contextual Externalism with Responsibility

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 6373d450-38ed-4c86-b978-b9b91fedf78f
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is epistemic responsibility required for knowledge?](is-epistemic-responsibility-required-for-knowledge-antithesis-01f86b41-458a-4c05-8e38-c0ddb425e9e6.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief corresponds with the external context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed responsibly**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief corresponds with the external context, and the belief is formed responsibly**
