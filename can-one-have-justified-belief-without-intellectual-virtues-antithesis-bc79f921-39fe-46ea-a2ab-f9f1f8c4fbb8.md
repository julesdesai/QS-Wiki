# Can one have justified belief without intellectual virtues?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** bc79f921-39fe-46ea-a2ab-f9f1f8c4fbb8
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue epistemology focusing on intellectual virtues](virtue-epistemology-focusing-on-intellectual-virtues-synthesis-d559d8c1-587e-4ccf-9e44-8233679e40bf.md)

## Content

**Envision a scenario where a person is indoctrinated into believing a true proposition, justifying it through reliable processes they are unaware of. Intuitively, they have justified belief.**, **This suggests that justification can exist without exercising intellectual virtues, challenging the necessity of virtues for justification.**
