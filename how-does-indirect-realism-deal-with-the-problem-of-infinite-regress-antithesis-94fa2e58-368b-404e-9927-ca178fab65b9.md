# How does Indirect Realism deal with the problem of infinite regress?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 94fa2e58-368b-404e-9927-ca178fab65b9
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Indirect Realism Summary](indirect-realism-summary-synthesis-1b3cd8c8-92a3-4a52-be84-8ec951a45683.md)

## Content

**Suppose knowledge depends on mental representations that correspond to reality.**, **If each mental representation requires another to verify its correspondence, an infinite regress of representations could occur.**, **An infinite regress would make it impossible to ever ground the knowledge claim in any ultimate reality.**
