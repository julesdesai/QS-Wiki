# Is conscious awareness of belief necessary for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 313feeba-e618-49b2-bf7f-e3fb55a6874b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Truth-Tracking Theory](truth-tracking-theory-synthesis-1814de97-222c-437f-bd26-a9ce4edff4f7.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person holds a true belief subconsciously, and the belief tracks truth across possible situations, but the person is unaware of holding this belief. Intuitively, we may find it challenging to attribute knowledge to this person due to their lack of conscious awareness.**, **A plausible principle suggests that conscious awareness of one's belief is a necessary condition for it to qualify as knowledge.**
