# What implications does this have for introspective knowledge?

## Content

**Consider a case where someone knows they are in pain. Even if it were not true that they were in pain, their belief might persist due to their intense sensation. Intuitively, knowing one is in pain seems valid.**, **This type of self-knowledge does not appear sensitive to unreal scenarios, yet it is widely recognized as genuine knowledge.**
