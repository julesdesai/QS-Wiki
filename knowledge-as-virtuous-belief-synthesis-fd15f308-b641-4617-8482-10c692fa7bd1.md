## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is the result of intellectual virtues**, **Intellectual virtues are traits like open-mindedness and intellectual diligence that lead to true beliefs**
