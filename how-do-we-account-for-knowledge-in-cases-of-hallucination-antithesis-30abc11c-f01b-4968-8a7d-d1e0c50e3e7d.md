# How do we account for knowledge in cases of hallucination?

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person hallucinates a situation that coincidentally aligns with reality. In such a scenario, intuitively we would question whether the person truly has knowledge, given that their mental representation was born out of a false perception.**
