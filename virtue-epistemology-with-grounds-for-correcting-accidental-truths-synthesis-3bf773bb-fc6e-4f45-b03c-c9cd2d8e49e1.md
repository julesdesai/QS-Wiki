# Virtue epistemology with grounds for correcting accidental truths

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief results from the exercise of intellectual virtue**, **Believers must have a mechanism to correct accidental truths for their belief to count as knowledge**
