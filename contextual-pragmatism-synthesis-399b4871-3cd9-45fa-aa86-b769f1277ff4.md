## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is practically successful or useful and has contextual justification**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is practically successful, and justified within a relevant context**
