# Knowledge as No False Lemmas

**Parent:** [Can sensitivity distinguish between coincidence and intent?](can-sensitivity-distinguish-between-coincidence-and-intent-antithesis-e30de9ed-82fe-4f2a-9926-4e7628102fc3.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, the proposition is true, and that justification does not rely on any false lemmas**
