# Objective Knowledge without Belief

## Content

**Knowledge is an objective truth that arises from intellectual virtue which reliably avoids error**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their intellectual virtue reliably avoids error**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is true, arises from intellectual virtue, and avoids error**
