**Parent:** [Does knowledge require more than tracking truth in some cases?](does-knowledge-require-more-than-tracking-truth-in-some-cases-antithesis-485f8cfc-01c8-42fd-87e8-002b2e7edc94.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief in the proposition could not easily have been false in the actual world**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief could not easily have been false**
