## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is the result of an intellectual virtue or ability**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is not the result of a lucky coincidence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief results from an intellectual virtue or ability, and the belief is not the result of a lucky coincidence**
