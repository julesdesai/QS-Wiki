## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a group of people to have common knowledge of a proposition, it is necessary that they each understand that each other hold the belief**, **For a proposition to be common knowledge, it is necessary that the proposition be true for all in the group**, **For a group of people to have common knowledge of a proposition, it is sufficient that they each believe the proposition, understand that each other hold the belief, and the proposition is true for all in the group**
