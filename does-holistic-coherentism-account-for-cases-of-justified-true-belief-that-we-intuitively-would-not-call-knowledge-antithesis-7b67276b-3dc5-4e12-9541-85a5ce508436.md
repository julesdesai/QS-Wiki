# Does holistic coherentism account for cases of justified true belief that we intuitively would not call knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7b67276b-3dc5-4e12-9541-85a5ce508436
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Holistic Coherentism](holistic-coherentism-synthesis-8571e558-3564-47a5-a21d-6c98ccb0e95f.md)

## Content

**A person can have a justified true belief through sheer guesswork or luck.**, **Intuitively, a belief formed through guesswork or luck does not amount to knowledge.**, **Holistic coherentism allows for the possibility of justified true belief without accounting for the source of justification.**
