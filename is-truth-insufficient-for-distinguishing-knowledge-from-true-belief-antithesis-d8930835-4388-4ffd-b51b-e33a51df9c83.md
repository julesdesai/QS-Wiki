# Is truth insufficient for distinguishing knowledge from true belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d8930835-4388-4ffd-b51b-e33a51df9c83
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilist Theory of Knowledge](reliabilist-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-f1c9c7cf-a360-4007-8370-716223f3575c.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which someone believes a lottery ticket will lose because most tickets do. The ticket indeed loses. Intuitively, the person does not know the ticket will lose; their belief is merely a statistically informed guess.**, **A belief being true because of luck or probability does not necessarily transform this belief into knowledge.**
