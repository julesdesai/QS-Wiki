# Does indirect realism adequately address the directness of certain perceptual experiences?

**Node ID:** e82ed2eb-fe93-4936-9a6e-714de09419b3

**Parent:** [Indirect Realism](indirect-realism-synthesis-0725136f-b8bb-483b-867f-86c96e5faac6.md)

## Content

**Some perceptual experiences, such as feeling pain or seeing a bright light, seem immediate and lacking in intermediary representation.**, **The immediacy of these experiences conflicts with the idea that all knowledge requires indirect perception through mental representations.**
