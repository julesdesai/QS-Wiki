**Parent:** [Can contextual sufficiency undermine the notion of universal knowledge?](can-contextual-sufficiency-undermine-the-notion-of-universal-knowledge-antithesis-2b570342-477e-49fd-a54b-e2777661709b.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is based on sufficient evidential grounds**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is based on sufficient evidential grounds**
