# Is coherence sufficient for justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 04871853-9c7b-4e8a-89af-dfebb96ab49c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualist Coherentism](contextualist-coherentism-synthesis-598dc05f-03d6-4aeb-b0db-fa7173390f96.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person believes they have extraordinary powers, and all their other beliefs are structured to support this belief, creating a coherent system. Intuitively, a belief system based solely on false premises and extraordinary claims does not seem justified.**, **The coherence of a belief with a systematically flawed set of beliefs appears insufficient for justification.**
