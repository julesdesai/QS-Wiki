# Knowledge as Coherentist Justification

**Parent:** [Is knowledge always reliant on proper cognitive function?](is-knowledge-always-reliant-on-proper-cognitive-function-antithesis-37df44dc-24f1-454a-ad20-558966d00fb2.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief coheres with the set of their other justified beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief coheres with the set of their other justified beliefs**
