# Does infinite justification violate human cognitive limits?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 62565051-e37d-4448-8a5d-c062d0ea3849
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherent Infinitism](coherent-infinitism-synthesis-f2c99927-f862-4ee8-8b5b-2fefd2717ee2.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that humans can cognitively process an infinite amount of information or reasons.**, **A plausible principle states that cognitive processes have natural limits. This principle together with the requirement for infinite reasons entails an incompatibility with actual human justification practices.**
