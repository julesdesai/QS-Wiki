## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have a true belief that is justified by an external factor**, **The foundational beliefs are required to be veridical and linked to reality**
