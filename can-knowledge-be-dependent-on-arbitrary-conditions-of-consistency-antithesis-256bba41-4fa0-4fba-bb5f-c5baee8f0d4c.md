# Can knowledge be dependent on arbitrary conditions of consistency?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 256bba41-4fa0-4fba-bb5f-c5baee8f0d4c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Phenomenal Realism with Consistency Conditions](phenomenal-realism-with-consistency-conditions-synthesis-05f9226f-1221-4391-b2ae-790270c7a451.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person has knowledge of a phenomenon when observed in controlled settings, but loses it in chaotic environments. Intuitively, such dependence on an arbitrary condition of consistency seems incorrect.**, **There is a plausible principle that knowledge should not hinge on specific, potentially arbitrary conditions of consistency. This principle together with the ability to gain insights in non-ideal settings entails that such conditions are not requisite for knowledge.**
