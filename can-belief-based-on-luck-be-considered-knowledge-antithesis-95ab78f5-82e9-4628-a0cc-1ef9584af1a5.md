# Can belief based on luck be considered knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 95ab78f5-82e9-4628-a0cc-1ef9584af1a5
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-71b6710d-dd35-475a-8146-f2431fcd7269.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person has a true belief because of a lucky guess. In such a scenario, intuitively we would not judge that the person knows the proposition.**, **There is a plausible principle that knowledge cannot be based on mere luck. This principle together with the claim that true belief resulting from luck is not knowledge entails that true belief alone is insufficient for knowledge.**
