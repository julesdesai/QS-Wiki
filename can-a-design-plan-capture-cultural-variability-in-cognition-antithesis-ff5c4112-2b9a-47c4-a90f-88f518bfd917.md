# Can a design plan capture cultural variability in cognition?

## Content

**Suppose culturally distinct groups have different cognitive strategies or heuristics, yet each claims justification for their beliefs. This scenario suggests a limitation in a universal design plan capturing these differences.**, **The assumption that a single design plan can justify beliefs across diverse cultural contexts is implausible given the variability in cognitive approaches.**
