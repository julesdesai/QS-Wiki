# Could the necessity of interpretation and correction imply that initial perceptions are unreliable?

## Content

**In a case where a mirage leads to the perception of water in a desert, intuitive knowledge must be corrected upon closer inspection.**, **Interpretations and corrections seem necessary due to the inherent unreliability of initial perceptions.**, **The requirement for these corrections implies that initial perceptions consistently misrepresent reality.**
