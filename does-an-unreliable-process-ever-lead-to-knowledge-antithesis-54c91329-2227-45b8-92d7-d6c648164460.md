# Does an unreliable process ever lead to knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 54c91329-2227-45b8-92d7-d6c648164460
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism](reliabilism-synthesis-41e7a61f-ad45-42cd-8dd7-ba40ca519ba9.md)

## Content

**An unreliable process has low frequency in producing true beliefs.**, **Consider a situation where an unreliable process results in a true belief by coincidence.**, **If an unreliable process can produce a true belief that counts as knowledge, then reliability is not necessary for knowledge.**
