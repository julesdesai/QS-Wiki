# How do self-fulfilling prophecies challenge causal connections in belief formation?

**Terminal:** No

## Content

**Imagine a self-fulfilling prophecy where belief in an event causes the event to occur.**, **The causal process here is circular, where belief causes the fact rather than being caused by it.**, **Circular causation challenges the unidirectional requirement of causal connection in knowledge formation.**
