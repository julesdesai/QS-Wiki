# Is the requirement for a causal connection applicable to testimonial knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 337b5901-36eb-476d-8b11-5dad9e624685
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Goldman's Causal Theory](goldmans-causal-theory-synthesis-55eaa724-2453-46d2-8e70-b1e77bdcb753.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person receives true information through trusted testimony without personally verifying it.**, **Testimonial beliefs can be justified without a direct causal connection to the original fact, relying on trustworthiness of the source.**, **A causal connection between belief and original fact may not account for justified testimonial knowledge.**
