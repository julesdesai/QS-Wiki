# Can incorrect belief negation non-requiring tracking really suffice for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 503b1f50-c817-4e1f-8700-d9ca95a59b97
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Knowledge Beyond Constant Tracking](knowledge-beyond-constant-tracking-synthesis-61b32f83-a9fc-402a-ba38-ae04df06d61f.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a proposition that is false, and a person does not believe it. Intuitively, their lack of belief provides no affirmative evidence of knowledge in the context of the proposition's falseness, suggesting belief negation alone without evidence support is insufficient for knowledge.**
