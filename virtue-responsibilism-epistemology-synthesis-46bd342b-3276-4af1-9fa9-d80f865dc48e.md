## Content

**Knowledge is true belief arising from a combination of intellectual virtue and cognitive responsibility**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief arises from a balance of intellectual virtue and cognitive responsibility**, **A person knows a proposition if they believe it, the proposition is true, and their belief arises from a balance of intellectual virtue and cognitive responsibility**
