# Is coherence between perceptions necessary for reliability?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 397b71a1-4ad1-4f73-99a5-aabdf6138502
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Instrumental Coherence Theory](instrumental-coherence-theory-synthesis-188d4652-e220-47fa-9e2f-da98cf583190.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that perceptions must always cohere for any belief to be reliable, as direct perception alone can sometimes suffice for reliability, such as a clear visual observation on a sunny day.**
