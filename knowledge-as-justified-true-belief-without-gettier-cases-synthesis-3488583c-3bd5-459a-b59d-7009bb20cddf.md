# Knowledge as Justified True Belief (without Gettier cases)

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 3488583c-3bd5-459a-b59d-7009bb20cddf
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can intellectual virtues guarantee knowledge in the presence of deceit?](can-intellectual-virtues-guarantee-knowledge-in-the-presence-of-deceit-antithesis-2ad598a3-1e16-42dd-b8a1-4c3fc412a50a.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no Gettier-type conditions present**
