## Content

**Knowledge is a true belief formed through the reliable exercise of the agent's cognitive abilities**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed by the agent using reliable cognitive abilities**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe it, the proposition is true, and their cognitive abilities reliably lead to this belief**
