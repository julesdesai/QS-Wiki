# Can a true belief accidentally formed still count as knowledge?

**Node ID:** 46715c6a-8ebb-4e7c-9b2c-3256df332fcc

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalism with Reliabilism](externalism-with-reliabilism-synthesis-979d7449-6923-4b84-9ee7-21896810f66d.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person forms a belief through a reliable process, but it is true merely by chance. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that the person does not have knowledge**, **Knowledge requires that the truth of the belief is not accidental, but rather connected to the justification**
