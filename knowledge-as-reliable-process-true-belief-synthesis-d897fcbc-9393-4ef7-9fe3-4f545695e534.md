# Knowledge as Reliable Process True Belief

**Parent:** [Can a belief be knowledge if justified by reliable processes but not by awareness of each premise?](can-a-belief-be-knowledge-if-justified-by-reliable-processes-but-not-by-awareness-of-each-premise-antithesis-a5372f32-0ae9-44f8-8139-258fade10bab.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief arise from a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief arises from a reliable cognitive process**
