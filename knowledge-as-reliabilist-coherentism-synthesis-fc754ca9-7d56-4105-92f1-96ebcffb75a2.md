**Parent:** [Can contextually justified beliefs avoid circularity?](can-contextually-justified-beliefs-avoid-circularity-antithesis-db937b4f-40e9-468a-b6dc-437b90359413.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres with a system of beliefs and is formed through a reliable process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief coheres with the system and is formed by a reliable process**
