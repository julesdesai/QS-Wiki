# Do technological aids alter standards of knowing?

## Content

**Suppose a student uses advanced technology to immediately check historical facts for their thesis. Their resulting belief about specific events is true and justified through technological aid. Intuitively, reliance on technology could affect our perception of the depth of their knowledge.**, **Technological reliance could imply variation in justification standards, affecting knowledge claims across different contexts.**
