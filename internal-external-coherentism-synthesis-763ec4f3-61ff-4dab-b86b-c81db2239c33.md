## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres with a system of beliefs and aligns with external reality**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief both coheres with a belief system and aligns with external reality**
