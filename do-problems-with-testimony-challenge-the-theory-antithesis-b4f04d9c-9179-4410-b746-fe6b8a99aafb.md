# Do problems with testimony challenge the theory?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b4f04d9c-9179-4410-b746-fe6b8a99aafb
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Nozick's Truth-Tracking Theory](nozicks-truth-tracking-theory-synthesis-3749df9e-e568-414c-88ec-cc6b23580530.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person gains a true belief through reliable testimony. Intuitively, we would judge that the testimony provides knowledge despite the recipient not directly tracking the truth.**, **There is a plausible principle that testimony can be a source of knowledge even when the truth-tracking condition isn't directly applicable. This principle could undermine the necessity of truth-tracking for knowledge.**
