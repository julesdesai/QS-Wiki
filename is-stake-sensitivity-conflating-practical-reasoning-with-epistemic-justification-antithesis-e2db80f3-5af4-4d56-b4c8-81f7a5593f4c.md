# Is stake-sensitivity conflating practical reasoning with epistemic justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e2db80f3-5af4-4d56-b4c8-81f7a5593f4c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Subject-Sensitive Invariantism](subject-sensitive-invariantism-synthesis-a38d2af6-3ec4-421c-a558-c594d0cbc8b7.md)

## Content

**Suppose practical decision-making based on stakes differs from epistemic justification based on evidence. Intuitively, it is plausible to distinguish between practical interests and justified true belief as criteria for knowledge.**, **There is a plausible principle that factors relevant to action and decision are distinct from those relevant to determining knowledge. This suggests that knowledge should be independent of practical stake considerations.**
