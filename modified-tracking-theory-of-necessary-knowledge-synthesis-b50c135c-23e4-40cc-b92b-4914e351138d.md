## Content

**A person knows a necessary proposition if they have a true belief of the proposition**, **A person knows a necessary proposition if it remains true in all logically possible worlds**, **A person knows a necessary proposition if the belief would still be held if it were true in slightly different situations**, **The truth of the belief must be due to the internal logical structure of the proposition rather than external contingencies**
