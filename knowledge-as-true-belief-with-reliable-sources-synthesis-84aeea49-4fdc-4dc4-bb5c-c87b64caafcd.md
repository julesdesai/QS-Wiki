# Knowledge as True Belief with Reliable Sources

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is formed through reliable processes**, **A belief is considered to be formed through reliable processes if it is produced by cognitive faculties functioning properly in a suitable environment**
