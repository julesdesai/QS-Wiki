## Content

**Knowledge is a true belief that could not have easily been false**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief, formed in nearby possible worlds, remains true**, **The safety condition requires that in similar situations, with the same process, the belief would not lead to false conclusions**, **This ensures that, even in slightly altered conditions, the belief continues to hold true**
