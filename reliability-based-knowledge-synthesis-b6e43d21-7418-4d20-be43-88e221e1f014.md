**Parent:** [Does belief serve as a check against misinformation in knowledge claims?](does-belief-serve-as-a-check-against-misinformation-in-knowledge-claims-antithesis-da532d60-b375-42b7-885f-2546f7876af7.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief in the proposition has been produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **A person knows a proposition if it is true, they believe it, and their belief is the result of a reliable cognitive process**
