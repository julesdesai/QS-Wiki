# Is reliance on external justification always reliable for knowledge acquisition?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f7b842ea-e9ed-45ab-8f56-19fae313282e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Knowledge with External Justification](knowledge-with-external-justification-synthesis-dcebe696-500e-46cf-a887-f5b9e8e9939c.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that external systems of justification are infallible or always accurately confirm the absence of defeaters.**, **Consider a scenario in which a trusted external source erroneously confirms the absence of defeaters for a true belief. Intuitively, we would hesitate to attribute knowledge to the person despite the external confirmation.**
