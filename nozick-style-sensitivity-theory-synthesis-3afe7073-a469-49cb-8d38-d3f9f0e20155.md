**Parent:** [Can a person still be considered to have knowledge even if their belief does not track truth in certain scenarios?](can-a-person-still-be-considered-to-have-knowledge-even-if-their-belief-does-not-track-truth-in-certain-scenarios-antithesis-4784699e-2a9b-4c42-9251-e59346ae6a20.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were not true, the person would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is not necessary that their belief tracks truth across all possible scenarios**
