# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with Epistemic Virtues

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 8cb3750e-5ab3-4363-84ad-ae57cf4cf7f9
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does implicit justification suffice for knowledge?](does-implicit-justification-suffice-for-knowledge-antithesis-79a718a6-34aa-4748-8074-93b659dda91d.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is a result of exercising epistemic virtues**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief results from exercising epistemic virtues**
