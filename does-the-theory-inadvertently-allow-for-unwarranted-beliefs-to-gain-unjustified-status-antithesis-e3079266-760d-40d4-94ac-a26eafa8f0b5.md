# Does the theory inadvertently allow for unwarranted beliefs to gain unjustified status?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e3079266-760d-40d4-94ac-a26eafa8f0b5
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism with Foundational Beliefs](infinitism-with-foundational-beliefs-synthesis-4273f090-f358-492e-9e7c-904c2a039ab2.md)

## Content

**In a system that allows foundational beliefs and infinite justification, certain beliefs might be accepted without proper scrutiny.**, **For instance, a belief superficially justified by a weak or irrelevant reason might be improperly elevated to justified status.**, **Such a scenario undermines the reliability and validity of justified beliefs within the system.**
