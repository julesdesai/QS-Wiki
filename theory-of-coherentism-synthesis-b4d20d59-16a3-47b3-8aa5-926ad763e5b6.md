## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is supported by a coherent set of interlocking beliefs**, **A coherent set of beliefs is one where all beliefs mutually support each other**
