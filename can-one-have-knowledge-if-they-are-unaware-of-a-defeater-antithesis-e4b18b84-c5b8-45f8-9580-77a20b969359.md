# Can one have knowledge if they are unaware of a defeater?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e4b18b84-c5b8-45f8-9580-77a20b969359
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [No Defeaters Theory](no-defeaters-theory-synthesis-e6eeb208-311d-4d09-a784-60d3e95f478f.md)

## Content

**It is possible for a person to have a justified true belief that they cannot distinguish from knowledge.**, **A person can be unaware of certain information that, if known, would undermine their belief.**, **If knowledge is only lacking when one becomes aware of a defeater, people could possess knowledge they don't recognize until presented with defeating evidence.**
