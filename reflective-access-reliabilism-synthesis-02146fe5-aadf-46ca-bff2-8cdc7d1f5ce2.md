## Content

**A belief is knowledge if it is produced by a reliable cognitive process that the agent can consciously reflect upon**, **A cognitive process is reliable if it tends to produce true beliefs over false ones**, **Reflective access requires that the agent can, in principle, be aware of the reliability of the process**
