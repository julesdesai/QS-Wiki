# Do individuals account for context in real-time judgments?

**Parent:** [Moderate Contextualism Summary](moderate-contextualism-summary-synthesis-54895291-30c8-42cf-8322-baea34575bd1.md)

## Content

**Suppose someone must quickly decide on the reliability of information during a crisis. Intuitively, it seems they would act on an absolute understanding of knowledge, rather than adjusting for the varying contexts.**, **There is an implicit expectation that knowledge retains integrity rapidly, which complex contextual adjustments might undermine.**
