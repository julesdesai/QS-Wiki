**Parent:** [Are coherence and truth always aligned?](are-coherence-and-truth-always-aligned-antithesis-54a2c05e-c3c2-492b-959a-c0fc0a65fd8c.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is justified by external factors**, **External factors are those that are independent of an individual's mental states**
