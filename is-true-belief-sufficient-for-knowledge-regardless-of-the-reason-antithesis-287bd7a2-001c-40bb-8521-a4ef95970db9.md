# Is true belief sufficient for knowledge regardless of the reason?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 287bd7a2-001c-40bb-8521-a4ef95970db9
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Experiential Foundationalism](experiential-foundationalism-synthesis-fd2d94ab-5595-4b29-a3f8-2962ee0c4e3c.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that knowledge requires both truth and justification. This principle together with the notion that knowledge is simply true belief entails that mere truth without justification is insufficient for knowledge.**
