# Do conflicting perceptions challenge Representational Realism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c7fdf3d1-277f-4dbe-97cf-3ceb928500ee
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Representational Realism](representational-realism-synthesis-2653bf9f-214b-48a1-b757-9a49097d2459.md)

## Content

**Different observers can perceive the same event differently, leading to conflicting mental representations.**, **For knowledge, Representational Realism requires the mental representation to correspond to a single truth of reality.**, **Conflicting mental representations challenge the notion that such correspondence can reliably occur.**
