# Knowledge as Virtue Externalism

**Parent:** [Could reliance on intellectual virtues lead to epistemic circularity?](could-reliance-on-intellectual-virtues-lead-to-epistemic-circularity-antithesis-df69d947-5e5a-4d4e-9743-d52b2d911924.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief to be produced by an external factor unrelated to intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is produced by an external factor unrelated to intellectual virtue**
