## Content

**For a person to have knowledge, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, they would not believe it**, **For a person to have knowledge, it is sufficient that their belief is true and sensitive to the truth**, **Sensitivity is a condition where if the proposition were false, the believer would not hold the belief**
