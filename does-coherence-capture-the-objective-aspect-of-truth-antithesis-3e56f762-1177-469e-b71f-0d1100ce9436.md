# Does coherence capture the objective aspect of truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3e56f762-1177-469e-b71f-0d1100ce9436
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism Summary](coherentism-summary-synthesis-7bdcae0a-f633-4c38-9857-77d6ba49435c.md)

## Content

**Suppose a set of beliefs is coherent yet detached from any external reality, such as in a person's intricate world of fiction. Intuitively, we would judge these beliefs as lacking truth since they don't correspond to objective reality. It is implausible that coherence alone captures truth without an objective aspect.**
