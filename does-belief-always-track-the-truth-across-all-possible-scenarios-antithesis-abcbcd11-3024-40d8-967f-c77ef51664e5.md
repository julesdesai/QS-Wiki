# Does belief always track the truth across all possible scenarios?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** abcbcd11-3024-40d8-967f-c77ef51664e5
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Truth-tracking theory of knowledge](truth-tracking-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-bf2c0c4d-aad3-4dd0-ab9e-ac7c51e27bd7.md)

## Content

**In a scenario where a person believes that a weather forecast predicts rain, and the forecast turns out to be correct due to a sudden storm, it is unclear whether the person's belief tracks the truth across different possible weather conditions.**, **It is implausible that a belief derived from an unreliable forecasting source can consistently track truth across all possible scenarios.**
