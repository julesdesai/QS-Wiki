## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a belief to qualify as knowledge, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a process that generally leads to truth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is produced by a process that generally leads to truth**
