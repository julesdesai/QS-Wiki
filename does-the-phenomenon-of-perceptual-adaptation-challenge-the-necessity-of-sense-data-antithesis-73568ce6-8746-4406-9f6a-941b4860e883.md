# Does the phenomenon of perceptual adaptation challenge the necessity of sense data?

## Content

**Perceptual adaptation, such as adjusting to visual inversion, suggests that prolonged exposure alters perception independently of immediacy in sense data.**, **Such adaptation brings into question the essential mediation role of sense data for understanding perception.**
