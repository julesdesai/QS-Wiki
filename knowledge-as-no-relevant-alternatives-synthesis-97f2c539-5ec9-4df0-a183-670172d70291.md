# Knowledge as No Relevant Alternatives

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does sensitivity fail when beliefs persist despite hallucination?](does-sensitivity-fail-when-beliefs-persist-despite-hallucination-antithesis-d059cc33-cc34-42c8-8515-b7190907032b.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that no relevant alternatives to the proposition are true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and no relevant alternatives to the proposition are true**
