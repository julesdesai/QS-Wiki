# Can knowledge be present in young children?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f8a6f67e-f187-4c7d-a5a7-41d8a350f827
**Depth:** 2
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Internalism](internalism-thesis-697c012e-ac77-4c5b-935c-0bf2d4d5ce83.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that young children reflectively access complex justification structures yet exhibit knowledge about simple facts like their own name or parents.**, **Consider a scenario where a child knows they are at home simply by being there, without consciously accessing any justification. Intuitively, we would judge the child knows they are home.**
