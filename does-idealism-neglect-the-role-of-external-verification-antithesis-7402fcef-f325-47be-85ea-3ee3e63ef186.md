# Does idealism neglect the role of external verification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7402fcef-f325-47be-85ea-3ee3e63ef186
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Idealism](idealism-synthesis-53ec409c-4a9b-4261-be64-9dda9f92eb94.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a person claims knowledge of a historical event solely through mental reconstruction. Intuitively, we either have to say that they know it without evidence or that verification requires external validation. If we say they know it without evidence, then evidence becomes unnecessary for knowledge. If we say validation is required, then external investigation challenges idealism's internal focus.**
