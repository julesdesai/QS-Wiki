**Parent:** [Is it possible for a justified true belief to fail to constitute knowledge due to luck?](is-it-possible-for-a-justified-true-belief-to-fail-to-constitute-knowledge-due-to-luck-antithesis-a58e1110-f9de-46ef-b9b2-27da4ad38ee9.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the process generating the belief is reliable**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is produced by a reliable process**
