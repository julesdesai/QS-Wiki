## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have a belief in the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have a belief in the proposition, the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process, and the proposition is true**
