# How does deferred reliability impact beliefs as knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2c866c85-d53d-42a9-bd78-1bb1e2f3f0bc
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Process Reliabilism](process-reliabilism-synthesis-73062a2b-1357-42ba-b901-d397b26dce96.md)

## Content

**Suppose reliability of a process depends on future verification, such as awaiting peer review in scientific publication, but currently remains speculative. Here, intuitively, such belief doesn't equate with knowledge.**, **Imagine an inventor presumes their device technology works based on prototypes without validation until later trials. We would intuitively say this isn't knowledge until verified.**
