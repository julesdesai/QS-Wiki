# Is indirect evidence a challenge for the causal theory?

**Parent:** [Causal theory of knowledge](causal-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-cf478e0f-32fe-4979-ba4a-203ad9c242c7.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where knowledge seems to arise from indirect evidence that lacks a direct causal link to the fact. Intuitively, such cases still appear to grant genuine knowledge.**, **A detective solving a crime through circumstantial evidence may know who the culprit is without a direct causal link to the criminal actions themselves.**
