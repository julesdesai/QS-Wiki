# Reliabilism for Knowledge

**Parent:** [Can safety suffice for knowledge?](can-safety-suffice-for-knowledge-antithesis-0469772a-2d78-41f2-9709-5e7f81a1af48.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief results from a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe it, the proposition is true, and their belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**
