# Does frequency of truth increase equate to reliability?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 200ffb08-2868-4b48-a311-a06ce677eee8
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilist Theory of Knowledge](reliabilist-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-fec1f444-460d-4835-9da9-5fd5405f3ea1.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person believes every other statement they read in a series of alternating true and false statements, resulting in a majority of true beliefs. Intuitively, this unreliable pattern doesn't constitute knowledge.**, **Reliability comprises more than a percentage of truth; consistent and systematic accuracy is essential for substantiating knowledge claims.**
