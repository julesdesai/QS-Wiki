# Are knowledge standards universally constant?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ad915f4e-6d46-47b4-9c5e-9db358f5ceef
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Subject-Sensitive Invariantism](subject-sensitive-invariantism-synthesis-a38d2af6-3ec4-421c-a558-c594d0cbc8b7.md)

## Content

**A person's belief in a true proposition in one context might not alter with more severe consequences in another, yet intuitively, their knowledge of the fact seems consistent.**, **It is conceivable that knowledge standards ought to remain constant and universal, aligning instead with evidence and truth value, not consequences experienced by individuals.**
