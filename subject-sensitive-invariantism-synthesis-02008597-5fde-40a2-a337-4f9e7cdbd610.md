## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, the justification depends partly on practical interests of the subject but not on external context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the practical interest-sensitive justification is met**
