# How do resilient beliefs fit with sensitivity and knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f1a8be38-8a0e-4045-8959-273f9f76810b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Sensitivity Condition View](sensitivity-condition-view-synthesis-f14f93ca-735d-497d-b9de-d52fabd7ef03.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a belief is resilient to change and would persist even with false information.**, **Intuitively, we either have to say that this person has knowledge due to resilience or lacks knowledge due to insensitivity.**
