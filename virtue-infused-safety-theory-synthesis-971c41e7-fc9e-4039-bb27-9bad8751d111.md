## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in nearby possible worlds where the person believes the proposition in a similar manner and employs epistemic virtues, the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, in nearby possible worlds where the person believes the proposition in a similar manner and employs epistemic virtues, the proposition is true**
