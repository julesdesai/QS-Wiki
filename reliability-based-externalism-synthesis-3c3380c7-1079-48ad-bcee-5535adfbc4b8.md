# Reliability-Based Externalism

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Could an external manipulation undermine one's justification?](could-an-external-manipulation-undermine-ones-justification-antithesis-e676fc40-034b-4759-84a2-fcff565ce861.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification for their belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process, and the proposition is true**
