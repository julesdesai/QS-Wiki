# Reliability Condition on Knowledge

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is true belief without safety condition sufficient for knowledge?](is-true-belief-without-safety-condition-sufficient-for-knowledge-antithesis-d1b4fe7d-c0f9-47ae-b261-c1c2c7bd4dfe.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is produced by a reliable method**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is reliably produced**
