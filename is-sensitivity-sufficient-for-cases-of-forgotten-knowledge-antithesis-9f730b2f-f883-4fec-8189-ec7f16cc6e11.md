# Is sensitivity sufficient for cases of forgotten knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9f730b2f-f883-4fec-8189-ec7f16cc6e11
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Knowledge as Sensitivity](knowledge-as-sensitivity-synthesis-a361f947-df1a-4345-a4d3-ea9ac7712828.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person once accurately believed that Paris is the capital of France and thus knew it, but over time they forget this fact.**, **If asked whether Paris is the capital of France, they would no longer have a belief about this proposition.**, **The sensitivity condition requires that they would not believe it were it false, but the absence of belief challenges this condition's applicability.**
