# Is Infinitism compatible with intuitive cases of epistemic closure?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 76168bd3-59b6-4c3d-8371-a4699d2d4c54
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism](infinitism-synthesis-dd7098a7-016a-4f9d-9b36-944f2be4f1f4.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which someone knows specific empirical truths through direct perception, like knowing the presence of a cat on their lap from looking down.**, **Intuitively, knowing this entails the belief in the presence of a cat involves finite reasoning.**, **If closure fails for these basic cases under Infinitism, then it challenges the coherence of practical epistemic reasoning.**
