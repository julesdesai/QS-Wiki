## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **Beliefs are justified by how they appear to the subject when there is additional support indicating reliability**, **If something seems true to a subject, and the source of this seeming is reliable, the subject has prima facie justification for believing it**
