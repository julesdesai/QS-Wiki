**Parent:** [Can foundational beliefs justify knowledge if they can't be traced to empirical evidence?](can-foundational-beliefs-justify-knowledge-if-they-cant-be-traced-to-empirical-evidence-antithesis-3aa9d805-22c1-49e4-a608-513780e07b9c.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that factors outside the agent's awareness justify the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and external factors justify the belief**
