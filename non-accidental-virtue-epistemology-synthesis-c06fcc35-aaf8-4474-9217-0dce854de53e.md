# Non-Accidental Virtue Epistemology

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** c06fcc35-aaf8-4474-9217-0dce854de53e
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does intellectual virtue always lead to knowledge?](does-intellectual-virtue-always-lead-to-knowledge-antithesis-2780d505-c1ef-4c00-91a9-22b002766986.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is non-accidentally true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief results from intellectual virtue without truth being a mere accident**
