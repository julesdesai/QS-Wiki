# Is pragmatic justification stable under changing circumstances?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 517b1586-60c3-48fc-bb3f-ed005689527e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Justification Theory](pragmatic-justification-theory-synthesis-838d6a9b-2af0-4e99-882f-62ebc82bed50.md)

## Content

**Suppose a belief is pragmatically justified under current circumstances but leads to negative outcomes when circumstances change. Intuitively, it seems the belief should lose its status as knowledge.**, **If the truth of a belief is contingent on stable conditions for pragmatic justification, it challenges whether it can consistently be considered true knowledge.**
