# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with No Defeaters

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** fc17750c-a53b-43a6-87f9-da4affda5b83
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is sensitivity a necessary condition for all types of knowledge?](is-sensitivity-a-necessary-condition-for-all-types-of-knowledge-antithesis-27b7bdff-63c3-450e-8c2d-e61a180b2b7d.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no defeaters undermining their justification**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, the proposition is true, and there are no defeaters undermining their justification**
