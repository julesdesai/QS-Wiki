# Can future predictions be justified by current perceptions?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a4d772f1-58c4-4acb-8132-d716a121e664
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Phenomenalism Summary](phenomenalism-summary-synthesis-ab29b70f-4a42-463b-b303-b38517d8aa22.md)

## Content

**Consider the practice of predicting weather patterns based on scientific models, which are often projections made independently of direct current perception. Intuitively, one has justified belief in these predictions, which seem to involve knowledge, even though they are not directly based on current perceptual experience.**
