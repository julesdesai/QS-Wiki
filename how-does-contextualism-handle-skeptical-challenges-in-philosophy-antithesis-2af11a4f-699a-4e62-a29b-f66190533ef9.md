# How does contextualism handle skeptical challenges in philosophy?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2af11a4f-699a-4e62-a29b-f66190533ef9
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism](contextualism-synthesis-c8874149-f3cc-4119-8896-6d48b3a50470.md)

## Content

**Skeptical scenarios, like the brain-in-a-vat hypothesis, present situations where ordinary beliefs are systematically doubted, raising the question of whether anything is truly known.**, **In such scenarios, intuitive judgments suggest that these skeptical hypotheses demand very high standards for knowledge, often entire dismissal of ordinary claims as knowledge.**, **Contextualism needs to address whether it can consistently maintain knowledge attributions when skepticism imposes far-reaching epistemic challenges.**
