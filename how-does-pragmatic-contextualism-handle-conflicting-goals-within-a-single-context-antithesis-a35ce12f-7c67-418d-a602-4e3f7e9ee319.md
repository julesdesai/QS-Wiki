# How does pragmatic contextualism handle conflicting goals within a single context?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a35ce12f-7c67-418d-a602-4e3f7e9ee319
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Contextualism](pragmatic-contextualism-synthesis-eb83c85f-dd41-4bfc-8e5a-553d55565568.md)

## Content

**Within a single context, individuals may pursue conflicting practical goals based on divergent beliefs.**, **Beliefs cannot universally achieve practical success if they support incompatible objectives within the same context.**, **Conflicting practical goals challenge the idea that achieving success validates a belief as knowledge, suggesting a need for additional criteria.**
