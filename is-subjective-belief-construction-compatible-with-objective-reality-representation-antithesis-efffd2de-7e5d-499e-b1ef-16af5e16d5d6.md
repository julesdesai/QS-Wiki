# Is subjective belief construction compatible with objective reality representation?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** efffd2de-7e5d-499e-b1ef-16af5e16d5d6
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Constructivist Coherentism](constructivist-coherentism-synthesis-df66a247-3331-4fc3-9810-0103a6210363.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where subjective experiences lead to conflicting belief constructions about the same aspect of reality. In such a scenario, intuitively it seems problematic to claim that conflicting beliefs can all accurately represent objective reality, suggesting incompatibility.**, **There is a plausible principle that objective reality should impose constraints on the acceptability of belief constructions; without such constraints, the truth of conflicting beliefs cannot be reliably adjudicated.**
