## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief is the result of either intellectual virtue or a reliable process**, **An intellectual virtue is a character trait that leads one to form true beliefs in a wide range of circumstances**, **A reliable process is a cognitive procedure that consistently yields true beliefs**
