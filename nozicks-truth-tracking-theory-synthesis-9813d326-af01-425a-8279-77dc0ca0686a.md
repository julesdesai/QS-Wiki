## Content

**Knowledge is justified true belief connected to truth through a tracking relation**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief tracks the truth across possible scenarios**, **A person knows a proposition if, in close possible worlds where the proposition is false, they would not believe it**, **A person knows a proposition if, in possible worlds where the proposition is true, they would believe it**
