# Can inaccurate instruments still yield knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c96f687f-6c2e-4f14-b6c0-6ea23ab44e27
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Representationalism with Instrumental Enhancement](representationalism-with-instrumental-enhancement-synthesis-27f89a71-7167-40fa-bf3b-4c0e55b55fea.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where an instrument provides mostly accurate readings but occasionally malfunctions, resulting in false representations. In such a scenario, intuitively we might judge that knowledge was still acquired at regular moments, yet how we determine which moments are true without independent verification challenges the view that knowledge necessitates accurate representation through instruments.**
