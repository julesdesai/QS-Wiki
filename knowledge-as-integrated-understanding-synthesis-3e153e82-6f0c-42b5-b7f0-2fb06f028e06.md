## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they understand the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their understanding integrates both theoretical knowledge and real-world application**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they understand the proposition with an integrated approach and the proposition is true**
