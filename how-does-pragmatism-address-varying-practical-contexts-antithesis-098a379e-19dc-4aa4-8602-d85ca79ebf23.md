# How does pragmatism address varying practical contexts?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 098a379e-19dc-4aa4-8602-d85ca79ebf23
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatism Summary](pragmatism-summary-synthesis-ddc2fd27-140a-48fe-b697-40a3c8c11bfc.md)

## Content

**Consider a proposition that has practical success in one context but is useless in another. Intuitively, the truth value of the proposition should not change based on context-dependent success.**, **It is implausible that knowledge is context-dependent in terms of its truthfulness or justification, as knowledge should be universal.**
