## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that justification for believing the proposition involves a finite chain of reasons ending in foundational beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and justification involves a finite chain of reasons ending in foundational beliefs**
