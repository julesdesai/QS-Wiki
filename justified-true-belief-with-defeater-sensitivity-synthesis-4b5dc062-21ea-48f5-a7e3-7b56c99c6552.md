**Parent:** [Is the non-abandonment of belief in face of reliable evidence a proper condition for knowledge?](is-the-non-abandonment-of-belief-in-face-of-reliable-evidence-a-proper-condition-for-knowledge-antithesis-2c9a5e4e-62c8-4638-abf3-d69ec6ec185c.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and they would cease to believe it upon encountering additional disconfirming information from reliable sources**
