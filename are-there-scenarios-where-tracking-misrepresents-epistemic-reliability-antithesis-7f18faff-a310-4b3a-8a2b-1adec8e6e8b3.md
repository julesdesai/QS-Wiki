# Are there scenarios where tracking misrepresents epistemic reliability?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7f18faff-a310-4b3a-8a2b-1adec8e6e8b3
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Tracking Theory](tracking-theory-synthesis-0bad637f-a0d8-4d67-8d05-73b0a7027f5a.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person has a belief that tracks the truth due to an unreliable method. Intuitively, this person's knowledge claim would seem unjustified.**, **It is implausible for knowledge to rest solely on truth tracking when the method lacks reliability.**
