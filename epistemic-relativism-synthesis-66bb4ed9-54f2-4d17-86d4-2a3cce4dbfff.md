## Content

**Knowledge is context-dependent**, **The standards for knowing a proposition vary with different frameworks or systems**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **A person's justification is evaluated based on the standards of the applicable epistemic framework**
