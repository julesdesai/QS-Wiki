# Knowledge as Contextual External Coherentism

**Parent:** [Can a false proposition ever be known if it coheres contextually?](can-a-false-proposition-ever-be-known-if-it-coheres-contextually-antithesis-5d0113ad-5dad-4a6c-b50b-83756f9f47d0.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres with a system of beliefs justified externally given the context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief coheres with a contextually justified external system of beliefs**
