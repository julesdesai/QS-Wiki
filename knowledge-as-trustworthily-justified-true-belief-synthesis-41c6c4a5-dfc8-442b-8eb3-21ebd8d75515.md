# Knowledge as Trustworthily Justified True Belief

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 41c6c4a5-dfc8-442b-8eb3-21ebd8d75515
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does knowledge require current engagement with a proposition?](does-knowledge-require-current-engagement-with-a-proposition-antithesis-8983b463-32d1-49ee-9115-b90989f58505.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified through trustworthy methods**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is justified through trustworthy methods**
