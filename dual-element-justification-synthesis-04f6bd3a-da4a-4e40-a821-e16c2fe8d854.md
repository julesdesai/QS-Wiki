## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, justification consists of two elements: an internal alignment and external validation**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and both justification elements are present**
