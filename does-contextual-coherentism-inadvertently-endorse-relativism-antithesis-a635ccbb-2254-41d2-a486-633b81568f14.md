# Does contextual coherentism inadvertently endorse relativism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a635ccbb-2254-41d2-a486-633b81568f14
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualist Coherentism](contextualist-coherentism-synthesis-2211d0a5-4c05-4516-984a-e004555aaa45.md)

## Content

**A belief is justified if it coheres with other beliefs relevant to the context.**, **If different contexts have radically different criteria, then justification could vary wildly across contexts.**, **This potential variability threatens to lead contextual coherentism towards relativism about knowledge.**
