# Is memory-based knowledge possible without perception?

**Terminal:** No

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a historian vividly recalls details of a past historical event they once experienced. Intuitively, we would say the historian knows these details based on memory rather than new direct perceptual experience.**, **Memory can provide a basis for knowing something even in the absence of current perceptual experience.**
