# Must the cause of belief be directly linked to the specific truth under consideration?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c40551d4-a9b4-4c4b-b39f-57c9be048d93
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Causal Theory of Knowledge](causal-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-916d423a-1723-4911-b4ab-5dfda1d6916a.md)

## Content

**In a case where Tony reads a reputable weather report predicting rain and believes it will rain, if the report is actually based on faulty data yet it still rains, Tony's belief is caused by the report, not the rain itself. It is implausible that Tony truly knows it will rain, given the flawed causality involved.**
