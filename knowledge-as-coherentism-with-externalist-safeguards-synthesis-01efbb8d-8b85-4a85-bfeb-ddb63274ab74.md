# Knowledge as Coherentism with Externalist Safeguards

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 01efbb8d-8b85-4a85-bfeb-ddb63274ab74
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can coherence avoid bias or cognitive distortion?](can-coherence-avoid-bias-or-cognitive-distortion-antithesis-57bf8977-5c8f-477c-8170-98744c8f3854.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief coheres with other beliefs in a network of beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the network of beliefs is externally verified against biases and cognitive distortions**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, their belief coheres with other beliefs in a network of beliefs, and the network is externally verified for biases and distortions**
