# Can we trust all sensory experiences to reflect reality?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 99ea87fe-7d48-467c-9483-2195c5a6a78f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Representationalism](representationalism-synthesis-c6ad7a19-c6b2-4a71-b6f5-a8020ca1b42c.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a person is experiencing a mirage of water in a desert. Intuitively, we either have to say that the sensory experience is representing reality inaccurately or that it is accurate but the external world is deceptive. If we say the experience is inaccurate, then sensory experiences can't always be relied upon for knowledge.**
