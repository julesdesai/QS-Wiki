# Knowledge as Safety-Ensured Reliability

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** d8707dc7-bc43-4642-bffb-afc12367406b
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is reliability alone sufficient for knowledge?](is-reliability-alone-sufficient-for-knowledge-antithesis-39939579-96f9-405e-9cf0-8dec2d5a4bb2.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from a process that ensures the belief would not be formed if false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief arises from a process ensuring it would not be believed if false**
