# Knowledge as Proper Function

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can someone have virtuous belief without truth?](can-someone-have-virtuous-belief-without-truth-antithesis-37c697cd-41d1-4e6c-94cb-f17f1c1f52d5.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief results from faculties functioning properly**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the faculties are aimed at truth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief results from faculties functioning properly, and the faculties are aimed at truth**
