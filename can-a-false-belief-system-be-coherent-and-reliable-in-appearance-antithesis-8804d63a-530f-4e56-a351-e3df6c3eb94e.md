# Can a false belief system be coherent and reliable in appearance?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 8804d63a-530f-4e56-a351-e3df6c3eb94e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilist Coherentism](reliabilist-coherentism-synthesis-d6c1c642-66ac-40b0-82ca-187cf7250017.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a false belief system is internally coherent and appears reliable, based on identical procedures used for verifying true systems. Intuitively, despite appearances, such a system lacks genuine reliability.**, **It is implausible that the mere appearance of coherence and reliability can suffice for genuine justification when the beliefs are false.**
