## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **The standards for justification vary with the context in which the knowledge claim is made**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the belief meets the contextual standards for justification, and the proposition is true**
