# Does sensitivity handle the problem of preemptive belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 77fac497-050f-44ee-82fa-10af9ce98140
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Knowledge as Sensitivity Theory](knowledge-as-sensitivity-theory-synthesis-cfe82d73-3c24-4801-8611-fbe2443e064c.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person, anticipating the results of an experiment, forms a belief about it before the results are known.**, **The truth of their belief may be contingent on the outcomes that later confirm it, yet sensitivity requires they should not believe it if it were false, opposing pre-justified belief formation.**
