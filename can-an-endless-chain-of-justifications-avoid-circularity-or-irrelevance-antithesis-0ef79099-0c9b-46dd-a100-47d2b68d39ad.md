# Can an endless chain of justifications avoid circularity or irrelevance?

**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism with An Emphasis on the Chain of Justifications](infinitism-with-an-emphasis-on-the-chain-of-justifications-synthesis-fbcbcc46-9003-42db-8878-9fc62d8799d4.md)

## Content

**Infinite chains of reasoning risk becoming circular, where the same reasons resurface, creating a lack of true new justificatory insight. Circular reasoning hinders establishing epistemic credentials for a belief.**, **Consider a chain where each link is an increasingly attenuated version of irrelevant matters. Intuitively, such endless justifications would not meaningfully support knowledge.**
