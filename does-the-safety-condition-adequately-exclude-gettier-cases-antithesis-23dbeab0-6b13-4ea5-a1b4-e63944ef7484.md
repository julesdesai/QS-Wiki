# Does the safety condition adequately exclude Gettier cases?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 23dbeab0-6b13-4ea5-a1b4-e63944ef7484
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Knowledge as Safety](knowledge-as-safety-synthesis-df26c783-c115-4102-a084-91df0b928704.md)

## Content

**Consider a classic Gettier case: A person believes a proposition based on strong evidence which, in turn, is untrue, but due to some unforeseen coincidence, the belief ends up being true.**, **In nearby possible worlds, similar lucky coincidences might ensure the proposition remains true, making the belief safely held without constituting knowledge.**
