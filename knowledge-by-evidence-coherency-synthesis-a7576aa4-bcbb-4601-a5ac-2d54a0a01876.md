**Parent:** [Is belief in truth across scenarios sufficient for knowledge?](is-belief-in-truth-across-scenarios-sufficient-for-knowledge-antithesis-4e3c06f2-4b08-455c-ba72-51256377ef1c.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief coheres with their overall set of evidence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief coheres with their overall set of evidence**
