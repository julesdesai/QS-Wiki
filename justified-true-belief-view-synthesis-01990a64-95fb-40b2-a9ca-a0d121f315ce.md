# Justified True Belief View

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 01990a64-95fb-40b2-a9ca-a0d121f315ce
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge without belief be epistemically reliable?](can-knowledge-without-belief-be-epistemically-reliable-antithesis-9f3357ea-602d-4ddd-ba76-baf8bef84b81.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person believes the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **A person knows a proposition if and only if it is true, they believe it, and they have justification for believing it**
