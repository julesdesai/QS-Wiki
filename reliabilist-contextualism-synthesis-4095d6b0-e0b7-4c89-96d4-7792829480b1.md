## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed through a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the standards for knowledge adjust with the context affecting the belief formation**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is formed through a reliable cognitive process, and contextual standards are met**
