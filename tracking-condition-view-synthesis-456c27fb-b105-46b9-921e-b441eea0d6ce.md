## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief tracks the truth across nearby possible worlds**, **Tracking involves maintaining a true belief when the proposition is true and losing it when the proposition is false**
