# How does coherence handle conflicting evidence?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e7cfca5d-128c-4825-a91e-306dd08e5cc5
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherent Phenomenalism](coherent-phenomenalism-synthesis-2365a190-e3fc-4d37-a83b-defd0eadffa2.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person encounters conflicting perceptual evidence within their set of experiences, such as witnessing a mirage and a real oasis. Intuitively, determining knowledge is difficult when coherence is disrupted by contradictory evidence.**, **There is a plausible principle that knowledge must accommodate potential conflicts in evidence, which pure coherence struggles to address.**
