# How does Gettier-like scenarios challenge Proper Functionalism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9031eb79-e3ac-48b9-9aa7-2caefa86b97c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Proper Functionalism Summary](proper-functionalism-summary-synthesis-5b044e3f-97d9-4386-9c2f-bd333212a4a8.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person forms a belief that it is noon because their watch, which normally functions properly, reads noon, but the watch has stopped working earlier. Even if it turns out to be noon, intuitively, we do not consider this a case of knowledge.**, **A belief that is accidentally true due to factors beyond one's cognitive faculties challenges the notion that knowledge is simply true belief produced by properly functioning cognitive faculties.**
