## Content

**Knowledge is context-independent**, **The standards for knowing a proposition are fixed regardless of context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **A person's justification is evaluated based on universal standards**
