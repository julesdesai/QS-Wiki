## Content

**A person knows a proposition if they have a true belief**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief is contextually safe**, **A belief is contextually safe if, in relevant nearby possible worlds determined by context, it is not false**, **The set of relevant nearby worlds is determined by the context in which the belief is assessed**
