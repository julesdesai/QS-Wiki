# Can a person have knowledge without conscious exercise of virtue?

## Content

**Consider a situation where someone's long-term habit leads them to believe a true statement, without any conscious intellectual effort, like reacting to well-practiced safety drills.**, **In such a case, knowledge seems to exist without conscious exercise of intellectual virtues, challenging their necessity in knowledge formation.**
