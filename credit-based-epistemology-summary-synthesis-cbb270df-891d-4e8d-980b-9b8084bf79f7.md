## Content

**Knowledge arises when the truth of a belief is creditable to one's cognitive efforts**, **The agent must earn credit for the truth of their belief through their cognitive abilities**, **Credit is assigned based on the knower's effective cognitive engagement**, **True beliefs formed independently of credit to the agent's abilities are not classified as knowledge**
