# Is mere consistency with unreliable beliefs enough for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** adc59d61-a071-4476-a8ed-8ae3f153b832
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentist Reliability Summary](coherentist-reliability-summary-synthesis-49034568-193c-4a88-8ae7-b062de898be5.md)

## Content

**A person holds a belief that is consistently supported by other beliefs, yet those other beliefs are derived from unreliable sources. Intuitively, this consistency is insufficient for qualifying the initial belief as knowledge.**
