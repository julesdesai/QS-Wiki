# Evidentialist Internalism

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 1903d6d1-54d5-4b17-8ecf-8eb6b30cc4d1
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is having true belief enough for knowledge?](is-having-true-belief-enough-for-knowledge-antithesis-9eddf01d-2a93-4cb9-881b-bce8711f3932.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person has justification for the belief based on evidence they can access internally**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for the belief based on accessible evidence, and the proposition is true**
