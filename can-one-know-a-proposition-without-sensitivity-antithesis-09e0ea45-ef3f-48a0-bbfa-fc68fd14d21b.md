# Can one know a proposition without sensitivity?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 09e0ea45-ef3f-48a0-bbfa-fc68fd14d21b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Sensitivity Theory of Knowledge](sensitivity-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-ba84f32c-948c-4607-9039-09527bd88c50.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where a person believes a true proposition based on reliable evidence, such that if the proposition were false, they would still believe it due to similar evidence.**, **The person still seems to know the proposition, despite the lack of sensitivity because they have strong justification and the proposition is true.**
