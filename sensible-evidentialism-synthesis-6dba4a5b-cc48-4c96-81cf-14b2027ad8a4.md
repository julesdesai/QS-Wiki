## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is justified by evidence available to them**, **Justification depends on the quality of evidence accessible to the believer**
