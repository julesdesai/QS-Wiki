# Does Indirect Realism undermine the certainty of scientific knowledge?

## Content

**Scientific knowledge often relies on empirical observation and perception.**, **Indirect realism suggests such observations are mediated by mental representations.**, **If representations can misrepresent reality, the certainty of scientific knowledge may be undermined.**
