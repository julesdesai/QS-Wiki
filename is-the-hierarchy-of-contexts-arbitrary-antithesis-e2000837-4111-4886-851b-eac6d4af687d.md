# Is the hierarchy of contexts arbitrary?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e2000837-4111-4886-851b-eac6d4af687d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism with Reliabilism](contextualism-with-reliabilism-synthesis-c7c3d2a5-9a25-4e3f-9e1f-65010441e024.md)

## Content

**Consider a situation where two contexts have different standards for knowledge, but there is no clear reasoning or criterion to determine which standard should take precedence.**, **The lack of an objective criterion for comparing standards across contexts results in an arbitrary hierarchy, undermining the coherence of context-dependent knowledge.**
