# How do perceptual errors challenge the concept of direct sensory experience?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 98dbdb00-bdb2-484c-b2dd-1685b1c88339
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Direct Realism with Cross-Modal Integration](direct-realism-with-cross-modal-integration-synthesis-1a006bbb-612c-4e5a-ba7b-987e18fe12d2.md)

## Content

**It is plausible that sensory experiences can lead to errors in perception, as when a mirage makes a desert appear to contain water.**, **In such cases, sensory experience does not accurately reflect the external object or situation.**, **Perceptual errors suggest that sensory experiences are not always reliable access points to external reality, challenging the direct link between perception and knowledge.**
