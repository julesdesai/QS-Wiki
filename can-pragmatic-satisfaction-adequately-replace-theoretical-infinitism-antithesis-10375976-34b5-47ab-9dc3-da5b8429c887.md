# Can pragmatic satisfaction adequately replace theoretical infinitism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 10375976-34b5-47ab-9dc3-da5b8429c887
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Infinitism](pragmatic-infinitism-synthesis-f73c0505-1eaa-4768-8143-11a2f8c9ca92.md)

## Content

**Theoretical infinitism implies a commitment to an unreachable ideal, whereas pragmatic satisfaction accepts a practical stopping point.**, **Consider a situation where someone uses reliable heuristics (e.g., a weather forecast) instead of infinite reasoning to decide on carrying an umbrella.**, **In practice, pragmatic methods are routinely effective despite lacking the infinite justification purportedly required by theoretical infinitism.**, **This suggests a disconnect between the pragmatic application of knowledge and the theoretical underpinning of infinitism.**
