# Does the framework dependency of knowledge allow for progress in understanding?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5ba22383-6b2d-4a53-960a-d5904145a9c7
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Epistemic Relativism](epistemic-relativism-synthesis-8a36f430-fbe7-4356-93bb-efe42975c670.md)

## Content

**Suppose knowledge within an epistemic community is relative to its framework, which is determined by specific norms and beliefs.**, **Over time, communities may evolve, reject, or alter these norms and beliefs.**, **If existing knowledge is strictly tied to evolving frameworks, the concept of accumulated, progressive understanding comes into question.**
