# Knowledge as Virtuous Believing

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Is true belief always knowledge with reliability?](is-true-belief-always-knowledge-with-reliability-antithesis-1fa94e69-2405-491a-b867-43214047158f.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief arises through intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief arises through intellectual virtue**
