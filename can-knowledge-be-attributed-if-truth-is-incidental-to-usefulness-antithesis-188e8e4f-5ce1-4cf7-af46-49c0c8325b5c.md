# Can knowledge be attributed if truth is incidental to usefulness?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 188e8e4f-5ce1-4cf7-af46-49c0c8325b5c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatism with Reliabilism](pragmatism-with-reliabilism-synthesis-504b62f9-b2fa-4831-be00-0daf59bd2488.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person follows a horoscope to make decisions, and this leads to successful outcomes by sheer chance. Intuitively, success does not make the belief knowledge.**, **The concept that knowledge must involve a direct connection to truth is at odds with utility-based evaluation in chance cases.**
