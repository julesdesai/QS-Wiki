# How does the possibility of systematic deception undermine coherent phenomenalism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a2e8389b-c496-426d-a08b-86265e6fda48
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherent Phenomenalism](coherent-phenomenalism-synthesis-762c00d9-ee32-4648-8d8e-67643edf20ba.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which individuals are subject to systematic deception, such as in a virtual reality simulation. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that while perceptions are coherent within the simulation, they do not provide knowledge of the actual external world.**
