## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief be guided by intellectual and moral virtues independently**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that each virtue independently contributes to the belief in the proposition**
