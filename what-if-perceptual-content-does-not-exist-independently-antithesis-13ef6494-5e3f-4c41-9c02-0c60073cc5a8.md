# What if perceptual content does not exist independently?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 13ef6494-5e3f-4c41-9c02-0c60073cc5a8
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Naïve Realism](naïve-realism-synthesis-960c5650-a545-46a7-9f9f-61ad44274751.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which perceptual content, such as color, depends on specific light wavelengths and sensory apparatus rather than existing independently in the world. Intuitively, if color does not exist directly as perceived, defiance of direct perception occurs.**, **It is implausible to claim that colors exist in objects independent of perception if scientific accounts of color perception are accurate.**
