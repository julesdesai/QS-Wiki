## Content

**Knowledge is not context-dependent**, **The standards for knowing a proposition are invariant but defined in relation to particular relations or roles**, **A person knows a proposition if they fulfill the invariant standards in reference to their role or relationship to others**, **Knowledge claims remain coherent across different contexts by fixing the standards relationally rather than contextually**
