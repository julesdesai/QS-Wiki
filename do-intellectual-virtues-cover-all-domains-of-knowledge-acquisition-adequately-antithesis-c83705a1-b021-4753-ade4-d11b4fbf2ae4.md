# Do intellectual virtues cover all domains of knowledge acquisition adequately?

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which someone gains knowledge through a natural talent, like perfect pitch, without effort or cultivation of virtue.**, **This challenges the claim that all knowledge requires active intellectual virtues, as the knowledge is naturally possessed without the person's engagement of any virtue.**
