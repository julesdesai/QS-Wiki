# Should false yet practically beneficial beliefs be recognized as knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 537530a1-ae13-46ac-a1ad-922f509d0dd3
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatism: Practical Consequences](pragmatism-practical-consequences-synthesis-0f8107eb-2a4a-42d8-a5a9-2983c41a2844.md)

## Content
