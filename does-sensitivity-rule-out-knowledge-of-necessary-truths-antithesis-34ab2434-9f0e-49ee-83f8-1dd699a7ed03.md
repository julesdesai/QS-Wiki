# Does sensitivity rule out knowledge of necessary truths?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 34ab2434-9f0e-49ee-83f8-1dd699a7ed03
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Sensitivity Theory of Knowledge](sensitivity-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-ba84f32c-948c-4607-9039-09527bd88c50.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that knowledge can include necessary truths, such as mathematical or logical truths. This principle together with the sensitivity requirement, which demands non-belief if false, seems incompatible because such truths cannot be false.**
