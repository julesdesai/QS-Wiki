# Is it plausible to link belief with practical stakes?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 93833c89-7bf3-49b6-bb18-278b0b7e57b6
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Encroachment Theory](pragmatic-encroachment-theory-synthesis-66e6d98a-2899-4b80-b05b-15066f046ddd.md)

## Content

**Imagine a situation where someone believes almost certainly that a historical fact is true but reporting it inaccurately has severe repercussions under specific circumstances.**, **It is implausible that the sheer practical importance surrounding this belief should interfere with the truth conditions or epistemic justification of their belief.**
