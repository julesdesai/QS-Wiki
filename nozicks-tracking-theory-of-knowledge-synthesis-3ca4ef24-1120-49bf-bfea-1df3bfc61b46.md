## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were true, the person would believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person believes the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the person believes the proposition, and if it were false they would not believe it, and if it were true they would believe it**
