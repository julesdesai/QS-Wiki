# Knowledge as Eliminating Relevant Alternatives

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge be attained if someone has a true belief that turns out true by luck?](can-knowledge-be-attained-if-someone-has-a-true-belief-that-turns-out-true-by-luck-antithesis-9394cbf7-2ac1-4f55-9cd4-715a63844f37.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they can eliminate relevant alternatives to the proposition being true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing it, the proposition is true, and they can eliminate relevant alternatives**
