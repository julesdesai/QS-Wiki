## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have reasons that conclusively support the proposition**, **These conclusive reasons constitute a guarantee against falsehood**
