# Knowledge as Infallible Justified True Belief

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is not based on any false lemmas**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification cannot be undermined by misleading or incomplete information**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is justified, the belief is not based on any false lemmas, and the justification cannot be undermined by misleading or incomplete information**
