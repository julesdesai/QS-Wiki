# Does the truth-conduciveness of cognitive systems necessarily lead to knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3cd8589f-752b-4be0-8789-c6d3d4523b21
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Functionalist View with Cognitive Systems](functionalist-view-with-cognitive-systems-synthesis-27113062-45e8-43f3-9420-ebe4ffe352b2.md)

## Content

**A cognitive system can be designed to maximize true beliefs but could still lead to false beliefs under certain circumstances**, **Truth-conduciveness alone does not guarantee the truth of every belief produced by the system**, **If a belief is false, then it cannot be considered knowledge regardless of the truth-conduciveness of the system**
