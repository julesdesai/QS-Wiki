## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is an appropriate causal connection between the proposition being true and the person believing it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there is an appropriate causal connection between the truth of the proposition and their belief**
