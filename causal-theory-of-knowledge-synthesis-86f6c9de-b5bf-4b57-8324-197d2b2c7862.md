# Causal Theory of Knowledge

**Depth:** 3

**Parent:** [Does knowledge exclude lucky guesses?](does-knowledge-exclude-lucky-guesses-antithesis-18098b40-6a38-47f7-9d44-822b036f6ea8.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief in the proposition is causally connected to the fact that makes the proposition true**
