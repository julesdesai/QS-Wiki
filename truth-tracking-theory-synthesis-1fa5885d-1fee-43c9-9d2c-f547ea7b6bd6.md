## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief tracks the truth by being both sensitive and adhering to a method that generally gets things right**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the belief tracks the truth, and the proposition is true**
