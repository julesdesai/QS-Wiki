# Knowledge as Reliabilism

**Parent:** [Does intelligence alone not account sufficiently for knowledge?](does-intelligence-alone-not-account-sufficiently-for-knowledge-antithesis-bbcda4ea-888e-4f78-8c39-22e40ca32171.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **A reliable cognitive process is one that produces a high ratio of true beliefs over false beliefs**
