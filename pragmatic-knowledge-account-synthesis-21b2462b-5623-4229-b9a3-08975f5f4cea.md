## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief has practical consequences or usefulness**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is not the result of manipulation**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is true, the belief has practical consequences or usefulness, and the belief is not the result of manipulation**
