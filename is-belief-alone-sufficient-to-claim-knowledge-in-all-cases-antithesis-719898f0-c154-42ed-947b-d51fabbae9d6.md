# Is belief alone sufficient to claim knowledge in all cases?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 719898f0-c154-42ed-947b-d51fabbae9d6
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Subject-Sensitive Invariantism](subject-sensitive-invariantism-synthesis-687e35ad-b9eb-49bd-a730-d8bc9269ea3a.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person believes a widely debunked conspiracy theory. In such a scenario, intuitively we would not say they know the conspiracy is true.**, **It is implausible that belief alone can suffice for knowledge without sufficient epistemic support.**
