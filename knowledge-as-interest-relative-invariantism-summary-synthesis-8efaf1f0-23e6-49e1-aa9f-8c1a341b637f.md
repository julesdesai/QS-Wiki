## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the fixed standards of knowledge vary with the practical interests of the subject**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the fixed standards of knowledge are met relative to the subject's interests**
