# Does reliability account for environmental luck?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a85e7e37-3247-45f7-8ee9-e7ec01fb496a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism Summary](reliabilism-summary-synthesis-14b8b19c-565a-493c-b5a5-1d512c9085eb.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where a generally unreliable environment happens to present accurate information that aligns with a person's beliefs, making them true by chance. Intuitively, the person lacks knowledge since the reliability of the process was incidental to the correctness of the belief.**, **This poses a challenge to the view that reliable processes are sufficient for justified true belief by highlighting the role of environmental luck.**
