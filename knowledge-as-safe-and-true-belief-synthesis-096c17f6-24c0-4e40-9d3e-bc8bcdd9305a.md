## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is safe from error nearby worlds where the belief is formed**, **Safety requires that in similar situations, the belief would not easily be false**
