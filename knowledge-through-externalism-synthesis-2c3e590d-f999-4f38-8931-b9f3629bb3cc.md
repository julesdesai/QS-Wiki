## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, the justification does not need to be consciously accessible to the believer**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the justification to be grounded in external factors beyond a person's immediate awareness**
