## Content

**Knowledge ascription involves a sensitivity condition**, **A person knows a proposition if changes in the truth value of the proposition would affect their belief**, **A belief is sensitive if in the nearest possible world where the proposition is false, the person does not believe it**, **A person knows a proposition if the proposition is true**
