# Counterfactual Dependence Knowledge

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Is knowledge possible with a broken causal chain?](is-knowledge-possible-with-a-broken-causal-chain-antithesis-7ab7bafc-e0af-408f-9631-95e462ba443f.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that belief depends counterfactually on the truth of the proposition**, **Counterfactual dependence means that if the proposition were not true, the person would not believe it**
