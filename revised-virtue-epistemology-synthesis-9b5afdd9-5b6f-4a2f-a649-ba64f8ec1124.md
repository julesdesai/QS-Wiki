## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person exercises intellectual virtues in forming the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person has justification for believing the proposition**, **Intellectual virtues include traits like open-mindedness and intellectual courage**, **A person knows a proposition if they believe it, have justification for believing it, and the proposition is true**
