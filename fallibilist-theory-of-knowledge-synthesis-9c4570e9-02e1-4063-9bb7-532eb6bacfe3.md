**Parent:** [Are memory beliefs sufficiently covered by foundationalism?](are-memory-beliefs-sufficiently-covered-by-foundationalism-antithesis-5cbf4cb3-75e4-4277-b17a-55710ee2b4d0.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification is strong enough to make the belief highly probable**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification makes the belief highly probable**
