# Does knowledge necessitate truth in a relevant nearby possible situation?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 697cc0a2-f39c-4a88-9d70-2c35d77ff5e0
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Knowledge as Tracking Theory](knowledge-as-tracking-theory-synthesis-4ddbf25f-905e-4551-9162-40f6ecb67e5a.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person forms a true belief about a proposition that is false in all relevant nearby possible situations due to misleading evidence. Intuitively, we would judge that the person does not truly know the proposition since it was accidentally true, challenging the necessity of truth across situations.**
