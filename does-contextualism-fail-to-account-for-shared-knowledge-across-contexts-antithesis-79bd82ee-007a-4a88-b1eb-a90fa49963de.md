# Does contextualism fail to account for shared knowledge across contexts?

**Parent:** [Contextualism with Sensitivity Condition](contextualism-with-sensitivity-condition-synthesis-dd26ba8e-d20d-4379-a4ae-bc06eaa28734.md)

## Content

**In a case where a community shares a belief based on common knowledge that is context-dependent**, **An individual from outside the community does not see the belief as knowledge due to different contextual standards**, **The divergence across contexts questions whether knowledge is genuinely shared or contextually siloed**
