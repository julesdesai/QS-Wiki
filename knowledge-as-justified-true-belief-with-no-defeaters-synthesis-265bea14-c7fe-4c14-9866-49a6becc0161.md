# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with No Defeaters

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge from a reliable process still fail due to competing processes that are equally valid?](can-knowledge-from-a-reliable-process-still-fail-due-to-competing-processes-that-are-equally-valid-antithesis-f01ec9df-633c-4988-a230-e60789134095.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that no defeaters exist that undermine the justification**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, have justification for believing the proposition, and no defeaters exist**
