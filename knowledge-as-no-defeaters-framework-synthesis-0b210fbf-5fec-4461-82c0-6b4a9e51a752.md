# Knowledge as No-Defeaters Framework

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 0b210fbf-5fec-4461-82c0-6b4a9e51a752
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is reliability alone sufficient for knowledge in all cases?](is-reliability-alone-sufficient-for-knowledge-in-all-cases-antithesis-668f24a2-f474-44f4-850e-a6a712899748.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there be no defeaters to their belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there are no defeaters to their belief**
