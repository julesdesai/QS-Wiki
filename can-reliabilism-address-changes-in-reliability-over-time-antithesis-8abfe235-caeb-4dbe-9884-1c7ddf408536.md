# Can Reliabilism address changes in reliability over time?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 8abfe235-caeb-4dbe-9884-1c7ddf408536
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism](reliabilism-synthesis-e69ee86a-1c0a-4d0e-840e-f25c67fd8e9a.md)

## Content

**Consider a case in which a reliable process at one time becomes unreliable due to changes in the environment, such as technological advances or updated information. Intuitively, if a process changes from reliable to unreliable, then past knowledge claims may need reassessment.**, **An epistemically reliable process must continue to be reliable across time and accounts for shifts in relevant circumstances affecting its reliability.**
