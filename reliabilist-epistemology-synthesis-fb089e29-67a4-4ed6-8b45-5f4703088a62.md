## Content

**A person knows a proposition if they have a true belief produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **Reliability of a process is defined by its tendency to produce true beliefs**, **Cognitive virtues are not necessary for knowledge but can enhance the reliability of a cognitive process**, **Knowledge can originate without the exercise of cognitive virtues if the belief arises from a generally reliable process**
