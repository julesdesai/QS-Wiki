## Content

**Knowledge is invariant justified true belief that incorporates the subject's stakes**, **The standards for justification do not vary with context but are sensitive to the stakes for the subject**, **For a person to know a proposition, it must be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, they must believe it**, **The required justification for knowledge takes into account the subject's practical interests**, **Justified beliefs meet standards that reflect the subject's circumstances without context variability**
