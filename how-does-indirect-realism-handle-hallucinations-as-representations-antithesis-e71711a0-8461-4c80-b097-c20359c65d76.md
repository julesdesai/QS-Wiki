# How does indirect realism handle hallucinations as representations?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e71711a0-8461-4c80-b097-c20359c65d76
**Depth:** 2
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Indirect Realism](indirect-realism-thesis-685db4d3-6670-4245-a0b7-dd17f1d7a4dc.md)

## Content

**Hallucinations are perceptions without corresponding external objects, such as seeing a nonexistent pink elephant.**, **If perception mediates knowledge, then hallucinations provide representations without reality.**, **The existence of inaccurate representations questions the reliability of perception in producing knowledge.**
