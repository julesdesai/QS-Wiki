## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief could not have easily been false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed in a safe manner**, **A belief is safe if it is true in most nearby possible worlds where the person uses the same reasoning to form the belief**
