# In what ways could an epistemic luck scenario affect the definition of knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 597a6fe1-a53f-4be6-8dde-3ca17a759952
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Phenomenal Conservatism: Seeming Justification](phenomenal-conservatism-seeming-justification-synthesis-de77a591-8397-48c7-98a8-42a761bdf13a.md)

## Content

**A person might stumble upon the truth through pure chance, as when finding an accurate answer on an exam by guessing. Though justified and true, such beliefs lack the control associated with knowledge, suggesting seeming justification allows for epistemic luck contrary to knowledge.**
