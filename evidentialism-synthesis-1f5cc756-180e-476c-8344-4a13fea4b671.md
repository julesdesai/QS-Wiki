## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **Justification for a belief consists in having strong supporting evidence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**
