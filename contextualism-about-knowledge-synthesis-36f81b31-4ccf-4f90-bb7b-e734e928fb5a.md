## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **The standards for knowing a proposition vary according to the context in which the claim to know is made**, **A person may know a proposition in one context while not knowing the same proposition in a different context**
