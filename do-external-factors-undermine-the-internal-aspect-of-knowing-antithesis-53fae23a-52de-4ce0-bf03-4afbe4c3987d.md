# Do external factors undermine the internal aspect of knowing?

## Content

**Imagine someone arrives at a true belief by chance, using a reliable process in an unreliable context.**, **The reliance on external factors that are beyond the person's understanding could mean we don't ascribe knowledge to them.**, **This suggests an internal understanding component may be essential alongside external reliability.**
