## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition coheres with a system of beliefs**, **A system of beliefs is coherent when it is logically consistent and systematically interconnected**
