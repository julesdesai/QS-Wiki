# Should subjective certainty negate sensitivity demands?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f749eb82-10de-4ed7-b34e-a949ff01abfd
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Nozick's Tracking Theory](nozicks-tracking-theory-synthesis-f1663159-56f4-4f6f-b53a-d60dc9dbfcb7.md)

## Content

**Contemplate a scenario in which an individual's absolute certainty and direct experience verify a simple physical fact. Intuitively, we might judge that subjective certainty in direct experience guarantees epistemic security irrespective of potential error imagination.**, **It is implausible to doubt such direct experience-derived knowledge by requiring hypothetical counterfactual sensitivity adjustments when visual certainty is robust.**
