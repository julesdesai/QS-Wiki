# Does safety require cognitive access to counterfactual scenarios?

**Depth:** 4

**Parent:** [Knowledge as Safety](knowledge-as-safety-synthesis-df26c783-c115-4102-a084-91df0b928704.md)

## Content

**Suppose it is necessary for someone to have cognitive access to scenarios where their belief could be false for them to know a proposition.**, **In such cases, a person may have a true belief in all nearby possible worlds but lack any cognitive access to those worlds, so intuitively, we judge that they lack knowledge.**
