# What ensures objectivity within a constructivist coherentist framework?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ad5771df-932c-4b93-81b2-72c050bc0021
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Constructivist Coherentism](constructivist-coherentism-synthesis-df66a247-3331-4fc3-9810-0103a6210363.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that objectivity in knowledge requires standards or constraints beyond individual subjective inputs. Without such standards, coherence can be manipulated to fit any subjective narrative.**, **Consider a scenario where multiple individuals construct coherent but radically different belief systems based on the same reality. In such a scenario, it is intuitively unsatisfactory to claim all systems equally reflect objective reality, indicating a lack of objective constraints.**
