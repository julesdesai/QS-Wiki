**Parent:** [Can changes in reliability affect the status of knowledge?](can-changes-in-reliability-affect-the-status-of-knowledge-antithesis-fa6b380e-c3db-4dc0-af0f-69cd2123c843.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is safe, meaning it could not have easily been false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is safe**
