# Knowledge as Safety

**Parent:** [Does intelligence alone not account sufficiently for knowledge?](does-intelligence-alone-not-account-sufficiently-for-knowledge-antithesis-bbcda4ea-888e-4f78-8c39-22e40ca32171.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that in nearly all nearby possible worlds where the person believes the proposition, it is true**, **Safety ensures that a belief is not only true but is very unlikely to be false given the conditions**
