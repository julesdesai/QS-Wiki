# Virtue Epistemology

**Node Type:** thesis
**Node ID:** 24d13f1e-1bc6-4601-84bc-769cf45ad303
**Depth:** 1
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [What is knowledge?](what-is-knowledge-question-308fcc82-4ce4-4be0-a8ca-588f405c6c60.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is a result of exercising intellectual virtue**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief results from exercising intellectual virtue**
