# Sensitivity-Based Virtue Epistemology

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 138fbfa2-2909-4d19-b3b4-d747194c89d9
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Should intellectual virtue always lead to truth?](should-intellectual-virtue-always-lead-to-truth-antithesis-2612182e-b45f-40cb-bba3-bebcd4434974.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the person to not believe the proposition if it were false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief to result from an intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, they would not believe if false, and the belief results from an intellectual virtue**
