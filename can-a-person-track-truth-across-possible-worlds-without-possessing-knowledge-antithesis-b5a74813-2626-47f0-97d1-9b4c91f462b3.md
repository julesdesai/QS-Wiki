# Can a person track truth across possible worlds without possessing knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b5a74813-2626-47f0-97d1-9b4c91f462b3
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Truth-Tracking Theory](truth-tracking-theory-synthesis-c14e1fed-7ecf-480a-90a0-7623f2270956.md)

## Content

**An individual might have a belief that changes in response to truth in various possible worlds, but it is still derived from unreliable methods. Intuitively, we would judge that such a belief does not count as knowledge.**, **A belief derived through unreliable means can track truth without amounting to knowledge.**
