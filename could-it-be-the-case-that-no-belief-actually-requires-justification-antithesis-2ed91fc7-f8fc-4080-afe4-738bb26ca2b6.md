# Could it be the case that no belief actually requires justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2ed91fc7-f8fc-4080-afe4-738bb26ca2b6
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism in Epistemology](infinitism-in-epistemology-synthesis-3dfd51b6-00d8-4b2a-a651-5af96def07b3.md)

## Content

**Consider a belief such as "The sun is shining" based purely on direct perception, which feels justified without needing further reasons.**, **Intuitively, many people seem to operate with justified beliefs without consciously engaging in an infinite justificatory process.**, **Claiming that every belief requires an infinite regress of reasons seems to conflict with everyday epistemic practices.**
