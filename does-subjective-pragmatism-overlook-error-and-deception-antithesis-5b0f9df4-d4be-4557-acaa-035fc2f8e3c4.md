# Does subjective pragmatism overlook error and deception?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5b0f9df4-d4be-4557-acaa-035fc2f8e3c4
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Subjective Pragmatism](subjective-pragmatism-synthesis-04efa8d0-e373-4741-b290-e8305183c9b5.md)

## Content

**It is implausible to ignore the roles of error and deception if knowledge is based on subjective belief utility.**, **Consider a situation where someone believes in a lie because it benefits them; viewing this as knowledge overlooks the deceptive origin of the belief.**
