# Is the truth of propositions in nearby possible worlds a sufficient condition for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c2238933-9cef-43f7-9803-cdfe2be90496
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Safety-based Account of Knowledge](safety-based-account-of-knowledge-synthesis-4053710d-87a3-4da5-a24a-d38f32fb1dbb.md)

## Content

**It is possible for a person to believe a true proposition in most nearby possible worlds, yet fail to know it because their method is unreliable.**, **Methods that lead to a true belief in nearby worlds can still involve processes that are epistemically flawed.**
