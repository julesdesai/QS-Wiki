## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **A belief is sensitive if the person would not hold it under contrary circumstances**
