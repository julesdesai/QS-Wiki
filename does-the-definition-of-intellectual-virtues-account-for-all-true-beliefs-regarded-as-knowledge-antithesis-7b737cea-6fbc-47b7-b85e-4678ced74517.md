# Does the definition of intellectual virtues account for all true beliefs regarded as knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7b737cea-6fbc-47b7-b85e-4678ced74517

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-72984f03-1ce3-46fd-9fd6-13203e340ace.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which an individual, without relying on intellectual virtues, still manages to form true beliefs consistently. Intuitively, we might still regard this as knowledge despite the absence of intellectual virtues.**, **The definition of intellectual virtues potentially excludes some true beliefs that should intuitively be considered knowledge.**
