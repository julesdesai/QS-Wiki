# How does reliabilism handle knowledge from communal processes?

## Content

**Knowledge derived from social networks or communal cognitive processes may be reliable without the individual having any insight or understanding of the process.**, **This scenario challenges the notion that an individual can possess knowledge if they lack understanding or insight, even with reliable processes.**
