## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is either formed through the exercise of intellectual virtue or justified by reliable cognitive processes**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is either formed through the exercise of intellectual virtue or justified by reliable cognitive processes**
