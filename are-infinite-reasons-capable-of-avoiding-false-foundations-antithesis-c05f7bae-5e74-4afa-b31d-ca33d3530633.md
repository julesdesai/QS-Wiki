# Are infinite reasons capable of avoiding false foundations?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c05f7bae-5e74-4afa-b31d-ca33d3530633
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism in Epistemic Justification](infinitism-in-epistemic-justification-synthesis-7e8bc411-aa66-4d88-bdc9-2fb4f4857304.md)

## Content

**Even an infinite chain of reasons does not inherently avoid false starting points if there is no mechanism ensuring the truth of each reason.**, **Without a basis for verifying each link in the chain, infinite reasons could lead to false beliefs as readily as finite or foundational ones.**
