## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in the nearest possible world where the proposition is false, they do not believe it**, **Sensitivity of belief ensures that a false belief would not occur in a near possible world**
