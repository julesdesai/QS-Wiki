# Knowledge as Reliably Justified True Belief

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Does truth derived from reliable processes lead to accidental knowledge?](does-truth-derived-from-reliable-processes-lead-to-accidental-knowledge-antithesis-dc014bcf-91f6-490d-af8e-cc51a09d1513.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that it is a justified true belief formed via a reliable process**, **The reliability of the process contributes to the justification of the belief**
