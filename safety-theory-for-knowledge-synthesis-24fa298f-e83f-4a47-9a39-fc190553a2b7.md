## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief in the proposition would not have been held if the proposition were false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is true and safe from error in relevant nearby possible worlds**
