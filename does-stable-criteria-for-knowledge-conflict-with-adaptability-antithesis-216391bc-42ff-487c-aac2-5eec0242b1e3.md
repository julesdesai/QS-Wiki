# Does stable criteria for knowledge conflict with adaptability?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 216391bc-42ff-487c-aac2-5eec0242b1e3
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Invariantism for Knowledge Assessment](invariantism-for-knowledge-assessment-synthesis-0fb1590d-9ce0-4644-a80f-68ae37bc3da5.md)

## Content

**Consider a situation where new scientific evidence about climate change causes a shift in what is considered justified belief. Intuitively, we would expect the standards of knowledge to adapt to new information.**, **It is implausible that the standards for knowing a proposition remain constant if what counts as justified belief can change with the discovery of new evidence.**
