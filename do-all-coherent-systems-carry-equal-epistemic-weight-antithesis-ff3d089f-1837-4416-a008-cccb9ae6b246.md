# Do all coherent systems carry equal epistemic weight?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ff3d089f-1837-4416-a008-cccb9ae6b246
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism Summary](coherentism-summary-synthesis-7bdcae0a-f633-4c38-9857-77d6ba49435c.md)

## Content

**Imagine two equally coherent belief systems, one rooted in scientific evidence and another in mythological narratives. Intuitively, we would favor the scientifically rooted system due to its empirical connections. It is implausible to treat all coherent systems as carrying equal epistemic weight without considering external justification factors.**
