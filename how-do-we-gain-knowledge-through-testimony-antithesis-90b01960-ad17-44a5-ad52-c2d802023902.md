# How do we gain knowledge through testimony?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 90b01960-ad17-44a5-ad52-c2d802023902
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Conceptual Empiricism](conceptual-empiricism-synthesis-7b58485d-3ea5-4513-ba7e-4d4d19b630bf.md)

## Content

**Testimony allows a person to know information without first-hand perceptual experiences.**, **Knowledge obtained through testimony may not depend on concepts derived directly from personal perceptual experiences.**, **Facts learned through testimony can be known without applying concepts exclusively generated by one's own perceptual experiences.**
