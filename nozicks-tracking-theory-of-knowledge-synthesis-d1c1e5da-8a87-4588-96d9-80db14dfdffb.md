## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the person to believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is true, the person believes the proposition, and the person's belief tracks the truth across possible worlds**
