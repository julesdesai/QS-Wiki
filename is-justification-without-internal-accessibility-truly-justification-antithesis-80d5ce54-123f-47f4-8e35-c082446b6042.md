# Is justification without internal accessibility truly justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 80d5ce54-123f-47f4-8e35-c082446b6042
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Moderate Externalism Summary](moderate-externalism-summary-synthesis-b4668bae-162e-4191-89bc-89b702818630.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where the only justification for a belief is an external process the person cannot perceive or understand. Intuitively, we would judge that such justification is inadequate to attribute knowledge to the person.**
