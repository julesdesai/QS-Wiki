## Content

**A person knows a proposition if the belief is formed through a process that is both reliable and safe**, **A reliable process is one that produces a higher proportion of true beliefs than false ones in relevant situations**, **A process is safe if it would not easily produce a false belief in nearby possible situations**, **A person knows a proposition if they believe the proposition**, **A person knows a proposition if the proposition is true**
