# Knowledge as True Belief with Reliability

**Parent:** [Can a lucky guess be considered knowledge under justified true belief?](can-a-lucky-guess-be-considered-knowledge-under-justified-true-belief-antithesis-0e25c4d3-798d-4bf1-a77b-4cff1c2136f3.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief to be justified**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is justified, and it is produced by a reliable process**
