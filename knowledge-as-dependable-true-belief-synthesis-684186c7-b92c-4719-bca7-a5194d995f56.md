# Knowledge as Dependable True Belief

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 684186c7-b92c-4719-bca7-a5194d995f56
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can beliefs formed under external manipulation still be considered knowledge?](can-beliefs-formed-under-external-manipulation-still-be-considered-knowledge-antithesis-11a082fc-9707-4c83-b316-a032b44d0ee7.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed through a reliable method**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is formed through a reliable method**
