# Is the Gettier problem a challenge for causal theories of knowledge?

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person has a justified true belief because they have reliable testimony but the testimony is accidentally true. Intuitively, we would judge that this is not knowledge.**, **Having a justified true belief that happens to be true by accident does not meet the traditional conception of knowledge.**
