# Can knowledge exist with truth as coincidence?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a86ecd19-779a-4b15-a486-ca637502acaf
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Refined Virtue Epistemology](refined-virtue-epistemology-synthesis-ed8e6ab8-d432-4955-9aec-982a4b148be7.md)

## Content

**A case in which some person believes a true proposition solely because of a fortunate coincidence without any intellectual virtue is not a case of knowledge.**, **Knowledge is true belief arising from intellectual virtue, and when truth is merely coincidental, the requirement of intellectual virtue is not fulfilled.**
