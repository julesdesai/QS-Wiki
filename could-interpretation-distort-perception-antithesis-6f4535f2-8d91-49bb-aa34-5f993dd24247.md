# Could interpretation distort perception?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 6f4535f2-8d91-49bb-aa34-5f993dd24247
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Critical Realism](critical-realism-synthesis-04d59a43-df5d-4ff1-bc30-b363f66f9a03.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person believes a distant object is a dog because they want it to be so, despite visual evidence suggesting otherwise. In this scenario, intuitively, personal interpretation distorts the factual perception of the object as a small rock.**, **It is possible that interpretation introduces biases, thereby leading to incorrect accounts of perceptual information.**
