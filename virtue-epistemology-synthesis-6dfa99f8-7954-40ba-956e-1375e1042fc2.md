## Content

**Knowledge is a true belief that arises from intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **A belief formed through an exercise of intellectual virtue is sufficient for the belief to count as knowledge**
