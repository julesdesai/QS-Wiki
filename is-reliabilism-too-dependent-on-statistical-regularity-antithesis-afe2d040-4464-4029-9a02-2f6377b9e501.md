# Is Reliabilism too dependent on statistical regularity?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** afe2d040-4464-4029-9a02-2f6377b9e501
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism](reliabilism-synthesis-e69ee86a-1c0a-4d0e-840e-f25c67fd8e9a.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that knowledge requires more than just statistical chances; it requires a connection between the truth of a belief and the reasons for which it was formed. This principle concerns the relationship between belief formation and truth beyond statistical regularity.**, **A belief formed based on statistical regularity may lack a meaningful connection to truth in cases where statistical regularity accidentally aligns with true belief.**
