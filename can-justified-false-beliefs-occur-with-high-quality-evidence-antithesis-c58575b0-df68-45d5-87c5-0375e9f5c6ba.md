# Can justified false beliefs occur with high-quality evidence?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c58575b0-df68-45d5-87c5-0375e9f5c6ba
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Evidentialist Theory of Knowledge](evidentialist-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-79c62cab-dbc0-4dae-ad91-54d5bcae1188.md)

## Content

**A person studies misleading but high-quality scientific studies and forms a false belief about a causal relation. Intuitively, we might judge the belief justified but not true.**, **The occurrence of justified false beliefs due to misleading yet quality evidence challenges the idea that high-quality evidence always leads to truth.**
