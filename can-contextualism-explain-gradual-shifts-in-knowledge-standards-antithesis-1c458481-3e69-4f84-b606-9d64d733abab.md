# Can contextualism explain gradual shifts in knowledge standards?

**Parent:** [Knowledge as Contextualism](knowledge-as-contextualism-synthesis-b56b56df-6d59-4c6a-8de4-4cbefdc8c784.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that knowledge standards evolve slowly and organically, not in sudden contextual shifts. This principle, together with contextualism, suggests that contextualism may not accurately reflect how knowledge standards change over time.**
