# Is fallibility compatible with knowledge as a stable belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** fc92ab15-62f1-4a88-bc0b-0d8639406530
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Fallibilist Coherentism](fallibilist-coherentism-synthesis-f085a715-bc4b-46dd-bc93-6874997728cd.md)

## Content

**Consider a knowledge claim that is weak or unstable due to potential revisions based on internal coherence changes.**, **If knowledge is seen as inherently subject to frequent revision, it conflicts with the intuition that knowledge should be a stable belief about the world.**
