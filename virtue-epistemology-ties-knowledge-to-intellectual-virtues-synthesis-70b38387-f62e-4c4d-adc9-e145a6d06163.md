## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief results from exercising intellectual virtues**, **Intellectual virtues include skills or faculties like perception, memory, and reasoning**
