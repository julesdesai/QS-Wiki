# Is depending on contextual cues circular in justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a9e230e3-aa78-471f-b71a-79f33f645375
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism about Justification](contextualism-about-justification-synthesis-1dd27965-c99f-4b62-ae25-50f61d9bceba.md)

## Content

**A case involves a belief justified by contextual cues, where these cues are only considered reliable because of pre-existing beliefs.**, **There is an issue of circular reasoning if justification requires belief systems that are themselves justified only by the initial belief in the reliability of those contexts.**
