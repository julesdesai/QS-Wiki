# Knowledge as Belief with Reliable Indicator

**Parent:** [Can intellectual virtue account for animal knowledge?](can-intellectual-virtue-account-for-animal-knowledge-antithesis-430fff4a-8190-4a9f-b6f8-e370ac0571e5.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed through a method that is a reliable indicator of truth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is formed through a reliable method which indicates truth**
