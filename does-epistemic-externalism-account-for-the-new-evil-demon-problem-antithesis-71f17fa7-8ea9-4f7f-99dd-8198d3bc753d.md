# Does epistemic externalism account for the new evil demon problem?

**Parent:** [Epistemic Externalism with Nozick's Tracking Theory](epistemic-externalism-with-nozicks-tracking-theory-synthesis-99a4169f-6bac-4b5a-b46b-52fe3916b903.md)

## Content

**Consider a person in a demon world who has the same experiences and beliefs as a person in the real world. Intuitively, the person in the demon world lacks knowledge despite justified beliefs.**, **A belief tracks the truth if it would not be held if it were false, and would be held if it were true.**, **The new evil demon problem questions the reliability aspect of tracking theory, as both persons believe they have knowledge, yet the beliefs do not track the truth in the demon world.**
