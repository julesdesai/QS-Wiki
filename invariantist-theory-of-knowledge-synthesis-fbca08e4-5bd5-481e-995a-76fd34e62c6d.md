## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have sufficient justification for believing the proposition**, **The standards for knowledge remain constant across different contexts**
