**Parent:** [Can contextually foundational justifications accommodate all types of knowledge?](can-contextually-foundational-justifications-accommodate-all-types-of-knowledge-antithesis-fb63a6fc-8341-4ec0-b350-9003ab45779f.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief rests on justifications from multiple sources, tuned to the context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief rests on justifications from multiple sources, tuned to the context**
