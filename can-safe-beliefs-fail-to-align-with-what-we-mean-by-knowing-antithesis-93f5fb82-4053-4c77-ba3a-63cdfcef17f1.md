# Can safe beliefs fail to align with what we mean by 'knowing'?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 93f5fb82-4053-4c77-ba3a-63cdfcef17f1
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Knowledge as Safety](knowledge-as-safety-synthesis-df26c783-c115-4102-a084-91df0b928704.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where someone forms a true belief through sheer luck, but in a way that no nearby possible worlds contain instances where the belief would be false, thus making it safely held.**, **Intuitively, such a scenario would not be one in which we say that the person knows the proposition, due to the element of luck.**
