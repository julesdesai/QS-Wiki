# Knowledge as Safety-First Theory

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 10a8c97d-a56c-449f-adf2-4636062c4c5a
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can false beliefs ever track truth?](can-false-beliefs-ever-track-truth-antithesis-c664d870-cd01-4ed9-84bc-a093fe69399a.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in nearby possible worlds where the person believes the proposition, the proposition is not false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the person believes the proposition, it is true, and it is safe from error in nearby possible worlds**
