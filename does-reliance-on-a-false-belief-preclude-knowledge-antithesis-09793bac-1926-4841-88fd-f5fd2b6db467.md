# Does reliance on a false belief preclude knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 09793bac-1926-4841-88fd-f5fd2b6db467
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Moderate Contextualism](moderate-contextualism-synthesis-623e938c-454c-4025-b6c6-9053aea58f91.md)

## Content

**Suppose an individual's belief in a proposition relies on another belief that is false.**, **Intuitively, we would judge that this dependency on a false belief means the individual does not know the proposition.**
