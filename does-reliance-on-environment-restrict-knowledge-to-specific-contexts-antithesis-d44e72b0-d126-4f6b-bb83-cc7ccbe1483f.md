# Does reliance on environment restrict knowledge to specific contexts?

**Node Type:** antithesis

**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Process Reliabilism with Environment-Sensitive Reliability](process-reliabilism-with-environment-sensitive-reliability-synthesis-cdd770c1-1646-4221-a17f-d7d94aac715e.md)

## Content

**Consider a principle suggesting that knowledge should be universally applicable rather than constrained by specific environmental contexts.**, **If environment-based reliability restricts a belief’s justification to its formation context, then this principle of universality is challenged.**, **It appears implausible that reliable justification bound to an environment supports the idea of universally applicable knowledge.**
