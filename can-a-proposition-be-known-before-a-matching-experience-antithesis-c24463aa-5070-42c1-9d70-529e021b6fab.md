# Can a proposition be known before a matching experience?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c24463aa-5070-42c1-9d70-529e021b6fab
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Disjunctivism Summary](disjunctivism-summary-synthesis-e9744a1e-a377-4989-9c30-b08459f11a98.md)

## Content

**Suppose someone logically deduces a proposition through reasoning and later has a perceptual experience matching that proposition. Intuitively, we would say they knew the proposition before the matching experience occurred.**, **Knowledge pre-existing a perceptual experience challenges the claim that veridical experiences exclusively generate knowledge of propositions.**
