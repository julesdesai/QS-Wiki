# Are all true beliefs inevitably useful?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b85c87a0-a1d7-4b08-853e-80fd6642aefa
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Theory of Knowledge](pragmatic-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-af5a1410-77c2-4ceb-b390-2ced8876b488.md)

## Content

**Consider someone who discovers a true fact about a distant galaxy that has no practical application to life on Earth. Intuitively, while the belief is true, it does not need to serve a practical purpose to be considered knowledge.**, **Some true beliefs are valued for their truth alone, not for any practical use they may provide.**
