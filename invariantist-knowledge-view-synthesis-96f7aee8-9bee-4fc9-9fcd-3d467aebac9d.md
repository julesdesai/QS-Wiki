## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **The standards for justification and belief are invariant, not depending on conversational context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for the proposition**
