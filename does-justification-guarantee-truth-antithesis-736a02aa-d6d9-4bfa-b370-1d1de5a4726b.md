# Does justification guarantee truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 736a02aa-d6d9-4bfa-b370-1d1de5a4726b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Evidentialism Summary](evidentialism-summary-synthesis-dea02623-290c-44c3-896b-2f967081eb1b.md)

## Content

**A justified belief can, in principle, still be false if the evidence upon which it is based is incorrect or incomplete, demonstrating that justification does not ensure truth.**, **There exists a plausible principle that while justification is aimed at truth, it does not infallibly achieve it, as evidenced by historically justified yet ultimately false scientific theories.**
