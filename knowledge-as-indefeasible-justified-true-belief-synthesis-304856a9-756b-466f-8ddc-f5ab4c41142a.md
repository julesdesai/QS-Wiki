# Knowledge as Indefeasible Justified True Belief

**Parent:** [Does the problem of epistemic luck challenge the sufficiency of justified true belief for knowledge?](does-the-problem-of-epistemic-luck-challenge-the-sufficiency-of-justified-true-belief-for-knowledge-antithesis-3cbbcb41-d004-4770-9f9c-3442a2dfdd58.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification is indefeasible**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have indefeasible justification for believing the proposition, and the proposition is true**
