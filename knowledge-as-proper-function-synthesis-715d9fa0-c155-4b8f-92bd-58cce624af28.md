# Knowledge as Proper Function

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is the result of properly functioning cognitive faculties in a congenial epistemic environment**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is produced by cognitive faculties functioning properly in a suitable environment**
