# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with No False Lemmas

**Parent:** [Does pragmatic usefulness make some false beliefs seem more like knowledge?](does-pragmatic-usefulness-make-some-false-beliefs-seem-more-like-knowledge-antithesis-92ca14d4-3c8d-4dcb-9859-e35866344840.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **The justification must not rely on any false assumptions or lemmas for the belief to constitute knowledge**
