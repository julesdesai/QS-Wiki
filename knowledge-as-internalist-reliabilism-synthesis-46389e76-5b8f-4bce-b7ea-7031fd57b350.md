## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed by a process reliably producing true beliefs and the person internally accesses its reliability**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is formed by a process that reliably produces true beliefs, and the person internally accesses the reliability of that process**
