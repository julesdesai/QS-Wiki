## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they would not believe the proposition if it were false**, **Intellectual virtues enhance sensitivity to truth, where sensitivity is the capacity to form true beliefs only when those beliefs are true**
