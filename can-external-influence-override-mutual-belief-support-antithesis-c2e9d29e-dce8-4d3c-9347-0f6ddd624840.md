# Can external influence override mutual belief support?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c2e9d29e-dce8-4d3c-9347-0f6ddd624840
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism Revised](coherentism-revised-synthesis-de283b0a-5fd8-4f4c-bf89-d22a34799ca8.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where scientific evidence contradicts a community's mutually supported belief about the efficacy of a traditional medicine. Intuitively, we would judge that the scientific evidence overrides the community's internal coherence of beliefs.**, **There is a plausible principle that external evidence can undermine internally coherent systems of belief, implying that mutual support is not decisive for justification.**
