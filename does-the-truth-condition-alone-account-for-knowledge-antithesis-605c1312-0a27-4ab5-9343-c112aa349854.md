# Does the truth condition alone account for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 605c1312-0a27-4ab5-9343-c112aa349854
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalism with Reliabilism](externalism-with-reliabilism-synthesis-c10e5c74-1dbc-479d-ba5e-469176c5d537.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that merely holding true beliefs constitutes knowledge.**, **Consider a person who randomly asserts a true fact without any underlying reason or reliable method for their assertion. Intuitively, we would not judge this person as knowing that fact.**
