# Knowledge as Safety and No-Luck Externalism

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is safety about beliefs context-sensitive?](is-safety-about-beliefs-context-sensitive-antithesis-dfc1a1a1-36ad-429f-b80c-4e14b4e208d2.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief would not have easily been false in similar situations**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the truth of the belief is not due to luck**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief would not have easily been false in similar situations, and the truth of the belief is not due to luck**
