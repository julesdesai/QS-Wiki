# How Does Reliabilism Handle "Truetemp" Cases?

## Content

**Imagine a person, unbeknownst to them, with an implanted reliable temperature sensor in their brain provides them with accurate temperature readings. Intuitively, we might not say they have knowledge if they have no awareness of the process.**, **Awareness and understanding of belief formation seem relevant to knowledge, which reliabilism does not explicitly require.**
