# Are intellectual virtues sufficient for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3075b182-b8b1-42cd-896c-e75181a29099
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-d7a894af-29e5-42fe-8e5e-957e2009566d.md)

## Content

**Suppose someone with exceptional analytical skills misinterprets evidence due to unforeseen cognitive biases, yet remains intellectually honest and diligent.**, **Despite possessing several intellectual virtues, their biases cause them to arrive at false conclusions, indicating that virtue alone may not be sufficient for true beliefs.**
