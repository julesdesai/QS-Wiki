# Are cases of environmental luck problematic for the Safety Condition?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d0cffa7a-3341-4cae-ad89-7e880befce61
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Safety Condition View](safety-condition-view-synthesis-739cc2e2-5111-4640-9af6-f9324f258620.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where a person accurately identifies a specific bird species based on expert knowledge, but unknown to them, there are life-like robotic replicas indistinguishable from actual birds. Intuitively, the person knows the bird species.**, **In nearby worlds where the person encounters a robotic replica instead, their belief would be false, suggesting the knowledge claim is unsafe due to environmental luck.**
