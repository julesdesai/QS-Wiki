# Nozickian Tracking Theory

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Does a true belief obtained through guesswork constitute knowledge?](does-a-true-belief-obtained-through-guesswork-constitute-knowledge-antithesis-9e09e7db-e03b-494a-b2e5-952f33de0026.md)

## Content

**A belief constitutes knowledge if it tracks the truth across possible worlds**, **A belief tracks the truth if, in the closest possible world where the proposition is true, the person believes it**, **A belief tracks the truth if, in the closest possible world where the proposition is false, the person does not believe it**, **Knowledge requires the belief to be sensitive to the truth of the proposition**
