# Could evolutionary biology undermine the claim we cannot know things as they are in themselves?

**Node ID:** 2496a628-5764-4a2a-ad1d-579a2587336d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

## Content

**Evolutionary success may equip organisms with cognitive mechanisms reliably tracking mind-independent truths.**, **If cognitive faculties evolved to maintain fitness through true beliefs about reality, this implies potential access to things in themselves.**, **An organism's survival suggests adequate interaction with the environment, challenging the notion that our knowledge is only of phenomena.**
