# Can emotional states interfere with cognitive task fulfillment and knowledge?

## Content

**Imagine a person is so anxious that it impairs their judgment in a task, leading them to an incorrect conclusion. Intuitively, this flawed task performance does not amount to knowledge.**, **The principle that emotional and psychological factors can undermine knowledge suggests that successful task fulfillment alone does not guarantee knowing.**
