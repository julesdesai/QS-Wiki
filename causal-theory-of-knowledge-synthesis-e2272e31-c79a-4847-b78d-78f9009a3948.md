## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is an appropriate causal connection between the belief and the fact**, **An appropriate causal connection is one that ensures the belief is produced by the fact or a reliable indicator of the fact**
