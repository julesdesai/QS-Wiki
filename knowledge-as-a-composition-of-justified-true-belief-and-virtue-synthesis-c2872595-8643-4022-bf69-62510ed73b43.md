# Knowledge as a Composition of Justified True Belief and Virtue

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** c2872595-8643-4022-bf69-62510ed73b43
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is it possible to have knowledge without truth?](is-it-possible-to-have-knowledge-without-truth-antithesis-6a810cf3-ecac-43a4-8a2a-61e8021151b3.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is both justified true belief and formed through intellectual virtues**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is both justified true belief and formed through intellectual virtues**
