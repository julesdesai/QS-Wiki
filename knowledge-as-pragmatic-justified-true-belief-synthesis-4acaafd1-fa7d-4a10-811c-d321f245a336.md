# Knowledge as Pragmatic Justified True Belief

**Parent:** [Do causal theories neglect the role of coherent systems?](do-causal-theories-neglect-the-role-of-coherent-systems-antithesis-a3456e70-2651-4704-bb91-6d5898b75a96.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief serves a pragmatic purpose that benefits the individual's actions or decisions**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification that serves a pragmatic purpose, and the proposition is true**
