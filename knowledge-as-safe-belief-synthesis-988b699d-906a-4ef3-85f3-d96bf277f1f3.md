# Knowledge as Safe Belief

**Parent:** [Can justified true belief from a source other than an intellectual virtue count as knowledge?](can-justified-true-belief-from-a-source-other-than-an-intellectual-virtue-count-as-knowledge-antithesis-0f45e3ca-1c35-4ff7-b420-42011ecd26b4.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is safe, meaning it could not easily have been false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is safe**
