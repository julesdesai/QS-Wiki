# How do issues with justification arise in relation to usefulness?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 232db609-6c8a-419f-9cca-30bf6df4274b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatism Summary](pragmatism-summary-synthesis-ddc2fd27-140a-48fe-b697-40a3c8c11bfc.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a belief is justified because it is widely shared, not because it's true. Intuitively, this social justification does not equate to knowledge if the belief is false.**, **Justification based on social acceptance or utility, without truth, can lead to the acceptance of false beliefs, which questions its adequacy for knowledge.**
