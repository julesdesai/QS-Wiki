# Can one fulfill a cognitive task yet lack knowledge due to environmental luck?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1f00d891-3018-4005-97c9-d6d75b48f1e9
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Epistemic Functionalism with Cognitive Task Fulfillment](epistemic-functionalism-with-cognitive-task-fulfillment-synthesis-3edd3165-e9fd-4e05-81df-6b63dbbb89eb.md)

## Content

**Imagine a situation where a person has a perfectly reliable cognitive process for identifying bird species, but they are in an environment with many realistic bird replicas. In such a scenario, even with task fulfillment, it appears the person might only get lucky in identifying real birds.**, **It seems implausible to claim that fulfilling the task in this unreliable environment results in knowledge.**
