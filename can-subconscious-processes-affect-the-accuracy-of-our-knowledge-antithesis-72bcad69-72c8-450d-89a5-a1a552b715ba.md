# Can subconscious processes affect the accuracy of our knowledge?

## Content

**Consider a case where a person has an unconscious bias that influences their perception of a situation. Intuitively, such a bias could lead to a misinterpretation of causal structures.**, **The presence of subconscious biases affecting perception suggests that knowledge may not always accurately reflect the external causal structures.**
