# Can justified true belief accommodate unknown defeaters?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** cd370541-2875-47ff-9d58-4874a0af992d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Knowledge as Justified True Belief](knowledge-as-justified-true-belief-synthesis-c9e46e7c-10b9-4866-9d14-1a29ea4ef1b4.md)

## Content

**Imagine a person holds a justified true belief about the location of their friend in a park based on seeing them there ten minutes ago**, **If unbeknownst to the person, their friend was transported elsewhere but managed to return in the meantime creating a mistaken justification, the belief remains true and justified**, **Despite having a justified true belief, the presence of an unknown defeater intuitively undermines the claim to knowledge**
