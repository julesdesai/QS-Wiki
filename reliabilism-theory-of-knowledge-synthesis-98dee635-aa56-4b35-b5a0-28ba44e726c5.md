## Content

**A person knows a proposition if their belief is the result of a reliable process**, **A reliable process is one that produces true beliefs more often than not**, **Knowledge does not require defeating counter-evidence, only that the belief-forming process is reliable**, **Counter-evidence that results from an unreliable process does not affect the status of knowledge**
