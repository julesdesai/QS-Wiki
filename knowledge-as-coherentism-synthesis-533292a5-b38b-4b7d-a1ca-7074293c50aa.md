## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief coheres with a comprehensive and consistent system of beliefs**, **Coherence requires that the belief logically integrates with the rest of the person's beliefs in a rational network**
