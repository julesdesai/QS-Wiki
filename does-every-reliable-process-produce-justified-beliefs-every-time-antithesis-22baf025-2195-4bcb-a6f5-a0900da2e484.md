# Does every reliable process produce justified beliefs every time?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 22baf025-2195-4bcb-a6f5-a0900da2e484
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilist Coherentism](reliabilist-coherentism-synthesis-d6c1c642-66ac-40b0-82ca-187cf7250017.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a highly reliable process occasionally produces an unjustified belief due to extraordinary circumstances. Intuitively, the reliability of the process does not guarantee that every belief it generates is justified.**, **It is implausible that a process can produce justified beliefs every time if occasional beliefs are intuitively unjustified.**
