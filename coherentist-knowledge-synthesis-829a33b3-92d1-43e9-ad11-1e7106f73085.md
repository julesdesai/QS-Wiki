## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification of their belief is provided by coherence among a web of beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is justified by coherence with other beliefs**
