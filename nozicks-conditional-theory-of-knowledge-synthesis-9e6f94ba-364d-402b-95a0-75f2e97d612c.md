## Content

**Knowledge results from the satisfaction of conditional relations to the truth**, **A person knows a proposition if, in nearby possible worlds where the proposition is false, they would not believe it**, **Knowledge requires a belief that tracks the truth across possible scenarios**, **A person does not need direct causal linkage to the truth for knowledge**
