## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed in an apt manner and supported by reliable meta-cognitive awareness**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is apt, and their meta-cognitive processes reliably endorse the belief**
