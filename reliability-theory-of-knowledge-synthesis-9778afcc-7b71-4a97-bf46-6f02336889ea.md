## Content

**Knowledge is a belief formed by a reliable process**, **A reliable process is one that produces a high ratio of true beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, their belief in the proposition must result from a reliable process**, **It is not necessary that beliefs track truth across possible worlds for knowledge**
