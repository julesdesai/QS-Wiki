## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the cognitive faculties of the person are functioning properly in a suitable environment**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the design plan of the person's cognitive faculties is aimed at truth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, their cognitive faculties are functioning properly in a suitable environment, and their cognitive faculties are aimed at truth**
