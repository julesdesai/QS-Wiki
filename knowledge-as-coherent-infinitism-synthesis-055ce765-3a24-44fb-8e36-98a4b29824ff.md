## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justifications for their belief form a coherent system that could be extended infinitely**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justifications for their belief form a coherent system that could be extended infinitely**
