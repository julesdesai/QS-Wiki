# Knowledge as Justified True Belief Without Gettier Problems

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their justified true belief is not subject to Gettier-type counterexamples**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have a justified true belief that is not subject to Gettier-type counterexamples**
