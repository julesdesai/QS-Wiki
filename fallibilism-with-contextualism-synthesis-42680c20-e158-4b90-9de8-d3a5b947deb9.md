## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A person's knowledge claim is determined by the context of the inquiry**, **Knowledge does not require the absence of all possible defeaters, only those relevant within a given context**
