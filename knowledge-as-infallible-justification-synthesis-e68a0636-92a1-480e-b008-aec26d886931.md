## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have infallible justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **Infallible justification is such that, if a person has it, they cannot be wrong about the proposition**
