**Knowledge is a true belief justified by an infinite chain of reasons**, **A person knows a proposition if they can, in principle, provide an infinite series of non-repeating reasons for the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**
