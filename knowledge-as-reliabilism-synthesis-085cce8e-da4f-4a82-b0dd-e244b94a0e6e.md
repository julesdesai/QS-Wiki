# Knowledge as Reliabilism

**Parent:** [Can sensitivity account for knowledge of necessary truths?](can-sensitivity-account-for-knowledge-of-necessary-truths-antithesis-5bbe5b5e-eef8-436c-90e5-a480dffc0a55.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief-forming process is reliable**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is formed through a reliable process**
