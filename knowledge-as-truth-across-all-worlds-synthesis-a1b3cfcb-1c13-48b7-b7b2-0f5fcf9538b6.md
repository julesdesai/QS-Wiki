# Knowledge as Truth Across All Worlds

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** a1b3cfcb-1c13-48b7-b7b2-0f5fcf9538b6
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is it possible for someone to fulfill the conditions for knowledge and yet not actually know?](is-it-possible-for-someone-to-fulfill-the-conditions-for-knowledge-and-yet-not-actually-know-antithesis-96f1017a-e52c-4edc-8d6a-d113800e4d85.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, and the proposition is true in all possible worlds**
