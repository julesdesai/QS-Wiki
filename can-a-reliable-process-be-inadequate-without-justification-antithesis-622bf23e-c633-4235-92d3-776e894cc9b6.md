# Can a reliable process be inadequate without justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 622bf23e-c633-4235-92d3-776e894cc9b6
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalism to knowledge with reliable cognitive process](externalism-to-knowledge-with-reliable-cognitive-process-synthesis-e78416a8-ed0b-42d6-b63b-0cc8fec7b97b.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person has beliefs formed through a reliable cognitive process but they cannot access any justification for why the process is reliable.**, **Even if the process is reliable, intuitively we might argue that the person lacks knowledge because they cannot justify their belief.**, **This points toward a potential role for justification in knowledge that goes beyond mere reliabilism.**
