## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in nearly all nearby possible worlds in which the person believes the proposition, the proposition is true**, **A belief is safe if it is true in most nearby possible worlds where it is held**
