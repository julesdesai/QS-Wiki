# Must coherence involve connection to empirical reality?

**Node ID:** 58648236-f221-4cc7-a48c-f38b9b339717
**Depth:** 4

**Parent:** [Hybrid Coherentist-Internalist View](hybrid-coherentist-internalist-view-synthesis-ffa44d27-7377-4f6d-8451-515975b92108.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that coherence should involve some connection to empirical reality. This principle together with the view that coherence alone is enough for justification raises doubts about beliefs disconnected from reality.**, **A coherent belief system that lacks any empirical grounding lacks meaningful justification.**
