# Knowledge as Nozick's Tracking Theory

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 3f488e3a-1ed5-41ce-ac57-fbcef6bfbe6b
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is justified belief infallible?](is-justified-belief-infallible-antithesis-1bc11ed6-8712-46c2-a339-490029245ddf.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A person knows a proposition if and only if, in relevant nearby possible worlds, if the proposition were false, they would not believe it**, **A person knows a proposition if and only if, in relevant nearby possible worlds, if the proposition were true, they would believe it**
