# How does proper functionalism handle knowledge through testimony?

**Parent:** [Proper Functionalism](proper-functionalism-synthesis-56ac2890-cc65-4132-90ba-c28a7f97b59c.md)

## Content

**A person can acquire knowledge through testimony without directly relying on their cognitive faculties to assess truth.**, **Knowledge derived from testimony challenges whether proper functioning of faculties is truly required for all forms of knowledge.**, **In testimony cases, the trust in another's proper functioning takes precedence over individual cognitive assessment.**
