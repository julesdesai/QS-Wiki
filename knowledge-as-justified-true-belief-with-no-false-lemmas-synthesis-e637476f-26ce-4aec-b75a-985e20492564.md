# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with No False Lemmas

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Does sensitivity appropriately address everyday epistemic practices?](does-sensitivity-appropriately-address-everyday-epistemic-practices-antithesis-7d73e869-00ca-4845-9598-d20b5f0081ff.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is not based on any false lemmas**
