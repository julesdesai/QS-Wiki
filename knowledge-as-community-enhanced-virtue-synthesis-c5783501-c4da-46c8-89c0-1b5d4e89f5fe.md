## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed through intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is validated by the epistemic standards of the relevant community**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is formed through intellectual virtue, and the belief is validated by the epistemic standards of the relevant community**
