# Does direct perception guarantee knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ab3dd813-6313-4d9f-91e3-db54b1fbe41b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Naïve Realism Summary](naïve-realism-summary-synthesis-ad05d8f4-c9d2-4024-8001-bf520360cf02.md)

## Content

**People sometimes perceive the world directly yet form false beliefs about what they perceive, such as mistaking a rope for a snake.**, **Knowing a proposition would require not just perception but also correct interpretation of what is perceived.**, **If knowledge results solely from direct perception, then direct perception should not lead to false beliefs or misinterpretations.**
