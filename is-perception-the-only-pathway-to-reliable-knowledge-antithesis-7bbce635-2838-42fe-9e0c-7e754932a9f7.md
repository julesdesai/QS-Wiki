# Is perception the only pathway to reliable knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7bbce635-2838-42fe-9e0c-7e754932a9f7
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Phenomenal Experience Consistency](phenomenal-experience-consistency-synthesis-dff6895e-5f7e-47e4-9fd7-5b1463da077a.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person learns about atoms through scientific theories and not direct perception. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that they have knowledge despite the lack of direct perceptual experience.**, **There is a plausible principle that theoretical knowledge based on explanations and predictions is reliable. This principle together with the scenario suggests that knowledge can arise independently of direct perception.**
