# Contextual Reliabilism with Truth Priority

**Parent:** [Can false propositions ever be known?](can-false-propositions-ever-be-known-antithesis-b84a2075-3e48-4474-a2c7-bccc55da4263.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A person knows a proposition if the belief is produced by a reliable process and contextual standards are met**, **A reliable process cannot produce knowledge based on false propositions**
