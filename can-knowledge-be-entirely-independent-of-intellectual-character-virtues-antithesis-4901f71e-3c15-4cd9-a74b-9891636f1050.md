# Can knowledge be entirely independent of intellectual character virtues?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 4901f71e-3c15-4cd9-a74b-9891636f1050
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Responsibilist Virtue Epistemology](responsibilist-virtue-epistemology-synthesis-23f2e823-6756-4d05-9cb6-b00f2dd07c18.md)

## Content

**It is plausible to believe that some individuals can know things due to expert intuition or immediate insight without demonstrating any particular intellectual character virtues.**, **A case can occur in which a person, renowned for their deep understanding of a subject, makes a correct guess without exercising any intellectual virtues, yet intuitively we might still credit this as knowledge.**
