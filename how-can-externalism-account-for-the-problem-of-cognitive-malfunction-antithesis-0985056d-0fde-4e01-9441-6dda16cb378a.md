# How can externalism account for the problem of cognitive malfunction?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0985056d-0fde-4e01-9441-6dda16cb378a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalism](externalism-synthesis-5b44df35-6663-4278-91dc-65b3fcbf870c.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case where a normally reliable process in a person’s mind is temporarily malfunctioning, yet it still leads to a true belief. It is implausible for the person to have knowledge in such a scenario.**, **A person knows a proposition if the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process, yet a momentary malfunction should not suffice for knowledge.**
