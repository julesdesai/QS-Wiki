# Does the time lag in perception suggest intermediaries in perception?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3b1c1cae-63e4-41c9-9676-cf6feb465022
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Naïve Realism](naïve-realism-synthesis-25d0b7e3-6f66-46c3-a45d-bb41c1dbe70b.md)

## Content

**Consider the fact that light takes time to travel from an object to our eyes, resulting in a perceptual lag. Intuitively, we judge that if perception involves a time delay, it may not be a direct encounter with the present state of the object.**
