# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with No Defeaters

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Can someone exhibit intellectual virtues without knowledge?](can-someone-exhibit-intellectual-virtues-without-knowledge-antithesis-d43ce30c-d689-4908-ab5f-6fcd26b5b4a3.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **A person knows a proposition if there are no defeaters for their justification**
