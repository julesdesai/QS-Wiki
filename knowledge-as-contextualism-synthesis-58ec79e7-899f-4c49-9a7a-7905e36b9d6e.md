**Parent:** [Can a belief be justified yet fail to count as knowledge?](can-a-belief-be-justified-yet-fail-to-count-as-knowledge-antithesis-6b603e22-f7c4-404c-afa9-aaa545d0c6c4.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified within a specific context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and it is justified within a specific context**
