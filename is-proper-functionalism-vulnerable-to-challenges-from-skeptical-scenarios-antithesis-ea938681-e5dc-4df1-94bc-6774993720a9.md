# Is Proper Functionalism vulnerable to challenges from skeptical scenarios?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ea938681-e5dc-4df1-94bc-6774993720a9
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Proper Functionalism](proper-functionalism-synthesis-17c9fccb-5f3e-4011-91c3-5213c66550ac.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person is in a virtual reality while believing they are in the real world, with cognitive faculties functioning properly for the simulated environment. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that the individual lacks knowledge of the actual world.**, **There is a plausible principle that knowledge requires beliefs about the actual world. This principle together with simulated environments entails that a person can't hold true knowledge in such scenarios.**
