# Is an infinite series necessary for all forms of knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 8fa64cd3-f992-42f2-93de-8211e1c0683b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism](infinitism-synthesis-c0072d0f-5658-4115-88b4-8385b90f0067.md)

## Content

**Mathematics, considered a model of knowledge, often relies on axioms and proofs that terminate; an infinite justification process appears unnecessary.**, **In empirical sciences, observations and finite principles often ground beliefs, suggesting that not all knowledge domains conform to the need for infinite series.**
