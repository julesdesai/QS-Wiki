# Can context undermine the concept of objective truth in knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e6c1cc0e-795f-43fe-8cea-fb0a12ae9142
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism with Dynamic Standards](contextualism-with-dynamic-standards-synthesis-b251edf1-06ee-458e-a02d-1c3435caef87.md)

## Content

**Objective truth claims rely on universal standards rather than context-sensitive standards.**, **Inserting context-sensitive standards suggests that truth can differ with changes in context.**, **If truth can vary with context, it undermines the concept of objective truth.**
