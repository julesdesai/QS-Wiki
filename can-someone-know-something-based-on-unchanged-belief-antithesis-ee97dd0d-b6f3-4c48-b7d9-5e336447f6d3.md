# Can someone know something based on unchanged belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ee97dd0d-b6f3-4c48-b7d9-5e336447f6d3
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Sensitivity Theory of Knowledge](sensitivity-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-ba84f32c-948c-4607-9039-09527bd88c50.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a person continues to believe a proposition regardless of its truth value, due to external reasons such as indoctrination or stubbornness.**, **Intuitively, we would say that such a belief cannot constitute knowledge because it does not respond to the truth of the proposition.**
