# Can a belief be true by luck and still considered safe?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a4465c32-882d-41b6-a1e9-a4b6ebb6db0d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Safety Theory of Knowledge](safety-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-526116dd-9931-4287-b7a5-0d90efcd6c5e.md)

## Content

**A person could have a belief that happens to be true by chance, despite the person's method of belief formation being unreliable.**, **In nearby possible worlds, the belief might not have been true because the conditions were slightly different, questioning the belief's safety.**
