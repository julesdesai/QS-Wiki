## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief stems from intellectual virtue**, **An intellectual virtue is a quality that reliably leads to truth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief is true and results from intellectual virtue**
