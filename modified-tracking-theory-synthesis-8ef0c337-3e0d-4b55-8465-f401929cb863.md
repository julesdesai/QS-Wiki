## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person would not believe the proposition if it were false in most nearby possible worlds**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person would believe the proposition if it were true in most nearby possible worlds**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that these modified tracking conditions hold**
