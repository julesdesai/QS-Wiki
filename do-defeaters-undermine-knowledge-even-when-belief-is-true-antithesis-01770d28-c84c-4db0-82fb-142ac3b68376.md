# Do defeaters undermine knowledge even when belief is true?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 01770d28-c84c-4db0-82fb-142ac3b68376
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology with Defeasibility](virtue-epistemology-with-defeasibility-synthesis-802a6edd-3a94-41b9-8765-bb5c975aae31.md)

## Content

**A situation where a person has a true belief that stems from intellectual virtue, but later encounters evidence against this belief which they ignore, intuitively does not seem like knowledge.**, **A belief must remain undefeated by contrary evidence to constitute knowledge, suggesting defeaters play a crucial role.**
