# Does phenomenalism undermine scientific realism?

**Parent:** [Phenomenalism suggests that physical objects are logical constructions of sensory experiences.](phenomenalism-suggests-that-physical-objects-are-logical-constructions-of-sensory-experiences-synthesis-70c8baf6-067c-48d6-b521-64b5ff284db6.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that scientific realism relies on a mind-independent reality. This principle together with the claim that there is no reality independent of experiences entails a tension with the empirical success of science, which assumes the existence of unperceived phenomena.**, **In practice, scientific observations often rely on indirectly detecting unobservable entities, challenging the phenomenalist view by suggesting some reality extends beyond direct sensory perception.**
