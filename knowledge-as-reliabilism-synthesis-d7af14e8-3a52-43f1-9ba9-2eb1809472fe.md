## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is formed through a reliable cognitive process**, **A belief formed by a reliable process does not require inferential justification from other beliefs**
