# Is coherence alone enough to guarantee the truth of beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0566bd80-9d5d-415c-b545-f63b4fe8b899
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Resilient Coherentism](resilient-coherentism-synthesis-5cd0236d-206b-433d-8e41-0e0d41938790.md)

## Content

**A set of beliefs can be internally coherent but not necessarily map onto factual truths about the world.**, **If coherence is sufficient for knowledge, then coherent false beliefs would also qualify as knowledge, which is counterintuitive.**
