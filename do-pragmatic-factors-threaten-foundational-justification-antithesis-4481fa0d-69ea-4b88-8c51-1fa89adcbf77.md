# Do pragmatic factors threaten foundational justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 4481fa0d-69ea-4b88-8c51-1fa89adcbf77
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundationalism with Pragmatic Grounds](foundationalism-with-pragmatic-grounds-synthesis-35d5e861-b788-4e52-a02b-4488aba3b8d2.md)

## Content

**A plausible principle is that justification should be truth-conducive rather than pragmatic. This principle together with employing pragmatic rather than solely epistemic grounds for justification questions the objectivity of justification.**, **Consider a scenario where a belief is justified because it is practical or useful rather than due to its truthfulness. In such a scenario, intuitively, the belief seems unjustified from a foundational standpoint.**
