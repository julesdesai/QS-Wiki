# Does misleading evidence still allow for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 56f30640-e257-4584-866b-fbd8554ab8af
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Evidentialist Reliabilism](evidentialist-reliabilism-synthesis-b2fd7651-b8ba-4bbd-aa9b-2c3c304a468b.md)

## Content

**There are cases where a person forms a true belief based on evidence that is misleading but accidentally reliable.**, **In such cases, it seems counterintuitive to consider the person as possessing knowledge, despite the process being coincidentally reliable and true.**
