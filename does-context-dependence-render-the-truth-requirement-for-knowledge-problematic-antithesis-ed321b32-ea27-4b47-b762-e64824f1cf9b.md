# Does context-dependence render the truth requirement for knowledge problematic?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ed321b32-ea27-4b47-b762-e64824f1cf9b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism with Virtue Epistemology](contextualism-with-virtue-epistemology-synthesis-4d017c8b-6b5b-48d7-b8c7-737c6c80044f.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which the truth of a proposition is constant across contexts but the standards for knowing it change. Intuitively, we either have to say that someone knows it or not based on context. If we say their knowledge depends on context, then the constant truth seems insufficient.**
