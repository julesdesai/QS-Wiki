# What if competing reliable processes yield conflicting beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 47a91202-1338-4d2f-a0ab-754dcf72693f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalism with Reliabilism](externalism-with-reliabilism-synthesis-c10e5c74-1dbc-479d-ba5e-469176c5d537.md)

## Content

**Suppose there are two reliable cognitive processes that independently lead to different beliefs about the same proposition. Intuitively, we would need to determine which, if either, belief constitutes knowledge. Without accessible justification, this determination cannot be made.**
