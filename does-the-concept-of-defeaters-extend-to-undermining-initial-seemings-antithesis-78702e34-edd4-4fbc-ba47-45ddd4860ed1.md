# Does the concept of defeaters extend to undermining initial seemings?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 78702e34-edd4-4fbc-ba47-45ddd4860ed1

**Terminal:** No

## Content

**Consider a situation where a person forms a belief that seems true, yet new evidence they encounter afterward completely invalidates the initial seeming. This intuitively suggests that the presence of defeaters can retroactively nullify seeming justification, challenging the view.**
