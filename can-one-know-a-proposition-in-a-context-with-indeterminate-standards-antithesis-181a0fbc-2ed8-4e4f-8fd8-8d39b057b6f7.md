# Can one know a proposition in a context with indeterminate standards?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 181a0fbc-2ed8-4e4f-8fd8-8d39b057b6f7

## Content

**There are contexts where the standards for knowledge are not clearly defined or are vague.**, **Intuitively, it seems problematic to claim one either knows or does not know a proposition when the standards themselves are unclear.**
