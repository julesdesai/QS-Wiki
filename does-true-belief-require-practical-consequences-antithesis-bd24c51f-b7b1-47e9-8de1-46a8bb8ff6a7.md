# Does true belief require practical consequences?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** bd24c51f-b7b1-47e9-8de1-46a8bb8ff6a7
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Theory of Knowledge](pragmatic-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-af5a1410-77c2-4ceb-b390-2ced8876b488.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person believes that it will rain tomorrow based solely on observing dark clouds and nothing else. Intuitively, their belief can be true even if it doesn’t affect their current actions or plans.**, **The concept of truth does not inherently involve practical consequences; a belief can be true regardless of whether it has any practical implications.**
