# Causal Theory of Knowledge

**Parent:** [Can knowledge exist without considering context-sensitive truth conditions?](can-knowledge-exist-without-considering-context-sensitive-truth-conditions-antithesis-a8bbe264-2eb5-4126-860f-12140e1dc9ba.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is a causal connection between the belief and the truth of the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there is a causal connection between the belief and the truth of the proposition**
