## Content

**Knowledge is a true belief justified by methods that are typically truth-conducive**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief is justified through methods that tend to produce truth and the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**
