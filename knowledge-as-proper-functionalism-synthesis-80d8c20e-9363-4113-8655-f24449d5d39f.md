## Content

**Knowledge is a true belief that results from the proper functioning of one's cognitive faculties**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their cognitive faculties are functioning properly and aimed at truth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**
