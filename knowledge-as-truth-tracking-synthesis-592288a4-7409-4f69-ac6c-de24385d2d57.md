**Parent:** [Is coherence enough for objective knowledge when consensus is absent among different belief systems?](is-coherence-enough-for-objective-knowledge-when-consensus-is-absent-among-different-belief-systems-antithesis-d57d406b-241e-47c2-a4d4-f854997f8d85.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief tracks the truth across relevant possible scenarios**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief tracks the truth across relevant possible scenarios**
