## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the person were to believe the proposition, then the proposition would be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and if the person were to not believe the proposition, then the proposition would not be true**
