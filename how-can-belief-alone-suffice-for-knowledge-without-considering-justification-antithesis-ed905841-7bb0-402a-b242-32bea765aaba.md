# How can belief alone suffice for knowledge without considering justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ed905841-7bb0-402a-b242-32bea765aaba
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism with Bounded Rationality](infinitism-with-bounded-rationality-synthesis-6de35f9a-9231-4ac2-b1a8-a1ab9be932b6.md)

## Content

**Knowledge has traditionally been understood as justified true belief.**, **If a person knows a proposition simply by believing it, then the role of justification is undermined.**, **Belief alone cannot account for the distinction between mere belief and knowledge without justification.**
