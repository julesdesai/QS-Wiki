## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their justification ultimately rests on foundational beliefs**, **Foundational beliefs are self-justified or evident without further justification**, **Infinite chains of justification can derive from foundational beliefs and maintain causal linkages**
