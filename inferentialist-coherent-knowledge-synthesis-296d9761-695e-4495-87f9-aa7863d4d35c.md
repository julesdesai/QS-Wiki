# Inferentialist Coherent Knowledge

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can intrinsic justification be objectively verified?](can-intrinsic-justification-be-objectively-verified-antithesis-2678cae6-3e26-41b9-9de6-e82d1f7a1e0d.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified through inferential processes**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres with a system of interconnected beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is justified through inferential processes, and the belief coheres with a system of interconnected beliefs**
