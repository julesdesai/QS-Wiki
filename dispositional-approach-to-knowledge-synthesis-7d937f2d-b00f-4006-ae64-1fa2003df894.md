## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the formation of belief is a reliable disposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the process involves understanding as one of the dispositions**
