## Content

**There is a plausible principle that knowledge should align with truth. A coherent set of beliefs can be false despite their coherence.**, **Suppose we have a case where a person’s beliefs about the world all cohere but are based on false assumptions. Intuitively, this does not seem to constitute knowledge.**
