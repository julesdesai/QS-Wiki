## Content

**A belief is knowledge if it is formed from a disposition to produce true beliefs**, **A disposition is a stable property that can produce true beliefs without presupposing specific truths**, **The truth of a belief is a necessary condition for it to be considered knowledge**, **The usefulness of a belief is not necessary for it to be considered knowledge**
