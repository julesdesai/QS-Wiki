## Content

**A person knows a proposition if their belief is not the result of epistemic luck**, **Knowledge requires that the belief must be true due to the reliability of the process**, **A belief is not knowledge if it is true merely due to luck**, **A true belief produced by a process is knowledge if it is not because of luck**
