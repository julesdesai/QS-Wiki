# Can knowledge occur without any intellectual virtues?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 57ba8fde-63dd-41ad-a768-a8179e8ae65b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-a1d061ab-4ce5-4544-95e5-a72c751db8d0.md)

## Content

**Suppose someone arrives at a true belief based only on prejudice, bias, or simple habitual thinking, which coincidentally happens to be correct.**, **It seems counterintuitive to call these knowledge if they lack any intellectually virtuous process or consideration.**
