## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **A proposition is known if it could not have easily been false when the belief was formed**, **Knowledge requires that the method of arriving at the belief aligns with the safety of the belief being true**
