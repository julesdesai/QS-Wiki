# Is coherence always possible with fluid and changing contexts?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 6b035d87-df82-4ed2-a282-f4dbe1a7f789
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualist Coherentism](contextualist-coherentism-synthesis-156207e7-3c9d-49a2-8bb7-7097c1d027cd.md)

## Content

**In a rapidly changing society, such as during a political revolution, the contextual information that contributes to coherence might shift frequently.**, **A belief that coheres with one system might quickly become incoherent as the context changes.**, **This instability raises doubts about the lasting justification of beliefs in dynamic contexts.**
