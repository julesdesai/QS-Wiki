## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is justified**, **The possibility of defeating evidence must not be present for the belief to constitute knowledge**, **For a person to know a proposition, the proposition must be true**
