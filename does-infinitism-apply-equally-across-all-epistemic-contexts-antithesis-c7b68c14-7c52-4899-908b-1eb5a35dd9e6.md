# Does Infinitism apply equally across all epistemic contexts?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c7b68c14-7c52-4899-908b-1eb5a35dd9e6
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism](infinitism-synthesis-c0072d0f-5658-4115-88b4-8385b90f0067.md)

## Content

**Different fields and contexts of knowledge, like science versus personal belief, have different standards and means of justification. It is implausible that all require an infinite chain of reasons.**, **In certain knowledge domains, finite causal explanations are preferred and effective, suggesting that infinite regress is not a universally applicable principle.**
