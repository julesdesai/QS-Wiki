## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from an intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is both true and arises from an exercise of intellectual virtue**
