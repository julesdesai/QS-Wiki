# Knowledge as True Belief with Infinitist Justification

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is justified by an infinite chain of reasoning**, **An infinite chain of reasoning means that there is an endless non-repeating series of reasons for the belief**
