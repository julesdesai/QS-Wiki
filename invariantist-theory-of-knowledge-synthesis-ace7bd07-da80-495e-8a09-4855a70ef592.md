## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief to be justified in a stable manner independent of stakes**, **The justification for the belief must be sufficient irrespective of changing conversational stakes**
