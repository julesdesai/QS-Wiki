# Does emphasizing pragmatic considerations lead to subjective justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 19dcf1b6-2a60-43d6-98ae-3f37dee94f72
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Coherentism](pragmatic-coherentism-synthesis-37d6c6a9-68e2-4800-8f97-9338457a8af1.md)

## Content

**The coherence of a belief system is assessed based on pragmatic considerations. This could lead to subjective justification because pragmatic success could vary greatly between individuals based on personal circumstances and goals.**, **There is a plausible principle that belief justification should be consistent and objective. This principle together with the variability of pragmatic needs implies that relying solely on pragmatic considerations might result in inconsistent justification standards.**
