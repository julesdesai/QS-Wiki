# Is infinite justification consistent with human limitations?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9b676d5d-3e3d-4825-b9e7-64f5f462e7e7
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Infinitism](pragmatic-infinitism-synthesis-c7c8ebf0-3a7f-4e28-af18-b590ada6528d.md)

## Content

**Human beings possess cognitive constraints that typically prevent processing an infinite number of justifications.**, **A theoretical model requiring infinite strings of justification may not align with our capacity to grasp or utilize that information effectively.**
