# Mixed Virtue and Safety Epistemology

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Does intellectual virtue always lead to knowledge?](does-intellectual-virtue-always-lead-to-knowledge-antithesis-2780d505-c1ef-4c00-91a9-22b002766986.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief safely could not have easily been false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief results from both intellectual virtue and retains a safety condition concerning truth**
