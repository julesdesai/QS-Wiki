## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is causally connected to the truth of the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified by a method which reliably produces true beliefs**
