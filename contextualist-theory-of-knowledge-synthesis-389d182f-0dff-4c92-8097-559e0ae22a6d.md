## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the standards for knowing are met given the context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the person believes the proposition, it is true, and the contextual standards for knowing are met**
