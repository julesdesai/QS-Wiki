# Is the exercising of cognitive virtues sufficient for achieving knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 82e09a85-88ab-42d2-a87f-47aef67329ac
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-ad239dac-f508-456d-984a-1344bc3c3ad8.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where an individual exercises cognitive virtues but forms a false belief due to misleading evidence. Intuitively, the person does not possess knowledge despite exercising virtues.**, **There is a plausible principle that mere exercising of cognitive virtues without true belief or accurate outcomes is insufficient for knowledge.**
