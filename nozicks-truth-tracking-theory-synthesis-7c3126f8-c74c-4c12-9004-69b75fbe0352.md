# Nozick's Truth-Tracking Theory

**Parent:** [Can perceptual illusions deceive reliable processes?](can-perceptual-illusions-deceive-reliable-processes-antithesis-629dcb34-dc45-44ff-9a41-897b86285112.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were true, the person would believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the person believes the proposition, it is true, and the belief tracks the truth across close possible worlds**
