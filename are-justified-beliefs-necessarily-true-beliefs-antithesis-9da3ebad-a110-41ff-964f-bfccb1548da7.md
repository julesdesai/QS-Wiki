# Are justified beliefs necessarily true beliefs?

**Node ID:** 9da3ebad-a110-41ff-964f-bfccb1548da7
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism with Foundational Beliefs](infinitism-with-foundational-beliefs-synthesis-4273f090-f358-492e-9e7c-904c2a039ab2.md)

## Content

**A belief can be justified based on available reasons even if it turns out to be false.**, **Consider a case where a belief is arrived at through logical reasoning yet circumstances later invalidate its truth. Intuitively, the belief was justified but not true.**, **A justified belief does not guarantee it aligns with the reality or truthfulness of a proposition.**
