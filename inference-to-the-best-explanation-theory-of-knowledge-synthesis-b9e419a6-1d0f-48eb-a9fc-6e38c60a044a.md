# Inference to the Best Explanation Theory of Knowledge

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Can someone have a justified true belief without knowledge?](can-someone-have-a-justified-true-belief-without-knowledge-antithesis-495ed798-5ec0-4fcd-940a-760bda6f1f35.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is the best explanation of the evidence available to them**, **The best explanation is one that is most coherent, simple, and comprehensive relative to alternatives**
