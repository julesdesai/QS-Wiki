# How can coherence account for radical belief changes?

**Node ID:** 06bff2e5-e627-4646-801c-f8a9a7b964ed

**Parent:** [Reliabilist Coherentism](reliabilist-coherentism-synthesis-2c1f8a9c-d9de-4c66-ad9a-56c41dc7c349.md)

## Content

**Radical changes in belief, if based on new reliable information, may disrupt existing coherence, questioning the role of coherence in justifying these changes.**, **If a belief system resists modification despite new reliable information, it may remain coherent but fail to justify newly introduced beliefs.**
