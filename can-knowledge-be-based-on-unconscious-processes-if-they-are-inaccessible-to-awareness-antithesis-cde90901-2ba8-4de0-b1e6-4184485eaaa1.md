# Can knowledge be based on unconscious processes if they are inaccessible to awareness?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** cde90901-2ba8-4de0-b1e6-4184485eaaa1
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Phenomenalism with Unconscious Processes](phenomenalism-with-unconscious-processes-synthesis-53c9374d-552a-49b3-b121-1513a04b6dab.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that knowledge can be grounded in unconscious processes if these processes are inaccessible to conscious awareness.**, **Suppose we have a person who unknowingly bases their beliefs on unconscious assumptions, then intuitively, we would not say that this person has knowledge since they are unaware of the basis for their beliefs.**
