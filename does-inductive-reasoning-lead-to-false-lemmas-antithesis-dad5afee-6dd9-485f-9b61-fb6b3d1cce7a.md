# Does inductive reasoning lead to false lemmas?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** dad5afee-6dd9-485f-9b61-fb6b3d1cce7a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [No False Lemmas View](no-false-lemmas-view-synthesis-031e6f6f-2b49-47e4-821c-d2421562acef.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a scientist infers from repeated observations of swans in the area that all swans are white. Intuitively, we either have to say that the scientist's conclusion is a reliable belief or that it is not. If we say the conclusion is not reliable, then their method of justification based on induction would involve a false lemma. If we say it is a reliable belief, then this contradicts the essence of inductive reasoning as inherently probabilistic.**
