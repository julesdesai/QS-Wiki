# Can tracking provide an account of epistemic closure?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9332599e-1e94-4263-a784-e78d16427311
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Tracking Theory](tracking-theory-synthesis-ba2ab1ae-08da-4d9d-a4db-0c45d3904537.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that knowledge is closed under known logical entailment.**, **If a person knows a proposition and knows it entails another, they should know the second proposition too.**, **Tracking theory does not necessarily allow for this transfer of knowledge in all cases of logical entailment.**
