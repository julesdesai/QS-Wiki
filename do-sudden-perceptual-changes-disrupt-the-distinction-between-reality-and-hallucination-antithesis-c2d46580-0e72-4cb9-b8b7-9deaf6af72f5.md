# Do sudden perceptual changes disrupt the distinction between reality and hallucination?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c2d46580-0e72-4cb9-b8b7-9deaf6af72f5
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Disjunctivism](disjunctivism-synthesis-e62747dd-0f26-4379-9406-3d7f6fb883b9.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person suddenly transitions from clear perception to a hallucination seamlessly, such as entering a hallucination while looking at an object. Intuitively, this blurs the distinction between real and hallucinatory experiences.**, **It is implausible that the nature of perception can change instantly, which challenges the idea that perception of reality and hallucination are fundamentally different experiences.**
