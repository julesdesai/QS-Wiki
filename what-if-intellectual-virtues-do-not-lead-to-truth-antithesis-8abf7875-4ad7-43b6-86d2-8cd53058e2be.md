# What if intellectual virtues do not lead to truth?

## Content

**Imagine person F practices open-mindedness and intellectual perseverance, yet their beliefs about a scientific phenomenon are constantly proven false as theories evolve. Intuitively, F's possession of intellectual virtues does not ensure knowledge.**, **Despite intellectually virtuous efforts, F’s beliefs are false, suggesting that having intellectual virtues does not necessarily provide knowledge if these virtues do not align with truth.**
