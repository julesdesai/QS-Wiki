## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, their belief must result from intellectual virtue or competence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification, the proposition is true, and the belief is formed through intellectual virtue**
