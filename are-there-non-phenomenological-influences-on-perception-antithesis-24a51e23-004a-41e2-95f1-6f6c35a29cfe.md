# Are there non-phenomenological influences on perception?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 24a51e23-004a-41e2-95f1-6f6c35a29cfe
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Phenomenological Perspective](phenomenological-perspective-synthesis-c5a75a88-a263-494b-bef9-8c1e426bd706.md)

## Content

**Empirical studies on perception show that factors like expectations and previous knowledge heavily influence how perception occurs.** **If perception were purely phenomenological, such external influences would be unaccounted for.**
