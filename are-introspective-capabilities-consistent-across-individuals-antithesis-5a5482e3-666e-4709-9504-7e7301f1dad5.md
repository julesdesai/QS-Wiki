# Are introspective capabilities consistent across individuals?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5a5482e3-666e-4709-9504-7e7301f1dad5
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Internalist Reliabilism Summary](internalist-reliabilism-summary-synthesis-afd99030-664a-4a62-9431-6f8bc1e36717.md)

## Content

**Internalist reliabilism presupposes individuals have equal capacity for introspective access to their cognitive processes.**, **Introspective abilities might vary significantly and affect access to perceived reliability.**, **This variation challenges the uniform applicability of internalist reliabilism.**
