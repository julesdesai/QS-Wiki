# Is knowledge ever possible if sensitivity entails infinite counterfactual conditions?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0490bf2e-e595-4422-991e-885f2c856ff5
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Process Reliabilism with Sensitivity](process-reliabilism-with-sensitivity-synthesis-3b7ba929-b462-4672-9048-c64e1338414e.md)

## Content

**Consider a person who knows they have hands because they can see and feel them; they have consistently verified their existence through robust processes. Intuitively, this is knowledge.**, **For sensitivity, they would need to hold this belief only when true, requiring considering countless “what if” scenarios: hallucinations, virtual reality settings, all hypothetically false, creating an impossible standard.**
