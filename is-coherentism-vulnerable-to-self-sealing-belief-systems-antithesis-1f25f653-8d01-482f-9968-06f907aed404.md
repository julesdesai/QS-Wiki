# Is coherentism vulnerable to self-sealing belief systems?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1f25f653-8d01-482f-9968-06f907aed404
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism View](coherentism-view-synthesis-4dcf0483-85cc-4ed6-8a5e-86aaec2b7626.md)

## Content

**Consider a belief system that has built-in defenses against all evidence or argument, such as a belief system that explains away all disconfirming evidence. Intuitively, such systems seem unjustified despite their internal coherence.**, **A plausible principle is that genuine justification should be responsive to truth-conducive evidence, which self-sealing systems are not.**
