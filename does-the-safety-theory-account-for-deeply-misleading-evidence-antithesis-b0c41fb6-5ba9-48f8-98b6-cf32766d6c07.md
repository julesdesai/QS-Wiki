# Does the Safety Theory account for deeply misleading evidence?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b0c41fb6-5ba9-48f8-98b6-cf32766d6c07
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Safety Theory](safety-theory-synthesis-a867b6c0-f011-4cc2-a9c9-8c3a93b64de5.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case where an individual has overwhelming evidence that misleadingly supports a false view, yet they have a true belief by chance that is safe from error**, **Intuitively, we would find it implausible to attribute knowledge to the person given that they are acting on misleading evidence**, **A belief being true in nearly all nearby possible worlds does not mean it is supported by non-misleading evidence necessary for knowledge**
