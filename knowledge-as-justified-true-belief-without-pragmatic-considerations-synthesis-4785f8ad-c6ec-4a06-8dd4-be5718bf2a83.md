# Knowledge as Justified True Belief without Pragmatic Considerations

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **Pragmatic usefulness is not a component of knowledge**
