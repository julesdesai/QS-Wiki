# Can animals possess knowledge without accessible justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9544fb52-d1b0-43f5-9d06-b03216a8660d
**Depth:** 2
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Internalism](internalism-thesis-697c012e-ac77-4c5b-935c-0bf2d4d5ce83.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that animals like dogs access justification in the same way humans do, yet they often seem to have knowledge of their environment and situation.**, **Consider a scenario in which a dog knows its way home despite not having accessible justification for this knowledge. Intuitively, we would judge that the dog knows how to navigate back home.**
