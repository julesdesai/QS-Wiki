# Are all predictive statements meaningless until verified?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ade1bcb9-7f18-4c52-933c-934e822d0786
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Verificationism](verificationism-synthesis-742a7e84-42a2-4d13-8c35-af2e03d93250.md)

## Content

**Predictive statements about the future, such as weather forecasts, are made meaningful through their outcomes rather than contemporaneous verification**, **If a statement only has meaning if it can be empirically verified, then predictive statements would not hold meaning until they are verified**, **It is implausible that predictions, which guide scientific and practical endeavors, are meaningless before verification**
