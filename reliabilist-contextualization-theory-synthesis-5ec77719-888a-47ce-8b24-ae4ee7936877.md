## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief be formed by a reliable method appropriate to both base and contextual conditions**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and it is formed by reliable methods that consider base and contextual factors**
