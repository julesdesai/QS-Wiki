# Knowledge Requires Defeater Sensitivity

**Parent:** [Is knowledge as context-sensitive justification vulnerable to Gettier problems?](is-knowledge-as-context-sensitive-justification-vulnerable-to-gettier-problems-antithesis-131d6257-86d6-4ec7-b29e-fd311d3c2f01.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no undefeated defeaters of the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there are no undefeated defeaters of the belief**
