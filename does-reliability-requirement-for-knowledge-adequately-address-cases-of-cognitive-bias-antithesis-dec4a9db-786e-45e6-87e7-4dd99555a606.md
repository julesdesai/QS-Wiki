# Does reliability requirement for knowledge adequately address cases of cognitive bias?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** dec4a9db-786e-45e6-87e7-4dd99555a606
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism about Knowledge](reliabilism-about-knowledge-synthesis-c84f45b2-357a-4b29-be66-6a09ffb029e2.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person uses personal intuition influenced by cognitive bias to predict outcomes. The process is internally reliable for the person but systematically incorrect. Intuitively, this is not knowledge**, **It is implausible for processes ruled by cognitive bias to satisfy the reliability condition for knowledge, even if they consistently produce true beliefs for that biased individual**
