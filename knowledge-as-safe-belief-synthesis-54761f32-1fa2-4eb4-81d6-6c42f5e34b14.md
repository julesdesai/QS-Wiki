# Knowledge as Safe Belief

**Parent:** [Can luck contribute to knowledge despite the exercise of intellectual virtue?](can-luck-contribute-to-knowledge-despite-the-exercise-of-intellectual-virtue-antithesis-74c102bd-47af-4e40-b9cd-7b321ca10009.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is safely formed**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is safely formed**
