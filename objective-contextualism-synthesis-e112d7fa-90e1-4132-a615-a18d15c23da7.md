## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, justification is affected by objective features of the context that can be consistently identified**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and they have objective justification given the specific context**
