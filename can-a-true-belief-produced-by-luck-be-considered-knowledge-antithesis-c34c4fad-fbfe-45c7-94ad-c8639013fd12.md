# Can a true belief produced by luck be considered knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c34c4fad-fbfe-45c7-94ad-c8639013fd12
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-4f2fdbe9-b5ae-4003-9ce0-28573a7cd0c4.md)

## Content

**A case where a person forms a true belief by guessing the outcome of a coin toss is not a case where we would consider the person to know the outcome**, **A true belief that results from guesswork or luck does not reliably produce true beliefs and therefore does not align with the concept of intellectual virtue**
