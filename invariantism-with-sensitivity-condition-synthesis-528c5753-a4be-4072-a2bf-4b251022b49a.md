## Content

**A person knows a proposition if their belief is sensitive to the truth**, **Sensitivity to the truth means that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **The standards for knowing a proposition do not vary with different contexts**
