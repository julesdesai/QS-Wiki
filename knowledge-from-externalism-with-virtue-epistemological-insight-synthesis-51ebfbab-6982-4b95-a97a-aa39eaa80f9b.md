## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief is produced by a cognitive virtue or skill**, **A cognitive virtue is a reliable cognitive faculty or habit that leads to true beliefs**
