**Parent:** [Can a person have knowledge without being able to articulate the evidence supporting their belief?](can-a-person-have-knowledge-without-being-able-to-articulate-the-evidence-supporting-their-belief-antithesis-1039bb0b-b24b-4916-9a7c-6904a4ff7bea.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief arises from intellectual virtues**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief arises from intellectual virtues**
