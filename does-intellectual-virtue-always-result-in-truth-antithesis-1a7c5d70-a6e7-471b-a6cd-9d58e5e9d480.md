# Does intellectual virtue always result in truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1a7c5d70-a6e7-471b-a6cd-9d58e5e9d480
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-d7a894af-29e5-42fe-8e5e-957e2009566d.md)

## Content

**A person who is intellectually humble might refrain from asserting what they know to avoid appearing overconfident, even when they are in possession of the truth.**, **It seems implausible that intellectual humility necessarily leads to the attainment or expression of truth, as in some contexts it might lead to withholding truth.**
