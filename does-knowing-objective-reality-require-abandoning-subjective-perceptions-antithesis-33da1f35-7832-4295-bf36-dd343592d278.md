# Does knowing objective reality require abandoning subjective perceptions?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 33da1f35-7832-4295-bf36-dd343592d278
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Dual Aspect Theory](dual-aspect-theory-synthesis-e0a4859a-34ba-4bc9-9368-89fce3ac2e21.md)

## Content

**Objective reality consists of facts and states of affairs independent of any particular person's perceptions.**, **Subjective perceptions are personal and can differ from one individual to another without any factual basis in objective reality.**, **It is implausible to completely set aside all subjective perceptions when forming an understanding of reality, as subjective experience is central to how humans process information.**
