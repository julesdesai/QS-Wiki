# Can a process be reliable but unknown to the believer?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b3cf17a9-a20b-4e37-bf50-47c13795a953
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism](reliabilism-synthesis-4db07ebc-4cb3-402b-b335-2b01121c3e1a.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person forms a belief through a reliable process without knowing about the process' reliability. Intuitively, this raises the question of whether the person truly knows the proposition**, **It is implausible that one can know a proposition if unaware of the process's reliability that leads to the belief**
