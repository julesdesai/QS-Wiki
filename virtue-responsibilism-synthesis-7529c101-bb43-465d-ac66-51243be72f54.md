## Content

**Intellectual virtues are character traits that consistently lead to true beliefs**, **The aim of intellectual virtues is to pursue truth**, **A person can possess intellectual virtues regardless of whether they form true beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief was formed through a reliable method influenced by intellectual virtue**
