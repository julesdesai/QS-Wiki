## Content

**Knowledge is true belief formed through the exercise of significant, though not exhaustive, intellectual virtue and responsibility**, **Intellectual virtues include character traits like open-mindedness and fairness**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief arises from sufficiently and reliably responsible intellectual engagement**
