## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to have knowledge, their justification for believing the proposition must be accessible to them**, **Access to justification involves the ability to reflect upon and articulate reasons for belief**
