# Must there be a reliable connection between a belief and its success?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 04eed6b8-7765-4283-ba96-c1cd9647f528
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatism Summary](pragmatism-summary-synthesis-d0b80c94-4161-403a-9bb9-e4c173237700.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person believes they will safely drive through traffic by closing their eyes and succeeds by luck. Intuitively, it seems this belief is not justified, despite its practical success.**, **There is a plausible principle that knowledge requires a reliable method for forming beliefs, not accidental or fortunate success.**
