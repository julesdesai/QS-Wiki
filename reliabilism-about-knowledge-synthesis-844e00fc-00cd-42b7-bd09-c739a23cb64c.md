# Reliabilism about Knowledge

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can someone know a false proposition?](can-someone-know-a-false-proposition-antithesis-1abf3e49-c35e-4442-9047-3b788f87fd00.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A person knows a proposition if and only if their belief in the proposition is produced by a reliable process**, **A reliable process consistently leads to true beliefs**
