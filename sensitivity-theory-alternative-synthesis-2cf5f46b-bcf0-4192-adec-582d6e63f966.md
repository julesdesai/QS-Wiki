## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **If the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is not required that there is a causal relationship between belief and truth**
