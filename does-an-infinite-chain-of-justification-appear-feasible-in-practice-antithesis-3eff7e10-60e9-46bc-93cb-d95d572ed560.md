# Does an infinite chain of justification appear feasible in practice?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3eff7e10-60e9-46bc-93cb-d95d572ed560
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism with Finite Justifications](infinitism-with-finite-justifications-synthesis-69ad7e03-7f20-4a4f-85ef-ebc999d85fe8.md)

## Content

**Consider the idea that justification requires an infinite chain of reasoning. In practice, it is difficult to conceive how an individual could engage with or verify such an infinite chain.**, **Suppose a belief requires potentially infinite reasons to be fully justified. It seems implausible for someone to ascertain or practically utilize such an infinite series in finite human time.**
