## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they can rule out relevant alternative propositions**, **The relevance of alternatives is determined by practical considerations involving error possibilities**
