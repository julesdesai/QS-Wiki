# Knowledge as Sensitivity

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge exist without belief in the proposition?](can-knowledge-exist-without-belief-in-the-proposition-antithesis-dda54a3e-bbb9-4654-b7a3-38e56f32e5da.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, their belief must be sensitive to truth**, **A belief is sensitive if, were the proposition false, the person would not believe it**
