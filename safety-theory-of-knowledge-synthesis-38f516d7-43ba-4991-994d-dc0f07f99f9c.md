## Content

**A belief is safe if it could not easily have been false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief in the proposition is safe**, **Safety in belief precludes knowledge gained by unreliable or lucky processes**
