# Are beliefs from non-design sources truly knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d08b9d8c-9299-42b6-9a2f-e1e91ee77c4c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Proper Functionalist Theory of Knowledge](proper-functionalist-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-fd446f74-9cc9-44f6-9f26-1e4a4f9b46ed.md)

## Content

**A person can form a true belief based on luck or coincidence without cognitive faculties being designed for truth.**, **Intuitively, we judge that beliefs formed by luck or coincidence do not constitute knowledge, even if true.**
