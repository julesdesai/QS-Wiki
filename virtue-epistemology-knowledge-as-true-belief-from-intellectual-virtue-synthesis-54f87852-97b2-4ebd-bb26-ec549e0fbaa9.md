# Virtue Epistemology: Knowledge as True Belief from Intellectual Virtue

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 54f87852-97b2-4ebd-bb26-ec549e0fbaa9
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can false defeaters wrongly undermine knowledge?](can-false-defeaters-wrongly-undermine-knowledge-antithesis-4babf768-f42b-480b-a491-706f21851f93.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from the exercise of an intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the person believes it, the proposition is true, and the belief results from an intellectual virtue**
