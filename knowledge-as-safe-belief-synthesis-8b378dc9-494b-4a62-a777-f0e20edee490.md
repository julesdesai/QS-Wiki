# Knowledge as Safe Belief

**Parent:** [Can knowledge originate from unconscious intellectual processes?](can-knowledge-originate-from-unconscious-intellectual-processes-antithesis-720bd5d6-b380-45db-8170-6cebc47a3dc5.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed in a way that reliably leads to true beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is formed in a way that reliably leads to true beliefs**
