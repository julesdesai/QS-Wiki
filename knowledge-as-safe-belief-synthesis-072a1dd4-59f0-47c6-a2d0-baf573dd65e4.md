# Knowledge as Safe Belief

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 072a1dd4-59f0-47c6-a2d0-baf573dd65e4
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge occur in a purely predictive context?](can-knowledge-occur-in-a-purely-predictive-context-antithesis-eea9d407-8aaf-484a-8e67-749412c37af3.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed in such a way that it could not easily have been false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is formed in a way that it could not easily have been false**
