# What happens if a belief is formed in a truth-preserving way but in a misleading environment?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** eaf23ac0-9358-4991-82fe-2cd25c872869
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Safety Theory](safety-theory-synthesis-2fb86082-7528-4d1d-a3e0-8c751a94ae5c.md)

## Content

**Consider a situation where a person forms a belief through a reliable process, like perceiving a mirage in the desert**, **While the process is generally truth-preserving, the environmental conditions mislead, producing a false belief which contradicts the safety condition as it assumes correct environmental inputs**
