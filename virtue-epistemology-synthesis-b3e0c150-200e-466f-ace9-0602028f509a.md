## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief results from the intellectual virtues of the believer**, **Intellectual virtues are traits that reliably lead to truth, like open-mindedness and rigor**
