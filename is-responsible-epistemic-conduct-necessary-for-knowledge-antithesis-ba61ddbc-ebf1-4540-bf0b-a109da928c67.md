# Is responsible epistemic conduct necessary for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ba61ddbc-ebf1-4540-bf0b-a109da928c67
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Responsibilist Virtue Epistemology](responsibilist-virtue-epistemology-synthesis-23f2e823-6756-4d05-9cb6-b00f2dd07c18.md)

## Content

**Consider an individual whose belief coincidentally aligns with a well-supported scientific theory without the individual engaging in responsible inquiry. In such a case, intuitively, the individual might still be considered to know the theory due to its truth alignment, regardless of their epistemic conduct.**, **Belief can coincide with responsible epistemic conduct and still be based on unreliable or false cognitive processes.**
