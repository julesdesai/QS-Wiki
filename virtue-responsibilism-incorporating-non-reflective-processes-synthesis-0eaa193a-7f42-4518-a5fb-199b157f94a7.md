## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief arises from an intellectual virtue or competence, which may include non-reflective processes such as perception**, **Non-reflective processes are integrated under intellectual virtues**
