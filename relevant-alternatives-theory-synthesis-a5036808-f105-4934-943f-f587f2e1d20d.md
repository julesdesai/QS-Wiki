## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief distinguishes the truth from relevant alternatives, where relevant alternatives do not include merely logically possible but highly improbable situations**
