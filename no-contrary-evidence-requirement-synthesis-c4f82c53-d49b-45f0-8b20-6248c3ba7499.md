# No Contrary Evidence Requirement

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** c4f82c53-d49b-45f0-8b20-6248c3ba7499
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is justified true belief always sufficient without considering contrary evidence?](is-justified-true-belief-always-sufficient-without-considering-contrary-evidence-antithesis-00d3b0cc-2081-4cf4-b724-695e379c8ad9.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is justified**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that no contrary evidence exists**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the belief is justified, the proposition is true, and no contrary evidence exists**
