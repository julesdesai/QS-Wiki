## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed through the exercise of intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition and the belief is formed through the exercise of intellectual virtue**
