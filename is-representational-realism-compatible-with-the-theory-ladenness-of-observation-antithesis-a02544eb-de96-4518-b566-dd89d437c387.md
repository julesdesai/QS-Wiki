# Is Representational Realism compatible with the theory-ladenness of observation?

**Node ID:** a02544eb-de96-4518-b566-dd89d437c387
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Representational Realism](representational-realism-synthesis-2653bf9f-214b-48a1-b757-9a49097d2459.md)

## Content

**The theory-ladenness of observation suggests that what we perceive is influenced by our prior beliefs and theories.**, **If mental representations are influenced by prior knowledge, then they may not correspond accurately to objective reality.**, **Representational Realism, which requires accurate correspondence for knowledge, must reconcile how theory-influenced perceptions fit this requirement.**
