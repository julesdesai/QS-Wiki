## Content

**Knowledge does not depend on the context of the knower but on the subject's needs or stakes**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief in the proposition is safe from error in nearby possible worlds**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **The subject’s stakes can influence whether they know a proposition, but the standards remain invariant across contexts**
