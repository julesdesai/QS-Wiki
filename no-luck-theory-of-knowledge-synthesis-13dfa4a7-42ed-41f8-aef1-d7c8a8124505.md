# No-Luck Theory of Knowledge

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 13dfa4a7-42ed-41f8-aef1-d7c8a8124505
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Do safety conditions suffice for knowledge?](do-safety-conditions-suffice-for-knowledge-antithesis-164fa220-35c6-48bf-962e-5742622ba52d.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the truth of their belief is not due to luck**, **A belief is considered knowledge if it is true and would not have been held true only due to luck**
