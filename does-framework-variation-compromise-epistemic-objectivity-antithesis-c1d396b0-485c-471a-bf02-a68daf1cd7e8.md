# Does framework variation compromise epistemic objectivity?

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which two doctors follow different medical frameworks resulting in conflicting treatment recommendations. Intuitively, we would judge that epistemic objectivity is compromised if framework variation is allowed.**, **There is a plausible principle that medical knowledge should have a degree of objectivity. This principle together with framework-dependent justification leads to conflicting medical practices.**
