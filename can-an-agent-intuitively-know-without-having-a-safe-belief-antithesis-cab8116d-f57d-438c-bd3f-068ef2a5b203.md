# Can an agent intuitively know without having a safe belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** cab8116d-f57d-438c-bd3f-068ef2a5b203
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Safe Intellectual Virtue Epistemology](safe-intellectual-virtue-epistemology-synthesis-f848b9b7-8a1e-463c-8e96-66e43c041eac.md)

## Content

**A belief is safe if in nearby possible situations it remains true.**, **Imagine a person who knows something intuitively, but in many slightly different circumstances, their belief would easily be false.**, **If intuition grants knowledge without safety, it challenges the requirement of safety for knowledge.**
