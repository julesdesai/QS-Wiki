# How does the view handle contradictory stable perceptions?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** deb8c7c9-9aee-4d4d-a8e7-7a3901feb3e2
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Phenomenal Stability View](phenomenal-stability-view-synthesis-fa414e6c-0cf2-4c6a-a8f2-9164813ceb3c.md)

## Content

**Consider a person who consistently perceives a mirage of an oasis while traversing a desert. This stable perception could suggest knowledge of water existence, yet intuitively it leads to false knowledge due to the mirage's illusory nature.**, **It is implausible that stability in perceptual experiences that are known to result from illusions can provide knowledge about external objects or conditions.**
