# Simplified Justification Conditions for Knowledge

**Parent:** [Is defeating evidence capable of transforming what counts as knowledge?](is-defeating-evidence-capable-of-transforming-what-counts-as-knowledge-antithesis-cad95d08-7754-454d-8867-fb7d2dabafbd.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is justified**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the belief is justified, and the proposition is true**
