## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **Knowledge requires that in nearly all nearby possible worlds where the person believes the proposition, the belief is true**, **The safety condition replaces the absence of defeaters as a condition for knowledge**
