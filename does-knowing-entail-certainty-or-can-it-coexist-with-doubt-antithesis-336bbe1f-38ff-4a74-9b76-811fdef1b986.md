# Does knowing entail certainty or can it coexist with doubt?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 336bbe1f-38ff-4a74-9b76-811fdef1b986
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [No-False-Lemma Theory](no-false-lemma-theory-synthesis-5710d9f4-02e8-4ed3-aa41-f8ff2a235ea5.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where an individual believes something with doubts about its truth.**, **In such instances, it is well felt that doubt interferes with the clarity and confidence normally associated with knowledge.**, **The presence of doubt and uncertainty problematizes the transition from true belief to knowledge.**
