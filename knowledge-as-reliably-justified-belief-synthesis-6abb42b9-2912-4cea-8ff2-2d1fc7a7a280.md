# Knowledge as Reliably Justified Belief

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 6abb42b9-2912-4cea-8ff2-2d1fc7a7a280
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is truth always necessary for knowledge?](is-truth-always-necessary-for-knowledge-antithesis-e555f433-ed3d-497b-af8a-84f49cc57705.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified in a reliable way**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition and that the belief is justified in a reliable way**
