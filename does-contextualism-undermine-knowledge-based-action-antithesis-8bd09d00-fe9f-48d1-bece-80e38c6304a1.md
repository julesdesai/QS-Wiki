# Does contextualism undermine knowledge-based action?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 8bd09d00-fe9f-48d1-bece-80e38c6304a1
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism with Reliabilism](contextualism-with-reliabilism-synthesis-c7c3d2a5-9a25-4e3f-9e1f-65010441e024.md)

## Content

**When the standards for knowing a proposition vary with different contexts, decisions and actions based on knowledge claims might be inconsistent or misguided.**, **If the context shifts unexpectedly, a person might act under the assumption of knowing something they actually do not, leading to potentially harmful consequences.**
