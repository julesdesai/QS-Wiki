# Knowledge as Virtue Epistemology

**Parent:** [Can sensitivity accommodate beliefs formed by reliable processes?](can-sensitivity-accommodate-beliefs-formed-by-reliable-processes-antithesis-241f7ce8-b8d6-4976-bfec-2657d4384eb9.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is a result of exercising epistemic virtues**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief results from epistemic virtue**
