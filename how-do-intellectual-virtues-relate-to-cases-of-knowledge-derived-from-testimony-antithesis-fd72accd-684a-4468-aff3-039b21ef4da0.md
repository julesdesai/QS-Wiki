## Content

**Envision a situation where a person believes a true proposition solely based on testimony, without exercising intellectual virtues. Intuitively, we would still attribute knowledge to the person.**, **The reliance on testimony can lead to justified true belief without the exercise of intellectual virtues, posing a challenge to the view.**
