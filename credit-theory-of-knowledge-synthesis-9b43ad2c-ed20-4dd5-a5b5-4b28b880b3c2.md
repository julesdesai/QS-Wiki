## Content

**Knowledge is true belief attributable to the exercise of intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that intellectual virtue is creditable for the true belief**, **A person knows a proposition if they believe it, the proposition is true, and intellectual virtue is creditable for their true belief**
