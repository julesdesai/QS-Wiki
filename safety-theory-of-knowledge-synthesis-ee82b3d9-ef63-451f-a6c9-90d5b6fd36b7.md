## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in most nearby possible worlds where the person believes the proposition, the proposition is true**, **This safety condition avoids knowledge being undermined by luck in the actual world**
