## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief arises from an intellectual virtue**, **An intellectual virtue is a reliable cognitive faculty or skill that contributes to the acquisition of truth**
