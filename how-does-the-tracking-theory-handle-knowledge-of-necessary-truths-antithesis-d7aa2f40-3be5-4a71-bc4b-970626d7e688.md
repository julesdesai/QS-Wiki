# How does the tracking theory handle knowledge of necessary truths?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d7aa2f40-3be5-4a71-bc4b-970626d7e688
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Tracking Theory of Knowledge](tracking-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-dda35d00-5b10-4342-b443-f197350806e5.md)

## Content

**Suppose someone knows a mathematical truth, such as 2+2=4, which is always the case. Intuitively, we would judge this knowledge does not require tracking contingent truths.**, **It is implausible that knowledge of necessary truths requires tracking since these truths do not change across scenarios.**
