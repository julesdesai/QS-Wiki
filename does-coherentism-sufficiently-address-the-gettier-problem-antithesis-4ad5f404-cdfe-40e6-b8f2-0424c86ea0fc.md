# Does coherentism sufficiently address the Gettier problem?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 4ad5f404-cdfe-40e6-b8f2-0424c86ea0fc
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism focuses on systematized justification](coherentism-focuses-on-systematized-justification-synthesis-825f84fd-be44-4c37-9d4c-4b4f5d772a40.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible distinction between true belief and knowledge. This distinction can be inadequately addressed by coherentism due to its focus on mutual support without external verification.**, **Consider a situation where a belief arises correctly by accident within a coherent system. Intuitively, this is a Gettier-style scenario indicating a problem for coherentism.**
