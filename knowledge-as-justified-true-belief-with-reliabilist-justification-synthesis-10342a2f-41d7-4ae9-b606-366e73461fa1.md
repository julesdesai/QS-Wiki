## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have reliable processes that produce true beliefs**, **Justification is grounded in the reliability of the belief-forming process**
