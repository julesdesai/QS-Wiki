# Can someone possess intellectual virtues yet fail to have knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 77533fbe-d5d5-4611-b25f-136d173a66a8
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-72984f03-1ce3-46fd-9fd6-13203e340ace.md)

## Content

**Imagine a person who possesses intellectual virtues but operates in a deceptive environment that constantly feeds them false information. Intuitively, we acknowledge that such a person may not have true beliefs and thus lacks knowledge.**, **Despite possessing intellectual virtues, external factors such as deception can prevent a person from attaining true beliefs.**
