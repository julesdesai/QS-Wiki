# Is the concept of self-evidence subjective rather than objective?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** acfdfe65-7d92-4974-9010-f714257fb510
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundationalism in Epistemology](foundationalism-in-epistemology-synthesis-4c27bb26-6321-4022-8e66-a85ea5d939d9.md)

## Content

**Individuals may have differing intuitions about what is self-evident.**, **If self-evident beliefs can vary between individuals, then they may not serve as a universal foundation.**, **Subjectivity in self-evidence means basic beliefs cannot be uniformly foundational.**
