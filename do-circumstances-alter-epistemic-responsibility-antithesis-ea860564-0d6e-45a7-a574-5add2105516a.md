# Do circumstances alter epistemic responsibility?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ea860564-0d6e-45a7-a574-5add2105516a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Subject-Sensitive Invariantism](subject-sensitive-invariantism-synthesis-2eef6ba9-ea95-4aea-9a96-5c60688dde0e.md)

## Content

**Consider a surgeon deciding on a treatment plan. The high stakes demand thorough knowledge. Intuitively, the weight of responsibility alters the surgeon's standard of knowing.**, **If knowledge varies with circumstances, it demands higher epistemic standards in critical scenarios and lesser ones in trivial situations, challenging a uniform standard for knowledge.**
