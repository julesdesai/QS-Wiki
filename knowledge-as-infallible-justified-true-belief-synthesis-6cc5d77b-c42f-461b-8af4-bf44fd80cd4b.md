**Parent:** [Can beliefs that meet all given criteria still fail to align with common intuitions about knowledge due to luck or coincidence?](can-beliefs-that-meet-all-given-criteria-still-fail-to-align-with-common-intuitions-about-knowledge-due-to-luck-or-coincidence-antithesis-75ce7e44-3f31-49aa-8b44-76c6c79e4c8f.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have infallible justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have infallible justification, and the proposition is true**
