# Does contextualism undermine epistemic responsibility?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3f8958e6-36a9-4742-9e75-759b72f6c2c0
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualist Theory of Knowledge](contextualist-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-56747199-fb08-45b9-8206-960c0717486b.md)

## Content

**Epistemic responsibility involves meeting justifiable knowledge standards consistently.**, **If standards are context-dependent, individuals could avoid epistemic responsibility by manipulating contexts.**, **It is problematic if individuals can shift contexts to escape responsibilities.**
