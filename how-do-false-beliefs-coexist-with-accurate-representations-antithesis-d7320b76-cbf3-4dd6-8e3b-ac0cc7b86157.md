# How do false beliefs coexist with accurate representations?

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Representationalism](representationalism-synthesis-c6ad7a19-c6b2-4a71-b6f5-a8020ca1b42c.md)

## Content

**Consider a person who holds a false belief but forms an accurate mental representation of reality when confronted with evidence. Intuitively, despite the false belief, the accurate representation results in knowledge.**, **There is a plausible principle that accurate representations can override false beliefs to provide knowledge. This principle implies that knowledge can arise even in the presence of false beliefs, challenging their assumed disqualification.**
