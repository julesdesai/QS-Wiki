# Is there a need for a deeper epistemic link than true belief for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c906f39e-ea57-49b4-a534-4c0feef51275
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatism Summary](pragmatism-summary-synthesis-d0b80c94-4161-403a-9bb9-e4c173237700.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where an investor has a true belief about stock growth due to insider information or a hunch, but it's purely coincidental. Intuitively, it seems wrong to say they know, despite the true belief.**, **There is a plausible principle that connects knowledge with a dependable method of forming beliefs rather than mere truth.**
