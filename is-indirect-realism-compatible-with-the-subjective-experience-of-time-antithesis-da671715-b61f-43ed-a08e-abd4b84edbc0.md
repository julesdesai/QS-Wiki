# Is indirect realism compatible with the subjective experience of time?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** da671715-b61f-43ed-a08e-abd4b84edbc0
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Indirect Realism](indirect-realism-synthesis-801f6a7d-c909-434a-bdd9-effe8ebd5964.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which perception lags behind reality due to processing time, such as the delay in hearing thunder after seeing lightning. Intuitively, this scenario suggests a discrepancy between perceived and actual events.**, **There is a plausible principle that perception should accurately reflect the temporal sequence of events. This principle together with the mediation of perception implies potential incompatibility with acknowledging the flow of time.**
