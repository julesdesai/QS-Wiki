## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **Whether a person knows a proposition can depend on the context of the attributor**, **In some contexts, it is sufficient for a person to know a proposition that they believe it, it is true, and they have justification according to the contextually relevant standards**
