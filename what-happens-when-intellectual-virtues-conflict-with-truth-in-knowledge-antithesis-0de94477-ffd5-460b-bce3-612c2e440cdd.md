# What happens when intellectual virtues conflict with truth in knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0de94477-ffd5-460b-bce3-612c2e440cdd
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-fbdf23c1-0af0-48fb-9b70-516c394a97b2.md)

## Content

**A person can exercise intellectual virtues such as open-mindedness and reflection, yet arrive at an incorrect belief due to misleading evidence.**, **It is possible to meet the standards of intellectual virtues without achieving true beliefs, which challenges the notion that virtues are necessary for knowledge.**
