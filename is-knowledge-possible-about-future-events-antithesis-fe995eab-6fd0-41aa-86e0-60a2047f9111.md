# Is knowledge possible about future events?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** fe995eab-6fd0-41aa-86e0-60a2047f9111
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Inference-Based Phenomenalism](inference-based-phenomenalism-synthesis-3c5287e4-c850-454f-b68f-cef5a0c2976c.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which someone predicts accurately a solar eclipse based on scientific calculations. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that the person knows the eclipse will occur.**, **There is a plausible principle that knowledge of future events can exist independently of perceptual experience.**
