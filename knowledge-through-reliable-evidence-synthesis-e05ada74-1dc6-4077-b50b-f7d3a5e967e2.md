## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have reliable evidence for the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have reliable evidence for the proposition and the proposition is true**
