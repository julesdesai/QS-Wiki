# Individual Epistemic Authority with Communal Influence

**Parent:** [Can knowledge be sufficiently justified by flawed communal norms?](can-knowledge-be-sufficiently-justified-by-flawed-communal-norms-antithesis-0ad2696e-d6da-4ed9-beab-ee6b9711676d.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification is individually assessed with reference to communal influence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification withstands individual and communal assessment**
