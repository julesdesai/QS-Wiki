## Content

**A belief is knowledge if it is produced by a safe cognitive process**, **A safe cognitive process is one where, in nearby possible scenarios, the belief would still be true**, **The truth of a belief is a necessary condition for it to be considered knowledge**, **The usefulness of a belief is not necessary for it to be considered knowledge**
