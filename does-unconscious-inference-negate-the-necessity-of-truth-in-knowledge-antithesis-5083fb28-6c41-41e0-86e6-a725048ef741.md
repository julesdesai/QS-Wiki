# Does unconscious inference negate the necessity of truth in knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5083fb28-6c41-41e0-86e6-a725048ef741
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Representationalism with Unconscious Inference](representationalism-with-unconscious-inference-synthesis-76027391-29c7-4fbe-9bae-d64299a42056.md)

## Content

**Unconscious inference might lead us to formulate beliefs that are false or unfounded.**, **For a belief to count as knowledge, it typically must be true.**, **If unconscious inference can lead to the formation and maintenance of untrue beliefs, then its role in constituting knowledge is disputable.**
