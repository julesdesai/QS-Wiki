# What about situations where the truth is unknowable?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b1d0434b-f1f0-4293-9e82-244f6f1f0f14
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Knowledge as Sensitivity](knowledge-as-sensitivity-synthesis-a361f947-df1a-4345-a4d3-ea9ac7712828.md)

## Content

**Suppose a proposition concerns an unknowable fact, such as the exact number of grains of sand on Earth at a moment.**, **A person might accurately guess, but cannot verify or justify the truth of their belief.**, **The sensitivity condition becomes problematic as we cannot determine how beliefs would fare in unknowable scenarios.**
