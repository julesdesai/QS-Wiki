# How does perceptual variation among individuals affect knowledge claims?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** bb716820-7bbc-4a53-aa2c-f957d9ff403b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextual Direct Realism](contextual-direct-realism-synthesis-9053ea14-9a99-4649-aa95-1bc3b223c980.md)

## Content

**Different individuals may perceive the same event in manifold ways depending on cultural, psychological, or situational factors.**, **If perceptual diversity results in varied, context-dependent knowledge claims, reconciling these differences becomes problematic.**, **It is implausible to maintain coherent knowledge claims when individual perceptions diverge significantly.**
