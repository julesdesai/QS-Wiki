## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are infallible reasons for the belief**, **It is sufficient for a person to know a proposition that they believe the proposition, that the proposition is true, and that there are infallible reasons for the belief**
