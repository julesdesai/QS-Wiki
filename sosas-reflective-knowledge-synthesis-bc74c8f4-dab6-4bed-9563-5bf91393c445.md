## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have direct knowledge that is apt, meaning that it arises from cognitive faculties functioning well**, **For a person to possess reflective knowledge, they must have an additional level of knowledge that affirms the reliability of the first-level knowledge**
