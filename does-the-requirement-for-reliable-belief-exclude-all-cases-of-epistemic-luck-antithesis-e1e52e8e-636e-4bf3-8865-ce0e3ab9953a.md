# Does the requirement for reliable belief exclude all cases of epistemic luck?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e1e52e8e-636e-4bf3-8865-ce0e3ab9953a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism](reliabilism-synthesis-41e7a61f-ad45-42cd-8dd7-ba40ca519ba9.md)

## Content

**Epistemic luck occurs when a true belief results from chance rather than a reliable process.**, **Consider a scenario where a person forms a true belief through a lucky guess, despite being generally unreliable.**, **If epistemic luck can lead to true belief, then reliance on reliable processes might not fully preclude knowledge gained by chance.**
