# Knowledge as Relevant Alternatives

**Parent:** [Does sensitivity handle cases where belief is resilient against necessary truths?](does-sensitivity-handle-cases-where-belief-is-resilient-against-necessary-truths-antithesis-a9158467-1604-4b2d-90ee-3dbbd1152e0b.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief to eliminate relevant alternative propositions**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief eliminates relevant alternative propositions**
