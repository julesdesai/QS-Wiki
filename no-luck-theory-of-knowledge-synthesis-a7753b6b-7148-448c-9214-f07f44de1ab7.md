# No-Luck Theory of Knowledge

**Parent:** [Does the causal theory account for the role of luck in knowledge acquisition?](does-the-causal-theory-account-for-the-role-of-luck-in-knowledge-acquisition-antithesis-8111e48b-2d9c-4359-b575-dfacd9548809.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person's belief is not true merely due to luck**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is not true merely due to luck**
