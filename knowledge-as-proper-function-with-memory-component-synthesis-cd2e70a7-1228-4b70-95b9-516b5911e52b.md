## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by faculties functioning properly according to a design plan**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the design plan includes a mechanism for retaining justification over time**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is produced by faculties functioning properly, and the design plan includes a mechanism for retaining justification over time**
