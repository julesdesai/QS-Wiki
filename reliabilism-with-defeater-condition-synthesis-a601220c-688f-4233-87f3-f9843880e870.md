# Reliabilism with Defeater Condition

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** a601220c-688f-4233-87f3-f9843880e870
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can false but systematically correlated beliefs be known?](can-false-but-systematically-correlated-beliefs-be-known-antithesis-0fe34563-ff19-4029-b42d-0e2894579847.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is no defeating evidence against the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief arises from a reliable process, and there is no defeating evidence**
