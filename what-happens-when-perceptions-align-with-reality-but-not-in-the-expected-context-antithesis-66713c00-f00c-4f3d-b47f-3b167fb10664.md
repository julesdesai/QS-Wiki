# What happens when perceptions align with reality but not in the expected context?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 66713c00-f00c-4f3d-b47f-3b167fb10664
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextual Direct Realism](contextual-direct-realism-synthesis-9053ea14-9a99-4649-aa95-1bc3b223c980.md)

## Content

**A person might correctly perceive a rare natural phenomenon, but outside its typical context, it might be misunderstood.**, **Accurate perception without the expected context could lead to false knowledge claims about causality or implications.**, **It is implausible for context-based knowledge to account for accurate but contextually atypical perceptions.**
