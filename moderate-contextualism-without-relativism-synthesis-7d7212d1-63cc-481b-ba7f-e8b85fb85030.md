## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief fits into a moderately context-sensitive system of justification**, **Moderate context-sensitivity refines the degree of justification needed without making it arbitrary**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief fits into a moderately context-sensitive system of justification**
