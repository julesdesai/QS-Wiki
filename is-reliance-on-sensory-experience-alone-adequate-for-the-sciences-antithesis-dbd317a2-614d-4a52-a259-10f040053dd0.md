# Is reliance on sensory experience alone adequate for the sciences?

**Parent:** [Phenomenalism](phenomenalism-synthesis-9cd8e1c9-9ac3-4393-aab4-24acb9d44183.md)

## Content

**Consider the role of theoretical entities in physics, such as electrons, which are not directly perceived but inferred from experimental results and theories. It intuitively suggests the limits of sensory experience for scientific knowledge**, **There is a plausible principle that scientific knowledge requires inference and explanation beyond mere sensory patterns to incorporate theoretical understanding**
