# Are infinite chains of justification pragmatically useless?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9cd1e3f5-16ca-453d-b8a5-8c725ae6469d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism](infinitism-synthesis-d655bb23-a2c0-4c5f-b0d3-18cd986ab587.md)

## Content

**In practical terms, attempting to identify or use an infinite chain of reasons for justification is unmanageable and impractical.**, **A scenario where individuals must provide an endless series of reasons to justify everyday decisions appears intuitively untenable.**, **Complexity and impracticality make infinite chains of justification unsuitable for real-world applications, threatening the functional value of the concept.**
