## Content

**Suppose two reliable cognitive processes within the same individual lead to contradictory beliefs about the same proposition. Intuitively, at least one of the beliefs cannot be knowledge if they contradict each other.**, **Contradictory beliefs challenge the idea that reliability alone can justify knowledge when two processes are equally reliable yet yield different conclusions.**
