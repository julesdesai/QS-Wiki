# Knowledge as Coherentism with Virtuous Agency

**Parent:** [Is knowledge still possible when the knower has no justification for their true belief?](is-knowledge-still-possible-when-the-knower-has-no-justification-for-their-true-belief-antithesis-d9d6141c-ec42-40cd-b83c-8bfd20f51a71.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres with a set of the person's beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief coheres with a set of the person's beliefs, and the belief arises from intellectual virtue**
