# Is it plausible that reliability can be lost but justification retained?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f833946d-3b4d-4cce-8ce9-d44a95b57aa3
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism Summary](reliabilism-summary-synthesis-14b8b19c-565a-493c-b5a5-1d512c9085eb.md)

## Content

**Suppose a normally reliable process suffers a temporary breakdown, resulting in a true belief formed during this period. The belief lacks justification during the malfunction despite beliefs formed previously being justified. Intuitively, malfunction undermines justification for beliefs formed during it.**, **This suggests that reliability needs continuous integrity, or justification can be undermined when reliability is compromised.**
