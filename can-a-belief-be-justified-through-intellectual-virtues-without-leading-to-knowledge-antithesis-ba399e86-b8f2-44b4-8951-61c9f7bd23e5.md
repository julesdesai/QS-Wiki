# Can a belief be justified through intellectual virtues without leading to knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ba399e86-b8f2-44b4-8951-61c9f7bd23e5
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-8a51f623-747d-4b74-932e-e428d202c7f1.md)

## Content

**Consider Isabella who investigates financial markets using critical thinking, concluding an investment will prosper.**, **Despite her rigorous reasoning, the market crashes due to unforeseen circumstances, making her belief false.**, **Her belief was justified and fostered by intellectual virtues but does not constitute knowledge.**
