# Knowledge as No Defeater

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief has no defeater, meaning there is no truth that would undermine the belief if known**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, that the proposition is true, and that there is no defeater for the belief**
