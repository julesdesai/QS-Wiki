## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is the result of the exercise of intellectual virtues**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have a true belief resulting from the exercise of intellectual virtues**
