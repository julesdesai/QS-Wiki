# Does the possibility of false beliefs undermine the concept of justified true belief?

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a person has strong justification for a belief, yet the belief is false. Intuitively, they do not have knowledge, highlighting that justification alone doesn't suffice for knowledge.**
