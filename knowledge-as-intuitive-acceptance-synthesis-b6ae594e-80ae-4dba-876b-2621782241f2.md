## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have an intuitive acceptance of the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the intuitive acceptance aligns with innate cognitive faculties**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have an intuitive acceptance of the proposition, the proposition is true, and the acceptance aligns with innate cognitive faculties**
