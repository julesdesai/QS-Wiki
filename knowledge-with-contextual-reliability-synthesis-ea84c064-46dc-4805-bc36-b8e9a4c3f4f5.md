**Parent:** [When might a belief-forming process fail to produce knowledge through inherent biases?](when-might-a-belief-forming-process-fail-to-produce-knowledge-through-inherent-biases-antithesis-01d2ff91-3807-42ba-aeae-e78af9ca6bc3.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed through a contextually reliable process**, **For a contextually reliable process, it is necessary that the process remains reliable across relevantly similar contexts**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is formed through a contextually reliable process**
