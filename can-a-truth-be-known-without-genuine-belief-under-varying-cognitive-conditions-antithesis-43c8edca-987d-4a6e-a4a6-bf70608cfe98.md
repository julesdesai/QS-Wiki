# Can a truth be known without genuine belief under varying cognitive conditions?

**Node ID:** 43c8edca-987d-4a6e-a4a6-bf70608cfe98

## Content

**Truth alone, without concurrent belief, appears insufficient for knowing if cognitive conditions are compromised, such as in states of confusion or distraction.**, **Consider a distracted individual repeating a truthful statement without awareness or belief. Intuitively, lacking sincere belief, we question whether this qualifies as knowledge.**
