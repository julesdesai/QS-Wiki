# Are future beliefs relevant to present knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 6f9b86e9-fb30-41a8-82cb-c797443dbcdb
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Nozick's Tracking Theory](nozicks-tracking-theory-synthesis-f1663159-56f4-4f6f-b53a-d60dc9dbfcb7.md)

## Content

**Suppose someone possesses a belief that currently tracks the truth, yet they will disbelieve it without new evidence in the future due to external influences. Intuitively, we either dismiss future evidence-irrelevant beliefs' impact on present knowledge or acknowledge its potential impact. If we dismiss it, then knowledge does not track future-sensitivity. If we acknowledge it, past and future irrelevant changes could erroneously question genuine knowledge.**
