# Knowledge as Causal Connection

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** c739502c-082d-4680-b15b-4207f0f52d13
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does belief based on reasoning from true premises always amount to knowledge?](does-belief-based-on-reasoning-from-true-premises-always-amount-to-knowledge-antithesis-a3257e0d-df5f-48ef-a3ce-853125175dac.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is appropriately causally connected to the fact that makes the proposition true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is appropriately causally connected to the fact that makes the proposition true**
