# Does sensitivity account for cases where the reasoning process itself is flawed?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 4104ba06-3f9e-4b2d-bce3-0c2f9cef05bd
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Sensitivity Condition on Knowledge](sensitivity-condition-on-knowledge-synthesis-bfa2da3c-dc32-483c-bef7-ea8100b18339.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person uses an unreliable method of reasoning but happens upon a true conclusion because of accidental correct steps.**, **Even if the proposition were false, the flawed methodology would lead the person to continue believing it. The sensitivity condition thus fails to hold.**
