# Does counting any true belief as knowledge diminish the concept?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 62fa5464-a81d-4b52-8446-7fee2d4833b2
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalism to knowledge with reliable cognitive process](externalism-to-knowledge-with-reliable-cognitive-process-synthesis-e78416a8-ed0b-42d6-b63b-0cc8fec7b97b.md)

## Content

**There could be cases where a person believes a true proposition, yet this belief is so fragile or context-dependent that it hardly seems like real knowledge.**, **We might feel that the mere facticity of a belief is not sufficient to elevate it to knowledge without further substantiation.**, **This suggests that robustness and stability in belief might be key components missing in this definition.**
