## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is the result of a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is true and formed through a cognitive process consistently yielding true beliefs**
