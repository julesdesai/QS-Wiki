# Can contextualist standards be reconciled with the notion of truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9f40abbc-1030-470a-8dd3-bc930f732a94
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism with Variable Justification](contextualism-with-variable-justification-synthesis-53c5a1dc-1391-4651-950b-f21901e771b6.md)

## Content

**Knowledge requires true belief according to contextualism. It is implausible that truth can vary depending on context.**, **Suppose a person knows a proposition in one context but not in another, assuming the truth remains constant. Intuitively, we would judge that truth should remain context-independent.**
