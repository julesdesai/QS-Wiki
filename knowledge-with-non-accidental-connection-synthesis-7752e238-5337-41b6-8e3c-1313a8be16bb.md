# Knowledge with Non-Accidental Connection

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 7752e238-5337-41b6-8e3c-1313a8be16bb
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is a causal connection between belief and truth always sufficient?](is-a-causal-connection-between-belief-and-truth-always-sufficient-antithesis-189bd7e7-42d5-4d84-a363-3b7a1dc82107.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is caused by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the reliable cognitive process tracks the truth in a non-accidental manner**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, their belief is caused by a reliable cognitive process, and that process tracks the truth non-accidentally**
