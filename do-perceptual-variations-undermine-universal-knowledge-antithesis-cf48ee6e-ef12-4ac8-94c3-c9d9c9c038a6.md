# Do perceptual variations undermine universal knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** cf48ee6e-ef12-4ac8-94c3-c9d9c9c038a6
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Constructivist Phenomenalism](constructivist-phenomenalism-synthesis-f704c8d8-fd4e-46dd-9dda-e9ccb460bf16.md)

## Content

**Human perception varies significantly across individuals due to sensory differences.**, **These perceptual differences can lead to varied interpretations of the same stimulus.**, **If knowledge is based on perception, then perceptual variation may undermine claims to universal knowledge.**
