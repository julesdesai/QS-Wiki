# Does idealism lead to solipsism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 8a7c465b-b26a-438a-b106-4a76ce3d6b57
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Idealism](idealism-synthesis-53ec409c-4a9b-4261-be64-9dda9f92eb94.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that others' mental frameworks do not lead to their knowledge.**, **Suppose we have a case in which a person claims their reality is the only construct, doubting the existence of others' perceptions. Intuitively, we either have to say they are correct or they are subject to solipsism. If we say they are correct, then others are denied existence. If we say they are subject to solipsism, then idealism may imply solipsism.**
