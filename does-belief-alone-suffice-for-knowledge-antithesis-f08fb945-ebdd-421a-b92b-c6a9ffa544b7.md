# Does belief alone suffice for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f08fb945-ebdd-421a-b92b-c6a9ffa544b7
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherent Infinitism](coherent-infinitism-synthesis-f2c99927-f862-4ee8-8b5b-2fefd2717ee2.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person believes a false proposition but is entirely convincing in their belief. Intuitively, merely believing a proposition does not amount to knowing it if it is false.**, **It is implausible that believing a proposition alone, without any consideration of truth or proper justification, can constitute knowledge.**
