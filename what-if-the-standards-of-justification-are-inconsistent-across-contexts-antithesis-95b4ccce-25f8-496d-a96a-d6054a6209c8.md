# What if the standards of justification are inconsistent across contexts?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 95b4ccce-25f8-496d-a96a-d6054a6209c8
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism with Practical Justification](contextualism-with-practical-justification-synthesis-ea5cbe4f-fc61-4beb-8391-b56cff88451e.md)

## Content

**In one context, a belief may meet the standards of justification, but in another context, it may fail to meet those standards.**, **If justification is sensitive to varying standards, then the consistency of the concept of knowledge is undermined.**
