# Can knowledge persist even when counterfactual beliefs are unchanged?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** fdd8ae17-dbf5-4567-9753-f931f876308b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Sensitivity Theory of Knowledge](sensitivity-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-102d682a-8eb5-4983-bf10-f287ee1765f9.md)

## Content

**Suppose a scientist believes a theory based on robust evidence, and continues to believe it even if the evidence were slightly different. Intuitively, the scientist still knows the theory.**, **Knowledge seems to require strong evidence rather than sensitivity to all possible false scenarios.**
