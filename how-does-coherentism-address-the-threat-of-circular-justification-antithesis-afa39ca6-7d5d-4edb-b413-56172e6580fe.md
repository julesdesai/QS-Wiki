# How does coherentism address the threat of circular justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** afa39ca6-7d5d-4edb-b413-56172e6580fe
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Integrated Coherentism](integrated-coherentism-synthesis-026ba0da-a1c0-49d0-b737-4a448975b203.md)

## Content

**Take a case where a belief in a coherent system is justified by another belief, which in turn is justified by the original belief. Intuitively, such circularity seems to undermine genuine justification.**, **There is a plausible principle that justification should not rely on circular reasoning. This principle implies that a coherent belief system suffering from circular justification lacks true integrity and reliability.**
