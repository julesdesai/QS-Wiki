## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is supported by an infinite chain of reasons**, **Epistemic justification arises from both foundational beliefs and coherence among beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they possess foundational beliefs, the belief system is coherent, they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is supported by an infinite chain of reasons**
