**Parent:** [Can one really know propositions about necessary truths where falsity is impossible?](can-one-really-know-propositions-about-necessary-truths-where-falsity-is-impossible-antithesis-689de924-edaf-4b01-b508-9402b3f86465.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **whether a belief counts as knowledge depends on the context**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that in the given context they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is contextually justified**
