## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by cognitive faculties functioning properly**, **For a person to know a proposition, conscious reflection on the justification is not required**
