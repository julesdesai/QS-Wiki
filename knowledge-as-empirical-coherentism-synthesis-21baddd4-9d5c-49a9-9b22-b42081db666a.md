# Knowledge as Empirical Coherentism

**Parent:** [Does infinitist coherentism meet the demand for epistemic accessibility?](does-infinitist-coherentism-meet-the-demand-for-epistemic-accessibility-antithesis-e5c30c82-c37e-49d4-a776-434c09144d46.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres with empirical justifying beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief coheres with empirical justifying beliefs**
