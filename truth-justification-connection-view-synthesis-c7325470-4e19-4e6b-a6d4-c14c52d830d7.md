## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification that is closely connected to the truth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is not necessary for them to believe the proposition**, **A person knows a proposition if it is true and their justification is closely connected to the truth**
