# Can someone have knowledge of future facts based on reliable predictions?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1322b1b5-f2c0-4b64-b812-c3acb3d40e78
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Causal Theory of Knowledge](causal-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-2f0f13d0-725f-4ae5-ba67-2c976061582b.md)

## Content

**Reliable predictions can be both justified and true, due to their basis on scientific evidence, without any causal link to future facts.**, **In a case where scientists predict an eclipse accurately due to mathematical models, there would be no causal connection to the future event, yet the belief might be considered knowledge.**
