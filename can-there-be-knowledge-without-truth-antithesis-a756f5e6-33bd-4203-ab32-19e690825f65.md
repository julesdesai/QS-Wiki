# Can there be knowledge without truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a756f5e6-33bd-4203-ab32-19e690825f65
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Knowledge as Safe Belief](knowledge-as-safe-belief-synthesis-12197f2f-4452-4dfc-962f-79c34ef8ef65.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person has justified belief in a proposition that is likely true based on reliable evidence. This appears as knowledge under many circumstances.**, **If the proposition turns out to be false due to unforeseen factors, it challenges the requirement that truth is necessary for knowledge.**
