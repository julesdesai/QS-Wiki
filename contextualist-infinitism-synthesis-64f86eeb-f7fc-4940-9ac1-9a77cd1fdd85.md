## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is supported by an infinite chain of reasons**, **The sufficiency and necessity of conditions for knowledge can vary based on context**, **In some contexts, coherence in a belief system may be necessary for knowledge**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the context determines the conditions are met, they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is supported by an infinite chain of reasons**
