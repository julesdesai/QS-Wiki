# Can genuine knowledge arise from brief, non-stable traits?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** caf01979-410c-431a-a3be-4cf3f052696a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-91237602-868c-4a49-88c0-4e168ec082b8.md)

## Content

**Sometimes a person can have a true belief through a momentary intellectual insight, which is not a stable trait.**, **If such insights occasionally yield true beliefs, this challenges the notion that only stable traits lead to knowledge.**
