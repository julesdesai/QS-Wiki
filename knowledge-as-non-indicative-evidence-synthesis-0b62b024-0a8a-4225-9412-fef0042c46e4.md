# Knowledge as Non-Indicative Evidence

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Does a failure to have conscious awareness of evidence block knowledge?](does-a-failure-to-have-conscious-awareness-of-evidence-block-knowledge-antithesis-11471942-2a73-4ab5-8201-52dff5fd0dba.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief be supported by non-conscious evidence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is supported by non-conscious evidence**
