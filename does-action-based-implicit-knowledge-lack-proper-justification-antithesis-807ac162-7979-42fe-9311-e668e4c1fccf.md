# Does action-based implicit knowledge lack proper justification?

## Content

**Implicit knowledge demonstrated through actions may be unaccompanied by an explicit rational basis.**, **Consider a case where someone correctly performs calculations in their head without understanding the mathematical rules consciously.**, **Without a conscious understanding, the person lacks justification for asserting they know the correct outcomes.**
