# How does Constructivism contend with the shared sensory perceptions across different individuals?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 8380b32e-5464-4131-86f4-e50bc0891a01
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Constructivism Summary](constructivism-summary-synthesis-38220637-7d58-4a33-978f-6de4b5b7771f.md)

## Content

**In shared sensory activities, such as attending a concert, multiple individuals report similar auditory experiences regardless of their diverse backgrounds and prior experiences. This suggests that some perceptual knowledge might be formatted in a manner independent of active individual construction, challenging the constructivist account of knowledge creation.**
