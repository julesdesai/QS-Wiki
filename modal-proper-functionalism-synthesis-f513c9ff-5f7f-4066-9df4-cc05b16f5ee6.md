## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is counterfactually dependent on truth**, **Proper functioning includes design plan functioning in an appropriate environment**, **Counterfactual dependence ensures that the belief would not have occurred in the presence of falsehood**
