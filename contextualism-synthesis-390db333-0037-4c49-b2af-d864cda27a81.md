## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief meets the truth conditions prevailing in their context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that all epistemic standards of the context are met and the belief is both true and held**
