# Can knowledge be knowledge if based on coincidental truths?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 6af1cfaa-2c78-4012-9b31-b0330afcc730
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatically Useful True Belief](pragmatically-useful-true-belief-synthesis-e72da4db-a488-4cc6-b424-04d735c0cd87.md)

## Content

**A person believes a particular lottery ticket will win, it does win, though there was no basis for such belief other than chance.**, **The belief was true and resulted in practical implications, such as monetary gain, yet it seems intuitively that this is not knowledge.**, **There is a plausible principle that knowledge should not be founded on mere coincidence.**
