# Are all cognitive character traits reliably leading to truth intellectual virtues?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1e0521bc-c865-499e-ab3f-78ee48ea0b9f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-0020c014-7b91-4782-b27a-7c265359a5c7.md)

## Content

**Suppose a character trait known to produce true beliefs in chaotic or unpredictable environments is considered.**, **Not all traits ensuring true belief under certain conditions define intellectual virtues as they lack general reliability.**, **The nature of traits that coincidentally yield truth but are not systematically rigorous challenges their classification as intellectual virtues.**, **Intellectual virtues demand a consistent result of true belief, distinguishing random true-belief traits from reliable character virtues.**
