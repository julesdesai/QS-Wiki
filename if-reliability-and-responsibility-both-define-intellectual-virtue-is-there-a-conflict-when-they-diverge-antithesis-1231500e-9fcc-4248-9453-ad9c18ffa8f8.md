# If reliability and responsibility both define intellectual virtue, is there a conflict when they diverge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1231500e-9fcc-4248-9453-ad9c18ffa8f8
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Mixed Virtue Epistemology](mixed-virtue-epistemology-synthesis-9b1d3c06-dd01-4be5-8e49-871c85befc1a.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person uses a highly reliable yet morally irresponsible method, like blackmail, to gain true beliefs. Intuitively, such beliefs do not seem to be true knowledge.**, **It is implausible that reliability alone, without moral responsibility, satisfies the conditions for knowledge, suggesting a potential tension in cases of divergence between reliability and responsibility.**
