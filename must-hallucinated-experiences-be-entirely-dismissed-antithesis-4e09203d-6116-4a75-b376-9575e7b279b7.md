# Must hallucinated experiences be entirely dismissed?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 4e09203d-6116-4a75-b376-9575e7b279b7
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Verificationism](verificationism-synthesis-742a7e84-42a2-4d13-8c35-af2e03d93250.md)

## Content

**A person experiencing hallucinations can derive knowledge about what they feel during an episode without requiring external verification**, **If hallucinations cannot be verified and thus do not constitute knowledge, then internal experiences during hallucinations would be dismissed as non-knowledge**, **It is implausible to dismiss the subjective knowledge gained from experiences even if they do not align with external reality**
