## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their true belief be the result of exercising intellectual virtues**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief is true and formed through intellectual virtue**
