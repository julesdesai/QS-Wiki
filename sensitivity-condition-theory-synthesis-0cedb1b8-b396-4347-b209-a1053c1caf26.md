# Sensitivity Condition Theory

**Parent:** [Does a belief produced by a reliable process become infallible knowledge?](does-a-belief-produced-by-a-reliable-process-become-infallible-knowledge-antithesis-bc4d98ed-f7ee-4df4-81f3-1501fdbbdf6d.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is sensitive to changes in the truth of the proposition**
