## Content

**It is implausible that philosophical discourse, which often aims for clarity and shared understanding, can thoroughly work with justification standards that vary contextually.**, **Suppose we have a philosophical discussion where participants depend on differing context-sensitive justifications. Intuitively, this would lead to confusion or misunderstanding about what is justified knowledge.**
