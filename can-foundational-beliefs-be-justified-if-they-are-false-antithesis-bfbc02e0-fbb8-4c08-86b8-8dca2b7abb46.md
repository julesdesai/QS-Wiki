# Can foundational beliefs be justified if they are false?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** bfbc02e0-fbb8-4c08-86b8-8dca2b7abb46
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundationalism with Reliable Basic Beliefs](foundationalism-with-reliable-basic-beliefs-synthesis-62bc80a9-ef75-4d49-9578-a29890370bd2.md)

## Content

**A foundational belief is one that does not derive justification from other beliefs.**, **A foundational belief can be false even if it doesn't derive justification from other beliefs.**, **A belief that is false cannot provide a basis for knowledge because knowledge requires truth.**
