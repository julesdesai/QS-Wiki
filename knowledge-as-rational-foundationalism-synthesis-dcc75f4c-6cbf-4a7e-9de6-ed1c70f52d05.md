**Parent:** [Can foundational beliefs be too subjective?](can-foundational-beliefs-be-too-subjective-antithesis-7019339f-85ba-483c-be8f-103fbfc5ef01.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief coheres with a network of foundational beliefs rooted in reason**, **The foundational beliefs are justified through a rational process rather than pure subjectivity**
