# Knowledge as True Belief with Comprehensive Competency

**Parent:** [Does the conceptual analysis capture all forms of knowledge?](does-the-conceptual-analysis-capture-all-forms-of-knowledge-antithesis-6bbc6995-b321-42cb-9ed3-065f88dcaeb9.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed through comprehensive competency**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is formed through comprehensive competency**
