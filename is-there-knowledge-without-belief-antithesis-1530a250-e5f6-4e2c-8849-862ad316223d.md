# Is there knowledge without belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1530a250-e5f6-4e2c-8849-862ad316223d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism: Reliable Processes](reliabilism-reliable-processes-synthesis-f70f1f60-dbef-46bd-b415-215191e514dd.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a person has true information stored unconsciously and can access it only under specific conditions that do not involve belief. Intuitively, it is questionable whether this amounts to knowledge.**, **There is a plausible principle that suggests knowledge is distinct from mere possession of information.**
