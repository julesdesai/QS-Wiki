# Does all evidence tie sufficiently to truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3ff5cb48-8f75-473f-8a5e-7123dfdd15ce
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Grounding Knowledge in Evidence](grounding-knowledge-in-evidence-synthesis-000c43a5-6d20-4fdc-9093-7ec5ca2a4c8d.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person has strong but irrelevant evidence to support a belief that happens to be true. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that the irrelevant evidence does not sufficiently tie the belief to the truth.**, **There is a plausible principle that only evidence directly related to the truth supports a claim to knowledge.**
