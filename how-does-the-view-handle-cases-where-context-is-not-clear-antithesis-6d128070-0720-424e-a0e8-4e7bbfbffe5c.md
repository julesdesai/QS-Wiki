# How does the view handle cases where context is not clear?

## Content

**Consider a scenario where there is ambiguity in determining the context of a conversation. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that it becomes difficult to know the precise standard of justification required.**, **A case where context is unclear could lead to different individuals applying conflicting standards. This would cause confusion about whether knowledge is present.**
