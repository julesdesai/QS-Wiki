# Knowledge Requires Strong Justification

**Parent:** [Is knowledge as context-sensitive justification vulnerable to Gettier problems?](is-knowledge-as-context-sensitive-justification-vulnerable-to-gettier-problems-antithesis-131d6257-86d6-4ec7-b29e-fd311d3c2f01.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is strongly justified, where strong justification excludes factors like luck**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is strongly justified, where strong justification excludes factors like luck**
