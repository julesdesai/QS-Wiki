## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **Knowledge requires an infinite chain of reasons for the belief that are non-repeating**, **Each reason in the chain must genuinely support the belief's truth**
