# Is equating knowledge with justified true belief too simplistic?

## Content

**Consider a case where a person holds a true belief with logical fallacies in reasoning. This suggests that something more than justification, truth, and belief is necessary.**, **There is a plausible principle that holistic reasoning and accurate understanding, not just belief correctness, are essential for knowledge, questioning the sufficiency of justified true belief.**
