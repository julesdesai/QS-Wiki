# Does intellectual virtue always ensure truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** be9e8b78-065a-4e94-b0a9-368171df5c81
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Safe Intellectual Virtue Epistemology](safe-intellectual-virtue-epistemology-synthesis-f848b9b7-8a1e-463c-8e96-66e43c041eac.md)

## Content

**Knowledge is true belief arising from intellectual virtue.**, **A person might exhibit intellectual virtues but reach false beliefs due to unreliable evidence or deception.**, **If a belief arising from intellectual virtue can still be false, then not all intellectually virtuous beliefs amount to knowledge.**
