## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed by a reliable process in some relevant contexts**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there are contexts where the belief is justified by a reliable process**
