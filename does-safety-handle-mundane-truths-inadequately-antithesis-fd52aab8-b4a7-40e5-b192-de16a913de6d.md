Intuitively, such beliefs are strong cases for knowledge due to our consistently reliable methods, but some argue safety could be questioned due to possible global disasters.**, **A person knows a proposition if the proposition is true. Despite true propositions, concerns about temporal changes suggest difficulties with mundane truths in the safety framework.**
