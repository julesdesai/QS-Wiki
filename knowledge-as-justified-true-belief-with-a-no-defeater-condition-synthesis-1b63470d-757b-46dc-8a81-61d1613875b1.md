# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with a No-Defeater Condition

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does safety wrongly imply infallibility in certain cases?](does-safety-wrongly-imply-infallibility-in-certain-cases-antithesis-8a2a0e71-b097-437c-9357-db71208e0577.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no defeaters to their justification for believing the proposition**
