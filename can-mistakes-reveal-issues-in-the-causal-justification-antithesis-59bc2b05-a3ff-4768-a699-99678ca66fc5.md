## Content

**Instances exist where beliefs result from causal processes that usually lead to knowledge but result in false beliefs, calling into question reliance on causality alone, such as reliance on a normally reliable perceptual process producing a visual illusion.**, **A causal connection that sometimes leads to falsehood suggests that it might not be sufficient for knowledge.**
