# Is reliability a necessary condition of intellectual virtue?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d9f27c8f-da4d-45d7-baa0-cf39f7d034a0
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Revised Virtue Epistemology](revised-virtue-epistemology-synthesis-e97d218b-557b-43e8-afcd-587c60b67e41.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a person's belief is true but formed in a way that is typically unreliable. Intuitively, the truth of this belief does not arise from intellectual virtue if it is not reliably formed.**, **A belief is not reliably formed if it results from an unreliable process, challenging the association with intellectual virtue.**
