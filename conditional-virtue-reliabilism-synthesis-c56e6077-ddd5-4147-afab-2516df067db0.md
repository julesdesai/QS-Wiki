## Content

**Knowledge is true belief that is conditionally reliant on intellectual virtues or reliable processes**, **Intellectual virtues include character traits like open-mindedness and fairness**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient if their belief arises from reliable processes or virtuous character traits, depending on context**
