# Is context-sensitivity in justification too vague to provide clear epistemic criteria?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3699423f-a1b2-42f3-834c-c95b27cb4a27
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Epistemological Disjunctivism](epistemological-disjunctivism-synthesis-82cbae05-c2e1-4b4d-af67-987574976695.md)

## Content

**Consider the case of receiving new information at different times that alters one's context-sensitive justification. It is implausible that continuously shifting contexts provide transparent criteria for the justification of knowledge.**
