## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **Knowledge requires that the belief could not easily be false in nearby possible worlds**, **The safety of the belief, rather than the absolute absence of defeaters, secures the justification status of belief**
