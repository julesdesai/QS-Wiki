## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **A belief is safe if it is true in most nearby possible worlds where it is held**, **Distant possible worlds are disregarded if it is rational to consider them irrelevant given the current epistemic situation**
