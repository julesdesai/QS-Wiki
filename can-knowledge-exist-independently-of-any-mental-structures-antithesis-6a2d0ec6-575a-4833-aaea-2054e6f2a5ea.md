# Can knowledge exist independently of any mental structures?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 6a2d0ec6-575a-4833-aaea-2054e6f2a5ea
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Empirical Idealism](empirical-idealism-synthesis-57e1bbec-13ac-4c5c-a0b5-84a9cd66f614.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that all knowledge requires the recognition of the mind's active role in shaping perception.**, **Consider a case in which an infant knows that certain objects are food sources without any developed recognition of mental influence.**, **There can be plausible cases where individuals have knowledge without any awareness of mental structures shaping reality.**
