## Content

**Contextual factors, such as environment and background information, affect how foundational beliefs are evaluated.**, **If foundational beliefs are influenced by contextual factors, their status as inherently foundational is undermined.**, **A theory of knowledge must account for the role of context, which foundationalism traditionally ignores.**
