## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have an infinite chain of non-repeating reasons for their belief**, **A belief is knowledge if supported by an infinite regress of reasons**
