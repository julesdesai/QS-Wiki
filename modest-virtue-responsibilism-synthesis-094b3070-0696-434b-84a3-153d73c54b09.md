## Content

**Knowledge is true belief formed through the exercise of intellectual virtue and responsibility**, **Intellectual virtues include character traits like open-mindedness and fairness**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **Intellectual virtue contributes to, but is not sufficient for, knowledge**, **A belief formed through intellectual virtue can be epistemically commendable even if the proposition is false**
