# Knowledge as Virtuous Belief

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Could a justified equilibrium exist without true propositions?](could-a-justified-equilibrium-exist-without-true-propositions-antithesis-ae86d138-3016-4f06-a7d4-13ee33044462.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed through the exercise of intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is formed through intellectual virtue**
