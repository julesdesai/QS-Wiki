## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the cognitive process is reliable in a fixed environment**, **The reliability of a cognitive process does not vary with environmental changes**
