# Can knowledge be undermined by Gettier-like cases?

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person believes a true proposition based on evidence that is independently verified, yet this evidence is coincidentally misleading. Intuitively, we would judge that the person does not have knowledge.**, **Consider a scenario in which a person believes a proposition that happens to be true, but their belief is based on faulty reasoning or luck. Intuitively, we would judge that the person does not have knowledge.**
