# Knowledge with Infallible Internal Justification

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 965ec20d-3c16-49f5-9b90-7855db0e09d4
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does infallible evidence lead to skepticism?](does-infallible-evidence-lead-to-skepticism-antithesis-1dc61fb7-a677-42df-9364-bc1bb747d787.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their internal justification for the belief is incapable of being overridden by any possible evidence**
