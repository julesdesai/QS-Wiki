# Knowledge with Safety Condition

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can a person know something when belief changes upon minor variations?](can-a-person-know-something-when-belief-changes-upon-minor-variations-antithesis-f0cdce38-10b8-410b-a4bd-21ae62e650ab.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if a belief were slightly different, it would still be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and in nearby possible situations, the belief is still true**
