## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no easily possible scenarios in which the process would produce a falsehood**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is produced by a process where there are no nearby possible worlds in which it produces falsehood**
