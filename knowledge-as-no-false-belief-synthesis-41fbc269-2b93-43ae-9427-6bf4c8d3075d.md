# Knowledge as No False Belief

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 41fbc269-2b93-43ae-9427-6bf4c8d3075d
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge exist without an appropriate causal connection?](can-knowledge-exist-without-an-appropriate-causal-connection-antithesis-ba73403c-c328-4724-a32b-1deeca8a4af3.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there be no false beliefs used in the reasoning leading to the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there are no false beliefs used in the reasoning**
