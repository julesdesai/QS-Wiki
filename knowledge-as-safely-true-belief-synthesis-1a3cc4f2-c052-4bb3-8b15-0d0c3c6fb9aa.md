# Knowledge as Safely True Belief

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Is a coherent but false belief system incompatible with externalism?](is-a-coherent-but-false-belief-system-incompatible-with-externalism-antithesis-99c1f630-c150-4524-aef6-2ad102a342f6.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief could not have easily been false**, **A belief is safely true if in nearby possible worlds where the person believes the proposition, it is true**
