# Can infinite reasons be relevant to justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a858a942-b773-418e-a5ae-3186921b1a24
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism](infinitism-synthesis-db00e7c1-6a13-4ca5-8f09-6fae7cd29e0f.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which reasons extend infinitely but become less relevant to the original belief over time. Intuitively, we would judge that such reasons do not contribute to genuine justification.**, **It is implausible that merely having an infinite number of reasons, regardless of their relevance, results in justification for a belief.**
