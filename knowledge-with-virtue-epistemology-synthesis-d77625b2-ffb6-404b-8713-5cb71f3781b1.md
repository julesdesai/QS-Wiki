# Knowledge with Virtue Epistemology

**Parent:** [Is it possible for a reliably formed belief to be false?](is-it-possible-for-a-reliably-formed-belief-to-be-false-antithesis-8de9376a-a3a8-44ef-ace0-e81263f6b3ce.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is the result of an intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is the result of an intellectual virtue**
