## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief has foundational justification in a relevant context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there is foundational justification in a context**
