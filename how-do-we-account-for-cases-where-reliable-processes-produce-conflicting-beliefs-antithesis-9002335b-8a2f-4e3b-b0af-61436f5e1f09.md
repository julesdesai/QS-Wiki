# How do we account for cases where reliable processes produce conflicting beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9002335b-8a2f-4e3b-b0af-61436f5e1f09
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism about Knowledge](reliabilism-about-knowledge-synthesis-c84f45b2-357a-4b29-be66-6a09ffb029e2.md)

## Content

**Suppose two individuals with similar reliable cognitive processes form contradictory beliefs about the same event based on the same kind of sensory perception. Intuitively, both cannot have knowledge if their conclusions conflict**, **It is implausible for reliable processes alone to always produce knowledge if they can lead to mutually exclusive conclusions**
