# Robust Safety Theory Against Expert Disagreement

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, the belief must not be easily false even if it encounters conflicting expert opinions**, **A belief is safe if it is not false in nearby possible worlds where opposing expert views exist but the method of belief formation is reliable**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief is true in a wide range of similar and nearby situations, including those with expert disagreement**, **Knowledge is resistant to being easily false under both minor changes and significant variances in expert consensus**
