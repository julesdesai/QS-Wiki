## Content

**Social norms and group dynamics can strongly influence how individuals perceive events and propositions.**, **If perception is modified by social context, it can lead to conformist or biased knowledge claims that reflect societal pressures rather than independent reality.**, **Knowledge claims influenced by social context may not provide a true representation of reality, thus undermining their credibility.**
