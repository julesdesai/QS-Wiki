## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person has infallible reasons for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is only possible if their justification rules out all possibilities of error**
