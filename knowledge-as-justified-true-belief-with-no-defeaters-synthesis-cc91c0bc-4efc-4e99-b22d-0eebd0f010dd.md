# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with No Defeaters

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** cc91c0bc-4efc-4e99-b22d-0eebd0f010dd
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is causal connection truly necessary for knowledge?](is-causal-connection-truly-necessary-for-knowledge-antithesis-b5c2e61a-2b67-4fea-8fe8-3f874b2b3541.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no defeaters to the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, the proposition is true, and there are no defeaters to the belief**
