# Can fixed standards capture the richness of epistemic judgments?

## Content

**Epistemic judgments often include a range of considerations, including reliability, context, and importance, beyond mere belief and truth.**, **A nuanced judgment might recognize knowledge in one setting yet not in another due to subtle contextual factors.**, **Fixed standards for knowledge risk oversimplifying the varied and complex nature of epistemic evaluation in practice.**
