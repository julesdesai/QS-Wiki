## Content

**A person knows a proposition if it is a true belief produced by an intellectual virtue**, **An intellectual virtue is a trait that reliably leads to truth, like open-mindedness or careful reasoning**, **Knowledge arises when a true belief is the result of an exercise of one's intellectual virtues**, **Beliefs formed through intellectual virtues are distinct from those formed by luck or accident**
