## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is produced by a reliable process**, **A reliable process is one that produces a high ratio of true beliefs**, **Awareness of the causal chain is not necessary for a belief to be considered knowledge**
