# Can the Safety Condition handle beliefs formed through memory?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 92e24124-a580-4074-a5ce-c306cb89d84e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Safety Condition View](safety-condition-view-synthesis-739cc2e2-5111-4640-9af6-f9324f258620.md)

## Content

**Imagine a person recalls the phone number of a friend accurately due to frequent reminders. Intuitively, this belief is based on reliable memory.**, **In nearby possible scenarios where reminders are forgotten or jumbled, the belief could easily be false, challenging its safety despite being formed through a reliable process.**
