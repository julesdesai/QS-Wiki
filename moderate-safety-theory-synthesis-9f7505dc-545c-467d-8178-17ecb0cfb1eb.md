# Moderate Safety Theory

## Content

**A person knows a proposition if they have a true belief**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief is moderately safe**, **A belief is moderately safe if, in a reasonable range of nearby possible worlds where the belief is held, it is not false**, **A reasonable range is determined by standards of epistemic justification**
