# How do high-context settings impact justification equivalence?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5543a187-8a0b-4845-95bb-01708b015527
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextual Justificationism](contextual-justificationism-synthesis-1b60e9a8-d801-480b-8adb-e726a7af0440.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a complex scientific theory is known in an academic setting but not in a lay setting; intuitively, differing contextual thresholds of understanding should not preclude equivalent justification.**, **If knowledge depends on context-specific standards, then it is implausible to equate justification quality across contexts.**
