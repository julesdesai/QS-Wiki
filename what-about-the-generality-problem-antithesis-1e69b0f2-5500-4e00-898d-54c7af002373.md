# What about the generality problem?

**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism Summary](reliabilism-summary-synthesis-c54e9ff1-39e1-4224-b3be-b0979177f411.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where someone forms a belief using a reliable process, but the process can be described at various levels of generality, such as "using vision" vs. "seeing from afar in fog." Intuitively, reliability may vary based on the chosen description, causing confusion about the justification of the belief.**, **Reliabilism struggles to specify which level of generality to apply to processes, thereby impacting the certainty of reliability-driven justification.**
