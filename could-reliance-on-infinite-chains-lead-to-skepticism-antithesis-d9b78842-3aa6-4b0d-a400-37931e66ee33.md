# Could reliance on infinite chains lead to skepticism?

## Content

**Suppose the requirement of infinite chains for justification results in few or no beliefs being properly justified.**, **In such a case, the ubiquity of unjustified beliefs could foster a form of intellectual skepticism.**, **There is a plausible principle that suggests epistemic theories should avoid leading to unreasonable skepticism.**
