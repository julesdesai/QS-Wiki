## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition be true**, **A belief is held safely if it would not be easily false in nearby possible worlds**, **A person knows a proposition if they have a belief that is both true and safe**
