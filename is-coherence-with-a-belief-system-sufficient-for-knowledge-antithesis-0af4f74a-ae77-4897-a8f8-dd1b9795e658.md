# Is coherence with a belief system sufficient for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0af4f74a-ae77-4897-a8f8-dd1b9795e658
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism: System of Beliefs](coherentism-system-of-beliefs-synthesis-446f5d76-49b9-4fbc-a49a-1aaf884652f2.md)

## Content

**A belief system can be internally coherent but completely detached from reality.**, **If a belief system is detached from reality, then a belief's coherence with that system does not ensure its truth.**, **A belief must be both justified and true to qualify as knowledge.**, **Thus, coherence alone is not sufficient for transforming a true belief into knowledge.**
