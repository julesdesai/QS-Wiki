# Does knowledge require more than just truth and reliability?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c50223c1-0b20-4929-a6d3-116f8a5cda19
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilist Theory of Knowledge](reliabilist-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-f16b250f-5f50-4247-9620-ff9329c04fe6.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person forms a true belief based on a reliable process but lacks any justification or supporting evidence. Intuitively, it seems they might not fully know it.**, **The principle that knowledge involves not only reliability and truth but also some form of justification challenges the view that reliability alone is sufficient for knowledge.**
