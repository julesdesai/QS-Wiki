# Are foundational beliefs accessible to introspection or universally agreed upon?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d9a83ff1-685f-4d59-8beb-0e532e7eb15b
**Depth:** 2
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Knowledge as Foundationalism](knowledge-as-foundationalism-thesis-025d3817-5b61-4fc3-a2ef-7f3fb1b6afd0.md)

## Content

**A belief is foundational if it requires no further justification and is immediately accessible.**, **However, introspective access to beliefs varies among individuals, leading to disagreement on what counts as foundational.**, **If foundational beliefs are not universally accessible or agreed upon, it challenges their role as a secure basis for knowledge.**
