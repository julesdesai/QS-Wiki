# How does learning impact the coherence model?

## Content

**A child learning new concepts frequently changes and disrupts their existing belief system.**, **Intuitively, learning involves integrating experiences that may initially lack coherence with pre-existing beliefs.**, **It is implausible that every new learning experience immediately contributes to a more coherent system.**
