# Can the barn facade scenario challenge the tracking theory of knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f97f5047-669d-413f-a51c-4fa7ae22ecd8
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Nozick's Tracking Theory](nozicks-tracking-theory-synthesis-56c7bb47-47e8-422c-831e-e887399874aa.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person driving through a countryside full of fake barn facades sees the only real barn and believes it is a barn. Intuitively, we would judge that the person does not know it is a barn despite the belief being true.**, **A person would believe there is a barn whether or not they are viewing a real barn or a facade, so their belief does not track the truth across nearby possible worlds.**
