## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in all nearby possible worlds where the person believes that proposition, it is true**, **A proposition is safely known if it could not easily have been false when believed**, **Knowledge requires belief in a proposition that is true in all close circumstances where the belief is formed**
