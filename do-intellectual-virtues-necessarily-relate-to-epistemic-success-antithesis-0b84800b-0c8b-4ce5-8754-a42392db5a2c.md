# Do intellectual virtues necessarily relate to epistemic success?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0b84800b-0c8b-4ce5-8754-a42392db5a2c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-f60d76d9-bfb9-483c-b262-fa52975172be.md)

## Content

**There is a possible case where a person is virtuous in their intellectual pursuits but continually encounters misleading situations, such as systematic manipulation.**, **Given that intellectual virtue in this case does not lead to epistemic success, it questions whether intellectual virtues are inherently tied to such success.**
