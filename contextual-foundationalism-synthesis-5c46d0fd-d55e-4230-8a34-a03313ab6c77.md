**Parent:** [Can beliefs that aren't foundational be knowledge?](can-beliefs-that-arent-foundational-be-knowledge-antithesis-a9863270-23df-4ae9-9585-b8f7116e1d1f.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **The foundational status of a belief may vary depending on the context of inquiry**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is foundational in the context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is foundational in the context**
