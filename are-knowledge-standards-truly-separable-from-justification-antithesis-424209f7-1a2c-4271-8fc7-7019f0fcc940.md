# Are knowledge standards truly separable from justification?

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person believes a true proposition based on weak but contextually sufficient evidence; intuitively, we might insist stronger evidence is needed for knowledge.**, **A plausible principle holds that knowledge and justification standards are intertwined and cannot be fully separated.**, **Treating knowledge standards as fixed while justification varies with context challenges the interdependence of these standards.**
