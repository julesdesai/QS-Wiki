# Is justification sufficient for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9170e663-2b38-4745-833a-31be42127aad
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Holism with Infinite Justification](holism-with-infinite-justification-synthesis-5f38dde0-d181-4939-9f1a-56e147cd5ef5.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person has a justified belief based on misleading evidence.**, **In such a scenario, the belief is justified but not true.**, **Therefore, justification alone may not be sufficient for knowledge.**
