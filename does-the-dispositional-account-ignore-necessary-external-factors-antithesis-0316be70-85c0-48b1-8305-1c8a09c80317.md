# Does the dispositional account ignore necessary external factors?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0316be70-85c0-48b1-8305-1c8a09c80317
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Dispositional Account of Knowledge](dispositional-account-of-knowledge-synthesis-697a3559-6974-4820-bdde-37c51f6f9430.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person has a complete dispositional ability to form true beliefs, yet external interference, like misleading evidence, systematically prevents forming true beliefs. In such a scenario, intuitively, we would judge that the person does not have knowledge even with the ability present**, **It is implausible to claim that internal abilities alone can account for all instances of knowledge, ignoring potentially necessary external conditions for its acquisition**
