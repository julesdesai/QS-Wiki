# Knowledge as Indefeasible Warrant

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 7c8b3023-6529-4725-a38a-0ec2b9cb92b0
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Could a belief be true but still lacking knowledge due to misleading evidence?](could-a-belief-be-true-but-still-lacking-knowledge-due-to-misleading-evidence-antithesis-b8aa93d5-417e-4457-8941-6217b1e1843e.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is based on indefeasible warrant**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is based on indefeasible warrant**
