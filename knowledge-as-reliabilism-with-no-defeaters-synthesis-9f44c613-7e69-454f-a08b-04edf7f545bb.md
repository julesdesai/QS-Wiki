# Knowledge as Reliabilism with No Defeaters

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge be undermined by defeating evidence that one ignores?](can-knowledge-be-undermined-by-defeating-evidence-that-one-ignores-antithesis-63165ec4-3b37-4af5-a48f-eeb5628d959a.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed by a reliable process with no undefeated counter-evidence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have a belief formed by a reliable process with no undefeated counter-evidence**
