## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is a result of reflective equilibrium among virtues**, **For intellectual virtues to inform knowledge, they must be coherent and balanced in guiding the knower towards truth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief results from reaching a reflective equilibrium**
