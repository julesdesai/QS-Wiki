# Virtue Epistemology Approach to Knowledge

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Could a justified true belief fail to be knowledge?](could-a-justified-true-belief-fail-to-be-knowledge-antithesis-63d30b57-87ef-4d38-9fb0-fae4867632ed.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed through the exercise of intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is formed virtuously**
