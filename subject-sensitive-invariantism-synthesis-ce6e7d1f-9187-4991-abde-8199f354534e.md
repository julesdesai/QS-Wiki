## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the impact of the proposition on the subject meets fixed standards**, **The determining standards for knowledge are sensitive to the practical context of the subject's situation**
