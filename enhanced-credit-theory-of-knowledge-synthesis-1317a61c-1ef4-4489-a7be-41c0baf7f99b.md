## Content

**Knowledge requires that the truth of belief is creditable to the believer**, **A belief counts as knowledge if the believer has intellectual credit for the belief being true**, **The believer earns credit when their cognitive abilities significantly contribute to forming a true belief**, **The belief must be true for reasons attributable to the believer’s cognitive abilities or their use of reliable heuristics**
