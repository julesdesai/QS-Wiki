# Can individual experiences provide knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** beb22147-564b-4933-8b3c-5f9ceac952cb
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Intersubjective Phenomenalism](intersubjective-phenomenalism-synthesis-0a89d160-2bee-479f-b8d3-046de6da5ef6.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that individual perceptual experiences can be a valid source of knowledge. This principle together with the idea that only intersubjective agreement provides knowledge suggests that individual experiences are insufficient for knowledge.**
