## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief arises from intellectual virtue**, **An intellectually virtuous process is one that is reliable, truth-conducive, and generally leads to knowledge**
