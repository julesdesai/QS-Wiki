## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is generated by properly functioning cognitive faculties**, **Properly functioning cognitive faculties are those working according to a design plan successfully aimed at truth**
