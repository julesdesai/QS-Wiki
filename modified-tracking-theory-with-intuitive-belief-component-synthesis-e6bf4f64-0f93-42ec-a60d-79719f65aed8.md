## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person would not believe the proposition if it were false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person would believe the proposition if it were true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that these tracking conditions hold or that the person has a reliable intuition of the proposition**
