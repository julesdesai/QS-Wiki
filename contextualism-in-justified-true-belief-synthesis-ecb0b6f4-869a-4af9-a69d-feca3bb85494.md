## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **The standards for justification can vary depending on the context, allowing finite chains of reasons to provide sufficient justification in some contexts**
