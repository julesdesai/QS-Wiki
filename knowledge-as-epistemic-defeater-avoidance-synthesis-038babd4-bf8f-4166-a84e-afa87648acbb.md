# Knowledge as Epistemic Defeater Avoidance

**Parent:** [How does the definition account for misleading environments?](how-does-the-definition-account-for-misleading-environments-antithesis-b166fc92-21a2-46f8-b35c-9158a02d7886.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no epistemic defeaters for the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there are no epistemic defeaters for the belief**
