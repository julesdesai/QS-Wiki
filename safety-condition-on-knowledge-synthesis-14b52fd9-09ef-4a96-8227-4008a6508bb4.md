# Safety Condition on Knowledge

**Parent:** [Can knowledge arise from an unreliable cognitive process?](can-knowledge-arise-from-an-unreliable-cognitive-process-antithesis-c9958569-0b1b-4281-af72-7f68ca8a9bf3.md)

## Content

**Knowledge requires a true belief that is safely true in nearby possible worlds**, **A belief is safely true if, in most close possible worlds where the person believes it, it is true**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief is both true and safe**, **For a belief to be considered knowledge, unsafe truths produced by unreliable processes are excluded**
