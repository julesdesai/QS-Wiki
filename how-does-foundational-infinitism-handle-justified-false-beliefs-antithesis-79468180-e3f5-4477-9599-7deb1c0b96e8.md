# How does Foundational Infinitism handle justified false beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 79468180-e3f5-4477-9599-7deb1c0b96e8
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundational Infinitism](foundational-infinitism-synthesis-8fb4ffc2-abd6-406c-b0e9-89dab8b3bfd6.md)

## Content

**Infinite chains of reasons do not guarantee that all beliefs within the chain are true, leading to the possibility of justified but false beliefs.**, **Justification through infinite reasons does not preclude errors at any point in the justification process, allowing falsehoods.**, **The presence of false beliefs within an infinite justificatory chain undermines the trust in such systems as a means to attain knowledge.**
