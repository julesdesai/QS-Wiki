**Parent:** [Can the conditions for knowledge be independent of one another?](can-the-conditions-for-knowledge-be-independent-of-one-another-antithesis-cd394d9e-b8f7-44e1-bf37-b2ac89d283f6.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they deserve credit for believing the proposition truly**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person's cognitive abilities contribute to the formation of the true belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is true due to the person's abilities**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they deserve credit for the truth of their belief and their cognitive abilities have contributed to its formation**
