# What about accidental truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0e73f5a0-77da-4f30-af3d-f8a62ede82e4
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Knowledge as Proper Functionalism](knowledge-as-proper-functionalism-synthesis-95adcceb-ce7f-44ef-aea6-bebac418915b.md)

## Content

**Consider a case in which a person accidentally stumbles onto the truth, such as solving a complex problem by chance. Intuitively, we are hesitant to attribute knowledge despite the truth of the belief.**, **It is implausible that accidental truths constitute knowledge, regardless of cognitive functioning.**
