**Parent:** [Is process reliabilism sufficient against the clairvoyant intuition objection?](is-process-reliabilism-sufficient-against-the-clairvoyant-intuition-objection-antithesis-61dbf7ce-199b-4143-ae67-600ce3cb1b74.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief results from intellectual virtues like insight or reason**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process, and their belief results from intellectual virtues like insight or reason**
