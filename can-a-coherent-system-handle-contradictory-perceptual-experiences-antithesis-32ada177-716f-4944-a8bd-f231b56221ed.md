# Can a coherent system handle contradictory perceptual experiences?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 32ada177-716f-4944-a8bd-f231b56221ed
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism](coherentism-synthesis-fe89b418-bb8b-4164-8874-fa4a88946376.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have experiences that suggest mutually exclusive conclusions, such as an optical illusion that appears as two different images.**, **Intuitively, such contradictory perceptions challenge the idea of a consistent incorporation into a single coherent system.**, **It seems implausible that every contradictory perception can be subsumed coherently without loss of explanation or justification.**
