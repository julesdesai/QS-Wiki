# Can virtuous belief formation still lead to false beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3baec576-91f0-4164-9917-d151072e3dec

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Integrated Virtue-Responsibilism](integrated-virtue-responsibilism-synthesis-06dcde70-c679-48b5-94ca-324e3064105a.md)

## Content
