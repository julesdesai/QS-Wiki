# How does cultural variation in perception challenge the view?

**Node ID:** 1dc30323-3b4f-4b4f-9888-7248bea5dfa4

**Parent:** [Coherent Phenomenalism](coherent-phenomenalism-synthesis-762c00d9-ee32-4648-8d8e-67643edf20ba.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that knowledge of objects is based solely on perception coherence when cultural differences lead to varied interpretations of the same perceptual experiences, suggesting divergence rather than coherence of knowledge.**
