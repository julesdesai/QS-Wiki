## Content

**The truth conditions of knowledge are invariant across contexts**, **Knowledge depends on the subject's practical situation**, **A person's urgency or need affects whether they know a proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**
