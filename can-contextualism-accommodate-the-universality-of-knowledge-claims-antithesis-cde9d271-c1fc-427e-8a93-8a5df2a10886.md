# Can contextualism accommodate the universality of knowledge claims?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** cde9d271-c1fc-427e-8a93-8a5df2a10886
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualist Theory of Knowledge](contextualist-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-bce49363-1810-4900-8ccf-1e421dbc62ca.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which scientific knowledge is shared globally across different contexts.**, **Intuitively, such knowledge should hold uniformly regardless of individual contexts or varying justification standards.**, **If contextualism entails that knowledge standards vary, it conflicts with the universality expected of global scientific knowledge.**
