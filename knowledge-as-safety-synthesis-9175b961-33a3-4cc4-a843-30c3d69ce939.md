# Knowledge as Safety

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can unreliable cognitive processes ever lead to knowledge?](can-unreliable-cognitive-processes-ever-lead-to-knowledge-antithesis-f2438539-4f5f-4776-9157-ed5cca0873a5.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in nearby possible worlds the person would not easily hold a false belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and in nearby possible worlds they would not easily hold a false belief**
