## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres within the totality of their justified belief system**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that each belief is part of an integrated and mutually supportive network**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief coheres within the totality of their justified belief system**
