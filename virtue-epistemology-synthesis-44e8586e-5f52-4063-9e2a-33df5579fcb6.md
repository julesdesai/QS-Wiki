## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief manifests intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief manifests intellectual virtue, the proposition is true, and they believe the proposition**
