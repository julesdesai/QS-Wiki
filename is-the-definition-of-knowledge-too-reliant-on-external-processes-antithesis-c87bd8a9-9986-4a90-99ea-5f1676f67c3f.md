# Is the definition of knowledge too reliant on external processes?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c87bd8a9-9986-4a90-99ea-5f1676f67c3f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalism to knowledge with reliable cognitive process](externalism-to-knowledge-with-reliable-cognitive-process-synthesis-e78416a8-ed0b-42d6-b63b-0cc8fec7b97b.md)

## Content

**Picture a thinker who obtains truth via a consistently working yet external mechanical device with no input or understanding from the thinker.**, **Although the process is reliable, the lack of intellectual engagement might lead us to withhold the label of knowledge.**, **This implies that some level of cognitive involvement may be crucial for true knowledge claims.**
