# Does coherentism provide adequate guidance for belief revision?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 909f46d6-5d41-41e4-b6a9-24663bc8aa4e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism View](coherentism-view-synthesis-4dcf0483-85cc-4ed6-8a5e-86aaec2b7626.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where a person discovers a new fact that contradicts a current belief in their coherent system. Intuitively, they may not know how to adjust their system since coherence could be maintained by modifying many possible beliefs.**, **A plausible principle suggests effective belief revision should provide clear criteria or methods, which mere coherence lacks.**
