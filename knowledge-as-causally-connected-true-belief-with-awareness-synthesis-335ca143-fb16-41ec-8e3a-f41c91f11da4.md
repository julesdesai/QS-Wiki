## Content

**Knowledge is a true belief caused by the facts that make the belief true**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief is causally connected to the truth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **A person must also be aware of the causal connection between their belief and the truth for knowledge**
