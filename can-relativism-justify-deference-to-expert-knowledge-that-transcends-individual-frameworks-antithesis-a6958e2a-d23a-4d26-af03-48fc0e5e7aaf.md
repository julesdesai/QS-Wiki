# Can relativism justify deference to expert knowledge that transcends individual frameworks?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a6958e2a-d23a-4d26-af03-48fc0e5e7aaf
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Relativism Summary](relativism-summary-synthesis-2665e075-79d4-4adb-9371-55cb5a5794f8.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which non-experts defer to expert consensus in a scientific field. Intuitively, this deference presupposes a level of objective expertise.**, **It is implausible that deference to expertise can be explained entirely by individual frameworks without acknowledging objective standards.**
