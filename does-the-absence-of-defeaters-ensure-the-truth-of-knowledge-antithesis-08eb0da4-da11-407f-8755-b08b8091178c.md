# Does the absence of defeaters ensure the truth of knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 08eb0da4-da11-407f-8755-b08b8091178c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [No Defeaters Theory](no-defeaters-theory-synthesis-e6eeb208-311d-4d09-a784-60d3e95f478f.md)

## Content

**Knowledge requires the truth of the proposition.**, **The absence of defeaters alone doesn't assure that a belief corresponds to the truth.**, **It merely indicates that nothing known undermines the belief, leaving its truth value susceptible to unrelated issues.**
