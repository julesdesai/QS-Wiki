# Is it possible to know something without being aware of having supporting evidence?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 32117718-ecf5-4a38-b134-00a0efbaa59b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Evidentialist View with Doxastic Involvement](evidentialist-view-with-doxastic-involvement-synthesis-75fddb9e-288b-457e-8f92-4c216b30a2a5.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person knows something through subconscious reasoning or forgotten evidence. In such a scenario, intuitively we might say that they know the proposition even if they cannot consciously access or articulate the evidence.**, **There is a plausible principle that a person can know something through evidence even when they are unaware of possessing that evidence.**
