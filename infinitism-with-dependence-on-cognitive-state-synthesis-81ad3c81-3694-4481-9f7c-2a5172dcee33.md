## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is an infinite, non-repeating chain of justification available**, **Justification is dependent on the cognitive state of the person and the proposition being true**
