# Does cognitive evaluation affect all perception?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 06f54b72-a919-48d8-a3a0-669138b02a64
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Critical Realism](critical-realism-synthesis-04d59a43-df5d-4ff1-bc30-b363f66f9a03.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where someone hears a loud noise and startles without any thought involved. In this scenario, intuitively, response occurs immediately and does not involve critical evaluation.**, **For many reflex actions or immediate perceptions, it seems implausible that cognition critically evaluates every sensory input before a reaction.**
