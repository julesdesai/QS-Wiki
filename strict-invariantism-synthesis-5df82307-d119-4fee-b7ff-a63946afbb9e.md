## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **The justification required for knowing a proposition remains constant and is not influenced by the subject's stakes or context**
