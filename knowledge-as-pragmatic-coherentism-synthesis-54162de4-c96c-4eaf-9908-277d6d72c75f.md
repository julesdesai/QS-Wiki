**Parent:** [What if coherence is compatible with different networks of beliefs?](what-if-coherence-is-compatible-with-different-networks-of-beliefs-antithesis-23224c60-c428-4ea8-8542-00695b2555ef.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief coheres with a network of beliefs that have practical success**, **A network of beliefs has practical success if beliefs within that network lead to successful prediction and action**
