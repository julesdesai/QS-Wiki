# Can infallibility coexist with scientific knowledge claims?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5ea0944c-b6c8-4dde-9b9d-43723738a67d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Knowledge as Infallibility](knowledge-as-infallibility-synthesis-8f0bcd35-bb4c-4192-816d-76a63a41903c.md)

## Content

**Scientific knowledge is often provisional and open to revision with new evidence, suggesting some possibility of error.**, **If infallibility is necessary for knowledge, then scientific claims do not qualify as knowledge due to potential error.**, **This contradicts the widely held view that scientific findings constitute knowledge.**
