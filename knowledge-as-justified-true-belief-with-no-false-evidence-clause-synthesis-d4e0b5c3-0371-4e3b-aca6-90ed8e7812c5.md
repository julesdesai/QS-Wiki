# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with No False Evidence Clause

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is justified**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification does not rely on any false evidence**
