# Knowledge as True Belief with Externalist Justification

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 0905f87d-8ae6-4f4d-953e-e6290f192947
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can belief be true without knowledge?](can-belief-be-true-without-knowledge-antithesis-655d7a8e-1741-4ddf-85c7-a393d2dd8421.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is justified by factors external to their mental states**, **External factors may include causal connections to the truth of the proposition**
