**Parent:** [Can coherence with justified beliefs allow for knowledge if those beliefs are false?](can-coherence-with-justified-beliefs-allow-for-knowledge-if-those-beliefs-are-false-antithesis-2f88010e-2d98-4ebc-92ec-b09456583fb8.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres with a contextually appropriate set of beliefs justified by the standards of that context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief coheres with a contextually appropriate set of justified beliefs**
