## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A person knows a proposition if there is an appropriate causal connection between the belief and the fact**, **An appropriate causal connection reliably links the belief with the reality it represents**
