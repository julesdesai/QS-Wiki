## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief has minimal foundational status**, **The foundational status is determined by a narrow set of universal conditions minimally influenced by context**
