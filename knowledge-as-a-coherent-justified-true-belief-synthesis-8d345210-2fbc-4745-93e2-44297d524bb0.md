# Knowledge as a Coherent Justified True Belief

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge be reduced to mere coherence with an entire belief system?](can-knowledge-be-reduced-to-mere-coherence-with-an-entire-belief-system-antithesis-1662f703-e96c-4d24-a72f-0b4f5a4da754.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres with the person's entire belief system**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief coheres with the person's entire belief system, and the belief is justified**
