# How does an infinite chain of justification for a proposition affect immediate knowledge claims?

## Content

**Knowledge claims are often made in real-time and with practical immediacy.**, **The requirement of an infinite chain of justification demands an unending, continuous reasoning process for any knowledge claim.**, **Real-time knowledge claims cannot synchronously accommodate infinite chains of reasoning.**
