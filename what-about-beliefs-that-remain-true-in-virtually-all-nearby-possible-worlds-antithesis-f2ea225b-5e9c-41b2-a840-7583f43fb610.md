# What about beliefs that remain true in virtually all nearby possible worlds?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f2ea225b-5e9c-41b2-a840-7583f43fb610
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Nozick's Sensitivity Theory](nozicks-sensitivity-theory-synthesis-094644b1-cf24-4462-a6c6-f40dc1c2de1a.md)

## Content

**In certain scenarios, a person's belief may remain true in nearly all nearby possible worlds but would not be considered knowledge.**, **Consider a case where someone believes the lottery ticket they possess will lose based on the odds; in all nearby worlds where they still hold the ticket, it indeed loses, but we hesitate to say they know it will lose.**
