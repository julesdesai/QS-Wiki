## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is safe**, **A belief is safe if in nearly all nearby possible worlds where they believe the proposition, it is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is safe**
