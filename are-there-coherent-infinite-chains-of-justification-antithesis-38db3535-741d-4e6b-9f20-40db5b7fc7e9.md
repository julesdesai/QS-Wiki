# Are there coherent infinite chains of justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 38db3535-741d-4e6b-9f20-40db5b7fc7e9
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherent Infinitism](coherent-infinitism-synthesis-f2c99927-f862-4ee8-8b5b-2fefd2717ee2.md)

## Content

**A plausible principle states that for a justification chain to be infinite, each reason must logically support the next. This principle together with the practical limits of human logic entails that an infinite coherent chain is unlikely to maintain coherence throughout.**
