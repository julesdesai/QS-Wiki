# Do all justified beliefs emanate from intellectual virtues?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5ead9b91-2400-4a73-8b35-34bfa185df08
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology: Intellectual Virtues](virtue-epistemology-intellectual-virtues-synthesis-1d322156-3f22-49a0-99a5-f932d24b0e66.md)

## Content

**Consider a person who accepts beliefs from authority figures without scrutiny and happens to be correct. Intuitively, such beliefs lack authentic justification.**, **The principle that justification involves active critical engagement rather than passive acceptance challenges the idea that all justified beliefs reflect intellectual virtues.**
