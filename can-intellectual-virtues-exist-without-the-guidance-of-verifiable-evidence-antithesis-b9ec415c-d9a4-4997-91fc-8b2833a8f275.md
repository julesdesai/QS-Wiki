# Can intellectual virtues exist without the guidance of verifiable evidence?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b9ec415c-d9a4-4997-91fc-8b2833a8f275
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-39ebccb5-104c-44e1-841f-55d82b6e37fc.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which an individual has strong intellectual virtues but operates without accessible or verifiable evidence regarding a particular situation. Intuitively, we might question whether intellectual virtues can function effectively or lead to truth in the absence of dependable evidence, thus challenging the connection between virtues and truth.**
