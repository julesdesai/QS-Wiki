# How can an infinite chain of reasons be accessible to a cognitive agent?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 48fa903a-d75c-40f2-8353-64d4914cc6d6
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism with Foundational Beliefs](infinitism-with-foundational-beliefs-synthesis-4273f090-f358-492e-9e7c-904c2a039ab2.md)

## Content

**A cognitive agent has finite cognitive capacities and cannot hold an infinite number of beliefs at once.**, **In a scenario where an agent claims to have an infinite chain of reasons, they may lack access to all reasons in their entirety.**, **It is implausible that a person can be aware of or access an infinite chain of reasons simultaneously.**
