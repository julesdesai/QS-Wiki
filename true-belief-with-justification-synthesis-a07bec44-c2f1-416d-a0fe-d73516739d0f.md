# True Belief with Justification

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does reliance solely on one's intellectual virtues disregard external evidence?](does-reliance-solely-on-ones-intellectual-virtues-disregard-external-evidence-antithesis-d5589128-23b4-4f18-a729-b8e4c78a1e41.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified by additional evidence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is justified by additional evidence**
