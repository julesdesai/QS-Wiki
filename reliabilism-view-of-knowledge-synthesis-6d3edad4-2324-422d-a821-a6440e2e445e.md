# Reliabilism View of Knowledge

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is it possible for someone to have knowledge without belief in a proposition?](is-it-possible-for-someone-to-have-knowledge-without-belief-in-a-proposition-antithesis-e5d34122-24e8-4032-abcb-07bb4a52b632.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief in the proposition results from a reliable cognitive process**, **A reliable cognitive process is one that consistently produces true beliefs**
