# No External Defeaters

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no internal defeaters that would undermine their justification for believing the proposition**, **External defeaters that the person is unaware of do not undermine knowledge**
