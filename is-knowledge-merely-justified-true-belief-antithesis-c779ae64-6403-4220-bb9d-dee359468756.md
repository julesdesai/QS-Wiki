# Is knowledge merely justified true belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c779ae64-6403-4220-bb9d-dee359468756
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism Revised](coherentism-revised-synthesis-de283b0a-5fd8-4f4c-bf89-d22a34799ca8.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that if someone knows a proposition, they cannot be wrong about it. This principle together with the claim that knowledge is justified true belief entails that justified true beliefs cannot be false.**, **Consider a scenario in which someone has a justified true belief that their lottery ticket is a losing ticket based on the enormous odds against winning, yet intuitively we would judge that they do not know this since they are risking error.**
