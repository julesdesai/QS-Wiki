# Knowledge as Sensitivity with Internal Coherence

**Parent:** [Does tracking fail to accommodate internalist insights?](does-tracking-fail-to-accommodate-internalist-insights-antithesis-df223629-7517-4628-892f-27c9d7f7e1fb.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief coheres with their other beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the person believes the proposition, it is true, their belief coheres with other beliefs, and the person would not believe it if it were false**
