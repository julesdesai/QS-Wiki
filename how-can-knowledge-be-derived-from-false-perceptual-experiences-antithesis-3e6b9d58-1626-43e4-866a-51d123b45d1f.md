# How can knowledge be derived from false perceptual experiences?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3e6b9d58-1626-43e4-866a-51d123b45d1f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Inferential Phenomenalism](inferential-phenomenalism-synthesis-2adfcff5-a571-43dd-87fa-6a59a7ba9de4.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person perceives a straight stick in water as bent due to the refraction of light. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that the person’s perception is false.**, **It is implausible that knowledge can be derived from perceptual experiences that are false.**, **A person who infers truths based on a false perception will not know propositions accurately.**
