# Can a person know a false proposition?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1715632b-7985-4a1b-a73e-332505d23347
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism with Reliabilism](contextualism-with-reliabilism-synthesis-c7c3d2a5-9a25-4e3f-9e1f-65010441e024.md)

## Content

**A case in which a person believes a proposition, their belief is produced by a reliable process, but the proposition is false is not a case of knowledge.**, **It is necessary for a proposition to be true for someone to know it. This indicates that truth is a part of the conditions of knowledge.**
