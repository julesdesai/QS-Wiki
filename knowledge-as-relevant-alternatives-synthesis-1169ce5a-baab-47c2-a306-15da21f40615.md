# Knowledge as Relevant Alternatives

**Parent:** [Is contextualism compatible with the phenomenology of knowing?](is-contextualism-compatible-with-the-phenomenology-of-knowing-antithesis-ba980be9-1b2c-4ae8-a59b-54b64b8aedf7.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A person knows a proposition if they can rule out all relevant alternatives to the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and relevant alternatives are ruled out**
