# Do all justified beliefs need a hierarchical structure?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d23fe992-c49f-4791-b2dc-77bbe6730689
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Structural Foundationalism](structural-foundationalism-synthesis-037c32e2-5bf4-4b6c-88a6-0e0659b44b9e.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a person's belief is justified through coherent explanatory connections rather than a hierarchical structure. Intuitively, we must say that the belief is justified even without hierarchical structuring.**, **It is implausible that every justified belief conforms to a hierarchy.**
