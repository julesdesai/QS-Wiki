# Reliabilism with a Sensitivity Condition

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can a belief change in response to false propositions and still qualify as knowledge?](can-a-belief-change-in-response-to-false-propositions-and-still-qualify-as-knowledge-antithesis-3f0501ad-fe82-4e8a-817d-53c0ef18f30f.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed by a reliable method**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief changes in response to changes in the truth of the proposition**
