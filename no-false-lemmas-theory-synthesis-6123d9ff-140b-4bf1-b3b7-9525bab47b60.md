## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person does not infer the proposition from any false beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that it is justified, true, and not inferred from false beliefs**
