# Does safety account for intuitive judgments in Gettier cases?

## Content

**Suppose someone sees what looks like their friend in a park based on their distinctive hat, but unbeknownst to them, a stranger is wearing the same hat nearby. Intuitively, the person does not have knowledge despite the true belief.**, **In nearby possible worlds, their friend could indeed be in the park, making the belief safe despite relying on misleading evidence.**
