# Causal Account of Knowledge

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is causally related to the fact that makes the belief true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process that is causally related to the fact making the belief true**
