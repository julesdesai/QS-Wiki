# Reliabilism as Knowledge

**Parent:** [Can animals possess knowledge without intellectual virtue?](can-animals-possess-knowledge-without-intellectual-virtue-antithesis-70846c0e-efd8-4f70-ae77-86b03162afd0.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**
