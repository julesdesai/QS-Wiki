**Parent:** [Do intellectual virtues vary significantly between domains?](do-intellectual-virtues-vary-significantly-between-domains-antithesis-97f2562b-648e-4239-a558-bdad51a79755.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief to be produced by a set of integrated intellectual virtues that work together**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is produced by a set of integrated intellectual virtues**
