# What about knowledge claims where propositions are vague or indeterminate?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ae75ee0a-b35a-45d7-900e-79ac8cae4d73
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Nozick’s Truth-Tracking Theory](nozicks-truth-tracking-theory-synthesis-5a62ad60-20de-4329-b52b-21f59b8da1c1.md)

## Content

**Consider a situation where a person forms a belief about a vague proposition that is generally true across different contexts but may have indeterminate truth-values in some specific cases. In such a scenario, intuitively, one might question whether the person has knowledge if the proposition's truth-value is not clearly defined across possible worlds, challenging the ability to track truth with precision.**
