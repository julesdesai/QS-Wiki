# Is it problematic to equate proper functioning with a design plan?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 56330990-7e7f-4085-a51e-3058b3b85183
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Proper Functionalism](proper-functionalism-synthesis-60a6689e-3dfa-4e29-ad7b-61cc33b34e1b.md)

## Content

**Equating proper functioning with adherence to a design plan may be problematic if the plan is flawed or outdated.**, **A static design plan might not accommodate the dynamic nature of environments affecting cognitive processing.**, **Challenges arise if properly functioning faculties, bound to an inadequate plan, produce false beliefs.**
