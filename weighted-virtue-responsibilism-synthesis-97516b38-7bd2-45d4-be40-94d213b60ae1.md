## Content

**Knowledge is true belief arising from intellectual virtue**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief arises from intellectual virtue, which is weighted according to its strength, and is not coincidentally true**, **Intellectual achievement requires that the belief is true and the intellectual virtue, weighted by strength, is responsible for it**, **A person knows a proposition if they believe it and the belief arises from adequately weighted intellectual virtue**
