# Objective Truth Focused Knowledge

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge be useless?](can-knowledge-be-useless-antithesis-8342035c-9026-400f-a9c5-75934a284046.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief corresponds to reality**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief corresponds to reality**
