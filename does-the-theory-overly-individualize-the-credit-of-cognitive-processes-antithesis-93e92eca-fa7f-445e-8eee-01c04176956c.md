# Does the theory overly individualize the credit of cognitive processes?

**Node ID:** 93e92eca-fa7f-445e-8eee-01c04176956c

**Terminal:** No

## Content

**It is implausible that all knowledge must be creditable to individual cognitive abilities, as some knowledge, such as language understanding, significantly depends on social learning and environmental interaction.**, **Social and environmental factors indicate that cognitive credit might extend beyond individual abilities, impacting the view of what comprises knowledge.**
