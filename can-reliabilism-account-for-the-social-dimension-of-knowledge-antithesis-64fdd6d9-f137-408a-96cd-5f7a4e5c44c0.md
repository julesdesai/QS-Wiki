# Can reliabilism account for the social dimension of knowledge?

## Content

**Consider a person P who receives information from a reliable social source but has no means to judge the reliability independently, like receiving breaking news from a generally reliable news station.**, **We might intuitively hesitate to claim full knowledge for P, highlighting a possible social epistemology issue for reliabilism.**
