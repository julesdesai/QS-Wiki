# Infinitist Justification View of Knowledge

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is not true merely due to epistemic luck**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is supported by an infinite chain of reasons**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, their belief is not true merely due to epistemic luck, and their belief is supported by an infinite chain of reasons**
