## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief aligns with a contextually adaptive set of foundational beliefs**, **The network of beliefs adapts to context to prevent distortion by external influences**
