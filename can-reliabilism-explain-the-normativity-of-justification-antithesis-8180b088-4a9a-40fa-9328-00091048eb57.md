# Can reliabilism explain the normativity of justification?

**Node ID:** 8180b088-4a9a-40fa-9328-00091048eb57
**Depth:** 4

**Parent:** [Reliabilism](reliabilism-synthesis-a405882b-2e95-4de6-9817-4516092396fb.md)

## Content

**Justification in epistemology not only involves successfully forming true beliefs but is also a guide for aligning our belief-forming practices to seek truth**, **Reliabilism emphasizes processes leading to truth, yet it might lack resources to explain why one ought to use such reliable processes**, **The emphasis on outcome alone seems to overlook why individuals should aim at reliability beyond mere statistical outcomes, thus neglecting the normativity aspect**
