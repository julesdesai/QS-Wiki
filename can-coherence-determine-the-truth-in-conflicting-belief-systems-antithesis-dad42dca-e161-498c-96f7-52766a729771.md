# Can coherence determine the truth in conflicting belief systems?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** dad42dca-e161-498c-96f7-52766a729771
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism Summary](coherentism-summary-synthesis-7bdcae0a-f633-4c38-9857-77d6ba49435c.md)

## Content

**Consider a situation where two individuals have mutually exclusive but internally coherent belief systems. Intuitively, we would judge that not both systems can be truthful since they contain contradictory claims. It is implausible for coherence alone to determine which system, if any, aligns with truth.**
