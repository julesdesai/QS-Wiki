# Can false beliefs be retracted easily under tracking theory?

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person forms a false belief due to misleading evidence but remains confident even with new contradictory evidence. Intuitively, this person cannot be said to know.**, **It is implausible for knowledge to be attributed to someone whose belief system fails to correct when met with true contrary evidence.**
