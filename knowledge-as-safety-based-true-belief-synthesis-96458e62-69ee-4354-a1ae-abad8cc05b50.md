# Knowledge as Safety-Based True Belief

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **A person knows a proposition if in nearby possible worlds where they form the belief in the same manner, the proposition is true**, **A belief is safe if it holds across close possible worlds**
