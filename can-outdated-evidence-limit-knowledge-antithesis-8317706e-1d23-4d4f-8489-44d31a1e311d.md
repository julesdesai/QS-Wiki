# Can outdated evidence limit knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 8317706e-1d23-4d4f-8489-44d31a1e311d
**Depth:** 2
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [No False Lemmas Theory](no-false-lemmas-theory-thesis-a96b2c39-d876-40fc-94b2-391826bfefda.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person believes a true proposition based on outdated evidence that no longer holds true. Intuitively, knowledge seems absent as the justification is unfounded.**, **Using outdated evidence as justification should disqualify a claim of knowledge even if the belief remains true by current standards.**
