## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the standards for knowledge are met within the relevant context**, **The standards for knowledge can change depending on different contexts or subject matters**
