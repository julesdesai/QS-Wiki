# Knowledge as Anti-Luck Virtue

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Could someone possess knowledge even with a belief not resulting from an intellectual virtue?](could-someone-possess-knowledge-even-with-a-belief-not-resulting-from-an-intellectual-virtue-antithesis-fef022ce-0e49-4531-8947-78a0f925b09d.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief to be gained in a way that excludes epistemic luck**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is gained in a way that excludes epistemic luck**
