# Knowledge as True Justified Belief with Intellectual Responsibility

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 056bdfe7-7861-4d61-91bf-eb848be88ec1
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Are all responsible beliefs knowledge?](are-all-responsible-beliefs-knowledge-antithesis-044a8463-00d8-4fae-a25b-4fd6464f58c2.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief arises through responsible cognitive efforts**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification, the proposition is true, and their belief arises through responsible cognitive efforts**
