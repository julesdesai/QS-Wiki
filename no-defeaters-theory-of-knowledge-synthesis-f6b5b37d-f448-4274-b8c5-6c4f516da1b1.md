## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have no defeaters for their belief**, **A defeater for a belief is an additional true proposition that would undermine the justification for the belief if it were included in the set of known propositions**
