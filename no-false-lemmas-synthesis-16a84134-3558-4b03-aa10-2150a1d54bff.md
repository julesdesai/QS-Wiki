# No False Lemmas

**Parent:** [Could someone have a true belief without incremental justification and still know?](could-someone-have-a-true-belief-without-incremental-justification-and-still-know-antithesis-71d1f749-7a1f-4803-95f3-7d7fec3cade8.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that any justification for the belief does not rely on any falsehood**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification does not rely on any falsehood**
