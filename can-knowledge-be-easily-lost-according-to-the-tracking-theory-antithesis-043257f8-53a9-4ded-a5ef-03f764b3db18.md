# Can knowledge be easily lost according to the tracking theory?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 043257f8-53a9-4ded-a5ef-03f764b3db18
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Tracking Theory of Knowledge](tracking-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-dda35d00-5b10-4342-b443-f197350806e5.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person knows a fact at one moment, but later doubts it due to misleading but false evidence. Intuitively, we might say the person still knows the fact if their original belief was well-justified.**, **It is implausible that the mere introduction of misleading evidence completely negates previously justified true belief, suggesting knowledge can withstand some degree of doubt.**
