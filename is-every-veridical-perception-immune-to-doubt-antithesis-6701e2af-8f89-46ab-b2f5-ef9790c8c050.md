# Is every veridical perception immune to doubt?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 6701e2af-8f89-46ab-b2f5-ef9790c8c050
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Disjunctivism](disjunctivism-synthesis-e62747dd-0f26-4379-9406-3d7f6fb883b9.md)

## Content

**Suppose someone perceives an actual event but doubts it due to context, like seeing a friend in disguise. Intuitively, perception's validity here is questioned despite being real.**, **It is implausible that a veridical perception is always accepted as valid without question. This challenges the idea that perception directly connects to reality without potential for doubt.**
