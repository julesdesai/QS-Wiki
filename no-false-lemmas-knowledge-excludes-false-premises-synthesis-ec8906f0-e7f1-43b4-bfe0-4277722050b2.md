# No-False-Lemmas: Knowledge excludes false premises

**Parent:** [Can the causal theory handle Gettier-style cases effectively?](can-the-causal-theory-handle-gettier-style-cases-effectively-antithesis-032b6ca3-d66a-4fd4-8b43-5aa9931f4133.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is not based on any false premises**, **A belief based on false premises cannot constitute knowledge**
