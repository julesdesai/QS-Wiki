# Does causal connection ensure epistemic responsibility?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d4777143-8251-4a77-be04-a15d88f9616b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalist Justification with Causal Connection](externalist-justification-with-causal-connection-synthesis-0c02280c-1b97-4965-a35c-599f5b7ce400.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a belief is causally connected to a fact due to a random event, such as lottery prediction magic. Intuitively, we would judge that while the belief might be true, it is not responsibly held knowledge.**, **A plausible principle states that knowledge requires not only a causal connection but also a responsible epistemic process.**
