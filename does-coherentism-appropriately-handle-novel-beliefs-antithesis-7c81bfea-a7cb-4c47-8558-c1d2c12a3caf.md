# Does coherentism appropriately handle novel beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7c81bfea-a7cb-4c47-8558-c1d2c12a3caf
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism Summary](coherentism-summary-synthesis-7bdcae0a-f633-4c38-9857-77d6ba49435c.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person suddenly believes a new proposition that cannot yet be integrated into their existing belief system. It is implausible that the absence of immediate coherence causes all new propositions to be unjustified, assuming they are otherwise true and verifiable.**
