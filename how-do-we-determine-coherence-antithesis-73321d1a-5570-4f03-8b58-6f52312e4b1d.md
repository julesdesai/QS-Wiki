## Content

**Consider a scenario where two different sets of beliefs are each coherent internally but contradict each other externally. Intuitively, coherence does not provide a means for choosing between these competing systems.**, **It is implausible that coherence alone can determine the superiority of one belief system over another equally coherent one.**
