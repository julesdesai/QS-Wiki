## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief coheres with other beliefs in a network of beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the coherence of beliefs be evaluated within the context relevant to the knowledge claim**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief coheres with other beliefs in a contextually relevant network of beliefs**
