## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief to result from a reliable process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is formed from a reliable process**
