# Knowledge as Virtue

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have intellectual virtues enabling justified belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they exercise their intellectual virtues, the proposition is true, and their belief is justified by those virtues**
