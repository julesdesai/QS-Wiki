# Does the presence of "lucky" evidence affect the tracking of truth in this theory?

**Node ID:** ec01aff7-336b-421a-b56f-4bcbbbc56252

**Terminal:** No

## Content

**Imagine a detective who holds a belief that a suspect is guilty based on planted evidence, and the suspect is indeed guilty.**, **If the evidence were false or not present, the detective's belief would vary regardless of actual guilt, highlighting independence from tracking the truth.**, **Other circumstances could lead the detective to maintain a belief in guilt, even if the superficial evidential condition varies widely across similar scenarios.**
