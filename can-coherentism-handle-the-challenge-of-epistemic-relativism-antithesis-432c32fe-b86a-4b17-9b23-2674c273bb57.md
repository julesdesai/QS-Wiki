# Can coherentism handle the challenge of epistemic relativism?

**Node ID:** 432c32fe-b86a-4b17-9b23-2674c273bb57

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism in Epistemology](coherentism-in-epistemology-synthesis-03124e37-be18-4868-b2cf-e370b5186105.md)

## Content

**Different coherent systems of beliefs might all claim to provide justification, even if they vastly differ from one another.**, **Cultural or personal differences might result in distinct belief systems that are internally coherent yet incompatible with other systems.**, **There is an intuition that not all belief systems can equally possess epistemic justification, raising concerns about relativism in coherentism.**
