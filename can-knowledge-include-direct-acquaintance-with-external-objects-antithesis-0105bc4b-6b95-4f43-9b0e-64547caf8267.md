# Can knowledge include direct acquaintance with external objects?

**Parent:** [Phenomenalist Instrumentalism](phenomenalist-instrumentalism-synthesis-4537874c-c8f8-46da-9468-0a115148189b.md)

## Content

**Suppose someone knows they are holding an apple because they can directly see and touch it. Intuitively, this knowledge seems more direct than mere predictions about future perceptual experiences involving the apple.**, **Knowledge by acquaintance appears to suggest direct knowledge of external objects rather than merely instrumental predictions, which challenges the limitation to theoretical constructs.**
