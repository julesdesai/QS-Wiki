## Content

**Knowledge depends on the context of the knower**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **A person knows a proposition if the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **The contextual standards for belief, truth, and justification vary independently**
