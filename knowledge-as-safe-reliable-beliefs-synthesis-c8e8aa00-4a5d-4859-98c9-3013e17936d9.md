# Knowledge as Safe Reliable Beliefs

**Parent:** [Does truth derived from reliable processes lead to accidental knowledge?](does-truth-derived-from-reliable-processes-lead-to-accidental-knowledge-antithesis-dc014bcf-91f6-490d-af8e-cc51a09d1513.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is produced by a reliable process and is safe**, **A belief is safe if in most nearby possible worlds where the person forms the belief in the same way, it is true**
