## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they can distinguish the true proposition from contextually relevant alternatives**, **Only contextually plausible alternatives should be considered to potentially undermine knowledge**
