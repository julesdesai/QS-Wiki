# Is cognitive faculty purpose an absolute knowledge criterion?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 89ebd377-b233-4f9e-8f7e-1e8f740bff38
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Proper Functionalist Knowledge Summary](proper-functionalist-knowledge-summary-synthesis-ea5a08e3-f799-4ced-bdad-582e7b9aaf66.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where a person's cognitive faculties were originally designed for survival, not truth-finding, but still form true beliefs. Intuitively, it seems their knowledge status is ambiguous.**, **The design purpose of cognitive faculties might not strictly correlate with the capacity for knowledge.**
