# Can we know something without the inclination to act on or assert it?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0e743df0-98da-49a7-8f05-e086e949a1ca
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [No-Belief Necessary View](no-belief-necessary-view-synthesis-562c34c9-8bb3-4b6d-bbdd-b0bc8f277594.md)

## Content

**Knowing a proposition usually involves being prepared to assert or act upon the truth of the proposition.**, **If a person has justification for a proposition and the proposition is true but they are not inclined to act or assert it due to lack of belief, it challenges the idea of fully incorporating knowledge.**
