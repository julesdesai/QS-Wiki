## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **It is necessary for a person to know a proposition that their belief is safe in the sense that it could not easily have been false**, **Safety can be assured either through intellectual virtues or through reliable perceptual processes**
