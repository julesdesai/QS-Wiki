## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in most nearby possible worlds where the person forms the belief in the proposition using the same method, the belief is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, in most nearby possible worlds where the belief is formed in the same way it is true, and the proposition is true**
