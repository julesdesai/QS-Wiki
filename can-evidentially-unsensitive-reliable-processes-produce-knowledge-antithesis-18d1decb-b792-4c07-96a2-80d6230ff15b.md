# Can evidentially unsensitive reliable processes produce knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 18d1decb-b792-4c07-96a2-80d6230ff15b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Process Reliabilism with Evidence Sensitivity](process-reliabilism-with-evidence-sensitivity-synthesis-e3e15d29-f0bc-4d87-b0aa-fbeab510c604.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a belief-forming process is reliable but does not adjust based on new evidence and still produces true beliefs. Intuitively, we would not recognize the resultant beliefs as knowledge.**, **A process must respond properly to the full scope of available evidence for its outputs to be considered knowledge.**
