# Virtue Epistemology View

**Parent:** [Does ruling out relevant alternatives necessarily ensure knowledge?](does-ruling-out-relevant-alternatives-necessarily-ensure-knowledge-antithesis-b5c9538f-2291-4153-ae18-995183ff44df.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief arises from intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification, the proposition is true and their belief arises from intellectual virtue**
