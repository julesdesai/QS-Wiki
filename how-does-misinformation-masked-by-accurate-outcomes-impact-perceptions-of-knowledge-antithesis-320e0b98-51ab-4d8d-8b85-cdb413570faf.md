# How does misinformation masked by accurate outcomes impact perceptions of knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 320e0b98-51ab-4d8d-8b85-cdb413570faf
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [No-False-Grounds Theory](no-false-grounds-theory-synthesis-00c0ff61-646c-4d63-9780-b055a1d82c9f.md)

## Content

**Imagine a news headline that contains a mix of true and false information, but a reader infers a true conclusion purely by chance.**, **The misleading nature of misinformation in this case challenges the perception that such a reader truly holds knowledge about the topic despite being accidentally correct.**, **The distinction between good fortune and genuine knowledge becomes pronounced, especially when false ground is invisible to the knower.**
