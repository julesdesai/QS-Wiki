## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, their belief must result from specific intellectual virtues**, **Specific intellectual virtues are cognitive abilities closely linked to epistemic reliability and include more than generic cognitive competencies**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have a true belief resulting from specific intellectual virtues**
