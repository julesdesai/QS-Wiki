# Does coherence guarantee the truth of an entire belief system?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9015b006-a4e0-4d98-a79c-7e32b81da20d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism: System of Beliefs](coherentism-system-of-beliefs-synthesis-446f5d76-49b9-4fbc-a49a-1aaf884652f2.md)

## Content

**Internal coherence of a belief system does not necessarily entail the truth of its beliefs.**, **False beliefs can form a coherent system.**, **To qualify as knowledge, a belief must be true, not just coherent.**, **Therefore, coherence alone cannot guarantee the truth of an entire belief system.**
