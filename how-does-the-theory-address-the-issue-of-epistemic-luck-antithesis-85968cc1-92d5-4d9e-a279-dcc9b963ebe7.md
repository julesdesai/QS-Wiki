# How does the theory address the issue of epistemic luck?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 85968cc1-92d5-4d9e-a279-dcc9b963ebe7
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Nozick's Conditional Theory of Knowledge](nozicks-conditional-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-ff394d52-e522-4d27-8272-d4808dc162bd.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where a person arrives at a true belief due to a random or lucky correlation rather than reliable processes. In this case, intuitively, there is no knowledge present despite the alignment with Nozick's conditions.**, **If true beliefs stem from fortunate coincidences or luck without proper reliability, the belief lacks grounding in knowledge.**
