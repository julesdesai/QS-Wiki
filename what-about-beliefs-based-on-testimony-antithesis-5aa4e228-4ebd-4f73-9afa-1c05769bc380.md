# What about beliefs based on testimony?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5aa4e228-4ebd-4f73-9afa-1c05769bc380
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Causal Theory of Knowledge](causal-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-9ece6694-b5cf-4143-897d-321cf0165994.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where person P listens to a weather forecast and believes it will rain because the forecaster says so. There is no direct causal link between P's belief and the truth about the weather, yet intuitively P may still know it will rain.**, **Testimony often transmits knowledge without a direct causal connection between the belief and the truth.**
