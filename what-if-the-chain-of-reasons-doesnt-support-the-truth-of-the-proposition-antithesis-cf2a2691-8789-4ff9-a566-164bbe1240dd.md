# What if the chain of reasons doesn't support the truth of the proposition?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** cf2a2691-8789-4ff9-a566-164bbe1240dd
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism with Foundational Beliefs](infinitism-with-foundational-beliefs-synthesis-4273f090-f358-492e-9e7c-904c2a039ab2.md)

## Content

**An infinite chain of reasons may contain beliefs that do not cohere or connect logically.**, **Suppose there is a belief justified by an infinite chain of reasons, but at least one belief in the chain is false or unwarranted. Intuitively, the entire chain might fail to support the truth of the main proposition.**, **A justified belief chain that includes even one false belief can undermine the truthfulness of the conclusion.**
