# Are defeaters too context-dependent to define knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 55c3babc-e4ae-47bd-9e6d-e39ba854741b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [No Defeaters Theory](no-defeaters-theory-synthesis-e6eeb208-311d-4d09-a784-60d3e95f478f.md)

## Content

**A defeater is information that would undermine justification if available.**, **The relevance and impact of potential defeaters vary immensely from one context to another.**, **Consequently, requiring the absence of defeaters might make knowledge excessively contingent on variable factors.**
