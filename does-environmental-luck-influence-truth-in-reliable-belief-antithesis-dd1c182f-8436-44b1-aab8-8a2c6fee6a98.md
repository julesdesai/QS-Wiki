# Does environmental luck influence truth in reliable belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** dd1c182f-8436-44b1-aab8-8a2c6fee6a98
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalism with Reliable Beliefs](externalism-with-reliable-beliefs-synthesis-44f393ff-3a5c-4b93-8788-7ab032b1badd.md)

## Content

**Suppose a hiker whose map-reading skills are highly reliable is navigating the wilderness.**, **Unbeknownst to the hiker, there has been a swift change in the landscape due to a landslide, yet the hiker still reaches the destination using the typical map navigation process.**, **The navigational success is due more to luck than the process, suggesting the resulting belief lacks the certainty to be deemed knowledge.**
