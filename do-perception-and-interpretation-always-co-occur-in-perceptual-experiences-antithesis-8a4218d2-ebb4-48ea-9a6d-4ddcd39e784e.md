# Do perception and interpretation always co-occur in perceptual experiences?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 8a4218d2-ebb4-48ea-9a6d-4ddcd39e784e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Dual Aspect Theory](dual-aspect-theory-synthesis-e0a4859a-34ba-4bc9-9368-89fce3ac2e21.md)

## Content

**Perception is the process by which sensory input is received from the environment.**, **Interpretation is the cognitive process by which perceived sensory information is understood and given meaning.**, **There can be cases where sensory reception occurs without significant interpretation, such as automatic reflexes or simple stimulus-response activities.**
