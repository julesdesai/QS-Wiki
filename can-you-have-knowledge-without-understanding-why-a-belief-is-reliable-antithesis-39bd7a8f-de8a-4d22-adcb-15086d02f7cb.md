# Can you have knowledge without understanding why a belief is reliable?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 39bd7a8f-de8a-4d22-adcb-15086d02f7cb
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Hybrid Externalism Summary](hybrid-externalism-summary-synthesis-2250092f-2630-4da2-af9c-09ed6c4e9cfe.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that a person knows a proposition without understanding why their belief is reliable. This understanding is generally considered critical to genuine knowledge.**, **Consider a scenario where a belief is true and reliable, but the person cannot comprehend the reliability. Intuitively, we would judge that the lack of comprehension indicates lack of knowledge.**
