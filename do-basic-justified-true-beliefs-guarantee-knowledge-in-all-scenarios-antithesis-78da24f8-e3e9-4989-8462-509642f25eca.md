# Do basic justified true beliefs guarantee knowledge in all scenarios?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 78da24f8-e3e9-4989-8462-509642f25eca

**Parent:** [Context-Sensitive Invariant Endorsement](context-sensitive-invariant-endorsement-synthesis-de3f576d-97e3-4b7e-b7cd-f089d4d156aa.md)

## Content

**A person, Jamie, believes that the museum is open today based on a trustworthy friend's assertion and holds this as a justified true belief. However, upon visiting the museum, it turns out the information was outdated, leading intuitively to the judgment that Jamie's belief wasn't really knowledge, as the justification failed to ensure truth.**
