# Can knowledge exist if justification is debunked later?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b34e8549-e48c-4dc6-986e-b7996afc7369
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology with Agent-Centric Justification](virtue-epistemology-with-agent-centric-justification-synthesis-7d83e915-f335-4c77-b1f8-3aa2f1497b9b.md)

## Content

**Consider person H who has justifiable true belief in a magical claim because they have been shown solid evidence of magic historically. However, future understanding debunks the magical claim. Intuitively, H did not know the claim despite their justified true belief.**, **The erosion of justification when historical evidence is shown to be false suggests that knowledge requires a robustness of justification which might not be aligned with fluctuating belief systems.**
