# Do instances of belief revision challenge truth tracking?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7ce5b04e-bfe1-469d-9881-eb1f87db7401
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Nozick's Truth Tracking Theory](nozicks-truth-tracking-theory-synthesis-6290274e-e060-4b88-85f5-f721b81423aa.md)

## Content

**Consider a situation where a person starts with a false belief, revises their belief based on evidence, and arrives at a true belief.**, **Intuitively, the person's subsequent true belief seems to qualify as knowledge despite initial error, questioning strict truth tracking.**
