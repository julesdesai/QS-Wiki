# How can Foundationalist Coherentism account for perceptual beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 92ae201b-7ab4-4443-b723-08a4898cac87
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundationalist Coherentism](foundationalist-coherentism-synthesis-b8a8c906-b541-4af2-8e95-f0f08da1d9c7.md)

## Content

**Perceptual beliefs include those about what we directly observe or experience through our senses.**, **If perceptual beliefs are considered basic and self-justifying, it assumes that our senses deliver indisputable truths, which might not account for perceptual errors.**, **The classification of perceptual beliefs as basic may overlook how they depend on coherence with other beliefs to be considered reliable or justified.**
