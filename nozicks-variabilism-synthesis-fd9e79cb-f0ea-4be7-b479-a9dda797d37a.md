## Content

**A person knows a proposition if they have a true belief**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief tracks the truth in varying nearby worlds**, **A belief tracks the truth if, in most nearby worlds in which the proposition is false, the person does not believe it and in most nearby worlds in which the proposition is true, the person believes it**
