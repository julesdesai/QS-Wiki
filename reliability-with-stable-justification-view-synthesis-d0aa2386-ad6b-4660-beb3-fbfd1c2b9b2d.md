## Content

**Knowledge requires that one's belief tracks the truth**, **A person's belief tracks the truth if, in relevant scenarios, they would not believe it if it were false**, **A person's belief tracks the truth if, in relevant scenarios, they do believe it when it is true**, **Knowledge requires that the justification for a belief remains stable over time**, **Knowledge does not obtain if the evidence for one's belief becomes unreliable over time**
