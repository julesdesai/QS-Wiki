# Does Coherence Always Resolve Disagreement?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** dabdcc5e-cd10-4148-8855-ce37ab3ce9aa
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Instrumental Coherence Theory](instrumental-coherence-theory-synthesis-188d4652-e220-47fa-9e2f-da98cf583190.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where individual A perceives an event directly and with an instrument, while individual B perceives it differently through their direct perception and another instrument. Both cases are coherent within themselves, suggesting that coherence does not necessarily resolve perceptual disagreements.**
