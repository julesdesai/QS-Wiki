# Knowledge as Safe Justified True Belief

**Parent:** [Can justified true belief be considered knowledge if formed through unreliable methods?](can-justified-true-belief-be-considered-knowledge-if-formed-through-unreliable-methods-antithesis-d3b53cad-4ed1-4b93-a15a-474d6ca67461.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief could not easily have been false given its formation**
