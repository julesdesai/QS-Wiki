# Is belief coupled with truth sufficient for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9db9463d-f45b-4627-b325-1c81bee8e9bc
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalist Virtue Contextualism](externalist-virtue-contextualism-synthesis-69e2f760-46ad-465f-a31e-67e150de071d.md)

## Content

**A person knows a proposition if they believe it and it is true is a claim about the nature of knowledge.**, **There is a plausible principle that knowledge requires more than mere belief and truth, such as justification or reason.**, **Belief coupled with truth without additional justification might not adequately capture the definition of knowledge.**
