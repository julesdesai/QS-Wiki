## Content

**Knowledge depends on the context of the knower**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief in the proposition is safe from error in nearby possible worlds**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be objectively true**, **The variation in standards for knowing a proposition across contexts does not affect the objective truth of the proposition**
