## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief tracks the truth across possible scenarios**, **Truth-tracking is consistent across different contexts**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the belief is true, and it tracks the truth**
