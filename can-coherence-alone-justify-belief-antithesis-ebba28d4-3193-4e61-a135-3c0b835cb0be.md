# Can coherence alone justify belief?

**Parent:** [Knowledge as Pragmatic Justification](knowledge-as-pragmatic-justification-synthesis-37551034-4a21-4a9a-b6a6-ef752276a308.md)

## Content

**It is possible for someone to hold a network of beliefs that are coherent with each other but nonetheless incorrect or based on false assumptions.**, **Justification solely based on coherence could support the knowledge of a false proposition if all beliefs are internally consistent but externally false.**
