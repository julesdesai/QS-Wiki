# Knowledge with a reliability condition

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Might sensitivity undermine knowledge in cases with strong supporting evidence?](might-sensitivity-undermine-knowledge-in-cases-with-strong-supporting-evidence-antithesis-29085a5d-4e8d-4596-8777-876b608d184e.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed through a reliable process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief formation process is reliable**
