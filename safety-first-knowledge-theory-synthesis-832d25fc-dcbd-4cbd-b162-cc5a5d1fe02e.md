## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief could not easily have been false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person would believe the proposition if it were true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that safety and belief conditions are met**
