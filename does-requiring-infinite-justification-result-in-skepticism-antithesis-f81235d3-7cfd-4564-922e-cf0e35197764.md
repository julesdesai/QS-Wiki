# Does requiring infinite justification result in skepticism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f81235d3-7cfd-4564-922e-cf0e35197764
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextual Infinitism](contextual-infinitism-synthesis-969d15cb-7035-4449-8e21-a18b5558491f.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that humans can access an infinite chain of reasons, as our cognitive capacities are finite**, **A plausible principle in epistemology is that knowledge must be attainable by human beings**, **Requiring infinite justification could lead to skepticism, as it seems impossible for anyone to satisfy the condition in ordinary cases of knowing mundane facts**
