**Parent:** [Is intellectual virtue necessary when external conditions secure truth?](is-intellectual-virtue-necessary-when-external-conditions-secure-truth-antithesis-747425a1-6a93-42ca-9537-0d2a5103eea6.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are external conditions securing the truth of the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there are external conditions securing the truth of the belief**
