# Knowledge as Coherentism with Reliabilist Elements

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification includes coherence among beliefs maintained by reliable processes**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification comes from coherent and reliable processes**
