## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification for the belief does not involve any false assumptions**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is justified, and the justification involves no false assumptions**
