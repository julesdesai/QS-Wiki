## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **A cognitive process is reliable if it produces true beliefs more often than not**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief is true and formed by a reliable process**
