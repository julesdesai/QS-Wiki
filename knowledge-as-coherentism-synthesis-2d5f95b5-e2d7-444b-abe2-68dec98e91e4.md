## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief to cohere with a system of beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient if the belief coheres within a justified system of beliefs and the proposition is true**
