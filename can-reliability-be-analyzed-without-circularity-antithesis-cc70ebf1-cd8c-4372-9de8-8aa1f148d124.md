# Can reliability be analyzed without circularity?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** cc70ebf1-cd8c-4372-9de8-8aa1f148d124
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilist Theory of Knowledge](reliabilist-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-b0906775-f1f5-4edb-9c31-9ab274e2992b.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a person claims to have knowledge because their belief is the result of a reliable process. This requires that they already know that the process is reliable.**, **It is implausible that one can know a process is reliable without normative justification of what 'reliable' means.**
