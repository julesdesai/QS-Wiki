# How does the possibility of foundational belief revision impact their foundational status?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2ee6e070-a1ca-423e-b066-9c63e4097fa7
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Fallibilist Foundationalism](fallibilist-foundationalism-synthesis-ed3484e7-a0b3-47a4-939c-ec83c0f7bb7c.md)

## Content

**A belief is justified if it rests on foundational beliefs.**, **Suppose foundational beliefs can be revised when new evidence or considerations arise, as with scientific theories that are generally reliable but subject to revision. If foundational beliefs are revised, intuitively, we have to say that their status as foundational might be undermined. If foundational beliefs can be revised, their foundational status is questionable because foundational beliefs should be beyond such revision.**
