## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is produced through an anti-self-deceptive reliable process external to the person’s belief system**, **Belief formation processes are relevant but do not require direct awareness by the individual**, **Any self-deceptive process disqualifies reliability**
