# Is there such a thing as too much reliance on virtue for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a4714faf-b1f7-4b8a-b715-1626773d78dc
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-deb477f8-4a11-4db0-ba96-00a9c4a7815c.md)

## Content

**Excessive emphasis on individual virtue may ignore the epistemic value of considering diverse perspectives and collaborating with others.**, **In a team problem-solving situation, lone reliance on personal intellectual virtue might lead to oversight of insights brought by team members, leading to a less robust understanding.**
