# Is truth sufficient for justification in knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 4702a5b5-559e-4928-850a-d1d3421c0a3c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [No-False-Lemma View](no-false-lemma-view-synthesis-d417d5a7-7b9d-4d6b-8a89-c64775b0283f.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a justified belief in a proposition stems from an unreliable source, yet the proposition is true. Intuitively, relying on an unreliable source undermines justification even if the proposition is true.**, **It is implausible that a true belief based on an unreliable source constitutes justified knowledge. Requirement for reliability questions the sufficiency of truth and some falsehood-free justification for genuine knowledge.**
