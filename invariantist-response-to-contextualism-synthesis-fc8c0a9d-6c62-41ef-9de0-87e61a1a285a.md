## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For knowledge to be attributed, it is necessary that the standards for knowledge are fixed and not context-dependent**, **The closure principle is preserved by ensuring knowledge standards are invariant**
