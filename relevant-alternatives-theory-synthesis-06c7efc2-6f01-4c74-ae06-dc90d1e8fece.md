## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they can rule out all relevant alternatives to the proposition being true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe it, it is true, and they can rule out all relevant alternatives**
