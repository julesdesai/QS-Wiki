# How do contextualist views handle truth across contexts?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b0841e5e-8219-4a9e-91e6-36a6f7d4153f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism with Safety Condition](contextualism-with-safety-condition-synthesis-de372d20-3394-4687-9741-2b1cb43580bc.md)

## Content

**In contextualism, the truth necessary for knowledge may appear to fluctuate with context-specific standards. It is implausible that a core component of knowledge like truth could be subject to change based on variable contexts.**, **Suppose a case where a true belief in one context does not count as knowledge in another due to different standards. Intuitively, this inconsistency seems to undermine truth as a stable foundation of knowledge.**
