# Can basic foundational beliefs truly exist without requiring justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7de3b958-f941-49c6-b636-1639988ff089
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundationalist Knowledge Summary](foundationalist-knowledge-summary-synthesis-d6291bb5-6e86-4606-a555-64e99394a6d4.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person believes an axiom is foundational without evidence. Intuitively, we would question whether the axiom truly needs no further justification.**, **It is implausible that any belief can serve as a genuine foundation for other beliefs without some kind of prior justification.**
