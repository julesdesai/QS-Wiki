# Knowledge as Justified True Belief without Gettier Problems

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **A person knows a proposition if these conditions are met and there are no Gettier-type cases**
