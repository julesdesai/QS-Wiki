## Content

**Knowledge is true belief that arises from intellectual virtue under conditions typical for that virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **The intellectual virtue must function correctly under standard conditions for it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe it, the proposition is true, and their intellectual virtue functions correctly under standard conditions**
