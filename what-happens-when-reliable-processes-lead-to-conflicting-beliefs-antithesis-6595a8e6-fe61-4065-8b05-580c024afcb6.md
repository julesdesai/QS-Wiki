# What happens when reliable processes lead to conflicting beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 6595a8e6-fe61-4065-8b05-580c024afcb6
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilist Coherentism](reliabilist-coherentism-synthesis-d6c1c642-66ac-40b0-82ca-187cf7250017.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which two reliable processes lead to conflicting but coherent belief systems. Intuitively, both systems cannot have justified beliefs even though they are coherent and produced by reliable processes.**, **It is implausible that two conflicting systems can both be justified if they are based on reliable and coherent processes.**
