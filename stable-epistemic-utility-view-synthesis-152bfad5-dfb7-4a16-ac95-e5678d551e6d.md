**Parent:** [Can a known proposition lose its status if utility diminishes?](can-a-known-proposition-lose-its-status-if-utility-diminishes-antithesis-a4049092-76cc-4854-bba3-460c6dbde60a.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief has stable practical utility over time**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, it is true, and the belief has stable practical utility over time**
