# Knowledge as Foundational Inferentialism

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Do all coherent sets lead to justified beliefs?](do-all-coherent-sets-lead-to-justified-beliefs-antithesis-c1f77ca5-7491-4622-a18b-5afa3c3393c8.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is inferred from foundational beliefs through valid reasoning**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is inferred from foundational beliefs through valid reasoning**
