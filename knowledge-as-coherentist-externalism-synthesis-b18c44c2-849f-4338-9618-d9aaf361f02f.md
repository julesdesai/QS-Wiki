# Knowledge as Coherentist Externalism

**Parent:** [How does disagreement among external factors challenge the view?](how-does-disagreement-among-external-factors-challenge-the-view-antithesis-0a12aec5-4d3f-422c-8c46-123ab587360a.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is part of a coherent system sustained by external factors**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is part of a coherent system sustained by external factors**
