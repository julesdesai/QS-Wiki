## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the person's belief to be formed in a way that it would not be true by luck**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, it is true, and their belief is sensitive to the truth**
