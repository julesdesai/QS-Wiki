# Does a true belief require proper epistemic grounding to be knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 31e613a4-f729-46f5-adb7-f48f959c6103
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-7601e066-11d5-4f0c-8c42-44a4cc1f650d.md)

## Content

**Imagine a person whose true belief arises directly from an intellectual virtue but lacks a proper epistemic grounding. Intuitively, this is not knowledge.**, **True beliefs must be supported by adequate epistemic grounding, not merely by the exercise of intellectual virtues.**
