## Content

**Knowledge is a true belief that tracks the truth across relevant situations**, **A belief tracks the truth if, in close possible worlds where the proposition is false, the person would not believe it**, **A belief tracks the truth if, in close possible worlds where the proposition is true, the person would believe it**, **Knowledge excludes cases where true beliefs arise from unreliable processes because they lack tracking**
