## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have an infinite, non-repeating chain of justification**, **Infinitism holds that an infinite series keeps providing new justifications without ending**
