# Knowledge as Cognitive Process Reliability

**Parent:** [Is it possible to have truth without belief and still know?](is-it-possible-to-have-truth-without-belief-and-still-know-antithesis-5697c945-d38f-416b-a908-7d180dc1c066.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition results from cognitive processes that are reliable**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that true propositions arise from reliable cognitive processes**, **Knowledge can exist even if there is no conscious belief formation at the time of recognizing the truth**
