## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, their belief must be formed through a process that reliably yields true beliefs**, **An intellectual virtue is a trait that contributes to forming reliable belief-forming processes**
