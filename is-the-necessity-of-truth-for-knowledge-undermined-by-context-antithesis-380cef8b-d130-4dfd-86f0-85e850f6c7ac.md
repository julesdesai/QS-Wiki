# Is the necessity of truth for knowledge undermined by context?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 380cef8b-d130-4dfd-86f0-85e850f6c7ac
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism with Defeat Condition](contextualism-with-defeat-condition-synthesis-644e1e82-6b43-472b-8e60-41eaf47756ee.md)

## Content

**A case in which some person believes a proposition due to misleading evidence, yet the proposition is true coincidentally, is not a case of knowledge.**, **A proposition that is true by chance, coupled with contextually variable standards, may not suffice to establish knowledge.**
