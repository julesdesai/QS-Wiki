# Can a person know a false proposition under this theory?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** aedffcc7-861d-4cee-947f-60f753f32bac
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism with Shared Justification](coherentism-with-shared-justification-synthesis-efbed405-50ec-4920-aeba-74abec1bb76e.md)

## Content

**A person can have a justified belief about a proposition even if the proposition is ultimately false.**, **If knowledge requires a true proposition, then justified belief in a false proposition cannot be knowledge.**
