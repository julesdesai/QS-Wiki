# Invariantist Theory of Knowledge

**Parent:** [Does contextual justification provide a stable standard for knowledge?](does-contextual-justification-provide-a-stable-standard-for-knowledge-antithesis-b9b1523c-839d-413f-aca4-30ee7b9e2155.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person's belief meets a fixed, invariant standard of justification**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief meets the invariant standard of justification**
