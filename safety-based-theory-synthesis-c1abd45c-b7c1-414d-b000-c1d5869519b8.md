## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **A belief is considered knowledge when it is formed in a manner that would not easily have been false**, **The closure principle is satisfied by ensuring beliefs are safely formed in all considered cases**
