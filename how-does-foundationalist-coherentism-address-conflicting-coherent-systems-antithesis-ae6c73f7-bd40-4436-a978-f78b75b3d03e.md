# How does Foundationalist Coherentism address conflicting coherent systems?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ae6c73f7-bd40-4436-a978-f78b75b3d03e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundationalist Coherentism](foundationalist-coherentism-synthesis-b8a8c906-b541-4af2-8e95-f0f08da1d9c7.md)

## Content

**Different coherent systems might exist where a set of beliefs supports each other internally and justifies beliefs, even if they substantiate contradictory conclusions.**, **The existence of multiple, conflicting coherent systems challenges the notion that coherence alone can assure the truth or justification of a belief.**, **A mechanism to differentiate between competing coherent systems is necessary to resolve which system leads to justified true beliefs.**
