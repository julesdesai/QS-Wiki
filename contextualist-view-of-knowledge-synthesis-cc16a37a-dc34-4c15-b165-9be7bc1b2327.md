## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person’s belief in the proposition aligns with epistemic standards relevant to the context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that it is true and their belief aligns with relevant contextual standards**
