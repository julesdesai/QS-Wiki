## Content

**Knowledge consists of true belief with a reliable and robust intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed by a reliable intellectual virtue**, **A person knows a proposition if they believe it, the proposition is true, and their belief is formed by a reliable and robust intellectual virtue**
