# Is contextually variable justification reliable?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 034dcc37-13fd-45f2-8dbd-21424043e8a7
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Epistemic Contextualism: Varying Standards Across Contexts](epistemic-contextualism-varying-standards-across-contexts-synthesis-e2822998-5307-4123-86a7-bb234ea90892.md)

## Content

**Standards of justification vary with the context and purpose of inquiry.**, **If justification varies by context, reliability of knowledge could be questioned, as knowledge might not be consistent across contexts.**, **Inconsistent knowledge negates the premise of knowledge as justified true belief.**
