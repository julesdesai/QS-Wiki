# Knowledge as Belief with High Probability

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 5f6763af-22f4-4b31-b34b-6b4607012898
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is truth an absolute necessity for knowledge?](is-truth-an-absolute-necessity-for-knowledge-antithesis-02e459af-0435-4b89-8d52-35fd5570e16d.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the probability of the proposition to be high**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing it, and the probability of the proposition is high**
