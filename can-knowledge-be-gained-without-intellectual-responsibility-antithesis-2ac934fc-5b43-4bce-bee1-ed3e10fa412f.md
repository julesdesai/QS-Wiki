# Can knowledge be gained without intellectual responsibility?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2ac934fc-5b43-4bce-bee1-ed3e10fa412f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Responsibilist Epistemology](responsibilist-epistemology-synthesis-69752ad8-5d3c-409a-957c-e718f4ec264b.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person forms a true belief on a whim without engaging with evidence or critically evaluating sources. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that the person could still gain knowledge.**, **There is a plausible principle that individuals can know propositions through lucky guesses, which do not require the fulfillment of intellectual responsibilities.**
