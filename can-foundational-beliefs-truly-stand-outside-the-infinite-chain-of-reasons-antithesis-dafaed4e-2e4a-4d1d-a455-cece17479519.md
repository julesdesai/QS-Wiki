# Can foundational beliefs truly stand outside the infinite chain of reasons?

## Content

**A foundational belief, by definition, does not rely on others for justification.**, **In a conceptualization requiring an infinite chain, foundational beliefs nonetheless play a role in grounding them.**, **It is questionable whether anything can exist outside an infinite framework if required to participate, conceptually, within it.**
