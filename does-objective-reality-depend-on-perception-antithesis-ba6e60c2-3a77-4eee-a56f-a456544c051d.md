# Does objective reality depend on perception?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ba6e60c2-3a77-4eee-a56f-a456544c051d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Phenomenal Realism](phenomenal-realism-synthesis-c387191c-1db1-4c6c-83ed-c50007cbb9d6.md)

## Content

**In a scenario where a tree falls in a forest with no one to hear it, one can question if the sound still exists objectively.**, **Philosophically, the sound of a falling tree in the absence of a perceiver raises doubts about objective reality's independence from perception.**, **Objective reality may not always be inferred solely from perception if it is considered contingent on perception.**
