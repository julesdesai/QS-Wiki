# Knowledge as Virtue Epistemology

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 82bcb83b-b57a-4fcb-a799-3fd5d789c47e
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is immediate true belief knowledge?](is-immediate-true-belief-knowledge-antithesis-ff410014-7bb3-41ab-a079-fe324cbf6c35.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is the result of an intellectual virtue**, **For a belief to result from an intellectual virtue, it is necessary that the belief arises from cognitive abilities reliably aimed at truth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is the result of an intellectual virtue**
