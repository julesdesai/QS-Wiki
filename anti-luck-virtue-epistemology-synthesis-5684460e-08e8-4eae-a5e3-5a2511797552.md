# Anti-Luck Virtue Epistemology

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Is remembering an intellectual virtue?](is-remembering-an-intellectual-virtue-antithesis-0db0ed7b-5ae2-40b4-9100-35a0f671c2b7.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is not acquired by luck but through reliable methods**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is not acquired by luck but through reliable methods**
