# Generous Virtue Epistemology

**Parent:** [What about knowledge through testimony?](what-about-knowledge-through-testimony-antithesis-bde690a7-f611-45a9-8b46-2e531ff59588.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief to derive from an intellectual virtue or socially trustworthy source**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief derives from an intellectual virtue or socially trustworthy source**
