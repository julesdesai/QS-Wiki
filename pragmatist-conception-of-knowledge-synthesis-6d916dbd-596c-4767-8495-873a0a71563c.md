## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification for their belief is based on practical consequences of holding that belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief's justification is tied to its practical utility**
