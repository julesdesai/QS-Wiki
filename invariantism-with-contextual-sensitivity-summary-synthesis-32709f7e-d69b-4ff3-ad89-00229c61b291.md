## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have justification, the proposition is true, and they believe it**, **Justification standards for knowledge are fixed but sensitive to different kinds of evidence or reasons presented in distinct contexts**
