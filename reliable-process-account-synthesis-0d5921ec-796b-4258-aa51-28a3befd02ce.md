## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief be produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **A reliable cognitive process is one that generally leads to true beliefs**
