# Knowledge as Coherent True Belief with Understanding

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Could there be true propositions known without practical utility?](could-there-be-true-propositions-known-without-practical-utility-antithesis-55d7b8cd-549b-444d-bc79-20149793e0ee.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief in the proposition coheres with their other beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they understand the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief coheres with their other beliefs, they understand the proposition, and the proposition is true**
