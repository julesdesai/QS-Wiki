## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they understand the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the way they come to understand the proposition is reliable**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they understand the proposition, the proposition is true, and the process of understanding is reliable**
