# Does having a justified belief guarantee truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b6ec3d74-06c3-4aaf-b68c-b95af983a606
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Experiential Foundationalism](experiential-foundationalism-synthesis-fd2d94ab-5595-4b29-a3f8-2962ee0c4e3c.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that a justified belief can still be false. This principle together with the claim that all justified beliefs are based on experiences suggests that justification does not always lead to truth.**
