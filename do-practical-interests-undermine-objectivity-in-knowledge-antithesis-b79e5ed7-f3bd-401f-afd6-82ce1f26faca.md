# Do practical interests undermine objectivity in knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b79e5ed7-f3bd-401f-afd6-82ce1f26faca
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Subject-Sensitive Invariantism](subject-sensitive-invariantism-synthesis-ef027452-1300-45f1-9d7c-023fdb6cd119.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a doctor, under pressure to provide quick diagnoses, believes a proposition about a patient's condition based on insufficient evidence. Intuitively, the involvement of practical interests risks bias and may undermine objectivity, challenging the view that practical interests can form a basis for knowledge.**
