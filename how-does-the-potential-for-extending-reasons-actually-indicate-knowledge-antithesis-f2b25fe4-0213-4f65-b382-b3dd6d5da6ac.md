# How does the potential for extending reasons actually indicate knowledge?

## Content

**Suppose a case where a belief is labeled true solely for the potential of infinite reason extensions. Intuitively, we judge this not to constitute knowledge without actual current support.**, **An infinite potential extension implies no immediate justification, making it indistinct from beliefs without rational backdrop.**
