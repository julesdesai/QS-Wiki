# Can a belief produced by a reliable process be false and still count as knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 4273251d-b29e-4e23-bdb0-b26a0e1bd245
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism bases knowledge on reliable processes](reliabilism-bases-knowledge-on-reliable-processes-synthesis-e8668809-0baa-4531-bd9d-063ab7893b2b.md)

## Content

**A belief can be produced by a reliable process but still turn out to be false in some instances due to unforeseen circumstances.**, **Intuitively, if a belief is false, it cannot count as knowledge regardless of the reliability of the process that produced it.**
