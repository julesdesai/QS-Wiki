# Does a person's emotional state affect what they know?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 381f55f1-6174-4614-9652-eccf0b052a91
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Subject-Sensitive Invariantism](subject-sensitive-invariantism-synthesis-ef027452-1300-45f1-9d7c-023fdb6cd119.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person, undergoing extreme emotional distress, firmly believes that their insurance policy is valid and true, yet they are unable to engage with the practical implications of this belief due to their emotional state. Intuitively, we would judge that their emotional state undermines their knowledge in this context.**
