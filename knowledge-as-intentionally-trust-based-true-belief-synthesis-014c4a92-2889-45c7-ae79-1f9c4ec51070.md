# Knowledge as Intentionally Trust-Based True Belief

**Parent:** [Could an individual know something unknowingly if all conditions are met coincidentally?](could-an-individual-know-something-unknowingly-if-all-conditions-are-met-coincidentally-antithesis-b1d41956-1193-49f8-a5d8-cc30c50a1d97.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is intentionally based on trust between knower and source**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is intentionally based on trust between knower and source**
