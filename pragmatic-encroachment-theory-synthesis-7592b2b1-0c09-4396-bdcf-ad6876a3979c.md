## Content

**The standards for knowing a proposition include practical factors, not merely epistemic ones**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that practical stakes do not undermine their epistemic justification**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the practical stakes do not render the justification inadequate**
