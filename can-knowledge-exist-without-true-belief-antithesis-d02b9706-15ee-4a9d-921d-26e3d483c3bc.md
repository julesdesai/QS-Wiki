# Can knowledge exist without true belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d02b9706-15ee-4a9d-921d-26e3d483c3bc
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Epistemic Contextualism: Varying Standards Across Contexts](epistemic-contextualism-varying-standards-across-contexts-synthesis-e2822998-5307-4123-86a7-bb234ea90892.md)

## Content

**Knowledge requires justified true belief to be valid.**, **A belief that is not true cannot serve as knowledge in any context.**, **If standards of justification vary with context, it is possible to have justification without truth, thus lacking knowledge.**
