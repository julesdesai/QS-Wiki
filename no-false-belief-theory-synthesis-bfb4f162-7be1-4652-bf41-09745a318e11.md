## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their justification includes no false beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification without false beliefs, and the proposition is true**
