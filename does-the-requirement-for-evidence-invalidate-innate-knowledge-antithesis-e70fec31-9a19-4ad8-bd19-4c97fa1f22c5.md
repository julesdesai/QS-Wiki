# Does the requirement for evidence invalidate innate knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e70fec31-9a19-4ad8-bd19-4c97fa1f22c5

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Evidentialist Theory of Knowledge](evidentialist-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-79c62cab-dbc0-4dae-ad91-54d5bcae1188.md)

## Content

**Suppose some people have intuitive understanding of basic mathematical truths without having learned them formally. Intuitively, we might judge they have knowledge despite not having evidence from external sources.**, **The possibility of possessing innate knowledge questions whether all knowledge requires evidential support.**
