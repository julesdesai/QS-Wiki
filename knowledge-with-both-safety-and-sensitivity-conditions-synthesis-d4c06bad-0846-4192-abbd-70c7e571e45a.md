# Knowledge with both safety and sensitivity conditions

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, the belief must cohere with a reliable method of belief formation**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not easily believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in similar situations, the person would not form a false belief**
