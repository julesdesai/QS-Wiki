# Infinitism as a Theory of Knowledge

**Parent:** [Is it possible for belief alone to determine knowledge?](is-it-possible-for-belief-alone-to-determine-knowledge-antithesis-3dbf96f7-ab22-4952-8b4b-5b81e6a722f7.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their justification consists of an infinite, non-repeating chain of reasons**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, it is true, and they have an infinite chain of justifications**
