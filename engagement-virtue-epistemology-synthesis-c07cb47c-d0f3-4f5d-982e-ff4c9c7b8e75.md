## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from intellectual virtue or competence**, **For a proposition to count as knowledge, it is necessary that there is engagement of intellectual virtues with the environment**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the engagement of intellectual virtues contributes to belief formation even if not directly perceived by the knower**
