# Knowledge as Modest Reflective Epistemology

**Parent:** [Must a belief be simultaneously justified by reliability and reflection to constitute knowledge?](must-a-belief-be-simultaneously-justified-by-reliability-and-reflection-to-constitute-knowledge-antithesis-a0aa3ec6-a162-4e17-84d8-8e3c4d578f2a.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they reflectively endorse the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and they reflectively endorse the belief**
