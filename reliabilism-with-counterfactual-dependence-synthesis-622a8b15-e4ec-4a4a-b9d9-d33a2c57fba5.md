# Reliabilism with Counterfactual Dependence

**Parent:** [Do Gettier cases undermine truth-tracking as necessary for knowledge?](do-gettier-cases-undermine-truth-tracking-as-necessary-for-knowledge-antithesis-555211fe-769d-49c3-8783-cbd5ef757874.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it requires that their belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief corresponds to the truth in the actual world**, **If the proposition were false, the reliable process would not produce a belief in it**, **If similar propositions were true in similar situations, the reliable process would still produce a belief in them**
