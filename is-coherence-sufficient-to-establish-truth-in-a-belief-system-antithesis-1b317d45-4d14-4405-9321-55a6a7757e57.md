# Is coherence sufficient to establish truth in a belief system?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1b317d45-4d14-4405-9321-55a6a7757e57
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism View](coherentism-view-synthesis-4dcf0483-85cc-4ed6-8a5e-86aaec2b7626.md)

## Content

**Consider a belief system that is coherent but based entirely on false premises, such as a coherent set of conspiracy theories. Intuitively, we would judge that coherence alone does not ensure the truth of any constituent belief.**, **There is a plausible principle that truth requires correspondence with the actual world, not just internal coherence.**
