# Reliable Virtue Responsibilism

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 1f5b1150-d4d5-41a7-ae8e-b76ad208c496
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge result from unreliable virtue?](can-knowledge-result-from-unreliable-virtue-antithesis-198a5c93-5914-4059-8ade-edb3b7cbf293.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief to result from a reliable intellectual virtue or skill**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief results from a reliable intellectual virtue or skill**
