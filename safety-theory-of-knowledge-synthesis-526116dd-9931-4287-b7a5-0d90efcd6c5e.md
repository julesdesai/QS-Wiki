# Safety Theory of Knowledge

**Depth:** 3
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can outdated evidence limit knowledge?](can-outdated-evidence-limit-knowledge-antithesis-8317706e-1d23-4d4f-8489-44d31a1e311d.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person's belief could not easily have been false**, **The safety condition ensures that in nearby possible worlds, the belief remains true**
