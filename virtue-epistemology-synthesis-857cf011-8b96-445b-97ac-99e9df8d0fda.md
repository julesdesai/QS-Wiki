## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief in the proposition results from an intellectual virtue**, **An intellectual virtue is a trait that systematically leads to the acquisition of true beliefs**, **Intellectual virtues include traits like open-mindedness and intellectual courage**
