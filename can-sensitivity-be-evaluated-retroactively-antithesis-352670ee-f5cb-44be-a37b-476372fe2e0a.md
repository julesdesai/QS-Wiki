# Can sensitivity be evaluated retroactively?

## Content

**Assume a person historically believed a scientific theory that was true at the time based on available evidence.**, **New findings later disprove the theory, changing its truth value.**, **Evaluating sensitivity post hoc is problematic as the condition relies on hypothetical judgment from a changing perspective.**
