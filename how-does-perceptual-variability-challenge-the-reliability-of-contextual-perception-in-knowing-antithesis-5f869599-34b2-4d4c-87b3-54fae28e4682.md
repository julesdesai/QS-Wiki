# How does perceptual variability challenge the reliability of contextual perception in knowing?

**Node ID:** 5f869599-34b2-4d4c-87b3-54fae28e4682
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

## Content

**The perception of an object can vary significantly depending on lighting conditions, distance, and angle of view.**, **A person's perception may be influenced by prior knowledge, expectations, and emotions, leading to different interpretations of the same sensory data.**, **If perception is so variable, any knowledge claim based on perception may not consistently correspond to reality.**
