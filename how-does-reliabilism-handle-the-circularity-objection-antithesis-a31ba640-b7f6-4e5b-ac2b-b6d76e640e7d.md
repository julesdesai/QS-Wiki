# How does reliabilism handle the circularity objection?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a31ba640-b7f6-4e5b-ac2b-b6d76e640e7d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilist Theory of Knowledge](reliabilist-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-e6fc2437-0f22-487d-91cb-ba903a4e1b1f.md)

## Content

**Assessing the reliability of a cognitive process depends on knowing it consistently yields truth.**, **Determining if it consistently yields truth often involves relying on the same processes in question.**, **This circularity raises challenges in independently establishing process reliability.**
