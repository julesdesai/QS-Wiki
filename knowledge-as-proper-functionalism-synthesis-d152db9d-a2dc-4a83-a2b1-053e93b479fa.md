# Knowledge as Proper Functionalism

**Parent:** [Is sensitivity too demanding?](is-sensitivity-too-demanding-antithesis-244c81f8-3a29-47c0-916d-1f4a40f0c7d1.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief results from a reliable cognitive process functioning properly**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief results from a reliable cognitive process functioning properly**
