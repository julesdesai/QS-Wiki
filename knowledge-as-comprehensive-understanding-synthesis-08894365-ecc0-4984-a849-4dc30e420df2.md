# Knowledge as Comprehensive Understanding

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they understand the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the understanding encompasses theoretical and practical dimensions**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they understand the proposition comprehensively and the proposition is true**
