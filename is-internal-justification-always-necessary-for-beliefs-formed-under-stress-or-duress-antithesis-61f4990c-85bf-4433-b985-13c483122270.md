# Is internal justification always necessary for beliefs formed under stress or duress?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 61f4990c-85bf-4433-b985-13c483122270
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Internalist Justification Theory](internalist-justification-theory-synthesis-60885d02-ba8c-49f7-a8ac-6cdf9db1e5a8.md)

## Content

**In a crisis, a firefighter believes a system will fail but cannot articulate their reasoning due to time constraints.**, **This belief is based on extensive expertise with similar systems during past incidents.**, **The belief is justified solely by the reliance on the firefighter's past experience, not internal access to detailed reasons.**
