# Are all reliable cognitive traits considered intellectual virtues?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b37d159a-cd9f-48e2-a422-0772dede6866
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-40b3d691-a5bb-479c-ada3-1931521a86e5.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person develops a reliable cognitive trait of forming beliefs based on stereotypes that happen to be true. Intuitively, one might be hesitant to call this an intellectual virtue, even if it leads to true beliefs.**, **Intellectual virtues are expected to have a moral or ethical dimension which is not satisfied by reliable traits that are morally questionable.**, **If not all reliable cognitive traits are intellectual virtues, then the connection between having such traits and attaining knowledge might be problematic.**
