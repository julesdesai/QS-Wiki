# Can non-virtuous belief be considered knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7d2ebd83-9c2e-4134-83c9-04f68b7666cd
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-5dae303e-e392-4dd3-a9fb-c518398dc86e.md)

## Content

**In a situation where a person's belief is true and they are strongly confident, despite no intellectual virtue being involved, it may still be perceived as knowledge.**, **The perception of knowledge in the absence of intellectual virtue challenges the necessity of virtue for knowledge.**
