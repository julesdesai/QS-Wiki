## Content

**For a belief to constitute knowledge, the belief must not only be true but could not easily have been false**, **A belief is safe if in nearby possible worlds where the belief is held, it is also true**, **The cognitive faculties must aim at producing true beliefs**, **A belief satisfying the safety condition constitutes knowledge if it is true**
