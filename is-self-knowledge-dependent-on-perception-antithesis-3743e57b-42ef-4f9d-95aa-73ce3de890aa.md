# Is self-knowledge dependent on perception?

**Node ID:** 3743e57b-42ef-4f9d-95aa-73ce3de890aa

**Parent:** [Inference-Based Phenomenalism](inference-based-phenomenalism-synthesis-3c5287e4-c850-454f-b68f-cef5a0c2976c.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person knows their own thoughts and feelings without perceptual input. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that this knowledge is independent of perception.**, **There is a plausible principle that self-knowledge can exist apart from perceptual experiences.**
