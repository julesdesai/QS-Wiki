## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed by a reliable cognitive process**, **The proposition's truth must be a conjunct in any reliable belief formation**
