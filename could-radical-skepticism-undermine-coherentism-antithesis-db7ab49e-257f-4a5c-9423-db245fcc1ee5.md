# Could radical skepticism undermine coherentism?

**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Integrated Coherentism](integrated-coherentism-synthesis-026ba0da-a1c0-49d0-b737-4a448975b203.md)

## Content

**Suppose we imagine a scenario in which all our beliefs are the result of an evil demon manipulating our cognitive processes. Intuitively, this possibility challenges the reliability of even a coherent set of beliefs.**, **There is a plausible principle that true justification must preclude radical skepticism to maintain secure knowledge. This principle suggests that coherentism cannot fully safeguard against external skeptical challenges.**
