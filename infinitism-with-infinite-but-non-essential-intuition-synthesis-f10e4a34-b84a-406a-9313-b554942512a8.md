## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **Justification for knowledge involves an infinite chain of reasons**, **Each reason in the chain provides some support for the next**, **Intuitive judgments accompany the chain without ending it**
