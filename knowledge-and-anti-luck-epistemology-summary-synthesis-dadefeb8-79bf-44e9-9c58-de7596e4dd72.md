## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is not true by mere luck**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the belief is not true by mere luck, and the proposition is true**
