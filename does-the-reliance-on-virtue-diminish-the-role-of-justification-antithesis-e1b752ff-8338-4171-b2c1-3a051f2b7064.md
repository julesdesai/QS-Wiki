# Does the reliance on virtue diminish the role of justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e1b752ff-8338-4171-b2c1-3a051f2b7064
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-8faa0d3d-af6f-4054-8090-da1e790293ff.md)

## Content

**The theory downplays the traditional importance of justification in accounting for knowledge.**, **In cases where virtues lead to true belief but lack explicit justification, the intuitive sense of knowledge as justified true belief is threatened.**, **If an epistemic virtue consistently results in true belief without any recognizable form of justification, the role of justification in knowledge seems diminished.**
