# Disposition-Based Reliabilism

**Parent:** [Can knowledge exist without direct causation?](can-knowledge-exist-without-direct-causation-antithesis-035da441-93e8-421c-8ac6-f511faa2abe4.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief disposition is formed by a reliable process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief disposition is formed by a reliable process**
