# Can contradictory propositions both be known?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 09342d6b-57c9-4601-a06d-f1cef2719737
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Sensitive belief theory of knowledge](sensitive-belief-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-a04ea135-b51c-4804-8a79-c6b4af4a4910.md)

## Content

**Imagine two people, one who sincerely believes a proposition and another who believes the logically contradictory proposition. Both would claim knowledge of their beliefs. Logically, at least one belief must be false, showing that not both beliefs can be sensitively held as knowledgeable.**, **Simultaneously holding or claiming knowledge of contradictory propositions illustrates issues with sensitivity where conflicting truths are concerned.**
