## Content

**Knowledge requires a causal connection between the truth of the proposition and the belief**, **The belief must be caused by the fact or state of affairs that makes the proposition true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **The causal relationship supports the belief irrespective of additional evidence**
