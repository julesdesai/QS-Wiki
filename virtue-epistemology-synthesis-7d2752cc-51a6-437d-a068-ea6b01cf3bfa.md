## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is attributable to an intellectual virtue or a cognitive ability**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is formed through the exercise of an intellectual virtue or cognitive ability**
