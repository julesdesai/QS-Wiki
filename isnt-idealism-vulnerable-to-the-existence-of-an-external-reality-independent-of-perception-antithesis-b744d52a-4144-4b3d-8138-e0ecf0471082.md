# Isn't idealism vulnerable to the existence of an external reality independent of perception?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b744d52a-4144-4b3d-8138-e0ecf0471082
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Idealism](idealism-synthesis-6a8ea4b4-8cf6-44ff-98fb-cf08805f781d.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a tree falls in a forest where no human is present to perceive it. Intuitively, the tree's existence and its fall do not depend on any mental phenomena or perception.**, **This scenario suggests that there are aspects of reality that exist independently of mental constructs or perceptual experiences.**
