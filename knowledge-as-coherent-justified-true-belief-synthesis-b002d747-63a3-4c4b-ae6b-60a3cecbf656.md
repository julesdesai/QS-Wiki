**Parent:** [Is the sufficiency of justified true belief with conditions affected by luck?](is-the-sufficiency-of-justified-true-belief-with-conditions-affected-by-luck-antithesis-b8df08a8-d3eb-4a12-9d24-73da170f808f.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres with a comprehensive justified belief system**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief coheres in a non-fallacious way with a justified belief system**
