## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief arises from both an intellectual virtue and moral virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief arises from the integration of intellectual and moral virtues**
