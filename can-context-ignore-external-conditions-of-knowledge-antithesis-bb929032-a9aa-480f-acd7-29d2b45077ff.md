# Can context ignore external conditions of knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** bb929032-a9aa-480f-acd7-29d2b45077ff
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualist Foundationalism](contextualist-foundationalism-synthesis-b757e054-5c50-4073-a4ee-c1ee757cbdbb.md)

## Content

**A case in which a person knows the time because their clock says "2 PM" but the clock stopped an hour ago does not allow internal context to determine knowledge.**, **External conditions that determine knowledge bear relevance even when internal context appears justified.**, **If context alone determined justification, external factors crucial for knowledge could be wrongly ignored.**
