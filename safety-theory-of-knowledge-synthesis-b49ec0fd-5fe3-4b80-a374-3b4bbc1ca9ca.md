# Safety Theory of Knowledge

**Parent:** [Could a belief be lucky to be true and yet not knowledge?](could-a-belief-be-lucky-to-be-true-and-yet-not-knowledge-antithesis-5b1ac342-667e-479c-93d8-e8d6a2a99c44.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that, in all nearby possible worlds where the person believes the proposition, the proposition remains true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief is formed in a safe manner**
