## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have an indication mechanism**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **The indication mechanism must reliably indicate true propositions more often than not**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition based on the indication mechanism**
