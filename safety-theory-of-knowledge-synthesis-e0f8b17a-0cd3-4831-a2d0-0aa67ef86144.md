## Content

**A person knows a proposition if their belief is true in most nearby possible worlds**, **A belief is safe if in nearby possible worlds where the person believes it, the belief is still true**, **Knowledge requires not just truth but a belief that could not easily have been false**, **This approach ensures that skepticism is addressed by focusing on the stability of belief across scenarios**
