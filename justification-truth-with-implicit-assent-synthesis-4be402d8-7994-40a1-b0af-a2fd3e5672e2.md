**Parent:** [Is the traditional tripartite definition of knowledge undermined without belief?](is-the-traditional-tripartite-definition-of-knowledge-undermined-without-belief-antithesis-edf49620-992a-4aa9-aaf7-277a12ac6932.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient to implicitly assent to the proposition**, **A person knows a proposition if it is true, they have justification for it, and assent can be implicit without explicit belief**
