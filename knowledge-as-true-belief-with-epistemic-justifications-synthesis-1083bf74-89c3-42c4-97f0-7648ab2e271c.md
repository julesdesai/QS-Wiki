## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are multiple epistemic forms of justification for the belief**, **Pragmatic consequences are not considered as a form of justification**
