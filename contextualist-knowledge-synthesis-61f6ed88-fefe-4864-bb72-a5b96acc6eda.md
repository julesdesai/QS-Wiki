## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they fulfill the epistemic standards prevailing in their context**, **Epistemic standards are context-dependent and vary with different epistemic situations**
