# Internalist Knowledge with Non-Utility Condition

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** d36f8c21-8e01-4da8-a8bb-53439b78afc9
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge be useless?](can-knowledge-be-useless-antithesis-8342035c-9026-400f-a9c5-75934a284046.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is justified internally**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is not the result of manipulation**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, their belief is justified internally, and the belief is not the result of manipulation**
