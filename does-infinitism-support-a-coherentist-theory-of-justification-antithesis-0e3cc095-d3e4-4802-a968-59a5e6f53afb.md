# Does Infinitism support a coherentist theory of justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0e3cc095-d3e4-4802-a968-59a5e6f53afb
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism](infinitism-synthesis-dd7098a7-016a-4f9d-9b36-944f2be4f1f4.md)

## Content

**Coherentism posits that beliefs are justified by their coherence with other beliefs in a system.**, **An infinite chain of reasons might suggest no coherent, stable system since it continuously unfolds without a self-sustaining closure.**, **This could mean that infinite justification does not support stable coherence, but rather challenges the foundational stability of coherent belief systems.**
