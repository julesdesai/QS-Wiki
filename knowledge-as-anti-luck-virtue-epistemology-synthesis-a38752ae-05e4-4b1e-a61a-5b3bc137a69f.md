# Knowledge as Anti-Luck Virtue Epistemology

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is not formed through luck**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have a true belief formed from an intellectually virtuous process without luck**
