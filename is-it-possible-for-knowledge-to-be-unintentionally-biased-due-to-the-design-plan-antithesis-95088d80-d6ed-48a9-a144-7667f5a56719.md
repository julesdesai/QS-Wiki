# Is it possible for knowledge to be unintentionally biased due to the design plan?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 95088d80-d6ed-48a9-a144-7667f5a56719
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Proper Function Theory](proper-function-theory-synthesis-eb8cad20-b2ac-4ebe-8b63-c23141145814.md)

## Content

**Suppose a case where the design plan for a cognitive faculty unintentionally incorporates a bias. Intuitively, the resulting belief may not qualify as knowledge if influenced by this bias.**, **A plausible principle is that bias in the design plan undermines the objectivity and reliability expected in claims of knowledge.**
