## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person deserves epistemic credit for the truth of the belief**, **For a belief to give epistemic credit to a person, the belief must be true because of the person's exercise of intellectual virtues**
