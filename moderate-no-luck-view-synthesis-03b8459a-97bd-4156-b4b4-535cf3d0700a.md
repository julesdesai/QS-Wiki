## Content

**Knowledge cannot fundamentally result from epistemic luck**, **Epistemic luck occurs when a belief is true because of factors unrelated to the reasons for the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, the influence of luck must be minimized**, **For a person to know a proposition, the means of arriving at the belief must be highly reliable**
