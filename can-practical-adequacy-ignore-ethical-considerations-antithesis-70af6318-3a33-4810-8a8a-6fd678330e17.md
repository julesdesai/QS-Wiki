# Can practical adequacy ignore ethical considerations?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 70af6318-3a33-4810-8a8a-6fd678330e17
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatism with Practical Adequacy](pragmatism-with-practical-adequacy-synthesis-7de50134-9521-4bfe-88f0-682c548d4774.md)

## Content

**Suppose a case where a person achieves their goals through unethical actions, such as cheating in a job application process to secure employment. Intuitively, we say that while the action was practically successful, it was not ethically justified or acceptable.**, **There is a plausible principle that practical adequacy should account for ethical considerations in the justification of beliefs, and acting unethically in achieving success raises questions about the adequacy of this concept.**
