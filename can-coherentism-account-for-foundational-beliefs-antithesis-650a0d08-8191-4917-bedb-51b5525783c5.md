# Can coherentism account for foundational beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 650a0d08-8191-4917-bedb-51b5525783c5
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism with Infinite Justification](coherentism-with-infinite-justification-synthesis-3b5a9da7-34d4-4bfb-819c-753bf0081c0b.md)

## Content

**Foundationalism holds that some beliefs are self-justified or justified by non-inferential means.**, **A belief system that requires all beliefs to be justified through coherence lacks non-inferentially justified foundational beliefs.**, **The absence of foundational beliefs challenges the coherence among interconnected beliefs.**
