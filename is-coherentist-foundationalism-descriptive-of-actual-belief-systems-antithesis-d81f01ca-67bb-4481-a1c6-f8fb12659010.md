# Is coherentist foundationalism descriptive of actual belief systems?

**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentist Foundationalism](coherentist-foundationalism-synthesis-d82a768f-5fd5-4bf6-9304-6b054d40d9c7.md)

## Content

**Consider a typical individual's belief system, which is often fragmented and inconsistent. Intuitively, such a system does not seem to fit the notion of a coherent belief network.**, **There is a plausible principle that a theoretical model should reflect actual states of belief. A system that routinely fails to capture this is problematic.**
