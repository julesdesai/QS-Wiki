# Can knowledge exist if belief basis remains untested?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f45f35c0-d61b-4bd8-b860-78ab653542ad
**Depth:** 2
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [No False Lemmas Theory](no-false-lemmas-theory-thesis-a96b2c39-d876-40fc-94b2-391826bfefda.md)

## Content

**A person believes a true proposition based on unverified assumptions that luckily turn out correct. Intuitively, we judge this scenario as lacking genuine knowledge.**, **Having justification that relies on assumptions which have not been tested should not suffice for knowledge even if the belief ends up being true.**
