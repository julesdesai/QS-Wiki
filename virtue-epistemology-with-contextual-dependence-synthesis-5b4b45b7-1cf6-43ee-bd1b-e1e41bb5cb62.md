# Virtue Epistemology with Contextual Dependence

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is a result of intellectual virtue**, **An intellectual virtue is a trait that systematically leads to acquiring true beliefs**, **The sufficiency of intellectual virtues for knowledge can vary depending on the context in which the belief is formed**
