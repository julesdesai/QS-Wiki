# Is there a boundary between reliable process and true insight?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f80c200f-ca32-4dfb-976d-50c50c6de25e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Epistemic Externalism](epistemic-externalism-synthesis-3f203114-9a4a-4d29-9451-dfb5538c3c76.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where an artist creates profound art due to subconscious influence without understanding their prowess. Intuitively, we would judge that creativity here lacks intentional insight.**, **There is a plausible principle that insight involves conscious engagement. This principle together with subconscious-driven art suggests a boundary between reliable processes and insight.**
