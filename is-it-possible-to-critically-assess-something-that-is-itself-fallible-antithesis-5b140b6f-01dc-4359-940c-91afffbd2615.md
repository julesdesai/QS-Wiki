# Is it possible to critically assess something that is itself fallible?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5b140b6f-01dc-4359-940c-91afffbd2615
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Critical Realism Summary](critical-realism-summary-synthesis-de08aecd-5e9b-4f9b-874a-d2dc97cb1697.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person sees a moving train and deduces it's moving right to left, while another deduces left to right based on fallible perceptual cues. Intuitively, these cues require assessment to ensure accurate interpretation.**, **Assumptions based on fallible perception suggest difficulty in assessment, since fallibility may lead to error-prone judgements.**, **The undertaking of critical assessments of perception with incorrect initial data may invalidate outcomes.**
