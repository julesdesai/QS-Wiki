# Modified Safety Theory for Conflicting Expertise

**Parent:** [Can the safety criterion accommodate conflicting expert opinions?](can-the-safety-criterion-accommodate-conflicting-expert-opinions-antithesis-ae35ad75-aa7b-46f1-901d-a48634932116.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, the belief must not be easily false**, **A belief is safe if in nearby possible worlds where the person forms the belief in the same manner and is exposed to similar expert opinions, the belief is not false**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief is true in a wide range of similar and nearby situations where there is consistency in expert consensus**, **Knowledge is immune to being easily false under minor changes in circumstances and variations in expert judgments**
