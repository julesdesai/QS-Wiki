# Are infinite chains of reasons internally contradictory?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c7c778b1-54ba-41f9-b62f-eecdc7f29085
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism](infinitism-synthesis-d655bb23-a2c0-4c5f-b0d3-18cd986ab587.md)

## Content

**An infinite chain of reasons can be seen as self-defeating if no point of closure exists for justification.**, **Suppose justification must be complete to equate to knowledge, intuitively an endless chain suggests incompleteness and contradiction in claiming justification.**, **Requiring each link to contribute to overall knowledge within an infinite chain makes the coherence of the justification process suspect.**
