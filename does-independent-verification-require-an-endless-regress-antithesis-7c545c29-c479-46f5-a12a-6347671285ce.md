# Does independent verification require an endless regress?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7c545c29-c479-46f5-a12a-6347671285ce
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Perception as Phenomenal-Independence](perception-as-phenomenal-independence-synthesis-76d6e707-cdc6-44d9-83d6-555caf4d40eb.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where every verification requires further confirmation. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that independent verification leads to an infinite regress of verification.**, **There is a plausible principle that knowledge should not require an infinite regress of justifications. This principle together with independent verification implies problematic infinite regress.**, **It is implausible for a process that requires endless confirmations to ground realistic and finite knowledge acquisition.**
