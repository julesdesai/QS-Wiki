## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the standards of justification are met according to the context of the knowledge claim**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person believes the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that contextual standards of justification are met, the proposition is true, and they believe it**
