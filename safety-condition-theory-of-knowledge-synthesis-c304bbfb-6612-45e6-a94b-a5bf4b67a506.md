## Content

**A person knows a proposition if their belief could not easily have been false**, **For knowledge, it must be that in nearly all nearby possible worlds where the person believes the proposition, the proposition is true**, **This safety condition prevents knowledge via beliefs that could easily have been incorrect**, **The safety condition ties the person's belief to the truth across nearby possible situations**
