## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary to include a component that excludes Gettier problems**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing it, the proposition is true, and it avoids Gettier problems**
