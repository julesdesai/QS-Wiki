# Can someone possess knowledge through testimony without intellectual virtue?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1b2a5144-1fa5-4285-be56-65d7b79a1478
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-5eebb8d4-de96-4ab7-90ca-fa4ae98a5219.md)

## Content

**Imagine a case where an individual accepts information from a reliable testimony without exercising any intellectual virtue themselves.**, **In such a case, intuitively, we may judge that the individual possesses knowledge based on the credibility of the testimony alone.**
