# Knowledge as Functional Success

**Parent:** [Do beliefs require first-person awareness to count as knowledge?](do-beliefs-require-first-person-awareness-to-count-as-knowledge-antithesis-985111a4-4016-4deb-8aea-1a25c391fa31.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief functions successfully in a relevant context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief functions successfully in a relevant context**
