## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in nearby possible worlds, the person does not have a false belief under similar conditions**, **A belief is safe if in most near situations it is true**
