## Content

**Knowledge is true belief that arises from intellectual virtue which reliably avoids error**, **Knowledge requires the belief to cohere with one's system of understanding**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief arises through an intellectually virtuous process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres with a comprehensive and coherent system of understanding**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is true, arises from virtue, and coheres with a coherent system of understanding**
