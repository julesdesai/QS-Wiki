# Is it possible to differentiate between justified beliefs and knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 508067e1-0942-40f5-8606-f32859a7cca5
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism View](infinitism-view-synthesis-bddda30f-213c-4b95-bb22-45afdb1e886a.md)

## Content

**Justification is traditionally seen as necessary but not sufficient for knowledge; true belief is also required.**, **If justification always requires an infinite chain, which is practically unattainable, then differentiating between mere belief and knowledge based on infinite justification becomes highly problematic.**, **Without feasible justification, knowledge claims may collapse into mere beliefs, blurring the distinction between justified belief and knowledge.**
