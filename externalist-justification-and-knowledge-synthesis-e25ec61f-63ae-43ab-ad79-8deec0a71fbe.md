## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have a reliable belief-forming process**, **A reliable belief-forming process is one that produces true beliefs in a high percentage of cases**
