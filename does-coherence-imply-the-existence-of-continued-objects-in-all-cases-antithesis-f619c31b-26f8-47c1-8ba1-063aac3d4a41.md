# Does coherence imply the existence of continued objects in all cases?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f619c31b-26f8-47c1-8ba1-063aac3d4a41
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Phenomenalism with Constructive Coherence](phenomenalism-with-constructive-coherence-synthesis-649780d2-d21b-4c5f-9b9f-162351fea396.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person vividly dreams of coherent experiences of a particular object every night. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that the object does not continuously exist in the external world.**, **It is implausible that objects exist merely due to the internal coherence of an individual's mental states if there is no external reality anchoring those states.**
