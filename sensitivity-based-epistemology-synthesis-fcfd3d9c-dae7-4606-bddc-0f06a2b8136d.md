# Sensitivity-Based Epistemology

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** fcfd3d9c-dae7-4606-bddc-0f06a2b8136d
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge be accidental?](can-knowledge-be-accidental-antithesis-cd67b21c-528b-4660-81ff-d2b6ef29aa7d.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **A belief is sensitive if in the nearest possible worlds where the proposition is false, the person does not believe it**
