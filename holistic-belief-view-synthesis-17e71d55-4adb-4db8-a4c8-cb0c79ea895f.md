## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification that supports the proposition within a holistic system of beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **A person knows a proposition if it is true, they have holistic justification, and they believe it**
