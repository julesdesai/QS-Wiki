# How does the coherentist account handle circular reasoning?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2d161d51-80e6-4a19-be2c-67f40088618f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentist Knowledge Summary](coherentist-knowledge-summary-synthesis-1fa3c944-9677-491a-8edd-ea154ce998c7.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which beliefs justify each other in a circular fashion. Intuitively, we might be uneasy about the robustness of such justification.**, **There is a plausible principle that circular justification lacks independent support. This principle suggests that mutual support should not rely on circular reasoning to provide robust grounds.**
