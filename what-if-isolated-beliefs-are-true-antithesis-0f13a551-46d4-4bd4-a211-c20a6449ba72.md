# What if isolated beliefs are true?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0f13a551-46d4-4bd4-a211-c20a6449ba72
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherence Theory of Knowledge](coherence-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-c64df5c7-cb02-4465-9b04-2cdc00a6f3a6.md)

## Content

**A belief can be true yet isolated if it does not cohere within the person’s belief system.**, **A person can have a true belief gained from a reliable informant, but if it does not fit within their other beliefs, it may be incoherent by this view.**
