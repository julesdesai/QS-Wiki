## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from a community's shared intellectual virtues**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person's belief benefits from social processes like testimony and peer review**
