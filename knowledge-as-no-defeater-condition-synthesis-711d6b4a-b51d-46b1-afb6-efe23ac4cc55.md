# Knowledge as No-Defeater Condition

**Parent:** [Does knowledge as safety account for the problem of cognitive luck?](does-knowledge-as-safety-account-for-the-problem-of-cognitive-luck-antithesis-229ee56a-483b-4c70-b84d-9a2608f84b18.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no defeaters for the person's belief in that proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there are no defeaters for the belief**
