# How does confirmation bias interfere with intellectual virtue leading to truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** fd94d19a-3ce0-4341-baed-cc342ba28122
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-43f1bd52-38ff-4c3b-a3d7-408481a71c52.md)

## Content

**Suppose a researcher selectively includes data supporting their hypothesis while disregarding contradicting evidence, resulting in true belief by chance. This true belief seems deficient in knowledge due to failure in upholding unbiased intellectual virtues.**, **When belief formation partly depends on biased reasoning, the link between intellectual virtue and accurate knowledge becomes questionable.**
