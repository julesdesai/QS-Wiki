# Knowledge as Contextual Sensitivity

**Parent:** [Does the existence of justified true belief without defeating evidence guarantee knowledge?](does-the-existence-of-justified-true-belief-without-defeating-evidence-guarantee-knowledge-antithesis-3e51f922-c69f-4ff1-99bc-80f50c0b167b.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified in the given context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is justified in the context**
