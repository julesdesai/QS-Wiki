# Does coherentism handle skepticism effectively?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 537a79e8-7cc3-46d9-a6cc-aeca1148b96c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism in Justified Belief](coherentism-in-justified-belief-synthesis-5c04c2a6-2653-47f8-81ab-a9d276d37a24.md)

## Content

**Skepticism raises the issue that, for any proposed set of beliefs, a coherent but false set could be imagined, such as the existence of an evil demon controlling perceptions. Intuitively, one might judge that coherence alone cannot assure truth or knowledge.**, **A plausible principle is that defeating skeptical scenarios requires more than internal coherence; it necessitates a connection to truth or reality.**
