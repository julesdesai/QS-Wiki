# Knowledge as Epistemic Responsibility

**Parent:** [Can conflicting reliable testimonies influence intellectual virtue and affect knowledge?](can-conflicting-reliable-testimonies-influence-intellectual-virtue-and-affect-knowledge-antithesis-69ce4cbd-32c3-4c6c-bcad-83873e153437.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed through epistemically responsible processes**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is formed through epistemically responsible processes**
