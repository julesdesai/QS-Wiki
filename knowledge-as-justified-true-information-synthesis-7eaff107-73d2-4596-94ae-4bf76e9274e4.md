# Knowledge as Justified True Information

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have strong justification for the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is not necessary that the person believes the proposition**
