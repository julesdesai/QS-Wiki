# Can justified belief be static in a changing world?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ead2ba55-9874-4d47-8711-aaac036bb58c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Adaptive Coherentism](adaptive-coherentism-synthesis-f17f0588-6de8-4c0d-a6e6-63e09dfee45f.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that beliefs must be responsive to evidence to remain justified. This principle together with the idea that coherence provides justification entails that coherence must dynamically incorporate new evidence.**, **Consider a scenario in which new evidence contradicts a person's coherent belief system. Intuitively, we would judge that they must revise their beliefs to remain justified.**
