# Invariantist Safety-Enhanced Justification

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** b7abe226-e835-4df7-bbfd-e3a7cd702b94
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can justified true belief always be sufficient for knowledge?](can-justified-true-belief-always-be-sufficient-for-knowledge-antithesis-cded4c1e-75ed-4e0b-8992-377a8d124108.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, justification must be such that the belief would not easily be false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their justification ensures the belief is safely true**
