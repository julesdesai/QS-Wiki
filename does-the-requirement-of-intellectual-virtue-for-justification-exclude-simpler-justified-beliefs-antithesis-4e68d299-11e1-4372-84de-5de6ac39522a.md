# Does the requirement of intellectual virtue for justification exclude simpler justified beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 4e68d299-11e1-4372-84de-5de6ac39522a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-ffa55650-0795-4012-9f33-14517d86090e.md)

## Content

**Consider a simple perceptual belief about the color of an object observed in plain daylight.**, **Such a belief might be justified without any complex intellectual virtue involved.**, **If justification is tied strictly to intellectual virtue, it might exclude straightforward perceptual cases.**
