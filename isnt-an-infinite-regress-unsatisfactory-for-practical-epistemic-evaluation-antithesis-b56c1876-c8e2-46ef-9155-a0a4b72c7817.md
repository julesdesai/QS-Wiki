# Isn't an infinite regress unsatisfactory for practical epistemic evaluation?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b56c1876-c8e2-46ef-9155-a0a4b72c7817
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilist Infinitism](reliabilist-infinitism-synthesis-31746681-ada4-44ae-aa39-47d03ad8abbb.md)

## Content

**An infinite regress requires that each step of the justification links to another without ending.**, **In practical epistemic evaluation, justification needs to be assessable within obtainable means, which an infinite regress does not allow.**
