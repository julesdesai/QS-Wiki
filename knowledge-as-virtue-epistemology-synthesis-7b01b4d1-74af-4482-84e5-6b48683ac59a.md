## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief results from an intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief results from an intellectual virtue and meets the other conditions**
