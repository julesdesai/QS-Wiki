# Does belief without personal access to the process equate with knowledge?

**Node ID:** d60e61a0-0635-47cd-a1c6-642502495495

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Process Reliabilism](process-reliabilism-synthesis-73062a2b-1357-42ba-b901-d397b26dce96.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person has no access to understanding if their process is reliable, as they follow orders without context or comprehension. We would intuitively deny knowledge possession here.**, **Suppose an animal non-sentiently follows a biologically reliable instinct without awareness. Intuitively, we judge animal lacks knowledge despite its success.**
