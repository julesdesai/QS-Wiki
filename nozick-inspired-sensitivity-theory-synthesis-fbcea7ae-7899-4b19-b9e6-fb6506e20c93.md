## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person would not believe the proposition if it were false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person believes the proposition based on conditions that are sensitive to the truth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the sensitive conditions and other conditions hold**
