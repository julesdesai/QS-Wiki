## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief is the result of intellectual virtue**, **An intellectual virtue is a character trait that leads one to form true beliefs in relevant circumstances**
