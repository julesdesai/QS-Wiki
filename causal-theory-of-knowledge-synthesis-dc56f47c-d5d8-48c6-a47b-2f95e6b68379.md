# Causal Theory of Knowledge

**Parent:** [Are there cases where justified true belief fails to be knowledge?](are-there-cases-where-justified-true-belief-fails-to-be-knowledge-antithesis-208d1ac2-78d9-4795-a8f0-d18d6837da73.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is a causal connection between the fact stated by the proposition and the person's belief in that proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person believes the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that there is a causal connection to the fact, the proposition is true, and they believe it**
