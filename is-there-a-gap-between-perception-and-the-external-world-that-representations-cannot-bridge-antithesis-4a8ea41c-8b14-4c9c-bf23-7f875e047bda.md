# Is there a gap between perception and the external world that representations cannot bridge?

**Node ID:** 4a8ea41c-8b14-4c9c-bf23-7f875e047bda
**Depth:** 4

## Content

**It is plausible that there is a gap between subjective perceptual experience and objective external reality.**, **If such a gap exists, then mental representations may not fully bridge and accurately correlate the two.**, **This raises questions about whether perceptions can ever provide fully reliable representations of external objects.**
