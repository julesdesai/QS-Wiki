# Can intellectual virtues be unknowingly flawed?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b121f327-58f5-472d-bbcf-ff3e65632d2c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-ffa55650-0795-4012-9f33-14517d86090e.md)

## Content

**Imagine a situation where a person's reasoning seems virtuous but is subtly influenced by cognitive bias.**, **In such a scenario, the resulting belief may be false, suggesting flaws in the perceived intellectual virtue.**, **If intellectual virtues can be unknowingly flawed, knowledge claims based on them may be dubious.**
