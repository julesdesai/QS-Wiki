# Knowledge as Safety with Defeater Sensitivity

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 2f9dd52b-9e03-47c6-8e32-c9ca7244875b
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Are all justified beliefs also safe beliefs?](are-all-justified-beliefs-also-safe-beliefs-antithesis-dc9a9532-bdd8-4cd6-812c-cdbe2285c1c3.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief could not easily have been false**, **For a justified belief to become known, it must remain unaffected by potential defeaters that are truth-relevant**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief could not easily have been false**
