# Knowledge as True Belief with Foundationalist Justification

**Parent:** [Does justification need to be objective?](does-justification-need-to-be-objective-antithesis-bee1cbd4-14ab-4979-bd88-05b974535a8f.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is justified by basic beliefs or self-evident truths**, **Basic beliefs do not require further justification**
