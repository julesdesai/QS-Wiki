# How does the theory handle false beliefs that seem justified?

**Node ID:** 6867d8ef-0d95-4c74-86e8-12c717bb522e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Tracking Theory of Knowledge](tracking-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-c5c60481-0513-43f2-8ed6-61505f94790c.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person has reasons that would normally justify a belief, yet the belief ends up being false.**, **Justified false beliefs suggest a failure to track truth despite the presence of justification.**, **Intuitively, the presence of justification should not be enough for knowledge if tracking fails.**
