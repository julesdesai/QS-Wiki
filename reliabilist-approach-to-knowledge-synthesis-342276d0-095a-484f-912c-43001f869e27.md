## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A belief is justified if it is produced by a reliable process, where a reliable process is one that produces true beliefs most of the time**
