## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, they must have no defeating evidence for the belief**, **A person knows a proposition if they have undefeated true belief in the proposition**
