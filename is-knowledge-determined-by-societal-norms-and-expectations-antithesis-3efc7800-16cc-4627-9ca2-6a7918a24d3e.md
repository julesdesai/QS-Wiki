# Is knowledge determined by societal norms and expectations?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3efc7800-16cc-4627-9ca2-6a7918a24d3e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Subject-Sensitive Invariantism](subject-sensitive-invariantism-synthesis-ef027452-1300-45f1-9d7c-023fdb6cd119.md)

## Content

**Consider a situation where a community adheres to traditional beliefs, such as rainmaking rituals. Intuitively, societal norms may elevate beliefs to the status of knowledge, but such elevation might undermine the constant standards for knowledge if practical interests are influenced by cultural expectations.**
