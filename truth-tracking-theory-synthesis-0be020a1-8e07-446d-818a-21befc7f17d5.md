## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief tracks the truth across possible situations**, **A belief tracks the truth if the person would not hold it if the proposition were false**
