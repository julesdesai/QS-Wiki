# Does testimony as a form of reliable process always lead to knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1e9b1667-4713-464d-a697-bb93dcc03748
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalist View](externalist-view-synthesis-75028f40-4ef7-4feb-a21d-1e6a75d787a6.md)

## Content

**Suppose someone gains a belief solely through testimony from an unreliable but lucky informant, and the belief is true by chance. Intuitively, it may seem that this is not genuine knowledge.**, **Testimony needs to be from a consistently reliable source to secure true knowledge, rather than being lucky.**
