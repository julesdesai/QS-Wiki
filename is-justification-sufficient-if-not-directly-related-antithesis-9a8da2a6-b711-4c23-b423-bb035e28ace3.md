# Is justification sufficient if not directly related?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9a8da2a6-b711-4c23-b423-bb035e28ace3
**Depth:** 2
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [No False Lemmas Theory](no-false-lemmas-theory-thesis-a96b2c39-d876-40fc-94b2-391826bfefda.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person has a justified belief about a general phenomenon, but the specific instance they believe is true for reasons unrelated to their justification. Intuitively, this disconnect seems insufficient for knowledge.**, **Knowledge requires the justification to be directly related to the truth of the specific proposition in question.**
