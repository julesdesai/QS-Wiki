# Knowledge with Reliable Belief Formation

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is not true merely due to epistemic luck**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is the result of a reliable belief-forming process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, their belief is not true merely due to epistemic luck, and their belief is the result of a reliable belief-forming process**
