## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from an intellectual virtue or competence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have a true belief arising from an intellectual virtue**
