# Is the content of perceptual experiences always propositional?

## Content

**Perceptual experiences are often described as having non-propositional content, such as a vague feeling or awareness.**, **Propositions suggest a structure that may not capture the entirety of perceptual experience.**, **Claiming that perceptual content relates directly to propositions about the world may oversimplify these experiences.**
