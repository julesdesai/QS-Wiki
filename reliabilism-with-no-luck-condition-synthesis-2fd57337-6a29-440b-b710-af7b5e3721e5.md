## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a reliable process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the acquisition of the belief does not involve luck**
