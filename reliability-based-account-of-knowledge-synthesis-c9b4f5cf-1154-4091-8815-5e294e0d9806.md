## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their method of forming the belief is reliable in producing true beliefs**, **Knowledge involves forming true beliefs through a method that consistently leads to truth**
