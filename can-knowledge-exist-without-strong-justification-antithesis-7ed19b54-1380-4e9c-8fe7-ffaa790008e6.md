# Can knowledge exist without strong justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7ed19b54-1380-4e9c-8fe7-ffaa790008e6
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Moderate Contextualism](moderate-contextualism-synthesis-a2419722-fa3b-4f65-af9b-d94e8c32837e.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person claims to know a proposition because they were told it by a notoriously unreliable source, yet the proposition is true. Intuitively, this suggests that strong justification is needed beyond a true belief to constitute knowledge.**
