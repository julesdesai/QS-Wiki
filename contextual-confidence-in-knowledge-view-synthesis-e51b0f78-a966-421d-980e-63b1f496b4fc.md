## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have confidence in asserting or acting upon it within context**, **A person knows a proposition if it is true, they have justification for it, and confidence in context**
