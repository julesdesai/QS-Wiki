## Content

**For a person or AI to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person or AI to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person or AI to know a proposition, their belief must be produced by a reliable process**, **A reliable process is one that generally leads to true beliefs**
