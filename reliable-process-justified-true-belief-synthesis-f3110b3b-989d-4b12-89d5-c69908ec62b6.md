**Parent:** [Is nearby-world truth condition sufficient for empirical knowledge?](is-nearby-world-truth-condition-sufficient-for-empirical-knowledge-antithesis-b42b92bc-c19f-49a5-abd2-a7c50f4fb615.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed through a reliable process**, **For a belief to be reliable, it is necessary that it produces a high ratio of true beliefs**
