# Knowledge as Properly Grounded True Belief

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Can knowledge be based on circular or mutually reinforcing justifications?](can-knowledge-be-based-on-circular-or-mutually-reinforcing-justifications-antithesis-3e353d87-5503-4b61-9d67-04ae475c19bb.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is grounded in authoritative testimony or a trustworthy method**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is grounded in authoritative testimony or a trustworthy method**
