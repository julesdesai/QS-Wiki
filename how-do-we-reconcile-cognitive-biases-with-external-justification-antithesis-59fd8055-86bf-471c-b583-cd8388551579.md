# How do we reconcile cognitive biases with external justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 59fd8055-86bf-471c-b583-cd8388551579
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Epistemic Externalism](epistemic-externalism-synthesis-3f203114-9a4a-4d29-9451-dfb5538c3c76.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case where a person prioritizes information confirming their beliefs due to cognitive biases. Intuitively, we either have to say that they are justified in their belief or that they are not. If we say they are justified, then external justification allows for biased belief. If we say they are not justified, then cognitive bias undermines the external justification.**, **It is implausible that cognitive biases provide justification for knowledge.**
