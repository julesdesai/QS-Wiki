## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person’s belief changes in response to changes in the truth of the proposition across possible situations**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they are consciously aware of their belief**
