# Can knowledge claims remain unaffected by personal consequences?

**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Subject-Sensitive Invariantism](subject-sensitive-invariantism-synthesis-2eef6ba9-ea95-4aea-9a96-5c60688dde0e.md)

## Content

**Suppose a student claims to know the material without concern for exam consequences. Intuitively, knowledge should not be diminished by a lack of personal stakes.**, **If knowledge is sensitive to personal consequences, it may lead to intellectual irresponsibility, where knowledge attribution varies needlessly with personal interests.**
