## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief coheres with a network of empirically grounded beliefs**, **Empirical grounding of a belief involves support from sensory experiences or scientific investigation**
