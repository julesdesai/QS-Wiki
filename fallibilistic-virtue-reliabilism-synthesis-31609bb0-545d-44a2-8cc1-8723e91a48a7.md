## Content

**Knowledge is true belief that arises from intellectual virtue which can tolerate some error**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be possibly true under most circumstances**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief arises from intellectual virtue that tolerates occasional errors**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe it, the proposition is highly likely true, and their intellectual virtue generally avoids errors**
