# Does coherentism account for the pragmatic success of beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** fefc39cd-ddce-4e36-9e05-8ee26af2826b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism Summary](coherentism-summary-synthesis-7bdcae0a-f633-4c38-9857-77d6ba49435c.md)

## Content

**A person holds a coherent set of beliefs that also enable them to navigate and succeed in their environment effectively. Intuitively, we would judge that the practical success of these beliefs contributes to their justification. It is implausible for coherentism to provide sufficient justification if it excludes pragmatic considerations.**
