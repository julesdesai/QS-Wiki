# Is true belief equivalent to knowledge without justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f980d760-985d-4cf4-a181-5932e398ef98
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherent Infinitism](coherent-infinitism-synthesis-f2c99927-f862-4ee8-8b5b-2fefd2717ee2.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person guesses correctly that it will rain tomorrow, but does not have any weather data. Intuitively, we would judge that a lucky guess does not constitute knowledge.**, **It is implausible that simply holding a true belief, without having justification, can constitute knowledge.**
