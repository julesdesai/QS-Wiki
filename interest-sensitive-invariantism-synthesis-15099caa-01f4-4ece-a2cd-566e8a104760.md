## Content

**Knowledge does not vary with context but is sensitive to the interests of the individual**, **A person knows a proposition if they believe it, if the proposition is true, and if their justification satisfies a consistent standard that is sensitive to their interests**, **The consistent standard for knowing a proposition aligns with the individual's long-term interests, not immediate practical decisions**
