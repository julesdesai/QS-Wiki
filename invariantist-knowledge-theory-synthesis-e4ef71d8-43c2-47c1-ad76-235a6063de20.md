## Content

**Knowledge depends on universal standards**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **The standards for belief, truth, and justification do not vary with context**
