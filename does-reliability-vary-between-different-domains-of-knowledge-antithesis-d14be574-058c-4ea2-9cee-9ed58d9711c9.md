# Does reliability vary between different domains of knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d14be574-058c-4ea2-9cee-9ed58d9711c9
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Process Reliabilism](process-reliabilism-synthesis-383ba069-e0f1-4291-bdc7-eacefc701040.md)

## Content

**Consider expertise in scientific phenomena versus predicting outcomes in games of chance. Intuitively, reliability in scientific methodology might have a more rigorous basis than in gaming predictions.**, **It is conceivable that what defines reliability shifts between domains, suggesting that reliability as a blanket requirement may lack a universal standard applicable to all knowledge claims.**
