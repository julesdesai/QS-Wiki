## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition when it is true and do not believe it when it is false in nearby possible worlds**, **A belief is tracked if it is true when believed and false when disbelieved across nearby possible worlds**
