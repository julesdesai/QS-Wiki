## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief formation process involves external factors ensuring epistemic success, including epistemic virtues or reliability**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that belief, truth, and external factors, including epistemic virtues or reliability, are present**
