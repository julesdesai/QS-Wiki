# Knowledge as No False Lemmas

**Parent:** [Is there a difference between having evidence and having knowledge?](is-there-a-difference-between-having-evidence-and-having-knowledge-antithesis-162a7e33-726b-4da4-8c1b-9733bceb7009.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is not based on any false assumptions**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition without reliance on false assumptions, and the proposition is true**
