# Can beliefs ever truly achieve infinite justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** afc00aa3-a101-4c0d-8c37-a7ae18d804a8
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Epistemic Infinitism with Practical Coherence](epistemic-infinitism-with-practical-coherence-synthesis-5f09710b-a3e2-4650-8169-f35a6a42697e.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which an individual believes a proposition due to an infinitely regressive chain of reasons. Intuitively, we question whether practical decision-making requires such an infinite chain.**, **There is a plausible principle that finite beings have limited cognitive resources. This principle together with the need for infinite justification suggests a disconnect between human capabilities and epistemic infinitism.**
