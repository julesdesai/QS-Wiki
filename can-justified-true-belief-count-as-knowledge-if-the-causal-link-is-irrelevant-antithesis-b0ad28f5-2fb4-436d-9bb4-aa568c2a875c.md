# Can justified true belief count as knowledge if the causal link is irrelevant?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b0ad28f5-2fb4-436d-9bb4-aa568c2a875c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Causal Theory of Knowledge](causal-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-a5ebdb75-c32b-47a3-8c6b-cdebc8ceca67.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where a person believes their friend will arrive because they were randomly thinking about them, and the friend does arrive due to unrelated reasons.**, **In such a case, intuitively, the belief might be seen as true and justified, yet lacks an appropriate causal link between belief and truth.**
