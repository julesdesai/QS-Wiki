# Knowledge as No False Grounds

**Parent:** [Can a belief be formed reliably yet lack truth due to equivocation?](can-a-belief-be-formed-reliably-yet-lack-truth-due-to-equivocation-antithesis-a88a2c60-0ab1-40a5-8668-8a2d52da430b.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is not based on any false assumptions**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that all grounds of the belief are true and the proposition itself is true**
