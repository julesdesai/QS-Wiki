# Is the reliability of sense perception universally applicable?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 370aaf06-0011-4719-9394-77f205e57fe4
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilist Theory of Knowledge](reliabilist-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-b27c6d4b-ac60-4e95-8268-38e201ff9a4c.md)

## Content

**Consider a case in which sense perception generally provides a reliable method for forming beliefs, but hallucinatory experiences occur.**, **It is implausible that beliefs formed under hallucinatory circumstances can always be classified as knowledge.**
