## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is a product of generally reliable cognitive faculties recognized through social standards**, **The reliability standard is recognized externally by common-sense norms rather than by the individual's cognitive awareness**
