## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it's necessary that their justification for the belief contains no false grounds**, **A false ground is any reason supporting the belief that is not true**
