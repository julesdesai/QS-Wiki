# How does solipsistic coherence affect epistemic claims?

## Content

**Suppose we have a scenario where a person has a coherent belief about their environment based only on their own mind, independent of actual external input. Intuitively, we would say their knowledge claim about the environment is not valid.**, **Knowledge about the external world seems to necessitate more than internal coherence, suggesting that interaction with external reality is required.**
