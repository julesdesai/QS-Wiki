# Does reliabilism fail to address complications from the new evil demon problem?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** cbc5b679-a95a-4036-8dd3-2997ca1703ff
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

## Content

**Suppose a person is in a situation where all their perceptual experiences are manipulated by an evil demon, yet their cognitive processes seem normal and reliable from their perspective. Intuitively, we would judge that the person lacks knowledge.**, **A person in such a scenario believes numerous propositions that are false.**, **The person's belief-forming processes appear reliable to them, suggesting they are justified, but they do not lead to true beliefs.**
