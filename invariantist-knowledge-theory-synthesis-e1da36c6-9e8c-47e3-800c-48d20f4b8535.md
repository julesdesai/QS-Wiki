## Content

**Knowledge does not depend on the context of the knower**, **The standards for knowing a proposition are the same across different contexts**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **A person's justification is evaluated based on fixed, objective standards**
