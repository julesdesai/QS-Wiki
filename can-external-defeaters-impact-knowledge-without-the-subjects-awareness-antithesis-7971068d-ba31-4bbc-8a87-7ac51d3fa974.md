# Can external defeaters impact knowledge without the subject's awareness?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7971068d-ba31-4bbc-8a87-7ac51d3fa974
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [No-Defeaters Theory of Knowledge](no-defeaters-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-0be8e529-9437-4b4e-96a3-c585180c1210.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that knowledge cannot depend on factors completely external to the subject's conscious experience.**, **Defeaters impacting a belief despite the subject's ignorance of them suggests knowledge would be contingent on external factors beyond justification and truth.**
