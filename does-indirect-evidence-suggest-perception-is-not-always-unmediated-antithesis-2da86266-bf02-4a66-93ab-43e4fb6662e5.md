# Does indirect evidence suggest perception is not always unmediated?

## Content

**Scientific instruments, like microscopes, can reveal aspects of objects not directly perceivable by the human eye.**, **The necessity of tools to extend human perception suggests that our ordinary perceptual experience does not always provide direct knowledge of the true nature of objects.**
