## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they are certain of the truth of the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the person believes the proposition, the proposition is true, and they are certain of the truth of the proposition**
