# Can reliability of cognitive processes lead to circular reasoning?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 39812a75-90d6-4719-ad37-b8b2d5da3081
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism with a finite chain of reasons](reliabilism-with-a-finite-chain-of-reasons-synthesis-a3f7a107-c974-4a8e-a7f2-d20f4743454a.md)

## Content

**A reliable cognitive process must have a source for its reliability, which may lead to a finite chain of reasons.**, **A finite chain of reasons based on reliability may cause the original justification to loop back upon itself.**, **If the justification loops back upon itself, it could be circular and thus undermine the reliability upon which justification relies.**
