**Parent:** [Can coherence fit within non-truth-tracking systems?](can-coherence-fit-within-non-truth-tracking-systems-antithesis-7a86547a-ebff-4e9c-861e-9ae0b246b005.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification involves a reliably truth-producing set of beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief fits within a reliably truth-producing system of beliefs**
