**The reliability of a cognitive process traditionally involves being able to assess its accuracy or consistency over time.**, **Imagine a scenario where someone frequently guesses answers correctly but is unaware of any reliable process behind this accuracy. Intuitively, without access to an explanation or method, the claim of knowledge seems unfounded.**
