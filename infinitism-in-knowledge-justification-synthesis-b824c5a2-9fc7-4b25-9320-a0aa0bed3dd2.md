# Infinitism in Knowledge Justification

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Can justified false beliefs ever qualify as knowledge?](can-justified-false-beliefs-ever-qualify-as-knowledge-antithesis-bca5516b-4c3b-4163-bbcb-2e2ac79b6c46.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is justification in the form of an infinite chain of reasons**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there is justification in the form of an infinite chain of reasons**
