## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed through both reliable individual processes and socially validated methods**, **A person knows a proposition if they believe it and the belief is validated by social consensus**, **Socially reliable processes include contributions from community dialogue and feedback that tend to produce true beliefs**, **A reliable process is one that produces true beliefs more often than false ones through a combination of individual and social factors**
