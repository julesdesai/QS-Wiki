## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief coheres with a broader system of beliefs**, **The broader system of beliefs must be internally consistent and justify each other**
