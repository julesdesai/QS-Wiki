# Can the variability of perceptual judgments across cultures and individuals undermine the properties-perception link?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0be351e0-62d0-4458-92c7-dbedddff150f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Naïve Realism](naïve-realism-synthesis-25d0b7e3-6f66-46c3-a45d-bb41c1dbe70b.md)

## Content

**In case studies, people from different cultures or backgrounds may perceive and describe the same objects differently, such as in aesthetic judgments. This individual variability intuitively suggests that perceptual assessments are not always reliable indicators of objects' properties.**
