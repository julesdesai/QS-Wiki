## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A person is justified in believing a proposition if and only if the belief is part of a coherent system of beliefs**, **A coherent system of beliefs is one where the beliefs support each other logically**
