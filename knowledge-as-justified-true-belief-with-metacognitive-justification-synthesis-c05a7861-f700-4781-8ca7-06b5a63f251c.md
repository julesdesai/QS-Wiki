# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with Metacognitive Justification

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** c05a7861-f700-4781-8ca7-06b5a63f251c
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge exist without reflective access?](can-knowledge-exist-without-reflective-access-antithesis-2e8ba0bd-fe14-45d6-b879-ac1686ec7380.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they possess metacognitive awareness of the justification process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and they possess metacognitive awareness of the justification process**
