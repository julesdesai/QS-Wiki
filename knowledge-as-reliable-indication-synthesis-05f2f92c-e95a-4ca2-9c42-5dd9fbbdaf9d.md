# Knowledge as Reliable Indication

**Parent:** [Could accidental truths be considered knowledge without causality?](could-accidental-truths-be-considered-knowledge-without-causality-antithesis-e3430c90-d3e6-460e-b015-2eb5869c3f10.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is a reliable indication of the truth of the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there is a reliable indication of the truth of the belief**
