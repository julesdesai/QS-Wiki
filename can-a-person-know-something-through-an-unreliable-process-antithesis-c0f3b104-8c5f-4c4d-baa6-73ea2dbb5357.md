# Can a person know something through an unreliable process?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c0f3b104-8c5f-4c4d-baa6-73ea2dbb5357
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalist View](externalist-view-synthesis-75028f40-4ef7-4feb-a21d-1e6a75d787a6.md)

## Content

**A reliable process is necessary for true knowledge.**, **Consider a scenario in which a person forms a belief through an unreliable process but the belief is accidentally true. Intuitively, we would judge that the person does not have knowledge in this scenario.**
