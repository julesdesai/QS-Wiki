# Knowledge as Nozick's Truth Tracking

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is it possible to have justified false beliefs?](is-it-possible-to-have-justified-false-beliefs-antithesis-87de74bf-55de-4107-803e-7162d39214f7.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A person is justified in believing a proposition if and only if the belief tracks the truth**, **A belief tracks the truth if the person would not believe it if it were false, and would believe it if it were true**
