## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief arises from exercising intellectual virtues**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person believes the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they exercise intellectual virtues, the proposition is true, and they believe it**
