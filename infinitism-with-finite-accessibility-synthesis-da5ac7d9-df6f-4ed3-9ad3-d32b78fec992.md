# Infinitism with Finite Accessibility

**Parent:** [Does the infinitism view make human knowledge impossible?](does-the-infinitism-view-make-human-knowledge-impossible-antithesis-56c6f6f5-61a6-45f2-9b6c-f80b0733b3b4.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that justification involves an infinite, non-repeating chain of consistent reasons**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there exists a potential infinite, non-repeating chain of consistent reasons justifying the belief, with finite segments accessible to the person**
