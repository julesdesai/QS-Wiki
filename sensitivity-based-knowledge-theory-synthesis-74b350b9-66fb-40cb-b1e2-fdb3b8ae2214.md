## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person would not believe the proposition if it were false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person's belief tracks the truth of the proposition in relevant situations**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that these sensitivity-based conditions hold**
