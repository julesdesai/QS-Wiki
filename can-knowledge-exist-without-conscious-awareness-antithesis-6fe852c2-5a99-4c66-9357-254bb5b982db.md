# Can knowledge exist without conscious awareness?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 6fe852c2-5a99-4c66-9357-254bb5b982db
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism with Non-Conscious Elements](coherentism-with-non-conscious-elements-synthesis-025108b6-66b5-4784-9945-6373af308ffa.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person has a belief that coheres with a network of non-conscious beliefs, but they are not consciously aware of this network. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that the person does not possess knowledge of the belief.**, **There is a plausible principle that conscious awareness of beliefs is necessary for knowledge. This principle together with the scenario mentioned entails that a belief cohering with a non-conscious network does not constitute knowledge.**
