# How does inter-subjective agreement handle error or illusion?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 4ed4c48d-0385-46a7-aa84-e73aba926ece
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Inter-subjective Phenomenalism](inter-subjective-phenomenalism-synthesis-c00af758-5073-4931-9585-2f560dddd897.md)

## Content

**A plausible principle is that knowledge must withstand the test of error or illusion. This principle together with requiring inter-subjective agreement proposes that by sharing errors, these can transform into 'knowledge', highlighting a critical flaw.**, **Imagine a case where multiple witnesses misidentify a weather balloon as a UFO. Intuitively, their shared agreement does not constitute genuine knowledge of UFOs, despite meeting inter-subjective criteria.**
