## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person believes the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is an appropriate causal connection between the truth of the proposition and the person's belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that these causal relations hold**
