# Is there a necessity for belief justification to include subjective awareness?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 4dba5efb-5f0e-4567-a8cb-b03c8ad92d4c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Evidential Externalism](evidential-externalism-synthesis-1f3be30d-cfb3-46d2-9d1a-294e056d7ea0.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where evidence exists outside a person's awareness, supporting their true belief. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that unless the person is aware of how the evidence supports their belief, they lack true justification.**, **A plausible principle is that justification intrinsically involves awareness of the pathways connecting belief to truth, which external evidence alone cannot fulfill without subjective involvement.**
