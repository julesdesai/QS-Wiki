## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief results from the exercise of intellectual virtue**, **For knowledge to be present, it is necessary that, if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it through the exercise of their intellectual virtues**
