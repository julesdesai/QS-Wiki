# Does coherence always indicate truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3e70c4b9-7847-4fc8-ab45-e08fc47ee365
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualist Coherentism](contextualist-coherentism-synthesis-598dc05f-03d6-4aeb-b0db-fa7173390f96.md)

## Content

**Consider a skilled conspiracy theorist whose beliefs form a highly coherent system, aligning with no external facts. Intuitively, coherence with a web of false beliefs does not indicate truth.**, **Coherence alone is not a reliable indicator of the truth of a belief, as coherent systems of false beliefs can exist.**
