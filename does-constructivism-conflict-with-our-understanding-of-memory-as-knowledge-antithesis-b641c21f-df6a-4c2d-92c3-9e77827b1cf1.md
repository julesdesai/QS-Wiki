# Does constructivism conflict with our understanding of memory as knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b641c21f-df6a-4c2d-92c3-9e77827b1cf1
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Constructivism](constructivism-synthesis-c5cc0372-1259-4a9f-927f-8a96c3c904c0.md)

## Content

**Memory can contain accurate knowledge of past events without active construction at the time of recall.**, **The presence of detailed memories suggests retention rather than new construction.**, **If knowledge is actively constructed, then memory, where construction isn't evident, presents a challenge to constructivism.**
