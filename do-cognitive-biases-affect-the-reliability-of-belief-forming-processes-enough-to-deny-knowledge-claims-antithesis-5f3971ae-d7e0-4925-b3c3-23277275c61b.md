# Do cognitive biases affect the reliability of belief-forming processes enough to deny knowledge claims?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5f3971ae-d7e0-4925-b3c3-23277275c61b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalism With Reliable Belief Formation](externalism-with-reliable-belief-formation-synthesis-2a34c494-e349-4b40-95af-530f8f0219c4.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case where a person forms a belief through what is considered a reliable process, yet biases inherent in cognition affect their beliefs. Intuitively, the existence of cognitive biases should negate claims to knowledge in such scenarios.**, **It is implausible to claim that a belief is reliably true if it can be significantly influenced by cognitive biases.**
