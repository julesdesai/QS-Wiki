## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief could not easily have been false in a similar situation**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient the belief is produced by a reliable process and is safe from error**
