# How does the rigidity of standards align with the influence of subjective factors in knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f7e29a6c-ba6d-4b0b-993c-d8098767bd93
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Subject-Sensitive Invariantism Summary](subject-sensitive-invariantism-summary-synthesis-fa9170ce-e58d-4311-9c0f-f4410b4d69a4.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which Emma is certain about her knowledge of history facts, while Fred is doubtful of the same facts. Intuitively, allowing their subjective feelings to affect knowledge would imply a change in standards, contradicting their fixed nature.**, **A principle that links epistemic standards with objectivity suggests that subjective factors should not generally impact these standards.**
