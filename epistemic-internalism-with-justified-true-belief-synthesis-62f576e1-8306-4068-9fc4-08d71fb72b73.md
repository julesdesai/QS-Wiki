## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A person knows a proposition if they have justification for believing the proposition**, **Justification requires that the person has access to reasons that make the belief likely to be true**
