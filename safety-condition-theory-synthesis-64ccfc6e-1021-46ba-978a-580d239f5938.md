## Content

**Knowledge is a true belief produced by a reliable process**, **A person knows a proposition if the belief is unlikely to be false in similar plausible situations**, **A person does not know a proposition if there are easily conceivable scenarios where the belief would be false**, **For a belief to be considered knowledge, it must not be true merely by accident or luck**, **The reliability of the process is related to the safety of the belief being true in nearby possible worlds**
