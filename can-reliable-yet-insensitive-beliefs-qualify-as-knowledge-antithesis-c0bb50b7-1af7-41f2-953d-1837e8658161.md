# Can reliable yet insensitive beliefs qualify as knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c0bb50b7-1af7-41f2-953d-1837e8658161
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Process Reliabilism with Sensitivity](process-reliabilism-with-sensitivity-synthesis-3b7ba929-b462-4672-9048-c64e1338414e.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person uses a reliable weather app that predicts rain tomorrow, and it does rain. The belief that it will rain was formed reliably, thus intuitively counting as knowledge.**, **Even if the rain prediction were false, the person would still believe it due to the app's track record, casting doubt on sensitivity's role.**
