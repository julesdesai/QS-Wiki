## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person’s subjective stakes in the truth of the proposition are considered**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the person’s subjective stakes in the proposition are accounted for**
