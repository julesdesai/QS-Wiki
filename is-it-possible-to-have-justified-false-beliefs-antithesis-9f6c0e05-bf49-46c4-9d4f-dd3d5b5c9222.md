# Is it possible to have justified false beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9f6c0e05-bf49-46c4-9d4f-dd3d5b5c9222
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliability Theory of Knowledge](reliability-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-971ec002-e8db-455e-806a-7b81feaf9154.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person has a belief produced by a reliable cognitive process and is justified in holding it, yet the belief is false. This possibility suggests that justification and reliability do not ensure truth.**, **A case in which justified belief does not lead to truth undermines the connection between justification and knowledge.**
