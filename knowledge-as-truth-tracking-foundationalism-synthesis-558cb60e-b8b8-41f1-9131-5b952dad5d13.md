# Knowledge as Truth-Tracking Foundationalism

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Is absolute belief always necessary for knowledge sufficiency?](is-absolute-belief-always-necessary-for-knowledge-sufficiency-antithesis-b016d809-07ce-4f5a-888f-cf36cb4b64eb.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person can track the truth of the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to track the truth of a proposition, it is necessary that they believe it when it is true and do not believe it when it is false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they track the truth of the proposition and it is true**
