# Is reliability always sufficient for justifying knowledge claims?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** adc25113-43a9-47ba-b955-133ea99816bb
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism](reliabilism-synthesis-41e7a61f-ad45-42cd-8dd7-ba40ca519ba9.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a belief is produced by a reliable process, but the believer has no understanding of the process or its reliability.**, **A cognitive process is reliable if it often produces true beliefs.**, **If a belief is formed by a reliable process but not understood by the believer, the justification of the belief may be insufficient for it to count as knowledge.**
