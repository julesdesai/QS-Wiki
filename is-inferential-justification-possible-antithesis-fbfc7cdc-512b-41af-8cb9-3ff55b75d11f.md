# Is inferential justification possible?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** fbfc7cdc-512b-41af-8cb9-3ff55b75d11f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Evidentialist Foundationalism](evidentialist-foundationalism-synthesis-8074c848-6a3d-411d-b5c9-e6a07bd8fe19.md)

## Content

**Inferential justification occurs when a belief is justified by being logically derived from other justified beliefs.**, **If inferential beliefs cannot be foundational, the denial of inferential knowledge weakens the structure of knowledge.**, **Denying inferential justification limits the way knowledge can be acquired and defended.**
