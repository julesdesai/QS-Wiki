# Knowledge as Tracking the Truth

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can a true belief from an unreliable source be considered knowledge?](can-a-true-belief-from-an-unreliable-source-be-considered-knowledge-antithesis-d923a6ce-0d6e-477f-8cb8-79a47baeb515.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were not true, the person would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition is true, the person believes it**
