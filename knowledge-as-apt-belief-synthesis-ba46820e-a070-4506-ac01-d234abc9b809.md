# Knowledge as Apt Belief

**Depth:** 5

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief manifests their cognitive abilities functioning correctly**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is apt due to correct cognitive abilities**
