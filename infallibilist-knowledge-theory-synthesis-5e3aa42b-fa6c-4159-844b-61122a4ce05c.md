## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their justification for the belief is infallible**, **Infallible justification means that it is impossible for the belief to be false if justified by such means**
