# Subject-Sensitive Invariantism

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, their justification must be sufficient given practical stakes**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification that is sensitive to practical stakes, and the proposition is true**
