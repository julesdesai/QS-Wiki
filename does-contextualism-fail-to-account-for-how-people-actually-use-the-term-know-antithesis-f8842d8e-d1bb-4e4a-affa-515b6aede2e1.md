# Does contextualism fail to account for how people actually use the term "know?"

## Content

**An empirical fact is that people tend to use the term "know" consistently across many contexts without explicit awareness of shifting standards. Knowledge attributions based on different context standards do not align with this observed linguistic behavior.**
