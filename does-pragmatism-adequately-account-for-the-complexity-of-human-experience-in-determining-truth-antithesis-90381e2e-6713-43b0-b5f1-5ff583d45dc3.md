# Does pragmatism adequately account for the complexity of human experience in determining truth?

**Node ID:** 90381e2e-6713-43b0-b5f1-5ff583d45dc3

## Content

**Consider the diversity of human experiences where what is useful or successful varies dramatically between individuals and contexts. Intuitively, the truth seems more universal than merely reflective of these variegated experiences**, **A plausible principle is that truth must account for universal elements or standards that transcend individual practical outcomes**
