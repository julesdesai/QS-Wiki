# What if reliable processes yield known but unconvincing beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 573f8b51-4ebe-483e-81a4-2156591e26b8
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalism with Reliabilism](externalism-with-reliabilism-synthesis-c10e5c74-1dbc-479d-ba5e-469176c5d537.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a situation where an individual forms a belief via a reliable process, but lacks any accessible justification that would convince others of the proposition's truth. Intuitively, this challenges the notion that the person truly knows the proposition.**
