# Knowledge as Relevant Alternatives

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge be both context-dependent and objective?](can-knowledge-be-both-context-dependent-and-objective-antithesis-0acbeede-f5c0-4d5d-b3d3-1548a8a55466.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they be able to rule out relevant alternatives to the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and they are able to rule out relevant alternatives**
