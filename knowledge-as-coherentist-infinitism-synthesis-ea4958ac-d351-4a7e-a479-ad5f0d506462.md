## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the infinite justification forms a coherent whole**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the infinite justification forms a coherent whole**
