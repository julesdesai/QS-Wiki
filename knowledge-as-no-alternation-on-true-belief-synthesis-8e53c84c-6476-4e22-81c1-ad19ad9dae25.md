# Knowledge as No Alternation on True Belief

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Is there a role for luck in reliable process knowledge?](is-there-a-role-for-luck-in-reliable-process-knowledge-antithesis-07a4e863-794a-4087-8fa6-15725afe5a82.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is not easily subject to alternation by external factors**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is stable against external factors**
