# Can structural theories substitute direct experiential knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5a99603d-e717-4b97-9704-d23ab2d94fb7
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Epistemic Structural Realism](epistemic-structural-realism-synthesis-4cc8fed2-62df-47a3-af1a-0c0e49fc23b5.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a person knows the structural design of a car engine without having direct experience of its operation. Intuitively, firsthand experience can impart practical knowledge that structural understanding alone cannot provide.**, **It is implausible that structural theories alone can fully substitute for the nuanced understanding that comes from direct experience in practical applications.**
