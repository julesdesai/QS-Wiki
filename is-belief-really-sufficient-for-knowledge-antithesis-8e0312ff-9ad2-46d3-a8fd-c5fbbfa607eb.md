# Is belief really sufficient for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 8e0312ff-9ad2-46d3-a8fd-c5fbbfa607eb
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Subject-Sensitive Invariantism](subject-sensitive-invariantism-synthesis-687e35ad-b9eb-49bd-a730-d8bc9269ea3a.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which some person sincerely believes a false proposition based on misunderstood evidence. In such a scenario, intuitively we would not judge that they know the proposition.**, **There is a plausible principle that belief alone cannot constitute knowledge without additional justification, which conflicts with the idea that belief is sufficient for knowledge.**
