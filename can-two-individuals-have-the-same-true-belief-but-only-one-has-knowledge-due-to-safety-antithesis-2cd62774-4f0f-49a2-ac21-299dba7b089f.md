# Can two individuals have the same true belief, but only one has knowledge due to safety?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2cd62774-4f0f-49a2-ac21-299dba7b089f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology with Safety Condition](virtue-epistemology-with-safety-condition-synthesis-2582b817-bed7-4f55-af09-985f5a9b310f.md)

## Content
