# Does contextualism undermine the objective nature of knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7289e835-79ff-4bc0-bcfc-70a95de4bd18
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism about Knowledge](contextualism-about-knowledge-synthesis-3f771a9e-26d6-43c4-b604-a31f8bd9947c.md)

## Content

**Knowledge is often considered objective, meaning it should be independent of individual contexts.**, **Contextualism implies that knowledge is tied to individual or group contexts.**, **It is problematic if what is known can vary dramatically between different contexts, undermining objectivity.**
