# Does the theory handle cases of irrelevant alternatives?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e2d29112-9f09-451f-972e-51b24fa3236b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Nozick’s Truth-Tracking Theory](nozicks-truth-tracking-theory-synthesis-5a62ad60-20de-4329-b52b-21f59b8da1c1.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where a person believes a true proposition based on evidence, but there are irrelevant alternatives that they have not considered. If the only relevant alternatives in close possible worlds where the proposition is false are never encountered by the person, intuitively, one might judge that the person's knowledge claim is suspect, because their truth-tracking is compromised when they fail to consider relevant possibilities.**
