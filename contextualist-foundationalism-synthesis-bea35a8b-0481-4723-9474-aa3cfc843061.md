## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is supported by foundational, non-inferential beliefs**, **Foundational beliefs depend on the context and can vary with different circumstances**
