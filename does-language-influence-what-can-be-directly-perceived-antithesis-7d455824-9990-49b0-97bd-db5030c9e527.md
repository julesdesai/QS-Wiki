# Does language influence what can be directly perceived?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7d455824-9990-49b0-97bd-db5030c9e527
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Critical Direct Realism](critical-direct-realism-synthesis-6d68b442-c014-46c1-8b6c-ef16694b0a4c.md)

## Content

**The linguistic framework can shape perception by determining the categories used to interpret sensory data.**, **Different languages can lead to different perceptual interpretations, challenging the universality of direct perception knowledge claims.**, **If cognitive processing filtered through language affects how we perceive, then the knowledge gained may not capture the real nature of objects.**
