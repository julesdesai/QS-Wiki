# How does the infallibility of knowledge fit with process reliabilism's fallibility?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 228687c8-f676-432b-a4f8-37ea4b678082
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Process Reliabilism](process-reliabilism-synthesis-456c7378-2f2e-48dd-af81-ab7fd499203a.md)

## Content

**Knowledge is justified true belief produced by a reliable process.**, **Process reliabilism admits occasional mistakes, meaning reliable processes can still yield errors.**, **The idea that knowledge can contain such errors seems at odds with the conventional idea of infallible knowledge.**
