# Is it feasible to account for all relevant alternatives in every situation?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** bd1c2054-436b-4f56-9c18-9b6df098ef71
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Relevant Alternatives Theory](relevant-alternatives-theory-synthesis-5db4d183-a5a9-469a-b4ff-d831c6a59a11.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that a person can identify and rule out all relevant alternatives in every situation due to practical limitations such as time, cognitive capacity, and available information.**, **Everyday examples show that people make decisions and claim knowledge based on limited information and consideration of alternatives.**
