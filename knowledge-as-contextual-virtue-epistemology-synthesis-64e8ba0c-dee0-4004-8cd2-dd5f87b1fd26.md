# Knowledge as Contextual Virtue Epistemology

**Parent:** [Can collective belief based on social norms be classified as knowledge?](can-collective-belief-based-on-social-norms-be-classified-as-knowledge-antithesis-79dfb01c-08ae-4d1c-acc1-b9f30d4ff8c1.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief results from intellectual virtues appropriate to the context of the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief results from contextually appropriate intellectual virtues**
