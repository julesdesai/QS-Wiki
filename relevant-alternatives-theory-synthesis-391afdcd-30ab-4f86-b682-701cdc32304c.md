## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they can rule out relevant alternative propositions that would make the proposition false**, **an alternative is relevant if it is a possibility considered in the context where the belief is formed**
