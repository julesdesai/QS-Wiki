# Can an intellectually virtuous agent still lack knowledge due to the influence of misleading evidence?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** dd353a8a-0fdb-4a4c-aa4a-987c7e30082b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Safe Intellectual Virtue Epistemology](safe-intellectual-virtue-epistemology-synthesis-f848b9b7-8a1e-463c-8e96-66e43c041eac.md)

## Content

**Knowledge is true belief arising from intellectual virtue.**, **Consider a scenario in which a person uses intellectual virtues but is presented with misleading evidence that leads to correct belief by luck.**, **If misleading evidence can lead a virtuous person accidentally to the truth, this challenges the necessity of virtue for knowledge.**
