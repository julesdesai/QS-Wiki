# Knowledge as Coherence with Externalist Justification

**Parent:** [Can coherence lead to reliability without additional criteria?](can-coherence-lead-to-reliability-without-additional-criteria-antithesis-937c66c3-04f3-4911-b254-602492a03061.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification involves a coherent set of beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief system is externally justified**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, there exists a coherent set of beliefs justifying the belief, and the belief system is externally justified**
