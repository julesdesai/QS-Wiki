## Content

**Knowledge is a true belief that arises from an intellectual virtue**, **A person knows a proposition if they believe it based on counterfactual dependence**, **A belief shows counterfactual dependence if in close possible worlds where the proposition is false, the person would not believe it**, **An intellectual virtue must ensure the counterfactual dependence of the belief for it to constitute knowledge**
