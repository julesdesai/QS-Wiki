# Is justified true belief enough for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5943e6f8-a58d-4f4c-82ec-ae2c88167aab
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism with a finite chain of reasons](reliabilism-with-a-finite-chain-of-reasons-synthesis-a3f7a107-c974-4a8e-a7f2-d20f4743454a.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where a person has a justified true belief due to coincidental evidence rather than reliable processes.**, **According to intuition, coincidental evidence should not lead to knowledge even if a belief is true and justified.**, **Hence, justified true belief alone might not account for knowledge in such coincidental situations.**
