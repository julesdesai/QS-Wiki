## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that none of their grounds for believing the proposition are false**, **All grounds for the proposition must be independent of false propositions**
