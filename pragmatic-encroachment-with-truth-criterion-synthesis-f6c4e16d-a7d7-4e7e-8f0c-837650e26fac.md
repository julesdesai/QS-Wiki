## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that practical interests affect the standard of justification**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief meets the standard adjusted by practical interests**
