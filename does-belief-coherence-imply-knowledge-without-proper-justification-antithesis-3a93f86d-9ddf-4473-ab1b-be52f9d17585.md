# Does belief coherence imply knowledge without proper justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3a93f86d-9ddf-4473-ab1b-be52f9d17585
**Depth:** 2
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism](coherentism-thesis-368b08e4-1a7c-4e5d-96a8-85ade01bb443.md)

## Content

**Coherence as a criterion for justification does not account for scenarios where an individual holds an unjustified true belief that coheres with their other beliefs.**, **There can be justified beliefs that fall outside of the system of an individual's other coherent beliefs due to hindsight or lack of complete information.**
