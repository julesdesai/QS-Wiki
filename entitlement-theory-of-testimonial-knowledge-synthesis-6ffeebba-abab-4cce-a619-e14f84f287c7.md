## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A belief formed through testimony can be knowledge if the subject has entitlement to accept the testimony**, **Entitlement does not require the hearer to track the truth directly**
