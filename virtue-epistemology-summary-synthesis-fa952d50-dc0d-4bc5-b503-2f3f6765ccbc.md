## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief arises from the exercise of intellectual virtue or competence that is reliably aimed at truth**, **For a belief to constitute knowledge, the agent must manifest their intellectual virtue in forming that belief**
