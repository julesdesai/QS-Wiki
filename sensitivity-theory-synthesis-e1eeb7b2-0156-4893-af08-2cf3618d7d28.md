## Content

**A person knows a proposition if they believe it**, **A belief is sensitive if, in the nearest possible world where the proposition is false, the person does not believe it**, **The method of forming the belief must be such that it would not affirm false propositions in similar scenarios**, **The connection between belief and truth must endure counterfactual scenarios**
