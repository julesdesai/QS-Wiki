# Does invariantist contextualism accommodate skeptical challenges?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 11ca2a0c-fe68-4bde-bb0b-8b1b99300908
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Invariantist Contextualism](invariantist-contextualism-synthesis-72e0f6e6-e984-47a7-9872-225586edec54.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that in responding to skepticism, one must explain why common-sense knowledge is not undermined by skeptical scenarios.**, **Invariantist contextualism suggests knowledge standards are fixed and do not vary, which may struggle to account for the persuasive power of skeptical arguments.**, **It is difficult to reconcile invariant knowledge standards with the need to counteract skepticism without contextually adjusted responses.**
