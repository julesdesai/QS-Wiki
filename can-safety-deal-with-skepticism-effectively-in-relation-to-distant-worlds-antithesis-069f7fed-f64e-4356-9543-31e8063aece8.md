# Can safety deal with skepticism effectively in relation to distant worlds?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 069f7fed-f64e-4356-9543-31e8063aece8
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Safety Condition Theory](safety-condition-theory-synthesis-b5a72759-42a3-4d9b-b74c-df40c84bdc9d.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that knowledge cannot be undermined by distant possibilities of error. However, the safety condition entails that any possibility of error, even in far-flung possible worlds, can affect the status of knowledge.**, **Consider a scenario where we learn we could be brains in vats in a distant possible world. Intuitively, we want to maintain our knowledge despite distant skeptical scenarios.**
