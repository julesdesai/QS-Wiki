# Does learnability impact the necessity of a design plan?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c7f9a1cf-595b-4fce-a9c7-7735ee7958d6
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Proper Functionalism](proper-functionalism-synthesis-252aa5eb-1857-4367-af60-24f7afe1c9be.md)

## Content

**It is possible for a person to learn and adapt their cognitive faculties without a pre-existing design plan, leading to true beliefs. Intuitively, such a process results in knowledge despite lacking a predetermined design plan that aims at truth.**, **Assume that a person can develop accurate cognitive processes through experience without a prior design plan. This raises doubts about whether a design plan is necessary for cognitive faculties to achieve knowledge.**
