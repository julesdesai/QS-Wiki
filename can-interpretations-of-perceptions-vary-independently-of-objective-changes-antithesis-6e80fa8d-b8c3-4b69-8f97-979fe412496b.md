# Can interpretations of perceptions vary independently of objective changes?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 6e80fa8d-b8c3-4b69-8f97-979fe412496b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Dual Aspect Theory](dual-aspect-theory-synthesis-e0a4859a-34ba-4bc9-9368-89fce3ac2e21.md)

## Content

**Interpretation involves the subjective understanding of perceived sensory input.**, **Objective changes refer to alterations in the physical environment that are independent of observer interpretation.**, **Two people can perceive an unchanging physical object in two different ways, demonstrating that interpretation can vary while the object remains unchanged.**
