## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, they must act as if the proposition is true in a consistently successful manner**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **A person knows a proposition if their actions compatible with it align with successful outcomes as determined by the truth of the proposition**
