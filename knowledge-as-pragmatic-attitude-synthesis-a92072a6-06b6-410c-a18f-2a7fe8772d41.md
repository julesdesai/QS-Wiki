## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have an attitude towards the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the attitude has practical consequences or usefulness**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have an attitude towards the proposition, the proposition is true, and the attitude has practical consequences or usefulness**
