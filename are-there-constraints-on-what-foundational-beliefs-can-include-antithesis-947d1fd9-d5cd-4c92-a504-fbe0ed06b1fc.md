# Are there constraints on what foundational beliefs can include?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 947d1fd9-d5cd-4c92-a504-fbe0ed06b1fc
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundationalism as a basis for justification](foundationalism-as-a-basis-for-justification-synthesis-96d7dd5d-5a4f-4ab7-a168-41aac5038abd.md)

## Content

**Consider a principle that foundational beliefs should be devoid of assumptions that require further evidence.**, **Foundational beliefs are sometimes based on complex perceptions or introspections that may themselves require justification.**, **If foundational beliefs are not self-evident or incontrovertible, they cannot serve as the basis for further justified beliefs without additional support.**
