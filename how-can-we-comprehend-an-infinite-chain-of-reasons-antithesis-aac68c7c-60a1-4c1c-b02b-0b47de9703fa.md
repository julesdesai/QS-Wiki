# How can we comprehend an infinite chain of reasons?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** aac68c7c-60a1-4c1c-b02b-0b47de9703fa
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism](infinitism-synthesis-4b9cf48f-532c-40a3-a960-718d00ef564e.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that human cognitive faculties can comprehend or handle infinite sequences, raising doubts about infinite justification.**, **Consider a person who insists their knowledge is based on an infinite chain, but when probed, they admit inability to comprehend the chain fully. Intuitively, this inability to comprehend undermines claims to knowledge.**, **A plausible principle holds that comprehensible reason chains are essential for justification, casting doubt on chains that are infinite.**
