## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **Knowledge does not solely depend on the internal states of the person**, **For a proposition to be known, the person must stand in an appropriate external relation to the truth of the proposition**
