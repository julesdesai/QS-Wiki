## Content

**Knowledge does not depend on the context of the knower**, **A person knows a proposition if the proposition is true**, **The standards for knowing a proposition are determined by the reliability of the process used to form the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have evidence supporting the proposition**, **The reliability threshold does not change with context**
