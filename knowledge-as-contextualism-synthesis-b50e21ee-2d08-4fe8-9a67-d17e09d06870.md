## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that evidential standards for knowing may vary depending on the context**, **The context determines which propositions must be ruled out for the person to know the initial proposition**
