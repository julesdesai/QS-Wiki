# Nozick's Truth Tracking Theory

**Parent:** [Can perceptual errors invalidate the necessity of causal connections for knowledge?](can-perceptual-errors-invalidate-the-necessity-of-causal-connections-for-knowledge-antithesis-d897414e-662e-4d62-abb4-ea98affa553d.md)

## Content

**A person knows a proposition if their belief adheres to the truth of the proposition**, **A person's belief adheres to the truth if, in possible worlds where the proposition is false, they do not believe it**, **A person's belief also adheres to the truth if, in possible worlds where the proposition is true, they do believe it**, **Knowledge does not require a direct causal connection but requires sensitivity to the truth**
