# Can externalism explain variations in cognitive process reliability?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b14c65da-b139-4c66-98ef-bda5712a5955
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalism](externalism-synthesis-5b44df35-6663-4278-91dc-65b3fcbf870c.md)

## Content

**Suppose that while awake your cognitive faculties are reliable, but while dreaming, they lead to error. It is implausible to hold all dreams as knowledge.**, **A epistemic view that allows dreaming states to mistakenly attribute reliability challenges the separation between knowledge and belief.**
