## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed through the exercise of epistemic virtues**, **Epistemic virtues include traits such as open-mindedness and intellectual courage**
