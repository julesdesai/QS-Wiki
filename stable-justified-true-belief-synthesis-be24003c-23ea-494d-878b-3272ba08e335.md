## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that belief stability is ensured across a wide range of conditions**, **For belief stability, it is necessary that the belief remains true and justified in slightly altered circumstances**
