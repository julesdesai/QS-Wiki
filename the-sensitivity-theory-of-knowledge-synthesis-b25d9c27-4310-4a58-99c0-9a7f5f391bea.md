## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, then the person would not believe it**, **The sensitivity condition ensures that the belief would change if the proposition were false**
