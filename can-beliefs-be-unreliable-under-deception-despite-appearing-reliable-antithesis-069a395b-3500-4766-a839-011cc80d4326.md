# Can beliefs be unreliable under deception despite appearing reliable?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 069a395b-3500-4766-a839-011cc80d4326
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Process Reliabilism](process-reliabilism-synthesis-73062a2b-1357-42ba-b901-d397b26dce96.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person forms a belief based on deception that seems like a reliable source, such as a fake news outlet that mimics legitimate news reporting. In this case, intuitively, we would say the belief lacks reliability and hence is not knowledge.**, **Suppose a scenario where a person has a belief formed from familiar and trusted methods whilst unknowingly undercut by a systematic deception by experts manipulating data. Intuitively, we judge that the person does not have knowledge.**
