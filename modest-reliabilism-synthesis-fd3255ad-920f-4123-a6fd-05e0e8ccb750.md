# Modest Reliabilism

**Parent:** [Can beliefs formed from unreliable processes still be true?](can-beliefs-formed-from-unreliable-processes-still-be-true-antithesis-f300d3c2-7b86-4dd0-b298-2d081cefaab6.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed through a reliable process**, **A belief formed through an unreliable process does not count as knowledge even if it is true**
