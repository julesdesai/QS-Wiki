# Does the involvement of passive cognitive states challenge the epistemic status of justified true belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** acbaf611-8486-4be3-aab5-a807bf66de6a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology Summary](virtue-epistemology-summary-synthesis-ebb76c32-727a-4eba-8f42-21c2ee3c5622.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person forms a true belief while dreaming, with no active cognitive processes involved. Intuitively, passive states lack the active engagement requisite for knowledge, challenging justified true belief as a sufficient condition.**
