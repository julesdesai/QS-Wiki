## Content

**A belief is sensitive if it would not be held if the proposition were false**, **A person knows a proposition if they believe it and that belief is sensitive to its truth**, **A person knows a proposition if the proposition is true**, **Sensitivity implies a connection between the belief and the truth of the proposition**, **A belief is safe if in nearby possible worlds where the belief is held, it is not false**
