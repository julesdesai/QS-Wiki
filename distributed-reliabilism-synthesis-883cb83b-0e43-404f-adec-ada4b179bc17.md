## Content

**For a group to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a group to know a proposition, it is necessary that their true belief arises from reliable distributed cognitive processes**, **Reliable distributed cognitive processes are those where the interaction among group members tends to produce a high ratio of true to false beliefs**
