## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is an infinite chain of reasons supporting the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is true and justified by an infinite chain of reasons**
