## Content

**Knowledge requires belief based on reliable methods**, **A person knows a proposition if they use reliable cognitive processes to form their belief**, **Reliability is defined in terms of the tendency of the process to produce true beliefs**, **Knowledge permits instances where reliable processes lead to true beliefs, even if the proposition could sometimes lead to false beliefs**
