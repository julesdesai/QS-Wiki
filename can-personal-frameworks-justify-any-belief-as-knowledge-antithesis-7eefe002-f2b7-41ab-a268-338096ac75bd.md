# Can personal frameworks justify any belief as knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7eefe002-f2b7-41ab-a268-338096ac75bd
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Epistemic Relativism with Justification Relative to Frameworks](epistemic-relativism-with-justification-relative-to-frameworks-synthesis-ad42e55b-b997-4355-ba09-4958e4df6c61.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which an individual, Dave, claims to know something because it fits within his personal framework, which includes astrology as a primary source of knowledge. Intuitively, we either have to say that Dave's claim is knowledge, or re-evaluate what constitutes legitimate knowledge. If we say Dave's claim is knowledge, then any belief could potentially gain the status of knowledge. If we re-evaluate, then not all frameworks justify true knowledge.**
