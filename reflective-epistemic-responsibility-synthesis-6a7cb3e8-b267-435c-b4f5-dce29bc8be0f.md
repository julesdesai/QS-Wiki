## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed by a reliable process**, **A reliable process is one that produces true beliefs more often than false ones**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they demonstrate epistemic responsibility by reflecting on the belief and its origins**
