## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in nearly all nearby possible worlds where they disbelieve the proposition, it is false**, **Sensitivity of belief ensures that false belief is not possible if true belief is maintained**
