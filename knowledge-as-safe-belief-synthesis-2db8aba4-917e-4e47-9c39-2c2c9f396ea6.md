# Knowledge as Safe Belief

**Parent:** [Do people's methods of arriving at true beliefs undermine reliability?](do-peoples-methods-of-arriving-at-true-beliefs-undermine-reliability-antithesis-9b404f76-dbe0-49e8-970e-ebd057b51a82.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is safe, meaning it could not easily have been false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, that the proposition is true, and that the belief is safe**
