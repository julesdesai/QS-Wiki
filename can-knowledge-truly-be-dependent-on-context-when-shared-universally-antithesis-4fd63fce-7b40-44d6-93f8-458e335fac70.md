# Can knowledge truly be dependent on context when shared universally?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 4fd63fce-7b40-44d6-93f8-458e335fac70
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextual Direct Realism](contextual-direct-realism-synthesis-9053ea14-9a99-4649-aa95-1bc3b223c980.md)

## Content

**Shared or universal knowledge presupposes that it holds across different contexts.**, **If knowledge is context-dependent, it challenges the universality of scientific and empirical knowledge.**, **It is implausible that context-based perceptions account for knowledge that is universally acknowledged.**
