# Knowledge as Externalist Justification

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can coherence ignore experiential evidence?](can-coherence-ignore-experiential-evidence-antithesis-395caeda-2967-4b2d-8d1b-968d8f96802b.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief has an external factor that contributes to its truth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and an external factor justifies the belief**
