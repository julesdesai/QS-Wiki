## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres with a consistent and comprehensive system of beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is an appropriate connection to the external world**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief coheres with their cognitive system, and there is a suitable connection with the external world**
