**Parent:** [Can a true belief that is socially validated still fail to constitute knowledge?](can-a-true-belief-that-is-socially-validated-still-fail-to-constitute-knowledge-antithesis-79f5b03b-45e4-4f5e-a4a1-6d380c1639fa.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief accurately reflects the state of the external world**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief accurately reflects the state of the external world**
