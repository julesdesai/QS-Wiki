## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person arrives at the belief through the exercise of epistemic virtues like carefulness and thoroughness**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is true, the person believes it, and the belief is the result of epistemic virtue**
