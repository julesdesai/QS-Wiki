## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is not only safe but also necessarily true**, **A belief is safe if it is true in all nearby possible worlds where it is held, but also necessarily true in some**
