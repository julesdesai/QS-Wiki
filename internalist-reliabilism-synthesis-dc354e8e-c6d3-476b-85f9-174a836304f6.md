## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have a justified belief in the proposition**, **A belief is justified if it is formed by using reasons accessible to the believer**, **The reliability of the belief-forming process is secondary to the belief being justified through accessible reasons**
