# Knowledge as Tracking the Truth

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Do high probability beliefs necessarily account for actual truth conditions?](do-high-probability-beliefs-necessarily-account-for-actual-truth-conditions-antithesis-66ec5823-7486-47cf-a51c-5f7a6c1a3615.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were true, the person would believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief tracks the truth**
