# Does infinite regress negate the possibility of stopping points in justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7c0ea0dc-4672-4490-9676-95643d02bde3
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Foundationalism with Infinite Background](foundationalism-with-infinite-background-synthesis-b6351c4d-7dab-447a-b3c7-52e8418919cd.md)

## Content

**An infinite regress implies no ultimate terminus for justification.**, **Without a terminus, there is no final stopping point where beliefs can be justified.**, **Without justification through an end point, the necessary conditions for knowledge may not be satisfied.**
