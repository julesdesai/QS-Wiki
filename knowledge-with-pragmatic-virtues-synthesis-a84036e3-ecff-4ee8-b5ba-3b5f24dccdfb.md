**Parent:** [Does belief in a false proposition ever lead to knowledge?](does-belief-in-a-false-proposition-ever-lead-to-knowledge-antithesis-aac118df-7ae1-4e29-a61a-76277b1285df.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief leads to successful practical outcomes**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their cognitive faculties are functioning properly**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, their belief leads to successful practical outcomes, and their cognitive faculties are functioning properly**
