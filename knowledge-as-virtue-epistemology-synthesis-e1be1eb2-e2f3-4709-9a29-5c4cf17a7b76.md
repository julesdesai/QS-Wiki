# Knowledge as Virtue Epistemology

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is a result of intellectual virtues reliably aimed at truth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is coherent with the individual’s overall intellectual character**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief results from intellectual virtues and cohere with the individual’s intellectual character**
