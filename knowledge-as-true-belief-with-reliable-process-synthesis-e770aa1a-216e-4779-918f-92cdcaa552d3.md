# Knowledge as True Belief with Reliable Process

**Parent:** [Can knowledge exist independently of psychological states like belief?](can-knowledge-exist-independently-of-psychological-states-like-belief-antithesis-69cf23b6-f03e-42f4-9eff-8461c27c5f03.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **A person knows a proposition if it is true, they believe it, and their belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**
