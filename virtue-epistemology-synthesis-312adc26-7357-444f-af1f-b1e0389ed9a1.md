## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their true belief is the result of intellectual virtue or competence**, **An intellectual virtue is a characteristic that reliably leads to the acquisition of true beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their true belief is attributable to the exercise of intellectual virtue**
