# No-defeat Condition

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** c8388365-4515-41ad-a8c5-efae8e90491b
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge change with variations in scenarios?](can-knowledge-change-with-variations-in-scenarios-antithesis-f56233ae-5233-4c5a-ab59-feb3e3c70edf.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no defeating evidence or reasons undermining the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there are no defeating evidence or reasons undermining the belief**
