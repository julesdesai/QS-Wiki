# How does finite human cognition handle infinite justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7c6eadfd-812f-4446-84e6-5a177766fe74
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Finite Infinitism](finite-infinitism-synthesis-ec56b28f-9687-4f6e-b141-48dc2e075636.md)

## Content

**Consider a person with limited cognitive resources attempting to achieve infinite justification. Intuitively, their capacity is insufficient to handle such demands.**, **Human cognitive abilities are finite, raising concerns about their ability to ever fulfill the requirement for potentially infinite reasons.**, **It is implausible that finite human cognition can accommodate demands for infinite justification without compromising practical reasoning.**
