# Can reflective access exceed what is minimally necessary?

**Node ID:** fe005c2d-6a6f-4a0b-8739-cb83cbf53e8f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Minimalist Internalism](minimalist-internalism-synthesis-c1aff540-8ee4-4dca-921e-260ac4a2c333.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where extensive reflective access is available yet minimal access is required. Intuitively, this suggests a discrepancy in the necessity and sufficiency of access requirements.**, **Extensive access may reveal flaws or uncertainties not discernible with minimal access.**, **The sufficiency of minimal access can be questioned when more detailed reflective access changes the epistemic evaluation.**
