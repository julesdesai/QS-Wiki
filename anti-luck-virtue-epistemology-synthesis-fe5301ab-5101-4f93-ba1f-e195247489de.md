## Content

**Knowledge is true belief arising from intellectual virtue**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief arises from intellectual virtue and is not due to luck**, **A belief is not knowledge if its truth is a matter of luck**, **A person knows a proposition if they believe it and their intellectual virtue eliminates luck in the belief's truth**
