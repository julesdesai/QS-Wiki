# Can beliefs formed under illusions or false premises lead to knowledge if they happen to be true?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 829513e4-6e4c-4a88-86e9-bb2b1f577047
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Truth-Tracking Theory](truth-tracking-theory-synthesis-3797788b-18c3-4507-a099-e2bd4b027069.md)

## Content

**Under the truth-tracking theory, a person might believe a true proposition only because of an illusion or misleading evidence.**, **If the proposition is true and the person believes it, truth-tracking theory might allow for this belief to count as knowledge, even if the basis for the belief is mistaken.**
