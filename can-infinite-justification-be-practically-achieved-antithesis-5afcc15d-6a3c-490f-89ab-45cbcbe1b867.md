# Can infinite justification be practically achieved?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5afcc15d-6a3c-490f-89ab-45cbcbe1b867
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism with Contextual Stops](infinitism-with-contextual-stops-synthesis-554d1150-ce74-450b-ad43-18770e414e5e.md)

## Content

**Justification involves an infinite chain of reasons, which may not be practically achievable for finite human beings.**, **If justification requires an infinite series, then no one can complete this series in reality.**, **A requirement that cannot be fulfilled by anyone seems impractical and thus questionable as a real standard for knowledge.**
