# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with Causal Theory

**Parent:** [Does hindsight reliability erode the basis for knowledge?](does-hindsight-reliability-erode-the-basis-for-knowledge-antithesis-447717b4-3d73-46be-bc19-d7b1d54ebb5a.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is a causal connection between the belief and the truth of the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, they have justification for the belief, and a causal connection exists between the belief and the truth of the proposition**
