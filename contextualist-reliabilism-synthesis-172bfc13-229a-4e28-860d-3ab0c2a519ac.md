## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A belief qualifies as knowledge if it is produced by a contextually reliable cognitive process**, **A contextually reliable process is one that produces true beliefs in the current context**
