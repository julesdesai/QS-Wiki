# Does intellectual virtue guarantee truth in belief for knowledge?

## Content

**A case in which someone exercises intellectual virtue, yet their findings are systematically false due to external errors, challenges the necessity of truth in knowledge via intellectual virtue.**, **Consider a highly virtuous scientist whose methods are impeccable yet all results are rendered false by faulty equipment. This challenges the link between intellectual virtue and truth.**
