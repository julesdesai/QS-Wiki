## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is supported by an infinite chain of reasons**, **Practical consequences and utility contribute to justification**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief aligns with practical utility, they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is supported by an infinite chain of reasons**
