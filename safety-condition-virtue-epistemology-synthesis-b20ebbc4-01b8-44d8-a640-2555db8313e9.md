## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **A belief is safe if in nearly all nearby possible worlds where the person believes the proposition, the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, their belief must be safe**
