# Is reflective awareness of belief changes relevant to knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ada0dfaa-fa56-4a4e-845d-da17cd1fc0bb
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Truth-Tracking Theory](truth-tracking-theory-synthesis-1814de97-222c-437f-bd26-a9ce4edff4f7.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person believes correctly and tracks truth across situations but lacks reflective awareness of how their belief changes with truth shifts. Intuitively, this lack might limit their knowledge attribution.**, **It is plausible to argue that reflective awareness of belief dynamics is an essential component for considering a belief as knowledge.**
