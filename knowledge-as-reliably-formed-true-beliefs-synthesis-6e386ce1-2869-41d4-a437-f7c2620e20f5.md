# Knowledge as Reliably Formed True Beliefs

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 6e386ce1-2869-41d4-a437-f7c2620e20f5
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Are false beliefs necessarily detrimental to knowledge?](are-false-beliefs-necessarily-detrimental-to-knowledge-antithesis-53753c4c-f8aa-4b03-af59-2f0ef215e343.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**
