# Knowledge as Cognitive Skill Epistemology

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition or exhibit a cognitive skill related to the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief or cognitive skill to be produced by a reliable process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition or exhibit a cognitive skill, the proposition is true, and the belief or skill is produced by a reliable process**
