# Does the complexity of justification challenge its connection to knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 07757210-1f17-415a-8572-3f3924474598
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Knowledge as Justified True Belief](knowledge-as-justified-true-belief-synthesis-62a63598-400f-4dfa-a0fa-a27f5f076869.md)

## Content

**Consider that justified belief relies on a complex web of reasons and evidence that can be misunderstood or misapplied without careful examination. Intuitively, this complexity can lead to justified true belief without actual knowledge.**, **The intricacy of justification processes can allow for errors that undermine the transition from belief to knowledge.**
