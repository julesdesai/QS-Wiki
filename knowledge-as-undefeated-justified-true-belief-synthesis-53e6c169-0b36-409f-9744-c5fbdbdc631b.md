# Knowledge as undefeated justified true belief

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 53e6c169-0b36-409f-9744-c5fbdbdc631b
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is knowledge achievable under deceptive contexts?](is-knowledge-achievable-under-deceptive-contexts-antithesis-7807c3f7-77c4-434d-ad98-08d66a7a8dd1.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for their belief which is not defeated by unknown factors**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is both justified and undefeated**
