# Balanced Mixed Internalist-Externalist Knowledge

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 633f1ea4-df32-43d0-96c0-4b4c2ef815b3
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Do external factors alone justify belief as knowledge?](do-external-factors-alone-justify-belief-as-knowledge-antithesis-51eba2e3-cf26-4482-921e-ab58a9d64e0e.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is supported by both adequate internal justification and external conditions which shield it from defeasibility**
