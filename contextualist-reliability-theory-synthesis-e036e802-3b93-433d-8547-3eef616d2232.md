## Content

**Knowledge is a true belief produced by a reliable process**, **A person knows a proposition if the belief is produced by a cognitive process that typically yields true beliefs**, **The reliability of a process depends on the context within which it operates**, **A person does not know a proposition if the belief is true purely by accidental or lucky circumstances given the context**, **Beliefs formed in unreliable contexts do not constitute knowledge even if they are true**
