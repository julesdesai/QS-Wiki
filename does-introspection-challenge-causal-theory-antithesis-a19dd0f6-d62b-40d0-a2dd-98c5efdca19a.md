# Does introspection challenge causal theory?

## Content

**In introspective knowledge, individuals appear to know their mental states without distinct external causal connections.**, **The inward-focused nature of introspection suggests knowledge without external causal processes.**, **The ability to ascertain mental states precisely raises doubts about the necessity of causal frameworks for such knowledge.**
