# Knowledge as Trust-Based Belief with Source Reliability

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 2963670e-9cbc-4bfb-9e42-086abad58caa
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does momentary trust equate to stable knowledge?](does-momentary-trust-equate-to-stable-knowledge-antithesis-c58bd050-f4f9-49d6-8ae1-75d1d50eee04.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the source of belief is reliable and not just trusted momentarily**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the source of belief is reliable**
