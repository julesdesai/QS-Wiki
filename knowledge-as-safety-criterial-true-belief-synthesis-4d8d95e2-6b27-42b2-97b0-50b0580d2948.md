# Knowledge as Safety-Criterial True Belief

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Does the causal connection criterion fail for mathematical knowledge?](does-the-causal-connection-criterion-fail-for-mathematical-knowledge-antithesis-dcfc072b-7020-4348-92fa-0e154a2f0038.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in nearby possible worlds, they truly believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and in nearby possible worlds, they truly believe the proposition**
