# Infinitism about Justification

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified by an infinite non-repeating chain of reasons**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have a true belief justified by an infinite non-repeating chain of reasons**
