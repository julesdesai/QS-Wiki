# Is coherence necessary for justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5753c294-f213-4bb6-88ea-e6ce36ea6f95
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalist Foundationalism](externalist-foundationalism-synthesis-3e0224db-6723-4288-8a1b-8819cbac7570.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person's beliefs do not coherently fit together but are individually externally justified, like beliefs formed from disparate reliable sources. Intuitively, we either have to say that coherence isn't needed for knowledge or that it is. If we say coherence isn't needed, then conflicting justified beliefs could still all be 'knowledge,' which seems implausible.**
