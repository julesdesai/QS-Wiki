# Can phenomenalism explain scientific objectivity?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** cf3f6f3b-559f-4b6a-9580-da6c9814cf68
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Phenomenalism Summary](phenomenalism-summary-synthesis-1942349a-6235-4079-9822-15fd8cc43040.md)

## Content

**Scientists independently verify results of an experiment and observe the same phenomena. Intuitively, scientific objectivity relies on a reality independent of any individual's sensory experience.**, **If physical objects are constructions out of sensory experiences, this undermines the idea of objective verification.**
