**Consider a scenario where the design plan of cognitive faculties is socially constructed rather than inherent. Intuitively, this suggests that such knowledge may vary across different societies, challenging the objective nature of knowledge.**, **The idea that socially constructed design plans determine proper function implies that knowledge is not universally applicable.**
