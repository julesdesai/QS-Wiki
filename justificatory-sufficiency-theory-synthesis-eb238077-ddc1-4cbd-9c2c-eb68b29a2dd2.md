## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition has adequate justification**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is true and has adequate justification**, **Belief is not a necessary condition for knowledge**
