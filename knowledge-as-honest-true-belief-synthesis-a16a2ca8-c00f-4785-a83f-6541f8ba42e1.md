# Knowledge as Honest True Belief

**Parent:** [Can a person know without any practical consequences at all?](can-a-person-know-without-any-practical-consequences-at-all-antithesis-a9bb272e-dd34-42f7-ab9d-8a89e7cd3520.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is held with honest conviction**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is held with honest conviction**
