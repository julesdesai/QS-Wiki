# Are subjective dimensions necessary for objective reality?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 4cfa6f81-1e49-45d7-ae72-d6fc45f2e075
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Phenomenological Realism](phenomenological-realism-synthesis-f972efdb-cd3c-4df6-b5d4-a15ff27a6c64.md)

## Content

**Consider a notion where scientific facts are established through objective measurement independent of subjective perception, like measuring the boiling point of water. Intuitively, we would regard this knowledge as objective and not necessarily dependent on subjective dimensions of perception.**, **Objective reality often relies on intersubjective verifiability that functions independently of individual subjective experiences.**
