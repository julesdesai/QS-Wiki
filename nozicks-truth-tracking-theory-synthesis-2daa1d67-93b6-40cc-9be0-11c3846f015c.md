**Parent:** [Is it possible for there to be knowledge without reliability in the cognitive process?](is-it-possible-for-there-to-be-knowledge-without-reliability-in-the-cognitive-process-antithesis-8f85ecd2-081f-4cbf-9850-5c780a040694.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person would not believe the proposition if it were false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person would believe the proposition if it were true**
