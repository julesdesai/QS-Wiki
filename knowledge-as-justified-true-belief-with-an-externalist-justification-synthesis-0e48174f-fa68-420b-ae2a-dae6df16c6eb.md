# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with an Externalist Justification

**Parent:** [Can the preface paradox undermine the concept of knowledge as justified true belief?](can-the-preface-paradox-undermine-the-concept-of-knowledge-as-justified-true-belief-antithesis-90217107-ce21-4fba-ae46-308c94814fd8.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their justification is reliably connected to the truth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their justification is reliably connected to the truth**
