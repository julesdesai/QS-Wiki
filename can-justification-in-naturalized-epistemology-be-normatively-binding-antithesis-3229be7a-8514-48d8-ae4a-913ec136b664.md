## Content

**Suppose justification consists solely in processes studied by natural sciences. Intuitively, they do not impose normative obligations on beliefs.**, **There is a plausible principle that epistemic justification requires normativity to oblige acceptance of beliefs.**, **Without normative force, naturalized epistemology struggles to demand adherence to justified beliefs.**
