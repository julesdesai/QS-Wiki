# Is the role of evidence underappreciated in JTB with fallibilism?

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that evidence should play a crucial role in the establishment of knowledge, yet in fallibilism, evidence can be misleading while still allowing for knowledge claims.**, **Consider a case where a person relies on evidence that only partially supports a true belief; intuitively, this weakens the foundation of considering their belief as knowledge.**
