## Content

**A person knows a proposition if they believe it**, **A person knows a proposition if the proposition is true**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they understand why their cognitive process is reliable**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they are aware of the reliability of their cognitive process**
