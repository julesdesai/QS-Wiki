## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed by cognitive faculties functioning properly according to a design plan**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient if the belief is formed by properly functioning cognitive faculties, the faculties are aimed at truth, and the faculties are in an appropriate epistemic environment**
