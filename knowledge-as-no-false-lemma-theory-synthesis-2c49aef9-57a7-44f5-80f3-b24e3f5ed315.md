# Knowledge as No-False-Lemma Theory

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 2c49aef9-57a7-44f5-80f3-b24e3f5ed315
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can a reliable process result in a true belief without knowledge?](can-a-reliable-process-result-in-a-true-belief-without-knowledge-antithesis-3928ce1b-b1dc-4a6f-821c-3a252f3c5065.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified and does not rest on any false lemmas**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is justified, and does not rest on any false lemmas**
