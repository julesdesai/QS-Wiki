## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from a reliable cognitive process**, **For a proposition to count as knowledge, it is necessary that reliable processes systematically lead to true beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief results from a reliable process**
