# Should we reconsider what counts as adequate evidence for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5b90b969-abfa-4b7d-a559-ccbdf095a6bf
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Evidentialist Theory of Knowledge](evidentialist-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-5b3e9103-4910-4e24-af7b-eaff612fb6ae.md)

## Content

**It is implausible to require that evidence be thoroughly comprehensive and infallibly supportive to count as adequate for knowledge.**, **Consider a scenario where someone deduces a scientific theory from very limited but accurate data. Intuitively, even with minimal evidence, they could possess knowledge.**
