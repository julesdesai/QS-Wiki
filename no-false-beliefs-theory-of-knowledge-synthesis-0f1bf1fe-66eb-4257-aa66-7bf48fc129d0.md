# No-False-Beliefs Theory of Knowledge

**Parent:** [Is sensitivity compatible with understanding past events as knowledge?](is-sensitivity-compatible-with-understanding-past-events-as-knowledge-antithesis-ef6d9c11-dd40-4245-a0df-535b0bd2b65f.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is not based on any false beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is not based on any false beliefs**
