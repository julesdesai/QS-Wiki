## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief results from the exercise of a reliable competence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is accurate, adroitly formed, and apt**
