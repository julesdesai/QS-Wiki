# Does epistemic relativism allow for epistemic injustice?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3853d76d-50ed-499f-a3bd-12bd548e7602
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Epistemic Relativism](epistemic-relativism-synthesis-8a36f430-fbe7-4356-93bb-efe42975c670.md)

## Content

**Consider a minority epistemic community with different norms from a dominant community.**, **Relativism implies that knowledge is valid within each community but may not be accepted by others, especially dominant ones.**, **Such a dynamic can lead to epistemic injustice, where the knowledge claims of the minority are dismissed or deemed less credible.**
