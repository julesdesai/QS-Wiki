# Reliabilism with Awareness of Reliability

**Parent:** [Can reliability alone account for all cases of knowledge?](can-reliability-alone-account-for-all-cases-of-knowledge-antithesis-5822ffe8-a0b6-4b63-a5e4-a127b3d68094.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person is aware of the reliability of their cognitive process**
