# Can knowledge exist without belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 49084c64-dd84-45fc-beae-f6d33b57aabd
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Knowledge as Tracking Theory](knowledge-as-tracking-theory-synthesis-4ddbf25f-905e-4551-9162-40f6ecb67e5a.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person has no conscious belief about a proposition but their subconscious behavior aligns perfectly with the truth of that proposition. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that the person might still know the proposition, even though they do not consciously believe it.**
