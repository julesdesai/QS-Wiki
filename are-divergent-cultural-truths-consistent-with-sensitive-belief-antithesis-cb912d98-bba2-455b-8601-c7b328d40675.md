**Suppose a person holds a belief due to prevailing cultural norms or teachings, which an individual from another culture sincerely contradicts. Intuitively, both persons claim knowledge, but this challenges sensitivity if neither would change their belief despite a contradiction.**, **Cultural determinants of belief producing rival truths highlight a potential failure in sensitivity, as adherence may overshadow reality.**
