## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are basic beliefs that do not require further justification**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and it is based on basic beliefs that are justified without further support**
