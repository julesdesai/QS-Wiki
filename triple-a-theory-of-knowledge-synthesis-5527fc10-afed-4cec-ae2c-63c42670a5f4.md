## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is accurate, adroit, and apt**, **A belief is apt when it is accurate because of the agent's intellectual abilities, which include understanding**
