# Does perceptual illusion challenge direct perception?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 27d636b5-46d8-48ff-9344-1f32b2e4c242
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Selective Direct Realism](selective-direct-realism-synthesis-744f6327-3f79-490f-be88-a0aa209da42b.md)

## Content

**In scenarios of perceptual illusion, observers report perceptions that contradict reality, such as seeing a stick bent in water when it is straight in air.**, **In scenarios where perception is influenced by environmental conditions, the perception does not directly relate to unaltered foundational parts of reality.**, **In cases of perceptual illusion, perceptions do not remain stable across varying conditions, challenging the reliability of direct perception as a source for knowing propositions.**
