# Knowledge with Counterfactual Sensitivity

**Parent:** [Can safety determine reliability when beliefs are coincidentally true?](can-safety-determine-reliability-when-beliefs-are-coincidentally-true-antithesis-86ba8b8d-5e03-4a03-8b38-eb98d5a4d9ba.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief tracks the truth across a range of possible scenarios**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief tracks the truth across a range of possible scenarios**
