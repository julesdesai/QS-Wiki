## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **The sensitivity condition ensures that in the closest possible worlds where the proposition is false, the person does not hold the belief**
