## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief does not depend on any false premises**, **A belief that is inferred from true premises alone qualifies as knowledge**
