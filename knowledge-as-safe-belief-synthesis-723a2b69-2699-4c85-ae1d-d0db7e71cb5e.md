## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief is formed in such a way that it could not easily have been false**, **A safe belief is one that, given the method of belief formation, remains true in all nearby possible worlds**
