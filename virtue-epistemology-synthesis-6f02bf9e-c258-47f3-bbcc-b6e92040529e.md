**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they arrive at the belief through intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is true and they arrive at the belief through intellectual virtue**
