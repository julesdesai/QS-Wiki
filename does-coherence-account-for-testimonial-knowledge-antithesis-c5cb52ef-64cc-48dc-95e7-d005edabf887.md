# Does coherence account for testimonial knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c5cb52ef-64cc-48dc-95e7-d005edabf887

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism Summary](coherentism-summary-synthesis-4ac849ed-bd0f-4658-91df-2567162ccdab.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which an individual gains a justified belief from testimony without integrating it into their broader belief system. The intuitive judgment might be that this person still possesses some knowledge**, **A plausible principle is that testimonial knowledge does not always require coherence with existing beliefs**
