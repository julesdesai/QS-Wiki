## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the contextual standards for knowing are consistent with the requirement for truth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they meet the truth requirement and the contextual standards**
