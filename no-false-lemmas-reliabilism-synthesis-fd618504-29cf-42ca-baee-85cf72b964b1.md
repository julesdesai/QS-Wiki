## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed by a reliable process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief does not rely on any false premise**, **A person knows a proposition if they believe it, the proposition is true, and their belief is formed by a reliable process without false premises**, **A reliable process is one that produces true beliefs more often than false ones**
