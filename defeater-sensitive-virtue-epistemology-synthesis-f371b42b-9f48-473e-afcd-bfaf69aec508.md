# Defeater-Sensitive Virtue Epistemology

**Parent:** [Can a true belief arrived at by unreliable virtue be considered knowledge?](can-a-true-belief-arrived-at-by-unreliable-virtue-be-considered-knowledge-antithesis-77622b68-dde0-4f16-8d6f-e09af6b88c84.md)

## Content

**Knowledge is true belief that is sensitive to the presence of defeaters**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A defeater is a reason that undermines one's justification for belief**, **Knowledge ceases if a defeater is present that the intellectual virtue cannot overcome**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe it, the proposition is true, their intellectual virtue leads to the belief, and no defeaters are present**
