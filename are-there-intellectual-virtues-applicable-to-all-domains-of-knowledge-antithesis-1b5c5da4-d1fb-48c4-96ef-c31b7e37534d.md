# Are there intellectual virtues applicable to all domains of knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1b5c5da4-d1fb-48c4-96ef-c31b7e37534d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-ffa55650-0795-4012-9f33-14517d86090e.md)

## Content

**Different domains of knowledge may prioritize different cognitive abilities, such as mathematical logic versus creative interpretation in literature.**, **An intellectual virtue in one domain might not transfer to another, challenging the universality of intellectual virtues.**, **This domain-specificity suggests that intellectual virtues may not be uniformly applicable across all areas of knowledge.**
