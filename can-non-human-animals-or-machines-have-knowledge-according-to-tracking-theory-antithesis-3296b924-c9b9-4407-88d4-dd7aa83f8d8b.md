# Can non-human animals or machines have knowledge according to tracking theory?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3296b924-c9b9-4407-88d4-dd7aa83f8d8b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Tracking Theory of Knowledge](tracking-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-d50e12e5-c945-480c-9bf6-a71471f277b2.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a machine consistently outputs correct information through programmed algorithms. Intuitively, we hesitate to attribute knowledge due to the absence of understanding or awareness typical of humans.**, **Suppose a theory allows knowledge where belief tracks truth across various scenarios, based on reliable outputs. Machines can satisfy this criteria; however, they lack conscious belief, challenging the appropriateness of ascribing knowledge.**
