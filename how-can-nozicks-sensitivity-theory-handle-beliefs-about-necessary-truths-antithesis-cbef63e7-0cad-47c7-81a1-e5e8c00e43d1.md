# How can Nozick's Sensitivity Theory handle beliefs about necessary truths?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** cbef63e7-0cad-47c7-81a1-e5e8c00e43d1
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Nozick's Sensitivity Theory](nozicks-sensitivity-theory-synthesis-6e1ddae7-9a08-404e-ac91-e01a19448f49.md)

## Content

**A belief that 2+2=4 is sensitive, as its falsity is not conceivable**, **According to the theory, a sensitive belief is one that would not be held if false**, **A belief about a necessary truth seems to remain even if we imagine its falsity, which contradicts the theory**
