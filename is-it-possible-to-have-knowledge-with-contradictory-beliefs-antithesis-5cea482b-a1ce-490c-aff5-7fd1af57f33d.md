# Is it possible to have knowledge with contradictory beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5cea482b-a1ce-490c-aff5-7fd1af57f33d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism with Pragmatic Justification](coherentism-with-pragmatic-justification-synthesis-aef723d7-2be0-4b42-a946-2acb73c17357.md)

## Content

**A coherent system of belief might contain contradictory beliefs as long as those contradictions are hidden or reconciled within the system itself.**, **If knowledge involves justified true belief, having contradictory beliefs undermines the truth condition of knowledge.**
