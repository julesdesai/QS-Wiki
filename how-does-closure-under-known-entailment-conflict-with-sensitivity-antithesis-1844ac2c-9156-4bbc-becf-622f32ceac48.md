# How does closure under known entailment conflict with sensitivity?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1844ac2c-9156-4bbc-becf-622f32ceac48
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Nozick's Conditional Entailment](nozicks-conditional-entailment-synthesis-db761f27-9caf-47b5-bb68-5ac892feba1b.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person knows P and P entails Q, but they do not recognize Q.**, **There is a plausible principle that if a person knows that P, and P entails Q, then they should know Q if they deduce it.**, **It is implausible that sensitivity suffices for closure under known entailment where the person might not consider related entailments.**
