# How does an over-reliance on technology question intellectual virtues?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f8965ab9-d1ad-4da3-9e6d-15f4ec475cdf
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-43f1bd52-38ff-4c3b-a3d7-408481a71c52.md)

## Content

**Imagine a situation where a scientist relies on advanced AI for data analysis, achieving consistently correct conclusions without understanding the underlying processes. These conclusions may align with truth but raise doubts about the scientist's intellectual virtue due to their dependency on technology.**, **Reliance on external aids without comprehension challenges the notion that intellectual virtue alone bridges belief and truth.**
