## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **If the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **If the proposition were true under slightly different conditions without lucky evidence, the person would still believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, the belief must not simply be based on a lucky coincidence**
