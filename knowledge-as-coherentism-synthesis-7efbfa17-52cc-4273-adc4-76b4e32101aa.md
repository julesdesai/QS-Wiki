# Knowledge as Coherentism

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification for their belief arises from a coherent system of interconnected beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification for their belief is derived from coherence among beliefs**
