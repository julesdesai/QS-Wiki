# Does Reliable Process Theory allow for knowledge transmission?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** bfcfa024-0bd8-4687-9f29-7a5bb5d189f0
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliable Process Theory](reliable-process-theory-synthesis-2b23f056-a980-462c-bb6c-659be3049cf1.md)

## Content

**Suppose a group of people pass a belief along, each relying on the reliability of the previous link. Intuitively, the initial reliability may wane, diminishing certainty in truth.**, **A plausible principle that knowledge transmission requires an assurance that each link maintains truthfulness challenges the adequacy of mere process-based reliability.**
