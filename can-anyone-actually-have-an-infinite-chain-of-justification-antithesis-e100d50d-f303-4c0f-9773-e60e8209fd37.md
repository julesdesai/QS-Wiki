# Can anyone actually have an infinite chain of justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e100d50d-f303-4c0f-9773-e60e8209fd37
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism View](infinitism-view-synthesis-bddda30f-213c-4b95-bb22-45afdb1e886a.md)

## Content

**For a belief to be justified under infinitism, one would need to have access to or be aware of an infinite chain of reasons.**, **Human beings are limited in their capacity to comprehend or retain an infinite series of any kind.**, **This human limitation implies that potentially no belief can be justified in the manner required by infinitism.**
