# Knowledge with Safety

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Does infallibilism make all empirical knowledge impossible?](does-infallibilism-make-all-empirical-knowledge-impossible-antithesis-4f502579-cefb-421f-8355-814ae0ac5100.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their justification ensures the belief would not have been false in nearby possible worlds**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification ensures the belief would not have been false in nearby possible worlds**
