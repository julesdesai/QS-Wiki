# Knowledge as Truth-Tracking without Dependence on Known Defeaters

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief tracks the truth independently of known defeaters**, **Truth-tracking entails changing beliefs in response to truth values across possible situations**
