## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they meet a fixed, high standard of justification**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have justification meeting the fixed standard, the proposition is true, and they believe it**
