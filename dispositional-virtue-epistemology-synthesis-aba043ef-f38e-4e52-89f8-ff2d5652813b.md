## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have a disposition to use reliable cognitive processes**, **The exercise of intellectual virtues enhances a person's dispositional reliability in forming true beliefs**
