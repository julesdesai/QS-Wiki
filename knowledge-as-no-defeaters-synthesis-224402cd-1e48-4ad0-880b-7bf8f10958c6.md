## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **There are no defeaters that would undermine the justification for the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have a true belief with no undefeated defeaters**
