## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person's belief meets the standards of justification appropriate to the context**, **The standards of justification vary depending on conversational or epistemic context**
