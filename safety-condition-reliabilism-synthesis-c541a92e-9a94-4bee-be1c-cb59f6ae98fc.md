## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief in the proposition results from a reliable cognitive process**, **A reliable cognitive process is one that consistently produces true beliefs**, **The belief must be safe from error in similar situations, where "safe" means that in nearby possible worlds, the belief would still be true**
