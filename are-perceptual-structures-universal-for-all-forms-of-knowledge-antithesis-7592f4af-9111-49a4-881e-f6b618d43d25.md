# Are perceptual structures universal for all forms of knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7592f4af-9111-49a4-881e-f6b618d43d25
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Epistemic Structuralism](epistemic-structuralism-synthesis-7fb538c7-d6b1-4a5d-b302-4f53eea0d56a.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a linguist knows about the structure of a dead language through written records without any speakers for auditory perception. Intuitively, we would judge they have linguistic knowledge despite the absence of immediate perceptual structures, challenging the universality of perceptual structures for all knowledge forms**
