# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with No Hidden Defeaters

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can a person have justified true belief without knowledge due to luck?](can-a-person-have-justified-true-belief-without-knowledge-due-to-luck-antithesis-b737a59f-ae8c-4f19-bfb7-d54214c767b9.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that no hidden defeaters exist to undermine the justification of the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, the proposition is true, and there are no hidden defeaters**
