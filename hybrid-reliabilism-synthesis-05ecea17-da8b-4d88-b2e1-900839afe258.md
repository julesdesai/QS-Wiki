## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **The belief must be produced by a reliable process that would not lead to a false belief in similar circumstances**, **No direct causal connection between belief and truth is required for knowledge**
