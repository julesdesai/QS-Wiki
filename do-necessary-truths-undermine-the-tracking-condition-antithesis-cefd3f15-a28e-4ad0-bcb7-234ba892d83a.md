# Do necessary truths undermine the tracking condition?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** cefd3f15-a28e-4ad0-bcb7-234ba892d83a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Tracking Theory of Knowledge](tracking-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-6d302593-f2de-4e10-8195-85a314e81400.md)

## Content

**Consider a necessary truth, such as a mathematical truth, which would be true in all possible situations.**, **For a belief in a necessary truth, there is no possible situation where the proposition is false.**, **If there is no situation where the proposition is false, it is not clear how belief can track truth by being sensitive to changes in truth value, which challenges the tracking requirement.**
