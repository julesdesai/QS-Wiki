## Content

**Knowledge consists of beliefs justified by an infinite chain of non-repeating reasons**, **For a person to know a proposition, it must be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it must be believed**, **The justification for the belief is an infinite series of implicative steps**, **Each step provides a reason for the preceding belief in the series**
