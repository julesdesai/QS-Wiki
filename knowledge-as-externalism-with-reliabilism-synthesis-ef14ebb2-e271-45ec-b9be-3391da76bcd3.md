## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and some conditions other than consciousness provide the justification through a reliable process**
