# Is infinite reason chaining required for everyday knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 675e96e6-5b77-404e-a4f1-53eccd7475a0
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Infinitism](pragmatic-infinitism-synthesis-954ef6f8-808a-47cf-95ad-717cbaeffb04.md)

## Content

**In practice, individuals often claim knowledge on the basis of limited, sufficient evidence, rather than infinite chains of justification.**, **If justified true beliefs are claimed in everyday scenarios without infinite chains, then such chains seem unnecessary.**
