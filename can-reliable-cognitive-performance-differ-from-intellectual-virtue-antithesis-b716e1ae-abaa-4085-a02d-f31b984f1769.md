# Can reliable cognitive performance differ from intellectual virtue?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b716e1ae-abaa-4085-a02d-f31b984f1769
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-a1d061ab-4ce5-4544-95e5-a72c751db8d0.md)

## Content

**It is possible for cognitive faculties to perform reliably without involving intellectual virtues such as open-mindedness or intellectual humility.**, **In some cases, a person might arrive at true beliefs through highly reliable faculties such as a well-calibrated device, yet lack any intellectual engagement or virtue.**
