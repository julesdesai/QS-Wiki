## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, the strength of belief required for justification is influenced by practical stakes**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the strength of belief required is sufficient given the practical stakes**
