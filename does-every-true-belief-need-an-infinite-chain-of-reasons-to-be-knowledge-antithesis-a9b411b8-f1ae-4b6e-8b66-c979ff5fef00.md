# Does every true belief need an infinite chain of reasons to be knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a9b411b8-f1ae-4b6e-8b66-c979ff5fef00
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology Infinitism](virtue-epistemology-infinitism-synthesis-700b1be0-40ec-4861-9f40-25460b6be3e1.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person holds a true belief through credible expert testimony without deriving an infinite chain of reasons.**, **In such a scenario, we would intuitively judge the person knows the proposition despite the absence of such an infinite chain.**, **It is implausible for knowledge to be contingent on infinite justification in ordinary cases.**
