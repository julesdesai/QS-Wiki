# Knowledge as Apt Performance with No Lucky Coincidences

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 2e3a9ebf-81dc-49fd-ad61-abef1f9b6894
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is it possible for a true belief arising from an apt performance to still not be knowledge?](is-it-possible-for-a-true-belief-arising-from-an-apt-performance-to-still-not-be-knowledge-antithesis-b47b66c7-1254-4ac3-a71a-8814028411f5.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief results from an apt mental performance without lucky coincidences**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief results from an apt mental performance without lucky coincidences**
