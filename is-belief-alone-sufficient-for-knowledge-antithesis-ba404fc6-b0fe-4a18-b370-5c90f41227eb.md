# Is belief alone sufficient for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ba404fc6-b0fe-4a18-b370-5c90f41227eb
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalism to knowledge with reliable cognitive process](externalism-to-knowledge-with-reliable-cognitive-process-synthesis-e78416a8-ed0b-42d6-b63b-0cc8fec7b97b.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where a person forms a belief through mere wishful thinking, with no reliable grounding.**, **Even if the belief turns out to be true, the lack of a reliable cognitive process means we would not typically count it as knowledge.**, **This challenges the idea that mere belief, even of a true proposition, guarantees knowledge.**
