# Can knowledge exist with intellectual virtue but absent true belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 39746d68-8d74-43bc-bcdb-66c9b3da1256
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology with Defeasibility](virtue-epistemology-with-defeasibility-synthesis-802a6edd-3a94-41b9-8765-bb5c975aae31.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person arrives at a false belief through intellectual virtue, such as careful reasoning from false premises. This scenario intuitively does not qualify as knowledge.**, **Intellectual virtue alone cannot justify a belief as knowledge without the accompanying truth of the belief.**
