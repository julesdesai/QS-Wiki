# Can knowledge really require sensitivity?

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person forms a belief based on strong evidence that they cannot easily access or encounter contrary to the proposition in nearest possible falsifying worlds. Intuitively, it seems this person can have knowledge despite their belief not being sensitive.**, **There is a plausible principle that people can have knowledge based on reliable evidence without considering nearest possible worlds where the proposition is false.**
