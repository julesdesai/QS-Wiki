# Are different senses affected uniformly by hallucinations?

**Parent:** [Disjunctivism](disjunctivism-synthesis-e62747dd-0f26-4379-9406-3d7f6fb883b9.md)

## Content

**Consider hallucinations known to affect some senses but not others, such as auditory without visual disruptions. Intuitively, this implies variability in perception's nature even across hallucinations.**, **A plausible principle is that consistent experiences across senses should behave uniformly, questioning if hallucinations really represent a uniform deviation from reality. This challenges the understanding of perceptual experiences in hallucinations.**
