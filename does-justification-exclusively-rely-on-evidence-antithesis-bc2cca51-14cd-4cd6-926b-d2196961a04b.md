# Does justification exclusively rely on evidence?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** bc2cca51-14cd-4cd6-926b-d2196961a04b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Evidentialist Theory of Knowledge](evidentialist-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-10c49977-d180-4d95-bcbb-8124324d7ccb.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person uses sound reasoning and logical inference from known truths to reach a true belief without any empirical evidence directly supporting this belief. In such a scenario, intuitively the reasoning alone might justify the belief.**, **There is a plausible principle that logical reasoning itself can provide justification, suggesting that evidence might not be the exclusive ground for justification.**
