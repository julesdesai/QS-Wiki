# Is the practical aspect of belief relevant to its status as knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 88b8ca11-7b02-480a-8835-c026dff95c55
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Knowledge as Justified True Belief](knowledge-as-justified-true-belief-synthesis-62a63598-400f-4dfa-a0fa-a27f5f076869.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person, Jessica, believes in a medical treatment's effectiveness based on robust scientific studies. However, she is unlikely to trust or act upon it, rendering her belief practically inert. Intuitively, this disconnect between belief and practical engagement suggests it might not be knowledge.**, **The practical applicability of a belief might play a crucial role in it qualifying as knowledge, rather than simply being true and justified.**
