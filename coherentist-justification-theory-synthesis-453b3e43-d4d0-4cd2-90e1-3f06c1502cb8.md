## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, the belief must be justified by coherence with other beliefs**, **Multiple justifications are not required if coherence exists**
