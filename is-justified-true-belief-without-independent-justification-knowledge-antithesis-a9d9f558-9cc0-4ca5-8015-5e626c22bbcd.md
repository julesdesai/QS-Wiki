# Is justified true belief without independent justification knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a9d9f558-9cc0-4ca5-8015-5e626c22bbcd
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism with Reliable Processes](reliabilism-with-reliable-processes-synthesis-a3ba1994-6d2c-475d-a166-1094fffd3d87.md)

## Content

**In some cases, an individual can have a belief that is both true and believed, yet lacks independent justification.**, **It seems counterintuitive to consider such a belief as knowledge without additional justification beyond the reliability of the cognitive process.**
