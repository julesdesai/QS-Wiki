# Can knowledge exist in the absence of conscious awareness of justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ad2fdbe6-e906-4576-b11b-5de027eb70ac
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Invariantist Knowledge Summary](invariantist-knowledge-summary-synthesis-7e971fef-e35c-4ec9-93e0-84ae5864f440.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a person has a justified true belief based on subconscious processing. Intuitively, we might question whether this counts as knowledge given the person's lack of awareness.**, **It is implausible to assert that a person knows something if they are entirely unaware of their justification for believing it.**
