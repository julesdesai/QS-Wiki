# Does Coherentism adequately accommodate perceptual knowledge?

**Parent:** [Coherentism about Knowledge](coherentism-about-knowledge-synthesis-39987724-1a23-4547-a925-9e75bbe8b421.md)

## Content

**Perceptual beliefs might seem justified independently of their role in a coherent system.**, **In a case where a person directly perceives a red apple, this belief seems justified without needing coherence with other beliefs.**, **The reliance on coherence for justification overlooks the apparent immediacy and foundational nature of perceptual knowledge.**
