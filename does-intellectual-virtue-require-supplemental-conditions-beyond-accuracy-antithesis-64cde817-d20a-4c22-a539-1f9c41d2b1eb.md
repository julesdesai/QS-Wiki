# Does intellectual virtue require supplemental conditions beyond accuracy?

## Content

**Imagine a case where a person habitually applies sound reasoning but stumbles upon a truth by coincidence rather than through this process.**, **There is a plausible claim that additional constraints are necessary to separate true beliefs formed coincidentally from those grounded in intellectual virtues.**, **Thus, the framework might need supplementation to reliably delineate knowledge from mere true belief.**
