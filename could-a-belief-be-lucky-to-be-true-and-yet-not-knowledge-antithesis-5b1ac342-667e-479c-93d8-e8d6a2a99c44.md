# Could a belief be lucky to be true and yet not knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5b1ac342-667e-479c-93d8-e8d6a2a99c44
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Causal Theory of Knowledge](causal-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-a5ebdb75-c32b-47a3-8c6b-cdebc8ceca67.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person believes that a lottery ticket will lose because the odds are overwhelmingly against winning, and indeed, the ticket loses.**, **Intuitively, the truth of the belief appears more coincidental than causally tied, making it questionable as knowledge.**
