# Can true beliefs without conscious deliberation be aptly formed?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** fa16848d-e704-49e2-a7cb-4110798c8cfe
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Triple-A Virtue Epistemology](triple-a-virtue-epistemology-synthesis-5ec13be3-cfbe-4179-96b3-378e0ebd05b2.md)

## Content

**Consider a person who, through habit, always leaves their keys in one spot and unknowingly forms the belief they are there when needed. The belief is accurate and right by habit, not conscious skillful action.**, **This case raises questions about whether automatic, non-deliberative processes can count as adroit and whether they can lead to apt beliefs.**
