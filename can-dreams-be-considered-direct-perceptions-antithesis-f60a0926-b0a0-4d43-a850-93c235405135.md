# Can dreams be considered direct perceptions?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f60a0926-b0a0-4d43-a850-93c235405135
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Naive Realism](naive-realism-synthesis-a575a970-7874-461b-b9b5-9de679546681.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person dreams of climbing a mountain. Intuitively, no direct acquaintance with an external object is happening.**, **If dreams involve experiences without any corresponding external objects, then experiential acquaintance with objects cannot be the sole producer of perception.**
