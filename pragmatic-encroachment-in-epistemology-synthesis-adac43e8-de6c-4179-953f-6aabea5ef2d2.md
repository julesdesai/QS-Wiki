# Pragmatic Encroachment in Epistemology

**Parent:** [Are pragmatic reasons for belief ever sufficient for knowledge?](are-pragmatic-reasons-for-belief-ever-sufficient-for-knowledge-antithesis-a390a872-db73-45cd-84ce-22b3d3c4abd4.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the practical stakes do not affect the justification**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is unaffected by practical stakes**
