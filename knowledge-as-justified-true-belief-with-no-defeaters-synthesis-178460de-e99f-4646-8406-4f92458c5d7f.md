# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with No Defeaters

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can habitual truth-tracking suffice for knowledge without virtue?](can-habitual-truth-tracking-suffice-for-knowledge-without-virtue-antithesis-d5e7d4bd-b367-460e-822e-d66f1e191c3f.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there be no defeaters to their belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, they have justification, and there are no defeaters**
