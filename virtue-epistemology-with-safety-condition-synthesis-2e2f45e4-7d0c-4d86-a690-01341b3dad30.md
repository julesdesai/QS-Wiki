# Virtue Epistemology with Safety Condition

**Parent:** [Is the connection between intellectual virtue and truth necessary?](is-the-connection-between-intellectual-virtue-and-truth-necessary-antithesis-c7dcb037-dad3-4b42-a22a-2f1de1974fcd.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief results from the exercise of intellectual virtue**, **For knowledge to occur, it is necessary that if the person believes a proposition due to intellectual virtue, then it is not easily possible that the belief could have been false**
