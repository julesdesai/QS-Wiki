# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with Dispositional Awareness

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** ff43ad96-7c85-4c65-8810-54ca953098f9
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is memory retention inconsistent with awareness demand?](is-memory-retention-inconsistent-with-awareness-demand-antithesis-61d674f7-88a0-41c6-8b4a-f598c1c98c5a.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, they must have a dispositional awareness of their justification, even if not occurrent**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, and have a dispositional awareness of their justification in conjunction with the proposition being true**
