# Externalist Coherentism

**Parent:** [What about beliefs formed from unreliable sources?](what-about-beliefs-formed-from-unreliable-sources-antithesis-bc228bbc-82ae-4a44-9fb5-0ca6f9d4cd51.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification involves a coherent set of beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are external conditions ensuring the reliability of belief formation**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief fits coherently within a system of beliefs, and there are external conditions ensuring the reliability of belief formation**
