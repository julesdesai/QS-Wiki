# Can the variability of context-sensitive standards undermine the practical use of knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** feeb947a-858e-4b96-ad7f-60392edd586e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism with Dynamic Standards](contextualism-with-dynamic-standards-synthesis-b251edf1-06ee-458e-a02d-1c3435caef87.md)

## Content

**Practical decision-making relies on stable knowledge claims.**, **Changes in context-sensitive standards mean what was known can alter unexpectedly.**, **If what is known can change with context, it undermines the ability to rely on knowledge in practical situations.**
