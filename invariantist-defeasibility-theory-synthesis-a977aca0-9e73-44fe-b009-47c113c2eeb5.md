## Content

**Knowledge requires having true belief undefeated by counter-evidence**, **A person knows a proposition if and only if their belief is true and justified at the time of realization**, **The justification for a belief must not be undermined by overriding reasons before realization**, **Misleading evidence that arises post-belief formation and prior to realization does not automatically negate the possibility of knowledge**
