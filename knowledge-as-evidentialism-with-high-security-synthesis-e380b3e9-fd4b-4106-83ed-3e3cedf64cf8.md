**Parent:** [Can knowledge be undermined by defeating evidence that one ignores?](can-knowledge-be-undermined-by-defeating-evidence-that-one-ignores-antithesis-63165ec4-3b37-4af5-a48f-eeb5628d959a.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is supported by strong evidence with no significant overlooked defeaters**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have a strongly evidenced belief with no significant overlooked defeaters**
