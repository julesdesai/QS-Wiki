## Content

**Synesthesia is a condition where one sensory experience involuntarily leads to another, such as seeing colors when hearing music.**, **This blending may lead to representations that don't correspond to segregated realities.**, **Synesthesia challenges the accuracy and independence of sensory-based representations.**
