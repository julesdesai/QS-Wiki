# Are all truth-conducive traits virtuous?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3bb732ff-10ba-4dda-8dbe-fdc7caf14aac
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-d7a894af-29e5-42fe-8e5e-957e2009566d.md)

## Content

**Consider a person who consistently achieves true beliefs through sheer stubbornness, refusing to change their beliefs once formed.**, **Stubbornness is not typically considered an intellectual virtue, yet it may lead to true beliefs in a number of scenarios, suggesting that not all truth-conducive traits qualify as virtues.**
