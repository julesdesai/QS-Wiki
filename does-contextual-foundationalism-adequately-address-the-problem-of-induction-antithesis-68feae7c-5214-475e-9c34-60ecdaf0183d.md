# Does Contextual Foundationalism adequately address the problem of induction?

**Parent:** [Contextual Foundationalism](contextual-foundationalism-synthesis-3cd555af-8ec3-406f-b70a-64f89852bc49.md)

## Content

**Consider the scenario where all observed swans are white and this leads to the belief that all swans are white. In such a scenario, it is possible to find a black swan and illustrate that the inductive belief was false.**, **A plausible principle suggests that relying on past observations for future predictions cannot ensure true belief. This principle challenges the contextual security of such foundational beliefs.**
