**Parent:** [Can knowledge be undermined by circumstances beyond the agent's control?](can-knowledge-be-undermined-by-circumstances-beyond-the-agents-control-antithesis-a1946a66-c4ab-4e9f-8ab2-64ac98391f25.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person's belief is formed through an intellectual virtue that includes an awareness of potential external disruptors**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition's justification includes sensitivity to external circumstances**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is true, virtuously formed, and appropriately sensitive to its environment**
