# Does Indirect Realism adequately explain the role of context in perception?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d894e498-ce60-4c1e-995b-bba60807e27c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Indirect Realism Summary](indirect-realism-summary-synthesis-1b3cd8c8-92a3-4a52-be84-8ec951a45683.md)

## Content

**Context significantly affects how individuals perceive objects and events.**, **A principle of perception is that contextual influence should be accounted for in knowledge claims.**, **If perception is only about mental representations detached from context, then it may fail to capture the full nature of perception.**
