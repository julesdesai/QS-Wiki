# Is self-awareness of justification necessary for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 722041b6-840a-40b6-8561-616385be367f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Modified Internalism with External Reliability](modified-internalism-with-external-reliability-synthesis-5d3adb72-ce8d-4976-b1d3-5d6a6f159cf7.md)

## Content

**Imagine a case where an AI system forms true beliefs and possesses justification as assessed by external standards of reliability.**, **The AI lacks awareness of its justification as it cannot self-reflect.**, **If accessible justification is necessary, the AI would lack knowledge, suggesting a potential flaw in requiring accessibility for knowledge.**
