## Content

**A person knows a proposition if there are no defeaters that would undermine their justification**, **A defeater is any evidence that would render their belief unjustified**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**
