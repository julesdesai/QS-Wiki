# Does epistemic relativism collapse into subjectivism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3882d121-0b54-48e6-bf43-16da6c81569c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Epistemic Relativism](epistemic-relativism-synthesis-8a36f430-fbe7-4356-93bb-efe42975c670.md)

## Content

**If knowledge is relative to frameworks and these frameworks are themselves subject to individual or community standards, then knowledge becomes flexible and subjective.**, **A person, by adopting a particular epistemic stance or framework, could claim knowledge merely based on belief and community-confirmation.**, **This collapses the distinction between opinion and knowledge, undermining objective or shared criteria for claims of knowledge.**
