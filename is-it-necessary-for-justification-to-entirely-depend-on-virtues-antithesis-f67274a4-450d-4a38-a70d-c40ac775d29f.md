# Is it necessary for justification to entirely depend on virtues?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f67274a4-450d-4a38-a70d-c40ac775d29f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology with Agent-Centric Justification](virtue-epistemology-with-agent-centric-justification-synthesis-7d83e915-f335-4c77-b1f8-3aa2f1497b9b.md)

## Content

**Suppose person I forms highly accurate beliefs about financial markets by using a proprietary algorithm they developed. Although they might not possess intellectual virtues themselves, they reach true beliefs frequently. Intuitively, it seems they have knowledge.**, **The necessity of intellectual virtues for knowledge seems undermined if alternative forms of justification, like technological tools, can produce reliable beliefs.**
