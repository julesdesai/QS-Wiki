# Virtue Epistemology Theory of Knowledge

**Parent:** [Is the safety condition necessary for knowledge?](is-the-safety-condition-necessary-for-knowledge-antithesis-f86e3a9f-26c2-40c4-aafe-76b164ce68a8.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief be the result of exercising epistemic virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief be the result of exercising epistemic virtue**
