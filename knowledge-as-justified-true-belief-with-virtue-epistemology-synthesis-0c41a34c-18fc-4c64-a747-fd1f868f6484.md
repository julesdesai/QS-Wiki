## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition as a result of intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed by exercising intellectual virtues**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition as a result of intellectual virtue, the proposition is true, and the belief is formed by exercising intellectual virtues**
