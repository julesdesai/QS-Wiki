## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, their belief must be causally grounded in the truth of the proposition**, **Causal grounding involves a direct causal link between the belief and the truth-making facts**, **The causal connection ensures that the belief tracks the truth based on factual information**, **Causal grounding eliminates the possibility of knowledge resulting from luck**
