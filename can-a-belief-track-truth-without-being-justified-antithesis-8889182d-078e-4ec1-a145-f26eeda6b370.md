# Can a belief track truth without being justified?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 8889182d-078e-4ec1-a145-f26eeda6b370
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Nozick's Tracking Theory](nozicks-tracking-theory-synthesis-92e582e4-32a5-4017-b62b-ea7485c8afb8.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person believes a proposition solely based on a lucky guess, and the proposition happens to be true. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that the person does not have knowledge, because knowledge typically requires justification.**, **It is implausible that knowledge can be attained purely by a belief's ability to track truth if no justification is accessible to the believer.**
