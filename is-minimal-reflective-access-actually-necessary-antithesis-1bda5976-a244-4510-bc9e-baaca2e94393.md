# Is minimal reflective access actually necessary?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1bda5976-a244-4510-bc9e-baaca2e94393
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Minimalist Internalism](minimalist-internalism-synthesis-c1aff540-8ee4-4dca-921e-260ac4a2c333.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where empirical evidence alone seems sufficient for justification, without any reflective access. Intuitively, such a case provides justification without needing reflection.**, **The necessity of reflective access for justification can be questioned if non-reflective empirical justification appears sufficient.**, **Supposing accessible justification must be reflective, empirical justification challenges this necessity.**
