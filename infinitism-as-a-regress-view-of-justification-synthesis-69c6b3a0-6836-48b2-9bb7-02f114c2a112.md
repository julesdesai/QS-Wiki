# Infinitism as a Regress View of Justification

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their justification includes an infinite series of non-repeating reasons**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there is an infinite chain of justification**
