# Is believing sufficient for knowing if a proposition is true?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2cfcb337-d4ed-4c4a-a628-5a9c4b862d08
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Subject-Sensitive Invariantism](subject-sensitive-invariantism-synthesis-ef027452-1300-45f1-9d7c-023fdb6cd119.md)

## Content

**Suppose John believes a proposition because of wishful thinking, and coincidentally, the proposition is true. Intuitively, merely having a true belief without evidence or justification does not appear to qualify as knowledge.**
