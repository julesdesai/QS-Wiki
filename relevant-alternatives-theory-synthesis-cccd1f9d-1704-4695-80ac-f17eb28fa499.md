## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they can distinguish the proposition from relevant alternatives**, **A relevant alternative is any proposition that could potentially undermine the belief if true**
