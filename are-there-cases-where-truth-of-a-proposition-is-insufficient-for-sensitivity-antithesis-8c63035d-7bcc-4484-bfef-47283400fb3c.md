# Are there cases where truth of a proposition is insufficient for sensitivity?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 8c63035d-7bcc-4484-bfef-47283400fb3c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism with Sensitivity Condition](contextualism-with-sensitivity-condition-synthesis-dd26ba8e-d20d-4379-a4ae-bc06eaa28734.md)

## Content

**Imagine a situation where a person P believes a scientific theory is true based on consensus but lacks awareness of its evidence**, **Even if the theory is true, if P cannot track evidence changes, their belief may not be sensitive**, **A belief that is not informed by relevant evidence lacks sensitivity even if the proposition is true**
