# Knowledge as Virtue Epistemology

**Parent:** [Can knowledge exist without the requirement for non-defeaters?](can-knowledge-exist-without-the-requirement-for-non-defeaters-antithesis-b3b7f044-2b2e-4eca-9021-f76ce5028b9b.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for their belief to result from intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief results from intellectual virtue**
