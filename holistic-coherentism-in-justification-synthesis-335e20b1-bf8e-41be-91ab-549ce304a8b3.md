## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is justified by a coherence of the belief system**, **Testimonial knowledge is justified if the belief coheres with other beliefs already held**
