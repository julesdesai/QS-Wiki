## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is justified by multiple interacting contexts**, **Different layers of context, such as pragmatic and evidential contexts, provide complementary justification**
