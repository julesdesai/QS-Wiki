# Does evolutionary reliability imply epistemic reliability?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 16c90195-f2be-4dcf-8fe0-aa5b70434413
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Evolutionary Foundationalism](evolutionary-foundationalism-synthesis-4b200bf3-94a7-4a07-af03-f4d0ca848734.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a belief that is evolutionarily reliable but epistemically dubious due to cultural biases, such as a stereotype. Intuitively, evolutionary reliability does not automatically confer epistemic reliability suitable for true knowledge.**
