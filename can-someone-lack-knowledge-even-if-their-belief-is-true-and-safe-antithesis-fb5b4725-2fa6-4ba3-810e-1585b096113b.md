# Can someone lack knowledge even if their belief is true and safe?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** fb5b4725-2fa6-4ba3-810e-1585b096113b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Safety Theory of Knowledge](safety-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-92f8baec-50d4-4810-a26f-7675d59c578a.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person believes a proposition based on a lucky guess that turns out to be true. Intuitively, we would judge that the person does not have knowledge.**, **There is a plausible principle that if knowledge depends on luck, it is undermined. A belief being true due to luck challenges its status as knowledge.**
