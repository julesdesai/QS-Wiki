## Content

**Knowledge requires a reliable connection to the truth**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief is safe from error**, **A belief is safe if, in nearby possible situations where the person still believes the proposition, the proposition is true**, **A person does not know a proposition if in nearby situations they would believe it falsely**
