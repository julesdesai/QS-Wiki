# Externalist Justification

**Parent:** [Do practical stakes affect what counts as knowledge?](do-practical-stakes-affect-what-counts-as-knowledge-antithesis-7d096b0b-8208-459a-9a3d-b91b30c80cf0.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, and the proposition is true**, **Justification standards are determined by factors external to the subject's awareness, unaffected by context**
