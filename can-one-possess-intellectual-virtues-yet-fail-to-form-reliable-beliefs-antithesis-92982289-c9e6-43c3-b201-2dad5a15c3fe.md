# Can one possess intellectual virtues yet fail to form reliable beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 92982289-c9e6-43c3-b201-2dad5a15c3fe
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Expanded Virtue Reliabilism](expanded-virtue-reliabilism-synthesis-be1f6636-aeca-4898-8b76-a37fb9c31f3b.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person exhibits intellectual virtues such as open-mindedness and insightful reasoning, but due to misleading evidence, they arrive at a false belief. In such a scenario, intuitively, we would judge that the person's intellectual virtues do not guarantee reliability.**, **There is a plausible principle that possessing intellectual virtues does not ensure that one's beliefs will always be reliably produced.**
