## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief would not be held if the proposition were false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the standards for knowledge adjust with the context affecting the belief formation**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, they would not hold the belief if it were false, and contextual standards are met**
