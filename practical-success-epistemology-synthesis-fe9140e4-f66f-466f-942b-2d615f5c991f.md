# Practical Success Epistemology

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is truth truly necessary for knowledge?](is-truth-truly-necessary-for-knowledge-antithesis-98c60ee0-de4d-4fd1-92e6-2c13d4d29dfa.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person believes the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief arises from intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief leads to successful action**, **An intellectual virtue is a cognitive faculty or character trait contributing to the formation of reliable beliefs**, **Knowledge is primarily about practical success rather than truth**
