## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed by a reliable process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have the proposition be true, and have the belief formed by a reliable process**
