# Can circular reasoning occur in infinite networks of justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 6a1fc948-c45f-43a9-b164-e86bf6122ce6
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherent Infinitism Summary](coherent-infinitism-summary-synthesis-eca6bdeb-868b-4e1e-9a63-23b9880f21ff.md)

## Content

**Infinite networks of interrelated reasons could include loops where a reason is justified by itself indirectly.**, **Circular justification is generally considered fallacious as it does not provide independent support for a belief.**, **An infinite network with circular justification might undermine the credibility of any reason within the network.**
