# Does knowledge truly depend on meta-cognitive skills?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2d1aaa1a-7f03-4b9f-9bff-66d67ab7c243
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Meta-Virtue Epistemology](meta-virtue-epistemology-synthesis-b3bd54fd-8ed8-46f5-92c6-aa5fa4222616.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that knowledge should not rely on cognitive abilities beyond the immediate task of obtaining truth.**, **Suppose a person has true belief but lacks awareness of their own intellectual processes, yet still forms correct beliefs. Intuitively, we consider this person knowledgeable despite the absence of meta-cognitive skills.**
