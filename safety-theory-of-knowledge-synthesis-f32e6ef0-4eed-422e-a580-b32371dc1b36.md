## Content

**A person knows a proposition if the proposition is true and they believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, their belief must not easily have been false under similar circumstances**, **The truth of the proposition does not depend on irrelevant false scenarios**, **Knowledge requires the belief to be formed reliably in similar, nearby situations**
