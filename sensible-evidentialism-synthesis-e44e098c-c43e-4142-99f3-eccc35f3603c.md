# Sensible Evidentialism

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** e44e098c-c43e-4142-99f3-eccc35f3603c
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is having true belief enough for knowledge?](is-having-true-belief-enough-for-knowledge-antithesis-9eddf01d-2a93-4cb9-881b-bce8711f3932.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that sufficient evidence for the belief logically supports the truth of the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have evidence that logically supports the truth, and the proposition is true**
