## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they directly perceive the truth of the proposition**, **Direct perception of reality provides immediate knowledge without extra causal chains**
