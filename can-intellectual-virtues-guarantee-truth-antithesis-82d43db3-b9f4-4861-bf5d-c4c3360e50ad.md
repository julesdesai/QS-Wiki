# Can intellectual virtues guarantee truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 82d43db3-b9f4-4861-bf5d-c4c3360e50ad
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology View](virtue-epistemology-view-synthesis-31f81092-b7b3-48dd-9f17-87af421f7694.md)

## Content

**There are scenarios where individuals possess character traits typically considered intellectual virtues, yet they still hold false beliefs.**, **A character trait can reliably lead to truth in most cases but still result in error due to unpredictable circumstances.**, **Reliability in leading to truth does not imply absolute infallibility in every single instance.**
