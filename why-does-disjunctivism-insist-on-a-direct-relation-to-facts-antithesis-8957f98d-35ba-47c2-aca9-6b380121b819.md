# Why does disjunctivism insist on a direct relation to facts?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 8957f98d-35ba-47c2-aca9-6b380121b819
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Disjunctivism](disjunctivism-synthesis-19882849-157e-4dc6-b31d-66173a7c79c0.md)

## Content

**Suppose we consider the everyday experience of viewing a rainbow. Intuitively, one does not have direct awareness of a real object, yet knowledge of rainbow phenomena is gained.**, **A principle that insists on direct relation forces us to exclude all indirect perceptual knowledge, which can be informative, challenging the direct relation view.**
