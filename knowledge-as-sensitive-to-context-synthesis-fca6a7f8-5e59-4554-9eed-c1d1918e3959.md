**Parent:** [Can a reliable process always guarantee knowledge in novel conditions?](can-a-reliable-process-always-guarantee-knowledge-in-novel-conditions-antithesis-d8349f5f-7ffb-4e52-b8de-3471129c9568.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process that is consistently reliable across varying contexts**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is produced by a cognitive process reliable across varying contexts**
