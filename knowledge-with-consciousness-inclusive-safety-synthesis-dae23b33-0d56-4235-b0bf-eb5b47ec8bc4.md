# Knowledge with Consciousness-Inclusive Safety

**Parent:** [Can knowledge exist without consciousness if conditions other than consciousness provide justification?](can-knowledge-exist-without-consciousness-if-conditions-other-than-consciousness-provide-justification-antithesis-09a1950d-9802-41d0-b35a-298baf014cf4.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is safe from error in nearby possible worlds and includes conscious awareness**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, some conditions other than consciousness provide the justification, and the belief is safe from error with conscious awareness**
