# Knowledge as Safe as True Belief

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 13ed19b9-0799-46d0-94d1-40d3f29b41f8
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Are reliable processes always enough to guarantee knowledge?](are-reliable-processes-always-enough-to-guarantee-knowledge-antithesis-a4eac425-80bb-43ce-a7a5-fa91c9b47282.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief does not easily become false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief does not easily become false**
