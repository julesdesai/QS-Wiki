# Infinitism with Contextual Justification

**Parent:** [Are pragmatic considerations a stable basis for universal knowledge claims?](are-pragmatic-considerations-a-stable-basis-for-universal-knowledge-claims-antithesis-c8d2ca61-050f-4e63-a3c2-5ce4f55c3f0d.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification involves an infinite chain of reasons that are contextually invariant**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and an infinite contextually invariant justification chain exists**
