# Knowledge as True Belief and Intellectual Understanding

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 49270177-c1a2-4842-8fec-7fa18e08496e
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can a person have knowledge without understanding?](can-a-person-have-knowledge-without-understanding-antithesis-9ef17afa-cbf1-4a4d-88fd-9764d488902b.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is a result of the person's intellectual virtues**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is a result of the person's intellectual virtues, and the person has understanding of the proposition**
