# Can contradictory evidence support knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** bc3d359d-0745-4972-9b10-f45be8857317
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Grounding Knowledge in Evidence](grounding-knowledge-in-evidence-synthesis-000c43a5-6d20-4fdc-9093-7ec5ca2a4c8d.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that contradictory evidence can legitimately support a claim to knowledge.**, **Consider a case where a person holds a belief based on mixed evidence, where some evidence points to truth and other evidence points to falsity. In such a case, intuitively we would judge that such contradictory evidence undermines the claim to knowledge.**
