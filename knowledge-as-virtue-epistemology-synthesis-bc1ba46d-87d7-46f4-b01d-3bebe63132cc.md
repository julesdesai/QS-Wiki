## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A person is justified in believing a proposition if and only if the belief arises from intellectual virtue**, **Intellectual virtues are qualities of character that necessarily lead to truth**
