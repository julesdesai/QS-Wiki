# Is there a way to independently verify knowledge across contexts?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3ac82c35-b2e5-4962-ace1-763d44442880
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism with Evidence Requirement](contextualism-with-evidence-requirement-synthesis-8260fba2-b1af-49c2-a4cf-403608671469.md)

## Content

**Imagine trying to establish whether someone knows a proposition across differing contexts with varying standards. Intuitively, it seems difficult to independently verify knowledge this way.**, **It is implausible that knowledge claims can be independently verified when they depend on disparate contextual standards.**
