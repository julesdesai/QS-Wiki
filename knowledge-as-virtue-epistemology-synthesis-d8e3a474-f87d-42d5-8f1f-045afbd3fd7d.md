**Parent:** [Does requiring an infinite, non-repeating chain render knowledge unstable?](does-requiring-an-infinite-non-repeating-chain-render-knowledge-unstable-antithesis-526ded6c-dc88-4a92-9fab-7be9d85c7311.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that belief in the proposition results from the exercise of cognitive virtues**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and belief in the proposition results from the exercise of cognitive virtues**
