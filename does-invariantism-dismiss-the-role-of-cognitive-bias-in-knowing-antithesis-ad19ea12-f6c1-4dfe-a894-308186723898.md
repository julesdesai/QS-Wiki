## Content

**People unknowingly subject to cognitive biases may still have true beliefs. Intuitively, such biases affect whether they 'truly' know.**, **A plausible principle holds that awareness and minimization of bias are crucial for authentic knowledge.**, **If epistemic standards are invariant, biases should not affect knowledge, which seems counterintuitive.**
