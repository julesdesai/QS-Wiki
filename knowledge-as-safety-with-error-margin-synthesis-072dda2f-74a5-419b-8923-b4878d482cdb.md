# Knowledge as Safety with Error Margin

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 072dda2f-74a5-419b-8923-b4878d482cdb
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge be too easily undermined?](can-knowledge-be-too-easily-undermined-antithesis-c6dc11ad-a3b9-4bd7-a666-011a03a26e23.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief could not easily have been false within a reasonable margin of error**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief could not easily have been false within a reasonable margin of error**
