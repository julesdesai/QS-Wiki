# Justified True Belief with No Defeaters

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [How does reliability account for subjective certainty?](how-does-reliability-account-for-subjective-certainty-antithesis-9528bb90-2466-4b7b-a71d-6e0562ba1708.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is justified**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no true propositions that, if added to their evidence, would defeat their belief**
