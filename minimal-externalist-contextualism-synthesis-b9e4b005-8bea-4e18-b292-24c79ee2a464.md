## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief arises from minimal intellectual virtue appropriate to the context**, **Minimal intellectual virtue is defined by only a basic alignment with situational norms without complete conformity**
