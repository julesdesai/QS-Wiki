# Can individuals have knowledge in dream-like states?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** c8a0098a-4628-4d89-85aa-46053f599a13
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Reliabilism](virtue-reliabilism-synthesis-43b268cc-327c-4b25-91ff-4eb7346ac08b.md)

## Content

**In a lucid dream scenario, someone might appear to have knowledge about dream events without holding true beliefs corresponding to the actual world.**, **From an intuitive standpoint, having dream knowledge without real-world true belief questions the necessity of belief as strictly bound to truth.**
