# Does the causal theory account for intuitive cases of knowledge in a demon world?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1edf2af9-7ace-40c1-9ef6-a485a08b178a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Causal Theory of Knowledge](causal-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-cee9090d-2e34-47f2-b4ee-a59054387264.md)

## Content

**Imagine a scenario where an evil demon manipulates a person's beliefs to align with true propositions entirely by chance. Intuitively, we might say the person lacks knowledge because their beliefs are manipulated, not because there is no causal connection.**, **In such scenarios, an individual's beliefs are true and believed, but the causal chain is with the demon, not the truth of the propositions themselves.**
