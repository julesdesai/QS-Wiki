## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their true belief is due to their epistemic virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have true belief due to epistemic virtues**
