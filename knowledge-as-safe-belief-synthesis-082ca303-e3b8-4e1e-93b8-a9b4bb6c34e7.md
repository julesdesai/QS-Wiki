# Knowledge as Safe Belief

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Does tracking require too much about nearby worlds?](does-tracking-require-too-much-about-nearby-worlds-antithesis-69212bb2-6d5d-491b-9f64-1179440896e5.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not easily believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the person believes the proposition, it is true, and the person would not easily believe it if it were false**
