# Can practical stakes alter the truth requirement for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** be5bf54c-0681-4d51-9faa-8a059b9940c8
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Subject-Sensitive Invariantism](subject-sensitive-invariantism-synthesis-ef027452-1300-45f1-9d7c-023fdb6cd119.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person needs to believe in a proposition for a critical life decision, and they do so based on partial evidence. Intuitively, practical stakes should not alter the absolute truth required for knowledge, yet intense stakes could seemingly justify lower standards of truth.**
