## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in similar situations, the belief would not easily be false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and in similar situations the belief would not easily be false**
