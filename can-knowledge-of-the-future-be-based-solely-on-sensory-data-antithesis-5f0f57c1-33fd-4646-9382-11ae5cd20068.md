# Can knowledge of the future be based solely on sensory data?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5f0f57c1-33fd-4646-9382-11ae5cd20068
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Empiricism Summary](empiricism-summary-synthesis-4e15833e-fa6c-416c-a78e-6563a82d7628.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which an individual predicts that the sun will rise tomorrow based on past sensory data. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that this knowledge of the future is not solely from new sensory data but from an inference.**, **Knowledge of future events often relies on inference rather than solely on current sensory data.**
