# Can justified true belief occur without accessing an infinite chain of reasons?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 8902f8ee-6157-4faa-a36f-159ab6944ecb
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology Infinitism](virtue-epistemology-infinitism-synthesis-700b1be0-40ec-4861-9f40-25460b6be3e1.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person has a justified true belief based on strong, albeit finite, evidence.**, **In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that the person knows the proposition.**, **It is implausible that knowledge would require discarding this judgment due to lack of infinite justification.**
