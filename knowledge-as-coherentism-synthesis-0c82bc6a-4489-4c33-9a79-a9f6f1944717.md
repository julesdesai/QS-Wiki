**Parent:** [Is a reliable cognitive process truly necessary for knowledge?](is-a-reliable-cognitive-process-truly-necessary-for-knowledge-antithesis-7937f71e-3066-4e62-aae6-1b211c38080a.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief coheres with the rest of the person's belief system**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the belief coheres with their belief system, and the proposition is true**
