## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have a justified belief that does not rely on any false premises**, **A false premise is a proposition that is believed as part of the justification, yet is not true**
