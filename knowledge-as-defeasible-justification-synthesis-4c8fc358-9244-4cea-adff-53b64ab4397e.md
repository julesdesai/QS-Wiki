# Knowledge as Defeasible Justification

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 4c8fc358-9244-4cea-adff-53b64ab4397e

**Parent:** [Is accurate prediction knowledge?](is-accurate-prediction-knowledge-antithesis-39c2a677-87b6-45bb-b67c-389bfc1356b1.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A belief qualifies as knowledge when it is justified in a way that cannot easily be defeated by further evidence**, **Defeasible justification involves having reasons that remain robust even when contrary evidence arises**
