## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, their belief must be safe**, **A person's belief is safe if it could not easily have been false**, **The safety of a belief requires that in nearly all nearby possible worlds where the person forms the belief in the same way, the belief is true**, **Safety prevents knowledge from depending on luck**
