## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person deserves credit for acquiring the true belief**, **Credit is due when the belief arises from the person's own reliable faculties or processes**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the person deserves credit based on the reliable process used**
