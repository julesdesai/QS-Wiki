## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person is sensitive to their stakes involved in the proposition**, **The standards for knowledge are invariant but sensitive to the subjective importance of the proposition**
