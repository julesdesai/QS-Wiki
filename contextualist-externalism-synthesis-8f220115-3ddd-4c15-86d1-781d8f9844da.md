## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that reliability is judged according to contextual standards and the belief is coherent within specific contexts**, **Belief formation processes should be contextually reliable and coherent within the relevant epistemic context**
