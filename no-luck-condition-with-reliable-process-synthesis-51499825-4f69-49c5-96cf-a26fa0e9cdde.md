# No Luck Condition with Reliable Process

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 51499825-4f69-49c5-96cf-a26fa0e9cdde
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is a belief formed by a reliable process always knowledge?](is-a-belief-formed-by-a-reliable-process-always-knowledge-antithesis-b6c42703-b778-4823-bf8f-1fdc86ab9777.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the truth of the proposition is not due to mere luck**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process, and the truth of the proposition is not due to mere luck**
