# Knowledge as Contextual Validity

**Parent:** [What about cases of deception?](what-about-cases-of-deception-antithesis-d9541818-e4e9-48c5-9762-fd93d81c25b9.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified in accordance with contextual standards of justification**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is justified by contextual standards**
