# Is Evidentialism consistent with beliefs formed in epistemically poor situations?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 85e66650-7322-49c3-b0de-50c624cf177a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Evidentialism Summary](evidentialism-summary-synthesis-a76d832e-447a-4168-b9be-6c867a1a7af1.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a person lives in a society with limited access to reliable information and forms beliefs based on available yet questionable evidence. Intuitively, we either have to say that their beliefs can or cannot be justified under these conditions. If we say they cannot be justified, evidentialism denies justification in unfortunate social circumstances.**
