# Safety-Based Theory of Knowledge

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is sensitivity applicable to testimonial knowledge?](is-sensitivity-applicable-to-testimonial-knowledge-antithesis-c2adc0c7-e014-4989-b15b-52d2eeee9b79.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief could not easily be false in close possible worlds**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is safely held**
