## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that justification is achieved through intellectual virtues guiding an infinite series of reasons**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification is achieved through intellectual virtues navigating an infinite series**
