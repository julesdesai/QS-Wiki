# Invariantism with Justification

**Parent:** [Can context dependence undermine objective truth?](can-context-dependence-undermine-objective-truth-antithesis-80fedd33-8544-4a48-ab7d-b56cd3d9157f.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified by a stable, invariant standard**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is justified by a stable, invariant standard**
