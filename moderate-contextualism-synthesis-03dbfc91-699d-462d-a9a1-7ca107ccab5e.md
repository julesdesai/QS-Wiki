## Content

**Knowledge depends on the context of the knower to a limited extent**, **The standards for knowing a proposition vary slightly with different contexts**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **A person's justification is evaluated based on moderately stable contextual standards**
