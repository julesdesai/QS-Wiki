# Do causal connections ensure infallibility?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 859c9525-e713-41c6-9e94-793a9c1d007d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Causal Theory of Knowledge](causal-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-2f0f13d0-725f-4ae5-ba67-2c976061582b.md)

## Content

**In cases where beliefs are based on causal connections, these connections can still lead to false beliefs if there is misleading information, suggesting fallibility.**, **Consider a situation where a thermometer is broken but accidentally displaying the correct temperature; here, the causal basis doesn't guarantee knowledge.**
