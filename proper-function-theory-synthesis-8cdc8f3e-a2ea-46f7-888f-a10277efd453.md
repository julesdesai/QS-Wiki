## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their cognitive faculties are functioning properly**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their cognitive environment is suitable for the functioning of their faculties**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, their cognitive faculties function properly in an appropriate environment, and the proposition is true**
