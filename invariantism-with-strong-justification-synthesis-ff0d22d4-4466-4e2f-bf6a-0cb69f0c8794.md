# Invariantism with Strong Justification

**Parent:** [Does contextualism undermine the objectivity of knowledge?](does-contextualism-undermine-the-objectivity-of-knowledge-antithesis-f9a0ccba-28f7-4c09-bce5-4c44420d3642.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the justification of the belief to meet strong epistemic standards**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification for the belief meets strong epistemic standards**
