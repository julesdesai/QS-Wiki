# Does the inability to complete an infinite chain negate knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 609143f6-8d63-453d-8b68-91391b374e56
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism and Non-Foundational Justification](infinitism-and-non-foundational-justification-synthesis-6eeef37c-3f39-4c5c-91c9-b90fbd483157.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that a person can consciously hold or access an infinite chain of supporting reasons.**, **Consider a thinker who has finite cognitive resources and time. Intuitively, we would judge that it is not feasible for them to possess knowledge if it requires actualizing an infinite process.**
