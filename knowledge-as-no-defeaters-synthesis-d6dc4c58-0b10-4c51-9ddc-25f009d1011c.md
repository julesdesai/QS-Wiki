# Knowledge as No Defeaters

**Parent:** [Can sensitivity criteria address knowledge formed through epistemic bootstrapping?](can-sensitivity-criteria-address-knowledge-formed-through-epistemic-bootstrapping-antithesis-fd4c4483-1a02-4600-9a10-9bbe532b09f7.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there be no defeaters which, if known, would undermine the belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there are no such defeaters**
