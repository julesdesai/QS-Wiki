# Does embodied cognition provide a complete explanation for knowledge?

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person in a sensory deprivation tank has coherent dreams that align with reality. Intuitively, the knowledge from these dreams does not involve bodily engagement, challenging the completeness of embodied cognition as an explanation.**
