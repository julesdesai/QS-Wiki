## Content

**Knowledge is a true belief that is dependent on the facts themselves in a counterfactual manner**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **The truth of the proposition counterfactually depends on the belief being true; if the truth conditions changed, the belief would change accordingly**
