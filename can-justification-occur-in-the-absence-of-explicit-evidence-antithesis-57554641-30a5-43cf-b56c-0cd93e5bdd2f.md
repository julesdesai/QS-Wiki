# Can justification occur in the absence of explicit evidence?

**Node ID:** 57554641-30a5-43cf-b56c-0cd93e5bdd2f

**Parent:** [Evidentialism](evidentialism-synthesis-fd1a4d8b-5b56-45a2-920f-de86623a4098.md)

## Content

**Suppose an experienced doctor diagnoses a patient based on subtle symptoms that are not evident through tests or standardized evidence. Intuitively, the diagnosis seems justified due to the doctor's expertise.**, **There is a principle suggesting that expertise can justify beliefs in absence of standard evidence, questioning the necessity for explicit evidence alignment for justification.**
