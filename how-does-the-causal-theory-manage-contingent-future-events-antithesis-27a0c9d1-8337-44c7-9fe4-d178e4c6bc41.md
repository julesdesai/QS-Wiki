# How does the causal theory manage contingent future events?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 27a0c9d1-8337-44c7-9fe4-d178e4c6bc41
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Causal Theory of Knowledge](causal-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-336c94a4-e4b0-4a1c-b1db-218c9b8f6add.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person believes it will rain tomorrow due to weather prediction models, yet no current or causal link exists to future rainfall. Intuitively, the person does not know it will rain tomorrow merely because current predictors suggest as much.**, **Knowledge about future contingents lacks a presently existing causal connection, challenging the notion that an appropriate causal link is necessary and sufficient for knowledge.**
