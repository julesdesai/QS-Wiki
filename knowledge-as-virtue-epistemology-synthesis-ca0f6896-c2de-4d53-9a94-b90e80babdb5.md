**Parent:** [Is there a distinction between simply true beliefs and robustly true beliefs for knowledge?](is-there-a-distinction-between-simply-true-beliefs-and-robustly-true-beliefs-for-knowledge-antithesis-1750c5f5-f6b8-4ca8-b598-55ff799b71b5.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed as a result of cognitive abilities or virtues**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is formed as a result of cognitive abilities or virtues**
