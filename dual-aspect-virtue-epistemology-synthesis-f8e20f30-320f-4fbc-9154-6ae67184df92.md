# Dual Aspect Virtue Epistemology

**Parent:** [Can knowledge be undermined by moral failings despite epistemic virtue?](can-knowledge-be-undermined-by-moral-failings-despite-epistemic-virtue-antithesis-58533f4c-cc40-488f-bfd1-5907841c3dcf.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from the coordinated exercise of epistemic virtues and moral virtues**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief arises from the coordinated exercise of epistemic virtues and moral virtues**
