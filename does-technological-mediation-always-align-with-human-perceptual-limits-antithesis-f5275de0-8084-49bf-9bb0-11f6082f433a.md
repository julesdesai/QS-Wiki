# Does technological mediation always align with human perceptual limits?

## Content

**Consider a device that records ultraviolet light, which humans cannot naturally perceive. Intuitively, this mediated perception might not equate to human understanding without translation.**, **It is implausible that all technological enhancements are assimilable within existing human perceptual frameworks.**, **Knowledge might be transitional if technology extends beyond human cognitive assimilation.**
