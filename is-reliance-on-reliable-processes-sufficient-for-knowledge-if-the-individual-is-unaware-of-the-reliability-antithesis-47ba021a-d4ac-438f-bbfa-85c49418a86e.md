# Is reliance on reliable processes sufficient for knowledge if the individual is unaware of the reliability?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 47ba021a-d4ac-438f-bbfa-85c49418a86e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalism with Reliable Processes](externalism-with-reliable-processes-synthesis-89e590de-768c-4370-a7df-27a5b4f5eaa5.md)

## Content

**Suppose someone forms true beliefs consistently using an accurate but complex instrument they cannot personally explain or comprehend.**, **Intuitively, if the individual is oblivious to the instrument’s reliability, it raises doubts about whether they genuinely know what they believe.**, **Lack of awareness about the reliableness strains the link between reliability and knowledge acquisition.**
