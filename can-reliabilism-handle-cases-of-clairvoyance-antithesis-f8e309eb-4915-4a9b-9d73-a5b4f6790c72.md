# Can reliabilism handle cases of clairvoyance?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f8e309eb-4915-4a9b-9d73-a5b4f6790c72
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism](reliabilism-synthesis-5d1929f3-45fe-4f02-bb7b-941a1c5017f0.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person forms a true belief through a reliable clairvoyant process, unbeknownst to them. Intuitively, despite the reliability, belief in clairvoyance without understanding is not genuine knowledge.**, **A plausible principle suggests that unexplained reliability, especially in mystical scenarios, doesn't suffice for knowledge. This conflicts with an account solely based on reliability.**
