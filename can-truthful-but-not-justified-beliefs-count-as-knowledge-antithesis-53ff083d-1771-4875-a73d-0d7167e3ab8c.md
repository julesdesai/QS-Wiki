# Can truthful but not justified beliefs count as knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 53ff083d-1771-4875-a73d-0d7167e3ab8c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilist Knowledge Summary](reliabilist-knowledge-summary-synthesis-4cf24049-6407-4422-bb1c-63a1d2cbefdb.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person forms a belief based on a true horoscope prediction. Intuitively, we would not classify this as knowledge.**, **In such a scenario, the belief is true, but the connection to truth appears coincidental rather than justified through a reliable process.**
