## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed by a reliable process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have internal access to the reasons why the process is reliable**, **A person knows a proposition if they believe it and have justification for the belief**, **A reliable process is one that produces true beliefs more often than false ones**
