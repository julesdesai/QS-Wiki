# Can a reliable belief-forming process produce false beliefs under Process Reliabilism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 4f38d99a-9407-47b7-8825-870deabb910c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Process Reliabilism with Evidence Sensitivity](process-reliabilism-with-evidence-sensitivity-synthesis-e3e15d29-f0bc-4d87-b0aa-fbeab510c604.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a belief is produced by a reliable process, yet the belief is false due to misleading evidence. Intuitively, we would judge that the belief does not constitute knowledge.**, **There is a plausible principle that knowledge requires true beliefs. This principle, together with the belief being false in the scenario, entails that the belief does not constitute knowledge.**
