## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is aimed at truth**, **A belief is aimed at truth if it derives from intellectual abilities that reliably yield true beliefs**
