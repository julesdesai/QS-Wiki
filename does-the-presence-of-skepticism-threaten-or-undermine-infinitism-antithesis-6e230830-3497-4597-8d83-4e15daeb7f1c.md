# Does the presence of skepticism threaten or undermine infinitism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 6e230830-3497-4597-8d83-4e15daeb7f1c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitist Epistemology](infinitist-epistemology-synthesis-6da6e9b6-0ddd-4346-9ef8-d27afad7386e.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which skepticism argues that infinite regress undermines knowledge by rendering it impossible to achieve. Intuitively, we question if knowledge itself is attainable under such skepticism.**, **There is a plausible concern that infinite regress invites skepticism rather than resolves it, as it might suggest no beliefs can be justified enough to count as knowledge.**
