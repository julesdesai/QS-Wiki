# Is it possible to have knowledge without constructing mental representations?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1ccbf570-e89c-44fe-aa24-698a1766fa7f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Epistemic Representationalism](epistemic-representationalism-synthesis-c86e8f4e-5440-4a21-8e04-4337bf022fd8.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person responds correctly to a problem without consciously forming a mental representation. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that the person can have knowledge if their response aligns with reality, despite the absence of mental representation.**
