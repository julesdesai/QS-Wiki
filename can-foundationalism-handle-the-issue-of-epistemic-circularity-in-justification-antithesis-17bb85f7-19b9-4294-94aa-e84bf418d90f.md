# Can foundationalism handle the issue of epistemic circularity in justification?

## Content

**Consider a person who uses a foundational belief to justify itself. Intuitively, this results in a circular form of justification that seems problematic.**, **There is a plausible principle that proper justification should avoid relying on circular reasoning to maintain epistemic integrity.**
