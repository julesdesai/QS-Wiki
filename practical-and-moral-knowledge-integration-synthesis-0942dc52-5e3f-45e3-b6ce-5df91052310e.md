## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for their belief to be shaped by practical reasoning that includes moral reflection**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief arises from a process involving both intellectual evaluation and moral reasoning**
