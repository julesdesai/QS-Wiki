## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A belief qualifies as knowledge if it is produced by a reliable cognitive process that the agent actively endorses**, **Active endorsement requires the agent to understand and monitor the reliability of the process**
