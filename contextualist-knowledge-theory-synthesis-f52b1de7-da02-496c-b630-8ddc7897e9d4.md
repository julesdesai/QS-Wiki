# Contextualist Knowledge Theory

**Parent:** [Can one ever truly attain absolute certainty?](can-one-ever-truly-attain-absolute-certainty-antithesis-9f27664d-6c7f-4a2f-aa9a-1cbc2907d227.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition as true given the context of their situation**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief be reliably formed in the relevant context**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition is true in that context, the proposition is true, and the belief is contextually reliable**
