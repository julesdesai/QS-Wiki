# Knowledge as Nozickian Belief

**Parent:** [Can a belief be justified if based on unreliable sources of information?](can-a-belief-be-justified-if-based-on-unreliable-sources-of-information-antithesis-3b8be787-4dc5-49b7-9cd6-9f34edce4ab0.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, they would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were true, they would believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the person believes the proposition, the proposition is true, if the proposition were false they would not believe it, and if the proposition were true they would believe it**
