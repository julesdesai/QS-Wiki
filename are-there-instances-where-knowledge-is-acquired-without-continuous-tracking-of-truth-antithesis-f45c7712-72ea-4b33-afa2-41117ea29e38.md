# Are there instances where knowledge is acquired without continuous tracking of truth?

**Node ID:** f45c7712-72ea-4b33-afa2-41117ea29e38

## Content

**There are instances where individuals gain knowledge without continuous verification or tracking of the truth in question.**, **A person may come to believe a proposition that they would not believe if false, yet without actively tracking its truth.**, **A person could maintain belief in a true proposition if it were true initially, even without belief tracking during moments when verification is impossible.**
