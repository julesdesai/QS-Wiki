# No-Luck Theory of Knowledge

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is not true merely by luck**, **To eliminate luck, there must be a robust explanatory connection between the belief and its truth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is true and the truth is not the result of luck**
