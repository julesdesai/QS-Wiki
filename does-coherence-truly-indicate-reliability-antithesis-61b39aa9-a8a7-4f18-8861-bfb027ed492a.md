# Does coherence truly indicate reliability?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 61b39aa9-a8a7-4f18-8861-bfb027ed492a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilist Coherentism](reliabilist-coherentism-synthesis-d6c1c642-66ac-40b0-82ca-187cf7250017.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a coherent set of beliefs is derived from a conspiracy theory. Intuitively, despite coherence, we might not judge these beliefs as reliable.**, **It is implausible that coherence alone can confirm the reliability of belief-generating processes without independent evidence of their truth.**
