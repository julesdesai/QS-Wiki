# Can a justified belief that aligns with community consensus still lack truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 636d672a-b727-4cec-a831-7457bb8e7044
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism with Shared Justification](coherentism-with-shared-justification-synthesis-efbed405-50ec-4920-aeba-74abec1bb76e.md)

## Content

**A person can hold a belief justified by community consensus that is nevertheless false.**, **If the truth is required for knowledge, justified beliefs aligned with community consensus that are false cannot constitute knowledge.**
