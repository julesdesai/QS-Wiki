# What about individuals who form beliefs through reliable but non-virtuous means?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 8ed2e593-686c-4440-9bf9-7b34f457821e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-5eebb8d4-de96-4ab7-90ca-fa4ae98a5219.md)

## Content

**Suppose an individual forms beliefs through a reliable cognitive process that lacks intellectual virtue, such as a perfectly functioning but non-reflective AI implant.**, **Intuitively, we would judge that these beliefs can constitute knowledge because of their correctness and reliability, even if they lack intellectual virtue.**
