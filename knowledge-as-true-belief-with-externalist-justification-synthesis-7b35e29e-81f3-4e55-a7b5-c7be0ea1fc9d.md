# Knowledge as True Belief with Externalist Justification

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is justified by external factors beyond the individual's consciousness**, **External factors may include sensory inputs or environmental conditions**
