## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed through a reliable cognitive process**, **A belief formed by a reliable cognitive process is likely to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed by an epistemic virtue**
