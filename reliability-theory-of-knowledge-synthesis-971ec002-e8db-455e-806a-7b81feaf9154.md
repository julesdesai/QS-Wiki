## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, their belief must be produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **A person’s belief must be true for them to have knowledge**, **A person’s belief must be justified for them to have knowledge**, **The reliability of the process must ensure that a person’s belief is unlikely to be false**
