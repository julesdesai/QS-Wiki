## Content

**Imagine a reliable belief-forming process that leads to a true belief by sheer reliability, as opposed to virtue of reason or evidence. Intuitively, the intrinsic value of knowledge seems diminished if it just results from reliable process use alone.**, **There is a plausible principle that the value of knowledge is higher than a mere accumulation of true beliefs, suggesting a deficiency in reliability alone attributing value.**
