# What if collective perceptions are manipulated or fallible?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** bad66531-5efc-4433-8062-63b36600dabe
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Inter-subjective Phenomenalism](inter-subjective-phenomenalism-synthesis-367c4cba-19af-4964-9bd7-aba768d0b0b6.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where collective perceptions are influenced by a social or cultural bias. In such a scenario, it is implausible to claim that knowledge confirmed by these perceptions is purely objective or reliable.**, **There is a plausible principle that knowledge must remain objective and unbiased. If shared perceptions are skewed by external factors, this undermines their role in confirming knowledge.**
