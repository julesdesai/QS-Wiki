# The Safety Theory of Knowledge

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in nearby possible worlds where the person believes the proposition, the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that belief does not rely on coincidental alignment with truth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that these safety conditions hold**
