# Is knowledge merely true belief without further requirements?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1446d7fd-9738-41c7-89f9-b64701830b7e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism with Finite Justifications](infinitism-with-finite-justifications-synthesis-69ad7e03-7f20-4a4f-85ef-ebc999d85fe8.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a person believes in a proposition simply because it is true. Intuitively, we would not consider this to be knowledge without some form of justification.**, **It is implausible that merely believing a true proposition qualifies as knowledge without a justification component.**
