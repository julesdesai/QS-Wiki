# Knowledge as Process Dependence with Constraint Mechanism

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Could a reliable process yield conflicting beliefs?](could-a-reliable-process-yield-conflicting-beliefs-antithesis-83f477ee-97d0-4538-8c96-62ca96fee31e.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed by processes constrained to prevent contradictions**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is formed through constrained reliable processes preventing conflicting beliefs**
