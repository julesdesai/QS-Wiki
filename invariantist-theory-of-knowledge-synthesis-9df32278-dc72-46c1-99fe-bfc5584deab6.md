# Invariantist Theory of Knowledge

**Parent:** [Can objective truth be contingent upon conversational context?](can-objective-truth-be-contingent-upon-conversational-context-antithesis-ac7d0061-c10c-493c-aa66-51603b024af1.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief to result from a reliable belief-forming process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief results from a reliable belief-forming process**
