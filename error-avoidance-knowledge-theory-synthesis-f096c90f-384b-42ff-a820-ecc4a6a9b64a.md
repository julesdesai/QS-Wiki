## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed in a way that avoids error**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person is consistently able to avoid false beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that these error-avoidance conditions hold**
