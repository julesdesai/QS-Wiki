# Virtue Epistemology as a Theory of Knowledge

**Parent:** [Does capturing rare events through causation constitute knowledge?](does-capturing-rare-events-through-causation-constitute-knowledge-antithesis-d291f9e0-d923-405b-bf8c-641557eade7e.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is a result of exercising intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief results from exercising intellectual virtue**
