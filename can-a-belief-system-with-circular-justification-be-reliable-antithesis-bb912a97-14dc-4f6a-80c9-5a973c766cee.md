# Can a belief system with circular justification be reliable?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** bb912a97-14dc-4f6a-80c9-5a973c766cee
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilist Coherentism](reliabilist-coherentism-synthesis-2c1f8a9c-d9de-4c66-ad9a-56c41dc7c349.md)

## Content

**A coherent belief system can rely on circular reasoning, which intuitively seems problematic for providing genuine justification.**, **A process that produces a coherent system can be systematically incorrect, suggesting that reliability based purely on coherence may be flawed.**
