# Does coherence account for the reliability of the belief formation process?

**Node ID:** 345e06bf-8db6-4c78-88c3-55dd2adc676f
**Depth:** 4

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Coherence Theory of Knowledge](pragmatic-coherence-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-a3c73806-e84d-41ba-8091-ad822ea4b799.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person forms a belief based on an unreliable cognitive process, yet the belief coheres with their other beliefs.**, **In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that the person does not have knowledge, as the process is unreliable regardless of coherence.**
