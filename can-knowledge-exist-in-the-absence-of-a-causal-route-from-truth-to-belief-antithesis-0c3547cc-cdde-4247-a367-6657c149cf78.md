# Can knowledge exist in the absence of a causal route from truth to belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0c3547cc-cdde-4247-a367-6657c149cf78
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Causal Theory of Knowledge](causal-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-916d423a-1723-4911-b4ab-5dfda1d6916a.md)

## Content

**Consider the case where Leslie foresees an event, such as a solar eclipse, based on a hunch rather than astronomical data, and the eclipse occurs. The absence of a causal route from the event to her belief makes it implausible she has knowledge of the eclipse, despite the correctness of her belief.**
