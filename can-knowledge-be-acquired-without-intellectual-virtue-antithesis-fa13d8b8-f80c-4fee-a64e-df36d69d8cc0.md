# Can knowledge be acquired without intellectual virtue?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** fa13d8b8-f80c-4fee-a64e-df36d69d8cc0
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-5dae303e-e392-4dd3-a9fb-c518398dc86e.md)

## Content

**A case in which a person forms a true belief due to luck, without any intellectual effort or careful reflection, challenges the necessity of intellectual virtue for knowledge.**, **In this scenario, intuitively, we might still consider the person to have knowledge, despite the absence of intellectual virtue.**
