# Is it possible for someone to have evidence and still not know?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a85cd6de-1987-4bde-b578-f8c26d2a18dc
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism with Evidence Requirement](contextualism-with-evidence-requirement-synthesis-8260fba2-b1af-49c2-a4cf-403608671469.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a person has all available evidence supporting a true proposition, but the proposition is actually false due to an unforeseen factor. Intuitively, it seems wrong to claim they knew the proposition.**, **A scenario could involve a person having all evidence for a true proposition, yet environmental factors render the proposition false, suggesting knowledge does not simply align with collected evidence.**
