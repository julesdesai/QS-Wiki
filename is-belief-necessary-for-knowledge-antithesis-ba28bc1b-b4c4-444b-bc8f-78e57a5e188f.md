# Is belief necessary for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ba28bc1b-b4c4-444b-bc8f-78e57a5e188f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalist Foundationalism](externalist-foundationalism-synthesis-3e0224db-6723-4288-8a1b-8819cbac7570.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person has stored information that is true and has external justification, like facts learned by rote memorization (e.g., capitals of countries), but does not actively hold these in belief. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that the person lacks knowledge despite having justified true propositions if they do not actively believe them.**
