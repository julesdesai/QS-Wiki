# Knowledge as Reliable Belief Plus Reflective Endorsement

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 11a7765f-90dc-4b1a-a3f4-d77475d24748
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does knowledge require conscious awareness of reliability?](does-knowledge-require-conscious-awareness-of-reliability-antithesis-498c3262-41ff-40ba-9e1c-a678d02828a5.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from a reliable process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they reflectively endorse the reliability or justification of their belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief arises from a reliable process, and they reflectively endorse the reliability or justification**
