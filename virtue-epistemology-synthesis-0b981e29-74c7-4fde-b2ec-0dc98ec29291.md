# Virtue Epistemology

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that belief in the proposition is a result of intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the person believes the proposition, it is true, and the belief results from intellectual virtue**
