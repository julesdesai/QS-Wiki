# Does context dependency make knowledge subjective rather than objective?

**Node ID:** b6c1f115-2432-4308-85cc-3dcbc7568489

**Parent:** [Perceptual Contextualism Summary](perceptual-contextualism-summary-synthesis-b9fb6be1-a4e1-46bf-91f2-7fc63c3abb0c.md)

## Content

**Perceptions can be deeply influenced by individual backgrounds, cultures, and personal experiences.**, **If knowledge is based on perceptions that vary with personal context, what is considered known may differ wildly between individuals.**, **Subjectivity introduced by context is a threat to the notion of objective knowledge.**
