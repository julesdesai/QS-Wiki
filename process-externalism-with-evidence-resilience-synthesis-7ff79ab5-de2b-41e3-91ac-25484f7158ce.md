# Process-Externalism with Evidence Resilience

**Parent:** [Can knowledge withstand future defeaters?](can-knowledge-withstand-future-defeaters-antithesis-abb05472-a192-4ada-a4e4-21b428b7d702.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is the result of a cognitive process that is resilient to new evidence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is the result of a cognitive process that remains resilient when exposed to future evidence**
