## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the reliability of the cognitive process is evaluated within a context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that a contextually appropriate reliable process leads to a true belief**
