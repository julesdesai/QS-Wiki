## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, the proposition must be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is not necessary for it to be coherent with other beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have non-inferential justification for it**
