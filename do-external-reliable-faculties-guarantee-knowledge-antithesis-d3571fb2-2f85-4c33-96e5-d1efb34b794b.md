# Do external reliable faculties guarantee knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d3571fb2-2f85-4c33-96e5-d1efb34b794b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-a1d061ab-4ce5-4544-95e5-a72c751db8d0.md)

## Content

**Consider an individual who trusts a highly reliable, external cognitive accomplice or device for forming beliefs, like a truth-indicating machine.**, **The intuition is that reliance on external devices, even if reliable, may not suffice for the individual's knowledge as it relies on something extrinsic, not one's own cognitive faculties.**
