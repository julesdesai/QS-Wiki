## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that in the context, the standards for knowledge are met**, **The standards for knowledge can vary depending on the conversational context and stakes involved**
