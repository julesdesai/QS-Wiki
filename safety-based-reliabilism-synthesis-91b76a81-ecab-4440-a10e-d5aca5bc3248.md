## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief in the proposition could not easily have been false**, **For a person to know a proposition, what counts as justification is determined by the reliability of the process used to arrive at the belief**
