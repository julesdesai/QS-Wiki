# Are there limits to foundational evidence?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e308f4ff-49d7-462f-86aa-1437ab6bf440
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Evidentialist Foundationalism](evidentialist-foundationalism-synthesis-8074c848-6a3d-411d-b5c9-e6a07bd8fe19.md)

## Content

**Empirical evidence, such as sensory experience, has inherent limitations and is often fallible.**, **Sensory experiences can vary across different perceivers, leading to inconsistent foundational beliefs.**, **The variability and limitations of sensory evidence question its role as a reliable foundation for all knowledge.**
