# Can an implicit falsehood challenge explicit truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1af31056-788c-4b60-a305-4a4bd0cf2f69
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [No False Grounds Theory](no-false-grounds-theory-synthesis-37dfac32-afee-4ea8-9a67-23e7efbc2507.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that implicit falsehoods cannot undermine the explicit truth of a belief.**, **Consider a scenario in which a person holds a true belief because of their generally accurate intuition, but their reasoning involves an implicit falsehood. Intuitively, even implicit errors can threaten the stability of truth.**
