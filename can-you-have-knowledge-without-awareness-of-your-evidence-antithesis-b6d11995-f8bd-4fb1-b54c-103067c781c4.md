# Can you have knowledge without awareness of your evidence?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** b6d11995-f8bd-4fb1-b54c-103067c781c4
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Evidentialist Reliabilism Summary](evidentialist-reliabilism-summary-synthesis-f38be777-625f-41ad-8d84-ebe55a9ee5fa.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person forms a true belief about the weather tomorrow because they have been reliably informed by a meteorologist, but they have no awareness of the evidence underlying this belief. Intuitively, we might judge that this person knows the weather tomorrow despite lacking awareness of their evidence.**, **It seems possible for a person to have knowledge without possessing awareness of specific evidence that makes their belief reliable.**
