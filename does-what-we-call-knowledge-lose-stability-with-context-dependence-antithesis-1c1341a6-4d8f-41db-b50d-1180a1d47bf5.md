# Does what we call "knowledge" lose stability with context dependence?

## Content

**Consider a world in which the word "knowledge" applies variably depending on fluctuating standards and contexts. Intuitively, this suggests an instability in what we understand "knowledge" to be.**, **The variability in standards for knowledge depending on context leads to an implausibly unstable conceptual use of the term "knowledge."**
