# How can an infinite regress of justifications be practically maintained?

**Parent:** [Infinitist Coherentism](infinitist-coherentism-synthesis-a30e3b29-79f2-4d20-a375-1e170fe170c4.md)

## Content

**If each belief requires another belief for its justification, then the series of beliefs must continue indefinitely.**, **Humans have limited cognitive capacities, suggesting that maintaining an endless chain of reasons is impractical.**, **An impractical theoretical framework challenges its applicability to real-world instances of knowledge acquisition.**
