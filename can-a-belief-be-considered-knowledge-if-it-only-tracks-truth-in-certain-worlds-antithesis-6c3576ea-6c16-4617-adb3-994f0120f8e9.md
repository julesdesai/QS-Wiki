# Can a belief be considered knowledge if it only tracks truth in certain worlds?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 6c3576ea-6c16-4617-adb3-994f0120f8e9
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Truth-Tracking View](truth-tracking-view-synthesis-ffb314b5-26cd-4e37-96a0-e81b3c75b895.md)

## Content

**Suppose there is a case where a person’s belief accurately tracks truth in some possible worlds but not in others. Intuitively, one might feel that such selective tracking undermines the requirement for belief to track truth in all possible worlds for it to qualify as knowledge.**, **It is implausible to demand that a belief must track truth across all possible worlds when some worlds are irrelevant or radically different from our own.**
