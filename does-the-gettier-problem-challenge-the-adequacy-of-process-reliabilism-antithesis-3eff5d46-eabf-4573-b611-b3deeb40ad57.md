# Does the Gettier problem challenge the adequacy of Process Reliabilism?

**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person, Smith, believes that the person who will get the job has ten coins in their pocket because the boss told him this, and his friend, Jones, has ten coins in his pocket and is the expected candidate. Unknown to Smith, he himself has ten coins in his pocket and will get the job. Intuitively, Smith has a justified true belief but not knowledge.**
