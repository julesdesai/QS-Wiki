# Is perception inherently subjective even when mediated by technology?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2a6b2cf8-8288-442e-bd47-f51b85f3ebbf
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Indirect Realism with Acknowledgment of Mediation](indirect-realism-with-acknowledgment-of-mediation-synthesis-e56d95ce-eeee-446c-93a6-a21b3a4bd4d7.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which two individuals use identical instruments but report different perceptions. Intuitively, this suggests subjective interpretation of mediated data.**, **It is implausible that reliable instruments eradicate all subjective biases inherent in perception.**, **A principle that states perception reflects objective reality falters if subjective differences persist despite consistent mediation.**
