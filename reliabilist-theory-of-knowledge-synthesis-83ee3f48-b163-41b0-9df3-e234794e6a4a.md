## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have a reliable process for arriving at the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **A person knows a proposition if and only if the proposition is true, they believe it, and they arrived at it via a reliable process**
