# Can testimony about perceptual experiences be genuinely accurate?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1b523642-8c1c-4c55-baa2-fe651f5849d5
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Testimonial Phenomenalism](testimonial-phenomenalism-synthesis-3205e188-94ee-499a-b528-9152e3586bc6.md)

## Content

**A situation exists where a person describes the taste of an exotic fruit that others have never tasted and base their knowledge on this description. Intuitively, the testimony offers an incomplete understanding of the fruit's taste.**, **Imagine a case where someone testifies about the appearance of a foreign landscape, yet others lack the sensory experience to confirm this account. Intuitively, the knowledge conveyed by such testimony is limited.**
