**Parent:** [Does relying on evidence always imply access to that evidence?](does-relying-on-evidence-always-imply-access-to-that-evidence-antithesis-4192957a-81e7-4eca-b81a-10cf7c654076.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have an entitlement to assume the existence of evidence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is not necessary for the person to be aware of specific evidence justifying their belief**
