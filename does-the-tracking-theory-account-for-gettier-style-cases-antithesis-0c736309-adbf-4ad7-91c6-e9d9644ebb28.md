# Does the tracking theory account for Gettier-style cases?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0c736309-adbf-4ad7-91c6-e9d9644ebb28
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Nozick's Tracking Theory](nozicks-tracking-theory-synthesis-92e582e4-32a5-4017-b62b-ea7485c8afb8.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a person believes a true proposition based on reasoning involving a false belief, as in Gettier cases. Intuitively, we would not say the person has knowledge.**, **A belief in a Gettier case can track the truth because in nearby possible worlds, if the proposition were not true, the belief might disappear even though it remains unjustified.**, **There is a plausible principle that knowledge must exclude luck, yet tracking can be influenced by luck in Gettier cases.**
