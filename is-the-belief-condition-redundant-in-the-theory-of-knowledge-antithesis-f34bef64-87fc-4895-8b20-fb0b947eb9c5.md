# Is the belief condition redundant in the theory of knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f34bef64-87fc-4895-8b20-fb0b947eb9c5
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Tracking Theory of Knowledge](tracking-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-6d302593-f2de-4e10-8195-85a314e81400.md)

## Content

**If a person knows a proposition, then they must believe it.**, **Some argue that knowledge implies belief as a matter of logic or definition, thus the belief condition adds nothing informative to the concept of knowledge.**, **To include belief as a separate condition in defining knowledge could be seen as redundant.**
