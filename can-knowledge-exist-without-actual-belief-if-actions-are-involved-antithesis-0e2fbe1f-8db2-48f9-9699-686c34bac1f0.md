# Can knowledge exist without actual belief if actions are involved?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 0e2fbe1f-8db2-48f9-9699-686c34bac1f0
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism with Dispositional Belief](contextualism-with-dispositional-belief-synthesis-1dbb9d87-3561-45d5-9c36-839374bee7ec.md)

## Content

**Consider a person who always locks the door when leaving the house out of habit but does not consciously think about believing it's necessary to lock the door.**, **It seems implausible that we would say this person knows the importance of locking the door if they have no awareness or belief regarding its necessity.**
