## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the person believes the proposition, it could not easily be false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have proper evidential support for their belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, it could not easily be false if they believe it, and they have proper evidential support**
