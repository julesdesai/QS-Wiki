## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have good reasons for believing the proposition**, **Good reasons are those that are objectively likely to lead to truth**
