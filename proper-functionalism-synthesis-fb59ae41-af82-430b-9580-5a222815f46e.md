## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their cognitive faculties are functioning properly**, **Proper functioning requires that cognitive faculties are aimed at truth and are in an appropriate environment**
