# Knowledge as Relevant Alternatives

**Parent:** [Does sensitivity to truth guarantee knowledge in cases of lucky guesses?](does-sensitivity-to-truth-guarantee-knowledge-in-cases-of-lucky-guesses-antithesis-7e03ce67-ae38-415b-8498-4b843e04a641.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they can rule out relevant alternatives to the proposition being true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and they can rule out relevant alternatives to the proposition being true**
