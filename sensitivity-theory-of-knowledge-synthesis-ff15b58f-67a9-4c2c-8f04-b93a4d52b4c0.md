# Sensitivity Theory of Knowledge

**Parent:** [Does mistaken belief in a nearby world undermine knowledge in the actual world?](does-mistaken-belief-in-a-nearby-world-undermine-knowledge-in-the-actual-world-antithesis-a5e53c7e-f556-4c70-a518-9383ed13f908.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a proposition to be known, if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **The person's belief must be sensitive to the truth of the proposition**
