# Does contextual coherentism fail with basic beliefs?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7721c784-d9bc-42b4-921f-85d6fc8ff4ea
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualist Coherentism](contextualist-coherentism-synthesis-2211d0a5-4c05-4516-984a-e004555aaa45.md)

## Content

**A belief is justified if it coheres with other beliefs relevant to the context.**, **Basic beliefs, if foundational, might not need coherence with other beliefs to be justified.**, **Contextual coherentism struggles to accommodate the independence of such basic beliefs.**
