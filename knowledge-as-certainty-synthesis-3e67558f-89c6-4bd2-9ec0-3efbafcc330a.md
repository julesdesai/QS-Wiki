## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for there to be no logical room for doubt**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that belief, truth, and certainty are met**
