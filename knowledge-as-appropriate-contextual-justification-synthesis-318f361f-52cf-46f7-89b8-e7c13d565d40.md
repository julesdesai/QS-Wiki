# Knowledge as Appropriate Contextual Justification

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Can beliefs formed under external manipulation still be considered knowledge?](can-beliefs-formed-under-external-manipulation-still-be-considered-knowledge-antithesis-11a082fc-9707-4c83-b316-a032b44d0ee7.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person is justified in believing the proposition within the relevant context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and they are justified in believing the proposition within the relevant context**
