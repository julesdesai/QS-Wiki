## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is the result of intellectual virtue**, **An intellectual virtue is a reliable cognitive disposition**, **A belief resulting from intellectual virtue can still be considered knowledge even if it avoids epistemic luck through reliable disposition**
