# Can perceptual illusions deceive reliable processes?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 629dcb34-dc45-44ff-9a41-897b86285112
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalist View](externalist-view-synthesis-75028f40-4ef7-4feb-a21d-1e6a75d787a6.md)

## Content

**A belief formed through a reliable process might still be influenced by perceptual illusions, leading to true belief by coincidence. Intuitively, one would question if this constitutes knowledge.**, **Reliability must account for cases of sensory misinterpretation or distortion.**
