# Can knowledge exist without cognitive achievement?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 5a1c1714-456f-4fbc-9cad-4d3a1a3dcde5
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-35af9163-7530-4447-9474-ccb688222939.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a person forms a true belief through repeated practice and dedication, leading to a cognitive achievement. Intuitively, we would say that this suggests knowledge requires cognitive achievement.**, **The scenario where someone forms a true belief solely by chance without cognitive achievement challenges the notion that knowledge affirms this principle.**
