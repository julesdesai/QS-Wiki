# Single-Standard Contextualism

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 44bdef91-a853-41f5-92a3-96296beca40e
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is belief alone sufficient for knowledge?](is-belief-alone-sufficient-for-knowledge-antithesis-5b3528ef-abfb-46de-80b8-72b1fd14c3bb.md)

## Content

**Knowledge depends on the context of the knower**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **The contextual standards for belief, truth, and justification are fixed for the context**
