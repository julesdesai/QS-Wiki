# Evidentialist Theory of Knowledge

**Parent:** [Is Experience Insufficient for Sensitivity?](is-experience-insufficient-for-sensitivity-antithesis-9ec50295-c555-455b-8fe2-9b0758e1eea7.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have sufficient evidence for the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and have sufficient evidence**
