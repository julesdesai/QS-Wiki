## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief to arise from the person's cognitive abilities or virtues**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is formed through the exercise of cognitive virtues**
