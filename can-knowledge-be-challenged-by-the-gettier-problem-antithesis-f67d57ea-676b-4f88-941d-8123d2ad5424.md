# Can knowledge be challenged by the Gettier problem?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f67d57ea-676b-4f88-941d-8123d2ad5424
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Knowledge First Epistemology](knowledge-first-epistemology-synthesis-5ffe7f85-d642-4212-8020-2dc118f603c3.md)

## Content

**In a scenario where a person believes a proposition based on justified true belief derived from misleading evidence, we intuitively hesitate to call it knowledge.**, **This intuition suggests that knowledge might not solely depend on justification, truth, and belief, which challenges knowledge being a fundamental epistemic concept.**
