# Does knowledge entail more than mere true belief?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3fe85f1e-1b60-451e-b32b-0e97baf3a2a8
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology Approach](virtue-epistemology-approach-synthesis-b14f243d-0108-46b0-abbb-b26bd31de791.md)

## Content

**Consider a case where a belief is true and formed through reliable methods but is incidental to the person's understanding. Intuitively, without an understanding aspect, it feels inadequate to claim it as knowledge.**, **A plausible principle in epistemology is that knowledge demands understanding beyond mere accuracy of belief.**
