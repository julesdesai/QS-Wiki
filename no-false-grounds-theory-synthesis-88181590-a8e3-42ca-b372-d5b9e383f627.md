# No False Grounds Theory

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that none of the grounds for the belief are false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and all grounds for the belief are true**
