# Can two identical processes yield different knowledge outcomes based on reliability?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 955a26d5-0023-4bda-989c-ddc56975248a
**Depth:** 2
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism](reliabilism-thesis-bbb40df2-3662-448b-8937-01052488b184.md)

## Content

**Suppose two people have the same process for belief, but one is in a world where the process is reliable and the other where it isn't**, **The person in the unreliable world may lack knowledge while the other has it, posing a challenge to assessing reliability**
