# Can knowledge be justified given assumptions of transitory truths?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2fba1d51-63b8-4e69-83a8-c9c26d0324b9
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Knowledge as Safety](knowledge-as-safety-synthesis-32d99a7a-9598-4d4c-84b2-a8ab3d13c5e9.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person believes it is noon because they checked a clock and it indeed is noon, but the clock is known to be faulty during certain hours. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that the person does not have knowledge due to transitory truths not reliably supporting the belief.**, **It is plausible that transitory truths are unstable and undermine the safety condition needed for knowledge.**
