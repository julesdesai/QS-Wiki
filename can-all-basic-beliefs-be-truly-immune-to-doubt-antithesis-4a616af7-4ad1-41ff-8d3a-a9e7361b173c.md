# Can all basic beliefs be truly immune to doubt?

## Content

**Consider a case where a person has a belief that seems basic but is influenced by cultural norms. Intuitively, such a belief seems questionable as basic.**, **It is implausible that all basic beliefs are immune to doubt considering psychological influences like bias and upbringing.**, **Suppose we have a scenario in which a person believes a proposition without evidence or reason due to tradition. This suggests it may not be genuinely basic since it's shaped by external factors.**
