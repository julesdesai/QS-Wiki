## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that belief formation involves reliable cognitive processes**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, it fits within a coherent system of beliefs, and it is formed by reliable processes**
