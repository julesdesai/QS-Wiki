# Infinitism with Inter-subjective Verification

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Can practical considerations justify any arbitrary chain of reasoning?](can-practical-considerations-justify-any-arbitrary-chain-of-reasoning-antithesis-6f397664-c51b-4676-b20d-3ecbcc44e2a2.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification involves an infinite chain of reasons verified by others**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the infinite justification chain is verified by others**
