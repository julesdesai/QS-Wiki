## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief has origins in an acquaintance with the justification**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the acquaintance played a formative role in the development of the belief**
