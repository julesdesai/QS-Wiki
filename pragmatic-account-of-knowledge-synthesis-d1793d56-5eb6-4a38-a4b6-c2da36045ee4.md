## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief be formed through a process that is reliable in the relevant context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is true, believed, and formed through a reliable context-sensitive process**
