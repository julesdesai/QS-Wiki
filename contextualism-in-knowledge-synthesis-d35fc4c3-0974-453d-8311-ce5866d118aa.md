## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the standards for justification are met within the relevant context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person's belief is not undermined by contextually relevant factors**
