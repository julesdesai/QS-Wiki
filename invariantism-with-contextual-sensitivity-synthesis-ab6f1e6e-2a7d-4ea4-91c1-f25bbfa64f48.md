**The standards for knowing a proposition are invariant in a strict sense but the interpretation of what those standards demand can be sensitive to context**, **A person knows a proposition if they believe it, it is true, and it meets invariant standards, interpreted appropriately for the context**, **Criterion application varies but the standards themselves remain fixed across contexts**
