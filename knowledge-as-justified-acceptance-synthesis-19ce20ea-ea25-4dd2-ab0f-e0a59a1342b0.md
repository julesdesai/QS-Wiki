# Knowledge as Justified Acceptance

**Parent:** [Are there cases in which acceptance is insufficient for knowledge?](are-there-cases-in-which-acceptance-is-insufficient-for-knowledge-antithesis-3c4d036e-39c9-4f83-9677-42e8290df6d5.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they accept the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the acceptance has practical consequences or usefulness**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the acceptance is justified by evidence or reasoning**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they accept the proposition, the proposition is true, the acceptance has practical consequences or usefulness, and the acceptance is justified**
