# Knowledge as Externalist Justification with Internal Assurance

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** fedaceee-87a3-4d80-8b6c-1f6f750b56b3
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can external justifications adequately address higher-order doubts?](can-external-justifications-adequately-address-higher-order-doubts-antithesis-bee89207-70bf-49cc-ada9-44fe65afedb5.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is both external justification and the individual has internal assurance of this justification**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there is external justification accompanied by internal assurance**
