# Are all true beliefs produced by reliable processes considered knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 804ff4d9-670d-4a18-bd8a-cbc78fb0c9e4
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Causal Theory of Knowledge](causal-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-4b0c5095-8474-46c5-ad2b-26df0561fddc.md)

## Content

**Consider a person who, by long-standing habit, believes in certain superstitions that incidentally prove true due to unrelated reliable processes.**, **Though the superstitions result in true beliefs, attributing them as knowledge feels incorrect if their basis is disconnected from reason or evidence.**
