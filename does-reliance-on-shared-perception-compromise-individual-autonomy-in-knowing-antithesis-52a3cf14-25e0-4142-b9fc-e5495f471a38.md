# Does reliance on shared perception compromise individual autonomy in knowing?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 52a3cf14-25e0-4142-b9fc-e5495f471a38
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Inter-subjective Phenomenalism](inter-subjective-phenomenalism-synthesis-c00af758-5073-4931-9585-2f560dddd897.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where a person independently sees a new star through a telescope. In such a scenario, the intuition is that the individual can autonomously know about the star, regardless of others' perceptions.**, **Requiring that others verify the experience undermines the autonomy of personal knowledge acquisition, suggesting a flaw in requiring inter-subjective agreement.**
