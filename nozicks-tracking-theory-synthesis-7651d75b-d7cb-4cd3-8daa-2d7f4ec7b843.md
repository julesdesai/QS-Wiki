## Content

**A person knows a proposition if they have a true belief**, **A person knows a proposition if their belief tracks the truth**, **A belief tracks the truth if, in the nearest possible world where the proposition is true, they believe it**, **A belief tracks the truth if, in the nearest possible world where the proposition is false, they do not believe it**
