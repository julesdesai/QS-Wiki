## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief be based on foundational beliefs**, **Foundational beliefs must provide a causal basis for the belief system**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is causally linked to foundational beliefs**
