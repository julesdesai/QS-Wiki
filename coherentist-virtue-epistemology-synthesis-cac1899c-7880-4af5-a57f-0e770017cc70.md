# Coherentist Virtue Epistemology

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Is it possible to have conflicting virtues?](is-it-possible-to-have-conflicting-virtues-antithesis-fc12be6b-fdbe-4588-8d77-c4aeaa91f561.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief to result from a coherent set of intellectual virtues**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief results from a coherent set of intellectual virtues**
