## Content

**Knowledge depends on the context of the knower**, **A person knows a proposition if they believe it**, **A person knows a proposition if the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **The variation in contextual standards for either belief, truth, or justification is limited to maintain a common core of knowledge definition across different contexts**
