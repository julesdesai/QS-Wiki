## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification meets the standards of the relevant social context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the belief is true and justified according to contextual standards**
