# Knowledge as Justified True Belief

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can knowledge be explained purely through true belief without considering justification?](can-knowledge-be-explained-purely-through-true-belief-without-considering-justification-antithesis-235cfc43-46c6-46d6-8898-906efad1f0c4.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **True belief contributes to knowledge when accompanied by justification**
