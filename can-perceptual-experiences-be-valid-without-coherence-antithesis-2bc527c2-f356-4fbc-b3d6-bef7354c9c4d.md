# Can perceptual experiences be valid without coherence?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2bc527c2-f356-4fbc-b3d6-bef7354c9c4d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism of Perception Summary](coherentism-of-perception-summary-synthesis-d17cb137-1bd2-4a1a-92c0-2f372bf13eee.md)

## Content

**A case can be imagined where a perceptual experience is justified and provides knowledge independently, regardless of its coherence with other beliefs.**, **An example of this is seeing a tree ahead, which is the first and only perceptual experience one has, yet intuitively provides direct knowledge of the tree's existence.**
