## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no defeaters for their belief**, **A defeater is any true proposition that would undermine their justification for the belief if known**
