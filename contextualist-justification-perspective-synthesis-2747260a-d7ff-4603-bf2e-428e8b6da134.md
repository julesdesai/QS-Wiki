## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification for their belief depends on the context in which the knowledge claim is made**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification aligns with the contextual standards of the situation**
