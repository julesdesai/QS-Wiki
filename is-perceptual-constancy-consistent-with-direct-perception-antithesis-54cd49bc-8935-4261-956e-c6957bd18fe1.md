# Is perceptual constancy consistent with direct perception?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 54cd49bc-8935-4261-956e-c6957bd18fe1
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Disjunctivism](disjunctivism-synthesis-15b36dc9-362d-489d-8156-bddc0dd6b522.md)

## Content

**Consider the phenomenon of perceptual constancy where an object's color or size is perceived the same despite changes in lighting or distance. Intuitively, this suggests some cognitive processing beyond direct perception.**, **Perceptual constancy indicates that perception involves cognitive processes that maintain consistent views, contradicting the straightforward division between reality and appearance.**
