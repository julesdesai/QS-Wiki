## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary to consider the context in which the belief is held**, **Intuition can count as a valid basis for knowledge in contexts where intuitive belief aligns with truth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that within the context, the belief is true and appropriately justified, be it through evidence or reliable intuition**
