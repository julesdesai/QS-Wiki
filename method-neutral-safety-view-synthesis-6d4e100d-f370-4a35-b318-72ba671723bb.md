## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in nearly all nearby possible worlds, it remains true regardless of the method applied for believing it**, **Ensuring method-neutral safety eliminates knowledge dependency on specific epistemic procedures**
