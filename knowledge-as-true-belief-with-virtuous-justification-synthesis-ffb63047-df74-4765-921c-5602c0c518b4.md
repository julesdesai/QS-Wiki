## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is justified through intellectual virtues**, **Intellectual virtues include traits like open-mindedness and intellectual courage**
