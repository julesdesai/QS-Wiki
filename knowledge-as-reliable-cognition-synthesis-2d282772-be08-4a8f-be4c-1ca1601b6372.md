## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief arises from cognitive processes that are reliably truth-conducive**, **Reliable cognitive processes consistently lead to true beliefs independent of virtue considerations**
