# Is tracking sufficient for knowing necessary truths?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** adc4a542-f19b-413d-aafe-c9ba969a6d7b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Nozick's Tracking Theory](nozicks-tracking-theory-synthesis-92e582e4-32a5-4017-b62b-ea7485c8afb8.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person believes a necessary proposition, such as a mathematical truth. In such a scenario, intuitively we would judge that the belief does track the truth in all possible worlds, but this does not seem sufficient for knowledge.**, **It is implausible to suggest that tracking across possible worlds fully captures the knowing of necessary truths, as these truths hold in all possible worlds by their nature.**
