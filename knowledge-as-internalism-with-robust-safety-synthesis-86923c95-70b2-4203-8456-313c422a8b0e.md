**Parent:** [Can a belief be justified if the justifying conditions are external and unknown to the believer?](can-a-belief-be-justified-if-the-justifying-conditions-are-external-and-unknown-to-the-believer-antithesis-b9515134-74b5-43e0-96f2-3371b31ce869.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is safe from error in most nearby possible worlds**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, internal mental states provide the justification for the belief and the belief is robustly safe from error**
