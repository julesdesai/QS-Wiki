# How do we handle Gettier cases under virtue epistemology?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1018762b-5aff-4a8a-8ed4-2087c8f10698
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology with Anti-Luck Condition](virtue-epistemology-with-anti-luck-condition-synthesis-d2e613e4-56dd-4edb-a19a-a70d714eba0b.md)

## Content

**In a Gettier-like case, someone forms a belief using strong intellectual virtues, such as logical reasoning, but the belief is accidentally true due to misleading evidence. Intuitively, the belief lacks knowledge since it relies on luck in the form of misleading circumstances.**, **Even if intellectual virtues are used, the presence of epistemic luck from external factors cannot guarantee the exclusion of luck, questioning the overall claim of virtue epistemology.**
