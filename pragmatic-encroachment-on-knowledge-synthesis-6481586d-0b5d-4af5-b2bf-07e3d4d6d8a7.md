## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that practical factors can partly determine whether a true, justified belief is knowledge**, **A person knows a proposition if it is true, they believe it, they have justification for it, and practical factors support it**
