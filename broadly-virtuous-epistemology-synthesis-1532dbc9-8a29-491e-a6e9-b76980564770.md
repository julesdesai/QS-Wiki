## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their true belief results from a broadly construed intellectual excellence**, **Intellectual excellence includes virtues and capacities for rational intuition**
