# Does intellectual responsibility guarantee reliability?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** ee6d5e76-c1c4-4bb4-8ab7-81ad9e11ad53
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Responsibilist Virtue Epistemology](responsibilist-virtue-epistemology-synthesis-d65854c6-8623-4bd4-aed3-f2a4bfae69c9.md)

## Content

**A person can be intellectually responsible yet rely on unreliable cognitive processes.**, **An unreliable process can sometimes produce true beliefs.**, **It seems problematic to count beliefs from unreliable processes as knowledge even if responsibly held.**
