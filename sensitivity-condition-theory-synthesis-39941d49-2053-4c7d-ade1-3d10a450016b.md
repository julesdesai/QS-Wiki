## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **Knowledge requires the belief to be sensitive to the truth**, **A belief is sensitive if, were the proposition false, the person would not believe it**
