# Can a person have knowledge without being aware of possible defeaters?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e7123856-1a55-40f5-a9c7-35a2e65137cc
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [No-Defeaters Theory of Knowledge](no-defeaters-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-0be8e529-9437-4b4e-96a3-c585180c1210.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person has a true belief based on incomplete information, unaware of potential defeaters. Intuitively, we might still believe they know the proposition.**, **Awareness or ignorance of defeaters does not intuitively affect the status of a justified true belief as knowledge.**
