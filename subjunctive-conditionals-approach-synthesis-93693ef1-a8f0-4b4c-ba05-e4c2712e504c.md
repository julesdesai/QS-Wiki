# Subjunctive Conditionals Approach

**Parent:** [Can there be knowledge without belief?](can-there-be-knowledge-without-belief-antithesis-c4e8ce99-d6e4-466a-a3d4-a5ebfd754238.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were true, the person would believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is not necessary that they consciously endorse the belief**
