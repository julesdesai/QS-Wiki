# Do intellectual virtues mischaracterize accidental knowledge?

## Content

**A case in which some person believes that a proposition through a random event such as a lottery win also turns out to coincide with intellectual virtues misleadingly attributed, does not appear to align with intellectual virtues truly. However, people might still attribute knowledge in such cases.**
