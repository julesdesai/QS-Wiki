## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief stems from a virtue or process which is pragmatically successful**, **Pragmatic success involves reliably achieving true beliefs as judged by practical outcomes**
