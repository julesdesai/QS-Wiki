# Knowledge as Sensitivity

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** cfba00e3-55cf-475a-8721-9702452e6e98
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is justified belief infallible?](is-justified-belief-infallible-antithesis-1bc11ed6-8712-46c2-a339-490029245ddf.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **A person is justified in believing a proposition if and only if their belief in that proposition would not persist if it were false**, **Sensitivity ensures that beliefs track the truth across possible scenarios**
