# Can infinite reasons truly lead to justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 1b55ed97-dde0-464e-ad1d-992f5b158e5f
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism in Epistemic Justification](infinitism-in-epistemic-justification-synthesis-7e8bc411-aa66-4d88-bdc9-2fb4f4857304.md)

## Content

**An infinite regress of reasons seems to make justification impossible because it prevents a final, definitive reason that unequivocally justifies the belief.**, **It is implausible to claim that an individual can comprehensively possess or understand an infinite number of reasons.**
