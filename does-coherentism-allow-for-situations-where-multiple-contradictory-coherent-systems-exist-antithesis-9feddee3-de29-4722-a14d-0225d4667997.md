# Does coherentism allow for situations where multiple contradictory coherent systems exist?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9feddee3-de29-4722-a14d-0225d4667997
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Coherentism](coherentism-synthesis-56ae39cf-eba6-4a99-aaa4-0dbe8a5aac85.md)

## Content

**There can be several internally coherent belief systems that are in direct contradiction with each other.**, **If multiple belief systems are deemed coherent, coherentism cannot resolve which one is correct without appealing to external criteria.**, **The existence of multiple contradictory yet coherent systems challenges the idea of coherence as the sole arbiter of justification.**
