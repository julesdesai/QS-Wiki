## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the truth of the proposition is not a matter of luck**, **A belief qualifies as knowledge if it would not be easily incorrect in similar scenarios**
