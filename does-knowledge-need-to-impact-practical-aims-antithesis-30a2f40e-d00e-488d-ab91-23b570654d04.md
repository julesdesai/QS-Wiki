# Does knowledge need to impact practical aims?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 30a2f40e-d00e-488d-ab91-23b570654d04
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Theory of Knowledge](pragmatic-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-af5a1410-77c2-4ceb-b390-2ced8876b488.md)

## Content

**Suppose a person knows the solution to a complex philosophical puzzle that has no bearing on their day-to-day actions or goals. Intuitively, their knowledge is genuine even if it doesn’t affect their practical aims.**, **For a person to know something, it must not necessarily influence their practical aims; rather, knowledge can exist independently of its practical impact.**
