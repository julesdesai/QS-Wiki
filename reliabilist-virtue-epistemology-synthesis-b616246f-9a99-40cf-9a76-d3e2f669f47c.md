## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a process that is generally reliable**, **A process is generally reliable if it produces a high percentage of true beliefs over false ones**
