# Is the reliability of cognitive processes universally applicable?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 11d4a689-649e-449d-881e-3107b0b61161
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism](reliabilism-synthesis-4db07ebc-4cb3-402b-b335-2b01121c3e1a.md)

## Content

**Suppose a process is reliable in one environment but not in another, such as color vision in good lighting versus bad lighting. Intuitively, it seems a belief based on such a process should not be considered knowledge in the poor lighting scenario**, **It is implausible that environmental context does not affect the reliability requirements for knowledge**
