# Can an external observer reliably assess the reliability of belief-forming processes?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 265e8775-2c92-4cad-92c6-b1439cf413d4
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilist View](reliabilist-view-synthesis-e6df444a-c8a0-46a3-91c3-0432faee253d.md)

## Content

**A process judged from an external standpoint may seem reliable but lacks reliability when variables invisible to the observer are included.**, **An experiment misjudged as consistent due to unobserved factors offers a problematic basis for reliable assessment.**
