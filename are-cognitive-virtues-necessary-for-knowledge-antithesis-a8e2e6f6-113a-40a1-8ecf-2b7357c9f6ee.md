# Are cognitive virtues necessary for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a8e2e6f6-113a-40a1-8ecf-2b7357c9f6ee
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-51f9ed1e-8cbb-4e95-8090-3e1317ce9636.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario where an individual makes a correct prediction by chance without any particular intellectual trait aiding the prediction.**, **Believing a true proposition without the aid of a cognitive virtue suggests that the virtue is not requisite for knowledge.**, **It is plausible for people to have true beliefs through reliable external processes unrelated to their intellectual character.**
