# Contextualist Tracking Theory

**Parent:** [What about cases where contradictory beliefs track the truth?](what-about-cases-where-contradictory-beliefs-track-the-truth-antithesis-c6e66ba2-0402-439b-9a68-227ce98d4a0e.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were true, the person would believe it**, **For the context in which a proposition is believed, it is necessary to determine the conditions under which beliefs track the truth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and for the context, the belief consistently tracks the truth**, **For the knowledge status to remain, no belief in the context should be contradictory with another tracked belief in the context**
