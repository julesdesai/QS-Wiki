## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief tracks the truth across close possible worlds**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they can verify or confirm the tracking mechanism, even if they do not understand it fully**
