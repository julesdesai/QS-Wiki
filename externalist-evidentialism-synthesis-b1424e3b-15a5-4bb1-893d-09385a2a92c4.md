## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that belief is justified based on available external evidence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is true and belief is justified by sufficient external evidence**
