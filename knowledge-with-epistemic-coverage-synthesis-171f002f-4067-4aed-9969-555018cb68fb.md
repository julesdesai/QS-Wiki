## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that all relevant factors, including environmental ones, are covered and pose no defeaters**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and all relevant factors are covered without defeaters**
