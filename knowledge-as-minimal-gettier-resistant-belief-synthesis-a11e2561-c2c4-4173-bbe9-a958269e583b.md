# Knowledge as Minimal Gettier-resistant Belief

**Parent:** [Can accidental truths formed by intellectual virtues be considered knowledge?](can-accidental-truths-formed-by-intellectual-virtues-be-considered-knowledge-antithesis-0bd48587-ac16-45de-b013-bdd928343aed.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief avoids Gettier-style problems**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief avoids Gettier-style problems**
