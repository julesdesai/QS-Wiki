# How can implicit knowledge be reconciled with the necessity of truth?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a35a1a82-b2be-4454-b701-7164a0c40f37
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Non-Reductive Physicalism on Implicit Knowledge](non-reductive-physicalism-on-implicit-knowledge-synthesis-6ae3dfb7-99c7-43ae-97da-3cb03e8b87cf.md)

## Content

**A scenario in which someone implicitly knows how to ride a bicycle but holds a false belief about the mechanics of riding raises questions about how truth is essential for knowledge.**, **In such a case, the person can ride effectively without understanding the truth about balance dynamics.**, **It is intuitively odd to say that someone has knowledge when their understanding of the truth is lacking.**
