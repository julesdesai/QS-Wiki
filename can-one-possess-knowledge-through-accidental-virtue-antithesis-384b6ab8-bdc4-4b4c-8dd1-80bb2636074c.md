# Can one possess knowledge through accidental virtue?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 384b6ab8-bdc4-4b4c-8dd1-80bb2636074c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-d7a894af-29e5-42fe-8e5e-957e2009566d.md)

## Content

**Consider a student who accidentally learns about a historical event through an unrelated social media post, without exercising any discernible intellectual virtue.**, **If the student possesses knowledge of this fact, it raises the possibility that the exercise of virtue might not be necessary for knowledge.**
