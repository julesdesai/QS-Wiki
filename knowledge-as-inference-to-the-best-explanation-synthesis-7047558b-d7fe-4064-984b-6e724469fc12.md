**Parent:** [Do cases of perceptual illusions that track truth in some instances challenge knowledge claims?](do-cases-of-perceptual-illusions-that-track-truth-in-some-instances-challenge-knowledge-claims-antithesis-0c007fe1-fa6f-4031-ab8a-82fc264e8397.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief constitutes the best explanation of the evidence available to them**
