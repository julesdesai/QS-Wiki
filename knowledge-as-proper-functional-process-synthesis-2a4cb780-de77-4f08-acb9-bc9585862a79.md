# Knowledge as Proper Functional Process

**Parent:** [Is a reliable cognitive process always sufficient for knowledge?](is-a-reliable-cognitive-process-always-sufficient-for-knowledge-antithesis-bc6da06b-3af6-42a9-b454-eb1e3659db8b.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed through cognitive faculties functioning properly in an appropriate environment**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is formed through cognitive faculties functioning properly in an appropriate environment**
