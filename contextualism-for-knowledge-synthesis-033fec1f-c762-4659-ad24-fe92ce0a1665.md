## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is justified considering the contextual standards of justification**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is true and the belief is justified in the prevailing context**
