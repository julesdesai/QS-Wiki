# Can true belief be disrupted by ignorance of relevant truths?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 358630b1-4e11-4e02-83cc-82d995785a4b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [No defeaters theory of knowledge](no-defeaters-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-293021ef-516a-4641-9c48-087d1223e339.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which an individual firmly believes in a true proposition based on personal experience. Intuitively, one would suggest that crucial yet unknown external factors, if discovered, might present a different perspective that negates the initial belief.**, **The obedience to truth and ignorance of countervailing truths seems inadequate as exclusive criteria for personal knowledge.**
