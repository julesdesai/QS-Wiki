# Must justification consider potential objections?

**Parent:** [No defeaters theory of knowledge](no-defeaters-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-293021ef-516a-4641-9c48-087d1223e339.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that knowledge requires the highest degree of justification. If the justified belief can be overturned by potentially discoverable information, it casts doubt on whether it was genuinely justified**, **A person's belief may conform to all known criteria for knowledge, yet if potentially refutable, it suggests a deeper requirement for justification beyond mere truth.**
