## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that coherence is determined by context-specific factors**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is formed via a reliable process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, coherence is determined by context-specific factors, and their belief is formed via a reliable process**
