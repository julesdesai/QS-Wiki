# Knowledge as Safe Belief

**Parent:** [Are unconsciously held beliefs derived from virtues knowledge?](are-unconsciously-held-beliefs-derived-from-virtues-knowledge-antithesis-e65bbc60-3b2d-44da-a80d-ebebb7a86885.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is such that in nearby possible worlds where they form the belief in the same way, the belief remains true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is safe**
