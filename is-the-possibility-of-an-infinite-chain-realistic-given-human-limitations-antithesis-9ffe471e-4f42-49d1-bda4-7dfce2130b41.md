# Is the possibility of an infinite chain realistic given human limitations?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 9ffe471e-4f42-49d1-bda4-7dfce2130b41
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Infinitism](infinitism-synthesis-fd5e1271-f2df-4333-a434-50b9d95350fd.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that a person could possess or construct an infinite series of reasons supporting a belief due to cognitive and lifespan constraints.**, **Human memory and reasoning capabilities are limited, thus calling into question the feasibility of an actual infinite chain of reasons.**, **Suppose justification requires an infinite series of reasons; in such a case, practical and psychological limitations preclude realization of such infinite justification.**
