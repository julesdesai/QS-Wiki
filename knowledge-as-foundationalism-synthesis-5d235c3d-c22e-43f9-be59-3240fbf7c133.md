## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is founded on basic beliefs that are self-evident or evident to the senses**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief is based on these basic beliefs**
