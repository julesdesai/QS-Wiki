# Evidentialism with Externalist Justification

**Parent:** [Is internal access to evidence necessary for knowledge?](is-internal-access-to-evidence-necessary-for-knowledge-antithesis-73b4938b-d60f-409b-9b04-81d41700803c.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is supported by adequate evidence, which need not be accessible yet must causally influence belief**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is supported by adequate evidence causally influencing the belief**
