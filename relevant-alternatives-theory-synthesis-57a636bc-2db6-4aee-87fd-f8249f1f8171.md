**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person can rule out relevant alternatives that might make the proposition false**, **The theory focuses on a person's ability to differentiate between actually possible scenarios**
