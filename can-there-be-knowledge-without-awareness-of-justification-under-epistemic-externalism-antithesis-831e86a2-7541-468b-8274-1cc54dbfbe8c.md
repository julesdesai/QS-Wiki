# Can there be knowledge without awareness of justification under epistemic externalism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 831e86a2-7541-468b-8274-1cc54dbfbe8c
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Epistemic Externalism](epistemic-externalism-synthesis-da0a1d28-64c8-4b8c-8417-38bce9b3007e.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person holds a true belief justified by external factors but lacks any awareness of what justifies the belief. Intuitively, we would judge that without such awareness, the belief cannot constitute knowledge.**, **A belief justified by unknown external factors seems insufficient for knowledge if the person is unaware of these justifications.**, **It is implausible that a person can have knowledge without being aware of the justification for their belief.**
