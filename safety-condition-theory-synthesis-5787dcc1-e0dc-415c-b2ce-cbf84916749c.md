## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is true in the actual world**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the person believes the proposition, then it is not easily false**, **If the proposition were not true, the person would not believe it**, **If the proposition were true in a similar situation, the person would still believe it**
