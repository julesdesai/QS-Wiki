# Contextualist Virtue Epistemology

**Parent:** [Is it possible to possess knowledge without conscious virtue?](is-it-possible-to-possess-knowledge-without-conscious-virtue-antithesis-2a4059bb-fab1-4561-bcc9-16f5b2f88060.md)

## Content

**Knowledge depends on the context in which the belief is formed**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief is formed in a context that supports the truth**, **Intellectual virtue contributes to understanding the context but is not a strict requirement for knowledge**
