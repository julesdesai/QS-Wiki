## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a process that the person has consciously endorsed and integrated into their cognitive system**, **Integration ensures belief is not coincidental because the process is part of a reliable cognitive framework**
