# Must intellectual virtues be unique to individuals?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** bf9fe2f9-d05e-4206-a81b-47524b44a3fd
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology Knowledge Summary](virtue-epistemology-knowledge-summary-synthesis-93cb2e65-9f1c-412f-9271-07ea69b7164c.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that intellectual virtues need to be uniquely individualized because similar virtues can apply to different people equally.**, **If virtues are not unique, then they may not provide a stable individual basis for forming true beliefs.**
