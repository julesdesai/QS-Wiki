# Knowledge as Coherence with Robust Objectivity

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does coherence adequately deal with biased information processing?](does-coherence-adequately-deal-with-biased-information-processing-antithesis-78659fd8-b40a-4a09-affe-d36040e0ee6a.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification involves a coherent set of beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the coherence of beliefs is robust against biased information**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and there exists a coherent set of beliefs robust against biases justifying the belief**
