## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For foundational beliefs, it is necessary that they are self-justifying or justified by experience**, **For non-foundational beliefs, it is necessary that they derive their justification from foundational beliefs or justified beliefs**, **Memories are justified if they are reliably connected to original experiences that were justified**
