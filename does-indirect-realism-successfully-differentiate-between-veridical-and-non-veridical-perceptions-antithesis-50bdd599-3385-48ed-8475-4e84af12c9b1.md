## Content

**Veridical perceptions are those that accurately represent reality, while non-veridical perceptions do not.**, **Indirect realism relies on mental representations that should correlate with reality to define veridicality, yet such correlations can sometimes be indistinguishable.**, **The difficulty in establishing clear criteria for distinguishing perceptions within indirect realism raises issues about its ability to handle veridicality distinctions.**
