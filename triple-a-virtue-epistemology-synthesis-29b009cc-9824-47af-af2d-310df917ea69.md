## Content

**Knowledge involves accuracy, adroitness, and aptness**, **Accuracy means the belief is true**, **Adroitness means the belief is formed through intellectual skill**, **Aptness means the truth of the belief is primarily because of the intellectual skill, not because of luck**, **For a person to know a proposition, the belief must be apt, even if external factors introduce some luck**
