## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that justification involves membership in a coherent system of beliefs**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the coherence of beliefs is achieved through reliable cognitive processes**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their beliefs are coherently connected through reliable processes**
