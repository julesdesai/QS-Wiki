# Knowledge as Virtue Epistemology with Anti-Luck Condition

**Node Type:** synthesis

**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Does reliability ignore luck affecting knowledge?](does-reliability-ignore-luck-affecting-knowledge-antithesis-f5d70159-4fac-4e3c-9412-1462fd59b9fd.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed through intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is not subject to significant luck**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have a true belief formed through intellectual virtue without reliance on luck**
