## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person holds no false beliefs related to the proposition**, **Knowledge requires the individual’s supporting beliefs to all be true**
