## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief depends on a stable and reliable relation to the truth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for them to understand the explanation behind the truth of the proposition**
