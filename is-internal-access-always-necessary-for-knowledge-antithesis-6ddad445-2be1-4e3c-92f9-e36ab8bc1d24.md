# Is internal access always necessary for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 6ddad445-2be1-4e3c-92f9-e36ab8bc1d24
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Internalist Reliabilism Summary](internalist-reliabilism-summary-synthesis-afd99030-664a-4a62-9431-6f8bc1e36717.md)

## Content

**Imagine a case where someone has true beliefs formed by reliable methods but lacks internal access to the reliability of these methods.**, **Their lack of internal awareness appears irrelevant to whether they truly have knowledge.**, **This casts doubt on the necessity of internal access for genuine knowledge.**
