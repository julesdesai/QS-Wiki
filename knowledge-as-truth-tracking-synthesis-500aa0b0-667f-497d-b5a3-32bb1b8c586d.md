# Knowledge as Truth-Tracking

**Parent:** [Are reliable processes necessary when beliefs are formed with no cognitive basis?](are-reliable-processes-necessary-when-beliefs-are-formed-with-no-cognitive-basis-antithesis-f78f42df-c00a-4987-9858-a105fe429a4a.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief tracks the truth through counterfactual scenarios**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief tracks the truth through counterfactual scenarios**
