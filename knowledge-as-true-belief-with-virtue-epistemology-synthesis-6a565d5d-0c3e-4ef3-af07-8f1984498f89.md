# Knowledge as True Belief with Virtue Epistemology

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief arises from intellectual virtue**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have virtuously acquired justification, and the proposition is true**
