## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed by a reliable process**, **A person knows a proposition if they believe it**, **For a person to know a proposition, the belief must be such that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **A reliable process is one that produces true beliefs more often than false ones**
