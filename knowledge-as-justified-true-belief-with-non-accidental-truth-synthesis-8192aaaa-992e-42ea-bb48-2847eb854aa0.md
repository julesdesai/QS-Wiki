## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed through a reliable process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the truth of the belief is not accidental, given how it was formed**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is formed through a reliable process, and the truth is not accidental**
