# Is coherent belief necessary for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f53972fa-7b98-4daa-8c35-9d0840c59eed
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalist View](externalist-view-synthesis-75028f40-4ef7-4feb-a21d-1e6a75d787a6.md)

## Content

**For knowledge, it is possible that a network of internally coherent beliefs is necessary, not just reliability of individual beliefs.**, **A person could have isolated reliable beliefs without coherence to other beliefs, making it questionable whether this is genuine knowledge.**
