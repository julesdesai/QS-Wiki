## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is reliably produced**, **For a person's belief to be reliably produced, it is necessary that it frequently aligns with the truth in the environments the person typically inhabits**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that their belief is reliably produced and the proposition is true**
