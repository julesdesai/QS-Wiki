# Normative Justification Augmentation

**Parent:** [Can mere reliability capture the normative aspect of knowledge?](can-mere-reliability-capture-the-normative-aspect-of-knowledge-antithesis-5035376d-44b3-488e-8063-2823480d07b1.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is normatively justified**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process, and the belief is normatively justified**
