# Can ethical principles hold meaning under verificationism?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 08417d9a-c15d-4637-af59-3e9bdca6e2cb
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Verificationism](verificationism-synthesis-742a7e84-42a2-4d13-8c35-af2e03d93250.md)

## Content

**Moral statements like "killing is wrong" cannot be empirically verified through perception alone**, **If a statement only has meaning if it can be empirically verified, then moral principles would be meaningless**, **It is implausible to claim that universally held moral principles lack meaning because they guide human behavior and societal laws**
