# Knowledge as True Belief with No False Lemmas

**Parent:** [Can justified false beliefs undermine the concept of knowledge as justified true belief?](can-justified-false-beliefs-undermine-the-concept-of-knowledge-as-justified-true-belief-antithesis-df9353dc-712d-49d9-bcef-ccc1ae6c2899.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief to be justified**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is justified, and no false belief is used in the justification process**
