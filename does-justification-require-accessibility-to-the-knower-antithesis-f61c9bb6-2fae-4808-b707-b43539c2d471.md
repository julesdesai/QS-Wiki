# Does justification require accessibility to the knower?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f61c9bb6-2fae-4808-b707-b43539c2d471
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism without Belief](reliabilism-without-belief-synthesis-5ad6402e-0bf8-4ba4-9e47-6461ce29b289.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that justification should be something a person can reflect upon and explain. This principle, together with the notion that justification does not need to be accessible to the knower, leads to a contradiction regarding personal accountability in knowing.**
