# How does indirect realism account for perceptual relativity?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 30fccfd7-cd16-42fe-9c8a-4e8027e9dfb3
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Indirect Realism](indirect-realism-synthesis-801f6a7d-c909-434a-bdd9-effe8ebd5964.md)

## Content

**Consider the scenario where the same object appears differently to observers in varying conditions, such as lighting or angle. Intuitively, this suggests the variation in mental representation without a clear way to determine which, if any, reflects reality.**, **There is a plausible principle that perceiving reality should yield consistent mental representations. This principle together with the observed inconsistency entails that indirect realism struggles to explain how perception leads to knowledge.**
