## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that, if the proposition were not true, the person would not believe it**, **This condition reflects a connection between belief and the truth of the proposition**
