## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief be justified based on epistemic standards**, **Epistemic standards are determined by the context of the person making the knowledge claim**
