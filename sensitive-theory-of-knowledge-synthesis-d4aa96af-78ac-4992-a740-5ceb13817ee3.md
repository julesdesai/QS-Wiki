## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary to handle all potential defeating evidence**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and all potential defeating evidence is handled**
