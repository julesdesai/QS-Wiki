# Does pragmatic encroachment threaten epistemic rights?

**Node Type:** antithesis

**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Pragmatic Encroachment with Influence of Practical Stakes](pragmatic-encroachment-with-influence-of-practical-stakes-synthesis-1e83d295-b88f-4064-889e-097b4fb938ca.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that one's epistemic rights, like the right to claim knowledge, should be contingent on varying practical stakes, as epistemic rights should be consistent and evidence-based.**, **Suppose we have a case in which a person set to undergo surgery must determine the success rate of the procedure. Intuitively, practical stakes should not limit the person’s epistemic right to claim knowledge of these statistics based on available evidence.**
