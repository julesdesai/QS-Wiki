# Knowledge as Process Reliabilism

**Parent:** [Can an unaware individual have knowledge through reliable processes?](can-an-unaware-individual-have-knowledge-through-reliable-processes-antithesis-52a2c1fd-389f-45a7-87a3-47fe7def6f4d.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the belief to be produced by an environment-appropriate reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is produced by an environment-appropriate reliable cognitive process**
