# Is sensitivity to truth relevant for a priori knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** d55d4664-f085-43c6-bb8e-57c4107f024d
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Nozick's Conditional Entailment](nozicks-conditional-entailment-synthesis-db761f27-9caf-47b5-bb68-5ac892feba1b.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person knows that all bachelors are unmarried based on the meaning of terms.**, **It is implausible to require sensitivity to hypothetical scenarios where the person would not believe it unless the proposition were true, as the truth stems from definitions.**, **A plausible principle suggests that a priori knowledge relies on comprehension, which seems detached from sensitivity considerations.**
