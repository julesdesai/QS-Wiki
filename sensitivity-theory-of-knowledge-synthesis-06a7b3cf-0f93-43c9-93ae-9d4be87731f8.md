## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were false, the person would not believe it**, **for a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief remains stable through most nearby possible worlds**
