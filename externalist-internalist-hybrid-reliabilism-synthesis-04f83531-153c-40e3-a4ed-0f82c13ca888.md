## Content

**Knowledge is a true belief formed by a process that is both reliable externally and justified internally**, **The external component involves the general reliability of the belief-forming process**, **The internal component involves the subject's justification for trusting the process relative to the context**, **Knowledge arises when there is harmony between external reliability and internal justification**
