## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, their belief must be apt**, **A person's belief is apt if it is true because of their intellectual competence**, **The truth of the belief must stem from the believer's skill or competence**, **Both internal competence and external enabling conditions are required for a belief to be apt**, **External conditions must assist, rather than hinder, the belief's truth acquisition**
