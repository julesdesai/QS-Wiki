# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with No Defeaters

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** 870ee8e9-cb4d-482e-81a6-1c61b5a8b8a1
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Can sensitivity account for knowledge of necessary truths?](can-sensitivity-account-for-knowledge-of-necessary-truths-antithesis-5bbe5b5e-eef8-436c-90e5-a480dffc0a55.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person has justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there are no defeating conditions that undermine the justification**
