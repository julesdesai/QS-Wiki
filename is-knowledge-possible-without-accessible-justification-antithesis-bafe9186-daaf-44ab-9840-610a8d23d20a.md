# Is knowledge possible without accessible justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** bafe9186-daaf-44ab-9840-610a8d23d20a
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Externalism to knowledge with reliable cognitive process](externalism-to-knowledge-with-reliable-cognitive-process-synthesis-e78416a8-ed0b-42d6-b63b-0cc8fec7b97b.md)

## Content

**In some cases, a person could have a belief that is formed through a reliable cognitive process but is completely unaware of such reliability.**, **Intuitively, we might judge that if the person is unaware of the reliability of their process, they lack knowledge.**, **This suggests that accessibility to justification may be necessary for knowledge.**
