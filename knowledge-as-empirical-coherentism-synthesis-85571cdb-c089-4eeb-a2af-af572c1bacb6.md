# Knowledge as Empirical Coherentism

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Can foundational beliefs be too subjective?](can-foundational-beliefs-be-too-subjective-antithesis-7019339f-85ba-483c-be8f-103fbfc5ef01.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief forms part of a coherent empirical network**, **The network of beliefs is evaluated against empirical data rather than subjective criteria**
