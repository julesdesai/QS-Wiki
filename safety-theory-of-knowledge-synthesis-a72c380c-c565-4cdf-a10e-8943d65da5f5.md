# Safety Theory of Knowledge

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in nearly all nearby possible worlds where the person forms the belief in the same way, the belief is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is true in nearly all nearby possible worlds when formed in the same way**
