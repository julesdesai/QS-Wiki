# Is the concept of epistemic virtue too vague?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 3180b772-5cf5-4d7f-8ad3-2626bf9e3a10
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-8faa0d3d-af6f-4054-8090-da1e790293ff.md)

## Content

**The notion of epistemic virtue might be insufficiently precise, making the definition too broad or non-restrictive.**, **Different interpretations of what counts as an epistemic virtue can lead to varying assessments of the same situation, which undermines the stability of the theory.**, **A precise and narrow definition of epistemic virtue is necessary for consistent application in evaluating knowledge claims.**
