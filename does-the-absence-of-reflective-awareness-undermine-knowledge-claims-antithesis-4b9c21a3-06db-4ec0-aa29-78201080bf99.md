# Does the absence of reflective awareness undermine knowledge claims?

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology With Virtuous Belief Formation](virtue-epistemology-with-virtuous-belief-formation-synthesis-53b228ae-7464-400d-be01-037da588b5a0.md)

## Content

**Suppose an individual forms true beliefs based on their intuitive expertise without reflective awareness of how or why their belief-forming process is reliable.**, **These beliefs align with truth through consistent reliability but lack conscious understanding or awareness.**, **Intuitively, the lack of reflective insight or understanding might imply that these aren't full-fledged knowledge claims despite their truth.**
