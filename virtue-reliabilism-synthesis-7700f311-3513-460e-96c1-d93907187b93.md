## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is reliably produced through an intellectual virtue**, **An intellectual virtue is a trait that systematically leads to acquiring true beliefs**, **Reliable production does not require the element of belief**
