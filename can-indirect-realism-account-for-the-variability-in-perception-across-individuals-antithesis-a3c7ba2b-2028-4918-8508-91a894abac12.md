# Can Indirect Realism account for the variability in perception across individuals?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a3c7ba2b-2028-4918-8508-91a894abac12
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Indirect Realism Summary](indirect-realism-summary-synthesis-1b3cd8c8-92a3-4a52-be84-8ec951a45683.md)

## Content

**Perception varies significantly among individuals based on differing sensory and cognitive processes.**, **A plausible principle is that knowledge, if objectively true, should not vary among individuals.**, **If knowledge is based on mental representations that differ among individuals, then objective knowledge is threatened.**
