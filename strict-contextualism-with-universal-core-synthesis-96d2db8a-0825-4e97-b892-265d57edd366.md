# Strict Contextualism with Universal Core

**Parent:** [Is contextually determined justification inconsistent with intuitive knowledge claims?](is-contextually-determined-justification-inconsistent-with-intuitive-knowledge-claims-antithesis-a3cc39d7-8764-4f97-85ba-81b5862dd1b6.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that justification is primarily determined by context but anchored to a universal core**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the justification meets contextually determined standards anchored by a universal core**
