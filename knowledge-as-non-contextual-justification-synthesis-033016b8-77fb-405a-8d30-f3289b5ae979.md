# Knowledge as Non-Contextual Justification

**Depth:** 5

**Parent:** [Can knowledge be independent of conversational context?](can-knowledge-be-independent-of-conversational-context-antithesis-2ae861b8-228c-4bd3-ba7d-46e953f369f8.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for their belief to be justified independent of context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have justified true belief articulated in a non-contextual manner**
