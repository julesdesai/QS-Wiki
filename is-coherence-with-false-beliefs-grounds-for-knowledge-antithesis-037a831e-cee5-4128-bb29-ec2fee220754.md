# Is coherence with false beliefs grounds for knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 037a831e-cee5-4128-bb29-ec2fee220754

**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualist Coherentism](contextualist-coherentism-synthesis-598dc05f-03d6-4aeb-b0db-fa7173390f96.md)

## Content

**A belief that the Earth is flat coheres with a network of flat Earth supporting beliefs and satisfies the coherence criteria but reflects no accurate knowledge.**, **Holding coherent but incorrect beliefs does not fulfill the requirements for knowledge, as it lacks a grounding in truth.**
