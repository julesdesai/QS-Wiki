# Knowledge as Anti-Luck Reliability

**Node Type:** synthesis
**Node ID:** e5995e3f-8691-4576-8af7-aad796441606
**Depth:** 5
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Is reliability alone sufficient for knowledge?](is-reliability-alone-sufficient-for-knowledge-antithesis-39939579-96f9-405e-9cf0-8dec2d5a4bb2.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the process producing the belief protects against epistemic luck**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the process producing the belief protects against epistemic luck**
