## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there is a causal connection between the belief and the truth of the proposition**, **The causal connection links the truth of the proposition to the person's belief in it**
