# Does truth-tracking address the skeptical challenge effectively?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 65d18d57-52d8-457b-b044-794d772579a7
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Nozick's Truth-Tracking Theory](nozicks-truth-tracking-theory-synthesis-1b212c88-0c4c-4dd5-88f4-3ea31a716dd5.md)

## Content

**It is implausible that truth-tracking conditions alone can refute radical skeptical scenarios that provide no distinction between true belief and mere appearance.**, **Consider a scenario in which a person is in a skeptical scenario such as a brain in a vat. In such a scenario, intuitively, the person meets no truth-tracking conditions but lacks knowledge regardless of thought experiment intuitions.**
