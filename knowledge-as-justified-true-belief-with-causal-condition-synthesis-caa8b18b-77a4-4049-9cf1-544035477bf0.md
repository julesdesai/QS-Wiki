# Knowledge as Justified True Belief with Causal Condition

**Parent:** [Is perceptual knowledge undermined by illusions followed by realizations of true facts?](is-perceptual-knowledge-undermined-by-illusions-followed-by-realizations-of-true-facts-antithesis-2f163b4d-c4d8-4b68-9f13-12425dd9c273.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person has justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that there exists a causal connection between the person's belief and the truth of the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, have justification for believing the proposition, the proposition is true, and there is a causal connection**
