# Is epistemic externalism vulnerable to skepticism about the external world?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e48538c6-0c03-49ca-8780-35667d021cb5
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Epistemic Externalism](epistemic-externalism-synthesis-da0a1d28-64c8-4b8c-8417-38bce9b3007e.md)

## Content

**Suppose we live in a scenario where our beliefs are systematically deceived by an evil demon. Intuitively, we would judge that we do not have knowledge in such a scenario.**, **Reliance on external factors for knowledge makes it difficult to address scenarios involving extreme skepticism, such as the evil demon hypothesis.**, **It is unlikely that a theory of knowledge that struggles with skepticism about the external world offers a satisfactory account.**
