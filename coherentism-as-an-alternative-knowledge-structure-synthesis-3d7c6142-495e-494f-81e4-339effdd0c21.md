# Coherentism as an alternative knowledge structure

**Parent:** [Can foundationalism accommodate moral knowledge?](can-foundationalism-accommodate-moral-knowledge-antithesis-becee479-b7d2-4eb2-955f-54d00c3d0445.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the justification involves a belief that coheres with other beliefs in a system**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief coheres with other beliefs in a system**
