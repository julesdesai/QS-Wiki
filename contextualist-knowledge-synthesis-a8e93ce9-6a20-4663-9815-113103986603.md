## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the person holds the belief in the proposition under contextually relevant standards**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that the proposition is true, the person believes it, and holds it to contextually legitimate standards which adjust to epistemic context**
