# Does contextualism accommodate skepticism adequately?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 46d4128d-b875-4a53-a100-49cb244d86c4
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Contextualism in Knowledge](contextualism-in-knowledge-synthesis-25340e84-3972-4eff-b5ab-6d3b7cc92f60.md)

## Content

**Suppose skepticism argues that any or all knowledge claims can be doubted. Intuitively, context-based standards might avoid skepticism superficially but do not provide genuine resolutions.**, **It is implausible to wholly accept contextualism if it only addresses skepticism by shifting standards rather than countering skeptical doubts directly.**
