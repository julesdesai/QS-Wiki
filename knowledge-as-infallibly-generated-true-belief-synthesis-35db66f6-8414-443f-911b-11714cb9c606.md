# Knowledge as Infallibly Generated True Belief

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is generated by a cognitive process that infallibly produces truth**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is generated by an infallible truth-producing cognitive process**
