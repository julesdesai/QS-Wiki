# Does direct perception involve non-conceptual content?

## Content

**Some philosophers argue that perception includes non-conceptual content, which cannot be critically evaluated in the same way as conceptual content.**, **Non-conceptual content might escape direct criticism, posing a problem for the requirement of critical evaluation.**, **This raises the question of how critical direct realism accounts for non-conceptual elements within perceptual experiences.**
