# Can the priority of truth ignore the role of justification in knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 6e368d16-bfef-4877-9d22-42a43a42c126
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [True Belief as Primary in Knowledge](true-belief-as-primary-in-knowledge-synthesis-f83d0a87-6cde-4f50-a75a-0024fde50cce.md)

## Content

**Justification plays a critical role in considering a belief as knowledge, even when it is true.**, **Experiencing justification legitimizes a belief as knowledge, which conflicts with defining truth as the primary factor.**, **Neglecting justification in favor of truth undermines how beliefs are supported as knowledge.**
