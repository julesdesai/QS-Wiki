# Is a justified true belief with unnoticed defeaters still not knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** a779e4c0-ab14-4b4f-bc46-2bc3b8c430c0
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [No-Defeaters Theory of Knowledge](no-defeaters-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-0be8e529-9437-4b4e-96a3-c585180c1210.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which a person's belief is justified and true, but an unnoticed defeater exists such that, had it been noticed, it would undermine their belief. Intuitively, this remains a case of knowledge before they become aware of the defeater.**, **Knowledge should hinge on actual justification and truth rather than hypothetical defeaters one might discover.**
