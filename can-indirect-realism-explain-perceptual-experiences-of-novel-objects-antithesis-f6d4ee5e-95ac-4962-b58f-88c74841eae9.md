# Can indirect realism explain perceptual experiences of novel objects?

**Depth:** 4

## Content

**When encountering a novel object, individuals can still form accurate perceptual judgments without prior mental representations.**, **Without existing mental representations to mediate, indirect realism faces challenges explaining how one can know propositions about novel objects.**, **The view that knowledge involves indirect perception relies on pre-existing representations, which may not apply in cases of novel object perception.**
