# Knowledge as No False Grounds

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that none of their grounds for belief are false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and none of their grounds for belief are false**
