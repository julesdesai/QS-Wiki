# Intellectual Virtue Augmented Reliability Theory

**Parent:** [Might reliance solely on reliability ignore other necessary epistemic virtues?](might-reliance-solely-on-reliability-ignore-other-necessary-epistemic-virtues-antithesis-3bf3833f-9553-4df2-a568-9051856136de.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is accompanied by intellectual virtue augmentation**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is produced by a reliable cognitive process, and the belief is accompanied by intellectual virtue augmentation**
