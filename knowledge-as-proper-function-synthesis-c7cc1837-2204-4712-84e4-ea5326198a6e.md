## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief results from the proper functioning of their cognitive faculties**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief results from the proper functioning of their cognitive faculties**
