# Does epistemic relativism lead to skepticism about external knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** e5a04ca6-969f-4a44-bd9a-2b95e16e98d4
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Epistemic Relativism](epistemic-relativism-synthesis-8a36f430-fbe7-4356-93bb-efe42975c670.md)

## Content

**Consider that epistemic relativism asserts that knowledge is determined by community norms.**, **If knowledge claims are strictly internal to epistemic communities, external or objective validation of these claims becomes problematic.**, **Hence, relativism implies skepticism about whether any knowledge claims can be objectively verified outside one's community standards.**
