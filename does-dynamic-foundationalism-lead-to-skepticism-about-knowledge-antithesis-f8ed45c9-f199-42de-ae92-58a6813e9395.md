# Does dynamic foundationalism lead to skepticism about knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** f8ed45c9-f199-42de-ae92-58a6813e9395
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Dynamic Foundationalism](dynamic-foundationalism-synthesis-25d83013-c58a-42d7-a045-2c4c94be4d38.md)

## Content

**If foundational beliefs are always revisable, certainty about any belief becomes impossible.**, **Knowledge requires some degree of certainty about the foundational beliefs it rests upon.**, **A changing foundational belief can never provide the certainty usually required for knowledge.**
