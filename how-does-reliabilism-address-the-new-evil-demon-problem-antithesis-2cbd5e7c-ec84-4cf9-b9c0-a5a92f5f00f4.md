# How does reliabilism address the new evil demon problem?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 2cbd5e7c-ec84-4cf9-b9c0-a5a92f5f00f4
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Reliabilism](reliabilism-synthesis-a405882b-2e95-4de6-9817-4516092396fb.md)

## Content

**Consider a scenario in which individuals are deceived by an evil demon such that all their perceptual beliefs are false, though they are as reasonable and justified as possible given their evidence**, **Intuitively, we would still judge that these individuals have justified beliefs even if they do not have true beliefs**, **Reliabilism would counterintuitively require us to say these individuals' beliefs are not justified, since the demon ensures their cognitive processes do not meet the reliability criterion**
