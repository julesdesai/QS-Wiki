# Is coherence a necessary condition for all types of knowledge?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 65162a12-b4f5-40dc-9c93-06067237606e
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Temporal Coherentism](temporal-coherentism-synthesis-f4dfebfd-2f02-4b67-a5dd-1a5ab136482a.md)

## Content

**It is implausible to claim that coherence is necessary for all knowledge, as some knowledge might arise from isolated but accurate observations. Intuitively, coherence is not required for these types of knowledge.**
