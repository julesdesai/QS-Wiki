## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is the result of an intellectual virtue or ability**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief is not true merely due to luck**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief results from an intellectual virtue or ability, and their belief is not true merely due to luck**
