## Content

**A belief constitutes knowledge if produced by a reliable process sensitive to truth-conducive evidence**, **A reliable process is one that generally produces true beliefs when the evidence aligns with reality**, **A belief that constitutes knowledge must properly account for misleading evidence**, **The belief-forming process must filter out or adjust to misleading evidence to ensure truth**
