**Parent:** [Is true belief formed by a temporarily reliable process really knowledge?](is-true-belief-formed-by-a-temporarily-reliable-process-really-knowledge-antithesis-90aef009-b4ee-482d-b838-75f1073dfc63.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is formed by a process that is reliable in the relevant context**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and the belief is formed by a process that is reliable in the relevant context**
