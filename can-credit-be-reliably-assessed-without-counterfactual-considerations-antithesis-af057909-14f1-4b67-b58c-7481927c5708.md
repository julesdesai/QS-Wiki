# Can credit be reliably assessed without counterfactual considerations?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** af057909-14f1-4b67-b58c-7481927c5708
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Credit Theory of Knowledge](credit-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-f65ebe79-7fd3-4c3c-9fb3-cd47f9656422.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case in which a person knows a fact, but upon minor counterfactual changes would not. Intuitively, we either have to say that the agent deserves credit now, or does not if conditions change. If we adjust the credit based on counterfactuals, it raises challenges in consistent credit assessments.**
