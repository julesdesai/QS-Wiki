# Can hallucinations provide a counterexample to indirect realism?

## Content

**Consider the case of a person experiencing a hallucination where they see a pink elephant, despite no such object existing. Intuitively, hallucinations disrupt the connection to reality.**, **Hallucinations suggest that sense data can mislead and therefore weaken the claim that they mediate accurate perceptions for knowledge.**
