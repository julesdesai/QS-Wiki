# Does credit require infallibility?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** dd751a01-51ab-4505-9e61-fad41ba8f44b
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Credit Theory of Knowledge](credit-theory-of-knowledge-synthesis-f65ebe79-7fd3-4c3c-9fb3-cd47f9656422.md)

## Content

**There is a plausible principle that credit attribution requires the belief-forming process to eliminate error entirely. This principle together with the presence of fallible cognitive processes entails that many knowledge ascriptions may not be creditable.**
