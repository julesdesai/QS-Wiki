## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that in all nearby possible worlds where the person believes the proposition, the proposition is true**, **Safety requires that a belief could not have easily been false**
