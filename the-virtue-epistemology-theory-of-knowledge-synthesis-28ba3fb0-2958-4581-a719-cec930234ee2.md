## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the proposition is true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their true belief arises from an act of intellectual virtue**, **The intellectual virtue condition requires the belief to be reliably formed through cognitive abilities**
