## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that their belief could not easily have been false**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have an implicit understanding that their belief could not easily have been false and the proposition is true**
