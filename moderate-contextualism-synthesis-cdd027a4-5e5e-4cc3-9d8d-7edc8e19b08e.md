## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **Contextual differences influence some aspects of justification but do not undermine the overall objectivity of knowledge**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and both context-dependent and invariant standards of justification are met**
