# Knowledge as Reliable Foundationalism

**Parent:** [Does holding false but coherent beliefs challenge the view?](does-holding-false-but-coherent-beliefs-challenge-the-view-antithesis-01d1593a-3fdc-40b2-a6a3-8f2aff6286af.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief is based on foundational beliefs that are self-evident or directly experienced**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief-forming process is reliable**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, the belief is based on foundational beliefs, and the belief-forming process is reliable**
