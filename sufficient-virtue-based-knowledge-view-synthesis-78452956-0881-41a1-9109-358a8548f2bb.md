## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they have justification for believing the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary and sufficient that their belief is formed through a dominant reliable cognitive ability or intellectual virtue, which is not overridden by unreliable methods**
