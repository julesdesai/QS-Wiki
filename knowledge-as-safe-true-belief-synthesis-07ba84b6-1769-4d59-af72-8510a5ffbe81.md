## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that the belief would not easily be false in similar situations**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they have a safe belief that is true**
