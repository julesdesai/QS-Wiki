# Is knowledge solely dependent on intellectual virtues?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 10b40a23-5071-4d98-9512-f9dcfcb40e55
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Virtue Epistemology](virtue-epistemology-synthesis-91237602-868c-4a49-88c0-4e168ec082b8.md)

## Content

**A person can arrive at a true belief through processes that are not the result of intellectual virtues, such as luck or guesswork.**, **Suppose someone gains a true belief through random chance rather than a stable intellectual trait; it seems incorrect to say they have knowledge.**
