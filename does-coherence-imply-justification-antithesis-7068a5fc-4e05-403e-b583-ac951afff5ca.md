# Does coherence imply justification?

**Node Type:** antithesis
**Node ID:** 7068a5fc-4e05-403e-b583-ac951afff5ca
**Depth:** 4
**Terminal:** No

**Parent:** [Instrumental Coherence Theory](instrumental-coherence-theory-synthesis-188d4652-e220-47fa-9e2f-da98cf583190.md)

## Content

**Suppose we have a case where multiple erroneous perceptions, both direct and instrumentally aided, coherently support a false conclusion like assuming the time from a broken clock that agrees with a mistaken eyewitness. Intuitively, this coherence does not imply justified belief.**
