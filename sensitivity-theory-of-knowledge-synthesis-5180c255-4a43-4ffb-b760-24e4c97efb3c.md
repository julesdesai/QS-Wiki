# Sensitivity Theory of Knowledge

**Parent:** [Can one know something if the belief is true merely by coincidence?](can-one-know-something-if-the-belief-is-true-merely-by-coincidence-antithesis-a165db68-850e-48aa-91f0-2f29c8c907af.md)

## Content

**For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that they believe the proposition**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary for the proposition to be true**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is necessary that if the proposition were not true, the person would not believe it in the actual world**, **For a person to know a proposition, it is sufficient that they believe the proposition, the proposition is true, and their belief responds sensitively to the actual world's circumstances**
